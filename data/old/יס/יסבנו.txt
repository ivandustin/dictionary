Etymology: The word "יסבנו" (pronounced "yisbenu") is a Hebrew verb in the past tense form. It is derived from the root ס.ב.נ (s.b.n), which means "to surround" or "to encircle."

Grammar: "יסבנו" is a verb in the past tense form, third person plural. In Hebrew, verbs are conjugated according to the subject and tense. The past tense form indicates that the action described by the verb has already happened.

Description: The word "יסבנו" can be translated to English as "they surrounded" or "they encircled." It describes an action where a group of people or objects formed a circle or surrounded something/someone.
