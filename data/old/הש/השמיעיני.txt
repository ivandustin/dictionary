Grammar: "הַשְׁמִיעִינִי" is a verb in the imperative form, second person singular, masculine. It is used to command or request someone to make someone else hear or listen.

Description: The word "הַשְׁמִיעִינִי" is often translated as "make me hear" or "cause me to listen." It is used in various contexts to express a desire or request for someone to communicate or convey information to the speaker.
