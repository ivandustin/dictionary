Grammar: הַשְׁמָדוּת is a noun in the singular form. It is derived from the root שָׁמַד (shamad) and is formed by adding the suffix וּת (ut) to the root. 

Description: הַשְׁמָדוּת refers to the act of destruction or annihilation. It can be used to describe the complete devastation or obliteration of something or someone.
