Etymology: The word "אבתיך" (pronounced "avotecha") is a Hebrew word that means "your fathers." It is derived from the Hebrew root אב (pronounced "av"), which means "father."

Grammar: "אבתיך" is a plural noun in Hebrew. It is formed by adding the suffix יך (pronounced "echa") to the root אב. The suffix יך indicates possession, meaning "your."
