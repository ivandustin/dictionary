Grammar: In Hebrew, "אביהם" is a possessive noun phrase. It consists of the noun "אב" (father) and the possessive suffix "-יהם" (their). The suffix "-יהם" indicates that the father belongs to a group of people who are being referred to as "them."

Description: "אביהם" is used to refer to the father of a group of people. It is commonly used in biblical texts and in modern Hebrew to indicate the paternal relationship of a person to their children.
