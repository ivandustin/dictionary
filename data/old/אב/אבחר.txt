Etymology: The word "אבחר" (pronounced "avchar") comes from the Hebrew root א-ב-ח-ר, which means "to choose" or "to select." 

Grammar: "אבחר" is a verb in the Hebrew language, specifically in the Qal (simple) conjugation. It is in the first person singular form, indicating that the subject is the speaker themselves. 

Description: The word "אבחר" means "I choose" or "I select" in Hebrew. It expresses the action of making a deliberate decision or picking something or someone out from a group.
