Etymology: The word "אבנה" (pronounced "avna") is a Hebrew word that means "I will build." It is derived from the Hebrew root א-ב-נ (aleph-bet-nun), which means "to build" or "to construct."

Grammar: "אבנה" is a verb in the first person singular future tense in Hebrew. It is used when the speaker wants to express their intention or desire to build something in the future.

Description: "אבנה" is a word that conveys the act of building or constructing. It can be used in various contexts, such as building a physical structure, creating something figurative or abstract, or even establishing relationships or connections.
