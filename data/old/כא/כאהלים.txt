Grammar: "כאהלים" is a plural noun in Hebrew. It is formed by adding the suffix ים (im) to the singular form כאהל (kahal). The plural form is used to refer to multiple assemblies or congregations.

Description: "כאהלים" refers to assemblies or congregations of people. It can be used to describe gatherings of people for various purposes, such as religious worship, community meetings, or social events. The word emphasizes the act of coming together and uniting as a group.
