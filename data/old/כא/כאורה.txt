Etymology: The word "כאורה" (pronounced "ka'ora") is a Hebrew word that means "as it seems" or "apparently." It is derived from the root כ-ו-ר, which means "to seem" or "to appear."

Grammar: "כאורה" is an adverb in Hebrew. It is used to indicate that something appears or seems to be a certain way, but it may not necessarily be true or accurate.

Description: "כאורה" is often used in conversation to express uncertainty or doubt about a situation or statement. It is similar to the English phrase "seemingly" or "apparently." It is commonly used to indicate that something appears to be true or likely, but further investigation or evidence is needed to confirm it.
