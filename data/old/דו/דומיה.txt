Etymology: The word "דומיה" (pronounced "domiyah") comes from the Hebrew root ד.מ.ה (D.M.H), which means "to resemble" or "to be similar". 

Grammar: "דומיה" is a feminine noun in Hebrew. It is derived from the root ד.מ.ה and is used to describe something that resembles or is similar to something else.
