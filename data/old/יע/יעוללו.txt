Etymology: The word "יעוללו" is a Hebrew verb in the future tense, derived from the root ע.ו.ל (ayin-vav-lamed), which means "to ascend" or "to go up."

Grammar: "יעוללו" is the third person plural future tense form of the verb עולל (olel), which means "to ascend" or "to go up." In Hebrew, verbs are conjugated according to tense, person, and number.
