Grammar: In Hebrew, קש is a verb that can be conjugated in different tenses and forms. It is often used in the present tense to describe something that is difficult or challenging. For example, "זה קש לי" (ze kash li) means "it is difficult for me."

Description: קש is a versatile word that can be used to describe various situations or tasks that require effort, skill, or perseverance. It can refer to physical difficulty, such as a challenging physical activity, or to mental difficulty, such as solving a complex problem.
