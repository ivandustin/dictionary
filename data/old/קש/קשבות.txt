Etymology: The word "קֶשֶׁבּוֹת" (keshbot) is a Hebrew noun derived from the root ק.ש.ב (k.sh.b), which means "to listen" or "to pay attention."

Description: "קֶשֶׁבּוֹת" refers to ears or the act of listening. It is commonly used to describe the physical organ of hearing or to indicate the act of paying attention or being attentive.
