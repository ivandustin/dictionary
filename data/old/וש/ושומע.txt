Grammar: "וְשׁוֹמֵעַ" is a verb in the present tense, third person masculine singular form. It is conjugated from the root שׁ.מ.ע (sh.m.a) with the addition of the prefix וְ (v') which means "and."

Description: The word "וְשׁוֹמֵעַ" is used to describe someone who is actively listening or hearing something. It implies attentiveness and understanding.
