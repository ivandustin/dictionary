- Singular:
  - אֲנִי שׁוֹאֵל (ani sho'el) - I ask

  - אַתְּ שׁוֹאֶלֶת (at sho'elet) - You ask (feminine singular)
  - הוּא שׁוֹאֵל (hu sho'el) - He asks
  - הִיא שׁוֹאֵלֶת (hi sho'elet) - She asks

- Plural:
  - אֲנַחְנוּ שׁוֹאְלִים (anachnu sho'alim) - We ask
  - אַתֶּם שׁוֹאְלִים (atem sho'alim) - You ask (masculine plural)
  - אַתֶּן שׁוֹאְלוֹת (aten sho'elot) - You ask (feminine plural)
  - הֵם שׁוֹאְלִים (hem sho'alim) - They ask (masculine)
  - הֵן שׁוֹאְלוֹת (hen sho'elot) - They ask (feminine)

Description: The verb "וְשָׁאַל" is used to express the act of asking or inquiring about something.
