Description: "וְשָׁמוֹ" is used in Hebrew to refer to someone's name. It is often used in biblical texts to introduce the name of a person or a deity. For example, in the phrase "וְשָׁמוֹ יְהוָה" (ve-shamo YHWH), it means "and his name is YHWH," referring to the Hebrew name for God.
