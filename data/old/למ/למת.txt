Etymology: The word "למת" (lemat) is a Hebrew word that comes from the root "למד" (lamad), which means "to learn" or "to study".

Grammar: "למת" is a verb in the Hebrew language. It is in the infinitive form, which means it is the base form of the verb and does not indicate tense or subject.

Description: "למת" is used to express the action of learning or studying. It can be used in various contexts, such as learning a subject, acquiring knowledge, or studying for an exam.
