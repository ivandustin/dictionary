Etymology: The word "למאר" (pronounced "le'mar" in Hebrew) is derived from the Hebrew root "מ.א.ר" which means "to say" or "to speak".

- Singular masculine: אֲנִי מוֹמֵר (ani momeir) - I say
- Singular feminine: אֲנִי מוֹמֶרֶת (ani momeret) - I say
- Plural masculine: אֲנַחְנוּ מוֹמְרִים (anachnu momrim) - We say
- Plural feminine: אֲנַחְנוּ מוֹמְרוֹת (anachnu momrot) - We say

Description: The word "למאר" is used to express the action of speaking or saying something. It can be used in various contexts and can refer to any type of verbal communication.
