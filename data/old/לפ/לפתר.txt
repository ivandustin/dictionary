Etymology: The word "לפתר" (lefatér) is a Hebrew verb that comes from the root פתר (p.t.r), which means "to solve" or "to interpret."

Grammar: "לפתר" is an infinitive verb form in the Hebrew language. It is commonly used in the context of solving problems or interpreting texts.

Description: "לפתר" is a versatile verb that can be used in various contexts. It can refer to solving a puzzle, deciphering a code, interpreting a dream, or finding a solution to a problem. It implies the act of unraveling or understanding something that was previously unclear or difficult to comprehend.
