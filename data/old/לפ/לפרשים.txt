Etymology: The word "לפרשים" is a Hebrew word that comes from the root "פרש" which means "to explain" or "to interpret".

Grammar: "לפרשים" is a plural noun in Hebrew. It is formed by adding the suffix "-ים" to the root word "פרש". The prefix "ל" indicates the infinitive form of the verb, which in this case means "to explain" or "to interpret".

Description: "לפרשים" refers to the act of explaining or interpreting something. It can be used to describe the process of providing a detailed explanation or interpretation of a text, a concept, or an event.
