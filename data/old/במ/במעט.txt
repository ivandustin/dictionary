Grammar: "במעט" (b'ma'at) is an adverb in Hebrew. It is used to describe a small quantity or a short duration of time. It can also be used to express a lack or scarcity of something.

For example, "אני רק ישבתי במעט" (ani rak yashavti b'ma'at) means "I only sat for a short time".
