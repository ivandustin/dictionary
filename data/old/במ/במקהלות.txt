Grammar: "במקהלות" is a plural noun in Hebrew. It is formed by adding the suffix ות (-ot) to the singular form, which is מקהלה (makhehela). The plural form is used to refer to multiple gatherings or assemblies.

Description: "במקהלות" is often used to describe events or situations where people come together in groups or assemblies. It can refer to various types of gatherings, such as concerts, choirs, public meetings, or any other organized gathering of people.
