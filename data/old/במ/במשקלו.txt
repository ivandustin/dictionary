Etymology: The word "במשקלו" (bemishkalo) is a Hebrew phrase that consists of two parts: "ב" (be) meaning "in" or "at" and "משקלו" (mishkalo) meaning "his weight". 

Grammar: "במשקלו" is a prepositional phrase in Hebrew. The preposition "ב" (be) indicates location or possession, and "משקלו" (mishkalo) is a noun meaning "his weight". Together, the phrase means "in his weight" or "at his weight".

Description: The phrase "במשקלו" is used to describe something that is done or measured according to someone's weight. It can be used in various contexts, such as in cooking recipes, medical assessments, or physical fitness evaluations.
