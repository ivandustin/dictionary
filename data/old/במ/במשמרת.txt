Etymology: The word "במשמרת" (bemishmeret) is a Hebrew word that comes from the root שמר (shamar), which means "to guard" or "to keep". The prefix ב (be) means "in" or "at". Therefore, "במשמרת" can be translated as "in the guard" or "in the keeping".

Grammar: "במשמרת" is a noun in the feminine singular form. It is used to describe a state or condition of being in the guard or keeping of something.

Description: "במשמרת" is often used in the context of describing someone or something that is under the protection, care, or responsibility of someone else. It implies a sense of guardianship or watchfulness.
