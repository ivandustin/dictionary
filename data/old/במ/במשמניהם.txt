Grammar: "במשמניהם" is a prepositional phrase in Hebrew. The preposition "ב" indicates a location or means, and "משמניהם" is a possessive noun meaning "their oils." Together, the phrase can be translated as "in their oils" or "with their oils."

Description: The phrase "במשמניהם" is often used in Jewish religious texts and refers to the anointing oil used in ancient times for various rituals and ceremonies. It signifies the consecration and dedication of objects or individuals to a sacred purpose.
