Etymology: The word "במעל" (pronounced "bama'al" in Hebrew) comes from the Hebrew root ע.ל.ה (ayin-lamed-hei), which means "to ascend" or "to go up." The prefix ב (bet) means "in" or "on." Therefore, במעל literally means "in ascent" or "on the way up."

Grammar: במעל is a preposition in Hebrew. It is used to indicate movement or direction upwards. It can also be used metaphorically to express progress or improvement.

Description: במעל is a versatile word that can be used in various contexts. It can refer to physical ascent, such as climbing stairs or going up a hill. It can also be used figuratively to describe advancement in a career, improvement in a situation, or progress in personal growth.
