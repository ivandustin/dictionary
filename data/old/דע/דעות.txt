Grammar: "דעות" is a plural noun in Hebrew. It is formed by adding the suffix ות (-ot) to the singular form דעה (da'ah). The plural form is used to refer to multiple opinions, thoughts, or ideas.

Description: "דעות" is commonly translated as "opinions" or "views" in English. It refers to the thoughts, beliefs, or perspectives that individuals hold on a particular subject. It can also be used to describe the collective opinions or viewpoints of a group of people.
