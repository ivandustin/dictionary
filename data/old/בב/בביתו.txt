Etymology: The word "בביתו" (babito) is a Hebrew phrase composed of two words: "ב" (ba) meaning "in" or "at" and "ביתו" (baito) meaning "his house". 

Grammar: "בביתו" is a prepositional phrase in Hebrew. The word "ב" (ba) is a preposition meaning "in" or "at", and "ביתו" (baito) is a possessive noun meaning "his house". Together, they form the phrase "בביתו" (babito) meaning "in his house".

Description: "בביתו" is used to indicate the location of someone or something in relation to a house. It is commonly used in Hebrew to describe someone being at their own house or someone else being at another person's house.
