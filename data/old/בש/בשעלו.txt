Grammar: "בְּשָׁעְלוּ" is conjugated in the past tense, third person plural form. It is commonly used in biblical Hebrew.

Description: "בְּשָׁעְלוּ" is used to describe a group of people who asked or inquired about something in the past. It indicates that the action of asking or inquiring has already taken place.
