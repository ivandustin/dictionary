Grammar: בְּשֻׁמָּעַה is a noun in the feminine singular form. It is used to describe the act of hearing or listening.

Description: בְּשֻׁמָּעַה refers to the act of hearing or listening. It can also be used to describe the understanding or comprehension that comes from listening. In Jewish tradition, בְּשֻׁמָּעַה is often associated with the attentive and active listening to the words of the Torah or other sacred texts.
