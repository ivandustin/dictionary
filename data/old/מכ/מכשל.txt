Grammar: "מכשל" (mikshol) is a masculine noun in Hebrew. It is singular and can be used in both the definite and indefinite forms. In the plural form, it becomes "מכשלים" (miksholim).

Description: "מכשל" (mikshol) refers to a stumbling block or obstacle, either literal or metaphorical.
