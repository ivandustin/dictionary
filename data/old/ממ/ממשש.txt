Etymology: The word "ממשש" (mem-shin-shin) is derived from the Hebrew root מ.ש.ש (m.sh.sh), which means "to feel, to touch, to grope." 

Grammar: "ממשש" is a verb in the present tense, third person singular form. It can also be conjugated in other tenses and forms to match the subject and context of the sentence.

Description: The word "ממשש" is used to describe the action of feeling or touching something in a groping or uncertain manner. It implies a sense of searching or exploring through touch.
