Etymology: The word "ממקמו" comes from the Hebrew language. It is a combination of the preposition "מ" (from) and the noun "מקום" (place).

Grammar: "ממקמו" is a prepositional phrase in Hebrew. It is used to indicate the origin or source of something. It is commonly translated as "from its place" or "from where it is."

Description: "ממקמו" is used to describe the movement or displacement of an object or person from its original location. It can be used in various contexts, such as describing the removal of an object from a specific place or the departure of a person from a certain location.
