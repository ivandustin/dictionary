Etymology: The word "ממטר" (memtar) comes from the Hebrew root מ-ט-ר (m-t-r), which means "to rain". 

Grammar: "ממטר" is a noun in Hebrew and it is in the singular masculine form. 

Description: "ממטר" refers to rain in Hebrew. It is used to describe the precipitation of water droplets from the atmosphere to the ground. Rain is an essential part of the water cycle and plays a crucial role in the growth of plants and the overall ecosystem.
