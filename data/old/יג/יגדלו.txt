Etymology: The word "יגדלו" (pronounced "yigdilu") is a Hebrew verb in the future tense, derived from the root גדל (pronounced "gadal"), which means "to grow" or "to enlarge."

Grammar: "יגדלו" is the third person plural future tense form of the verb גדל. In Hebrew, verbs are conjugated according to the subject and tense. The future tense is used to express actions that will happen in the future.

Description: The word "יגדלו" can be translated to English as "they will grow" or "they will enlarge." It indicates that a group of people or things will experience growth or expansion in the future.
