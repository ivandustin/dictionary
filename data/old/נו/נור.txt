Grammar: In Hebrew, "נור" (nur) is a noun. It is a masculine noun and is singular. It can be used in different grammatical forms, such as the definite form "הנור" (ha-nur) meaning "the light" or the plural form "נורות" (nurot) meaning "lights."

Description: "נור" (nur) refers to a light source, such as a lamp or a candle. It is used to illuminate a space or provide light in the darkness. In a broader sense, it can also symbolize enlightenment, knowledge, or guidance.
