Etymology: The word "נושה" (noseh) comes from the Hebrew root נ-ש-ה (n-sh-h), which means "to travel" or "to journey."

Grammar: "נושה" is a verb in the Hebrew language. It is in the present tense, masculine singular form. It can also be conjugated to match different genders, numbers, and tenses.

Description: The word "נושה" means "he travels" or "he journeys" in Hebrew. It describes the action of someone going on a trip or traveling from one place to another.
