Etymology: The word "נועד" (no'ed) comes from the Hebrew root נ.ע.ד (n.a.d), which means "to intend" or "to purpose".

Grammar: "נועד" is a verb in the past tense, third person singular form. It is derived from the root נ.ע.ד (n.a.d) and is conjugated in the binyan Pa'al (active voice).
