- Feminine singular: אַתְּ נוֹדֵעַ (at nodea) - You know (feminine)
- Masculine plural: אַתֶּם נוֹדְעִים (atem nod'im) - You know (masculine plural)
- Feminine plural: אַתֶּן נוֹדְעוֹת (aten nod'ot) - You know (feminine plural)
