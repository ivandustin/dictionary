Etymology: The word "נותרו" (notru) is derived from the Hebrew root נ.ת.ר (n.t.r), which means "to remain" or "to be left."

Grammar: "נותרו" is a verb in the past tense, third person plural form. It is used to describe an action that was done by a group of people or objects in the past.

Description: "נותרו" means "they were left" or "they remained" in Hebrew. It indicates that a group of people or objects were not taken or used, and they are still present or available.
