Etymology: The word "חבוש" comes from the Hebrew root ח-ב-ש (ch-b-sh), which means "to bind" or "to tie". 

Grammar: "חבוש" is a verb in the Hebrew language. It is in the masculine singular form in the present tense. 

Description: "חבוש" means "to bind" or "to tie" in Hebrew. It refers to the act of fastening or securing something with a rope, string, or other material. It can also be used metaphorically to describe being bound or restricted in some way.
