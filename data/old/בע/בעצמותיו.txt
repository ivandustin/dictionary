Etymology: The word "בעצמותיו" (ba'atzmotav) is a Hebrew phrase composed of two parts. "ב" (ba) means "in" or "with," and "עצמותיו" (atzmotav) means "his bones." 

Grammar: "בעצמותיו" is a prepositional phrase in Hebrew. The preposition "ב" (ba) indicates a location or means, and "עצמותיו" (atzmotav) is a possessive pronoun meaning "his bones." Together, the phrase means "in his bones" or "with his bones."

Description: "בעצמותיו" is a phrase used to emphasize a deep and personal connection to something. It suggests that something is deeply ingrained in a person's being or is an integral part of their identity.
