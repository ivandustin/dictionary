Grammar: "בעדת" (ba'adat) is a noun in the Hebrew language. It is in the singular form and is feminine. It can be used as a subject, object, or possessive noun in a sentence.

Description: "בעדת" (ba'adat) is a term used in Hebrew to refer to an assembly or congregation of people. It can be used in various contexts, such as a religious gathering, a group of people with a common purpose, or a committee. The word carries a sense of unity and collective action.
