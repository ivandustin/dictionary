Grammar: "בעצמי" is a prepositional phrase in Hebrew. The preposition "ב" indicates the relationship between the subject and the object, and "עצמי" functions as the object of the preposition. It is used to express the idea of doing something independently or without assistance.

Description: The term "בעצמי" is commonly used in everyday Hebrew to convey self-reliance or the act of doing something by oneself. It emphasizes personal agency and independence.
