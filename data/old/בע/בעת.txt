Grammar: "בעת" (be'et) is a prepositional phrase in Hebrew. The word "ב" (be) means "in" or "at", and "עת" (et) means "time". When combined, "בעת" (be'et) means "at the time of" or "in the moment of".

Description: "בעת" (be'et) is used to indicate a specific time or moment when something happens or is supposed to happen. It is often used in Hebrew to express the timing or occurrence of an event.
