- Singular: אני יטרף (ani yitraf) - I tear
- Singular feminine: אני יטרפת (ani yitraft) - I tear
- Plural masculine: אנחנו יטרפים (anachnu yitrafim) - We tear
- Plural feminine: אנחנו יטרפות (anachnu yitrafot) - We tear

Description: The verb "יטרף" means "to tear" or "to rip apart" in Hebrew. It is used to describe the action of forcefully separating or dividing something into pieces. This verb can be used in both literal and figurative contexts.
