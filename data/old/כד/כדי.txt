Grammar: In Hebrew, כְּדִי is an adverb that means "enough" or "sufficiently." It is used to indicate that something is adequate or satisfactory.

Description: כְּדִי is a versatile word that can be used in various contexts. It can be used to express satisfaction or contentment with a certain amount or level of something.
