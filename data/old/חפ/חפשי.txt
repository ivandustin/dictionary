Grammar: "חפשי" is an adjective in the masculine singular form. In Hebrew, adjectives agree in gender, number, and definiteness with the noun they modify. For example, if you want to say "free woman," you would use the feminine singular form, which is "חפשיה" (chofshiya).

Description: The word "חפשי" means "free" in Hebrew. It is used to describe something or someone that is not under the control or influence of others, that is independent or liberated. It can refer to political freedom, personal freedom, or freedom of choice.
