Etymology: The word "חפר" (chafar) comes from the Hebrew root ח-פ-ר, which means "to dig" or "to excavate". 

Grammar: "חפר" is a verb in the Hebrew language. It belongs to the binyan Pa'al (פָּעַל) and is conjugated in various tenses and forms to match the subject and the context of the sentence.

Description: "חפר" is used to describe the action of digging or excavating the ground. It can be used literally, referring to physical digging, or metaphorically, referring to searching, investigating, or delving into a subject or matter.
