Grammar: In Hebrew grammar, "אקלידה" (aklida) refers to the declension of nouns. It involves the changes in form that nouns undergo to indicate their grammatical function in a sentence, such as gender, number, and case.

Description: "אקלידה" (aklida) is an important aspect of Hebrew grammar as it helps determine the role and relationship of nouns in a sentence. By understanding the rules of declension, one can correctly use and interpret Hebrew nouns in various contexts.
