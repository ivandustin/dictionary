Etymology: The word "אכול" (akol) comes from the Hebrew root כ-ו-ל (k-w-l), which means "to eat." 

Grammar: "אכול" is a verb in the Hebrew language. It is in the present tense, masculine singular form. 

Description: "אכול" means "to eat" in Hebrew. It is used to describe the action of consuming food or taking in nourishment.
