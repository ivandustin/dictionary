Grammar: "הקנאת" is a noun in the Hebrew language. It is in the singular form and is used to describe the act of being jealous or envious.

Description: "הקנאת" refers to the feeling of jealousy or envy. It is the state of being resentful or covetous of someone else's possessions, qualities, or achievements. Jealousy can arise from a variety of reasons, such as insecurity, competition, or a desire for what others have.
