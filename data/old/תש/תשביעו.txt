Etymology: The word "תשביעו" comes from the Hebrew language. It is derived from the root שבע (shava), which means "to swear" or "to take an oath." The word is formed by adding the prefix ת- (ta) to the root, which indicates the second person plural form of the verb.

Grammar: "תשביעו" is a verb in the second person plural form in the future tense. It is used when addressing a group of people and means "you will swear" or "you will take an oath."

Description: "תשביעו" is a command or a statement that instructs or predicts that a group of people will swear or take an oath in the future. It can be used in various contexts, such as legal proceedings, religious ceremonies, or personal commitments.
