Etymology: The word "תשקול" (teshakul) is derived from the Hebrew root שקל (shakal), which means "to weigh" or "to balance". 

Grammar: "תשקול" is a noun in the Hebrew language. It is in the singular form and belongs to the masculine gender. 

Description: "תשקול" refers to the act of weighing or balancing. It can also be used metaphorically to describe the consideration or evaluation of different factors or options in order to make a decision or reach a conclusion.
