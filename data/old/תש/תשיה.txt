- Feminine plural: אַתֶּן מְשַׂחֲחוֹת (aten mesachachot) - You (feminine) talk

Description: "תֵּשִׁיחַ" means "to talk" or "to converse" in Hebrew. It refers to the act of engaging in verbal communication with someone. It can be used in various contexts, such as having a conversation, discussing a topic, or expressing thoughts and ideas through speech.
