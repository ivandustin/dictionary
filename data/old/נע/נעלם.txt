Grammar: "נעלם" is a noun in the masculine singular form. It is also used as a verb in the past tense, meaning "he hid" or "it was hidden."

Description: "נעלם" refers to something that is hidden, concealed, or unknown. It can be used to describe a physical object that is not visible or a concept that is not easily understood. In a figurative sense, it can also refer to something that is kept secret or not revealed.
