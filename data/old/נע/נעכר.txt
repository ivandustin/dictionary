Grammar: נעקר is a verb in the past tense, third person singular form. It is derived from the root ע-ק-ר and is conjugated using the binyan (verb structure) called Nifal. 

Description: נעקר means "was uprooted" or "was removed" in Hebrew. It is used to describe the action of pulling out or extracting something from its place, often referring to plants or objects being removed from the ground.
