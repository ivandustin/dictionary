Grammar: "והצאתי" is a verb form in the future tense, first person singular. In Hebrew, verbs are conjugated based on the subject, tense, and gender. The future tense is used to express actions that will happen in the future. The first person singular form indicates that the subject of the verb is the speaker.

Description: "והצאתי" translates to "I will go out" or "I will exit" in English. It is a statement made by the speaker expressing their intention or prediction of going out or exiting in the future.
