Grammar: "וַהוּאַח" is a noun phrase in Hebrew. The word "וַהוּא" functions as a conjunction meaning "and he", and "אַח" is a noun meaning "brother". Together, they form the phrase "and he brother".

Description: "וַהוּאַח" is a biblical Hebrew phrase found in the Old Testament. It is used to refer to a specific person's brother.
