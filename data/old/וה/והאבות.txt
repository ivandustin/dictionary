Description: "וְהָאָבוֹת" is used in Hebrew to refer to the patriarchs or forefathers of the Jewish people, specifically Abraham, Isaac, and Jacob. It is often used in religious and historical contexts to emphasize the lineage and heritage of the Jewish people.
