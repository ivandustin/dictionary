Etymology: The word "נָרוֹ" (narow) comes from the Hebrew root נ-ר-ה (n-r-h), which means "to shine" or "to give light." 

Grammar: In Hebrew, נָרוֹ (narow) is a noun that means "candle" or "lamp." It is in the masculine singular form.
