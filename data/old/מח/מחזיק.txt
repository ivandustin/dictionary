Grammar: "מחזיק" is a verb in the present tense, masculine singular form. It can also be conjugated to match different genders, numbers, and tenses.

Description: The word "מחזיק" means "to hold" or "to grasp" in Hebrew. It can refer to physically holding onto something or figuratively holding onto an idea, belief, or position.
