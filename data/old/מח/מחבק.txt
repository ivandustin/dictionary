Etymology: The word "מחבק" (makhbek) is derived from the Hebrew root חבק (kh-b-k), which means "to embrace" or "to hug". 

Grammar: In Hebrew, "מחבק" is a verb in the present tense, masculine singular form. It can also be conjugated to match different genders, numbers, and tenses. 

Description: "מחבק" is used to describe the act of embracing or hugging someone or something tightly. It conveys a sense of warmth, affection, and closeness.
