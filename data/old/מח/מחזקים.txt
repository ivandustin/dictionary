Etymology: The word "מחזקים" (pronounced "makhazkim") is a Hebrew word derived from the root חזק (chazak), which means "to strengthen" or "to fortify."

Grammar: "מחזקים" is the plural form of the verb "לחזק" (lachazek), which means "to strengthen" or "to fortify." It is conjugated in the present tense, masculine plural form.

Description: "מחזקים" is used to describe a group of people or individuals who are strengthening or fortifying something. It can refer to physical or metaphorical strengthening, such as strengthening a building, a relationship, or one's own character.
