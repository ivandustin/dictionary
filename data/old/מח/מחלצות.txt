Etymology: The word "מחלצות" (makhlotzot) is derived from the Hebrew root ח-ל-ץ (ch-l-tz), which means "to rescue" or "to deliver."

Grammar: "מחלצות" is a feminine plural noun in Hebrew. It is formed by adding the suffix ות (ot) to the root ח-ל-ץ (ch-l-tz).

Description: "מחלצות" refers to female rescuers or deliverers. It can be used to describe women who save or deliver others from danger, harm, or difficult situations.
