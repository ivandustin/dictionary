Grammar: "מחללים" is the plural form of the verb "לחלל" (lekhallel), which means "to desecrate" or "to profane." It is in the present tense and active voice. 

Description: "מחללים" is used to describe individuals or a group of people who engage in actions that violate or disrespect something sacred or holy. It can refer to desecrating religious objects, breaking religious laws, or disrespecting sacred spaces.
