Grammar: "מופתי" is a masculine singular adjective in Hebrew. It agrees in gender, number, and definiteness with the noun it modifies.

Description: The word "מופתי" is used to describe something or someone as persuasive, convincing, or influential. It implies that the subject has the ability to sway or win over others with their words or actions.
