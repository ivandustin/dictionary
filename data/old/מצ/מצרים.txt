Grammar: "מִצְרִים" is a masculine plural noun in Hebrew. It is often used to refer to the people, culture, or land of Egypt.

Description: "מִצְרִים" is a significant term in Jewish history and appears frequently in the Hebrew Bible. It is associated with the story of the Israelites' enslavement and subsequent liberation from Egypt, as described in the Book of Exodus. The term can also be used more broadly to refer to the ancient civilization of Egypt.
