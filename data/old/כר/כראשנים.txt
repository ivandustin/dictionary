Grammar: "כראשנים" is an adverb in Hebrew. It is used to describe actions or situations that occurred in the past or were done previously. It is often used to emphasize the similarity or continuity between past and present actions.

Description: "כראשנים" is a versatile word that can be used in various contexts. It is commonly used in storytelling, historical accounts, or when referring to traditions or customs that have been passed down through generations.
