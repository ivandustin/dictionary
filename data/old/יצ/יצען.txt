- אֲנִי יוֹצֵא (ani yotze) - I go out

- הֵן יוֹצְאוֹת (hen yotze'ot) - They (feminine) go out

Description: The word "יצען" is used to describe the action of going out or exiting from a place. It can be used in various contexts, such as leaving a building, departing from a location, or coming out of a situation.
