Etymology: The word "נְסִיתוֹ" (nesito) is a Hebrew word derived from the root נסה (n-s-h), which means "to test" or "to try."

Description: נְסִיתוֹ (nesito) refers to a test or trial that someone undergoes. It can be used in various contexts, such as a test of knowledge, a challenge, or a difficult situation that someone must face.
