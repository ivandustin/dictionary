Grammar: In Hebrew, נָסוֹר is a verb in the Qal (simple) conjugation, third person masculine singular form. It is also used in other conjugations and forms to indicate different tenses, persons, and genders.

Description: נָסוֹר is used to describe the act of fleeing or escaping from a certain place or situation. It can be used in a literal sense, such as running away from danger, or in a figurative sense, such as avoiding a difficult task or responsibility.
