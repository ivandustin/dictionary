Etymology: The word "ידעון" (pronounced "yedaon") comes from the Hebrew root י-ד-ע, which means "to know" or "to understand". It is derived from the noun יָדַע (yada), which means "knowledge" or "understanding".

Grammar: "ידעון" is a masculine noun in Hebrew. It is in the singular form, and its plural form is "ידעונים" (yedaonim). It can be used with definite or indefinite articles, such as "הידעון" (ha-yedaon) meaning "the knowledge" or "a knowledge".

Description: "ידעון" refers to knowledge, understanding, or awareness. It can be used to describe both factual knowledge and intellectual understanding. It is often used in contexts related to education, learning, or expertise.
