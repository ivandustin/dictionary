Grammar: In Hebrew, ידעני is an adjective that describes someone who is knowledgeable or well-informed. It is in the masculine singular form. The feminine singular form is ידענית (yedanit), and the plural form for both genders is ידענים (yedanim).

Description: ידעני is used to describe someone who has a deep understanding or expertise in a particular subject or field. It implies that the person possesses extensive knowledge and is well-versed in the topic.
