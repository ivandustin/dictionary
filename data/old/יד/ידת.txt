Etymology: The word "ידע" (pronounced "yada") comes from the Hebrew language. It is derived from the root י-ד-ע (y-d-a), which means "to know" or "to understand."

Grammar: In Hebrew, "ידע" is a verb in the past tense, third person singular form. It can also be conjugated in other tenses and forms to match different subjects and contexts.

Description: The word "ידע" means "to know" or "to have knowledge" in Hebrew. It refers to the act of acquiring information, understanding, or being aware of something. It can also imply familiarity or expertise in a particular subject or skill.
