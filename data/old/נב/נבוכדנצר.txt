Description: "נבוכדנצר" refers to Nebuchadnezzar II, who was the king of Babylon from 605 BC to 562 BC. He is known for his conquests and the destruction of the First Temple in Jerusalem. Nebuchadnezzar is mentioned in the Hebrew Bible, particularly in the books of Jeremiah, Daniel, and Kings.
