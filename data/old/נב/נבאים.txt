Grammar: "נבאים" (Nevi'im) is the plural form of the word "נביא" (navi), which means "prophet" in Hebrew. It is used to refer to the collection of prophetic books in the Hebrew Bible.

Description: The Nevi'im is one of the three main sections of the Hebrew Bible, also known as the Tanakh. It consists of the books of the prophets, including Isaiah, Jeremiah, Ezekiel, and the Twelve Minor Prophets. These books contain the prophecies, teachings, and narratives of the prophets who spoke on behalf of God to the people of Israel.
