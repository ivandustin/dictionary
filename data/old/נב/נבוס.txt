Grammar: In Hebrew, "נבוס" is a verb in the present tense, third person singular form. It can also be conjugated in other tenses and forms to match different subjects and contexts.

Description: The word "נבוס" is used to describe the act of prophesying or foretelling future events. It is often associated with the ability to predict or foresee future outcomes or to communicate divine messages.
