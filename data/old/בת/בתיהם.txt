Grammar: "בתיהם" is a noun in Hebrew and it is in the feminine gender. It is formed by adding the possessive suffix "-יהם" to the noun "בת". The suffix "-יהם" indicates possession by a plural masculine subject.

Description: "בתיהם" is used to refer to the daughter(s) of a group of people, specifically a group of males. It is commonly used in biblical and traditional Hebrew texts.
