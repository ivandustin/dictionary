Etymology: The word "הִדִיחְנוּ" (pronounced: hidiḥnu) is a Hebrew verb that comes from the root ד.ח.נ (D.Ch.N). 

Grammar: הִדִיחְנוּ is the past tense, first person plural form of the verb. It is conjugated in the hif'il (causative) binyan (verb structure). 

Description: הִדִיחְנוּ means "we amused" or "we entertained" in English. It is used to describe the action of causing someone to be amused or entertained.
