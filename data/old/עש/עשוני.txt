Grammar: In Hebrew, "עשוני" is an adjective that is used to describe something or someone as "doing" or "making." It is formed by adding the suffix נִי (ni) to the root ע-ש-נ (Ayin-Shin-Nun).

Description: The word "עשוני" is used to describe someone or something that is actively engaged in doing or making. It implies a sense of productivity, creativity, or accomplishment.
