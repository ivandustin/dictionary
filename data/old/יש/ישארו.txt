Grammar: "ישארו" is the third person plural future tense form of the verb "לשאר" (lish'or), which means "to remain" or "to stay." In Hebrew, verbs are conjugated according to the subject and tense of the sentence.

Description: "ישארו" is used to express an action that will happen in the future, specifically referring to the third person plural subject. It indicates that multiple individuals or objects will remain or stay in a certain place or state.
