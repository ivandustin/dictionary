- Singular:
  - 1st person: אֲנִי יֵשֵׁת (Ani yeshet) - I exist
  - 2nd person: אַתָּה יֵשֵׁת (Atah yeshet) - You exist (masculine)

  - 3rd person: הוּא/הִיא יֵשֵׁת (Hu/Hi yeshet) - He/She exists

- Plural:

  - 3rd person: הֵם/הֵן יֵשְׁתִּים (Hem/Hen yeshetim) - They exist
