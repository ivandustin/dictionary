Etymology: The word "ישפיקו" (Yeshpiku) is a Hebrew verb in the future tense, derived from the root שפק (sh-p-k), which means "to draw out" or "to extract."

Grammar: "ישפיקו" is the third person plural future tense form of the verb. In Hebrew, verbs are conjugated according to the subject and the tense of the sentence. The future tense is used to express actions that will happen in the future.

Description: The word "ישפיקו" can be translated as "they will draw out" or "they will extract" in English. It is commonly used in various contexts to describe the act of pulling or extracting something from a source.
