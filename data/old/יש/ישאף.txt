- Singular: אני יושאף (ani yoshef), אתה יושאף (atah yoshef), את יושאפת (at yoshefet)
- Plural: אנחנו יושאפים (anachnu yoshefim), אתם יושאפים (atem yoshefim), הם/הן יושאפים (hem/hen yoshefim)

Description: The word "ישאף" is used to express a strong desire or longing for something or someone. It can be used in various contexts, such as expressing a desire for food, love, success, or any other object of desire.
