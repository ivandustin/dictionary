Grammar: "דַּרְכָּה" is a feminine noun in Hebrew. It is in the singular form and can be used as both a subject and an object in a sentence. 

Description: "דַּרְכָּה" refers to a way, path, or road. It can be used literally to describe a physical route or metaphorically to represent a course of action or a manner of behavior.
