Grammar: "בראתי" is a verb form in the past tense, specifically the first person singular form. In Hebrew, verbs are conjugated according to the subject and tense of the sentence. The verb "בראתי" indicates that the speaker (first person) performed the action of creation in the past.

Description: "בראתי" is a Hebrew word that translates to "I created" in English. It is used to express the act of bringing something into existence or making something from nothing. This word is commonly used in religious and biblical contexts, referring to the creation of the world by God.
