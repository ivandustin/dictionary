Grammar: "ברור" is an adjective in Hebrew, and it is used to describe something that is clear, evident, or obvious. It can also be used to describe someone who is intelligent or sharp-minded.

Description: "ברור" is a versatile word that can be used in various contexts. For example, you can say "התשובה ברורה" (pronounced "hatshuva barura"), which means "the answer is clear". It can also be used to express certainty or agreement, such as saying "ברור שאני אשתך" (pronounced "barur she'ani ishtecha"), which means "it's obvious that I'm your spouse".
