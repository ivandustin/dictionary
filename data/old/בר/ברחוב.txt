Grammar: "ברחוב" is a prepositional phrase in Hebrew. The word "בר" is a preposition meaning "on" or "in", and "חוב" is a noun meaning "street". When combined, they form a phrase that describes a location or position.

Description: "ברחוב" is commonly used in Hebrew to indicate that something is happening or located on the street.
