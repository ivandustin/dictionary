Etymology: The word "תחפוז" (tehafuz) is a Hebrew word that comes from the root ח.פ.ז (ch.p.z), which means "to search" or "to investigate."

Grammar: "תחפוז" is a verb in the Hebrew language. It is conjugated in the present tense as follows:
- Singular: אני תחפוז (ani tehafuz) - I search
- Plural: אנחנו תחפוזים (anachnu tehafuzim) - We search

Description: "תחפוז" means "to search" or "to investigate" in Hebrew. It is used to describe the act of looking for something or trying to find information. It can be used in various contexts, such as searching for a lost item, investigating a crime, or researching a topic.
