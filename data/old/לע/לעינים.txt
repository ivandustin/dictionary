Grammar: "לעינים" is a prepositional phrase in Hebrew. The preposition "ל" indicates direction or purpose, and it is followed by the noun "עינים" in the plural form. In this context, it means "for the eyes" or "to the eyes."

Description: The phrase "לעינים" is often used in Hebrew to indicate that something is intended for visual perception or observation. It can be used to describe objects, actions, or events that are meant to be seen or witnessed.
