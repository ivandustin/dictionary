The prefix "ל" (le) indicates "to" or "for", and the suffix "ם" (am) indicates "them". Therefore, "לעונם" can be translated as "to answer them" or "for their response".

Description: "לעונם" is used to express the idea of responding or answering to someone or something. It can be used in various contexts, such as responding to a question, a request, or a statement. The word emphasizes the act of providing a response or reply to someone.
