Etymology: The word "שוליך" comes from the Hebrew root ש-ל-ך, which means "to throw" or "to cast". 

Grammar: "שוליך" is a masculine singular noun in Hebrew. It belongs to the binyan Pa'al (פָּעַל) and is in the present tense. 

Description: "שוליך" refers to someone who throws or casts something. It can also be used metaphorically to describe someone who discards or gets rid of something.
