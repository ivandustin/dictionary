"בְגָלוּלִיהּ" is composed of the preposition "בְ" (pronounced "b'") meaning "in" and the noun "גָלוּלִיהּ" (pronounced "galuliyah") meaning "her idolatry". 

Description: The phrase "וּבְגָלוּלִיהּ" is used in Hebrew to describe someone or something being involved in idolatry or worshiping idols. It implies a state of being engrossed or immersed in idolatrous practices.
