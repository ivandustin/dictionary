Grammar: "וִבְתִים" is a plural noun in Hebrew. The prefix "וִ" (vav-yod) means "and", and the root word "בַּיִת" (bayit) is modified to form the plural by adding the suffix "ִים" (yod-mem).

Description: "וִבְתִים" refers to multiple houses. It is commonly used in Hebrew to describe more than one dwelling place or residential building.
