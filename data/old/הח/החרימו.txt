Etymology: The word "החרימו" (HaCharimu) is a Hebrew verb that comes from the root ח-ר-ם (Ch-R-M). 

Grammar: "החרימו" is the plural form of the verb in the past tense. It is in the third person masculine plural form. 

Description: "החרימו" means "they destroyed" or "they annihilated" in Hebrew. It is used to describe an action of complete destruction or devastation.
