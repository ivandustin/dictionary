Etymology: The Hebrew word "החפרי" (HaChafri) comes from the root ח.פ.ר (ch.p.r), which means "to dig" or "to excavate". 

Grammar: "החפרי" is an adjective in the masculine singular form. It is used to describe something or someone that is related to digging or excavation.

Description: "החפרי" can be used to describe various things, such as archaeological sites, excavation tools, or people involved in the field of archaeology. It indicates a connection to digging or excavation activities.
