Grammar: "החילים" (HaChayalim) is a masculine plural noun in Hebrew. It is formed by adding the definite article "ה" (ha) at the beginning of the word, followed by the root word "חיל" (chayil), and the plural suffix "ים" (im).

Description: "החילים" (HaChayalim) refers to "the soldiers" or "the warriors" in Hebrew. It is commonly used to describe members of the military or armed forces.
