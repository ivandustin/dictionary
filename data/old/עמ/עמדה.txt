Grammar: "עמדה" (amidah) is a feminine noun in Hebrew. It is the singular form of the word, and its plural form is "עמדות" (amidot).

The amidah consists of a series of blessings and supplications, and it is recited silently by individuals or aloud in communal prayer services.
