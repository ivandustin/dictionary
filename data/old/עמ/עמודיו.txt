Grammar: "עמודיו" is a plural noun in Hebrew. It is formed by adding the suffix יו (ayv) to the root עמוד (Amud). The suffix יו indicates possession, so the word can be translated as "his pillars" or "its pillars."

Description: "עמודיו" refers to the pillars or columns that support a structure. It can be used in a literal sense to describe physical pillars in a building or figuratively to represent the foundational principles or beliefs that uphold something.
