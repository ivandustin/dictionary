- Singular: אֲנִי נוֹמֵקֵר (ani nomeker) - I am defined

- Feminine singular: אַתְּ נוֹמֶקֶרֶת (at nomekeret) - You (feminine) are defined
- Masculine plural: אַתֶּם נוֹמְקְרִים (atem nomekerim) - You (masculine plural) are defined
- Feminine plural: אַתֶּן נוֹמְקְרוֹת (aten nomekerot) - You (feminine plural) are defined

Description: The word "נמקר" means "to define" in English. It is used to describe the act of giving a clear and precise meaning to something or someone.
