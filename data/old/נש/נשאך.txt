Etymology: The word "נשאל" (nashal) is derived from the Hebrew root נ-ש-א (n-sh-a), which means "to ask" or "to inquire."

Grammar: "נשאל" is a verb in the past tense, third person singular form. It is conjugated from the root נ-ש-א (n-sh-a) with the addition of the past tense suffix -ל (-al).

Description: The word "נשאל" means "was asked" or "was inquired" in Hebrew. It is used to describe a situation where someone asked a question or made an inquiry in the past.
