It is conjugated in the present tense as follows: אני תופגע (ani tof-ga) - I offend, אתה תופגע (atah tof-ga) - you offend (masculine singular), את תופגעי (at tof-ga'i) - you offend (feminine singular), הוא/היא תופגע (hu/hi tof-ga) - he/she offends, אנחנו נתופגע (anachnu nitof-ga) - we offend, אתם תתופגעו (atem titof-ga'u) - you offend (masculine plural), אתן תתופגענה (aten titof-ga'nah) - you offend (feminine plural), הם/הן יתופגעו (hem/hen yitof-ga'u) - they offend.

Description: The word "תפגע" is used to describe the action of causing offense or harm to someone. It can refer to both physical and emotional harm.
