Grammar: "תפלנה" is a noun in the Hebrew language. It is in the feminine plural form, indicating multiple prayers or supplications.

Description: "תפלנה" refers to prayers or supplications in the Jewish tradition. It is commonly used to describe the act of praying or the prayers themselves.
