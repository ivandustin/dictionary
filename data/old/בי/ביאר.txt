Etymology: The word "ביאר" (bi'ar) comes from the Hebrew root ב-א-ר (b-a-r), which means "to explain" or "to clarify". 

Grammar: "ביאר" is a verb in the Hebrew language. It is in the past tense, third person singular form. 

Description: The word "ביאר" means "he explained" or "he clarified" in Hebrew. It is used to describe the action of providing a clear and detailed explanation or interpretation of something.
