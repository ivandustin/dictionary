Etymology: The word "בידיהם" is a Hebrew word that consists of two parts: "ביד" (b'yad) meaning "in the hand" and "הם" (hem) meaning "them". 

Grammar: "בידיהם" is a plural noun in Hebrew. It is formed by adding the suffix "-יהם" to the word "ביד" to indicate possession by a group of people.
