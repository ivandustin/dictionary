  - 2nd person: אַתְּ לוֹנֶפֶת (at lonéfet) - You flutter (feminine)
  - 3rd person: הוּא/הִיא לוֹנֵפֵת (hu/hi lonéfet) - He/She flutters
- Plural:
  - 1st person: אֲנַחְנוּ לוֹנְפוֹת (anachnu lon'fot) - We flutter
  - 2nd person: אַתֶּם לוֹנְפִים (atem lon'fim) - You flutter (masculine)
  - 2nd person: אַתֶּן לוֹנְפוֹת (aten lon'fot) - You flutter (feminine)
  - 3rd person: הֵם/הֵן לוֹנְפִים (hem/hen lon'fim) - They flutter

Description: "לנפת" (lenafet) describes the action of fluttering or flapping, usually referring to the movement of wings or other objects that resemble wings. It can be used both literally and metaphorically to convey a sense of light, rapid, and repetitive movement.
