Grammar: "בקנאתי" (baknati) is a first-person singular possessive form of the word קנאה (kin'ah), which means "jealousy" or "envy". The prefix ב (b-) indicates that the jealousy is directed towards someone or something. The suffix י (-i) indicates that the jealousy belongs to the speaker.

Description: "בקנאתי" (baknati) is a Hebrew phrase that translates to "in my jealousy" or "with my envy". It is used to express possessive jealousy or envy, indicating that the speaker is feeling jealous or envious of someone or something.
