Grammar: "שיהיה" is a future tense verb form in the Hebrew language. It is conjugated in the third person singular masculine form. The verb form consists of the prefix ש- (Sh-) indicating future tense, followed by the root ה-י-ה (H-Y-H) and the suffix -ה (-eh) denoting the third person singular masculine.

Description: "שיהיה" is used to express future actions or events in Hebrew. It is commonly used in sentences to indicate that something will happen or exist in the future.
