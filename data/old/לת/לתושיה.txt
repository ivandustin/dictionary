Etymology: The word "לתושיה" (letushia) is derived from the Hebrew root "ל.ת.ש" (L.T.Sh), which means to study or to learn.

Grammar: "לתושיה" is a noun in the Hebrew language. It is in the feminine form and is used to describe the act of studying or learning.

Description: "לתושיה" refers to the process of studying or learning. It can be used to describe formal education, such as studying in school or university, as well as self-study or acquiring knowledge through various means.
