Grammar: "לתשועת" (leteshu'at) is a prepositional phrase in Hebrew. The preposition "ל" (l) indicates direction or purpose, and it is followed by the noun "תשועת" (teshu'at) which functions as the object of the preposition. 

Description: "לתשועת" (leteshu'at) is used to express the idea of "for the purpose of salvation" or "for the sake of deliverance". It is often used in religious or spiritual contexts to convey the concept of seeking or praying for salvation or deliverance from a difficult or challenging situation.
