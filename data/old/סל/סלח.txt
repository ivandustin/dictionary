Grammar: In Hebrew, סלח is a verb in the imperative form. It is used to give a command or instruction, telling someone to forgive or pardon.

Description: סלח is a powerful word in Hebrew that carries the meaning of forgiveness and pardon. It is often used in religious and moral contexts, emphasizing the importance of forgiving others and seeking forgiveness.
