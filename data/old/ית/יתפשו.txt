Etymology: The word "יתפשו" (Yitpashu) is a Hebrew verb in the future tense, derived from the root פ-ש-ש (P-SH-SH).

Grammar: "יתפשו" is the third person plural future tense form of the verb פשש (Pashash), which means "to catch" or "to seize" in Hebrew. The prefix י- (Yod) indicates the future tense, and the suffix -ו (Vav) indicates the third person plural.

Description: "יתפשו" is used to describe an action that will be performed by a group of people in the future. It implies that they will catch or seize something.
