Grammar: In Hebrew, יתרה is a feminine noun. It is usually used in the singular form, but it can also be used in the plural form יתרות (Yeterot). It can function as a subject, object, or complement in a sentence.

Description: יתרה is a versatile word that can have different meanings depending on the context. It can refer to a surplus or excess of something, such as money, time, or resources. It can also be used to describe something that is left over or remaining after the main part has been used or taken.
