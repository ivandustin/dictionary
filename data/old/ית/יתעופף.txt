Grammar: יתעוף is a verb in the future tense, third person singular form. In Hebrew, verbs are conjugated based on the subject and the tense of the sentence. The prefix י indicates the future tense, and the root עוף is modified to fit the third person singular subject.

Description: יתעוף is used to describe the action of flying or soaring in the future. It can be used in various contexts, such as describing the flight of birds, airplanes, or even metaphorically to describe someone's aspirations or dreams.
