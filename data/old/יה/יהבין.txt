Etymology: The word "יהבין" (Yahbin) is a Hebrew word derived from the root ה.ב.נ (H.B.N), which means "to understand" or "to comprehend."

- Singular: אֲנִי יוֹבֵן (ani yovén) - I understand
- Masculine singular: אַתָּה יוֹבֵן (atah yovén) - You understand
- Feminine singular: אַתְּ יוֹבֶנֶת (at yovenet) - You understand
- Masculine plural: אַתֶּם יוֹבְנִים (atem yovnim) - You understand
- Feminine plural: אַתֶּן יוֹבְנוֹת (aten yovnot) - You understand

Description: "יהבין" is a verb that expresses the action of understanding or comprehending something. It is used to describe the mental process of grasping or perceiving the meaning or significance of information, ideas, or concepts.
