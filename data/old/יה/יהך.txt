- Feminine singular: אַתְּ יוֹחֶדֶת (at yochedet) - You (feminine) unite
- Masculine plural: אַתֶּם יוֹחְדִים (atem yochdim) - You (masculine plural) unite
- Feminine plural: אַתֶּן יוֹחְדוֹת (aten yochdot) - You (feminine plural) unite
