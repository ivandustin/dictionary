Grammar: "להשבע" is an infinitive verb form in the Hebrew language. It is formed by adding the prefix ל (le-) to the root שבע (sh-b-a). The verb is conjugated according to the tense, gender, and number of the subject in a sentence.

Description: "להשבע" is a verb that is used to express the act of swearing or taking an oath. It can be used in various contexts, such as making a promise, confirming the truthfulness of a statement, or pledging allegiance. The verb is commonly used in legal, religious, and formal settings.
