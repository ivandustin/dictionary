Etymology: The word "להרים" (lehareem) is a Hebrew verb that comes from the root ה.ר.מ (h.r.m), which means "to lift" or "to raise."

Grammar: "להרים" is an infinitive verb form in the Hebrew language. It belongs to the binyan Pa'al (פָּעַל) and is conjugated in the present tense as follows:
- Singular: אני מֵרִים (ani merim) - I lift
- Plural: אנחנו מֵרִימִים (anachnu merimim) - We lift

Description: The verb "להרים" is used to express the action of lifting or raising something physically or metaphorically. It can refer to lifting objects, raising one's voice, or elevating one's status or position.
