Etymology: The word "להורת" (lehoret) is a Hebrew verb that comes from the root ה.ו.ר (h.w.r), which means "to conceive" or "to become pregnant."

Grammar: "להורת" is an infinitive verb form in the Hebrew language. It belongs to the binyan Pa'al (פָּעַל) and is conjugated in the present tense as follows:
- Singular: אני מורה (ani moreh) - I conceive
- Plural: אנחנו מורים (anachnu morim) - We conceive

Description: The word "להורת" is used to describe the act of conceiving or becoming pregnant. It is commonly used in the context of human reproduction.
