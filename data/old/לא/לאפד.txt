- אני אופד (ani o'fed) - I collect
- אתה אופד (atah o'fed) - You collect (masculine singular)

- היא אופדת (hi o'fedet) - She collects
- אנחנו אופדים (anachnu o'fedim) - We collect
- אתם אופדים (atem o'fedim) - You collect (masculine plural)
- אתן אופדות (aten o'fedot) - You collect (feminine plural)
- הם אופדים (hem o'fedim) - They collect (masculine)
- הן אופדות (hen o'fedot) - They collect (feminine)

Description: The verb "לאפד" is used to describe the action of gathering or collecting things together. It can be used in various contexts, such as collecting objects, gathering information, or assembling a group of people.
