Etymology: The word "לאיפה" (le'eyfoh) is a Hebrew word that means "where". It is derived from the root א.י.פ (a.y.f), which means "to where" or "to what place".

Grammar: "לאיפה" is an interrogative pronoun in Hebrew, used to ask about the location or destination of something or someone. It is commonly used in questions such as "לאיפה אתה הולך?" (le'eyfoh atah holech?), which means "Where are you going?".

Description: "לאיפה" is a versatile word that is used in various contexts to inquire about the location or destination of something or someone. It is an essential word in everyday conversations and is used to gather information about directions, places, or actions.
