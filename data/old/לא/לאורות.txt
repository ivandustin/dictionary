Etymology: The word "לאורות" (L'orot) is a Hebrew word that comes from the root word "אור" (or), which means "light". The suffix "ות" (ot) is added to form a plural noun.

Grammar: "לאורות" (L'orot) is a feminine plural noun in Hebrew. It is used to refer to multiple sources of light.

Description: "לאורות" (L'orot) is often used metaphorically to describe sources of knowledge, enlightenment, or guidance. It can also refer to literal sources of light, such as lamps or torches.
