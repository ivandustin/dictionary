Grammar: "לאות" is a noun in the singular form. It is used to refer to a sign, mark, or symbol that represents something.

Description: "לאות" is a versatile word that can be used in various contexts. It can refer to physical signs or symbols, such as road signs or symbols used in mathematics or music. It can also be used metaphorically to describe something that serves as an indication or evidence of something else.
