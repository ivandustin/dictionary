Grammar: "לקאת" (lek'et) is a verb in the Hebrew language. It is conjugated in the third person singular form. The verb is used to describe the action of gathering or collecting something.

Description: "לקאת" (lek'et) is often used in the context of agriculture or harvesting. It refers to the act of gathering leftover crops or grains that were missed during the initial harvest.
