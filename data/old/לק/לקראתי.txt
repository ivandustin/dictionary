Grammar: "לקראתי" is a prepositional phrase in Hebrew. The preposition "ל" (le) means "to" or "towards," and "קראתי" is the first person singular form of the verb "קרא" (kara), which means "to call" or "to read." Together, they form the phrase "לקראתי," which means "to me" or "towards me."

Description: "לקראתי" is used to indicate that something is directed or intended for the speaker. It can be used in various contexts, such as expressing anticipation or preparation for an event or situation.
