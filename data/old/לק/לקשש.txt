Etymology: The word "לקשש" (lekashesh) is a Hebrew verb that comes from the root "ק.ש.ש" (k.sh.sh). 

Grammar: "לקשש" is an active verb in the Hebrew language. It is conjugated in the present tense as follows:
- Singular: אני לקשש (ani lekashesh) - I am investigating
- Plural: אנחנו לקששים (anachnu lekasheshim) - We are investigating

Description: The word "לקשש" means "to investigate" or "to examine" in English. It is often used to describe the act of thoroughly examining or researching a subject or situation.
