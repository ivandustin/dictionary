Etymology: The word "ינחלם" (Yinachlem) is a Hebrew verb derived from the root נ-ח-ל (N-Ch-L). 

Grammar: "ינחלם" is a verb in the third person singular masculine form. It is in the future tense and the reflexive form. 

Description: The word "ינחלם" is used to describe an action that will be done by the subject to oneself in the future. It implies a sense of self-reflection or self-improvement.
