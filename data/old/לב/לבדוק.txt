Etymology: The word "לבדוק" (levadok) is a Hebrew verb that comes from the root word "בדק" (badak), which means "to examine" or "to check."

Grammar: "לבדוק" is an infinitive verb form in Hebrew. It belongs to the binyan (verb conjugation) called "Pi'el," which is often used to express intensive or repeated action.
