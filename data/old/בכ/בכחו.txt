Etymology: The word "בכחו" (b'kocho) is a Hebrew phrase that translates to "by his power" or "by his strength." It is derived from the Hebrew root כח (koach), which means "strength" or "power."

Grammar: "בכחו" (b'kocho) is a prepositional phrase in Hebrew. The word "ב" (b) means "by" or "with," and "כחו" (kocho) is the possessive form of the noun כח (koach), meaning "his power" or "his strength."

Description: "בכחו" (b'kocho) is used in Hebrew to express the idea of accomplishing something through one's own power or strength. It emphasizes the individual's ability to achieve a goal or overcome an obstacle independently.
