Grammar: "וְגָשַם" is a Hebrew verb in the present tense, third person singular form. It is derived from the root ג-ש-ם (g-sh-m) and is conjugated with the prefix וְ (vav) to indicate the conjunction "and" and the vowel pattern קָטַל (qatal) to indicate the present tense.
