Grammar: "כְּלִים" is a plural noun in Hebrew. It belongs to the masculine gender and is used to refer to multiple objects or utensils.

Description: In Hebrew, "כְּלִים" refers to various types of vessels or utensils, such as pots, pans, dishes, and other containers used for cooking, serving, or storing food. These objects are essential in daily life and are commonly found in kitchens and households.
