Grammar: "יותר" is an adverb in Hebrew. It is used to compare two things, indicating that one thing is more or less than the other. It is often translated as "more" in English.

Description: "יותר" is a versatile word that is commonly used in everyday Hebrew conversations. It is used to express comparative degrees, such as "more beautiful," "more intelligent," or "more expensive." It can also be used to indicate a preference, such as "I like this one more." Overall, "יותר" is an essential word in Hebrew for expressing comparisons and preferences.
