Grammar: In Hebrew, נדבה is a feminine noun. It is usually used in the singular form, but it can also be used in the plural form נְדָבוֹת (Nedavot). It can be modified by adjectives or possessive pronouns to indicate different types of offerings or donations.

Description: נדבה refers to a voluntary act of giving or offering something willingly, without any obligation or expectation of reward.
