Grammar: "גדרות" is a plural noun in Hebrew. It is formed by adding the suffix "-ות" (-ot) to the singular form of the word, which is "גדר" (gader). 

Description: "גדרות" refers to fences or barriers that are used to enclose or separate areas. It can be used to describe physical fences made of wood, metal, or other materials, as well as metaphorical barriers or boundaries. Fences are commonly used for security, privacy, or to mark property lines.
