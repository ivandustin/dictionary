The first person singular past tense form is used when the subject of the sentence is "I" and the action happened in the past.

Description: "גדלתי" is a Hebrew word that translates to "I grew" or "I enlarged" in English. It is used to express the action of growing or enlarging something in the past tense, specifically when the speaker is referring to themselves as the subject.
