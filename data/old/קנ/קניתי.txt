Grammar: "קניתי" is the past tense form of the verb קנה (kaneh), which means "to acquire" or "to buy." In Hebrew, verbs are conjugated based on the subject and the tense of the sentence. The first person singular past tense form of קנה is קניתי, which means "I acquired" or "I bought."

Description: "קניתי" is a Hebrew verb that indicates an action of acquiring or buying that was done in the past by the speaker. It is used when the subject of the sentence is "I" and the action happened in the past.
