- Singular:

  - 2nd person: אַתָּה בָּכָה (atah bakah) - You (masculine) weep
  - 3rd person: הוּא בָּכָה (hu bakah) - He weeps, הִיא בָּכָה (hi bakah) - She weeps

- Plural:

  - 3rd person: הֵם בָּכִים (hem bakim) - They (masculine) weep, הֵן בָּכוֹת (hen bakot) - They (feminine) weep
