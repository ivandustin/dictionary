Grammar: "באופנים" is a plural noun in Hebrew. It is formed by adding the suffix "-ים" (-im) to the singular form "אופן" (ofen). The plural form indicates that there are multiple ways or manners being referred to.

Description: "באופנים" is often used in Hebrew to describe someone's behavior or attitude. It implies that the person is acting in a polite, considerate, or appropriate manner. It can also be used to describe a situation or event that is conducted in a proper or respectful way.
