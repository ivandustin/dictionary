Etymology: The word "בארצי" (ba'artzi) is a Hebrew word that means "in my country." It is derived from the Hebrew word "ארץ" (eretz), which means "land" or "country."

Grammar: "בארצי" (ba'artzi) is a prepositional phrase in Hebrew. It consists of the preposition "ב" (ba), meaning "in," and the possessive pronoun "ארצי" (artzi), meaning "my country." It is used to indicate possession or location.
