Etymology: The word "באלהיו" (pronounced "ba'alhayu") is a Hebrew phrase that translates to "in his life" or "in his lifetime." It is composed of two parts: "ב" (pronounced "ba") meaning "in" or "during," and "אלהיו" (pronounced "alhayu") meaning "his life" or "his lifetime."

Grammar: "באלהיו" is a prepositional phrase in Hebrew. The preposition "ב" indicates location or time, and it is followed by the noun "אלהיו" which means "his life" or "his lifetime." In Hebrew, prepositions are used to indicate relationships between words in a sentence.
