Grammar: "באולתו" is a noun phrase in Hebrew. It consists of the preposition "ב" (be) meaning "in" or "with", the root word "אול" (ul) meaning "ignorance", and the possessive suffix "תו" (to) meaning "his/her/its". 

Description: "באולתו" is used to describe someone or something that is acting or behaving in a state of ignorance. It implies a lack of knowledge, understanding, or awareness.
