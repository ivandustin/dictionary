Etymology: The word "באחור" (ba'achor) is a Hebrew word that comes from the root letters א-ח-ר (A-Ch-R), which means "behind" or "back". 

Grammar: "באחור" is a preposition in Hebrew that means "behind" or "in the back of". It is used to indicate the location or position of something in relation to another object or place. 

Description: "באחור" is a versatile word that can be used in various contexts. It is commonly used to describe the location of something that is situated behind or in the back of another object or place. For example, you can say "הכרית נמצאת באחור השולחן" (The pillow is behind the table). It can also be used metaphorically to describe something that is hidden or not easily seen, such as "האמת נמצאת באחור הסיפור" (The truth is behind the story).
