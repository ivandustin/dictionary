Grammar: "וָעֵנְנוּ" is a conjugated form of the verb ענה (anah), which means "to answer" or "to respond." It is in the plural, masculine, past tense, and perfect aspect. The prefix "וָ" (va-) indicates the conjunction "and," and the suffix "-נוּ" (-nu) indicates the first person plural pronoun "we."

Description: "וָעֵנְנוּ" translates to "and we answered" or "and we responded" in English. It is used to express the action of multiple people answering or responding to something.
