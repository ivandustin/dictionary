Grammar: "וֺקְפִים" is a masculine plural noun in Hebrew. It belongs to the binyan (verb conjugation) called "קָטַל" (qatal), which is used to form the past tense.

Description: "וֺקְפִים" refers to "covers" or "enclosures." It can be used to describe various objects or structures that serve as a protective covering or enclosure.
