Etymology: The word "יחרק" (Yachraq) comes from the Hebrew root ח-ר-ק (ch-r-q), which means "to burn" or "to scorch."

Description: The word "יחרק" means "to burn" in Hebrew. It is used to describe the action of setting something on fire or causing it to be consumed by fire. It can also be used metaphorically to describe intense emotions or strong desires.
