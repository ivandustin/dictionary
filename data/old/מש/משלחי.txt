Grammar: "משלחי" (mashlachi) is a plural noun in Hebrew. It is used to refer to a group of people who are sent or dispatched for a specific purpose.

Description: "משלחי" (mashlachi) can be translated as "dispatchers" or "senders" in English. It is often used to describe individuals or teams who are responsible for sending or delivering something, such as messengers, couriers, or agents.
