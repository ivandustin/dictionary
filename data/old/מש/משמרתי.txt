Etymology: The word "משמרתי" comes from the Hebrew root שמר (shamar), which means "to guard" or "to keep". The suffix תי (ti) indicates the possessive form, meaning "my" or "of mine".

Grammar: "משמרתי" is a Hebrew adjective in the masculine singular form. It is used to describe something that belongs to the speaker or is associated with the speaker.
