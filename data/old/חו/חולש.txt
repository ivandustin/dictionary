Grammar: "חולש" is a noun in Hebrew, and it is in the masculine singular form. It can also be used as a verb in the present tense, such as "אני חולש" (ani cholash), which means "I am weak."

Description: "חולש" refers to a state of weakness or feebleness. It can describe physical weakness, such as lack of strength or energy, as well as emotional or mental weakness. It can also be used metaphorically to describe a weak or feeble situation or argument.
