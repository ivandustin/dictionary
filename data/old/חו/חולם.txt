Etymology: The word "חולם" (cholm) comes from the Hebrew root ח-ו-ל (ch-w-l), which means "to dream." 

Grammar: "חולם" is a masculine noun in Hebrew. It is in the singular form. 

Description: "חולם" means "dream" in Hebrew. It refers to the series of thoughts, images, and sensations that occur during sleep. It can also be used metaphorically to describe a cherished aspiration or goal.
