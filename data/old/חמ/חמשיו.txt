Grammar: "חמשיו" is a noun in Hebrew. It is the masculine form of the word, and it means "fifth." It is used to describe the position or order of something in a series, indicating that it is the fifth in line.

Description: "חמשיו" is used to indicate the ordinal number "fifth" in Hebrew. It is commonly used in various contexts, such as describing the fifth day of the week (יום חמישי, yom chamishi), the fifth month of the year (חודש חמישי, chodesh chamishi), or the fifth item in a list.
