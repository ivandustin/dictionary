- Feminine singular: אַתְּ חוֹמֶקֶת (at chomeket) - You (feminine) are hiding
- Masculine plural: אַתֶּם חוֹמְקִים (atem chomkim) - You (masculine plural) are hiding
- Feminine plural: אַתֶּן חוֹמְקוֹת (aten chomkot) - You (feminine plural) are hiding
