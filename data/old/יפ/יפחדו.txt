Etymology: The word "יפחדו" (Yifchadu) is a Hebrew verb in the future tense, derived from the root פ-ח-ד (P-Ch-D). 

Grammar: "יפחדו" is the third person plural future tense form of the verb פחד (pachad), which means "to fear" or "to be afraid" in Hebrew. The future tense indicates that the action of fearing will occur in the future.

Description: "יפחדו" is used to describe the action of multiple individuals fearing or being afraid of something in the future. It can be used in various contexts to express fear or apprehension about a particular situation or event.
