Etymology: The word "יפיק" (Yafik) is derived from the Hebrew root י-פ-ק (Y-P-Q), which means "to draw out" or "to extract."

Grammar: "יפיק" (Yafik) is a verb in the Hebrew language. It is conjugated in the present tense as follows:
- Singular: אני יופיק (Ani yofik) - I draw out
- Plural: אנחנו יופיקים (Anachnu yofikim) - We draw out

Description: The word "יפיק" (Yafik) is used to describe the action of drawing out or extracting something. It can be used in various contexts, such as extracting information, extracting resources, or extracting a solution to a problem.
