Etymology: The word "אצרות" (atzarot) comes from the Hebrew root צרה (tsarah), which means "to gather" or "to store". It is derived from the noun אצר (otzar), meaning "treasure" or "storehouse".

Grammar: "אצרות" is a plural noun in Hebrew. It is formed by adding the suffix ות (-ot) to the singular form אצר (otzar).
