- אתה אצפצף (atah atzpatsaf) - You tweet (masculine singular)
- את אצפצפת (at atzpatsafet) - You tweet (feminine singular)
- הוא אצפצף (hu atzpatsaf) - He tweets
- היא אצפצפה (hi atzpatsafah) - She tweets
- אנחנו אצפצפים (anachnu atzpatsafim) - We tweet
- אתם אצפצפים (atem atzpatsafim) - You tweet (masculine plural)

- הם אצפצפים (hem atzpatsafim) - They tweet (masculine)
- הן אצפצפות (hen atzpatsafot) - They tweet (feminine)

Description: "אצפצף" is a verb that specifically refers to the action of tweeting, similar to the sound made by birds. In modern usage, it is commonly associated with posting messages on social media platforms, particularly Twitter.
