Grammar: In Hebrew, "אצל" (etzel) is a preposition that means "at" or "with". It is used to indicate proximity or possession. It is often used to express the idea of being at someone's place or having something in one's possession.

Description: "אצל" (etzel) is a versatile word that can be used in various contexts. It can be used to indicate physical location, such as "אני אצל הבית" (I am at home). It can also be used to indicate possession, such as "הספר אצלי" (The book is with me).
