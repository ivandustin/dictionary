Grammar: התפל is a verb in the reflexive form, which means that the subject of the verb is also the object. It is conjugated in the present tense as follows:

- Singular: אני מתפל (Ani mitpale), "I judge myself"
- Plural: אנחנו מתפלים (Anachnu mitpaleem), "We judge ourselves"
