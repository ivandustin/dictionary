Grammar: התפלל is a verb in the Hebrew language. It is conjugated in the reflexive form, which means that the subject is performing the action on themselves. In the present tense, it is conjugated as follows:
- Singular: אני מתפלל (ani mitpallel) - I pray
- Plural: אנחנו מתפללים (anachnu mitpallelim) - We pray

Description: התפלל is a fundamental concept in Judaism, referring to the act of praying or engaging in prayer.
