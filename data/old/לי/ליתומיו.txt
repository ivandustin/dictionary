Grammar: "ליתומיו" is a compound word consisting of two parts: "ליתו" (litu) and "מיו" (mio). It is formed by combining the negative particle "ליתו" with the interrogative pronoun "מיו." In Hebrew grammar, it is used to ask a negative question, similar to the English phrase "Who doesn't...?"
