Grammar: In Hebrew, "עליו" is a preposition that is used to indicate the location or position of something on top of or above something else. It is commonly used to express the idea of "on" or "upon" in various contexts.

It can indicate the physical location of something on top of or above something else, such as "the book is on the table" (הספר על השולחן). Additionally, it can be used to indicate the subject or topic of a conversation or discussion, such as "let's talk about the weather" (נדבר על המזג אוויר).
