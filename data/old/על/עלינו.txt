Etymology: The word "עלינו" (pronounced "alaynu") is a Hebrew word that translates to "upon us" or "our responsibility." It is derived from the Hebrew root ע-ל-ה (ayin-lamed-hei), which means "to ascend" or "to go up."

Grammar: "עלינו" is a pronoun in Hebrew that is used to express the idea of something being the responsibility or duty of a group of people. It is often used in religious or ceremonial contexts, such as in the Jewish prayer "עלינו לשבח" (Alaynu Leshabeach), which means "It is upon us to praise."

Description: "עלינו" is a powerful word that conveys a sense of obligation and duty. It emphasizes the collective responsibility of a group or community to fulfill a certain task or duty. It is often used to express a sense of commitment and dedication to a particular cause or purpose.
