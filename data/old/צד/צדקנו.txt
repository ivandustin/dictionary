Grammar: "צדקנו" is a verb in the past tense, first-person plural form. It indicates that "we" (the speaker and others) have performed an action related to justice or righteousness.

Description: The word "צדקנו" can be translated as "we have justified" or "we have acted justly." It implies that the speaker and others have done something that aligns with principles of fairness, righteousness, or moral correctness.
