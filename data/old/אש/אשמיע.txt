Etymology: The word "אַשְמִיעַ" (Ashmi'a) is derived from the Hebrew root שמע (sh-m-a), which means "to hear" or "to listen". 

Grammar: אַשְמִיעַ is a verb in the Hebrew language. It is conjugated in the first person singular form, present tense. 

Description: אַשְמִיעַ means "I will make heard" or "I will cause to be heard" in English. It implies the action of making something or someone known or heard by others.
