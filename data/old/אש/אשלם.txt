Etymology: The word "אשלם" (Ashlam) is derived from the Hebrew root "שלם" (Sh.L.M), which means "to pay" or "to complete." 

Grammar: "אשלם" is a verb in the Hebrew language. It is conjugated in the first person singular form of the future tense. 

Description: The word "אשלם" translates to "I will pay" or "I will complete" in English. It expresses the action of fulfilling a financial obligation or finishing a task.
