Etymology: The word "אשימנה" (Ashimnah) is a Hebrew word that comes from the root word "שמן" (shaman), which means "to anoint" or "to smear with oil." 

Grammar: "אשימנה" is a verb in the Hebrew language, specifically in the future tense. It is conjugated in the third person singular form. 

Description: The word "אשימנה" is used to express an action that will happen in the future. It can be translated as "he/she/it will anoint" or "he/she/it will smear with oil."
