- Singular masculine: מְחַנֵּן (mechanein)
- Singular feminine: מְחַנֶּנֶת (mechaneenet)
- Plural masculine: מְחַנְּנִים (mechannenim)
- Plural feminine: מְחַנְּנוֹת (mechannenot)

Description: The verb "לחונן" means "to show favor" or "to be gracious". It is often used to describe an act of kindness or mercy shown towards someone. It can also imply bestowing blessings or benefits upon someone.
