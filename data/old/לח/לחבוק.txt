- אתם מחבקים (atem me'chavkim) - You (masculine plural) embrace
- אתן מחבקות (atan me'chavkot) - You (feminine plural) embrace
- הם מחבקים (hem me'chavkim) - They (masculine) embrace
- הן מחבקות (hen me'chavkot) - They (feminine) embrace

Description: "לחבוק" is a Hebrew verb that means "to embrace" or "to hug." It refers to the act of holding someone tightly in one's arms as a gesture of affection, comfort, or greeting.
