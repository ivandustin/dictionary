Etymology: The word "לחה" (lachah) comes from the Hebrew root "לחה" (lamed, chet, hey), which means "to lick" or "to moisten." 

Grammar: "לחה" is a verb in the Hebrew language, specifically in the past tense form. It is conjugated in the third person singular feminine form.
