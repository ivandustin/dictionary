Etymology: The word "הבדיל" (Ha'badil) is derived from the Hebrew root ב-ד-ל (B-D-L), which means "to separate" or "to distinguish."

Grammar: "הבדיל" is a verb in the Hebrew language. It is conjugated in the past tense, third person, singular form. The root letters ב-ד-ל are modified to fit the grammatical structure of the verb.

Description: "הבדיל" means "he/she/it separated" or "he/she/it distinguished" in Hebrew. It is often used to describe the act of setting something apart or making a distinction between two or more things.
