Grammar: "האשרות" is a plural noun in Hebrew. It is formed by adding the suffix ות (ot) to the root אשר (ashar). The definite article ה (ha) is added at the beginning to indicate that it is a specific group of blessings.

Description: "האשרות" refers to the blessings that are recited before and after meals in Jewish tradition. These blessings express gratitude to God for providing sustenance and nourishment. They are an important part of Jewish rituals and are recited before eating bread, as well as other food and drinks.
