- אני שוכב (ani shokhev) - I lie down
- אתה שוכב (ata shokhev) - You (masculine singular) lie down
- הוא/היא שוכב (hu/hi shokhev) - He/She lies down
- אנחנו שוכבים (anachnu shokvim) - We lie down
- אתם שוכבים (atem shokvim) - You (masculine plural) lie down
- הם/הן שוכבים (hem/hen shokvim) - They lie down

Description: "לשכב" (lishkav) is a common Hebrew verb used to describe the action of lying down or sleeping. It can be used in various contexts, such as referring to physical rest or relaxation, going to bed, or taking a nap.
