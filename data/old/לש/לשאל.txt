Etymology: The word "לשאל" (lesh'ol) comes from the Hebrew root "שאל" (sha'al), which means "to ask" or "to inquire".

Grammar: "לשאל" (lesh'ol) is an infinitive verb form in Hebrew. It is commonly used in the present tense to express the act of asking or inquiring.

Description: "לשאל" (lesh'ol) is a versatile verb in Hebrew that can be used in various contexts. It can refer to asking a question, seeking information, or making an inquiry. It is often used to express curiosity, request for clarification, or to gather knowledge.
