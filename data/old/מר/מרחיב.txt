Etymology: The word "מרחיב" (marḥiv) is derived from the Hebrew root רחב (rḥb), which means "wide" or "broad".

Grammar: "מרחיב" is a verb in the Hebrew language. It belongs to the binyan Pa'al (פָּעַל) and is conjugated in the present tense as follows:
- Singular masculine: מרחיב (marḥiv)
- Singular feminine: מרחיבה (marḥivah)
- Plural masculine: מרחיבים (marḥivim)
- Plural feminine: מרחיבות (marḥivot)

Description: The word "מרחיב" means "to widen" or "to broaden" in Hebrew. It is often used to describe the action of making something wider or more spacious. It can also be used metaphorically to express the idea of expanding or broadening one's knowledge, perspective, or understanding.
