Grammar: In Hebrew, "מרקע" (marke'a) is a masculine noun. It is singular and can be used in both the definite and indefinite forms. The plural form of "מרקע" is "מרקעות" (marke'ot).

Description: "מרקע" (marke'a) refers to the ground, land, or soil. It can also be used metaphorically to describe the foundation or basis of something. In legal contexts, it can refer to real estate or property.
