Etymology: The word "מרומי" (pronounced "meromi") is a Hebrew word that comes from the root רום (rum), which means "to rise" or "to elevate." The suffix י (i) is added to indicate possession or belonging.

Grammar: In Hebrew, "מרומי" is an adjective that describes something as being elevated or high. It is in the masculine singular form. The word can be modified to match different genders, numbers, and cases.
