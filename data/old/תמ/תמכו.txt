Grammar: "תמקו" is the plural form of the verb, used when referring to a group of people or objects completing an action. In Hebrew, verbs are conjugated according to the subject and tense of the sentence. The plural form is typically used when the subject is a group of three or more individuals or objects.

Description: "תמקו" is a versatile verb that can be used in various contexts. It can describe the completion of a task, the finishing of a project, or the fulfillment of a goal. It implies that the action has been carried out to its conclusion, indicating a sense of accomplishment or achievement.
