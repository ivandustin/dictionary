Etymology: The word "תמוט" (tamut) comes from the Hebrew root מות (mwt), which means "to die" or "to perish". 

Grammar: "תמוט" is a verb in the Hebrew language. It is in the present tense, third person singular form. 

Description: "תמוט" means "he/she/it dies" or "he/she/it perishes" in Hebrew. It is used to describe the act of someone or something ceasing to live or exist.
