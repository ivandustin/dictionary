Grammar: In Hebrew, "המשנה" (HaMishnah) is a noun that is used with the definite article "ה" (ha) to indicate "the Mishnah." It is in the singular form.

Description: The Mishnah is a foundational text of Jewish law and tradition. It is part of the Talmud, which is a collection of Jewish legal and ethical teachings.
