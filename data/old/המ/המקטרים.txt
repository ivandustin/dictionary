Etymology: The word "המקטרים" (HaMaktarim) is a Hebrew word that comes from the root ק.ט.ר (k.t.r), which means "to burn" or "to ignite". The prefix ה (ha) means "the". Therefore, "המקטרים" can be translated as "the burners" or "the igniters".

Grammar: "המקטרים" is a plural noun in Hebrew. It is formed by adding the suffix ים (im) to the root ק.ט.ר (k.t.r). The prefix ה (ha) indicates definiteness, making it "the burners" or "the igniters".
