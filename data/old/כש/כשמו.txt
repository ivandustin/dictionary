Etymology: The word "כשמו" (k'shmo) is a Hebrew phrase that translates to "as its name." It is composed of two parts: "כ" (k) meaning "as" or "like," and "שמו" (shmo) meaning "its name."

Grammar: "כשמו" is a prepositional phrase in Hebrew. It is used to compare or describe something by referring to its name.

Description: The phrase "כשמו" is commonly used in Hebrew to indicate that something is being referred to by its name. It is often used to clarify or emphasize the specific name of a person, place, or thing.
