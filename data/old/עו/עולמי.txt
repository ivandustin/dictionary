Etymology: The word "עולמי" (olami) comes from the Hebrew root ע-ל-ם (ayin-lamed-mem), which means "world" or "universe". The suffix י (yod) is added to indicate possession or belonging.

Grammar: "עולמי" (olami) is an adjective in Hebrew. It is in the masculine singular form. It can be used to describe something that is related to or belongs to the world or universe.
