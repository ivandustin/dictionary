Etymology: The word "עוד" (pronounced "od") comes from the Hebrew language. It is derived from the Hebrew root ע.ו.ד (Ayin-Vav-Dalet), which means "to add" or "to continue."

Grammar: "עוד" is an adverb in Hebrew. It is used to express the idea of "still," "yet," or "again." It is often used to indicate that something is continuing or happening in addition to what has already occurred.

Description: "עוד" is a versatile word that can be used in various contexts. It can be used to express the idea of "more" or "additional," as in "עוד כוס קפה" (another cup of coffee). It can also be used to indicate that something is still happening or continuing, as in "עוד לא סיימתי" (I haven't finished yet).
