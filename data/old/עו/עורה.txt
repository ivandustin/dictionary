Grammar: "עורה" is a verb in the Hebrew language. It belongs to the binyan (verb conjugation) called "piel," which often indicates an intensive or causative action. In this case, "עורה" is in the present tense, third person singular form.

Description: The word "עורה" means "to wake up" or "to arise" in Hebrew. It is commonly used to describe the action of waking up from sleep or becoming alert. It can also be used metaphorically to describe a spiritual or intellectual awakening.
