Grammar: In Hebrew grammar, "וָאֹת" is known as the direct object marker. It is used to mark the direct object of a verb, indicating that the action of the verb is being performed on that object.

Description: "וָאֹת" is a small but important word in Hebrew grammar. It helps to clarify the relationship between the subject and the object in a sentence. Without it, the meaning of a sentence can be ambiguous.
