Grammar: "וָאֵלָה" is a conjunction in Hebrew, specifically a combination of the conjunction "וְ" (pronounced "vuh") meaning "and" and the pronoun "אֵלֶה" meaning "these." It is used to connect two or more nouns, phrases, or clauses together.

Description: "וָאֵלָה" is commonly used in the Hebrew Bible (Tanakh) to introduce a list or series of items. It is often translated as "and these" or "and these are." It is used to present a group of things or people that are being mentioned or described.
