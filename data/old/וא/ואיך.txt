It is a combination of the conjunction "וְ" (pronounced "ve") meaning "and" and the interrogative pronoun "אֵיךְ" (pronounced "eykh") meaning "how."

Grammar: "וָאֵיךְ" is a Hebrew word that functions as an adverb, specifically an interrogative adverb. It is used to ask questions about the manner or way in which something is done.

Description: "וָאֵיךְ" is commonly translated as "how" in English. It is used to inquire about the method, manner, or way in which an action is performed or a situation is experienced.
