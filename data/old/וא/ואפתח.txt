Grammar: "וָאַפְתַּח" is a conjugated form of the verb in the past tense, first person singular. It is composed of the prefix וָ (vav) indicating past tense, the root אפתח (ptch), and the suffix תַּ־ (ta-) indicating first person singular.

Description: "וָאַפְתַּח" translates to "and I opened" in English. It is used to describe an action of opening or unlocking something.
