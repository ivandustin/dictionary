Grammar: "אדוניהם" is a plural noun in Hebrew. It is formed by adding the suffix "-יהם" (-hem) to the word "אדוני" (adoni). The suffix "-יהם" (-hem) indicates possession, meaning "their" in English.

Description: "אדוניהם" is used to refer to a group of people who possess or have authority over something. It is often translated as "their lords" or "their masters" in English.
