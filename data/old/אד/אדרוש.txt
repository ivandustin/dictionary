Etymology: The word "אדרוש" (pronounced "ad-rosh") is a Hebrew word that comes from the root ד-ר-ש (D-R-Sh), which means "to seek" or "to inquire." 

Grammar: אדרוש is a verb in the first person singular form of the future tense in Hebrew. It is derived from the root ד-ר-ש (D-R-Sh) and is conjugated in the future tense by adding the appropriate suffixes and vowel patterns.

Description: אדרוש is translated to English as "I will seek" or "I will inquire." It expresses the action of actively searching for or seeking something. It can be used in various contexts, such as seeking knowledge, information, guidance, or answers.
