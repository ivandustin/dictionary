Etymology: The word "נזהר" (nizhar) comes from the Hebrew root ז-ה-ר (z-h-r), which means "to be careful" or "to be cautious."

Grammar: "נזהר" (nizhar) is a verb in the Hebrew language. It is conjugated in the present tense as follows:
- Singular masculine: אני נזהר (ani nizhar) - I am careful
- Singular feminine: אני נזהרת (ani nizharet) - I am careful
- Plural masculine: אנחנו נזהרים (anachnu nizharim) - We are careful
- Plural feminine: אנחנו נזהרות (anachnu nizharot) - We are careful

Description: The word "נזהר" (nizhar) describes the act of being cautious or careful. It implies being attentive and taking precautions to avoid potential harm or mistakes.
