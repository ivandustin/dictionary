Grammar: "וֹדֵר" is a verb in the present tense, third person masculine singular form. It is derived from the binyan (verb structure) called "פָּעַל" (pa'al), which is one of the seven verb structures in Hebrew.

Description: "וֹדֵר" means "he dwells" or "he abides" in Hebrew. It is used to describe someone who resides or lives in a particular place.
