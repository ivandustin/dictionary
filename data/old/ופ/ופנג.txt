- Singular: אני ופנג (ani vafang) - I turn
- Plural: אנחנו ופנגים (anachnu vafangim) - We turn

Description: "ופנג" is used to describe the action of turning or rotating an object or oneself. It can be used in various contexts, such as physically turning an object or changing direction while walking.
