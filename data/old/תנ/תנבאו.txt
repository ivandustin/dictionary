Etymology: The word "תנבאו" (pronounced "tena'bau") is a Hebrew verb that comes from the root נ-ב-א (n-b-a), which means "to prophesy" or "to foretell." 

Grammar: "תנבאו" is the plural form of the verb in the future tense, meaning "you (plural) will prophesy" or "you (plural) will foretell." It is used when addressing a group of people.

Description: "תנבאו" is a command or an instruction given to a group of people to prophesy or foretell. It is often used in religious or spiritual contexts, where individuals are called upon to deliver messages or predictions from a divine source.
