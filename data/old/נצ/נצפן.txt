- Masculine plural: אַתֶּם נִצְפָּנִים (atem nitzpanim) - You hide (masculine plural)
- Feminine plural: אַתֶּן נִצְפַּנּוֹת (aten nitzpanot) - You hide (feminine plural)

Description: The word "נצפן" means "to hide" or "to conceal" in Hebrew. It is used to describe the action of keeping something out of sight or making it inaccessible to others.
