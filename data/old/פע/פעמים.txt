Grammar: "פעמים" is a plural noun in Hebrew. It is used to refer to multiple occurrences or repetitions of something. 

Description: "פעמים" is commonly used in everyday Hebrew to express the idea of "times" or "occurrences". It can be used to indicate how many times something has happened or will happen. For example, "אני ראיתי אותו שלוש פעמים" (I saw him three times) or "הוא תמיד מגיע בזמן עשר פעמים" (He always arrives on time ten times).
