Etymology: The word "פעלתי" (pronounced "pe'alti") is a Hebrew verb form that belongs to the binyan Pa'al (פָּעַל) in Hebrew grammar. It is derived from the root letters פ-ע-ל (P-A-L), which convey the meaning of "to do" or "to act."

Grammar: "פעלתי" is the first-person singular past tense form of the verb in the active voice. It indicates that the action was performed by the speaker in the past. In Hebrew, verbs are conjugated according to tense, person, and number.

Description: The word "פעלתי" translates to "I did" or "I acted" in English. It is used to express an action that the speaker performed in the past.
