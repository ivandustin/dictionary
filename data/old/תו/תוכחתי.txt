Grammar: "תוכחתי" is an adjective in the masculine singular form. In Hebrew, adjectives agree in gender, number, and definiteness with the noun they modify. The feminine singular form is תוכחתית (tokhechetit), the masculine plural form is תוכחתיים (tokhechatiyim), and the feminine plural form is תוכחתיות (tokhechatiyot).

Description: The word "תוכחתי" describes something that is related to rebuke or admonishment. It can be used to describe a person, behavior, or speech that is characterized by giving constructive criticism or expressing disapproval in order to correct or improve.
