Etymology: The word "יאמינו" (pronounced "yaaminu") is a Hebrew verb derived from the root אמן (pronounced "amen"), which means "to believe" or "to trust". 

Grammar: "יאמינו" is the third person plural future tense form of the verb אמן. In Hebrew, verbs are conjugated according to tense, person, and number. The future tense indicates an action that will happen in the future. The third person plural form refers to a group of people or entities.

Description: "יאמינו" means "they will believe" or "they will trust" in English. It is used to express the future action of a group of people or entities placing their trust or belief in something or someone.
