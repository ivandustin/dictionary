Description: In Hebrew, "שֵׁת" has multiple meanings depending on the context. It can refer to a foundation, base, or support. It can also mean a plant or shoot, particularly in botanical contexts. Additionally, "שֵׁת" can be used metaphorically to describe something that is established or set in place.
