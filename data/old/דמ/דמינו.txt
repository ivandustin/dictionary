Etymology: The word "דמינו" (pronounced "diminu") comes from the Hebrew language. It is derived from the root ד-מ-ן (D-M-N), which means "to compare" or "to resemble."

Grammar: "דמינו" is a verb in the past tense, third person plural form. It is commonly used in biblical Hebrew.

Description: The word "דמינו" means "they compared" or "they resembled" in English. It is used to describe an action of comparing or finding similarities between two or more things.
