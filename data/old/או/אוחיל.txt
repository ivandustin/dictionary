- אוֹחֵל (ohel) - I wait/hope
- אוֹחֵלָה (ohela) - You (feminine) wait/hope
- אוֹחֵלְתִּי (ohelti) - You (masculine) wait/hope
- אוֹחֵלָה (ohela) - He/She waits/hopes
- אוֹחֵל (ohel) - We wait/hope
- אוֹחְלוּ (ochlu) - You (plural) wait/hope
- אוֹחְלוּ (ochlu) - They wait/hope

Description: אוחיל is a verb that expresses the action of waiting or hoping for something. It can be used in various contexts, such as waiting for a person, an event, or a specific outcome. It conveys a sense of anticipation and expectation.
