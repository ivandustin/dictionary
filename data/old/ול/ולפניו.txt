Grammar: "וָלְפָנִיו" is a prepositional phrase in Hebrew. The preposition "וְ" (v') connects it to the following word or phrase, indicating a relationship of location or direction. The noun "פָּנִים" (pa'nim) is in the plural form, indicating multiple faces or fronts.

Description: "וָלְפָנִיו" is used in Hebrew to describe something that is positioned or located in front of someone or something. It can be used both literally, referring to a physical location, or figuratively, indicating a position of importance or priority.
