Etymology: The word "וָלַד" (pronounced "valad") comes from the Hebrew root יָלַד (yalad), which means "to give birth" or "to bear children."

Grammar: "וָלַד" is a verb in the Hebrew language. It is conjugated in different tenses, persons, and numbers to indicate the act of giving birth or bearing children.

Description: "וָלַד" is used to describe the action of a female animal or human giving birth to offspring. It signifies the process of bringing new life into the world.
