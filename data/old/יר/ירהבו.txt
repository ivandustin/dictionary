Etymology: The word "ירהבו" (Yerhavu) is a Hebrew word.

Grammar: "ירהבו" is a verb in the third person plural form. It is derived from the root ר-ה-ב (R-H-B), which means "to shoot" or "to throw."

Description: "ירהבו" is used in the Hebrew Bible (Old Testament) and is often translated as "they shoot" or "they throw." It is used to describe the action of shooting or throwing something forcefully.
