Grammar: "ירדי" is a plural noun in Hebrew. It is formed by adding the suffix י (-i) to the root י-ר-ד (Y-R-D). The plural form indicates that there are multiple individuals or objects that are descending or going down.

Description: The word "ירדי" is used to refer to individuals or objects that are descending or going down. It can be used in various contexts, such as describing people going down a staircase, objects falling from a higher place, or even metaphorically to describe a decrease or decline in something.
