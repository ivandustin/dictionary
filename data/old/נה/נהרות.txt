Grammar: "נהרות" is a plural noun in Hebrew. It is formed by adding the suffix ות (-ot) to the singular form נהר (nahar), which means "river". In Hebrew, nouns have different forms for singular and plural, and the plural form is used when referring to more than one river.

Description: "נהרות" refers to multiple rivers. It is commonly used in Hebrew to describe a collection or group of rivers.
