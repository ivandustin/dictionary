Grammar: In Hebrew, רחש is a noun that is used to describe the action of whispering or hissing. It can also be used as a verb, meaning "to whisper" or "to hiss."

Description: רחש is often used to describe the sound made by a snake or any other creature that hisses. It can also refer to the act of whispering, usually in a secretive or conspiratorial manner.
