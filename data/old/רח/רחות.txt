Etymology: The word "רחות" (rechot) is derived from the Hebrew root רחה (rachah), which means "to widen" or "to expand." It is related to the noun רחב (rachav), which means "wide" or "broad."

Grammar: "רחות" is a plural noun in Hebrew. It belongs to the feminine gender and is used to refer to wide or broad areas or spaces.
