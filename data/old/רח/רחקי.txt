Grammar: "רחקי" is the plural form of the adjective רחוק (rachok), which means "far" or "distant." It is used to describe objects or places that are physically far away or metaphorically distant in time, space, or relationship.

Description: "רחקי" is used to describe multiple objects or places that are far away from the speaker or from a specific point of reference. It can be used to indicate physical distance, such as "רחקי מקום" (faraway place), or metaphorical distance, such as "רחקי זמן" (distant time) or "רחקי יחסים" (distant relationship).
