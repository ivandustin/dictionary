Etymology: The word "כמוהם" comes from the Hebrew language. It is a combination of the preposition "כ" (meaning "like" or "as") and the plural form of the noun "מוהם" (meaning "them").

Grammar: "כמוהם" is a pronoun in Hebrew that means "like them" or "as them". It is used to compare or refer to a group of people or things that have already been mentioned.

Description: "כמוהם" is a versatile word that can be used in various contexts. It is often used to emphasize similarity or equality between two or more entities. For example, if someone says "הם רצים כמוהם" (they run like them), it means that they run in a similar manner to the group of people previously mentioned.
