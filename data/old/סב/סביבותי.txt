Etymology: The word "סביבותי" comes from the Hebrew root ס.ב.ב (s.b.b), which means "around" or "surrounding". The suffix י (i) indicates possession or belonging.

Grammar: "סביבותי" is an adjective in the masculine singular form. It agrees with a masculine singular noun it modifies.

Description: "סביבותי" means "surrounding" or "environmental" in Hebrew. It is used to describe something that is related to or connected with the surrounding or environment.
