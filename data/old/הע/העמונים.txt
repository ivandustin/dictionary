Grammar: "העמונים" is a plural noun in Hebrew. It is formed by adding the suffix ים (im) to the root word עַם (am). The prefix ה (ha) indicates the definite article "the."

Description: "העמונים" refers to the Ammonites, an ancient Semitic people who lived in the region of modern-day Jordan. They are mentioned in the Hebrew Bible and were known for their conflicts with the Israelites. The Ammonites had their own kingdom and worshiped their own gods.
