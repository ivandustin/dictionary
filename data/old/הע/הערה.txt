Etymology: The word "הערה" (ha'ara) comes from the Hebrew root ע.ר.ה (ayin.reish.hei), which means "to comment" or "to remark."

Grammar: "הערה" is a feminine noun in Hebrew. It is singular and can be used in both the definite and indefinite forms.

Description: "הערה" is commonly translated as "comment" or "remark" in English. It refers to a statement or observation made in response to something, often to provide additional information or clarification. In various contexts, it can also mean "note," "annotation," or "suggestion."
