Etymology: The word "העמסים" (Ha'amassim) is a Hebrew word derived from the root ע.מ.ס (A.M.S), which means "to load" or "to burden". 

Grammar: "העמסים" is a plural noun in Hebrew. It is formed by adding the suffix ים (im) to the root word עמס (amass). The prefix ה (ha) indicates the definite article "the".

Description: "העמסים" refers to the burdens or loads that someone carries. It can be used in a literal sense to describe physical loads or in a figurative sense to describe responsibilities, tasks, or emotional burdens.
