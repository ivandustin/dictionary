Etymology: The word "הענקות" (Ha'anakot) is a Hebrew noun derived from the root ענק (Anak), which means "to give" or "to bestow." 

Grammar: "הענקות" is a plural noun in Hebrew, indicating multiple instances of giving or bestowing. It is formed by adding the suffix ות (-ot) to the root ענק (Anak). 

Description: "הענקות" refers to the act of giving or bestowing something, often used in the context of awards, grants, or scholarships. It can also refer to the act of granting or conferring a privilege or honor.
