Etymology: The Hebrew word "הָעַפֵּל" (ha'apel) comes from the root עָפַל (afal), which means "to climb" or "to ascend."

Description: הָעַפֵּל is used to describe the action of climbing or ascending. It can be used both literally, to refer to physically climbing or ascending a height, and metaphorically, to describe progressing or advancing in a certain area or aspect.
