Grammar: In Hebrew, חרוץ is a masculine noun. It is usually used in the singular form, but it can also be used in the plural form חרוצים (charutzim).

Description: חרוץ refers to a groove, furrow, or a narrow channel that is carved or dug into a surface. It can be used to describe a physical indentation or a metaphorical concept.
