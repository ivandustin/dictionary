Grammar: "זרועתי" is a noun in the singular form. It is a possessive noun, indicating ownership or possession. In this case, it means "my planting" or "my sowing".

Description: "זרועתי" refers to something that belongs to the speaker and is related to planting or sowing. It can be used to describe a personal garden, a field, or any other area where the speaker plants or sows seeds.
