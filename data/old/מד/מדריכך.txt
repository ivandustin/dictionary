Grammar: "מדריך" is a masculine noun in Hebrew. It is singular and can be used in both the singular and plural forms. In the plural form, it becomes "מדריכים" (madrikhim).

Description: "מדריך" refers to a guide or a leader who provides assistance, direction, or instruction to others. It can be used to describe a person who leads a group, such as a tour guide, a teacher, or a mentor.
