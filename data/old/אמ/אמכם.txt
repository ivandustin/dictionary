- Singular:
  - First person: אָמְקֵם (omkem)
  - Second person: תָּמְקֵם (tomkem)
  - Third person: יָמְקֵם (yomkem)

- Plural:
  - First person: נָמְקֵם (nomkem)
  - Second person: תִּמְקְמוּ (timkemu)
  - Third person: יָמְקְמוּ (yomkemu)
