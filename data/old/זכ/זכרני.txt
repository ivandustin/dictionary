Etymology: The word "זכרני" comes from the Hebrew language. It is derived from the root word "זכר" (zakar), which means "to remember" or "to mention".

Grammar: "זכרני" is a verb in the imperative form. It is conjugated in the second person singular masculine, and it means "remember me" or "mention me".

Description: "זכרני" is a command or request for someone to remember or mention the speaker. It is often used when asking someone to keep the speaker in mind or to bring them up in conversation.
