Etymology: The word "מבארת" (miv'aret) is derived from the Hebrew root באר (ba'ar), which means "to explain" or "to clarify."

Grammar: "מבארת" (miv'aret) is a feminine noun in the singular form. It is used to describe something that provides an explanation or clarification.

Description: "מבארת" (miv'aret) refers to a source or tool that is used to explain or clarify something. It can be a written text, a commentary, or any other form of explanation.
