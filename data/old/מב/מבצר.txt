Grammar: "מבצר" is a masculine noun in Hebrew. It is singular and can be used in both the definite and indefinite forms. In the plural form, it becomes "מבצרים" (mivtzarim).

Description: "מבצר" refers to a fortress or stronghold, which is a fortified structure designed to protect and defend against attacks. It is typically built with strong walls, towers, and other defensive features. In ancient times, fortresses were used for military purposes, providing a strategic advantage in battles.
