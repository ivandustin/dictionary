Grammar: "סרתם" is the plural form of the verb סר (sar), which means "to turn aside" or "to deviate." It is conjugated in the past tense, third person, masculine plural.

Description: "סרתם" is used to describe a group of people who have turned aside or deviated from a certain path or course of action. It implies that they have strayed from the right path or have gone astray.
