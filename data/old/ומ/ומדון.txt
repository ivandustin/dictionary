Etymology: The word "וֹמְדוֹן" (vomdon) is derived from the Hebrew root ו־מ־ד (v-m-d), which means "to measure" or "to weigh."

Grammar: "וֹמְדוֹן" is a masculine noun in the singular form. It belongs to the binyan Pa'al (פָּעַל) and is in the construct state (סְמִיכוּת).

Description: "וֹמְדוֹן" refers to a measuring instrument or a scale used to weigh objects. It can also be used metaphorically to describe a standard or criterion by which something is evaluated or judged.
