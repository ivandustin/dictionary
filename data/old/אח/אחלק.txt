Etymology: The word "אחלק" (ahalak) comes from the Hebrew root ח-ל-ק (ch-l-q), which means "to divide" or "to share". 

Grammar: "אחלק" is a verb in the present tense, first person singular form. It is conjugated from the root ח-ל-ק (ch-l-q) with the addition of the first person singular pronoun אני (ani), meaning "I". 

Description: The word "אחלק" (ahalak) means "I will divide" or "I will share" in Hebrew. It is used to express the action of dividing or sharing something with others.
