Grammar: אחוז is a noun in Hebrew and it is in the singular masculine form. It can also be used as a verb in the present tense, such as אני אחוז (I possess) or אתה אחוז (you possess).

Description: אחוז can have several meanings depending on the context. It can refer to possession, ownership, or control over something. It can also mean a share or a portion of something. Additionally, it can be used metaphorically to describe a grasp or understanding of a concept or idea.
