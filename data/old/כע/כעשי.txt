Grammar: כָּעַשִׁי is a verb in the Qal (simple) stem, which indicates the basic action of lying or deceiving. It is conjugated in the third person singular masculine form. 

Description: כָּעַשִׁי is used to describe someone who is deceitful, dishonest, or untrustworthy. It can refer to both literal lying and figurative deception.
