Grammar: "לְדַבֵּרְךָ" is an infinitive construct form of the verb "דַּבֵּר" (dabber) in the second-person singular. It is used to express the idea of speaking or talking to someone, specifically addressing the second-person singular "you."

Description: "לְדַבֵּרְךָ" is a phrase that can be translated as "to speak to you" or "for you to speak." It implies the act of communication or conversation directed towards the person being addressed.
