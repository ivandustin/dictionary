Grammar: "לדוד" (L'dod) is a prepositional phrase in Hebrew. The preposition "ל" (L) means "to" or "for", and "דוד" (dod) is the noun form of the root word, meaning "beloved". Together, "לדוד" (L'dod) can be translated as "to/for David" or "for the beloved".

Description: "לדוד" (L'dod) is a phrase that appears in the Hebrew Bible, specifically in the title of Psalm 23: "מזמור לדוד" (Mizmor L'dod), which is commonly translated as "A Psalm of David".
