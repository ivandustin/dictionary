Grammar: In Hebrew, "בְּצַד" (betzad) is a preposition that means "beside" or "next to". It is used to indicate the location or position of something in relation to another object.

Description: "בְּצַד" (betzad) is a versatile word that is commonly used in everyday Hebrew conversations. It is used to describe the proximity or adjacency of objects or people.
