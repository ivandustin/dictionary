  - אתם תוגבלים (atem togbelim) - You limit (masculine plural)
  - אתן תוגבלות (aten togbelot) - You limit (feminine plural)
  - הם תוגבלים (hem togbelim) - They limit (masculine)

Description: "תגבל" means "to limit" or "to restrict" in Hebrew. It is used to describe the act of setting boundaries or confining something within certain limits.
