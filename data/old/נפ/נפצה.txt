Etymology: The word "נפצה" (Nafzah) comes from the Hebrew root נ.פ.צ (N.P.Ts), which means "to scatter" or "to disperse."

Grammar: "נפצה" is a verb in the past tense, third person singular form. It is derived from the root נ.פ.צ (N.P.Ts) and is conjugated in the binyan Pa'al (פָּעַל) or the active voice.

Description: "נפצה" is used to describe an action of scattering or dispersing something. It can refer to physically scattering objects or dispersing a group of people.
