Etymology: The word "נפרשות" (Nifreshot) is derived from the Hebrew root פרש (parash), which means "to spread out" or "to expand."

Grammar: "נפרשות" is the plural feminine form of the verb נפרש (nifresh), which means "to spread out" or "to expand." It is conjugated in the present tense and refers to multiple feminine subjects.

Description: "נפרשות" is used to describe actions or states of multiple feminine subjects that are spreading out or expanding. It can be used in various contexts, such as describing the spreading out of information, ideas, or physical objects.
