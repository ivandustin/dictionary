Etymology: The word "נפיסים" (Nafisim) is a Hebrew word derived from the root נ.פ.ס (N.P.S), which means "to scatter" or "to disperse." 

Grammar: "נפיסים" is a plural noun in Hebrew. It is formed by adding the suffix "-ים" (-im) to the root word. 

Description: "נפיסים" refers to "scattered" or "dispersed" things or entities. It can be used to describe objects, ideas, or people that are spread out or not concentrated in one place.
