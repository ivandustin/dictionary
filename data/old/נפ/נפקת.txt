Etymology: The word "נפקת" (Nafket) is derived from the Hebrew root נ.פ.ק (N.P.K), which means "to go out" or "to emerge."

Grammar: "נפקת" is a verb in the past tense, third person singular form. It is commonly used in Modern Hebrew.

Description: "נפקת" is used to describe the action of something or someone coming out or emerging from a certain place or situation. It can be used in various contexts, such as a person coming out of a room, an idea emerging from a discussion, or a result arising from a process.
