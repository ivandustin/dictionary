Etymology: The word "כתבה" (ktavah) comes from the Hebrew root כ-ת-ב (k-t-v), which means "to write." 

Grammar: "כתבה" is a feminine noun in Hebrew. It is the singular form of the word, and its plural form is "כתבות" (ktavot). 

Description: "כתבה" is commonly used to refer to an article or a written piece in Hebrew. It can be found in newspapers, magazines, or online publications.
