Grammar: "עפרם" is a noun in the Hebrew language. It is in the masculine plural form, indicating multiple instances of dust or earth.

Description: "עפרם" refers to fine particles of dirt or soil that are scattered or dispersed. It can also be used metaphorically to describe something insignificant or lowly. In biblical and poetic contexts, it can symbolize mortality or the transient nature of human existence.
