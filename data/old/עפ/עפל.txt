Etymology: The word "עפל" (pronounced "afal") comes from the Hebrew language. It is derived from the root עפל, which means "to fly" or "to soar."

- Singular: אֲנִי עוֹפֵל (ani ofel) - I fly
- Plural: אֲנַחְנוּ עוֹפְלִים (anachnu ofelim) - We fly

Description: The word "עפל" is used to describe the action of flying or soaring through the air. It can be used both literally, to refer to the physical act of flying, and metaphorically, to describe a feeling of freedom or elevation.
