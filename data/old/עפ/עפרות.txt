Etymology: The word "עפרות" (afarot) comes from the Hebrew root עפר (afar), which means "dust" or "earth". 

Grammar: "עפרות" is a plural noun in Hebrew. It is the plural form of "עפרה" (afarah), which means "dust" or "powder". 

Description: "עפרות" refers to small particles of dust or fine powder. It can be used to describe the dust that accumulates on surfaces or the particles that are stirred up in the air.
