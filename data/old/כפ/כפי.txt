Etymology: The word "כפי" (pronounced "kafi") is a Hebrew word that means "according to" or "as per." It is derived from the Hebrew root כפה (k-p-h), which means "to bend" or "to curve."

Grammar: "כפי" is a preposition in Hebrew and is used to indicate conformity or accordance with something. It is often followed by a noun or a verb in the infinitive form.

Description: "כפי" is a versatile word that is commonly used in Hebrew to express conformity or accordance with a certain standard, rule, or expectation. It is used to indicate that something is done or should be done in a specific way, as per a certain requirement or guideline.
