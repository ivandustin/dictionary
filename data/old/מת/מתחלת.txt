Etymology: The word "מתחלת" (Matḥelet) is derived from the Hebrew root תחלה (tḥlh), which means "beginning" or "start."

Grammar: "מתחלת" is a feminine noun in Hebrew. It is in the singular form and can be used with definite or indefinite articles.

Description: "מתחלת" is used to refer to the beginning or start of something. It can be used in various contexts, such as the beginning of a story, a journey, an event, or a process. It signifies the initial stage or point in time when something starts.
