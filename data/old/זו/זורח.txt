Grammar: In Hebrew, זורח is a verb in the present tense, masculine singular form. It can also be conjugated in different tenses, genders, and numbers to match the subject of the sentence.

Description: זורח is often translated as "to rise" or "to shine". It is used to describe the action of the sun or any other celestial body appearing above the horizon at the beginning of the day. It can also be used metaphorically to describe the emergence or development of something, such as a talent or an idea.
