Grammar: "מאלמים" is a plural noun in Hebrew. It is the masculine form of the word, which refers to male teachers or instructors. The feminine form is "מאלמות" (ma'almot), which refers to female teachers or instructors.

Description: "מאלמים" is a term used in Hebrew to refer to teachers or instructors. It is commonly used in the context of education, particularly in schools or educational institutions.
