Etymology: The word "מנעת" (mena'at) comes from the Hebrew root נעת (na'at), which means "to prevent" or "to hinder".

Grammar: "מנעת" (mena'at) is a noun in the feminine singular form. It is derived from the verb נעת (na'at) and is used to describe something that prevents or hinders.
