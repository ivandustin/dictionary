Grammar: "מַנְרֵת" (manret) is a feminine noun in Hebrew. It is used to refer to a lamp or a light source.

Description: In Jewish tradition, the menorah holds great significance. It is associated with the miracle of Hanukkah, where a small amount of oil in the Temple's menorah lasted for eight days.
