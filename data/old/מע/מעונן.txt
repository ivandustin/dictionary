Etymology: The word "מעונן" comes from the Hebrew root ענן (anan), which means "cloud". 

Grammar: "מעונן" is a verb in the present tense, third person singular form. It is derived from the noun "ענן" (anan), which means "cloud". The verb form "מעונן" means "to be cloudy" or "to become cloudy".

Description: "מעונן" is used to describe the weather when the sky is covered with clouds. It indicates that there is a significant amount of cloud cover, which can block the sun and result in a darker and cooler atmosphere.
