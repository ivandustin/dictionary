Etymology: The word "מערגות" (ma'aragot) is derived from the Hebrew root ע.ר.ג (ayin.reish.gimel), which means "to weave" or "to braid". 

Grammar: "מערגות" is a plural noun in Hebrew. It belongs to the feminine gender and is used to refer to multiple braids or weaves.

Description: "מערגות" is a term used to describe braids or weaves, typically referring to hair braids. It can also be used metaphorically to describe intertwined or interwoven things.
