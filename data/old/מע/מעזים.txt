Etymology: The word "מעזים" (me'azim) is derived from the Hebrew root עזז (azaz), which means "to be strong" or "to strengthen". 

Grammar: "מעזים" is a masculine plural noun in Hebrew. It is used to describe a group of people or things that are strong or powerful. 

Description: "מעזים" refers to individuals or a collective group who possess physical or mental strength, courage, or resilience. It can also be used metaphorically to describe something that is forceful or impactful.
