Grammar: "מעור" (ma'or) is a masculine noun in Hebrew. It is singular and can be used in both the definite and indefinite forms. In the plural form, it becomes "מעורות" (ma'orot).

Description: "מעור" (ma'or) refers to a source of light or illumination. It can be used to describe natural light, such as sunlight or moonlight, as well as artificial light, such as a lamp or a candle. In a figurative sense, it can also refer to enlightenment or knowledge.
