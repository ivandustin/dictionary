Grammar: "וְכַדְמִי" is a conjunction phrase in Hebrew. It is used to connect two elements or ideas, indicating that they are similar or alike. It is commonly used in comparisons or to express similarity.

Description: "וְכַדְמִי" is a versatile phrase that can be used in various contexts. It allows for comparisons and emphasizes the similarity between two things or concepts. It is often used in storytelling, poetry, and everyday conversations to draw parallels and make connections.
