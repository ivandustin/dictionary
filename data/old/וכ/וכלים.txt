Grammar: "וּכְלִים" is a plural noun in Hebrew. It is formed by adding the prefix וּ (u) to the singular form כְּלִי (keli). The plural form indicates that there are multiple vessels or tools.

Description: "וּכְלִים" refers to vessels or tools that are used for various purposes. It can include items such as utensils, containers, instruments, or any other objects that serve a specific function.
