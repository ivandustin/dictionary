Etymology: The word "מְקוֹל" (m'kol) comes from the Hebrew root ק.ו.ל (q.w.l), which means "voice" or "sound". 

Grammar: "מְקוֹל" is a noun in the Hebrew language. It is in the singular form and is masculine.
