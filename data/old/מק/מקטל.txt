Grammar: "מקטל" is a noun in Hebrew, and it is in the masculine singular form. It can also be used as a verb in the present tense, past tense, or future tense, depending on the context.

Description: "מקטל" refers to a person who kills or slays someone. It can be used to describe a murderer or an assassin. In modern Hebrew, it is also used metaphorically to describe someone who is very successful or dominant in a certain field or activity.
