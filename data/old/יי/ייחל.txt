Grammar: "יחל" is a verb in the Hebrew language. It is conjugated in the present tense as follows:
- Singular: אני יוחל (ani yochal) - I wait/hope
- Plural: אנחנו יוחלים (anachnu yochalim) - We wait/hope

Description: The word "יחל" is used to express the act of waiting or hoping for something. It can refer to waiting for a specific event or outcome, or having hope for a desired result. It is often used in a positive context, indicating anticipation or optimism.
