Etymology: The word "ידע" (pronounced "yada") comes from the Hebrew language. It is derived from the root י-ד-ע (y-d-a), which means "to know" or "to understand."

Description: ידע means "to know" or "to be aware of" in Hebrew. It refers to having knowledge or understanding about something or someone. It can also imply familiarity or recognition. The word can be used in various contexts, such as knowing facts, understanding concepts, recognizing people, or being aware of a situation.
