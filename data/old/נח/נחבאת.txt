Etymology: The word "נחבאת" (nachbata) is derived from the Hebrew root נ-ח-ב-א (n-ch-b-a), which means "to hide" or "to conceal."

Grammar: "נחבאת" (nachbata) is a verb in the feminine singular form. It is conjugated in the past tense and in the third person.

Description: "נחבאת" (nachbata) means "she hid" or "she concealed" in Hebrew. It is used to describe an action of hiding or concealing something or someone.
