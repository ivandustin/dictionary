Etymology: The word "נחיה" (nachiyah) comes from the Hebrew root נ-ח-י (n-ch-y), which means "to live" or "to exist."

Grammar: "נחיה" is a verb in the present tense, first person plural form. It is derived from the root נ-ח-י and is conjugated in the plural form to indicate that the subject is a group of people.

Description: "נחיה" means "we will live" or "we will exist" in Hebrew. It expresses the future tense and indicates that a group of people will continue to live or exist in the future.
