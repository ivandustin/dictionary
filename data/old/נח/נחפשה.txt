Etymology: The word "נחפשה" (Nechapeshah) is a Hebrew word that comes from the root נ-ח-פ-ש, which means "to search" or "to investigate."

Grammar: נחפשה is a verb in the past tense, feminine singular form. It is conjugated in the passive voice, indicating that the subject of the verb is being searched or investigated.

Description: נחפשה is used to describe the action of searching or investigating something. It can refer to a physical search or investigation, as well as a metaphorical or intellectual one. The word implies a deliberate and thorough examination of a subject or object.
