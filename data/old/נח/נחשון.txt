Description: "נחשון" is a biblical Hebrew word that refers to a type of metal serpent or snake. It is mentioned in the Book of Numbers in the Bible, where Moses is instructed to make a bronze serpent and put it on a pole to save the Israelites from a plague of venomous snakes.
