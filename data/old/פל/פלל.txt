- Masculine singular: אַתָּה מִתְפַּלֵּל (atah mitpalel) - You (masculine) pray
- Feminine singular: אַתְּ מִתְפַּלֶּלֶת (at mitpalelet) - You (feminine) pray
- Masculine plural: אַתֶּם מִתְפַּלְּלִים (atem mitpallelim) - You (masculine plural) pray
- Feminine plural: אַתֶּן מִתְפַּלְּלוֹת (aten mitpallelot) - You (feminine plural) pray

Description: The word "פלל" is commonly translated as "to pray" in English. It refers to the act of communicating with a higher power, expressing gratitude, making requests, or seeking guidance.
