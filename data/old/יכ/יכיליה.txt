Etymology: The word "יכיליה" (Yekhilah) is a Hebrew word derived from the root כ-ו-ל (k-w-l), which means "to contain" or "to hold."

Description: "יכיליה" means "he/she/it will contain" or "he/she/it will hold" in English. It is used to express the action of containing or holding something in the future.
