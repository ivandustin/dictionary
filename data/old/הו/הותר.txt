Etymology: The word "הותר" (pronounced "hoteir") comes from the Hebrew root ה.ת.ר (h.t.r), which means "to permit" or "to allow."

Grammar: "הותר" is a verb in the Hebrew language. It belongs to the binyan Pa'al (פָּעַל) and is conjugated in the past tense as הֻתַּר (hutar), in the present tense as מוּתָר (mutar), and in the future tense as יִתָּר (yitar).

Description: The word "הותר" means "to be permitted" or "to be allowed." It is often used to express permission or authorization for an action or behavior.
