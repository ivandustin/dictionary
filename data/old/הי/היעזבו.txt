Etymology: The word "היעזבו" (HaYe'azvu) is a Hebrew verb in the past tense, derived from the root ע-ז-ב (Ayin-Zayin-Bet). 

Grammar: "היעזבו" is the third person plural form of the verb, indicating that a group of people or objects abandoned or left something/someone.

Description: "היעזבו" means "they abandoned" or "they left" in Hebrew. It is used to describe an action where a group of people or objects intentionally left or deserted someone or something.
