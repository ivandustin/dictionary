Grammar: "יקומו" is the third person plural future tense form of the verb קום (kum), which means "to rise" or "to stand up." In Hebrew, verbs are conjugated according to tense, person, and number.

Description: "יקומו" is used to express an action that will happen in the future and is performed by a group of people or things. It can be translated as "they will arise" or "they will stand up." The exact meaning and context of the word depend on the surrounding sentence or conversation.
