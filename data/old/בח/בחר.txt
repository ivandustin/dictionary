Etymology: The word "בחר" (b'har) comes from the Hebrew root ב-ח-ר (b-ch-r), which means "to choose" or "to select". 

Grammar: "בחר" is a verb in the Hebrew language. It is conjugated in various tenses and forms to match the subject and the tense of the sentence. 

Description: "בחר" means "to choose" or "to select" in Hebrew. It is used when someone makes a decision or picks something out of a group of options. It can be used in various contexts, such as choosing a person for a job, selecting an item from a menu, or making a decision between different alternatives.
