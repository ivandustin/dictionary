Etymology: The word "בחין" comes from the Hebrew root "ב.ח.נ" which means "to examine" or "to discern". 

Grammar: "בחין" is a masculine noun in Hebrew. It is in the singular form. 

Description: In Hebrew, "בחין" refers to a person who has the ability to discern or judge things accurately. It can also be used to describe someone who is perceptive or insightful.
