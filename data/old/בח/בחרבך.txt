Etymology: The word "בחרבך" (b'cherevcha) is a Hebrew phrase composed of two parts. "ב" (b) means "in" or "with," and "חרבך" (cherevcha) means "your sword." 

Grammar: "בחרבך" is a prepositional phrase in Hebrew. The preposition "ב" (b) means "in" or "with," and "חרבך" (cherevcha) is the possessive form of the noun "חרב" (cherev), which means "sword." The suffix "-ך" (-cha) indicates the second person singular possessive form, meaning "your."

Description: The phrase "בחרבך" (b'cherevcha) can be translated as "with your sword" or "in your sword." It is often used metaphorically to express the idea of relying on one's own strength, power, or abilities to achieve something.
