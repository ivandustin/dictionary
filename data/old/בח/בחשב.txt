- Singular: אני בחושב (ani b'choshav) - I think
- Plural: אנחנו בחושבים (anachnu b'choshavim) - We think

Description: "בחשב" (b'chashav) is used to express the act of thinking or calculating. It can refer to both intellectual and analytical thinking, as well as to the process of considering or pondering something.
