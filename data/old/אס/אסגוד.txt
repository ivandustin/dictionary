- Singular: אני אסגוד (ani asgod) - I praise
- Plural: אנחנו אסגודים (anachnu asgodim) - We praise

Description: "אסגוד" is an action word that describes the act of praising or glorifying someone or something. It is often used in religious or spiritual contexts to express devotion or admiration towards a higher power.
