Grammar: "קראהו" is a conjugated form of the verb קרא (kara), which is in the past tense, third person, singular, masculine form. The suffix הו (hu) indicates that the object of the verb is masculine and singular.

Description: The word "קראהו" is used to describe an action where someone called or summoned another person. It can also be used in the context of reading, where someone read something aloud to another person.
