Grammar: "פגשתי" is the past tense form of the verb, which indicates that the action of meeting or encountering has already happened in the past. The suffix "-תי" (-ti) indicates that the subject of the verb is the first person singular pronoun "I" in Hebrew.

Description: "פגשתי" is used to express that the speaker (first person singular) has met or encountered someone or something in the past. It is commonly used in everyday conversations to talk about personal experiences or encounters.
