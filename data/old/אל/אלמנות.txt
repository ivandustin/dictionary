Etymology: The word "אלמנות" (almonot) is derived from the Hebrew root א-ל-מ-ן (aleph-lamed-mem-nun), which means "widow." 

Grammar: "אלמנות" is a feminine noun in Hebrew. It is the plural form of "אלמנה" (almana), which means "widow." 

Description: "אלמנות" refers to the state or condition of being a widow or widows collectively. It is used to describe a group of women who have lost their husbands and are now living as widows.
