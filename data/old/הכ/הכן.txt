- Singular: אֲנִי מְכַנֶּה (ani mekhaneh) - I prepare
- Singular feminine: אֲנִי מְכַנֶּהָ (ani mekhaneha) - I prepare (feminine)
- Plural masculine: אֲנַחְנוּ מְכַנִּים (anachnu mekhannim) - We prepare
- Plural feminine: אֲנַחְנוּ מְכַנּוֹת (anachnu mekhannot) - We prepare (feminine)
