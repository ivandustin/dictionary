Grammar: "הכוכבים" is a plural noun in Hebrew. It is formed by adding the suffix ים (-im) to the singular form כוכב (kochav), which means "star." The definite article "ה" (Ha) is added at the beginning to indicate "the."

Description: "הכוכבים" refers to the celestial bodies that are visible in the night sky. In Hebrew literature and culture, the stars often symbolize beauty, guidance, and the vastness of the universe. They have been a subject of fascination and inspiration for humans throughout history.
