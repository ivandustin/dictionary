- אתן מתרגמות (atan mitargemot) - You (plural, feminine) translate
- הם מתרגמים (hem mitargemim) - They (masculine) translate
- הן מתרגמות (hen mitargemot) - They (feminine) translate

Description: "תהרגם" means "to translate" in Hebrew. It refers to the act of converting text or speech from one language to another, while preserving its meaning and intent. Translating is an important skill that allows people to communicate and understand different languages and cultures.
