Grammar: "ויתמו" is a third-person plural, past tense, masculine form of the verb. It is conjugated in the prefix-conjugation (וַיִּקְטְלוּ) and in the perfect tense (קָטַל). The prefix "וַיִּ" indicates past tense and the suffix "וּ" indicates third-person plural, masculine.

Description: The word "ויתמו" means "and they died" in English. It is used in the Hebrew Bible (Old Testament) to describe the death of multiple individuals.
