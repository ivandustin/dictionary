Grammar: "וילקד" is a verb in the past tense, third person singular form. It is derived from the root letters ו-ל-ק-ד (V-L-K-D).

Description: The word "וילקד" is not commonly used in modern Hebrew. It is an archaic form that appears in the Hebrew Bible (Old Testament) in the book of Genesis, specifically in Genesis 4:17.
