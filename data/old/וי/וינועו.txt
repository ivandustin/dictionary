Etymology: The word "וינועו" comes from the Hebrew language. It is a verb form derived from the root word "נוע" (nun-vav-ayin), which means "to move" or "to shake."

Grammar: "וינועו" is a conjugated form of the verb in the past tense, third person plural, in the Hebrew language. It is commonly used in biblical texts.

Description: "וינועו" means "they moved" or "they shook" in English. It is used to describe the action of multiple individuals or objects moving or shaking.
