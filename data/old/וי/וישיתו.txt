Grammar: "וישיתו" is a verb form in the Hebrew language. It is in the past tense, third person plural, and masculine gender. It is commonly used in biblical texts.

Description: The word "וישיתו" is translated as "and they placed" or "and they set" in English. It is often used to describe an action of placing or setting something in a specific location.
