Grammar: "וישגם" is a verb in the past tense, third person, singular form. It is commonly used in biblical Hebrew.

Description: The word "וישגם" is found in the Hebrew Bible and is often translated as "he reached them" or "he attained them." It is used to describe someone reaching or attaining a certain goal, destination, or object.
