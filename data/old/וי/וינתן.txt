Grammar: "וינתן" is a verb in the past tense, third person, singular form. It is commonly used in biblical Hebrew to describe an action of giving.

Description: "וינתן" is used in the Hebrew Bible (Tanakh) to describe various instances of giving. It can refer to God giving something to someone, or to people giving something to each other. The word is often used to convey the idea of a gift or a bestowal of something valuable.
