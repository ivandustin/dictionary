Grammar: "ויתנו" is the third person plural form of the verb נתן in the past tense. It is used to describe an action that was done by a group of people or entities.

Description: "ויתנו" is often translated as "they gave" in English. It indicates that a group of individuals or entities performed the action of giving something. The specific context and object of the verb would determine what was given.
