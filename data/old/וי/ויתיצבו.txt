Grammar: "ויתיצבו" is a third-person plural verb in the past tense, which means "they stood" or "they positioned themselves." It is formed by adding the prefix ו- (vav) to the root י-צ-ב (y-ts-b) and the suffix -ו (vav) to indicate the plural form.

Description: "ויתיצבו" is used in the Hebrew Bible (Tanakh) and is often translated as "they stood" or "they took their stand."
