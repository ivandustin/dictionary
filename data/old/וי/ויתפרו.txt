Description: The word "ויתפרו" is found in the Hebrew Bible (Genesis 1:22) and is part of the creation narrative. It describes the command given by God to the creatures of the sea and the birds of the sky to "be fruitful and multiply." This verb form emphasizes the idea of procreation and the increase in numbers.
