Etymology: The word "וישנו" (pronounced "vayishnu") is a Hebrew verb that comes from the root י-ש-נ (y-sh-n), which means "to sleep" or "to rest."

Grammar: "וישנו" is a third-person plural verb in the past tense. It is formed by combining the prefix ו- (vav) with the root י-ש-נ (y-sh-n) and adding the appropriate suffixes to indicate the subject and tense.

Description: "וישנו" means "they slept" or "they rested" in Hebrew. It is commonly used to describe the action of sleeping or taking a rest.
