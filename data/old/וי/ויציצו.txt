Grammar: "ויציצו" is the third person plural, past tense, masculine form of the verb צ.ו.צ (ts.w.ts). It is conjugated in the binyan Pa'al (פָּעַל), which is the most common verb structure in Hebrew.

Description: "ויציצו" means "they sprouted" or "they blossomed" in Hebrew. It is often used to describe the growth or blooming of plants or flowers.
