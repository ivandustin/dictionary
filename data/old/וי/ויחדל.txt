Grammar: "ויחדל" is a verb in the Hebrew language, specifically in the past tense. It is conjugated in the third person singular masculine form, which is commonly used in biblical texts.

Description: The word "ויחדל" is found in the Hebrew Bible (Tanakh) and is often translated as "and he ceased" or "and he stopped."
