Grammar: "ויחרד" is a verb in the past tense, third person singular form. It is commonly used in biblical Hebrew narratives to describe someone's fear or trembling.

Description: "ויחרד" is often translated as "he trembled" or "he was afraid" in English. It is used to depict a person's emotional state of fear or anxiety.
