Etymology: The word "בגללי" (beglili) is a Hebrew word that comes from the root letters ב-ג-ל, which means "because of" or "due to". The suffix -י (-i) indicates possession or belonging.

Grammar: "בגללי" (beglili) is a prepositional phrase in Hebrew. It is composed of the preposition "ב" (be), meaning "in" or "because of", and the possessive pronoun suffix "-י" (-i), meaning "my". Together, they form the phrase "בגללי" (beglili), which translates to "because of me" or "due to me".

Description: "בגללי" (beglili) is used to express the cause or reason for something, specifically when the speaker is referring to themselves as the cause. It is often used to take responsibility or attribute something to oneself.
