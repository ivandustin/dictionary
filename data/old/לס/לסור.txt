Grammar: "לסור" is a verb in the Hebrew language. It is in the infinitive form, which means it is the base form of the verb and does not indicate tense or subject. It is often used with other words or conjugated to match the tense, person, and number of the subject in a sentence.

Description: "לסור" is a versatile verb that can have several meanings depending on the context. It can mean "to turn aside", "to deviate", "to veer", or "to divert". It is often used to describe physical movements, such as turning off a path or changing direction. It can also be used metaphorically to describe a change in course or behavior.
