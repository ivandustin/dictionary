Etymology: The word "לסחר" (lesachar) comes from the Hebrew root "ס.ח.ר" (s.ch.r), which means "to trade" or "to do business". 

Grammar: "לסחר" is a verb in the infinitive form. It is commonly used in the Hebrew language to express the act of trading or engaging in business activities. 

Description: "לסחר" refers to the action of buying and selling goods or services for profit. It can also imply engaging in commercial activities or conducting business transactions.
