Grammar: "הזקנים" is a plural noun in Hebrew. It is formed by adding the suffix ים (-im) to the singular form זקן (zaken). The definite article "ה" (Ha) is added at the beginning to indicate "the".

Description: "הזקנים" refers to a group of elderly individuals or elders. In Jewish tradition, the term is often used to refer to the wise and respected leaders of the community, particularly in religious contexts. These elders are often seen as sources of wisdom, guidance, and authority.
