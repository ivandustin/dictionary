Grammar: "הזב" is a noun in Hebrew and is used to refer to a fly. It is a masculine noun and is usually used in the singular form. In the plural form, it becomes "הזבים" (hazavim).

Description: A fly is a small insect with two wings that can fly and often buzzes around. Flies are known for their ability to annoy humans and animals by landing on food or buzzing around their heads. They are found in various habitats and are attracted to decaying organic matter.
