Grammar: "הנקה" is a noun in the Hebrew language. It is in the feminine form and is used to describe the act of cleaning or purifying.

Description: "הנקה" refers to the process of cleaning or purifying something. It can be used in various contexts, such as cleaning a physical object, purifying oneself spiritually, or cleansing a particular area.
