Grammar: הַנָּאֵם is a masculine noun in the singular form. It is often used as a title or epithet for a prophet or a person who delivers a divine message.

Description: הַנָּאֵם is used in Hebrew to refer to a person who speaks on behalf of a higher authority, such as a prophet or a spokesperson. It emphasizes the authority and importance of the message being delivered.
