Etymology: The word "אהבי" (ahavi) is a Hebrew word that means "my love" or "my beloved." It is derived from the root אהב (ahav), which means "to love."

Grammar: In Hebrew, "אהבי" (ahavi) is a possessive form of the noun "אהבה" (ahava), which means "love." The suffix "-י" (-i) indicates possession, so "אהבי" (ahavi) translates to "my love" or "my beloved."
