Grammar: "שכחנו" is the past tense form of the verb, which means "we forgot" in Hebrew. It is conjugated in the plural form and in the past tense.

Description: "שכחנו" is used to express the action of forgetting in the past tense, specifically when referring to a group of people. It indicates that "we" (a plural group) have forgotten something.
