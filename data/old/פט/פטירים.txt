Grammar: "פטירים" is a plural noun in Hebrew. It is formed by adding the suffix "-ים" to the singular form of the word, which is "פטיר" (pronounced "pitir").

Description: "פטירים" refers to written notices or announcements that inform others about the death of a person. These notices typically include details such as the name of the deceased, date of death, funeral arrangements, and sometimes a brief biography or tribute.
