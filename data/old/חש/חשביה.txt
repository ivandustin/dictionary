Grammar: "חשביה" is a feminine noun in Hebrew. It is written with the letters ח (chet), ש (shin), ב (bet), י (yod), and ה (hey). The word is used to describe a calculation, computation, or estimation.

Description: "חשביה" refers to the act of calculating or computing. It can also refer to a mathematical calculation, an estimation, or a reckoning. In modern Hebrew, it is commonly used in contexts related to mathematics, accounting, or financial calculations.
