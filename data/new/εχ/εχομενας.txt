The word εχομενας is a Greek word that comes from the verb "έχω" (echo), which means "to have" or "to hold". The suffix "-μενος" (-menos) is a participle suffix that is used to form adjectives from verbs. Therefore, εχομενας can be translated as "having" or "holding".

εχομενας is a masculine noun in the nominative case. It is a participle that functions as an adjective, modifying a noun in the sentence. The word can be declined in different cases, depending on its function in the sentence.

εχομενας is a word that describes someone or something that is holding or possessing something.
