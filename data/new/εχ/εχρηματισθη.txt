Word: εχρηματισθη

Etymology: 
The word εχρηματισθη is a Greek verb that comes from the root word χρηματίζω (chrēmatízō), which means "to transact business" or "to give advice." The prefix ε- (e-) in this word indicates a past tense form, so εχρηματισθη means "was transacted" or "was advised."

Grammar:
εχρηματισθη is a third-person singular past passive indicative form of the verb χρηματίζω. In Greek, verbs change their endings to indicate tense, mood, voice, and person. The prefix ε- indicates the past tense, while the -θη ending indicates the passive voice. The stem of the verb, χρηματισ-, remains the same.

Description:
εχρηματισθη is a Greek verb that means "was transacted" or "was advised." It is a past tense form of the verb χρηματίζω, which is used to describe business transactions or giving advice.
