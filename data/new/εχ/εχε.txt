Word: εχε

Etymology: The word εχε comes from the Greek language. It is derived from the verb "έχω" (echo), which means "to have" or "to hold".

Grammar: εχε is a verb in the imperative mood, which is used to give commands or make requests. It is the second person singular form of the verb "έχω" (echo).

Description: εχε is a command form of the verb "έχω" (echo), which means "have" or "hold". It is often used in Greek to give instructions or commands, such as "εχε το βιβλίο" (have the book) or "εχε το νερό" (hold the water).
