Word: ηρωίδου

Etymology:
The word ηρωίδου (heroidou) is derived from the Greek word ηρωίς (herois), which means "heroine" or "female hero". The suffix -δου (-dou) is a genitive ending, indicating possession or relationship.

Grammar:
ηρωίδου (heroidou) is a feminine noun in the genitive case. It is the genitive singular form of ηρωίς (herois).

Description:
ηρωίδου (heroidou) is a Greek word that refers to a female hero or heroine. It can be used to describe a woman who is brave, courageous, and heroic in her actions or deeds. The word is often used in literature and mythology to describe female characters who exhibit these qualities.
