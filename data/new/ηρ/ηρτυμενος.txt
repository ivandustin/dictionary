Word: ηρτυμένος

Grammar: ηρτυμένος is a participle in the passive voice, masculine gender, singular number, and nominative case. It is derived from the verb "ερτυω" (ertuo), which is in the perfect tense, passive voice, and participle form.

Description: ηρτυμένος can be translated as "prepared" or "made ready". It is often used to describe something that has been made ready for a specific purpose or event. For example, one might say "ηρτυμένος ο στρατός" (ertymenos o stratos) to mean "the army is prepared" or "the army is ready".
