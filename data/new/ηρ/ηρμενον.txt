The word "ηρμενον" (hermenon) is a Greek noun that means "interpretation" or "explanation". 

Etymology: The word comes from the Greek verb "ερμηνεύω" (hermeneuo), which means "to interpret" or "to explain". 

Grammar: "Ηρμενον" is a neuter noun in the singular form. 

Description: The word is often used in the context of interpreting or explaining a text or a message. It can also refer to the act of translating from one language to another. In philosophy, the term "hermeneutics" refers to the theory and methodology of interpretation.
