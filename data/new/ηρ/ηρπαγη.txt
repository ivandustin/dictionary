The word "ηρπαγη" (herpāgē) is a Greek noun that means "a seizure" or "a snatching away". It is derived from the verb "ἁρπάζω" (harpazō), which means "to seize" or "to snatch away". In terms of grammar, "ηρπαγη" is a feminine noun in the nominative singular form. It can also be declined in other cases and numbers according to the rules of Greek grammar.
