The word ηρνησω (erniso) is a verb in the Greek language. It is derived from the root word "αρνέομαι" (arneomai), which means "to deny" or "to refuse". The prefix "ερ" (er) is added to the root word to form the verb ηρνησω (erniso), which means "I deny" or "I refuse".

It belongs to the first conjugation of verbs, which means that it follows the regular pattern of conjugation for verbs that end in "-ω" (o). The verb is formed by adding the present tense ending "-ω" (o) to the stem "αρνησ-" (arnis-).
