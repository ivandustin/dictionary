Word: υαλινην

Etymology: The word υαλινην (yalinen) is derived from the Greek word υάλινος (yalinos), which means "made of glass". 

Grammar: υαλινην is a feminine singular accusative form of the adjective υάλινος (yalinos). It is used to describe a noun in the accusative case that is feminine and singular. 

Description: υαλινην describes something that is made of glass or has the appearance of glass. It can be used to describe a variety of objects, such as a glass vase, a glass window, or a glass sculpture. The word emphasizes the transparent and fragile nature of glass.
