The word "κεκοιμηται" is a Greek verb in the perfect tense, passive voice, and third person singular form. It comes from the root verb "κοιμάω" which means "to sleep". The prefix "κε-" indicates that the action is completed and the perfect tense indicates that the action happened in the past and has ongoing effects in the present. Therefore, "κεκοιμηται" means "he/she/it has been put to sleep" or "he/she/it is asleep".
