Word: κεκρυμμένον

Etymology:
The word κεκρυμμένον comes from the Greek verb κρύπτω (kryptō), which means "to hide" or "to conceal". The prefix κε- (ke-) indicates that the action is completed or perfect, so κεκρυμμένον means "hidden" or "concealed".

Grammar:
κεκρυμμένον is a neuter singular adjective in the nominative case. It agrees in gender, number, and case with the noun it modifies.

Description:
κεκρυμμένον describes something that is hidden or concealed. It can be used to describe physical objects that are hidden from view, as well as abstract concepts that are not readily apparent. The word can also be used metaphorically to describe hidden motives or intentions.
