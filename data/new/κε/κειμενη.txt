The word "κειμενη" (keimeni) is a Greek adjective that means "lying down" or "resting". 

Etymology: The word comes from the Greek verb "κείμαι" (keimai), which means "to lie down" or "to be at rest".

Grammar: "Κειμενη" is the feminine singular form of the adjective. It agrees in gender, number, and case with the noun it modifies.

Description: The word can be used to describe a person or object that is lying down or at rest. It can also be used figuratively to describe something that is inactive or dormant. For example, "Η κειμενη γάτα κοιμάται" (I keimeni gata koimatai) means "The lying down cat is sleeping".
