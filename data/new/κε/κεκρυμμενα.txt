Word: κεκρυμμένα

Etymology:
The word κεκρυμμένα is derived from the Greek verb κρύπτω (kryptō), which means "to hide" or "to conceal". The prefix κε- (ke-) indicates that the action is completed or the state is permanent.

Grammar:
κεκρυμμένα is the neuter plural form of the past participle of the verb κρύπτω (kryptō). It is used as an adjective to describe something that has been hidden or concealed.

Description:
κεκρυμμένα refers to things that have been hidden or concealed from view. It can be used to describe physical objects that have been hidden away, as well as abstract concepts that have been kept secret or hidden from public knowledge. The word can also be used metaphorically to describe hidden meanings or intentions.
