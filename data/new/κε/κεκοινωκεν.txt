The word "κεκοινωκεν" is a perfect tense verb in Greek, which means "he/she/it has shared in common" or "he/she/it has made common". The word is derived from the Greek root "κοινός" (koinos), which means "common" or "shared". The prefix "κε-" indicates the perfect tense, which refers to an action that was completed in the past and has ongoing effects in the present. The verb ending "-εν" indicates the third person singular form. In summary, "κεκοινωκεν" means "he/she/it has made something common/shared".
