Etymology: The word "φαινοντι" (phainonti) comes from the Greek verb "φαίνω" (phaino), which means "to show" or "to appear". 

Grammar: "Φαινοντι" is the present participle form of the verb "φαίνω" (phaino) in the third person singular. It is used to describe an ongoing action of showing or appearing in the present tense.

Description: "Φαινοντι" is a Greek word that can be translated as "he/she/it is showing" or "he/she/it is appearing". It is commonly used in Greek sentences to indicate an ongoing action of showing or appearing.
