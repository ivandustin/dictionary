Etymology: The word "φαγητέ" comes from the Greek verb "φαγεῖν" (phagein), which means "to eat."

Grammar: "Φαγητέ" is the imperative form of the verb "φαγεῖν" (phagein) in Greek. It is used to give a command or instruction to eat.
