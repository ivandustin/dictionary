Etymology: The word "φαινεται" (pronounced "phainetai") comes from the Greek verb "φαίνομαι" (phainomai), which means "to appear" or "to be visible". 

Grammar: "Φαινεται" is the third person singular form of the present tense of the verb "φαίνομαι". It is used to describe an action or state that is happening in the present and is performed by a third person. 

Description: "Φαινεται" is a Greek word that can be translated as "it appears" or "it seems". It is often used to express an observation or an opinion about something that is evident or obvious.
