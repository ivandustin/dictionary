Etymology: The word "φανερα" (phanēra) comes from the Greek language. It is derived from the verb "φαίνω" (phainō), which means "to show" or "to make visible."

Grammar: In Greek, "φανερα" is a feminine noun in the plural form. It belongs to the first declension and has the nominative case ending "-α" and the accusative case ending "-ας."

Description: "Φανερα" is used to describe things that are visible, evident, or apparent. It can also refer to things that are clear, obvious, or manifest.
