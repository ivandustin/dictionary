Etymology: The word "φανερόν" (phanēron) comes from the Greek adjective "φανερός" (phanēros), meaning "visible" or "apparent". 

Grammar: In Greek, "φανερόν" is a neuter noun in the singular form. 

Description: "Φανερόν" is used to describe something that is evident, clear, or obvious. It refers to something that can be easily seen or understood.
