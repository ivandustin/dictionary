Etymology: The word "φαγείν" (phageín) comes from the Greek verb "φαγεῖν" (phageîn), which means "to eat."

Grammar: "Φαγεῖν" is an infinitive verb form in the Greek language. It belongs to the aorist tense, which expresses an action that is completed in the past.

Description: "Φαγεῖν" is used to describe the act of eating in Greek. It is a versatile verb that can be used in various contexts and conjugated to match different subjects and tenses.
