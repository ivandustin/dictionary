Etymology: The word "φαγω" (phago) comes from the Greek verb "φαγεῖν" (phagein), which means "to eat."

Grammar: "Φαγω" (phago) is a verb in the Greek language. It is in the present tense, first person singular form.

Description: "Φαγω" (phago) means "I eat" in Greek. It is used to express the action of consuming food or taking in nourishment.
