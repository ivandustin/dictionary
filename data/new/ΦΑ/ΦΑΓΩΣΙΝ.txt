Etymology: The word "φαγωσιν" (phagōsin) comes from the Greek verb "φαγεῖν" (phagein), which means "to eat" or "to consume."

Grammar: "Φαγωσιν" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the accusative case and is derived from the verb "φαγεῖν" (phagein) in the aorist tense.
