The word "αιροντος" (airontos) is a Greek word that is derived from the verb "αίρω" (airo), which means "to lift" or "to take up". In its participle form, "αιροντος" can be translated as "lifting" or "taking up". 

In terms of grammar, "αιροντος" is a masculine noun in the genitive case. It can be used as a noun or an adjective, depending on the context. 

Overall, "αιροντος" is a word that describes an action of lifting or taking up, and can be used in various contexts in the Greek language.
