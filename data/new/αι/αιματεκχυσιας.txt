Word: αιματεκχυσίας

Etymology: The word αιματεκχυσίας comes from the Greek words αίμα (aima) meaning "blood" and τεκχυσίας (tekchysias) meaning "shedding" or "pouring out". 

Grammar: αιματεκχυσίας is a noun in the plural form. 

Description: αιματεκχυσίας refers to the act of shedding or pouring out blood, usually in a violent or traumatic manner. It can be used to describe a physical injury or a metaphorical one, such as the loss of life or the destruction of a community. The word is often used in medical contexts to describe bleeding or hemorrhaging.
