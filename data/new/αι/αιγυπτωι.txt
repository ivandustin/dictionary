The word "αιγυπτωι" (aigyptoi) is a Greek word that means "Egyptian" or "of Egypt". It is derived from the name of the country Egypt, which in Greek is "Αίγυπτος" (Aigyptos). 

In terms of grammar, "αιγυπτωι" is a masculine singular adjective in the dative case. It is often used to describe people or things that are from or related to Egypt.

Overall, "αιγυπτωι" is a useful word for anyone studying ancient Greek history or literature, as it is frequently used in texts from that time period.
