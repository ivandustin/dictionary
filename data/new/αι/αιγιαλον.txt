The word "αιγιαλον" (aigialon) is a Greek noun that refers to a beach or a shore. 

Etymology: The word comes from the ancient Greek word "αἰγιαλός" (aigialos), which also means beach or shore. 

Grammar: "Αιγιαλον" is a masculine noun in the singular form. 

Description: The word is commonly used in modern Greek to describe a sandy or pebbly beach by the sea. It can also refer to the area near the beach, including the promenade or the coastal road. The word is often used in tourism and travel contexts, as Greece is known for its beautiful beaches.
