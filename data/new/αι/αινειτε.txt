Word: αινειτε

Etymology:
The word αινειτε (aineite) is derived from the Greek verb αινέω (aineo), which means "to praise" or "to extol". It is a present tense, second person plural form of the verb.

Grammar:
In Greek, verbs change their form depending on the tense, mood, voice, and person. αινειτε is a present tense, second person plural form of the verb αινέω (aineo), which means "you praise" or "you extol".
