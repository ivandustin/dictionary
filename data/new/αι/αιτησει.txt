Word: αιτησει

Etymology:
The word αιτησει (aitisi) is derived from the Greek verb αιτέω (aiteo), which means "to ask" or "to request". The suffix -σει (-sei) is the third person singular future tense ending.

Grammar:
The word αιτησει (aitisi) is a verb in the third person singular future tense. It can be translated as "he/she/it will ask" or "he/she/it will request".

Description:
The word αιτησει (aitisi) is commonly used in modern Greek to express a future action of asking or requesting something. It can be used in various contexts, such as in formal or informal situations, in personal or professional settings, and in written or spoken communication.
