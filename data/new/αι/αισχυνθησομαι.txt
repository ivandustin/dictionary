Word: αισχυνθησομαι

Etymology:
The word αισχυνθησομαι (aischynthēsomai) is a compound word in Greek, consisting of two parts: αισχυντος (aischuntos) meaning "shameful" and θησομαι (thēsomai) meaning "I will become". Therefore, αισχυνθησομαι means "I will become shameful" or "I will be ashamed".

Grammar:
αισχυνθησομαι is a first-person singular future passive indicative verb in Greek. It is formed by adding the future tense suffix -σομαι to the stem αισχυνθ-, which is derived from the adjective αισχυντος.

Description:
The word αισχυνθησομαι is used to describe the feeling of shame or embarrassment. It is often used in a moral or ethical context, to express the idea of feeling ashamed of one's actions or behavior. The word can also be used in a more general sense, to describe the feeling of embarrassment or awkwardness in social situations.
