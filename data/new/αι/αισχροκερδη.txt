Word: αισχροκερδη

Etymology:
The word αισχροκερδη comes from the Greek words αισχρός (aischros) meaning "shameful" or "disgraceful" and κέρδος (kerdos) meaning "profit" or "gain". Therefore, αισχροκερδη can be translated as "shameful gain" or "disgraceful profit".

Description:
The word αισχροκερδη is used to describe someone who gains profit in a shameful or unethical way. It can also refer to the act of gaining profit through dishonest or immoral means.
