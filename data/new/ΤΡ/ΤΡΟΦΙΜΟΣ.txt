Etymology: The word "τροφιμος" (trophimos) comes from the Greek word "τροφή" (trophē), meaning "nourishment" or "food".

Grammar: In Greek, "τροφιμος" is a masculine noun in the nominative case. It belongs to the second declension and has the genitive form "τροφίμου" (trophimou).

Description: "Τροφιμος" is a Greek word that refers to someone or something that is nourishing or provides nourishment. It can be used to describe food, nutrients, or anything that sustains and supports life.
