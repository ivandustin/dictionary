Etymology: The word "τριτον" comes from the Greek word "τρίτος" (tritos), meaning "third".

Grammar: In Greek, "τριτον" is a neuter noun in the accusative case. It is the accusative form of the adjective "τρίτος" (tritos), which means "third".

Description: "Τριτον" is used to refer to the third item or element in a series or sequence. It can also be used to indicate the third time something happens or the third occurrence of an event.
