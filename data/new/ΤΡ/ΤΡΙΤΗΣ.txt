Etymology: The word "τριτης" (trites) comes from the Greek word "τρίτος" (tritos), meaning "third".

Grammar: In Greek, "τριτης" (trites) is a masculine noun in the nominative case. It belongs to the third declension.

Description: "Τριτης" (trites) is the Greek word for "third". It is used to indicate the ordinal number three in a series.
