Etymology: The word "τρυφαίναν" comes from the Greek verb "τρυφάω" (tryphao), which means "to live luxuriously" or "to indulge in pleasure."

Grammar: "τρυφαίναν" is the third person singular, aorist active indicative form of the verb "τρυφάω" (tryphao). It is used to describe someone or something that lived luxuriously or indulged in pleasure in the past.

Description: "τρυφαίναν" is a Greek verb that describes the action of living luxuriously or indulging in pleasure. It implies a state of enjoyment and satisfaction in one's lifestyle or activities.
