Etymology: The word "τροφας" (trophas) comes from the Greek word "τρέφω" (trefo), which means "to nourish" or "to feed". 

Grammar: In Greek, "τροφας" is a noun in the masculine gender. It is in the nominative case, singular number. 

Description: "Τροφας" refers to a person who provides nourishment or food. It can be used to describe someone who feeds or takes care of others, such as a caregiver or a provider.
