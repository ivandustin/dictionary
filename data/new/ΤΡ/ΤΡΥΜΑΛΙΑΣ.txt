Grammar: "Τρυμαλιας" is a masculine noun in the Greek language. It belongs to the second declension and has the genitive form "τρυμαλίου" (trymaliou). 

Description: "Τρυμαλιας" refers to someone or something that is extremely strong, powerful, or destructive. It can be used to describe a person who is physically or mentally imposing, or an event or situation that causes great damage or devastation.
