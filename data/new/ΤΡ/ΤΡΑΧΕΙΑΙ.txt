Etymology: The word "τραχείαι" comes from the Greek word "τραχύς" (trachýs), meaning rough or harsh.

Grammar: "τραχείαι" is a feminine noun in the plural form. It belongs to the first declension in Greek grammar.

Description: "τραχείαι" refers to rough or harsh conditions or circumstances. It can also describe a rough or harsh manner of speaking or behavior.
