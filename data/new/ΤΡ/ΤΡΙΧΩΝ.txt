Etymology: The word "τριχων" (trichon) comes from the Greek word "τρίχα" (tricha), meaning "hair". 

Grammar: In Greek, "τριχων" is a noun in the masculine gender. It is in the nominative case, singular number. 

Description: "Τριχων" refers to a single hair on the human body. It can also be used metaphorically to describe something very small or insignificant.
