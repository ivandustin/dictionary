Etymology: The word "τρεφεί" (trefei) comes from the Greek verb "τρέφω" (trefo), which means "to nourish" or "to feed".

Grammar: "Τρεφεί" is a verb in the present tense, third person singular form. It is conjugated from the verb "τρέφω" (trefo), which belongs to the first conjugation class in Greek.

Description: "Τρεφεί" means "he/she/it nourishes" or "he/she/it feeds" in Greek. It is used to describe the action of providing food or sustenance to someone or something.
