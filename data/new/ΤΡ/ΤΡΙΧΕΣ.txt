Etymology: The word "τριχες" comes from the Greek word "τρίχα" (tricha), which means "hair". 

Grammar: "Τριχες" is a noun in the plural form. It is a feminine noun and belongs to the second declension in Greek grammar. 

Description: "Τριχες" refers to hairs or strands of hair. It can be used to describe the hair on the human body or the hair of animals.
