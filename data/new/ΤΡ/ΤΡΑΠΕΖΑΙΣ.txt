Etymology: The word "τραπεζαίος" (trapezaios) comes from the Greek word "τράπεζα" (trapeza), meaning "table" or "bank". It refers to someone who works at a bank or is involved in banking activities.

Grammar: In Greek, "τραπεζαίος" is a masculine noun in the nominative case. It belongs to the second declension and has the plural form "τραπεζαίοι" (trapezaioi).

Description: "Τραπεζαίος" is used to describe a person who works in a bank, such as a bank employee, teller, or banker. They are responsible for various tasks related to banking operations, such as handling transactions, providing customer service, and managing financial accounts.
