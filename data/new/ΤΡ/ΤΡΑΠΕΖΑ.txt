Etymology: The word "τραπέζα" (trapeza) comes from the Greek word "τράπεζα" (trapeza), which means "table" or "bank". It is derived from the verb "τρέπω" (trepo), meaning "to turn" or "to change", indicating the function of a table as a place where things are exchanged or transactions take place.

Grammar: In Greek, "τραπέζα" is a feminine noun. It belongs to the second declension and its plural form is "τραπέζες" (trapezes).

Description: "Τραπέζα" is the Greek word for "table" or "bank". It can refer to a piece of furniture used for dining or other activities, as well as to a financial institution where money is deposited, borrowed, or exchanged.
