Etymology: The word "τροφή" (trofí) comes from the Ancient Greek word "τροφή" (trophḗ), which means "nourishment" or "food".

Grammar: In Greek, "τροφή" is a feminine noun. It belongs to the second declension and has the genitive form "τροφής" (trofís).

Description: "Τροφή" is the Greek word for "food" or "nourishment". It refers to any substance that is consumed by living organisms to provide energy, promote growth, and maintain life.
