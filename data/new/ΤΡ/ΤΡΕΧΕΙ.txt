Etymology: The word "τρεχει" (trechei) comes from the Greek verb "τρέχω" (trecho), which means "to run".

Grammar: "Τρεχει" (trechei) is the third person singular form of the present tense of the verb "τρέχω" (trecho), which means "he/she/it runs" in Greek.
