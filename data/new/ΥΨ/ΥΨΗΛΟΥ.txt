Etymology: The word "υψηλού" (ypsiloú) comes from the Greek adjective "υψηλός" (ypsilós), which means "high" or "tall".

Grammar: "Υψηλού" is the genitive form of the adjective "υψηλός" (ypsilós). In Greek grammar, the genitive case is used to indicate possession or origin.

Description: "Υψηλού" is an adjective in Greek that describes something as "high" or "tall". It is used to indicate the vertical extent or height of an object or a person.
