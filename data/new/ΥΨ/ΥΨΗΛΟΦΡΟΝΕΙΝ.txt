Etymology: The word "υψηλοφρονείν" comes from the Greek roots "υψηλός" (ypsilos) meaning "high" and "φρονείν" (fronein) meaning "to think". 

Grammar: "Υψηλοφρονείν" is a verb in the Greek language. It is in the present tense, active voice, and infinitive form. 

Description: "Υψηλοφρονείν" is a Greek word that means "to think highly" or "to have high thoughts". It refers to having elevated or noble thoughts, often associated with wisdom, intelligence, or a lofty mindset.
