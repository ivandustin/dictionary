The word "εκεινηι" (ekeini) is a feminine singular pronoun in Greek, meaning "that" or "she". 

Etymology: The word comes from the ancient Greek word "ἐκεῖνος" (ekeinos), which means "that one" or "he". 

Grammar: In Greek, pronouns decline according to gender, number, and case. "Εκεινηι" is the feminine singular form in the nominative case. 

Description: "Εκεινηι" is commonly used in Greek to refer to a feminine noun that has already been mentioned or is known to the speaker and listener. It can also be used to refer to a distant object or person.
