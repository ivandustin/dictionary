The word "εκζητων" (ekzetón) is a Greek noun that means "examiner" or "investigator". 

Etymology: The word is derived from the Greek verb "εκζητέω" (ekzetéo), which means "to examine" or "to investigate". 

Grammar: "Εκζητων" is a masculine noun in the nominative case.
