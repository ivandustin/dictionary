Word: εκείνη

Etymology: 
The word εκείνη (ekini) is a feminine demonstrative pronoun in Greek, meaning "that" or "she". It is derived from the ancient Greek word "ekeinos", which means "that one" or "he". 

Grammar:
In Greek, εκείνη is declined according to the gender, number, and case of the noun it refers to. For example, if it refers to a feminine singular noun in the nominative case, it would be εκείνη. If it refers to a masculine singular noun in the accusative case, it would be εκείνον.

Description:
εκείνη is a versatile word that can be used to refer to a feminine noun or to emphasize a particular object or person. It is commonly used in everyday speech and writing in Greek.
