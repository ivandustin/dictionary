Etymology:
The word εκπεσωσιν is a Greek word that comes from the verb εκπίπτω (ekpipto), which means "to fall out" or "to fall from." The word is formed by adding the suffix -σιν (-sin) to the verb stem εκπεσ- (ekpes-).

εκπεσωσιν is a third-person plural aorist subjunctive active verb form in Greek. It is used to express a hypothetical action that may or may not happen in the future. The aorist tense indicates that the action is viewed as a single event, while the subjunctive mood indicates that the action is uncertain or hypothetical.
