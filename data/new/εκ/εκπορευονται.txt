Word: εκπορευονται

Etymology: 
The word εκπορευονται comes from the Greek roots εκ (ek) meaning "out" and πορευομαι (poreuomai) meaning "to go". Therefore, the word εκπορευονται means "to go out" or "to come forth".

Grammar:
εκπορευονται is a verb in the present tense, third person plural form. It is derived from the verb πορευομαι (poreuomai) which means "to go". The prefix εκ (ek) adds the meaning of "out" or "forth". The verb is in the active voice, indicating that the subject is performing the action.

Description:
εκπορευονται is a Greek verb that means "to go out" or "to come forth". It is often used in a figurative sense to describe the emergence or manifestation of something. The verb can be used in a variety of contexts, such as describing the emergence of a new idea, the appearance of a physical object, or the coming forth of a person or group.
