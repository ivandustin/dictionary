The word "εκπεσειν" (ekpesein) is a Greek verb that means "to fall out" or "to drop out". 

Etymology: The word is derived from the combination of two Greek words: "εκ" (ek) meaning "out" and "πεσειν" (pesein) meaning "to fall". 

Grammar: "εκπεσειν" is an infinitive verb in Greek, which means it is the basic form of the verb and does not indicate tense, person, or number. 

Description: The word "εκπεσειν" is often used to describe objects or people falling or dropping out of a particular place or situation. It can also be used metaphorically to describe someone losing their position or status in a group or society.
