Word: εκζητήσεις

Etymology: The word εκζητήσεις comes from the Greek verb εκζητώ, which means to investigate or to examine thoroughly. The suffix -σεις is added to the verb stem to form the noun εκζητήσεις, which means investigations or inquiries.

Grammar: εκζητήσεις is a feminine noun in the plural form. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their gender, number, and case. The plural form indicates that there are multiple investigations or inquiries.

Description: εκζητήσεις refers to the act of investigating or examining something thoroughly. It can be used in various contexts, such as legal investigations, scientific research, or journalistic inquiries. The word implies a systematic and rigorous approach to gathering information and analyzing data.
