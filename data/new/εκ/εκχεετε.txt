The word "εκχεετε" (ekcheete) is a verb in the Greek language. 

Etymology: The word is derived from the Greek root "εκχέω" (ekcheo), which means "to pour out" or "to discharge". 

Grammar: "εκχεετε" is the present tense, second person plural form of the verb "εκχέω". It is used to address a group of people and means "you pour out" or "you discharge". 

Description: The word "εκχεετε" is often used in a figurative sense to describe the act of expressing or releasing emotions, thoughts, or ideas. It can also be used in a literal sense to describe the act of pouring out a liquid or substance.
