The word "εκβαλλει" is a Greek verb that means "to throw out" or "to expel". It is derived from the prefix "εκ-" which means "out" and the verb "βαλλω" which means "to throw". In terms of grammar, "εκβαλλει" is a present tense, third person singular form of the verb. It can be conjugated in various tenses and persons to indicate different actions and subjects.
