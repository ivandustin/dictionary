Word: εκρυψας

Etymology: 
The word εκρυψας is a Greek verb that comes from the root word κρύπτω (kryptó), which means "to hide" or "to conceal". The prefix εκ- (ek-) means "out of" or "from", so εκρυψας can be translated as "having hidden" or "having concealed".

Grammar:
εκρυψας is the aorist tense of the Greek verb κρύπτω (kryptó), which means that it refers to a completed action in the past. It is also in the second person singular, which means that it is addressing one person who performed the action of hiding or concealing.

Description:
εκρυψας can be translated as "you hid" or "you concealed". It is a verb that describes an action of hiding or concealing something or someone. The context in which it is used will determine what was hidden or concealed.
