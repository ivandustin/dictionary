Word: εκβαλλοντα

Etymology: 
The word εκβαλλοντα comes from the Greek verb εκβάλλω (ekballō), which means "to throw out" or "to expel". The suffix -οντα (-onta) is the present participle form of the verb, indicating that the action is currently happening.

Grammar:
εκβαλλοντα is the present participle form of the verb εκβάλλω (ekballō), which means "throwing out" or "expelling". It is in the masculine gender and nominative case, indicating that the subject is a male who is currently performing the action of throwing out or expelling.
