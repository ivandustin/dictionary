The word εκχυθησεται is derived from the Greek verb εκχύνω (ekchyno), which means "to pour out" or "to spill". The suffix -ται (-tai) indicates that the verb is in the third person singular, passive voice, and present tense.

εκχυθησεται is a verb in the Greek language. It is in the third person singular, passive voice, and present tense. The verb belongs to the first conjugation of Greek verbs, which means that it follows the regular pattern of conjugation for verbs that end in -ω (-o).

The word εκχυθησεται describes an action of something being poured out or spilled.
