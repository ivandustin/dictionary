Word: εκπλησσεσθαι

Etymology: The word εκπλησσεσθαι comes from the Greek verb εκπλήσσω (ekplēssō), which means "to strike out of, to strike with panic, to amaze, to astonish". The suffix -σθαι (-sthai) indicates the reflexive form of the verb, meaning "to be amazed, to be astonished".

Grammar: εκπλησσεσθαι is a reflexive verb in the present tense, passive voice, and infinitive mood. It is formed by adding the reflexive suffix -σθαι to the stem εκπλησσ-.

Description: εκπλησσεσθαι means "to be amazed, to be astonished". It is often used to describe a sudden and unexpected reaction to something surprising or shocking. The verb can be used in a variety of contexts, such as in response to a surprising event, a shocking revelation, or an unexpected outcome.
