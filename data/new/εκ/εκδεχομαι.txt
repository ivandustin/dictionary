Grammar: "Εκδεχομαι" is a first-conjugation verb in the present tense, and its stem is "εκδεχ-". In the past tense, it becomes "εξεδεχθην" (exedechthen) and in the future tense, it becomes "θα εκδεχομαι" (tha ekdechomai). 

Description: The verb "εκδεχομαι" is often used in the context of waiting for something or someone, such as waiting for a bus or waiting for a friend to arrive. It can also be used in a more abstract sense, such as waiting for an opportunity or waiting for a decision to be made.
