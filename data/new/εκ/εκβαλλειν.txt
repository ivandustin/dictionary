Word: εκβαλλειν

Etymology:
The word εκβαλλειν comes from the Greek roots εκ (ek) meaning "out" and βαλλειν (ballein) meaning "to throw". Therefore, εκβαλλειν means "to throw out".

Grammar:
εκβαλλειν is a verb in the present tense, active voice, and infinitive mood. It is formed by adding the suffix -ειν to the stem εκβαλλ-.

Description:
εκβαλλειν is a Greek verb that means "to throw out". It can be used in a literal sense, such as throwing out garbage, or in a figurative sense, such as throwing out negative thoughts or emotions. It is a versatile verb that can be used in various contexts and situations.
