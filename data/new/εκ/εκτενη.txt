Word: εκτενη

Etymology: The word εκτενη comes from the Greek word "εκτενής" (ektenis), which means "intensive" or "extensive". It is derived from the prefix "εκ-" (ek-), meaning "out" or "beyond", and the adjective "τενής" (tenis), meaning "stretched" or "extended".

Grammar: εκτενη is an adjective in the feminine singular form. It is used to describe something that is extensive, thorough, or detailed.

Description: εκτενη is a word that is often used to describe a thorough or detailed examination or analysis of something. It can also be used to describe a person who is diligent, persistent, or hardworking in their efforts. For example, one might say "Οι εκτενείς έρευνες απέδωσαν καρπούς" (The extensive research paid off) or "Η εκτενής προσπάθεια του έφερε επιτυχία" (Her diligent effort brought success).
