Word: εκβαλων

Etymology:
The word εκβαλων comes from the Greek roots εκ (ek) meaning "out" and βαλλω (ballo) meaning "to throw". Therefore, εκβαλων means "one who throws out" or "casting out".

Grammar:
εκβαλων is a present participle in the masculine gender, singular number, and nominative case. It is derived from the verb εκβαλλω (ekballō) which means "to cast out" or "to expel". The present participle form indicates that the action of casting out is ongoing or continuous.

Description:
εκβαλων is a word that describes someone who is continuously casting out or expelling something. It can also be used in a figurative sense, such as someone who is constantly removing negative thoughts or emotions from their mind.
