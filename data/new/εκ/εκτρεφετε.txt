The word "εκτρέφετε" (ektrepete) is a verb in the present tense, second person plural form, in the Greek language. 

Etymology: The word is derived from the Greek root words "εκ" (ek) meaning "out" and "τρέφω" (trefō) meaning "to nourish" or "to raise". Therefore, "εκτρέφετε" can be translated to mean "you are raising or nurturing something outwards".

Grammar: The word is a regular verb in the present tense, second person plural form. It is conjugated from the root verb "τρέφω" (trefō) which means "to nourish" or "to raise". The verb is in the active voice, indicating that the subject is performing the action of raising or nurturing.

Description: "εκτρέφετε" is a versatile verb that can be used in a variety of contexts. It can refer to raising or nurturing animals, plants, or even people. It can also be used metaphorically to describe the act of cultivating or developing a skill or talent.
