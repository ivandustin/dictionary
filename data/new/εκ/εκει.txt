Word: εκεί

Etymology: The word εκεί (ekei) comes from the ancient Greek word "ἐκεῖ" (ekei), which means "there" or "in that place".

Grammar: εκεί is an adverb in Greek, which means it modifies the verb in a sentence. It is used to indicate a location or a place. It is often used with prepositions such as "σε" (se) or "στο" (sto) to indicate the specific location.

Description: εκεί is a common word in Greek and is used in everyday conversation. It is used to indicate a location or a place, and can be used in a variety of contexts. For example, "Πού είναι ο Τάσος; Εκεί στο πάρκο" (Where is Tasos? He is there in the park). It is important to note that the pronunciation of the word is "ekei" and not "ek-i".
