Word: εκείσε (ekese)

Etymology: The word εκείσε (ekese) is derived from the Greek word εκεί (ekei), which means "there". The suffix -σε (-se) is added to indicate the direction of the action, in this case, "to there".

Grammar: εκείσε (ekese) is a verb in the aorist tense, which is used to describe a completed action in the past. It is also in the third person singular form.

Description: εκείσε (ekese) means "he/she/it went there" or "he/she/it placed something there". It is often used to indicate a physical location or movement towards a specific place.
