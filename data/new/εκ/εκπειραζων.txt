Etymology: The word is derived from the prefix "εκ-" (ek-), which means "out of" or "from," and the verb "πειράζω" (peirazō), which means "to try or test." 

Grammar: "εκπειραζων" is the present participle form of the verb, which means it describes an ongoing action. It is in the masculine gender, singular number, and nominative case.
