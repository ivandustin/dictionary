Word: εκβαλλομενοι

Etymology: 
The word εκβαλλομενοι comes from the Greek verb εκβάλλω (ekballō), which means "to throw out" or "to expel". The suffix -μενοι (-menoi) is a present participle ending, indicating that the action of throwing out is ongoing or continuous.

Grammar:
εκβαλλομενοι is a present participle in the masculine plural form. It is derived from the verb εκβάλλω (ekballō), which is in the present tense, and the participle ending -μενοι (-menoi). The word functions as an adjective, describing a group of people who are continuously throwing out or expelling something.

Description:
εκβαλλομενοι can be translated to "those who are throwing out" or "those who are expelling". It describes a group of people who are actively engaged in the process of throwing out or expelling something. The context in which the word is used will determine what is being thrown out or expelled.
