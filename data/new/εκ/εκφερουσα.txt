Word: εκφερουσα

Etymology: The word εκφερουσα comes from the Greek verb εκφέρω (ekfero), which means "to carry out" or "to bring forth". The suffix -ουσα (-ousa) is the feminine present participle form of the verb.

Grammar: εκφερουσα is a feminine singular form of the present participle in Greek. It is used to describe a female subject who is currently carrying out or bringing forth an action.

Description: εκφερουσα can be translated to English as "carrying out" or "bringing forth". It is often used in Greek to describe a woman who is currently in the process of giving birth.
