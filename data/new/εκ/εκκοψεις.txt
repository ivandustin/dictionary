Word: εκκοψεις

Grammar: εκκοψεις is a feminine noun in the plural form. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their gender, number, and case. The plural form of εκκοψεις indicates that there are multiple cuttings or prunings.

Description: εκκοψεις refers to the act of cutting off or pruning parts of a plant, tree, or shrub. This is a common practice in gardening and agriculture to promote growth and maintain the health of the plant. The cuttings or prunings can be used for propagation or as compost.
