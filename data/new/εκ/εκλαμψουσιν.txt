The word "εκλαμψουσιν" (eklampsoosin) is a Greek verb in the future tense, third person plural form. It comes from the root word "εκλάμπω" (eklampó), which means "to shine forth" or "to flash." 

In terms of grammar, "εκλαμψουσιν" is a regular future tense verb in Greek, formed by adding the appropriate endings to the root word. 

In terms of description, "εκλαμψουσιν" can be translated to mean "they will shine forth" or "they will flash." It implies a sudden and bright burst of light or brilliance.
