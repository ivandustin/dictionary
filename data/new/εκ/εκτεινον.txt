The word "εκτεινον" (ekténon) is a verb in the Greek language. 

Etymology: The word is derived from the Greek root "εκτείνω" (ektéino), which means "to stretch out" or "to extend". 

Grammar: "εκτεινον" is the present active imperative form of the verb "εκτείνω". It is used to give a command or instruction to someone to stretch out or extend something. 

Description: The word "εκτεινον" is often used in the context of physical actions, such as stretching out one's arms or legs. It can also be used metaphorically to mean extending or expanding something, such as a business or a project.
