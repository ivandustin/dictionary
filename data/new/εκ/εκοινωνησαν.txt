Word: εκοινωνησαν

Etymology: The word εκοινωνησαν is derived from the Greek word κοινωνέω (koinonéo), which means "to share, to communicate, to participate". The prefix ε- (e-) in this word indicates the past tense.

Grammar: εκοινωνησαν is a verb in the past tense, third person plural form. It is the aorist tense of the verb κοινωνέω (koinonéo), which means that it refers to a completed action in the past.

Description: εκοινωνησαν means "they communicated, they shared, they participated" in Greek. It is often used to describe a situation where people shared information or ideas with each other. The word can also be used in a broader sense to describe any kind of interaction or collaboration between people.
