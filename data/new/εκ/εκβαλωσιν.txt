Word: εκβαλωσιν

Etymology: The word εκβαλωσιν comes from the Greek verb εκβάλλω (ekballō), which means "to throw out" or "to expel". The suffix -σιν (-sin) is a third person plural ending in the aorist tense, indicating that the action of throwing out or expelling has already taken place.

Grammar: εκβαλωσιν is a noun in the accusative case, which is used to indicate the direct object of a verb. In this case, it is the direct object of the verb εκβάλλω (ekballō), meaning "they threw out" or "they expelled".
