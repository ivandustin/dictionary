Word: εκδοχη

Etymology: The word εκδοχη comes from the Greek verb εκδέχομαι (ekdechomai), which means "to wait for" or "to expect". The noun form εκδοχη refers to something that is expected or anticipated.

Grammar: εκδοχη is a feminine noun in Greek. Its plural form is εκδοχες (ekdoches).

Description: εκδοχη can be translated to "alternative", "substitute", or "backup". It is often used to refer to a plan or course of action that is prepared in case the original plan fails or is not possible. It can also refer to a person or thing that is seen as a replacement or alternative to another.
