Grammar: εκηρυξαμεν is the first person plural aorist active indicative form of the verb κηρύσσω (kerusso). In Greek, the aorist tense is used to describe a past action that is viewed as a single event, without any reference to its duration. The active voice indicates that the subject (we) performed the action of proclaiming or announcing.
