Word: εκφοβος

The word εκφοβος comes from the Greek words εκ (ek) meaning "out" and φοβος (phobos) meaning "fear". Therefore, the literal translation of εκφοβος is "out of fear".

εκφοβος is an adjective in the Greek language. It is declined according to gender, number, and case. For example, in the masculine singular nominative case, it is εκφοβος (ekfobos), while in the feminine singular nominative case, it is εκφοβη (ekfobi).
