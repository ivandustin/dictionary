The word "εκδοτον" (ekdoton) is a Greek noun that means "publisher" or "editor". 

Etymology: The word is derived from the Greek verb "έκδοση" (ekdosi), which means "publication" or "issue". The suffix "-τον" (-ton) is added to the stem "εκδοτ-" (ekdot-) to form the noun "εκδοτον" (ekdoton).

Grammar: "Εκδοτον" is a masculine noun in the singular form. In the plural form, it becomes "εκδότες" (ekdotes).

Description: The word "εκδοτον" refers to a person or a company that publishes or edits books, magazines, newspapers, or other written works. In modern Greek, the word is commonly used to refer to a publishing house or a publishing company.
