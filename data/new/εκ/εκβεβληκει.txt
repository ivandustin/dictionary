The word "εκβεβληκει" is a Greek verb that means "to throw out" or "to eject". 

Etymology: The word is derived from the prefix "εκ-" which means "out" and the verb "βαλλω" which means "to throw". 

Grammar: "εκβεβληκει" is a third person singular present active indicative form of the verb. 

Description: The word is commonly used in Greek to describe the act of forcefully removing something or someone from a place or situation. It can also be used metaphorically to describe the act of rejecting or dismissing an idea or proposal.
