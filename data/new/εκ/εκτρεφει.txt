Word: εκτρέφει (ektrefei)

Etymology: The word εκτρέφει comes from the Greek verb "τρέφω" (trefō), which means "to nourish" or "to feed". The prefix "εκ-" (ek-) in this case means "out of" or "from", so εκτρέφει literally means "to nourish out of" or "to bring up from".

Grammar: εκτρέφει is a present tense, third person singular verb in Greek. It is conjugated from the verb "τρέφω" (trefō) and means "he/she/it nourishes" or "he/she/it brings up".

Description: εκτρέφει is a verb that describes the act of nurturing or raising something or someone. It can refer to the act of raising animals, plants, or even children. It is often used in the context of agriculture or animal husbandry, but can also be used in a more general sense to describe the act of nurturing or fostering growth in any area of life.
