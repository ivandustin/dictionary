The word εκβαλω comes from the Greek roots εκ (ek) meaning "out" and βαλλω (ballo) meaning "to throw". Therefore, the literal meaning of εκβαλω is "to throw out".

εκβαλω is a verb that is commonly used in the Greek language to describe the act of throwing something out or expelling something from a place or a group.
