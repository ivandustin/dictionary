Grammar: The word is in the present participle form, which is used to describe an ongoing action or state. It is formed by adding the suffix "-μενος" (-menos) to the present stem of the verb. 

Description: The word "εκδεχομενος" is commonly used in Greek to describe someone who is waiting for something or someone. It can also be used to describe a state of anticipation or expectation. For example, "Είμαι εκδεχόμενος την άφιξη του φίλου μου" (I am waiting for the arrival of my friend).
