In terms of grammar, "εκφερειν" is a regular verb in the present tense, with the conjugation "εκφέρω" (ekfero) in the first person singular. It can also be conjugated in other tenses and moods, such as the past tense "εξήγαγα" (exigaga) and the subjunctive mood "να εκφέρω" (na ekfero).

The word can be used in various contexts, such as in legal proceedings where a verdict is "εκφερόμενος" (ekferomenos), or in speech therapy where a patient is taught to "εκφέρει" (ekferi) certain sounds or words correctly.
