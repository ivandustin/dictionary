The word "εκβληθεντος" is a Greek adjective that comes from the verb "εκβάλλω" (ekballō), which means "to throw out" or "to expel". The adjective form means "thrown out" or "expelled". 

In terms of grammar, "εκβληθεντος" is in the masculine gender, singular number, and nominative case. 

Overall, the word "εκβληθεντος" describes something or someone that has been forcefully removed or expelled from a place or situation.
