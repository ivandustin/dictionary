The word "εγειραντος" is a Greek verb in the present participle form. It comes from the root verb "εγείρω" which means "to wake up" or "to rise". The present participle form indicates that the action is ongoing or continuous. 

In terms of grammar, "εγειραντος" is in the masculine gender, singular number, and nominative case. It can be translated to "the one who is waking up" or "the one who is rising".

Overall, "εγειραντος" is a descriptive word that refers to someone who is currently in the process of waking up or rising.
