Word: εγραφεν

Etymology: εγραφεν is a Greek word that comes from the verb γράφω (grapho), which means "to write."

Grammar: εγραφεν is the third person singular form of the aorist tense of the verb γράφω (grapho). In Greek, the aorist tense is used to describe a completed action in the past.

Description: εγραφεν means "he/she/it wrote" in English. It is a past tense verb that describes a completed action of writing.
