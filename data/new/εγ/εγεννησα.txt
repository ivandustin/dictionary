The word "εγεννησα" is a Greek verb that means "I gave birth" or "I bore". It is the first person singular form of the aorist tense of the verb "γεννάω" (gennao), which means "to give birth" or "to beget". The word is commonly used in the New Testament of the Bible to describe the birth of Jesus Christ. In terms of grammar, "εγεννησα" is a regular verb that follows the conjugation pattern of the aorist tense in Greek.
