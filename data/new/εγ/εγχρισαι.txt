The word εγχρισαι comes from the Greek verb εγκρίνω (enkríno), which means "to approve" or "to sanction". It is derived from the prefix εν- (en-), meaning "in" or "into", and the verb κρίνω (kríno), meaning "to judge" or "to decide".
