Word: εγνωκεν

Grammar: εγνωκεν is the third person singular form of the perfect tense of the Greek verb γινώσκω. It is often translated as "he/she/it has known" or "he/she/it knows." The verb is in the active voice, which means that the subject is performing the action of knowing.

Description: εγνωκεν is a verb that indicates a completed action of knowing or understanding. It can be used to describe someone who has gained knowledge or insight about a particular subject or situation.
