The word "εγγιζοντων" is a present participle in Greek, derived from the verb "εγγίζω" which means "to approach" or "to draw near". The word is in the masculine plural form, indicating that it refers to a group of people who are currently approaching or drawing near to something or someone. 

In terms of grammar, the word functions as an adjective, modifying a noun or pronoun in the sentence. For example, in the sentence "Οι εγγιζόντων στρατιώτες είναι έτοιμοι για μάχη" (The approaching soldiers are ready for battle), "εγγιζόντων" modifies the noun "στρατιώτες" (soldiers).

Overall, "εγγιζοντων" is a versatile word that can be used in a variety of contexts to describe a group of people who are approaching or drawing near to something or someone.
