Word: εγνωρισαν

Etymology: The word εγνωρισαν comes from the Greek verb γνωρίζω (gnorízo), which means "to make known" or "to introduce". The prefix ε- (e-) indicates the past tense, so εγνωρισαν means "they made known" or "they introduced".

Grammar: εγνωρισαν is the third person plural past tense form of the verb γνωρίζω. It is a regular verb in the Greek language, and it belongs to the first conjugation group. The verb stem is γνωρισ-, and the past tense suffix for the third person plural is -αν.

Description: εγνωρισαν is a verb that describes an action that was completed in the past. It indicates that a group of people made something known or introduced someone or something to others. The context in which the word is used will determine the specific meaning and implications of the action.
