The word "εγγιζει" (engizei) is a verb in the Greek language. 

Etymology: The word comes from the root "εγγίζω" (engizo), which means "to approach" or "to come near".

Description: The word "εγγιζει" is used to describe the action of approaching or coming near to something or someone. It can be used in a physical sense, such as approaching a person or object, or in a more abstract sense, such as approaching a solution to a problem.
