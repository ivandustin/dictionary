The word εγκαταλειποντες is a Greek word that comes from the verb εγκαταλείπω (egkataleípo), which means "to abandon" or "to leave behind". The word is formed by combining the prefix εν- (en-), which means "in" or "inside", with the verb καταλείπω (kataleípo), which means "to leave behind" or "to forsake".
