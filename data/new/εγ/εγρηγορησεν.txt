Word: εγρηγορησεν

The indicative mood is used to make a statement or ask a question.

The word is used in the New Testament of the Bible, specifically in the Gospel of Matthew and the Gospel of Mark, to describe the moment when Jesus' disciples fell asleep while he was praying in the Garden of Gethsemane, and he woke them up.
