The word "εγερει" (egerei) is a verb in the Greek language. 

Etymology: The word comes from the Greek verb "εγείρω" (egero), which means "to wake up" or "to raise up". 

Grammar: "Εγερει" is the third person singular present tense form of the verb "εγείρω". It is an active verb, meaning that the subject of the sentence is performing the action. 

Description: The word "εγερει" can be translated to English as "he/she/it wakes up" or "he/she/it raises up". It is often used in the context of waking up from sleep or raising something up physically.
