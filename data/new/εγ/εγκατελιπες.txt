Word: εγκατελιπες

Etymology: The word εγκατελιπες comes from the Greek roots εν (en), meaning "in" or "within," and καταλείπω (kataleipo), meaning "to leave behind" or "to abandon." 

Grammar: εγκατελιπες is a noun in the Greek language, specifically a masculine noun in the nominative singular form. 

Description: εγκατελιπες refers to someone who has been left behind or abandoned. It can also refer to a person who has been neglected or forgotten. The word is often used in a negative context, implying a sense of loss or abandonment.
