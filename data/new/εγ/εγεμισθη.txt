The word "εγεμισθη" is a Greek verb in the aorist passive tense, which means "to be filled" or "to be filled up". It is derived from the root word "γεμίζω" (gemizo), which means "to fill" or "to fill up". In terms of grammar, it is a third person singular form of the aorist passive indicative mood. 

Example sentence: Το ποτήρι εγεμίσθη με νερό. (To potiri egemisthi me nero) - The glass was filled with water.
