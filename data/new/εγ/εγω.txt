Word: εγω

Etymology: The word εγω (ego) comes from the Greek language and is derived from the Proto-Indo-European root *éǵh₂, meaning "I".

Grammar: εγω is a first-person singular pronoun in Greek, meaning "I". It is used to refer to oneself in a sentence.

Description: εγω is a very common word in the Greek language and is used in everyday conversation. It is an important pronoun that helps to identify the speaker in a sentence. It is also used in various expressions and idioms in Greek.
