The word "εγνωρισα" is a verb in the Greek language. Its etymology can be traced back to the root word "γνωρίζω" (gnorizo), which means "to make known" or "to introduce". 

In terms of grammar, "εγνωρισα" is in the first person singular past tense of the verb "γνωρίζω". It can be translated to "I introduced" or "I made known". 

As for its description, "εγνωρισα" is a verb that implies an action of introducing or making someone or something known to others. It can be used in various contexts, such as introducing oneself to someone, introducing a new idea or concept to a group, or introducing a person to a new environment.
