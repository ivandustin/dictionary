Word: εγγιζουσαν

Etymology: The word εγγιζουσαν comes from the Greek verb εγγίζω (engízo), which means "to approach" or "to come near". The suffix -ουσαν (-ousan) indicates that the word is in the present participle form, indicating ongoing action.

Grammar: εγγιζουσαν is a verb in the present participle form, indicating ongoing action. It is in the third person plural, indicating that the subject is a group of people or things.

Description: εγγιζουσαν describes an ongoing action of a group of people or things approaching or coming near something. The context of the sentence would determine what exactly they are approaching or coming near.
