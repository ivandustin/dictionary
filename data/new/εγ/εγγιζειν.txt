The word "εγγιζειν" (engizein) is a Greek verb that means "to approach" or "to draw near". It is derived from the Greek word "εγγυς" (engys), which means "near" or "close". 

In terms of grammar, "εγγιζειν" is an infinitive verb form, which means it is not conjugated to match a specific subject or tense. It is often used in combination with other verbs to form compound verb phrases, such as "προσπαθώ να εγγισω τον στόχο μου" (prospatho na engiso ton stoho mou), which means "I am trying to reach my goal".

Overall, "εγγιζειν" is a versatile verb that can be used in a variety of contexts to describe physical or metaphorical proximity or closeness.
