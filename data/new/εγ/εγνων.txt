Word: εγνων

Etymology:
The word εγνων (egnon) is a past tense form of the Greek verb γιγνώσκω (gignósko), which means "to know" or "to understand". The verb comes from the Proto-Indo-European root *ǵneh₃- which means "to know".

Grammar:
εγνων is a first person singular past tense form of the verb γιγνώσκω (gignósko). It is in the aorist tense, which is used to describe a completed action in the past. The verb is also active, indicating that the subject (in this case, "I") performed the action.
