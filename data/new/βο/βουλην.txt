The word βουλην (boulen) is a Greek noun that means "will" or "wish". It comes from the verb βούλομαι (boulomai), which means "to wish" or "to want". In terms of grammar, βουλην is a singular accusative noun in the Greek language. It can be used in various contexts, such as expressing one's desire or intention, or as a legal term referring to a decision or decree.
