Word: βολισαντες

Etymology:
βολισαντες is a Greek word that comes from the verb βάλλω (ballō), which means "to throw". The suffix -σαντες (-santes) is a participle suffix that indicates past action, so βολισαντες means "those who have thrown".

Grammar:
βολισαντες is a plural masculine noun in the nominative case. It is a participle, which means it functions as an adjective and a verb at the same time. As an adjective, it describes a group of people who have thrown something. As a verb, it indicates that the action of throwing has been completed.
