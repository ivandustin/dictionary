βοηθησον is a Greek verb in the imperative mood, second person singular form. It comes from the root word βοήθεια (boetheia), which means "help" or "assistance". The verb form βοηθησον is derived from the present tense stem βοηθε (boethe), which means "I help".

βοηθησον is a second person singular verb in the imperative mood. In Greek, the imperative mood is used to give commands or make requests. The verb form βοηθησον is in the aorist tense, which is used to describe a completed action in the past.
