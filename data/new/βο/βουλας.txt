The word βουλας (boulas) is a Greek noun that means "will" or "wish". It is derived from the Greek verb βούλομαι (voulomai), which means "to want" or "to wish". In terms of grammar, βουλας is a masculine noun in the nominative singular form. It can also be declined in other cases and numbers, such as βουλαν (accusative singular) or βουλαι (nominative plural). In ancient Greek, βουλας was often used in political contexts to refer to the will or decision of a council or assembly.
