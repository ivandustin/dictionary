The word "βοηθει" (boethei) is a Greek verb that means "to help" or "to assist". It comes from the root word "βοήθεια" (boetheia), which means "aid" or "assistance". In terms of grammar, it is a present tense verb in the second person singular form. It can also be conjugated in other tenses and persons, such as "βοηθώ" (boetho) in the first person singular present tense.
