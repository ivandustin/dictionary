The word βοσκομενη (boskomeni) is a Greek verb in the present participle form, which means "she who is grazing" or "she who is pasturing". It comes from the root word βόσκω (bosko), which means "to graze" or "to pasture". In Greek grammar, the present participle is used to describe an ongoing action or state, and it can function as an adjective or a noun.
