Word: βοσκόντες

Etymology: βοσκόντες is a present active participle of the Greek verb βόσκω (bosko), which means "to feed" or "to graze". The word comes from the Proto-Indo-European root *bʰeh₂g- which means "to eat".

Grammar: βοσκόντες is a masculine plural nominative form of the present active participle of the verb βόσκω. It is used to describe a group of people who are currently feeding or grazing animals.

Description: βοσκόντες is a word that describes people who are actively engaged in the act of feeding or grazing animals. It can be used to describe shepherds, farmers, or anyone who is responsible for the care and feeding of livestock. The word has a sense of activity and movement, as it describes people who are actively engaged in a task.
