The word "πταιει" (ptaiēi) is a verb in the Greek language. Its etymology can be traced back to the ancient Greek word "πταίω" (ptaiō), which means "to stumble" or "to make a mistake". 

In terms of grammar, "πταιει" is a present tense, third person singular form of the verb "πταίω". It is often used to describe someone who is making a mistake or stumbling in some way. 

Overall, "πταιει" is a word that conveys the idea of error or mistake, and is often used in a negative context.
