Etymology: The word "βρωσιν" (pronounced "vrosin") comes from the Greek verb "βρώσκω" (pronounced "vrosko"), which means "to eat" or "to consume."

Grammar: "Βρωσιν" is a noun in the accusative case. It is the direct object form of the noun "βρῶσις" (pronounced "vrosis"), which means "food" or "nourishment."

Description: "Βρωσιν" refers to the act of eating or consuming food. It can also be used to describe the food itself.
