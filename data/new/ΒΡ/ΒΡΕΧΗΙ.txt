Etymology: The word "βρεχη" (brechei) comes from the Greek verb "βρέχω" (brecho), which means "to rain". 

Grammar: βρεχη (brechei) is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the singular form and is feminine. 

Description: βρεχη (brechei) refers to rain in Greek. It is used to describe the water that falls from the sky in the form of droplets. Rain is an essential part of the Earth's water cycle and plays a crucial role in the ecosystem.
