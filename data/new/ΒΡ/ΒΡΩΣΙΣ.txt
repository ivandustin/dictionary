Etymology: The word "βρωσις" (brosis) comes from the Greek verb "βρωσκω" (brosko), which means "to eat" or "to devour."

Grammar: In Greek, "βρωσις" is a feminine noun of the third declension. It is in the nominative case.

Description: "Βρωσις" refers to the act of eating or consuming food. It can also be used to describe the act of devouring or consuming something in a more general sense.
