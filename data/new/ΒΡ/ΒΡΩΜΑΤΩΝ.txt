Etymology: The word "βρωματών" comes from the Greek verb "βρωματίζω" (vromatízo), which means "to feed" or "to nourish."

Grammar: "Βρωματών" is a noun in the plural form. It belongs to the second declension in Greek grammar.

Description: "Βρωματών" refers to food or nourishment. It can be used to describe any type of edible substance that is consumed for sustenance or pleasure.
