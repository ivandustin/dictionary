ζοφον (zophon) is a Greek word that means "darkness" or "gloom". It is derived from the Greek word "ζοφος" (zophos), which also means darkness. In terms of grammar, ζοφον is a noun in the accusative case. It can be used to describe a physical or metaphorical state of darkness or gloom. For example, it can be used to describe a dark and gloomy room, or a person's mood or outlook on life.
