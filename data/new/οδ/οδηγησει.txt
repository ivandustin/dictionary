Word: οδηγησει

Etymology: The word οδηγησει comes from the Greek verb οδηγώ (odigó), which means "to guide" or "to lead". It is formed by adding the suffix -σει (-sei) to the stem of the verb.

Grammar: οδηγησει is the third person singular future tense of the verb οδηγώ (odigó). It is used to indicate that someone or something will guide or lead in the future.

Description: οδηγησει is a verb that expresses the action of guiding or leading. It can be used in various contexts, such as in driving, navigation, or mentoring. For example, "Ο πατέρας μου θα οδηγήσει το αυτοκίνητο στο αεροδρόμιο" (My father will drive the car to the airport).
