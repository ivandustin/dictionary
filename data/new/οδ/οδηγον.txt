The word "οδηγον" (odigon) is a Greek noun that means "guide" or "leader". It is derived from the verb "οδηγέω" (odigeo), which means "to lead" or "to guide". In terms of grammar, "οδηγον" is a masculine noun in the accusative case. It can be used to refer to a person who leads or guides others, or to a tool or instrument that helps someone navigate or find their way.
