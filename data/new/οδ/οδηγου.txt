Word: οδηγού (odigou)

Etymology: The word οδηγού comes from the Greek verb οδηγώ (odigó), which means "to guide" or "to lead". The suffix -ού (-ou) indicates possession, so οδηγού means "of the guide" or "of the leader".

Grammar: οδηγού is a noun in the genitive case, which is used to show possession or relationship. It is masculine and singular, and its article is ο (o).

Description: οδηγού can refer to a guide, leader, or instructor. It is often used in the context of driving, where it can refer to a driving instructor or a guidebook for a particular route. It can also be used more generally to refer to someone who provides guidance or direction in any context.
