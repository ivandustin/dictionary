Word: τας

Etymology: τας is a Greek word that is the feminine accusative plural form of the definite article τός (tós).

Grammar: τας is a definite article in Greek that is used to indicate a specific feminine noun in the accusative case. It is declined according to the gender, number, and case of the noun it modifies.

Description: τας is used to indicate a specific feminine noun in the accusative case. It is often translated as "the" in English. For example, τας κόρας (tas kóras) means "the girls" in English.
