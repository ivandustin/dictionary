ταχινη (takhinē) is a Greek word that means "quick" or "fast". It is derived from the Greek word ταχύς (takhys), which also means "quick" or "fast". In terms of grammar, ταχινη is a feminine noun in the nominative singular form. It can be used to describe a person, object, or action that is fast or quick. For example, one might say "Η ταχινή κίνηση του αθλητή εντυπωσίασε το κοινό" (The fast movement of the athlete impressed the audience).
