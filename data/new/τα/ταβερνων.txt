Word: ταβερνων

Etymology:
The word ταβερνων comes from the Greek word ταβέρνα (taverna), which means a small restaurant or pub. The suffix -ων (-on) is a genitive plural ending, indicating that the word refers to multiple taverns.

Grammar:
ταβερνων is a noun in the genitive plural form. In Greek, nouns decline to show their grammatical function in a sentence, and the genitive case is used to indicate possession or relationship between two nouns.

Description:
ταβερνων refers to multiple taverns, which are small restaurants or pubs where people gather to eat, drink, and socialize. In ancient Greece, taverns were often located near ports or marketplaces and served as meeting places for travelers and locals alike. Today, taverns can be found throughout Greece and are known for their traditional cuisine and relaxed atmosphere.
