The word ταπεινωθησεται is a Greek verb that comes from the root word ταπεινός (tapeinos), which means "humble" or "lowly." The verb form ταπεινωθησεται is in the future passive indicative tense, which means "he/she/it will be humbled."

ταπεινωθησεται is a verb that means "he/she/it will be humbled." The word is often used in a religious or spiritual context to describe the act of humbling oneself before a higher power.
