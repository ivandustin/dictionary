ταχινην (takhinēn) is a Greek word that means "quickly" or "speedily". 

Etymology: The word ταχινην comes from the Greek adjective ταχύς (tachys), which means "swift" or "quick". 

Grammar: ταχινην is an adverb in Greek, which means it modifies a verb, an adjective, or another adverb. 

Description: ταχινην is often used in Greek to describe actions that are done quickly or with great speed. It can also be used to describe the pace of a conversation or the speed of a vehicle.
