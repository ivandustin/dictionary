ταχεῖον (tacheion) is a Greek word that means "swift" or "fast". It is derived from the Greek word ταχύς (tachys), which means "quick" or "speedy". In terms of grammar, ταχεῖον is a neuter noun in the singular form. It can be used as an adjective to describe something that is fast or swift, or as a noun to refer to something that is quick or speedy.
