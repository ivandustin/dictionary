ταξει (taxi) is a Greek word that can be translated to mean "order" or "arrangement". It comes from the verb τάσσω (tasso), which means "to arrange" or "to put in order". In terms of grammar, ταξει is a third person singular form of the verb τάσσω in the future tense. It can be used to express a future action or state of being that is ordered or arranged. For example, "Ταξει θα πάμε στο πάρτι αύριο" (Taxi tha pame sto party avrio) means "We will go to the party tomorrow in an orderly manner".
