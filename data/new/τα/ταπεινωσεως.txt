Word: ταπεινωσεως

Etymology:
The word ταπεινωσεως (tapeinōseōs) is derived from the Greek verb ταπεινόω (tapeinóō), which means "to humble" or "to lower oneself." The suffix -σις (-sis) is added to the verb to form the noun ταπεινωσεως, which means "humiliation" or "abasement."

ταπεινωσεως is a word that describes the act of humbling oneself or being humiliated.
