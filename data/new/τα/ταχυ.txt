ταχυ (tachy) is a Greek word that means "fast" or "quick". It is derived from the Greek word "tachys" which also means "fast". In terms of grammar, ταχυ is an adjective that can be used to describe a person, object, or action that is fast or quick. For example, "ταχύ αυτοκίνητο" (tachy aftokinito) means "fast car" and "ταχύς τρόπος" (tachys tropos) means "quick way".
