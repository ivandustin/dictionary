ταχυς (tachys) is an adjective in Greek that means "swift" or "fast". It is derived from the Greek word ταχύτης (tachytis), which means "speed". In terms of grammar, ταχυς is a masculine singular nominative form. It can also be declined in other cases and genders, such as ταχεία (tacheia) for feminine singular nominative, or ταχέων (tacheon) for masculine plural genitive.
