Etymology: The word "ειρηνη" (eirēnē) comes from the Greek language. It is derived from the Greek word "εἰρήνη" (eirēnē), which means "peace" or "tranquility".

Grammar: In Greek, "ειρηνη" (eirēnē) is a feminine noun. It is in the nominative case and singular form.

Description: "Ειρηνη" (eirēnē) is a concept that refers to a state of harmony, calmness, and absence of conflict or hostility. It is often associated with the absence of war or violence and the presence of tranquility and serenity. In Greek mythology, Eirene was also the goddess of peace.
