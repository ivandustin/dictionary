Etymology: The word "ειπα" (eipa) comes from the Greek verb "λέγω" (lego), which means "to say" or "to speak".

Grammar: "Ειπα" (eipa) is the first person singular past tense of the verb "λέγω" (lego). It is used to indicate that the speaker said or spoke something in the past.

Description: "Ειπα" (eipa) is a verb form that is commonly used in Greek to express past actions or statements made by the speaker. It is often used in storytelling or when recounting events that have already happened.
