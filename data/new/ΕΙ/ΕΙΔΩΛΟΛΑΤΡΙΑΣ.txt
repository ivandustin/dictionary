Grammar: "Ειδωλολατρίας" is a noun in the genitive case, singular form. In Greek grammar, the genitive case is used to indicate possession or origin.

Description: "Ειδωλολατρίας" refers to the act or practice of worshiping or adoring idols. It is often used in a religious or spiritual context to describe the worship of physical representations or images as objects of devotion.
