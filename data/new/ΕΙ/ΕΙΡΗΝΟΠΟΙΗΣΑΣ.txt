The word "ειρηνοποίησης" is a Greek noun that comes from the combination of two words: "ειρήνη" (eiríni), meaning "peace," and "ποίηση" (poíisi), meaning "making" or "creation." Therefore, "ειρηνοποίησης" can be translated as "the act of making peace" or "the creation of peace." In terms of grammar, it is a genitive singular noun in the Greek language.
