Etymology: The word "ειπης" (eipēs) comes from the Greek verb "εἶπον" (eipon), which means "I said" or "I spoke". 

Grammar: "Ειπης" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the masculine gender and in the nominative case. 

Description: "Ειπης" refers to a person who speaks or someone who is known for their speech. It can also be used to describe a speaker or an orator.
