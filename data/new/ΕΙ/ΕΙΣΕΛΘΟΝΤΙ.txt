Grammar: "εισελθοντι" is the aorist active participle form of the verb "εἰσέρχομαι" (eiserchomai). In Greek grammar, the aorist tense is used to express a past action that is viewed as a whole, without specifying its duration or completion. The active participle form indicates that the subject of the verb is performing the action.
