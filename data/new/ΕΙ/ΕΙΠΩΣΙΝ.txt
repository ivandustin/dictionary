Grammar: "Ειπωσιν" is the third person plural form of the verb "εἶπον" (eipon) in the aorist tense. In Greek grammar, the aorist tense is used to express past actions that are viewed as a whole, without specifying their duration or repetition.

Description: "Ειπωσιν" is used to refer to a group of people (third person plural) who have spoken or said something in the past. It can be translated as "they said" or "they spoke" in English.
