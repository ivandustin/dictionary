Grammar: "εισελθοντα" is the accusative form of the verb "εισέρχομαι" in the masculine singular. In Greek, verbs change their form based on the grammatical case, number, and gender of the noun they are associated with. The accusative case is used to indicate the direct object of a verb, in this case, the noun that is being entered.
