Etymology: The word "ειδετε" (eidete) comes from the Greek verb "εἴδω" (eido), which means "to see" or "to know."

Grammar: "Ειδετε" is the second person plural form of the verb "εἴδω" (eido) in the aorist tense. It is used to address a group of people and means "you all saw" or "you all knew."

Description: "Ειδετε" is a verb in the Greek language that expresses the action of seeing or knowing. It is used to refer to a past event or knowledge that a group of people had.
