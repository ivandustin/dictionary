Etymology: The word "εισπορευόμενων" comes from the Greek verb "εισπορεύομαι" (eisporévomai), which means "to enter" or "to go in". The suffix "-ων" (-on) indicates the present participle form of the verb, indicating ongoing action.

Grammar: "Εισπορευόμενων" is the genitive plural form of the present participle of the verb "εισπορεύομαι". In Greek grammar, the genitive case is used to indicate possession or origin, and the plural form indicates multiple individuals or objects.

Description: "Εισπορευόμενων" can be translated as "of those entering" or "of those going in". It is used to describe a group of people or objects that are currently in the process of entering or going into a place or situation.
