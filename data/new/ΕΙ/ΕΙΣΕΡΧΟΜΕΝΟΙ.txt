Grammar: "Εισερχόμενοι" is the plural form of the present participle of the verb "εισέρχομαι". It is used to describe a group of people who are currently entering or going in.

Description: "Εισερχόμενοι" is an adjective that describes people who are in the act of entering or going in. It can be used to refer to individuals or a group of people who are entering a place, such as a building, a room, or a specific area.
