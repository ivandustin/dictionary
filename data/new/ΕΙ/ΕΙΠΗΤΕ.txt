Grammar: "ειπητε" is the second person plural, aorist tense, active voice form of the verb "εἶπον" (eipon). In Greek grammar, the aorist tense is used to express a completed action in the past.

Description: "ειπητε" is used to address a group of people and instruct them to speak or say something. It is commonly used in commands, requests, or instructions.
