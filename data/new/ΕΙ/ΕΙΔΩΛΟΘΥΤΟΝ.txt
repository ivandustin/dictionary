Grammar: "Ειδολοθυτον" is a neuter noun in the singular form. In Greek grammar, neuter nouns typically end in "-ον" in the nominative singular.

Description: "Ειδολοθυτον" refers to something that deceives or deludes the senses, often in the context of illusions or deceptive appearances. It can be used metaphorically to describe something that is misleading or deceptive in nature.
