Etymology: The word "ειρηνική" (eirēnikē) comes from the Greek word "εἰρήνη" (eirēnē), meaning "peace."

Grammar: "Ειρηνική" is an adjective in the feminine form, used to describe something or someone as peaceful.

Description: "Ειρηνική" is used to describe a state or situation that is characterized by peace, tranquility, or harmony. It can also refer to a person who is peaceful in nature or behavior.
