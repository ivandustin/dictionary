Etymology: The word "ετεροδιδασκαλείν" comes from the Greek words "ετερος" meaning "other" and "διδασκαλείν" meaning "to teach". 

Grammar: "ετεροδιδασκαλείν" is a verb in the Greek language. It is in the present tense, active voice, and infinitive form. 

Description: "ετεροδιδασκαλείν" means "to teach someone else" or "to instruct another person". It refers to the act of teaching or instructing someone who is not the usual or primary teacher.
