Grammar: "Ετοιμασατε" is the second person plural imperative form of the verb "ετοιμάζω" (etoimázō). It is used to give a command or instruction to a group of people, telling them to prepare or get ready for something.

Description: "Ετοιμασατε" is a verb form that is commonly used in Greek to give commands or instructions. It is often used in contexts where there is a need for preparation or readiness, such as in cooking, organizing events, or getting ready for a task or activity.
