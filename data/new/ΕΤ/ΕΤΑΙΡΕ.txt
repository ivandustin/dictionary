Etymology: The word "εταιρεία" (etaireía) comes from the Greek word "ἑταιρεία" (hetaireía), which means "association" or "company". It is derived from the word "ἑταῖρος" (hetaîros), meaning "companion" or "associate".

Grammar: "Εταιρεία" is a feminine noun in Greek. It belongs to the second declension and has the plural form "εταιρείες" (etaireíes).

Description: In modern Greek, "εταιρεία" refers to a company or a business organization. It is used to describe a legal entity that is formed by individuals for the purpose of conducting commercial or industrial activities.
