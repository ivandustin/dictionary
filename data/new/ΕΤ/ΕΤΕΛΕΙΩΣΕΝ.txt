Grammar: "Ετελειώσεν" is the third person singular aorist active indicative form of the verb "τελειώνω" (teleiṓnō) in the Greek language. The aorist tense indicates a completed action in the past, and the active voice indicates that the subject of the verb performs the action.

Description: "Ετελειώσεν" is a word used in Greek to describe the completion or finishing of an action or task. It signifies that something has been accomplished or brought to an end.
