Etymology: The word "ετοιμασία" comes from the Greek verb "ετοιμάζω" (etoimázo), which means "to prepare" or "to get ready". It is derived from the adjective "έτοιμος" (étimos), meaning "ready" or "prepared".

Grammar: "Ετοιμασία" is a feminine noun in the Greek language. It belongs to the second declension and has the plural form "ετοιμασίες" (etimasiés).

Description: "Ετοιμασία" refers to the act or process of preparing or getting ready for something. It can be used in various contexts, such as preparing for an event, organizing materials, or making arrangements.
