Etymology: The word "ετεκνοτροφησεν" comes from the Greek roots "ετεκνο" meaning "to bear children" and "τροφησεν" meaning "to nourish". 

Grammar: "ετεκνοτροφησεν" is a verb in the aorist tense, third person singular form. It is derived from the verb "τροφέω" (to nourish) and the noun "τέκνον" (child).

Description: "ετεκνοτροφησεν" means "he/she/it nourished children" in Greek. It refers to the act of providing care, support, and sustenance to one's own or someone else's children.
