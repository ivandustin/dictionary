Grammar: "Ετεχθη" (etechthi) is the third person singular form of the aorist passive tense of the verb "τίθημι" (tithemi). In Greek grammar, the aorist tense is used to express a completed action in the past, while the passive voice indicates that the subject of the verb is being acted upon.
