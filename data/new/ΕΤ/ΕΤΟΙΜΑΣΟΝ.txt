Etymology: The word "etymology" comes from the Greek word "etymologia," which means "study of the true sense or origin of a word."

Grammar: Grammar refers to the set of rules and principles that govern the structure and use of language. It includes the study of syntax, morphology, phonetics, and semantics.

Description: "Etymology" is the study of the origin and history of words, including their changes in form and meaning over time. It involves tracing the roots and development of words, as well as understanding their connections to other languages.
