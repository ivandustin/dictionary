Etymology: The word "ετοιμάζε" comes from the Greek verb "ετοιμάζω" (etoimázo), which means "to prepare" or "to get ready."

Grammar: "Ετοιμάζε" is the imperative form of the verb "ετοιμάζω" in the second person singular. It is used to give a command or instruction to someone, telling them to prepare or get ready.

Description: "Ετοιμάζε" is a word used in Greek to tell someone to prepare or get ready for something. It is commonly used in everyday conversations and can be used in various contexts, such as preparing for an event, getting ready for a task, or making arrangements.
