Etymology: The word "ετοιμος" (etimos) comes from the Greek word "ετος" (etos), meaning "year". It originally referred to something that is timely or ready for a specific time or occasion.

Grammar: "Ετοιμος" is an adjective in the masculine gender, singular form. It can also be declined to match the gender, number, and case of the noun it modifies.

Description: "Ετοιμος" means "ready" or "prepared" in Greek. It describes something or someone that is fully prepared or in a state of readiness for a particular purpose or situation.
