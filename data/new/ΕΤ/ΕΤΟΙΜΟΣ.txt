Etymology: The word "ετοιμος" (etimos) comes from the Greek word "ἔτοιμος" (etimos), which means "ready" or "prepared".

Grammar: In Greek, "ετοιμος" is an adjective that agrees in gender, number, and case with the noun it modifies. It can be used to describe a person, object, or situation that is ready or prepared.

Description: "Ετοιμος" is a versatile word that can be used in various contexts. It can describe someone who is prepared or ready for a task or event. It can also describe an object or situation that is in a state of readiness. Overall, "ετοιμος" conveys the idea of being prepared, equipped, or in a state of readiness.
