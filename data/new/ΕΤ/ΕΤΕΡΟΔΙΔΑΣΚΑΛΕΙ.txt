Grammar: "Ετεροδιδασκαλεί" is a verb in the present tense, third person singular form. It is derived from the verb "διδάσκω" (didasko), which means "to teach." The prefix "ετερο-" adds the meaning of "teaching differently" or "teaching in a different way."

Description: "Ετεροδιδασκαλεί" refers to the act of teaching in a different or alternative way. It implies a departure from traditional teaching methods or approaches. This term is often used in educational contexts to describe innovative or unconventional teaching practices.
