Etymology: The word "ετοιμασω" (etimaso) comes from the Greek verb "ετοιμάζω" (etimazo), which means "to prepare" or "to get ready". It is derived from the adjective "έτοιμος" (etimos), meaning "ready" or "prepared".

Grammar: "Ετοιμάσω" is the first person singular future tense of the verb "ετοιμάζω" (etimazo). In Greek, verbs are conjugated based on the person, number, tense, and mood. The future tense indicates an action that will happen in the future.

Description: "Ετοιμάσω" is a Greek verb that means "I will prepare" or "I will get ready". It is used to express an action that will be done in the future.
