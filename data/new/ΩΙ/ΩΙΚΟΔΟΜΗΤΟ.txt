Grammar: "Ωικοδομητο" is a noun in the singular form. It belongs to the neuter gender and is declined as such. 

Description: "Ωικοδομητο" refers to the act of constructing or building a house or any other structure. It can also be used to describe the process of development or improvement in a broader sense.
