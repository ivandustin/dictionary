Etymology: The word "ωικοδομούν" comes from the Greek roots "ωικός" (oikos), meaning "house" or "dwelling," and "δομή" (dome), meaning "to build." 

Grammar: "Ωικοδομούν" is the present tense, third person plural form of the verb "ωικοδομώ" (oikodomó), which means "to build" in Greek. 

Description: "Ωικοδομούν" is used to describe the action of building or constructing something. It is commonly used in the context of construction projects or the creation of physical structures.
