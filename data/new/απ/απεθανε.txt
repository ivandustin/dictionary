Word: απεθανε

Etymology: απεθανε is a Greek verb that comes from the combination of the prefix απο- (apo-), meaning "away from," and the verb θανατος (thanatos), meaning "death." Therefore, απεθανε means "to die" or "to pass away."

Grammar: απεθανε is the third person singular aorist indicative active form of the verb αποθνῄσκω (apothnēskō), which means "to die." In Greek, verbs have different forms depending on the tense, mood, voice, and person. The aorist tense refers to a completed action in the past, and the indicative mood indicates a statement of fact. The active voice means that the subject of the verb is performing the action.

Description: απεθανε is a simple and straightforward verb that means "to die." It is commonly used in Greek literature and everyday language to refer to the act of passing away or the state of being dead.
