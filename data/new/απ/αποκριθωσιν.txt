The word "αποκριθωσιν" (apokrithōsin) is a Greek verb in the future tense, third person plural form. It comes from the root word "αποκρίνομαι" (apokrinomai), which means "to answer" or "to reply". 

In terms of grammar, "αποκριθωσιν" is a regular future tense form of the verb, with the suffix "-ωσιν" indicating the third person plural. 

Overall, "αποκριθωσιν" can be translated to mean "they will answer" or "they will reply".
