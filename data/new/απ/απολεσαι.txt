The word "απολεσαι" (apolesai) is a verb in the Greek language. 

Etymology: The word is derived from the Greek verb "απόλλυμι" (apollymi), which means "to destroy" or "to perish". 

Grammar: "απολεσαι" is the second person singular, aorist tense, middle voice form of the verb "απόλλυμι". The middle voice indicates that the subject is both the doer and the receiver of the action. 

Description: The word "απολεσαι" means "you destroyed" or "you perished" in English. It is used to describe an action that was completed in the past.
