Word: απολυσω

Etymology:
The word απολυσω (apolyso) is a Greek verb that comes from the prefix απο- (apo-), meaning "away from," and the verb λυω (lyo), meaning "to loosen" or "to release." Therefore, απολυσω (apolyso) means "to release" or "to let go."
