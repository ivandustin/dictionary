The word "αποστελει" (apostelei) is a verb in the Greek language. It is derived from the root word "αποστέλλω" (apostello), which means "to send away" or "to dispatch". 

In terms of grammar, "αποστελει" is in the present tense, third person singular form. It can be translated to "he/she/it sends away" or "he/she/it dispatches". 

Overall, "αποστελει" is a simple verb that describes the act of sending something or someone away. It can be used in a variety of contexts, such as sending a message, a package, or a person to a specific destination.
