The word "απεδοσθε" is a Greek verb that is in the aorist tense and passive voice. It comes from the root word "αποδίδωμι" which means "to give back" or "to return". The prefix "απο-" indicates separation or departure, so "απεδοσθε" can be translated as "you were given back" or "you were returned". 

In terms of grammar, "απεδοσθε" is conjugated in the second person plural, which means it refers to a group of people. The aorist tense indicates that the action was completed in the past, while the passive voice indicates that the subject received the action rather than performing it themselves.

Overall, "απεδοσθε" is a specific form of the verb "αποδίδωμι" that describes a past action of being given back or returned, and is conjugated in the second person plural.
