Word: αποκατεσταθη

Etymology: αποκατα- (prefix meaning "restoration") + εσταθη (past tense of the verb "στέκομαι" meaning "to stand")

Grammar: αποκατεστάθη is a third person singular past tense passive voice verb in Greek.

Description: αποκατεστάθη means "was restored" or "was reinstated" in English. It is often used in a religious or political context to describe the restoration of a person or institution to a previous state or position.
