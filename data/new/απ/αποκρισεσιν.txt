The word "αποκρισεσιν" (apokrisesin) is a Greek noun that means "answers" or "replies". 

Etymology: The word is derived from the Greek verb "αποκρίνομαι" (apokrinomai), which means "to answer" or "to reply". The suffix "-σεις" (-seis) is added to the verb stem to form the noun "αποκρισεσιν" (apokrisesin).

Grammar: "αποκρισεσιν" is a feminine noun in the accusative plural form. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their gender, number, and case. The accusative case is used to indicate the direct object of a verb.

Description: The word "αποκρισεσιν" is commonly used in Greek to refer to answers or replies given in response to a question or request. It can be used in various contexts, such as in a conversation, an interview, or a written document.
