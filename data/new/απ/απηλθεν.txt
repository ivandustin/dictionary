Word: απηλθεν

Etymology: απηλθεν is a Greek word that comes from the verb ἀπέρχομαι (apérchomai), which means "to go away" or "to depart."

Grammar: απηλθεν is the aorist tense of the verb ἀπέρχομαι (apérchomai), which means that it refers to a completed action in the past. It is in the third person singular form.

Description: απηλθεν is a word that means "he/she/it went away" or "he/she/it departed." It is often used in the context of someone leaving a place or situation. The word can also be used metaphorically to describe someone leaving a relationship or abandoning a cause.
