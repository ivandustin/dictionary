The word "απεκρυψας" is a Greek verb that means "to hide" or "to conceal". It is derived from the prefix "απο-" which means "away from" or "off" and the verb "κρύπτω" which means "to hide". 

In terms of grammar, "απεκρυψας" is the aorist active participle of the verb "κρύπτω". The aorist tense in Greek refers to a past action that is viewed as a single event, while the active voice indicates that the subject of the verb is performing the action. The participle form is used to modify a noun or pronoun, and in this case, "απεκρυψας" would mean "having hidden" or "the one who hid".

Overall, "απεκρυψας" is a descriptive word that can be used to indicate an action of hiding or concealing something or someone.
