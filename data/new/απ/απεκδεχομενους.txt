Word: απεκδεχομενους

The word απεκδεχομενους is a present participle form of the Greek verb απεκδέχομαι (apekdechomai), which means "to wait for eagerly" or "to expect with anticipation". The word is derived from the prefix απο- (apo-), meaning "away from", and the verb εκδέχομαι (ekdechomai), meaning "to receive" or "to await".

απεκδεχομενους can be translated to "awaiting eagerly" or "expecting with anticipation". It is often used in a religious or spiritual context, such as waiting eagerly for the coming of a savior or the fulfillment of a prophecy. The word conveys a sense of hopeful anticipation and patient waiting.
