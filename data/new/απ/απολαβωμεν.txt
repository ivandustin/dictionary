Word: απολαβωμεν

Etymology: απολαβωμεν is a Greek verb that comes from the word απολαμβάνω (apólambanó), which means "to receive, to obtain, to take."

Grammar: απολαβωμεν is a first-person plural aorist active subjunctive verb in Greek. It is formed by adding the suffix -ωμεν (-omen) to the stem απολαβ- (apolab-).

Description: απολαβωμεν is a verb that expresses the idea of "let us receive" or "let us obtain" in Greek. It is often used in a subjunctive mood to express a wish or a suggestion. For example, "ας απολαβωμεν τη ζωή μας" (as apolavomen ti zoí mas) means "let us enjoy our lives."
