The word "αποστασιαν" (apostasian) is a Greek verb that means "to distance oneself" or "to apostatize". 

Etymology: The word comes from the Greek root "αποστασία" (apostasia), which means "defection" or "apostasy". 

Grammar: "αποστασιαν" is the third person singular form of the verb in the aorist tense. 

Description: The word is often used in a religious context to describe someone who has abandoned their faith or religious beliefs. It can also be used more broadly to describe someone who has distanced themselves from a particular group or ideology.
