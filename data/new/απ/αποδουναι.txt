Word: αποδουναι

Etymology:
The word αποδουναι is a Greek verb that comes from the combination of two words: από (apo) meaning "away from" and δούναι (dounai) meaning "to give". Therefore, αποδουναι means "to give away" or "to distribute".

Grammar:
αποδουναι is a first-person singular present active indicative form of the verb αποδίδωμι (apodidomi), which means "to give back" or "to return". It is a regular verb in Greek and follows the conjugation pattern of the verb δίδωμι (didomi).

Description:
αποδουναι is a verb that is commonly used in Greek to express the act of giving away or distributing something. It can be used in various contexts, such as giving away money, food, or other items. The verb can also be used metaphorically to express the act of sharing knowledge or information.
