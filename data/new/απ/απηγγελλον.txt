Word: απηγγελλον

Etymology:
The word απηγγελλον (apēngellon) is a Greek verb that comes from the prefix απο- (apo-), meaning "away from," and the verb αγγελλω (angellō), meaning "to announce" or "to report." Therefore, απηγγελλον means "to report away from" or "to announce from a distance."

Grammar:
απηγγελλον is the imperfect tense, third person plural form of the verb απαγγελλω (apangellō), which means "to report" or "to announce." In this form, it indicates an ongoing or repeated action in the past.

Description:
απηγγελλον is a verb that describes the act of reporting or announcing something from a distance or away from the speaker. It can be used in various contexts, such as reporting news, announcing a message, or relaying information. The imperfect tense form suggests that the action was ongoing or repeated in the past, indicating a continuous or habitual reporting or announcing.
