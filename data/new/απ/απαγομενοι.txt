The word "απαγομενοι" is a Greek verb in the passive participle form. It comes from the verb "απάγω" which means "to lead away" or "to take away". The passive participle form indicates that the action is being done to the subject, rather than the subject doing the action. 

In terms of grammar, "απαγομενοι" is in the masculine plural nominative case. This means that it is referring to a group of males who are being led away or taken away. 

Overall, "απαγομενοι" can be translated to "being led away" or "being taken away" in English.
