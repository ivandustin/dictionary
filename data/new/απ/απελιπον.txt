Word: απελιπον

The word απελιπον (apélipon) is a verb in the past tense form of the Greek word απολείπω (apoleípo), which means "to leave behind" or "to abandon". It is derived from the prefix απο- (apo-), meaning "away from", and the verb λείπω (leípo), meaning "to leave".

απελιπον is a past tense verb in the first person singular form. It belongs to the aorist tense, which is used to describe a completed action in the past. The verb is conjugated from the root word απολείπω (apoleípo), which is a regular verb in the Greek language.
