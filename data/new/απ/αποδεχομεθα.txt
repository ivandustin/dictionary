Word: αποδεχομεθα

Etymology: απο- (apo-) meaning "away from" and δεχομαι (dechomai) meaning "to receive" or "to accept". Therefore, αποδεχομεθα (apodechometha) means "we accept" or "we receive away from".

Grammar: αποδεχομεθα is a first-person plural present indicative middle/passive verb in Greek. It is formed by adding the suffix -μεθα (-metha) to the stem αποδεχο- (apodecho-).

Description: αποδεχομεθα is a formal and polite way of saying "we accept" or "we receive". It is often used in official or professional settings, such as in business or legal documents.
