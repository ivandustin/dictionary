The word "απολογουμεθα" is a Greek verb in the present tense, middle voice, and first person plural form. It comes from the root word "απολογία" which means "defense" or "apology". 

In terms of grammar, the middle voice indicates that the subject is both the doer and the receiver of the action. In this case, "απολογουμεθα" can be translated as "we are defending ourselves" or "we are making our own defense". 

Overall, "απολογουμεθα" is a word that is often used in legal or formal contexts to express the act of defending oneself or making a case for one's actions.
