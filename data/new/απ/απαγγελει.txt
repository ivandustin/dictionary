Word: απαγγελει

The word απαγγελει (apangelei) is derived from the Greek verb απαγγέλλω (apangello), which means "to announce" or "to declare". It is composed of the prefix απο- (apo-), which means "away from", and the verb αγγέλλω (angello), which means "to tell" or "to report".

Grammar:
απαγγελει is a verb in the present tense, third person singular form. It belongs to the first conjugation of Greek verbs, which means that it follows the regular pattern of verbs that end in -ω (o) in the present tense.

Description:
The word απαγγελει is used to describe the act of announcing or declaring something. It can be used in various contexts, such as legal proceedings, public speeches, or personal conversations.
