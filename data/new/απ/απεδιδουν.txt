Word: απεδίδουν

Etymology:
The word απεδίδουν is a Greek verb that comes from the root word δίδωμι (didomi), which means "to give." The prefix απο- (apo) means "away from" or "off," so απεδίδουν can be translated as "they gave away" or "they gave off."

Grammar:
απεδίδουν is a third-person plural past tense verb in the active voice. It is conjugated from the verb δίδωμι (didomi) in the imperfect tense. The imperfect tense is used to describe ongoing or repeated actions in the past.

Description:
απεδίδουν can be translated as "they were giving away" or "they used to give off." It implies a continuous or repeated action in the past. The context of the sentence would determine the exact meaning of the verb.
