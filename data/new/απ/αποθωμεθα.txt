Word: αποθωμεθα

Etymology: αποθωμεθα is a Greek verb that comes from the combination of the prefix απο- (apo-), meaning "away from," and the verb θωμαι (thomai), meaning "to rush, to run." Therefore, αποθωμεθα means "we rush away from."

Grammar: αποθωμεθα is a first-person plural present middle/passive indicative verb in Greek. It is conjugated from the verb αποτρεχω (apotrexo), which means "to run away from."
