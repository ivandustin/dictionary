Word: αποκείται

Etymology: αποκείται is a Greek word that comes from the combination of two words: από (apó) meaning "from" and κείμαι (keímai) meaning "to lie". 

Grammar: αποκείται is a verb in the present tense, third person singular form. It is a passive verb, meaning that the subject is being acted upon rather than performing the action. 

Description: αποκείται is often translated to English as "lies in" or "consists of". It is used to describe the location or source of something. For example, "Η απάντηση αποκείται στο βιβλίο" (The answer lies in the book) or "Το πρόβλημα αποκείται στην έλλειψη πόρων" (The problem consists of the lack of resources).
