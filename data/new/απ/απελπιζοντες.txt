In terms of grammar, "απελπιζοντες" is in the active voice and is conjugated in the present tense. It is also in the masculine plural form, indicating that the subject of the verb is a group of males.

Overall, "απελπιζοντες" can be translated to "despairing" or "losing hope" in English.
