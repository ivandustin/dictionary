The word αποδιδοναι comes from the Greek verb αποδίδωμι (apodidomi), which means "to give back, to return, to restore". It is derived from the prefix απο- (apo-), meaning "away from", and the verb δίδωμι (didomi), meaning "to give".

αποδιδοναι is a present participle in the Greek language, which means it is a verb form that describes an ongoing action. It is formed by adding the suffix -ντας (-ntas) to the stem of the verb αποδίδωμι (apodidomi). The present participle can be used as an adjective or as part of a verb phrase.
