The word "αποδωις" (apodōis) is a Greek verb that means "to give back" or "to repay." 

Etymology: The word comes from the prefix "apo-" meaning "away from" or "off," and the verb "didōmi" meaning "to give." 

Grammar: "αποδωις" is a second-person singular present active imperative verb. 

Description: The word is often used in the context of repaying a debt or returning something that was borrowed. It can also be used in a more abstract sense, such as "to give back" in terms of making amends or making up for something.
