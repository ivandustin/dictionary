The word "αποστρεψουσιν" (apostrepsousin) is a Greek verb in the future tense, third person plural form. It comes from the root word "αποστρέφω" (apostrepho), which means "to turn away" or "to avert." 

In terms of grammar, "αποστρεψουσιν" is a regular verb in the future tense, following the standard conjugation pattern for verbs ending in "-ω" in Greek.
