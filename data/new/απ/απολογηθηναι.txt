Word: απολογηθηναι

Etymology: απολογηθηναι is a Greek verb that comes from the word απολογία (apologia), which means "defense" or "apology". The verb form απολογηθηναι means "to make a defense" or "to apologize".

Grammar: απολογηθηναι is a passive aorist infinitive verb in Greek. It is formed by adding the prefix απο- (apo-) to the verb λογίζομαι (logizomai), which means "to reason" or "to calculate". The passive voice indicates that the subject of the verb is receiving the action, rather than performing it.

Description: απολογηθηναι is a word that refers to the act of making a defense or apologizing. It is often used in legal or formal contexts, where someone is required to explain or justify their actions.
