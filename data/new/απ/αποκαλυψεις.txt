The word "αποκαλυψεις" (apokalypseis) is a Greek noun that means "revelations" or "unveilings". It is derived from the verb "αποκαλυπτω" (apokalypto), which means "to reveal" or "to uncover". In grammar, it is a plural noun in the nominative case. It is often used in a religious context, referring to the revelations in the Book of Revelation in the New Testament.
