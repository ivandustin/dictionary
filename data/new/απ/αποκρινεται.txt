Word: αποκρίνεται

Etymology: απο- (apo-) means "away from" or "off", and κρίνω (krino) means "to judge" or "to decide". Therefore, αποκρίνεται (apokrinetai) means "to answer" or "to reply" in Greek.

Grammar: αποκρίνεται is a verb in the present tense, third person singular form. It is conjugated from the verb αποκρίνομαι (apokrinomai), which means "to answer" or "to reply".

Description: αποκρίνεται is a common Greek verb used in everyday conversation. It is often used in formal settings, such as in business or academic contexts, as well as in informal situations. The verb is used to indicate a response or reply to a question or statement.
