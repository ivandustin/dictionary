The word "απογραφεσθαι" is a Greek verb that means "to be registered" or "to be enrolled". It is derived from the prefix "απο-" which means "away from" or "off", and the verb "γραφειν" which means "to write". In terms of grammar, "απογραφεσθαι" is a passive form of the verb, indicating that the subject is being acted upon rather than performing the action.
