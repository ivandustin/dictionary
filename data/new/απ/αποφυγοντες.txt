The word "αποφυγοντες" is a verb in the present participle form in Greek. It is derived from the verb "αποφεύγω" which means "to avoid" or "to escape". The prefix "απο-" means "away from" or "out of", while the root "φεύγω" means "to flee" or "to run away". 

In terms of grammar, "αποφυγοντες" is the present participle form of the verb, which means it describes an ongoing action. It can be translated to "avoiding" or "escaping" in English. 

Overall, "αποφυγοντες" is a word that describes the action of avoiding or escaping from something, and is commonly used in Greek language and literature.
