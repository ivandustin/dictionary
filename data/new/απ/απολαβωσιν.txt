Grammar: απολαβωσιν is a noun in the accusative plural form. It is derived from the verb απολαμβάνω and the suffix -σιν (-sin), which is used to form the accusative plural of nouns in the third declension.

Description: απολαβωσιν refers to the act of receiving or obtaining something. It can be translated as "receipts" or "earnings" in English.
