The word "αποχρησει" is a Greek verb that means "to misuse" or "to abuse". 

Etymology: The word is derived from the prefix "απο-" which means "away from" or "off" and the verb "χρησιμοποιώ" which means "to use". 

Grammar: "αποχρησει" is a first person singular present tense verb. It can be conjugated in different tenses and persons according to the subject and the context of the sentence. 

Description: The word "αποχρησει" is often used to describe the act of using something in a way that is not intended or appropriate, resulting in harm or damage. It can also refer to the misuse of power or authority.
