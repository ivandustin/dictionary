Word: αποβαλητε

Etymology: The word αποβαλητε comes from the Greek verb αποβάλλω (apovállo), which means "to throw away" or "to discard". The suffix -τε (-te) is added to the verb stem to form the imperative mood, which is used to give commands or orders.

Grammar: αποβαλητε is the second person plural imperative form of the verb αποβάλλω. It is used to give a command or order to a group of people, telling them to throw something away or discard it.
