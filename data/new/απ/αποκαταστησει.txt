Word: αποκαταστήσει (apokatastísi)

Etymology: The word αποκαταστήσει comes from the Greek prefix απο- (apo-), meaning "away from," and the verb καταστήσει (katastísi), meaning "to restore, to establish." Therefore, αποκαταστήσει means "to restore, to bring back to a previous state."

Grammar: αποκαταστήσει is a verb in the future tense, third person singular form. It is derived from the verb καταστήσει and the prefix απο-.

Description: αποκαταστήσει is a Greek verb that refers to the act of restoring or bringing back something to its previous state. It can be used in various contexts, such as restoring a building, repairing a relationship, or recovering from an illness. The word emphasizes the idea of returning something to its original condition or position.
