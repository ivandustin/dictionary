Word: αποκατηλλαγητε

Etymology: The word αποκατηλλαγητε is derived from the Greek words από (apo) meaning "away from" and καταλλαγή (katallagē) meaning "reconciliation". 

Grammar: αποκατηλλαγητε is a verb in the imperative mood, second person plural form. It is used to give a command or instruction to a group of people. 

Description: The word αποκατηλλαγητε means "be reconciled" or "make peace". It is often used in a religious or spiritual context, urging individuals to reconcile with God or with each other.
