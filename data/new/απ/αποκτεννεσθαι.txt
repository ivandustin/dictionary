Word: αποκτεννεσθαι

Etymology: απο- (apo-, prefix meaning "away from") + κτεννειν (ktennein, "to kill"). Therefore, αποκτεννεσθαι means "to be killed, to be put to death".

Grammar: αποκτεννεσθαι is a verb in the passive voice, present tense, infinitive form.

Description: αποκτεννεσθαι is a Greek word that refers to the act of being killed or put to death. It is often used in a legal or judicial context, such as in reference to a sentence of death. The word can also be used metaphorically to describe the end of something, such as the death of a relationship or the end of a career.
