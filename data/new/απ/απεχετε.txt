Word: απεχετε

Etymology: απεχετε is a Greek verb that comes from the combination of the prefix απο- (apo-), meaning "away from," and the verb εχω (echo), meaning "to have." Therefore, απεχετε can be translated as "to have away from" or "to keep away from."

Grammar: απεχετε is a second-person plural present imperative verb in Greek. It is used to give a command or instruction to a group of people, telling them to keep away from something or someone.
