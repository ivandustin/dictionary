The word "αποστολου" is a Greek noun that means "apostle" or "messenger". It is derived from the Greek verb "αποστέλλω" (apostellō), which means "to send away" or "to dispatch". In Greek grammar, "αποστολου" is a second declension masculine noun, with the genitive form being "αποστόλου" (apostolou). It is often used in reference to the twelve apostles of Jesus Christ, who were sent out to spread his teachings and message.
