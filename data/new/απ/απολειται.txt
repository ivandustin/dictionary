The word "απολειται" is a verb in the Greek language. Its etymology can be traced back to the prefix "απο-" which means "away from" or "off" and the verb "λείπω" which means "to be missing" or "to be left behind". 

In terms of grammar, "απολειται" is a third-person singular present indicative form of the verb "λείπω". It is used to describe a situation where something or someone is missing or absent. 

In description, "απολειται" can be used in various contexts such as describing a missing object, a person who is absent, or a situation where something is lacking. It can also be used figuratively to describe a feeling of emptiness or loss.
