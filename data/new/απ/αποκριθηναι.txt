Word: αποκριθηναι

Etymology: απο- (apo-) meaning "away from" and κρίνω (krinō) meaning "to judge" or "to decide". Therefore, αποκριθηναι (apokrithēnai) means "to answer" or "to reply".

Grammar: αποκριθηναι is a verb in the aorist tense, passive voice, and infinitive mood in Greek.

Description: αποκριθηναι is a common Greek verb used to indicate the act of answering or replying to a question or statement. It is often used in the New Testament of the Bible, as well as in other Greek literature. The passive voice suggests that the subject of the sentence is receiving the action of being answered or replied to.
