Word: αποδωσω

Etymology:
The word αποδωσω (apodoso) is a Greek verb that comes from the prefix από (apo), meaning "away from," and the verb δίδωμι (didomi), meaning "to give." Therefore, αποδωσω means "to give away" or "to return."

Grammar:
αποδωσω is a first-person singular future tense verb in Greek. It belongs to the first conjugation of verbs, which means that it follows the regular pattern of verbs that end in -ω.

Description:
αποδωσω is a versatile verb that can be used in various contexts. It can mean "to give back," "to return," "to pay," "to render," or "to yield." It is often used in legal or financial contexts, such as returning borrowed money or paying a debt. It can also be used in a more general sense, such as giving back something that was borrowed or returning a favor.
