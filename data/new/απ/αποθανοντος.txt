The word "αποθανοντος" is a Greek verb in the present participle form, which means "dying" or "in the act of dying". It is derived from the verb "αποθνῄσκω" (apothnḗskō), which means "to die" or "to pass away". The word is formed by adding the suffix "-οντος" (-ontos) to the stem of the verb. In terms of grammar, it is a present participle in the genitive case, which is used to indicate possession or relationship.
