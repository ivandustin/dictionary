Word: απεσταλμένοι

Etymology: απο- (apo-, meaning "away from") + στέλλω (stello, meaning "to send") + -μένοι (-menoi, a suffix indicating passive voice and plural number). Therefore, απεσταλμένοι means "those who have been sent away".

Grammar: απεσταλμένοι is a plural masculine adjective in the nominative case, agreeing with a masculine plural noun in the sentence.

Description: απεσταλμένοι is a Greek word that is commonly used in the New Testament of the Bible to refer to the apostles, who were sent by Jesus to spread his teachings. The word can also be used more generally to refer to anyone who has been sent on a mission or task.
