The prefix "απο-" (apo-) in this case means "away from" or "completely". Therefore, "αποθνηισκουσιν" can be translated to mean "they will completely die" or "they will die off". 

In terms of grammar, "αποθνηισκουσιν" is a regular future tense verb in the third person plural form. It follows the standard conjugation pattern for verbs ending in "-ω" (ō) in the future tense. 

Overall, "αποθνηισκουσιν" is a strong and definitive word that conveys a sense of finality and inevitability.
