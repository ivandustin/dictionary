Word: απεδεξατο

Etymology: απεδεξατο is a Greek verb that comes from the prefix απο- (apo-), meaning "away from," and the verb δεχομαι (dechomai), meaning "to receive." Therefore, απεδεξατο means "to reject" or "to refuse."

Grammar: απεδεξατο is a third-person singular aorist middle indicative verb in Greek. It is conjugated from the verb δεχομαι (dechomai), which means "to receive." The aorist tense indicates a completed action in the past, while the middle voice indicates that the subject is performing the action on themselves.
