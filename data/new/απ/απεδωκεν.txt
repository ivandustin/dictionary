Word: απεδωκεν

απεδωκεν is a Greek word that comes from the verb αποδίδωμι (apodidomi), which means "to give back, to return, to repay". It is formed by the prefix απο- (apo-), which means "away from", and the verb δίδωμι (didomi), which means "to give".

απεδωκεν is the third person singular aorist indicative active form of the verb αποδίδωμι (apodidomi). In Greek, the aorist tense is used to describe a past action that is viewed as a single event, without any reference to its duration or repetition.
