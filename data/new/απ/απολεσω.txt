The word "απολεσω" (apoleso) is a verb in the Greek language. 

Etymology: The word is derived from the prefix "απο-" (apo-), which means "away from" or "off", and the verb "λείπω" (leipo), which means "to leave" or "to be missing". 

Grammar: "απολεσω" is a first-person singular present tense verb, meaning "I destroy" or "I ruin". It belongs to the class of verbs that end in "-ω" and are called "ω-verbs". 

Description: The word "απολεσω" is used to describe an action of destroying or ruining something completely. It can be used in various contexts, such as in warfare, natural disasters, or personal relationships.
