The word "αποδος" (apodos) is a Greek verb that means "to give back" or "to return." It is derived from the prefix "apo-" which means "away from" and the verb "didomi" which means "to give." 

In terms of grammar, "αποδος" is a second person singular imperative verb, meaning it is used to give a command or instruction to someone directly. 

In usage, "αποδος" can be used in a variety of contexts, such as returning a borrowed item, giving back change, or even in a figurative sense of returning a favor or repaying a debt.
