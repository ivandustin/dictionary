The word "αποσπαν" is a verb in the Greek language. Its etymology can be broken down into two parts: "από" meaning "from" or "away from" and "σπαν" which is not a standalone word but is likely derived from the verb "σπάω" meaning "to break" or "to tear apart". 

In terms of grammar, "αποσπαν" is a present tense verb in the third person singular form. It can be conjugated in other tenses and persons as well. 

The meaning of "αποσπαν" is to separate or to detach something from a larger whole. It can also be used figuratively to mean to distract or to divert someone's attention.
