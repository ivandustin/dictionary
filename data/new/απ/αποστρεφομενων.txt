The word "αποστρεφομενων" is a present participle in Greek, derived from the verb "αποστρέφω" (apostrepho), which means "to turn away" or "to avert." The participle form indicates an ongoing action of turning away or averting. 

In terms of grammar, "αποστρεφομενων" is in the genitive case, which indicates possession or relationship to another noun or pronoun. It can be translated as "of those who are turning away" or "of those who are averting."

Overall, "αποστρεφομενων" is a descriptive word that describes a group of people or things that are actively turning away or averting something.
