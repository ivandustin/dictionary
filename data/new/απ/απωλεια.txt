Word: απωλεια

Grammar: απωλεια is a feminine noun in Greek. It is declined in the singular and plural, and its nominative, genitive, and accusative forms are all the same.

Description: απωλεια refers to loss or destruction, either physical or metaphorical. It can be used to describe the loss of a person, object, or idea, as well as the destruction of a building or other structure.
