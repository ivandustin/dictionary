The word "αποκτενει" is a verb in the Greek language. Its etymology can be traced back to the combination of two words: "από" meaning "from" or "away" and "κτείνω" meaning "to kill". Therefore, "αποκτενει" means "to kill or murder someone or something and remove it from a certain place or situation".

In terms of grammar, "αποκτενει" is a present tense verb in the third person singular form. It can be conjugated in different tenses and persons according to the subject and the context of the sentence.

In terms of description, "αποκτενει" is a strong and intense verb that implies violence and aggression. It is often used in the context of crime, war, or other situations where killing or eliminating someone or something is necessary or justified.
