Word: απολαβομένος

Etymology: The word απολαβομένος comes from the Greek verb απολαμβάνω (apalamvano), which means "to receive, to obtain". The suffix -μένος (-menos) is a participle ending, indicating that the word is in the passive voice and refers to someone or something that is receiving or obtaining something.

Grammar: απολαβομένος is a participle in the passive voice, masculine gender, singular number, and nominative case. It is derived from the verb απολαμβάνω, which is in the present tense, indicative mood, and active voice.

Description: απολαβομένος is an adjective that describes someone or something that is being received or obtained. It can be translated as "received" or "obtained" in English. For example, in the sentence "Ο απολαβόμενος μισθός του ήταν υψηλός" (O apolavomenos misthos tou itan ypsilos), which means "His received salary was high", απολαβομένος describes the salary that was received by someone.
