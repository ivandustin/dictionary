The word "απορφανισθεντες" is a Greek verb in the passive participle form. It comes from the root word "απορφανίζω" which means "to orphan" or "to leave fatherless". The passive participle form indicates that the subject has been orphaned or left fatherless. 

In terms of grammar, "απορφανισθεντες" is in the nominative plural form, which means it is the subject of a sentence and refers to multiple people or things that have been orphaned. 

Overall, "απορφανισθεντες" is a word that describes a state of being orphaned or left fatherless.
