The word "αποκαλυψαι" (apokalypsei) is a verb in the Greek language. 

Etymology: The word comes from the Greek root "apokalupto" which means "to uncover" or "to reveal". 

Grammar: "αποκαλυψαι" is in the present tense, active voice, and indicative mood. It is the third person singular form of the verb. 

Description: The word means "to reveal" or "to disclose". It is often used in a religious or spiritual context, referring to the revelation of divine truths or the end of the world. It can also be used in a more general sense to mean "to uncover" or "to make known".
