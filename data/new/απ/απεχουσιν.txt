Word: απεχουσιν

The word απεχουσιν is a Greek verb in the present tense, third person plural form of the verb απέχω (apecho), which means "to be away from" or "to keep away from". The word is derived from the prefix απο- (apo-), meaning "away from", and the verb εχω (echo), meaning "to have" or "to hold".

απεχουσιν is a verb that describes the action of a group of people or things keeping away from something or being away from something.
