Word: απεχει

Etymology:
The word απεχει (apechei) is a Greek verb that comes from the prefix από (apo) meaning "away from" and the verb εχω (echo) meaning "to have". The combination of these two words gives the meaning of "to be away from" or "to keep away from".

απεχει is a present tense verb in the third person singular form. It is conjugated from the verb απέχω (apecho), which means "to abstain" or "to keep away from". The verb is often used in the context of abstaining from something, such as food or drink.
