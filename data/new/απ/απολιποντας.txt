The word "απολιποντας" is a Greek verb in the present participle form, which means "leaving behind" or "abandoning". 

Etymology: The word is derived from the Greek prefix "απο-" which means "away from" or "off" and the verb "λείπω" which means "to leave".

Grammar: "απολιποντας" is the present participle form of the verb "απολείπω" which is a regular verb in Greek. It is formed by adding the suffix "-οντας" to the stem of the verb.

Description: The word "απολιποντας" is commonly used in Greek language to describe the act of leaving something behind or abandoning something. It can be used in various contexts such as leaving a place, abandoning a project, or giving up on a relationship.
