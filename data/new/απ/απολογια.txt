Word: απολογια

Etymology: The word απολογια comes from the Greek word απολογία (apologia), which means "defense" or "justification". It is derived from the verb απολογούμαι (apologoumai), which means "to defend oneself".

Grammar: απολογια is a feminine noun in Greek. Its plural form is απολογίες (apologies).

Description: απολογια refers to a formal defense or justification of one's actions or beliefs. It can also refer to an apology or an expression of regret. In legal contexts, it refers to a plea or defense presented in court. The word is commonly used in Greek Orthodox Christianity to refer to a formal statement of faith or defense of the faith.
