Word: απογραφή

Etymology: The word απογραφή comes from the Greek verb απογράφω (apographo), which means "to register" or "to record". The prefix από (apo) means "from" or "away from", and the root word γράφω (grapho) means "to write". Therefore, απογραφή literally means "to write away from" or "to record from".

Grammar: απογραφή is a feminine noun in Greek. Its plural form is απογραφές (apografes).

Description: απογραφή refers to the act of registering or recording information, usually related to population or property. In Greece, there are two types of απογραφή: the απογραφή του πληθυσμού (apografí tou plithysmou), which is a census conducted every ten years, and the απογραφή ακινήτων (apografí akiníton), which is a property registration process. The word can also be used more broadly to refer to any type of registration or recording of information.
