Word: αποκατάσταση (apokatástasi)

Etymology: The word αποκατάσταση comes from the Greek prefix απο- (apo-), meaning "away from," and the verb καταστήσει (katastísei), meaning "to establish, to set up." Therefore, αποκατάσταση literally means "to establish away from," or "to restore."

Grammar: αποκατάσταση is a feminine noun in the singular form. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their gender, number, and case. The word αποκατάσταση is in the nominative case, which is used for the subject of a sentence.

Description: αποκατάσταση refers to the act of restoring or repairing something that has been damaged or destroyed. It can be used in various contexts, such as the restoration of a building, the recovery of health after an illness, or the reestablishment of peace after a conflict. The word implies a process of returning something to its original state or condition.
