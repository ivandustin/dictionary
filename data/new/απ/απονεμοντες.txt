The word "απονεμοντες" is a Greek verb in the present participle form. It comes from the root word "νέμω" (nemo), which means "to distribute" or "to allot". The prefix "απο-" (apo) in this case means "away from" or "off". 

In terms of grammar, "απονεμοντες" is a present participle verb form, which means it describes an ongoing action in the present. It is also in the masculine plural form, indicating that the subject of the verb is a group of males. 

Overall, "απονεμοντες" can be translated to mean "distributing away" or "allotting off". However, the exact meaning and context of the word would depend on the sentence it is used in.
