Word: αποκαταλλαξαι

Etymology: The word αποκαταλλαξαι comes from the Greek prefix απο- (apo-), meaning "away from," and the verb καταλλάσσω (katallasso), meaning "to reconcile." Therefore, αποκαταλλαξαι means "to reconcile completely" or "to make peace."

Grammar: αποκαταλλαξαι is a verb in the aorist tense, active voice, and infinitive mood. It is formed by adding the prefix απο- to the verb καταλλάσσω and conjugating it in the aorist tense.

Description: αποκαταλλαξαι is a Greek verb that means to reconcile completely or to make peace. It implies a complete restoration of a relationship or a situation that was previously broken or damaged. This word is often used in a religious context to describe the reconciliation between God and humanity.
