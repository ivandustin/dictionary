The word "αποτρεπου" is a Greek verb that means "to turn away" or "to avert". 

Etymology: The word is derived from the prefix "απο-" which means "away from" and the verb "τρέπω" which means "to turn". 

Grammar: "αποτρεπου" is a present tense, active voice, indicative mood verb in Greek. 

Description: The word is commonly used in Greek to describe the act of avoiding or preventing something from happening. It can also be used to describe the act of turning someone or something away from a particular direction or course of action.
