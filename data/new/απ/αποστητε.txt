The word "αποστητε" is a verb in the Greek language. It is the second person plural imperative form of the verb "αποσταίνω" (apostaino), which means "to stand away from" or "to withdraw from." 

Etymology: The word "αποσταίνω" comes from the prefix "από" (apo), meaning "away from," and the verb "ίστημι" (istimi), meaning "to stand."

Grammar: "αποστητε" is the second person plural imperative form of the verb "αποσταίνω." This means that it is a command directed towards a group of people, telling them to "stand away from" or "withdraw from" something.
