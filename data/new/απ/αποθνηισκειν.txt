Word: αποθνησκειν

Etymology: The word αποθνησκειν comes from the Greek roots από (apo) meaning "away from" and θνήσκειν (thnēskein) meaning "to die". 

Grammar: αποθνησκειν is a verb in the present tense, active voice, and infinitive mood. 

Description: αποθνησκειν means "to die" or "to pass away" in Greek. It is a common verb used to describe the act of dying or the state of being dead. The word can be used in a literal sense, as in the death of a person or animal, or in a figurative sense, as in the death of an idea or a relationship.
