Word: απαγγελλομεν

Etymology:
The word απαγγελλομεν (apangellomen) is a Greek verb that comes from the prefix απο- (apo), meaning "away from," and the verb αγγελλω (angello), meaning "to announce" or "to report." Therefore, απαγγελλομεν can be translated as "to report away from" or "to announce from a distance."

απαγγελλομεν is a present tense verb in the first person plural form. It is conjugated from the root verb αγγελλω (angello) and the prefix απο- (apo). The verb is active, indicating that the subject is performing the action of reporting or announcing.

απαγγελλομεν is a versatile verb that can be used in various contexts.
