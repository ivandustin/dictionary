Word: απεκυλισε

Etymology: The word απεκυλισε is derived from the Greek verb αποκυλίω (apokylíō), which means "to unroll" or "to unwind". The prefix απο- (apo-) means "away from" or "off", while the root word κυλίω (kylíō) means "to roll" or "to wind".

Grammar: απεκυλισε is a verb in the past tense, third person singular form. It is conjugated from the verb αποκυλίω (apokylíō) and means "he/she/it unrolled" or "he/she/it unwound".

Description: απεκυλισε is a Greek word that describes the action of unrolling or unwinding something. It can be used in a literal sense, such as unrolling a scroll or unwinding a spool of thread, or in a figurative sense, such as unraveling a mystery or untangling a complicated situation.
