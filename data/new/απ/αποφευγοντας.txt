Word: αποφεύγοντας

Etymology: απο- (apo-) meaning "away from" and φεύγω (fevgo) meaning "to flee" or "to avoid". Therefore, αποφεύγοντας (apofevgonas) means "avoiding" or "evading".

Grammar: αποφεύγοντας is the present participle form of the verb αποφεύγω (apofevgo), which means "to avoid" or "to evade". It is in the active voice, masculine gender, and nominative case.

Description: αποφεύγοντας is a Greek word that describes the act of avoiding or evading something. It can be used in various contexts, such as avoiding a difficult situation, evading a question, or steering clear of danger. The word is commonly used in everyday conversation and writing in Greece.
