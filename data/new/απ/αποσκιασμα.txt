Word: αποσκιασμα

Etymology: The word αποσκιασμα comes from the Greek prefix από (apo), meaning "away from," and the noun σκιά (skia), meaning "shadow." Together, αποσκιασμα means "casting a shadow away from something."

Grammar: αποσκιασμα is a noun in the Greek language, and it is in the singular form. Its gender is neuter.

Description: αποσκιασμα refers to the act of casting a shadow away from something. It can also be used metaphorically to describe something that obscures or overshadows something else. For example, in a political context, one might say that a scandal has cast an αποσκιασμα over a politician's career.
