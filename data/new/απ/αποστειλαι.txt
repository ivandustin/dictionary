Word: αποστείλαι

Etymology:
The word αποστείλαι is a verb in the Greek language. It is derived from the combination of two words: από (apó) meaning "from" and στέλλω (stélo) meaning "to send". Therefore, αποστείλαι means "to send away" or "to dispatch".

Grammar:
αποστείλαι is a second person singular aorist active imperative verb in the Greek language. It is used to give a command or order to someone to send away or dispatch something.

Description:
αποστείλαι is a verb that is commonly used in the Greek language. It is often used in formal or official contexts, such as in business or legal documents, to give instructions or orders to send something away or dispatch it. It can also be used in everyday conversation to give a command or order to someone to send something away.
