The word "απορίες" (apories) is a Greek noun that means "doubts" or "uncertainties". It comes from the Greek verb "απορώ" (aporó), which means "to be at a loss" or "to be in a difficult situation". In terms of grammar, "απορίες" is a plural noun in the feminine gender. It can be used in various contexts, such as expressing confusion or perplexity about a situation or expressing a lack of knowledge or understanding about a particular topic.
