The word "αποστας" (apostas) is a Greek noun that means "apostate" or "defector". It comes from the Greek verb "αποστατέω" (apostateo), which means "to revolt" or "to desert". In terms of grammar, "αποστας" is a masculine noun in the nominative case. It can also be declined in other cases, such as the genitive "αποστατου" (apostatou) or the accusative "αποστατη" (apostati).
