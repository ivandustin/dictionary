Word: αποκαλυπτεται

Etymology: αποκαλυπτεται is a Greek word derived from the verb αποκαλύπτω (apokalypto), which means "to uncover" or "to reveal". The word is composed of two parts: από (apo), meaning "from" or "away", and καλύπτω (kalypto), meaning "to cover" or "to conceal".

Grammar: αποκαλυπτεται is a present tense, passive voice, indicative mood verb in Greek. It is the third person singular form of the verb αποκαλύπτω (apokalypto).

Description: αποκαλυπτεται means "is revealed" or "is uncovered" in English. It is often used in a figurative sense to describe the unveiling or disclosure of something that was previously hidden or unknown.
