Word: αποφθεγγομαι

Etymology:
The word αποφθεγγομαι (apophthengomai) comes from the Greek roots από (apo) meaning "away from" and φθέγγομαι (phthengomai) meaning "to utter, to speak". It is a compound word that means "to speak out, to declare".

Grammar:
αποφθεγγομαι is a verb in the Greek language. It is in the present tense, indicative mood, and active voice. The conjugation of the verb is as follows:

- Singular: αποφθέγγομαι (apophthengomai)
- Plural: αποφθέγγεσθε (apophthengesthe)

Description:
αποφθεγγομαι is a Greek verb that means "to speak out, to declare". It is often used to describe someone who speaks with authority or who makes a bold statement. The word is commonly used in Greek literature and is often associated with wisdom and knowledge. It is a powerful word that conveys a sense of confidence and conviction.
