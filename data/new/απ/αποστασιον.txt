The word "αποστασιον" (apostasion) is a Greek noun that means "distance" or "separation". 

Etymology: The word comes from the Greek verb "αποσταίνω" (apostaino), which means "to stand away from" or "to separate". 

Grammar: "αποστασιον" is a neuter noun in the singular form. 

Description: The word is often used in a literal sense to refer to physical distance or separation between two objects or people. It can also be used in a figurative sense to describe a rift or disagreement between individuals or groups.
