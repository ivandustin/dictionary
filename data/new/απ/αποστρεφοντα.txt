Grammar: "αποστρεφοντα" is the present participle form of the verb, which means it describes an ongoing action of "turning away" in the present tense. It is also in the masculine gender and nominative case, indicating that it refers to a subject that is male and is the subject of the sentence. 

Description: The word "αποστρεφοντα" can be used to describe a person or thing that is actively turning away from something or someone. For example, "Ο άντρας που περπατάει είναι αποστρεφόμενος από τον ήλιο" (The man walking is turning away from the sun).
