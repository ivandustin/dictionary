The word "αποκρισει" (apokrisei) is a Greek noun that means "answer" or "response". 

Etymology: The word is derived from the Greek verb "αποκρίνομαι" (apokrinomai), which means "to answer" or "to reply". 

Grammar: "αποκρισει" is a feminine noun in the nominative case. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their gender, number, and case. 

Description: The word "αποκρισει" is commonly used in everyday conversations and formal settings to refer to a reply or response to a question or statement. It can also be used in a broader sense to refer to any kind of answer or solution to a problem.
