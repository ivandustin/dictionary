The word "αποστρεψει" (apostrepsei) is a verb in the Greek language. 

Etymology: The word is derived from the prefix "από" (apo), meaning "away from," and the verb "στρέφω" (strefó), meaning "to turn." Therefore, "αποστρέφω" (apostréfo) means "to turn away from."

Grammar: "αποστρεψει" is in the third person singular form of the future tense. It means "he/she/it will turn away from."

Description: The word is often used in a figurative sense, meaning to reject or refuse something. It can also be used in a literal sense, such as turning away from a physical object or direction.
