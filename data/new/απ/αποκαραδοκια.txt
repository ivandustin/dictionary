Word: αποκαραδοκια

Etymology: The word αποκαραδοκια comes from the Greek prefix από (apo), meaning "away from," and the verb καραδοκώ (karadokó), meaning "to wait eagerly." Therefore, αποκαραδοκια means "to no longer wait eagerly" or "to give up hope."

Grammar: αποκαραδοκια is a noun in the feminine gender in the singular form.

Description: αποκαραδοκια refers to the feeling of giving up hope or no longer waiting eagerly for something to happen. It can be used to describe a situation where someone has lost faith in a particular outcome or has become disillusioned with a particular situation. It is a complex emotion that can be associated with feelings of disappointment, frustration, and sadness.
