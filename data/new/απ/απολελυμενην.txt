In terms of grammar, "απολελυμενην" is in the feminine singular accusative case. This means that it is the direct object of the sentence and refers to a female noun.

In description, "απολελυμενην" is a word that denotes a completed action in the past. It is often used in the New Testament of the Bible to describe the state of being set free from sin or bondage.
