Word: αποκτειναντων

Etymology: απο- (apo-) meaning "away from" and κτείνω (kteinó) meaning "to kill". Therefore, αποκτειναντων (apokteinantōn) means "those who kill" or "killers".

Grammar: αποκτειναντων is the genitive plural form of the verb αποκτείνω (apokteínō), which means "to kill". It is used to indicate possession or association with a group of killers.

Description: αποκτειναντων is a Greek word that refers to a group of people who are responsible for killing or causing death. It can be used in various contexts, such as in historical accounts, literature, or legal documents. The word has a strong connotation of violence and aggression.
