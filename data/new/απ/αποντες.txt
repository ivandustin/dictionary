Word: αποντες

Etymology:
The word αποντες is derived from the Greek verb απόντω (apónto), which means "to be absent" or "to be away". The suffix -ες (-es) is added to the verb stem to form the present participle, which means "being absent" or "being away".

Grammar:
αποντες is the present participle of the verb απόντω (apónto), which is in the active voice and present tense. The word is masculine and plural, agreeing with the subject it modifies.

Description:
αποντες describes a group of people who are currently absent or away from a particular location or situation. It can also be used to describe a group of people who habitually or regularly are absent or away from a particular location or situation.
