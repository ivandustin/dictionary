The word "αποτελεσθεισα" is a Greek verb in the passive voice, aorist tense, feminine gender, and nominative case. It is derived from the verb "αποτελεω" which means "to complete" or "to accomplish". The suffix "-σθεισα" indicates that the verb is in the passive voice and refers to a completed action in the past. Therefore, "αποτελεσθεισα" can be translated as "having been completed" or "having been accomplished".
