Word: απεστερημένων

Etymology: The word απεστερημένων is derived from the Greek verb αποστερέω (aposteréo), which means "to deprive" or "to take away". The suffix -μένων (-menon) is a passive participle ending, indicating that the word is in the plural form and refers to something that has been deprived or taken away.

Grammar: απεστερημένων is a plural adjective in the masculine gender, agreeing with a noun in the same gender and number. It is declined according to the third declension of Greek adjectives, with the stem απεστερημεν- and the ending -ων.

Description: απεστερημένων describes something that has been deprived or taken away. It can be used to refer to a wide range of things, such as possessions, rights, or opportunities.
