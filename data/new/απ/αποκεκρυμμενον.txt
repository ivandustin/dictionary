The suffix -μένον (-meno) is a participle ending, indicating that the word is in the passive voice and describes something that has been hidden or concealed.

Grammar: αποκεκρυμμένον is a Greek adjective in the masculine gender, singular number, and nominative case. It is used to describe a noun that is hidden or concealed.
