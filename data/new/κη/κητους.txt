The word "κητους" (kētous) is a Greek noun that means "sea monster" or "whale". It is derived from the Greek word "κητος" (kētos), which also means "sea monster" or "whale". In terms of grammar, "κητους" is a masculine noun in the nominative singular form. It can also be declined in other cases, such as the genitive "κητου" (kētou) or the accusative "κητον" (kēton).
