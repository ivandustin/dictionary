Word: κηρυσσομεν

Etymology:
The word κηρυσσομεν (kerusson) is a present active indicative form of the Greek verb κηρυσσω (kerusso), which means "to proclaim" or "to announce". The word has its roots in the ancient Greek language and is derived from the word κηρυξ (kerux), which means "herald" or "messenger".

κηρυσσομεν is a Greek verb that is used to describe the act of proclaiming or announcing something.
