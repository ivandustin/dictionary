Etymology: The word "αμεμπτοι" comes from the Greek language. It is derived from the combination of the prefix "α-" (meaning "not") and the word "μεμπτοι" (meaning "blameworthy"). 

Grammar: "αμεμπτοι" is the plural form of the adjective "αμεμπτος" in Greek. It is used to describe multiple things or people that are not blameworthy or faultless. 

Description: The word "αμεμπτοι" is used to describe a group of individuals or things that are free from blame or fault. It implies that they are without reproach or criticism.
