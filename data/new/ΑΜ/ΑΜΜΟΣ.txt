Etymology: The word "αμμος" (ammos) comes from the Ancient Greek word "ἄμμος" (ammos), meaning "sand".

Grammar: In Greek, "αμμος" (ammos) is a noun of the masculine gender. It is in the nominative case and singular number.

Description: "Αμμος" (ammos) refers to "sand" in Greek. It is a granular material composed of finely divided rock and mineral particles. Sand is commonly found on beaches, deserts, and riverbeds. It is often used in construction, landscaping, and as a filtration medium.
