Etymology: The word "αμετακινητοι" comes from the Greek roots "α" (a), meaning "not," and "μετακινητοι" (metakinitoi), meaning "movable." 

Grammar: "αμετακινητοι" is an adjective in the masculine plural form. It is used to describe objects or things that cannot be moved or are immovable.
