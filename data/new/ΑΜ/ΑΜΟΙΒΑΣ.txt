Etymology: The word "αμοιβάς" (amoivas) comes from the Greek verb "αμείβω" (ameívo), which means "to exchange" or "to reciprocate."

Grammar: "Αμοιβάς" is a noun in the singular form. It belongs to the masculine gender and the nominative case.

Description: "Αμοιβάς" refers to the concept of reciprocity or exchange. It can be used to describe a mutual exchange of actions, favors, or benefits between two or more parties.
