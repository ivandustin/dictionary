Description: "Αμαρτιαν" refers to a specific instance or act of sin or error. It is used to describe a wrongdoing or a moral transgression. In Greek Orthodox theology, "αμαρτιαν" is often associated with the concept of original sin and the need for repentance and forgiveness.
