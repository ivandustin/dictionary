Etymology: The word "αμνού" (amnou) comes from the Greek language. It is derived from the ancient Greek word "ἀμνός" (amnós), which means "lamb".

Grammar: "Αμνού" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the singular form and belongs to the masculine gender.

Description: "Αμνού" refers to a lamb, which is a young sheep. It is commonly used to describe the animal itself or its meat.
