Etymology: The word "αμαρτηση" (amartisi) comes from the Greek verb "αμαρτάνω" (amartano), which means "to sin" or "to make a mistake". 

Grammar: "Αμαρτηση" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the singular form and belongs to the feminine gender. 

Description: "Αμαρτηση" refers to the act of sinning or making a mistake. It is often used in a moral or religious context to describe the wrongdoing or transgression of a person.
