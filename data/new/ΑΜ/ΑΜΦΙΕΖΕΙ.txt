Grammar: "αμφιεζει" is the third person singular form of the present tense of the verb "αμφιέζω" (amfíezo). In Greek grammar, verbs are conjugated based on the person, number, tense, and mood. The third person singular form indicates that the subject of the sentence is a singular third person (he/she/it) and the action is happening in the present.

Description: "αμφιεζει" describes the action of dressing up or adorning someone or something. It can be used to talk about someone putting on clothes or accessories to enhance their appearance.
