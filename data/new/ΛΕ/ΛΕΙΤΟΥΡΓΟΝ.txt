Etymology: The word "λειτουργον" (leitourgon) comes from the Greek roots "λειτουργία" (leitourgia) meaning "public service" and "ἔργον" (ergon) meaning "work". 

Grammar: "Λειτουργον" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the singular form and belongs to the neuter gender. 

Description: "Λειτουργον" refers to a public service or work performed for the benefit of others. It can also refer to a liturgical function or duty, particularly in the context of religious ceremonies or rituals.
