Grammar: "Λεγουσαι" is the present participle form of the verb "λέγω" (lego) in the feminine gender. In Greek grammar, the present participle is used to describe ongoing actions or states. The feminine form indicates that the subject of the action is female.

Description: "Λεγουσαι" can be translated as "saying" or "speaking" in English. It is used to describe a female subject who is currently engaged in the act of speaking or saying something.
