Grammar: "Λελυσαι" is the aorist tense, active voice, indicative mood, and second person singular form of the verb "λύω" (lyo). In Greek grammar, the aorist tense is used to express a completed action in the past.

Description: "Λελυσαι" is a verb form that means "you have loosened" or "you have untied" in English. It indicates that the action of loosening or untying has been completed by the second person singular subject.
