Etymology: The word "λεγετε" (pronounced "legete") comes from the Greek verb "λέγω" (lego), which means "to say" or "to speak".

Grammar: "Λεγετε" is the second person plural form of the verb "λέγω" (lego) in the present tense. It is used when addressing a group of people and means "you all say" or "you all speak".

Description: "Λεγετε" is a verb form that is commonly used in Greek to address or refer to a group of people. It is often used in commands, instructions, or when asking a group of people to say or speak something.
