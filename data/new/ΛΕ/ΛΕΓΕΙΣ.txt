Etymology: The word "λεγείς" (pronounced "legeis") comes from the Greek verb "λέγω" (lego), which means "to say" or "to speak".

Grammar: "Λεγείς" is the second person singular form of the present tense of the verb "λέγω" (lego). It is used when addressing one person directly and means "you say" or "you speak".

Description: "Λεγείς" is a word in the Greek language that is used to refer to the act of speaking or saying something. It is a verb form that is commonly used in everyday conversations and written texts.
