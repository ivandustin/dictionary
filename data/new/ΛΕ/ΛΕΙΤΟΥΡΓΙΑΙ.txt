Etymology: The word "λειτουργία" comes from the Greek roots "λεῖτος" (leitos), meaning "public" or "belonging to the people," and "ἔργον" (ergon), meaning "work" or "function." 

Grammar: "Λειτουργία" is a feminine noun in the Greek language. It is in the singular form. 

Description: "Λειτουργία" is a Greek word that can be translated as "service," "function," or "duty." It refers to the act of performing a public service or fulfilling a specific role or function within a community or organization. In a religious context, it can also refer to a liturgical or ceremonial service.
