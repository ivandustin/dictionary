Etymology: The word "γελωντες" (gelontes) comes from the Greek verb "γελάω" (gelao), which means "to laugh". 

Grammar: "Γελωντες" is the plural form of the noun "γελωντης" (gelontes), which means "laughers" or "those who laugh". It is a masculine noun in the nominative case.

Description: "Γελωντες" refers to a group of people who are laughing or known for their laughter. It can be used to describe individuals or a collective group.
