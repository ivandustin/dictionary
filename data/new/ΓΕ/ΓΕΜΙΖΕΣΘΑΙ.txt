Etymology: The word "γεμίζεσθαι" (gemízesthai) comes from the Greek verb "γεμίζω" (gemízo), which means "to fill" or "to be filled". 

Grammar: "Γεμίζεσθαι" is a reflexive verb in the present tense, middle voice, and infinitive mood. It is used to express the action of filling oneself or getting filled.

Description: "Γεμίζεσθαι" describes the act of becoming full or being filled. It can refer to physical filling, such as filling a container with a substance, or metaphorical filling, such as filling oneself with emotions or thoughts.
