Etymology: The word "γεγραμμένα" comes from the Greek verb "γράφω" (grapho), which means "to write". The prefix "γε-" (ge-) indicates completion or perfect tense, so "γεγραμμένα" can be translated as "written" or "having been written".

Grammar: "Γεγραμμένα" is the plural form of the adjective "γεγραμμένος" (gegrammenos), which agrees with a plural noun. It is used to describe multiple things that have been written.

Description: "Γεγραμμένα" is an adjective in Greek that means "written" or "having been written". It is used to describe things that have been put into writing or recorded.
