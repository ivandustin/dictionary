Etymology: The word "γεγραμμένας" comes from the Greek verb "γράφω" (grapho), which means "to write". The prefix "γε-" (ge-) indicates completion or perfect tense, so "γεγραμμένας" can be translated as "having been written".

Grammar: "Γεγραμμένας" is the feminine form of the adjective, in the nominative case, singular number, and perfect tense. It agrees with a feminine noun in gender, number, and case.

Description: "Γεγραμμένας" describes something that has been written or inscribed. It implies that the action of writing has been completed in the past.
