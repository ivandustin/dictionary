Etymology: The word "γεγοναν" (gegonan) comes from the Greek verb "γίγνομαι" (ginomai), which means "to become" or "to happen". 

Grammar: "Γεγοναν" is the third person plural form of the verb "γίγνομαι" in the aorist tense. It is used to indicate an action that has already happened in the past.

Description: "Γεγοναν" is a Greek word that means "they have become" or "they have happened". It is commonly used to express a completed action or event in the past.
