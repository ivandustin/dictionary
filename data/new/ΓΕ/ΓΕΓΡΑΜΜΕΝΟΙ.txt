Grammar: "Γεγραμμένοι" is the plural form of the verb "γεγραμμένος" (gegrammenos), which is the past participle of "γράφω". It is used to describe a group of people who have been written or inscribed in some way.

Description: "Γεγραμμένοι" can be translated as "written" or "inscribed". It is often used in a figurative sense to describe people who have been officially registered, recorded, or included in a particular group or category.
