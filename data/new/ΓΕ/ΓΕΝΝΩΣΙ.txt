Etymology: The word "γεννώσι" (gennósi) comes from the Greek verb "γεννώ" (gennó), which means "to give birth" or "to generate."

Grammar: "Γεννώσι" is a noun in the plural form. It belongs to the third declension in Greek grammar.

Description: "Γεννώσι" refers to the act of giving birth or the process of childbirth. It can also be used to describe the offspring or descendants of a person or animal.
