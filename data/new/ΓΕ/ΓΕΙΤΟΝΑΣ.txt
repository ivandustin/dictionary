Grammar: "Γείτονας" is a masculine noun in the Greek language. It belongs to the second declension and has the nominative singular form "γείτονας". In the plural form, it becomes "γείτονες".

Description: "Γείτονας" refers to a person who lives near or next to someone else, usually in the same neighborhood or building. It is commonly used to describe someone who is geographically close to another person and can be considered a neighbor.
