Etymology: The word "γενεαλογούμενος" comes from the Greek words "γενεά" (genea) meaning "generation" and "λογούμενος" (logoumenos) meaning "being spoken of". 

Grammar: "Γενεαλογούμενος" is a noun in the masculine gender, singular number, and nominative case. 

Description: "Γενεαλογούμενος" refers to someone or something that is being traced or recorded in a genealogy. It is commonly used to describe a person who is being mentioned or included in a family tree or lineage.
