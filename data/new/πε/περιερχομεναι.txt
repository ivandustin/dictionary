The word περιερχομεναι is a present participle form of the Greek verb περιέρχομαι (perierchomai), which is derived from the prefix περι- (peri-), meaning "around," and the verb ἔρχομαι (erchomai), meaning "to come." Therefore, περιερχομεναι can be translated as "coming around" or "going around."
