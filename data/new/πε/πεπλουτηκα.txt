The word "πεπλουτηκα" is a conjugated form of the Greek verb "πλουτέω" (ploutéo), which means "to be rich" or "to have wealth". The verb is in the perfect tense, indicating a completed action with ongoing effects. 

In terms of grammar, "πεπλουτηκα" is in the first person singular, indicating that the speaker is the one who has become rich. The prefix "πε-" indicates the perfect tense, while the stem "πλουτ-" indicates the verb root.

Overall, "πεπλουτηκα" can be translated to "I have become rich" or "I am wealthy".
