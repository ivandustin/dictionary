The word "περπερευεται" is a Greek verb that means "to walk or move around aimlessly or restlessly." 

Etymology: The word is derived from the Greek root "περι-" which means "around" or "about," and "περπατώ" which means "to walk." 

Grammar: "περπερευεται" is a third-person singular present indicative form of the verb "περπατώ." 

Description: The word describes a type of movement that is characterized by aimlessness or restlessness. It can be used to describe someone who is wandering around without a clear purpose or direction.
