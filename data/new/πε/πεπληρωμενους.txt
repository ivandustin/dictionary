The word πεπληρωμένους is a Greek word that comes from the verb πληρόω (plēróō), which means "to fill" or "to complete". The word is in the perfect participle form, which indicates that the action of filling or completing has already been done in the past and the result is still present.

πεπληρωμένους is a masculine plural accusative form of the perfect participle of the verb πληρόω. It is used as an adjective to modify a noun in the accusative case. In Greek, adjectives agree in gender, number, and case with the noun they modify. Therefore, πεπληρωμένους agrees with a masculine plural accusative noun.
