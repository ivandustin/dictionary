Word: περυσι

Etymology: περυσι is a Greek word that comes from the ancient Greek word πέρυσι (pérysi), which means "last year."

Grammar: περυσι is an adverb in Greek, which means that it modifies a verb, an adjective, or another adverb. It is used to indicate that something happened or existed in the past, specifically in the year before the current one.

Description: περυσι is a common word in Greek that is used to refer to the previous year. It is often used in everyday conversation, as well as in written texts. For example, one might say "πέρυσι τέτοια εποχή" (pérysi tétia epochí), which means "this time last year." It is a useful word for expressing past events and for comparing them to the present.
