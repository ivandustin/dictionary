The word περιελαμψεν is a Greek verb that comes from the combination of two words: περι (peri) meaning "around" or "about" and ελαμψεν (elampsen) meaning "shone" or "radiated". Therefore, the word περιελαμψεν can be translated as "shone around" or "radiated about".

περιελαμψεν is a verb that describes a bright and radiant light that shines around or about something.
