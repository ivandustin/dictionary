Word: περιλαμψαν

Etymology: 
The word περιλαμψαν is a verb form in the aorist tense of the Greek word περιλάμπω (perilampō), which means "to shine around" or "to illuminate". The word is derived from the prefix περι- (peri-), meaning "around", and the verb λάμπω (lampō), meaning "to shine".

Grammar:
περιλαμψαν is the third person singular aorist active indicative form of the verb περιλάμπω. In Greek grammar, the aorist tense is used to describe a past action that is viewed as a single event, without any reference to its duration or repetition. The active voice indicates that the subject of the verb is performing the action.

Description:
περιλαμψαν can be translated as "he/she/it shone around" or "he/she/it illuminated". The word is often used in a metaphorical sense, to describe someone or something that stands out or shines brightly. It can also be used in a literal sense, to describe the act of shining a light or illuminating a space.
