The word "πετραι" (petrai) is a Greek noun in the plural form, which means "rocks" or "stones". It is derived from the Greek word "πέτρα" (petra), which means "rock" or "stone". In terms of grammar, it is a third declension noun, with the nominative plural form being "πετραι". It can be used to refer to any type of rock or stone, whether natural or man-made.
