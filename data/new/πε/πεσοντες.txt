Word: πεσόντες

Etymology: πεσόντες is a Greek word that comes from the verb πίπτω (pípto), which means "to fall". The suffix -οντες (-ontes) is a participle ending that indicates the present active participle, meaning "those who are falling".

Grammar: πεσόντες is the present active participle of the verb πίπτω (pípto), which means "to fall". It is in the masculine gender and plural number, agreeing with the noun it modifies.

Description: πεσόντες is a word that describes a group of people who are currently falling. It can be used in a literal sense, such as a group of people falling off a cliff, or in a figurative sense, such as a group of people experiencing a sudden decline in their fortunes.
