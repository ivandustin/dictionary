Word: περισσευητε

Etymology: The word περισσευητε is a verb form in the Greek language, specifically in the present tense, second person plural. It is derived from the root word περισσεύω (perisseuō), which means "to be in excess, to abound, to be left over."

Description: περισσευητε is a verb that commands or requests the listener to "abound" or "be in excess."
