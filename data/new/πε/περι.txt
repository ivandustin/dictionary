Etymology:
The word "περι" comes from the Greek language and means "around" or "about". It is a preposition that is commonly used in Greek to indicate the location or direction of something.

Grammar:
In Greek, "περι" is a preposition that is used to indicate the location or direction of something. It is often followed by a noun or pronoun to complete the phrase. For example, "περι του σπιτιου" means "around the house".
