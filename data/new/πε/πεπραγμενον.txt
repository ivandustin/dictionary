Word: πεπραγμένον

Etymology:
The word πεπραγμένον is a past participle of the Greek verb πράττω (pratto), which means "to do" or "to act". The prefix πε- (pe-) indicates that the action is completed or has been done in the past. Therefore, πεπραγμένον means "having been done" or "having been accomplished".

Grammar:
πεπραγμένον is a neuter gender noun in the singular form. It is declined in the nominative case as follows:

Singular: πεπραγμένον
Plural: πεπραγμένα

Description:
πεπραγμένον is a word that describes something that has been done or accomplished. It can be used to refer to a completed action or task, or to describe something that has already been achieved. In Greek literature, it is often used to describe heroic deeds or accomplishments.
