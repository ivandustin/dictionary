Word: πεπληρωμένα

Etymology: The word πεπληρωμένα is derived from the Greek verb πληρόω (plēróō), which means "to fill" or "to complete". The prefix πεπ- (pep-) indicates that the action of filling or completing has already been done in the past.

Grammar: πεπληρωμένα is the plural neuter form of the past participle of the verb πληρόω. It is used to describe plural neuter nouns that have been filled or completed in the past.

Description: πεπληρωμένα is an adjective that describes something that has been filled or completed in the past. It can be used to describe a wide range of things, such as a container that has been filled with water, a task that has been completed, or a person who has been fully trained.
