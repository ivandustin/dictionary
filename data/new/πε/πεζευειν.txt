The word "πεζευειν" (pezeuein) is a Greek verb that means "to walk on foot" or "to go on foot". It is derived from the Greek word "πεζός" (pezos), which means "on foot". In terms of grammar, "πεζευειν" is a present active infinitive verb, meaning it is the unconjugated form of the verb that expresses the action of walking on foot in the present tense.
