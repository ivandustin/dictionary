Word: πειθομεν

Etymology:
The word πειθομεν (peithomen) is a present participle form of the Greek verb πείθω (peitho), which means "to persuade" or "to convince". The word is derived from the Proto-Indo-European root *bʰeydʰ- ("to persuade, to convince").
