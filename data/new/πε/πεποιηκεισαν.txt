The word πεποιηκεισαν is a Greek verb that comes from the root word ποιέω (poiéo), which means "to make" or "to do". The prefix πεποιηκ- (pepoiék-) is the perfect tense form of the verb, indicating that the action of making or doing has been completed in the past.

πεποιηκεισαν can be translated to English as "they have made" or "they have done".
