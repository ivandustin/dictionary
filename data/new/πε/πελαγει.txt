The word "πελαγει" (pelagei) is a verb in the Greek language. It comes from the noun "πέλαγος" (pelagos), which means "sea" or "ocean". The verb "πελαγει" means "to sail on the sea" or "to navigate the ocean". 

In terms of grammar, "πελαγει" is a present tense verb in the third person singular form. It is conjugated from the root verb "πλέω" (pleo), which means "to sail" or "to swim". 

Overall, "πελαγει" is a descriptive word that refers to the act of sailing or navigating the sea.
