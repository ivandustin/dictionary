Word: περιετεμεν

Etymology: περιετεμεν is a Greek word that comes from the verb περιτέμνω (peritémno), which means "to circumcise." The prefix περι- (peri-) means "around" or "about," while the root τέμνω (témno) means "to cut."

Description: περιετεμεν describes something that has been circumcised or cut around.
