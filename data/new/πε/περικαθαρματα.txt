Word: περικαθαρματα

Etymology:
The word περικαθαρματα is derived from the Greek words περι (peri) meaning "around" and καθαρμα (katharma) meaning "cleansing" or "purification". It is a compound word that refers to the act of cleaning or purifying something thoroughly.

περικαθαρματα is a plural noun in the neuter gender. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their gender, number, and case. The plural form of the word indicates that it refers to multiple objects or things that need to be cleaned or purified.

περικαθαρματα is a term that is often used in the context of religious or spiritual practices.
