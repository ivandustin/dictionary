Word: περισσότεραι

Etymology: The word περισσότεραι is derived from the Greek word περισσός (perissós), which means "excessive" or "abundant". The suffix -τεραι (-terai) is a feminine plural ending, indicating that the word is referring to multiple things.

Grammar: περισσότεραι is a feminine plural adjective in the nominative case. It is used to describe multiple feminine nouns, indicating that there is an excess or abundance of something.

Description: περισσότεραι is often used to describe a surplus or abundance of something, such as resources or wealth. It can also be used to describe an excess of emotions or feelings. In modern Greek, it is commonly used to mean "more" or "additional".
