Word: περιεστησαν

Etymology: The word περιεστησαν is a Greek verb that comes from the combination of two words: περι (peri), which means "around" or "about," and εστησαν (estisan), which means "stood." Therefore, the literal meaning of περιεστησαν is "stood around" or "surrounded."

Grammar: περιεστησαν is the third person plural aorist indicative active form of the verb περιίστημι (peristemi), which means "to stand around" or "to surround." In this form, it indicates that the action of standing around or surrounding was completed in the past.

Description: περιεστησαν is a verb that describes an action of surrounding or standing around something or someone. It can be used in various contexts, such as describing a group of people surrounding a person or an object, or a situation where something is enclosed or encircled by something else. The word is commonly used in Greek literature and can be found in various texts, including the New Testament.
