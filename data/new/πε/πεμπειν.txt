The word πεμπειν comes from the Greek verb πέμπω (pempo), which means "to send". The suffix -ειν (-ein) is added to the verb stem to form the present tense.

πεμπειν is a versatile verb that can be used in a variety of contexts. It can be used to describe the act of sending a message, a package, or a person to a specific destination.
