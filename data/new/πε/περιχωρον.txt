Word: περιχωρον

Etymology:
The word περιχωρον comes from the Greek words περι (peri) meaning "around" and χωρος (choros) meaning "space" or "place". Therefore, the word περιχωρον means "surrounding space" or "surrounding place".

Grammar:
περιχωρον is a neuter noun in the singular form. In Greek, neuter nouns have the same form for both the nominative and accusative cases. The genitive form of περιχωρον is περιχωρου.

Description:
περιχωρον refers to the surrounding area or space around a particular place or location. It can be used to describe the environment or surroundings of a city, town, or village. It can also refer to the immediate area around a building or structure. The word can be used in both a literal and figurative sense, to describe the physical or metaphorical space around something.
