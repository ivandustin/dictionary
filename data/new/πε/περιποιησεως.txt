Word: περιποιησεως

Etymology: The word περιποιησεως comes from the Greek verb περιποιέω (peripoiéō), which means "to take care of" or "to look after". The suffix -σεως (-seōs) is a genitive form that indicates possession or belonging.

Grammar: περιποιησεως is a noun in the genitive case, which means it is used to show possession or belonging. It is a feminine noun and is part of the third declension in Greek grammar.

Description: περιποιησεως refers to the act of taking care of something or someone, or the state of being well-cared for. It can be translated as "care", "maintenance", or "nurture". The word is often used in the context of personal grooming or self-care, but it can also refer to the care of animals, plants, or objects.
