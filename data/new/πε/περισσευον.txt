Word: περισσευον

Grammar: περισσευον is a present participle in the masculine gender, singular number, and nominative case. It is used to describe something that is currently in a state of excess or abundance.

Description: περισσευον can be used to describe a variety of things that are in excess or abundance, such as food, money, or time.
