Word: περατων

Etymology:
The word περατων is derived from the Greek word πέρας (peras) which means "limit" or "boundary". The suffix -των (-ton) is a common ending in Greek words that denotes a place or location.

Grammar:
περατων is a noun in the masculine gender and the nominative case. It is the singular form of the word, and its plural form is περάτονες (peratones).

Description:
περατων refers to a boundary or limit, often used in the context of a physical boundary such as a border or edge. It can also be used metaphorically to refer to a limit or boundary in a non-physical sense, such as a limit to one's patience or abilities.
