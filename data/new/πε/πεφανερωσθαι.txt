Word: πεφανερωσθαι

Etymology:
The word πεφανερωσθαι is a Greek verb that comes from the root word φανερός (phanerós), which means "visible" or "apparent." The prefix πε- (pe-) indicates a completed action, so the word πεφανερωσθαι means "to have been made visible" or "to have been revealed."

Grammar:
πεφανερωσθαι is a passive verb in the aorist tense, which means that the action of being made visible or revealed has already been completed. The verb is also in the infinitive form, which means that it is not conjugated to match a specific subject or tense.

Description:
πεφανερωσθαι is a word that describes the act of something being made visible or revealed. It can be used in a literal sense, such as when something that was previously hidden or unknown is now made visible or known. It can also be used in a figurative sense, such as when a truth or secret is revealed.
