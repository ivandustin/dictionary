Word: περιεθηκαν

Etymology: περι- (peri-, meaning "around") + εθηκαν (ethēkan, meaning "placed"). The word is derived from the Greek verb τίθημι (tithēmi, meaning "to place").

Grammar: περιεθηκαν is a verb in the past tense, third person plural form. It is the aorist tense of the verb περιτίθημι (peritithēmi, meaning "to place around").

Description: περιεθηκαν means "they placed around" or "they surrounded" in English. It is often used in a literal sense, such as when describing the placement of a fence or barrier around something. However, it can also be used figuratively to describe the act of surrounding or enclosing something with words or ideas.
