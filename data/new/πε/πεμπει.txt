Etymology: The word "πέμπω" (pempo) comes from the Proto-Indo-European root *pem(p)-, which means "to send". 

Grammar: "Πεμπει" (pempei) is a present tense verb in the indicative mood. It is conjugated as follows: 

- Πεμπω (pempo) - I send 
- Πεμπεις (pempeis) - You send 
- Πεμπει (pempei) - He/she/it sends 
- Πεμπουμε (pempoume) - We send 
- Πεμπετε (pempete) - You (plural) send 
- Πεμπουν (pempoun) - They send
