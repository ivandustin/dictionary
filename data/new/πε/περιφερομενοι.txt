Word: περιφερομενοι

Etymology:
The word περιφερομενοι is a present participle form of the Greek verb περιφέρω (periféro), which means "to carry around" or "to bear". The word is derived from the combination of two Greek words: περί (peri), meaning "around" or "about", and φέρω (féro), meaning "to carry" or "to bear".
