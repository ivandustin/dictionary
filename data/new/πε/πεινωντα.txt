The word "πεινωντα" (peinonta) is a present participle form of the Greek verb "πεινάω" (peináo), which means "to be hungry". The word is derived from the noun "πείνα" (peína), which means "hunger". 

In terms of grammar, "πεινωντα" is a masculine singular form of the present participle, which is used to describe an ongoing action. It can be translated as "being hungry" or "while being hungry". 

Overall, "πεινωντα" is a word that describes the state of being hungry and is commonly used in Greek literature and everyday conversation.
