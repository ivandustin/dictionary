Word: περισσευομεν

Etymology: περισσευομεν is a Greek verb that comes from the root word περισσεύω (perisseúō), which means "to be in excess" or "to abound". The verb is in the present tense and active voice, and it is conjugated in the first person plural.

- Περισσεύεις (perisseúeis) - You are in excess

- Περισσευόμαστε (perissevómaste) - We are in excess
- Περισσεύετε (perisseúete) - You are in excess
