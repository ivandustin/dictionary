The word "περιπεσόντες" is a Greek verb in the aorist participle form, which means "having fallen around" or "having fallen upon". It is derived from the root word "πίπτω" (pípto), which means "to fall". In terms of grammar, it is a participle, which is a type of verb that functions as an adjective or adverb in a sentence. It is declined according to gender, number, and case, and can be used to modify a noun or pronoun.
