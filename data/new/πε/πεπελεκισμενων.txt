Word: πεπελεκισμένων

Grammar: πεπελεκισμένων is the genitive plural form of the participle πεπελεκισμένος (pelekismenos), which is used as an adjective. It agrees in gender, number, and case with the noun it modifies. In this form, it is used to describe a group of things that have been chopped or hacked.
