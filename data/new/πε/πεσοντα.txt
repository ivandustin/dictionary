Word: πεσόντα

Etymology: πεσόντα is the plural form of the Greek verb πεσώ, which means "to fall". It is derived from the ancient Greek word πίπτω (píptō), which also means "to fall".

Grammar: πεσόντα is the plural form of the past participle of the verb πεσώ. It is used to describe multiple things that have fallen.

Description: πεσόντα is an adjective that describes things that have fallen. It can be used to describe a variety of things, such as leaves that have fallen from a tree, or objects that have fallen to the ground. In Greek mythology, the term is often used to describe fallen heroes or gods.
