Word: περιφεροντες

Etymology: περι- (peri-, around) + φέρω (pherō, to carry) + -οντες (-ontes, present participle suffix) = περιφερόντες (peripheróntes, those who carry around)

Grammar: περιφερόντες is a present participle in Greek, which means it describes an ongoing action. It is in the masculine gender and plural number, indicating that it refers to a group of people who are currently carrying something around.

Description: περιφερόντες can be translated as "those who are carrying around" or "those who are transporting." The word implies that the people in question are actively engaged in carrying something from one place to another. The context in which the word is used will determine what exactly is being carried or transported.
