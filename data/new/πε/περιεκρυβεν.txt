Word: περιεκρυβεν

Etymology: περι- (peri-, meaning "around") + εκρυβεν (ekryben, meaning "hid") = περιεκρυβεν (periekryben, meaning "hid around")

Grammar: περιεκρυβεν is a verb in the imperfect tense, third person singular, active voice, and indicative mood in Ancient Greek.

Description: περιεκρυβεν means "he/she/it was hiding around" in Ancient Greek. It is derived from the verb εκρυβεν (ekryben, meaning "hid") with the prefix περι- (peri-, meaning "around"). The imperfect tense indicates that the action was ongoing in the past, and the active voice indicates that the subject was performing the action.
