Word: πενθουσι

Etymology: The word πενθουσι comes from the Greek verb πενθέω (pentheo), which means "to mourn" or "to grieve". It is derived from the noun πένθος (penthos), which means "mourning" or "grief".

Grammar: πενθουσι is a verb in the present tense, third person plural form. It is conjugated from the verb πενθέω (pentheo), which is a regular verb in the Greek language.
