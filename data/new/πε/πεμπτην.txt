The word "πεμπτην" (pemptin) is a Greek adjective that means "fifth" in English. 

Etymology: The word comes from the Greek word "πέμπτος" (pemptos), which also means "fifth". 

Grammar: "πεμπτην" is the accusative singular form of the feminine gender.
