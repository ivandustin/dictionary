The word "πετραις" (petrais) is a Greek noun that means "rocks" or "stones". It is derived from the Greek word "πέτρα" (petra), which also means "rock" or "stone". In terms of grammar, "πετραις" is a feminine noun in the nominative plural form. It can be used to describe a collection of rocks or stones, or to refer to a specific type of rock or stone.
