Word: πεπιστευκοτων

Etymology:
The word πεπιστευκοτων is a Greek word that is derived from the verb πιστεύω (pisteuō), which means "to believe" or "to have faith." The word is in the perfect participle form, which indicates that the action of believing has been completed in the past and has ongoing effects in the present.

Grammar:
πεπιστευκοτων is a masculine plural noun in the genitive case. It is used to describe a group of people who have believed or have faith in something. The word is often used in a religious context to refer to believers or followers of a particular faith.
