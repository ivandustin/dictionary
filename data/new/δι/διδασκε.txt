The word διδασκε (didaske) is a verb in the Greek language, which means "to teach" or "to instruct". It is derived from the root word διδασκαλία (didaskalia), which means "teaching" or "instruction". 

In terms of grammar, διδασκε is a present tense verb in the second person singular form. It can also be conjugated in other tenses and persons, such as διδάσκω (didasko) in the first person singular present tense, διδάσκεις (didaskeis) in the second person singular present tense, and διδάσκουσι (didaskousi) in the third person plural present tense.

Overall, διδασκε is a versatile and important verb in the Greek language, used to describe the act of teaching and instructing others.
