Therefore, διαστρεφοντα can be translated as "distorting" or "perverting" in English.

In terms of grammar, διαστρεφοντα is a present participle in the active voice, masculine gender, and nominative case. It is often used to describe a person or thing that is currently engaged in the action of distorting or perverting something.
