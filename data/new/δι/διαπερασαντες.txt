Word: διαπερασαντες

Etymology:
δια- (dia-) means "through"
περασαντες (perasantes) is the aorist participle of the verb περάω (perao), which means "to cross over"

Therefore, διαπερασαντες (diaperasantes) means "having crossed over through"

Grammar:
διαπερασαντες is a masculine plural nominative participle in Ancient Greek.

Description:
διαπερασαντες is a word that describes a group of people who have crossed over through something, such as a river or a mountain pass. It is often used in historical or mythological contexts to describe the feats of heroes or armies.
