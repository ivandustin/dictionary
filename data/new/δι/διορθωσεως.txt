Word: διορθωσεως

Etymology: διορθωσεως is a Greek word that comes from the verb διορθόω (diorthóo), which means "to correct" or "to set right."

Grammar: διορθωσεως is a noun in the genitive case, which is used to show possession or relationship. It is the genitive singular form of the noun διόρθωσις (diorthósis), which means "correction" or "rectification."

Description: διορθωσεως refers to the act of correcting or setting something right. It can be used in various contexts, such as correcting a mistake in a text, fixing a problem in a machine, or improving a situation that has gone wrong. The word emphasizes the importance of making things right and ensuring that they are functioning properly.
