The word διασωσαι (diáso̱sai) is a Greek verb in the aorist tense, which means "to save" or "to rescue." It is derived from the root word σῴζω (sózo̱), which also means "to save" or "to heal." In Greek grammar, διασωσαι is a second-person singular form of the verb, meaning "you (singular) saved" or "you (singular) rescued."
