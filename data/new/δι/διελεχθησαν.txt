Word: διελεχθησαν

Etymology:
The word διελεχθησαν is a Greek verb that comes from the root word λεγω (lego), which means "to speak" or "to say." The prefix δι- (di-) in this word means "through" or "thoroughly," so διελεχθησαν can be translated as "they spoke thoroughly" or "they had a thorough conversation."

Grammar:
διελεχθησαν is a third-person plural aorist passive indicative verb in Greek. The aorist tense indicates that the action happened in the past, and the passive voice indicates that the subject received the action of the verb. The indicative mood indicates that the verb is a statement of fact.

Description:
διελεχθησαν is a word that describes a thorough conversation or discussion that took place in the past. It implies that the conversation was detailed and comprehensive, and that all parties involved had a chance to express their thoughts and opinions. The word can be used in a variety of contexts, from casual conversations among friends to more formal discussions in academic or professional settings.
