Word: διδαξει

Etymology: διδαξει is a verb in the future tense of the Greek language. It comes from the root word διδάσκω (didasko), which means "to teach" or "to instruct."

Grammar: διδαξει is a third-person singular future tense verb in the active voice. It is conjugated from the root verb διδάσκω (didasko) and the future tense suffix -ει.

Description: διδαξει means "he/she/it will teach" in Greek. It is commonly used in sentences where someone is talking about a future event or action that involves teaching or instructing. For example, "Ο καθηγητής θα διδάξει το μάθημα αύριο" (The professor will teach the lesson tomorrow).
