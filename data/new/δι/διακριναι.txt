The word διακρίναι comes from the Greek verb διακρίνω (diakrinō), which means "to distinguish" or "to separate". It is derived from the prefix δια- (dia-), meaning "through" or "across", and the verb κρίνω (krinō), meaning "to judge" or "to decide".

διακρίναι is a Greek verb that means "to distinguish" or "to separate".
