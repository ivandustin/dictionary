- Εγώ διώκω (Egō diōkō) - I pursue
- Εσύ διώκεις (Esy diōkeis) - You pursue

- Εμείς διώκουμε (Emeis diōkoume) - We pursue
- Εσείς διώκετε (Eseis diōkete) - You (plural) pursue
- Αυτοί/Αυτές/Αυτά διώκουν (Autoi/Autes/Auta diōkoun) - They pursue

In terms of description, διωκω can be used in various contexts to describe the act of pursuing or chasing after something or someone.
