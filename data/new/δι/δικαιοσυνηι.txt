The word "δικαιοσυνηι" (dikaiosynēi) is a Greek noun that means "justice" or "righteousness". It is derived from the adjective "δίκαιος" (dikaios), which means "just" or "righteous". In Greek grammar, "δικαιοσυνηι" is a feminine noun in the dative case. It is often used in philosophical and theological contexts to refer to the concept of moral and divine justice.
