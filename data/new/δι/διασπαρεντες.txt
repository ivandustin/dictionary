Word: διασπαρεντες

Etymology:
The word διασπαρεντες is derived from the Greek verb διασπείρω (diaspeírō), which means "to scatter" or "to disperse". The suffix -εντες (-entes) is a participle ending that indicates the present active participle form of the verb.

Grammar:
διασπαρεντες is the present active participle form of the Greek verb διασπείρω (diaspeírō). It is in the masculine nominative plural form, which means it refers to a group of males who are currently scattering or dispersing.

Description:
The word διασπαρεντες describes a group of males who are currently scattering or dispersing. It can be used to describe a physical scattering, such as a group of people scattering in different directions, or a more abstract scattering, such as a group of ideas or thoughts being dispersed among different people or places.
