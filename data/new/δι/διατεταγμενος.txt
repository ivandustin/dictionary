The word διατεταγμένος comes from the Greek verb διατάσσω (diatássō), which means "to arrange" or "to order". The word is formed from the prefix δια- (dia-), which means "through" or "across", and the verb τάσσω (tássō), which means "to arrange" or "to put in order".

διατεταγμένος is a past participle of the verb διατάσσω (diatássō), which means "arranged" or "ordered". It is a masculine singular nominative form, which means it is used to describe a singular masculine subject in the nominative case.

διατεταγμένος is an adjective that describes something that has been arranged or ordered in a particular way.
