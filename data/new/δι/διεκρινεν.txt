Word: διεκρίνεν

Etymology:
The word διεκρίνεν comes from the Greek verb διακρίνω (diakrinō), which means "to distinguish" or "to separate". It is derived from the prefix δια- (dia-), meaning "through" or "across", and the verb κρίνω (krinō), meaning "to judge" or "to decide".

Grammar:
διεκρίνεν is a verb in the past tense, third person singular form. It is the aorist tense of the verb διακρίνω (diakrinō), which means that it refers to a completed action in the past. The verb is active, indicating that the subject of the sentence is performing the action.

Description:
διεκρίνεν is a word that describes the act of distinguishing or separating something. It can be used in a variety of contexts, such as distinguishing between two options, separating different elements, or making a decision.
