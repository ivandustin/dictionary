Word: διασποράν

Etymology: διασποράν comes from the Greek word διασπορά (diaspora), which means "dispersion" or "scattering". It is derived from the verb διασπείρω (diaspeirō), which means "to scatter" or "to disperse".

Grammar: διασποράν is a noun in the accusative case, which means it is the direct object of a verb. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their grammatical function in a sentence.

Description: διασποράν refers to the act of scattering or dispersing, often used in the context of the dispersion of a group of people. It can also refer to the state of being scattered or dispersed.
