Word: διαλογισμούς

Etymology: The word διαλογισμούς comes from the Greek word διαλογισμός (dialogismos), which means "reasoning" or "thinking". It is derived from the verb διαλογίζομαι (dialogizomai), which means "to reason" or "to think through".

Grammar: διαλογισμούς is a plural noun in the Greek language. It is in the accusative case, which is used to indicate the direct object of a verb.

Description: διαλογισμούς refers to the act of reasoning or thinking through a problem or situation. It can also refer to a philosophical or intellectual discussion or debate. In modern Greek, it is often used to describe the practice of meditation or contemplation.
