Word: διαπερών

Etymology:
δια- (dia-) means "through" or "across"
περάω (peráo) means "to pass through" or "to cross over"

Therefore, διαπερών (diaperón) means "passing through" or "crossing over".

Grammar:
διαπερών is the present participle of the verb διαπεράω (diaperáo), which means "to pass through" or "to cross over". It is in the masculine gender, singular number, and nominative case.

Description:
διαπερών can be used to describe someone or something that is currently passing through or crossing over a particular place or obstacle. It can also be used metaphorically to describe someone who is persevering through a difficult situation or overcoming obstacles.
