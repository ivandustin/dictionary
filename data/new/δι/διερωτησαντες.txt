Word: διερωτησαντες

διερωτησαντες is a Greek word that comes from the verb διερωτάω (di-er-o-ta-o), which means "to ask, to inquire". The suffix -αντες (-antes) is a participle ending that indicates the action is in the past tense and the subject is plural.

διερωτησαντες is a past participle in the masculine plural form. It is used to describe a group of people who have asked or inquired about something in the past. In Greek, the verb form changes depending on the gender, number, and tense of the subject.
