Word: διαδεξαμενοι

Etymology: The word διαδεξαμενοι comes from the Greek verb διαδέχομαι (diadéchomai), which means "to receive, to take over". The suffix -μενοι (-menoi) is a participle ending, indicating that the word is in the present tense and refers to a group of people who are currently receiving or taking over something.

Description: διαδεξαμενοι can be translated as "those who are receiving/taking over". It is often used in a political or organizational context to refer to a group of people who are taking over a position or responsibility from someone else.
