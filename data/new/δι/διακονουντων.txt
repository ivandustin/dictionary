διακονουντων is a present active participle in the genitive case, masculine gender, and plural number. It is formed by adding the suffix -οντ- to the present stem διακονε- and the ending -ων to indicate the genitive plural.

διακονουντων is used in the New Testament of the Bible to refer to those who serve or minister in various capacities within the Christian community.
