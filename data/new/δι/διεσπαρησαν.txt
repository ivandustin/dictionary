Word: διεσπαρησαν

Etymology:
διεσπαρησαν is a Greek verb that comes from the root word σπείρω (speírō), which means "to sow" or "to scatter". The prefix δι- (di-) adds the meaning of "thoroughly" or "completely", so διεσπαρησαν means "they scattered completely".

Grammar:
διεσπαρησαν is the third person plural aorist indicative active form of the verb διασπείρω (diaspeírō), which means "to scatter" or "to disperse". The aorist tense indicates a completed action in the past, and the indicative mood indicates a statement of fact. The active voice indicates that the subject (they) performed the action.

Description:
διεσπαρησαν describes an action of scattering or dispersing that was completed in the past. The subject (they) performed the action, but it is not specified who or what was scattered. The verb can be used in a literal sense, such as scattering seeds in a field, or in a figurative sense, such as dispersing a crowd or breaking up a group.
