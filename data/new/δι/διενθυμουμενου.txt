Word: διενθυμουμενου

Etymology:
The word διενθυμουμενου is a Greek word that comes from the verb διενθυμέομαι (dienthyméomai), which means "to ponder over, to consider carefully". The word is formed by combining the prefix δι- (di-), which means "through" or "thoroughly", with the verb ἐνθυμέομαι (enthyméomai), which means "to think, to reflect".

Grammar:
διενθυμουμενου is a present participle in the genitive case, masculine gender, and singular number. It is derived from the verb διενθυμέομαι (dienthyméomai), which is a compound verb formed by the prefix δι- (di-) and the verb ἐνθυμέομαι (enthyméomai). The present participle form indicates that the action of pondering or considering is ongoing or continuous.

Description:
διενθυμουμενου can be translated as "of one who is pondering/considering/thinking carefully". It describes a person who is engaged in deep reflection or contemplation, carefully considering a matter or idea.
