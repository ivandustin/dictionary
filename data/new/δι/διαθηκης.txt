Word: διαθήκης

Grammar: διαθήκης is a noun in the genitive case, which is used to show possession or relationship. It is the genitive singular form of διαθήκη (diathēkē).

Description: διαθήκης refers to a will or testament, which is a legal document that outlines a person's wishes for the distribution of their property and assets after their death.
