Word: διεμεριζον

Etymology: διεμεριζον is a Greek verb that comes from the prefix δι- (meaning "apart" or "through") and the verb μεριζω (meaning "to divide" or "to distribute"). 

Grammar: διεμεριζον is the present participle form of the verb διαμεριζω, which means "to divide" or "to separate". It is in the active voice, masculine gender, and nominative case. 

Description: διεμεριζον describes someone or something that is currently dividing or separating. It can also be used to describe a person who is divisive or causing division.
