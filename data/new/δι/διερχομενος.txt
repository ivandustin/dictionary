The word διερχόμενος (di-er-kho-me-nos) is a present participle in Greek, which means "passing through" or "going through". It is derived from the verb διέρχομαι (di-er-kho-mai), which means "to pass through" or "to go through". 

In terms of grammar, διερχόμενος is a masculine singular nominative form of the present participle. It can be used as an adjective to describe a noun, or as a verb in a sentence.

Example sentences:
- Ο διερχόμενος τρένο έκανε θόρυβο. (The passing train made noise.)
- Βλέποντας τον διερχόμενο, τον χαιρέτισε. (Seeing the passerby, he greeted him.)

Overall, διερχόμενος is a versatile word that can be used in various contexts to describe movement or passage through a place or time.
