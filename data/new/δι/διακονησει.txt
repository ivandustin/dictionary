The word διακονησει (diakonēsei) is a Greek verb in the future tense, which means "he/she/it will serve" or "he/she/it will minister". It is derived from the noun διάκονος (diakonos), which means "servant" or "minister". In the New Testament, the word is often used to refer to those who serve in the church, such as deacons or ministers. In Greek grammar, διακονησει is a third-person singular future active indicative verb.
