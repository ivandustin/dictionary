διηλθεν is a third-person singular aorist indicative active form of the verb διέρχομαι (diérchomai). In Greek, the aorist tense is used to describe a completed action in the past, without any indication of the duration or repetition of the action. The indicative mood indicates that the verb is a statement of fact.
