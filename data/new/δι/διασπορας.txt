Word: διασποράς

Description: διασποράς is a term that is often used to refer to the dispersion of a group of people, particularly in the context of the Jewish diaspora. It can also be used more broadly to refer to any kind of scattering or dispersal, such as the dispersion of seeds or the scattering of ashes.
