In terms of grammar, διηνοιγεν is in the imperfect tense, indicating an ongoing or repeated action in the past. It is also in the indicative mood, which suggests a statement of fact. 

Overall, διηνοιγεν can be translated as "he/she/it was opening thoroughly" or "he/she/it kept opening up completely".
