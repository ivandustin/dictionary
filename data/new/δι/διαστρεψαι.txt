Word: διαστρεψαι

Etymology:
διαστρέφω (diastrepho) is a Greek verb that means "to distort" or "to twist". The word διαστρεψαι (diastrepsei) is the aorist tense of this verb, which means it refers to a completed action in the past.

διαστρεψαι can be translated to English as "I distorted" or "I twisted". It is a verb that describes an action that has already been completed in the past.
