Word: διαπορευόμενου

Etymology:
The word διαπορευόμενου is a present participle of the Greek verb διαπορεύομαι (diaporevomai), which means "to pass through, to traverse". The prefix δια- (dia-) means "through" and the verb πορεύομαι (porevomai) means "to go, to travel". 

Grammar:
διαπορευόμενου is a present participle in the genitive case, masculine gender, and singular number. It is derived from the verb διαπορεύομαι (diaporevomai), which is in the present tense, middle voice, and indicative mood. 

Description:
The word διαπορευόμενου describes something or someone that is currently passing through or traversing a particular place or area. It can be used to describe a person, an animal, or an object that is in motion. The genitive case indicates possession or association, so the word can also be used to describe something that belongs to or is associated with the subject that is passing through.
