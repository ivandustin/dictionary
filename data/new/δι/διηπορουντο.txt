Word: διηπορουντο

Etymology:
διηπορουντο is a Greek verb that comes from the combination of two words: δια (dia) meaning "through" or "across" and απορεω (aporeo) meaning "to be at a loss" or "to be in doubt". Therefore, διηπορουντο means "they were in doubt" or "they were uncertain".

διηπορουντο is a verb that describes a state of uncertainty or doubt. It is often used in ancient Greek literature to describe characters who are unsure of what to do or how to proceed.
