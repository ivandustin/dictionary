Word: διανοηματα

Etymology:
The word διανοηματα (dianoemata) is derived from the Greek verb διανοέομαι (dianoéomai), which means "to think over, to consider, to reflect upon". The noun form διανοημα (dianoema) means "thought, idea, concept".

Grammar:
διανοηματα is a plural noun in the Greek language. It is the plural form of διανοημα (dianoema).

Description:
διανοηματα refers to thoughts, ideas, or concepts that are formed through reflection and contemplation. It can also refer to mental processes such as reasoning, understanding, and perception. In modern Greek, the word is often used to describe philosophical or intellectual ideas.
