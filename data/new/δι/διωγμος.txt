The word διωγμός (diogmos) comes from the Greek verb διώκω (dioko), which means "to pursue" or "to chase". The suffix -μός (-mos) is added to the verb stem to form a noun indicating the action or result of the verb. Therefore, διωγμός (diogmos) means "pursuit" or "persecution".
