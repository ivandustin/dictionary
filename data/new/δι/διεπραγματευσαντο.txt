Word: διεπραγματευσαντο

Etymology:
The word διεπραγματευσαντο is a Greek verb that comes from the combination of two words: δια (dia) meaning "through" or "across" and πραγματεύομαι (pragmateuomai) meaning "to negotiate" or "to transact business". Therefore, the word διεπραγματευσαντο means "they negotiated through" or "they transacted business across".

διεπραγματευσαντο is a verb that describes the act of negotiating or transacting business through or across something.
