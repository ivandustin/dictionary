Word: δικαιουντες

δικαιουντες is a Greek word that comes from the root word δικαιόω (dikaióō), which means "to justify" or "to declare righteous". The suffix -ουντες (-ountes) is a present participle ending, indicating that the word is describing an action that is currently happening.
