Word: διδασκαλίαις

Grammar: διδασκαλίαις is a feminine noun in the dative plural form. In Greek, the dative case is used to indicate the indirect object of a verb or the object of certain prepositions.

Description: διδασκαλίαις refers to teachings or lessons that are given by a teacher or instructor. It can also refer to the act of teaching or instructing. The word is often used in a religious context to refer to the teachings of a particular faith or belief system.
