The word διδοντος (didontos) is a present participle form of the Greek verb δίδωμι (didomi), which means "to give". The present participle form indicates that the action of giving is currently happening or ongoing. 

In terms of grammar, διδοντος is in the genitive case, which is used to show possession or relationship. It can be translated as "of giving" or "belonging to giving". 

Etymologically, δίδωμι comes from the Proto-Indo-European root *deh₃-, which means "to give". This root is also the origin of the English words "donate", "endow", and "dowry". 

Overall, διδοντος is a form of the Greek verb δίδωμι that indicates ongoing action and possession or relationship.
