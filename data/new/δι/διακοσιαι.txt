Word: διακοσιαι

Etymology: διακοσιαι is a Greek word that comes from the word διακόσιοι (diakosioi), which means "two hundred."

Grammar: διακοσιαι is a feminine noun in the nominative plural form. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their gender, number, and case. The nominative case is used for the subject of a sentence.

Description: διακοσιαι refers to the number two hundred. It is often used in Greek to describe quantities or amounts of things. For example, if someone wanted to say "two hundred books," they would say "διακόσια βιβλία" (diakosia vivlia).
