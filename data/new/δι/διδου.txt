The word διδου (didou) is a Greek verb in the present tense, which means "give" or "offer". It is derived from the ancient Greek word δίδωμι (didōmi), which has the same meaning. In terms of grammar, διδου is a second person singular form of the verb, which means it is used when addressing one person directly. It can also be used in the imperative mood, which means it is a command or request to give or offer something.
