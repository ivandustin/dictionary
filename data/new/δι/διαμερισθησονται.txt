Word: διαμερισθησονται

Etymology: δια- (dia-) meaning "through" or "across", μερίζω (merizo) meaning "to divide", and -σονται (-sontai) indicating future tense and passive voice. Therefore, διαμερισθησονται means "they will be divided through".

Grammar: διαμερισθησονται is a third-person plural future passive indicative verb in Ancient Greek. It is formed by adding the future tense suffix -σονται to the passive voice form of the verb διαμερίζω (di-amerizo).

Description: διαμερισθησονται is a verb that describes a future action in which something will be divided through. It is often used in a metaphorical sense to describe a separation or division between people or groups.
