The word διαδιδωσιν (diadidōsin) is a Greek noun that means "distribution" or "dissemination". It is derived from the verb διαδίδωμι (diadidōmi), which means "to distribute" or "to spread". In terms of grammar, διαδιδωσιν is in the accusative case and singular number. It is often used in the context of spreading information or ideas, and can also refer to the distribution of goods or resources.
