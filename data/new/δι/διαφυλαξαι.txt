Word: διαφυλάξαι

Etymology:
δια- (dia-) - prefix meaning "through, across"
φυλάσσω (phylássō) - verb meaning "to guard, protect"

διαφυλάξαι (diaphyláxai) is the aorist tense of the verb διαφυλάσσω (diaphylássō), which means "to guard carefully, preserve, protect".

Grammar:
διαφυλάξαι is a second person singular aorist active imperative verb in Ancient Greek.

Description:
διαφυλάξαι is a command to someone to guard or protect something carefully. It is often used in a military context, but can also be used in a more general sense.
