Etymology: The word "τηρηση" (tērēsē) comes from the Greek verb "τηρέω" (tēreō), which means "to keep" or "to preserve."

Grammar: "Τηρηση" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the singular form and belongs to the feminine gender.

Description: "Τηρηση" refers to the act of keeping, preserving, or observing something. It can also mean the act of following rules, regulations, or instructions.
