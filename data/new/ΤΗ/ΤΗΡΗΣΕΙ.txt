Etymology: The word "τηρησει" comes from the Greek verb "τηρέω" (tireo), which means "to keep" or "to observe."

Grammar: "τηρησει" is a verb in the future tense, third person singular form. It is derived from the verb "τηρέω" and is conjugated in the future tense to indicate an action that will happen in the future.

Description: "τηρησει" means "he/she/it will keep" or "he/she/it will observe" in Greek. It is used to express an action that will be performed in the future by a third person singular subject.
