Word: βλεπεις

Etymology: βλεπεις is a Greek verb that comes from the root word βλέπω (blepo), which means "to see" or "to look". It is a present tense verb in the second person singular form.

Grammar: βλεπεις is a present tense verb in the second person singular form. It is a conjugation of the verb βλέπω (blepo), which means "to see" or "to look". In Greek, verbs are conjugated based on the person, number, and tense of the subject.

Description: βλεπεις is a Greek verb that means "you see" or "you are looking". It is commonly used in everyday conversation and can be used to describe a physical action or a mental state.
