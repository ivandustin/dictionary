The word βλεπηις (blepēis) is a Greek noun that means "a female spectator" or "a female observer". It is derived from the verb βλέπω (blépō), which means "to see" or "to look". In terms of grammar, βλεπηις is a feminine noun in the nominative case. It can also be spelled as βλέπης (blepēs) in the masculine form.
