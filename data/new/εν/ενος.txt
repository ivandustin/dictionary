The word "ενος" (enos) is a Greek word that means "one" or "single". It is derived from the Greek word "είς" (eis), which also means "one". In terms of grammar, "ενος" is a masculine singular genitive form of the Greek word "είς". It is commonly used in Greek language to indicate possession or ownership. For example, "το βιβλίο ενός φίλου" (to vivlio enos filou) means "the book of a friend".
