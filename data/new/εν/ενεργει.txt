The word "ενεργει" (energei) is a Greek verb that means "to work" or "to operate". 

Etymology: The word comes from the Greek root "ενεργός" (energos), which means "active" or "energetic". 

Grammar: "Ενεργει" is a present tense verb in the third person singular form. It can be conjugated in different tenses and persons to indicate different actions and subjects. 

Description: The word "ενεργει" is commonly used in everyday Greek language to describe someone who is actively working or doing something. It can also be used in a more abstract sense to describe the functioning or operation of a system or machine.
