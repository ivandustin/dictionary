Etymology:
The word ενεδυναμωθη is a Greek word that comes from the root word δυναμόω (dynamóo), which means "to strengthen" or "to empower". The prefix εν- (en-) means "in" or "within", so the word ενεδυναμωθη can be translated as "to be strengthened within".
