The word "εντρεπων" (entrepōn) is a Greek adjective that means "bashful", "modest", or "shy". 

Etymology: The word comes from the Greek verb "εντρέπω" (entrepō), which means "to shame" or "to embarrass". The suffix "-ων" (-ōn) is added to the verb stem to form the adjective.

Grammar: "Εντρεπων" is a masculine singular nominative form of the adjective. It can also be declined to match the gender, number, and case of the noun it modifies.

Description: The word "εντρεπων" is often used to describe someone who is hesitant or reluctant to speak or act in a certain way due to a sense of shame or embarrassment. It can also be used to describe someone who is modest or humble in their behavior or demeanor.
