Etymology: The word is derived from the Greek roots "εν" (en) meaning "in" or "within" and "δεικνυω" (deiknyo) meaning "to show". 

Grammar: "ενδεικνυσθαι" is a passive verb in the present tense, infinitive mood, and middle voice.
