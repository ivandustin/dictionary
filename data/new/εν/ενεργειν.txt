The word "ενεργειν" (energein) is a Greek verb that means "to act" or "to work". It comes from the Greek word "ενέργεια" (energeia), which means "energy" or "activity". 

In terms of grammar, "ενεργειν" is a present active infinitive verb. It is often used in the context of describing someone's actions or behavior, such as "He is actively working on the project" or "She is energetically pursuing her goals". 

Overall, "ενεργειν" is a versatile verb that can be used in a variety of contexts to describe someone's actions or behavior.
