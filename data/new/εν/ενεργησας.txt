Word: ενεργησας

Grammar: ενεργησας is a second person singular aorist active indicative form of the verb ενεργέω. This means that it is used to describe an action that you (the second person singular) completed in the past. The aorist tense is often used to describe a specific event that happened in the past, rather than a continuous or ongoing action.
