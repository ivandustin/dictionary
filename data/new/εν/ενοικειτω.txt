The word "ενοικειτω" is a Greek verb in the present tense, third person singular form. It comes from the root word "οικέω" which means "to dwell" or "to inhabit". The prefix "εν-" adds the meaning of "in" or "within". 

In terms of grammar, "ενοικειτω" is a present active imperative verb, which means it is a command or request for someone to do something in the present moment. 

Overall, the word "ενοικειτω" can be translated to mean "let him/her/it dwell within" or "let him/her/it inhabit".
