Word: ενθυμήσεις

Etymology:
The word ενθυμήσεις (enthyméseis) is derived from the Greek verb ενθυμούμαι (enthymoúmai), which means "to remember" or "to recall". The word is formed by adding the suffix -σεις (-seis) to the stem of the verb.

Grammar:
ενθυμήσεις is a plural noun in the nominative case. It belongs to the third declension of Greek nouns, which means that its stem changes depending on the case and number. The word is feminine in gender.

Description:
ενθυμήσεις refers to memories or recollections. It can also be translated as "remembrances" or "reminiscences". The word is often used in a nostalgic or sentimental context, to describe fond memories of the past.
