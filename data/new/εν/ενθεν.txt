Etymology:
The word ενθεν (enthén) is a Greek adverb that means "from here" or "hence." It is derived from the combination of the preposition εν (en), meaning "in" or "at," and the adverb θεν (then), meaning "there" or "from there."

In Greek, ενθεν is an adverb that modifies verbs, adjectives, or other adverbs. It does not change form based on gender, number, or case.
