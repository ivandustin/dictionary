The word "εναλιων" (enalion) is a Greek adjective that means "related to the sea" or "marine". It is derived from the Greek words "εναλίος" (enalios), which means "belonging to the sea", and "ναῦς" (naus), which means "ship". In terms of grammar, "εναλιων" is a masculine singular adjective in the nominative case.
