The word "ενεδρευοντες" is a Greek verb that means "to lie in wait" or "to ambush". It is derived from the Greek word "ενέδρα" which means "ambush". In terms of grammar, "ενεδρευοντες" is the present participle form of the verb "ενεδρεύω" which means "to lie in wait". It is used to describe an ongoing action of lying in wait or ambushing.
