The word "ενως" (enōs) is a Greek adjective that means "one" or "single". It is derived from the Greek word "εἷς" (heis), which also means "one". In terms of grammar, "ενως" is a masculine singular form of the adjective. It can be used to describe a single object or person, or to emphasize the uniqueness of something.
