Word: εναυαγησαν

Etymology: The word εναυαγησαν comes from the Greek words εν (en) meaning "in" and αυαγος (auggos) meaning "shipwreck". 

Grammar: εναυαγησαν is a verb in the past tense, third person plural form. It is the aorist tense of the verb ναυαγέω (naufageo) which means "to suffer shipwreck". 

Description: εναυαγησαν describes the action of a group of people suffering a shipwreck. It can also be used metaphorically to describe a situation where someone or something has failed or been destroyed.
