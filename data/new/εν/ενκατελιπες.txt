The word "ενκατελιπες" is a Greek verb that means "to abandon" or "to leave behind". It is derived from the prefix "en-" meaning "in" or "into", and the verb "katelipō" meaning "to leave behind". In terms of grammar, it is a second person singular aorist active indicative verb. It is often used in a negative context, such as when someone abandons their responsibilities or duties.
