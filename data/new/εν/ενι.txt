Word: ενι

Etymology: The word ενι (eni) is a Greek adverb that means "in one" or "together". It is derived from the Greek word εν (en), which means "in" or "within".

Grammar: ενι is an adverb in Greek and does not change form based on gender, number, or case.

Description: ενι is used to indicate unity or togetherness. It is often used in phrases such as "ενι και παντα" (eni kai panta), which means "in one and all" or "all together". It can also be used to indicate a single entity or thing, such as "ενι σωμα" (eni soma), which means "one body".
