The word "ενδυσεως" (enduseos) is a Greek noun that means "dressing" or "clothing". It is derived from the verb "ενδύω" (endyo), which means "to put on" or "to dress". In terms of grammar, "ενδυσεως" is a genitive singular noun, which means it is used to indicate possession or relationship to another noun. 

Example sentence: Η ενδυσεως του βασιλιά ήταν πολυτελής. (The clothing of the king was luxurious.)
