The word "ενετυλιξεν" is a Greek verb that means "wrapped up" or "enveloped".

Grammar: "Ενετυλιξεν" is the third person singular aorist indicative active form of the verb. In Greek, the aorist tense is used to describe a completed action in the past.

Description: The word "ενετυλιξεν" is often used in a figurative sense to describe a situation where something is completely surrounded or enclosed. It can also be used in a literal sense to describe the act of wrapping or enveloping something.
