The word εντειλαμενος comes from the Greek verb "εντέλλω" (entéllō), which means "to command" or "to order". The word is formed from the prefix "εν-" (en-), which means "in" or "on", and the verb "τέλλω" (téllō), which means "to accomplish" or "to perform".

Grammar:
εντειλαμενος is a participle in the masculine gender, nominative case, singular number, and perfect tense. It is derived from the verb εντέλλω, which is a second conjugation verb in the active voice.

Description:
The word εντειλαμενος describes someone who has been commanded or ordered to do something and has completed the task.
