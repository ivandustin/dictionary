Word: εντρυφώντες

Grammar: εντρυφώντες is a present participle in the masculine plural form. It is used to describe a group of people who are currently reveling or enjoying deeply. In Greek, present participles can function as adjectives or as part of a verb tense.

Description: εντρυφώντες describes a group of people who are deeply enjoying or reveling in something. It can be used to describe a group of friends who are having a great time together, or a group of scholars who are deeply engaged in a particular subject.
