The word "ενθυμηθεντος" is a Greek verb in the aorist participle form. It comes from the root word "ενθυμέομαι" which means "to remember" or "to recall". The aorist participle form indicates that the action of remembering or recalling has been completed in the past.

In terms of grammar, "ενθυμηθεντος" is in the masculine gender, singular number, and nominative case. It can be translated to mean "having remembered" or "having recalled".

Overall, "ενθυμηθεντος" is a word that describes the act of remembering or recalling something that has already happened in the past.
