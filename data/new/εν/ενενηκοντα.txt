Word: ενενηκοντα

Etymology:
The word ενενηκοντα (enenekonta) is a Greek word that means "ninety" in English. It is derived from the Greek words εννέα (ennea) meaning "nine" and κοντά (konta) meaning "tens".

Grammar:
In Greek, ενενηκοντα is a numeral adjective that is used to indicate the quantity of ninety. It is in the masculine gender, singular number, and nominative case.

Description:
The word ενενηκοντα is commonly used in Greek to indicate the number ninety. It is often used in counting, measurements, and other numerical contexts. It is also used in various expressions and idioms in the Greek language.
