The word "ενεκεντρισθης" is a Greek verb that means "to be centered" or "to be focused." 

Etymology: The word is derived from the Greek words "εν" (en), meaning "in" or "within," and "κεντρον" (kentron), meaning "center." 

Grammar: "ενεκεντρισθης" is a passive voice verb in the aorist tense, which means that the action of being centered or focused has already taken place. 

Description: The word can be used to describe a person or thing that is centered or focused on a particular goal or objective. It can also be used in a more abstract sense to describe a state of mind or a feeling of being centered or grounded.
