The word "ενεργουμενης" is a Greek adjective that is derived from the verb "ενεργέω" (energeō), which means "to work" or "to be active". The adjective form can be translated to mean "active" or "in action". 

In terms of grammar, "ενεργουμενης" is in the feminine singular nominative case. It can also be declined to match the gender, number, and case of the noun it modifies.

Overall, "ενεργουμενης" is a descriptive word that is often used to describe a person or thing that is actively engaged in some kind of action or work.
