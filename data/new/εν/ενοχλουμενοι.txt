Word: ενοχλουμενοι

Etymology: The word ενοχλουμενοι comes from the Greek verb ενοχλώ (enochló), which means "to disturb" or "to bother". The suffix -μενοι (-menoi) is a passive participle ending, indicating that the word refers to people who are being disturbed or bothered.

Grammar: ενοχλουμενοι is the plural form of the passive participle of the verb ενοχλώ. It is used to describe a group of people who are being disturbed or bothered by something or someone.

Description: ενοχλουμενοι is a Greek word that describes a group of people who are experiencing discomfort or annoyance due to some external factor. This could be anything from a noisy neighbor to a persistent headache. The word is often used in a negative context, as it implies that the people in question are being inconvenienced or bothered in some way.
