Word: εναγκαλισαμενος

Etymology:
The word εναγκαλισαμενος is derived from the Greek verb "αγκαλιάζω" (ankalíazo), which means "to embrace" or "to hug". The prefix "εν" (en) is added to the verb to indicate that the action is completed, resulting in the past participle form "εναγκαλισαμενος" (enankalísamenos).

Grammar:
εναγκαλισαμενος is a past participle form of the verb "αγκαλιάζω" (ankalíazo), which means "embraced" or "hugged". It is a masculine singular form, indicating that the subject of the sentence is a male who performed the action of embracing or hugging.

Description:
εναγκαλισαμενος is a Greek word that describes the action of embracing or hugging someone. It is often used to express affection, comfort, or support. The word can be used in various contexts, such as in literature, poetry, or everyday conversation.
