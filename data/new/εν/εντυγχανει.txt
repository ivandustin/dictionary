The word "εντυγχανει" (entychanei) is a verb in the Greek language. 

Etymology: The word comes from the ancient Greek verb "ἐντυγχάνω" (entygchano), which means "to meet, encounter, come in contact with". 

Grammar: "Εντυγχανει" is the third person singular present indicative form of the verb. It can be translated to "he/she/it meets, encounters, comes in contact with". 

Description: The word is often used in a figurative sense, to describe a meeting or encounter with someone or something, either in a physical or a metaphorical sense. It can also be used to describe a sudden realization or understanding of something.
