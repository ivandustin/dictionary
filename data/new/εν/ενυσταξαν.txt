Word: ενυσταξαν

Etymology: The word ενυσταξαν comes from the Greek verb ενυστάζω (enystázo), which means "to stay awake all night" or "to keep vigil". It is derived from the words εν (en), meaning "in" or "within", and υστερα (ystera), meaning "night".

Grammar: ενυσταξαν is a verb in the past tense, third person plural form. It is conjugated from the verb ενυστάζω (enystázo), which is a regular verb in the Greek language.

Description: ενυσταξαν describes the action of a group of people staying awake all night or keeping vigil.
