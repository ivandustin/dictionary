The word "ενεγκαι" is a Greek verb in the aorist tense, which means "to bring in" or "to carry in". It is derived from the root word "φέρω" (fero), which means "to carry" or "to bear". In terms of grammar, it is a second person singular aorist active imperative verb, which means it is a command directed towards one person to perform the action of bringing something in.
