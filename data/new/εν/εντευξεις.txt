Word: εντευξεις

Etymology: The word εντευξεις comes from the Greek verb "εντυγχάνω" (entychano) which means "to meet, to encounter". The noun form "εντευξεις" (entychies) means "conversation, dialogue, discussion".

Grammar: εντευξεις is a feminine noun in the plural form. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their gender, number, and case. The plural form of εντευξεις is used to refer to multiple conversations or discussions.

Description: εντευξεις refers to a conversation or discussion between two or more people. It can be used in various contexts, such as in politics, education, or social gatherings. The word implies a certain level of formality and structure, as opposed to a casual chat or small talk. In some cases, εντευξεις can also refer to a formal interview or interrogation.
