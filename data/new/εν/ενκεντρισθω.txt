The word "ενκεντρισθω" is a Greek verb that means "to be centered" or "to be focused." 

Etymology: The word is derived from the Greek roots "εν" meaning "in" or "within," and "κεντρον" meaning "center." 

Grammar: "ενκεντρισθω" is a passive verb in the aorist tense, third person singular form. 

Description: The word is often used in a figurative sense to describe a person or thing that is focused or centered on a particular goal or objective. It can also be used in a literal sense to describe something that is physically centered or balanced.
