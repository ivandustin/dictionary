Word: ενεκεν

Etymology: ενεκεν is a Greek preposition that means "for the sake of" or "on account of". It is derived from the Greek words "εν" (en) meaning "in" or "on" and "εκεινος" (ekeinos) meaning "that one" or "that thing".

Grammar: ενεκεν is a preposition in Greek grammar. It is used to indicate the reason or cause for something. It is often followed by a noun or pronoun in the genitive case.
