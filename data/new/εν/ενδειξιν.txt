Word: ενδειξιν

Etymology: The word ενδειξιν comes from the Greek word "ενδείκνυμι" (endeiknymi), which means "to show" or "to demonstrate".

Grammar: ενδειξιν is a feminine noun in the Greek language. It is in the singular form and its nominative case is ενδειξιν.

Description: ενδειξιν refers to an indication or a sign that shows or demonstrates something. It can also refer to evidence or proof that supports a claim or an argument. In modern Greek, it is commonly used in legal contexts to refer to evidence presented in court.
