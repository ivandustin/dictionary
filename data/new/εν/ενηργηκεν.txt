The word "ενηργηκεν" is a Greek verb that means "has acted" or "has worked." It is the perfect tense form of the verb "ενεργέω" (energeō), which comes from the root word "ενέργεια" (energeia) meaning "energy" or "activity." In grammar, it is a third-person singular verb in the perfect tense, indicating that the action has been completed in the past and has ongoing effects in the present.
