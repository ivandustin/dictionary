Etymology: The word "εατε" comes from the Greek language. It is derived from the Greek verb "εατώ" (eató), which means "to eat".

Grammar: "εατε" is the second person plural imperative form of the verb "εατώ" (eató). In Greek grammar, the imperative mood is used to give commands or make requests.
