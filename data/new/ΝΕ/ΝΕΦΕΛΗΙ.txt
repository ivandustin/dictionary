Etymology: The word "νεφελη" (nephelē) comes from the Greek language. It is derived from the Proto-Indo-European root *nebh- meaning "cloud" or "mist".

Grammar: In Greek, "νεφελη" is a feminine noun. It belongs to the third declension and has the nominative singular form "νεφελη". The genitive singular form is "νεφελης" and the accusative singular form is also "νεφελην".

Description: "Νεφελη" means "cloud" in Greek. It refers to a visible mass of condensed water vapor floating in the atmosphere. Clouds are an important part of the Earth's weather system and play a role in the water cycle. They can vary in shape, size, and altitude, and are often associated with different weather conditions.
