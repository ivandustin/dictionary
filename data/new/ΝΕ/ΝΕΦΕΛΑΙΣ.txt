Etymology: The word "νεφελαις" (nefelais) comes from the Greek word "νεφέλη" (nefelē), meaning "cloud". 

Grammar: "Νεφελαίς" is a feminine noun in the nominative case. It is the plural form of "νεφέλη" (nefelē), which is a singular noun. 

Description: "Νεφελαίς" refers to clouds in Greek. It is used to describe the visible mass of water droplets or ice crystals suspended in the atmosphere. Clouds play an important role in weather patterns and can vary in shape, size, and altitude.
