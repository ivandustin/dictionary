Etymology: The word "νεκρού" (nekróu) comes from the Greek word "νεκρός" (nekrós), which means "dead" or "lifeless".

Grammar: "Νεκρού" (nekróu) is the genitive form of the word "νεκρός" (nekrós). In Greek grammar, the genitive case is used to indicate possession or origin.

Description: "Νεκρού" (nekróu) is an adjective in Greek that means "of the dead" or "belonging to the dead". It is used to describe something that is related to or associated with death or the deceased.
