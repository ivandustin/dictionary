Etymology: The word "νεφελην" (nefelen) comes from the Greek word "νεφέλη" (nefeli), meaning "cloud". 

Grammar: "Νεφελην" is a noun in the accusative case, singular form. 

Description: "Νεφελην" refers to a cloud in Greek. It is used to describe a visible mass of condensed water vapor floating in the atmosphere.
