Etymology: The word "νεωτερας" (neōteras) comes from the Greek word "νέος" (neos), meaning "new" or "young."

Grammar: "Νεωτερας" is a noun in the masculine gender and singular number in Greek. It belongs to the third declension.

Description: "Νεωτερας" is a Greek word that can be translated as "young man" or "youth." It refers to a male person who is in the early stages of adulthood or is considered to be young.
