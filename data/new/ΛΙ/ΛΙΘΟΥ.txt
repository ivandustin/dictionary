Etymology: The word "λιθου" (lithou) comes from the Greek word "λίθος" (lithos), meaning "stone". 

Grammar: "Λιθου" (lithou) is a noun in the genitive case, which is used to indicate possession or relationship. It is the genitive form of the word "λίθος" (lithos).

Description: "Λιθου" (lithou) refers to "of stone" or "belonging to stone". It is used to describe something that is made of stone or has a connection to stone.
