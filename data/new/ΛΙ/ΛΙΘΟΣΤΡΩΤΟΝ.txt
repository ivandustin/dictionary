Etymology: The word "λιθοστρώτον" comes from the Greek roots "λίθος" (lithos) meaning "stone" and "στρώνω" (strono) meaning "to spread or pave". 

Grammar: "Λιθοστρώτον" is a noun in the neuter gender. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their gender, number, and case. 

Description: "Λιθοστρώτον" refers to a paved road or a stone-paved street. It is commonly used to describe ancient roads or streets that were constructed using stones.
