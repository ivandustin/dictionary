Etymology: The word "λιθων" (lithon) comes from the Greek word "λίθος" (lithos), meaning "stone".

Grammar: "Λιθών" (lithon) is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the masculine gender and singular number.

Description: "Λιθών" (lithon) refers to a stone or a rock. It can be used to describe any solid, naturally occurring substance composed of minerals.
