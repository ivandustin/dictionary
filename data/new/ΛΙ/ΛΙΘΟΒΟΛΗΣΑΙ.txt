Etymology: The word "λιθοβολησαί" comes from the Greek roots "λίθος" (lithos) meaning "stone" and "βάλλω" (ballo) meaning "to throw". 

Grammar: "λιθοβολησαί" is a verb in the aorist tense, third person plural form. It is derived from the verb "λιθοβολέω" (lithoboleo), which means "to stone" or "to throw stones at".

Description: "λιθοβολησαί" refers to the act of stoning or throwing stones at someone or something. It is often used in the context of punishment or execution by stoning.
