Etymology: The word "λιπαρα" (lipara) comes from the Greek word "λιπαρός" (liparos), which means "greasy" or "fatty".

Grammar: "Λιπαρα" is a feminine noun in the Greek language. It belongs to the first declension and has the plural form "λιπαρες" (lipares).

Description: "Λιπαρα" is used to describe something that is greasy or fatty. It can refer to food that has a high fat content or to substances that have an oily or slippery texture.
