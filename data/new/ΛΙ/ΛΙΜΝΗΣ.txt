Etymology: The word "λιμνης" (limnēs) comes from the Greek language. It is derived from the Proto-Indo-European root *leb- meaning "to pour, to flow". 

Grammar: In Greek, "λιμνης" (limnēs) is a masculine noun in the nominative case. It belongs to the second declension and has the genitive form "λιμνητος" (limnētos).

Description: "λιμνης" (limnēs) means "lake" in Greek. It refers to a large body of water surrounded by land.
