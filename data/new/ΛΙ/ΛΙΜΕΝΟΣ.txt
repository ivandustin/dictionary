Etymology: The word "λιμένος" (liménos) comes from the Ancient Greek word "λιμήν" (limḗn), meaning "harbor" or "port".

Grammar: "Λιμένος" is a masculine noun in Greek. It belongs to the second declension and its genitive form is "λιμένου" (liménou).

Description: "Λιμένος" refers to a harbor or a port, which is a place along a coast or a river where ships can dock, load, and unload cargo or passengers. It is an important location for trade and transportation.
