The word "ηυκαιρουν" (eukairoun) is a verb in the Greek language. 

Etymology: The word is derived from the Greek root "εὐκαιρία" (eukairia), which means "opportunity" or "favorable time". 

Grammar: The verb "ηυκαιρουν" is in the present tense, third person plural form. It is an active verb, meaning that the subject of the sentence is performing the action. 

Description: The word "ηυκαιρουν" means "they take advantage of the opportunity" or "they make the most of the favorable time". It implies that the subject is seizing a moment of opportunity to achieve a desired outcome.
