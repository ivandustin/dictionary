Word: ηυξανεν

Etymology: ηυξανεν is a Greek word that comes from the verb αυξάνω (auxano), which means "to increase" or "to grow". It is derived from the Proto-Indo-European root *h₂ewg-.

Grammar: ηυξανεν is the third person singular, aorist tense form of the verb αυξάνω (auxano) in Greek. The aorist tense is used to describe a completed action in the past.

Description: ηυξανεν is used in the New Testament of the Bible, specifically in the Gospel of Luke (2:40), to describe the growth and development of Jesus as a child.
