The word "ηυδοκησεν" (eudokesen) is a Greek verb that means "to be pleased" or "to take pleasure in." It is derived from the Greek words "eu" (good) and "dokimazo" (to test, approve). In terms of grammar, "ηυδοκησεν" is in the aorist tense, which indicates a completed action in the past. It is also in the indicative mood, which expresses a statement of fact. Overall, "ηυδοκησεν" is a word that describes a state of satisfaction or enjoyment.
