Etymology: The word "κτίσαντι" comes from the Greek verb "κτίζω" (ktízo), which means "to build" or "to create." It is derived from the noun "κτίσις" (ktísis), meaning "creation" or "foundation."

Grammar: "Κτίσαντι" is the dative singular form of the verb "κτίζω" (ktízo) in the aorist tense. It is used to indicate the indirect object of the action, meaning "to the one who built" or "to the one who created."

Description: "Κτίσαντι" is a Greek word that refers to the act of building or creating. It is often used in a religious or poetic context to describe the act of divine creation or the foundation of something.
