Etymology: The word "κτίσεως" (ktiseos) comes from the Greek verb "κτίζω" (ktizo), which means "to create" or "to build."

Grammar: "Κτίσεως" is a noun in the genitive case, singular number, and masculine gender in Greek grammar.

Description: "Κτίσεως" refers to the act of creating or building. It can also be used to describe the process of establishing or founding something.
