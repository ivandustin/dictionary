Etymology: The word "αριθμητική" (arithmētikē) comes from the Greek word "ἀριθμητική" (arithmētikē), which means "the art of counting" or "the science of numbers". It is derived from the Greek word "ἀριθμός" (arithmos), meaning "number".

Grammar: "Αριθμητική" is a feminine noun in the Greek language. It belongs to the first declension and has the same form in both the singular and plural.

Description: "Αριθμητική" refers to the field of mathematics that deals with numbers, their properties, and their operations. It encompasses various branches such as arithmetic, algebra, geometry, and calculus. In Greek, it can also refer to the act of counting or the ability to perform calculations.
