Etymology: The word "αρνησαμένος" (arnisamenos) comes from the Greek verb "αρνούμαι" (arnoumai), which means "to deny" or "to refuse."

Grammar: "Αρνησαμένος" is the masculine form of the past participle of the verb "αρνούμαι." It is used to describe someone who has denied or refused something in the past.

Description: "Αρνησαμένος" is an adjective that describes someone who has refused or denied something. It can be used to describe someone who has rejected an offer, declined an invitation, or denied an accusation.
