Etymology: The word "αρτών" (artón) comes from the Greek word "άρτος" (ártos), which means "bread". 

Grammar: "Αρτών" is the plural form of the noun "άρτος" (ártos), which means "bread" in Greek. It belongs to the second declension of Greek nouns.

Description: "Αρτών" refers to multiple loaves of bread. It is commonly used in Greek to indicate more than one bread.
