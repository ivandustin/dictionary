Etymology: The word "αρνησασθαι" comes from the Greek verb "αρνέομαι" (arnéomai), which means "to deny" or "to refuse."

Grammar: "αρνησασθαι" is the aorist middle infinitive form of the verb "αρνέομαι" (arnéomai). The aorist tense indicates a past action, and the middle voice suggests that the subject is performing the action on themselves.

Description: "αρνησασθαι" is a Greek verb that means "to deny oneself" or "to refuse." It implies a deliberate act of rejecting or renouncing something.
