Etymology: The word "αρπασαί" (arpasai) comes from the Greek verb "αρπάζω" (arpazo), which means "to snatch" or "to seize". 

Grammar: "Αρπασαί" is the plural form of the noun "αρπασή" (arpasi), which means "a snatch" or "a seizure". It belongs to the first declension in Greek nouns, and it is feminine in gender. 

Description: "Αρπασαί" refers to multiple snatches or seizures. It can be used in various contexts, such as describing the act of forcefully taking something away from someone or capturing something quickly and unexpectedly.
