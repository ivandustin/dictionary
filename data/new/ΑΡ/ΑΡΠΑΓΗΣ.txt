Etymology: The word "αρπαγης" (arpagis) comes from the Greek verb "αρπαζω" (arpazo), which means "to snatch" or "to seize". 

Grammar: "Αρπαγης" is a masculine noun in the nominative case. It belongs to the second declension in Greek grammar. 

Description: "Αρπαγης" refers to a person who snatches or seizes something forcefully. It can also be used metaphorically to describe someone who takes advantage of others or acts in a predatory manner.
