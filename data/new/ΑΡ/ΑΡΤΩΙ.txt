Etymology: The word "αρτοι" (artoi) comes from the Greek word "ἄρτος" (artos), which means "bread". 

Grammar: "Αρτοι" is the plural form of the noun "αρτος" (artos), which means "bread" in Greek. It belongs to the second declension of Greek nouns.

Description: "Αρτοι" refers to multiple loaves of bread. In Greek cuisine, bread is a staple food and is often served with meals or used as a base for various dishes.
