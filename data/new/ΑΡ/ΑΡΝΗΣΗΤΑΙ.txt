Etymology: The word "αρνησηται" comes from the Greek verb "αρνούμαι" (arnoumai), which means "to deny" or "to refuse."

Grammar: "αρνησηται" is the third person singular form of the verb "αρνούμαι" in the present tense. It is in the active voice and indicative mood.

Description: "αρνησηται" means "he/she/it denies" or "he/she/it refuses" in Greek. It is used to express the action of denying or refusing something.
