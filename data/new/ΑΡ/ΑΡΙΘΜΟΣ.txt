Grammar: In Greek grammar, "αριθμός" is a masculine noun of the second declension. It has singular and plural forms, and it can be declined in different cases (nominative, genitive, dative, accusative, and vocative) to indicate its role in a sentence.

In addition to its numerical meaning, "αριθμός" can also refer to a figure, a numeral, or a digit.
