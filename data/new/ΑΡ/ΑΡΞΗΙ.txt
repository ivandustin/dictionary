Etymology: The word "αρχηί" (arxei) comes from the Greek word "αρχή" (archē), which means "beginning" or "origin".

Grammar: "αρχηί" (arxei) is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the singular form and belongs to the feminine gender.

Description: "αρχηί" (arxei) refers to the act of beginning or starting something. It can also mean the principle, source, or origin of something. In a broader sense, it can be used to describe the concept of initiation or the first step in a process.
