Etymology: The word "αρτυσετε" comes from the Greek verb "αρτυω" (artyo), which means "to prepare" or "to make ready".

Grammar: "αρτυσετε" is the second person plural form of the verb "αρτυω" in the future tense. It is used to address a group of people and means "you will prepare" or "you will make ready".

Description: "αρτυσετε" is a verb in the Greek language that expresses an action of preparing or making something ready in the future. It is commonly used in various contexts, such as cooking, organizing events, or getting ready for a task.
