Grammar: "αρξησθε" is the second person plural, aorist passive subjunctive form of the verb "αρξίζω" (arxízo). It is used to express a hypothetical or potential action in the past, where the subject is the receiver of the action.

Description: "αρξησθε" is a verb form that indicates a passive action in the past. It is used when the subject of the sentence is not actively performing the action, but rather receiving it.
