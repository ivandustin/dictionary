Etymology: The word "αρνούνται" comes from the Greek verb "αρνούμαι" (arnoumai), which means "to deny" or "to refuse." It is derived from the ancient Greek word "ἀρνέομαι" (arneomai), which has the same meaning.

Grammar: "Αρνούνται" is the third person plural form of the verb "αρνούμαι" in the present tense. It is used to describe the action of multiple people denying or refusing something.

Description: "Αρνούνται" is a Greek word that expresses the act of denying or refusing. It can be used in various contexts to describe situations where individuals or groups reject or decline something.
