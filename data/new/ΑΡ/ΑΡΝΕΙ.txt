Etymology: The word "αρνει" comes from the Greek verb "αρνούμαι" (arnoumai), which means "to deny" or "to refuse." 

Grammar: "Αρνει" is the third person singular form of the verb "αρνούμαι" in the present tense. It is used to describe an action of denial or refusal performed by a third person.

Description: "Αρνει" is a Greek word that expresses the act of denying or refusing something. It can be used in various contexts to indicate the rejection of an offer, request, or statement.
