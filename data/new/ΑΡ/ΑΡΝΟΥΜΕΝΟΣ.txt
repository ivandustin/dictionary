Etymology: The word "αρνούμενος" (arnoumenos) comes from the Greek verb "αρνούμαι" (arnoumai), which means "to deny" or "to refuse."

Grammar: "Αρνούμενος" is a masculine noun in the nominative case. It is the present participle form of the verb "αρνούμαι" (arnoumai), which is in the present tense, active voice, and indicative mood.

Description: "Αρνούμενος" is a Greek word that translates to "denying" or "refusing" in English. It is used to describe someone who is actively denying or refusing something.
