Etymology: The word "αρνησεται" (arnisetai) comes from the Greek verb "αρνούμαι" (arnoumai), which means "to deny" or "to refuse."

Grammar: "Αρνησεται" is the third person singular form of the present tense of the verb "αρνούμαι" (arnoumai). It is used to describe an action of denial or refusal that is being performed by a third person.

Description: "Αρνησεται" is a Greek word that means "he/she/it denies" or "he/she/it refuses." It is commonly used to express the act of rejecting or declining something.
