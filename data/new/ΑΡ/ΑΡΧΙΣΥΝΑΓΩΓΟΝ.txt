Etymology: The word "αρχισυναγωγον" comes from the Greek roots "αρχι-" meaning "chief" or "leader" and "συναγωγή" meaning "synagogue". 

Grammar: "αρχισυναγωγον" is a noun in the singular form. It is a compound word consisting of the prefix "αρχι-" and the noun "συναγωγή". 

Description: "αρχισυναγωγον" refers to the leader or chief of a synagogue in Greek. It is used to describe the person who has the highest authority and responsibility in a synagogue.
