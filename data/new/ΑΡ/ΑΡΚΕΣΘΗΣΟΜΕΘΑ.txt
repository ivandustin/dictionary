Grammar: "αρκεσθησομεθα" is a first-person plural future passive reflexive verb form in Greek. It is used when the subject of the sentence is both the doer and the receiver of the action. In this case, it means "we will be satisfied" or "we will content ourselves".

Description: "αρκεσθησομεθα" expresses the idea of being content or satisfied in the future. It implies that the subject will find fulfillment or satisfaction in a particular situation or with a certain outcome.
