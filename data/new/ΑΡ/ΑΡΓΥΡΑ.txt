Etymology: The word "αργυρα" (argyra) comes from the Greek word "ἄργυρος" (argyros), which means "silver". 

Grammar: In Greek, "αργυρα" is a feminine noun in the singular form. 

Description: "Αργυρα" refers to the metal silver. It is a lustrous, white, and precious metal that has been used for various purposes throughout history, including currency, jewelry, and decorative items.
