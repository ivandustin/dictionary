Etymology: The word "αρχαί" (archai) comes from the Greek word "αρχαίος" (archaios), which means "ancient" or "old".

Grammar: "Αρχαί" is a plural noun in the Greek language. It is the plural form of the word "αρχή" (archi), which means "beginning" or "origin".

Description: "Αρχαί" refers to ancient times or ancient things. It can be used to describe historical periods, artifacts, or concepts that are related to the past.
