The word "εζητειτε" is a conjugated form of the Greek verb "ζητώ" (zito), which means "to seek" or "to search for". It is in the second person plural, past continuous tense, indicating that "you were seeking" or "you were searching for" something. In terms of grammar, it is a regular verb in the past continuous tense, formed by adding the suffix "-ετε" to the stem "ζητ-".
