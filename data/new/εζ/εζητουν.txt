Word: εζητούν

Etymology: The word εζητούν is a conjugated form of the Greek verb ζητώ (zito), which means "to seek" or "to search for". The prefix ε- (e-) indicates the present tense and the third person plural.

Grammar: εζητούν is a present tense verb in the third person plural form. In Greek, verbs change their endings to indicate the tense, mood, and person. The verb ζητώ belongs to the first conjugation group, which means that it follows a regular pattern of conjugation.

Description: εζητούν is a verb that expresses the action of seeking or searching for something. It can be used in various contexts, such as searching for information, looking for a lost item, or seeking a solution to a problem. The present tense form indicates that the action is currently taking place.
