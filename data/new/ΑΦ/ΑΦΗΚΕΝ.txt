Etymology: The word "αφηκεν" comes from the Greek verb "αφίημι" (aphíēmi), which means "to let go" or "to leave behind."

Grammar: "αφηκεν" is the third person singular, aorist active indicative form of the verb "αφίημι" (aphíēmi). In Greek grammar, the aorist tense is used to express a past action that is viewed as a whole, without specifying its duration or repetition.

Description: "αφηκεν" is used to describe an action of leaving or letting go that has already taken place in the past. It indicates that someone or something has been left behind or abandoned.
