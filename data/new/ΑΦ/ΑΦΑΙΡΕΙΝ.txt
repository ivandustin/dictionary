Etymology: The word "αφαίρειν" (aphairein) comes from the Greek verb "αφαιρέω" (aphaireo), which means "to take away" or "to remove".

Grammar: "Αφαίρειν" (aphairein) is an infinitive verb form in Greek. It belongs to the first conjugation class and is used to express the action of taking away or removing something.
