Etymology: The word "αφορίζει" comes from the Greek verb "αφορίζω" (aphorízo), which means "to separate" or "to distinguish."

Grammar: "Αφορίζει" is the third person singular form of the verb "αφορίζω" in the present tense. It is used to describe an action performed by a singular subject.

Description: "Αφορίζει" is a Greek word that means "he/she/it separates" or "he/she/it distinguishes." It is commonly used to indicate the act of separating or distinguishing something from others.
