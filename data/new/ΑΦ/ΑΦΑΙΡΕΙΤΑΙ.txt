Etymology: The word "αφαίρειται" comes from the Greek verb "αφαιρώ" (aphairo), which means "to remove" or "to take away".

Grammar: "Αφαίρειται" is the third person singular form of the verb "αφαιρώ" in the present tense. It is in the passive voice, indicating that the action is being done to the subject.

Description: "Αφαίρειται" is a Greek word that means "it is removed" or "it is taken away". It is used to describe the action of something being taken away or removed from a certain place or situation.
