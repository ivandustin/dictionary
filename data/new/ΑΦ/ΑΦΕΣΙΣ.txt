Etymology: The word "αφεσις" (aphesis) comes from the Greek verb "αφιημι" (aphiemi), which means "to let go" or "to release". 

Grammar: "Αφεσις" is a feminine noun in Greek. It is in the nominative case and singular form. 

Description: "Αφεσις" refers to the act of letting go, releasing, or forgiving. It can also mean a pardon or remission of a debt or punishment. In a broader sense, it can be used to describe the act of setting free or liberation.
