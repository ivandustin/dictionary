Etymology: The word "αφησει" comes from the Greek verb "αφίνω" (afíno), which means "to leave" or "to let go". 

Grammar: "αφησει" is the third person singular future tense form of the verb "αφίνω" (afíno). It is used to express an action that will happen in the future and is performed by a third person.

Description: "αφησει" is a Greek word that means "he/she/it will leave" or "he/she/it will let go". It is commonly used in everyday speech and writing to talk about future actions or events.
