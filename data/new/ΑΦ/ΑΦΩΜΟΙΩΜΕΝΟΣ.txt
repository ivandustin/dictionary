Grammar: "Αφωμοιωμένος" is a masculine singular adjective in Greek. It agrees in gender, number, and case with the noun it modifies.

Description: The word "αφωμοιωμένος" describes something or someone that has been assimilated or made similar to something else. It implies that the subject has undergone a process of becoming similar or adopting the characteristics of another entity.
