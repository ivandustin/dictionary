Etymology: The word "αφορώντες" comes from the Greek verb "αφορώ" (aphoró), which means "to concern" or "to pertain to." The suffix "-ντες" (-ntes) is added to the verb stem to form the present participle, indicating that the subject is performing the action of the verb.

Grammar: "Αφορώντες" is the present participle form of the verb "αφορώ" (aphoró) in Greek. It is used to describe a continuous action or state that is happening at the present time. In terms of grammar, it functions as an adjective or a noun.

Description: "Αφορώντες" can be translated as "concerning" or "pertaining to" in English. It is often used to introduce a topic or subject that is being discussed or considered. It indicates that the following information or discussion is related to the subject mentioned.
