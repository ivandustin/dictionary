Etymology: The word "αφειδιαί" comes from the Greek word "αφειδής" (afeidís), which means "careless" or "negligent".

Grammar: "αφειδιαί" is a feminine noun in the nominative plural form. It belongs to the first declension in Greek grammar.

Description: "αφειδιαί" refers to careless or negligent actions or behavior. It describes a lack of attention or concern for the consequences of one's actions.
