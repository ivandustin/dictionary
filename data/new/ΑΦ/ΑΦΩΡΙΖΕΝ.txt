Etymology: The word "αφωριζεν" comes from the Greek verb "αφορίζω" (aphorízo), which means "to separate" or "to distinguish."

Grammar: "αφωριζεν" is the third person singular form of the verb "αφορίζω" in the present tense. It is in the active voice and indicative mood.

Description: "αφωριζεν" is used to describe an action of separating or distinguishing something or someone from others. It can also imply setting boundaries or making distinctions.
