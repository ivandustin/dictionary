Etymology: The word "αφοριεί" comes from the Greek verb "αφορώ" (aphoró), which means "to separate" or "to distinguish". 

Grammar: "Αφοριεί" is the third person singular form of the verb "αφορώ" in the present tense. It is used to describe the action of separating or distinguishing something.

Description: "Αφοριεί" is a Greek verb that is often used to express the act of setting apart or differentiating something from others. It can be used in various contexts, such as distinguishing between different options, categorizing items, or separating one thing from another.
