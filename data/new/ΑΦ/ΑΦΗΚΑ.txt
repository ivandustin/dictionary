Etymology: The word "αφηκα" (aphēka) comes from the Greek verb "αφίημι" (aphiēmi), which means "to let go" or "to release."

Grammar: "Αφηκα" is the first person singular form of the aorist tense in Greek. It is used to express an action that happened in the past and is completed.
