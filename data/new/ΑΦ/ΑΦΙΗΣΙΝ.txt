Etymology: The word "αφιήσιν" comes from the Greek verb "αφίημι" (aphiemi), which means "to let go" or "to release."

Grammar: "αφιήσιν" is a noun in the accusative case, singular number, and feminine gender. It is derived from the verb "αφίημι" and is used to refer to the act of letting go or releasing.
