Etymology: The word "αφεσίν" comes from the Greek word "αφίημι" (aphíēmi), which means "to let go" or "to release."

Grammar: "Αφεσίν" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the singular form and belongs to the neuter gender.

Description: "Αφεσίν" refers to forgiveness or pardon in Greek. It is the act of letting go of resentment, anger, or the desire for revenge towards someone who has wronged you. It is a concept that is often associated with compassion, understanding, and reconciliation.
