The word "στοματι" (stomati) is a Greek word that means "in the mouth" or "oral". It is derived from the Greek word "στόμα" (stoma), which means "mouth". In terms of grammar, "στοματι" is a preposition that is used to indicate the location of something in relation to the mouth. It can also be used as an adverb to describe an action that is done orally. Overall, "στοματι" is a useful word in Greek for describing anything related to the mouth or oral activities.
