The word "στρατευεται" (stratueetai) is a verb in the present tense, third person singular form, of the Greek language. 

Etymology: The word comes from the Greek root "στρατεία" (strateia), which means "military expedition" or "campaign". 

Grammar: "στρατευεται" is a conjugated form of the verb "στρατεύομαι" (strateuomai), which means "to go on a military campaign" or "to serve in the army". It is in the present tense, indicating that the action is happening currently. The third person singular form indicates that the subject of the sentence is a he/she/it.

Description: "στρατευεται" is a verb that describes the action of going on a military campaign or serving in the army. It can be used in a variety of contexts, such as describing a historical battle or discussing current military operations.
