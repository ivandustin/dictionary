The word στρατιωταις (stratiotais) is a plural form of the Greek noun στρατιώτης (stratiotes), which means "soldier" or "warrior". The word is derived from the noun στρατός (stratos), meaning "army", and the verb ἀγω (ago), meaning "to lead". Therefore, στρατιώτης literally means "one who is led to war".
