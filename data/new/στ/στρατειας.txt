Word: στρατειας

Etymology:
The word στρατειας comes from the Greek word στρατεία (strateia), which means "military expedition" or "campaign". It is derived from the word στρατός (stratos), which means "army".

Grammar:
στρατειας is a noun in the genitive case, which is used to show possession or relationship. In this case, it means "of the military expedition" or "of the campaign".

Description:
στρατειας refers to a military expedition or campaign. It can be used to describe a specific military operation or a more general military campaign. The word is often used in historical contexts to describe ancient battles or wars.
