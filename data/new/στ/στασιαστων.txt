The word "στασιαστων" (stasiastōn) is a Greek noun that means "rebel" or "insurgent". It is derived from the verb "στασιάζω" (stasiázō), which means "to rebel" or "to cause an uprising". 

In terms of grammar, "στασιαστων" is a masculine noun in the genitive plural form. It is often used in historical or political contexts to refer to individuals or groups who rebel against a government or authority. 

Overall, "στασιαστων" is a word that carries a strong connotation of resistance and opposition.
