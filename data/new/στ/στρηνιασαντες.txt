Word: στρηνιασαντες

Etymology: The word στρηνιασαντες is derived from the Greek verb στρηνιάω (strēniáō), which means "to revel, to carouse, to indulge in excess." The suffix -ασαντες (-asantes) is a past participle ending, indicating that the word refers to a group of people who have engaged in the act of revelry.

Grammar: στρηνιασαντες is a plural noun in the nominative case. It is the past participle form of the verb στρηνιάω, which means "to revel." The word is formed by adding the suffix -ασαντες to the stem of the verb.

Description: στρηνιασαντες refers to a group of people who have engaged in revelry or excessive indulgence. The word has a negative connotation, suggesting that the behavior of these individuals is inappropriate or immoral.
