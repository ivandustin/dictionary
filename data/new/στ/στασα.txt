The word "στασα" (stasa) is a Greek noun that means "stop" or "halt". It is derived from the verb "σταματώ" (stamato), which means "to stop" or "to stand still". 

In terms of grammar, "στασα" is a feminine noun in the singular form. It can be used as a subject or object in a sentence, and it can be modified by adjectives or other nouns.

A possible sentence using "στασα" could be: "Η στάσα του λεωφορείου ήταν στο κέντρο της πόλης." (I stasa tou leoforeiou itan sto kentro tis polis.) which means "The bus stop was in the center of the city."
