Word: στρατοπεδων

Etymology:
The word στρατοπεδων is derived from two Greek words: στρατός (stratos) meaning "army" and πεδάων (pedaon) meaning "camping". Therefore, the word στρατοπεδων literally means "army camp".

Grammar:
στρατοπεδων is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the singular form and belongs to the second declension. The genitive form of the word is στρατοπέδου (stratopedou).

Description:
στρατοπεδων refers to a military camp or a base where soldiers are stationed. It can also refer to a temporary encampment set up by an army during a military campaign. The word is commonly used in the context of ancient Greek history and warfare.
