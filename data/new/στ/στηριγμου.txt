The word "στηριγμός" (stirigmos) is a Greek noun that means "support" or "prop". It comes from the verb "στηρίζω" (stirizo), which means "to support" or "to prop up". In terms of grammar, "στηριγμός" is a masculine noun in the nominative case. It can also be declined in other cases, such as the genitive "στηριγμού" (stirigmu) or the accusative "στηριγμό" (stirigmo).
