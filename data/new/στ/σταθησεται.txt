Word: σταθησεται

Etymology:
The word σταθησεται is a Greek verb in the future tense, third person singular form of the verb σταθήσομαι (stathisomai), which means "to stand". The root of the word is στα- (sta-), which means "to stand" or "to be firm".

Grammar:
σταθησεται is a future tense verb in the third person singular form. In Greek, verbs have different forms depending on the tense, aspect, mood, voice, and person. The future tense indicates an action that will happen in the future.

Description:
σταθησεται is a verb that means "he/she/it will stand" in English. It is often used in the context of standing up or remaining in a standing position. The word can also be used metaphorically to mean "to remain firm" or "to endure".
