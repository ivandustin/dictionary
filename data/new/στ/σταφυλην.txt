Word: σταφυλην

Etymology:
The word σταφυλην (staphylēn) is derived from the Greek word σταφυλή (staphylē), which means "grape". The suffix -ην (-ēn) is a common suffix used to form nouns in Greek.

Grammar:
σταφυλην is a singular noun in the accusative case in Greek. It is often used to refer to a single grape.

Description:
σταφυλην is a term used in Greek to refer to a single grape. It is commonly used in the context of winemaking and grape cultivation. The word can also be used metaphorically to refer to something small and insignificant.
