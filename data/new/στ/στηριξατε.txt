Word: στηριξατε

Etymology: στηρίζω (stirízo) + -ατε (-ate) (suffix indicating second person plural past tense)

Grammar: στηριξατε is the second person plural past tense form of the verb στηρίζω (stirízo), which means "to support" or "to prop up". It is a regular verb in the Greek language and belongs to the first conjugation.

Description: στηριξατε is a verb form that indicates an action that was completed in the past by a group of people addressed in the second person plural. It can be translated as "you all supported" or "you all propped up". This verb form is commonly used in everyday speech and writing in Greek.
