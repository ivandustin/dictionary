Etymology: The word "υστερηματα" comes from the Greek word "υστερημα" (hystērēma), which means "deficiency" or "lack".

Grammar: "Υστερηματα" is a noun in the plural form in Greek. It belongs to the neuter gender and is declined according to the second declension.

Description: "Υστερηματα" refers to deficiencies or lacks in something. It can be used to describe shortcomings, inadequacies, or missing elements in various contexts.
