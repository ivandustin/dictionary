Etymology: The word "υστερησα" (υστερησα) comes from the Greek verb "υστερώ" (υστερώ), which means "to be late" or "to delay".

Grammar: "υστερησα" is the first person singular past tense form of the verb "υστερώ" in Greek. It indicates that the speaker (or the subject) was late or delayed in the past.

Description: "υστερησα" is a Greek word that specifically refers to the action of being late or delayed in the past. It is commonly used in everyday conversations to express that someone was not on time or did not meet a deadline.
