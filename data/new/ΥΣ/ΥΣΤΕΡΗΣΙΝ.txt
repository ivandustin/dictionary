Etymology: The word "υστερησιν" (ustērisin) comes from the Greek verb "υστερέω" (ustereō), which means "to be late" or "to be behind." It is derived from the adjective "υστερος" (usteros), meaning "later" or "behind."

Grammar: "Υστερησιν" is a noun in the accusative case, singular number, and feminine gender. It is the accusative form of the noun "υστερησις" (usterēsis), which means "lateness" or "delay."
