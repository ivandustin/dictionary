Grammar: "Υστερείσθαι" is a verb in the Greek language. It is in the present tense, passive voice, and infinitive mood. The passive voice indicates that the subject of the verb is being acted upon. The infinitive mood suggests that the action is not being performed by a specific subject.

Description: "Υστερείσθαι" is a Greek verb that conveys the meaning of being behind or lagging behind. It implies a delay or a failure to keep up with something or someone.
