Etymology: The word "ημερα" (imera) comes from the Ancient Greek word "ἡμέρα" (hēméra), which means "day".

Grammar: In Greek, "ημερα" is a feminine noun. It is in the nominative case and singular form.

Description: "Ημερα" means "day" in Greek. It is used to refer to the period of time between sunrise and sunset, or a 24-hour period. It can also be used metaphorically to refer to a specific point in time or a particular day of the week.
