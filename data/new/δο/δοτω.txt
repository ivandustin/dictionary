The word "δοτω" (dotō) is a verb in the Greek language. It is the third person singular present active imperative form of the verb "δίδωμι" (dídōmi), which means "to give". The etymology of the word can be traced back to the Proto-Indo-European root *deh₃-, which means "to give". In terms of grammar, "δοτω" is a command or request for someone to give something. It is often used in the context of giving a gift or offering assistance.
