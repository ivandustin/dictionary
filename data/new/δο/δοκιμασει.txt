The word "δοκιμασει" (dokimasei) is a verb in the Greek language. Its etymology can be traced back to the ancient Greek word "δοκιμάζω" (dokimazo), which means "to test" or "to examine." 

In terms of grammar, "δοκιμασει" is in the present tense and active voice. It is the third person singular form of the verb "δοκιμάζω." 

The word "δοκιμασει" can be translated to English as "he/she/it tests" or "he/she/it examines." It is often used in contexts related to evaluating or assessing something or someone.
