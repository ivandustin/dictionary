The word "δουλειας" (douleias) is a Greek noun that means "work" or "job". It is derived from the Greek word "δουλος" (doulos), which means "slave" or "servant". In terms of grammar, "δουλειας" is in the genitive case, indicating possession or relationship to another noun or pronoun. It can be used to describe a specific job or task, or to refer to work in general.
