The word "δουλευει" (doulevei) is a verb in the Greek language. It is derived from the root word "δουλεύω" (doulevo), which means "to work" or "to serve". 

In terms of grammar, "δουλευει" is in the present tense and third person singular form. It is often used to describe someone who is currently working or serving in some capacity. 

Overall, "δουλευει" is a common and versatile verb in Greek, used in a variety of contexts to describe work or service.
