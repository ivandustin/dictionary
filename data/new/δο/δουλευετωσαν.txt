The word "δουλευετωσαν" is a verb in the Greek language. It is the third person plural form of the verb "δουλεύω" (doulevo), which means "to work" or "to serve". The word is derived from the noun "δοῦλος" (doulos), which means "slave" or "servant". In terms of grammar, "δουλευετωσαν" is in the present tense and active voice.
