Word: δοξάζοντες

Etymology: δοξάζω (doxazo) is a Greek verb meaning "to glorify" or "to praise". The suffix -οντες (-ontes) is a present participle ending, indicating that the word refers to people who are currently glorifying or praising.

The participle form indicates that the action of glorifying or praising is ongoing and simultaneous with another action.

Description: δοξάζοντες is a Greek word that refers to people who are currently glorifying or praising.
