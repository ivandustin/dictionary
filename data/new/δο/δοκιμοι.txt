The word "δοκιμοι" (dokimoi) is a Greek adjective that means "approved" or "tested." It comes from the Greek verb "δοκιμάζω" (dokimazo), which means "to test" or "to prove." In terms of grammar, "δοκιμοι" is a masculine plural form of the adjective, used to describe a group of people or things that have been tested and approved. It can also be translated as "tried and true" or "proven."
