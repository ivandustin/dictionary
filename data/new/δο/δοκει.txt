Word: δοκει

Etymology:
The word δοκει (dokei) is a Greek verb that comes from the root word δοκεω (dokeo), which means "to seem" or "to appear." It is derived from the Proto-Indo-European root *dek-, which means "to take, accept."

Description:
The verb δοκει is often used to express an opinion or a belief about something.
