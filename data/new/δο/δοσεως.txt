The word "δοσεως" (doseos) is a Greek noun that means "giving" or "dosing". It is derived from the verb "δίδωμι" (didomi), which means "to give". In terms of grammar, "δοσεως" is a genitive singular noun, indicating possession or relationship to another noun. 

Example sentence: Η δοσεως του φαρμακευτικου προϊοντος πρεπει να ειναι ακριβης. (The dosing of the pharmaceutical product should be precise.)
