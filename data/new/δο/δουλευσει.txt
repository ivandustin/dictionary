Word: δουλευσει

Etymology: δουλεύω (doulevo) is a Greek verb that means "to work" or "to serve". The word δουλευσει (douleusei) is the third person singular future active indicative form of this verb.

Grammar: δουλευσει is a future tense verb in Greek. It is conjugated from the root verb δουλεύω (doulevo) and the future tense suffix -σει (-sei). The verb is in the active voice and indicative mood.

Description: δουλευσει is a Greek verb that means "he/she/it will work" or "he/she/it will serve". It is commonly used in everyday conversation and writing to express future actions related to work or service. The verb can be used in a variety of contexts, such as discussing future job plans or describing someone's work ethic.
