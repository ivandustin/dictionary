Word: δουλευετε

Etymology: δουλεύω (doulevo) is a Greek verb meaning "to work" or "to serve". The word δουλευετε (douleuete) is the present tense, second person plural form of this verb.

Grammar: δουλευετε is a present tense verb in the second person plural form. It is used to address a group of people and means "you work" or "you are working".

Description: δουλευετε is a common verb in modern Greek and is used in everyday conversation. It is often used in the context of work or employment, but can also be used more broadly to refer to any kind of activity or effort.
