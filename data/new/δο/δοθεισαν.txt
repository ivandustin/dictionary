Word: δοθεισαν

Etymology:
δοθεισαν is a Greek word that comes from the verb δίδωμι (didomi), which means "to give". It is in the passive voice and past tense form.

Grammar:
δοθεισαν is a feminine noun in the nominative case, singular number. It is the past participle of the verb δίδωμι (didomi), which means "given".

Description:
δοθεισαν is used to describe something that has been given or granted. It is often used in legal or official contexts to indicate that a particular right or privilege has been bestowed upon someone. In Greek mythology, it is also used to describe gifts given by the gods to mortals.
