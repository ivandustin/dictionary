The word δοκω (dokō) is a Greek verb that means "to seem" or "to appear". It comes from the Greek root word δοκεῖν (dokein), which means "to think" or "to suppose". In terms of grammar, δοκω is a present tense verb in the first person singular form. It can also be conjugated in other tenses and persons, such as δοκεῖς (dokeis) for second person singular or δοκοῦμεν (dokoumen) for first person plural. Overall, δοκω is a versatile verb that is commonly used in everyday conversation and writing.
