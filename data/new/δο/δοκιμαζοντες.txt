The word δοκιμαζοντες (dokimazontes) is a Greek verb in the present participle form. It comes from the root word δοκιμάζω (dokimazo), which means "to test" or "to examine." The -οντες (-ontes) ending indicates that the action is ongoing or continuous.

In terms of grammar, δοκιμαζοντες is a present participle, which means it is a verb form that functions as an adjective. It describes an ongoing action or state of being. In this case, it describes people who are continuously testing or examining something.

Overall, δοκιμαζοντες is a word that describes a continuous process of testing or examining. It can be used in a variety of contexts, such as scientific experiments, product testing, or even personal introspection.
