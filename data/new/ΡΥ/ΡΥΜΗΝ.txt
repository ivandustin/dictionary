Etymology: The word "ρυμην" (rýmēn) comes from the Greek verb "ρυμίζω" (rymízō), which means "to arrange" or "to put in order."

Grammar: "Ρυμην" is a noun in the singular form. It belongs to the third declension in Greek grammar.

Description: "Ρυμην" refers to a row or line of objects that are arranged in a straight or orderly manner. It can also be used metaphorically to describe a series or sequence of events or actions.
