Etymology: The word "ρυπού" (rhy-poú) comes from the Greek verb "ρυπώ" (rhy-pó), which means "to pollute" or "to contaminate."

Grammar: "Ρυπού" is a noun in the singular form. It is in the genitive case, indicating possession or origin.

Description: "Ρυπού" refers to something that is polluted or contaminated. It can be used to describe various types of pollution, such as air pollution, water pollution, or soil pollution.
