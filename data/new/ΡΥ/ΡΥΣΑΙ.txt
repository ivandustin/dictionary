Etymology: The word "ρυσαί" comes from the Greek verb "ρύομαι" (ryomai), which means "to flow" or "to rush". 

Grammar: "Ρυσαί" is the plural form of the noun "ρύση" (rysi), which means "stream" or "current" in Greek. It belongs to the first declension and is feminine in gender. 

Description: "Ρυσαί" refers to multiple streams or currents. It can be used to describe the flow of water, air, or any other substance that moves in a continuous and steady manner.
