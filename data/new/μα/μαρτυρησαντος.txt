Word: μαρτυρησαντος

Grammar: μαρτυρησαντος is the genitive singular form of the participle μαρτυρήσας (marturesas), which means "having testified" or "having borne witness". In Greek, the participle can function as an adjective, noun, or verb, depending on the context.

Description: μαρτυρησαντος is a word that describes someone or something that has testified or borne witness.
