μαχαιρης (machairēs) is a Greek noun that means "knife" or "dagger". It is derived from the Greek word "μάχαιρα" (máchaira), which also means "knife" or "dagger". In terms of grammar, μαχαιρης is a masculine noun in the nominative singular form. It can also be declined in other cases, such as the genitive μαχαιρη (machairē) or the accusative μαχαιραν (machairan).
