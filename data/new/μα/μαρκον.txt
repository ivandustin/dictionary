The word "μαρκον" (markon) is a Greek noun that means "a mark" or "a sign." It is derived from the Greek verb "μαρκάω" (markao), which means "to mark" or "to stamp." In terms of grammar, "μαρκον" is a neuter noun in the singular form. It can be used to describe a physical mark or symbol, as well as a metaphorical one, such as a sign of something significant or noteworthy.
