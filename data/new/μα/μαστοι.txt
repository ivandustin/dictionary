Word: μαστοί

Etymology: The word μαστοί (mastoi) is derived from the Greek word μαστός (mastós), which means "breast" or "mammary gland". 

Grammar: μαστοί is a plural noun in Greek. It is the nominative plural form of μαστός. 

Description: μαστοί refers to the breasts or mammary glands of a female mammal. In ancient Greek mythology, the goddess Hera was often depicted with large and prominent μαστοί. The word is still used in modern Greek to refer to the breasts of a woman.
