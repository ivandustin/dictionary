Word: μαργαριτων

Grammar: μαργαριτων is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the genitive case and is the plural form of μαργαριτης (margarítēs). 

Description: μαργαριτων refers to pearls, which are small, round, shiny objects that are formed inside the shells of certain mollusks.
