The word "μαχομενοις" (machomenois) is a form of the Greek verb "μάχομαι" (machomai), which means "to fight" or "to battle". It is in the dative plural form, indicating that it is the indirect object of the sentence. 

In terms of grammar, "μαχομενοις" is a present participle, which means it is a verb form that functions as an adjective. It describes the subject of the sentence as being in the act of fighting or battling. 

Overall, "μαχομενοις" can be translated as "to/for the ones who are fighting/battling".
