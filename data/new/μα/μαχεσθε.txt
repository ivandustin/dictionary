In terms of grammar, "μαχεσθε" is a second person plural verb in the present tense. It is formed by adding the suffix "-εσθε" to the root word "μάχη". 

Overall, "μαχεσθε" is a strong and commanding word that is often used in contexts related to warfare or conflict.
