μακαρίου (makariou) is an adjective in Greek that means "blessed" or "happy". It comes from the Greek word μακάριος (makarios), which also means "blessed" or "happy". In Greek mythology, the term was used to describe the blessed state of the gods and heroes in the afterlife. In grammar, μακαρίου is in the genitive case, which is used to show possession or relationship.
