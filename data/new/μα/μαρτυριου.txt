Word: μαρτυριου

Etymology: The word μαρτυριου comes from the Greek word μαρτυρία (marturia), which means testimony or witness. The suffix -ου (-ou) is a genitive ending, indicating possession or relationship.

Grammar: μαρτυριου is a genitive singular form of the noun μαρτυρία (marturia). In Greek grammar, the genitive case is used to indicate possession, origin, or relationship.

Description: μαρτυριου is a Greek word that means "of testimony" or "of witness". It is often used in religious contexts, referring to the testimony of a witness or martyr. The word can also be used more broadly to refer to any kind of testimony or evidence.
