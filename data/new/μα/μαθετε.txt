The word "μαθετε" (mathete) is a verb in Greek, which means "learn" or "study". 

Etymology: The word comes from the Greek root "μαθ-" (math-), which means "to learn" or "to know". 

Grammar: "μαθετε" is the second person plural imperative form of the verb "μανθάνω" (manthano), which means "to learn". In other words, it is a command telling a group of people to learn or study something. 

Description: The word "μαθετε" is commonly used in educational contexts, such as in classrooms or textbooks. It is often used to encourage students to actively engage in the learning process and to take responsibility for their own education.
