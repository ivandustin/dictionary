Etymology: The word "των" (ton) is a Greek preposition that means "of" or "belonging to." It is derived from the ancient Greek word "τοῦ" (tou), which has the same meaning.

Grammar: "Των" (ton) is the genitive form of the Greek definite article "ο" (o), which means "the." In Greek, the definite article changes its form depending on the gender, number, and case of the noun it modifies. The genitive case is used to indicate possession or origin.
