Word: κωλυόντων

Etymology: κωλύω (kōlúō) + -οντ- (-ont-), present active participle suffix

Grammar: Present active participle, masculine plural genitive case

Description: κωλυόντων is a Greek word that means "hindering" or "preventing". It is derived from the verb κωλύω (kōlúō), which means "to hinder" or "to prevent". The present active participle suffix -οντ- (-ont-) indicates that the action is ongoing and being performed by the subject. In this case, the subject is masculine plural, indicating that there are multiple individuals hindering or preventing something.
