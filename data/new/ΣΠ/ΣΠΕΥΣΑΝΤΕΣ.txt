Etymology: The word "σπευσάντες" comes from the Greek verb "σπεύδω" (speúdo), which means "to hurry" or "to hasten". The suffix "-αντες" is a participle ending, indicating a group of people who have performed the action of the verb.

Grammar: "Σπευσάντες" is the plural form of the verb "σπεύδω" in the aorist tense. It is used to describe a group of people who have hurried or hastened.

Description: "Σπευσάντες" is a Greek word that can be translated as "those who have hurried" or "those who have hastened". It is often used to refer to a group of people who have acted quickly or with urgency.
