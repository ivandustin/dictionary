Etymology: The word "σπερματών" (spermatón) comes from the Greek word "σπέρμα" (sperma), meaning "seed" or "sperm."

Grammar: "Σπερματών" is a noun in the plural form. It belongs to the second declension in Greek grammar.

Description: "Σπερματών" refers to the plural form of the word "σπέρμα" (sperma), which means "seed" or "sperm." It is commonly used in biology and reproductive sciences to describe the reproductive cells of plants or animals.
