Etymology: The word "σποδών" (spodón) comes from the Greek verb "σποδίζω" (spodízo), which means "to scatter ashes" or "to reduce to ashes". 

Grammar: "Σποδών" is a noun in the masculine gender and in the nominative case. It is the singular form of the word. 

Description: "Σποδών" refers to ashes or the remains of something that has been burned. It can be used in a literal sense to describe the residue left after a fire, or in a metaphorical sense to represent the remnants or traces of something that has been destroyed or lost.
