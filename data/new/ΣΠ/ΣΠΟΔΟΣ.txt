Etymology: The word "σποδός" (spodos) comes from the Ancient Greek word "σποδός" (spodos), meaning "ashes" or "dust".

Grammar: In Greek, "σποδός" is a masculine noun in the singular form.

Description: "Σποδός" refers to the fine particles that remain after something has been burned or reduced to ashes. It can also be used metaphorically to describe something that is completely destroyed or reduced to nothing.
