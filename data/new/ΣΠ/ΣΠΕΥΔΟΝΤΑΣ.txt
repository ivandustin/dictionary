Grammar: "Σπευδοντας" is the present participle form of the verb "σπεύδω" (speudo) in Greek. It is used to describe an ongoing action of "hurrying" or "hastening". In Greek grammar, the present participle can function as an adjective or as part of a verb phrase.

Description: "Σπευδοντας" is a word in the Greek language that describes someone or something that is currently hurrying or hastening. It can be used to describe a person who is in a rush or a situation that requires quick action.
