Etymology: The word "σπουδή" (spoudi) comes from the Ancient Greek word "σπουδή" (spoudē), which means "diligence" or "zeal."

Grammar: In Greek, "σπουδή" is a feminine noun. It is declined as follows:

- Singular: η σπουδή (i spoudi) 
- Plural: οι σπουδές (i spoudes)

Description: "Σπουδή" is a Greek word that refers to the concept of diligence, zeal, or earnestness. It is often used to describe a serious and focused approach to learning, studying, or pursuing a goal. It can also be used to express a sense of urgency or importance in one's actions or endeavors.
