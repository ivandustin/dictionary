Etymology: The word "μωμηθηι" (mōmēthēi) comes from the Greek verb "μωμάομαι" (mōmáomai), which means "to blame" or "to find fault with." 

Grammar: "μωμηθηι" is a verb in the aorist tense, active voice, and subjunctive mood. It is the third person singular form of the verb.

Description: The word "μωμηθηι" is used to express the action of blaming or finding fault with someone or something. It implies a critical or negative judgment towards the subject.
