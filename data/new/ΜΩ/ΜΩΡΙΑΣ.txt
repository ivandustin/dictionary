Etymology: The word "μωριας" (mōrias) comes from the Greek word "μωρός" (mōros), meaning "foolish" or "stupid".

Grammar: "Μωριας" (mōrias) is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the singular form and belongs to the masculine gender.

Description: "Μωριας" (mōrias) refers to foolishness or stupidity. It is used to describe someone who lacks intelligence or common sense.
