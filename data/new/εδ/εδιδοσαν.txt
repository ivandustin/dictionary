The word "εδιδοσαν" is a conjugated form of the Greek verb "δίδωμι" (didomi), which means "to give". It is in the third person plural, past tense, indicative mood. 

Etymology: The word "δίδωμι" comes from the Proto-Indo-European root *deh₃- ("to give"), which is also the origin of the English word "donate".

Grammar: "εδιδοσαν" is a regular conjugation of "δίδωμι" in the past tense. The prefix "ε-" indicates the past tense, and the suffix "-αν" indicates the third person plural. 

Description: "εδιδοσαν" describes an action of giving that happened in the past, performed by a group of people.
