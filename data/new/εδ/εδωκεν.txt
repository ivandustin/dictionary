Word: εδωκεν

Etymology: εδωκεν is a Greek verb that comes from the root word δίδωμι (didomi), which means "to give."

Grammar: εδωκεν is the third person singular aorist indicative active form of the verb δίδωμι (didomi). In Greek, the aorist tense is used to describe a completed action in the past.

Description: εδωκεν means "he/she/it gave" in English. It is a verb that describes the act of giving something to someone else. In the context of Greek literature, εδωκεν is often used to describe the actions of gods or heroes who give gifts or blessings to mortals.
