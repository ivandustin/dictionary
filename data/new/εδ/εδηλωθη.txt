Grammar: εδηλωθη is a third person singular past tense form of the verb δηλόω (delo-o). It is in the aorist tense, which is used to describe a completed action in the past. The word is also passive, indicating that the subject received the action rather than performed it.

Description: εδηλωθη can be translated to English as "was declared, was made known, was revealed". It is often used in legal or official contexts to indicate that something has been officially announced or made public.
