The word εδραιωμα (edraioma) comes from the Greek verb εδραιοω (edraioo), which means "to make firm, establish, strengthen". It is derived from the noun εδρα (edra), which means "a seat, base, foundation".

εδραιωμα is a noun in the Greek language. It is a neuter noun and its plural form is εδραιώματα (edraiomata).
