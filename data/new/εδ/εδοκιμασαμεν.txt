The word εδοκιμασαμεν is a Greek verb that comes from the root word δοκιμάζω (dokimazo), which means "to test" or "to prove". The prefix ε- (e-) is added to the beginning of the word to indicate that the action was completed in the past tense.

εδοκιμασαμεν is the first person plural form of the aorist tense in Greek. The aorist tense is used to describe a completed action in the past, without indicating the duration or repetition of the action. The -μεν (-men) ending indicates that the subject of the verb is "we".
