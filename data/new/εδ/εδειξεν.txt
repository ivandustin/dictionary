Word: εδειξεν

Etymology: εδειξεν is a Greek verb that comes from the root word δείκνυμι (deiknymi), which means "to show" or "to point out."

Grammar: εδειξεν is the third person singular aorist indicative active form of the verb δείκνυμι (deiknymi). In Greek, the aorist tense is used to describe a completed action in the past.

Description: εδειξεν means "he/she/it showed" or "he/she/it pointed out" in English. It is a verb that describes an action of demonstrating or indicating something to someone else.
