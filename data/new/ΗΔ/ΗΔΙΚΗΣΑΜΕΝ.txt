The word "ηδικησαμεν" is a Greek verb in the past tense, first person plural form. It comes from the root word "ηδικέω" (idikeo), which means "to wrong" or "to do injustice." The suffix "-σαμεν" (-samen) indicates the past tense and first person plural form. Therefore, "ηδικησαμεν" translates to "we wronged" or "we did injustice."
