Etymology: The word "γνώσεως" (gnóseos) comes from the Greek verb "γιγνώσκω" (ginósko), which means "to know" or "to understand."

Grammar: "Γνώσεως" is a noun in the genitive case, singular form. In Greek grammar, the genitive case is used to indicate possession or relationship.

Description: "Γνώσεως" refers to knowledge or understanding. It represents the concept of acquiring information, facts, or skills through learning, experience, or study.
