Grammar: "Γνωστοις" is the plural form of the adjective "γνωστός" (gnostos), which means "known" or "familiar" in Greek. It agrees with plural nouns of the masculine, feminine, and neuter genders.

Description: "Γνωστοις" is used to describe multiple things or people that are known or familiar to someone. It indicates that the subject or object is recognized or acknowledged by others.
