Etymology: The word "γνωσομαι" (gnosomai) comes from the Greek verb "γιγνώσκω" (ginosko), which means "to know" or "to understand". It is derived from the Proto-Indo-European root *ǵneh₃-.

Grammar: "Γνωσομαι" is a verb in the future tense, first person singular form. In Greek, verbs are conjugated based on tense, mood, voice, and person. The future tense indicates an action that will happen in the future.

Description: "Γνωσομαι" is a Greek verb that means "I will know" or "I will understand". It expresses the idea of acquiring knowledge or comprehension in the future.
