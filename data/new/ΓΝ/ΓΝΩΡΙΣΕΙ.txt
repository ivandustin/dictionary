Etymology: The word "γνωρίσει" comes from the Greek verb "γνωρίζω" (gnorízo), which means "to get to know" or "to become acquainted with." It is derived from the noun "γνῶσις" (gnósis), meaning "knowledge" or "understanding."

Grammar: "Γνωρίσει" is the third person singular future tense form of the verb "γνωρίζω" (gnorízo). In Greek grammar, verbs are conjugated according to person, number, tense, mood, and voice. The future tense indicates an action that will happen in the future.

Description: "Γνωρίσει" is a Greek word that translates to "he/she/it will get to know" or "he/she/it will become acquainted with" in English. It expresses the idea of someone or something acquiring knowledge or familiarity with someone or something else.
