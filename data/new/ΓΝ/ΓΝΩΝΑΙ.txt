Etymology: The word "γνωναί" (gnonai) comes from the Greek verb "γιγνώσκω" (ginosko), which means "to know" or "to understand."

Grammar: "Γνωναί" is a noun in the plural form. It belongs to the third declension in Greek grammar.

Description: "Γνωναί" refers to "knowings" or "understandings." It can be used to describe the act of knowing or understanding something, or it can refer to the knowledge or understanding itself.
