Etymology: The word "γνωσεται" (gnosetai) comes from the Greek verb "γινώσκω" (ginosko), which means "to know" or "to understand."

Grammar: "Γνωσεται" is the third person singular form of the verb "γινώσκω" in the future tense. It is used to indicate that someone or something will know or understand something in the future.

Description: "Γνωσεται" is a Greek word that expresses the idea of future knowledge or understanding. It implies that at some point in the future, someone or something will acquire knowledge or understanding about a particular subject.
