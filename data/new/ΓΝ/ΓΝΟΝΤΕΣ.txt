Etymology: The word "γνωντες" (gnontes) comes from the Greek verb "γιγνώσκω" (ginosko), which means "to know" or "to understand."

Grammar: "Γνωντες" is the plural form of the verb "γιγνώσκω" (ginosko) in the aorist tense. It is used to describe a group of people who have acquired knowledge or understanding of something.

Description: "Γνωντες" is a Greek word that can be translated as "knowing" or "having known." It is often used in a context where a group of people is described as having gained knowledge or understanding of a particular subject or situation.
