Etymology: The word "γνώση" (gnósi) comes from the Greek verb "γιγνώσκω" (ginósko), which means "to know" or "to understand."

Grammar: "Γνώση" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the feminine gender and is used in the singular form.

Description: "Γνώση" refers to knowledge or understanding. It represents the awareness or familiarity that one has about a particular subject or topic. It can also refer to expertise or wisdom gained through learning or experience.
