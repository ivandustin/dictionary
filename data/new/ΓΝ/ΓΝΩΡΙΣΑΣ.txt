Grammar: "Γνωρισας" is the aorist tense of the verb "γνωρίζω" (gnorizo) in the second person singular. The aorist tense is used to express a completed action in the past. In this form, it means "you made known" or "you introduced."

Description: "Γνωρισας" is a Greek word that refers to the act of making someone or something known or introducing them. It can be used in various contexts, such as introducing someone to others, making something known to others, or bringing attention to something.
