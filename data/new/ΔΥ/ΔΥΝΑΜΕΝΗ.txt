Etymology: The word "δυναμενη" (dynamene) comes from the Greek verb "δύναμαι" (dynamai), which means "to be able" or "to have power." It is derived from the noun "δύναμις" (dynamis), meaning "power" or "ability."

Grammar: "Δυναμενη" is the feminine form of the present participle of the verb "δύναμαι" (dynamai). In Greek grammar, the present participle is used to express ongoing or continuous action. The feminine form indicates that the subject of the verb is female.
