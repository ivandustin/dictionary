Etymology: The word "δυνατόν" comes from the Greek word "δυνατός" (dunatos), meaning "possible" or "capable."

Grammar: "Δυνατόν" is a neuter singular adjective in Greek. It can be used as an adverb to mean "possibly" or "perhaps."

Description: "Δυνατόν" is used to express the idea of something being possible or capable of happening. It can be used to indicate a likelihood or probability. In some contexts, it can also convey a sense of astonishment or incredulity.
