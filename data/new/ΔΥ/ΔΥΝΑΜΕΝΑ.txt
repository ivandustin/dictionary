Etymology: The word "δυναμενα" comes from the Greek verb "δύναμαι" (dynamai), which means "to be able" or "to have power." It is derived from the Greek noun "δύναμις" (dynamis), meaning "power" or "ability."

Grammar: "δυναμενα" is the plural form of the Greek noun "δυναμενον" (dynamenon), which is the present participle of the verb "δύναμαι" (dynamai). It is used to indicate the action of being able or having power in the present tense.

Description: "δυναμενα" refers to things or individuals that are able or have the power to do something. It can be used to describe a wide range of abilities or powers, depending on the context in which it is used.
