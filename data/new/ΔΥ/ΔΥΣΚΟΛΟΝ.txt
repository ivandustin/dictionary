Grammar: "Δυσκολον" is a neuter noun in the singular form. In Greek grammar, it belongs to the second declension. It can be used as both a subject and an object in a sentence.

Description: "Δυσκολον" is an adjective that describes something as difficult, hard, or challenging. It is often used to describe tasks, situations, or concepts that require effort, skill, or perseverance to overcome or understand.
