Etymology: The word "δυναίμην" comes from the Greek verb "δύναμαι" (dynamai), which means "to be able" or "to have power." 

Grammar: "Δυναίμην" is the first person singular form of the verb "δύναμαι" in the imperfect tense. It is used to express an ongoing or repeated action in the past.

Description: "Δυναίμην" is an ancient Greek word that means "I was able" or "I had the power." It is often used in literature and historical texts to describe the abilities or powers of a person or a deity.
