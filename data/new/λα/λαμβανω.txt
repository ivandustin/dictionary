The word λαμβάνω (lamvano) comes from the Ancient Greek verb λαμβάνειν (lambanein), which means "to take" or "to receive". It is derived from the Proto-Indo-European root *leb- which means "to take" or "to seize".

- Singular: λαμβάνω (lamvano) - I take/receive
- Plural: λαμβάνουμε (lamvanoume) - We take/receive
