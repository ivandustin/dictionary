Word: λαβετε

Etymology: λαβετε is a Greek verb in the imperative mood, which means "take" or "seize". It comes from the Greek word λαμβάνω (lambanō), which means "to take" or "to receive".

Grammar: λαβετε is a second-person plural imperative verb in the present tense. It is used to give a command or instruction to a group of people. The verb is formed by adding the suffix -ετε to the stem λαβ-.
