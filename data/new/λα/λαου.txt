The word "λαου" (laou) is a Greek noun that means "of the people" or "belonging to the people." It is derived from the Greek word "λαός" (laos), which means "people" or "nation." In terms of grammar, "λαου" is in the genitive case, which is used to indicate possession or relationship. 

Example sentence: Το βιβλίο αυτό είναι του λαού. (To vivlio afto einai tou laou.) - This book belongs to the people.
