Word: λαλησει

Etymology: λαλησει is a Greek verb that comes from the root word λαλέω (laleo), which means "to speak" or "to talk."

Grammar: λαλησει is a third-person singular future active indicative form of the verb λαλέω. It is used to indicate that someone or something will speak or talk in the future.

Description: λαλησει is a verb that is commonly used in Greek language. It is often used in everyday conversations and in written texts. The word can be used to describe any situation where someone or something will speak or talk in the future. For example, "Ο Πέτρος θα λαλήσει στο τηλέφωνο" (O Petros tha lalesei sto tilefono) means "Peter will speak on the phone."
