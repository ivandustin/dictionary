Word: λαλια

Etymology: The word λαλια comes from the Greek verb λαλέω (laleo), which means "to talk" or "to speak". 

Grammar: λαλια is a feminine noun in Greek. It is in the singular form and belongs to the first declension. 

Description: λαλια refers to the act of speaking or talking. It can also refer to idle talk or chatter. In ancient Greek literature, λαλια was often used to describe the speech of women or children. Today, the word is still used in modern Greek to refer to speech or conversation.
