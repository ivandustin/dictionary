Word: λαλουσαι

Etymology: λαλουσαι is a form of the Greek verb λαλέω (laleo), which means "to speak" or "to talk". It is derived from the Proto-Indo-European root *leh₂- ("to speak").

Grammar: λαλουσαι is the present participle, feminine, nominative, plural form of λαλέω. It is used to describe a group of females who are currently speaking or talking.

Description: λαλουσαι is a word that describes a group of females who are actively engaged in speaking or talking. It can be used in a variety of contexts, such as in literature, poetry, or everyday conversation. The word conveys a sense of liveliness and energy, as if the women are animatedly discussing something of great importance.
