Word: λαμβανομενον

Etymology: λαμβανομενον is a present participle of the Greek verb λαμβάνω (lamváno), which means "to take" or "to receive". The word is derived from the Proto-Indo-European root *leh₂p-.

Grammar: λαμβανομενον is a masculine, singular, nominative, present participle in the Greek language. It is used to describe an action that is currently taking place.

Description: λαμβανομενον is a word that describes an ongoing action of taking or receiving something. It can be used in various contexts, such as in a sentence like "The package is being received by the recipient" or "The student is taking notes during the lecture". The word is commonly used in Greek literature and everyday conversation.
