Word: λαμβανουσιν

Etymology: λαμβανουσιν is a present tense verb form in Greek, derived from the root word λαμβάνω (lamvano), which means "to take" or "to receive".

Grammar: λαμβανουσιν is the third person plural present tense form of the verb λαμβάνω (lamvano) in Greek. It is used to indicate that a group of people are currently taking or receiving something.

Description: λαμβανουσιν is a Greek verb that is commonly used in everyday conversation. It is often used to describe the act of taking or receiving something, such as food, money, or information. The present tense form of the verb indicates that the action is currently taking place.
