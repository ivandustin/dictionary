Grammar: λαζαρον is a noun in the singular form. It is a masculine noun and belongs to the second declension in Greek grammar. The genitive form of the word is λαζαρου.

Description: λαζαρον is a Greek word that refers to a person who is poor, destitute, or a beggar. It is often used to describe someone who is homeless or living in extreme poverty.
