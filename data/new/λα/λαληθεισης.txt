Word: λαληθείσης

Etymology:
The word λαληθείσης is a Greek word that comes from the verb λαλέω (laleo), which means "to speak" or "to talk". The word is in the aorist passive participle form, which means "having been spoken" or "having been talked about".

Grammar:
λαληθείσης is a feminine singular form of the aorist passive participle of the verb λαλέω. It is in the genitive case, which means it is used to show possession or relationship to another noun or pronoun in the sentence.

Description:
The word λαληθείσης is used in Greek to describe something that has been spoken or talked about. It can be used to refer to a person, an event, or an idea that has been discussed or mentioned in conversation or writing. The word is often used in a passive sense, indicating that the subject of the conversation or writing is being talked about by someone else.
