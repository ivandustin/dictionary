The word λατρευον (latreuon) is a Greek verb that means "to worship" or "to serve as a priest." It comes from the root word λατρεία (latreia), which means "worship" or "service." In terms of grammar, λατρευον is the present participle form of the verb λατρεύω (latreuō), which means "to serve" or "to worship." It is often used in the context of religious worship or devotion.
