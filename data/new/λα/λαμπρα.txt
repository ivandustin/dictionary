λαμπρα (lampra) is a feminine noun in Greek that means "brightness" or "radiance". It comes from the adjective λαμπρός (lampros), which means "shining" or "bright". In terms of grammar, it is a first declension noun and can be used in the nominative, genitive, dative, accusative, and vocative cases. It is often used in poetry and literature to describe the radiance of light or beauty.
