λαχανα (lachana) is a Greek word that refers to vegetables or greens. It comes from the ancient Greek word λάχανον (lachanon), which also means vegetables. In terms of grammar, λαχανα is a feminine noun in the singular form. It can be used to describe a variety of vegetables, such as lettuce, spinach, and cabbage. In Greek cuisine, λαχανα is often used in salads, soups, and stews.
