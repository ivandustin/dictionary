Word: λαμπρως

The word λαμπρως (lampros) is derived from the Greek word λαμπρός (lampros), which means "bright" or "shining". It is related to the verb λάμπω (lambo), which means "to shine" or "to radiate".

λαμπρως is used to describe something that is shining or bright. It can also be used to describe something that is brilliant, splendid, or glorious.
