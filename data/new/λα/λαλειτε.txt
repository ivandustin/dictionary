Word: λαλεῖτε (laleite)

Etymology: λαλεῖτε is a present tense verb form in Greek, derived from the root word λαλέω (laleo), which means "to speak" or "to talk."

Grammar: λαλεῖτε is a second person plural present active indicative verb in Greek. It is used to address a group of people and means "you all speak" or "you all are speaking."

Description: λαλεῖτε is a common verb in Greek and is used in various contexts, such as in everyday conversation, religious texts, and literature. It is often used to encourage or command a group of people to speak or to continue speaking.
