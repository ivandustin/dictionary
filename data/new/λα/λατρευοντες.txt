The word λατρευοντες (latreuontes) is a Greek verb that means "to worship" or "to serve as a priest." It comes from the root word λατρεία (latreia), which means "worship" or "service." In terms of grammar, λατρευοντες is the present participle form of the verb λατρεύω (latreuó), which means "to serve" or "to worship." It is used to describe someone who is currently engaged in the act of worship or service.
