Word: λατρευοντα

Etymology: λατρεύω (latreuo) is a Greek verb meaning "to serve, worship, or adore." The -οντα (-onta) ending is the present participle form, indicating that the word refers to someone who is currently serving or worshiping.

Grammar: λατρευοντα is the present participle form of the verb λατρεύω (latreuo) in the third person singular. It is in the active voice and the present tense.

Description: λατρευοντα describes someone who is currently serving or worshiping. It can be used to refer to someone who is performing religious or spiritual acts of devotion, or someone who is serving in a more general sense, such as serving customers in a restaurant.
