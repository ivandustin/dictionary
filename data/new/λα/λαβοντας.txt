Word: λαβόντας

Etymology: λαβόντας is the present participle of the Greek verb λαμβάνω (lamváno), which means "to take" or "to receive".

Grammar: λαβόντας is a masculine accusative plural form of the present participle. It is used to describe a group of people who are currently taking or receiving something.

Description: λαβόντας can be translated as "taking" or "receiving" in English. It is often used in Greek literature and language to describe a group of people who are actively taking or receiving something, such as a gift or a message.
