Word: λανθανει

Etymology: λανθάνω (lanthánō) is a Greek verb meaning "to escape notice, be concealed". The word λανθανει (lanthanei) is the present tense, third person singular form of this verb.

Grammar: λανθανει is a present tense, indicative mood, active voice form of the verb λανθάνω. It is used to describe an action that is currently happening in the present.

Description: λανθανει is a Greek word that means "it escapes notice" or "it is concealed". It can be used to describe something that is hidden or not easily seen or noticed. The word can also be used metaphorically to describe something that is not obvious or apparent.
