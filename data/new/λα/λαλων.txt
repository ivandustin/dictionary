Word: λαλων

Etymology:
The word λαλων (lalōn) is a present participle form of the Greek verb λαλέω (laleō), which means "to talk" or "to speak." The verb λαλέω is derived from the Proto-Indo-European root *leh₂- which means "to speak" or "to tell."

Grammar:
λαλων is a present participle form of the verb λαλέω. Present participles are formed by adding -ων (-ōn) to the present stem of the verb. They function as adjectives and describe an ongoing action. In this case, λαλων describes someone who is currently speaking or talking.
