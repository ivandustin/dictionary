Word: λαβόντες

Etymology: λαβόντες is the aorist participle of the Greek verb λαμβάνω (lambanō), which means "to take" or "to receive".

Grammar: λαβόντες is a masculine plural nominative participle, which means it is describing a group of people who have taken or received something. It is often translated as "having taken" or "having received".

It is often used to describe the actions of the disciples or other followers of Jesus, who are depicted as taking or receiving various things, such as food, money, or instructions.
