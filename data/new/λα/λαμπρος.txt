The word λαμπρός (lampros) comes from the Greek root word λαμπρός (lampros), which means "bright" or "shining". It is derived from the verb λάμπω (lampo), which means "to shine" or "to radiate".

Grammar:
λαμπρός is an adjective in the masculine singular form. It can be used to describe a person, object, or situation that is bright, shining, or brilliant.
