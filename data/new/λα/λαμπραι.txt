Word: λαμπραι

Etymology:
The word λαμπραι (lampai) is derived from the Greek word λαμπρός (lampros), which means "bright" or "shining". It is the feminine plural form of the adjective λαμπρός.

Grammar:
In Greek, adjectives agree in gender, number, and case with the noun they modify. Therefore, λαμπραι is the feminine plural form of the adjective λαμπρός, and it modifies a feminine plural noun.

Description:
The word λαμπραι is used to describe a group of feminine nouns that are bright or shining. It can be used to describe physical objects, such as stars or jewels, or abstract concepts, such as ideas or personalities.
