The word "νηστευοντες" is a Greek verb that means "to fast" or "to abstain from food". It is derived from the Greek word "νηστεία" (nesteia), which means "fasting" or "abstinence from food". In terms of grammar, "νηστευοντες" is the present participle form of the verb "νηστεύω" (nesteuo), which means "to fast" or "to abstain from food".
