The word "νηστευοντων" is a present participle in Greek, derived from the verb "νηστεύω" (nesteuo), which means "to fast" or "to abstain from food". The present participle form indicates that the action of fasting is currently ongoing or in progress. 

In terms of grammar, "νηστευοντων" is in the genitive case, which indicates possession or association. It can be translated as "of those who are fasting" or "belonging to those who are fasting". 

Overall, "νηστευοντων" is a word that describes a group of people who are currently abstaining from food, and it is often used in religious or spiritual contexts.
