The word "νηστευσαντες" is a Greek verb that means "to fast" or "to abstain from food". It is derived from the Greek word "νηστεία" (nesteia), which means "fasting" or "abstinence from food". 

In terms of grammar, "νηστευσαντες" is the aorist participle form of the verb "νηστεύω" (nesteuo), which means "to fast". The aorist tense indicates a completed action in the past, while the participle form functions as an adjective, describing the subject of the sentence.

Overall, "νηστευσαντες" is a word that describes the act of fasting or abstaining from food in the past.
