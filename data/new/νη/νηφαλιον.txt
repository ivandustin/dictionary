νηφάλιον (nephalion) is a neuter noun in the singular form. In Greek, neuter nouns have the same form for both the nominative and accusative cases. Therefore, νηφάλιον (nephalion) can be used as both the subject and object of a sentence.

νηφάλιον (nephalion) is a word that describes a state of being sober or clear-headed.
