Word: νησιον

The word νησιον (nēsion) is a Greek word that means "island". It is derived from the Greek word νησος (nēsos), which also means "island".

νησιον is a neuter noun in the singular form. In Greek, neuter nouns have the same form for both the nominative and accusative cases. The genitive form of νησιον is νησιου (nēsiou), the dative form is νησιῳ (nēsiō), and the accusative form is also νησιον.

Description:
νησιον is a word that refers to a small island. It is often used in ancient Greek literature to describe the islands of the Aegean Sea. The word can also be used metaphorically to refer to something small and isolated.
