Grammar: "κλεψεις" is the second person singular form of the present tense of the verb "κλέπτω". It is conjugated from the stem "κλέπτ-" and the ending "-εις". 

Description: The word "κλεψεις" is a common verb in the Greek language, used to describe the act of stealing. It can be used in various contexts, such as in a sentence like "Μην κλέψεις τα χρήματά μου" (Min klepseis ta hrimata mou), which means "Don't steal my money".
