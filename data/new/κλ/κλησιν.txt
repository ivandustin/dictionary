The word "κλησιν" (klēsin) is a Greek noun that means "a calling" or "an invitation". It is derived from the verb "καλέω" (kaleō), which means "to call" or "to summon". In terms of grammar, "κλησιν" is a third-declension feminine noun in the singular form. It can be used in various contexts, such as in religious or social settings, to refer to a call or invitation to attend an event or gathering.
