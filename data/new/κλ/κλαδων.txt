Word: κλαδων

Etymology:
The word κλαδων (kladon) is derived from the Greek word κλάδος (klados), which means "branch" or "twig". It is a noun in the singular form.

Grammar:
In Greek, κλαδων is a masculine noun in the singular form. It belongs to the second declension and its genitive form is κλάδου (kladou).

Description:
κλαδων is a term used to describe a branch or a twig of a tree or a plant. It is often used in botany and agriculture to refer to the smaller parts of a plant. The word can also be used metaphorically to describe a smaller part of a larger system or organization.
