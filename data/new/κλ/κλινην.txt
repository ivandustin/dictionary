The word "κλινην" (klinēn) is a Greek noun that means "bed" or "couch". It is derived from the verb "κλίνω" (klinō), which means "to lean" or "to incline". In terms of grammar, it is a feminine singular accusative noun in the second declension. It can also be spelled as "κλίνην" with an accent on the second syllable.
