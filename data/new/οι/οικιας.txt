Word: οικιας

Etymology: οικιας comes from the Greek word οἰκία (oikía), which means "house" or "dwelling."

Grammar: οικιας is a noun in the genitive case, which is used to show possession or relationship. It is the genitive singular form of οἰκία.

Description: οικιας refers to "of the house" or "of the household." It is often used in the context of family relationships or ownership of property. For example, "τὰ ὅπλα τῆς οἰκίας" (tà hópla tês oikías) means "the weapons of the household."
