Word: οικονομία

Etymology: The word οικονομία comes from the Greek words οίκος (oikos) meaning "household" and νόμος (nomos) meaning "law". Therefore, οικονομία originally meant "the management of a household".

Grammar: οικονομία is a feminine noun in Greek. Its plural form is οικονομίες.

Description: οικονομία has several meanings in Greek. It can refer to the management of a household or a business, the economy of a country, or the way resources are allocated and used in general. In modern Greek, it is mostly used to refer to the economy of a country or the financial system.
