Word: οικιακοι

Etymology: The word οικιακοι comes from the Greek word οικία (oikía), which means "house" or "home". The suffix -κοι (-koi) is added to indicate that the word refers to something related to the home or household.

Grammar: οικιακοι is an adjective in the masculine plural form. It is used to describe something that is related to the household or home.

Description: οικιακοι is often used to describe household items or chores, such as cleaning, cooking, or organizing. It can also be used to describe a person who is skilled in these tasks or who takes care of the home. In a broader sense, οικιακοι can refer to anything related to the domestic sphere, including family relationships and social customs.
