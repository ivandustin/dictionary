Word: οικοδομών

Grammar: Οικοδομών is the present participle of the verb οικοδομώ, which means "building" or "constructing". It is in the masculine gender, singular number, and nominative case.

Description: Οικοδομών refers to a person who is currently building or constructing something. It can be used to describe a professional builder or construction worker, or someone who is simply engaged in building or constructing something as a hobby or personal project.
