Word: οικοδεσπότης

Etymology: The word οικοδεσπότης comes from the Greek words οίκος (oikos) meaning "house" and δεσπότης (despotis) meaning "master" or "lord". Therefore, οικοδεσπότης literally means "house master" or "lord of the house".

Grammar: οικοδεσπότης is a masculine noun in Greek. Its plural form is οικοδεσπότες.

Description: οικοδεσπότης refers to the owner or master of a house or household. It can also refer to the host of a gathering or event held in a house. In modern Greek, it is commonly used to refer to the landlord of a rental property.
