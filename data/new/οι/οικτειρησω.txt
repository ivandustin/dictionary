Word: οικτειρησω

Etymology:
The word οικτειρησω comes from the Greek root word οικτείρω (oikteirō), which means "to pity" or "to have compassion". The suffix -σω (-sō) is a first-person singular future tense ending, indicating that the speaker will perform the action of pitying or having compassion in the future.

Grammar:
Οικτειρησω is a first-person singular future tense verb in the Greek language. It is formed by adding the future tense ending -σω to the root word οικτείρω. The verb is in the active voice, indicating that the subject (I) will perform the action of pitying or having compassion.

Description:
Οικτειρησω is a Greek verb that means "I will pity" or "I will have compassion". It is a future tense verb, indicating that the action of pitying or having compassion will take place at some point in the future. This verb is often used in a religious or moral context, expressing a sense of empathy or sympathy towards others who are suffering or in need.
