Word: οικοδομας

Etymology:
The word οικοδομας comes from the Greek words οικος (oikos) meaning "house" and δομας (domas) meaning "builder". Therefore, οικοδομας means "house builder" or "constructor".

Grammar:
In Greek, οικοδομας is a masculine noun in the nominative case. It can also be declined in other cases, such as the genitive case οικοδομα (oikodoma) or the accusative case οικοδομα (oikodoma).

Description:
Οικοδομας refers to a person who builds or constructs houses or other buildings. It can also refer to a construction worker or a contractor. The word is commonly used in the construction industry and related fields.
