Word: οικοδομη

Etymology: The word οικοδομη comes from the Greek roots οίκος (oikos) meaning "house" and δόμηση (domisi) meaning "construction". Therefore, οικοδομη literally means "house construction".

Grammar: οικοδομη is a feminine noun in Greek. Its plural form is οικοδομές (oikodomés).

Description: οικοδομη refers to the process of building or constructing a house or any other type of building. It can also be used metaphorically to describe the process of building or developing something, such as a relationship or a business. In Greek, it is a common term used in the construction industry and is often associated with the idea of creating something new and valuable.
