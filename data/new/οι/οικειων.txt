Word: οικειων

Etymology: οικειων comes from the Greek word οίκειος (oikeios), which means "belonging to one's household" or "familiar". 

Grammar: οικειων is a noun in the genitive case, which is used to show possession or relationship. 

Description: οικειων can be translated to mean "of one's own" or "belonging to oneself". It is often used in the context of personal possessions or property. For example, if someone were to say "τα οικειων μου" (ta oikeion mou), it would mean "my own things" or "my personal belongings".
