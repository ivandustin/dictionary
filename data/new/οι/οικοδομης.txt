Grammar: οικοδομης is a feminine noun in the Greek language. It is in the genitive case in this form, which means it is used to show possession or relationship to another noun or pronoun in a sentence.

Description: οικοδομης refers to the process of constructing or building something, such as a house, a building, or a structure.
