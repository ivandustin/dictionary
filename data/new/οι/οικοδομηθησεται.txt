Word: οικοδομηθησεται

Grammar: οικοδομηθησεται is a third person singular, passive, indicative form of the verb οικοδομέω (oikodomeo). It is in the future tense and indicates that something will be built or constructed.

Description: οικοδομηθησεται is a Greek verb that means "it will be built" or "it will be constructed". It is often used in the context of construction or building projects. The word can also be used metaphorically to describe the process of personal growth or development.
