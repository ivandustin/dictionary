Word: οικοδομησαντι

Etymology: The word οικοδομησαντι is derived from the Greek verb οικοδομέω (oikodomeo), which means "to build" or "to construct". The suffix -σαντι (-santi) is the aorist active participle ending, indicating that the action of building has been completed.

Grammar: οικοδομησαντι is the aorist active participle of the verb οικοδομέω. It is in the masculine gender, singular number, and dative case. It functions as an adjective modifying a noun in the dative case.

Description: οικοδομησαντι describes someone who has completed the action of building or constructing. In context, it could refer to a person who has built something, such as a house or a temple.
