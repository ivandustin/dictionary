The word "οικοδεσποτειν" (oikodespotein) is a Greek verb that means "to be the master of the house" or "to rule the household". It is derived from the words "οίκος" (oikos), meaning "house", and "δεσπότης" (despotes), meaning "master" or "lord". In terms of grammar, it is a present active infinitive verb.
