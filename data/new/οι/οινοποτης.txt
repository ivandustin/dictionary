Word: οινοπότης

Etymology: The word οινοπότης comes from the Greek words οίνος (oinos) meaning "wine" and πίνω (pino) meaning "to drink". Therefore, οινοπότης literally means "wine drinker".

Grammar: οινοπότης is a masculine noun in Greek. Its plural form is οινοπότες. 

Description: οινοπότης refers to a person who drinks wine regularly or excessively. It can have both positive and negative connotations depending on the context. In ancient Greece, wine was considered a symbol of wealth and social status, so being a οινοπότης was often associated with high social standing. However, in modern times, excessive drinking of wine or any other alcoholic beverage is generally frowned upon and can lead to health problems and addiction.
