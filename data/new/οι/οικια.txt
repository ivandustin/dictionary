Word: οικια

Etymology: οικια comes from the Greek word οἶκος (oikos), meaning "house" or "dwelling."

Grammar: οικια is a feminine noun in Greek. Its nominative singular form is οικιά, and its genitive singular form is οικίας.

Description: οικια refers to a house or dwelling, and can also be used to refer to a household or family. In modern Greek, it is often used to refer to a building or structure.
