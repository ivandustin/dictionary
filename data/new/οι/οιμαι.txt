The word οιμαι (oimai) is a Greek verb that means "to lament" or "to mourn." It is derived from the Greek word οἶμος (oimos), which means "lamentation" or "mourning."

Οιμαι is a first-person singular present indicative active form of the verb οιμάομαι (oimáomai), which means "to lament" or "to mourn." It belongs to the middle voice in Greek, which means that the subject is both the agent and the recipient of the action.

Οιμαι is a word that expresses a deep sense of sorrow and grief. It is often used in the context of mourning the loss of a loved one or expressing sadness over a tragic event.
