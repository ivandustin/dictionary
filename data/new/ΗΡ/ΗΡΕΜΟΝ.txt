Etymology: The word "ηρεμον" (eremon) comes from the Greek adjective "ηρεμος" (eremos), meaning "calm" or "quiet".

Grammar: "ηρεμον" is a noun in the neuter gender. It is in the singular form.

Description: "ηρεμον" refers to a state of calmness or tranquility. It can describe a peaceful atmosphere or a serene state of mind.
