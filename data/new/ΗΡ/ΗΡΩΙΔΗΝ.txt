Grammar: "ηρωιδην" is an adjective in the feminine singular form. In Greek, adjectives agree in gender, number, and case with the noun they modify. So, if the noun is feminine, singular, and in the nominative case, the adjective will also be feminine, singular, and in the nominative case.

Description: "ηρωιδην" describes something or someone that possesses qualities or characteristics associated with a hero. It can be used to describe heroic actions, behavior, or attributes.
