The word "ηρωτησαν" is a Greek verb in the past tense, third person plural form. It comes from the verb "ερωτώ" (erotó), which means "to ask" or "to inquire". The suffix "-σαν" (-san) indicates the past tense and the third person plural subject. In terms of grammar, "ηρωτησαν" is a conjugated form of the verb "ερωτώ" in the aorist tense. It is used to describe an action of asking or inquiring that happened in the past, performed by a group of people.
