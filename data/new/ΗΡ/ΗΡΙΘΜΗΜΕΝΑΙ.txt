The word "ηριθμημέναι" is the plural form of the Greek verb "ηριθμέω" (erithmeo), which means "to count" or "to number." It is derived from the noun "αριθμός" (arithmos), meaning "number." In Greek grammar, "ηριθμημέναι" is the feminine nominative plural form of the verb, used to describe multiple feminine subjects that are being counted or numbered.
