Etymology: The word "ηρχετο" (ercheto) comes from the Greek verb "έρχομαι" (erchomai), which means "to come" or "to arrive".

Grammar: "ηρχετο" is the third person singular, past tense form of the verb "έρχομαι" (erchomai). It is in the indicative mood and the imperfect tense.

Description: "ηρχετο" is used to describe an action that was ongoing or repeated in the past. It indicates that someone or something was coming or arriving repeatedly or continuously in the past.
