Etymology: The word "σβεσαί" (svesai) comes from the Greek verb "σβήνω" (sveno), which means "to extinguish" or "to put out." 

Grammar: "Σβεσαί" is the third person singular, aorist tense, subjunctive mood of the verb "σβήνω." It is used to express a hypothetical action or a wish.

Description: "Σβεσαί" is a Greek word that means "may he/she/it extinguish" or "may he/she/it put out." It is often used in prayers or wishes for something to be extinguished or put out.
