The word "εμβαπτομένος" is a Greek adjective that comes from the verb "εμβάπτω" (embápto), which means "to immerse" or "to dip into." The word is formed by adding the suffix "-μένος" (-menos) to the verb stem. In Greek grammar, this suffix is used to form the passive voice of verbs.
