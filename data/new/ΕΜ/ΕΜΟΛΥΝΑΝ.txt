Etymology: The word "εμολυναν" comes from the Greek language. It is derived from the verb "μολύνω" (molyno), which means "to pollute" or "to contaminate."

Grammar: "Εμολυναν" is the third person plural form of the verb "μολύνω" in the past tense. It is in the indicative mood and active voice. In Greek grammar, verbs change their form based on tense, mood, voice, and person.

Description: "Εμολυναν" is a verb that describes an action of polluting or contaminating. It indicates that a group of people or things polluted or contaminated something in the past.
