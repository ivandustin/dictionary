Etymology: The word "εμνησθημεν" comes from the Greek verb "μνησθηναι" (mnēsthēnai), which means "to remember" or "to recall". The prefix "εμ-" (em-) is added to the verb to indicate the first person plural form, meaning "we remember" or "we recall".

Grammar: "Εμνησθημεν" is the first person plural form of the verb "μνησθηναι" (mnēsthēnai) in the aorist tense. In Greek grammar, the aorist tense is used to express a past action that is viewed as a whole, without specifying its duration or repetition. The first person plural form indicates that the action is performed by a group of people.
