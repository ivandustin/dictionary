Grammar: "Εμπλησθώ" is a first-person singular passive aorist subjunctive form of the verb "πληθω" (plēthō), which means "to fill." The aorist tense indicates a completed action in the past, and the subjunctive mood expresses possibility or potentiality.

Description: The verb "εμπλησθώ" is used to describe the state of being filled or full. It can be used in various contexts, such as being filled with emotions, thoughts, or physical objects. The passive form suggests that the subject is being filled by an external force or agent.
