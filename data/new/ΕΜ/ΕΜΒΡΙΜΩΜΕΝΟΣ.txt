Grammar: "Εμβριμώμενος" is the masculine singular form of the present participle of the verb "εμβριμάομαι" (embrimaomai). In Greek grammar, the present participle is used to describe ongoing actions or states. It agrees in gender, number, and case with the noun it modifies.
