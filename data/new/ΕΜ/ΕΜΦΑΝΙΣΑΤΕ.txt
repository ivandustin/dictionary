The "-ατε" ending indicates the second person plural, and the "εμφανί" stem is modified to fit the aorist tense.

Description: "Εμφανίσατε" is a verb form used to address a group of people and express an action of showing or making something visible in the past. It is commonly used in Greek to refer to a collective action performed by multiple individuals.
