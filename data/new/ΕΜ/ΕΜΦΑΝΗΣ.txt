Grammar: "Εμφάνης" is an adjective in the masculine form. In Greek, adjectives agree in gender, number, and case with the noun they modify. It can be declined to match the gender and number of the noun it describes.

Description: The word "εμφάνης" is used to describe something or someone that is visible, apparent, or evident. It is often used to indicate that something is clearly and visibly present or noticeable.
