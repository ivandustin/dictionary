Etymology: The word "εμηνυσεν" comes from the Greek verb "μηνύω" (mēnýō), which means "to announce" or "to declare."

Grammar: "Εμηνυσεν" is the third person singular form of the aorist tense of the verb "μηνύω" (mēnýō) in the Greek language. It is in the indicative mood and active voice.

Description: "Εμηνυσεν" is a verb that signifies an action of announcing or declaring something. It is used to express the act of making a statement or giving information about a particular subject.
