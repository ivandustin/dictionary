Etymology: The word "εμφάνη" (emphani) comes from the Greek verb "εμφαίνω" (emphaino), which means "to appear" or "to manifest." 

Grammar: "Εμφάνη" is a noun in the singular form. It belongs to the feminine gender in Greek grammar.

Description: "Εμφάνη" refers to the act of appearing or manifesting. It can also be used to describe someone's appearance or presence.
