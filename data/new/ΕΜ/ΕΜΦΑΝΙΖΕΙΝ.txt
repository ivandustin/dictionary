Etymology: The word "εμφανίζειν" comes from the Greek verb "φαίνω" (phaino), which means "to show" or "to appear". The prefix "εμ-" (em-) is added to indicate the action of making something visible or manifest.

Grammar: "Εμφανίζειν" is the infinitive form of the verb in Greek. It belongs to the first conjugation class and is a regular verb. In the present tense, it is conjugated as follows: εμφανίζω (I show), εμφανίζεις (you show), εμφανίζει (he/she/it shows), εμφανίζουμε (we show), εμφανίζετε (you show), εμφανίζουν (they show).

Description: "Εμφανίζειν" is a Greek verb that means "to show" or "to display". It is used to indicate the action of making something visible or manifest. It can be used in various contexts, such as showing something to someone, presenting information, or revealing a hidden object or truth.
