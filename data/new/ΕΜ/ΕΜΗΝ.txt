Etymology: The word "εμην" comes from the Greek language. It is derived from the verb "είμαι" (eimai), which means "to be" in Greek.

Grammar: "Εμην" is the first person singular form of the verb "είμαι" in the past tense. It is used to indicate that the subject of the sentence was in a state of being in the past.
