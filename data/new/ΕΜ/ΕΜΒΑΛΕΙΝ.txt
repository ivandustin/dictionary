Etymology: The word "εμβαλείν" (emvaleín) comes from the Greek roots "εν" (en), meaning "in," and "βάλλειν" (bállein), meaning "to throw." 

Grammar: "Εμβαλείν" is a verb in the Greek language. It is in the active voice, present tense, and infinitive mood. 

Description: "Εμβαλείν" means "to throw in" or "to insert" in English. It is used to describe the action of putting something inside or into something else.
