Etymology: The word "εμπαικται" comes from the Greek verb "εμπαικτεύω" (empaiktevo), which means "to mock" or "to ridicule". 

Grammar: "Εμπαικται" is a noun in the plural form. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their gender, number, and case. The plural form indicates that there are multiple objects or individuals being referred to.

Description: "Εμπαικται" refers to people who mock or ridicule others. It can be used to describe individuals who engage in teasing, making fun of others, or mocking their actions or words.
