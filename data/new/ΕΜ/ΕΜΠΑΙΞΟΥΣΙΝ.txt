Etymology: The word "εμπαιξουσιν" comes from the Greek verb "εμπαιζω" (empaizo), which means "to mock" or "to ridicule". The suffix "-ουσιν" (-ousin) indicates the third person plural present tense form of the verb.

Grammar: "Εμπαιξουσιν" is a verb in the Greek language. It is in the third person plural present tense form, indicating that the subject of the verb is a group of people who are currently mocking or ridiculing.
