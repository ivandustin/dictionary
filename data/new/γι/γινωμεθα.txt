The word γινωμεθα is derived from the Greek verb γίνομαι (ginomai), which means "to become" or "to happen". The suffix -μεθα (-meta) is a reflexive suffix that indicates that the action of the verb is being performed on oneself.

γινωμεθα can be translated as "we are becoming" or "we are happening to ourselves". It is a reflexive form of the verb γίνομαι (ginomai), which is a very common and versatile verb in Greek.
