Word: γινωσκωσι

Etymology: γινωσκωσι is a conjugated form of the Greek verb γινώσκω (ginósko), which means "to know" or "to understand". The verb comes from the Proto-Indo-European root *ǵneh₃-.

Grammar: γινωσκωσι is the third person plural present active subjunctive form of γινώσκω. In Greek, the subjunctive mood is used to express doubt, possibility, or hypothetical situations.

Description: γινωσκωσι is a verb form that indicates that a group of people (third person plural) are currently knowing or understanding something in a hypothetical or uncertain situation. It is often used in conditional sentences or in expressions of doubt or uncertainty.
