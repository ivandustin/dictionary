Word: γινωσκοντες

Etymology:
The word γινωσκοντες (ginōskontes) is a present participle form of the Greek verb γινώσκω (ginōskō), which means "to know" or "to understand". The verb is derived from the Proto-Indo-European root *ǵneh₃- which means "to know".

Grammar:
γινωσκοντες is a present participle form of the verb γινώσκω (ginōskō), which means "knowing" or "understanding". It is in the present tense and active voice, and it is declined in the nominative plural form.
