Word: εισερχομενους

Etymology: 
The word εισερχομενους comes from the Greek verb εισέρχομαι (eiserchomai), which means "to enter" or "to go in". The word is formed from the prefix εις (eis), meaning "into", and the verb ερχομαι (erchomai), meaning "to come".

Grammar:
εισερχομενους is the present participle form of the verb εισέρχομαι (eiserchomai), which means "entering" or "going in". It is in the masculine accusative plural form, indicating that it refers to a group of people who are entering or going in.

εισερχομενους is a Greek word that describes a group of people who are entering or going in. It can be used in a variety of contexts, such as describing people entering a building, a room, or a city.
