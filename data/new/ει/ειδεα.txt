Etymology: The word comes from the Greek root "εἶδος" (eidos), which means "form" or "shape". 

Grammar: "Ειδεα" is a feminine noun in the nominative plural form. 

Description: The word is often used in philosophical and intellectual contexts to refer to abstract concepts or mental constructs. It can also be used in everyday language to refer to someone's thoughts or opinions.
