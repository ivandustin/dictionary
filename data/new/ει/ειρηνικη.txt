Word: ειρηνική

Etymology: The word ειρηνική (eiriniki) comes from the Greek word ειρήνη (eirini), which means "peace". The suffix -ική (-iki) is added to the end of the word to form an adjective, meaning "related to peace" or "peaceful".

Grammar: ειρηνική is a feminine adjective in the nominative singular form. It agrees in gender, number, and case with the noun it modifies.

Description: ειρηνική is used to describe something that is peaceful or related to peace. It can be used to describe a person, place, or thing. For example, ειρηνική χώρα (eiriniki chora) means "peaceful country", and ειρηνική διαμάχη (eiriniki diamachi) means "peaceful conflict".
