The word "εισενεγκειν" (eisenenkein) is a Greek verb that means "to bring in" or "to introduce." 

Etymology: The word is derived from the combination of two Greek words: "εις" (eis) meaning "into" and "ενεγκειν" (enenkein) meaning "to bring." 

Grammar: "εισενεγκειν" is an infinitive verb form in Greek, which means it is not conjugated to match a specific subject or tense. 

Description: The word is often used in the context of introducing something or someone into a particular place or situation. It can also be used metaphorically to describe the act of bringing in or introducing an idea or concept.
