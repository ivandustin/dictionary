The word "εισελθοντι" is a Greek verb in the aorist tense, which means "having entered" or "having gone in". It is derived from the verb "εισέρχομαι" (eiserchomai), which means "to enter" or "to go in". In terms of grammar, it is a participle, which is a type of verb that functions as an adjective or noun in a sentence.
