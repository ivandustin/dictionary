The word "ειργασμένα" is a Greek adjective that is derived from the verb "εργάζομαι" (ergazomai), which means "to work". The adjective form is used to describe something that has been worked on or produced through labor. 

In terms of grammar, "ειργασμένα" is in the feminine plural form, indicating that it is describing multiple feminine objects or nouns. 

Overall, "ειργασμένα" can be translated to English as "worked" or "produced through labor".
