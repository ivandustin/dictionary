The word "εισενεγκηις" (eisenegēkēs) is a Greek noun that means "bringing in" or "introduction." 

Etymology: The word is derived from the Greek words "εις" (eis), meaning "into," and "ενεγκη" (enegēkē), meaning "to bring in."

Grammar: "Εισενεγκηις" is a feminine noun in the nominative singular form. 

Description: The word is often used in the context of introducing something or someone, such as a new idea or a guest. It can also refer to the act of bringing something into a place or situation.
