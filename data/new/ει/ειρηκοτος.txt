The word "ειρηκοτος" (eirēkotos) is a Greek adjective that means "said, spoken, or declared." It is derived from the verb "εἴρω" (eirō), which means "to say" or "to speak." In terms of grammar, it is a participle in the perfect tense, which indicates that the action of speaking has been completed in the past and has ongoing relevance in the present.
