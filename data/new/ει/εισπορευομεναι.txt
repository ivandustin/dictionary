The word "εισπορευομεναι" is a Greek verb in the present participle form, which means "entering" or "going in". It is derived from the combination of two Greek words: "εις" (eis) meaning "into" and "πορεύομαι" (poreuomai) meaning "to go". In terms of grammar, it is a feminine plural form, indicating that the subject is a group of female entities that are entering or going in.
