Word: εισδεξομαι

The word εισδεξομαι (eisdexomai) is a compound word in Greek, consisting of the prefix εις (eis) meaning "into" or "towards" and the verb δεχομαι (dechomai) meaning "to receive" or "to accept". Therefore, εισδεξομαι means "to receive into oneself" or "to accept".

εισδεξομαι is a first-person singular future middle indicative form of the verb δεχομαι (dechomai). The middle voice indicates that the subject is both the doer and the receiver of the action. εισδεξομαι is a Greek verb that describes the act of receiving or accepting something into oneself.
