The word "ειρηκεναι" is a Greek verb in the perfect tense, which means "to have said" or "to have spoken". It is derived from the root word "εἶπον" (eipon), which means "I said". In terms of grammar, it is a transitive verb, which means it requires a direct object. 

Example sentence: Έχει ειπωθεί πολλά για αυτό το θέμα. (Echei eipothei polla gia auto to thema.) - "A lot has been said about this topic."
