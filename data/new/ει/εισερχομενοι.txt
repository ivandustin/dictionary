Word: εισερχομενοι

Etymology: 
The word εισερχομενοι comes from the Greek verb εισέρχομαι (eiserchomai), which means "to enter" or "to go in". The word is formed from the prefix εις (eis), meaning "into", and the verb ερχομαι (erchomai), meaning "to come".
