The word "εισελθοντος" is a Greek verb in the aorist participle form. It comes from the root verb "εισέρχομαι" which means "to enter" or "to go in". The aorist tense indicates a completed action in the past.

In terms of grammar, "εισελθοντος" is in the masculine gender, singular number, and nominative case. It can function as a subject or a predicate nominative in a sentence.

Example sentence: "Εισελθόντος του ανδρός, η συνάντηση ήταν ευχάριστη." (Entering the man, the meeting was pleasant.)
