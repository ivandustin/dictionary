Word: εισερχομενον

Etymology: 
The word εισερχομενον comes from the Greek verb εισέρχομαι (eiserchomai), which means "to enter" or "to go in". The word is formed from the prefix εις (eis), meaning "into", and the verb ερχομαι (erchomai), meaning "to come".

Grammar:
εισερχομενον is the present participle of the verb εισέρχομαι (eiserchomai). As a participle, it functions as an adjective and describes a noun or pronoun. In this case, it describes something that is currently entering or going in.

Description:
εισερχομενον is a Greek word that describes something that is currently entering or going in. It can be used to describe a physical object or a concept. For example, one might use this word to describe a person entering a room, or a thought entering one's mind.
