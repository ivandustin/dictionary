Grammar: The word is feminine and singular in form, and it is in the present tense. 

Description: "Εισδραμουσα" is used to describe the action of someone or something entering a place or space. It can be used in various contexts, such as describing a person entering a room, a vehicle entering a garage, or a thought entering one's mind.
