It is derived from the verb "εισέρχομαι" (eiserchomai), which means "to enter" or "to go in". In terms of grammar, it is a participle, which is a type of verb that functions as an adjective or adverb in a sentence. It can be used to describe a noun or pronoun, or to indicate a time or condition. The etymology of the word comes from the combination of the prefix "εις" (eis), meaning "into", and the verb "έρχομαι" (erchomai), meaning "to come". Therefore, "εισελθοντες" literally means "having come into".
