Word: ειρηνοποιοι

Etymology: The word ειρηνοποιοι comes from the Greek words ειρήνη (eiríni), meaning "peace," and ποιέω (poiéo), meaning "to make." Therefore, ειρηνοποιοι literally means "peacemakers."

Description: ειρηνοποιοι refers to people who work to bring about peace and resolve conflicts. It is often used to describe diplomats, negotiators, and mediators who work to settle disputes between countries or groups.
