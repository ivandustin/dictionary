The word "ειδοτες" (eidotes) is a Greek adjective that means "knowing" or "informed". It is derived from the verb "οἶδα" (oida), which means "to know". In terms of grammar, "ειδοτες" is the masculine plural nominative form of the adjective. It can be used to describe a group of people who are knowledgeable or well-informed about a particular subject.
