Word: εικοσι

Etymology: The word εικοσι (eikosi) comes from the ancient Greek εἴκοσι (eíkosi), which means "twenty".

Grammar: εικοσι is a numeral adjective in Greek, which means it is used to indicate a quantity or number. It is used to represent the number 20.

Description: εικοσι is a simple and commonly used word in Greek, used to represent the number 20. It is often used in counting, measurements, and other numerical contexts.
