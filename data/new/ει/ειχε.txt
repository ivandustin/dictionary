The word "ειχε" is a verb in the Greek language, which means "had" in English. 

Etymology: The word comes from the Greek verb "έχω" (echo), which means "to have". 

Grammar: "Ειχε" is the past tense of the verb "έχω" (echo) in the third person singular.
