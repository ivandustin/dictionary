Word: ειδησουσιν

The suffix -σουσιν (-sousin) is added to the root to indicate the future tense, making the word mean "they will see" or "they will know".

The verb is in the active voice, indicating that the subject is performing the action of seeing or knowing. The indicative mood indicates that the verb is a statement of fact.
