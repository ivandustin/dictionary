Word: υπολαμβάνω

Etymology:
The word υπολαμβάνω comes from the Greek roots υπό (hypo) meaning "under" and λαμβάνω (lamvano) meaning "to take". Therefore, the literal meaning of υπολαμβάνω is "to take under" or "to assume".

Grammar:
υπολαμβάνω is a verb in the Greek language. It belongs to the first conjugation and is a regular verb. It is conjugated in all tenses and moods, and its stem is υπολαμβαν-.

Description:
υπολαμβάνω is a versatile verb that can be translated in various ways depending on the context. It can mean "to assume", "to suppose", "to presume", "to take for granted", "to take into account", "to consider", "to understand", "to comprehend", "to grasp", "to accept", "to receive", "to take on", "to undertake", "to take charge of", "to take responsibility for", and more. It is often used in formal and academic contexts, as well as in everyday speech.
