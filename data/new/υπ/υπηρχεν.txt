Grammar: "Υπηρχεν" is a regular verb in the past imperfect tense, which is used to describe an ongoing or repeated action in the past. It is conjugated from the stem "υπήρχ-", with the addition of the suffix "-εν" for the third person singular. 

Description: The word "υπηρχεν" can be translated to English as "was existing" or "was present". It is often used in historical or narrative contexts to describe the state of things or events in the past.
