Word: υπονοειτε

Etymology: The word υπονοειτε (yponeite) is derived from the Greek verb υπονοώ (yponoó), which means "to insinuate" or "to suggest indirectly."

Grammar: υπονοειτε is a verb in the present tense, second person plural form. It is the conjugated form of the verb υπονοώ (yponoó).

Description: υπονοειτε is a Greek verb that means "you insinuate" or "you suggest indirectly." It is often used in situations where someone is trying to imply something without directly stating it. The verb can be used in a variety of contexts, such as in personal relationships, politics, or business.
