Word: υπαρξεις

Grammar: υπαρξεις is a feminine noun in the plural form. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their gender, number, and case. The plural form of υπαρξεις indicates that there are multiple existences or beings.

Description: υπαρξεις is a philosophical term that refers to the concept of existence or being.
