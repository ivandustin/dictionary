Word: υπεστείλαμην

The word υπεστείλαμην (ypesteílamen) is a compound word in Greek, consisting of the prefix υπο- (ypo-), meaning "under" or "beneath," and the verb στέλλω (stéllo), meaning "to send" or "to dispatch." The prefix υπο- in this context indicates a sense of restraint or holding back, so the word υπεστείλαμην can be translated as "I held myself back" or "I restrained myself."

υπεστείλαμην is the first person singular aorist indicative middle voice form of the verb υποστέλλω (ypostéllo), which means "to hold back" or "to restrain oneself." υπεστείλαμην is a Greek verb that describes an act of self-restraint or self-control.
