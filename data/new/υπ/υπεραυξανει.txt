Word: υπεραυξανει

Etymology: The word υπεραυξανει comes from the Greek prefix υπερ- (hyper-), meaning "above" or "beyond," and the verb αυξάνω (auxano), meaning "to increase" or "to grow." 

Grammar: υπεραυξανει is a verb in the present tense, third person singular form. It is derived from the verb αυξάνω (auxano) and the prefix υπερ- (hyper-). 

Description: υπεραυξανει means "he/she/it is increasing greatly" or "he/she/it is growing excessively." It is often used to describe something that is growing or increasing beyond what is considered normal or expected.
