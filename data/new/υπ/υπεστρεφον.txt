Word: υπεστρεφον

Etymology:
The word υπεστρεφον is derived from the Greek verb "στρέφω" (strefó), which means "to turn" or "to twist". The prefix "υπερ-" (yper-) means "over" or "beyond", so υπεστρεφον can be translated as "I turned over" or "I turned beyond".
