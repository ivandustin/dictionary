The word "υπογραμμον" (ypogrammon) is a Greek noun that literally means "underline". It is derived from the combination of the prefix "υπο-" (ypo-), meaning "under", and the noun "γραμμή" (grammi), meaning "line". 

In grammar, "υπογραμμον" can refer to an underscore or an underline used to emphasize a word or phrase in written text. It can also refer to a horizontal line used to separate text or to indicate a break in a sentence.

Overall, "υπογραμμον" is a term used in typography and writing to indicate emphasis or separation in text.
