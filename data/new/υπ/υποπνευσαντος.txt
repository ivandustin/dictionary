Word: υποπνευσαντος

Etymology: The word υποπνευσαντος is derived from the Greek words υπό (hypo) meaning "under" and πνέω (pneó) meaning "to breathe". 

Grammar: υποπνευσαντος is a Greek verb in the aorist tense, passive voice, and participle mood. It is the genitive singular form of the verb υποπνέω (hypopnéō), which means "to breathe gently" or "to whisper".

Description: υποπνευσαντος is a word that describes someone or something that has been whispered or breathed gently upon. It can also refer to a person who has been influenced or inspired by a gentle whisper or suggestion.
