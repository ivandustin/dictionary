The word υποζωννύντες (ypozonnyntes) is a present participle form of the verb υποζωννύω (ypozonnyo), which means "to gird under." The word is derived from the combination of the prefix υπο- (ypo-), meaning "under," and the verb ζωννύω (zonnyo), meaning "to gird."
