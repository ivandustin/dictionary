Word: υπεδείξεν

Etymology: υπεδείξεν is a Greek verb that comes from the combination of two words: υπό (hypo), which means "under" or "beneath," and δείκνυμι (deiknumi), which means "to show" or "to point out." Therefore, υπεδείξεν means "to show or point out something from under or beneath."

Grammar: υπεδείξεν is the third person singular aorist indicative active form of the verb υποδείκνυμι (ypodeiknymi), which means "to indicate" or "to demonstrate." The aorist tense indicates a completed action in the past.

Description: υπεδείξεν is a formal and literary word that is not commonly used in everyday speech. It is often used in legal or academic contexts to indicate the act of pointing out or demonstrating something.
