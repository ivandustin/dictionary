Word: υπομνησιν

Etymology:
The word υπομνησιν (ypomnēsin) is derived from the Greek verb υπομιμνήσκω (ypomimnēskō), which means "to remind" or "to bring to mind". The noun form υπομνησιν (ypomnēsin) means "reminder" or "memory aid".

Grammar:
υπομνησιν (ypomnēsin) is a feminine noun in the singular form. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their gender, number, and case. The word υπομνησιν (ypomnēsin) is in the nominative case, which is used for the subject of a sentence.

Description:
υπομνησιν (ypomnēsin) refers to something that serves as a reminder or memory aid. It can be a physical object, such as a note or a photograph, or a mental cue that helps someone remember something.
