Word: υποκρισία (υποκρισει is a misspelling)

Etymology: The word υποκρισία comes from the ancient Greek word ὑπόκρισις (hypókrisis), which means "acting" or "pretending". It is derived from the verb ὑποκρίνομαι (hypokrínomai), which means "to play a part" or "to pretend".

Grammar: υποκρισία is a feminine noun in Greek. Its plural form is υποκρίσεις (hypókriseis).

Description: υποκρισία refers to the act of pretending or feigning. It can also refer to insincerity or hypocrisy. In modern Greek, it is commonly used to describe the behavior of someone who is not being genuine or honest.
