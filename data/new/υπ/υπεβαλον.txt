The word υπεβαλον comes from the Greek verb υποβάλλω (hypoballo), which means "to submit" or "to present". It is derived from the prefix υπο- (hypo-), meaning "under" or "beneath", and the verb βάλλω (ballo), meaning "to throw" or "to cast".
