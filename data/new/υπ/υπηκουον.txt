The word υπηκουον (ypēkouon) is a verb in the ancient Greek language. It is derived from the root word υπακούω (ypakouō), which means "to obey" or "to listen to". The word is formed by adding the suffix -ον (-on) to the present participle stem of the verb.

υπηκουον is a verb in the present tense, active voice, indicative mood, and third person singular form.
