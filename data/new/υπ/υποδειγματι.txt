Word: υποδείγματι

Etymology:
The word υποδείγματι (ypodeigmati) is derived from the Greek words υπό (ypo) meaning "under" and δείγμα (deigma) meaning "example". The suffix -τι (-ti) is added to form the noun.

Grammar:
υποδείγματι is a neuter noun in the singular form.

Description:
υποδείγματι refers to a model or a pattern that is used as an example to follow. It can also refer to a specimen or a sample that is used to demonstrate a particular quality or characteristic. The word is often used in the context of science, research, and education.
