Word: υποδειγματα

Etymology:
The word υποδειγματα comes from the Greek root words υπό (hypo) meaning "under" or "below" and δείγμα (deigma) meaning "example" or "sample". Therefore, υποδειγματα can be translated to mean "under examples" or "samples below".

Grammar:
υποδειγματα is a plural noun in the Greek language. It belongs to the neuter gender and is declined according to the second declension. The nominative singular form of the word is υπόδειγμα (hypodeigma).

Description:
υποδειγματα refers to examples or samples that are used as a reference or model for something else. It can be used in various contexts, such as in science, art, or education. For example, in science, υποδειγματα can refer to specimens used for research or analysis. In art, it can refer to models or prototypes used for creating new works. In education, it can refer to examples used for teaching or learning purposes.
