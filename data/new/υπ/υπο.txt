Etymology: The word "υπο" comes from the ancient Greek word "ὑπό" (hupó), which has the same meaning. 

Grammar: "Υπο" is a preposition and is usually followed by a noun or pronoun in the accusative case. It can also be used as a prefix in compound words. 

Description: "Υπο" is a common word in Greek and is used in many different contexts. For example, it can be used to describe the position of an object in relation to another object, such as "το βιβλίο είναι υπό το τραπέζι" (the book is under the table).
