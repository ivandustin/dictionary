The word υποστρέφω (ypostrefó) comes from the combination of two Greek words: υπό (ypó) meaning "under" or "beneath" and στρέφω (strefó) meaning "to turn" or "to twist". Therefore, the literal meaning of υποστρέφω is "to turn under" or "to turn beneath".
