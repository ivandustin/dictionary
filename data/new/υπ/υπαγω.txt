Word: υπαγω

Etymology:
The word υπαγω (ypago) comes from the Greek roots υπό (ypo) meaning "under" and αγω (ago) meaning "to lead". Therefore, the literal meaning of υπαγω is "to lead under" or "to go under".

Grammar:
υπαγω is a verb in the Greek language. It belongs to the first conjugation and is classified as a regular verb. It is conjugated in all tenses and moods, and its stem is υπαγ-.

Description:
υπαγω is a versatile verb that can be used in various contexts. It can mean "to go under", "to withdraw", "to depart", "to yield", "to submit", "to surrender", "to retire", "to pass away", "to die", and "to be subject to".
