Word: υπερεκτεινομεν

Etymology:
The word υπερεκτεινομεν (hypereteinomen) is a compound word in Greek, consisting of two parts: υπερ (hyper) meaning "over" or "beyond" and εκτεινομεν (ekteinen) meaning "to stretch" or "to extend". Therefore, the word υπερεκτεινομεν can be translated as "over-stretching" or "over-extending".

The word υπερεκτεινομεν is a present participle in Greek, which means that it describes an ongoing action. It is in the active voice and the masculine gender, agreeing with the subject of the sentence. The word can be used as an adjective, modifying a noun, or as a verb, indicating an action.

The word υπερεκτεινομεν can be used to describe a physical or metaphorical action of over-stretching or over-extending something beyond its limits.
