Word: υπομνησω

Etymology:
The word υπομνησω (ypomniso) comes from the Greek root words υπο (ypo) meaning "under" and μνηση (mnisi) meaning "memory". Therefore, the word υπομνησω means "to remind" or "to bring to memory".

Grammar:
υπομνησω is a verb in the Greek language. It is in the first person singular form of the future tense. The verb stem is υπομνησ- and the future tense ending for the first person singular is -ω.

Description:
υπομνησω is a Greek verb that means "to remind" or "to bring to memory". It is often used in everyday conversation and writing to remind someone of something they may have forgotten. The verb can be used in various contexts, such as reminding someone of an appointment, a task, or a piece of information. It is a useful word to know when communicating in Greek.
