Word: υπερηφανοις

Etymology: The word υπερηφανοις comes from the Greek words υπέρ (huper) meaning "above" or "beyond" and ηφανός (efanos) meaning "proud" or "arrogant". 

Grammar: υπερηφανοις is a plural form of the adjective υπερήφανος (huperifanos), which means "proud" or "arrogant". It is in the masculine, feminine, and neuter gender and in the dative case. 

Description: υπερηφανοις is used to describe a group of people who are proud or arrogant. It can be used in a negative sense to criticize someone for being too full of themselves or in a positive sense to describe someone who is confident and self-assured.
