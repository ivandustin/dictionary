The word υπομνησει (ypomnisi) is derived from the Greek verb υπομνησκω (ypomnisko), which means "to remind" or "to remember". The word is formed from the prefix υπο- (ypo-), meaning "under" or "below", and the verb μνησκω (mnisko), meaning "to remember".

υπομνησει is a noun in the singular form, which is the third person singular of the future tense of the verb υπομνησκω (ypomnisko). It can be translated to English as "he/she/it will remind".
