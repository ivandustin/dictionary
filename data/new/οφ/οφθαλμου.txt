Word: οφθαλμού (ophthalmou)

Etymology: The word οφθαλμού comes from the ancient Greek word ὀφθαλμός (ophthalmós), which means "eye". 

Grammar: οφθαλμού is a noun in the genitive case, which is used to show possession or relationship. It is the genitive form of the word οφθαλμός. 

Description: οφθαλμού refers to "of the eye" or "belonging to the eye". It is often used in medical terminology to describe conditions or treatments related to the eye. For example, "οφθαλμολογικός" (ophthalmologikos) means "related to ophthalmology" or "pertaining to the study of the eye".
