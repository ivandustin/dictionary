Word: οφειλει

Etymology: οφειλει comes from the Greek word "οφείλω" (opheílo), which means "to owe" or "to be indebted".

Grammar: οφειλει is a verb in the present tense, third person singular form. It is a regular verb in Greek, and its conjugation follows the pattern of the verb "λέω" (léo).

Description: οφειλει is a verb that expresses the idea of owing something to someone. It can be used in various contexts, such as financial debts, moral obligations, or duties. For example, "Οφείλεις να πληρώσεις το λογαριασμό σου" (Ofeíleis na plirósis to logariasmó sou) means "You owe to pay your bill".
