Word: οφθησομαι

The word οφθησομαι is a future passive indicative form of the Greek verb ὁράω (horaō), which means "to see". The prefix οφθ- (ophth-) is derived from the Greek word ὀφθαλμός (ophthalmos), which means "eye". Therefore, οφθησομαι literally means "I will be seen".
