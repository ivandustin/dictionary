Etymology: The word "πτωχά" (ptocha) comes from the Greek word "πτωχός" (ptochos), which means "poor" or "needy". 

Grammar: "Πτωχά" is a noun in the plural form. It is the accusative case of the noun "πτωχή" (ptochi), which means "poverty" or "destitution".
