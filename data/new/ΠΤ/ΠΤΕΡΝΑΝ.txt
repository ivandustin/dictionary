Grammar: "Πτερναν" is a verb in the Greek language. It is in the present tense, indicative mood, and active voice. It is the third person singular form of the verb.

Description: "Πτερναν" means "he/she/it kicks" in English. It is used to describe the action of forcefully striking something with the foot.
