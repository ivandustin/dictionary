Etymology: The word "υαλινην" (yalinin) comes from the Greek word "υαλί" (yali), meaning "glass".

Grammar: "υαλινην" (yalinin) is a feminine noun in the accusative case. It is the accusative form of the adjective "υαλίνης" (yalinis), which means "made of glass" or "glassy".

Description: "υαλινην" (yalinin) is used to describe something that is made of glass or has the characteristics of glass. It can refer to objects such as glass bottles, windows, or other glassware.
