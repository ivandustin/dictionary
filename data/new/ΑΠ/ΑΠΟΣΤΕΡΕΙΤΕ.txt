Etymology: The word "αποστερείτε" comes from the Greek verb "αποστερώ" (aposteró), which means "to deprive" or "to take away".

Grammar: "Αποστερείτε" is the second person plural form of the verb "αποστερώ" in the present tense. It is used when addressing a group of people and means "you deprive" or "you take away".

Description: "Αποστερείτε" is a verb in the Greek language that expresses the action of depriving or taking away something. It is commonly used in various contexts to describe the act of withholding or denying something from someone or something.
