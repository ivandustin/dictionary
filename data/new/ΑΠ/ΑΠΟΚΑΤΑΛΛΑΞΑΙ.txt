Etymology: The word "αποκαταλλαξαι" comes from the Greek roots "απο-" (apo-), meaning "away" or "from," and "καταλλασσω" (katallasso), meaning "to reconcile" or "to make amends." 

Grammar: "αποκαταλλαξαι" is a verb in the aorist tense, active voice, and infinitive mood. It is the third person singular form of the verb.

Description: "αποκαταλλαξαι" means "to reconcile" or "to make amends" in Greek. It refers to the act of restoring friendly relations or resolving conflicts between two or more parties.
