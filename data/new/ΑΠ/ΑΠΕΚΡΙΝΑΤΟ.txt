Etymology: The word "απεκρινατο" comes from the Greek verb "αποκρίνομαι" (apokrinomai), which means "to answer" or "to reply".

Grammar: "απεκρινατο" is the third person singular, aorist tense form of the verb "αποκρίνομαι" (apokrinomai) in Greek. It is used to indicate that someone answered or replied to something in the past.

Description: "απεκρινατο" is a Greek word that means "he/she/it answered" or "he/she/it replied". It is used to describe the action of responding to a question, statement, or request.
