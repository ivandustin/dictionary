Etymology: The word "απελυσε" (apelyse) comes from the Greek verb "απελυω" (apelyo), which means "to release" or "to set free". 

Grammar: "Απελυσε" is the third person singular form of the aorist tense in the active voice of the Greek verb "απελυω" (apelyo). It is used to indicate that someone or something released or set free someone or something else.

Description: "Απελυσε" is a Greek word that means "he/she/it released" or "he/she/it set free". It is commonly used in various contexts to describe the action of setting someone or something free from a constraint or captivity.
