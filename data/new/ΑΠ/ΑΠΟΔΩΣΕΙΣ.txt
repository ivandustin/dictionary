Etymology: The word "αποδώσεις" comes from the Greek verb "αποδίδωμι" (apodídōmi), which means "to give back" or "to render." It is derived from the combination of the prefix "από" (apó), meaning "from" or "away," and the verb "δίδωμι" (dídōmi), meaning "to give."

Grammar: "Αποδώσεις" is a noun in the plural form. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their gender, number, and case. The plural form indicates that there are multiple instances of the noun.

Description: "Αποδώσεις" refers to the act of giving back or rendering something. It can be used in various contexts, such as returning borrowed items, repaying debts, or delivering performances. The word implies a sense of reciprocity or fulfilling an obligation.
