Grammar: "απελθωσιν" is the third person plural aorist subjunctive form of the verb "απέρχομαι" (aperchomai). In Greek grammar, the aorist tense is used to express a past action without specifying its duration or completion. The subjunctive mood is used to express possibility, doubt, or uncertainty.
