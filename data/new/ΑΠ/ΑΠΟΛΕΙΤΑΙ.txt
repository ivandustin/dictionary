Etymology: The word "απολείται" comes from the Greek verb "απολείπω" (apoleípo), which means "to leave behind" or "to abandon". It is derived from the prefix "απο-" (apo-), meaning "away from", and the verb "λείπω" (leípo), meaning "to leave".

Grammar: "απολείται" is the third person singular form of the verb "απολείπω" in the present tense. It is used to describe an action or state of being that is happening in the present and is performed or experienced by a third person.

Description: "απολείται" is a Greek verb that can be translated as "is left behind" or "is abandoned". It implies that something or someone is being left behind or abandoned by someone else.
