Etymology: The word "απεληλυθεισαν" comes from the Greek verb "απέρχομαι" (aperchomai), which means "to go away" or "to depart". It is formed by adding the prefix "απο-" (apo-), meaning "away" or "from", to the verb "έρχομαι" (erchomai), meaning "to come".

Grammar: "απεληλυθεισαν" is the third person plural, past perfect tense form of the verb "απέρχομαι" (aperchomai). In Greek grammar, the past perfect tense is used to indicate an action that was completed before another past action. The third person plural form indicates that the subject of the verb is a group of people or things.
