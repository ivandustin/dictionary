Etymology: The word "απολογίαν" (apologia) comes from the Greek verb "απολογούμαι" (apologoumai), which means "to defend oneself" or "to make a defense." It is derived from the prefix "από" (apo), meaning "from" or "away," and the verb "λέγω" (lego), meaning "to speak" or "to say."
