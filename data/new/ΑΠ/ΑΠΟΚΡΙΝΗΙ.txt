Etymology: The word "αποκρινηι" (apokrinēi) comes from the Greek verb "αποκρίνομαι" (apokrinomai), which means "to answer" or "to reply."

Grammar: "αποκρινηι" is a verb in the present tense, active voice, and indicative mood. It is conjugated in the third person singular form.

Description: The word "αποκρινηι" means "he/she/it answers" or "he/she/it replies" in Greek. It is used to describe the action of responding to a question, statement, or request.
