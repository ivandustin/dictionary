Etymology: The word "απτου" (aptoú) comes from the Greek verb "απτομαι" (aptomai), which means "to touch" or "to grasp".

Grammar: "απτου" (aptoú) is a form of the Greek verb "απτομαι" (aptomai) in the present tense, second person singular. It is used when addressing someone directly and means "you touch" or "you grasp".

Description: "απτου" (aptoú) is a verb form that indicates the action of touching or grasping. It is used to express the act of physically making contact with something or someone.
