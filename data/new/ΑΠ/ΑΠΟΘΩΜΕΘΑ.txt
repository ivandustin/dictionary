Etymology: The word "αποθωμεθα" comes from the Greek verb "αποθώ" (apotho), which means "to put away" or "to store". The suffix "-μεθα" (-metha) indicates the reflexive form, meaning "we put away" or "we store".

Grammar: "αποθωμεθα" is a first-person plural present middle/passive form of the verb "αποθώ". In Greek grammar, the middle/passive voice indicates that the subject is both the doer and the receiver of the action.

Description: "αποθωμεθα" is used to express the action of putting something away or storing it. It can also be used metaphorically to mean "to set aside" or "to reserve".
