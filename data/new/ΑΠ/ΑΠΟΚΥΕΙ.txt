Etymology: The word "αποκυεί" (apokyei) comes from the Greek verb "αποκυέω" (apokyeo), which means "to give birth" or "to bring forth."

Grammar: "Αποκυεί" is a verb in the present tense, third person singular form. It is derived from the verb "αποκυέω" and is conjugated in the present tense as follows: αποκυεί (apokyei) - he/she/it gives birth.
