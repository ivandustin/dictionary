Grammar: "αποφθέγγεσθαι" is an infinitive verb form in Greek. It belongs to the middle voice, which indicates that the subject is both the doer and the receiver of the action. In English, it can be translated as "to speak out" or "to utter."

Description: "αποφθέγγεσθαι" is a Greek verb that refers to the act of speaking or uttering something. It implies expressing thoughts, opinions, or ideas in a clear and articulate manner. It can also be used to describe the act of making a statement or delivering a speech.
