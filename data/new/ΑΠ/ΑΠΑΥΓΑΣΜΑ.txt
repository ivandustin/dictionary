Etymology: The word "απαυγασμα" comes from the Greek roots "από" (apo), meaning "from" or "away," and "αυγασμός" (avgasmos), meaning "radiation" or "emission." 

Grammar: "Απαυγασμα" is a noun in the Greek language. It is a neuter noun of the second declension. 

Description: "Απαυγασμα" refers to the act or process of emitting or radiating light or energy. It can also be used to describe the resulting light or energy that is emitted.
