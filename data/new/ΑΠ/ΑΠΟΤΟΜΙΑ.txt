Etymology: The word "αποτομία" comes from the Greek word "απότομος" (apotomos), which means "sharp" or "abrupt". 

Grammar: "Αποτομία" is a feminine noun in Greek. It is in the singular form. 

Description: "Αποτομία" refers to the quality or state of being abrupt, sharp, or sudden. It can be used to describe a physical or metaphorical sharpness or abruptness in various contexts, such as in behavior, speech, or changes in direction.
