Etymology: The word "απογεγραμμένων" comes from the Greek roots "από" (apo), meaning "from" or "away," and "γεγραμμένος" (gegrammenos), meaning "written." 

Grammar: "Απογεγραμμένων" is the plural form of the verb "απογράφω" (apografo), which means "to register" or "to record." It is in the passive voice and present tense. 

Description: "Απογεγραμμένων" refers to a group of people or things that have been registered or recorded. It can be used in various contexts, such as in official documents, census records, or any situation where individuals or items are listed or documented.
