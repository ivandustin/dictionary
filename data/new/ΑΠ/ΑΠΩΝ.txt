Etymology: The word "απων" (apōn) comes from the Greek verb "απέχω" (apechō), which means "to be away" or "to be absent". 

Grammar: "Απων" is an adjective in the masculine singular form. It is used to describe someone or something that is absent, missing, or not present.

Description: "Απων" is often used to describe a person who is physically or emotionally absent, or someone who is not fulfilling their responsibilities or obligations. It can also be used to describe something that is lacking or missing in a particular situation.
