Etymology: The word "αποδοχής" comes from the Greek verb "αποδέχομαι" (apodechomai), which means "to accept" or "to receive".

Grammar: "Αποδοχής" is a noun in the genitive case, indicating possession or origin. It is derived from the root word "αποδοχή" (apodoxi), which means "acceptance" or "reception".

Description: "Αποδοχής" refers to the act or state of accepting or receiving something. It can be used to describe the acceptance of an idea, a proposal, a gift, or any other form of acknowledgment or approval.
