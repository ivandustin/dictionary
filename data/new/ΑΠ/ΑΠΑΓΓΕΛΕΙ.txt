Etymology: The word "απαγγελεί" comes from the Greek verb "απαγγέλλω" (apangéllo), which means "to announce" or "to proclaim."

Grammar: "Απαγγελεί" is a verb in the present tense, third person singular form. It belongs to the first conjugation class of Greek verbs, which is characterized by the ending "-εί" in the present tense for the third person singular.

Description: "Απαγγελεί" is used to describe the action of announcing or proclaiming something. It can refer to making a formal announcement, reciting a poem or speech, or delivering a message.
