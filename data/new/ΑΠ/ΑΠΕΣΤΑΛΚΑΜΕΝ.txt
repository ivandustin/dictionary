Etymology: The word "απεσταλμένος" (apostalménos) comes from the Greek verb "αποστέλλω" (apostélo), which means "to send." The suffix "-μένος" (-ménos) is a passive participle ending, indicating that the subject of the verb is being acted upon.

Grammar: "Απεσταλμένος" is the masculine singular form of the passive participle of the verb "αποστέλλω." It is used to describe someone or something that has been sent or dispatched.

Description: "Απεσταλμένος" is an adjective that describes someone or something that has been sent or commissioned to perform a task or fulfill a purpose. It implies that the person or thing is acting on behalf of someone else or a higher authority.
