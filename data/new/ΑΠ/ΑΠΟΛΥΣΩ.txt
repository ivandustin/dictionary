Grammar: "απολύω" (apolyo) is a first-conjugation verb in Greek, which means it follows a regular pattern of conjugation. It is an active verb, meaning it describes an action performed by the subject. It can be conjugated in various tenses, moods, and persons to indicate different aspects and nuances of the action.
