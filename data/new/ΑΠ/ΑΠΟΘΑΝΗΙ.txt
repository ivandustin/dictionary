Etymology: The word "αποθανη" comes from the Greek verb "αποθνῄσκω" (apothnḗskō), which means "to die" or "to pass away."

Grammar: "Αποθανη" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the singular form and belongs to the feminine gender.

Description: "Αποθανη" refers to the act of dying or the state of being dead. It is commonly used to describe the cessation of life or the end of a person's existence.
