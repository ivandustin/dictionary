Etymology: The word "αποκρισεσιν" comes from the Greek verb "αποκρίνομαι" (apokrínomai), which means "to answer" or "to reply". 

Grammar: "αποκρισεσιν" is the plural form of the noun "απόκριση" (apókrisi), which means "answer" or "reply" in Greek. It belongs to the third declension of Greek nouns.

Description: "αποκρισεσιν" refers to multiple answers or replies. It is used when talking about more than one response to a question or a situation.
