Etymology: The word "απουσιαί" comes from the Greek word "απουσία" (apousía), which means "absence" or "lack". 

Grammar: "απουσιαί" is the plural form of the noun "απουσιαί" (apousiá), which is a feminine noun in Greek. It belongs to the first declension and is declined as follows:

Singular:
- Nominative: η απουσία (i apousía) 
- Genitive: της απουσίας (tis apousías)
- Accusative: την απουσία (tin apousía)
- Vocative: απουσία (apousía)

Plural:
- Nominative: οι απουσιαί (i apousié)
- Genitive: των απουσιών (ton apousión)
- Accusative: τις απουσιαίς (tis apousiés)
- Vocative: απουσιαί (apousié)

Description: "απουσιαί" refers to absences or lacks in a plural sense. It can be used to describe multiple instances of absence or lack in various contexts.
