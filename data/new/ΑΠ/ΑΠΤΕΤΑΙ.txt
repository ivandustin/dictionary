Etymology: The word "απτεται" (apte̱tai) comes from the Greek verb "απτομαι" (aptomai), which means "to touch" or "to grasp".

Grammar: "Απτεται" is the third person singular form of the present tense of the verb "απτομαι" (aptomai). It is used to describe an action that is happening in the present and is performed by a third person.

Description: "Απτεται" is a Greek verb that means "he/she/it touches" or "he/she/it grasps". It is used to indicate the action of physically making contact with something or someone.
