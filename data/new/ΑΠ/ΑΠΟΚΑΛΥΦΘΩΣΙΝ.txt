Grammar: "αποκαλυψην" is the accusative form of the noun "αποκάλυψη" (apokalypsi), which means "revelation" or "unveiling". In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their grammatical role in a sentence, such as nominative, accusative, genitive, etc.
