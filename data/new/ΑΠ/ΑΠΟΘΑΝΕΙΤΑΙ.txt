Etymology: The word "αποθανείται" comes from the Greek verb "αποθνήσκω" (apothnḗskō), which means "to die". It is formed by adding the reflexive suffix "-είται" (-eítai) to the verb stem.

Grammar: "Αποθανείται" is the third person singular form of the verb "αποθνήσκω" in the present tense. It is in the passive voice, indicating that the subject is receiving the action of dying.

Description: "Αποθανείται" is a Greek word that means "he/she/it dies" in English. It is used to describe the act of someone or something ceasing to live or exist.
