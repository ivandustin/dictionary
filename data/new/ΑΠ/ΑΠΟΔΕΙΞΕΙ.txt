Etymology: The word "αποδείχει" comes from the Greek verb "αποδεικνύω" (apodeiknyo), which means "to demonstrate" or "to prove".

Grammar: "αποδείχει" is the third person singular form of the verb "αποδεικνύω" in the present tense. It is used to describe an action or state that is being demonstrated or proven by someone or something.

Description: "αποδείχει" is a Greek verb that is commonly used in various contexts to express the act of demonstrating or proving something. It implies providing evidence or arguments to support a claim or statement.
