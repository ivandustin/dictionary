Grammar: "απηεισαν" is the third person plural, aorist active indicative form of the verb "απηεισα" (apheisa). In Greek grammar, the aorist tense is used to express a past action that is viewed as a whole, without specifying its duration or repetition. The active voice indicates that the subject of the verb performs the action.
