Etymology: The word "αποκεκρυμμένον" comes from the Greek roots "από" (apo), meaning "away" or "from," and "κρύπτω" (krypto), meaning "to hide" or "to conceal." 

Grammar: "Αποκεκρυμμένον" is a Greek adjective in the masculine gender, singular number, and nominative case. It is used to describe something that is hidden or concealed.

Description: "Αποκεκρυμμένον" refers to something that is not easily seen or known, intentionally kept secret or concealed. It can be used to describe hidden information, undisclosed intentions, or concealed objects.
