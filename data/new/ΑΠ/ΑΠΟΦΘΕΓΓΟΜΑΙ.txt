Etymology: The word "αποφθέγγομαι" (apofthéngomai) comes from the combination of the prefix "από" (apó), meaning "from," and the verb "φθέγγομαι" (fthéngomai), meaning "to utter" or "to speak." 

Grammar: "αποφθέγγομαι" is a verb in the Greek language. It belongs to the first conjugation class and is conjugated in the present tense as follows: εγώ αποφθέγγομαι (egó apofthéngomai) - I utter, εσύ αποφθέγγεσαι (esý apofthéngese) - you utter, αυτός/αυτή/αυτό αποφθέγγεται (aftós/aftí/aftó apofthéngetai) - he/she/it utters, and so on.

Description: The verb "αποφθέγγομαι" is used to express the act of uttering or speaking something. It can refer to expressing thoughts, opinions, or statements in a clear and articulate manner. The verb is commonly used in formal or literary contexts.
