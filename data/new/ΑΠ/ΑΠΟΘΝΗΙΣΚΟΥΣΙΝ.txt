Etymology: The word "αποθνήσκουσιν" comes from the Greek verb "αποθνήσκω" (apothnēskō), which means "to die". The suffix "-ουσιν" (-ousin) indicates the third person plural present tense form of the verb.

Grammar: "Αποθνήσκουσιν" is a verb in the Greek language. It is in the third person plural present tense form, indicating that the subject of the sentence is a group of people or things that are currently dying.

Description: "Αποθνήσκουσιν" is a word used to describe the action of dying in the Greek language. It is often used in literature, poetry, and religious texts to convey the concept of death or the act of dying.
