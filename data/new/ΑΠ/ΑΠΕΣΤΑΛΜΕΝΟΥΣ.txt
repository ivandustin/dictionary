Etymology: The word "απεσταλμένος" (apostalmenos) comes from the Greek verb "αποστέλλω" (apostello), which means "to send". The suffix "-μένος" (-menos) is a participle ending that indicates the passive voice.

Grammar: "Απεσταλμένος" is the masculine singular form of the adjective. It agrees in gender, number, and case with the noun it modifies. In this form, it is in the nominative case.

Description: "Απεσταλμένος" is an adjective in Greek that means "sent" or "dispatched". It is used to describe someone or something that has been sent or appointed for a specific purpose.
