Etymology: The word "αποδωί" (apodói) comes from the Greek verb "αποδίδωμι" (apodídomi), which means "to give back" or "to return". 

Grammar: "Αποδωί" is a noun in the singular form. It is a feminine noun, and its gender is determined by the ending "-ί".
