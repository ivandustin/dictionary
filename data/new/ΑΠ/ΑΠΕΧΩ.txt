- Απέχω (apecho) - I have
- Απέχεις (apecheis) - You have (singular)
- Απέχει (apechei) - He/She/It has
- Απεχουμε (apechoume) - We have
- Απέχετε (apechete) - You have (plural)
- Απέχουν (apechoun) - They have
