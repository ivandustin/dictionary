Etymology: The word "αποδώσω" (apodóso) comes from the Greek verb "δίδωμι" (dídōmi), which means "to give." The prefix "απο-" (apo-) in this word indicates the action of giving something back or returning it.

Grammar: "Αποδώσω" is the first person singular future tense of the verb "αποδίδωμι" (apodídōmi), which means "to give back" or "to return." It is formed by adding the future tense ending "-σω" (-so) to the verb stem.

Description: "Αποδώσω" is a Greek verb that expresses the action of giving something back or returning it. It can be used in various contexts, such as returning borrowed items, repaying debts, or restoring something to its original state.
