Etymology: The word "αποκρινεται" (apokrinetai) comes from the Greek verb "αποκρίνομαι" (apokrinomai), which means "to answer" or "to reply".

Grammar: "αποκρινεται" is the third person singular form of the verb "αποκρίνομαι" in the present tense. It is in the indicative mood and active voice.

Description: "αποκρινεται" means "he/she/it answers" or "he/she/it replies" in Greek. It is used to describe the action of responding to a question, statement, or request.
