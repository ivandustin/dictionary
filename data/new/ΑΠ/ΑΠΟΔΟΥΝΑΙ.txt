Etymology: The word "αποδουναί" (apodounai) comes from the Greek verb "αποδίδωμι" (apodidomi), which means "to give back" or "to return". It is derived from the prefix "απο-" (apo-), meaning "away" or "from", and the verb "δίδωμι" (didomi), meaning "to give".

Grammar: "αποδουναί" is a verb in the present tense, third person singular form. It is conjugated from the verb "αποδίδωμι" (apodidomi), which is a regular verb in Greek.
