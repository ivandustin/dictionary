Grammar: "Απεδετο" is a verb in the past tense, third person plural form. It is conjugated from the verb "αποδένω" (apodéno), which means "to untie" or "to unfasten." 

Description: "Απεδετο" describes an action that has already taken place in the past, where a group of people untied or unfastened something.
