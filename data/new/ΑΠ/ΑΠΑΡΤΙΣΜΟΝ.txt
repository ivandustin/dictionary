Grammar: "Απαρτισμόν" is a noun in the accusative case, singular form. In Greek grammar, nouns change their form depending on their role in the sentence. The accusative case is used to indicate the direct object of a verb or the object of certain prepositions.

Description: "Απαρτισμόν" refers to the act or process of separating or dividing something into parts or components. It can also refer to the arrangement or organization of these parts. The word is often used in contexts related to composition, construction, or classification.
