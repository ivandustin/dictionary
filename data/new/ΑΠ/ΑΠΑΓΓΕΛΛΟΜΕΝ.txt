Grammar: "απαγγελλομεν" is the present tense, indicative mood, active voice, first person plural form of the verb "απαγγέλλω" (apangéllō).

Description: "απαγγελλομεν" is a Greek verb that is used to express the action of announcing or proclaiming something.
