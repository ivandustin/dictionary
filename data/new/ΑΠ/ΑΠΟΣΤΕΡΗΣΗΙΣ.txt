Etymology: The word "αποστερήσεις" comes from the Greek verb "αποστερώ" (aposteró), which means "to deprive" or "to withhold."

Grammar: "Αποστερήσεις" is a noun in the plural form. It belongs to the third declension in Greek grammar.

Description: "Αποστερήσεις" refers to the act of depriving or withholding something from someone. It can also be used to describe the state of being deprived or lacking something.
