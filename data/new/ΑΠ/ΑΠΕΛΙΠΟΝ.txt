Grammar: "Απελπισμός" is a masculine noun in the singular form. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their gender, number, and case. As a noun, it can be declined and used in various grammatical contexts.

Description: "Απελπισμός" refers to the state of being in despair or feeling hopeless. It is an intense feeling of discouragement, often accompanied by a sense of powerlessness or resignation. It can be used to describe a person's emotional state or a situation that seems hopeless.
