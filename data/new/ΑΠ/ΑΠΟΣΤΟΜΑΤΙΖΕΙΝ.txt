Etymology: The word "αποστοματιζειν" comes from the Greek roots "από" (apo), meaning "away from," and "στόμα" (stoma), meaning "mouth." 

Grammar: "αποστοματιζειν" is a verb in the present tense, active voice, and indicative mood. It is the third person singular form of the verb.

Description: "αποστοματιζειν" is a Greek verb that means "to speak out," "to express oneself," or "to voice one's opinion." It refers to the act of articulating thoughts or ideas verbally.
