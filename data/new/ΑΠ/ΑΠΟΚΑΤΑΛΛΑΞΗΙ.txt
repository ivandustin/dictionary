Etymology: The word "αποκαταλλαξη" comes from the Greek roots "απο" (apo), meaning "away" or "from," and "καταλλαγη" (katallagi), meaning "reconciliation" or "restoration." 

Grammar: "Αποκαταλλαξη" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the singular form and belongs to the feminine gender.

Description: "Αποκαταλλαξη" refers to the act or process of reconciling or restoring a relationship or situation. It implies the resolution of conflicts or differences and the establishment of harmony or agreement.
