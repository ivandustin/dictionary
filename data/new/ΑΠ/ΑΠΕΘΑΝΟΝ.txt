Etymology: The word "απεθανον" comes from the Greek verb "αποθνῄσκω" (apothnēskō), which means "to die" or "to pass away."

Grammar: "Απεθανον" is the aorist tense of the verb "αποθνῄσκω" (apothnēskō), which indicates a completed action in the past. It is the third person singular form.

Description: "Απεθανον" is a Greek word that means "he/she/it died" in English. It is used to describe the action of someone or something passing away or ceasing to live.
