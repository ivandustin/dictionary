Grammar: "Απεχουσαν" is the third person singular form of the verb "απέχω" in the past tense. It is in the active voice and indicative mood. The verb "απέχω" belongs to the first conjugation class in Greek.

Description: "Απεχουσαν" is used to describe an action that took place in the past and was performed by a third person singular subject. It indicates that the subject was keeping away or staying distant from something or someone.
