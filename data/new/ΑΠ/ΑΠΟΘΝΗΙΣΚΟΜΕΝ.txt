Etymology: The word "αποθνήσκωμεν" comes from the Greek verb "αποθνήσκω" (apothnēskō), which means "to die". The suffix "-μεν" (-men) is added to the verb to indicate the first person plural form, making it "αποθνήσκωμεν" (apothnēskōmen), meaning "we die".

Grammar: "Αποθνήσκωμεν" is the first person plural form of the verb "αποθνήσκω" (apothnēskō) in the present tense. It is used to express the action of dying performed by a group of people.

Description: "Αποθνήσκωμεν" is a word in the Greek language that specifically refers to the act of dying in the first person plural form. It is often used in literature, poetry, or philosophical discussions to convey the concept of mortality or the inevitability of death.
