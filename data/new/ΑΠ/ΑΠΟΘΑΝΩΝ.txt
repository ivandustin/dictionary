Etymology: The word "αποθανών" comes from the Greek verb "αποθνήσκω" (apothnēskō), which means "to die". It is derived from the combination of the prefix "απο-" (apo-), meaning "away" or "from", and the verb "θνήσκω" (thnēskō), meaning "to die".

Grammar: "αποθανών" is the present participle form of the verb "αποθνήσκω" (apothnēskō), which means "dying" or "being in the process of dying". It is used to describe someone or something that is currently in the state of dying.
