Etymology: The word "απελθουσαι" comes from the Greek verb "απέρχομαι" (aperchomai), which means "to go away" or "to depart."

Grammar: "απελθουσαι" is the third person singular, aorist tense, middle voice form of the verb "απέρχομαι" (aperchomai). The aorist tense is used to express a completed action in the past, and the middle voice indicates that the subject is performing the action on itself.

Description: "απελθουσαι" is a Greek verb form that describes the action of someone or something going away or departing. It is used to indicate that the subject has completed the action of leaving a place.
