Etymology: The word "απόθεσις" (apothēsis) comes from the Greek verb "ἀποτίθημι" (apotithēmi), which means "to place away" or "to deposit." It is derived from the prefix "ἀπό" (apo), meaning "away," and the verb "τίθημι" (tithēmi), meaning "to place" or "to put."

Grammar: "Απόθεσις" is a feminine noun in Greek. It belongs to the second declension and has the genitive form "αποθέσεως" (apotheseos). In the plural form, it becomes "αποθέσεις" (apotheseis).
