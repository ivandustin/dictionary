Etymology: The word "αποσκιασμα" comes from the Greek roots "από" (apo), meaning "away" or "from," and "σκιά" (skia), meaning "shadow." 

Grammar: "Αποσκιασμα" is a noun in the Greek language. It is a neuter noun and its plural form is "αποσκιάσματα" (aposkiasmata).

Description: "Αποσκιασμα" refers to the act or process of casting a shadow or the resulting shadow itself. It can also be used metaphorically to describe something that obscures or overshadows something else.
