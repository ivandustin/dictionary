Etymology: The word "αποκυλίσει" comes from the Greek verb "αποκυλίνω" (apokylíno), which means "to roll away" or "to unroll". It is derived from the prefix "απο-" (apo-), meaning "away from", and the verb "κυλίνω" (kylíno), meaning "to roll".

Grammar: "αποκυλίσει" is the third person singular future tense form of the verb "αποκυλίνω" (apokylíno). In Greek grammar, verbs are conjugated based on the person, number, tense, and mood. The future tense indicates an action that will happen in the future.

Description: "αποκυλίσει" is a Greek verb form that translates to "he/she/it will roll away" or "he/she/it will unroll" in English. It describes a future action of rolling or unrolling something.
