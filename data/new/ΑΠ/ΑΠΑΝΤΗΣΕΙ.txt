Etymology: The word "απαντησει" comes from the Greek verb "απαντάω" (apantáo), which means "to answer" or "to reply". 

Grammar: "απαντησει" is the third person singular form of the verb "απαντάω" in the future tense. It is used to indicate that someone or something will answer or reply in the future.

Description: "απαντησει" is a Greek word that refers to the act of answering or replying. It can be used in various contexts, such as in conversations, correspondence, or any situation where a response is expected.
