Etymology: The word "απαρνηση" (aparnisi) comes from the Greek verb "απαρνούμαι" (aparnoumai), which means "to deny" or "to renounce." 

Grammar: "Απαρνηση" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the singular form and belongs to the feminine gender. 

Description: "Απαρνηση" refers to the act of denying or renouncing something or someone. It can also be used to describe the act of disowning or rejecting a belief, idea, or principle.
