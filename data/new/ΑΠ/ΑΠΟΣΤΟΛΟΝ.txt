Grammar: "Αποστολον" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the singular form and belongs to the neuter gender. In the nominative case, it is spelled as "αποστολον". In other cases, its form may change depending on the grammatical context.

Description: "Αποστολον" refers to the concept of "apostle" in English. In Christian theology, an apostle is a person who is sent by Jesus Christ to spread his teachings and establish his church.
