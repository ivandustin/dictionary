Etymology: The word "αποστασία" comes from the Greek word "απόσταση" (apóstasi), which means "distance" or "separation". It is derived from the verb "αποσταίνω" (apostaíno), meaning "to stand away" or "to separate".

Grammar: "Αποστασία" is a feminine noun in Greek. It belongs to the third declension and has the plural form "αποστασίες" (apostasíes).

Description: "Αποστασία" refers to the act or state of being distant or separated. It can be used to describe physical distance or separation, as well as metaphorical or emotional distance between people or ideas.
