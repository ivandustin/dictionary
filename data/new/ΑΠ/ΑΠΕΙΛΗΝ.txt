Etymology: The word "απειλην" (apeilēn) comes from the Greek verb "απειλώ" (apeilō), which means "to threaten" or "to menace."

Grammar: "Απειλην" is a noun in the accusative case, singular number, and masculine gender in the Greek language.

Description: "Απειλην" refers to a threat or a menace. It is used to describe a situation or action that poses a danger or harm to someone or something.
