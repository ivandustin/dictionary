It is derived from the prefix "απο-" (apo-), meaning "away" or "from", and the verb "καθιστώ" (kathistó), meaning "to establish" or "to set up".

Grammar: "αποκαταστησει" is the third person singular form of the future tense of the verb "αποκαθιστώ" (apokathistó). In Greek grammar, verbs are conjugated based on the person, number, tense, and mood. The future tense indicates an action that will happen in the future.
