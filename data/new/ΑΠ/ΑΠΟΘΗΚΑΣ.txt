Etymology: The word "αποθήκας" (apothekas) comes from the Greek word "αποθήκη" (apothiki), which means "warehouse" or "storehouse". It is derived from the verb "αποτίθημι" (apotithemi), meaning "to put away" or "to store".

Grammar: "Αποθήκας" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the masculine gender and singular number. In the nominative case, it is spelled as "αποθήκας".
