Etymology: The word "απελθω" (apelltho) comes from the Greek verb "απέρχομαι" (aperchomai), which means "to go away" or "to depart". It is derived from the prefix "απο-" (apo-), meaning "away from", and the verb "έρχομαι" (erchomai), meaning "to come".

Grammar: "απελθω" is a verb in the Greek language. It belongs to the aorist tense and is in the active voice. It is conjugated in the first person singular form.
