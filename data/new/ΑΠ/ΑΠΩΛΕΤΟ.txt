Etymology: The word "απολετο" comes from the Greek word "απολείπω" (apoleípo), which means "to leave behind" or "to abandon."

Grammar: "Απολετο" is a verb in the Greek language. It is in the past tense, third person singular form.

Description: "Απολετο" is used to describe an action of leaving something or someone behind, abandoning or forsaking them. It indicates that the subject has left or abandoned something in the past.
