Etymology: The word "αποκεκυλισμένον" comes from the Greek roots "από" (apo), meaning "from" or "away," and "κεκυλισμένον" (kekylismeno), meaning "rolled" or "curled." 

Grammar: "Αποκεκυλισμένον" is a Greek adjective in the masculine gender, singular number, and nominative case. It is used to describe something that has been unrolled or unfolded.
