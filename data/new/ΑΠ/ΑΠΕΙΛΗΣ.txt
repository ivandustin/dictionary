Etymology: The word "απειλης" (apeilēs) comes from the Greek verb "απειλώ" (apeilō), which means "to threaten" or "to menace". 

Grammar: "Απειλης" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the singular form and belongs to the masculine gender. 

Description: "Απειλης" refers to a threat or a menace. It can be used to describe a situation or an action that is intended to cause harm, fear, or danger.
