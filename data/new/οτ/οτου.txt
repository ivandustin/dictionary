Word: οτου

Etymology: οτου is a conjunction in Greek that means "when" or "whenever". It is derived from the combination of the words "οτε" (ote) and "ου" (ou).

Grammar: οτου is a subordinating conjunction that introduces a subordinate clause indicating time. It is often used to express a repeated or habitual action in the past or present.

Description: οτου is a common word in Greek and is used in both written and spoken language. It is an important conjunction for expressing time relationships in sentences.
