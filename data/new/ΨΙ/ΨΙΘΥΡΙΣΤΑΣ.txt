Etymology: The word "ψιθυριστας" comes from the Greek word "ψιθυρίζω" (psithyrizo), which means "to whisper" or "to murmur".

Grammar: In Greek, "ψιθυριστας" is a noun in the masculine gender. It is in the nominative case, singular form.

Description: "ψιθυριστας" refers to a person who whispers or murmurs. It can be used to describe someone who speaks in a soft, hushed voice or someone who communicates secretly or confidentially.
