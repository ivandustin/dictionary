The word "εορακα" (eoraka) is a Greek verb in the perfect tense, which means "I have seen" or "I saw".

Grammar: "Εορακα" is the first person singular form of the perfect tense, which means that it refers to the speaker who has seen something in the past. The verb can also be conjugated in other persons and numbers, such as "εορακες" (eorakes) for the second person singular, "εορακεν" (eoraken) for the third person singular, and so on.
