Word: εξεπετασα

Etymology: The word εξεπετασα comes from the Greek verb εξετάζω (exetazo), which means "to examine" or "to investigate". The prefix εξ- (ex-) means "out" or "thoroughly", while the root word τάζω (tazo) means "to search" or "to examine".

Grammar: εξεπετασα is a verb in the past tense, first person singular form. It is conjugated from the verb εξετάζω (exetazo) and means "I examined" or "I investigated".

Description: εξεπετασα is a word that describes the action of examining or investigating something thoroughly. It can be used in various contexts, such as in academic research, medical examinations, or legal investigations. The word implies a careful and detailed examination of a subject, with the aim of uncovering important information or insights.
