The word "εξεπλει" is a verb in the Greek language. 

Etymology: The word is derived from the prefix "εξ-" which means "out of" or "from" and the verb "πλέω" which means "to sail". Therefore, "εξεπλει" can be translated as "to sail out of" or "to depart by ship".

Grammar: "Εξεπλει" is a third person singular past tense verb in the indicative mood. It is conjugated from the verb "πλέω" in the aorist tense. 

Description: The word "εξεπλει" describes the action of departing or sailing out of a place by ship. It can be used in various contexts such as describing a ship leaving a port or a person leaving a place by boat.
