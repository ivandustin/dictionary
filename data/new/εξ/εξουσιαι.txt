Word: εξουσίαι

Etymology: The word εξουσίαι comes from the Greek word εξουσία (exousia), which means power or authority. The suffix -αι (-ai) is added to form the plural.

Grammar: εξουσίαι is a feminine noun in the plural form. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their gender, number, and case. The plural form indicates that there are multiple authorities or powers.

Description: εξουσίαι refers to authorities or powers, often used in a legal or political context. It can also refer to the rights or privileges granted to someone in a position of power. In the New Testament, the word is often used to describe the authority of Jesus Christ.
