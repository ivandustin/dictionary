Word: εξανεστησαν

Etymology: The word εξανεστησαν is a compound word in Greek, consisting of the prefix εξαν- (meaning "out of" or "from") and the verb εστησαν (meaning "they stood"). Therefore, the word εξανεστησαν means "they stood up" or "they rose up".

Grammar: εξανεστησαν is a verb in the past tense, third person plural form. It is a regular verb in Greek, following the conjugation pattern of the verb "στέκω" (to stand).

Description: εξανεστησαν is a word that describes an action of a group of people standing up or rising up. It can be used in various contexts, such as a political uprising, a protest, or a rebellion. The word carries a sense of defiance and resistance against authority or oppression.
