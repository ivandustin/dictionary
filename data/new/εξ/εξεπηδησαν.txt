Word: εξεπηδησαν

Etymology: The word εξεπηδησαν comes from the Greek verb εξεπηδώ, which means "to jump out" or "to spring forth". The prefix εξ- indicates "out of" or "from", while the root πηδώ means "to jump".

Grammar: εξεπηδησαν is a verb in the past tense, third person plural form. It is conjugated from the verb εξεπηδώ, which is a regular verb in Greek.

Description: εξεπηδησαν means "they jumped out" or "they sprang forth". It is often used to describe sudden and unexpected movements or actions.
