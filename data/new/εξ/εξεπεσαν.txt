Word: εξεπεσαν

Etymology: εξεπεσαν is a Greek verb that comes from the combination of the prefix "εξ-" which means "out of" or "from" and the verb "πίπτω" which means "to fall". Therefore, εξεπεσαν means "they fell out of" or "they fell from".

Grammar: εξεπεσαν is a third-person plural past tense verb in Greek. It is conjugated from the verb "πίπτω" (to fall) in the aorist tense. The verb is composed of the prefix "εξ-" and the root "πεσ-". The ending "-αν" indicates the third-person plural past tense.

Description: εξεπεσαν is a verb that describes an action of falling out of or falling from something. It can be used in various contexts, such as falling out of a tree, falling from a building, or falling off a bike. The verb is commonly used in Greek literature and everyday language.
