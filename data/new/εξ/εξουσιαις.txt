Word: εξουσίαις

Etymology: The word εξουσίαις comes from the Greek word εξουσία (exousia), which means "power" or "authority". The suffix -αις (-ais) is a plural ending, indicating that the word refers to multiple powers or authorities.

Grammar: εξουσίαις is a feminine noun in the dative plural form. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their gender, number, and case. The dative case is used to indicate the indirect object of a verb or the object of certain prepositions.

Description: εξουσίαις refers to powers or authorities, often in a legal or political context. It can also refer to the rights or privileges granted to someone in a position of authority.
