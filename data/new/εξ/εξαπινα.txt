Word: εξαπινα

Etymology: The word εξαπινα comes from the Greek adverb εξαπίνης (exapínis), which means "suddenly" or "unexpectedly".

Grammar: εξαπινα is a noun in the feminine gender in the singular form.

Description: εξαπινα is used to describe something that happens suddenly or unexpectedly. It can refer to a surprise or a sudden change in circumstances. For example, "Η εξαπίνης βροχή μας έκανε να τρέξουμε στο σπίτι" (The sudden rain made us run to the house).
