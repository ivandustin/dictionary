The word "εξωμολογησεν" (exomologesen) is a Greek verb that means "to confess" or "to acknowledge." 

Etymology: The word is derived from the Greek prefix "ex-" meaning "out" or "away from," and the verb "homologeo" meaning "to agree" or "to confess." 

Grammar: "Εξωμολογησεν" is the third person singular aorist indicative active form of the verb. 

Description: The word is often used in a religious context to refer to confessing one's sins or acknowledging one's faith. It can also be used more generally to mean admitting or confessing to something.
