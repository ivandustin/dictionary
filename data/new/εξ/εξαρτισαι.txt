Word: εξαρτισαι

Etymology: The word εξαρτισαι comes from the Greek verb εξαρτώ, which means "to depend on" or "to be connected to". The suffix -σαι indicates that the verb is in the reflexive form, meaning "to depend on oneself" or "to rely on oneself".

Grammar: εξαρτισαι is a reflexive verb in the present tense, second person singular form. It is conjugated from the verb εξαρτώ, which is a regular verb in the Greek language.

Description: εξαρτισαι means "to rely on oneself" or "to depend on oneself". It is often used to describe a person who is self-sufficient and able to take care of themselves without relying on others. The word can also be used in a broader sense to describe a situation or circumstance that requires self-reliance and independence.
