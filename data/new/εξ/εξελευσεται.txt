Word: εξελευσεται

Etymology: 
The word εξελευσεται is a Greek verb in the future tense, third person singular form of the verb εξέρχομαι (exerchomai), which means "to go out" or "to come out". The root of the word is έξω (exo), which means "outside".

Grammar:
εξελευσεται is a future tense verb in the third person singular form. In Greek, verbs change their endings to indicate the tense, mood, and person. The future tense indicates an action that will happen in the future. The third person singular form indicates that the subject of the verb is a singular noun or pronoun.

Description:
εξελευσεται means "he/she/it will go out" or "he/she/it will come out". The word can be used in various contexts, such as describing a person leaving a building or a group of people emerging from a cave. It is a common verb in Greek and is used in everyday conversation.
