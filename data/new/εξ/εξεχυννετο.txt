Word: εξεχυννετο

Etymology: The word εξεχυννετο comes from the Greek verb εξεχύννω (exechýnno), which means "to pour out" or "to spill over". It is derived from the prefix εξ- (ex-), meaning "out of", and the verb χύννω (chýnno), meaning "to pour".

Grammar: εξεχυννετο is a verb in the past tense, third person singular form. It is in the indicative mood and the active voice. The verb stem is εξεχυνν-, and the suffix -ετο indicates the past tense and third person singular.

Description: εξεχυννετο describes an action that happened in the past, where something was poured out or spilled over. The context of the sentence would determine what exactly was poured out or spilled over.
