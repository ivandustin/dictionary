Word: εξουθενημένα

Etymology: The word εξουθενημένα comes from the Greek verb εξουθενώνω (exouthenóno), which means "to make nothing of" or "to despise". The suffix -μένα (-mena) is a feminine plural form that indicates the past participle of the verb.

Grammar: εξουθενημένα is a feminine plural adjective in the nominative case. It agrees in gender and number with the noun it modifies.

Description: εξουθενημένα is an adjective that describes something that has been made to feel worthless or insignificant. It can be used to describe a person, an object, or an idea that has been devalued or disrespected. The word carries a negative connotation and implies a sense of humiliation or defeat.
