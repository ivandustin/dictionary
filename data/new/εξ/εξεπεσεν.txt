The word "εξεπεσεν" is a Greek verb that means "fell out" or "dropped out". It is derived from the prefix "εξ-" which means "out of" and the verb "πίπτω" which means "to fall". In terms of grammar, "εξεπεσεν" is the third person singular aorist indicative active form of the verb. This means that it refers to a completed action in the past, performed by a single subject.
