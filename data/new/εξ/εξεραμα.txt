Word: εξεραμα

Etymology: The word εξεραμα comes from the Greek verb εξεράω (exerao), which means "to examine thoroughly" or "to investigate". 

Grammar: εξεραμα is a noun in the neuter gender and singular number. 

Description: εξεραμα refers to a thorough investigation or examination of something. It can also be used to describe a detailed report or analysis of a particular subject.
