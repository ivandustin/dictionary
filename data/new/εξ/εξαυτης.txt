The word "εξαυτης" (exautis) is a Greek adverb that means "immediately" or "instantly". 

Etymology: The word is derived from the prefix "ex-" meaning "out of" or "from" and the noun "autes" meaning "self". 

Grammar: "Εξαυτης" is an adverb and does not change form based on gender or number. 

Description: The word is commonly used in modern Greek to express immediacy or urgency in a situation. It can be used in a variety of contexts, such as in a sentence like "Πρέπει να φύγω εξαυτης" (I have to leave immediately).
