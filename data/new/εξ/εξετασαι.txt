Word: εξετασαι

Etymology: The word εξετασαι comes from the Greek verb "εξετάζω" (exetazo), which means "to examine" or "to investigate". It is derived from the prefix "εξ-" (ex-), which means "out of" or "from", and the verb "τάσσω" (tasso), which means "to arrange" or "to put in order".

Grammar: εξετασαι is a second person singular present active indicative form of the verb "εξετάζω" (exetazo). It is used to address one person and indicates that the action of examining or investigating is currently taking place.

Description: εξετασαι is a verb that is commonly used in Greek to express the act of examining or investigating something or someone. It can be used in various contexts, such as in education, law, medicine, and science. The word implies a thorough and careful examination or investigation, often with the goal of discovering or revealing important information.
