Word: εξεπλησσοντο

Etymology: 
The word εξεπλησσοντο comes from the Greek verb "ἐξίστημι" (exístēmi), which means "to amaze, astonish, or surprise." The word is formed by adding the prefix "ἐξ-" (ex-), which means "out of" or "from," to the verb "ἵστημι" (hístēmi), which means "to stand."

Grammar:
εξεπλησσοντο is the third person plural, imperfect, middle/passive voice form of the Greek verb "ἐξίστημι" (exístēmi). The middle/passive voice indicates that the subject is both the agent and the recipient of the action. In this case, the subject is plural and the action is being amazed, astonished, or surprised.

Description:
εξεπλησσοντο is a Greek verb that describes a state of being amazed, astonished, or surprised. The word can be used to describe a wide range of emotions, from mild surprise to complete shock.
