Word: εξεχυθησαν

Etymology: The word εξεχυθησαν comes from the Greek verb εκχύνω (ekchyno), which means "to pour out" or "to spill". The prefix εξ- (ex-) means "out of" or "from", so εξεχυθησαν literally means "they were poured out".

Grammar: εξεχυθησαν is a verb in the past tense, third person plural form. It is the aorist tense of the verb εκχύνω, which indicates a completed action in the past.

Description: εξεχυθησαν means "they were poured out" or "they spilled out". It can be used to describe a liquid or substance that has been spilled or poured out of a container. It can also be used metaphorically to describe a situation where emotions or feelings have been expressed or released in a sudden or intense way.
