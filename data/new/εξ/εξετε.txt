Word: εξετε

Etymology: The word εξετε comes from the Greek verb "εξετάζω" (exetazo), which means "to examine" or "to investigate". 

Grammar: εξετε is a verb in the second person plural, present tense, active voice, and indicative mood. 

Description: εξετε is a Greek verb that means "you examine" or "you investigate". It is often used in academic or scientific contexts to refer to the process of researching or studying a particular topic. The verb can also be used in a more general sense to describe the act of carefully examining or scrutinizing something.
