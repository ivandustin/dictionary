Word: εξεπτυσάτε

Etymology: The word εξεπτυσάτε is a compound word in Greek, consisting of the prefix εξ- (meaning "out of" or "from") and the verb πτύω (meaning "to spit"). Therefore, εξεπτυσάτε literally means "to spit out" or "to eject".
