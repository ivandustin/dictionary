Word: εξεχυθη

Etymology: εξεχυθη is a Greek word that comes from the verb εκχύνω (ekchyno), which means "to pour out" or "to spill."

Grammar: εξεχυθη is the third person singular aorist passive indicative form of the verb εκχύνω (ekchyno). In Greek, the aorist tense is used to describe a past action that is viewed as a single event, while the passive voice indicates that the subject of the sentence is being acted upon.

Description: εξεχυθη can be translated to English as "was poured out" or "was spilled." It is often used in the context of liquids being spilled or poured out, but can also be used metaphorically to describe emotions or ideas being expressed or revealed.
