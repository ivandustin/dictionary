The word εξετείνεν is a Greek verb that comes from the root word "τείνω" (teíno), which means "to stretch" or "to extend". The prefix "εξ-" (ex-) means "out" or "from", so εξετείνεν can be translated as "to stretch out" or "to extend out".

εξετείνεν is a third-person singular past tense verb in the active voice. It is in the aorist tense, which indicates a completed action in the past. The verb is also in the indicative mood, which is used to make a statement or ask a question.
