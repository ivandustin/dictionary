Word: εξηγουμενων

Etymology: 
The word εξηγουμενων comes from the Greek verb εξηγέομαι (exēgéomai), which means "to explain" or "to interpret". The suffix -ων (-ōn) is a present participle ending, indicating that the word refers to something that is currently being explained or interpreted.

Grammar:
εξηγουμενων is the genitive plural form of the present participle of the verb εξηγέομαι. In Greek, the genitive case is used to indicate possession or relationship, and the plural form indicates that there are multiple things being explained or interpreted.

Description:
εξηγουμενων can be translated to "of those being explained/interpreted". It is often used in Greek texts to refer to a group of people or things that are currently being explained or interpreted. For example, in a philosophical text, εξηγουμενων might refer to a group of concepts or ideas that the author is currently discussing and interpreting.
