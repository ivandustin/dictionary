Word: εξηγησαμενος

Etymology: 
The word εξηγησαμενος comes from the Greek verb εξηγέομαι (exēgéomai), which means "to explain" or "to interpret". The word is formed from the prefix εξ- (ex-), meaning "out of" or "from", and the verb ηγέομαι (hēgéomai), meaning "to lead" or "to guide".

Grammar:
εξηγησαμενος is the past participle form of the verb εξηγέομαι (exēgéomai). It is a masculine singular form, which means that it refers to a male who has explained or interpreted something. The word can be used as an adjective, modifying a noun, or as a predicate, describing the subject of a sentence.

Description:
εξηγησαμενος is a word that describes someone who has explained or interpreted something. It can be used to refer to a person who has provided a clear and concise explanation of a complex topic or who has translated a text from one language to another.
