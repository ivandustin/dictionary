Grammar: εξεγερει is a verb in the present tense, third person singular form. It is conjugated from the verb εξεγείρω (exegero), which is a regular verb in Greek.

Description: εξεγερει means "he/she/it wakes up" or "he/she/it rouses" in English. It is often used in the context of waking up from sleep or arousing someone from a state of rest or inactivity.
