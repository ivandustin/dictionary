In terms of grammar, "εξεκλεισθη" is a passive voice verb in the aorist tense. It is conjugated in the third person singular form, which means that it refers to a single subject who is the recipient of the action. 

Overall, "εξεκλεισθη" is a word that describes the act of being excluded or shut out from something. It can be used in a variety of contexts, such as social exclusion, exclusion from a group or organization, or exclusion from a particular event or activity.
