Word: εξακοσιοι

Etymology: The word εξακοσιοι (exakosioi) is derived from the Greek words εξακόσια (exakosia) meaning six hundred and εκατόν (ekaton) meaning hundred. It literally means "six hundred".

Grammar: εξακοσιοι is an adjective in Greek language. It is in masculine plural form and agrees with the noun it modifies in gender, number and case.

Description: εξακοσιοι is used to describe a quantity of six hundred. It can be used to describe a number of things such as six hundred people, six hundred dollars, six hundred pages, etc. In Greek history, it is often used to describe the number of soldiers in an army or the number of ships in a fleet.
