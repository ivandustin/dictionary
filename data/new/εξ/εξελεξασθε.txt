The word is in the aorist tense and the middle voice, indicating that the subject is performing the action on themselves.

In terms of grammar, "εξελεξασθε" is a second person plural verb, meaning it is used when addressing a group of people. It is also in the indicative mood, which is used to make statements or ask questions.

The word can be translated to mean "you chose for yourselves" or "you selected for yourselves". It implies that the subject had a choice and made a decision.
