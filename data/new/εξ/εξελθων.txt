The word "εξελθων" is a Greek verb that means "to go out" or "to exit". It is derived from the prefix "εξ-" which means "out of" and the verb "ερχομαι" which means "to come". In terms of grammar, "εξελθων" is the aorist participle of the verb "εξερχομαι" which indicates a completed action in the past. It can be translated as "having gone out" or "having exited".
