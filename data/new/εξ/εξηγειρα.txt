The word "εξηγειρα" (exigira) is a verb in the Greek language. 

Etymology: The word is derived from the Greek root "εξ-" (ex-), meaning "out of" or "from," and "ηγείρω" (igiro), meaning "to raise" or "to awaken." 

Grammar: The verb is in the first person singular, past tense, indicative mood. 

Description: The word means "I woke up" or "I raised up" in English. It is commonly used to describe the act of waking up from sleep or raising something up from a lower position.
