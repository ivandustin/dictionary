The word "εξεπεσατε" is a verb in the Greek language. Its etymology can be broken down into two parts: "εξ-" which means "out of" or "from" and "πεσατε" which is the second person plural form of the verb "πίπτω" meaning "to fall". Therefore, "εξεπεσατε" can be translated to "you fell out of" or "you fell from".

In terms of grammar, "εξεπεσατε" is in the second person plural form of the aorist tense. This tense is used to describe a completed action in the past. The prefix "εξ-" adds the meaning of "out of" or "from" to the verb "πίπτω".

Overall, "εξεπεσατε" is a verb that describes a completed action of falling out of or from something.
