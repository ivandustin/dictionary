The word "ιατρου" (iatrou) is a Greek noun that means "doctor" or "physician". It is derived from the Greek word "ιατρός" (iatros), which also means "doctor" or "healer". In terms of grammar, "ιατρου" is in the genitive case, which is used to indicate possession or relationship. Therefore, "ιατρου" can be translated as "of the doctor" or "belonging to the doctor".
