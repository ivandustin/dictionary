Word: βραχιονι

Etymology:
The word βραχιονι (brakhioni) is derived from the Greek word βραχίων (brakhíon), which means "arm". It is a noun in the singular form.

In Greek, βραχιονι (brakhioni) is a noun of the second declension. It is masculine in gender and singular in number.

Description:
βραχιονι (brakhioni) refers to the arm, specifically the upper arm or bicep. It is a common word in Greek and is used in various contexts, such as anatomy, sports, and everyday conversation. The word can also be used metaphorically to refer to strength or power.
