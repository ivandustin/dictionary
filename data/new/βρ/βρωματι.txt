The word "βρωματι" (bromati) is a Greek noun that means "food" or "nourishment". 

Etymology: The word comes from the Greek verb "βρωματίζω" (bromatizo), which means "to feed" or "to nourish". 

Grammar: βρωματι is a neuter noun in the singular form. 

Description: The word is commonly used in modern Greek to refer to any type of food or meal. It can also be used in a figurative sense to refer to something that nourishes or sustains a person, such as knowledge or spiritual guidance.
