Word: βρωματα

Etymology:
The word βρωματα (bromata) is derived from the Greek word βρωμα (broma), which means food or nourishment. The suffix -ατα (-ata) is a pluralizing suffix in Greek.

βρωματα is a plural noun in Greek. It is the plural form of βρωμα (broma), which means food or nourishment. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their gender, number, and case. βρωματα is a neuter noun in the nominative plural case.

βρωματα refers to food or nourishment in general. It can be used to describe any type of food or nourishment, whether it is for humans or animals.
