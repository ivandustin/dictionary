Word: βρεχει

Etymology: βρεχει comes from the Greek verb βρέχω (brecho), which means "to rain". 

Grammar: βρεχει is the present tense, third person singular form of the verb βρέχω (brecho). It is used to describe the action of raining in the present moment. 

Description: βρεχει is a Greek word that means "it is raining". It is commonly used in weather reports and everyday conversation to describe the current weather conditions. The word is often accompanied by other words to describe the intensity or duration of the rain, such as "heavy rain" or "light rain".
