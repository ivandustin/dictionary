The word βροντην (pronounced broh-NEE-ten) is a noun in the Greek language. 

Etymology: The word comes from the Greek verb βροντάω (brontao), which means "to thunder". 

Grammar: βροντην is a singular noun in the accusative case. 

Description: The word βροντην refers to the sound of thunder. It can also be used metaphorically to describe a loud noise or commotion.
