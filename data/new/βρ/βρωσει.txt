The word βρωσει (brosi) is a Greek verb in the future tense, which means "he/she/it will eat". 

Etymology: The word βρωσει comes from the Greek verb βρώσκω (brosko), which means "to eat". 

Grammar: βρωσει is a third-person singular future tense verb in the active voice. 

Description: βρωσει is a simple verb that describes the action of eating in the future. It can be used in various contexts, such as in a sentence like "Αύριο θα βρωσεις με τους φίλους σου;" (Aurio tha broseis me tous filous sou? - "Will you eat with your friends tomorrow?").
