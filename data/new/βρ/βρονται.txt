Word: βρονται

Etymology: βρονται is a Greek verb that comes from the noun βροντή (brontē), meaning "thunder". 

Grammar: βρονται is a third-person plural present indicative active verb in Greek. It is conjugated from the verb βροντάω (brontáō), which means "to thunder". 

Description: βρονται is used to describe the act of thundering or making a loud noise like thunder. It can also be used metaphorically to describe a loud or forceful action or event.
