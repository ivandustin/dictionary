Etymology: The word "ζωσαί" (zōsai) comes from the Greek verb "ζῶ" (zō), which means "I live" or "I am alive."

Grammar: "ζωσαί" is the third person singular, present tense form of the verb "ζῶ" (zō) in the indicative mood. It is used to describe someone or something that is currently alive.

Description: "ζωσαί" is a Greek word that means "he/she/it lives" or "he/she/it is alive." It is used to express the state of being alive or the act of living.
