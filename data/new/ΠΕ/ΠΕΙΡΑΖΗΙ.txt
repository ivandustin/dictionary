Etymology: The word "πειραζη" (peirazi) comes from the Greek verb "πειράζω" (peirazo), which means "to test" or "to try". 

Grammar: "πειραζη" is a noun in the singular form. It belongs to the third declension in Greek grammar. 

Description: "πειραζη" refers to a test, trial, or experiment. It can also mean temptation or provocation.
