Etymology: The word "πέτρα" (petra) comes from the Ancient Greek word meaning "rock" or "stone".

Grammar: In Greek, "πέτρα" is a feminine noun. It belongs to the third declension and has a singular form.

Description: "Πέτρα" is a common Greek word that refers to a solid, naturally occurring substance composed of minerals. It is often used to describe a large piece of rock or stone.
