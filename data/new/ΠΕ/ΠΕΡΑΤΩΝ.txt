Etymology: The word "περατών" comes from the Greek verb "περατώνω" (peratono), which means "to complete" or "to finish."

Grammar: "Περατών" is a noun in the singular form. It belongs to the third declension in Greek grammar.

Description: "Περατών" refers to someone or something that completes or finishes a task or an action. It can also be used to describe the act of completion or finishing itself.
