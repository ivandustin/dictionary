Etymology: The word "περίψημα" comes from the Greek word "περι-" (peri-), meaning "around" or "about," and "ψήμα" (psíma), meaning "mark" or "imprint." 

Grammar: "Περίψημα" is a noun in the Greek language. It is a neuter noun and its plural form is "περίψηματα" (perípsimata).

Description: "Περίψημα" refers to a mark or imprint that is made around or about something. It can also be used metaphorically to describe a lasting impression or impact that something or someone has.
