Etymology: The word "περιεδραμον" comes from the Greek words "περί" (peri), meaning "around" or "about," and "εδραμον" (edramon), meaning "I ran." 

Grammar: "περιεδραμον" is the first person singular past tense form of the verb "περιδρέχω" (peridrecho), which means "I run around" or "I circulate."

Description: "περιεδραμον" describes an action that occurred in the past, where the subject ran around or circulated.
