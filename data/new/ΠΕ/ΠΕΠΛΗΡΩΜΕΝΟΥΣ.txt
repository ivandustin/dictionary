Etymology: The word "πεπληρωμένους" comes from the Greek verb "πληρόω" (plēróō), which means "to fill" or "to complete". The prefix "πεπ-" indicates the perfect tense, indicating a completed action in the past.

Grammar: "Πεπληρωμένους" is the masculine plural form of the adjective in the perfect participle tense. It agrees with a plural masculine noun in gender, number, and case.

Description: "Πεπληρωμένους" means "filled" or "completed" in Greek. It describes something that has been fully filled or accomplished in the past.
