Etymology: The word "πεντακις" (pentakis) comes from the Greek word "πέντε" (pente), meaning "five". It is combined with the suffix "-κις" (-kis), which denotes multiplication or repetition.

Grammar: "Πεντακις" is an adverb in Greek. It is used to indicate that something happens or is done five times.
