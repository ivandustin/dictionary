Etymology: The word "περιοχή" comes from the Greek word "περί" (peri), meaning "around" or "about," and the word "οχή" (ochi), meaning "place" or "area." 

Grammar: "Περιοχή" is a feminine noun in Greek. It belongs to the second declension and its plural form is "περιοχές" (perioches). 

Description: "Περιοχή" refers to a region, area, or territory that is defined by certain boundaries or characteristics. It can be used to describe a geographical area, a specific district within a city, or a designated zone for a particular purpose.
