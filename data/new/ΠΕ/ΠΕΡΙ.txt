Etymology: The word "περι" (peri) comes from the Greek language and means "around" or "about".

Grammar: In Greek, "περι" is a preposition that is used to indicate movement or location around something. It is often followed by a noun or pronoun in the genitive case.

Description: "περι" is a versatile word that can be used in various contexts. It can indicate physical movement or location around an object or place, as well as metaphorical concepts such as discussing a topic or being concerned about something.
