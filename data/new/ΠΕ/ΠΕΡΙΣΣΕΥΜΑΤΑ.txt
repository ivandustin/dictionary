Etymology: The word "περισσεύματα" comes from the Greek word "περισσεύω" (perisseúo), which means "to abound" or "to be in excess."

Grammar: "Περισσεύματα" is a noun in the plural form. In Greek grammar, nouns have different forms depending on their gender, number, and case. "Περισσεύματα" is the plural form of the noun "περίσσευμα" (perisseúma), which means "surplus" or "excess."

Description: "Περισσεύματα" refers to things that are left over or in excess. It can be used to describe surplus goods, extra resources, or anything that exceeds what is needed or expected.
