Etymology: The word "πεινώμεν" (peinómen) comes from the Greek verb "πεινάω" (peináo), which means "to be hungry" or "to starve." 

Grammar: "Πεινώμεν" is the present tense, first person plural form of the verb "πεινάω." It is used to express that "we are hungry" or "we starve."

Description: "Πεινώμεν" is a Greek word that describes the state of being hungry or starving. It is used to express the feeling of hunger in the first person plural form.
