Etymology: The word "πειραν" (peiran) comes from the Greek verb "πειράω" (peirao), which means "to attempt" or "to try". 

Grammar: "Πειραν" is a noun in the accusative case, singular number, and feminine gender. 

Description: "Πειραν" refers to the act of attempting or trying something. It can also be used to describe a trial, experiment, or test.
