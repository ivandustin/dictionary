Etymology: The word "πέδας" (pédas) comes from the Ancient Greek word "πέδον" (pédon), meaning "ground" or "soil."

Grammar: In Greek, "πέδας" is a masculine noun in the nominative case. It belongs to the second declension and has a plural form of "πέδες" (pédes).

Description: "Πέδας" refers to a flat, solid surface or ground. It can also be used metaphorically to describe a foundation or basis for something.
