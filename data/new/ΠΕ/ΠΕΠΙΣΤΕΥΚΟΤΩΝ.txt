Grammar: "Πεπιστευκοτων" is the genitive plural form of the verb "πεπιστεύκω" (pepisteúko). In Greek grammar, the genitive case is used to indicate possession or origin.

Description: "Πεπιστευκοτων" is a Greek word that translates to "of those who have believed" or "of the believers" in English. It is commonly used in religious contexts to refer to individuals who have faith or trust in a particular belief system or doctrine.
