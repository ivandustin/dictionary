Etymology: The word "πεπληρωμέναι" comes from the Greek verb "πληρόω" (plēróō), which means "to fill" or "to complete". The prefix "πε-" (pe-) indicates a perfective aspect, indicating a completed action.

Grammar: "Πεπληρωμέναι" is the feminine plural form of the verb "πληρόω" in the perfect tense. It is used to describe multiple female subjects who have completed an action or have been filled with something.

Description: "Πεπληρωμέναι" is a Greek word that can be translated as "filled" or "completed" in English. It is used to indicate that something has been fully accomplished or that someone has been completely filled with a certain quality or substance.
