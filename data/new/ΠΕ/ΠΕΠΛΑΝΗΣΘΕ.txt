Etymology: The word "πεπλανησθε" comes from the Greek verb "πλανάω" (planáo), which means "to wander" or "to roam". The prefix "πε-" (pe-) indicates a perfective aspect, indicating completion or a state resulting from an action.

Grammar: "πεπλανησθε" is the second person plural form of the verb "πλανάω" in the aorist tense. It is in the middle voice, indicating that the subject is performing the action on themselves or is affected by the action.

Description: "πεπλανησθε" can be translated as "you all have wandered" or "you all have roamed". It suggests that a group of people have completed the action of wandering or roaming.
