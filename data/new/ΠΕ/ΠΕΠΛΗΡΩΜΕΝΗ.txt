Etymology: The word "πεπληρωμένη" comes from the Greek verb "πληρώνω" (plirono), which means "to fill" or "to complete". The prefix "πε-" (pe-) indicates the perfect tense, indicating that the action has been completed in the past.

Grammar: "Πεπληρωμένη" is the feminine form of the past participle of the verb "πληρώνω". It agrees in gender, number, and case with the noun it modifies.

Description: "Πεπληρωμένη" is an adjective in Greek that means "filled" or "completed". It is used to describe something that has been fully filled or completed in the past.
