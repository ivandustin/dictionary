Etymology: The word "περισσεύσαν" comes from the Greek verb "περισσεύω" (perisseúo), which means "to abound" or "to be in excess."

Grammar: "περισσεύσαν" is the aorist active participle form of the verb "περισσεύω" (perisseúo). It is used to describe something that has abounded or been in excess in the past.
