Grammar: "περιεπατεῖς" is a second-person singular present active indicative form of the verb "περιεπατέω" in Greek. It is used to address someone directly and indicate that they are currently walking around or wandering about.

Description: "περιεπατεῖς" is a Greek word that describes the action of walking around or wandering about. It can be used to refer to physical movement or to describe someone's behavior or mindset of being restless or constantly moving.
