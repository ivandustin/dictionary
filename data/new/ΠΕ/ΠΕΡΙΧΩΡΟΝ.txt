Etymology: The word "περιχωρον" comes from the Greek word "περιχώρα" (perichora), which means "surrounding area" or "region".

Grammar: "περιχωρον" is a noun in the neuter gender. It is the accusative singular form of the word.

Description: "περιχωρον" refers to the surrounding area or region of a place. It can be used to describe the area that is around or near a specific location.
