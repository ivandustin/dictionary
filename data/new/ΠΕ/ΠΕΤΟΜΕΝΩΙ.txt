Etymology: The word "πετομένωι" (petomenoi) comes from the Greek verb "πετάομαι" (petáomai), which means "to fly". It is derived from the noun "πτέρυξ" (ptéryx), meaning "wing".

Grammar: "Πετομένωι" is a verb in the present tense, active voice, and indicative mood. It is the third person singular form of the verb "πετάομαι" (petáomai), which means "to fly".
