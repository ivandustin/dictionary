Etymology: The word "περιαγων" comes from the Greek words "περί" (peri), meaning "around" or "about," and "αγων" (agon), meaning "contest" or "struggle." 

Grammar: "περιαγων" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the singular form and belongs to the third declension. 

Description: "περιαγων" refers to a contest or struggle that takes place around or about something. It can be used metaphorically to describe a situation or event that involves intense competition or conflict.
