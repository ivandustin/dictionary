Grammar: "Πεπληρωμένην" is the feminine singular form of the past participle of the verb "πληρόω" (plēróō). It is in the accusative case, indicating that it is the direct object of a verb or a preposition.

Description: "Πεπληρωμένην" means "filled" or "completed" in Greek. It is used to describe something that has been fully filled or accomplished.
