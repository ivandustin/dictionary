Etymology: The word "περισσόν" comes from the Greek word "περισσός" (perissós), meaning "excessive" or "abundant".

Grammar: "Περισσόν" is a noun in the neuter gender. It is the accusative singular form of the word.

Description: "Περισσόν" is used to describe something that is excessive, abundant, or more than what is necessary. It can refer to an excess or surplus of something.
