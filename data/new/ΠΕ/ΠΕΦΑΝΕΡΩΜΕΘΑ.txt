Etymology: The word "πεφανερωμένη" comes from the Greek verb "φανερόω" (phaneroo), which means "to make visible" or "to reveal". The suffix "-μένη" (-meni) is added to the verb stem to form the past participle, indicating that the action of making something visible or revealing has been completed.

Grammar: "Πεφανερωμένη" is the feminine form of the past participle of the verb "φανερόω" (phaneroo). It agrees in gender, number, and case with the noun it modifies. In this case, it is in the singular feminine nominative form.

Description: "Πεφανερωμένη" is an adjective that describes something or someone that has been made visible or revealed. It can be used to indicate that something was previously hidden or unknown but has now become apparent or evident.
