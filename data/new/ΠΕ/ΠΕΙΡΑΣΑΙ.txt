Etymology: The word "πειρασαί" comes from the Greek verb "πειράω" (peiráō), which means "to try" or "to attempt". 

Grammar: "πειρασαί" is the plural form of the noun "πειρασμός" (peirasmós), which means "temptation" or "trial". It belongs to the third declension in Greek grammar.

Description: "πειρασαί" refers to multiple temptations or trials. It can be used to describe various challenging situations or experiences that test one's character, willpower, or faith.
