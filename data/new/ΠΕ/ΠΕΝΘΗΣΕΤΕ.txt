Grammar: "Πενθησετε" is the second person plural form of the verb "πενθέω" in the future tense. It is used to address a group of people and means "you will mourn" or "you will grieve".

Description: "Πενθησετε" is a Greek verb that expresses the action of mourning or grieving. It implies a deep sense of sadness or sorrow. The word can be used in various contexts, such as expressing grief over the loss of a loved one or lamenting a tragic event.
