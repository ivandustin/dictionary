Etymology: The word "περισσεύετε" comes from the Greek verb "περισσεύω" (perisseúo), which means "to abound" or "to be in excess."

Grammar: "περισσεύετε" is the second person plural form of the present tense of the verb "περισσεύω" (perisseúo). It is used to address a group of people and means "you abound" or "you have in excess."

Description: "περισσεύετε" is a Greek verb that expresses the idea of having an abundance or surplus of something. It can be used in various contexts to describe having more than what is needed or expected.
