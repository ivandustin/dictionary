Grammar: In Greek, "περισσεύω" is a verb in the present tense, active voice, and indicative mood. It is conjugated in various forms to match the subject and tense of the sentence. For example, "περισσεύω" is the first person singular form, meaning "I abound" or "I have in excess."

Description: "περισσεύω" is a versatile word that conveys the idea of abundance, surplus, or excess. It is often used to describe having more than what is needed or expected.
