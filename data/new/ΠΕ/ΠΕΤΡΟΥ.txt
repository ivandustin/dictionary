Etymology: The word "πετρού" (petrou) comes from the Greek word "πέτρα" (petra), which means "rock" or "stone".

Grammar: "Πετρού" is a noun in the genitive case. In Greek grammar, the genitive case is used to indicate possession or origin.

Description: "Πετρού" is a Greek word that means "of the rock" or "of the stone". It is used to describe something that belongs to or is related to a rock or stone.
