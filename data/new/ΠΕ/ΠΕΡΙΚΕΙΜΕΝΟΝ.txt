Etymology: The word "περικειμένον" comes from the Greek word "περικείμενος" (perikeimenos), which is the present participle of the verb "περικείμαι" (perikeimai), meaning "to surround" or "to enclose".

Grammar: "περικειμένον" is a neuter noun in the singular form. In Greek grammar, neuter nouns have the same form for both the nominative and accusative cases.

Description: "περικειμένον" refers to something that is surrounded or enclosed. It can be used to describe an object or a concept that is encompassed by something else.
