Grammar: "Περισσότεραι" is the plural form of the adjective "περισσότερη" (perissóteri), which means "more" or "greater" in the feminine gender. It is used to compare two or more things, indicating that one is superior or has a larger quantity than the other.

Description: "Περισσότεραι" is used to describe multiple feminine nouns or pronouns that have a greater degree or quantity compared to others. It is commonly used in Greek language to express comparative superiority or to indicate a larger amount or number.
