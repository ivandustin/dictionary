Etymology: The word "πεδίνου" (pedinou) comes from the Greek word "πεδίο" (pedio), which means "field" or "plain". 

Grammar: "Πεδίνου" is a noun in the genitive case, singular form. In Greek grammar, the genitive case is used to indicate possession, origin, or relationship.

Description: "Πεδίνου" refers to something that belongs to or is related to a field or plain. It can be used to describe various objects or concepts that are associated with open, flat areas of land.
