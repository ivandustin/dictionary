Etymology: The word "περισσείαν" comes from the Greek word "περισσεύω" (perisseúo), which means "to abound" or "to be in excess".

Grammar: "περισσείαν" is a noun in the accusative case, singular number, and feminine gender in Greek grammar.

Description: "περισσείαν" refers to an excess or surplus of something. It can be used to describe an abundance or an extra amount of a particular thing.
