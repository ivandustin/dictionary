Etymology: The word "περιπατητής" comes from the Greek verb "περιπατώ" (peripató), which means "to walk around" or "to stroll". It is derived from the combination of the preposition "περί" (peri), meaning "around", and the verb "πατώ" (pató), meaning "to walk".

Grammar: "περιπατητής" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the masculine gender and singular number. In Greek grammar, nouns have different forms depending on their gender, number, and case.
