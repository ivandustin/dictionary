Etymology: The word "πεπληρωταί" comes from the Greek verb "πληρόω" (plēróō), which means "to fill" or "to complete". The prefix "πε-" (pe-) indicates a perfective aspect, indicating a completed action.

Grammar: "πεπληρωταί" is a verb form in the Greek language. It is in the perfect tense, indicating a completed action with ongoing results. The suffix "-ται" (-tai) indicates the third person singular form.
