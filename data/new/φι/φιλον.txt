Word: φιλον

Etymology:
The word φιλον (philon) is a Greek word that means "friend" or "beloved." It is derived from the Greek word φίλος (philos), which means "loving" or "friendly."

Grammar:
In Greek, φιλον is a noun that is declined in the masculine gender, singular number, and nominative case. Its plural form is φίλοι (filoi).

Description:
φιλον is a term used to refer to a male friend or a beloved person. It is often used in Greek literature and poetry to express affection and friendship. The word has a positive connotation and is associated with warmth, kindness, and loyalty.
