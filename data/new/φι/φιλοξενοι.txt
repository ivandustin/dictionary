Word: φιλοξενοι

Etymology: The word φιλοξενοι comes from the Greek words φίλος (philos) meaning "friend" or "loving" and ξένος (xenos) meaning "stranger" or "foreigner". Therefore, φιλοξενοι means "loving strangers" or "welcoming foreigners".

Grammar: φιλοξενοι is a plural noun in the nominative case. It is the masculine form of the word, which means it refers to a group of male individuals who are welcoming to strangers or foreigners.

Description: φιλοξενοι is a term used to describe people who are hospitable and welcoming to strangers or foreigners. It is a positive trait that is highly valued in Greek culture, as it reflects the idea of treating others with kindness and respect. The word can be used to describe individuals, groups, or even entire communities who are known for their hospitality and generosity towards outsiders.
