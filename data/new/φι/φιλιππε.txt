The word "φιλιππε" (pronounced "philippe") is a Greek name that means "lover of horses". It is derived from the Greek words "φίλος" (phílos), meaning "friend" or "lover", and "ἵππος" (híppos), meaning "horse". In terms of grammar, "φιλιππε" is a masculine noun in the nominative case. It is often used as a personal name in Greece and other countries with Greek influence.
