The word φιαλην (phialen) is derived from the Greek word φιάλη (phialē), which means a shallow bowl or dish. It is believed to have originated from the ancient Greek word φιάλης (phialēs), which means a broad, flat dish or platter.

φιαλην is a noun in the accusative case in Greek. It is a singular form of the word φιάλη (phialē), which is a feminine noun.
