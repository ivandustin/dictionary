Word: φιλοξενον

Accusative: το φιλοξενον (to philoxenon)
Vocative: φιλοξενον (philoxenon)

Description: φιλοξενον is a Greek word that describes the act of being hospitable and welcoming to strangers. It is a virtue that has been highly valued in Greek culture since ancient times.
