Etymology: The word "ιδωσιν" (idōsin) comes from the Greek verb "εἴδω" (eidō), which means "to see" or "to perceive."

Grammar: "Ιδωσιν" is a third-person plural present active indicative form of the verb "εἴδω" (eidō). It is used to express that "they see" or "they perceive."

Description: "Ιδωσιν" is a word in the Greek language that refers to the action of seeing or perceiving done by a group of people. It is commonly used in various contexts to describe the act of visual perception.
