Etymology: The word "ιδιαί" (idiai) comes from the Greek adjective "ιδιαίτερος" (idiateros), which means "particular" or "special."

Grammar: "Ιδιαί" (idiai) is the plural form of the feminine adjective "ιδιαίτερη" (idiateri), which means "particular" or "special." It is used to describe multiple feminine nouns.

Description: "Ιδιαί" (idiai) is used to emphasize that something or someone is unique, distinct, or exceptional. It highlights the individuality or special qualities of the feminine nouns it modifies.
