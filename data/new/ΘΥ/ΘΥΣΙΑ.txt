Grammar: In Greek, "θυσία" is a feminine noun of the first declension. It can be used in both the singular and plural forms. The plural form is "θυσίες" (thysies).

Description: "Θυσία" refers to a religious ritual in which an offering, typically an animal or food, is made to a deity as an act of worship or devotion. It is a common practice in many cultures and religions throughout history. The purpose of a sacrifice can vary, including seeking favor from the gods, expressing gratitude, or atoning for sins.
