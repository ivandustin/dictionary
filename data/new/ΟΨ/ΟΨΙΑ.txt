Etymology: The word "οψια" comes from the Greek word "ὄψις" (opsis), which means "sight" or "vision."

Grammar: "Οψια" is a feminine noun in the Greek language. It belongs to the third declension and has the genitive form "οψιας" (opsias).

Description: "Οψια" refers to the time of day just before sunset, also known as dusk or twilight. It is the period when the sun is below the horizon, but its light still illuminates the sky.
