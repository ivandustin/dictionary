Grammar: "οψομεθα" is a first-person plural future middle indicative form of the verb "οψομαι" (opsomai). In Greek grammar, the future tense indicates an action that will happen in the future. The middle voice indicates that the subject is performing the action on or for themselves.

Description: "οψομεθα" can be translated as "we will see" or "we will look at" in English. It expresses the idea that the subject (we) will perform the action of seeing or looking at something in the future.
