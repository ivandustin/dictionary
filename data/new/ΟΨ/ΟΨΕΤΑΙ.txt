Etymology: The word "οψεται" comes from the Greek verb "οράω" (orao), which means "to see" or "to behold". It is conjugated in the future tense, third person singular form.

Grammar: "Οψεται" is a verb in the future tense, third person singular form. In Greek grammar, verbs change their endings to indicate tense, person, and number. The future tense indicates an action that will happen in the future.
