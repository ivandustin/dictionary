The word "γαμιζων" (gamizon) is a present participle form of the Greek verb "γαμίζω" (gamizo), which means "to marry" or "to wed". It is derived from the noun "γάμος" (gamos), which means "marriage". In terms of grammar, "γαμιζων" is a masculine singular nominative form, used to describe a male who is currently getting married or in the process of getting married.
