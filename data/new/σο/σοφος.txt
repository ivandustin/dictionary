The word σοφος (sophos) comes from the Greek root word σοφία (sophia), which means wisdom. It is believed to have originated in ancient Greece, where it was used to describe individuals who possessed great knowledge and understanding.

When used to describe a neuter singular noun in the nominative case, it is σοφόν (sophon).
