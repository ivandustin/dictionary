Word: σοφια

Etymology:
The word σοφια (sophia) is a Greek word that means "wisdom". It is derived from the Greek word "sophos" which means "wise" or "clever".

In Greek, σοφια is a feminine noun. It is in the nominative case, which is used for the subject of a sentence. The word can also be declined in other cases, such as the genitive case (σοφιας) which is used to show possession.

Description:
Sophia is a concept that has been highly valued in Greek philosophy and culture. It is often associated with knowledge, understanding, and insight. In Greek mythology, Sophia was also the name of a goddess of wisdom. The word has been used in various contexts, such as in religious texts, literature, and philosophy.
