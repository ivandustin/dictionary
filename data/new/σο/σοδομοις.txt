Word: σοδομοις

Etymology:
The word σοδομοις (sodomos) is a Greek word that is derived from the name of the city of Sodom, which is mentioned in the Bible. The city of Sodom was known for its wickedness and was destroyed by God in the Old Testament.

σοδομοις is a noun in the dative plural form. It is used to refer to the people of Sodom.
