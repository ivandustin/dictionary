Word: σου

Etymology:
The word "σου" is a pronoun in the Greek language that means "your" or "yours". It is derived from the ancient Greek word "σός" (sos), which means "your own" or "yours".

Grammar:
In Greek grammar, "σου" is a second-person singular possessive pronoun. It is used to indicate possession or ownership of something by the person being addressed. For example, "Αυτό είναι το σου βιβλίο" (This is your book).

Description:
"Σου" is a very common word in the Greek language and is used in everyday conversation. It is often used in combination with other words to form phrases such as "σου λέω" (I'm telling you) or "σου ευχαριστώ" (Thank you). It is also used in formal writing and literature.
