The word "ευποιιας" (eupoiias) is a Greek noun that means "goodwill" or "kindness". 

Etymology: The word is derived from the Greek words "ευ" (eu) meaning "good" and "ποιια" (poiia) meaning "making" or "doing". 

Grammar: "Ευποιιας" is a feminine noun in the genitive case. 

Description: The word is often used to describe a positive attitude or disposition towards others, and is associated with acts of generosity, compassion, and benevolence. It is a common theme in Greek philosophy and is considered a virtue in many cultures.
