It is a compound verb made up of the prefix ευ (eu) meaning "good" and the verb αγγελιζω (angelizo) meaning "to proclaim". The suffix -μεθα (-metha) indicates that the verb is in the middle voice, which means that the subject is both the doer and the receiver of the action.

Description: ευαγγελιζομεθα is a Greek verb that means "we proclaim good news" or "we evangelize".
