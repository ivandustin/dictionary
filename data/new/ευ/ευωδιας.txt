The word "ευωδιας" (euōdias) is a Greek noun that means "fragrance" or "sweet smell". 

Etymology: The word is derived from the Greek words "eu" meaning "good" and "ōdē" meaning "odor" or "smell".

Grammar: "Ευωδιας" is a feminine noun in the genitive case. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their grammatical function in a sentence.

Description: The word "ευωδιας" is often used in the context of pleasant smells, such as the fragrance of flowers or perfume. It can also be used metaphorically to describe something that is pleasing or delightful.
