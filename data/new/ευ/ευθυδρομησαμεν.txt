Word: ευθυδρομησαμεν

Etymology: The word ευθυδρομησαμεν is derived from the Greek words ευθύς (euthys) meaning "straight" and δρομή (dromi) meaning "running". It is the aorist tense of the verb ευθυδρομέω (euthydromeo), which means "to run straight".

Grammar: ευθυδρομησαμεν is a verb in the aorist tense, which is used to describe a completed action in the past. It is the first person plural form of the verb ευθυδρομέω (euthydromeo), which means "we ran straight".

Description: ευθυδρομησαμεν describes the action of running straight in the past. It implies that the subject was focused and determined in their running, without deviating from a straight path.
