The word ευκαιρως (eukairōs) is derived from the Greek words ευ (eu) meaning "good" and καιρος (kairos) meaning "time". Therefore, the word ευκαιρως means "at a good time" or "opportune".

ευκαιρως is an adverb in Greek. It is used to describe when something happens at a good time or is opportune.
