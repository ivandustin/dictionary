Word: ευλογησεν

Description: ευλογησεν is a word that appears in the New Testament of the Bible, specifically in Luke 24:53. The verse reads, "And were continually in the temple, praising and blessing God. Amen." The word ευλογησεν is translated as "blessed" in this context, indicating that the people were praising and giving thanks to God.
