The word "ευθειαν" (euthéian) is a Greek adverb that means "straight" or "directly." It is derived from the adjective "ευθύς" (euthús), which also means "straight" or "direct." In terms of grammar, "ευθειαν" is an adverb of manner, indicating how an action is performed. It can be used to modify verbs, adjectives, or other adverbs. For example, "He walked straight to the door" could be translated as "Περπάτησε ευθείαν προς την πόρτα" (Perpátise euthéian pros tin pórta).
