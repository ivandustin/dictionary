Word: ευλαβεις

Etymology: The word ευλαβεις comes from the Greek root words ευ- (eu-), meaning "good" or "well," and λαβειν (labein), meaning "to take" or "to seize." Together, ευλαβεις means "careful" or "cautious."

Grammar: ευλαβεις is an adjective in the masculine plural form. It is often used to describe people who are careful or cautious in their actions or behavior.

Description: ευλαβεις is a word that is often used in Greek to describe someone who is careful, cautious, or conscientious. It can be used to describe someone who is careful in their actions, or someone who is cautious in their words.
