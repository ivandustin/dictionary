Word: ευρεθησεται

Etymology:
The word ευρεθησεται is a Greek verb that comes from the root word εὑρίσκω (heurisko), which means "to find" or "to discover". The verb is in the future passive indicative tense, which means "it will be found".

Grammar:
ευρεθησεται is a third-person singular verb in the future passive indicative tense. In Greek, verbs change their endings to indicate tense, mood, voice, and person. The future tense indicates that the action will happen in the future, while the passive voice indicates that the subject of the verb is being acted upon.

Description:
The word ευρεθησεται is often used in the context of finding or discovering something. It can be translated as "it will be found" or "it will be discovered". The word is commonly used in religious texts, such as the Bible, to describe the discovery of something important or significant.
