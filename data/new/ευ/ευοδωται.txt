The word "ευοδωται" is a Greek verb that means "to prosper" or "to succeed". It is derived from the Greek words "ευ" (eu), meaning "good" or "well", and "οδος" (odos), meaning "way" or "path". In terms of grammar, "ευοδωται" is a third-person plural present indicative active verb. It describes an action that is currently happening in the present and is being performed by multiple people.
