Word: ευηγγελισατο

Etymology: The word ευηγγελισατο comes from the Greek roots ευ- (eu-), meaning "good," and ευαγγελιζω (euangelizo), meaning "to announce good news" or "to evangelize." 

Grammar: ευηγγελισατο is a verb in the aorist tense, third person singular form. It is derived from the verb ευαγγελιζω (euangelizo), which means "to announce good news" or "to evangelize." 

Description: ευηγγελισατο means "he/she/it evangelized" or "he/she/it announced good news." It is often used in a religious context to refer to the spreading of the gospel or good news of Jesus Christ.
