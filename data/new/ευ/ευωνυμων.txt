The word "ευωνυμων" (euonymon) is a Greek adjective that means "having a good name" or "well-named". It is derived from the Greek words "ευ" (eu), meaning "good" or "well", and "ωνυμος" (onumos), meaning "named" or "having a name". 

In terms of grammar, "ευωνυμων" is a masculine, singular, nominative adjective. It can be used to describe a person, place, or thing that has a good or appropriate name.

Overall, "ευωνυμων" is a positive and complimentary term that is often used to praise someone or something for having a fitting or admirable name.
