The word "ευξαιμην" (euxaimēn) is a Greek adjective that means "calm" or "serene". It is derived from the combination of the prefix "ευ-" (eu-), meaning "good" or "well", and the noun "ξαινω" (xainō), meaning "to calm" or "to soothe". 

In terms of grammar, "ευξαιμην" is a feminine singular form of the adjective. It can be used to describe a person, a situation, or a state of mind that is peaceful and tranquil. 

Overall, "ευξαιμην" is a word that conveys a sense of calmness and tranquility, and is often used to describe a peaceful state of mind or a serene environment.
