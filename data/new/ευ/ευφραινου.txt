The word "ευφραινου" (euphrainou) is a verb in Greek that means "rejoice" or "be glad". 

Etymology: The word comes from the combination of two Greek words: "ευ" (eu) meaning "good" and "φρήν" (phrēn) meaning "mind" or "spirit". 

Grammar: "ευφραινου" is a second person singular present active imperative verb. 

Description: The word is often used in a religious or spiritual context, such as in the Bible or in hymns. It is also used in everyday language to express joy or happiness.
