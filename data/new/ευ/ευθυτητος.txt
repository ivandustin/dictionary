Word: ευθυτητος

Etymology: The word ευθυτητος comes from the Greek words ευθύς (euthys) meaning "straight" and τητα (tita) meaning "quality of being". Therefore, ευθυτητος means "having the quality of being straight".

Grammar: ευθυτητος is an adjective in the Greek language. It is in the masculine gender, singular number, and nominative case.

Description: ευθυτητος is used to describe something that is straight or direct. It can be used to describe a physical object, such as a straight line or a straight road, or it can be used to describe a person's behavior or actions, such as being honest and straightforward.
