The word "ευθυνοντος" is a Greek verb in the present participle form, which means "responsible" or "accountable". 

Etymology: The word is derived from the Greek root "ευθύς" (euthys) which means "straight" or "direct", and the suffix "-νοντος" (-nontos) which is a present participle ending indicating ongoing action.

Grammar: "Ευθυνοντος" is the present participle form of the verb "ευθύνω" (euthyno), which means "to hold responsible" or "to be accountable". It is in the masculine gender, singular number, and nominative case.

Description: The word "ευθυνοντος" is often used in legal or professional contexts to describe someone who is responsible for a particular task or outcome. It can also be used in a more general sense to describe someone who takes responsibility for their actions or decisions.
