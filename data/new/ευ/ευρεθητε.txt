Word: ευρεθητε

Etymology: ευρεθητε is a Greek word that comes from the verb ευρίσκω (eurisko), which means "to find" or "to discover". The word is in the passive voice and the aorist tense, indicating that the action of finding has already taken place.

Grammar: ευρεθητε is the second person plural form of the aorist passive indicative of the verb ευρίσκω. In English, it would be translated as "you were found".

Description: ευρεθητε is a verb form that indicates that a group of people were found or discovered. The context in which it is used would determine the specific meaning and implications of the word.
