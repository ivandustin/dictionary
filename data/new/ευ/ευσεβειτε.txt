The word "ευσεβειτε" (eusebeite) is a verb in the Greek language. It is derived from the adjective "ευσεβής" (eusebēs), which means "pious" or "devout". The verb form means "to be pious" or "to act in a devout manner". 

In terms of grammar, "ευσεβειτε" is a second person plural present active imperative verb. This means that it is a command directed towards a group of people to perform the action of being pious or devout. 

Overall, "ευσεβειτε" is a word that emphasizes the importance of being devout and pious in one's actions and beliefs.
