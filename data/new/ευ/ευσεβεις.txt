In terms of grammar, "ευσεβεις" is in the masculine nominative plural form. It can also be declined in other cases and genders to agree with the noun it modifies.

In description, "ευσεβεις" is often used to describe someone who is deeply religious or devoted to their faith. It can also be used more broadly to describe someone who is morally upright and conscientious in their actions.
