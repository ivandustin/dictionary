The word "ευαγγελιζηται" (evangelizetai) is a Greek verb that means "to evangelize" or "to preach the gospel." It is derived from the Greek word "ευαγγέλιον" (evangelion), which means "good news" or "gospel." In terms of grammar, "ευαγγελιζηται" is a present tense, third person singular form of the verb.
