Word: ευτονως

Etymology: The word ευτονως comes from the Greek words ευ (eu) meaning "good" and τονος (tonos) meaning "tone". 

Grammar: ευτονως is an adverb in Greek. It is used to describe the way in which something is pronounced or spoken. 

Description: ευτονως is used to describe the proper or correct way of pronouncing words in Greek. It is often used in language instruction or in discussions about proper pronunciation. The word emphasizes the importance of speaking with good tone and clarity.
