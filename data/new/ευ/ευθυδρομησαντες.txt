Word: ευθυδρομησαντες

Etymology: The word ευθυδρομησαντες is derived from the Greek words ευθύς (euthys) meaning "straight" and δρόμος (dromos) meaning "run" or "race". The suffix -αντες (-antes) is a participle ending, indicating that the word is a past participle.

Grammar: ευθυδρομησαντες is a past participle in the Greek language. It is in the masculine plural form, indicating that it refers to a group of people who have performed the action of running straight.

Description: ευθυδρομησαντες can be translated to mean "having run straight" or "having run in a straight line". It is often used in the context of sports or racing, where running in a straight line is important. The word can also be used metaphorically to describe someone who is focused and determined in their actions.
