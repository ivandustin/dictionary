The word "ευηγγελισαμεθα" is a Greek verb that means "we preached good news" or "we evangelized." It is derived from the root word "εὐαγγέλιον" (euangelion), which means "good news" or "gospel." 

In terms of grammar, "ευηγγελισαμεθα" is a first-person plural aorist indicative active verb. The aorist tense indicates a completed action in the past, while the indicative mood indicates a statement of fact. The active voice indicates that the subject (in this case, "we") performed the action.

Overall, "ευηγγελισαμεθα" is a word that describes the act of spreading good news or evangelizing, and it has its roots in the Greek word for "gospel."
