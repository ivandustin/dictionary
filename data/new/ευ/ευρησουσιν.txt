Word: ευρησουσιν

The future tense indicates an action that will happen in the future. The third person plural form indicates that the subject of the verb is a group of people.

Description: ευρησουσιν is a verb that describes the action of a group of people finding or discovering something in the future. It is often used in a prophetic or predictive sense, such as in religious texts or literature.
