Word: ευθεως

Etymology:
The word ευθεως (eftheos) is a Greek adverb that means "immediately" or "straightway". It is derived from the combination of two Greek words: ευ (eu) meaning "good" or "well" and θεως (theos) meaning "god". Therefore, the literal meaning of ευθεως is "well or good in the sight of God".

Grammar:
ευθεως is an adverb in Greek, which means that it modifies the verb in a sentence. It is often used to indicate that an action is done without delay or hesitation. In English, it can be translated as "immediately", "at once", or "straightaway".
