The word "ευηγγελισεν" (euangelisen) is a Greek verb that means "to evangelize" or "to proclaim good news." It is derived from the Greek words "eu" meaning "good" and "angelos" meaning "messenger." In grammar, it is a third person singular aorist indicative active verb, which means that it describes a completed action in the past. The word is often used in the context of spreading the Christian gospel or sharing good news in general.
