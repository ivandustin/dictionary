The word "ευλαβειας" (eulabeias) is a Greek noun that means "piety" or "reverence." It is derived from the Greek words "ευ" (eu), meaning "good," and "λαβειν" (labein), meaning "to take." The word is often used in religious contexts to describe a deep respect or devotion to a deity or higher power. In terms of grammar, "ευλαβειας" is a feminine noun in the genitive case.
