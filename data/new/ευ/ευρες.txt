The word "ευρες" is a Greek noun that means "findings" or "discoveries". 

Etymology: The word comes from the Greek verb "εὑρίσκω" (heurisko), which means "to find" or "to discover". 

Grammar: "Ευρες" is a plural noun in the nominative case. 

Description: The word is often used in scientific or archaeological contexts to refer to the results of research or excavations. It can also be used more generally to refer to any kind of findings or discoveries.
