The word "ευμεταδοτους" is a Greek adjective that means "well-transmitted" or "well-delivered". It is derived from the combination of the prefix "ευ-" meaning "well" and the verb "μεταδίδω" meaning "to transmit" or "to deliver". 

In terms of grammar, "ευμεταδοτους" is in the masculine plural form, and it agrees with the noun it modifies in gender, number, and case. 

In usage, "ευμεταδοτους" can be used to describe a message, information, or news that has been transmitted accurately and effectively. It can also be used to describe a person who is skilled at delivering messages or information.
