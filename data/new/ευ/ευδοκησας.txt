Word: ευδοκησας

Etymology:
The word ευδοκησας is a Greek verb that comes from the root word ευδοκεω (eudokeo), which means "to be well pleased" or "to approve." The verb form ευδοκησας (eudokesas) is in the aorist tense, which indicates a completed action in the past.
