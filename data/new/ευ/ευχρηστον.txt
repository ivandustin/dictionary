Word: ευχρηστον

Etymology: The word ευχρηστον comes from the Greek roots ευ- (eu-), meaning "good" or "well," and χρηστος (chrēstos), meaning "useful" or "convenient." 

Grammar: ευχρηστον is an adjective in the neuter gender, singular form. It is used to describe something that is easy to use or convenient.

Description: ευχρηστον is a word that describes something that is user-friendly or easy to use. It can be used to describe a variety of things, such as a tool, a piece of software, or a website. The word implies that the thing being described is designed with the user in mind, and that it is intuitive and efficient.
