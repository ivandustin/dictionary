The word "ευλογιαι" (eulogiai) is a Greek noun that means "blessings" or "praises." 

Etymology: The word comes from the Greek root "ευ-" (eu-), meaning "good" or "well," and "λογος" (logos), meaning "word" or "speech." 

Grammar: "Ευλογιαι" is a plural noun in the nominative case. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their gender, number, and case. 

Description: The word "ευλογιαι" is often used in religious contexts to refer to blessings or praises given to God. It can also be used more generally to refer to good wishes or expressions of gratitude.
