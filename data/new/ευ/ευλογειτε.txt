Word: ευλογειτε

Etymology: The word ευλογειτε comes from the Greek root words ευ (eu) meaning "good" and λογος (logos) meaning "word" or "speech". Together, ευλογειτε means "to speak well of" or "to bless".

Grammar: ευλογειτε is a verb in the present tense, second person plural form. It is often used as a command or request to bless or speak well of someone or something.

Description: ευλογειτε is a common word used in Greek Orthodox religious services and prayers. It is often used by priests or other religious leaders to bless the congregation or specific individuals. The word can also be used in everyday conversation to express good wishes or to give praise.
