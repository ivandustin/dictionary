Word: ευβουλος

Etymology:
The word ευβουλος comes from the Greek roots ευ- (eu-), meaning "good" or "well," and βουλη (boulē), meaning "counsel" or "advice." Therefore, ευβουλος can be translated as "having good counsel" or "wise."

Grammar:
In Greek, ευβουλος is an adjective that can be used to describe a person or thing that possesses good counsel or wisdom. It can be declined in various cases, genders, and numbers to agree with the noun it modifies.

Description:
ευβουλος is a positive attribute that is highly valued in Greek culture. It is often used to describe wise leaders, advisors, or counselors who provide good advice and guidance. The word can also be used to describe a person who is generally wise and prudent in their decision-making.
