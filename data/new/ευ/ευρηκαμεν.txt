Word: ευρηκαμεν

Grammar: ευρηκαμεν is the first person plural aorist tense of the verb ευρίσκω (eurisko). In Greek, the aorist tense is used to describe a past action that is completed or has already happened. The first person plural form indicates that the action was performed by a group of people.
