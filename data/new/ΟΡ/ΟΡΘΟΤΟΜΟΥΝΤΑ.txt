Etymology: The word "ορθογραφία" comes from the Greek roots "ορθός" (orthos), meaning "correct" or "straight," and "γραφή" (grafi), meaning "writing." 

Grammar: "Ορθογραφία" is a feminine noun in Greek. It is used to refer to the correct spelling and writing of words in a language.

Description: "Ορθογραφία" is an important aspect of language and communication. It involves following the established rules and conventions of spelling and writing in a particular language. Proper orthography ensures clarity and consistency in written communication.
