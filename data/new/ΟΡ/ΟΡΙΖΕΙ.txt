Etymology: The word "οριζει" comes from the Greek verb "ορίζω" (orizo), which means "to define" or "to determine".

Grammar: "Οριζει" is the third person singular form of the verb "ορίζω" in the present tense. It is used when referring to a singular subject.

Description: "Οριζει" is a Greek word that means "he/she/it defines" or "he/she/it determines". It is commonly used in various contexts to express the act of setting boundaries, establishing limits, or providing a clear definition for something.
