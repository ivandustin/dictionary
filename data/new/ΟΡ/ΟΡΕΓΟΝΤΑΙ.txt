Grammar: "ὁρεγονται" is the third person plural form of the present middle/passive indicative of the verb "ὁρέγω." It is used to express that a group of people desires or longs for something.

Description: "ὁρεγονται" is a Greek word that describes the action of a group of people desiring or longing for something. It can be used in various contexts to express the collective desire or longing of a group.
