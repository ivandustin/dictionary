Etymology: The word "ορων" (orōn) comes from the Greek verb "οράω" (oráō), which means "to see" or "to behold."

Grammar: "Ορων" (orōn) is a noun in the masculine gender, singular number, and nominative case.

Description: "Ορων" (orōn) refers to a mountain or a hill in Greek. It is commonly used in geographical contexts to describe elevated landforms.
