Grammar: In Greek, "όρκος" is a masculine noun of the second declension. It belongs to the category of nouns that end in "-ος" in the nominative singular form.

Description: "Όρκος" refers to an oath or a solemn promise made by someone. It is a formal declaration that binds the person who takes the oath to fulfill a certain commitment or obligation.
