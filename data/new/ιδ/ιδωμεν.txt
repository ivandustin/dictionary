Word: ιδωμεν

Etymology:
The word ιδωμεν (idomen) is a conjugated form of the Greek verb εἶδον (eidon), which means "I saw" or "I have seen". The verb εἶδον comes from the Proto-Indo-European root *weyd- which means "to see".

The subjunctive mood is used to express doubt, possibility, or hypothetical situations.

The word ιδωμεν (idomen) is used in Greek to express the idea of "let us see" or "let us find out".
