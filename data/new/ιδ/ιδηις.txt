In terms of grammar, "ιδηις" is a feminine adjective in the nominative singular form. It can also be declined to match the gender, number, and case of the noun it modifies. For example, "ιδηίς" (idēís) is the masculine form, and "ιδηίδα" (idēída) is the feminine accusative form.

Overall, "ιδηις" is a word that is closely associated with Greek mythology and the mountain of Ida.
