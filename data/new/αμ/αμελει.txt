The word "αμελει" (amelei) is a verb in Greek that means "to neglect" or "to disregard". 

Etymology: The word comes from the Greek root "μελ-" (mel-), which means "to care" or "to be concerned". The prefix "α-" (a-) in this case is a negation, meaning "not" or "without". 

Grammar: "αμελει" is a present tense verb in the third person singular form. It can also be conjugated in other tenses and persons, such as "αμελώ" (ameló) in the first person singular present tense, or "αμελήσει" (amelísei) in the third person singular future tense. 

Description: The word "αμελει" is often used to describe someone who is careless or negligent in their actions or responsibilities. It can also be used in a more general sense to describe a lack of attention or concern for something.
