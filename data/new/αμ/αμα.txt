Word: αμα

Etymology: The word αμα (ama) is a Greek adverb that means "at the same time" or "simultaneously". It is derived from the ancient Greek word ἅμα (hama), which has the same meaning.

Grammar: αμα is an adverb in Greek and is used to indicate that two or more actions are happening at the same time. It is often used in conjunction with other adverbs or conjunctions to create complex sentences.

Description: αμα is a versatile word that can be used in a variety of contexts. For example, it can be used to describe two people doing something together, such as "Πήγαμε αμα στο σινεμά" (We went to the cinema together). It can also be used to describe two actions happening at the same time, such as "Τρώω αμα που βλέπω τηλεόραση" (I eat while watching TV).
