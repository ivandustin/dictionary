Word: αμπελουργον

Etymology: The word αμπελουργον comes from the Greek words αμπέλι (ampéli) meaning "vine" and έργο (érgo) meaning "work" or "task". Therefore, αμπελουργον refers to the work or task related to vineyards.

Grammar: αμπελουργον is a noun in the neuter gender in the singular form.

Description: αμπελουργον refers to the work or task related to vineyards, such as planting, pruning, harvesting, and maintaining the vines. It is a term commonly used in the agricultural industry and is particularly relevant in regions where wine production is a significant part of the economy.
