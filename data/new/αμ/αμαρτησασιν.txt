The word "αμαρτησασιν" is a Greek verb in the aorist tense, third person plural form. It comes from the root word "αμαρτία" which means "sin" or "error". The verb form "αμαρτησασιν" means "they sinned" or "they made an error". In terms of grammar, it is a regular aorist verb with a reduplication of the first syllable and the suffix "-σασιν" indicating the third person plural.
