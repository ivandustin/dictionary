The word "αμμον" (ammon) is a Greek noun that means "sand" or "grain of sand". It is derived from the Greek word "άμμος" (ammos), which also means "sand". In terms of grammar, "αμμον" is a masculine noun in the singular form. It can be used to describe the texture of a surface or the composition of a substance. For example, "Η παραλία είναι γεμάτη από αμμόνια" (The beach is full of sand grains).
