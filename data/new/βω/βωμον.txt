Word: βωμον

Etymology:
βωμον (bōmon) is a Greek word that means "altar" or "raised platform". It is derived from the Greek word βωμός (bōmos), which means "an altar for sacrifice".

Grammar:
βωμον (bōmon) is a noun in the singular form. In Greek, it belongs to the second declension, which means that its stem ends in -ον (on) and its nominative singular form ends in -ος (os).

Description:
βωμον (bōmon) refers to an altar or a raised platform used for religious or sacrificial purposes. In ancient Greece, altars were often located in temples or other sacred places and were used for offerings to the gods. The word can also be used metaphorically to refer to a place of worship or reverence.
