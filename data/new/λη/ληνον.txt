Word: ληνόν (lenon)

Etymology: The word ληνόν (lenon) comes from the ancient Greek word λήνη (lenē), which means "a trough or basin for washing clothes." 

Grammar: ληνόν (lenon) is a masculine noun in the singular form. 

Description: ληνόν (lenon) refers to a trough or basin used for washing clothes. In ancient Greece, it was a common household item used for washing clothes by hand. The word can also be used metaphorically to refer to a difficult or laborious task, as washing clothes by hand was a time-consuming and physically demanding task.
