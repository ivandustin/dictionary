The word "λημψονται" (lemptontai) is a verb in the Greek language, specifically in the future tense and middle voice. It is derived from the root word "λαμβάνω" (lambano), which means "to take" or "to receive". 

In terms of grammar, "λημψονται" is a third-person plural form, indicating that the subject is a group of people or things. The middle voice suggests that the subject is acting upon themselves, rather than having something done to them by an external agent.

Overall, "λημψονται" can be translated to mean "they will take for themselves" or "they will receive for themselves".
