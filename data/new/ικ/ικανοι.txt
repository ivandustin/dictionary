Word: ικανοί

Grammar: ικανοί is the plural form of the adjective ικανός (ikanós), which means "able" or "capable". It is used to describe a group of people or things that have the ability to do something.

Description: ικανοί is an adjective that is often used to describe people who are skilled or capable in a particular area. It can also be used to describe things that are capable of performing a certain function or task. For example, one might say "Οι ικανοί μαθητές έλυσαν το πρόβλημα" (The capable students solved the problem) or "Το μηχάνημα είναι ικανό να κόψει το μέταλλο" (The machine is capable of cutting metal).
