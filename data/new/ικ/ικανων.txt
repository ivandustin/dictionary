Word: ικανων

Etymology: ικανων comes from the Greek word "ικανός" (ikanós), which means "able" or "capable".

Grammar: ικανων is a noun in the nominative case, singular form. It is a masculine noun.

Description: ικανων refers to someone who is capable or able to do something. It can also refer to someone who is competent or qualified in a particular field. In ancient Greek literature, ικανων was often used to describe warriors who were skilled in battle.
