τραπεζα (trapeza) is a Greek word that means "table" or "bank". 

Etymology: The word comes from the ancient Greek word "trapezion" which means "a table for moneychangers". 

Grammar: τραπεζα is a feminine noun in Greek and its plural form is τραπέζες (trapezes). 

Description: In modern Greek, τραπεζα is commonly used to refer to a bank or a financial institution. It can also refer to a table or a surface used for dining or working.
