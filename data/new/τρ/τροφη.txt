Word: τροφή

Etymology: The word τροφή (trophē) comes from the Greek verb τρέφω (trephō), which means "to nourish" or "to feed". 

Grammar: τροφή is a feminine noun in Greek. It is in the singular form and its nominative case is τροφή. 

Description: τροφή means "food" or "nourishment" in Greek. It refers to any substance that is consumed in order to sustain life and provide energy to the body. In a broader sense, it can also refer to anything that nourishes or sustains something else, such as the soil that nourishes plants or the education that nourishes the mind.
