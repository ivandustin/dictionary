Word: τρεχοντες

Etymology: τρέχω (trécho) is a Greek verb meaning "to run". τρεχοντες (trekhontes) is the present participle form of the verb, meaning "running".

Grammar: τρεχοντες is a masculine plural noun in the nominative case. It is used to describe a group of people who are currently running.

Description: τρεχοντες can be translated to "those who are running" or "the runners". It is often used in Greek literature and poetry to describe athletes or soldiers in battle.
