τριακοντα (triakonta) is a Greek word that means "thirty" in English. It is derived from the Greek words "τριάκοντα" (triakonta), which means "thirty," and "τρία" (tria), which means "three." In terms of grammar, τριακοντα is a cardinal number in Greek and is used to indicate the quantity of thirty. It can be used in various contexts, such as counting objects or expressing age.
