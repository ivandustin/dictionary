τρομου (tromou) is a Greek noun that means "terror" or "fear". 

Etymology: The word τρομου comes from the Greek verb τρέμω (tremo), which means "to tremble" or "to shake". 

Grammar: τρομου is a singular noun in the genitive case. 

Description: τρομου is often used in Greek literature and poetry to describe a feeling of intense fear or terror. It can also be used to describe a physical trembling or shaking caused by fear.
