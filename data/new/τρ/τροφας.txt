τροφάς (trofas) is a Greek noun that means "food" or "nourishment". It is derived from the verb τρέφω (trefō), which means "to nourish" or "to feed". In terms of grammar, τροφάς is a feminine noun in the nominative singular form. It can also be declined in other cases and numbers, such as τροφή (trofē) in the nominative plural form.
