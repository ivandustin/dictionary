Word: τριτος

Etymology:
The word τριτος (tritos) is derived from the Greek word τρίτος (tritos), which means "third". It is a derivative of the word τρεῖς (treis), which means "three".

Grammar:
τριτος is an adjective in the masculine gender, singular number, and nominative case. It can also be declined in other cases, genders, and numbers to agree with the noun it modifies.

Description:
τριτος is used to describe the third item in a series or sequence. It can also be used to describe something that is of third importance or rank. In Greek mythology, the third day of the month was considered to be an auspicious day for certain activities.
