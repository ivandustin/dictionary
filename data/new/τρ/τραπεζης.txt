Grammar: τραπεζης is a masculine noun in the nominative case. In the genitive case, it becomes τραπέζης (trapezes). 

Description: The word τραπεζης can refer to a piece of furniture used for dining or working, or to a financial institution where money is deposited or borrowed. In modern Greek, the word for "bank" is τράπεζα (trapeza), which is derived from the same root as τραπεζης.
