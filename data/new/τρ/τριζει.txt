The word "τριζει" (trizei) is a verb in the Greek language. 

Etymology: The word comes from the root "τρίζω" (trizo), which means "to creak" or "to squeak". 

Grammar: "τριζει" is in the present tense, third person singular form. It is conjugated from the verb "τρίζω". 

Description: The word "τριζει" describes the action of something making a creaking or squeaking sound. It can be used to describe the sound of a door, a floorboard, or any other object that makes a similar noise.
