τριτου (tritou) is a Greek word that means "of the third". It is derived from the Greek word τρίτος (tritos), which means "third". In grammar, τριτου is the genitive singular form of τρίτος, which is used to indicate possession or relationship to the third item in a series. 

For example, in the phrase "το βιβλίο του τρίτου", which means "the book of the third", τριτου indicates that the book belongs to or is related to the third item in a series. 

Overall, τριτου is a useful word in Greek for indicating the position or relationship of something to the third item in a series.
