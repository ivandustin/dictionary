τρεχει (trechei) is a Greek verb in the present tense, third person singular form. It comes from the root word τρέχω (trecho), which means "to run". The verb τρεχει is commonly used to describe someone or something that is currently running or moving quickly. 

In terms of grammar, τρεχει is a regular verb in the present tense, following the conjugation pattern of verbs ending in -ω. It can be conjugated in all six persons (first, second, and third person singular and plural) and in all tenses (present, past, future, etc.).

Example sentences:
- Ο σκύλος τρέχει στο πάρκο. (The dog is running in the park.)
- Η ομάδα τρέχει για να προλάβει τον αγώνα. (The team is running to make it to the game on time.)
- Το παιδί τρέχει προς τη μητέρα του. (The child is running towards his mother.)
