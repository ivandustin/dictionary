τραγων (tragōn) is a Greek word that means "he-goat" or "billy goat". It is derived from the Greek word τράγος (tragos), which also means "goat". In terms of grammar, τραγων is a masculine noun in the nominative case. It can also be declined in other cases, such as the genitive τράγωνος (tragōnos) or the accusative τράγωνα (tragōna).
