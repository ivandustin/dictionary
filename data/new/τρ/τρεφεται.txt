τρέφεται (trephetai) is a Greek verb in the present tense, third person singular form. It comes from the root word τρέφω (trefo), which means "to nourish" or "to feed". 

In terms of grammar, τρέφεται is a passive voice verb, indicating that the subject is receiving the action of the verb rather than performing it. 

Example sentence: Το παιδί τρέφεται με υγιεινά τρόφιμα. (To paidi trephetai me ygieina trofima) - The child is nourished with healthy food.
