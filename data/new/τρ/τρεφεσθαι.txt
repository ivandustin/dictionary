The word τρεφεσθαι comes from the Greek verb τρέφω (trefō), which means "to nourish" or "to feed". The suffix -σθαι (-sthai) indicates the reflexive form of the verb, meaning "to be nourished" or "to feed oneself".

τρεφεσθαι is a present middle/passive infinitive form of the verb τρέφω. In Greek, the middle voice indicates that the subject is both the doer and the receiver of the action. The passive voice indicates that the subject is the receiver of the action.
