τραπεζαις (trapezais) is a Greek word that is the plural form of τράπεζα (trapeza), which means "table" or "bank". The word is derived from the ancient Greek word τράπεζα, which referred to a table used for dining or playing games. In modern Greek, τράπεζα is primarily used to refer to a financial institution or bank. 

In terms of grammar, τραπεζαις is a feminine noun in the nominative or accusative plural case. It can also be spelled as τραπέζαις (trapezais) with an accent on the second syllable. 

Overall, τραπεζαις is a versatile word that can refer to both a piece of furniture and a financial institution, depending on the context in which it is used.
