Etymology: The word "θανατον" (thanaton) comes from the Greek word "θάνατος" (thanatos), meaning "death". 

Grammar: "Θανατον" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the accusative case, singular number.

Description: "Θανατον" refers to "death" in Greek. It is used to describe the permanent cessation of all vital functions in a living organism.
