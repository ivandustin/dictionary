Etymology: The word "θαυμασαντες" comes from the Greek verb "θαυμάζω" (thaumázō), which means "to marvel" or "to wonder". The suffix "-αντες" (-antes) is a participle ending, indicating the plural form of the verb.

Grammar: "θαυμασαντες" is the plural form of the verb "θαυμάζω" in the aorist tense. It is used to describe a group of people who were marveling or wondering about something in the past.

Description: "θαυμασαντες" is a Greek word that can be translated as "marveling" or "wondering". It is often used to express astonishment or admiration for something extraordinary or unexpected.
