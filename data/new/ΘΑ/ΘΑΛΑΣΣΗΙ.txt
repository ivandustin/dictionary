Etymology: The word "θαλασση" (thalassi) comes from the Greek word "θάλασσα" (thalassa), which means "sea". 

Grammar: In Greek, "θαλασση" is a feminine noun. It is in the nominative case and singular form. 

Description: "Θαλασση" refers to the sea in Greek. It is a common word used to describe large bodies of saltwater, such as oceans or seas.
