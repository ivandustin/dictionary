Etymology: The word "θαυμαστα" (thavmasta) comes from the Greek verb "θαυμάζω" (thavmazo), which means "to marvel" or "to wonder". It is derived from the noun "θαῦμα" (thavma), meaning "wonder" or "marvel".

Grammar: "Θαυμαστα" is the plural form of the adjective "θαυμαστός" (thavmastós), which means "marvelous" or "wonderful". In Greek, adjectives agree in gender, number, and case with the noun they modify. Therefore, "θαυμαστα" is used to describe plural nouns that are marvelous or wonderful.

Description: "Θαυμαστα" is an adjective used to describe things or situations that are extraordinary, impressive, or awe-inspiring. It is often used to express admiration or astonishment towards something remarkable or exceptional.
