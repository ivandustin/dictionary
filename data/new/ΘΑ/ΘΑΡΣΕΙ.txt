Grammar: "θαρσεί" is the third person singular form of the verb "θαρσέω" in the present tense. It is used to describe an action or state of being that is happening in the present and is performed by a third person.

Description: "θαρσεί" is a Greek word that means "he/she/it is bold" or "he/she/it has courage". It is used to describe someone who is brave, confident, or fearless in facing challenges or difficult situations.
