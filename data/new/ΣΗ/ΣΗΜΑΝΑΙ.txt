Etymology: The word "σημαναί" (sēmanaí) comes from the Greek verb "σημαίνω" (sēmaínō), which means "to signify" or "to indicate."

Grammar: "Σημαναί" is a noun in the plural form. It belongs to the third declension in Greek grammar.

Description: "Σημαναί" refers to signs or indications. It can also be used to describe symbols or signals that convey meaning or information.
