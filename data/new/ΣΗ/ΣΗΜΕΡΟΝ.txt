Etymology: The word "σημερον" (sēmeron) comes from the Ancient Greek word "σήμερον" (sēmeron), which means "today".

Grammar: In Greek, "σημερον" is an adverb that means "today". It is used to refer to the current day or the present time.

Description: "Σημερον" is a commonly used word in Greek to indicate the current day or the present time. It is often used in everyday conversations and written texts to specify the day or time being referred to.
