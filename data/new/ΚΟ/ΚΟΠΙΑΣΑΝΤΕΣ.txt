Etymology: The word "κοπιασάντες" comes from the Greek verb "κοπιάζω" (kopiazó), which means "to toil" or "to work hard". The suffix "-άντες" is added to the verb stem to form a noun that denotes the people who perform the action.

Grammar: "Κοπιασάντες" is a plural noun in the masculine gender. It is formed by adding the suffix "-άντες" to the verb stem "κοπιάζ-". In Greek grammar, nouns change their endings to indicate gender, number, and case.

Description: "Κοπιασάντες" refers to a group of people who have worked hard or toiled. It can be used to describe individuals who have put in a significant amount of effort or labor in a particular task or project.
