Etymology: The word "κοινωνείτω" comes from the Greek verb "κοινωνέω" (koinoneo), which means "to share" or "to participate in." It is derived from the noun "κοινωνία" (koinonia), meaning "fellowship" or "community."

Grammar: "Κοινωνείτω" is the third person singular imperative form of the verb "κοινωνέω" in Greek. It is used to give a command or instruction to someone, telling them to share or participate in something.

Description: "Κοινωνείτω" is a Greek word that encourages sharing and participation. It is often used in a religious or moral context, urging individuals to contribute to the well-being of a community or society.
