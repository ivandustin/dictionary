Etymology: The word "κοινωνείτε" comes from the Greek verb "κοινωνέω" (koinonéo), which means "to share" or "to participate in." It is derived from the noun "κοινωνία" (koinonía), meaning "community" or "fellowship."

Grammar: "Κοινωνείτε" is the second person plural form of the present tense in Greek. It is used when addressing a group of people and means "you share" or "you participate in."
