Etymology: The word "κοπιώ" (kopio) comes from the Greek verb "κοπιάω" (kopiao), which means "to toil" or "to work hard." It is derived from the noun "κόπος" (kopos), meaning "labor" or "effort."

Grammar: "Κοπιώ" is the first person singular present tense form of the verb "κοπιάω" (kopiao). It is used to express the action of working hard or toiling in the present.
