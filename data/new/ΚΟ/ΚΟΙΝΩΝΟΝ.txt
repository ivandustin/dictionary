Grammar: "Κοινωνον" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the singular form and belongs to the second declension. In the nominative case, it is spelled as "κοινωνον". It can also be declined in other cases, such as genitive (κοινωνου), dative (κοινωνῳ), and accusative (κοινωνον).

Description: "Κοινωνον" refers to a person who participates in or shares in something. It can be translated as "participant", "sharer", or "contributor". The word is often used in the context of social or communal activities, indicating someone who is actively involved in a community or fellowship.
