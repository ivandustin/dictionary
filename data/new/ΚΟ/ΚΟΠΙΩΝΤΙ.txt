Grammar: "κοπιωντι" is the present participle form of the verb "κοπιάω" in the third person singular. In Greek grammar, the present participle is used to describe ongoing actions or states. It can function as an adjective or as part of a verb phrase.

Description: "κοπιωντι" describes someone or something that is currently toiling or working hard. It indicates continuous action or state of labor.
