Grammar: "κοπιωσας" is the second person singular form of the aorist tense of the verb "κοπιάω" (kopiao). In Greek, verbs change their form based on the tense, mood, voice, and person. The aorist tense is used to express a completed action in the past.

Description: "κοπιωσας" is a Greek word that translates to "you worked hard" or "you toiled" in English. It is used to describe someone who has put in a significant amount of effort or labor into a task or activity.
