Etymology: The word "κοσμοκρατορας" comes from the Greek words "κόσμος" (kosmos) meaning "world" and "κρατερός" (krateros) meaning "ruler" or "powerful". 

Grammar: In Greek, "κοσμοκρατορας" is a noun in the masculine gender. It is in the nominative case, which is used for the subject of a sentence.

Description: "Κοσμοκρατορας" refers to a ruler or a powerful figure who has control or dominion over the world. It can be used metaphorically to describe someone who has great influence or authority over a particular domain or sphere.
