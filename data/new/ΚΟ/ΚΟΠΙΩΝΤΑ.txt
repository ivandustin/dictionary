Etymology: The word "κοπιοντα" comes from the Greek verb "κοπιάω" (kopiao), which means "to toil" or "to work hard". 

Grammar: "κοπιοντα" is the present participle form of the verb "κοπιάω" (kopiao) in the masculine gender, singular number, and accusative case. 

Description: "κοπιοντα" is used to describe someone or something that is currently toiling or working hard. It indicates ongoing action or state.
