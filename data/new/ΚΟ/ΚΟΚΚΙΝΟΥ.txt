Etymology: The word "κοκκίνου" (kokkinou) comes from the Greek word "κόκκινος" (kokkinos), which means "red". 

Grammar: "κοκκίνου" is the genitive form of the word "κόκκινος" (kokkinos), which is an adjective in Greek. It agrees in gender, number, and case with the noun it modifies. 

Description: "κοκκίνου" is used to describe something that is red in color. It can be used to describe objects, animals, or even people with red hair.
