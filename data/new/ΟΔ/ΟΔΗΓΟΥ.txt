Etymology: The word "οδηγου" (odigou) comes from the Greek verb "οδηγώ" (odigo), which means "to guide" or "to lead". 

Grammar: "Οδηγου" is a noun in the genitive case. In Greek grammar, the genitive case is used to indicate possession, origin, or relationship.

Description: "Οδηγου" refers to the genitive form of the noun "οδηγός" (odigos), which means "guide" or "leader" in Greek. It is used to describe someone who leads or guides others, either literally or metaphorically.
