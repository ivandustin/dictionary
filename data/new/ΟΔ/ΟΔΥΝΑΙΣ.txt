Etymology: The word "οδυναις" (odunais) comes from the Greek word "οδυνη" (oduni), which means "pain" or "sorrow".

Grammar: "Οδυναις" is a noun in the plural form. It belongs to the third declension in Greek grammar.

Description: "Οδυναις" refers to pains or sorrows. It can be used to describe physical or emotional pain.
