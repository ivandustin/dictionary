Etymology: The word "οδων" (odōn) comes from the Greek word "ὁδός" (hodos), meaning "road" or "way".

Grammar: "Οδων" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the masculine gender and in the nominative case.

Description: "Οδων" refers to a road or a way. It can be used to describe a physical path or route that people or vehicles can travel on. It can also be used metaphorically to represent a course of action or a direction in life.
