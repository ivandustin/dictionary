Etymology: The word "οδηγον" (odigon) comes from the Greek verb "οδηγεω" (odigeo), which means "to lead" or "to guide". 

Grammar: "Οδηγον" is a noun in the singular form. It belongs to the second declension in Greek grammar. 

Description: "Οδηγον" refers to a guide or a leader who shows the way or provides direction. It can also be used to describe a manual or a handbook that provides instructions or guidance.
