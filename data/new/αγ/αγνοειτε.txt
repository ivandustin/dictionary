Word: αγνοειτε

Etymology: The word αγνοειτε comes from the Greek verb αγνοώ (agnóo), which means "to ignore" or "to be unaware of". It is formed by adding the suffix -είτε (-eíte) to the present tense stem αγνο- (agno-).

Grammar: αγνοειτε is the second person plural present tense form of the verb αγνοώ (agnóo). It is used to address a group of people and means "you ignore" or "you are unaware of".

Description: αγνοειτε is a verb that expresses the action of ignoring or being unaware of something. It can be used in various contexts, such as when someone is not paying attention to important information or when they are deliberately choosing to ignore something. The word is commonly used in everyday speech and writing in Greek.
