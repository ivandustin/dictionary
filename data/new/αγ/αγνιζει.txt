The word "αγνιζει" (agnizei) is a verb in the Greek language. 

Etymology: The word comes from the Greek root "αγνός" (agnos), which means "pure" or "clean". 

Grammar: "αγνιζει" is in the present tense, third person singular form. It is a regular verb that follows the conjugation pattern of verbs ending in "-ιζω" (-izo). 

Description: The word means "to purify" or "to cleanse". It is often used in a religious or spiritual context, referring to the act of purifying oneself or one's soul. It can also be used in a literal sense, such as purifying water or cleaning a surface.
