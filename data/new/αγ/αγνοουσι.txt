The word "αγνοουσι" (agnousi) is a verb in Greek that means "they ignore" or "they are unaware of". 

Etymology: The word comes from the Greek verb "αγνοέω" (agnoeo), which means "to ignore" or "to be unaware of". 

Grammar: "αγνοουσι" is the third person plural form of the present tense of the verb "αγνοέω". 

Description: The word is often used to describe a situation where someone is not aware of something or is intentionally ignoring it. It can also be used to describe a lack of knowledge or understanding about a particular topic.
