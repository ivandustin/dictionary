Word: αγνοει

The word αγνοει (agnoei) is a verb in the present tense, third person singular form of the Greek verb αγνοώ (agnoó), which means "to ignore" or "to be unaware of". The verb comes from the ancient Greek word αγνος (agnos), which means "unknown" or "ignorant".

αγνοει is a present tense, third person singular form of the verb αγνοώ. In Greek, verbs change their endings to indicate the tense, mood, and person of the subject. The word αγνοει is used to describe the action of ignoring or being unaware of something.
