Word: αγρυπνιαις

αγρυπνιαις is a feminine noun in the dative plural case. In Greek, the dative case is used to indicate the indirect object of a verb or the object of certain prepositions.

αγρυπνιαις refers to multiple instances of sleeplessness or insomnia. It can be used to describe a state of being unable to sleep or a period of time during which one experiences sleeplessness.
