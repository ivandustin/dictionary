In terms of grammar, "αγνισωσιν" is a regular verb in the present tense, and it is conjugated according to the rules of the Greek language. 

As for its usage, "αγνισωσιν" is often used in a religious or spiritual context, referring to the act of purifying oneself or one's soul. It can also be used more generally to refer to the act of cleansing or purifying something.
