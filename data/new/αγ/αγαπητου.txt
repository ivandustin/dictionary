Word: αγαπητού

Etymology: αγαπητού is a Greek word that comes from the root word αγαπη (agape), which means love. The suffix -τού (-tou) is a possessive suffix that means "of" or "belonging to". Therefore, αγαπητού means "belonging to love" or "of love".

Grammar: αγαπητού is a masculine singular genitive form of the adjective αγαπητός (agapitos), which means "beloved" or "dear". In Greek, adjectives agree in gender, number, and case with the noun they modify. In this case, αγαπητού modifies a masculine singular noun in the genitive case.

Description: αγαπητού is a term of endearment used to refer to someone who is beloved or dear. It can be used to describe a person, object, or even a concept. In Greek culture, expressing love and affection is highly valued, and αγαπητού is a common way to show this sentiment.
