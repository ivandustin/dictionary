Word: αγαλλιάσει

Etymology: The word αγαλλιάσει comes from the Greek verb αγαλλιάω (agalliaó), which means "to exult, to rejoice, to be glad". The suffix -σει (-sei) is the third person singular future tense ending.

Grammar: αγαλλιάσει is a verb in the future tense, third person singular. It is formed by adding the future tense ending -σει to the stem αγαλλιά-.

Description: αγαλλιάσει means "he/she/it will exult/rejoice/be glad" in Greek. It is often used in a religious or spiritual context, to express joy and celebration.
