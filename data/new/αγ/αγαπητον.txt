Word: αγαπητον

Etymology: αγαπητον is a Greek word that comes from the root word αγαπη (agape), which means love. The suffix -τον (-ton) is a common ending in Greek that is used to form adjectives.

Grammar: αγαπητον is an adjective in the masculine singular form. It is used to describe something or someone that is beloved or dear.

Description: αγαπητον is a term that is often used in Greek literature and poetry to express love and affection. It can be used to describe a person, object, or even an idea that is cherished and valued. In modern Greek, it is still commonly used to express endearment towards loved ones.
