The word "αγραμματοι" is a Greek term that literally means "illiterate" or "unlettered". It is derived from the Greek words "α-" (a-), which means "not", and "γράμμα" (gramma), which means "letter" or "writing". In terms of grammar, "αγραμματοι" is a plural noun in the nominative case. It is often used to describe people who are unable to read or write, or who lack formal education.
