The word αγνοησαντες is derived from the Greek verb αγνοέω (agnoeo), which means "to ignore" or "to be unaware of". The suffix -αντες (-antes) is a participle ending that indicates the action of the verb is in the past tense and the subject is plural.

αγνοησαντες is a past participle in the Greek language. It is in the masculine gender, plural number, and nominative case. It is often used as an adjective to describe a group of people who have ignored or been unaware of something.
