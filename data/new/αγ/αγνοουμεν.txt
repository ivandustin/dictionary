In terms of grammar, "αγνοουμεν" is in the present tense and the passive voice. It is also in the masculine, plural form, indicating that the subject is a group of males.

In terms of description, "αγνοουμεν" can be translated to mean "being ignored" or "being unaware of". It is often used to describe a situation where someone or something is being overlooked or not given attention. 

Overall, the word "αγνοουμεν" highlights the importance of paying attention and being aware of one's surroundings.
