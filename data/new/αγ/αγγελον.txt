The word "αγγελον" (angelon) is a Greek noun that means "messenger" or "angel". It is derived from the Greek verb "αγγελλω" (angello), which means "to announce" or "to proclaim". In terms of grammar, "αγγελον" is a masculine noun in the accusative case. It can be used to refer to both human messengers and divine beings in Greek mythology and Christianity.
