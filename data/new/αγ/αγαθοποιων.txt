The word "αγαθοποιων" (agathopoion) is a Greek noun that means "a doer of good" or "a benefactor". It is derived from the Greek words "αγαθός" (agathos) meaning "good" and "ποιών" (poion) meaning "doing". In terms of grammar, it is a masculine noun in the nominative case. It can be used to describe someone who performs good deeds or acts of kindness towards others.
