The word "αγιασμόν" (agiasmon) is a Greek noun that means "blessing" or "consecration". It comes from the verb "αγιάζω" (agiazo), which means "to make holy" or "to sanctify". In terms of grammar, it is a masculine noun in the accusative case. It can be used in various contexts, such as in religious ceremonies or in everyday language to express good wishes or gratitude.
