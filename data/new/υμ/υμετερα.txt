Word: υμετερα

Etymology: υμετερα is a Greek word that comes from the combination of two words: υμεις (you) and ετερος (other). Therefore, υμετερα means "your other" or "belonging to you".

Grammar: υμετερα is an adjective in the feminine gender, singular number, and nominative case. It is used to describe a feminine noun that belongs to the person or people being addressed.

Description: υμετερα is a word that expresses possession or ownership. It is commonly used in Greek to indicate that something belongs to the person or people being addressed. For example, if someone says "υμετερα κατοικια" (your house), they are referring to the house that belongs to the person or people they are speaking to.
