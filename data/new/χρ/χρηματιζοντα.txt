The word "χρηματιζοντα" (chrematizonta) is a present participle in Greek, derived from the verb "χρηματίζω" (chrematizo), which means "to transact business" or "to make money." 

In terms of grammar, the word is in the present tense and active voice, and it is declined to agree with the gender, number, and case of the noun it modifies. 

As for its usage, "χρηματιζοντα" can be used to describe someone who is currently engaged in business or money-making activities. It can also be used figuratively to describe someone who is focused on material gain or profit. 

Overall, "χρηματιζοντα" is a versatile word that can be used in a variety of contexts related to business and finance.
