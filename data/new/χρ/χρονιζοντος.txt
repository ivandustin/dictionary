The word "χρονιζοντος" is a present participle form of the Greek verb "χρονίζω" (chronizo), which means "to delay" or "to procrastinate". 

In terms of grammar, "χρονιζοντος" is in the genitive case, masculine gender, and singular number. It is often used as an adjective to describe a person who is delaying or procrastinating.

Overall, "χρονιζοντος" is a word that describes someone who is putting off something that needs to be done, often to the frustration of others.
