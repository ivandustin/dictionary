The word "χρησον" (chreson) is a Greek noun that means "useful thing" or "necessary thing". 

Etymology: The word comes from the Greek verb "χρῆσθαι" (chresthai), which means "to use" or "to make use of". 

Grammar: "Χρησον" is a neuter noun in the singular form. 

Description: The word "χρησον" is often used in Greek literature to refer to objects or tools that are necessary or useful for a particular task. It can also be used to describe a person who is helpful or beneficial in some way.
