The word "χρηματισθεντες" is a Greek verb that is derived from the noun "χρῆμα" (chrēma), meaning "money" or "property". The verb form means "to be informed" or "to receive a message", and is often used in the context of financial transactions or business dealings. 

In terms of grammar, "χρηματισθεντες" is the passive participle form of the verb "χρηματίζω" (chrēmatizō), which means "to transact business" or "to give advice". The passive participle form indicates that the subject of the verb is receiving the action, rather than performing it.

Overall, "χρηματισθεντες" can be translated as "having been informed" or "having received a message", with a specific emphasis on financial or business-related information.
