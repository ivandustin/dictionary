Word: χρονιζει

Etymology: The word χρονιζει comes from the Greek verb χρονίζω (chronízo), which means "to last a long time" or "to endure". It is derived from the noun χρόνος (chrónos), meaning "time".

Grammar: χρονιζει is a verb in the present tense, third person singular form. It is conjugated from the verb χρονίζω (chronízo), which is a regular verb in Greek.

Description: χρονιζει means "lasts a long time" or "endures" in English. It is often used to describe something that takes a long time to happen or to describe a person who is patient and persistent.
