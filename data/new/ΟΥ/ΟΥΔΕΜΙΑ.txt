Grammar: "Ουδεμία" is a feminine singular form of the adjective, used to describe a feminine singular noun. It agrees in gender, number, and case with the noun it modifies.

Description: "Ουδεμία" means "no" or "none" in Greek. It is used to indicate the absence or lack of something. It is often used in negative sentences or to negate the existence of something.
