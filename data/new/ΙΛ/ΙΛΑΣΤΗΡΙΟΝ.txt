Etymology: The word "ιλαστήριον" (ilastirion) comes from the Greek verb "ιλάσκομαι" (ilaskomai), which means "to appease" or "to propitiate." It is derived from the noun "ιλασμός" (ilasmos), meaning "propitiation" or "atonement."
