The word "ουρανωι" (ouranoi) is a Greek adjective that means "heavenly" or "celestial". It is derived from the Greek word "ουρανός" (ouranos), which means "sky" or "heaven". In terms of grammar, "ουρανωι" is in the masculine nominative singular form. It can be used to describe anything that is related to the heavens or the celestial realm.
