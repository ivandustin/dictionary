Word: ουραις

Etymology: Ουραις is a Greek word that comes from the noun ουρά (oura), which means "tail". Ουραις is the plural form of ουρά.

Grammar: Ουραις is a feminine noun in Greek. It is in the nominative plural form.

Description: Ουραις refers to tails, specifically the tails of animals. It can also be used metaphorically to refer to the end or conclusion of something.
