Word: ουτοι

Etymology: ουτοι is a Greek pronoun that means "these" or "those". It is derived from the Greek word "οὗτος" (houtos), which means "this".

Grammar: ουτοι is a plural pronoun in Greek. It is used to refer to a group of people or things that are near the speaker or the listener.

Description: ουτοι is a common word in Greek that is used in everyday conversation. It is often used to refer to a group of people or things that are being discussed. For example, "Ουτοι οι φοιτητές είναι πολύ έξυπνοι" (These students are very smart).
