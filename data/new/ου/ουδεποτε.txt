Word: ουδεποτε

Etymology: ουδεποτε is a compound word in Greek, consisting of the negative particle ου (ou) meaning "not" and the adverb ποτε (pote) meaning "ever" or "at any time". Therefore, ουδεποτε means "never" or "not ever".

Grammar: ουδεποτε is an adverb in Greek, which means it modifies the verb in a sentence. It is used to indicate that something has never happened or will never happen.

Description: ουδεποτε is a common word in Greek and is used in everyday conversation. It is often used to express a negative sentiment or to deny something. For example, "Δεν έχω ποτέ πάει στην Ιαπωνία" (Den exo pote paei stin Iaponia) means "I have never been to Japan". It can also be used in combination with other negative words, such as "ούτε ποτέ" (oute pote) meaning "never ever".
