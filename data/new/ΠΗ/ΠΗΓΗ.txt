Etymology: The word "πηγή" (pigi) comes from the Ancient Greek word "πηγή" (pēgē), meaning "source" or "spring".

Grammar: In Greek, "πηγή" is a feminine noun. It is declined as follows:
- Singular: η πηγή (i pigi) - the source
- Plural: οι πηγές (i piges) - the sources

Description: "Πηγή" refers to a natural source of water, such as a spring, well, or fountain. It can also be used metaphorically to describe the origin or starting point of something.
