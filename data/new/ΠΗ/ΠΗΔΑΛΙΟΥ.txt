Grammar: "Πηδάλιο" is a noun in the Greek language. It is a neuter noun and its plural form is "πηδάλια" (pidalia).

Description: "Πηδάλιο" refers to the pedal of a vehicle or a musical instrument. It is the part that is operated by foot to control various functions. For example, in a car, the accelerator, brake, and clutch pedals are all referred to as "πηδάλια".
