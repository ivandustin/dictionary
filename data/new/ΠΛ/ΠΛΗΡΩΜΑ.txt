Grammar: "Πληρωμα" is a noun in the Greek language. It is a neuter noun of the first declension, and its nominative singular form is "πληρωμα." In the plural form, it becomes "πληρωματα" (plērōmata).

Description: "Πληρωμα" is a Greek word that can be translated as "fullness," "completeness," or "abundance." It refers to something that is filled or complete, lacking nothing. In a spiritual or philosophical context, it can also refer to the totality of existence or the divine essence.
