Etymology: The word "πληρωθείτε" (pronounced "pleróthite") comes from the Greek verb "πληρόω" (pleróo), which means "to fill" or "to complete". It is derived from the noun "πλήρης" (pléres), meaning "full" or "complete".

Grammar: "Πληρωθείτε" is the second person plural form of the verb "πληρόω" in the aorist tense, passive voice, and subjunctive mood. It is used to address a group of people and expresses the idea of being filled or completed by someone or something.

Description: The word "πληρωθείτε" is often used in a religious or spiritual context to convey the idea of being filled or fulfilled by a higher power or divine presence. It can also be used metaphorically to express the idea of being satisfied or content in a general sense.
