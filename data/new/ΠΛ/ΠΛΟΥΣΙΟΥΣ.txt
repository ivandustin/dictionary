Grammar: "Πλουσίους" is the plural form of the adjective "πλούσιος" in the nominative case. In Greek, adjectives agree in gender, number, and case with the noun they modify. Therefore, "πλουσίους" is used to describe plural masculine nouns in the nominative case.

Description: "Πλουσίους" is an adjective that describes multiple rich or wealthy individuals. It can be used to refer to a group of people who possess a significant amount of wealth or abundance.
