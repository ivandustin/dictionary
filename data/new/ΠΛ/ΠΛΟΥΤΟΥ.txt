Etymology: The word "πλουτού" (ploutou) comes from the Greek verb "πλουτέω" (plouteo), which means "to be rich" or "to have abundance". 

Grammar: "Πλουτού" is the 2nd person singular imperative form of the verb "πλουτέω" (plouteo). It is used to give a command or instruction to someone, specifically telling them to "be rich" or "have abundance".

Description: "Πλουτού" is a Greek word that encourages someone to seek wealth and abundance. It can be seen as a motivational word, urging individuals to strive for financial success and prosperity.
