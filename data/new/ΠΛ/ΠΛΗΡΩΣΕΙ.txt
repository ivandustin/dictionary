Etymology: The word "πληρώσει" comes from the Greek verb "πληρώνω" (plerono), which means "to fill" or "to complete."

Grammar: "Πληρώσει" is the third person singular future tense form of the verb "πληρώνω" (plerono), which means "he/she/it will fill" or "he/she/it will complete."

Description: "Πληρώσει" is a Greek word that refers to an action that will be completed in the future. It is commonly used to express the idea of filling or completing something.
