Etymology: The word "πληρώσαι" (plērṓsai) comes from the Greek verb "πληρόω" (plēróō), which means "to fill" or "to complete."

Grammar: "πληρώσαι" is the aorist active infinitive form of the verb "πληρόω" (plēróō). In Greek grammar, the aorist tense is used to express a completed action in the past. The active voice indicates that the subject of the verb is performing the action.

Description: "πληρώσαι" is a verb form that expresses the action of filling or completing something. It can be translated as "to fill" or "to complete" in English.
