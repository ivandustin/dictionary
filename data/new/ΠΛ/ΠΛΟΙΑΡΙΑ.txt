Etymology: The word "πλοιαρία" comes from the Greek word "πλοῖον" (ploion), meaning "ship" or "vessel."

Grammar: "Πλοιαρία" is a feminine noun in Greek. It belongs to the second declension and has the plural form "πλοιαρίες" (ploiaries).

Description: "Πλοιαρία" refers to a small boat or vessel, typically used for transportation or recreational purposes. It can also refer to a ship or boat in general.
