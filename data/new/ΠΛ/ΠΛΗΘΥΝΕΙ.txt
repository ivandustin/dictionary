Etymology: The word "πληθυνεί" comes from the Greek verb "πληθύνω" (plithýno), which means "to multiply" or "to increase in number".

Grammar: "Πληθυνεί" is the third person singular form of the present tense of the verb "πληθύνω" (plithýno). It is used to describe an action that is happening in the present and is performed by a third person.

Description: "Πληθυνεί" means "he/she/it multiplies" or "he/she/it increases in number" in Greek. It is used to indicate that someone or something is multiplying or increasing in quantity or size.
