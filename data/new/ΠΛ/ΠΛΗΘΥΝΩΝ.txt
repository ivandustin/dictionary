Grammar: "Πληθύνων" is the present participle form of the verb "πληθύνω" in the masculine singular nominative case. In Greek grammar, the present participle is used to describe ongoing or continuous actions. The masculine singular nominative case indicates that the subject of the verb is a singular masculine noun.

Description: "Πληθύνων" can be translated as "multiplying" or "increasing". It describes the action of multiplying or increasing in number.
