Etymology: The word "πληρούν" (plēroún) comes from the Greek verb "πληρόω" (plēróō), which means "to fill" or "to complete."

Grammar: "Πληρούν" is the present participle form of the verb "πληρόω" (plēróō) in Greek. It is used to describe an ongoing action of filling or completing.

Description: "Πληρούν" is a Greek word that refers to the act of filling or completing something. It can be used in various contexts, such as filling a container with liquid, completing a task or assignment, or fulfilling a requirement. The word emphasizes the action of bringing something to its full or desired state.
