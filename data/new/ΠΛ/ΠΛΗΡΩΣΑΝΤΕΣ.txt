Etymology: The word "πληρωσάντες" comes from the Greek verb "πληρόω" (plēróō), which means "to fill" or "to complete". The suffix "-σάντες" is the aorist participle form of the verb, indicating past action.

Grammar: "πληρωσάντες" is the aorist participle form of the verb "πληρόω" (plēróō), which means "having filled" or "having completed". It is used to describe an action that was completed in the past.

Description: "πληρωσάντες" is a Greek word that is used to describe an action of filling or completing something in the past. It is commonly used in Greek literature and can be found in various contexts.
