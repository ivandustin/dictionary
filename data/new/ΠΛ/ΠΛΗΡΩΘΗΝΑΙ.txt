Etymology: The word "πληρωθείναι" (plirothíne) comes from the Greek verb "πληρόω" (pliróo), which means "to fill" or "to complete". The suffix "-θείναι" (-thíne) is added to form the passive voice.

Grammar: "Πληρωθείναι" is the infinitive form of the verb and is used to express the action of being filled or completed. It is in the passive voice, indicating that the subject of the sentence is being acted upon.

Description: "Πληρωθείναι" is a verb in the Greek language that describes the process of being filled or completed. It can be used in various contexts to indicate the fulfillment or accomplishment of something.
