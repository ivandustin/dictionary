Etymology: The word "πλαναί" (planaí) comes from the Greek verb "πλανάω" (planaó), which means "to wander" or "to roam". 

Grammar: "Πλαναί" is the plural form of the noun "πλάνη" (pláni), which means "wandering" or "roaming". It is a feminine noun in Greek.

Description: "Πλαναί" refers to a group of people or things that are wandering or roaming. It can be used to describe a group of wanderers, nomads, or people who are constantly on the move.
