Etymology: The word "πλειοσίν" (pleiosin) comes from the Greek word "πλείων" (pleion), meaning "more" or "greater".

Grammar: "Πλειοσίν" is a noun in the accusative case, singular number.

Description: "Πλειοσίν" is a Greek word that means "more" or "greater". It is often used to indicate a larger quantity or a higher degree.
