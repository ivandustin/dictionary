Etymology: The word "πληρωση" (plirosi) comes from the Greek verb "πληρώνω" (plirono), which means "to fill" or "to complete".

Grammar: "Πληρωση" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the singular form and belongs to the feminine gender.

Description: "Πληρωση" refers to the act of filling or completing something. It can be used in various contexts, such as filling out a form, completing a task, or fulfilling a requirement.
