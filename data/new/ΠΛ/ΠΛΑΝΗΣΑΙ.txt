Etymology: The word "πλανησαί" (planēsai) comes from the Greek verb "πλανάω" (planáō), which means "to wander" or "to roam". 

Grammar: "πλανησαί" is the aorist imperative form of the verb "πλανάω" (planáō), which is used to give a command or instruction. In this form, it is specifically addressing the second person singular (you) and is in the aorist tense, which indicates a completed action in the past.
