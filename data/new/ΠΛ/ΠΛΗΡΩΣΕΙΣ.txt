Grammar: "Πληρωσεις" is the plural form of the noun "πληρωση" (plērōsē), which means "fulfillment" or "completion". It belongs to the third declension in Greek grammar.

Description: "Πληρωσεις" refers to multiple instances of fulfillment or completion. It can be used in various contexts, such as completing a task, meeting a requirement, or achieving a goal.
