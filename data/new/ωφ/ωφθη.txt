Etymology: The word comes from the Greek root "optikos", which means "relating to sight". 

Grammar: "ωφθη" is a third person singular aorist passive indicative verb. 

Description: The word is often used in the New Testament of the Bible to describe instances of miraculous healing or divine revelation.
