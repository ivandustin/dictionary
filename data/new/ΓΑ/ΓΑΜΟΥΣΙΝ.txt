Etymology: The word "γαμουσιν" comes from the Greek word "γάμος" (gamos), meaning "marriage" or "wedding."

Grammar: "Γαμουσιν" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the accusative case and is the plural form of the word "γαμούσιν" (gamousin).

Description: "Γαμουσιν" refers to multiple weddings or marriages. It is used to describe the act of getting married or the ceremony itself.
