Etymology: The word "γαμών" (gamōn) comes from the Greek verb "γαμώ" (gamō), which means "to marry" or "to wed."

Grammar: "Γαμών" (gamōn) is a noun in the masculine gender and singular number. It belongs to the second declension in Greek grammar.

Description: "Γαμών" (gamōn) refers to a man who is getting married or has recently married. It is commonly used to describe the groom in a wedding context.
