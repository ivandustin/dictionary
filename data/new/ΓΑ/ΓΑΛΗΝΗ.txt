Etymology: The word "γαλήνη" (galini) comes from the Greek language. It is derived from the ancient Greek word "γαλήνη" (galene), meaning "calmness" or "tranquility".

Grammar: In Greek, "γαλήνη" (galini) is a feminine noun. It belongs to the second declension and has a singular form.

Description: "Γαλήνη" (galini) refers to a state of calmness, tranquility, or peace. It can be used to describe a peaceful atmosphere, a serene environment, or a peaceful state of mind.
