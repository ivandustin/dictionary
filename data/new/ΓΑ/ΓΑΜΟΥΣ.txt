Etymology: The word "γαμούς" (gamous) comes from the Greek language. It is derived from the ancient Greek word "γάμος" (gamos), which means "marriage" or "wedding".

Grammar: In Greek, "γαμούς" is a noun in the plural form. It belongs to the second declension and is classified as a masculine noun.

Description: "Γαμούς" refers to weddings or marriage ceremonies. It is used to describe the event or celebration that takes place when two people get married.
