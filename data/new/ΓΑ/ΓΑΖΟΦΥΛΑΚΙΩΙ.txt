Etymology: The word "γαζοφυλακιο" (gazofylakio) comes from the Greek words "γάζα" (gaza) meaning "treasure" and "φυλακή" (fylaki) meaning "prison". It literally translates to "treasure prison".

Grammar: "Γαζοφυλακιο" is a noun in the singular form. In Greek grammar, it belongs to the neuter gender.

Description: "Γαζοφυλακιο" is a term used in Greek to describe a place where valuable items or treasures are stored securely. It can refer to a vault, a strongroom, or a treasury.
