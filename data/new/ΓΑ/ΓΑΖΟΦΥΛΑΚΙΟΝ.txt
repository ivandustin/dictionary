Etymology: The word "γαζοφυλακιον" (gazofylakion) comes from the Greek words "γαζα" (gaza) meaning "treasure" and "φυλακη" (fylaki) meaning "guard" or "prison". It literally translates to "treasure guard" or "treasury".

Grammar: In Greek, "γαζοφυλακιον" is a noun of neuter gender. It is in the singular form.

Description: "Γαζοφυλακιον" refers to a treasury or a place where valuable items or money are kept under guard. It can also be used metaphorically to describe a place or situation that is highly protected or secure.
