Word: θυραις

Etymology: θυραις is a Greek word that comes from the root word θύρα (thura), which means "door". The suffix -αις (-ais) is a plural ending, so θυραις means "doors".

Description: θυραις refers to multiple doors. It can be used to describe the physical doors of a building or structure, or it can be used metaphorically to refer to opportunities or possibilities.
