Word: θυσον

Etymology:
The word θυσον comes from the Greek verb θύω (thýō), which means "to sacrifice." It is derived from the Proto-Indo-European root *dʰewh₁- ("to smoke, to sacrifice").

Grammar:
θυσον is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the singular form and is in the accusative case. The nominative form of the word is θυσός (thysós).

Description:
θυσον refers to a sacrificial offering, usually an animal that is offered to a deity in ancient Greek religion. The word is often used in the context of religious rituals and ceremonies. In modern Greek, the word is also used to refer to a barbecue or grill.
