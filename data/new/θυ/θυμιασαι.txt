Word: θυμιασαι

Etymology:
The word θυμιασαι comes from the Greek verb θυμιάζω (thymiázō), which means "to burn incense." It is derived from the noun θυμίαμα (thymíama), which means "incense."

Grammar:
θυμιασαι is a verb in the aorist tense, active voice, and second person singular form. It is conjugated from the verb θυμιάζω (thymiázō).

Description:
θυμιασαι is a Greek verb that means "to burn incense." In ancient Greek culture, burning incense was a common practice in religious ceremonies and offerings to the gods. The verb is used in the New Testament of the Bible to describe the act of burning incense in the temple. Today, the word is still used in Greek Orthodox religious services to refer to the burning of incense as part of the liturgy.
