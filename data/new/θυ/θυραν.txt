The word θυραν (thuran) is a noun in Greek language. 

Etymology: The word θυραν (thuran) is derived from the Greek word θύρα (thyra) which means "door". 

Grammar: θυραν (thuran) is in the accusative case, singular number. 

Description: θυραν (thuran) refers to "a door" in Greek. It can be used to describe the physical object that is used to close off an entrance or exit, or it can be used metaphorically to refer to an opportunity or a gateway to something.
