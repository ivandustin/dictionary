The word "γηρασκον" (geraskon) is a Greek verb that means "to grow old" or "to age". It is derived from the noun "γῆρας" (geras), which means "old age" or "senility". In terms of grammar, "γηρασκον" is a present participle, which means it describes an ongoing action or state of being. It can be used to describe a person who is aging or an object that is becoming old or worn out.
