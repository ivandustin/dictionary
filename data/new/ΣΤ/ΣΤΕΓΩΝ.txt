Etymology: The word "στεγων" (stegon) comes from the Greek verb "στέγω" (stego), which means "to cover" or "to protect". 

Grammar: "Στεγων" is a noun in the masculine gender and singular number. 

Description: "Στεγων" refers to a roof or a covering that provides protection from the elements. It can also be used metaphorically to describe something that offers shelter or security.
