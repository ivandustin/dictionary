Etymology: The word "στεγην" (stegēn) comes from the Greek verb "στέγω" (stegō), which means "to cover" or "to protect". 

Grammar: "Στεγην" is a noun in the singular form. It belongs to the third declension in Greek grammar. 

Description: "Στεγην" refers to a roof or a covering that provides shelter or protection. It can also be used metaphorically to describe a refuge or a safe place.
