Etymology: The word "στελλεσθαι" (stellesthai) comes from the Greek verb "στέλλω" (stello), which means "to send" or "to dispatch".

Grammar: "Στελλεσθαι" is the present middle/passive infinitive form of the verb "στέλλω" (stello). In Greek, verbs have different forms depending on the voice (active, middle, or passive), tense, mood, and person.

Description: "Στελλεσθαι" is an infinitive form of the verb that expresses the action of sending or dispatching. It can be used in various contexts and tenses to indicate the act of sending someone or something.
