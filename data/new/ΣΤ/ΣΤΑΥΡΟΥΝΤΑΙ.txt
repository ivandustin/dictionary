Grammar: "Σταυρούνται" is the third person plural form of the verb "σταυρόω" in the present tense. It is in the passive voice and indicates that the subject is being crucified.

Description: "Σταυρούνται" is a Greek verb that describes the action of crucifying someone or something. It is often used in a figurative sense to mean being subjected to suffering, sacrifice, or persecution.
