Grammar: "Στηρίζων" is the present participle form of the verb "στηρίζω" in the masculine gender, singular number, and nominative case. In Greek grammar, the present participle is used to describe ongoing or continuous actions.

Description: "Στηρίζων" refers to a person or thing that is currently supporting or stabilizing something. It can be translated as "supporting" or "stabilizing" in English.
