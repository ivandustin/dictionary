Etymology: The word "στρατοπέδων" comes from the Greek roots "στρατός" (stratos), meaning "army," and "πέδον" (pedon), meaning "ground" or "field." 

Grammar: "Στρατοπέδων" is a noun in the plural form. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their gender, number, and case. 

Description: "Στρατοπέδων" refers to "camps" or "military encampments." It is used to describe the temporary settlements or bases where armies or military forces are stationed during military operations or training exercises.
