Etymology: The word "στεφανοι" (stefanoi) comes from the Greek word "στέφανος" (stefanos), meaning "crown" or "wreath". 

Grammar: "Στεφανοι" is the plural form of the noun "στεφανος" (stefanos). It is a masculine noun in the nominative case. 

Description: "Στεφανοι" refers to multiple crowns or wreaths. In Greek culture, crowns and wreaths were often used as symbols of honor, victory, or celebration.
