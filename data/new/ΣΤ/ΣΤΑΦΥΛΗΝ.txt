Etymology: The word "σταφυλην" (staphylēn) comes from the Greek word "σταφυλή" (staphylē), meaning "grape". 

Grammar: "Σταφυλην" is a noun in the singular form. It is a masculine noun in the accusative case.

Description: "Σταφυλην" refers to a grape or a bunch of grapes. It is commonly used in Greek to describe the fruit of the grapevine.
