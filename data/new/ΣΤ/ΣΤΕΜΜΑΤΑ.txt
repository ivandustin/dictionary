Etymology: The word "στεμματα" (stemmata) comes from the Greek word "στέμμα" (stémma), meaning "crown" or "wreath."

Grammar: "Στεμματα" is a noun in the plural form in Greek. It belongs to the second declension and is classified as a neuter noun.

Description: "Στεμματα" refers to "crowns" or "wreaths" in Greek. It is used to describe the ornamental headpieces worn as a symbol of honor or authority.
