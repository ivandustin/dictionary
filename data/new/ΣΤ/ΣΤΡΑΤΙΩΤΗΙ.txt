Etymology: The word "στρατιωτης" (stratiotes) comes from the Greek word "στρατος" (stratos), meaning "army" or "military," combined with the suffix "-ωτης" (-otes), which denotes a person or agent. 

Grammar: "Στρατιωτης" is a noun in the masculine gender and singular number. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their gender, number, and case.
