Etymology: The word "στηρίξαι" (stiríxei) comes from the Greek verb "στηρίζω" (stirízo), which means "to support" or "to stabilize." 

Grammar: "Στηρίξαι" is the imperative form of the verb "στηρίζω" in the 3rd person singular. It is used to give a command or instruction to someone, telling them to support or stabilize something.

Description: "Στηρίξαι" is a Greek word that is used to express the action of supporting or stabilizing something. It is often used in a figurative sense, indicating the need to provide assistance or help in a difficult situation.
