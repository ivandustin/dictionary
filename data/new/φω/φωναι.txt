The word φωναι (phonai) is derived from the Greek word φωνή (phonē), which means "voice" or "sound". It is a plural form of the word, which means "voices" or "sounds".

Plural:

- Genitive: φωνῶν (phonōn)

φωναι is a term used in ancient Greek literature to refer to the sounds or voices made by a group of people or animals. It can also refer to the sounds of musical instruments or other objects.
