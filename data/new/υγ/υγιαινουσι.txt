Word: υγιαινουσι

Etymology: The word υγιαινουσι comes from the Greek verb υγιαίνω (hygiaino), which means "to be healthy" or "to be sound". The suffix -ουσι (-ousi) is the third person plural present indicative active ending in Ancient Greek.

Grammar: υγιαινουσι is a verb in the present tense, third person plural form. It is an active indicative verb, meaning that it indicates an action that is currently happening and is being performed by the subject of the sentence.

Description: υγιαινουσι is a Greek verb that means "they are healthy" or "they are sound". It is often used to describe the physical or mental state of a group of people. The word emphasizes the importance of maintaining good health and well-being.
