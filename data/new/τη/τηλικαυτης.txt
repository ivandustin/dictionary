The word τηλικαυτης is a compound word in Greek, consisting of two parts: τηλικος (telikos) meaning "so great" or "so much" and καυτης (kautes) meaning "burnt". Therefore, τηλικαυτης can be translated as "burnt so much" or "burnt to a great extent".

τηλικαυτης is a word that is not commonly used in modern Greek. It is mostly found in ancient Greek texts and literature.
