τηκεται (teketai) is a Greek verb in the present tense, third person singular form. It comes from the root word τηκ-, which means "to melt" or "to fuse". 

In terms of grammar, τηκεται is a passive voice verb, indicating that the subject is receiving the action rather than performing it. 

Example sentence: Το κερί τηκεται στον ήλιο. (To keri teketai ston iliο.) - The candle is melting in the sun.
