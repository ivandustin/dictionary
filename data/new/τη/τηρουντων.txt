The word "τηρουντων" is a present participle in Greek, derived from the verb "τηρέω" (tēréō), which means "to keep, guard, observe, maintain, preserve". The present participle form indicates that the action of keeping or guarding is currently ongoing. 

In terms of grammar, "τηρουντων" is in the genitive case and masculine gender, agreeing with the noun it modifies. 

Overall, "τηρουντων" can be translated as "of those who are keeping/guarding/observing/maintaining/preserving".
