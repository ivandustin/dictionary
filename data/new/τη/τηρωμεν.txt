The word "τηρωμεν" is a verb in the present tense, first person plural form, in the Greek language. It comes from the root word "τηρέω" which means "to keep, guard, or observe". The verb form "τηρωμεν" indicates that "we are keeping, guarding, or observing" something. In terms of grammar, it is a regular verb that follows the conjugation pattern of the present tense in Greek.
