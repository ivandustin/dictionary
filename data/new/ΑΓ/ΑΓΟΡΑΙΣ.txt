Etymology: The word "αγοραίος" (agoraios) comes from the Greek word "αγορά" (agora), which means "marketplace". It is derived from the verb "αγοράζω" (agorazo), meaning "to buy". 

Grammar: "Αγοραίος" is an adjective in the masculine gender, singular form. In Greek grammar, adjectives agree in gender, number, and case with the noun they modify.
