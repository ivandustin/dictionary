Grammar: "Αγνοησαντες" is the third person plural, aorist active participle of the verb "αγνοέω" (agnoeo). In Greek grammar, the aorist tense is used to express a completed action in the past. The participle form indicates that the action of ignoring or being unaware of has been done by multiple people.

Description: "Αγνοησαντες" is a Greek word that means "they ignored" or "they were unaware of". It is used to describe a group of people who have disregarded or not paid attention to something.
