Etymology: The word "αγορασαί" comes from the Greek verb "αγοράζω" (agorazo), which means "to buy" or "to purchase". 

Grammar: "Αγορασαί" is the aorist tense, third person singular form of the verb "αγοράζω" (agorazo). The aorist tense is used to describe a completed action in the past. 

Description: "Αγορασαί" is used to indicate that someone (third person singular) bought or purchased something in the past.
