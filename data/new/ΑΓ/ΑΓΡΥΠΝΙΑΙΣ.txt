Etymology: The word "αγρυπνιαίς" (agrypniais) comes from the Greek word "αγρυπνία" (agrypnia), which means "sleeplessness" or "insomnia". The suffix "-αις" (-ais) indicates the plural form.

Grammar: "αγρυπνιαίς" is a feminine noun in the plural form. In Greek grammar, nouns have different forms depending on their gender, number, and case.

Description: "αγρυπνιαίς" refers to multiple instances of sleeplessness or insomnia. It is used to describe a condition or state of being unable to sleep.
