Etymology: The word "αγρίππα" (agrippa) comes from the Greek language. It is derived from the ancient Greek word "ἄγριος" (agrios), meaning "wild" or "savage."

Grammar: "Αγρίππα" is a noun in the Greek language. It is a masculine noun of the second declension, and its nominative singular form is "αγρίππας" (agrippas). It can be declined in various cases and numbers to indicate different grammatical functions.
