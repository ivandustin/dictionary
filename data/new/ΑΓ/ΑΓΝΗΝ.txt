Etymology: The word "αγνην" (agnin) comes from the Greek adjective "αγνή" (agni), which means "pure" or "chaste". 

Grammar: "Αγνην" is the accusative form of the feminine adjective "αγνή" (agni), which agrees with a feminine noun in the accusative case.
