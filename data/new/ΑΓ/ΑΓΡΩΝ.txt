Etymology: The word "αγρων" (agron) comes from the Greek word "ἀγρός" (agros), which means "field" or "land".

Grammar: In Greek, "αγρων" is a noun in the masculine gender. It is in the nominative case and singular number.

Description: "Αγρων" is a term used in Greek to refer to a field or a piece of land. It can be used to describe agricultural land, farmland, or any open space used for cultivation or farming purposes.
