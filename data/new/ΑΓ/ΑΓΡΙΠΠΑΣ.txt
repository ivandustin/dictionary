Grammar: "Αγρίππας" is a masculine noun in the nominative case. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their gender, number, and case. The word "αγρίππας" is singular and refers to a specific wild horse or stallion.

Description: "Αγρίππας" is a term used in Greek to describe a wild horse or stallion. It emphasizes the untamed and free nature of the animal.
