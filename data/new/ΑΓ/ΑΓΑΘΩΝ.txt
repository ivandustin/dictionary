Etymology: The word "αγαθων" (agathon) comes from the Greek language. It is derived from the adjective "ἀγαθός" (agathos), meaning "good" or "virtuous."

Grammar: "Αγαθων" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the masculine gender and the nominative case.

Description: "Αγαθων" refers to a person or thing that is good, virtuous, or morally upright. It can also be used as a proper name.
