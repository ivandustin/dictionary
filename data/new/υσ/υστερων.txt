Word: υστερων

Etymology:
The word υστερων (hysteron) is a Greek adjective that means "later" or "subsequent". It is derived from the Greek word υστερος (hysteros), which means "behind" or "later". 

Grammar:
In Greek, υστερων is a masculine singular adjective in the nominative case. It can be used to modify a noun, indicating that the noun is later or subsequent in time or order. 

Description:
The word υστερων can be used to describe something that comes later or is subsequent to something else. For example, it can be used to describe a later edition of a book, a subsequent event in a series, or a later stage in a process. It can also be used to describe a person who is late or tardy.
