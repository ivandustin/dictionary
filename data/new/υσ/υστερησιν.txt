Word: υστερησιν

Etymology: The word υστερησιν comes from the Greek word υστερέω (hystereó), which means "to be late" or "to lag behind."

Grammar: υστερησιν is a noun in the accusative case, singular number, and feminine gender.

Description: υστερησιν refers to a delay or lateness in time. It can also be used to describe a deficiency or lack of something. In Greek mythology, the goddess Hestia was sometimes referred to as υστερησιν, as she was the goddess of the hearth and home, and her presence was necessary for a household to function properly.
