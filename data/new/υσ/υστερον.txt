Word: υστερον

Etymology:
The word υστερον comes from the Greek language. It is derived from the Greek word "ὕστερος" (hysteros), which means "later" or "after". 

Grammar:
υστερον is an adverb in Greek language. It is used to indicate that something will happen later or after a certain time. It is often used in the phrase "ὕστερον ἀπό" (hysteron apo), which means "later than" or "after".

Description:
υστερον is a useful word in Greek language that is used to indicate that something will happen later or after a certain time. It is commonly used in everyday conversation and in written texts. It is important to note that the word is an adverb and should be used accordingly in sentences.
