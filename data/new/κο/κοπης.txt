The word "κοπης" (kopēs) is a Greek noun that means "cutting" or "strike". It is derived from the verb "κόπτω" (koptō), which means "to cut" or "to strike". In terms of grammar, "κοπης" is a feminine noun in the nominative singular form. It can also be declined in other cases and numbers, such as the genitive singular "κοπῆς" (kopēs) or the accusative plural "κοπάς" (kopas). Overall, "κοπης" is a versatile word that can be used in various contexts, such as in describing physical actions or emotional experiences.
