The word "κορεσθεντες" is a Greek verb in the passive participle form. It comes from the root verb "κορέω" which means "to be satiated" or "to be filled". The passive participle form indicates that the subject has been filled or satiated by something. 

In terms of grammar, "κορεσθεντες" is in the nominative plural form, which means it is the subject of the sentence. 

Overall, the word "κορεσθεντες" can be translated to mean "being filled" or "having been satiated".
