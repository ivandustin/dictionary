The word "κοινωνον" (koinōnon) is a Greek noun that means "partner" or "fellowship". It is derived from the root word "κοινός" (koinos), which means "common" or "shared". In terms of grammar, it is a masculine noun in the singular form. It can be used to describe a person who shares in a common interest or goal, or to refer to a group of people who share a common bond or relationship.
