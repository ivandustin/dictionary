Word: κολυμβηθρα

Etymology:
The word κολυμβηθρα comes from the Greek word κολυμβάω (kolymbao) which means "to swim". The suffix -θρα (-thra) is a common suffix in Greek that denotes a place or location.

Grammar:
In Greek, κολυμβηθρα is a feminine noun. Its plural form is κολυμβηθρες (kolymbethres).

Description:
κολυμβηθρα refers to a swimming pool or a place where one can swim. It can also refer to a diving pool or a place where diving is practiced. The word is commonly used in modern Greek to refer to a public swimming pool or a swimming facility.
