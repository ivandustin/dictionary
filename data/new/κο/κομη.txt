Word: κομη

The word κομη (komē) is derived from the Greek word κόμη (kómē), which means "hair of the head". It is related to the Greek word κόμητος (kómētos), which means "long-haired".

In Greek, κομη is a feminine noun. Its nominative singular form is κομη, and its genitive singular form is κομης. The word can be declined in all cases, singular and plural.

Description:
κομη is a term used to describe the hair on a person's head. It can refer to the length, style, or texture of the hair.
