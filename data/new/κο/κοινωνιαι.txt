The word "κοινωνιαι" (koinoniai) is a plural noun in Greek that can be translated to mean "fellowships" or "communities." 

Etymology: The word comes from the Greek root "κοινωνός" (koinonos), which means "partner" or "sharer." The suffix "-αι" (-ai) indicates that it is a plural noun.

Grammar: As mentioned, "κοινωνιαι" is a plural noun. It is feminine in gender and belongs to the first declension in Greek. 

Description: The word is often used in a religious context to refer to a group of people who share a common faith and participate in communal activities. It can also be used more broadly to refer to any group of people who share a common interest or goal.
