Word: κορβαναν

Grammar: κορβαναν is a noun in the accusative case. It is the direct object of a sentence and refers to a specific offering or sacrifice.

Description: κορβαναν is a term used in the New Testament to refer to a type of offering or sacrifice made in the Jewish temple.
