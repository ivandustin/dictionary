Word: ψευδομαρτυρίαν

The word ψευδομαρτυρίαν is a compound word in Greek, consisting of two parts: ψευδο- (pseudos) meaning "false" and μαρτυρίαν (martyrian) meaning "testimony". Therefore, the word ψευδομαρτυρίαν means "false testimony".

Grammar:
ψευδομαρτυρίαν is a noun in the accusative case in Greek. It is a feminine noun, and its singular form is ψευδομαρτυρία.

Description:
ψευδομαρτυρίαν is a term used in legal contexts to refer to false testimony or perjury. It is a serious offense in many legal systems and can result in criminal charges. The term is also used in religious contexts to refer to false witness or false testimony against someone.
