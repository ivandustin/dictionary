Word: ψευδωνυμου

Etymology:
The word ψευδωνυμου comes from the Greek words ψευδής (pseudēs) meaning "false" and ὄνομα (onoma) meaning "name". Therefore, ψευδωνυμου means "false name" or "pseudonym".

Grammar:
ψευδωνυμου is a noun in the singular form. In Greek, it is written in the nominative case. The word is composed of three syllables: ψευ-δω-νύ-μου. The stress falls on the third syllable (νύ).

Description:
ψευδωνυμου refers to a name that is not the real or legal name of a person, but rather a name that they have chosen to use instead. This can be for various reasons, such as privacy, anonymity, or artistic expression. Pseudonyms are commonly used by writers, artists, and performers.
