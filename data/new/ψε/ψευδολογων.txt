The word "ψευδολογων" (pseudologon) is a Greek term that combines the words "pseudos" (false) and "logos" (word). It refers to someone who speaks falsely or deceitfully, often with the intention of misleading others. 

In terms of grammar, "ψευδολογων" is a noun in the masculine gender and singular number. 

Overall, "ψευδολογων" is a negative term that describes someone who is dishonest and untrustworthy in their speech.
