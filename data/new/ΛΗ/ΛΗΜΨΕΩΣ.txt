Etymology: The word "λημψεως" (lēmpseōs) comes from the Greek verb "λαμβάνω" (lambanō), which means "to take" or "to receive."

Grammar: "Λημψεως" is a noun in the genitive case, singular form. In Greek grammar, the genitive case is used to indicate possession, origin, or relationship.

Description: "Λημψεως" refers to the act of taking or receiving something. It can also be translated as "reception" or "acquisition."
