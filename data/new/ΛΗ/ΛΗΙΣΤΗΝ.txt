Etymology: The word "ληιστην" (lēistēn) comes from the Greek verb "ληίζω" (lēízō), which means "to forget" or "to neglect."

Grammar: "Ληιστην" is a noun in the accusative case, singular number, and masculine gender.

Description: "Ληιστην" refers to someone who forgets or neglects something or someone. It can also be used metaphorically to describe someone who is careless or indifferent.
