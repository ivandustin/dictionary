Grammar: "Ατενισασα" is a verb in the past tense, singular number, and feminine gender. It belongs to the aorist tense, which expresses an action that occurred in the past without specifying its duration or completion.

Description: "Ατενισασα" describes the action of a female subject gazing intently or fixing her eyes upon something. It implies a focused and concentrated observation.
