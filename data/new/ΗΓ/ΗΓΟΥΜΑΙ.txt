Etymology: The word "ηγούμαι" comes from the Greek verb "ἡγέομαι" (hēgéomai), which means "to lead" or "to guide."

Grammar: "Ηγούμαι" is a verb in the Greek language. It belongs to the first conjugation class and is conjugated in the present tense as follows:

- Εγώ ηγούμαι (ego ēgoúmai) - I lead
- Εσύ ηγείσαι (esý ēgeísai) - You lead
- Αυτός/Αυτή/Αυτό ηγείται (afós/afí/aftó ēgeítai) - He/She/It leads
- Εμείς ηγούμαστε (emeís ēgoúmaste) - We lead
- Εσείς ηγείσθε (eseís ēgeísthe) - You lead
- Αυτοί/Αυτές/Αυτά ηγούνται (afí/afé/afá ēgoúntai) - They lead

Description: "Ηγούμαι" is a verb that expresses the action of leading or guiding. It can be used in various contexts, such as leading a group, guiding someone's actions, or being in charge of a situation.
