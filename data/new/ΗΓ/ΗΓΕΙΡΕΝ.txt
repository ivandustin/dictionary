Etymology: The word "ηγείρεν" comes from the Greek verb "ἐγείρω" (egeirō), which means "to raise" or "to wake up."

Grammar: "ηγείρεν" is the third person singular form of the aorist tense of the verb "ἐγείρω" (egeirō) in Ancient Greek. It is in the indicative mood and active voice.

Description: "ηγείρεν" is a verb that means "he/she/it raised" or "he/she/it woke up" in English. It is used to describe an action of raising or waking up someone or something.
