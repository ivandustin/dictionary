The word "ηγερθησαν" is a Greek verb in the past tense, third person plural form. It comes from the root verb "εγείρω" (egero), which means "to wake up" or "to rise." In this form, "ηγερθησαν" means "they woke up" or "they rose." It is commonly used in Greek to describe the action of waking up or rising from sleep or a state of rest.
