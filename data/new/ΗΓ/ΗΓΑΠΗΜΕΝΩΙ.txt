Etymology: The word "ηγαπημένοι" comes from the Greek verb "αγαπώ" (agapó), which means "to love". The suffix "-μένοι" (-menoi) is added to the verb stem to form the adjective "ηγαπημένοι" (igapiménoi), which translates to "beloved" or "loved ones".

Grammar: "Ηγαπημένοι" is the plural form of the adjective "ηγαπημένος" (igapiménos), which is used to describe multiple beloved or loved ones. In Greek, adjectives agree in gender, number, and case with the noun they modify. Therefore, if the noun is masculine, the adjective will also be masculine in its plural form.

Description: "Ηγαπημένοι" is a Greek adjective that is commonly used to express affection or endearment towards a group of people. It is often used to refer to close friends, family members, or loved ones.
