Etymology: The word "γυμνοτητι" (gymnotiti) comes from the Greek words "γυμνός" (gymnos), meaning "naked," and "τητα" (tita), meaning "state" or "condition." 

Grammar: "Γυμνοτητι" is a noun in the singular form. It belongs to the feminine gender in Greek grammar.

Description: "Γυμνοτητι" refers to the state or condition of being naked. It is used to describe the absence of clothing or covering on the body.
