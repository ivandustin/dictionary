Grammar: "Γυναικεῖοι" is the masculine plural form of the adjective. In Greek grammar, adjectives agree in gender, number, and case with the noun they modify. So, "γυναικεῖοι" is used to describe or modify masculine plural nouns.

Description: "Γυναικεῖοι" is an adjective that means "of or relating to women" or "feminine." It is used to describe things that are specifically associated with or characteristic of women.
