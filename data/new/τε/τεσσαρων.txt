Grammar: τεσσαρων is a masculine noun in the genitive case. It is the genitive form of the Greek word τέσσερες (tésseres), which means "four".

Description: τεσσαρων is used to indicate the number "four" in Greek. It is often used in counting and in numerical expressions. For example, τεσσαρων can be used to indicate the number of people in a group, the number of items in a set, or the number of years that have passed.
