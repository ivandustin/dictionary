τεχνης (technēs) is a Greek noun that means "art, craft, skill, or expertise." 

Etymology: The word comes from the Greek root τέχνη (téchnē), which means "art, craft, or skill." 

Grammar: τεχνης is a feminine noun in the nominative singular form. 

Description: The word τεχνης is often used to describe a particular skill or expertise in a specific field, such as music, medicine, or carpentry. It can also refer to the art or craft itself, such as painting or sculpture. In ancient Greece, τεχνης was highly valued and considered an essential part of a well-rounded education. Today, the word is still used in modern Greek to refer to various forms of art and craftsmanship.
