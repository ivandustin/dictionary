The word "τελευτων" (teleutōn) is a Greek noun that means "dying" or "death." It is derived from the verb "τελευτάω" (teleutáō), which means "to die" or "to come to an end." In terms of grammar, "τελευτων" is a present participle, which means it describes an ongoing action or state of being. It can be used as a noun to refer to someone who is dying or as an adjective to describe something related to death.
