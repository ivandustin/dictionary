Word: τεταγμένοι

Etymology:
The word τεταγμένοι (tetagmenoi) is the perfect passive participle of the Greek verb τάσσω (tasso), which means "to arrange" or "to put in order". The word is derived from the root ταγ- (tag-), which means "to put in order" or "to arrange".

Grammar:
τεταγμένοι is a masculine plural adjective in the nominative case. It agrees in gender, number, and case with the noun it modifies.

Description:
τεταγμένοι is used to describe a group of people who have been arranged or put in order. It can also be used to describe soldiers who are in formation or who have been deployed in a specific order. In a broader sense, it can refer to any group of people who have been organized or arranged in a particular way.
