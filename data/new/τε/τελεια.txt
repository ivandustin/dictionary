τελεια (teleia) is a Greek word that can be translated to mean "perfect" or "complete". It is derived from the Greek word τέλος (telos), which means "end" or "goal". In grammar, τελεια is a feminine noun in the nominative singular form. It can also be used as an adjective to describe something that is perfect or complete. In Greek philosophy, τελεια is often associated with the concept of achieving one's ultimate purpose or goal in life.
