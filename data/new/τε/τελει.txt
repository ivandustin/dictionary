The word "τελει" (telei) is a Greek verb that means "to complete" or "to finish." It is derived from the Greek word "τέλος" (telos), which means "end" or "goal." 

In terms of grammar, "τελει" is a present tense verb in the active voice. It can be conjugated to match the subject of the sentence, such as "εγώ τελειώνω" (ego teleiono) meaning "I am finishing" or "αυτός τελειώνει" (autos teleionei) meaning "he is finishing."

In everyday usage, "τελει" can be used to describe the completion of a task or the end of a process. For example, "Τελείωσα τη δουλειά μου" (Teleiosa ti douleia mou) means "I finished my work." 

Overall, "τελει" is a versatile verb that can be used in a variety of contexts to describe the completion or finishing of something.
