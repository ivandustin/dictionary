The word "τεσσαρσι" (tessarsi) is a Greek word that means "four". It is derived from the Greek word "τέσσερα" (téssera), which also means "four". In terms of grammar, "τεσσαρσι" is the dative plural form of "τέσσερα". It is used to indicate that something is being given to or done for four people or things. For example, "Δώσε μου τέσσερα μήλα" (Dóse mou téssera míla) means "Give me four apples", while "Δώσε τέσσερα μήλα στους φίλους μου" (Dóse téssera míla stous filous mou) means "Give four apples to my friends".
