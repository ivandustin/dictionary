The word "τεθεικατε" is a Greek verb that is in the perfect tense and second person plural form. It comes from the root verb "τίθημι" which means "to place" or "to set". The prefix "τε-" adds emphasis to the action of placing or setting. 

In terms of grammar, the perfect tense indicates that the action of placing or setting has been completed in the past and has ongoing relevance in the present. The second person plural form indicates that the action was performed by a group of people.

Overall, "τεθεικατε" can be translated to mean "you all have placed" or "you all have set".
