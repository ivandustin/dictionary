Word: τεταρταιος

Etymology:
The word τεταρταιος (tetartaios) is derived from the Greek word τέταρτος (téttartos), which means "fourth." The suffix -αιος (-aios) is added to indicate possession or belonging. Therefore, τεταρταιος means "belonging to the fourth."

- Genitive: τεταρταιου

Description:
τεταρταιος can be used to describe something that belongs to the fourth in a series or sequence. For example, it can be used to describe the fourth day of the week (Thursday) or the fourth book in a series. It can also be used to describe someone who is fourth in line or fourth in rank.
