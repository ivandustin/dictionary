τεκμήριοις (tekmeriois) is a Greek word that is derived from the root word τεκμήριον (tekmerion), which means "proof" or "evidence". The suffix -οις (-ois) is a plural ending, so τεκμήριοις refers to "proofs" or "evidences". 

In terms of grammar, τεκμήριοις is a noun in the dative plural case. It can be translated as "to the proofs" or "for the evidences" in English.

Overall, τεκμήριοις is a word that emphasizes the importance of providing evidence or proof to support an argument or claim.
