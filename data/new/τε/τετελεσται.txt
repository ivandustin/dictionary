τετελεσται (tetélestai) is a Greek word that means "it is finished" or "it is accomplished". It is a perfect tense verb form of the Greek word "teleo", which means "to bring to an end" or "to complete". The word is most famously known for being the last words spoken by Jesus Christ on the cross, as recorded in the Gospel of John. In Greek grammar, τετελεσται is a third person singular perfect indicative active verb.
