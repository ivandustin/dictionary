τελειοτερας (teleioteras) is a Greek word that is the feminine form of the adjective τελειότερος (teleioteros), which means "more perfect" or "more complete". 

Etymology: The word τελειοτερας comes from the root word τέλειος (teleios), which means "perfect" or "complete". The suffix -τερος (-teros) is a comparative suffix, which means "more" or "comparatively".

Grammar: τελειοτερας is a feminine adjective in the nominative singular form. It agrees in gender, number, and case with the noun it modifies.

Description: τελειοτερας is used to describe something that is more perfect or complete than something else. It is often used in a comparative sense, such as "more perfect than" or "more complete than".
