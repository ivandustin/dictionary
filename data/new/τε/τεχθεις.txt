The word τεχθεις is a Greek word that comes from the verb τίκτω (tikto), which means "to give birth" or "to produce." The word τεχθεις is the aorist participle of this verb, which means "having been born" or "having been produced."

For example, in the masculine singular nominative case, it is τεχθείς (techtheis), while in the feminine singular nominative case, it is τεχθεῖσα (techtheisa).

The word τεχθεις is used to describe someone or something that has been born or produced.
