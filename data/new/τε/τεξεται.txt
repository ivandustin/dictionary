The word "τεξεται" (texetai) is a verb in the Greek language. 

Etymology: The word is derived from the Greek verb "τεκταινω" (tektaíno), which means "to weave" or "to fabricate". 

Grammar: "τεξεται" is a third person singular, present tense, indicative mood verb. 

Description: The word "τεξεται" means "he/she/it weaves" or "he/she/it fabricates". It is often used metaphorically to describe the act of creating or constructing something, whether it be a physical object or an abstract concept.
