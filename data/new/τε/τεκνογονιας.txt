τεκνογονιας (tekno-gonias) is a Greek word that combines the roots τέκνο (tekno), meaning "child" or "offspring," and γονή (goni), meaning "birth" or "generation." The word can be translated as "childbirth" or "the act of giving birth to children." 

In terms of grammar, τεκνογονιας is a noun in the genitive case, indicating possession or relationship. It is also a compound word, made up of two separate roots that have been combined to create a new word with a specific meaning.

Overall, τεκνογονιας is a word that refers to the process of giving birth to children, and it has its roots in ancient Greek language and culture.
