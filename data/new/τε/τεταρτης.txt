τεταρτης (tetartes) is a Greek word that means "fourth" in English. 

Etymology: The word comes from the Greek word "τέταρτος" (téartos), which means "fourth".

Grammar: τεταρτης is a feminine noun in the nominative case. Its genitive form is τετάρτης (tetártis).

Description: The word is used to indicate the ordinal number "fourth" in a series or sequence. For example, "τεταρτης ημέρα" (tetartes iméra) means "the fourth day". It can also be used to describe the fourth item in a list or the fourth person in a group.
