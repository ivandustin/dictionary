Grammar: τετρακισχίλιοι is an adjective in the masculine plural form. It is used to describe a noun in the same case, number, and gender. For example, τετρακισχίλιοι στρατιώτες (tetrakis-khilioi stratiótes) means "four thousand soldiers".
