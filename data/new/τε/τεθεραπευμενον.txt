Word: τεθεραπευμένον

The word τεθεραπευμένον comes from the Greek verb θεραπεύω (therapevo), which means "to heal" or "to cure". The prefix τε- (te-) is a perfect tense marker, indicating that the action of healing has been completed in the past.
