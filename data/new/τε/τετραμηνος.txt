Word: τετραμηνος

Etymology:
The word τετραμηνος comes from the Greek words τέσσερις (tésseris) meaning "four" and μήνας (ménas) meaning "months". Therefore, τετραμηνος literally means "four months".

Grammar:
τετραμηνος is an adjective in the masculine gender, singular number, and nominative case. It can also be declined in other cases and genders to agree with the noun it modifies.

Description:
τετραμηνος is used to describe something that lasts for a period of four months. It can be used to describe a school semester, a work contract, or a pregnancy. It is also used in the medical field to describe a four-month period of time for monitoring a patient's health condition.
