Word: πωποτε

Etymology: The word πωποτε is a Greek adverb that means "ever, at any time". It is derived from the combination of the Greek words ποτέ (pote) meaning "ever" and πως (pos) meaning "how".

Grammar: πωποτε is an adverb in Greek and is used to modify verbs, adjectives, or other adverbs. It is usually placed before the verb or adjective it modifies.

Description: πωποτε is a versatile word that can be used in a variety of contexts. It is often used to express the idea of "ever" or "at any time", as in "Have you ever been to Greece?" (Πήγες ποτέ στην Ελλάδα;) or "I can come to your house at any time" (Μπορώ να έρθω στο σπίτι σου πωποτε). It can also be used to express surprise or disbelief, as in "Did you really do that?" (Το έκανες πωποτε;). Overall, πωποτε is a useful and common word in Greek that can be used in a variety of situations.
