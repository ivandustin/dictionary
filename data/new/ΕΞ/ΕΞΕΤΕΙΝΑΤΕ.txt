Etymology: The word "εξετείνατε" comes from the Greek verb "εξετείνω" (exeteíno), which means "to extend" or "to stretch out". It is formed by adding the suffix "-ατε" (-ate) to the verb stem.

Grammar: "Εξετείνατε" is the second person plural form of the verb "εξετείνω" in the past tense. It is used when addressing a group of people and means "you all extended" or "you all stretched out".

Description: The word "εξετείνατε" describes an action of extending or stretching out. It can be used in various contexts, such as physically stretching out an object or metaphorically extending a concept or idea.
