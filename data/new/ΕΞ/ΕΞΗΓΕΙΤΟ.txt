Etymology: The word "εξηγητο" comes from the Greek word "εξηγώ" (exigo), which means "to explain" or "to interpret."

Grammar: "Εξηγητο" is a noun in the Greek language. It is the neuter form of the adjective "εξηγητός" (exigetos), which means "explainable" or "interpretable."

Description: "Εξηγητο" refers to something that can be explained or interpreted. It is used to describe a concept, idea, or situation that can be understood or clarified through explanation.
