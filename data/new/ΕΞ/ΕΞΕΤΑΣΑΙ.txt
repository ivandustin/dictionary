Grammar: "Εξετάσαι" is the second person singular form of the verb "εξετάζω" in the present tense. It is used when addressing one person and means "you examine" or "you test".

Description: "Εξετάσαι" is a verb in the Greek language that is used to express the action of examining or testing something or someone. It can be used in various contexts, such as academic exams, medical tests, or evaluating the performance of a person or a system.
