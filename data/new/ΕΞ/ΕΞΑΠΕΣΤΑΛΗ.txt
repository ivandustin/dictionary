Grammar: "Εξαπεσταλη" is a verb in the passive voice, past tense, third person singular form. It is derived from the aorist tense of the verb "αποστέλλω" (apostello), which means that the action it describes is completed in the past.

Description: "Εξαπεσταλη" is used to express the idea of someone or something being sent or dispatched. It can refer to the act of sending a message, a letter, or a person to a specific destination.
