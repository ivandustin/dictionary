Grammar: "Εξεγερει" is the third person singular form of the present tense of the verb "εξεγείρω" (exegéiro). In Greek grammar, verbs are conjugated based on the person, number, tense, and mood. The present tense indicates an action that is happening at the current moment.

Description: "Εξεγερει" is a Greek word that can be translated as "he/she/it wakes up" or "he/she/it arouses". It describes the action of someone or something waking up from sleep or being stirred into action.
