Grammar: "Εξαπορούμενοι" is the plural form of the present participle of the verb "εξαπορώ". It is used to describe a group of people who are currently in a state of being perplexed or at a loss.

Description: "Εξαπορούμενοι" is an adjective that describes a state of confusion, uncertainty, or perplexity. It can be used to describe individuals or groups who are unsure about something or find themselves in a difficult situation.
