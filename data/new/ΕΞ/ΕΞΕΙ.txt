Grammar: "Εξει" is the third person singular form of the verb "έχει" in the present tense. It is used to indicate that someone or something has or possesses something.

Description: "Εξει" is a Greek word that is commonly used in everyday speech. It is used to express possession or ownership. For example, if someone asks "Τι έχεις;" (Ti echeis?), which means "What do you have?", you can respond with "Έχω ένα αυτοκίνητο" (Eho ena aftokinito), meaning "I have a car".
