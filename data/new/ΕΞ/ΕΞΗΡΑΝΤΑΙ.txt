Etymology: The word "εξηρανται" comes from the Greek verb "ξηραίνω" (xiranó), which means "to dry" or "to wither". The suffix "-ται" (-tai) indicates the third person singular form in the present tense, passive voice.

Grammar: "Εξηρανται" is a verb in the Greek language. It is in the present tense, passive voice, and third person singular form. The verb indicates that the subject is being dried or withered by someone or something else.
