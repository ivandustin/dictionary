Etymology: The word "εξιστασθαι" (existansthai) comes from the Greek verb "εξίστημι" (existēmi), which means "to stand apart" or "to be amazed". It is derived from the prefix "εξ-" (ex-), meaning "out" or "away", and the verb "ίστημι" (istēmi), meaning "to stand".
