Etymology: The word "εξαυτης" (exautēs) comes from the Greek prefix "εξ" (ex), meaning "out of" or "from," and the word "αυτης" (autēs), meaning "self" or "itself." 

Grammar: "Εξαυτης" is a noun in the feminine gender and singular number. It is declined as follows: 

- Nominative: εξαυτης
- Genitive: εξαυτης
- Accusative: εξαυτην
- Vocative: εξαυτη

Description: "Εξαυτης" is a Greek word that means "immediately" or "straightaway." It is used to indicate that something happens or is done without delay or hesitation.
