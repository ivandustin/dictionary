Etymology: The word "εξομολόγουμαι" comes from the Greek roots "εξ" (ex), meaning "out," and "ομολογώ" (omologo), meaning "confess." 

Grammar: "Εξομολόγουμαι" is a verb in the Greek language. It belongs to the first person singular form of the present tense. 

Description: "Εξομολόγουμαι" means "to confess" or "to admit" in English. It is used when someone openly acknowledges or admits something, often in a religious or personal context.
