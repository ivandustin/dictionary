Etymology: The word "εξεληλυθεν" comes from the Greek verb "εξέρχομαι" (exérchomai), which means "to go out" or "to come forth."

Grammar: "εξεληλυθεν" is the third person singular form of the verb "εξέρχομαι" in the perfect tense. It is used to indicate that someone or something has gone out or come forth in the past.

Description: "εξεληλυθεν" is a Greek word that describes an action of going out or coming forth that has already happened. It can be used in various contexts, such as someone leaving a place or an idea being expressed.
