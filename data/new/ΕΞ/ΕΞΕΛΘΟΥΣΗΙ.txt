Etymology: The word "εξελθουση" comes from the Greek verb "εξέρχομαι" (exérchomai), which means "to go out" or "to exit".

Grammar: "Εξελθουση" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the feminine gender and in the singular form.

Description: "Εξελθουση" refers to the act of going out or exiting. It can be used to describe physical movement, such as leaving a place, or metaphorical movement, such as moving on from a situation or state of being.
