Grammar: "εξελθε" is the imperative form of the verb "εξέρχομαι" (exerchomai) in the second person singular. It is used to give a command or instruction to someone, telling them to "go out" or "exit".

Description: "εξελθε" is a Greek word that is commonly used in everyday language. It is often used in situations where someone needs to leave a place or exit a location. It can be used in various contexts, such as telling someone to leave a room, a building, or a specific area.
