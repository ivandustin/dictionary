Etymology: The word "εξεστίν" comes from the Greek word "εξίστημι" (exístimi), which means "to stand out" or "to deviate from the norm."

Grammar: "Εξεστίν" is a verb in the present tense, third person singular form. It is derived from the ancient Greek verb "εξίστημι" (exístimi), which means "to stand out" or "to deviate from the norm."

Description: "Εξεστίν" is used to describe something or someone that stands out or deviates from the norm. It can be used to express surprise, astonishment, or admiration for something or someone that is extraordinary or exceptional.
