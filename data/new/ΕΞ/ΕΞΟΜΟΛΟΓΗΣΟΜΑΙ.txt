Etymology: The word "εξομολόγησημαι" comes from the Greek roots "εξ" (ex), meaning "out," and "ομολογέω" (omologéo), meaning "to confess." 

Grammar: "Εξομολόγησημαι" is a verb in the Greek language. It belongs to the first person singular form of the present tense. 

Description: "Εξομολόγησημαι" means "I confess" in English. It is used to express the act of admitting or acknowledging something, often in a religious or spiritual context.
