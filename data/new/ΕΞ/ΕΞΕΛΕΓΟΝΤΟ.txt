Grammar: "Εξελέγχοντο" is the third person plural, past tense, passive voice form of the verb "εξελέγχω" (exeléncho). In Greek grammar, verbs change their form based on tense, voice, mood, person, and number. The past tense indicates that the action happened in the past, the passive voice indicates that the subject of the verb is being acted upon, and the third person plural indicates that the subject is multiple people.
