Etymology: The word "εξουσιών" comes from the Greek word "εξουσία" (exousía), which means "authority" or "power".

Grammar: "Εξουσιών" is the plural form of the noun "εξουσία" (exousía) in Greek. It belongs to the third declension and is of feminine gender.

Description: "Εξουσιών" refers to "authorities" or "powers" in Greek. It can be used to describe individuals or institutions that have the power or authority to make decisions, enforce laws, or govern a certain area or domain.
