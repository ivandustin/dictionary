Grammar: "Εξεκαυθησαν" is the third person plural form of the verb "εξεκαυθώ" in the past tense. It is in the indicative mood and the aorist tense. The verb is conjugated in the passive voice, indicating that the subject of the sentence is the receiver of the action.

Description: "Εξεκαυθησαν" is a Greek word that describes the action of being burned up or consumed by fire. It can be used to refer to objects, buildings, or even people that have been completely destroyed by fire.
