Etymology: The word "εξηραύνησαν" comes from the Greek verb "ξηραίνω" (xeraino), which means "to dry" or "to make dry". The prefix "εξ-" (ex-) in this word indicates a completed action or a state resulting from an action.

Grammar: "Εξηραύνησαν" is the third person plural, past tense form of the verb "ξηραίνω" (xeraino). It is in the active voice and indicative mood. The verb is conjugated in the aorist tense, which is used to express a completed action in the past.

Description: "Εξηραύνησαν" means "they dried" in English. It refers to the action of making something dry or removing moisture from something.
