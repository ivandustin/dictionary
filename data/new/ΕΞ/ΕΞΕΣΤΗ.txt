Etymology: The word "εξεστη" (exesti) comes from the Greek verb "εξίστημι" (existēmi), which means "to be out of one's mind" or "to be beside oneself". 

Grammar: "Εξεστη" is a verb in the Greek language. It is in the present tense, indicative mood, and active voice. 

Description: "Εξεστη" is used to describe a state of being mentally or emotionally disturbed, to be out of one's mind, or to be beside oneself. It implies a loss of control or rationality.
