Etymology: The word "εξορυξαντες" comes from the Greek verb "εξορύσσω" (exorýsso), which means "to dig out" or "to excavate". It is derived from the prefix "εξ-" (ex-), meaning "out", and the verb "ορύσσω" (orýsso), meaning "to dig".

Grammar: "Εξορυξαντες" is the plural form of the verb "εξορύσσω" in the aorist tense. It is in the active voice and indicative mood. The verb is conjugated in the third person plural, referring to a group of people who performed the action of digging out or excavating.
