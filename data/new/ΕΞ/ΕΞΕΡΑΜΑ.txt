Etymology: The word "εξεραμα" (exerama) comes from the Greek verb "εξεράω" (exerao), which means "to examine" or "to investigate."

Grammar: "Εξεραμα" is a noun in the Greek language. It is a neuter noun of the first declension, and its plural form is "εξεραματα" (exeramata).

Description: "Εξεραμα" refers to an examination, investigation, or inquiry. It is often used to describe a thorough and detailed examination of a subject or a situation.
