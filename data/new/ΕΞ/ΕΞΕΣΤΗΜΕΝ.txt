Etymology: The word "εξεστημένη" comes from the Greek verb "εξίστημι" (exístimi), which means "to be out of one's mind" or "to be astonished". The suffix "-μένη" (-méni) is added to form the feminine form of the adjective.

Grammar: "Εξεστημένη" is the feminine form of the adjective in Greek. It agrees in gender, number, and case with the noun it modifies.

Description: "Εξεστημένη" is an adjective in Greek that describes someone or something as being out of their mind or astonished. It is used to express a state of extreme surprise, shock, or disbelief.
