Etymology: The word "εξεχυννετο" comes from the Greek verb "εξεχύννω" (eksechýno), which means "to pour out" or "to spill." It is derived from the prefix "εξ-" (ex-), meaning "out," and the verb "χύννω" (chýno), meaning "to pour."

Grammar: "Εξεχυννετο" is the third person singular, past tense, middle voice form of the verb "εξεχύννω" (eksechýno). In Greek grammar, the middle voice indicates that the subject is performing the action on itself or for its own benefit.
