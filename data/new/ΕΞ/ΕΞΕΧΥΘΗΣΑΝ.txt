Grammar: "εξεχυθησαν" is the third person plural, aorist passive indicative form of the verb "εξέχω" (exécho). In Greek grammar, the aorist tense is used to express a past action that is viewed as a whole, without specifying its duration or repetition. The passive voice indicates that the subject of the verb is being acted upon.

Description: "εξεχυθησαν" is a Greek verb that means "they poured out" or "they gushed forth" in English. It describes an action of liquid or substance flowing out or being released forcefully.
