Grammar: "Εξελέξατο" is the third person singular, aorist middle voice form of the verb "εξελέγχω" (exeléncho). In Greek grammar, the aorist tense is used to express a past action that is viewed as a whole, without specifying its duration or repetition. The middle voice indicates that the subject of the verb is performing the action on or for itself.
