Grammar: "Εξεκλείσθη" is the third person singular, aorist passive indicative form of the verb "εξεκλείω" (exekleio). In Greek grammar, the aorist tense is used to express a completed action in the past, and the passive voice indicates that the subject of the verb is being acted upon.

Description: "Εξεκλείσθη" is a Greek word that can be translated as "he/she/it was excluded" or "he/she/it was shut out". It describes an action of being excluded or shut out from a certain place or situation.
