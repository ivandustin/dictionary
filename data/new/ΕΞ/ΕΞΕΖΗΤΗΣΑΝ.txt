Etymology: The word "εξεζητησαν" comes from the Greek verb "εξεζητώ" (exezito), which means "to search thoroughly" or "to investigate".

Grammar: "Εξεζητησαν" is the third person plural, past tense form of the verb "εξεζητώ" (exezito). It is used to describe an action that was completed by a group of people in the past.

Description: "Εξεζητησαν" is a Greek word that means "they searched thoroughly" or "they investigated". It implies that a group of people conducted a thorough search or investigation.
