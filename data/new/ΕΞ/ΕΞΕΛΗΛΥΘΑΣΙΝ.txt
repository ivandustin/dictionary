Etymology: The word "εξεληλυθασιν" comes from the Greek verb "εξέρχομαι" (exérchomai), which means "to go out" or "to come forth."

Grammar: "Εξεληλυθασιν" is a verb form in the Greek language. It is the third person plural, aorist active indicative form of the verb "εξέρχομαι" (exérchomai).

Description: "Εξεληλυθασιν" is used to describe an action that has been completed in the past. It indicates that a group of people or things has gone out or come forth from a certain place.
