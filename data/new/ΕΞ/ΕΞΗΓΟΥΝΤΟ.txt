Etymology: The word "εξηγούντο" comes from the Greek verb "εξηγώ" (exigo), which means "to explain" or "to interpret."

Grammar: "Εξηγούντο" is the third person plural, past tense, passive voice form of the verb "εξηγώ" (exigo). It is used to indicate that something was being explained or interpreted by someone in the past.

Description: "Εξηγούντο" is a Greek word that refers to the act of explaining or interpreting something. It is used to describe the action of providing clarification or understanding about a particular topic or concept.
