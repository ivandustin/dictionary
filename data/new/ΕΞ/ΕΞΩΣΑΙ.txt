Etymology: The word "εξωσαί" comes from the Greek word "εξω" (exo) meaning "outside" and the verb "σαίνω" (saino) meaning "to shake". 

Grammar: "Εξωσαί" is a noun in the plural form. It belongs to the third declension in Greek grammar. 

Description: "Εξωσαί" refers to external or outward shaking or trembling. It can be used to describe a physical shaking or trembling that occurs on the outside of something.
