Grammar: In Greek, "εξουσία" is a feminine noun. It is declined in the singular and plural forms, and it can be used in various grammatical cases depending on its role in a sentence.

Description: "Εξουσία" refers to the concept of power, authority, or control. It can be used to describe the ability or right to do something, as well as the influence or dominance over others. In a political context, it often refers to the power held by a government or ruler. It can also be used in a more general sense to describe any form of power or control, whether it be personal, social, or institutional.
