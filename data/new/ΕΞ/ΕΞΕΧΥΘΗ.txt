Etymology: The word "εξεχυθη" (exechythi) comes from the Greek verb "εξεχύννυμι" (exechynnumi), which means "to pour out" or "to spill." 

Grammar: "Εξεχυθη" is a verb in the aorist tense, passive voice, and indicative mood. It is the third person singular form.

Description: "Εξεχυθη" is used to describe the action of pouring out or spilling a liquid. It can also be used metaphorically to describe the spreading or overflowing of something, such as emotions or information.
