Grammar: "Εξουθενημένος" is the masculine singular form of the adjective. In Greek, adjectives agree in gender, number, and case with the noun they modify. So, if the noun is feminine, plural, or in a different case, the form of the adjective will change accordingly.

Description: The adjective "εξουθενημένος" describes someone or something that is physically or mentally exhausted, drained of energy, or feeling weary. It can be used to express a state of extreme tiredness or fatigue.
