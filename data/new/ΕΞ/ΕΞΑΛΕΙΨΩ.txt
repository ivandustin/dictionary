Etymology: The word "εξαλείψω" (exaleípso) comes from the Greek roots "εξ" (ex), meaning "out" or "away," and "αλείφω" (aleífo), meaning "to anoint" or "to smear." 

Grammar: "Εξαλείψω" is a verb in the Greek language. It belongs to the first person singular form of the future tense. 

Description: "Εξαλείψω" means "I will erase" or "I will wipe out" in English. It is used to express the action of completely removing or eliminating something.
