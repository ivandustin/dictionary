Grammar: "Δεδεμένην" is the feminine singular accusative form of the adjective "δεδεμένος" (deménos). In Greek, adjectives agree in gender, number, and case with the noun they modify. In this case, "δεδεμένην" is used to modify a feminine singular noun in the accusative case.

Description: "Δεδεμένην" describes something that is bound or tied. It can be used to refer to a physical object that is tied with a rope or string, or it can be used metaphorically to describe a person who is emotionally or mentally bound or constrained.
