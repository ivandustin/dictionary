Etymology: The word "δεικνυμι" (deiknymi) comes from the Greek language. It is derived from the Proto-Indo-European root *deik- which means "to show" or "to point out".

Grammar: In Greek, "δεικνυμι" is a verb in the present tense. It belongs to the first conjugation class and is conjugated as follows:

- Singular: εγώ δείχνω (ego deíchno) - I show
- Plural: εμείς δείχνουμε (emeís deíchnoume) - we show

Description: "δεικνυμι" means "to show" or "to indicate" in Greek. It is used to express the action of presenting or pointing out something to someone.
