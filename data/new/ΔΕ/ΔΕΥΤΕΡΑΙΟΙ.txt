Etymology: The word "δευτεραίοι" comes from the Greek word "δεύτερος" (deuteros), meaning "second".

Grammar: "Δευτεραίοι" is the plural form of the adjective "δεύτερος" (deuteros), which means "second" in Greek. It agrees in gender, number, and case with the noun it modifies.

Description: "Δευτεραίοι" is used to describe or refer to multiple things or people that come after the first in a sequence or order. It can also be used to indicate the second occurrence of something.
