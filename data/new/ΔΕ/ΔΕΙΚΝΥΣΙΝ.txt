Etymology: The word "δεικνύσιν" (deiknýsin) comes from the Greek verb "δείκνυμι" (deíknymi), which means "to show" or "to demonstrate."

Grammar: "Δεικνύσιν" is the present active participle form of the verb "δείκνυμι" in the third person singular. It is used to describe someone or something that is currently showing or demonstrating.

Description: "Δεικνύσιν" is a Greek word that refers to the act of showing or demonstrating. It can be used in various contexts, such as indicating or pointing out something, presenting evidence or proof, or displaying a skill or talent.
