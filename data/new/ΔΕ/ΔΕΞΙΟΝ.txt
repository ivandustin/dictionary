Etymology: The word "δεξιόν" (dexiόn) comes from the Greek word "δεξιός" (dexiόs), meaning "right" or "on the right side". 

Grammar: "Δεξιόν" is a neuter noun in the singular form. 

Description: "Δεξιόν" refers to the right hand or the right side. It can also be used metaphorically to mean something that is favorable, skillful, or advantageous.
