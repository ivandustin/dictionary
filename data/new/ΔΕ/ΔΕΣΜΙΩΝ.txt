Etymology: The word "δεσμιών" comes from the Greek word "δεσμός" (desmos), meaning "bond" or "tie."

Grammar: "Δεσμιών" is a noun in the plural form. It belongs to the second declension in Greek grammar.

Description: "Δεσμιών" refers to bonds, ties, or restraints. It can be used to describe physical or metaphorical bonds that hold something together or restrict its movement.
