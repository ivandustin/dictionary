Grammar: "Δεξαμενος" is a masculine noun in the Greek language. It belongs to the second declension and has the nominative singular form "δεξαμενος". In the plural, it becomes "δεξαμενοι".

Description: "Δεξαμενος" refers to a reservoir or a tank used for storing liquids, especially water. It can also be used metaphorically to describe a person who is capable of receiving or containing a large amount of something, such as knowledge or information.
