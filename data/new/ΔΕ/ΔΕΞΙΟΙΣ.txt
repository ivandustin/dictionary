Etymology: The word "δεξιοί" (dexioi) comes from the Greek word "δεξιός" (dexios), which means "right" or "on the right side". It is derived from the Proto-Indo-European root *deḱs-, meaning "right" or "to the right".

Grammar: "Δεξιοί" is the plural form of the adjective "δεξιός" (dexios), which means "right" or "on the right side". In Greek grammar, adjectives agree in gender, number, and case with the noun they modify. Therefore, "δεξιοί" is used to describe plural nouns that are on the right side or have a right-leaning characteristic.
