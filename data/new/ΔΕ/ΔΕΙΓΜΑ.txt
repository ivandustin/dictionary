Etymology: The word "δείγμα" (deigma) comes from the Greek verb "δείκνυμι" (deiknumi), which means "to show" or "to demonstrate". 

Grammar: "Δείγμα" is a neuter noun in Greek. It belongs to the second declension and its plural form is "δείγματα" (deigmata). 

Description: "Δείγμα" refers to a sample, specimen, or example that is used to represent a larger group or category. It can also mean a demonstration or a display of something.
