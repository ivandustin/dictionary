Etymology: The word "δεξαί" (dexai) comes from the Greek verb "δέχομαι" (dechomai), which means "to receive" or "to accept".

Grammar: "Δεξαί" is the second person singular, future tense of the verb "δέχομαι" (dechomai). It is used to express an action that will happen in the future and is directed towards the second person singular.

Description: "Δεξαί" is a Greek word that means "you will receive" or "you will accept". It is used to indicate that the person being addressed will receive or accept something in the future.
