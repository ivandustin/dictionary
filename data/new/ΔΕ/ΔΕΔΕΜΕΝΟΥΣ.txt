Etymology: The word "δεδεμένους" comes from the Greek verb "δεμένος" (deménos), which means "bound" or "tied". It is derived from the verb "δέω" (déo), meaning "to bind" or "to tie".

Grammar: "Δεδεμένους" is the plural form of the adjective "δεδεμένος" (deménos), which means "bound" or "tied". It is used to describe multiple objects or individuals that are bound or tied.
