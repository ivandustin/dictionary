Grammar: "Δείξει" is the third person singular future tense form of the verb "δείχνω." In Greek grammar, the future tense is used to express actions or states that will happen in the future.

Description: "Δείξει" is a verb form that indicates an action or state that will be shown or indicated in the future. It can be translated as "he/she/it will show" or "he/she/it will indicate" in English.
