Grammar: "Δεξαμενη" is a feminine noun in Greek. It belongs to the second declension and has the nominative singular form "δεξαμενη". In the plural, it becomes "δεξαμενες".

Description: "Δεξαμενη" refers to a reservoir or a tank used for storing liquids, especially water. It can also be used metaphorically to describe a place or situation where something is accumulated or stored.
