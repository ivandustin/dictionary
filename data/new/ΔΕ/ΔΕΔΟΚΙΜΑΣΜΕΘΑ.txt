Grammar: "Δεδοκιμασμένα" is the plural form of the adjective "δεδοκιμασμένος" (dedokimamenos), which means "tested" or "tried." It agrees in gender, number, and case with the noun it modifies.

Description: "Δεδοκιμασμένα" is used to describe something that has been tested or proven. It implies that the subject has undergone a process of evaluation or examination to determine its quality, reliability, or effectiveness.
