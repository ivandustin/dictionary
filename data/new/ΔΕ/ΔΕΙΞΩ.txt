Etymology: The word "δείξω" (pronounced "deixo") comes from the Greek verb "δείκνυμι" (pronounced "deiknymi"), which means "to show" or "to demonstrate."

Grammar: "Δείξω" is the first person singular future tense of the verb "δείκνυμι." It is used to express an action that will happen in the future and is performed by the speaker.

Description: "Δείξω" is a Greek word that translates to "I will show" in English. It is commonly used to indicate the speaker's intention to demonstrate or present something in the future.
