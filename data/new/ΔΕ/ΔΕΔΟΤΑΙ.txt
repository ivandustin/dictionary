Etymology: The word "δεδοται" comes from the Greek verb "δίδωμι" (didōmi), which means "to give". It is in the perfect tense, indicating a completed action with ongoing effects.

Grammar: "δεδοται" is the third person singular form of the verb "δίδωμι" in the perfect tense. In Greek, verbs change their form based on the person, number, tense, and mood.

Description: "δεδοται" means "he/she/it has given" in English. It is used to describe an action that has been completed in the past but still has relevance or ongoing effects in the present.
