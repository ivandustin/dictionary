Etymology: The word "δεκτωι" (dektoi) comes from the Greek verb "δέχομαι" (dechomai), which means "to receive" or "to accept".

Grammar: "Δεκτωι" is a noun in the dative case, singular number, and masculine gender in the Greek language.

Description: "Δεκτωι" refers to the recipient or the receiver of something. It is used to indicate the person or entity that is receiving or accepting something.
