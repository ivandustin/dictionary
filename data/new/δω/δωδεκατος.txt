Word: δωδεκατος

Etymology:
The word δωδεκατος (dōdekatos) is derived from the Greek word δώδεκα (dōdeka) which means "twelve". The suffix -τος (-tos) is a common adjectival suffix in Greek.

Grammar:
δωδεκατος is an adjective in the masculine gender, singular number, and nominative case. It is used to describe a noun as "twelfth" in a series.

Description:
δωδεκατος is a Greek word that means "twelfth". It is commonly used in ordinal numbering, such as the twelfth day of the month or the twelfth chapter of a book. The word can also be used to describe something that is the twelfth in a series or sequence.
