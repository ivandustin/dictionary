The word "αδικησαντος" is a Greek verb in the aorist tense, which means "having done wrong" or "having acted unjustly". It is derived from the root word "αδικέω" (adikéo), which means "to act unjustly" or "to do wrong". In terms of grammar, "αδικησαντος" is the genitive singular masculine form of the participle of "αδικέω".
