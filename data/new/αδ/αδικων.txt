The word "αδικων" (adikon) is a Greek adjective that means "unjust" or "unfair". It is derived from the Greek word "άδικος" (adikos), which means "unjust" or "wrongful". In terms of grammar, "αδικων" is in the masculine singular form, and it is used to describe a noun that is also masculine and singular. For example, "ο αδικων άνθρωπος" (o adikon anthropos) means "the unjust man".
