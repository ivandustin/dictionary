Word: αδικοι

Etymology:
The word αδικοι (adikoi) is derived from the Greek word αδικία (adikia), which means injustice or wrongdoing. The word is formed by adding the suffix -οι (-oi) to the stem αδικ- (adik-).

Grammar:
αδικοι (adikoi) is a plural form of the adjective αδίκης (adikis), which means unjust or unfair. It is used to describe a group of people who are unjust or unfair.
