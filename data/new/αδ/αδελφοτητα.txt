The word "αδελφοτητα" (adelphotita) is a Greek noun that means "brotherhood" or "fraternity". It is derived from the Greek words "αδελφός" (adelphos), meaning "brother", and "-τητα" (-tita), a suffix used to form abstract nouns indicating a state or quality. In terms of grammar, it is a feminine noun in the singular form. The word is often used to describe a close bond or relationship between people who share common interests or goals.
