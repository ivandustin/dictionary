Word: ψιχιων

Etymology:
The word ψιχιων is derived from the Greek word ψιχή (psichē), which means "a small piece" or "a crumb". The suffix -ων (-ōn) is a common Greek suffix used to form nouns.

Grammar:
ψιχιων is a noun in the singular form.

Description:
ψιχιων refers to a small piece or crumb of something, such as bread or cake. It can also be used metaphorically to refer to something small or insignificant.
