Etymology: The word "οκνησης" comes from the Greek verb "οκνέω" (oknéo), which means "to hesitate" or "to be reluctant". 

Grammar: "Οκνησης" is a noun in the genitive case, singular form. In Greek grammar, nouns change their form depending on their case, number, and gender. The genitive case is used to indicate possession or origin.
