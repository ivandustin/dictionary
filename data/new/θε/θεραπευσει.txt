Word: θεραπευσει

Etymology: θεραπευσει is a Greek word that comes from the verb θεραπεύω (therapevo), which means "to heal" or "to cure".

Grammar: θεραπευσει is the third person singular future tense of the verb θεραπεύω (therapevo). It can be translated as "he/she/it will heal" or "he/she/it will cure".

Description: θεραπευσει is a verb that refers to the act of healing or curing. It can be used in various contexts, such as in medicine, where it refers to the treatment of a disease or injury, or in a more general sense, where it can refer to the process of making something better or improving a situation.
