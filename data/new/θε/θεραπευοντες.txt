Word: θεραπευοντες

Etymology: θεραπευοντες is a Greek word derived from the verb θεραπεύω (therapevo), which means "to heal" or "to cure". The suffix -οντες (-ontes) is a present participle ending, indicating that the word refers to people who are currently healing or curing.

Grammar: θεραπευοντες is the present participle form of the verb θεραπεύω (therapevo), which means "healing" or "curing". It is a masculine plural noun, indicating that it refers to a group of male individuals who are currently healing or curing.

Description: θεραπευοντες refers to healers or curers, specifically a group of male individuals who are currently engaged in the act of healing or curing. The word can be used in a variety of contexts, such as in medicine, therapy, or spiritual healing. It implies an active and ongoing process of healing, rather than a one-time event.
