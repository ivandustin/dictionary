Word: θεραπευετε

Etymology: θεραπευετε is a Greek verb that comes from the root word θεραπεία (therapeia), which means "healing" or "therapy". The verb form θεραπευετε means "you heal" or "you treat".

Grammar: θεραπευετε is the second person plural form of the present tense in Greek. It is used to address a group of people and means "you all heal" or "you all treat".

Description: θεραπευετε is a verb that is commonly used in the medical field in Greece. It is used to describe the act of healing or treating patients. The verb can also be used in a broader sense to describe any kind of therapy or treatment, whether it is physical or emotional.
