Word: θεσσαλονικεως

Grammar: θεσσαλονικεως is a masculine noun in the genitive case. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their grammatical function in a sentence. The genitive case is used to indicate possession or origin.

Description: θεσσαλονικεως is a word that refers to someone or something that is from or related to Thessaloniki, which is the second-largest city in Greece. It can be used to describe a person, object, or concept that has a connection to this city.
