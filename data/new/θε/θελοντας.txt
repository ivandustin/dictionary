Word: θελοντας

Etymology: θελοντας is a present participle form of the Greek verb θέλω (thélo), which means "to want" or "to wish". The word comes from the Proto-Indo-European root *wel-, which means "to wish" or "to will".

Grammar: θελοντας is the present participle form of the verb θέλω (thélo), which means "wanting" or "wishing". It is used to describe an ongoing action or state of wanting or wishing. In Greek, present participles are formed by adding -ντας (-ntas) to the stem of the verb.

Description: θελοντας is a Greek word that describes someone who is currently wanting or wishing for something. It can be used in a variety of contexts, such as expressing desires, needs, or preferences. For example, "Οι φίλοι μου θελοντας να πάνε στην παραλία" (My friends are wanting to go to the beach).
