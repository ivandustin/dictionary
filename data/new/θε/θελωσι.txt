Word: θελωσι

Description: θελωσι is a verb that expresses the idea of wanting or wishing for something. It can be used in a variety of contexts, such as expressing desires, preferences, or intentions. For example, "Θελωσι να φαω" (thélo na fáo) means "They want to eat". The verb θελωσι is commonly used in everyday conversation and is an important part of the Greek language.
