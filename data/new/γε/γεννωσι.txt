The word "γεννωσι" (gennōsi) is a Greek verb that means "they give birth" or "they produce." It comes from the root word "γεννάω" (gennáō), which means "to beget" or "to bring forth." In terms of grammar, "γεννωσι" is the third person plural present indicative active form of the verb. It can be used to describe the act of giving birth or producing offspring, as well as the act of creating or bringing something into existence.
