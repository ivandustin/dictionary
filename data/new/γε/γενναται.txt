Word: γεννάται

Etymology: The word γεννάται comes from the Greek verb γεννάω (gennáo), which means "to give birth" or "to beget". It is derived from the Proto-Indo-European root *ǵenh₁- which means "to produce, beget".

Present tense: γεννάται
Past tense: εγεννήθη (egeníthi)
Future tense: θα γεννηθεί (tha gennithí)
Perfect tense: έχει γεννηθεί (échei gennithí)

Description: γεννάται means "is born" or "is being born". It is used to describe the act of giving birth or being born. The word can be used in a literal sense to describe the birth of a human or animal, or in a figurative sense to describe the beginning or creation of something.
