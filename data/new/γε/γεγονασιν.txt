Word: γεγονασιν

Grammar: γεγονασιν is a verb form in the perfect tense, which indicates a completed action in the past with ongoing relevance to the present. The active voice indicates that the subject of the sentence is performing the action. The indicative mood indicates that the verb is a statement of fact.

Description: γεγονασιν is used in Greek to indicate that something has happened or has been born. It can be translated as "they have become" or "they were born". The context of the sentence will determine the exact meaning.
