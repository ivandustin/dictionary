Word: γεγραμμένα

Etymology:
The word γεγραμμένα is a Greek word that comes from the verb γράφω (grapho), which means "to write." The word is the perfect participle form of the verb, which means "written" or "having been written."

Grammar:
In Greek, γεγραμμένα is a neuter plural adjective that agrees with the gender and number of the noun it modifies. It is in the perfect tense, which indicates a completed action in the past with ongoing effects in the present.

Description:
The word γεγραμμένα is often used to describe something that has been written or recorded. It can refer to a written document, a book, or any other form of written communication.
