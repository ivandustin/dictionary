Word: γενηματος

Etymology: The word γενηματος comes from the Greek word γέννημα (gennēma), which means "offspring" or "product". The suffix -τος (-tos) is a genitive ending, indicating possession or relationship.

Grammar: γενηματος is a noun in the genitive case, indicating possession or relationship. It is masculine in gender and singular in number.

Description: γενηματος is a Greek word that refers to something that is produced or generated, such as an offspring or a product. In the genitive case, it can indicate possession or relationship, such as "the product of the company" or "the offspring of the king".
