Word: γελασετε

Etymology: γελασετε is a Greek verb that comes from the root word γελάω (gelao), which means "to laugh". The suffix -σετε (-sete) is the second person plural ending for the future tense.

Grammar: γελασετε is a second person plural verb in the future tense. It is used to address a group of people and means "you will laugh".

Description: γελασετε is a verb that expresses the action of laughing in the future tense. It is often used in a playful or lighthearted context, such as when telling a joke or making a humorous comment. The word can also be used to encourage others to have a good time and enjoy themselves.
