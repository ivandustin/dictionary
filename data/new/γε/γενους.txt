The word γενους (genous) is derived from the Greek word γένος (genos), which means "race," "kind," or "family." It is related to the Greek word γίγνομαι (ginomai), which means "to become" or "to be born."

γενους is a term used in biology to refer to a taxonomic rank above the level of species. It is often translated as "genus" in English.
