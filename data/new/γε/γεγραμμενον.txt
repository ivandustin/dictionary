Word: γεγραμμένον

Etymology:
The word γεγραμμένον comes from the Greek verb γράφω (grapho), which means "to write." The word is a past participle form of the verb, which means "written."

Grammar:
γεγραμμένον is a neuter gender noun in the singular form. It is in the nominative case, which means it is the subject of the sentence.

Description:
γεγραμμένον is a word that describes something that has been written. It can be used to refer to a written document, a letter, or any other written material. The word can also be used to describe a person who is skilled in writing or who has written something of importance. In Greek literature, the word is often used to refer to the written word of God or the scriptures.
