Word: γεγραμμένοις

Grammar: γεγραμμένοις is a participle in the perfect tense, passive voice, and masculine gender. It is used as an adjective to describe a noun or pronoun. In this form, it means "having been written."

Description: γεγραμμένοις is a word commonly found in Greek texts, particularly in the New Testament. It is often used to describe something that has been written or inscribed, such as a letter or a message.
