Etymology: The word "γεννάω" comes from the Greek root "γενν-" which means "to produce" or "to generate". 

Grammar: "γεννωσα" is a conjugated form of the verb "γεννάω". It is in the aorist tense, which is used to describe a completed action in the past. The first person singular form indicates that the action was performed by the speaker.
