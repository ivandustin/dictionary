Word: γεμισατε

Etymology: γεμισατε is a Greek verb that comes from the root word γεμίζω (gemízo), which means "to fill" or "to stuff". The suffix -ατε (-ate) is the second person plural imperative ending, which means that the verb is in the command form and is directed towards a group of people.

Grammar: γεμισατε is a second person plural imperative verb in the aorist tense. It is used to give a command or instruction to a group of people. The verb is formed by adding the suffix -ατε to the root word γεμίζω.

Description: γεμισατε is a command that means "fill" or "stuff". It is often used in cooking or food preparation to instruct someone to fill a container or stuff a food item. It can also be used in a figurative sense to mean "to fill up" or "to complete".
