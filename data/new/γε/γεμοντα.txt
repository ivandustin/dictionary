The word "γεμοντα" (gemonta) is a present participle in Greek, derived from the verb "γεμίζω" (gemizo) which means "to fill". The participle form indicates that the action of filling is currently ongoing or in progress. 

In terms of grammar, "γεμοντα" is in the masculine singular nominative case, which means it is the subject of the sentence. 

Etymologically, "γεμίζω" comes from the Proto-Indo-European root *gʰem-, meaning "to be full". 

Overall, "γεμοντα" can be translated as "filling" or "being filled".
