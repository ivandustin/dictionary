Word: γεμει (gem-ei)

Etymology: The word γεμει comes from the Greek verb γεμίζω (gemizo), which means "to fill" or "to fill up". 

Grammar: γεμει is a verb in the present tense, active voice, and indicative mood. It is conjugated in the third person singular form. 

Description: γεμει is a Greek verb that means "he/she/it fills" or "he/she/it is filling". It is often used to describe the act of filling something up, such as a container or a space. It can also be used metaphorically to describe the act of filling up one's time or schedule. 

Example sentence: Ο κήπος γεμεί με λουλούδια. (O kipos gemi me louloudia.) - The garden is filled with flowers.
