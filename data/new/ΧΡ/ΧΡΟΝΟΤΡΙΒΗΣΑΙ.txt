Etymology: The word "χρονοτριβησαί" comes from the Greek words "χρόνος" (chronos), meaning "time," and "τριβή" (tribe), meaning "waste" or "spend." 

Grammar: "Χρονοτριβησαί" is a verb in the Greek language. It is in the aorist tense, indicative mood, and active voice. 

Description: "Χρονοτριβησαί" means "to waste time" or "to spend time." It refers to the act of using time in an unproductive or inefficient manner.
