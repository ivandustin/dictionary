Etymology: The word "χρυσού" (chrysoú) comes from the Greek word "χρυσός" (chrysós), meaning "gold."

Grammar: "Χρυσού" (chrysoú) is a noun in the genitive case. In Greek grammar, the genitive case is used to indicate possession or origin.
