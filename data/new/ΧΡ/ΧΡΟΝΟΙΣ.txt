Etymology: The word "χρονοις" (chronois) comes from the Greek word "χρόνος" (chronos), meaning "time". 

Grammar: "Χρονοις" is a noun in the plural form. It belongs to the second declension in Greek grammar. 

Description: "Χρονοις" refers to "times" or "periods" in Greek. It can be used to indicate specific points in time or to describe recurring or continuous periods.
