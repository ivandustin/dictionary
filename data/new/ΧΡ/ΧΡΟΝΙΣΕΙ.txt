Etymology: The word "χρονισει" comes from the Greek verb "χρονίζω" (chronízo), which means "to delay" or "to postpone".

Grammar: "Χρονισει" is the third person singular form of the future tense of the verb "χρονίζω" (chronízo). It is used to indicate an action that will happen in the future.

Description: "Χρονισει" is a Greek word that can be translated as "he/she/it will delay" or "he/she/it will postpone". It is commonly used in everyday speech and writing to express the idea of delaying or postponing an action or event.
