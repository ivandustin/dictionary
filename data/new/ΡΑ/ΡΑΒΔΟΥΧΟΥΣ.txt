Etymology: The word "ραβδούχος" comes from the Greek word "ράβδος" (ravdos), meaning "rod" or "staff", and the suffix "-ούχος" (-ouchos), which denotes possession or occupation. 

Grammar: "Ραβδούχος" is a masculine noun in the singular form. In Greek grammar, it belongs to the second declension and follows the regular pattern for nouns ending in "-ος" in the nominative case.

Description: "Ραβδούχος" refers to a person who carries or holds a staff or rod. It can also be used metaphorically to describe someone who has authority or power.
