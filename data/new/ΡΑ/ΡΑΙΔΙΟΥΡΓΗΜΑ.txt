Etymology: The word "ραιδιουργημα" comes from the Greek words "ραιδιος" (raidos), meaning "easy" or "quick," and "εργον" (ergon), meaning "work" or "action." 

Grammar: "Ραιδιουργημα" is a noun in the Greek language. It is a neuter noun and its plural form is "ραιδιουργηματα" (raidiourgimata).

Description: "Ραιδιουργημα" refers to something that is done quickly or easily, often implying a clever or skillful action. It can be used to describe a work or action that is accomplished with efficiency and dexterity.
