Etymology: The word "τιμων" (timon) comes from the Greek verb "τιμάω" (timao), which means "to honor" or "to value". 

Grammar: "Τιμών" is a noun in the masculine gender and singular number. It belongs to the second declension in Greek grammar. 

Description: "Τιμών" refers to a person who is honorable, esteemed, or respected. It can also be used to describe someone who is of high rank or status.
