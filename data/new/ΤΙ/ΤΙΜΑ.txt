Etymology: The word "τίμα" (tima) comes from the Greek verb "τιμάω" (timao), which means "to honor" or "to value". 

Grammar: "Τίμα" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the singular form and is classified as a neuter noun. 

Description: "Τίμα" refers to the concept of honor or value. It can also be used to describe a price or a fee.
