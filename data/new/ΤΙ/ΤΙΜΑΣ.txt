Etymology: The word "τιμας" (timas) comes from the Greek verb "τιμάω" (timao), which means "to honor" or "to value."

Grammar: "Τιμας" is a noun in the nominative case, singular number, and masculine gender in Greek.

Description: "Τιμας" refers to a person who is honored or esteemed. It can also be used to describe someone who is respected or valued by others.
