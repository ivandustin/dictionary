Etymology: The word "τιμιότητα" comes from the Greek word "τίμιος" (timios), meaning "honorable" or "valuable", and the suffix "-τητα" (-tita), which denotes the quality or state of being. 

Grammar: "Τιμιότητα" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the singular form and belongs to the feminine gender. 

Description: "Τιμιότητα" refers to the quality or state of being honorable, valuable, or esteemed. It is often used to describe someone's character, actions, or reputation.
