Etymology: The word "τίτλος" (titlos) comes from the Ancient Greek word "τίτλος" (títlos), meaning "title" or "inscription".

Grammar: In Greek, "τίτλος" (titlos) is a masculine noun in the nominative case. It belongs to the second declension and has the plural form "τίτλοι" (titloi).

Description: "Τίτλος" (titlos) refers to a title or heading that is used to label or identify something, such as a book, article, or document. It is commonly used to indicate the name or subject of a work.
