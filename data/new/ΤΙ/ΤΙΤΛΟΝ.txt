Grammar: "Τίτλον" is a noun in the accusative case. In Greek grammar, nouns change their form depending on their role in the sentence. The accusative case is used to indicate the direct object of a verb or the object of certain prepositions.

Description: "Τίτλον" refers to a title or heading. It is often used to indicate the name or label of a book, article, or other written work. It can also refer to a caption or heading in a document or a sign.
