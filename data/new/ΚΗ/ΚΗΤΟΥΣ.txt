Etymology: The word "κητούς" (kētoús) comes from the Greek language. It is derived from the ancient Greek word "κῆτος" (kētos), which means "sea monster" or "whale."

Grammar: "Κητούς" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the masculine gender and the nominative case.
