Etymology: The word "κηρυξωσιν" (kēryxōsin) comes from the Greek verb "κηρύσσω" (kēryssō), which means "to proclaim" or "to announce."

Grammar: "κηρυξωσιν" is a verb form in the Greek language. It is in the present tense, active voice, subjunctive mood, and third person plural.

Description: The word "κηρυξωσιν" is derived from the verb "κηρύσσω" and indicates an action of proclaiming or announcing something. In this specific form, it refers to a third person plural subject performing the action in the present time.
