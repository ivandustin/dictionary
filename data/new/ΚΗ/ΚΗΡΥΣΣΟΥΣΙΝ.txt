Etymology: The word "κηρυσσουσιν" comes from the Greek verb "κηρύσσω" (kerusso), which means "to proclaim" or "to announce."

Grammar: "κηρυσσουσιν" is the third person plural form of the verb "κηρύσσω" (kerusso) in the present tense. It is in the active voice and indicative mood.

Description: "κηρυσσουσιν" is a Greek word that means "they proclaim" or "they announce." It is used to describe the action of making a public declaration or announcement.
