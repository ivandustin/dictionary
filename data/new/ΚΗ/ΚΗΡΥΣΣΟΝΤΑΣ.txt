Grammar: "κηρυσσωντας" is the present participle form of the verb "κηρυσσω" (kēryssō) in Greek. It is used to describe an ongoing action of proclaiming or announcing in the present tense. In this form, it agrees with the subject in gender, number, and case.

Description: "κηρυσσωντας" refers to someone who is currently proclaiming or announcing something. It can be used to describe a person who is publicly declaring a message, often in a formal or authoritative manner.
