Etymology: The word "κηρυξω" (kēryxō) comes from the Greek verb "κηρύσσω" (kēryssō), which means "to proclaim" or "to announce." 

Grammar: "κηρυξω" is a verb in the Greek language. It is in the future tense and first person singular form. 

Description: "κηρυξω" is used to express the action of proclaiming or announcing something in the future. It can be translated as "I will proclaim" or "I will announce."
