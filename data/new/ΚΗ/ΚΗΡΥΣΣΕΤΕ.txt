Etymology: The word "κηρυσσετε" comes from the Greek verb "κηρυσσω" (kerusso), which means "to proclaim" or "to announce."

Grammar: "κηρυσσετε" is the second person plural form of the verb "κηρυσσω" (kerusso) in the present tense. It is used when addressing a group of people and means "you all proclaim" or "you all announce."

Description: "κηρυσσετε" is a command form of the verb "κηρυσσω" (kerusso). It is used to instruct or urge a group of people to proclaim or announce something.
