Etymology: The word "κηρυσσοντα" comes from the Greek verb "κηρυσσω" (kerusso), which means "to proclaim" or "to announce."

Grammar: "κηρυσσοντα" is the present participle form of the verb "κηρυσσω" (kerusso) in Greek. It is in the masculine gender, singular number, and accusative case.

Description: "κηρυσσοντα" describes something or someone that is proclaiming or announcing. It can refer to a person who is actively making a public announcement or declaration.
