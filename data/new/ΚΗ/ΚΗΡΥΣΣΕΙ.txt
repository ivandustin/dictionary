Etymology: The word "κηρυσσει" comes from the Greek verb "κηρύσσω" (kerusso), which means "to proclaim" or "to announce."

Grammar: "κηρυσσει" is the third person singular form of the present tense of the verb "κηρύσσω" (kerusso) in Greek. It is in the active voice and indicative mood.

Description: "κηρυσσει" is a Greek word that means "he/she/it proclaims" or "he/she/it announces." It is used to describe the action of making a public declaration or announcement.
