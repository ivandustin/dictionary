Etymology: The word "κηπον" (kēpon) comes from the Greek language. It is derived from the ancient Greek word "κῆπος" (kēpos), meaning "garden" or "orchard."

Grammar: In Greek, "κηπον" (kēpon) is a noun in the accusative case. It is the singular form of the word, and it refers to a garden or a small cultivated area.

Description: "Κηπον" (kēpon) is a term used in Greek to describe a garden or a small cultivated area where plants, flowers, and sometimes vegetables are grown. It can refer to a private garden, a public park, or even a small plot of land used for gardening purposes.
