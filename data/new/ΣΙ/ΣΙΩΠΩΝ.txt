Etymology: The word "σιωπών" (siopon) comes from the Greek verb "σιωπάω" (siopao), which means "to be silent" or "to keep quiet."

Grammar: "Σιωπών" is the present participle form of the verb "σιωπάω" in the masculine gender, singular number, and nominative case. It is used to describe someone who is currently silent or keeping quiet.

Description: "Σιωπών" refers to a person who is silent or not speaking. It can be used to describe someone who chooses not to speak or someone who is naturally quiet.
