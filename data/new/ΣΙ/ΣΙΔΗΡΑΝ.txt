Etymology: The word "σιδηραν" comes from the Greek word "σίδηρος" (sideros), meaning "iron". 

Grammar: "σιδηραν" is a noun in the accusative case, singular form. 

Description: "σιδηραν" refers to "iron" in the Greek language. It is a hard, strong, and malleable metal commonly used in construction, manufacturing, and various other industries.
