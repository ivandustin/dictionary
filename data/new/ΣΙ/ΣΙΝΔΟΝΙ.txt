Etymology: The word "σινδονι" (sindoni) comes from the Greek word "σινδών" (sindon), which means "fine linen cloth" or "linen shroud." 

Grammar: "Σινδόνι" is a noun in the singular form. It belongs to the third declension in Greek grammar.

Description: "Σινδόνι" refers to a type of fine linen cloth or shroud. It is often associated with burial shrouds or the linen cloth used to wrap a body.
