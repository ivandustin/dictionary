Word: οστεων

Grammar: Οστεων is a noun in the genitive plural form in Greek. It is used to indicate possession or relationship to a noun in the singular form.

Description: Οστεων refers to bones in Greek. It is commonly used in medical and anatomical contexts to describe the skeletal system. The word can also be used metaphorically to refer to something that is the foundation or structure of something else.
