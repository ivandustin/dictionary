Grammar: αλλαγησονται is a third-person plural form of the future passive indicative mood of the verb αλλάζω. This means that it refers to a future action that will be done to the subject of the sentence.

Description: αλλαγησονται can be translated to English as "they will be changed" or "they will be altered". The subject of the sentence is not specified, so it could refer to any noun in the context of the sentence. The word implies that something will be transformed or modified in the future.
