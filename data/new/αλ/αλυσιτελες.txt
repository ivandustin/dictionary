The word "αλυσιτελες" (alysiteles) is a Greek adjective that means "inefficient" or "unproductive". 

Etymology: The word is derived from the Greek words "αλυσίτελος" (alysitelos), which means "unprofitable" or "useless", and "τέλος" (telos), which means "goal" or "end". 

Grammar: "αλυσιτελες" is a masculine, singular, nominative adjective in Greek.
