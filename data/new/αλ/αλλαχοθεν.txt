Word: αλλαχοθεν

Etymology:
The word αλλαχοθεν is derived from the Greek words αλλος (allos) meaning "other" and θεν (then) meaning "from". Therefore, the word αλλαχοθεν means "from another place" or "from elsewhere".

Grammar:
αλλαχοθεν is an adverb in Greek. It is used to describe the origin or source of something. It is often used in phrases such as "αλλαχοθεν καταφυγείς" (alakhóthen kataphygeís) which means "refugee from another place".
