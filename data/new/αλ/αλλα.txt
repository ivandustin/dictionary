The word αλλα (alla) is a conjunction in the Greek language. It is derived from the ancient Greek word ἀλλά (allá), which means "but" or "however".

Grammar:
In Greek grammar, αλλα is a coordinating conjunction that is used to connect two clauses or phrases that express contrasting ideas. It is often used to introduce a statement that contradicts or contrasts with the previous statement.

αλλα is a common word in the Greek language and is used in everyday conversation and writing.
