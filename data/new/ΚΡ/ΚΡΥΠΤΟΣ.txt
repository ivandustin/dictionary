Etymology: The word "κρυπτός" (kryptos) comes from the Greek verb "κρύπτω" (krypto), which means "to hide" or "to conceal." 

Grammar: In Greek, "κρυπτός" is an adjective that agrees in gender, number, and case with the noun it modifies. It has three forms: κρυπτός (masculine), κρυπτή (feminine), and κρυπτό (neuter). 

Description: The word "κρυπτός" means "hidden" or "concealed" in Greek. It is used to describe something that is not easily seen or known, or something that is kept secret or private.
