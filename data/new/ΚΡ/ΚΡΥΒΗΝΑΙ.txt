Etymology: The word "κρυβηναι" comes from the Greek verb "κρύβω" (kryvo), which means "to hide" or "to conceal."

Grammar: "κρυβηναι" is the aorist infinitive form of the verb "κρύβω" (kryvo). In Greek grammar, the aorist tense is used to express a past action without specifying its duration or completion.

Description: "κρυβηναι" is used to describe the action of hiding or concealing something. It can refer to physically hiding an object or concealing information or emotions.
