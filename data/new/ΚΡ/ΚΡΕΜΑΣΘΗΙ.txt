Etymology: The word "κρεμασθηί" (kremasthēi) comes from the Greek verb "κρεμάννυμι" (kremannymi), which means "to hang" or "to suspend."

Grammar: "κρεμασθηί" is a noun in the singular form. It belongs to the third declension in Greek grammar.

Description: "κρεμασθηί" refers to a type of hanging or suspension. It can be used to describe the act of hanging something or someone, or the state of being suspended.
