Grammar: "Κρινομένος" is a masculine singular participle in the present middle-passive voice. It is derived from the verb "κρίνω" (krino), which is a first-conjugation verb. In Greek grammar, participles are verbal adjectives that can modify nouns or function as verbs.

Description: "Κρινομένος" is used to describe someone or something that is currently being judged or decided upon. It implies a state of being in the process of evaluation or assessment.
