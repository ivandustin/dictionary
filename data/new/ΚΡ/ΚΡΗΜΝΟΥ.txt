Etymology: The word "κρημνού" (krēmnou) comes from the Greek word "κρημνός" (krēmnos), which means "precipice" or "cliff". 

Grammar: "Κρημνού" is a noun in the genitive case. In Greek grammar, the genitive case is used to indicate possession, origin, or relationship.

Description: "Κρημνού" refers to the genitive form of the noun "κρημνός" (krēmnos), which means "precipice" or "cliff". It is used to describe the relationship or possession of something related to a precipice or cliff.
