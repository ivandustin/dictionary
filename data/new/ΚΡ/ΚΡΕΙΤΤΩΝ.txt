Etymology: The word "κρειττων" (krettōn) comes from the Greek verb "κρείττων" (kreittōn), which means "to be superior" or "to excel."

Grammar: "Κρειττων" is a masculine noun in the nominative singular form. It belongs to the third declension in Greek grammar.

Description: "Κρειττων" is an adjective that means "better" or "superior" in Greek. It is often used to compare two or more things, indicating that one is of higher quality or more advantageous than the other(s).
