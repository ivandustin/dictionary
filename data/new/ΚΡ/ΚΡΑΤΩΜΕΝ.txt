Grammar: "κρατώμεν" is the present tense, active voice, first person plural form of the verb "κρατέω" (kratéo). It is used to indicate that "we hold" or "we possess" something.

Description: "κρατώμεν" is a Greek verb that expresses the action of holding or possessing something.
