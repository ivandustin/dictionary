Grammar: "Λαμβάνων" is the present participle form of the verb "λαμβάνω" (lamváno) in the masculine gender. It is used to describe someone who is currently taking or receiving something.

Description: "Λαμβάνων" is a Greek word that refers to someone who is in the act of taking or receiving something. It can be used to describe a person who is actively receiving an object, a concept, or an idea.
