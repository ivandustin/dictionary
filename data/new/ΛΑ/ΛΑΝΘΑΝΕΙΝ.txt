Etymology: The word "λανθανείν" comes from the Greek verb "λανθάνω" (lanthano), which means "to escape notice" or "to remain hidden". 

Grammar: "Λανθανείν" is the present tense form of the verb in the Greek language. It is in the active voice and is used to express the action of "escaping notice" or "remaining hidden". 

Description: "Λανθανείν" is a verb that describes the act of going unnoticed or remaining hidden. It can be used in various contexts to indicate someone or something being concealed, overlooked, or not being seen or recognized.
