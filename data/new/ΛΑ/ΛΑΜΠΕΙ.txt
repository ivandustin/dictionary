Etymology: The word "λαμπεί" (lampei) comes from the Greek verb "λάμπω" (lampo), which means "to shine" or "to radiate light".

Grammar: "Λαμπεί" is the third person singular form of the verb "λάμπω" (lampo) in the present tense. It is used to describe an action or state of shining or radiating light.

Description: "Λαμπεί" is a Greek word that can be translated as "it shines" or "it radiates light". It is often used to describe the brightness or brilliance of something.
