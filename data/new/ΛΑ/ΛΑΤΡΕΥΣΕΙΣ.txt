Grammar: "Λατρευτής" is a masculine noun in the Greek language. It belongs to the second declension and has the nominative singular form "λατρευτής." In the plural form, it becomes "λατρευτές."

Description: "Λατρευτής" refers to a person who worships or serves a deity or a higher power. It can also be used to describe someone who is devoted to a particular cause or ideal.
