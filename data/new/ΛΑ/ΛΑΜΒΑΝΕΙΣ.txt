Etymology: The word "λαμβάνεις" comes from the Greek verb "λαμβάνω" (lambanō), which means "to take" or "to receive".

Grammar: "Λαμβάνεις" is the second person singular form of the present tense in Greek. It is used when addressing one person and means "you take" or "you receive".

Description: "Λαμβάνεις" is a verb in the Greek language that expresses the action of taking or receiving something. It is commonly used in everyday conversations and written texts.
