Etymology: The word "λαλούντες" comes from the Greek verb "λαλέω" (laleo), which means "to speak" or "to talk". It is derived from the Proto-Indo-European root *leh₂-.

Grammar: "Λαλούντες" is the plural form of the Greek noun "λαλούς" (lalous), which means "speaker" or "talker". It belongs to the third declension of Greek nouns.

Description: "Λαλούντες" refers to a group of people who are speaking or talking. It can be used to describe individuals engaged in conversation or a gathering of people engaged in verbal communication.
