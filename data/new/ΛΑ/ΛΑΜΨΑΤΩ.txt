Etymology: The word "λαμψατω" (lamptō) comes from the Greek verb "λαμπω" (lampō), which means "to shine" or "to radiate light".

Grammar: "Λαμψατω" is a verb in the imperative mood, third person singular form. It is derived from the aorist tense of the verb "λαμπω" (lampō).

Description: "Λαμψατω" is a command or an exhortation in Greek, meaning "let it shine" or "let it radiate light". It is often used metaphorically to encourage someone to let their talents, abilities, or positive qualities shine or be seen by others.
