Etymology: The word "λαβών" (lavón) comes from the Greek verb "λαμβάνω" (lambáno), which means "to take" or "to receive."

Grammar: "Λαβών" is the masculine singular form of the participle of the verb "λαμβάνω" (lambáno). It is in the nominative case.

Description: "Λαβών" is a Greek word that means "taking" or "receiving." It is often used as a participle to describe someone who is in the act of taking or receiving something.
