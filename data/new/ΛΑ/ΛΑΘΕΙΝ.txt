Etymology: The word "λαθειν" (lathain) comes from the Greek verb "λανθάνω" (lanthano), which means "to hide" or "to escape notice". 

Grammar: "Λαθειν" is the infinitive form of the verb and is commonly used in Greek grammar. It belongs to the first conjugation class of Greek verbs.

Description: "Λαθειν" is a Greek verb that means "to hide" or "to conceal". It is often used to describe the act of keeping something secret or not being seen or noticed.
