Etymology: The word "λανθανετω" (lanthaneto) comes from the Greek verb "λανθάνω" (lanthano), which means "to hide" or "to be concealed."

Grammar: "Λανθανετω" is a verb in the imperative mood and the third person singular form. It is derived from the aorist tense of the verb "λανθάνω" (lanthano).

Description: "Λανθανετω" is a command in Greek, meaning "let it be hidden" or "let it be concealed." It is often used figuratively to express the idea of keeping something secret or not revealing the truth.
