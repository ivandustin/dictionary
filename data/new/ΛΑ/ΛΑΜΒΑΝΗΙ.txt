Etymology: The word "λαμβάνηι" (lamvani) comes from the Greek verb "λαμβάνω" (lamvano), which means "to take" or "to receive".

Grammar: "Λαμβάνηι" is a verb in the present tense, active voice, indicative mood, and third person singular form. It is conjugated from the verb "λαμβάνω" (lamvano).

Description: "Λαμβάνηι" is used to express the action of taking or receiving something. It can also be used metaphorically to indicate understanding or comprehending something.
