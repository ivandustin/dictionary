Etymology: The word "λαμψει" (lampei) comes from the Greek verb "λάμπω" (lampo), which means "to shine" or "to radiate light".

Grammar: "Λαμψει" is the future tense form of the verb "λάμπω" (lampo) in the third person singular. It is used to express an action that will happen in the future.

Description: "Λαμψει" is a Greek word that means "he/she/it will shine" or "he/she/it will radiate light". It is often used metaphorically to describe someone or something that will stand out or excel in a particular field or situation.
