Etymology: The word "λανθανεί" comes from the Greek verb "λανθάνω" (lanthano), which means "to hide" or "to be concealed".

Grammar: "Λανθανεί" is the third person singular form of the present tense of the verb "λανθάνω" (lanthano) in Greek. It is used to describe an action or state of hiding or being concealed in the present time.

Description: "Λανθανεί" is a Greek verb that conveys the idea of hiding or being concealed. It can be used in various contexts to describe physical or metaphorical hiding, secrecy, or being unnoticed.
