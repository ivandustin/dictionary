Etymology: The word "λαλώ" (lalō) comes from the Greek verb "λαλέω" (laleō), which means "to speak" or "to talk".

Grammar: "Λαλώ" is the first person singular present tense form of the verb "λαλέω". It is used when the subject of the sentence is "I" and the action is happening in the present.

Description: "Λαλώ" is a Greek word that means "I speak" or "I talk". It is used to express the action of speaking or talking in the present tense.
