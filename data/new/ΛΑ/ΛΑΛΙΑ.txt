Etymology: The word "λαλιά" comes from the Greek verb "λαλώ" (laló), which means "to speak" or "to talk".

Grammar: "Λαλιά" is a feminine noun in Greek. It belongs to the second declension and has the plural form "λαλιές".

Description: "Λαλιά" refers to the act of speaking or talking. It can also be used to describe the sound or voice produced when speaking. Additionally, it can be used metaphorically to refer to someone's speech or words.
