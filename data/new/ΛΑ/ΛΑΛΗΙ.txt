Etymology: The word "λαληί" (lalēi) comes from the Greek verb "λαλέω" (laleō), which means "to talk" or "to speak."

Grammar: "Λαληί" (lalēi) is a noun in the singular form. It is a feminine noun in the nominative case.

Description: "Λαληί" (lalēi) refers to the act of speaking or talking. It can also be used to describe someone who talks a lot or is talkative.
