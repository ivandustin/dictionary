Etymology: The word "λαχώμεν" (lachómen) comes from the Greek verb "λαγχάνω" (lancháno), which means "to obtain by lot" or "to win by chance." It is derived from the noun "λαχείο" (lacheío), meaning "lot" or "prize."

Grammar: "Λαχώμεν" is the first person plural form of the verb "λαγχάνω" (lancháno) in the present tense. It is used to express the action of obtaining or winning something by chance or by drawing lots.
