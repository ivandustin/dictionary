Etymology: The word "λαβούσα" (lavousa) comes from the Greek verb "λαμβάνω" (lamvano), which means "to take" or "to receive".

Grammar: "Λαβούσα" is a feminine noun in the singular form. It belongs to the first declension in Greek grammar.

Description: "Λαβούσα" is a Greek word that means "receiver" or "recipient". It refers to a person or thing that receives or takes something.
