Etymology: The word "λαλεῖς" (laleis) comes from the Greek verb "λαλέω" (laleo), which means "to speak" or "to talk."

Grammar: "Λαλεῖς" is the second person singular form of the present indicative active of the verb "λαλέω." It is used when addressing someone directly and means "you speak" or "you talk."

Description: "Λαλεῖς" is a Greek word that refers to the act of speaking or talking. It is used to describe the action of someone expressing themselves verbally.
