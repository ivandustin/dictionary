Etymology: The word "λαμβανετε" comes from the Greek verb "λαμβάνω" (lambáno), which means "to take" or "to receive".

Grammar: "λαμβανετε" is the present tense form of the verb in the second person plural in Greek. It is used when addressing a group of people and means "you take" or "you receive".

Description: "λαμβανετε" is a verb in the Greek language that expresses the action of taking or receiving something. It is commonly used in everyday conversations and written texts.
