Etymology: The word "λαλώσιν" (lalōsin) comes from the Greek verb "λαλέω" (laleō), which means "to speak" or "to talk".

Grammar: "Λαλώσιν" is the present active participle of the verb "λαλέω" (laleō). It is in the third person plural form and is used to describe a group of people who are currently speaking or talking.

Description: "Λαλώσιν" refers to a group of people who are speaking or talking. It can be used to describe a conversation or a discussion taking place among multiple individuals.
