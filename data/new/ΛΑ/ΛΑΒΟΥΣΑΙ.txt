Etymology: The word "λαβουσαι" (lavousai) comes from the Greek verb "λαμβάνω" (lambanō), which means "to take" or "to receive."

Grammar: "Λαβουσαι" is the aorist tense, second person, plural form of the verb "λαμβάνω" (lambanō). It is used to indicate a completed action in the past.

Description: "Λαβουσαι" is a verb form that is used to address a group of people and instruct them to take or receive something. It can be translated as "you all took" or "you all received" in English.
