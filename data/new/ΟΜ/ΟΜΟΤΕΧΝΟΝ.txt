Etymology: The word "ομοτεχνον" comes from the Greek roots "ομος" meaning "same" and "τεχνη" meaning "art" or "craft". It literally translates to "same art" or "same craft".

Grammar: "Ομοτεχνον" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the singular form and belongs to the neuter gender.

Description: "Ομοτεχνον" refers to someone who practices the same craft or art as another person. It can also be used to describe a fellow craftsman or a colleague in the same profession.
