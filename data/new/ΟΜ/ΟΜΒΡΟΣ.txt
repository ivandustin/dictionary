Etymology: The word "όμβρος" comes from the Greek language. It is derived from the ancient Greek word "ὄμβρος" (ombros), meaning "rain."

Grammar: "Όμβρος" is a masculine noun in Greek. It belongs to the second declension and has the genitive form "όμβρου" (ombrou).

Description: "Όμβρος" refers to rain in the Greek language. It is used to describe the precipitation of water droplets from the atmosphere, typically in the form of drops falling from clouds.
