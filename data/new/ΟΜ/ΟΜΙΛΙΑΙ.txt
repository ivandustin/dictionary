Etymology: The word "ομιλιαί" comes from the Greek word "ομιλία" (omilia), which means "speech" or "conversation".

Grammar: "Ομιλιαί" is the plural form of the noun "ομιλία" (omilia), which is a feminine noun in Greek. It belongs to the second declension and follows the regular pattern for feminine nouns ending in -ία.

Description: "Ομιλιαί" refers to speeches or conversations. It can be used to describe formal or informal talks, discussions, or presentations.
