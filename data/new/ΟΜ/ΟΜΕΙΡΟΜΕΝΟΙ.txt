Etymology: The word "ομειρομενοι" comes from the Greek verb "ομειρομαι" which means "to lament" or "to mourn". It is derived from the noun "ομος" meaning "grief" or "sorrow".

Grammar: "Ομειρομενοι" is the plural form of the verb "ομειρομαι" in the present tense, middle voice, and indicative mood. It is used to describe a group of people who are currently lamenting or mourning.

Description: "Ομειρομενοι" refers to a collective group of individuals who are expressing grief, sorrow, or mourning. It implies that these individuals are actively engaged in lamenting or mourning a particular event or situation.
