Etymology: The word "ομοσημαίνει" comes from the Greek roots "ομος" meaning "same" and "σημαίνει" meaning "to signify". 

Grammar: "Ομοσημαίνει" is a verb in the Greek language. It is in the present tense, third person singular form. 

Description: "Ομοσημαίνει" means "it signifies" or "it means" in English. It is used to express that something has a particular meaning or signifies something.
