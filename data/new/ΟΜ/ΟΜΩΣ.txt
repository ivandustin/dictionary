Etymology: The word "ομως" comes from the Greek language. It is derived from the ancient Greek word "ὅμως" (homōs), which means "nevertheless" or "however".

Grammar: "Ομως" is an adverb in Greek. It is used to introduce a contrasting or concessive statement, indicating a contradiction or opposition to a previous statement.

Description: "Ομως" is commonly used in spoken and written Greek to express a shift in thought or to introduce a different perspective. It is often used to indicate a contradiction, disagreement, or unexpected outcome.
