Etymology: The word "ομοιωσω" comes from the Greek verb "ομοιόω" (omoióo), which means "to make similar" or "to resemble." It is derived from the adjective "ομοιος" (omios), meaning "similar" or "alike."

Grammar: "Ομοιώσω" is the first person singular future tense of the verb "ομοιόω" (omoióo) in Greek. It indicates an action that will happen in the future and is performed by the speaker.
