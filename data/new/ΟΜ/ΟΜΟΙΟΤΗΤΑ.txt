Etymology: The word "ομοιοτητα" comes from the Greek roots "ομοιος" (homios), meaning "similar" or "alike," and "τητα" (tita), which is a suffix indicating a state or quality. 

Grammar: "Ομοιοτητα" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the singular form and is feminine.

Description: "Ομοιοτητα" refers to the quality or state of being similar or alike. It is used to describe the resemblance or likeness between two or more things.
