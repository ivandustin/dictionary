Etymology: The word "ομοσημεία" comes from the Greek roots "ομος" meaning "same" and "σημείο" meaning "sign" or "mark". 

Grammar: "Ομοσημεία" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the plural form, with the singular form being "ομοσημείο". 

Description: "Ομοσημεία" refers to similarities or resemblances between two or more things. It can also mean signs or indications that suggest a connection or similarity between different elements.
