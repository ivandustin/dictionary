Etymology: The word "συνκαλεί" (synkalei) comes from the Greek roots "συν" (syn), meaning "together," and "καλεί" (kalei), meaning "to call." 

Grammar: "Συνκαλεί" is a verb in the present tense, third person singular form. It is derived from the verb "καλώ" (kalo), meaning "to call," with the prefix "συν" (syn) added to indicate the action of calling together.

Description: "Συνκαλεί" is used to describe the act of calling or summoning someone or something together. It implies bringing people or things together for a specific purpose or gathering.
