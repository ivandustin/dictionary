Etymology: The word "συνεργοί" (synergoi) comes from the Greek root words "συν" (syn), meaning "together," and "ἔργον" (ergon), meaning "work." Therefore, "συνεργοί" can be translated as "co-workers" or "collaborators."

Grammar: "Συνεργοί" is a plural noun in the nominative case. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their grammatical role in a sentence. The plural form of "συνεργοί" is used when referring to multiple co-workers or collaborators.

Description: "Συνεργοί" refers to a group of people who work together or collaborate on a project or task. It implies a sense of cooperation and teamwork. The word can be used in various contexts, such as in a professional setting or in a social or creative endeavor.
