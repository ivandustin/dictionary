Etymology: The word "συνστρατιωτήν" comes from the Greek roots "συν-" meaning "together" and "στρατιωτής" meaning "soldier". 

Grammar: "Συνστρατιωτήν" is a noun in the singular form. It is a masculine noun and belongs to the second declension in Greek grammar. 

Description: "Συνστρατιωτήν" is a Greek word that translates to "comrade" or "fellow soldier" in English. It refers to someone who serves in the military alongside others, sharing the same goals and experiences.
