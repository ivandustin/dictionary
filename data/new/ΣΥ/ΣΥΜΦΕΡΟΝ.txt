Etymology: The word "συμφερον" (symferon) comes from the Greek verb "συμφέρω" (symfero), which means "to be advantageous" or "to be profitable". 

Grammar: "Συμφερον" is a noun in the singular form. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their gender, number, and case. 

Description: "Συμφερον" refers to something that is advantageous, beneficial, or profitable. It can be used to describe a situation, a decision, or an action that brings benefits or advantages.
