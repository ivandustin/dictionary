In Greek grammar, nouns have different forms depending on their number (singular or plural), gender (masculine, feminine, or neuter), and case (nominative, genitive, accusative, etc.). The plural form "συναγωγών" indicates that there are multiple assemblies or gatherings.

Description: "Συναγωγών" refers to synagogues, which are Jewish places of worship and community gathering. Synagogues serve as centers for prayer, study, and social activities for Jewish communities.
