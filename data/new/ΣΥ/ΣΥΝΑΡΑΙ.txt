Etymology: The word "συναραι" (synarai) comes from the Greek roots "συν" (syn), meaning "together," and "αραι" (arai), meaning "to join." 

Grammar: "Συναραι" is a verb in the Greek language. It is in the present tense, indicative mood, and active voice. 

Description: "Συναραι" means "to join together" or "to unite" in Greek. It is used to describe the action of bringing separate entities or individuals together into a single unit or group.
