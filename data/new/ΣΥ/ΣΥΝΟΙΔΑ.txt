Etymology: The word "συνοιδα" (synoida) comes from the Greek roots "συν" (syn), meaning "together," and "οἶδα" (oida), meaning "to know." 

Grammar: "Συνοιδα" is a verb in the present tense, first person singular form. It is derived from the verb "οἶδα" (oida), which means "to know." 

Description: "Συνοιδα" is used to express the idea of knowing or being aware of something together with someone else. It implies a shared knowledge or understanding.
