Etymology: The word "συντυχείν" (syntuchein) comes from the Greek roots "συν" (syn), meaning "together," and "τυχείν" (tychein), meaning "to happen" or "to occur." 

Grammar: "Συντυχείν" is a verb in the Greek language. It is in the present tense, active voice, and infinitive form. 

Description: "Συντυχείν" means "to meet" or "to encounter" in Greek. It refers to the act of coming across or running into someone or something by chance or accident.
