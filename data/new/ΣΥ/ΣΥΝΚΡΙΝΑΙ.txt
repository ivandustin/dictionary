Etymology: The word "συνκριναι" (synkrinai) comes from the Greek roots "συν" (syn), meaning "together," and "κριναι" (krinai), meaning "to judge" or "to separate." 

Grammar: "Συνκριναι" is a verb in the present tense, active voice, and indicative mood. It is the third person singular form of the verb. 

Description: "Συνκριναι" means "to compare" in Greek. It refers to the act of examining two or more things to determine their similarities and differences.
