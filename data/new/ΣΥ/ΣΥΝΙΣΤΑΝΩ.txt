- Συνιστάνω (synistáno) - I recommend/suggest
- Συνιστάνεις (synistáneis) - You recommend/suggest
- Συνιστάνει (synistánei) - He/she/it recommends/suggests

- Συνιστάνετε (synistánete) - You (plural) recommend/suggest

Description: "Συνιστάνω" is a Greek verb that means "to recommend" or "to suggest." It is used when someone advises or proposes something to another person. It can be used in various contexts, such as recommending a book, suggesting a course of action, or endorsing a product.
