Grammar: "συνειχετο" is the third person singular, imperfect tense form of the verb "συνέχω" (synecho) in the Greek language. The imperfect tense indicates an ongoing or continuous action in the past.

Description: "συνειχετο" can be translated as "he/she/it was holding together" or "he/she/it was containing" in English. It describes an action that was happening continuously in the past.
