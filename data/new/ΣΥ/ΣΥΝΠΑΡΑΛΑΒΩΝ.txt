Etymology: The word "συνπαραλαβών" comes from the Greek roots "συν-" meaning "together" and "παραλαβή" meaning "reception" or "acceptance". 

Grammar: "Συνπαραλαβών" is a noun in the masculine gender, singular number, and nominative case. 

Description: "Συνπαραλαβών" refers to someone who participates or takes part in receiving or accepting something together with others.
