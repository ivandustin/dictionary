Etymology: The word "συνεδρίου" comes from the Greek roots "συν" (syn), meaning "together," and "ἑδρίον" (hedrion), meaning "seat" or "assembly." 

Grammar: "Συνεδρίου" is a noun in the genitive case, singular number, and masculine gender. It is used to indicate possession or association.

Description: "Συνεδρίου" refers to a meeting, conference, or assembly. It can also be used to describe a council or a gathering of people for a specific purpose, such as a legislative body or a committee.
