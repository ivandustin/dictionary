Etymology: The word "συνστρατιωτης" comes from the Greek roots "συν" (syn), meaning "together" or "with," and "στρατιωτης" (stratiotes), meaning "soldier." 

Grammar: "Συνστρατιωτης" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the masculine gender and singular number. 

Description: "Συνστρατιωτης" refers to a fellow soldier or comrade in arms. It is used to describe someone who serves in the military alongside others.
