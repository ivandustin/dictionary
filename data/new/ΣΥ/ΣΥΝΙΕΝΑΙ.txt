Etymology: The word "συνιεναι" (synienai) comes from the Greek roots "συν" (syn), meaning "together," and "ιεναι" (ienai), meaning "to go." Therefore, the literal meaning of "συνιεναι" is "to go together."

Grammar: "Συνιεναι" is a verb in the Greek language. It belongs to the present tense, indicative mood, and active voice. It is conjugated as follows:

- Singular: συνίεναι (synienai)
- Plural: συνίεναν (synienan)

Description: "Συνιεναι" is a word that expresses the action of going together or accompanying someone. It can also be used metaphorically to describe the act of understanding or comprehending something together with someone else.
