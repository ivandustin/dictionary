Grammar: "Συνσχηματίζεσθε" is a verb in the present tense, middle voice, second person plural form. It is derived from the verb "συνσχηματίζω" (synschēmatizō), which means "to conform oneself" or "to be conformed."

Description: "Συνσχηματίζεσθε" is used to express the idea of conforming oneself to something or someone. It implies adapting or adjusting one's behavior, thoughts, or appearance to fit a particular standard or expectation.
