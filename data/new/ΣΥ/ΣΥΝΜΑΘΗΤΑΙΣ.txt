Etymology: The word "συνμαθηταίς" comes from the Greek roots "συν" (syn), meaning "together," and "μαθηταίς" (mathētais), meaning "students." 

Grammar: "Συνμαθηταίς" is a noun in the plural form, used to refer to a group of students who are studying together or are classmates.

Description: "Συνμαθηταίς" is a Greek word that specifically refers to fellow students or classmates. It emphasizes the shared experience of learning and studying together.
