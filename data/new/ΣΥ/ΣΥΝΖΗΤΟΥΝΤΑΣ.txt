Etymology: The word "συνζητούντας" comes from the Greek verb "συζητώ" (synzito), which means "to discuss" or "to debate." The suffix "-ούντας" (-ountas) is added to the verb stem to indicate the present participle form, which in this case means "discussing" or "debating."

Grammar: "Συνζητούντας" is the present participle form of the verb "συζητώ" (synzito) in Greek. It is used to describe an ongoing action of discussing or debating in the present tense. In Greek grammar, the present participle can function as an adjective or as part of a verb phrase.

Description: "Συνζητούντας" is a Greek word that means "discussing" or "debating." It is used to describe the action of engaging in a conversation or argument with others to exchange ideas, opinions, or information.
