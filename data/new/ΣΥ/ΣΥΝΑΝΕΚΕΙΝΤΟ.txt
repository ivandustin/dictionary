Etymology: The word "συνανεκειντο" comes from the Greek roots "συν" (syn), meaning "together," and "ανεκειντο" (anekheinto), meaning "to lie down." 

Grammar: "Συνανεκειντο" is a verb in the past tense, middle voice, and indicative mood. It is the third person plural form of the verb "συνανακειμαι" (synanakeimai), which means "to lie down together" or "to recline together."

Description: "Συνανεκειντο" describes an action of multiple individuals lying down or reclining together. It can be used in various contexts, such as describing people sitting together at a table or reclining together on a couch.
