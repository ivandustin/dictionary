Etymology: The word "συνανακειμένους" comes from the Greek roots "συν" (syn), meaning "together," and "ανακείμενος" (anakeimenos), meaning "lying down." 

Grammar: "Συνανακειμένους" is a Greek adjective in the masculine accusative plural form. It is used to describe multiple people who are lying down together.

Description: "Συνανακειμένους" is a word that describes a group of people who are lying down together. It can be used to refer to people who are reclining at a table, such as during a meal or a social gathering.
