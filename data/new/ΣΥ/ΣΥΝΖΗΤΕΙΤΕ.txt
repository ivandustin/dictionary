Grammar: "Συνζητείτε" is the second person plural form of the present tense in Greek. It is used when addressing a group of people and means "you discuss" or "you are discussing." The verb is conjugated in the present tense, indicative mood, and active voice.

Description: "Συνζητείτε" is a verb that describes the action of engaging in a discussion or conversation with others. It implies a collaborative or interactive exchange of ideas, opinions, or information.
