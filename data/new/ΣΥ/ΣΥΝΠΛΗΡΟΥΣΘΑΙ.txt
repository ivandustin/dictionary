Etymology: The word "συνπληρούσθαι" comes from the Greek roots "συν-" meaning "together" and "πληροῦν" meaning "to fill". 

Grammar: "Συνπληρούσθαι" is a verb in the present tense, passive voice, and infinitive mood. It is formed by adding the suffix "-ούσθαι" to the stem "συνπληρο-". 

Description: "Συνπληρούσθαι" means "to be filled together" or "to be completed together". It implies the action of filling or completing something in collaboration or cooperation with others.
