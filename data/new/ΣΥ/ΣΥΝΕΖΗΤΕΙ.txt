Grammar: "Συνεζητεί" is the third person singular form of the present tense of the verb "συνεζητώ" in Greek. It is used to describe an action that is happening in the present and is performed by a third person.

Description: "Συνεζητεί" means "he/she/it discusses" or "he/she/it debates" in English. It is used to express the act of engaging in a conversation or debate with others, seeking to exchange ideas and opinions.
