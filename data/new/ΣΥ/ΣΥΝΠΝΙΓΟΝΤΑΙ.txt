Etymology: The word "συνπνευγονται" (synpneugontai) comes from the Greek roots "συν" (syn), meaning "together," and "πνευγω" (pneugo), meaning "to breathe." 

Grammar: "Συνπνευγονται" is a verb in the present tense, third person plural form. It is derived from the verb "πνευγω" (pneugo), which means "to breathe." The prefix "συν" (syn) adds the meaning of "together," indicating that multiple individuals are breathing together.
