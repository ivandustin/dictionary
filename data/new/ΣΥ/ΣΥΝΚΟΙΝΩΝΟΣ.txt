Etymology: The word "συνκοινωνος" (synkoinonos) comes from the Greek roots "συν" (syn), meaning "together," and "κοινωνος" (koinonos), meaning "partner" or "sharer." 

Grammar: "Συνκοινωνος" is a noun in the masculine gender. It is in the singular form. 

Description: "Συνκοινωνος" refers to someone who shares or participates in something together with others. It can be used to describe a partner, a collaborator, or a co-worker.
