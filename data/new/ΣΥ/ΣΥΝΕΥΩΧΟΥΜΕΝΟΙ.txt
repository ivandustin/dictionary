Etymology: The word "συνευχούμενοι" comes from the Greek verb "συνεύχομαι" (syneúchomai), which means "to pray together" or "to offer joint prayers." It is derived from the prefix "συν-" (syn-), meaning "together," and the verb "εύχομαι" (éuchomai), meaning "to pray" or "to wish."

Grammar: "Συνευχούμενοι" is the present participle form of the verb "συνεύχομαι" in the masculine plural nominative case. It is used to describe a group of people who are currently praying together or offering joint prayers.

Description: "Συνευχούμενοι" refers to a group of individuals who are engaged in the act of praying together. It implies a collective or communal aspect of prayer, where multiple people come together to offer their prayers or supplications.
