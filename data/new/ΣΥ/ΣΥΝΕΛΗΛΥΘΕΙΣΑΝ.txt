Grammar: "Συνεληλυθεισαν" is the third person plural form of the verb "συνέρχομαι" in the aorist tense. The aorist tense in Greek is used to express a completed action in the past. The suffix "-σαν" (-san) indicates the third person plural.

Description: "Συνεληλυθεισαν" is a Greek word that means "they came together" or "they gathered". It describes a group of people or things coming together in a specific location or for a specific purpose.
