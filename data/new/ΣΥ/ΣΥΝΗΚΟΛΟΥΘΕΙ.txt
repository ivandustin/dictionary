Etymology: The word "συνηκολουθεί" comes from the Greek roots "συν-" meaning "together" and "ακολουθώ" meaning "to follow". 

Grammar: "Συνηκολουθεί" is a verb in the present tense, third person singular form. It is derived from the verb "ακολουθώ" (to follow) with the prefix "συν-" (together). 

Description: "Συνηκολουθεί" means "he/she/it follows along" or "he/she/it accompanies" in Greek. It is used to describe the action of someone or something following or accompanying another person or thing.
