Grammar: "Συνηγείρεν" is the third person singular, imperfect tense form of the verb "συνηγείρω" (synēgeirō) in the Greek language. The imperfect tense indicates an ongoing or incomplete action in the past.

Description: "Συνηγείρεν" is a verb that means "he/she/it was raising up" or "he/she/it was gathering together" in English. It describes an action of bringing things or people together or lifting them up in the past.
