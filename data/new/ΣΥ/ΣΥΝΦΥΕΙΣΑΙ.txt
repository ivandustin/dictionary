Etymology: The word "συνφυείσαι" comes from the Greek roots "συν-" meaning "together" and "φύω" meaning "to grow". 

Grammar: "συνφυείσαι" is a verb in the present tense, third person, plural form. It is derived from the verb "συνφύω" which means "to grow together" or "to coexist".

Description: "συνφυείσαι" is used to describe the action of multiple things or entities growing or existing together. It implies a sense of harmony or unity in growth.
