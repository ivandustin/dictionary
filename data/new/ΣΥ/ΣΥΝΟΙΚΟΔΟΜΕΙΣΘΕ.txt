Etymology: The word "συνοικοδομείσθε" comes from the Greek roots "συν-" meaning "together" and "οἰκοδομέω" meaning "to build". 

Grammar: "Συνοικοδομείσθε" is a verb in the present tense, passive voice, second person plural form. It is derived from the verb "οἰκοδομέω" (to build) with the prefix "συν-" (together).

Description: "Συνοικοδομείσθε" is a Greek verb that means "you are being built together" or "you are building together". It implies a collaborative effort in constructing or developing something.
