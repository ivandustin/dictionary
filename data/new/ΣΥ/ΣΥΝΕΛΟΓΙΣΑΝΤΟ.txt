Etymology: The word "συνελογισαντο" comes from the Greek verb "συνελογίζομαι" (syne-logízomai), which is a compound word formed by the prefix "συν-" (syn-), meaning "together," and the verb "λογίζομαι" (logízomai), meaning "to reason" or "to calculate."

Grammar: "Συνελογισαντο" is the third person plural, aorist middle indicative form of the verb "συνελογίζομαι" in Greek. The aorist tense indicates a completed action in the past, the middle voice indicates that the subject is performing the action on themselves, and the indicative mood indicates a statement of fact.

Description: "Συνελογισαντο" means "they reasoned together" or "they calculated together" in English. It implies that a group of people engaged in a process of logical thinking or coming to a conclusion collectively.
