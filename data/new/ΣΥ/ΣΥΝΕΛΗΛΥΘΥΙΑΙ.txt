Etymology: The word "συνεληλυθυίαι" comes from the Greek roots "συν" (syn), meaning "together," and "ἐληλυθύῳ" (elēlythuō), meaning "I have come." 

Grammar: "Συνεληλυθυίαι" is a noun in the plural form. It belongs to the third declension in Greek grammar.

Description: "Συνεληλυθυίαι" is a Greek word that translates to "meetings" or "gatherings" in English. It refers to the act of coming together or assembling for a specific purpose.
