Etymology: The word "συνπνείω" (synpneiō) comes from the Greek roots "συν" (syn), meaning "together," and "πνείω" (pneiō), meaning "to breathe." 

Grammar: "Συνπνείω" is a verb in the Greek language. It belongs to the first conjugation class and is conjugated in the present tense as follows: εγώ συνπνέω, εσύ συνπνέεις, αυτός/αυτή/αυτό συνπνέει, εμείς συνπνέουμε, εσείς συνπνέετε, αυτοί/αυτές συνπνέουν.

Description: "Συνπνείω" is a Greek verb that means "to breathe together" or "to breathe in unison." It implies a collective or synchronized action of breathing.
