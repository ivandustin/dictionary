Etymology: The word "συνκοινωνούς" (synkoinonous) is derived from the Greek words "συν" (syn), meaning "together," and "κοινωνός" (koinonos), meaning "partner" or "sharer." 

Grammar: "Συνκοινωνούς" is a noun in the plural form, used to refer to multiple individuals who are partners or sharers in something.

Description: "Συνκοινωνούς" is a Greek word that describes a group of people who share or participate in something together. It implies a sense of collaboration, cooperation, and mutual involvement.
