Etymology: The word "συνκοινωνησητε" comes from the Greek roots "συν" (syn), meaning "together," and "κοινωνέω" (koinonéo), meaning "to communicate." 

Grammar: "Συνκοινωνησητε" is a verb in the second person plural form of the aorist tense in Greek. It is used to address a group of people and means "you all communicated together" or "you all have communicated together."

Description: "Συνκοινωνησητε" is a Greek verb that emphasizes the act of communicating together or sharing information with others. It implies a sense of collaboration and cooperation in the process of communication.
