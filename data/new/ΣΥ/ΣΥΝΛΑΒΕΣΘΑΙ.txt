Etymology: The word "συνλαβέσθαι" comes from the Greek roots "συν-" meaning "together" and "λαμβάνω" meaning "to take". 

Grammar: "Συνλαβέσθαι" is a verb in the Greek language. It is in the passive voice and in the aorist tense. 

Description: "Συνλαβέσθαι" means "to be taken together" or "to be captured". It is used to describe the act of being caught or captured by someone or something.
