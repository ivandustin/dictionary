Etymology: The word "συνμετοχοι" comes from the Greek roots "συν" (syn), meaning "together," and "μετοχοι" (metokhoi), meaning "participants" or "sharers." 

Grammar: "Συνμετοχοι" is a noun in the plural form. In Greek grammar, nouns have different forms depending on their gender, number, and case. 

Description: "Συνμετοχοι" refers to a group of people who participate or share something together. It can be used to describe individuals who are jointly involved in an activity, project, or event.
