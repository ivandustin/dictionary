Etymology: The word "συνμετοχα" (synmetocha) comes from the Greek roots "συν" (syn), meaning "together," and "μετοχή" (metochi), meaning "participation" or "sharing." 

Grammar: "Συνμετοχα" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the plural form and belongs to the neuter gender. 

Description: "Συνμετοχα" refers to a group of people who participate or share something together. It can be used to describe a collective participation or sharing of an activity, responsibility, or experience.
