Grammar: "Συνεχεον" is a present participle in the Greek language. It is formed by adding the suffix "-ον" (-on) to the present stem of the verb "συνεχέω" (synecheo). The present participle is used to describe ongoing or continuous actions.

Description: "Συνεχεον" is used to describe something that is continuously or constantly happening or being held together. It implies a sense of continuity and uninterrupted connection.
