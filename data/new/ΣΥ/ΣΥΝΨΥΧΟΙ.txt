Etymology: The word "συνψυχοι" comes from the Greek roots "συν" (syn), meaning "together," and "ψυχή" (psyche), meaning "soul" or "spirit." 

Grammar: "Συνψυχοι" is a noun in the plural form. It is the nominative case of the word, which means it is used as the subject of a sentence or clause.

Description: "Συνψυχοι" refers to a group of people who share a deep emotional or spiritual connection. It implies a sense of unity and harmony among individuals who are closely connected on a soulful level.
