Etymology: The word "συνλαλούντες" comes from the Greek roots "συν-" meaning "together" and "λαλούντες" meaning "speaking". 

Grammar: "Συνλαλούντες" is a plural noun in the present active participle form of the verb "λαλώ" (to speak) in Greek. It is used to describe a group of people who are speaking together or having a conversation.

Description: "Συνλαλούντες" refers to individuals who are engaged in a conversation or speaking together. It can be used to describe a group of people who are discussing a topic, having a dialogue, or communicating with each other verbally.
