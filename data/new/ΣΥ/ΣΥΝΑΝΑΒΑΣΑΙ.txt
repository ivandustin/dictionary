Etymology: The word "συναναβασαί" comes from the Greek roots "συν" (syn), meaning "together," and "αναβαίνω" (anabaino), meaning "to ascend" or "to go up." 

Grammar: "Συναναβασαί" is a verb in the Greek language. It is in the present tense, third person plural form. 

Description: The word "συναναβασαί" describes the action of multiple individuals ascending or going up together.
