Etymology: The word "συνεσταυρώμαι" comes from the Greek roots "συν-" (syn-), meaning "together," and "σταυρός" (stauros), meaning "cross." 

Grammar: "Συνεσταυρώμαι" is a verb in the Greek language. It is in the present tense, passive voice, and indicative mood. 

Description: "Συνεσταυρώμαι" means "I am crucified together with" or "I am co-crucified." It is used to express the idea of being crucified alongside someone else or being united in suffering or sacrifice.
