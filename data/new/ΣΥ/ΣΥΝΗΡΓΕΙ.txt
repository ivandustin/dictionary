Etymology: The word "συνηργεί" comes from the Greek roots "συν-" meaning "together" and "ἔργον" meaning "work". 

Grammar: "Συνηργεί" is a verb in the present tense, third person singular form. It is derived from the verb "συνεργώ" which means "to work together" or "to cooperate". 

Description: "Συνηργεί" is used to describe the action of working together or cooperating with someone or something. It implies a joint effort or collaboration towards a common goal.
