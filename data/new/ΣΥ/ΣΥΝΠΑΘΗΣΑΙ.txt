Etymology: The word "συνπαθεί" comes from the Greek roots "συν-" meaning "together" and "πάθος" meaning "suffering" or "passion". It can be translated as "to suffer together" or "to share in suffering".

Grammar: "Συνπαθεί" is a verb in the present tense, third person singular form. It is derived from the verb "συνπαθώ" which means "to sympathize" or "to share in someone's suffering or emotions". It is conjugated in the present tense as follows: εγώ συνπαθώ, εσύ συνπαθείς, αυτός/αυτή/αυτό συνπαθεί, εμείς συνπαθούμε, εσείς συνπαθείτε, αυτοί/αυτές συνπαθούν.

Description: "Συνπαθεί" is a Greek verb that expresses the act of sharing in someone's suffering or emotions, showing empathy or sympathy towards them. It implies a sense of understanding and compassion for the feelings and experiences of others.
