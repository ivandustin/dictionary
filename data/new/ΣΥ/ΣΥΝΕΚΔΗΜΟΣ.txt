Etymology: The word "συνεκδημος" comes from the Greek roots "συν" (syn), meaning "together," and "εκδημος" (ekdemos), meaning "resident" or "domiciled." 

Grammar: "Συνεκδημος" is a noun in the masculine gender. In Greek grammar, it belongs to the second declension. 

Description: "Συνεκδημος" refers to someone who is a fellow resident or cohabitant. It is used to describe someone who lives in the same place or community as another person.
