Etymology: The word "συνκεχυμένη" comes from the Greek roots "συν" (syn), meaning "together," and "κεχυμένη" (kechymeni), meaning "mixed." 

Grammar: "Συνκεχυμένη" is the feminine form of the adjective in Greek. It agrees in gender, number, and case with the noun it modifies. 

Description: "Συνκεχυμένη" is an adjective in Greek that translates to "mixed" or "confused" in English. It is used to describe something that is composed of different elements or is in a state of disorder or disarray.
