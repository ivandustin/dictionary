Etymology: The word "συνχαιρω" (synchairō) comes from the Greek roots "συν" (syn), meaning "together," and "χαιρω" (chairō), meaning "to rejoice." 

Grammar: "Συνχαιρω" is a verb in the Greek language. It is conjugated in various forms to express different tenses, moods, and persons. 

Description: "Συνχαιρω" is a Greek word that means "to rejoice together" or "to be glad with someone." It conveys the idea of sharing joy or expressing happiness in the company of others.
