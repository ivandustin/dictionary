Etymology: The word "συνπαραλαβοντες" comes from the Greek roots "συν" (syn), meaning "together," and "παραλαμβάνω" (paralambano), meaning "to receive." 

Grammar: "Συνπαραλαβόντες" is a verb form in the present active participle tense in Greek. It is the plural form of the verb "συνπαραλαμβάνω" (synparalambano), which means "to receive together" or "to take along with."

Description: "Συνπαραλαβόντες" is used to describe a group of people who are receiving or taking something together. It implies a collective action or participation in receiving or taking something.
