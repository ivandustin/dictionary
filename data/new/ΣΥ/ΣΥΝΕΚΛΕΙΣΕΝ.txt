Etymology: The word "συνεκλείσεν" comes from the Greek roots "συν" (syn), meaning "together," and "εκλείσεν" (ekleisen), meaning "to close." 

Grammar: "Συνεκλείσεν" is a verb in the aorist tense, third person singular form. It is derived from the verb "συνκλείω" (synkleio), which means "to close together" or "to enclose."

Description: "Συνεκλείσεν" is used to describe an action of closing or enclosing something together. It implies that multiple objects or entities are being closed or enclosed simultaneously.
