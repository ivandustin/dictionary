Etymology: The word "συνκαλεσμένος" (synkalesmenos) comes from the Greek roots "συν" (syn), meaning "together," and "καλεσμένος" (kalesmenos), meaning "called." 

Grammar: "Συνκαλεσμένος" is a masculine noun in the singular form. It is derived from the verb "καλώ" (kalo), meaning "to call," and the prefix "συν" (syn), which indicates a sense of togetherness or collaboration. 

Description: "Συνκαλεσμένος" is used to describe someone who has been called or invited to an event or gathering. It implies that the person has been summoned to join others in a specific activity or occasion.
