Etymology: The word "συνκεκερασμένους" comes from the Greek roots "συν" (syn), meaning "together," and "κεκερασμένος" (kekerasmenos), meaning "mixed" or "blended."

Grammar: "Συνκεκερασμένους" is the plural form of the adjective "συνκεκερασμένος" in Greek. It agrees with a plural noun in gender, number, and case.

Description: "Συνκεκερασμένους" describes something or someone that is mixed or blended together. It can be used to describe a physical mixture or a metaphorical blending of different elements or qualities.
