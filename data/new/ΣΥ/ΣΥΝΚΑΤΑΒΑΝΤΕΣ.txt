Etymology: The word "συνκαταβαντες" is derived from the Greek words "συν" (syn), meaning "together," and "καταβαίνω" (katabaino), meaning "to go down." 

Grammar: "Συνκαταβαντες" is a Greek noun in the plural form. It is a compound word formed by combining the prefix "συν" (syn) with the verb "καταβαίνω" (katabaino). 

Description: "Συνκαταβαντες" refers to a group of people who go down or descend together. The exact context and usage of this word may vary depending on the specific context in which it is used.
