Etymology: The word "συνζητούντων" comes from the Greek verb "συνζητώ" (synzito), which means "to discuss" or "to debate." It is derived from the combination of the prefix "συν-" (syn-), meaning "together," and the verb "ζητώ" (zito), meaning "to seek" or "to inquire."

Grammar: "Συνζητούντων" is the present participle form of the verb "συνζητώ" (synzito) in the third person plural. It is used to describe an ongoing action of multiple people discussing or debating something.

Description: "Συνζητούντων" is a Greek word that refers to the act of multiple individuals engaging in a discussion or debate. It implies a collaborative and interactive process of exchanging ideas and opinions.
