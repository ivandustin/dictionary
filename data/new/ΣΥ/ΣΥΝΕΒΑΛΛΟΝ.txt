Etymology: The word "συνεβαλλον" comes from the Greek roots "συν" (syn), meaning "together," and "βαλλω" (ballo), meaning "to throw." 

Grammar: "Συνεβαλλον" is the past tense form of the Greek verb "συνβαλλω" (synballo), which means "to throw together" or "to contribute." It is conjugated in the first person plural.

Description: "Συνεβαλλον" is used to describe an action of throwing or contributing something together with others. It implies a collaborative effort or joint contribution towards a common goal.
