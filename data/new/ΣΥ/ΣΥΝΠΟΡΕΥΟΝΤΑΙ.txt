Etymology: The word "συνπορεύονται" comes from the Greek roots "συν-" meaning "together" and "πορεύομαι" meaning "to go". 

Grammar: "Συνπορεύονται" is a verb in the present tense, third person plural form. It is derived from the verb "πορεύομαι" (to go) with the prefix "συν-" (together). 

Description: "Συνπορεύονται" means "they go together" or "they accompany each other" in English. It is used to describe a group of people or things that are moving or progressing together.
