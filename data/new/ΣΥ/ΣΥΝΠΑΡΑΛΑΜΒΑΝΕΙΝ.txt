Etymology: The word "συνπαραλαμβάνειν" comes from the Greek roots "συν-" meaning "together" and "παραλαμβάνειν" meaning "to receive". 

Grammar: "Συνπαραλαμβάνειν" is a verb in the Greek language. It is in the present tense, active voice, and infinitive mood. 

Description: "Συνπαραλαμβάνειν" means "to receive together" or "to take along with". It implies the action of receiving or taking something or someone in the company of others.
