Etymology: The word "συναυξάνεσθαι" comes from the Greek roots "συν-" meaning "together" and "αὔξησις" meaning "growth". 

Grammar: "συναυξάνεσθαι" is a verb in the present middle/passive infinitive form. It is derived from the verb "αυξάνω" (to grow) with the prefix "συν-" added to indicate the action of growing together or increasing in conjunction with something or someone else.

Description: "συναυξάνεσθαι" is a Greek verb that means "to grow together" or "to increase in conjunction with". It implies a mutual or simultaneous growth or increase. It can be used in various contexts, such as describing the growth of two or more things happening at the same time or the increase of something in relation to another factor.
