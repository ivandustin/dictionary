Etymology: The word "συνπεριλαμβάνων" comes from the Greek roots "συν-" meaning "together" and "περιλαμβάνω" meaning "to include". 

Grammar: "Συνπεριλαμβάνων" is a present participle in the masculine gender, singular number, and nominative case. It is derived from the verb "συνπεριλαμβάνω" which means "to include together".

Description: "Συνπεριλαμβάνων" is used to describe something or someone that is currently including or encompassing others or something else. It is often used in a context where multiple elements are being considered or taken into account together.
