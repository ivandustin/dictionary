Etymology: The word "συστρεφομένων" comes from the Greek verb "συστρέφω" (systréfo), which means "to turn together" or "to gather". The suffix "-μένων" (-menon) is a present participle ending, indicating continuous action.

Grammar: "Συστρεφομένων" is the genitive plural form of the present participle of the verb "συστρέφω". In Greek grammar, the genitive case is used to indicate possession or origin, among other functions. The plural form indicates that the noun it modifies refers to multiple objects.

Description: "Συστρεφομένων" can be translated as "of the turning/gathering". It is used to describe a group of objects or individuals that are currently in the process of turning or gathering together.
