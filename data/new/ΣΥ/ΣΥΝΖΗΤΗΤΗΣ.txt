Grammar: "Συνζητητής" is a noun in the masculine gender. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their grammatical case. For example, in the nominative case, "συνζητητής" is the subject of a sentence, while in the accusative case, it is the direct object. 

Description: "Συνζητητής" refers to a person who engages in discussions or debates, someone who actively seeks to exchange ideas and opinions with others. It can be translated as "debater," "discussant," or "interlocutor" in English.
