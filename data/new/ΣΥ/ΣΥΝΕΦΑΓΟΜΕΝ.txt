Etymology: The word "συνεφαγομεν" comes from the Greek roots "συν" (syn), meaning "together," and "εφαγομεν" (ephagomen), meaning "we ate." 

Grammar: "Συνεφαγομεν" is a verb in the first person plural form of the aorist tense in the Greek language. It indicates that a group of people ate together in the past.

Description: "Συνεφαγομεν" is a word used to describe the action of eating together. It implies a sense of communal or shared eating experience.
