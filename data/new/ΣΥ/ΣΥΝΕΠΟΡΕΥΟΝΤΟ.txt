Etymology: The word "συνεπορευόντο" comes from the Greek roots "συν-" meaning "together" and "ἐπορεύοντο" meaning "they were going". 

Grammar: "Συνεπορευόντο" is a verb in the past tense, third person plural form. It is derived from the verb "πορεύομαι" meaning "to go" and the prefix "συν-" which indicates a collective action.

Description: "Συνεπορευόντο" is a Greek word that translates to "they were going together" in English. It describes a group of people or things moving or traveling together.
