Etymology: The word "συμφυτοι" (symphytoi) comes from the Greek roots "συν" (syn), meaning "together," and "φυτόν" (phyton), meaning "plant." 

Grammar: "Συμφυτοι" is a plural noun in the Greek language. It belongs to the third declension and is of masculine gender. 

Description: "Συμφυτοι" refers to plants that grow together or are closely associated with each other. It can also be used metaphorically to describe people or things that are closely connected or intertwined.
