Etymology: The word "συνεπεμψαμεν" comes from the Greek language. It is a compound word consisting of the prefix "συν-" (meaning "together" or "with") and the verb "ἐπέμπω" (meaning "to send"). 

Grammar: "συνεπεμψαμεν" is the first person plural aorist active indicative form of the verb "ἐπέμπω" in Greek. It indicates that "we sent together" or "we sent with someone/something".

Description: "συνεπεμψαμεν" is a verb form used in Greek to express the action of sending something or someone together with someone else. It implies a joint effort or collaboration in the act of sending.
