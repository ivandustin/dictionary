Etymology: The word "συμφορον" comes from the Greek roots "συν" (syn), meaning "together," and "φορά" (phorá), meaning "to bear" or "to carry." 

Grammar: "Συμφορον" is a noun in the singular form. In Greek grammar, it belongs to the third declension, which means it can have different endings depending on its grammatical case (nominative, genitive, dative, accusative, or vocative).

Description: "Συμφορον" is a Greek word that can be translated as "misfortune," "calamity," or "disaster." It refers to an unfortunate event or a difficult situation that brings suffering or distress.
