Etymology: The word "συνεπέθεντο" (synepeθento) comes from the Greek roots "συν" (syn), meaning "together," and "ἐπιθέντο" (epiθento), meaning "to place upon." 

Grammar: "Συνεπέθεντο" is a verb in the aorist tense, third person plural form. It is derived from the verb "συνεπιτίθημι" (synepitithemi), which means "to place upon together" or "to impose together."

Description: "Συνεπέθεντο" is used to describe the action of placing or imposing something together or collectively. It implies a joint effort or collaboration in placing or imposing something.
