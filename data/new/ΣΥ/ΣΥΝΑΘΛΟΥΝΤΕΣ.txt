Etymology: The word "συναθλούντες" comes from the Greek roots "συν" (syn), meaning "together," and "αθλούντες" (athlountes), meaning "competing" or "contending." 

Grammar: "Συναθλούντες" is a verb form in the present active participle tense in Greek. It is derived from the verb "αθλέω" (athleo), meaning "to compete" or "to contend," and the prefix "συν" (syn), which indicates a collective or joint action. 

Description: "Συναθλούντες" is used to describe a group of people who are competing or contending together. It implies a sense of collaboration and teamwork in the context of athletic or competitive activities.
