Grammar: "Συνεχομένη" is the feminine form of the present participle of the verb "συνεχώ" (synechó). In Greek grammar, the present participle is used to form continuous or ongoing actions. It agrees in gender, number, and case with the noun it modifies.

Description: "Συνεχομένη" is an adjective that describes something or someone that is continuously or constantly happening or being done. It implies a state of continuity or uninterrupted action.
