Grammar: "Συνεπνιγον" is the past tense form of the verb "συνεπνίγω" in the first person plural. It indicates that the action of strangling or suffocating was performed by a group of people in the past.

Description: "Συνεπνιγον" describes an action where a group of people strangled or suffocated someone or something. It implies a collective effort in performing this act.
