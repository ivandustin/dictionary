Etymology: The word "συνπαροντες" (symparontes) comes from the Greek roots "συν" (syn), meaning "together," and "παροντες" (parontes), meaning "present." 

Grammar: "Συνπαροντες" is a plural noun in the nominative case. It is formed by adding the suffix "-οντες" to the verb "παρων" (paron), which means "to be present." The prefix "συν" indicates that the action is done together or in association with others.

Description: "Συνπαροντες" refers to a group of people who are present or attending an event or gathering together. It can also be used metaphorically to describe people who are united or working together towards a common goal.
