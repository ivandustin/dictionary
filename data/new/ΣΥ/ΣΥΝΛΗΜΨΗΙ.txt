Etymology: The word "συνλημψηι" (synlēmpsēi) comes from the Greek roots "συν" (syn), meaning "together," and "λημψη" (lēmpsē), meaning "to take." 

Grammar: "Συνλημψηι" is a verb in the present tense, third person singular form. It is derived from the verb "συνλαμβάνω" (synlambanō), which means "to comprehend" or "to understand."

Description: "Συνλημψηι" refers to the act of comprehending or understanding something together with someone else. It implies a shared understanding or agreement between two or more individuals.
