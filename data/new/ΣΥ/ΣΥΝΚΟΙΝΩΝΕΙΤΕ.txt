Etymology: The word "συνκοινωνείτε" (synkoinōneite) is derived from the Greek words "συν" (syn), meaning "together," and "κοινωνείτε" (koinōneite), meaning "you communicate." 

Grammar: "Συνκοινωνείτε" is the second person plural form of the verb "συνκοινωνώ" (synkoinōnō), which means "to communicate together" or "to share." It is used to address a group of people and indicates that they are communicating or sharing something collectively.

Description: "Συνκοινωνείτε" is a Greek verb that emphasizes the idea of communication or sharing among a group of people. It implies a sense of collaboration, cooperation, or mutual involvement in a particular activity or endeavor.
