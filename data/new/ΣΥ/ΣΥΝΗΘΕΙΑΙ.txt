Etymology: The word "συνηθειαι" (synētheiai) comes from the Greek root words "συν" (syn), meaning "together," and "ηθος" (ēthos), meaning "habit" or "custom." 

Grammar: "Συνηθειαι" is a noun in the plural form. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their gender, number, and case. 

Description: "Συνηθειαι" refers to habits or customs that are practiced or followed by a group of people. It can also imply a sense of familiarity or regularity in behavior.
