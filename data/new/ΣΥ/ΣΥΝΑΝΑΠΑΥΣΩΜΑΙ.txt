Etymology: The word "συναναπαύσωμαι" comes from the Greek roots "συν" (syn), meaning "together," and "ἀναπαύω" (anapauo), meaning "to rest." 

Grammar: "Συναναπαύσωμαι" is a verb in the Greek language. It is in the present tense, first person singular form. The verb is conjugated from the root "συναναπαύομαι" (synanapauomai), which means "to rest together."

Description: "Συναναπαύσωμαι" is a Greek verb that expresses the action of resting or taking a break together with someone or something else. It implies a shared or collective rest.
