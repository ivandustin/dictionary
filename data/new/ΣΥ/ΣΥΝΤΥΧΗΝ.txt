Etymology: The word "συντυχήν" comes from the Greek roots "συν-" meaning "together" and "τυχή" meaning "chance" or "luck". 

Grammar: "Συντυχήν" is an adverb in Greek. It is used to indicate that something happens by chance or unexpectedly. 

Description: "Συντυχήν" is a versatile word that can be used in various contexts. It is often used to express surprise or astonishment when something happens unexpectedly or by chance. It can also be used to describe a situation or event that occurs suddenly or without prior planning.
