Etymology: The word "συνοικούντες" comes from the Greek roots "συν-" meaning "together" and "οἰκοῦντες" meaning "living". 

Grammar: "Συνοικούντες" is a verb form in the present active participle tense in Greek. It is derived from the verb "οἰκέω" meaning "to dwell" or "to live". The present active participle form indicates that the action of living together is happening in the present.
