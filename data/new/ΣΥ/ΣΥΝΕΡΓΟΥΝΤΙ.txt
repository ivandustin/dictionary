Etymology: The word "συνεργούντι" comes from the Greek verb "συνεργώ" (synergo), which means "to work together" or "to cooperate." It is derived from the combination of the prefix "συν-" (syn-), meaning "together," and the verb "εργάζομαι" (ergazomai), meaning "to work."

Grammar: "Συνεργούντι" is the present participle form of the verb "συνεργώ" (synergo) in the third person singular. In Greek grammar, the present participle is used to describe ongoing actions or states. It can function as an adjective or as part of a verb phrase.

Description: "Συνεργούντι" describes someone or something that is currently working together or cooperating with others. It implies active participation and collaboration.
