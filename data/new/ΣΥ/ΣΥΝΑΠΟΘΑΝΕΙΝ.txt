Etymology: The word "συναποθανείν" comes from the Greek roots "συν" (syn), meaning "together," and "αποθανείν" (apothanein), meaning "to die." 

Grammar: "Συναποθανείν" is a verb in the Greek language. It is in the present tense, active voice, and infinitive mood. 

Description: "Συναποθανείν" means "to die together" or "to die at the same time." It refers to the act of multiple individuals dying simultaneously or in close succession.
