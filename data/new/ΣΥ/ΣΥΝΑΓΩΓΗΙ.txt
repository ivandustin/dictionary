Grammar: "Συναγωγή" is a feminine noun in the Greek language. It belongs to the second declension and its genitive form is "συναγωγής" (synagogis). In the plural form, it becomes "συναγωγές" (synagoges).

Description: "Συναγωγή" refers to a place of assembly or gathering, specifically used to describe a Jewish place of worship and community center. It is commonly known as a synagogue.
