Etymology: The word "υγιαινουση" (pronounced: yee-ge-ne-see) comes from the Greek verb "υγιαινω" (pronounced: yee-ge-ne-o), which means "to be healthy" or "to be in good health."

Grammar: "Υγιαινουση" is a feminine noun in the singular form. It is in the present participle form, which indicates an ongoing action or state of being. In this case, it refers to the act of being healthy or in good health.

Description: "Υγιαινουση" is used to describe a person, thing, or situation that is in a state of good health. It can also be used metaphorically to describe something that is functioning well or is in a positive condition.
