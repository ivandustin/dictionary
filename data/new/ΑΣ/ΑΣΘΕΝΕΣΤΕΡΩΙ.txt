Grammar: "ασθενεστερωι" is the dative singular form of the adjective "ασθενής" (asthenés), which means "weaker" or "sicker". In Greek grammar, the dative case is used to indicate the indirect object or the recipient of an action.

Description: "ασθενεστερωι" is used to describe something or someone that is weaker or sicker compared to something else. It is commonly used in comparative sentences to express a degree of weakness or sickness.
