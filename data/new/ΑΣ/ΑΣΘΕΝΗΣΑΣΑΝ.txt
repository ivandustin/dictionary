The aorist tense indicates a completed action in the past, and the active voice indicates that the subject of the verb performs the action.

Description: "ασθενησασαν" describes a feminine subject that has completed the action of being weak or sick in the past. It can be used as an adjective to modify a noun or as a verb in a sentence.
