The suffix "-αντες" (-antes) is added to the verb stem to form the past participle, indicating that the action of missing or failing to hit the target has been completed.

Grammar: "Αστοχησαντες" is the past participle form of the verb "αστοχέω" (astochéo) in the third person plural. It is used to describe a group of people who have missed or failed to hit the target in the past.
