Etymology: The word "ασθενεστερα" comes from the Greek word "ασθενής" (asthenís), which means "weak" or "sick". The suffix "-τερα" (-tera) is added to indicate the comparative form, meaning "weaker" or "more sick".

Grammar: "ασθενεστερα" is a feminine adjective in the nominative singular form. In Greek, adjectives agree in gender, number, and case with the noun they modify. It can be declined to match the gender, number, and case of the noun it describes.

Description: "ασθενεστερα" is used to describe something or someone that is weaker or more sick compared to something else. It is often used in medical or health-related contexts to indicate a comparative degree of illness or weakness.
