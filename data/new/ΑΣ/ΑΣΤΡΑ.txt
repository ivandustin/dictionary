Etymology: The word "αστρα" (astra) comes from the Greek word "ἀστήρ" (astḗr), meaning "star". 

Grammar: "Αστρα" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the plural form and belongs to the neuter gender. 

Description: "Αστρα" refers to stars in Greek. It can be used to describe the celestial bodies that are visible in the night sky. Stars are luminous spheres of plasma held together by gravity and emit light and heat. They play a significant role in astronomy and have been studied for centuries to understand the universe.
