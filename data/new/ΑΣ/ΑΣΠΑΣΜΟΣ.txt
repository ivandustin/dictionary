Etymology: The word "ασπασμος" (as-pas-mos) comes from the Greek verb "ασπαζομαι" (as-pa-zo-mai), which means "to greet" or "to welcome." 

Grammar: "Ασπασμος" is a masculine noun in the singular form. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their gender, number, and case. 

Description: "Ασπασμος" refers to the act of greeting or welcoming someone. It can also be used to describe a salutation or a gesture of goodwill.
