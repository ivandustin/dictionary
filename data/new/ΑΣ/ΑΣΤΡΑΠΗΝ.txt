Etymology: The word "αστραπην" (astrapēn) comes from the Greek word "αστραπή" (astrapē), meaning "lightning". 

Grammar: "Αστραπην" is a noun in the accusative case, singular form. 

Description: "Αστραπην" refers to a lightning bolt or a flash of lightning. It is a sudden and powerful discharge of electricity in the atmosphere, often accompanied by thunder. Lightning is a natural phenomenon that occurs during thunderstorms and is caused by the buildup and release of electrical energy in the atmosphere.
