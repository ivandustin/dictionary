Etymology: The word "ασμένως" (asmenos) comes from the Greek verb "ασμένω" (asmeno), which means "to rejoice" or "to be glad". It is derived from the adjective "ασμένος" (asmenos), meaning "joyful" or "cheerful".

Grammar: "Ασμένως" is an adverb in Greek. It is used to modify verbs, adjectives, or other adverbs to indicate the manner in which an action is performed or a quality is expressed. In this case, it describes the way in which someone is rejoicing or being glad.
