Etymology: The word "ασπαζεσθαι" (as-pa-zes-thai) comes from the Ancient Greek verb "ἀσπάζομαι" (as-pa-zo-mai), which means "to greet" or "to welcome." It is derived from the noun "ἀσπάσμα" (as-pas-ma), meaning "a greeting" or "a salutation."

The infinitive form is used to express the action of the verb without specifying a subject or tense.

Description: "Ασπάζεσθαι" is a Greek verb that means "to greet" or "to welcome." It is used to express the act of showing friendly or polite behavior towards someone upon meeting or parting.
