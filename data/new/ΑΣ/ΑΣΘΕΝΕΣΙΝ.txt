Etymology: The word "ασθενεσίν" comes from the Greek word "ασθένεια" (astheneia), which means "weakness" or "illness". The suffix "-σίν" is added to indicate a state or condition.

Grammar: "Ασθενεσίν" is a noun in the accusative case, singular number, and neuter gender.

Description: "Ασθενεσίν" refers to the state or condition of being weak or ill. It can be used to describe physical or mental weakness or illness.
