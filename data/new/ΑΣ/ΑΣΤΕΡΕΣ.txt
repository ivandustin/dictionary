Etymology: The word "αστερες" (asteres) comes from the Greek word "ἀστήρ" (astḗr), meaning "star".

Grammar: "Αστερες" is a noun in the plural form. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their gender, number, and case. "Αστερες" is the plural form of the noun "αστήρ" (astḗr), which means "star" in Greek.

Description: "Αστερες" refers to stars in the Greek language. Stars are celestial bodies that emit light and are visible in the night sky.
