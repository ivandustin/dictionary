Etymology: The word "ασπίλοι" (aspiloi) comes from the Greek word "ασπίς" (aspis), meaning "shield". It is derived from the Proto-Indo-European root *h₂ep-, meaning "to protect".

Grammar: "Ασπίλοι" is a plural noun in Greek. It belongs to the third declension and is of masculine gender. In the singular form, it is "ασπίλος" (aspilos).

Description: "Ασπίλοι" refers to shields in Greek. It is used to describe the protective equipment used in warfare or combat. Shields were commonly used in ancient times to defend against attacks and provide protection to the person holding it.
