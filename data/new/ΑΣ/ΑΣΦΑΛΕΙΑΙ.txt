Etymology: The word "ασφαλεία" (asphaleía) comes from the Greek word "ἀσφάλεια" (aspháleia), which means "safety" or "security". It is derived from the adjective "ἀσφαλής" (asphalḗs), meaning "secure" or "safe".

Grammar: "Ασφαλεία" is a feminine noun in Greek. It is in the nominative case and singular form. In the plural form, it becomes "ασφαλείες" (asphaleíes).

Description: "Ασφαλεία" refers to the concept of safety or security. It can be used to describe the state of being protected from harm, danger, or risk. In various contexts, it can refer to physical safety, such as in the case of security measures or precautions, or it can have a broader meaning, encompassing aspects such as financial security or personal well-being.
