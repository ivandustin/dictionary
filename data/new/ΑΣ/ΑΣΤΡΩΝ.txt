Etymology: The word "αστρων" (astron) comes from the Greek word "ἄστρον" (astron), meaning "star". 

Grammar: "Αστρων" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the nominative case and is plural. 

Description: "Αστρων" refers to stars in Greek. It can be used to describe celestial bodies that emit light and are visible in the night sky.
