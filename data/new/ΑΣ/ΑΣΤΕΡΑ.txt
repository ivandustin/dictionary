Etymology: The word "αστερα" (astera) comes from the Greek word "ἀστήρ" (astḗr), meaning "star".

Grammar: "Αστερα" (astera) is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the singular form.

Description: "Αστερα" (astera) refers to a star in Greek. It is used to describe celestial bodies that emit light and are visible in the night sky.
