Etymology: The word "βοηθειτε" (boētheite) comes from the Greek verb "βοηθέω" (boētheō), which means "to help" or "to assist". 

Grammar: "Βοηθείτε" is the second person plural imperative form of the verb "βοηθέω". It is used to give a command or request to a group of people, telling them to help or assist.

Description: "Βοηθείτε" is a Greek word that is used to ask for help or assistance. It is commonly used in situations where someone needs support or aid from others.
