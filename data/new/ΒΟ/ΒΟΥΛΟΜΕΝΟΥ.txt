Etymology: The word "βουλομένου" (vouloménou) comes from the Greek verb "βούλομαι" (voúlomai), which means "to want" or "to wish."

Grammar: "Βουλομένου" is the genitive singular form of the verb "βούλομαι" (voúlomai). In Greek grammar, the genitive case is used to indicate possession, origin, or relationship.
