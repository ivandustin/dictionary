Grammar: πορειαις is a noun in the dative plural form. In Greek grammar, the dative case is used to indicate the indirect object of a verb or the object of certain prepositions.

Description: πορειαις refers to journeys or processions, specifically in the context of ancient Greek culture and religion. It can also be used metaphorically to refer to a series of events or a progression towards a goal.
