Word: πομασιν

The word πομασιν (pomasin) is a Greek word that is derived from the verb πομάζω (pomazo), which means "to graze" or "to feed". The suffix -σιν (-sin) is a common ending in Greek that is used to form nouns.

πομασιν is a noun in the singular form. It is a feminine noun, as indicated by the ending -ιν (-in). In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their gender, number, and case.

πομασιν refers to a pasture or a grazing land where animals are fed. It can also refer to the act of grazing or feeding animals.
