Word: ποτοις

Etymology:
The word ποτοις (potis) is a Greek word that means "drinks" or "beverages". It is derived from the Greek word πίνω (pino) which means "to drink".

Grammar:
ποτοις is a masculine dative plural noun in Greek. It is often used to refer to drinks or beverages in general.

Description:
ποτοις is a common word in Greek that is used to refer to drinks or beverages. It can be used in a variety of contexts, such as ordering drinks at a restaurant or discussing different types of beverages. In Greek culture, drinking is often a social activity, and the word ποτοις reflects this by being a plural noun that is often used in the context of groups of people sharing drinks together.
