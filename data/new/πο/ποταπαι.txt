The word "ποταπαι" (potapai) is a Greek adjective that means "of what kind" or "what sort of." It is derived from the combination of the words "ποτε" (pote), meaning "ever" or "at any time," and "τις" (tis), meaning "someone" or "something." 

In terms of grammar, "ποταπαι" is a feminine plural form of the adjective, used to modify a feminine plural noun. 

Overall, the word is used to express uncertainty or curiosity about the nature or identity of something or someone.
