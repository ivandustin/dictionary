The word "πορευθεισαι" is a Greek verb in the aorist tense, passive voice, and feminine gender. It is the third person singular form of the verb "πορεύομαι" (poreúomai), which means "to go, to travel, to journey." The suffix "-θεισαι" indicates that the subject of the verb is a group of women. Therefore, "πορευθεισαι" can be translated as "they (a group of women) went/traveled/journeyed."
