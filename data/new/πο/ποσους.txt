Word: ποσούς

Etymology: ποσούς is a Greek word that comes from the root word πόσος (posos), which means "how much" or "how many."

Grammar: ποσούς is the accusative plural form of the adjective ποσός (posos), which means "how much" or "how many." It is used to modify a noun and indicate quantity.

Description: ποσούς is commonly used in Greek to ask questions about quantity, such as "how many" or "how much." It can also be used to describe a specific quantity, such as "a certain number" or "a certain amount." In addition, it can be used to express a comparison, such as "more than" or "less than."
