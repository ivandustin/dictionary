The word "πορευομενοις" is a Greek verb in the present participle form, which means "going" or "traveling". It is derived from the root verb "πορεύομαι" (poreúomai), which means "to go" or "to travel". In terms of grammar, it is a present participle in the dative plural form, which indicates that the action of going or traveling is being done by multiple people.
