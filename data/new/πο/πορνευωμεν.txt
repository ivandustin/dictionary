The word "πορνευωμεν" (porneuomen) is a Greek verb that means "to engage in sexual immorality" or "to commit fornication." It is derived from the noun "πορνεία" (porneia), which means "prostitution" or "fornication." In terms of grammar, "πορνευωμεν" is a present active indicative verb in the first person plural form, meaning "we are committing fornication."
