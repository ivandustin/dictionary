Word: ποιησει

Etymology: ποιησει is a Greek verb that comes from the root word ποιέω (poiéo), which means "to make" or "to create."

Grammar: ποιησει is a third-person singular future active indicative form of the verb ποιέω. It means "he/she/it will make" or "he/she/it will create."

Description: ποιησει is a verb that expresses the action of making or creating something in the future. It can be used in various contexts, such as in art, literature, music, and even in everyday life. For example, one can use ποιησει to describe the act of writing a poem, composing a song, or building a house.
