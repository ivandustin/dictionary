Word: πορευσονται

Etymology:
The word πορευσονται (poreusontai) is a verb form in the Greek language. It is derived from the root word πορεύομαι (poreuomai), which means "to go, to travel". The suffix -σονται (-sontai) is a third-person plural future indicative ending, indicating that the action of the verb will take place in the future.

Grammar:
πορευσονται is a third-person plural future indicative form of the verb πορεύομαι (poreuomai). In Greek grammar, the future indicative tense is used to describe actions that will take place in the future. The third-person plural form indicates that the subject of the verb is a group of people.

Description:
πορευσονται can be translated to English as "they will go" or "they will travel". It is a common verb form in Greek and is used to describe a group of people moving from one place to another.
