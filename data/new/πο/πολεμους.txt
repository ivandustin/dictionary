Word: πολεμους

Etymology: πολεμους is a Greek word that comes from the root word πόλεμος (polemos), which means "war" or "battle". 

Grammar: πολεμους is the plural form of πόλεμος (polemos), which means "wars" or "battles". It is a masculine noun in the second declension.

Description: πολεμους refers to multiple wars or battles. It can be used in historical or contemporary contexts to describe conflicts between nations, groups, or individuals. The word carries a connotation of violence and struggle.
