The word "πολλαις" (pollais) is a feminine form of the Greek adjective "πολύς" (polys), which means "many" or "much". It is often used to describe a large quantity or number of something. In terms of grammar, "πολλαις" is in the dative case, indicating that it is the indirect object of a verb or the object of a preposition. It can also be used as an adjective to modify a noun.
