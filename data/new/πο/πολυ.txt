Word: πολυ

Etymology: πολυ is a Greek word that means "many" or "much". It is derived from the Greek word "polus", which has the same meaning.

Grammar: πολυ is an adjective in Greek and can be used to describe a noun. It has three genders (masculine, feminine, and neuter) and three cases (nominative, genitive, and accusative).

Description: πολυ is a versatile word that can be used in many contexts. It can be used to describe a large quantity of something, such as "πολλά βιβλία" (many books). It can also be used to describe something that is complex or difficult, such as "πολύπλοκος" (complicated).
