Word: ποιωμεν

Etymology:
The word ποιωμεν (poiomen) is derived from the Greek verb ποιέω (poieo), which means "to make" or "to do". It is a present tense, first-person plural form of the verb.

Grammar:
ποιωμεν is a present tense, first-person plural form of the Greek verb ποιέω (poieo). It is used to indicate that the subject (we) are currently making or doing something.

Description:
