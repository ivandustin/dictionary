Word: ποιησομεν

Etymology:
The word ποιησομεν (poiēsomen) is a Greek verb in the future tense, which means "we will make" or "we will do". It is derived from the root word ποιέω (poiéō), which means "to make" or "to do".

Grammar:
ποιησομεν is a first-person plural future active indicative verb in Greek. It is formed by adding the future tense suffix -σο- to the stem ποιη-, followed by the first-person plural ending -μεν.

Description:
ποιησομεν is a versatile verb that can be used in a variety of contexts. It can refer to physical actions, such as making or building something, or to more abstract actions, such as creating or producing something. In Greek literature, it is often used in the context of poetry or artistic creation.
