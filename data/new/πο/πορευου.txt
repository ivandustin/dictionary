The word "πορευου" (poreuou) is a verb in the Greek language, specifically in the present active imperative form. It is derived from the root word "πορεύομαι" (poreuomai), which means "to go, to travel, to journey." 

In terms of grammar, "πορευου" is a second person singular command, meaning it is used to give a direct order or instruction to someone. It can be translated to "go" or "be on your way."

Overall, the word "πορευου" is a simple and straightforward command in Greek, commonly used in everyday conversation.
