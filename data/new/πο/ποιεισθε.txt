Word: ποιεισθε

The word ποιεισθε is derived from the Greek verb ποιέω (poiéō), which means "to make" or "to do". The suffix -σθε (-sthe) is the second person plural ending in the present tense, indicating that the action is being performed by a group of people.

ποιεισθε is a second person plural present passive indicative verb in Greek. It is formed by adding the suffix -σθε to the stem ποιει- (poi-ei-), which is the present tense stem of the verb ποιέω.
