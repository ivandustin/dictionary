The word "ποιμανει" (poimanei) is a verb in the Greek language. It is derived from the noun "ποιμήν" (poimēn), which means "shepherd". The verb "ποιμανει" means "to shepherd" or "to tend to a flock of sheep". 

In terms of grammar, "ποιμανει" is a present tense verb in the third person singular form. It can be conjugated in different tenses and persons to indicate different actions and subjects. 

Overall, "ποιμανει" is a word that is commonly used in the context of agriculture and animal husbandry, particularly in reference to the care and management of sheep.
