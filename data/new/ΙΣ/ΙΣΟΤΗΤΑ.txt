Grammar: "Ισοτητα" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the singular form and belongs to the feminine gender. 

Description: "Ισοτητα" refers to the concept of equality or the state of being equal. It is used to describe a condition where two or more things have the same value, status, or importance. This term is commonly used in various fields, including mathematics, social sciences, and philosophy, to discuss fairness, justice, and balance.
