Etymology: The word "ισχυραί" (ischyrai) comes from the Greek adjective "ισχυρός" (ischyros), which means "strong" or "powerful". 

Grammar: "Ισχυραί" is the feminine plural form of the adjective "ισχυρός" (ischyros). It is used to describe plural feminine nouns.
