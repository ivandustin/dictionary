Etymology: The word "ισχυος" (ischyos) comes from the Greek word "ισχυς" (ischys), meaning "strength" or "power".

Grammar: "Ισχυος" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the genitive case, which is used to indicate possession or relationship.

Description: "Ισχυος" refers to the strength or power of someone or something. It can be used to describe physical strength, as well as mental or emotional strength. It is often used in contexts related to sports, warfare, or any situation where strength is a key factor.
