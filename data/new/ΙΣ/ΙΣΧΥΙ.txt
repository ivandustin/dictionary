Etymology: The word "ισχυί" (ischyi) comes from the Greek verb "ισχύω" (ischyō), which means "to be strong" or "to have power".

Grammar: "Ισχυί" (ischyi) is a noun in the singular form. It belongs to the third declension in Greek grammar.

Description: "Ισχυί" (ischyi) refers to strength, power, or might. It can be used to describe physical strength, as well as mental or emotional strength. It can also be used metaphorically to indicate influence or authority.
