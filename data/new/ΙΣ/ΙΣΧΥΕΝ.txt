Etymology: The word "ισχυεν" (ischyen) comes from the Greek verb "ισχυω" (ischyo), which means "to be strong" or "to have power".

Grammar: "Ισχυεν" is the third person singular form of the verb "ισχυω" in the present tense. It is used to describe an action or state of being that is currently happening or is generally true.

Description: "Ισχυεν" can be translated as "he/she/it is strong" or "he/she/it has power". It is used to indicate the strength or power of a person or thing.
