Etymology: The word "ισχυρών" (ischyron) comes from the Greek verb "ισχύω" (ischyo), which means "to be strong" or "to have power."

Grammar: "Ισχυρών" is the plural form of the adjective "ισχυρός" (ischyros), which means "strong" or "powerful." It is used to describe multiple strong or powerful things or individuals.

Description: "Ισχυρών" is an adjective that is commonly used in Greek to describe objects, people, or situations that possess strength or power. It can be used in various contexts, such as describing physical strength, mental fortitude, or influential individuals.
