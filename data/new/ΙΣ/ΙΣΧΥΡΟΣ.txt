Etymology: The word "ισχυρός" (ischyros) comes from the Greek root "ισχύς" (ischys), meaning "strength" or "power".

Grammar: "Ισχυρός" is an adjective in the Greek language. It is used to describe something or someone as strong, powerful, or robust.

Description: "Ισχυρός" is a versatile word that can be used to describe various aspects of strength. It can refer to physical strength, such as a strong person or a powerful force. It can also describe mental or emotional strength, such as a strong character or a resilient individual. Additionally, it can be used to describe something as sturdy or durable. Overall, "ισχυρός" is a word that conveys a sense of power and strength in different contexts.
