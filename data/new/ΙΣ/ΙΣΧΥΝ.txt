Etymology: The word "ισχυν" (ischyn) comes from the Greek verb "ισχύω" (ischýo), which means "to be strong" or "to have power".

Grammar: "Ισχυν" is a noun in the singular form. It belongs to the third declension in Greek grammar.

Description: "Ισχυν" refers to strength, power, or force. It can be used to describe physical strength, as well as the power or influence that someone or something possesses.
