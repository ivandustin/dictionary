Etymology: The word "ισχυον" (ischyon) comes from the Greek verb "ισχυω" (ischyo), which means "to be strong" or "to have power".

Grammar: "Ισχυον" is a noun in the neuter gender, singular number, and nominative case.

Description: "Ισχυον" refers to something that is strong, powerful, or influential. It can be used to describe a person, an object, or an abstract concept.
