Etymology: The word "ισχυροτεροι" comes from the Greek word "ισχυρός" (ischyros), meaning "strong" or "powerful". The suffix "-τεροι" (-teroi) is added to indicate the comparative form, meaning "stronger" or "more powerful".

Grammar: "Ισχυροτεροι" is the plural form of the adjective "ισχυρός" (ischyros) in the comparative degree. It is used to describe multiple nouns that are stronger or more powerful compared to others.

Description: "Ισχυροτεροι" is an adjective in Greek that describes a group of nouns as being stronger or more powerful. It can be used to compare the strength or power of different individuals, objects, or concepts.
