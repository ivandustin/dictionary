Grammar: "Ισοψυχον" is a noun in the neuter gender. It is in the singular form.

Description: "Ισοψυχον" refers to someone or something that has an equal or balanced mind or soul. It can describe a person who remains calm and composed in all situations, or it can refer to a state of equilibrium or balance in one's mental or emotional state.
