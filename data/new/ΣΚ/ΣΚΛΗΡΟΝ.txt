Etymology: The word "σκληρον" (sklēron) comes from the Greek adjective "σκληρός" (sklēros), meaning "hard" or "difficult".

Grammar: In Greek, "σκληρον" (sklēron) is a neuter noun in the singular form.

Description: "Σκληρον" (sklēron) refers to something that is hard or difficult. It can be used to describe physical objects that are tough or resistant, as well as abstract concepts or situations that are challenging or demanding.
