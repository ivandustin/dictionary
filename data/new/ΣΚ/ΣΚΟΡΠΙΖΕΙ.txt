Etymology: The word "σκορπίζει" comes from the Greek verb "σκορπίζω" (skorpízo), which means "to scatter" or "to disperse."

Grammar: "Σκορπίζει" is the third person singular form of the present tense of the verb "σκορπίζω" (skorpízo). It is used to describe an action being performed by a singular subject.

Description: "Σκορπίζει" is a Greek word that means "he/she/it scatters" or "he/she/it disperses." It is commonly used to describe the act of spreading or scattering something in various contexts.
