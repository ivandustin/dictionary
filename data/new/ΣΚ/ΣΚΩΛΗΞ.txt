Etymology: The word "σκωληκίς" (skolēkís) comes from the Ancient Greek word "σκώληξ" (skṓlēx), meaning "worm" or "maggot."

Grammar: In Greek, "σκωληκίς" is a feminine noun in the nominative case. It belongs to the second declension and has the plural form "σκωληκίδες" (skolēkídes).

Description: "Σκωληκίς" refers to a worm or a maggot. It is commonly used to describe various types of worms or larvae, both in scientific and everyday contexts.
