Etymology: The word "σκοτούς" (skotous) comes from the Greek language. It is derived from the noun "σκότος" (skotos), meaning "darkness" or "obscurity."

Grammar: "Σκοτούς" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the accusative case, singular number.

Description: "Σκοτούς" refers to "darkness" or "obscurity" in Greek. It is used to describe the absence of light or the state of being in the dark.
