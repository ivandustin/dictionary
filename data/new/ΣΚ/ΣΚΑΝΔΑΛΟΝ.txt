Etymology: The word "σκανδαλον" (skandalon) comes from the Greek language. It is derived from the verb "σκανδαλίζω" (skandalizo), which means "to cause offense" or "to stumble." 

Grammar: In Greek, "σκανδαλον" is a noun of neuter gender. It belongs to the second declension and has the nominative singular form "σκανδαλον."
