Etymology: The word "σκοτειν" (skotein) comes from the Greek word "σκοτεινός" (skoteinós), which means "dark" or "obscure".

Grammar: "Σκοτειν" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the singular form and belongs to the neuter gender.

Description: "Σκοτειν" refers to darkness or the state of being dark. It can be used to describe the absence of light or the lack of visibility.
