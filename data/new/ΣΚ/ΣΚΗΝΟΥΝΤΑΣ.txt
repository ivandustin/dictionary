Etymology: The word "σκηνουντας" (skēnountas) comes from the Greek verb "σκηνοῦντας" (skēnoûntas), which is the present active participle of the verb "σκηνόω" (skēnóō). 

Grammar: "Σκηνουντας" is the present active participle form of the verb "σκηνόω" in Greek. It is used to describe someone who is currently "setting up a tent" or "pitching a tent". 

Description: "Σκηνουντας" refers to the action of setting up a tent or pitching a tent. It is commonly used in the context of camping or outdoor activities where a temporary shelter is needed.
