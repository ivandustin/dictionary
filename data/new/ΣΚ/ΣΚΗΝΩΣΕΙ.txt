Etymology: The word "σκηνώσει" comes from the Greek verb "σκηνόω" (skēnóō), which means "to set up a tent" or "to stage a play." It is derived from the noun "σκηνή" (skēnē), meaning "tent" or "stage."

Grammar: "Σκηνώσει" is the third person singular future tense form of the verb "σκηνόω" (skēnóō). In Greek, verbs change their endings to indicate tense, person, and number. The future tense indicates an action that will happen in the future.

Description: "Σκηνώσει" is a verb form that describes an action of setting up a tent or staging a play in the future. It can be used to talk about someone's plans or intentions to create a theatrical performance or to physically set up a tent.
