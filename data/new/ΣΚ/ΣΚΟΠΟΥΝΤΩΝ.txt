Etymology: The word "σκοπούντων" comes from the Greek verb "σκοπέω" (skopéo), which means "to observe" or "to aim at". The suffix "-ούντων" is the present participle form of the verb, indicating ongoing action.

Grammar: "Σκοπούντων" is the present participle form of the verb "σκοπέω" in the third person plural. It is used to describe a group of people who are currently observing or aiming at something.

Description: "Σκοπούντων" can be translated as "observing" or "aiming at" in English. It is often used in contexts where a group of people is actively watching or focusing on a particular target or objective.
