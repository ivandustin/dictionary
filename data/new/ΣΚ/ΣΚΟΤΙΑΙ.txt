Etymology: The word "σκοτιαί" comes from the Greek word "σκότος" (skotos), meaning "darkness" or "shadow".

Grammar: "Σκοτιαί" is the plural form of the noun "σκοτιά" (skotia), which means "darkness" or "shadows" in Greek. It belongs to the feminine gender and is declined as a first-declension noun.

Description: "Σκοτιαί" refers to multiple instances of darkness or shadows. It can be used to describe the absence of light or the presence of shadows in a particular area or situation.
