Etymology: The word "ηυχοντο" (pronounced "eef-hon-to") comes from the Greek verb "εύχομαι" (pronounced "ef-ho-meh"), which means "to wish" or "to pray." 

Grammar: "ηυχοντο" is the third person plural form of the verb "εύχομαι" in the past tense. It is in the indicative mood and the active voice. 

Description: "ηυχοντο" is used to describe an action of wishing or praying that was performed by a group of people in the past. It indicates that the action was done by multiple individuals.
