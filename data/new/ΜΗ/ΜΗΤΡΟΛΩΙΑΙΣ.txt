It is a compound word derived from the Greek roots "μήτηρ" (mētēr), meaning "mother," and "λόγος" (lógos), meaning "word" or "study." In Greek, "μητρολωιαίς" refers to the study or analysis of the origins, history, and development of words and their meanings.

In terms of grammar, the Greek word "μητρολωιαίς" does not have a direct translation. However, it can be understood as a noun that describes the field of study related to etymology.
