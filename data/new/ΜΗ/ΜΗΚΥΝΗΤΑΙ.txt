μηκυνηταί (mēkynētai) is the plural form of the Greek word μηκυνητής (mēkynētēs), which means "lengthener" or "extender." It is derived from the verb μηκύνω (mēkynō), meaning "to lengthen" or "to extend." In Greek grammar, μηκυνηταί is used to refer to a specific group of people or objects that perform the action of lengthening or extending.
