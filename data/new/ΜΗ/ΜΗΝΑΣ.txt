Etymology: The word "μηνας" (mēnas) comes from the Ancient Greek word "μήν" (mēn), meaning "month."

Grammar: In Greek, "μηνας" (mēnas) is a masculine noun in the nominative case. It is the singular form of the word, and it means "month."
