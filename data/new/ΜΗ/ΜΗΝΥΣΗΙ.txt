Etymology: The word "μηνυσηι" comes from the Greek word "μηνυσηι" (mēnusēi), which means "to accuse" or "to denounce".

Grammar: "μηνυσηι" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the singular form.

Description: "μηνυσηι" refers to the act of accusing or denouncing someone. It is commonly used in legal contexts to describe the formal process of bringing charges against someone in a court of law.
