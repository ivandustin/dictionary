Word: αψηται

Grammar: αψηται is a plural noun in Greek. It is the nominative form of the word, which means it is used as the subject of a sentence.

Description: αψηται refers to uncooked or raw food. It can also be used metaphorically to describe something that is unfinished or incomplete. In Greek cuisine, αψηται is often used to refer to dishes that are not cooked, such as salads or dips.
