Etymology: The word "χειμώνος" (cheimónos) comes from the Greek word "χεῖμα" (cheîma), meaning "winter."

Grammar: In Greek, "χειμώνος" is a noun in the genitive case. It is the genitive form of the word "χειμών" (cheimón), which means "winter."

Description: "Χειμώνος" refers to the season of winter. It is used to describe the coldest and often harshest season of the year, characterized by low temperatures, shorter days, and often snow or freezing conditions.
