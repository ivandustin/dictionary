The word "εμμενει" (emmeni) is a verb in Greek that means "to remain" or "to stay". 

Etymology: The word comes from the Greek root "μένω" (meno) which means "to stay" or "to remain". The prefix "εμ-" (em-) is added for emphasis.

Grammar: "Εμμενει" is a third person singular present tense verb. It can be conjugated in different tenses and moods to match the subject and context of the sentence.

Description: The word "εμμενει" is often used to describe someone or something that remains in a particular place or situation despite challenges or difficulties. It can also be used in a more literal sense to describe physical objects that remain in a certain position or location.
