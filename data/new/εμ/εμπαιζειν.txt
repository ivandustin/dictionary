Word: εμπαιζειν

Etymology: The word εμπαιζειν comes from the Greek root words εν (en) meaning "in" and παιζειν (paizein) meaning "to play". Therefore, εμπαιζειν means "to play with/in".

Grammar: εμπαιζειν is a verb in the present tense, active voice, and infinitive form. It is often used in the context of teasing or mocking someone.

Description: εμπαιζειν is a Greek verb that is commonly used to describe the act of teasing or mocking someone in a playful or lighthearted manner. It can also be used to describe the act of playing with something or someone. The word is often used in informal settings and is commonly used among friends and family members.
