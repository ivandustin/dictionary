Word: εμωρανθησαν

Etymology: The word εμωρανθησαν comes from the Greek verb μωραίνω (moraino), which means "to make foolish" or "to become foolish". The verb is derived from the adjective μωρός (moros), which means "foolish" or "stupid".

Grammar: εμωρανθησαν is a past tense verb in the passive voice. It is the third person plural form of the verb μωραίνω (moraino).
