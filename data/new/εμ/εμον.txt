The word "εμον" (emon) is a Greek adjective that means "my" or "mine". It is derived from the Greek pronoun "εγώ" (ego), which means "I". In terms of grammar, "εμον" is a possessive adjective that agrees in gender, number, and case with the noun it modifies. For example, "το βιβλίο μου" (to vivlio mou) means "my book", where "μου" (mou) is the possessive form of "εμον" for the first person singular.
