Word: εμαθετε

Etymology: εμαθετε is a Greek verb that comes from the root word μανθάνω (manthano), which means "to learn" or "to understand."

Grammar: εμαθετε is the second person plural aorist indicative active form of the verb μανθάνω (manthano). In English, it translates to "you learned" or "you understood."

Description: εμαθετε is a verb that indicates the act of learning or understanding something. It is often used in the context of gaining knowledge or information through study or experience. The word can be used in a variety of settings, from academic learning to personal growth and development.
