Word: εμφανίζω (emfanízo)

Etymology: The word εμφανίζω comes from the Greek root words εμφάνιση (emfánisi) meaning "appearance" and φαίνω (fáino) meaning "to show". 

Grammar: εμφανίζω is a verb in the Greek language. It belongs to the first conjugation and is a regular verb. It is conjugated in all tenses and moods.

Description: εμφανίζω means "to appear" or "to show". It is often used to describe the act of making something visible or bringing something to light. It can also be used to describe the act of presenting oneself or making oneself known.
