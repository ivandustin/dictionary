Word: εμφανιζουσιν

The word εμφανιζουσιν (emphanizoosin) is a Greek verb in the present active indicative form. It is derived from the root word εμφανιζω (emphanizo), which means "to make visible" or "to reveal." The suffix -ουσιν (-oosin) indicates that the verb is in the third person plural form.

The word εμφανιζουσιν (emphanizoosin) is used in the New Testament of the Bible, specifically in the Gospel of John 14:21. It is translated as "they who reveal" or "they who make visible."
