The word "εμης" (emis) is a Greek possessive pronoun that means "ours" in English. 

Etymology: The word comes from the Greek root "emo-" meaning "mine" or "my own."

Grammar: "Εμης" is a first-person plural possessive pronoun in Greek. It is used to indicate that something belongs to "us" or is "ours."

Description: "Εμης" is a common word in Greek and is used in everyday conversation. It is often used to express ownership or possession of something. For example, "Αυτό το σπίτι είναι εμης" (This house is ours).
