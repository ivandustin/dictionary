The word "εμβαινοντος" (embainontos) is a Greek verb in the present participle form, which means "boarding" or "getting on board". 

Etymology: The word is derived from the Greek verb "εμβαίνω" (embaino), which means "to go on board" or "to embark". 

Grammar: "εμβαινοντος" is the present participle form of the verb "εμβαίνω" (embaino), which is used to describe an ongoing action of boarding or getting on board. It is in the masculine gender, singular number, and genitive case. 

Description: The word "εμβαινοντος" is commonly used in Greek literature and language to describe the act of boarding a ship, a vehicle, or any other means of transportation. It can also be used metaphorically to describe the act of joining or participating in a group or activity.
