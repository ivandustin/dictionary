Word: εμνημονευσεν

Etymology: The word εμνημονευσεν comes from the Greek root words εμνημόνευσε (emnēmóneuse) and μνημονεύω (mnēmoneúō), which mean "to remember" or "to recall."

Grammar: εμνημονευσεν is a verb in the aorist tense, third person singular form. In Greek, the aorist tense is used to describe a past action that is viewed as a single event, without any reference to its duration or repetition.

Description: εμνημονευσεν means "he/she/it remembered" in English. It is a common word in Greek literature and is often used to describe a character's recollection of a past event or memory. The word can also be used in a more general sense to describe the act of remembering or recalling something.
