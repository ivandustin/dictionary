The word "εμποριαν" (emporian) is a noun in the Greek language. 

Etymology: The word comes from the Greek verb "εμπορεύομαι" (emporévomai), which means "to trade" or "to do business". 

Grammar: "Εμποριαν" is a masculine noun in the singular form. It belongs to the second declension of Greek nouns and its genitive form is "εμποριανού" (emporianou). 

Description: The word "εμποριαν" refers to a person who is engaged in trade or commerce. It can also refer to a merchant or a trader. In modern Greek, the word is often used to describe a businessperson or an entrepreneur.
