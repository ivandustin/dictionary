Word: εμερισεν

Etymology: The word εμερισεν is derived from the Greek verb μερίζω (merizo), which means "to divide" or "to distribute". It is a past tense form of the verb, specifically the third person singular aorist indicative active.

Grammar: εμερισεν is a verb in the Greek language, specifically a past tense form of the verb μερίζω. It is in the third person singular aorist indicative active, indicating that the action of dividing or distributing was completed in the past by a single subject.

Description: εμερισεν is a Greek verb that means "he/she/it divided" or "he/she/it distributed". It is a past tense form of the verb μερίζω, which is commonly used in Greek to describe the act of dividing or distributing something among people or groups.
