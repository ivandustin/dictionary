Word: εμβαντα

Etymology: The word εμβαντα comes from the Greek verb εμβαίνω (emvaino), which means "to embark" or "to board". The suffix -τα (-ta) is a nominalizing suffix, which turns the verb into a noun.

Grammar: εμβαντα is a neuter noun in the singular form. It is the nominative case, which means it is the subject of a sentence.

Description: εμβαντα refers to the act of boarding or embarking on a ship or a vehicle. It can also refer to the cargo or the goods that are being loaded onto a ship or a vehicle. The word is commonly used in the context of transportation and shipping.
