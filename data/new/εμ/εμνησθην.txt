Word: εμνησθην

Etymology: εμνησθην is a Greek verb that comes from the root word μνημονεύω (mnēmoneuō), which means "to remember". The prefix ε- (e-) indicates the first person singular aorist passive form of the verb.

Grammar: εμνησθην is a first person singular aorist passive form of the Greek verb μνημονεύω (mnēmoneuō). In this form, the verb indicates that the subject (I) received the action of the verb (to be remembered) in the past.
