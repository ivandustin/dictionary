Word: εμερισθη

Etymology:
The word εμερισθη comes from the Greek verb μερίζω (merizo), which means "to divide" or "to distribute". The prefix ε- (e-) indicates the past tense, and the suffix -θη (-the) indicates the passive voice. Therefore, εμερισθη means "was divided" or "was distributed".

Grammar:
εμερισθη is a third-person singular past passive indicative form of the verb μερίζω (merizo). It is in the aorist tense, which indicates a completed action in the past. The passive voice indicates that the subject of the sentence (which is not specified in this form) received the action of the verb.
