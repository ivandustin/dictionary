The word "ωμεν" (omen) is a Greek verb in the present tense, first person plural form. It is derived from the root word "οίμοι" (oimoi), which is an interjection expressing sorrow or regret. In terms of grammar, "ωμεν" is a conjugated form of the verb "οίμοι" and is used to indicate present tense action by the speaker and others. It can be translated to mean "we are" or "we do".
