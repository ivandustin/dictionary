Etymology: The word "νοσσιάν" comes from the Greek word "νοσσός" (nossos), meaning "nestling" or "young bird". 

Grammar: "Νοσσιάν" is a noun in the singular form. It is a masculine noun in Greek.

Description: "Νοσσιάν" refers to a young bird that is still in the nest, or a nestling. It is commonly used to describe the offspring of birds.
