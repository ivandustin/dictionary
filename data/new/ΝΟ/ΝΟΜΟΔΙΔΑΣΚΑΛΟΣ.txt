Etymology: The word "νομοδιδασκαλος" comes from the Greek words "νομος" (nomos) meaning "law" and "διδασκαλος" (didaskalos) meaning "teacher". 

Grammar: "Νομοδιδασκαλος" is a noun in the masculine gender. It is in the singular form. 

Description: "Νομοδιδασκαλος" refers to a teacher or instructor of law. It is commonly used to describe someone who teaches or educates others in matters related to the law.
