Etymology: The word "νοτον" (noton) comes from the Greek word "νότος" (notos), which means "south wind". 

Grammar: "Νοτον" (noton) is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the accusative case and singular number. 

Description: "Νοτον" (noton) refers to the south wind. In Greek mythology, Notos was one of the four Anemoi (wind gods) and was associated with the south wind. The south wind was often considered warm and associated with summer.
