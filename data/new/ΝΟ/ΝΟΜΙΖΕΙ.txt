Etymology: The word "νομίζει" comes from the Greek verb "νομίζω" (nomízo), which means "to think" or "to believe".

Grammar: "Νομίζει" is the third person singular form of the present tense of the verb "νομίζω" (nomízo). It is used to express that someone or something thinks or believes.

Description: "Νομίζει" is a Greek word that can be translated as "he/she/it thinks" or "he/she/it believes". It is commonly used in everyday speech and writing to express someone's opinion or belief about something.
