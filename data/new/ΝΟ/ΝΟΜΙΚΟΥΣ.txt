Etymology: The word "νομικούς" comes from the Greek word "νομικός" (nomikos), which means "pertaining to law" or "legal".

Grammar: "Νομικούς" is the plural form of the Greek adjective "νομικός" (nomikos), which means "legal" or "related to law". It is used to describe multiple people or things that are connected to the field of law.

Description: "Νομικούς" refers to individuals who are involved in the legal profession or have knowledge and expertise in the field of law. It can be used to describe lawyers, legal scholars, judges, or anyone who works in the legal system.
