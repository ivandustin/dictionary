Etymology: The word "νομικών" (nomikón) comes from the Greek word "νόμος" (nómos), meaning "law" or "custom".

Grammar: "Νομικών" is the plural form of the adjective "νομικός" (nomikós), which means "legal" or "juridical". It agrees with a plural noun in gender, number, and case.

Description: "Νομικών" is used to describe something that is related to or associated with the field of law. It can refer to legal matters, legal professionals, or anything pertaining to the study or practice of law.
