Etymology: The word "νομικάς" (nomikas) comes from the Greek word "νόμος" (nomos), meaning "law" or "custom".

Grammar: "Νομικάς" is a masculine noun in the Greek language. It belongs to the second declension and is in the nominative case.

Description: "Νομικάς" is a Greek word that translates to "lawyer" or "legal expert" in English. It refers to a person who is trained and knowledgeable in the field of law and provides legal advice or representation to individuals or organizations.
