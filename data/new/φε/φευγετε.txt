Word: φευγετε

Etymology: φευγετε is a Greek verb that comes from the root word φεύγω (feúgo), which means "to flee" or "to escape."

Grammar: φευγετε is the second person plural imperative form of the verb φεύγω (feúgo). It is used to give a command or order to a group of people to flee or escape.

Description: φευγετε is a strong and urgent command in Greek, often used in emergency situations or when danger is imminent. It is a call to action, urging people to quickly and decisively leave a dangerous or threatening situation. The word carries a sense of urgency and importance, emphasizing the need for immediate action.
