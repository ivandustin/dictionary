Word: φεροντες

Etymology: φεροντες is a present active participle of the Greek verb φέρω (phero), which means "to carry" or "to bear". The word is derived from the Proto-Indo-European root *bʰer-.

Grammar: φεροντες is a masculine plural nominative form of the present active participle of φέρω. It is used to describe a group of people who are currently carrying or bearing something.

Description: φεροντες can be translated to "carrying" or "bearing" in English. It is often used in Greek literature and texts to describe a group of people who are carrying or bearing something, such as a burden or a weapon.
