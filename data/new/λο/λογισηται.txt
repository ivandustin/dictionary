The word "λογισηται" (logisetai) is a verb in the Greek language. 

Etymology: The word is derived from the root word "λογίζομαι" (logizomai), which means "to calculate" or "to reckon". 

Grammar: "λογισηται" is in the third person singular form of the present tense, indicative mood, and passive voice. 

Description: The word means "is calculated" or "is reckoned" in English. It is often used in mathematical or financial contexts to indicate a calculation or estimation.
