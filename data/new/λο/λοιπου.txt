The word "λοιπού" (loipou) is a Greek adjective that means "remaining" or "leftover". It is derived from the noun "λοιπός" (loipos), which means "the rest" or "the remaining". In terms of grammar, "λοιπού" is in the genitive case, which is used to indicate possession or relationship between nouns. 

Example sentence: Τα λοιπού προϊόντα θα διατηρηθούν στο ψυγείο. (Ta loipou proïonta tha diatirithoun sto psygeio.) - The remaining products will be stored in the refrigerator.
