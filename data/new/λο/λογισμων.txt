The word λογισμων (logismōn) is a Greek noun that can be translated to mean "calculations" or "reckonings." It is derived from the verb λογίζομαι (logizomai), which means "to calculate" or "to reckon." 

In terms of grammar, λογισμων is a masculine noun in the genitive case. It is the plural form of λογισμός (logismos), which is the singular form of the noun. 

In ancient Greek, λογισμων was often used in the context of accounting or bookkeeping, as well as in philosophical discussions about logic and reasoning. Today, it is still used in modern Greek to refer to calculations or computations.
