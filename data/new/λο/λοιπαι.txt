The word "λοιπαι" (loipai) is a plural noun in Greek that means "the rest" or "the remaining ones". It is derived from the adjective "λοιπός" (loipos), which means "remaining" or "leftover". In terms of grammar, it is a third declension noun, with the nominative plural form being "λοιπαί" (loipai). It can be used to refer to a group of people or things that are left over after others have been taken away or used up.
