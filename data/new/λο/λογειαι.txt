The word "λογειαι" (logeiai) is a plural noun in Greek that means "sayings" or "discourses". It is derived from the verb "λέγω" (lego), which means "to say" or "to speak". In terms of grammar, it is a third declension noun, with the nominative plural form being "λογειαι". It can be used to refer to a collection of sayings or speeches, such as those of a philosopher or a religious leader.
