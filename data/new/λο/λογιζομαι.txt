λογιζομαι comes from the Greek word λόγος (logos), meaning "word" or "reason", and the verb ζω (zo), meaning "to live". The combination of these two words gives the meaning of "to reason" or "to calculate".

λογιζομαι is a Greek verb in the present tense, middle or passive voice, and indicative mood. It is a first-conjugation verb, meaning that it follows the regular pattern of verbs that end in -ω.

λογιζομαι has several meanings in Greek, including "to count", "to calculate", "to consider", "to think", and "to reckon".
