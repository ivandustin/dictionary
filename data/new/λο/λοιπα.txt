Word: λοιπά

Etymology: λοιπά comes from the Greek word "λοιπός" (loipós), which means "remaining" or "leftover".

Grammar: λοιπά is a plural neuter adjective in Greek. It is used to describe things that are left over or remaining after everything else has been taken care of.

Description: λοιπά is a versatile word that can be used in a variety of contexts. It can be used to describe things that are left over after a meal, or to refer to the remaining items on a to-do list. It can also be used to describe anything that is left over or remaining in a particular situation.
