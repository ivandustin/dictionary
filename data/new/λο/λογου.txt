The word "λογου" (logou) is a Greek noun that can be translated to mean "word" or "speech." It is derived from the Greek root "λόγος" (logos), which has a wide range of meanings including "reason," "thought," "account," and "discourse." 

In terms of grammar, "λογου" is in the genitive case, which is used to indicate possession or relationship. It can be translated as "of the word" or "of the speech."

Overall, "λογου" is a versatile word that can be used in a variety of contexts to refer to language, communication, and expression.
