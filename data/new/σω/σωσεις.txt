Word: σωσεις

Grammar: σωσεις is a second-person singular future active indicative form of the verb σῴζω (sózo). It can be translated as "you will save" or "you will rescue".

It is often used in religious or spiritual contexts, such as in the New Testament of the Bible, where it is used to describe the salvation of souls.
