The word "σωτηριου" (soteriou) is a Greek noun that means "salvation" or "deliverance." It is derived from the Greek word "σωτήρ" (soter), which means "savior" or "deliverer." In terms of grammar, "σωτηριου" is in the genitive case, which is used to indicate possession or relationship. 

In Christian theology, "σωτηριου" is often used to refer to the salvation that comes through faith in Jesus Christ. It is also used in a broader sense to refer to any kind of deliverance or rescue from danger or harm. 

Overall, "σωτηριου" is a word that conveys a sense of hope and redemption, emphasizing the idea of being saved or rescued from a difficult situation.
