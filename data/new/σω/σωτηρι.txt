The word "σωτηρι" (soteri) is a Greek noun that means "salvation" or "deliverance." It is derived from the Greek verb "σῴζω" (sozo), which means "to save" or "to rescue." In terms of grammar, "σωτηρι" is a third-declension noun in the singular form. It can be used as a subject, object, or predicate noun in a sentence. Overall, "σωτηρι" is a significant term in Christian theology, as it refers to the salvation and deliverance that comes through faith in Jesus Christ.
