The word "σωσθενην" (sōsthénēn) is a Greek adjective that means "healthy" or "strong." It is derived from the verb "σῴζω" (sṓzō), which means "to save" or "to heal." In terms of grammar, "σωσθενην" is in the accusative case, singular number, and feminine gender. It can be used to describe a person, animal, or object that is in good health or has a strong constitution.
