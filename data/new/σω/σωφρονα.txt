The word "σωφρονα" (sophrona) is a Greek adjective that means "moderate," "temperate," or "self-controlled." It comes from the Greek word "σωφροσύνη" (sophrosyne), which means "soundness of mind" or "prudence." In terms of grammar, "σωφρονα" is in the feminine singular form, and it is declined like other first-declension adjectives in Greek.
