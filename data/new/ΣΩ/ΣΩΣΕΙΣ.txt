Etymology: The word "σωσείς" (sōseis) comes from the Greek verb "σῴζω" (sōzō), which means "to save" or "to rescue."

Grammar: "Σωσείς" is the second person singular future tense of the verb "σῴζω" (sōzō). It is used to refer to an action that will happen in the future and is directed towards the second person singular "you."
