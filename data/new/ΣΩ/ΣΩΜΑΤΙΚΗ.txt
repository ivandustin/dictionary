Etymology: The word "σωματική" (somatici) comes from the Greek word "σῶμα" (soma), meaning "body".

Grammar: In Greek, "σωματική" is an adjective in the feminine form. It agrees with a feminine noun in gender, number, and case.

Description: "Σωματική" is a Greek adjective that means "bodily" or "physical". It is used to describe something that is related to the body or physical aspects of a person or thing.
