Etymology: The word "ταμείοις" comes from the Greek word "ταμεῖον" (tameion), which means "treasury" or "fund". 

Grammar: "ταμείοις" is the dative plural form of the noun "ταμεῖον" (tameion). In Greek grammar, the dative case is used to indicate the indirect object of a verb or the object of certain prepositions.

Description: "ταμείοις" refers to multiple treasuries or funds. It can be used to describe a collection of financial resources or a place where money is stored or managed.
