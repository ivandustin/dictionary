Etymology: The word "ταπεινοφροσύνη" comes from the Greek roots "ταπεινός" (tapeinos) meaning "humble" and "φρόνηση" (phronēsē) meaning "mindset" or "attitude".

Grammar: "Ταπεινοφροσύνη" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the singular form and belongs to the feminine gender.

Description: "Ταπεινοφροσύνη" refers to the quality or state of being humble. It is the virtue of having a modest and unpretentious attitude towards oneself and others. It involves recognizing one's limitations, being respectful and considerate towards others, and not seeking attention or praise.
