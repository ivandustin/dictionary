Etymology: The word "ταυρών" comes from the Greek word "ταῦρος" (tauros), which means "bull".

Grammar: "ταυρών" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the masculine gender and in the nominative case.

Description: "ταυρών" refers to the bull, which is a large and powerful mammal belonging to the Bovidae family. Bulls are known for their muscular build, horns, and aggressive behavior. In various cultures, bulls have been associated with strength, fertility, and power.
