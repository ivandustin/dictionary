Etymology: The word "ταφον" (tafon) comes from the Greek word "τάφος" (taphos), meaning "grave" or "tomb".

Grammar: In Greek, "ταφον" is a noun in the accusative case. It is the singular form of the word, with the plural form being "τάφους" (tafous).

Description: "Ταφον" refers to a grave or a tomb, which is a place where a deceased person is buried. It is often used to describe the physical location where a body is laid to rest.
