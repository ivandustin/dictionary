Etymology: The word "ταπεινοφρονες" comes from the Greek roots "ταπεινός" (tapeinos) meaning "humble" and "φρόνησις" (phronesis) meaning "mind" or "thinking". 

Grammar: "ταπεινοφρονες" is a noun in the plural form. It is derived from the adjective "ταπεινός" (tapeinos) meaning "humble" and the noun "φρόνησις" (phronesis) meaning "mind" or "thinking". 

Description: "ταπεινοφρονες" refers to individuals who possess humility or have a humble mindset. It describes people who do not have an inflated sense of self-importance and are modest in their thoughts and actions.
