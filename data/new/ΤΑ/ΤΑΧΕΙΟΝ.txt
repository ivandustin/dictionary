Etymology: The word "ταχείον" (tacheion) comes from the Greek word "ταχύς" (tachys), meaning "swift" or "fast."

Grammar: In Greek, "ταχείον" is a neuter noun in the singular form.

Description: "Ταχείον" is a Greek word that translates to "post office" or "mail." It refers to a place where letters and packages are sent and received.
