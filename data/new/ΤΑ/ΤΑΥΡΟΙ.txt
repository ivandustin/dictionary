Etymology: The word "ταυροι" (tauroi) comes from the Greek language. It is derived from the Greek word "ταῦρος" (tauros), which means "bull".

Grammar: In Greek, "ταυροι" (tauroi) is the plural form of the noun "ταῦρος" (tauros). It belongs to the second declension and is classified as a masculine noun.
