Etymology: The word "ταφοις" (tafois) comes from the Greek word "τάφος" (tafos), which means "grave" or "tomb". 

Grammar: "Ταφοις" is a noun in the plural form. It belongs to the second declension in Greek grammar. 

Description: "Ταφοις" refers to graves or tombs. It can be used to describe the physical location where a deceased person is buried or the structure that contains the remains of the deceased.
