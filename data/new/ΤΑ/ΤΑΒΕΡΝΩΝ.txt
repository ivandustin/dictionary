Etymology: The word "ταβέρνων" comes from the Greek word "ταβέρνα" (taverna), which originally referred to a small inn or tavern where people could gather to eat, drink, and socialize.

Grammar: "Ταβέρνων" is the genitive plural form of the noun "ταβέρνα" in Greek. In Greek grammar, the genitive case is used to indicate possession or origin.

Description: "Ταβέρνων" refers to multiple taverns in Greek. It is used to describe or refer to a collection or group of taverns.
