Etymology: The word "ταπεινός" (tapinos) comes from the Greek root "ταπεινός" (tapeinos), which means "low" or "humble".

Grammar: In Greek, "ταπεινός" is an adjective that agrees in gender, number, and case with the noun it modifies. It can be declined in various forms to match the noun it describes.
