Etymology: The word "ταχεως" (tacheos) comes from the Greek word "ταχύς" (tachys), meaning "quick" or "fast".

Grammar: In Greek, "ταχεως" is an adverb that is used to describe the manner in which an action is performed. It is derived from the adjective "ταχύς" (tachys) and is used to indicate that something is done quickly or rapidly.
