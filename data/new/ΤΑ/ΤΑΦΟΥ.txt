Etymology: The word "ταφού" (tafou) comes from the Greek word "τάφος" (tafos), which means "grave" or "tomb". 

Grammar: "Ταφού" is a noun in the singular form. It is a masculine noun and belongs to the second declension in Greek grammar. 

Description: "Ταφού" refers to a grave or a tomb, which is a place where a deceased person is buried. It is commonly used to describe the physical location where a body is laid to rest.
