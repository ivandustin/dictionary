Etymology: The word "ταφήν" (tafín) comes from the Greek verb "τάφω" (táfo), which means "to bury." 

Grammar: "Ταφήν" is a noun in the singular form. It belongs to the third declension in Greek grammar. 

Description: "Ταφήν" refers to the act of burying or burial. It can also be used to describe a burial place or tomb.
