Etymology: The word "οπτου" (optou) comes from the Greek verb "οπτομαι" (optomai), which means "to see" or "to observe". 

Grammar: "Οπτου" is a noun in the genitive case. In Greek, nouns change their form depending on their grammatical role in a sentence. The genitive case is used to indicate possession or origin.

Description: "Οπτου" is a Greek word that means "of sight" or "of vision". It is often used in phrases or expressions to refer to something related to seeing or observing.
