Etymology: The word "αετού" (aetou) comes from the Greek word "αετός" (aetos), which means "eagle". 

Grammar: "Αετού" is a noun in the genitive case. In Greek, nouns change their form depending on their grammatical role in a sentence. The genitive case is used to indicate possession or origin.
