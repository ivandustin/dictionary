Etymology: The word "τοιχε" (toiche) comes from the Greek word "τοίχος" (toichos), meaning "wall".

Grammar: "τοιχε" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the singular form.

Description: "τοιχε" refers to a wall, typically a vertical structure that separates or encloses an area. It can be made of various materials such as bricks, stones, or concrete. Walls serve as a barrier, providing protection, privacy, and structural support to buildings or outdoor spaces.
