Etymology: The word "τολμάν" (tolman) comes from the Greek verb "τολμάω" (tolmao), which means "to dare" or "to have courage". 

Grammar: "Τολμάν" is a noun in the singular form. It belongs to the neuter gender in Greek.

Description: "Τολμάν" refers to a person who is daring or courageous. It can also be used to describe someone who takes risks or is willing to face challenges.
