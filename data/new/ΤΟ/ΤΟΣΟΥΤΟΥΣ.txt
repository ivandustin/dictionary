Etymology: The word "τοσούτους" comes from the Greek language. It is a combination of the words "τόσος" (meaning "so much" or "such") and "οὗτος" (meaning "this"). 

Grammar: "τοσούτους" is a masculine plural form of the adjective "τοσοῦτος" (meaning "so much" or "such"). It is used to describe plural masculine nouns. 

Description: "τοσούτους" is used to indicate a large or significant number or quantity of something. It can be used to describe people, objects, or abstract concepts.
