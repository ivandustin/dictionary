Etymology: The word "τοσούτος" (tosoútos) comes from the combination of the Greek words "τόσος" (tósos) meaning "so much" and "οὗτος" (hoûtos) meaning "this." 

Grammar: "Τοσούτος" is an adjective in the masculine singular form. It agrees with the noun it modifies in gender, number, and case. It can also be declined to match the gender, number, and case of the noun it modifies.

Description: "Τοσούτος" is used to express a large or significant amount or degree of something. It can be translated as "so much," "such," or "this much." It is often used to emphasize the extent or quantity of something.
