Etymology: The word "τοσούτου" (tosoútou) comes from the Greek language. It is a combination of the words "τόσος" (tósos), meaning "so much" or "such," and "ούτος" (oútos), meaning "this." 

Grammar: "Τοσούτου" is a pronoun in the genitive case. It is used to indicate possession or to express a quantity or degree of something. It can be translated as "of this much" or "of such a degree."

Description: "Τοσούτου" is a versatile word that can be used in various contexts. It is commonly used to express a specific amount or degree of something, emphasizing its magnitude. It can also be used to compare or contrast different quantities or degrees.
