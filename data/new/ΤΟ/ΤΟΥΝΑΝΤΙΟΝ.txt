Etymology: The word "τουναντίον" comes from the Greek phrase "τοῦ ἀντίου" (toú antíou), which means "on the contrary" or "opposite."

Grammar: "Τουναντίον" is an adverb in Greek. It is used to express a contrary or opposite meaning to what has been previously stated.

Description: "Τουναντίον" is a versatile word that can be used in various contexts to indicate a contrary or opposite situation. It is often used to introduce a contrasting statement or to emphasize a different perspective.
