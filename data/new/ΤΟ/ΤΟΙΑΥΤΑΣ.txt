Etymology: The word "τοιαύτας" comes from the Greek language. It is a combination of the words "τοιαῦτα" (toiauta), meaning "such things," and the suffix "-ς" (-s), which indicates the feminine gender.

Grammar: "τοιαύτας" is a feminine noun in the nominative case. In Greek grammar, nouns have different forms depending on their gender, number, and case. The "-ς" ending indicates the nominative singular form of the noun.

Description: "τοιαύτας" refers to "such things" in Greek. It is used to describe or refer to things that are similar or of the same kind.
