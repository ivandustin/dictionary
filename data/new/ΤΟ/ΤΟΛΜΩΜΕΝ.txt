Etymology: The word "τολμώμεν" comes from the Greek verb "τολμάω" (tolmao), which means "to dare" or "to have courage". The suffix "-μεν" is added to the verb stem to form the present tense plural form, indicating that the subject of the verb is "we".

Grammar: "τολμώμεν" is the present tense plural form of the verb "τολμάω" (tolmao) in Greek. It is used to express an action or state of daring or having courage that is performed by a group of people.

Description: "τολμώμεν" is a word that describes the act of daring or having courage. It is used to indicate that a group of people is collectively engaging in an action that requires bravery or boldness.
