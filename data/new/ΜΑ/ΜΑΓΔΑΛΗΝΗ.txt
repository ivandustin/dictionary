Description: "Μαγδαληνή" is commonly used as a given name for girls in Greece. It is also associated with Mary Magdalene, a biblical figure who was one of the followers of Jesus Christ. Mary Magdalene is often depicted as a repentant sinner who became a devoted disciple of Jesus.
