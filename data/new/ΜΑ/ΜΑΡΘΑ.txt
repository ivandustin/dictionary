- Nominative: μαρθα
- Genitive: μαρθας
- Dative: μαρθᾳ
- Accusative: μαρθαν
- Vocative: μαρθα

Description: "Μαρθα" is a name commonly used in Greek-speaking countries. It is often associated with the biblical figure Martha, who was the sister of Mary and Lazarus. The name has a strong and timeless quality, and it is still used today.
