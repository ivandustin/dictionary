μαρτυρούντι (marturúnti) is a Greek verb in the present tense, third person plural form. It comes from the root word μαρτυρία (marturía), which means "testimony" or "witness." The verb μαρτυρούντι is derived from the noun μάρτυρας (mártiras), which means "witness." In Greek grammar, this verb belongs to the third conjugation class. It is used to describe the action of testifying or bearing witness to something.
