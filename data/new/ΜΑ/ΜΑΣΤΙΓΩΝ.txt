Grammar: "Μαστιγών" is the present participle form of the verb "μαστιγόω" in the third person singular, active voice. It is used to describe someone who is currently whipping or flogging.

Description: "Μαστιγών" refers to a person who is actively whipping or flogging someone or something. It is often used in historical or literary contexts to describe a person who inflicts punishment or discipline through the use of a whip.
