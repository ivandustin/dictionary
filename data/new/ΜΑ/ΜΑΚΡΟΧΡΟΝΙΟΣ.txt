μακροχρονιος (makrochronios) is a Greek word that can be broken down into two parts: "μακρός" (makros) meaning "long" and "χρόνος" (chronos) meaning "time." 

In terms of etymology, the word μακροχρονιος is derived from the combination of these two Greek words. 

In grammar, μακροχρονιος is an adjective that describes something as being long-lasting or enduring over a long period of time. It can be used to describe various things, such as a long-term project, a long-lasting relationship, or a long-lasting effect.
