Etymology: The word "μακαρισμός" (makarismos) comes from the Greek word "μακάριος" (makarios), meaning "blessed" or "happy."

Grammar: "Μακαρισμός" is a noun in the Greek language. It is a masculine noun of the second declension.

Description: "Μακαρισμός" refers to the act or state of being blessed or happy. It can also be used to describe a blessing or a wish for happiness.
