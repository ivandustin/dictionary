Word: ολιγου

Etymology: The word ολιγου comes from the Greek word "ολίγος" (oligos), which means "few" or "little".

Grammar: ολιγου is an adverb in Greek, which means "almost" or "nearly". It is often used in combination with other words to indicate a small or insignificant amount.

Description: ολιγου is a versatile word that can be used in a variety of contexts. For example, it can be used to describe a small amount of something, such as "ολιγου νερό" (oligou nero) which means "a little water". It can also be used to indicate that something is almost or nearly complete, such as "ολιγου τελείωσα" (oligou teleiosa) which means "I almost finished". Overall, ολιγου is a useful word to know in Greek as it can be used in many different situations.
