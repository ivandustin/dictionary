Word: ολιγα

Etymology:
The word ολιγα (oliga) is derived from the Greek adjective "ολίγος" (oligos), which means "few" or "little". 

Grammar:
Ολιγα is a neuter plural noun in Greek. It is the plural form of the adjective "ολίγος" (oligos), which means "few" or "little". 

Description:
Ολιγα is used to describe a small quantity or number of something. It can also be used to describe a brief or short period of time. For example, "Ολιγα λεπτά" (oliga lepta) means "a few minutes".
