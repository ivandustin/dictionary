Etymology: The word "ξενισθώμεν" comes from the Greek verb "ξενίζω" (xenizo), which means "to host" or "to entertain as a guest". It is derived from the noun "ξένος" (xenos), meaning "stranger" or "foreigner".

Grammar: "ξενισθώμεν" is a verb in the first person plural, present subjunctive mood, passive voice. It is formed by adding the suffix "-θώμεν" to the stem "ξενισ-" of the verb "ξενίζω".

Description: The word "ξενισθώμεν" expresses the idea of "let us be hosted" or "let us be entertained as guests". It implies a passive action of being welcomed or accommodated by someone else.
