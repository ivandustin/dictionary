Etymology: The word "γλωσσαίς" (glōssais) comes from the Greek word "γλῶσσα" (glōssa), which means "tongue" or "language."

Grammar: "Γλωσσαίς" is a noun in the plural form. It is the dative plural of the noun "γλῶσσα" (glōssa), which refers to "tongues" or "languages."

Description: "Γλωσσαίς" is used in Greek to refer to multiple tongues or languages. It can be used to describe the different languages spoken by people or the different dialects within a language.
