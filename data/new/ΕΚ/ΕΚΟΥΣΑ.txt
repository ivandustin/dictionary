Etymology: The word "εκούσα" (ekousa) comes from the Greek verb "ακούω" (akouo), which means "to hear". It is formed by adding the prefix "εκ-" (ek-) to the verb.

Grammar: "Εκούσα" is the first person singular past tense of the verb "ακούω" (akouo), which means "I heard" in English. It belongs to the indicative mood and the aorist tense.

Description: "Εκούσα" is a verb form used to express an action that happened in the past. It specifically refers to the act of hearing something.
