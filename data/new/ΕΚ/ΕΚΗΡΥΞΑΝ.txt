Etymology: The word "εκηρυξαν" (ekiryxan) comes from the Greek verb "κηρύσσω" (kirysso), which means "to proclaim" or "to announce."

Grammar: "Εκηρύξαν" is the third person plural, aorist tense of the verb "κηρύσσω" (kirysso). In Greek, verbs change their form based on tense, mood, voice, and person.

Description: "Εκηρύξαν" is a verb in the Greek language that means "they proclaimed" or "they announced." It is used to describe an action performed by a group of people in the past.
