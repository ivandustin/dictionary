Etymology: The word "εκχεαί" (ekcheai) comes from the Greek verb "εκχέω" (ekcheo), which means "to pour out" or "to shed." It is derived from the prefix "εκ-" (ek-), meaning "out," and the verb "χέω" (cheo), meaning "to pour."

Grammar: "Εκχεαί" is a noun in the plural form. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their gender, number, and case. The plural form indicates that there are multiple instances of the noun.

Description: "Εκχεαί" refers to the act of pouring out or shedding. It can be used metaphorically to describe the release or expression of emotions, thoughts, or ideas.
