Etymology: The word "εκτησατο" comes from the Greek verb "εκτινω" (ektino), which means "to acquire" or "to obtain".

Grammar: "εκτησατο" is the third person singular, aorist tense of the verb "εκτινω" (ektino). It is in the indicative mood and active voice.

Description: "εκτησατο" is a Greek word that means "he/she/it acquired" or "he/she/it obtained". It is used to describe the action of acquiring or obtaining something in the past.
