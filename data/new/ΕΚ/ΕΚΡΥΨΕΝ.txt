Etymology: The word "εκρυψεν" (ekrypsen) comes from the Greek verb "κρύπτω" (krypto), which means "to hide" or "to conceal". The prefix "εκ" (ek) indicates the action of coming out or away from something.

Grammar: "Εκρυψεν" is the third person singular form of the aorist tense of the verb "κρύπτω" (krypto). In Greek, verbs have different forms depending on the tense, mood, voice, and person. The aorist tense is used to express a completed action in the past.

Description: "Εκρυψεν" means "he/she/it hid" in Greek. It is used to describe an action of hiding or concealing something in the past.
