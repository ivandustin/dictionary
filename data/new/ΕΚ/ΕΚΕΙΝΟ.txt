Etymology: The word "εκείνο" (ekino) comes from the Greek language. It is derived from the ancient Greek word "ἐκεῖνος" (ekeinos), which means "that" or "those."

Grammar: "Εκείνο" is a pronoun in the Greek language. It is used to refer to something or someone that is distant in space or time. It can be translated as "that" or "those" in English.
