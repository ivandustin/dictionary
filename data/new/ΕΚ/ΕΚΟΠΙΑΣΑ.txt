Etymology: The word "εκοπιασα" (ekopiasa) comes from the Greek verb "κοπιάω" (kopiao), which means "to toil" or "to work hard". The prefix "εκ-" (ek-) in this word indicates the completion or intensity of the action.

Grammar: "Εκοπιασα" is the first person singular past tense form of the verb "κοπιάω" (kopiao) in Greek. It is used to indicate that the speaker worked hard or toiled in the past.

Description: "Εκοπιασα" is a verb form that describes an action of working hard or toiling in the past. It can be used to talk about physical or mental effort put into a task or activity.
