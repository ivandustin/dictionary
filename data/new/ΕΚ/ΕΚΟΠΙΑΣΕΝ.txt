Grammar: "εκοπιασεν" is the third person singular form of the aorist tense of the verb "κοπιάω" (kopiao) in Greek. The aorist tense is used to express a completed action in the past.

Description: "εκοπιασεν" can be translated as "he/she/it toiled" or "he/she/it worked hard" in English. It describes a past action of someone exerting effort or working hard.
