Grammar: "Εκπεπληρωκεν" is the third person singular form of the verb "πληρόω" in the perfect tense. The perfect tense is used to indicate a completed action in the past that has ongoing relevance in the present.

Description: "Εκπεπληρωκεν" means "he/she/it has fulfilled" or "he/she/it has completed" in English. It is used to describe an action that has been finished in the past but has a continuing effect or relevance in the present.
