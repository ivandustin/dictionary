Grammar: "Εκληθη" is the third person singular aorist passive indicative form of the verb "καλέω" (kaleō). In Greek grammar, the aorist tense is used to express a completed action in the past, and the passive voice indicates that the subject of the verb is being acted upon.

Description: "Εκληθη" is a Greek word that translates to "he/she/it was called" or "he/she/it was summoned" in English. It refers to the action of someone being called or summoned by someone else.
