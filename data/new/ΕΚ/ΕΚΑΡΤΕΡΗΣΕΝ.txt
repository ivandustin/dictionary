Grammar: "εκαρτερησεν" is the third person singular form of the aorist tense of the verb "εκαρτερεω" (ekartereo). In Greek, verbs have different forms depending on the person, number, tense, and mood. The aorist tense is used to express a completed action in the past.

Description: "εκαρτερησεν" describes the action of waiting eagerly or patiently. It indicates that someone or something waited for a certain period of time with anticipation or perseverance.
