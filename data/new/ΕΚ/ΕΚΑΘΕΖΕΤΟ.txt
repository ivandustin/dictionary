Grammar: "Εκαθεζετο" is the third person singular form of the aorist tense of the verb "καθέζομαι" (kathézomai). It is in the indicative mood and the middle voice. The aorist tense is used to express a completed action in the past, and the middle voice indicates that the subject is performing the action on themselves.
