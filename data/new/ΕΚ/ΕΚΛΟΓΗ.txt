Etymology: The word "εκλογη" (eklogi) comes from the Greek verb "εκλεγομαι" (eklegomai), which means "to choose" or "to select". 

Grammar: "Εκλογη" is a feminine noun in Greek. It belongs to the second declension and its genitive form is "εκλογης" (eklogis). 

Description: "Εκλογη" refers to the act of choosing or selecting someone or something. It can also refer to an election, especially in the context of politics.
