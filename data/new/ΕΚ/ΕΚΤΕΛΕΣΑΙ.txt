Etymology: The word "εκτελεσαι" comes from the Greek verb "εκτελώ" (ekteló), which means "to perform" or "to execute".

Grammar: "Εκτελεσαι" is the second person singular imperative form of the verb "εκτελώ" (ekteló). It is used to give a command or instruction to someone, specifically addressing one person.

Description: "Εκτελεσαι" is a Greek word that means "execute" or "perform". It is often used in a context where someone is being instructed or commanded to carry out a specific action or task.
