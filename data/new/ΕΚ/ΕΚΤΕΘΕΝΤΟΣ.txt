Grammar: "Εκτεθεντος" is the masculine form of the past participle of the verb "εκτιθημι" (ektithemi). In Greek grammar, the past participle is used to form compound tenses and passive voice. It agrees in gender, number, and case with the noun it modifies.

Description: "Εκτεθεντος" is an adjective that means "exhibited" or "displayed". It is used to describe something that has been placed outside or shown to the public.
