Grammar: "Εκδεχομαι" is a verb in the Greek language. It belongs to the first conjugation class and is conjugated in the present tense as follows: εκδέχομαι (ekdechomai) - I receive, εκδέχεσαι (ekdechesai) - you receive, εκδέχεται (ekdechetai) - he/she/it receives, εκδεχόμαστε (ekdechomaste) - we receive, εκδέχεστε (ekdecheste) - you receive, εκδέχονται (ekdechontai) - they receive.

Description: "Εκδεχομαι" is a versatile verb that can be used in various contexts. It generally means "to receive" or "to accept," but it can also imply waiting for something or someone. It can be used to express anticipation, expectation, or readiness to receive or accept something.
