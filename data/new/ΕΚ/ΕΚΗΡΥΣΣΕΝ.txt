Etymology: The word "εκηρυσσεν" (ekērysse) comes from the Greek verb "κηρύσσω" (kērýssō), which means "to proclaim" or "to announce."

Grammar: "Εκηρυσσεν" is the third person singular form of the aorist tense of the verb "κηρύσσω" (kērýssō) in the Greek language. It is in the indicative mood and active voice.

Description: "Εκηρυσσεν" is a verb that means "he/she/it proclaimed" or "he/she/it announced" in Greek. It is used to describe the action of making a public declaration or announcement.
