Etymology: The word "εκαυματισθησαν" comes from the Greek verb "καυματίζομαι" (kaumatízomai), which means "to get burned". The prefix "εκ-" (ek-) indicates the action of getting burned from the outside.

Grammar: "εκαυματισθησαν" is the third person plural form of the verb "καυματίζομαι" in the aorist tense. The aorist tense is used to describe a completed action in the past.

Description: "εκαυματισθησαν" means "they got burned" in English. It is used to describe the action of multiple people getting burned.
