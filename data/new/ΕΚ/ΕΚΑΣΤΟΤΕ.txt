Etymology: The word "εκαστοτε" comes from the Greek words "εκαστος" (ekastos) meaning "each" or "every" and "τε" (te) meaning "and". 

Grammar: "Εκαστοτε" is an adverb in Greek. It is used to describe something that happens or is true at any given time or in any given situation. It is often used in combination with other words to specify the context or condition.

Description: "Εκαστοτε" is a versatile word that can be used in various contexts. It is commonly used in formal or academic writing to indicate that something is true or applicable in any situation.
