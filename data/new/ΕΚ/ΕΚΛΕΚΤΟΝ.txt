Etymology: The word "εκλεκτον" (eklekton) comes from the Greek word "εκλεκτος" (eklektos), which means "chosen" or "selected". 

Grammar: "Εκλεκτον" is a neuter noun in the singular form. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their gender, number, and case. 

Description: "Εκλεκτον" refers to something or someone that is chosen or selected. It can be used to describe a person, an object, or a concept that is considered special, preferred, or of high quality.
