Etymology: The word "εκβληθεντος" comes from the Greek verb "εκβαλλω" (ekballō), which means "to throw out" or "to expel". The suffix "-τος" (-tos) is added to the verb stem to form the past participle, indicating a completed action.

Grammar: "Εκβληθεντος" is the masculine singular form of the past participle of the verb "εκβαλλω" (ekballō). In Greek grammar, past participles are used to form compound verb tenses and can also function as adjectives.

Description: "Εκβληθεντος" describes something or someone that has been thrown out or expelled. It can be used to refer to a person who has been ejected from a place or a thing that has been forcefully removed or discarded.
