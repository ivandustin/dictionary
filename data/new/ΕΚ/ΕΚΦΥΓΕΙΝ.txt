Etymology: The word "εκφυγείν" (ekfygeín) comes from the Greek roots "εκ" (ek), meaning "out" or "from," and "φυγή" (fygí), meaning "escape" or "flight." 

Grammar: "Εκφυγείν" is a verb in the Greek language. It is in the present tense, active voice, and infinitive form. 

Description: The word "εκφυγείν" means "to escape" or "to flee" in English. It refers to the act of getting away or avoiding a dangerous or unwanted situation.
