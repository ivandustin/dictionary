Etymology: The word "εκδωσεται" (ekdosētai) is derived from the Greek verb "εκδιδωμι" (ekdidōmi), which means "to give out" or "to publish."

Grammar: "Εκδωσεται" is the third person singular form of the verb "εκδιδωμι" in the present tense, passive voice, and indicative mood. It indicates that the subject is receiving the action of giving out or publishing.

Description: "Εκδωσεται" is a Greek word that means "it is given out" or "it is published." It is used to describe the action of distributing or making something available to the public.
