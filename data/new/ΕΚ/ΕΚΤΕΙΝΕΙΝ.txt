Etymology: The word "εκτείνειν" (ektínein) comes from the Greek roots "εκ" (ek), meaning "out," and "τείνειν" (teínein), meaning "to stretch." 

Grammar: "Εκτείνειν" is a verb in the Greek language. It is in the present tense, active voice, and indicative mood. 

Description: "Εκτείνειν" means "to extend" or "to stretch out" in English. It is used to describe the action of making something longer, wider, or larger in size or scope.
