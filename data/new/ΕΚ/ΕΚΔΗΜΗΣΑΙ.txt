Grammar: "εκδημησαι" is the aorist tense, active voice, subjunctive mood, and second person singular form of the verb "εκδημέω" (ekdimeo). The aorist tense is used to express a completed action in the past, the active voice indicates that the subject performs the action, the subjunctive mood expresses possibility or potentiality, and the second person singular form is used when addressing one person.
