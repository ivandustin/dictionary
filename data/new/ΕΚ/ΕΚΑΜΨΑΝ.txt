Etymology: The word "εκαμψαν" comes from the Greek verb "κάμπτω" (kampto), which means "to bend" or "to flex". The prefix "εκ-" (ek-) indicates the action of bending away or outwards.

Grammar: "Εκάμψαν" is the third person plural, aorist tense of the verb "κάμπτω" (kampto). In Greek grammar, the aorist tense is used to express a completed action in the past.

Description: "Εκάμψαν" is a Greek word that means "they bent" or "they flexed". It describes the action of multiple individuals bending or flexing something.
