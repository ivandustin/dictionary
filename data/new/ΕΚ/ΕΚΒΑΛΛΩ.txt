Grammar: "Εκβαλλω" is a verb in the Greek language. It belongs to the first conjugation class and is conjugated in various tenses, moods, and voices to indicate different actions and conditions.

Description: "Εκβαλλω" is a versatile verb that can have several meanings depending on the context. It can mean "to throw out," "to expel," "to eject," or "to cast out." It is often used to describe the act of forcefully removing something or someone from a place or situation.
