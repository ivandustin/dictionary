Grammar: "Εκκοπτεται" is the third person singular form of the verb "εκκόπτω" (ekkópto) in the present tense. It is in the passive voice, indicating that the subject is being acted upon. The verb belongs to the first conjugation class in Greek.

Description: "Εκκοπτεται" is a Greek word that means "it is being cut out" or "it is being interrupted". It is used to describe an action or process in which something is being cut or interrupted by an external force.
