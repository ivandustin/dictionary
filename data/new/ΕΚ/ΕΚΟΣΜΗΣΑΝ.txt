Etymology: The word "εκοσμησαν" (ekosmisan) comes from the Greek verb "κοσμέω" (kosmeo), which means "to adorn" or "to decorate". The prefix "εκ-" (ek-) in this word indicates the completion or accomplishment of the action.

Grammar: "εκοσμησαν" is the third person plural, aorist tense form of the verb "κοσμέω" (kosmeo). The aorist tense is used to describe a completed action in the past.

Description: "εκοσμησαν" is a Greek verb that means "they adorned" or "they decorated". It is used to describe the action of adorning or decorating something in the past.
