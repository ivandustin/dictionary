The word "εκπλησσομενος" is a Greek adjective that means "astonished" or "amazed". Here is the breakdown of the requested information:

Etymology: The word "εκπλησσομενος" comes from the Greek verb "εκπλήσσω" (ekplí̱sso̱), which means "to strike out" or "to amaze". The suffix "-μενος" (-menos) is a participle ending that indicates the passive voice.

Grammar: "Εκπλησσομενος" is the masculine singular form of the adjective. In Greek, adjectives agree in gender, number, and case with the noun they modify.

Description: When used to describe a person, "εκπλησσομενος" means that the person is in a state of astonishment or amazement. It implies that something unexpected or surprising has happened, causing the person to be amazed.
