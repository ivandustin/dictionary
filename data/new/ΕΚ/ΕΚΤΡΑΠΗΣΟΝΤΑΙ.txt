Etymology: The word "εκτραπησονται" comes from the Greek verb "εκτρεπω" (ektrepo), which means "to turn aside" or "to divert". The suffix "-σονται" (-sontai) indicates the third person plural form of the verb in the future tense.

Grammar: "Εκτραπησονται" is a verb in the Greek language. It is in the third person plural form of the future tense. The verb belongs to the passive voice, indicating that the subject of the sentence receives the action.

Description: "Εκτραπησονται" is used to describe an action that will be turned aside or diverted by the subject in the future. It implies that the subject will avoid or deviate from a certain course of action.
