Etymology: The word "εκερδησα" (ekerdēsa) comes from the Greek verb "κερδίζω" (kerdízo), which means "to gain" or "to win".

Grammar: "Εκερδησα" is the first person singular form of the aorist tense in Greek. It is used to indicate a completed action in the past.
