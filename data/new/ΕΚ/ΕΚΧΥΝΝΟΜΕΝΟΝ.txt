Etymology: The word "εκχυννομενον" comes from the Greek verb "εκχυννω" (ekchynno), which means "to pour out" or "to shed." It is derived from the prefix "εκ" (ek), meaning "out," and the verb "χυννω" (chynno), meaning "to pour."

Grammar: "Εκχυννομενον" is the present participle form of the verb "εκχυννω" in the passive voice. It is used to describe an ongoing action of pouring out or shedding.

Description: "Εκχυννομενον" is an adjective that describes something that is currently being poured out or shed. It can be used to refer to liquids, such as water or tears, being poured out or spilled. It can also be used metaphorically to describe emotions or feelings being expressed or released.
