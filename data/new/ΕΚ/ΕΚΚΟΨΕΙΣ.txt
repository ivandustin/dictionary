Grammar: "Εκκοψεις" is a noun in the plural form. In Greek grammar, nouns have different forms depending on their gender, number, and case. The plural form indicates that there are multiple instances of the noun.

Description: "Εκκοψεις" refers to "excisions" or "cuttings out." It can be used in various contexts, such as in medicine to describe the removal of a part of the body or in art to refer to the act of cutting out shapes or figures.
