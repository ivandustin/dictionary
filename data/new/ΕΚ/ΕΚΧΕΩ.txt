Etymology: The word "εκχεω" (ekcheo) comes from the Greek roots "εκ" (ek), meaning "out," and "χεω" (cheo), meaning "to pour." 

Grammar: "Εκχεω" is a verb in the Greek language. It belongs to the first conjugation and is in the present tense. 

Description: "Εκχεω" means "to pour out" or "to empty" in English. It is used to describe the action of pouring a liquid or emptying a container.
