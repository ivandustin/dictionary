Etymology:
The word "γραμματι" comes from the Greek word "γράμμα" (gramma) which means "letter" or "writing". 

Grammar:
"Γραμματι" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the singular form and is feminine in gender. 

Description:
"Γραμματι" refers to the act of writing or the art of writing. It can also refer to a written document or a letter. In modern Greek, it is commonly used to refer to the field of literature or writing as a profession.
