Word: γραψαι

Etymology: γραψαι is a Greek verb that comes from the root word γράφω (grapho), which means "to write."

Grammar: γραψαι is the aorist tense of the Greek verb γράφω (grapho), which means "to write." The aorist tense is used to describe a completed action in the past.

Description: γραψαι means "to have written" or "to write" in the past tense. It is often used in Greek literature to describe a completed action, such as writing a letter or a book.
