Word: γραφομενα

Etymology: γραφομενα is the present participle of the Greek verb γράφω (grapho), which means "to write". The suffix -μενα (-mena) indicates that the action is ongoing or in progress.

Grammar: γραφομενα is a neuter noun in the plural form. It is derived from the verb γράφω and functions as a verbal noun, meaning "things that are being written".

Description: γραφομενα refers to written things or documents that are currently being written or composed. It can also refer to the act of writing itself. In modern Greek, it is often used to refer to written works, such as books or articles.
