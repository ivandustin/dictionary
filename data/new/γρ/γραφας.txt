The word "γραφας" (graphas) is a Greek noun that means "writings" or "documents". It is derived from the Greek verb "γράφω" (grapho), which means "to write". In terms of grammar, "γραφας" is a masculine noun in the nominative case. It can also be declined in other cases, such as the genitive "γραφᾶ" (grapha) or the accusative "γραφάν" (graphan).
