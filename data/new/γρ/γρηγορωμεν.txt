Word: γρηγορωμεν

Etymology:
The word γρηγορωμεν (grēgorōmen) is a present participle form of the Greek verb γρηγορέω (grēgoreō), which means "to be awake, to be watchful, to be vigilant." The word is derived from the root word γρηγορός (grēgoros), which means "watchful, vigilant."

γρηγορωμεν is a present participle form of the Greek verb γρηγορέω (grēgoreō). It is in the active voice, and it is in the first person plural form. The present participle form indicates that the action of being watchful or vigilant is ongoing or continuous.
