Word: γραφει

Etymology: γραφει comes from the Greek verb γράφω (grapho), which means "to write" or "to draw".

Grammar: γραφει is the present tense, third person singular form of the Greek verb γράφω (grapho). It is used to describe an action that is currently happening in the present.

Description: γραφει is a verb that is commonly used in modern Greek. It can be used to describe the act of writing or drawing, as well as other related actions such as typing or composing. In addition, it can also be used in a figurative sense to describe the act of recording or documenting something.
