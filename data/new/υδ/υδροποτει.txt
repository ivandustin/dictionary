The word "υδροποτει" is a Greek verb that means "to drink water". 

Etymology: The word is derived from the Greek words "υδρο" (hydro) meaning water and "πίνω" (pino) meaning to drink. 

Grammar: "Υδροποτει" is a present tense verb in the third person singular form. 

Description: The word is commonly used in everyday Greek language to describe the act of drinking water. It can also be used metaphorically to describe someone who is very thirsty or in need of water.
