μνημονεύοντες (mnēmoneúontes) is a Greek word that can be translated as "remembering" or "memorizing" in English. It is the present participle form of the verb μνημονεύω (mnēmoneúō), which means "to remember" or "to memorize." 

Etymology: The word μνημονεύοντες comes from the root word μνημονεύω, which is derived from the noun μνήμη (mnēmē), meaning "memory" or "remembrance." The suffix -οντες (-ontes) is added to the root to form the present participle, indicating ongoing action.

Grammar: In Greek, verbs have different forms depending on tense, mood, voice, and person. The word μνημονεύοντες is in the present participle form, which is used to describe ongoing or continuous action. It is declined to match the gender, number, and case of the noun it modifies.

Description: μνημονεύοντες is used to describe people who are currently engaged in the act of remembering or memorizing something. It can refer to individuals who are actively trying to commit information to memory or who have a natural ability to remember things easily.
