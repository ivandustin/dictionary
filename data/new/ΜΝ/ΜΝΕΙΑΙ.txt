Etymology: The word "μνεία" (mnēia) comes from the Greek root "μνήμη" (mnēmē), meaning "memory" or "remembrance".

Grammar: "Μνεία" is a feminine noun in the Greek language. It is in the nominative case and singular form.

Description: "Μνεία" refers to the act of remembering or the state of being remembered. It can also mean a memorial or a commemoration. In a broader sense, it can be used to describe any form of remembrance or memorialization.
