Etymology: The word "μνημεία" comes from the Greek word "μνήμη" (mnēmē), meaning "memory" or "remembrance".

Grammar: "μνημεία" is a noun in the plural form in Greek. It belongs to the third declension and is neuter in gender.

Description: "μνημεία" refers to monuments or memorials that are built to commemorate or remember a person, event, or historical site. These can include statues, plaques, buildings, or any other structure that serves as a reminder of the past.
