Etymology: The word "μνησθήσομαι" (mnēsthēsomai) comes from the Greek verb "μιμνήσκω" (mimnēskō), which means "to remember" or "to recall."

Grammar: "μνησθήσομαι" is a future tense form of the verb "μιμνήσκω" (mimnēskō), specifically in the first person singular. It is used to express the action of remembering or recalling something that will happen in the future.

Description: "μνησθήσομαι" is a Greek verb that conveys the idea of remembering or recalling something in the future. It is often used to express the intention or determination to remember or keep something in mind for future reference.
