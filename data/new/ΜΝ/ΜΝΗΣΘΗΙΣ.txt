Etymology: The word "μνησθηίς" (mnēsthēis) comes from the Greek verb "μνάομαι" (mnaomai), which means "to remember" or "to recall."

Grammar: "Μνησθηίς" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the nominative case and singular number.

Description: "Μνησθηίς" refers to the act of remembering or recalling something. It can also be used to describe a memory or a recollection.
