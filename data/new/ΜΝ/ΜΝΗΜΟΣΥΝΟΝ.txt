Grammar: μνημοσύνον is a neuter noun in the singular form. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their gender, number, and case. 

Description: μνημοσύνον refers to the act or process of remembering or recalling something from the past. It can also be used to describe a memorial or a commemoration of a person or an event.
