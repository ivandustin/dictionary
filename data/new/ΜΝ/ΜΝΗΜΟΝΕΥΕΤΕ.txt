μνημονεύετε (mnimonévete) is a Greek verb that means "to memorize" or "to commit to memory." It is derived from the noun μνήμη (mními), which means "memory." In terms of grammar, μνημονεύετε is the second person plural form of the present tense in the active voice. It is used when addressing a group of people and instructing them to memorize or commit something to memory.
