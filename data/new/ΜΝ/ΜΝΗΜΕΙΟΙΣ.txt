μνημείοις (mnēmeiois) is the plural form of the Greek word μνημεῖον (mnēmeion), which means "monument" or "memorial". It is derived from the Greek word μνήμη (mnēmē), meaning "memory" or "remembrance". In grammar, μνημεῖον is a neuter noun in the second declension. It can be used to refer to physical structures or objects that are built to commemorate or honor someone or something.
