Etymology: The word "μνημην" comes from the Greek language. It is derived from the verb "μνάομαι" (mnáomai), which means "to remember" or "to bear in mind."

Grammar: "Μνημην" is a noun in the singular form. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their gender, number, and case. The word "μνημην" is in the masculine gender and the nominative case.

Description: "Μνημην" refers to the act of remembering or recollection. It can also be used to describe a memorial or a monument that serves as a reminder of something or someone.
