Etymology: The word "μνείαν" (mnēian) comes from the Greek word "μνήμη" (mnēmē), meaning "memory" or "remembrance".

Grammar: "Μνείαν" is a noun in the accusative case, singular form.

Description: "Μνείαν" refers to the act of remembering or commemorating someone or something. It can also mean a memorial or a remembrance.
