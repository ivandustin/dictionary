Etymology: The word "οισούσιν" comes from the Greek verb "οίομαι" (oíomai), which means "to think" or "to suppose." It is conjugated in the third person plural present tense.

Grammar: "Οισούσιν" is a verb form in the Greek language. It belongs to the present tense and is used to express the action of thinking or supposing in the third person plural.
