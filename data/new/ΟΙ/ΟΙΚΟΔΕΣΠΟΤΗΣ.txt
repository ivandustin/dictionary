Etymology: The word "οικοδεσπότης" comes from the Greek words "οίκος" (oikos) meaning "house" and "δεσπότης" (despotes) meaning "master" or "lord". 

Grammar: "Οικοδεσπότης" is a noun in the masculine gender. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their case, number, and gender. 

Description: "Οικοδεσπότης" refers to the host or hostess of a house or an event. It is used to describe someone who welcomes and entertains guests in their home or at a gathering. The term implies a sense of hospitality and responsibility for the well-being of the guests.
