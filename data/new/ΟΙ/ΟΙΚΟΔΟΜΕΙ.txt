Etymology: The word "οικοδομεί" comes from the Greek root words "οίκος" (oikos) meaning "house" and "δομή" (dome) meaning "building". 

Grammar: "Οικοδομεί" is a verb in the present tense, third person singular form. It is derived from the verb "οικοδομώ" (oikodomo), which means "to build" in Greek.

Description: "Οικοδομεί" is used to describe the action of building or constructing something, typically a physical structure such as a house, building, or monument. It can also be used metaphorically to describe the process of developing or improving something, such as relationships, skills, or knowledge.
