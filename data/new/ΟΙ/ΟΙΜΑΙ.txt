Etymology: The word "οἶμαι" (oimai) comes from the Ancient Greek verb "οἴομαι" (oíomai), which means "to think" or "to suppose."

Grammar: "Οἶμαι" is a first-person singular present indicative form of the verb "οἴομαι." It is used to express personal opinion or belief.

Description: "Οἶμαι" is a Greek word that is often translated as "I think" or "I suppose" in English. It is commonly used to express uncertainty or speculation.
