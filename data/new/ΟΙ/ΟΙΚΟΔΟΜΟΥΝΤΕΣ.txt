Grammar: "Οικοδομούντες" is the present participle form of the verb "οικοδομώ" in the third person plural. In Greek grammar, the present participle is used to describe ongoing actions or states. It can function as an adjective or as part of a verb phrase.

Description: "Οικοδομούντες" refers to a group of people who are currently building or constructing something. It can be used to describe workers, builders, or any individuals engaged in the act of building.
