Grammar: "οικοδομείν" is the present active infinitive form of the verb "οικοδομέω" (oikodomeo) in Greek. It belongs to the first conjugation class of Greek verbs and is used to express the action of building or constructing.

Description: "οικοδομείν" is a verb in Greek that specifically refers to the act of building or constructing something, usually a physical structure like a house or a building. It can also be used metaphorically to describe the process of developing or improving something, such as relationships, knowledge, or skills.
