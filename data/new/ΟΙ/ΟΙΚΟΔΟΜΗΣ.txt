Etymology: The word "οικοδομης" (oikodomēs) comes from the Greek roots "οικος" (oikos) meaning "house" and "δομη" (domē) meaning "building". 

Grammar: "Οικοδομης" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the singular form and belongs to the feminine gender. 

Description: "Οικοδομης" refers to the act of building or construction. It can also be used to describe the profession or occupation of a builder or constructor.
