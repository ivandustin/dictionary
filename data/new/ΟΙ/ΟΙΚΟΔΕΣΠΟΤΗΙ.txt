Etymology: The word "οικοδεσποτης" comes from the Greek words "οικος" (oikos) meaning "house" and "δεσποτης" (despotis) meaning "master" or "lord". 

Grammar: "Οικοδεσπότης" is a noun in the masculine gender. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their case, number, and gender. 

Description: "Οικοδεσπότης" refers to the host or the person who welcomes and takes care of guests in their home. It can also be used metaphorically to describe someone who is in charge or has authority over a certain domain or situation.
