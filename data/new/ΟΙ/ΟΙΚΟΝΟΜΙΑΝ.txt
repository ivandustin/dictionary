Grammar: "οικονομίαν" is the accusative form of the noun "οικονομία" (oikonomía) in Greek. It is a feminine noun and is declined according to the rules of Greek grammar.

Description: "οικονομίαν" refers to the concept of economy or the management of resources, whether it be on a household, business, or national level.
