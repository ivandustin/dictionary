Etymology: The word "οινοποτης" (oinopotes) comes from the Greek words "οινος" (oinos) meaning "wine" and "ποτης" (potes) meaning "drinker". 

Grammar: "Οινοποτης" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the masculine gender and the nominative case. 

Description: "Οινοποτης" refers to a person who drinks wine, a wine drinker, or a wine connoisseur.
