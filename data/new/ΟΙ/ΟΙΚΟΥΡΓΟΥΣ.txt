Etymology: The word "οικουργούς" comes from the Greek words "οίκος" (oikos) meaning "house" and "έργο" (ergo) meaning "work". 

Grammar: "Οικουργούς" is a noun in the masculine gender and plural number. It belongs to the second declension in Greek grammar.

Description: "Οικουργούς" refers to individuals who are skilled in various household tasks and chores. They are known for their ability to efficiently manage and maintain a household.
