Etymology: The word "οικία" (oikia) comes from the Greek word "οἶκος" (oikos), meaning "house" or "dwelling."

Grammar: "Οικία" is a feminine noun in Greek. It belongs to the first declension and its plural form is "οικίες" (oikies).

Description: "Οικία" refers to a house or a dwelling place. It can also be used to describe a household or a family. In a broader sense, it can refer to a home or a residence.
