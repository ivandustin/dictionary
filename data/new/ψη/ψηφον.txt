The word "ψηφον" (psiphon) is a Greek noun that means "vote" or "ballot". 

Etymology: The word comes from the Greek verb "ψηφίζω" (psiphizo), which means "to count" or "to vote". 

Grammar: "ψηφον" is a singular noun in the accusative case. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their grammatical function in a sentence. 

Description: The word "ψηφον" is commonly used in the context of elections or decision-making processes where people cast their votes. It can also refer to the physical ballot or the act of counting votes.
