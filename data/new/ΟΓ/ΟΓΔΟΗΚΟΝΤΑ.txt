Etymology: The word "ογδοηκοντα" (ogdoikonta) comes from the Greek words "ογδοος" (ogdoos) meaning "eight" and "κοντα" (konta) meaning "ten". It literally translates to "eighty".

Grammar: "Ογδοηκοντα" is a Greek numeral adjective that represents the number 80. It is in the masculine form and declines according to the gender, number, and case of the noun it modifies.

Description: "Ογδοηκοντα" is used to express the number 80 in Greek. It is commonly used in counting, measurements, and various other contexts where numerical values are required.
