Etymology: The word "εριθεία" (eritheia) comes from the Greek word "εριθεύς" (eritheus), which means "striving" or "ambitious."

Grammar: "Εριθεία" is a feminine noun in Greek. It is in the singular form.

Description: "Εριθεία" refers to the quality or characteristic of being ambitious, striving, or eager to achieve success. It can also imply a sense of competitiveness or a desire to outdo others.
