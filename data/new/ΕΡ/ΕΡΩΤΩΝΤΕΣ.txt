Etymology: The word "ερωτώντες" comes from the Greek verb "ερωτώ" (erotó), which means "to ask" or "to inquire". The suffix "-ντες" (-ntes) is added to the verb stem to form the present participle, indicating that the action is ongoing or in progress.

Grammar: "Ερωτώντες" is the present participle form of the verb "ερωτώ" (erotó) in Greek. It is used to describe people who are currently asking or inquiring about something. In Greek grammar, the present participle can function as an adjective or as part of a verb phrase.
