Grammar: "Ερωτώμεν" is the present tense, passive voice, and first person plural form of the verb "ερωτάω". In Greek grammar, verbs are conjugated based on tense, voice, mood, person, and number.

Description: The verb "ερωτώμεν" is used to express the act of asking or inquiring about something. It can be used in various contexts and can be conjugated to match different subjects and tenses.
