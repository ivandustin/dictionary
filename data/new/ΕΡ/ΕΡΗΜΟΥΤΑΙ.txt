Grammar: "Ερημούται" is the third person singular form of the verb "ερημόω" in the present tense. It is used to describe an action or state of being that is happening in the present and is performed by a third person.

Description: "Ερημούται" is a Greek word that means "he/she/it is deserted" or "he/she/it is abandoned." It is used to describe a person, place, or thing that has been left empty, uninhabited, or forsaken.
