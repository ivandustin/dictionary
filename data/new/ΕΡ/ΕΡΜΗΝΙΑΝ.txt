Etymology: The word "ερμηνιαν" comes from the Greek word "ερμηνεία" (ermeneía), which means "interpretation" or "explanation". 

Grammar: "Ερμηνιαν" is a noun in the singular form. It belongs to the third declension in Greek grammar. 

Description: "Ερμηνιαν" refers to the act or process of interpreting or explaining something. It can also refer to the result or outcome of interpretation. In general, it is used to describe the act of making something clear or understandable by providing an explanation or interpretation.
