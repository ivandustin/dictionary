Etymology: The word "ερωτησω" (erótiso) comes from the Greek verb "ερωτάω" (erotáo), which means "to ask" or "to inquire".

Grammar: "Ερωτησω" is the first person singular form of the future tense of the verb "ερωτάω" (erotáo). It is used when the speaker wants to express their intention to ask or inquire about something in the future.

Description: "Ερωτησω" is a Greek word that represents an action of asking or inquiring about something in the future. It is commonly used in everyday conversations and formal contexts to express the speaker's intention to seek information or clarification.
