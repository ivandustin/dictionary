Etymology: The word "ερχομένα" (erchomena) comes from the Greek verb "έρχομαι" (erchomai), which means "to come" or "to arrive".

Grammar: "Ερχόμενα" is the plural form of the present participle of the verb "έρχομαι" (erchomai). In Greek grammar, the present participle is used to form the continuous present tense and can also function as an adjective.

Description: "Ερχόμενα" is a Greek word that can be translated as "coming" or "arriving" in English. It is used to describe something that is currently in the process of coming or arriving. It can be used as a verb, adjective, or noun depending on the context.
