Etymology: The word "ερωταν" (erōtan) comes from the Greek verb "ερωτάω" (erōtaō), which means "to ask" or "to inquire".

Grammar: "Ερωταν" (erōtan) is the present tense, indicative mood, active voice, and first person singular form of the verb "ερωτάω" (erōtaō).

Description: "Ερωταν" (erōtan) is a Greek word that means "I ask" or "I inquire". It is used to express the act of asking a question or seeking information.
