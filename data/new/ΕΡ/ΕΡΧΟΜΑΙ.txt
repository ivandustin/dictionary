Grammar: "Ερχομαι" is a first-conjugation verb in Greek, belonging to the present tense. It is an irregular verb, as it undergoes stem changes in some forms. The conjugation of "ερχομαι" includes various forms such as ερχομαι (I come), ερχεσαι (you come), ερχεται (he/she/it comes), ερχομαστε (we come), ερχεστε (you come), and ερχονται (they come).

Description: "Ερχομαι" is a versatile verb that is commonly used in Greek to express the action of coming or going.
