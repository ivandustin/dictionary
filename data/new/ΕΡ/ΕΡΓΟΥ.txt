Etymology: The word "εργού" (ergou) comes from the Greek verb "εργάζομαι" (ergazomai), which means "to work" or "to labor."

Grammar: "Εργού" is the second person singular form of the verb "εργάζομαι" (ergazomai) in the present tense. It is used when addressing one person directly.

Description: "Εργού" is a Greek word that means "you work" or "you are working." It is used to express the action of working or laboring in the present tense when speaking to one person.
