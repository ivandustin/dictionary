The word "ερχομένου" (erchomenou) is a Greek word that is derived from the verb "έρχομαι" (erchomai), which means "to come" or "to arrive." It is the present participle form of the verb, which is used to describe an ongoing action or state of coming or arriving. In terms of grammar, "ερχομένου" is in the genitive case, which is used to indicate possession or origin. It can be translated as "of coming" or "of arriving" in English.
