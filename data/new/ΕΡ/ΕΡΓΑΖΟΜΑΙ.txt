Etymology: The word "εργαζομαι" (ergazomai) comes from the Ancient Greek word "ἐργάζομαι" (ergázomai), which means "to work" or "to labor".

Grammar: "Εργάζομαι" is a verb in the Greek language. It belongs to the first conjugation class and is conjugated in the present tense as follows:

- Εγώ εργάζομαι (ergázomai) - I work
- Εσύ εργάζεσαι (ergázesai) - You work (singular)
- Αυτός/Αυτή/Αυτό εργάζεται (ergázetai) - He/She/It works
- Εμείς εργαζόμαστε (ergazómaste) - We work
- Εσείς εργάζεστε (ergázeste) - You work (plural)
- Αυτοί/Αυτές εργάζονται (ergázonde) - They work

Description: "Εργάζομαι" is a common Greek verb that means "to work" or "to be employed". It is used to describe the action of engaging in labor or performing a job.
