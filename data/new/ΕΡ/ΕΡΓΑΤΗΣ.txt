Grammar: In Greek, "εργατης" is a masculine noun in the nominative case. It belongs to the second declension and has a stem ending in -ητ-. The plural form of this noun is "εργατες" (ergates).

Description: "Εργατης" is a Greek word that translates to "worker" or "laborer" in English. It refers to someone who performs physical or manual labor as part of their occupation.
