Etymology: The word "ερμηνεία" comes from the Greek verb "ερμηνεύω" (ermeneúo), which means "to interpret" or "to explain."

Grammar: "Ερμηνεία" is a feminine noun in Greek. It is in the singular form.

Description: "Ερμηνεία" refers to the act of interpreting or explaining something. It can also mean an interpretation or explanation itself. In various contexts, it can be used to describe the process of understanding and making sense of something, such as a text, a piece of art, or a situation.
