Etymology: The word "εργον" (ergon) comes from the Greek language and it means "work" or "action".

Grammar: In Greek, "εργον" is a noun of neuter gender. It can be used in both singular and plural forms.

Description: "Εργον" is a versatile word that can refer to various types of work or actions. It can be used to describe physical labor, tasks, or activities. It can also be used in a broader sense to refer to any kind of work, effort, or action, including intellectual or creative work.
