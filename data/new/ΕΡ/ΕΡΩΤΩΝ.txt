Etymology: The word "ερωτών" comes from the Greek verb "ερωτώ" (erotó), which means "to ask" or "to inquire".

Grammar: "Ερωτών" is the present participle form of the verb "ερωτώ" (erotó), which is used to form the continuous present tense. It is commonly used to describe an ongoing action of asking or inquiring.

Description: "Ερωτών" refers to someone who is currently asking or inquiring about something. It can be used to describe a person who is actively seeking information or clarification.
