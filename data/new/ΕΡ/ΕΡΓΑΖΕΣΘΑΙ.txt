The word "εργαζεσθαι" (ergazesthai) is a Greek verb that means "to work" or "to be employed." Here is a breakdown of the requested information:

Etymology: The word comes from the ancient Greek root "ἔργον" (ergon), which means "work" or "task," and the verb suffix "-ζεσθαι" (-zesthai), which indicates the middle voice in Greek verbs.

Grammar: "Εργαζεσθαι" is a present middle/passive infinitive form of the verb. In Greek, verbs have different forms depending on the voice (active, middle, or passive), tense, mood, and person.

Description: The verb "εργαζεσθαι" is used to express the action of working or being employed. It can refer to any kind of work or labor, whether physical or mental. The middle voice indicates that the subject is both the doer and the receiver of the action.
