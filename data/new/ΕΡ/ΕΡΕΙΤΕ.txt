Etymology: The word "ερειτε" comes from the Greek verb "ερείδω" (ereido), which means "to ask" or "to inquire".

Grammar: "Ερειτε" is the present tense, indicative mood, active voice, and second person plural form of the verb "ερείδω" (ereido). It is used when addressing a group of people and means "you ask" or "you inquire".

Description: "Ερειτε" is a verb in the Greek language that is used to express the action of asking or inquiring. It is commonly used in conversations and written texts to address a group of people and ask for information or clarification.
