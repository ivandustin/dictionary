Etymology: The word "εργων" (ergon) comes from the Greek verb "εργάζομαι" (ergázomai), which means "to work" or "to labor."

Grammar: "Εργων" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the masculine gender and the nominative case.

Description: "Εργων" refers to a work or a labor. It can be used to describe a physical or mental effort, a task, or an occupation.
