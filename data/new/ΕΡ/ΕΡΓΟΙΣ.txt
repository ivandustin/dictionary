Etymology: The word "εργοις" (ergois) comes from the Greek word "έργο" (ergo), which means "work" or "action". 

Grammar: "Εργοις" is a noun in the plural form. It belongs to the second declension in Greek grammar. 

Description: "Εργοις" refers to "works" or "actions" in Greek. It can be used to describe various types of activities or tasks that someone performs.
