Word: ρεραντισμένοι

Etymology:
The word ρεραντισμένοι is derived from the Greek verb ρεραντίζω (rerantízo), which means "to water, to sprinkle". The suffix -μένοι (-menoi) is a participle ending that indicates the passive voice and the present tense. Therefore, ρεραντισμένοι means "those who have been watered/sprinkled".
