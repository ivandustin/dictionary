Word: εφανερωσα

The aorist tense is used to describe a completed action in the past, while the active voice indicates that the subject of the verb is performing the action. The indicative mood is used to make a statement or ask a question.

Description: εφανερωσα means "I revealed" or "I made known" in English. It is a verb that describes the act of bringing something to light or making it known to others.
