Word: εφημερου

Etymology: The word εφημερου comes from the Greek word εφημερία (ephemería), which means "daily" or "diary". The suffix -ου (-ou) is added to the end of the word to indicate possession or belonging.

Grammar: εφημερου is a noun in the genitive case, which is used to indicate possession or belonging. It is often used in Greek to refer to a daily newspaper or a diary.

Description: εφημερου is a Greek word that refers to something that is daily or related to a diary. It can be used to describe a daily newspaper or a diary that is kept on a daily basis. In modern Greek, it is often used to refer to a daily planner or agenda.
