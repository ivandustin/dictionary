The word εφωνησεν is a Greek verb that comes from the root word φωνή (phoné), which means "voice" or "sound". The prefix ε- (e-) is a common Greek prefix that can indicate "out" or "away". Therefore, εφωνησεν can be translated as "he/she/it called out" or "he/she/it spoke out".
