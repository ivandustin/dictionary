The word εφανερωθη (efanerothi) is a Greek verb that comes from the root word φανερός (faneros), which means "visible" or "apparent". The verb is in the aorist tense and passive voice, and it means "was revealed" or "became apparent".

εφανερωθη is a third-person singular aorist passive indicative verb in Greek. The aorist tense indicates a completed action in the past, while the passive voice indicates that the subject received the action of the verb. In this case, the subject is not specified, but it is implied that something or someone was revealed or became apparent.
