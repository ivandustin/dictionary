Word: εφωνει

Etymology: The word εφωνει comes from the Greek verb φωνέω (phonéo), which means "to speak" or "to sound". The prefix ε- (e-) is added to the beginning of the verb to indicate the third person singular present tense.

Grammar: εφωνει is a third person singular present tense verb in Greek. It is conjugated from the verb φωνέω (phonéo), which is a regular verb in Greek. The verb is in the active voice and indicative mood.

Description: εφωνει means "he/she/it speaks" or "he/she/it is speaking" in Greek. It is commonly used in everyday conversation and can be used to describe someone who is currently speaking or someone who speaks regularly.
