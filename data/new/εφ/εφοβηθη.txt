Word: εφοβηθη

The word εφοβηθη (efovithi) is a verb in the Greek language, which is derived from the root word φοβος (fobos), meaning fear. The prefix ε- (e-) is added to the root word to indicate the past tense, making the word εφοβηθη (efovithi) mean "was afraid" or "became afraid".
