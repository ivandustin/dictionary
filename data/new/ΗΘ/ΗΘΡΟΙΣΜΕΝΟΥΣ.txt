Etymology: The word "ηθροισμένους" comes from the Greek verb "ηθροίζω" (ethroizo), which means "to gather" or "to assemble". The suffix "-μένος" (-menos) is added to the verb stem to form the past participle, indicating a state of being gathered or assembled.

Grammar: "Ηθροισμένους" is the masculine plural form of the past participle of the verb "ηθροίζω". It is used to describe a group of people who have been gathered or assembled.

Description: "Ηθροισμένους" is an adjective that describes a group of people who have come together or been brought together in one place. It implies a sense of unity or collective action.
