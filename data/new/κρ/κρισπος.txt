The word κρισπος comes from the Greek language. It is derived from the root word κρίνω (krinō), which means "to separate" or "to judge". The suffix -πος (-pos) is a common Greek suffix that is used to form adjectives. Therefore, κρισπος can be translated to mean "judgmental" or "discerning".

In Greek, κρισπος is an adjective that is used to describe a person or thing that is discerning or judgmental.
