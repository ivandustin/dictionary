The word "κρινειν" (krinein) is a Greek verb that means "to judge" or "to decide". It is derived from the Greek word "κρίσις" (krisis), which means "judgment" or "decision". In terms of grammar, "κρινειν" is a present active infinitive verb in Greek. It can be conjugated in various tenses and moods to indicate different aspects of judgment or decision-making. Overall, "κρινειν" is a fundamental word in Greek language and culture, as it reflects the importance of making sound judgments and decisions in life.
