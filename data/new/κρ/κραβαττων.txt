The word "κραβαττων" (kravatton) is a Greek noun that refers to a type of bed or couch. It is derived from the verb "κραβαττίζω" (kravattizo), which means "to lie down on a bed or couch." In terms of grammar, it is a masculine noun in the nominative singular form. The word can be described as a piece of furniture used for sleeping or resting, typically with a mattress and a frame.
