Word: κραζοντων

Grammar: κραζοντων is a present participle in the genitive plural form. In Greek, present participles are formed by adding the suffix -οντας (-ontas) to the stem of the verb. The genitive plural form indicates that the participle is modifying a noun in the genitive plural case.

Description: κραζοντων can be translated as "of those who are shouting" or "of those who are crying out". It is often used in the context of a crowd or group of people who are making noise or protesting. The word can also be used metaphorically to describe a situation where there is a lot of noise or commotion.
