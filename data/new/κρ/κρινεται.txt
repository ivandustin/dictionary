Word: κρινεται

Etymology: κρινεται is a present tense verb in Greek, derived from the root word κρίνω (krino), which means "to judge" or "to decide". 

Grammar: κρινεται is a third person singular present tense verb in Greek. It is in the passive voice, indicating that the subject is being acted upon rather than performing the action. 

Description: κρινεται can be translated to English as "is judged" or "is being judged". It is often used in a legal or judicial context, indicating that someone or something is being evaluated or assessed. It can also be used more broadly to indicate a general assessment or evaluation of a situation or person.
