Word: οθονία (othonia)

Etymology:
The word οθονία comes from the ancient Greek word "ὀθόνη" (othonē), which means "a sheet or a piece of cloth." In modern Greek, οθονία refers to a screen, usually a computer or television screen.

Grammar:
In Greek, οθονία is a feminine noun. Its plural form is οθόνιες (othonies).

Description:
Οθονία is a common word in modern Greek, used to refer to any type of screen. It can be found in various contexts, such as in technology, entertainment, and education. With the rise of digital media, οθονία has become an essential part of our daily lives.
