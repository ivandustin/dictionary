Word: εβασταζετο

Etymology: The word εβασταζετο comes from the Greek verb βαστάζω (bastazo), which means "to carry" or "to bear". The prefix ε- (e-) indicates the third person singular form of the verb in the imperfect tense.

Grammar: εβασταζετο is a third person singular form of the Greek verb βαστάζω (bastazo) in the imperfect tense. The imperfect tense is used to describe ongoing or repeated actions in the past.

Description: εβασταζετο can be translated to English as "he/she/it was carrying" or "he/she/it used to carry". The word implies that the action of carrying was ongoing or repeated in the past.
