Word: εβδομης

Etymology: The word εβδομης (ebdomis) comes from the Greek word επτά (epta) which means "seven". It is derived from the Proto-Indo-European root *septḿ̥ (seven).

Grammar: εβδομης is a feminine noun in the nominative singular form. It is the ordinal number for "seventh" in Greek.

Description: εβδομης is used to describe the position of something in a sequence or order, specifically as the seventh item. It can also be used to describe the seventh day of the week, which is Saturday in the Greek calendar.
