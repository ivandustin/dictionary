Word: εβαλαν

Etymology: The word εβαλαν comes from the Greek verb βάλλω (ballō), which means "to throw" or "to cast".

Grammar: εβαλαν is the third person plural past tense form of the verb βάλλω (ballō), which means "they threw" or "they cast".

Description: εβαλαν is a verb that describes an action of throwing or casting something. It is commonly used in everyday speech and writing in the Greek language. The word can be used in various contexts, such as in sports, cooking, or construction.
