Etymology: The word "σοφοίς" (sophois) comes from the Greek adjective "σοφός" (sophos), which means "wise" or "clever". 

Grammar: In Greek, "σοφοίς" is the dative plural form of the adjective "σοφός". It is used to indicate the indirect object or the recipient of an action.
