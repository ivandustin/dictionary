Etymology: The word "σοφων" (sophon) comes from the Greek word "σοφός" (sophos), which means "wise" or "clever".

Grammar: "Σοφων" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the masculine gender and the nominative case.

Description: "Σοφων" refers to a wise or clever person. It can also be used to describe someone who possesses knowledge, intelligence, or expertise in a particular field.
