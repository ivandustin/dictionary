Grammar: "Σοδομων" is a noun in the genitive case, singular number, masculine gender. It is the genitive form of the noun "Σόδομα" (Sódoma).

Description: "Σοδομων" refers to the city of Sodom, which is mentioned in the Bible. It is known for its wickedness and was destroyed by God according to the biblical account. The story of Sodom and Gomorrah is often used as a symbol of divine punishment for immorality.
