Etymology: The word "σοφοί" (sophoi) comes from the Greek adjective "σοφός" (sophos), which means "wise" or "clever". 

Grammar: "Σοφοί" is the plural form of the word "σοφός" in Greek. It is used to refer to a group of wise or knowledgeable individuals.

Description: "Σοφοί" is a term used in Greek to describe people who possess wisdom, knowledge, or expertise in a particular field. It can be used to refer to scholars, philosophers, or individuals who are considered wise and intelligent.
