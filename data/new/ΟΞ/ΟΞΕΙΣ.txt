Etymology: The word "οξείς" (oxeis) comes from the Greek adjective "οξύς" (oxys), which means "sharp" or "acidic".

Grammar: "Οξείς" is the plural form of the adjective "οξύς" (oxys) in Greek. It is used to describe multiple nouns that are sharp or acidic.
