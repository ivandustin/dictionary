τυπικως (typikos) is an adverb in Greek that means "typically" or "according to the norm". 

Etymology: The word τυπικως comes from the Greek word τυπικός (typikos), which means "typical" or "conforming to a type or pattern". 

Grammar: τυπικως is an adverb that modifies verbs, adjectives, or other adverbs. It does not change form based on gender, number, or case. 

Description: τυπικως is used to describe something that is done in a typical or customary way. It can also be used to indicate conformity to a standard or norm. For example, "Η συμπεριφορά του ήταν τυπικώς επαγγελματική" (His behavior was typically professional).
