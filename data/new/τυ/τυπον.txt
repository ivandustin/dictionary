The word "τυπον" (typon) is a Greek noun that means "a type" or "a model." It is derived from the Greek verb "τυποω" (typoō), which means "to strike" or "to impress." In grammar, it is classified as a masculine noun of the second declension. It can be used in various contexts, such as in printing, where it refers to a metal or wooden block with a raised letter or design used for printing.
