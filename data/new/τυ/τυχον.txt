τυχον (tychon) is a Greek adverb that means "by chance" or "perhaps". 

Etymology: The word τυχον comes from the Greek verb τυγχάνω (tygchano), which means "to happen" or "to chance upon". 

Grammar: τυχον is an adverb and does not change form based on gender, number, or case. 

Description: τυχον is often used to express uncertainty or possibility in a sentence. For example, "Τυχον θα πάω στο σινεμά απόψε" (Tychon tha pao sto sinema apopse) means "Perhaps I will go to the cinema tonight".
