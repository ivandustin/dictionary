τυχικός (tychikos) is an adjective in Greek that is derived from the word τύχη (tychi), meaning "luck" or "fortune". It is often used to describe something that is related to luck or chance. 

In terms of grammar, τυχικός is a masculine singular adjective in the nominative case. It can also be declined to match the gender, number, and case of the noun it modifies. 

Example sentences:
- Η τυχική επιτυχία δεν είναι πάντα εγγυημένη. (Tyhiki epitichia den einai panta eggyimeni.) - "Luck-based success is not always guaranteed."
- Ο τυχικός αριθμός στο λαχείο ήταν ο αριθμός μου! (O tychikos arithmos sto lacheio itan o arithmos mou!) - "The lucky number in the lottery was my number!"
