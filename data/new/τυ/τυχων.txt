τυχων (tychōn) is a Greek word that means "chance" or "luck". It is derived from the verb τυγχάνω (tygchánō), which means "to happen" or "to occur". In terms of grammar, τυχων is a masculine noun in the nominative singular form. It can also be declined in other cases and numbers, such as τυχόντος (tychóntos) in the genitive singular form. In Greek mythology, Tychon was a giant who fought against the gods.
