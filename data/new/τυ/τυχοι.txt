Word: τυχοι

Etymology:
The word τυχοι (tychoi) is derived from the Greek word τύχη (tyche), which means "chance" or "luck". It is a plural form of the noun τύχη.

τυχοι is a noun in the nominative plural form in the Greek language. It belongs to the second declension of Greek nouns.

Description:
τυχοι is a word that refers to "chances" or "luck" in the Greek language. It is often used in the context of fate or destiny, and can also refer to random events or circumstances.
