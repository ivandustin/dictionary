The word φρονησετε comes from the Greek verb φρονέω (phronéō), which means "to think, to have understanding, to be wise." The suffix -σετε (-sete) is the second person plural ending for the imperative mood, which is used to give commands or orders.

φρονησετε is a second person plural imperative verb in Greek. It is used to give a command or order to a group of people. The verb is formed by adding the suffix -σετε to the stem φρονε-, which is derived from the verb φρονέω.
