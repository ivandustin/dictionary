Etymology: 
The word φρονοῦντες comes from the Greek verb φρονέω (phroneo), which means "to think, to have understanding, to be of a certain mind or opinion." The suffix -οῦντες (-ountes) is a present participle ending, indicating that the word refers to people who are currently engaged in the action of thinking or having a certain mindset.
