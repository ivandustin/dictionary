In terms of grammar, "σκευει" is in the present tense and active voice. It is conjugated in the third person singular form, which means that it refers to a single subject performing the action of the verb. 

The word "σκευει" can be translated to mean "he/she/it prepares" or "he/she/it makes ready". It is often used in the context of preparing or arranging something, such as a meal or a room. 

Overall, "σκευει" is a versatile verb that can be used in a variety of situations where preparation or arrangement is involved.
