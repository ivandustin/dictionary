Word: σκοτισθησεται

Etymology: The word σκοτισθησεται is derived from the Greek verb σκοτίζω (skotízo), which means "to darken" or "to obscure". The suffix -θησεται (-thésetai) is a passive future tense ending, indicating that the subject will be darkened or obscured in the future.

Grammar: σκοτισθησεται is a third person singular passive future indicative form of the verb σκοτίζω (skotízo). It can be translated as "he/she/it will be darkened/obscured".

Description: σκοτισθησεται is a Greek verb form that describes a future action of being darkened or obscured. It can be used in various contexts, such as describing the darkening of the sky during a storm or the obscuring of a person's vision due to an illness.
