Word: σκοπούντες

Etymology: The word σκοπούντες comes from the Greek verb σκοπέω (skopéo), which means "to look at, to observe, to aim at". The suffix -ούντες (-ountes) is a present participle ending, indicating that the action of the verb is ongoing.

Grammar: σκοπούντες is a present participle in the active voice, masculine gender, plural number. It is derived from the verb σκοπέω, which is a first conjugation verb. The present participle is formed by adding the suffix -ούντες to the stem of the verb.

Description: σκοπούντες can be translated as "aiming, looking at, observing". It describes a group of people who are currently engaged in the act of aiming or observing something. For example, in the sentence "Οι στρατιώτες σκοπούντες τους εχθρούς" (The soldiers are aiming at the enemies), σκοπούντες indicates that the soldiers are currently aiming at their enemies.
