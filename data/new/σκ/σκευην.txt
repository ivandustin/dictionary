The word "σκευην" (skeuen) is a Greek noun that means "vessel" or "container". It is derived from the Greek word "σκεῦος" (skeuos), which also means "vessel" or "container". 

In terms of grammar, "σκευην" is in the accusative case, which is used to indicate the direct object of a verb. 

In description, "σκευην" can refer to any type of container or vessel, such as a jar, pot, or pitcher. It can also be used metaphorically to refer to a person's body as a vessel for their soul or spirit.
