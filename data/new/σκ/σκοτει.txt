The word "σκοτει" (skotei) is a Greek verb that means "to darken" or "to obscure". It comes from the Greek word "σκότος" (skotos), which means "darkness" or "obscurity". In terms of grammar, "σκοτει" is a present tense verb in the third person singular form. It can be used to describe the act of making something dark or obscure, or to describe a situation that is unclear or difficult to understand.
