The word "σκληρον" (skleron) is a Greek adjective that means "hard" or "difficult". It is derived from the Greek word "σκληρός" (skleros), which also means "hard" or "harsh". In terms of grammar, "σκληρον" is a neuter singular form of the adjective. It can be used to describe physical objects, as well as abstract concepts or emotions.
