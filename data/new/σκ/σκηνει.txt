The word "σκηνει" (skenei) is a verb in Greek that means "to dwell" or "to reside". 

Etymology: The word comes from the Greek root "σκηνή" (skene), which means "tent" or "shelter". In ancient times, people would live in tents, and the word "σκηνει" was used to describe the act of dwelling in a tent.

Grammar: "σκηνει" is a present tense verb in the third person singular form. It can be conjugated in different tenses and persons to indicate different actions and subjects.

Description: The word "σκηνει" is often used in the context of temporary dwelling or staying in a place for a short period of time. It can also be used metaphorically to describe a person's state of mind or emotions, such as "dwelling on a thought" or "residing in sadness".
