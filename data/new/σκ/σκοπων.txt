Word: σκοπων

Etymology:
The word σκοπων (skopon) is derived from the Greek verb σκοπέω (skopeo), which means "to look at, to observe, to examine." The suffix -ων (-on) is a masculine noun ending in the nominative singular form.

Grammar:
σκοπων (skopon) is a masculine noun in the nominative singular form. It belongs to the second declension in Greek grammar.

Description:
σκοπων (skopon) refers to a person who observes or examines something closely. It can also refer to a goal or objective that someone is aiming for. In modern Greek, the word σκοπων (skopon) is often used in the context of photography, where it refers to the viewfinder or the lens of a camera.
