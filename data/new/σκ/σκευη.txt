Word: σκευη

Etymology:
The word σκευη (skeue) is derived from the Greek word σκεῦος (skeuos), which means "vessel" or "container". It is related to the verb σκεύω (skeuo), which means "to prepare" or "to equip".

Grammar:
σκευη is a plural noun in Greek. It belongs to the third declension and has a feminine gender. The nominative singular form of the word is σκεῦος (skeuos).

Description:
σκευη refers to "vessels" or "containers" used for holding or carrying things. It can refer to a wide range of objects, from simple household items like bowls and jars to more complex tools and equipment used in various industries. The word is commonly used in the context of cooking and food preparation, as well as in the fields of medicine, chemistry, and manufacturing.
