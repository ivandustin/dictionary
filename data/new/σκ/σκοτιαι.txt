The word "σκοτιαι" (skotiai) is a plural noun in Greek that means "darkness" or "obscurity". It is derived from the adjective "σκοτεινός" (skoteinos), which means "dark" or "gloomy". In terms of grammar, it is a feminine noun in the nominative case. It can be used to describe physical darkness or metaphorical obscurity, such as a lack of understanding or knowledge.
