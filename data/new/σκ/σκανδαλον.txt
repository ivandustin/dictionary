Word: σκανδαλον

Etymology:
The word σκανδαλον (skandalon) comes from the Greek root word σκανδαλεύω (skandaleuo), which means "to cause to stumble" or "to trip up." In ancient Greek, the word was used to refer to a trap or snare that would cause someone to stumble or fall.
