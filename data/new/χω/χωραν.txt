The word "χωραν" (chóran) is a Greek noun that means "country" or "region". It is derived from the Greek word "χώρα" (chóra), which means "land" or "territory". 

In terms of grammar, "χωραν" is in the accusative case, which is used to indicate the direct object of a verb. For example, in the sentence "Πηγαίνω στη χώραν" (Pigaino sti chóran), which means "I am going to the country", "χωραν" is the direct object of the verb "πηγαίνω" (pigaino).

Overall, "χωραν" is a simple and straightforward noun that is commonly used in Greek to refer to a country or region.
