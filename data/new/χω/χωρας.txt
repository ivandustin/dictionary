The word "χωρας" (chōras) is a Greek noun that means "country" or "region". It is derived from the Greek verb "χωρέω" (chōreō), which means "to make room" or "to give space". In terms of grammar, "χωρας" is a second declension feminine noun, with the genitive form being "χώρας" (chōras).
