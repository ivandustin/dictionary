Etymology: The word "οσμη" (osmē) comes from the Ancient Greek word "ὀσμή" (osmḗ), meaning "smell" or "odor".

Grammar: In Greek, "οσμη" (osmē) is a feminine noun. It is declined as follows:

- Singular: η οσμή (i osmē) - the smell
- Plural: οι οσμές (oi osmés) - the smells

Description: "Οσμη" (osmē) refers to the sense of smell or the perception of odors. It can also be used to describe a specific smell or odor.
