The word "νεκρα" (nekra) is a feminine noun in Greek that means "dead bodies" or "corpses". It is derived from the Greek word "νεκρός" (nekros), which means "dead" or "lifeless". In terms of grammar, "νεκρα" is in the nominative plural form. It can be used to describe a group of deceased individuals or as a reference to the state of being dead.
