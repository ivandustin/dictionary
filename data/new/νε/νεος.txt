Word: νεος

νεος is a Greek word that means "new" or "young". It is derived from the Proto-Indo-European root *newo-, which means "new" or "young".

νεος is an adjective in Greek, and it has three genders: masculine, feminine, and neuter. It declines according to the gender, number, and case of the noun it modifies.

Description:
νεος is a versatile word that can be used to describe anything that is new or young. It can refer to a person, an object, an idea, or a concept.
