Grammar: "Πυθεσθαι" is the present middle/passive infinitive form of the verb "πυθομαι" in Greek. It is used to express the action of inquiring or asking in the present tense, and it can be used in both the middle and passive voice.

Description: "Πυθεσθαι" is a Greek verb that is often translated as "to inquire" or "to ask". It is commonly used in the context of seeking information or knowledge. The verb can be used in various tenses, moods, and voices to convey different shades of meaning.
