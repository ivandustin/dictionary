Etymology: The word "πυραν" (pyran) comes from the Greek word "πῦρ" (pyr), meaning "fire". 

Grammar: "Πυραν" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the singular form and is classified as a feminine noun. 

Description: "Πυραν" refers to a fire or a flame. It can be used to describe the visible, burning part of a fire.
