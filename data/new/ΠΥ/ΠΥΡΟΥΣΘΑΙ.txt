Etymology: The word "πυρούσθαι" (pyrousthai) comes from the Greek verb "πύρω" (pyro), meaning "to burn". 

Grammar: "πυρούσθαι" is a verb in the middle voice, present tense, indicative mood, and third person singular form. It is derived from the verb "πύρω" (pyro) and belongs to the thematic conjugation.

Description: "πυρούσθαι" is an ancient Greek verb that means "to be burned". It is used to describe the action of being consumed by fire or undergoing combustion.
