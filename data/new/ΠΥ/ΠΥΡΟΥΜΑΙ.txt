Grammar: "Πυρούμαι" is a first-person singular present middle/passive indicative form of the verb "πύρω" (pyro). It is used to express the action of burning in the present tense, with the subject being the speaker or the object being affected by the action.

Description: "Πυρούμαι" is a Greek verb that specifically refers to the act of being burned or being on fire. It can be used to describe the state of something that is currently burning or the experience of being burned.
