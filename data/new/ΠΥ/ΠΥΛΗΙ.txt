Etymology: The word "πυλη" (pylē) comes from the Ancient Greek word "πύλη" (pulē), meaning "gate" or "entrance".

Grammar: In Greek, "πυλη" (pylē) is a feminine noun. It belongs to the third declension and has the nominative singular form "πυλη" (pylē), the genitive singular form "πυλης" (pylēs), and the accusative singular form "πυλην" (pylēn).

Description: "Πυλη" (pylē) refers to a gate or an entrance, typically used to enter or exit a building, city, or enclosed space. It can also be used metaphorically to represent an opening or access point to something.
