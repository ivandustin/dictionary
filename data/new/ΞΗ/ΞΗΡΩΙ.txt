Etymology: The word "ξηρων" (xērōn) comes from the Greek verb "ξηραίνω" (xērainō), which means "to dry". 

Grammar: ξηρων is a noun in the nominative case, singular form. 

Description: ξηρων refers to something that is dry or arid. It can be used to describe a dry climate, land, or any other object or substance that lacks moisture.
