The word "γνοντα" is a form of the Greek verb "γιγνώσκω" (gignósko), which means "to know" or "to understand". It is in the present participle form, which indicates ongoing action or a state of being. The word is commonly used in Greek literature and philosophy to describe the process of gaining knowledge or understanding. In terms of grammar, "γνοντα" is in the accusative case, which is used to indicate the direct object of a verb.
