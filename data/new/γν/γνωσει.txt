The word "γνωσει" (gnosei) is a Greek verb in the future tense, meaning "will know" or "will understand". 

Etymology: The word comes from the Greek root "γνω-" (gno-), meaning "to know" or "to understand". 

Grammar: "γνωσει" is a second-person singular future tense verb, meaning it is used to describe an action that will happen in the future and is directed towards one person. 

Description: The word "γνωσει" is commonly used in Greek language to express the idea of gaining knowledge or understanding about something in the future. It can be used in a variety of contexts, such as in personal conversations, academic discussions, or philosophical debates.
