Word: γνωρισουσιν

Etymology:
The word γνωρισουσιν is a verb form in the Greek language, specifically in the future tense. It is derived from the root word γνωρίζω (gnorízo), which means "to make known" or "to introduce." The suffix -ουσιν (-ousin) indicates the third person plural future tense.

Grammar:
As mentioned, γνωρισουσιν is a verb form in the Greek language. It is in the future tense and is conjugated for the third person plural. The verb stem is γνωρίζω (gnorízo), and the suffix -ουσιν (-ousin) is added to indicate the future tense and third person plural.

Description:
The word γνωρισουσιν is a future tense verb form that means "they will make known" or "they will introduce" in Greek. It is often used in the context of introducing people or ideas to others. The word is commonly used in formal settings, such as business or academic environments, where introductions are important.
