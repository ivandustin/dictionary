Word: γνωμης

Etymology:
The word γνωμης (gnomis) is derived from the Greek word γνώμη (gnómi), which means "opinion" or "judgment". It is a noun in the genitive case, which indicates possession or relationship to another noun.

Grammar:
γνωμης is a noun in the genitive case, which is used to show possession or relationship to another noun. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their grammatical function in a sentence. The genitive case is often used to indicate possession, origin, or relationship.

γνωμης is a Greek word that means "of opinion" or "of judgment". It is often used in the context of expressing one's opinion or giving advice.
