Word: γνωσονται

Etymology:
γνωσονται is a Greek verb in the future tense, third person plural form of the verb γιγνώσκω (ginósko), which means "to know" or "to recognize". The word comes from the Proto-Indo-European root *ǵneh₃- which means "to know".

Grammar:
γνωσονται is a future tense verb in the third person plural form. In Greek, verbs change their endings to indicate tense, person, and number. The future tense is used to describe actions that will happen in the future.

Description:
γνωσονται is a verb that indicates that a group of people will know or recognize something in the future. The context of the sentence will determine what exactly they will know or recognize. For example, "Οι φίλοι μου γνωσονται τον δρόμο προς το σπίτι μου" (My friends will recognize the way to my house).
