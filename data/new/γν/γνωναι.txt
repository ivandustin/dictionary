The word "γνωναι" (gnonai) is a Greek verb in the infinitive form, which means "to know" or "to understand". It is derived from the Greek root word "γνω-" (gno-), which means "to know". In terms of grammar, it belongs to the aorist tense, which is used to describe a completed action in the past. 

In Greek, there are several different tenses and forms of verbs, each with their own specific uses and nuances. The aorist tense is often used to describe a single, completed action in the past, without any ongoing or continuing effects. 

Overall, "γνωναι" is a common and important verb in Greek, used to express knowledge, understanding, and comprehension.
