The word "γνους" (gnous) is a Greek noun that means "knowledge" or "understanding". It is derived from the Greek verb "γιγνώσκω" (gignosko), which means "to know" or "to understand". In terms of grammar, "γνους" is a masculine noun in the nominative case. It can also be declined in other cases, such as the genitive "γνώσεως" (gnoseos) or the accusative "γνῶσιν" (gnosin). Overall, "γνους" is a fundamental concept in Greek philosophy and is often associated with wisdom and intellectual pursuits.
