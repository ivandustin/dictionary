Word: γνωρίσω

Etymology:
The word γνωρίσω (gnoríso) is derived from the Greek verb γνωρίζω (gnorízo), which means "to make known" or "to introduce." It comes from the root word γνῶσις (gnósis), which means "knowledge" or "understanding."

γνωρίσω is a first-person singular future tense verb in Greek. It is conjugated from the root verb γνωρίζω (gnorízo), which is a regular verb in the Greek language. The verb stem is γνωρίσ-, and the future tense suffix is -ω.

γνωρίσω is a Greek verb that means "I will introduce" or "I will make known." It is often used in social situations when introducing someone to another person or group.
