The word γνωστοι (gnostoi) is derived from the Greek word γνῶσις (gnosis), which means knowledge or understanding. The suffix -τοι (-toi) is a plural ending, indicating that the word refers to a group of people who possess knowledge or understanding.

γνωστοι is a plural noun in the nominative case. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their grammatical function in a sentence. The nominative case is used for the subject of a sentence or the predicate nominative, which renames the subject.

Description:
γνωστοι is a Greek word that means "known" or "famous." It is often used to describe people who are well-known or recognized for their achievements or accomplishments. The word can also be used to describe something that is familiar or recognized by many people.
