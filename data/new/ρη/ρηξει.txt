The word "ρηξει" (ríxi) is a verb in the future tense in the Greek language. It comes from the root word "ρήγνυμι" (rígnymi), which means "to break" or "to shatter". In its future tense form, "ρηξει" means "he/she/it will break" or "he/she/it will shatter". 

In terms of grammar, "ρηξει" is a third-person singular verb in the future tense. It is conjugated from the stem "ρηξ-", with the addition of the future tense ending "-ει". 

Overall, "ρηξει" is a simple yet powerful verb that conveys the idea of breaking or shattering something in the future.
