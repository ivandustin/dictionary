Etymology: The word "μισθώτων" comes from the Greek word "μισθός" (misthós), meaning "wage" or "salary", and the suffix "-ωτος" (-ōtos), which denotes a person or thing related to the root word.

Grammar: "μισθώτων" is a noun in the plural form. It belongs to the second declension in Greek grammar.

Description: "μισθώτων" refers to "tenants" or "renters" in Greek. It is used to describe individuals or groups of people who rent or lease a property from someone else.
