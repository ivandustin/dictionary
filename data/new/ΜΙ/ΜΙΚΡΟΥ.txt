Etymology: The word "μικρού" (mikroú) comes from the Greek adjective "μικρός" (mikrós), meaning "small" or "little".

Grammar: "Μικρού" (mikroú) is the genitive form of the adjective "μικρός" (mikrós). In Greek grammar, the genitive case is used to indicate possession or origin.

Description: "Μικρού" (mikroú) is a Greek word that means "of small" or "of little". It is used to describe something that belongs to or is related to something small or little.
