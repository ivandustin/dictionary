Word: οψεται

Etymology: οψεται is a Greek verb in the future tense, third person singular form of the verb οράω (orao), which means "to see" or "to perceive".

Grammar: οψεται is a future tense verb in the third person singular form. In Greek, verbs change their endings to indicate tense, person, and number. The future tense indicates an action that will happen in the future.

Description: οψεται can be translated to "he/she/it will see" or "he/she/it will perceive". It is a common verb in Greek and is used in various contexts, such as in everyday conversation, literature, and religious texts.
