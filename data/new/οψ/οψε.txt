Word: οψε

Etymology: The word οψε comes from the Greek word "ὄψις" (opsis), which means "sight" or "vision".

Grammar: οψε is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the singular form and is feminine in gender.

Description: οψε refers to the act of seeing or the sense of sight. It can also refer to a vision or a sight that is seen. In modern Greek, it is often used in the phrase "με την οψη του καιρου" (me tin opsi tou kairou), which means "with the appearance of the weather".
