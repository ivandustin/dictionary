Word: οψιας

Etymology: The word οψιας comes from the Greek word οψία, which means "evening" or "dusk". 

Grammar: οψιας is a noun in the genitive case, which is used to show possession or relationship. It is the genitive singular form of the noun οψία. 

Description: οψιας refers to something that belongs to or is related to the evening or dusk. It can be used to describe the time of day, the light or colors of the sky during that time, or anything else that is associated with the evening.
