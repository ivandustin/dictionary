Etymology: The word "δωσει" (dosei) comes from the Greek verb "δίνω" (díno), which means "to give."

Grammar: "Δωσει" (dosei) is the third person singular future tense form of the verb "δίνω" (díno). It is used to express an action that will happen in the future.
