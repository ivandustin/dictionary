Etymology: The word "δωσουσιν" comes from the Greek verb "δίδωμι" (didōmi), which means "to give". It is in the future tense, third person plural form.

Grammar: "δωσουσιν" is a verb in the future tense, third person plural form. In Greek grammar, verbs change their endings to indicate tense, mood, voice, and person. The future tense indicates an action that will happen in the future. The third person plural form indicates that the subject of the verb is a group of people or things.

Description: "δωσουσιν" is used to express the action of giving in the future tense. It can be translated as "they will give" or "they will be giving" in English, depending on the context.
