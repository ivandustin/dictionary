Etymology: The word "δωρον" (doron) comes from the Greek language and means "gift" or "present".

Grammar: In Greek, "δωρον" is a noun of neuter gender. It is in the singular form.

Description: "Δωρον" refers to a gift or present that is given to someone as an expression of goodwill, love, or appreciation. It can be a physical object, money, or an intangible gesture. The act of giving a gift is often associated with special occasions, celebrations, or as a way to show gratitude.
