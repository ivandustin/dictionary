Etymology: The word "γραψης" (grapsis) comes from the Greek verb "γράφω" (grapho), which means "to write". 

Grammar: "Γραψης" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the nominative case and singular number. 

Description: "Γραψης" refers to a person who writes or a writer. It can also be used to describe someone who is skilled in writing or has a profession related to writing, such as an author or a scribe.
