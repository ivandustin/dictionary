Etymology: The word "γραμμασιν" (grammasin) comes from the Greek word "γράμμα" (gramma), meaning "letter" or "writing". The suffix "-σιν" (-sin) is a plural form.

Grammar: "Γραμμασιν" is a noun in the plural form. In Greek grammar, nouns have different forms depending on their gender, number, and case. The plural form indicates that there are multiple letters or writings.

Description: "Γραμμασιν" refers to letters or writings in general. It can be used to describe a collection of letters or written documents.
