Etymology: The word "γραφε" (graphe) comes from the Greek verb "γράφω" (grapho), which means "to write" or "to draw".

Grammar: "Γραφε" (graphe) is a verb in the imperative form. It is the second person singular form of the verb "γράφω" (grapho), which means "write" or "draw".

Description: "Γραφε" (graphe) is a command or instruction to write or draw something. It is used to tell someone to put something in writing or to create a drawing.
