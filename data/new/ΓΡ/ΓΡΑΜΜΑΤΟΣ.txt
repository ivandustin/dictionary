Grammar: "Γραμματος" (grammatos) is a noun in the genitive case. In Greek grammar, the genitive case is used to indicate possession, origin, or relationship.

Description: "Γραμματος" (grammatos) refers to something that is related to writing or letters. It can be used to describe a written document, a letter of the alphabet, or anything else that is associated with writing.
