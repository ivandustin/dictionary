The word "ιππου" (ip-pou) is a Greek noun that means "of a horse" or "belonging to a horse". It is derived from the Greek word "ιππος" (ip-pos), which means "horse". In terms of grammar, "ιππου" is in the genitive case, which is used to show possession or relationship. 

Example sentence: Το καλάθι του ιππού είναι γεμάτο από φορτία. (To kalathi tou ip-pou einai gemato apo for-tia) - The horse's basket is full of loads.
