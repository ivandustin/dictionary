Grammar: "βιωτικαίς" is a feminine plural form of the adjective "βιωτικός" (biōtikos), which means "pertaining to life" or "related to living organisms". In Greek grammar, adjectives agree in gender, number, and case with the nouns they modify. In this case, "βιωτικαίς" is used to describe feminine plural nouns.

Description: "βιωτικαίς" is used to describe things that are related to or associated with life or living organisms. It can be used in various contexts, such as biology, ecology, or any field that deals with the study of living organisms.
