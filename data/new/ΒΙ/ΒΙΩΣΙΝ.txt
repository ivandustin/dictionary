Etymology: The word "βιωσιν" (biōsin) comes from the Greek verb "βιῶ" (biō), which means "to live" or "to experience."

Grammar: "Βιωσιν" is a noun in the accusative case, singular number, and feminine gender in Greek.

Description: "Βιωσιν" refers to the act of living or experiencing life. It can also be translated as "life" or "way of life."
