Etymology: The word "βιωσαί" (pronounced "viosai") comes from the Greek verb "βιώνω" (pronounced "viono"), which means "to live" or "to experience". 

Grammar: "Βιωσαί" is the 2nd person singular imperative form of the verb "βιώνω". It is used to give a command or instruction to someone, telling them to live or experience something.

Description: "Βιωσαί" is a Greek word that encourages someone to live or experience something. It can be used in various contexts, such as motivating someone to fully embrace life, encouraging them to try new things, or urging them to make the most out of their experiences.
