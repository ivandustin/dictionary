Etymology: The word "ατερ" comes from the Greek prefix "a-" which means "not" or "without", and the Greek word "τερμα" (terma) which means "limit" or "boundary". 

Grammar: "Ατερ" is a masculine singular adjective in the nominative case. It can be declined to match the gender, number, and case of the noun it modifies.
