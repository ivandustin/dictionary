The word "ατενισαι" (atenisai) is a verb in the Greek language. 

Etymology: The word comes from the Greek verb "ατενίζω" (atenizo), which means "to gaze intently" or "to stare". 

Grammar: "ατενισαι" is in the aorist tense, which indicates a completed action in the past. It is also in the active voice, meaning the subject of the sentence is performing the action. 

Description: The word "ατενισαι" means "to stare intently" or "to gaze fixedly". It implies a focused and concentrated attention on something or someone. It can also be used figuratively to describe a deep contemplation or reflection on a subject.
