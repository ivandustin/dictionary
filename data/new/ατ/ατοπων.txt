The word "ατοπων" is a Greek adjective that means "out of place" or "inappropriate". 

Etymology: The word is derived from the Greek prefix "a-" meaning "not" or "without", and the Greek noun "τόπος" meaning "place". 

Grammar: "ατοπων" is in the masculine plural form, and it agrees with the noun it modifies in gender, number, and case. 

Description: The word is often used to describe something that is not fitting or suitable for a particular situation or context. It can also be used to describe behavior or actions that are considered improper or unacceptable.
