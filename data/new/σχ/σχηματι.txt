The word "σχηματι" (skhēmati) is a Greek noun that means "form" or "shape". 

Etymology: The word comes from the Greek verb "σχηματίζω" (skhēmatizō), which means "to give form or shape to". 

Grammar: "Σχηματι" is a neuter noun in the singular form. 

Description: The word is often used in the context of art, geometry, and philosophy to refer to the external appearance or structure of something. It can also be used metaphorically to describe the outward appearance or behavior of a person or thing.
