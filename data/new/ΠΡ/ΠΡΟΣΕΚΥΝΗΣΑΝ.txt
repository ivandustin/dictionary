It is derived from the combination of the preposition "πρός" (prós), meaning "towards," and the verb "κυνέω" (kynéo), meaning "to kiss" or "to bow down."

Description: "Προσεκύνησαν" is a Greek word that translates to "they worshiped" or "they bowed down before."
