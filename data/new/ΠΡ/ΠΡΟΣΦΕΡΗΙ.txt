Etymology: The word "προσφέρης" (prosferis) comes from the Greek verb "προσφέρω" (prosfero), which means "to offer" or "to present". 

Grammar: "Προσφέρης" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the masculine gender and the nominative case. 

Description: "Προσφέρης" refers to a person who offers or presents something. It can be used to describe someone who gives a gift, makes a donation, or provides assistance.
