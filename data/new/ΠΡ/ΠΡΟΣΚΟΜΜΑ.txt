Etymology: The word "προσκομμα" (proskomma) comes from the Greek verb "προσκόπτω" (proskopto), which means "to stumble" or "to trip over". 

Grammar: "Προσκομμα" is a noun in the Greek language. It is a neuter noun and its plural form is "προσκομματα" (proskommata).

Description: "Προσκομμα" refers to an obstacle or a stumbling block. It can be used both literally, to describe a physical obstruction that causes someone to trip or stumble, and figuratively, to describe a difficulty or hindrance in achieving a goal or making progress.
