Etymology: The word "προσκαιροι" comes from the Greek language. It is a plural form of the adjective "προσκαιρος" (proskairos), which means "temporary" or "transient."

Grammar: In Greek, "προσκαιροι" is the plural form of the adjective "προσκαιρος." It is used to describe multiple temporary or transient things or individuals.

Description: "Προσκαιροι" is an adjective that describes something or someone as temporary or transient. It implies that the subject is not permanent and will eventually change or cease to exist.
