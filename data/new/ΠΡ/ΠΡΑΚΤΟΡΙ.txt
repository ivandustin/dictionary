Etymology: The word "πρακτορι" (praktori) comes from the Greek word "πράκτωρ" (praktor), which means "agent" or "representative". 

Grammar: "Πρακτορι" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the singular form and belongs to the neuter gender. 

Description: "Πρακτορι" refers to an agent or representative who acts on behalf of someone else. It can be used in various contexts, such as a business agent, a secret agent, or a representative of an organization.
