Grammar: "Προσφερειν" is a verb in the Greek language. It is in the present tense, active voice, and infinitive mood. 

Description: "Προσφερειν" means "to offer" in English. It is used to express the action of presenting or giving something to someone as a gift, sacrifice, or contribution.
