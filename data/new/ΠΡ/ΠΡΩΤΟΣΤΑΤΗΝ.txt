Etymology: The word "πρωτοστατην" (pronounced "protostatin") comes from the Greek words "πρῶτος" (protos) meaning "first" and "στατήρ" (statēr) meaning "one who stands". 

Grammar: "Πρωτοστατην" is a noun in the masculine gender. It is in the singular form.

Description: "Πρωτοστατην" refers to a person who takes the lead or stands first in a particular situation or organization. It can also be used to describe someone who is a pioneer or a trailblazer in their field.
