Etymology: The word "προδραμών" (pronounced prodramón) comes from the Greek language. It is a compound word formed by combining the prefix "προ-" (pro-), meaning "before" or "in front of," and the verb "δραμών" (dramón), meaning "running." 

Grammar: "Προδραμών" is a noun in the masculine gender, singular number, and nominative case. In Greek grammar, nouns change their form based on gender, number, and case. 

Description: The word "προδραμών" refers to someone or something that runs ahead or goes before others. It can be used metaphorically to describe a person who takes the lead or acts in advance of others.
