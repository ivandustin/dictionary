Together, they form the verb "προσκυνήσετε," which conveys the act of showing reverence or worship towards someone or something.

Grammar: "Προσκυνήσετε" is the second person plural form of the future tense in Greek. It is used when addressing a group of people and instructing them to worship or bow down in reverence.
