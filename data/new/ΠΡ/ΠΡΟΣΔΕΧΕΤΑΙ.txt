Etymology: The word "προσδέχεται" (prosdechetai) comes from the Greek verb "προσδέχομαι" (prosdechomai), which means "to welcome" or "to receive." It is derived from the combination of the preposition "προς" (pros), meaning "towards," and the verb "δέχομαι" (dechomai), meaning "to accept" or "to receive."

Grammar: "Προσδέχεται" is the third person singular form of the present tense in Greek. It is used to describe an action that is happening in the present and is performed by a third person singular subject. In this case, it means "he/she/it welcomes" or "he/she/it receives."
