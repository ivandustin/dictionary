Etymology: The word "προσφοράς" (prosforás) comes from the Greek verb "προσφέρω" (prosféro), which means "to offer" or "to present".

Grammar: "Προσφοράς" is a noun in the genitive case, singular form. In Greek grammar, the genitive case is used to indicate possession, origin, or relationship.

Description: "Προσφοράς" refers to an offering or a gift that is given to someone. It can also be used to describe an act of presenting or offering something to someone as a gesture of goodwill or respect.
