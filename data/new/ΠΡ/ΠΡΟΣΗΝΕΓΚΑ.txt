Etymology: The word "προσηνεγκα" (prosinegka) comes from the Greek verb "προσφέρω" (prosfero), which means "to offer" or "to present".

Grammar: "Προσηνεγκα" is the first person singular form of the aorist tense of the verb "προσφέρω" (prosfero). It is used to indicate that the action of offering or presenting has been completed in the past.

Description: "Προσηνεγκα" is a Greek word that means "I offered" or "I presented". It is commonly used to express the act of giving or presenting something to someone.
