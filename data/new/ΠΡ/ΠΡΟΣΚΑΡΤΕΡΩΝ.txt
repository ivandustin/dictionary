Etymology: The word "προσκαρτερών" comes from the Greek verb "προσκαρτερέω" (proskarteréo), which means "to persist" or "to persevere." It is derived from the combination of the prefix "προς" (pros), meaning "towards," and the verb "καρτερέω" (karteréo), meaning "to be strong" or "to endure."

Grammar: "Προσκαρτερών" is the present participle form of the verb "προσκαρτερέω" (proskarteréo). In Greek grammar, the present participle is used to describe ongoing actions or states. It can function as an adjective or as part of a verb phrase.

Description: "Προσκαρτερών" describes someone or something that is persisting, persevering, or enduring. It implies a sense of determination and dedication to a particular task or goal.
