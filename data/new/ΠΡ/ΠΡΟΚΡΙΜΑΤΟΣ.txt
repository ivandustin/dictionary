Etymology: The word "προκριματος" comes from the Greek word "προ" (pro), meaning "before" or "in front of," and the word "κριμα" (krima), meaning "judgment" or "decision." 

Grammar: "Προκριματος" is a noun in the masculine gender and singular number in Greek. It belongs to the second declension class of nouns.

Description: "Προκριματος" refers to the act of preselection or pre-judgment. It can also be used to describe someone who has been chosen or approved in advance for a certain position or opportunity.
