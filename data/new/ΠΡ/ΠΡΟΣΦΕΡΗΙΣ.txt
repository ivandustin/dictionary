Etymology: The word "προσφερης" (prosferis) comes from the Greek verb "προσφέρω" (prosfero), which means "to offer" or "to present". 

Grammar: In Greek, "προσφερης" is a masculine noun in the nominative case. It belongs to the second declension and has the genitive form "προσφέρου" (prosferou). 

Description: "Προσφερης" is a Greek word that can be translated as "offerer" or "donor". It refers to someone who presents or offers something, usually in a religious or ceremonial context.
