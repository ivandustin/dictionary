Grammar: "Προσκαλείται" is the third person singular form of the verb "προσκαλώ" in the present tense. It is used to describe the action of inviting or calling someone in the present time, when the subject of the sentence is a third person singular entity.

Description: "Προσκαλείται" is a Greek verb that expresses the act of inviting or calling someone. It can be used in various contexts, such as inviting someone to an event, summoning someone to a meeting, or calling upon someone for assistance.
