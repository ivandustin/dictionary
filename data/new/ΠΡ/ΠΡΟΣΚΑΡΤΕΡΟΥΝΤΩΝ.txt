Etymology: The word "προσκαρτερούντων" is derived from the Greek verb "προσκαρτερέω" (proskarteréo), which means "to persist" or "to persevere." The suffix "-ούντων" indicates the present participle form, indicating ongoing action.

Grammar: "Προσκαρτερούντων" is the present participle form of the verb "προσκαρτερέω" in the third person plural. It is used to describe a group of people who are persisting or persevering in a certain action or state.

Description: "Προσκαρτερούντων" can be translated as "those who persist" or "those who persevere." It refers to a group of individuals who are actively and continuously engaging in a particular action or maintaining a specific state.
