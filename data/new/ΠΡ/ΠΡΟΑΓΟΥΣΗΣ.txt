Etymology: The word "προαγωγής" comes from the Greek root words "προ-" meaning "before" or "in front of" and "αγωγή" meaning "leading" or "guiding". 

Grammar: "Προαγωγής" is a noun in the genitive case, singular form. In Greek grammar, the genitive case is used to indicate possession, origin, or relationship.

Description: "Προαγωγής" refers to the act of promoting or advancing someone or something to a higher position or rank. It can also be used to describe the process or action of leading or guiding someone or something forward.
