Grammar: In Greek, "πραγμα" is a neuter noun. It belongs to the third declension and has the genitive form "πράγματος" (pragmatos).

Description: "Πραγμα" is a common Greek word that is used to refer to a thing, an object, or a matter. It can also be used to describe a situation, an event, or an affair. The word is often used in everyday language to talk about concrete or abstract things.
