Etymology: The word "προσφερονται" (prosferontai) is a Greek verb in the present tense, passive voice, and indicative mood. It comes from the verb "προσφέρω" (prosfero), which means "to offer" or "to present" in Greek.

Grammar: "προσφερονται" is the third person plural form of the verb "προσφέρω" (prosfero) in the present tense, passive voice, and indicative mood. In Greek grammar, verbs change their form based on the person, number, tense, voice, and mood.

Description: The word "προσφερονται" (prosferontai) describes an action of offering or presenting something. In the third person plural form, it indicates that multiple individuals or objects are being offered or presented.
