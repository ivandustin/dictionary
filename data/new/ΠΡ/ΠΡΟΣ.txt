Etymology: The word "προς" (pros) comes from the Ancient Greek language. It means "towards" or "in the direction of."

Grammar: In Greek, "προς" (pros) is a preposition. It is used to indicate movement or direction towards something or someone.

Description: "Προς" (pros) is a versatile preposition that is commonly used in Greek to express various relationships and actions. It can indicate physical movement towards a location or person, as well as metaphorical movement or direction in a more abstract sense. It is an essential word in Greek grammar and is used in a wide range of contexts.
