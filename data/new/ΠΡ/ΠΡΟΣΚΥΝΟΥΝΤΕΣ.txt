Etymology: The word "προσκυνούντες" comes from the Greek verb "προσκυνώ" (proskynó), which means "to worship" or "to bow down before." It is derived from the combination of the preposition "πρός" (pros), meaning "towards," and the verb "κυνέω" (kynéo), meaning "to kiss" or "to bow down."
