Etymology: The word "προκοπή" (prokopi) comes from the Greek verb "προκόπτω" (prokopto), which means "to advance" or "to make progress". 

Grammar: "Προκοπή" is a feminine noun in the Greek language. It belongs to the third declension and has the genitive form "προκοπής" (prokopis). 

Description: "Προκοπή" refers to progress, advancement, or improvement in various aspects of life, such as personal development, education, career, or society as a whole. It implies moving forward, overcoming obstacles, and achieving success.
