Etymology: The word "προσεπέσεν" comes from the Greek verb "προσέπιπτω" (prosepipto), which is a compound of "πρός" (pros) meaning "towards" and "ἐπίπτω" (epipto) meaning "to fall upon". 

Grammar: "προσεπέσεν" is the third person singular aorist indicative active form of the verb "προσέπιπτω" (prosepipto) in Greek. It is used to describe an action that happened in the past.

Description: "προσεπέσεν" is a Greek word that means "he/she/it fell upon" or "he/she/it happened to". It is often used to describe an unexpected or sudden occurrence.
