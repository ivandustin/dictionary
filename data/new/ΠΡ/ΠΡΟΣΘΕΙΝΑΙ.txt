Etymology: The word "προσθείναι" comes from the Greek verb "προσθέτω" (prosthetó), which means "to add" or "to append".

Grammar: "Προσθείναι" is the infinitive form of the verb in Greek. It belongs to the first conjugation class and is used in the active voice. It can be conjugated in various tenses, moods, and persons to indicate different actions related to adding or appending.

Description: "Προσθείναι" is a Greek verb that primarily means "to add" or "to append". It is commonly used to describe the action of including something extra or joining something to an existing entity. It can be used in various contexts, such as mathematics, cooking, or general discussions about adding or including something.
