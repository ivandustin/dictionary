Grammar: "Προσευχή" is a feminine noun in the Greek language. It belongs to the second declension and has the plural form "προσευχές" (proseuxés). In Greek grammar, nouns are declined according to gender, number, and case.

Description: "Προσευχή" refers to the act of prayer or a prayer itself.
