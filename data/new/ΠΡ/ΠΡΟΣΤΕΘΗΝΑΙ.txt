Grammar: "προστεθηναι" is the aorist passive infinitive form of the verb "προστίθημι" (prostíthemi). In Greek grammar, the aorist tense is used to express a past action without specifying its duration or completion. The passive voice indicates that the subject of the verb is being acted upon.
