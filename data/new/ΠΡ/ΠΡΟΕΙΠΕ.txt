Grammar: "Προειπείν" is an infinitive verb form in the Greek language. It belongs to the aorist tense, which expresses an action that occurred in the past without specifying its duration or completion. The verb is formed by adding the prefix "προ-" to the verb stem "εἰπεῖν."

Description: The word "προειπείν" means "to foretell" or "to predict" in English. It refers to the act of speaking or saying something beforehand, often in the context of predicting future events or outcomes.
