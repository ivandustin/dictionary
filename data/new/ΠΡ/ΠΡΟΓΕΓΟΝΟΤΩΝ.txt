Etymology: The word "προγεγονότων" comes from the Greek roots "προ-" meaning "before" and "γεγονότων" meaning "events" or "occurrences". 

Grammar: "προγεγονότων" is a noun in the genitive plural form. In Greek grammar, the genitive case is used to indicate possession, origin, or relationship.

Description: "προγεγονότων" refers to events or occurrences that have happened in the past. It can be used to describe historical events or any past happenings.
