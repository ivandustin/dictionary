Etymology: The word "προσδοκαί" (prosdokai) comes from the Greek verb "προσδοκάω" (prosdokao), which means "to expect" or "to anticipate." 

Grammar: "Προσδοκαί" is a noun in the plural form. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their gender, number, and case. 

Description: "Προσδοκαί" refers to expectations or anticipations. It can be used to describe the hopes or predictions that someone has for a future event or outcome.
