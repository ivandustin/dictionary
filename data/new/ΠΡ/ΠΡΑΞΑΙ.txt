Etymology: The word "πραξαι" (praxai) comes from the Greek verb "πράσσω" (prasso), which means "to do" or "to act".

Grammar: "Πραξαι" is the third person singular form of the aorist tense in the Greek language. It is used to describe an action that occurred in the past.
