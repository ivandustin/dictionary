Etymology: The word "πρασσων" (prasson) comes from the Greek verb "πραττω" (pratto), which means "to do" or "to act".

Grammar: In Greek, "πρασσων" is the present participle form of the verb "πραττω" (pratto). It is used to describe someone or something that is currently doing or performing an action.
