Etymology: The word "προκεχειρισμένον" comes from the Greek roots "προ-" meaning "before" and "κεχειρισμένον" meaning "used". 

Grammar: "Προκεχειρισμένον" is a Greek adjective in the masculine gender, singular number, and nominative case. It is used to describe something that has been previously used or handled.

Description: "Προκεχειρισμένον" refers to an item or object that has been used or handled before. It is often used to describe second-hand or pre-owned items.
