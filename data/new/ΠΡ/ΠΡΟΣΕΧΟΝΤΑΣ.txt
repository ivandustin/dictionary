Etymology: The word "προσεχοντας" (prosechontas) is derived from the Greek verb "προσέχω" (prosecho), which means "to pay attention" or "to be careful." 

Grammar: "Προσεχοντας" is the present participle form of the verb "προσέχω" (prosecho), which is used to indicate ongoing or continuous action. In Greek grammar, the present participle is formed by adding the suffix "-οντας" (-ontas) to the verb stem.

Description: "Προσεχοντας" can be translated to English as "paying attention" or "being careful." It is used to describe someone who is actively focusing their attention on something or someone, or someone who is being cautious and attentive.
