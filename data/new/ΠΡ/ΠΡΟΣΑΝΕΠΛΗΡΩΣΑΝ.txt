The word "προσανεπληρώσαν" is a Greek verb in the past tense, third person plural form. It comes from the combination of the prefix "προς" (pros), meaning "towards" or "in addition to," and the verb "ανεπληρώνω" (anepliróno), meaning "to complete" or "to fulfill." 

In terms of grammar, "προσανεπληρώσαν" is a regular verb that follows the conjugation pattern of the verb "ανεπληρώνω." It is in the aorist tense, which indicates a completed action in the past.

As for its meaning, "προσανεπληρώσαν" can be translated as "they fulfilled" or "they completed."
