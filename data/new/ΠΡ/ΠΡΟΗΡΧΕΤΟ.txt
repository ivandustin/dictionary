Etymology: The word "προηρχετο" (proērcheto) comes from the Greek roots "προ-" (pro-), meaning "before," and "ἔρχομαι" (erchomai), meaning "to come." 

Grammar: "προηρχετο" is a verb in the third person singular, past tense, indicative mood, and active voice. It is derived from the verb "προέρχομαι" (proerchomai), which means "to come before" or "to precede."

Description: "προηρχετο" describes an action that occurred in the past, specifically an action of coming before or preceding something or someone. It implies that the action happened before another event or action.
