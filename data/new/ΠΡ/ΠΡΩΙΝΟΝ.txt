Etymology: The word "πρωινόν" (pronounced "pro-ee-non") comes from the Greek language. It is derived from the word "πρωί" (pronounced "pro-ee"), which means "morning."

Grammar: In Greek, "πρωινόν" is a neuter noun. It is the singular form, and its plural form is "πρωινά" (pronounced "pro-ee-na").

Description: "Πρωινόν" refers to the meal eaten in the morning, commonly known as breakfast. It typically consists of various food items such as bread, eggs, cereal, fruits, and beverages like coffee or tea. Breakfast is considered an important meal to provide energy and nutrients to start the day.
