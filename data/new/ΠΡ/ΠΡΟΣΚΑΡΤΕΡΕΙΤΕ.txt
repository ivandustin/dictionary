Etymology: The word "προσκαρτερείτε" comes from the Greek verb "προσκαρτερώ" (proskarteró), which means "to wait for eagerly" or "to persist in." It is derived from the combination of the prefix "προς" (pros), meaning "towards," and the verb "καρτερώ" (karteró), meaning "to wait" or "to endure."

Grammar: "Προσκαρτερείτε" is the second person plural form of the present tense in Greek. It is used to address a group of people and means "you wait eagerly" or "you persist in."
