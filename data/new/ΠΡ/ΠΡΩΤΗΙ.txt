Etymology: The word "πρωτηί" (pronounced "proti") comes from the Greek word "πρῶτος" (prōtos), meaning "first" or "foremost."

Grammar: "Πρωτηί" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the feminine gender and singular number.

Description: "Πρωτηί" refers to the first day of the month in the Greek calendar. It is equivalent to the English word "first" or "first day."
