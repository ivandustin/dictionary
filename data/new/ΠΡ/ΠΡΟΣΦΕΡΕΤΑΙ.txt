Etymology: The word "προσφέρεται" comes from the Greek verb "προσφέρω" (prosfero), which means "to offer" or "to present".

Grammar: "Προσφέρεται" is the third person singular form of the verb "προσφέρω" in the present tense. It is in the indicative mood and the passive voice.

Description: "Προσφέρεται" is a Greek word that means "it is offered" or "it is presented". It is commonly used to describe something that is being given or provided to someone.
