Etymology: The word "προσεθέτο" (prosetheto) comes from the Greek roots "πρός" (pros), meaning "towards" or "to," and "τίθημι" (tithemi), meaning "to place" or "to set." 

Grammar: "Προσεθέτο" is a verb in the Greek language. It is in the past tense, third person singular form. 

Description: "Προσεθέτο" means "he/she/it added" or "he/she/it placed" in English. It is used to indicate the action of adding or placing something in a specific location.
