Etymology: The word "προστρεχοντες" (prostrekondes) comes from the Greek verb "προστρέχω" (prostrecho), which means "to run towards" or "to hasten towards". It is derived from the prefix "προς" (pros), meaning "towards", and the verb "τρέχω" (trecho), meaning "to run".

Grammar: "προστρεχοντες" is the present participle form of the verb "προστρέχω" (prostrecho) in Greek. The present participle is used to describe an ongoing action or state. In this case, it means "running towards" or "hastening towards".
