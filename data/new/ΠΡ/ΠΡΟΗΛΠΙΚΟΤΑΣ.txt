Etymology: The word "προηλπικότας" comes from the Greek word "προηλπίζω" (proilpízo), which means "to anticipate" or "to expect beforehand". The suffix "-τας" is added to form the noun form.

Grammar: "Προηλπικότας" is a masculine noun in the singular form. It belongs to the third declension in Greek grammar.

Description: "Προηλπικότας" refers to someone who has anticipated or expected something beforehand. It can also be used to describe someone who has foreseen or predicted an event or outcome.
