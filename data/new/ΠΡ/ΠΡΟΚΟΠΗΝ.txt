Etymology: The word "προκοπήν" (prokopin) comes from the Greek verb "προκόπτω" (prokopto), which means "to advance" or "to make progress". 

Grammar: "Προκοπήν" is a noun in the accusative case, singular form. In Greek grammar, nouns change their form depending on their role in the sentence (subject, object, etc.) and their number (singular or plural).

Description: "Προκοπήν" refers to progress, advancement, or improvement. It can be used in various contexts, such as personal development, career advancement, or societal progress.
