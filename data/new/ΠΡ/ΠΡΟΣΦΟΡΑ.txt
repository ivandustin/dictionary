Etymology: The word "προσφορά" (prosforá) comes from the Greek verb "προσφέρω" (prosfero), which means "to offer" or "to present". 

Grammar: "Προσφορά" is a feminine noun in Greek. It belongs to the first declension and its plural form is "προσφορές" (prosfores). 

Description: "Προσφορά" refers to an offering, a gift, or a contribution. It can also be used to describe an act of presenting or giving something to someone. In a broader sense, it can refer to any kind of offering or sacrifice made in a religious or ceremonial context.
