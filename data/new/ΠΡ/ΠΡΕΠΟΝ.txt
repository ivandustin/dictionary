Etymology: The word "πρεπον" comes from the Greek word "πρέπειν" (prepein), which means "to be fitting" or "to be suitable."

Grammar: In Greek, "πρεπον" is an adjective. It can be used to describe something that is appropriate, proper, or fitting.

Description: "Πρεπον" is used to describe something that is in accordance with what is considered appropriate or suitable in a given context. It implies a sense of propriety and conformity to social norms or standards.
