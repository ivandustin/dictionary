Etymology: The word "πραξεσιν" (praxesin) comes from the Greek verb "πράσσω" (prasso), which means "to do" or "to act".

Grammar: "Πραξεσιν" is a noun in the accusative case, singular number, and feminine gender in Greek grammar.

Description: "Πραξεσιν" refers to the act of doing or performing something. It can also be translated as "action" or "deed".
