Etymology: The word "προσευχομαι" (proseuchomai) comes from the Greek roots "προς" (pros), meaning "towards" or "to," and "ευχη" (euchē), meaning "prayer." 

Grammar: "Προσευχομαι" is a verb in the Greek language. It is in the present tense, indicative mood, and active voice. It is conjugated in various forms to indicate different subjects, tenses, moods, and voices.

Description: "Προσευχομαι" means "to pray" in Greek. It refers to the act of communicating with a higher power, expressing reverence, supplication, or thanksgiving. Prayer is an important aspect of many religious and spiritual practices.
