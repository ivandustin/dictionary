Grammar: In Greek, "πρωτοτοκος" is an adjective that agrees in gender, number, and case with the noun it modifies. It can be declined in different forms depending on the gender and number of the noun it describes.

Description: "Πρωτοτοκος" is a Greek word that is often translated as "firstborn" or "firstborn child".
