Etymology: The word "προσευχαίς" (proseuchais) comes from the Greek verb "προσεύχομαι" (proseuchomai), which means "to pray" or "to make a prayer." 

Grammar: "Προσευχαίς" is a noun in the plural form. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their gender, number, and case. The word "προσευχαίς" is in the feminine gender and the nominative case.

Description: "Προσευχαίς" refers to prayers or supplications. It is commonly used in religious contexts to describe the act of communicating with a higher power or deity through prayer.
