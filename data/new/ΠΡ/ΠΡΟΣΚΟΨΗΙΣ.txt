Grammar: "Προσκοψηίς" is a feminine noun in the Greek language. It belongs to the third declension and has the genitive form "προσκοψείας" (proskopseias). In the singular, it can be used as both the subject and the object of a sentence. In the plural, it takes the form "προσκοψείς" (proskopseis).
