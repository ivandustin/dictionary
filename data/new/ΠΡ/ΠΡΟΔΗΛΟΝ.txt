Etymology: The word "προδηλον" comes from the Greek roots "προ-" meaning "before" and "δηλον" meaning "clear" or "evident". 

Grammar: "προδηλον" is an adjective in the neuter gender, singular form. It is used to describe something that is clearly evident or obvious.

Description: "προδηλον" is used to emphasize that something is clearly apparent or easily understood. It is often used to indicate that a fact or truth is so obvious that it does not require further explanation.
