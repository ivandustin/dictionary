Grammar: "Προφητικών" is the plural form of the adjective "προφητικός" in Greek. It agrees with plural nouns in gender, number, and case.

Description: "Προφητικών" is used to describe things that are related to or characteristic of prophecy. It can refer to prophetic writings, predictions, or the ability to foresee future events.
