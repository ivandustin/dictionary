Etymology: The word "προσκεφαλαίον" comes from the Greek words "πρός" (pros), meaning "towards" or "to," and "κεφαλή" (kephalē), meaning "head." 

Grammar: "Προσκεφαλαίον" is a neuter noun in the singular form. In Greek grammar, neuter nouns typically end in "-ον" in the nominative case.

Description: "Προσκεφαλαίον" refers to a headrest or a pillow used to support the head while sleeping or resting. It can also be used metaphorically to describe something that provides comfort or support.
