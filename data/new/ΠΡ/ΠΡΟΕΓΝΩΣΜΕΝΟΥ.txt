Etymology: The word "προεγνωσμένου" comes from the Greek roots "προ-" meaning "before" and "εγνωσμένος" meaning "known". 

Grammar: "προεγνωσμένου" is a Greek adjective in the genitive case. It is used to describe something that has been predetermined or predestined.

Description: "προεγνωσμένου" refers to something that has been known or determined in advance. It implies that a certain outcome or course of events has been decided or planned beforehand.
