Etymology: The word "προσφεροντων" (prosferonton) is the present participle form of the Greek verb "προσφέρω" (prosfero), which means "to offer" or "to present."

Grammar: "Προσφεροντων" is the present participle form of the verb "προσφέρω" in the plural, masculine, genitive case. In Greek grammar, the present participle is used to describe ongoing actions or states.

Description: "Προσφεροντων" can be translated as "of offering" or "of presenting" in English. It is used to indicate possession or association with the act of offering or presenting something.
