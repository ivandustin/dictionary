Etymology: The word "προστηναι" comes from the Greek verb "προστάττω" (prostátto), which means "to command" or "to order."

Grammar: "προστηναι" is the aorist tense of the Greek verb "προστάττω" (prostátto), which is a transitive verb. It is conjugated in the third person singular.

Description: "προστηναι" is a Greek verb that means "he/she/it commanded" or "he/she/it ordered." It is used to express an action of commanding or ordering someone to do something.
