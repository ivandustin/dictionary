The word "επεσκεψασθε" (epeskepsasthe) is a Greek verb in the aorist tense, second person plural form. It is derived from the root word "επισκέπτομαι" (episkeptomai), which means "to visit" or "to inspect". 

In terms of grammar, "επεσκεψασθε" is a conjugated form of the verb "επισκέπτομαι" in the aorist tense, which is used to describe a completed action in the past. The second person plural form indicates that the action was performed by a group of people.

Overall, "επεσκεψασθε" can be translated to mean "you all visited" or "you all inspected" in English.
