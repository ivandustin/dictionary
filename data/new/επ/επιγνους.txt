Word: επιγνους

Etymology:
The word επιγνους comes from the Greek root words επί (epi) meaning "upon" or "over" and γνώση (gnose) meaning "knowledge" or "understanding". Therefore, επιγνους can be translated to mean "having knowledge upon" or "having understanding over".

Grammar:
επιγνους is an adjective in the masculine gender, singular number, and nominative case. It can also be declined to match the gender, number, and case of the noun it modifies.

Description:
επιγνους is a word that describes someone who has a deep understanding or knowledge of a particular subject. It can also be used to describe someone who is wise or experienced in a certain area. In Greek philosophy, επιγνους was used to describe someone who had achieved a higher level of knowledge or enlightenment.
