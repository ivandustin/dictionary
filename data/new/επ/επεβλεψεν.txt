In terms of grammar, "επεβλεψεν" is a third-person singular verb in the aorist tense. It can be conjugated in other tenses and persons, such as "επεβλεψα" (epéblepsa) in the first-person singular aorist tense, or "επεβλεψαν" (epéblepsan) in the third-person plural aorist tense.
