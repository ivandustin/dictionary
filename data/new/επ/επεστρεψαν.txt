The word επεστρεψαν (epestrepsan) is a Greek verb that comes from the root word στρέφω (strefó), which means "to turn" or "to twist." The prefix επι- (epi-) adds the meaning of "back" or "again," so επεστρεψαν (epestrepsan) can be translated as "they turned back" or "they returned."
