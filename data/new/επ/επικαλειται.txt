Word: επικαλείται

Etymology:
The word επικαλείται (epikaleitai) is derived from the Greek verb επικαλέω (epikaleo), which means "to call upon" or "to invoke". It is composed of the prefix επι- (epi-), which means "upon" or "on", and the verb καλέω (kaleo), which means "to call".

Grammar:
επικαλείται is a third-person singular present indicative active form of the verb επικαλέω (epikaleo). It is used to describe an action that is currently happening in the present and is being performed by a single person or thing.

Description:
The word επικαλείται is often used in a religious or spiritual context to describe the act of calling upon a deity or higher power for assistance or guidance. It can also be used in a more general sense to describe the act of calling upon someone or something for help or support.
