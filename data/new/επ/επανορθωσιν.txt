Word: επανορθωσιν

Grammar: επανορθωσιν is a noun in the accusative case, which means it is the direct object of a verb. It is a feminine noun, and its singular form is επανόρθωση.

Description: επανορθωσιν refers to the act of correcting or rectifying something that has gone wrong or has been done incorrectly.
