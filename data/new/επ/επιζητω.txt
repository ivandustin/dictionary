Word: επιζητώ

Etymology: The word επιζητώ comes from the combination of the prefix επι- (epi-), meaning "on" or "upon," and the verb ζητώ (zito), meaning "to seek" or "to search for." Therefore, επιζητώ means "to seek upon" or "to search for."

Grammar: επιζητώ is a verb in the present tense, active voice, and indicative mood. It can be conjugated in all tenses and moods according to the rules of Greek grammar.

Description: επιζητώ means "to seek" or "to search for" something with persistence and determination. It implies a strong desire or need to find something, whether it is a physical object or an abstract concept. The word can be used in various contexts, such as searching for a job, seeking knowledge, or pursuing a goal.
