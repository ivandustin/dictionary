The word "επιστραφητω" is a Greek verb in the imperative mood, which means "let it be returned" or "let it be restored". It is derived from the verb "επιστρέφω" (epistrefo), which means "to return" or "to come back". The suffix "-τω" (-to) is added to the stem of the verb to form the imperative mood. In Greek grammar, the imperative mood is used to give commands or make requests.
