Word: επεθυμει

Etymology: The word επεθυμει comes from the Greek verb "επιθυμέω" (epithyméo), which means "to desire" or "to long for". The prefix "επι-" (epi-) means "upon" or "over", while "θυμός" (thymós) means "passion" or "desire".

Grammar: επεθυμει is a verb in the third person singular, present tense, active voice, indicative mood. It is derived from the verb "επιθυμέω" (epithyméo), which is a regular verb in Greek.

Description: επεθυμει means "he/she/it desires" or "he/she/it longs for" in Greek. It is often used to express a strong desire or craving for something.
