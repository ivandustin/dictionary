Word: επιστηριζοντες

Etymology: 
The word επιστηριζοντες comes from the Greek verb επιστηρίζω (epistirízo), which means "to support" or "to prop up". The suffix -οντες (-ontes) is a present participle ending, indicating that the word refers to people who are currently supporting or propping up something.

Grammar:
επιστηριζοντες is a present participle in the active voice, masculine gender, and plural number. It is derived from the verb επιστηρίζω, which is a first conjugation verb in the present tense. The participle form is often used to describe ongoing actions or states, and in this case, it refers to people who are currently supporting or propping up something.

Description:
επιστηριζοντες can be translated to English as "supporting" or "propping up". It describes people who are actively providing support or reinforcement to something, whether it be a physical object or an idea. The word can be used in a literal sense, such as supporting a building or structure, or in a figurative sense, such as supporting a political candidate or ideology.
