The word "επιτελεισθε" is a verb in the present passive indicative form in Greek. It comes from the root word "επιτελέω" which means "to complete" or "to accomplish". The passive voice indicates that the action is being done to the subject of the sentence rather than the subject doing the action. 

In terms of grammar, "επιτελεισθε" is conjugated in the second person plural, meaning it is addressing a group of people. 

Overall, "επιτελεισθε" can be translated to mean "you are being completed" or "you are being accomplished".
