The word "επιδεικνυς" (epideiknus) is a Greek verb that means "to exhibit" or "to demonstrate." 

Etymology: The word is derived from the Greek roots "επι-" (epi-), meaning "upon" or "over," and "δεικνυω" (deiknuo), meaning "to show" or "to point out."

Grammar: "Επιδεικνυς" is a second-person singular present active indicative verb in Greek. It can also be conjugated in other tenses and moods, such as the aorist tense ("επεδειξας," epedeixas) or the imperative mood ("επιδειξον," epideixon).

Description: The word "επιδεικνυς" is often used in contexts where someone is showing or demonstrating something to others, such as in a presentation or a performance. It can also be used in a more figurative sense, such as when someone is exhibiting a particular trait or behavior.
