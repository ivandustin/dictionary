The word "επιστατα" (epistata) is a Greek noun that means "leader" or "chief". It is derived from the verb "επιστατεω" (epistateo), which means "to preside over" or "to be in charge of". In terms of grammar, "επιστατα" is a masculine noun in the nominative case. It can be used to refer to a person who holds a position of authority or leadership, or to describe someone who is skilled or knowledgeable in a particular area.
