The word "επιδωσει" (epido̱sei) is a Greek verb in the future tense, which means "to give back" or "to return". 

Etymology: The word is derived from the prefix "επι-" (epi-), which means "upon" or "on", and the verb "διδωμι" (didomi), which means "to give". 

Grammar: "Επιδωσει" is a second-person singular future active indicative form of the verb. 

Description: The word is commonly used in everyday Greek language to express the act of returning something to its rightful owner or giving something back that was borrowed. It can also be used in a figurative sense to mean "to reciprocate" or "to respond in kind".
