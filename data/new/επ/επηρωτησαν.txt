Word: επηρώτησαν

Etymology: The word επηρώτησαν comes from the Greek verb επερωτώ (eperotó), which means "to ask" or "to inquire". The verb is formed by adding the prefix επι- (epi-) to the verb ρωτώ (rotó), which means "to ask".

Grammar: επηρώτησαν is the third person plural, past tense form of the verb επερωτώ. It is a regular verb in Greek and follows the conjugation pattern of verbs ending in -ω. The verb is in the indicative mood, which is used to make statements or ask questions.

Description: επηρώτησαν means "they asked" or "they inquired" in English. It is a common verb in Greek and is used in everyday conversation. The word can be used in various contexts, such as asking for information, seeking clarification, or expressing curiosity.
