Word: επαισχυνεται

Etymology:
The word επαισχυνεται comes from the Greek root words επί (epi) meaning "on" or "upon" and αισχύνομαι (aischynomai) meaning "to feel shame or disgrace". Therefore, επαισχυνεται means "to feel ashamed or embarrassed".

Grammar:
επαισχυνεται is a verb in the present tense, third person singular form. It is derived from the verb αισχύνομαι (aischynomai) and has the prefix επί (epi) added to it.

Description:
επαισχυνεται is a Greek word that describes the feeling of shame or embarrassment. It is often used to describe a person who is ashamed of their actions or behavior. The word can also be used to describe a situation or event that causes someone to feel ashamed or embarrassed.
