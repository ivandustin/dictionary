Word: επιστρεφουσιν

Etymology: 
The word επιστρεφουσιν is a present active indicative form of the Greek verb επιστρέφω (epistrepho), which means "to turn back" or "to return". The word is derived from the prefix επι- (epi-), which means "upon" or "on", and the verb στρέφω (strepfo), which means "to turn".

Grammar:
