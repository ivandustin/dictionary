Word: επιχορηγίας

Etymology: The word επιχορηγίας comes from the Greek words επί (epi), meaning "on" or "upon," and χορηγία (chorēgia), meaning "sponsorship" or "financing." 

Grammar: επιχορηγίας is a feminine noun in the genitive case. 

Description: επιχορηγίας refers to the act of providing financial support or sponsorship for a particular cause or project. It can also refer to the funds or resources provided for this purpose. The word is commonly used in the context of government or corporate funding for various initiatives, such as education, research, or the arts.
