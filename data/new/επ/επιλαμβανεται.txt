Word: επιλαμβάνεται

Etymology: The word επιλαμβάνεται comes from the Greek verb "λαμβάνω" (lamvano) which means "to take" or "to seize", and the prefix "επι-" (epi-) which means "upon" or "over". Therefore, επιλαμβάνεται means "to take hold of" or "to seize upon".

επιλαμβάνομαι (I take hold of)
επιλαμβάνεσαι (you take hold of)

επιλαμβανόμαστε (we take hold of)

Description: επιλαμβάνεται is a verb that describes the act of taking hold of something or seizing upon something. It can be used in a literal sense, such as physically grabbing onto an object, or in a figurative sense, such as taking control of a situation or seizing an opportunity.
