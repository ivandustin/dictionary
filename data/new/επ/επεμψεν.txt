Word: επεμψεν

Etymology: επεμψεν is a Greek verb that comes from the root word "πεμπω" (pempo), which means "to send". The prefix "επι" (epi) adds the meaning of "to send upon" or "to send for a purpose".

Grammar: επεμψεν is a third-person singular aorist active indicative verb in Greek. It is the past tense form of the verb πεμπω (pempo), which means "to send". The aorist tense indicates a completed action in the past.

Description: επεμψεν means "he/she/it sent" in Greek. It is a verb that describes the act of sending something or someone for a specific purpose. The context in which it is used will determine the exact meaning and purpose of the sending.
