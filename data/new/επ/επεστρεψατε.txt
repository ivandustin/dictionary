Grammar: "επεστρεψατε" is the second person plural form of the aorist tense in the indicative mood. It is used to describe an action that was completed in the past. 

Description: The word "επεστρεψατε" is commonly used in everyday Greek language to express the act of returning or coming back to a place or situation. It can be used in various contexts, such as returning home, returning to work, or returning to a previous state of mind.
