Word: επλουτησατε

Grammar: επλουτησατε is the second person plural aorist indicative active form of the verb πλουτέω. In Greek, the aorist tense is used to describe a completed action in the past. The indicative mood indicates a statement of fact. The active voice indicates that the subject of the sentence is performing the action.

Description: επλουτησατε can be translated to English as "you became rich" or "you were enriched". It is a past tense verb form that describes a completed action of becoming rich or being enriched.
