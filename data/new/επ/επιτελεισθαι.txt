The word "επιτελεισθαι" (epiteleisthai) is a Greek verb that means "to accomplish" or "to complete". 

Etymology: The word is derived from the Greek roots "επι-" (epi-), meaning "upon" or "over", and "τελειος" (teleios), meaning "complete" or "perfect". 

Grammar: "Επιτελεισθαι" is a passive verb in the present tense, third person singular form. It can also be conjugated in other tenses and persons, such as "επιτελείται" (epiteleitai) in the present tense, third person plural form. 

Description: The word is often used in contexts related to achieving goals or completing tasks. It can also be used in a more abstract sense, such as "to fulfill a purpose" or "to bring something to fruition".
