The word "επαναγκες" (epananges) is a Greek noun that can be translated to mean "necessity" or "compulsion". 

Etymology: The word is derived from the prefix "επαν-" (epan-), meaning "again" or "re-", and the noun "αναγκη" (anagki), meaning "necessity" or "need". 

Grammar: "Επαναγκες" is a feminine noun in the singular form.
