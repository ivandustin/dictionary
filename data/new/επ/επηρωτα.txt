Word: επηρωτα

Etymology:
The word επηρωτα (epirota) is derived from the Greek verb επερωτώ (eperoto), which means "to ask" or "to inquire". It is formed by adding the prefix επι- (epi-), which means "upon" or "over", to the verb ρωτώ (roto), which means "to ask".

Grammar:
επηρωτα is a verb in the present tense, second person singular form. It is used when addressing someone directly and asking them a question. The verb is conjugated as follows:

επηρωτάς (epirotas) - you ask

Description:
επηρωτα is a common Greek verb that is used in everyday conversation. It is often used to ask for information or clarification on a particular topic. The verb can be used in a formal or informal setting, depending on the context. It is a versatile verb that can be used in a variety of situations, from asking for directions to asking for someone's opinion.
