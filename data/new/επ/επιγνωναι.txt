Word: επιγνωναι

Etymology:
The word επιγνωναι comes from the Greek root words επί (epi) meaning "on" or "upon" and γνώσις (gnosis) meaning "knowledge". Therefore, επιγνωναι means "to know upon" or "to recognize".

Grammar:
επιγνωναι is a verb in the Greek language. It is in the aorist tense, which indicates a past action that is completed. The verb is also in the infinitive form, which means it is not conjugated to match a specific subject or tense.

Description:
επιγνωναι is a Greek verb that means "to recognize" or "to acknowledge". It is often used in a spiritual or religious context to describe recognizing or acknowledging the truth or existence of a higher power. The word can also be used in a more general sense to describe recognizing or acknowledging something in a non-religious context.
