The word "επαινον" (epainon) is a Greek noun that means "praise" or "commendation". It comes from the verb "επαινεω" (epaineo), which means "to praise" or "to commend". In terms of grammar, "επαινον" is a neuter noun in the singular form. It can be used in various contexts, such as expressing admiration for someone's achievements or expressing gratitude for someone's help.
