Word: επισφαλους

Etymology: The word επισφαλους comes from the Greek words επί (epi) meaning "on" or "upon" and σφαλερός (sfaleros) meaning "unstable" or "insecure". 

Grammar: επισφαλους is an adjective in the Greek language. It is in the masculine, singular, nominative case. 

Description: επισφαλους is used to describe something that is uncertain, unreliable, or insecure. It can refer to a person, situation, or thing that is not trustworthy or dependable. It is often used in a negative context to express doubt or skepticism.
