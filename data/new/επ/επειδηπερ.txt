Word: επειδήπερ

Etymology: επειδήπερ is a compound word in Greek, consisting of επειδή (epide) meaning "since" or "because" and περ (per) meaning "indeed" or "truly". It is commonly used in formal or literary Greek.

Grammar: επειδήπερ is a conjunction in Greek, used to introduce a causal or explanatory clause. It is often translated as "since" or "because" in English. It is followed by a verb in the indicative mood.

Description: επειδήπερ is a useful word in Greek for expressing causality or providing an explanation for a situation. It is commonly used in formal or literary contexts, and may not be as commonly used in everyday speech.
