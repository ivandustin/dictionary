Etymology: The word is derived from the Greek verb "επιστρέφω" (epistrefo), which means "to return" or "to come back". The suffix "-αντες" (-antes) is added to the stem of the verb to form the past participle.

Grammar: The word is in the masculine plural form, indicating that it refers to a group of people who have returned. It is also in the aorist tense, which is used to describe a completed action in the past.

Description: "Επιστρεψαντες" is a versatile word that can be used in various contexts to describe a group of people who have returned from a journey, a mission, or an event.
