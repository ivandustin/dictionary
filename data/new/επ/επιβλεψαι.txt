The word "επιβλεψαι" (epiblepsai) is a Greek verb in the aorist tense, which means it refers to a completed action in the past. It is derived from the root word "επιβλεπω" (epiblepo), which means "to look upon" or "to observe." 

In terms of grammar, "επιβλεψαι" is a second person singular form of the verb, which means it is addressing one person directly. 

Overall, the word "επιβλεψαι" can be translated to mean "you looked upon" or "you observed."
