The word "επιφανη" (epiphani) is a Greek adjective that means "manifest" or "apparent". It comes from the Greek word "επιφαίνω" (epiphaino), which means "to appear" or "to become visible". 

In terms of grammar, "επιφανη" is a feminine singular form of the adjective, and it can be used to describe a feminine noun. For example, "η επιφανη αλήθεια" (i epiphani alithia) means "the apparent truth".

Overall, "επιφανη" is a versatile word that can be used in various contexts to describe something that is visible or apparent.
