Word: εποπτευσαντες

Etymology: The word εποπτευσαντες comes from the Greek verb εποπτεύω (epoptevo), which means "to oversee" or "to watch over". The suffix -σαντες (-santes) is a participle ending that indicates past tense and active voice.

Grammar: εποπτευσαντες is a past participle in the active voice, masculine gender, and plural number. It is derived from the verb εποπτεύω (epoptevo), which is a first conjugation verb in the active voice.

Description: εποπτευσαντες is a Greek word that means "having overseen" or "having watched over". It is often used in the context of supervision or surveillance, and can refer to someone who has monitored a situation or activity.
