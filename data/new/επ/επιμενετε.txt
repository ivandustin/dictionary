Word: επιμενετε

Etymology: 
The word επιμενετε comes from the Greek verb επιμένω (epiméno), which means "to persist" or "to persevere". It is derived from the prefix επι- (epi-), meaning "upon" or "over", and the verb μένω (méno), meaning "to remain" or "to stay".

Grammar:
επιμενετε is a verb in the present tense, second person plural form. It is the present active indicative form of the verb επιμένω (epiméno).

Description:
επιμενετε is a Greek verb that means "you persist" or "you persevere". It is often used to encourage someone to continue with a task or to keep going despite difficulties. The word can be used in a variety of contexts, such as in sports, work, or personal relationships.
