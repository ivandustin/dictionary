Word: επικουριας

Etymology: The word επικουριας comes from the Greek word επικουρία (epikouría), which means "assistance" or "support". It is derived from the prefix επι- (epi-), meaning "upon" or "in addition to", and the noun κουρά (kourá), meaning "help" or "aid".

Grammar: επικουριας is a noun in the genitive case, which is used to show possession or relationship. In this case, it means "of assistance" or "of support".

Description: επικουριας refers to the act of providing assistance or support to someone or something. It can also refer to a person or group that provides such assistance or support. In Greek philosophy, the term was used to describe the belief that the gods provided assistance to humans in times of need. Today, the word is commonly used in modern Greek to refer to various forms of support, such as financial aid or military assistance.
