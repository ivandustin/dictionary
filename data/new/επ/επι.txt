The word "επι" (epi) is a Greek preposition that can have several meanings depending on the context. 

Etymology: The word "επι" comes from the Proto-Indo-European root *h₁epi, meaning "upon, on, over". 

Grammar: "Επι" is a preposition that is usually followed by the accusative case in Greek. It can also be used as a prefix in compound words. 

Description: Some of the meanings of "επι" include: 

- On, upon, over: Το βιβλίο είναι επάνω στο τραπέζι. (The book is on the table.)
- Towards, to: Πήγαινε επί τον τόπον. (Go to the place.)
- In addition to, besides: Επί τοις άλλοις, δεν έχω χρόνο. (Besides, I don't have time.)
- About, concerning: Μιλάμε επί του θέματος. (We're talking about the subject.)
- With, together with: Πήγαμε επί τους φίλους μας. (We went with our friends.)
