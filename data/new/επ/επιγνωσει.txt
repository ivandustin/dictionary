The word "επιγνωσει" (epignosei) is a Greek noun that means "knowledge" or "recognition". It is derived from the verb "επιγινωσκω" (epiginosko), which means "to know thoroughly" or "to recognize". 

In terms of grammar, "επιγνωσει" is in the singular form and in the dative case, indicating that it is the indirect object of a verb or a preposition. 

Overall, "επιγνωσει" refers to a deep understanding or recognition of something, often used in a spiritual or philosophical context.
