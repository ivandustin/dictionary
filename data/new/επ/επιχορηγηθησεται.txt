Word: επιχορηγηθησεται

Etymology: The word επιχορηγηθησεται is a compound word in Greek, formed by the prefix επι- (meaning "on" or "upon") and the verb χορηγέω (meaning "to supply" or "to provide"). The suffix -θησεται is a passive voice ending, indicating that the action is being done to the subject of the sentence.

Grammar: επιχορηγηθησεται is a third person singular future passive indicative verb in Greek. It is formed by adding the future tense ending -σεται to the passive voice stem of the verb χορηγέω. The prefix επι- indicates that the action will be done upon the subject.

Description: επιχορηγηθησεται means "it will be provided" or "it will be supplied" in English. It is often used in legal or bureaucratic contexts to indicate that something will be given or provided to someone.
