The word "επιγεια" (epigeia) is a Greek adjective that means "earthly" or "terrestrial". 

Etymology: The word is derived from the Greek words "επί" (epi), meaning "on" or "upon", and "γη" (ge), meaning "earth" or "land".

Grammar: "Επιγεια" is a feminine adjective in the nominative singular form. In Greek, adjectives agree in gender, number, and case with the noun they modify.

Description: The word "επιγεια" is often used to describe things that are related to or characteristic of the earth or the physical world, as opposed to the spiritual or heavenly realm. It can also be used to describe things that are practical, down-to-earth, or realistic.
