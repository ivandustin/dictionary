The word "επιφαινοντων" is a Greek verb in the present participle form. It comes from the root word "επιφαίνω" which means "to appear" or "to shine upon". The present participle form indicates that the action is ongoing or continuous.

In terms of grammar, "επιφαινοντων" is in the present tense and active voice. It is also in the masculine, plural form, indicating that the subject is a group of males.

In context, "επιφαινοντων" could be used to describe a group of people or objects that are currently appearing or shining upon something. For example, "Οι αστέρες επιφαινοντων στον ουρανό" (The stars are shining in the sky).
