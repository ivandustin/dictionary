Word: επιγραψω

Etymology: The word επιγραψω comes from the Greek words επί (epi) meaning "on" or "upon" and γράφω (grapho) meaning "to write". Therefore, επιγραψω means "to write upon" or "to inscribe".

Grammar: επιγραψω is a verb in the Greek language. It is in the present tense, active voice, and indicative mood. The verb stem is γραψ-, and the ending -ω indicates the first person singular form.

Description: επιγραψω means "to write upon" or "to inscribe" in Greek. It can refer to writing on a surface, such as a wall or a piece of paper, or to engraving on a hard material, such as stone or metal. The word can also be used metaphorically to mean "to impress upon" or "to leave a lasting impression".
