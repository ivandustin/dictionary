The word επεχρίσεν is a Greek verb that comes from the root word χρίζω (chrizo), which means "to anoint" or "to smear with oil." The prefix επε- (epe-) means "upon" or "on top of," so επεχρίσεν can be translated as "he anointed upon" or "he smeared with oil on top of."

επεχρίσεν is a third-person singular aorist active indicative verb in the Greek language.
