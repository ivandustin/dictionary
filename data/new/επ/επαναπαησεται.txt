Word: επαναπαησεται

Etymology: The word επαναπαησεται is a compound word in Greek, formed by the prefix επανα- (meaning "again" or "re-") and the verb παίζω (meaning "to rest" or "to lie down"). Therefore, the word επαναπαησεται means "to rest again" or "to lie down again".

Grammar: επαναπαησεται is a third person singular verb in the future tense, passive voice, and indicative mood. It is formed by adding the future tense suffix -σεται to the verb παίζω, and the prefix επανα- indicates repetition or continuation of the action.

Description: επαναπαησεται is a Greek verb that describes the action of resting or lying down again. It can be used in various contexts, such as when someone takes a break and then resumes resting, or when someone goes back to bed after waking up. The word emphasizes the idea of repetition or continuation of the action of resting.
