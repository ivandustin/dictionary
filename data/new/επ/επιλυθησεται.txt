Word: επιλυθησεται

Etymology: 
The word επιλυθησεται is a compound word in Greek, consisting of the prefix επι- (epi-), meaning "upon" or "over", and the verb λυθησεται (lythisetai), meaning "will be solved" or "will be resolved". 

Grammar: 
επιλυθησεται is a third person singular future passive indicative verb in Greek. It is formed by combining the prefix επι- with the verb λυθησεται, which is in the future tense, passive voice, and indicative mood. 

Description: 
The word επιλυθησεται means "will be solved" or "will be resolved" in Greek. It is often used in the context of problem-solving or finding solutions to difficult situations. The word implies that a solution will be found in the future, but it does not specify when or how the solution will be achieved.
