Word: επεζητούν

Etymology: The word επεζητούν comes from the Greek verb "ζητώ" (zito) which means "to seek" or "to search for". The prefix "επε-" (epe-) is added to the beginning of the verb to indicate a continuous or repeated action.

Grammar: επεζητούν is a present tense verb in the third person plural form. It is conjugated from the verb "ζητώ" (zito) which is a regular verb in Greek.

Description: επεζητούν means "they seek" or "they search for" in English. It is often used to describe a continuous or repeated action of searching for something.
