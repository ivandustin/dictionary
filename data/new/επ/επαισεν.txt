The word "επαισεν" (epaisen) is a verb in the Greek language. It is the aorist tense, third person singular form of the verb "παιζω" (paizo), which means "to play".

Grammar: "Επαισεν" (epaisen) is in the aorist tense, which is used to describe a completed action in the past. The third person singular form indicates that the subject of the sentence is a he, she, or it.

Description: "Επαισεν" (epaisen) can be translated to mean "he/she/it played" in English. It is a simple verb that describes an action that took place in the past. The context of the sentence would provide more information about who or what was playing.
