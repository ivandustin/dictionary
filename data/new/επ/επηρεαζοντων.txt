Word: επηρεαζοντων

Etymology: The word επηρεαζοντων is derived from the Greek verb επηρεαζω (epireazo), which means "to influence" or "to affect". The suffix -οντων (-onton) is the present participle form of the verb, indicating that the action is ongoing.

Grammar: επηρεαζοντων is the present participle form of the verb επηρεαζω (epireazo), which is in the active voice and the third person plural. It is used to describe a continuous action of influencing or affecting.

Description: επηρεαζοντων can be translated to "influencing" or "affecting" in English. It is often used in the context of describing how one thing is affecting another, such as in the sentence "Η καταιγίδα επηρεάζει την κυκλοφορία των αυτοκινήτων" (The storm is affecting the traffic of the cars).
