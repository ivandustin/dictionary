The word "επιμενε" (epimene) is a Greek verb that means "to persist" or "to persevere". 

Etymology: The word is derived from the Greek prefix "επι-" (epi-), meaning "upon" or "over", and the verb "μενω" (meno), meaning "to stay" or "to remain". 

Grammar: "Επιμενε" is a second person singular present imperative form of the verb. It is used to give a command or instruction to someone to persist or persevere in a certain action or behavior. 

Description: The word "επιμενε" is often used in a positive context, encouraging someone to continue with their efforts despite difficulties or obstacles. It can also be used in a negative context, warning someone not to persist in a harmful or destructive behavior.
