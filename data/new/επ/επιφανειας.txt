The word "επιφανειας" (epiphaneias) is a Greek noun that means "surface" or "appearance". 

Etymology: The word is derived from the Greek word "επιφάνεια" (epiphaneia), which means "manifestation" or "appearance". 

Grammar: "Επιφανειας" is in the genitive case, which is used to show possession or relationship. 

Description: The word can be used to refer to the surface of an object or the appearance of something. For example, "Η επιφάνεια του νερού ήταν ήρεμη" (I epifaneia tou nerou itan eremi) means "The surface of the water was calm". It can also be used metaphorically to refer to the outward appearance of a person or situation.
