The word "επερχομενοις" is a Greek verb in the present participle form, which means "coming" or "approaching". It is derived from the verb "έρχομαι" (erchomai), which means "to come". In terms of grammar, it is a masculine, plural, dative case form. It is commonly used in Greek literature and language to describe a group of people or things that are approaching or coming towards a certain location or situation.
