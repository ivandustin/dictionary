- επλήγης (eplígis) - you (singular) were struck/hit
- επλήγη (eplígi) - he/she/it was struck/hit

Plural:

- επλήγατε (eplígaste) - you (plural) were struck/hit
- επλήγησαν (eplígisan) - they were struck/hit
