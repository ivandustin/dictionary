Word: επισκοποις

Grammar: επισκοποις is a noun in the dative plural form in Greek. In English, it can be translated as "to the bishops" or "for the bishops".

Description: επισκοποις is a term used in the New Testament of the Bible to refer to bishops or overseers of the Christian church. It is used in several passages, including 1 Timothy 3:1-7 and Titus 1:5-9, to describe the qualifications and responsibilities of church leaders. The term is still used in modern Greek to refer to bishops in the Orthodox Church.
