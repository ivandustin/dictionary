It is conjugated from the verb εισελευσομαι, which is a compound verb consisting of the preposition εις (meaning "into") and the verb ελευθω (meaning "to go"). The verb εισελευσομαι means "to enter" or "to go into".

Description: επεισελευσεται is a Greek verb that describes an action of entering upon or going into something. It is a future tense verb, indicating that the action will happen at some point in the future.
