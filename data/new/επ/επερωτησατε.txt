Word: επερωτησατε

Etymology: The word επερωτησατε comes from the Greek verb επερωτάω (eperotao), which means "to ask" or "to inquire". The suffix -σατε (-sate) is the second person plural form of the aorist tense, indicating that the action of asking was performed by a group of people.

Grammar: επερωτησατε is a verb in the second person plural form of the aorist tense in Greek. It is used to address a group of people and indicates that they asked a question or made an inquiry in the past.

Description: επερωτησατε is a Greek verb that means "you all asked" or "you all inquired". It is commonly used in everyday conversation and formal settings to indicate that a group of people asked a question or made an inquiry. The word can be used in a variety of contexts, such as in a classroom setting, during a business meeting, or in a social gathering.
