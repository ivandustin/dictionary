Word: επιμελως

Etymology:
The word επιμελως (epimelos) is derived from the Greek words επι (epi) meaning "upon" or "over" and μελω (melo) meaning "to care for" or "to be concerned about". Therefore, επιμελως can be translated as "carefully" or "with great concern".

Grammar:
επιμελως is an adverb in Greek, which means that it modifies the verb in a sentence. It is often used to describe how an action is performed, indicating that it is done with great care or attention to detail.

Description:
επιμελως is a word that conveys a sense of diligence and attentiveness. It is often used to describe someone who is meticulous in their work or who takes great care in performing a task. It can also be used to describe a situation or circumstance that requires careful attention or consideration. Overall, επιμελως is a word that emphasizes the importance of being thorough and conscientious in one's actions.
