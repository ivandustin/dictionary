The word "επερωτωντα" is a present participle in Greek, which means "asking" or "inquiring". It is derived from the verb "επερωταω" (eperotao), which means "to ask" or "to inquire". The word is formed by adding the present participle suffix "-ντα" (-nta) to the stem of the verb. In terms of grammar, it is a masculine singular form in the nominative case.
