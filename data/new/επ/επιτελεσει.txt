The word "επιτελεσει" (epitelesei) is a Greek verb in the future tense, which means "to complete" or "to accomplish". 

Etymology: The word is derived from the Greek root "επιτελέω" (epiteleo), which means "to bring to completion" or "to finish". 

Grammar: "Επιτελεσει" is the third person singular form of the future tense of the verb "επιτελέω". 

Description: The word "επιτελεσει" is often used in Greek to express the idea of completing a task or achieving a goal. It can be used in various contexts, such as in business, education, or personal life. For example, "Θα επιτελέσει το έργο του σήμερα" (Tha epi telesei to ergo tou simera) means "He/She will complete his/her work today".
