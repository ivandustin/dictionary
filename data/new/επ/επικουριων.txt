Word: επικουριων

Etymology: The word επικουριων comes from the Greek word επικουρία (epikouría), which means "assistance" or "support". The suffix -ων (-on) is added to form the adjective form, meaning "providing assistance" or "supportive".

Grammar: επικουριων is an adjective in the masculine singular form. It is used to describe a person or thing that provides assistance or support.

Description: επικουριων is a word that describes someone or something that is supportive or helpful. It can be used to describe a person who provides assistance in a time of need, or a tool or resource that helps to accomplish a task. The word has a positive connotation and is often used to describe someone who is reliable and dependable.
