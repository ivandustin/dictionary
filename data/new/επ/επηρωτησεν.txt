Word: επηρώτησεν

Etymology: 
The word επηρώτησεν (epērōtēsen) is a verb in the aorist tense, third person singular form of the Greek word επερωτάω (eperōtaō), which means "to ask, inquire, question". The word is derived from the prefix επι- (epi-), which means "upon" or "over", and the verb ρωτάω (rōtaō), which means "to ask".
