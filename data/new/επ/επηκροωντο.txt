Word: επηκροωντο

Etymology:
The word επηκροωντο is derived from the Greek verb επακροάομαι (epakroáomai), which means "to listen attentively" or "to hear with attention". The prefix επι- (epi-) means "upon" or "over", while the root word ακροάομαι (akroáomai) means "to hear". The suffix -ντο (-nto) indicates the third person plural passive voice.

επηκροωντο is a verb in the ancient Greek language. It is in the third person plural passive voice, indicating that the subject of the sentence is receiving the action of the verb. The tense of the verb is aorist, which indicates a completed action in the past.

Description:
The word επηκροωντο describes a group of people who were being attentively listened to or heard by someone else.
