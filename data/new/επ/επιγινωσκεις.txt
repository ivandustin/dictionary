Word: επιγινωσκεις

Etymology: The word επιγινωσκεις comes from the Greek root words επι (epi) meaning "upon" or "over" and γινωσκω (ginosko) meaning "to know". Therefore, επιγινωσκεις means "to know upon" or "to recognize".

Grammar: επιγινωσκεις is a verb in the present tense, second person singular form. It is derived from the verb γινωσκω (ginosko) which means "to know". The prefix επι (epi) adds the meaning of "upon" or "over" to the verb.

Description: επιγινωσκεις is a Greek verb that means "to recognize" or "to acknowledge". It is often used in a spiritual or religious context to refer to recognizing or acknowledging the truth or existence of a higher power or deity. In everyday language, it can also be used to mean "to understand" or "to be aware of".
