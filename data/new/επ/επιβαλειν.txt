Word: επιβαλειν

Etymology:
The word επιβαλειν comes from the Greek verb επιβάλλω (epibállō), which is a combination of the prefix επι- (epi-), meaning "on" or "upon," and the verb βάλλω (bállō), meaning "to throw" or "to cast." Therefore, επιβαλειν literally means "to throw upon" or "to impose."

The stem of επιβαλειν is επιβαλ-, and it can be conjugated in various tenses, moods, and voices to express different meanings.

επιβαλειν is a versatile verb that can be used in different contexts to convey different meanings.
