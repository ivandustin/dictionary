Word: επιστρέφετε

Etymology: 
The word επιστρέφετε comes from the Greek verb επιστρέφω (epistrefo), which means "to turn back" or "to return". The verb is formed from the prefix επι- (epi-), which means "upon" or "on", and the verb στρέφω (strefo), which means "to turn".

Grammar:
επιστρέφετε is the second person plural form of the present tense of the verb επιστρέφω. It is used to address a group of people and means "you are returning" or "you are turning back".

Description:
επιστρέφετε is a common verb in Greek and is used in various contexts. It can be used to describe physical actions, such as returning to a place or turning back while walking. It can also be used metaphorically to describe a change in direction or a change in opinion. In addition, it can be used in a more formal context, such as in official documents or announcements.
