Word: επιστολη

Etymology: The word επιστολη comes from the Greek word ἐπιστολή (epistolḗ), which means "letter" or "missive". It is derived from the verb ἐπιστέλλω (epistéllō), which means "to send a message".

Grammar: επιστολη is a feminine noun in Greek. It is declined in the singular and plural forms, and can be used as the subject or object of a sentence. For example, "Η επιστολη είναι γραμμένη στα ελληνικά" (The letter is written in Greek).

Description: επιστολη refers to a written message or letter that is sent from one person to another. It can be used to convey information, express emotions, or communicate ideas. In modern Greek, επιστολη can also refer to an email or other electronic message.
