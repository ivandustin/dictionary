Word: επερώτημα

Etymology: The word επερώτημα (eperótima) comes from the Greek verb επερωτώ (eperotó), which means "to ask" or "to inquire". The suffix -μα (-ma) is added to the verb stem to form a noun, indicating the result or product of the action.

Grammar: επερώτημα is a neuter noun in the singular form. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their gender, number, and case. The word can be declined in various cases, such as επερωτήματος (genitive case), επερωτήματι (dative case), and επερωτήματα (plural form).

Description: επερώτημα refers to a question or an inquiry. It can be used in various contexts, such as in a conversation, an interview, or a survey. The word implies a desire for information or clarification on a particular topic. It is a common word in Greek language and is used in both formal and informal settings.
