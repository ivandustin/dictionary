The word "επνιγεν" (epnigen) is a Greek verb in the aorist tense, which means it refers to a completed action in the past. It comes from the root word "πνίγω" (pnigo), which means "to choke" or "to suffocate". The prefix "επ-" (ep-) in this case adds emphasis to the action, indicating that the choking or suffocating was particularly severe or intense. 

In terms of grammar, "επνιγεν" is a third-person singular verb, meaning it refers to a single subject (he/she/it) who performed the action. 

Overall, "επνιγεν" describes a past event in which someone or something was choked or suffocated in a forceful or intense manner.
