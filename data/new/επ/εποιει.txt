Word: εποιει

Etymology:
The word εποιει (epoiei) is a conjugated form of the Greek verb ποιέω (poiéo), which means "to make" or "to do". The verb comes from the Proto-Indo-European root *kʷey- which means "to make" or "to build".

Grammar:
εποιει (epoiei) is the third person singular, imperfect indicative active form of the verb ποιέω (poiéo). In Greek, the imperfect tense is used to describe actions that were ongoing or repeated in the past. The indicative mood indicates that the action is a statement of fact.
