The word επισκευασαμενοι is a Greek verb that comes from the root word επισκευάζω (episkevázo), which means "to repair" or "to fix". The suffix -μενοι (-menoi) indicates that the verb is in the past participle form, meaning "having repaired" or "having fixed".
