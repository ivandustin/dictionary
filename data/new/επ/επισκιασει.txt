Word: επισκιασει

Etymology: The word επισκιασει comes from the Greek verb επισκιάζω (episkiázo), which means "to overshadow" or "to cast a shadow over". It is derived from the prefix επι- (epi-), meaning "over" or "upon", and the verb σκιάζω (skiázo), meaning "to cast a shadow".

Grammar: επισκιασει is a verb in the Greek language, specifically in the present tense, third person singular form. It is a regular verb that follows the conjugation pattern of the verb σκιάζω.

Description: The word επισκιασει describes the action of overshadowing or casting a shadow over something or someone. It can be used in a literal sense, such as when an object or person is blocking the sun and casting a shadow, or in a figurative sense, such as when a negative event or circumstance is overshadowing a positive one.
