Etymology: The word is derived from the Greek verb "επαγγελλω" (epangello), which means "to promise" or "to pledge". 

Grammar: "Επαγγελιων" is a masculine noun in the genitive case, which is often used in the New Testament to refer to the "Gospel" or "Good News" of Jesus Christ.
