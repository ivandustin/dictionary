Word: επιβαρώ

Etymology: The word επιβαρώ comes from the combination of the prefix επι- (epi-), meaning "on" or "upon," and the verb βαρύνω (varyno), meaning "to burden" or "to weigh down." 

Grammar: επιβαρώ is a Greek verb in the present tense, first person singular form. It belongs to the first conjugation of Greek verbs, which means that it follows the regular pattern of verbs that end in -ω. 

Description: The verb επιβαρώ means "to burden" or "to weigh down." It is often used to describe a situation where someone or something is causing a heavy load or burden on someone else. For example, "Η δουλειά μου με επιβαρώνει πολύ" (I am heavily burdened by my work). It can also be used in a more figurative sense, such as "Η απόφαση του δικαστηρίου με επιβάρυνε πολύ" (The court's decision weighed heavily on me).
