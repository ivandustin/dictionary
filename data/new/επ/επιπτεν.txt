The word "επιπτεν" (epipten) is a Greek verb that means "to fall upon" or "to happen suddenly". 

Etymology: The word is derived from the Greek prefix "επι-" (epi-), meaning "upon" or "on", and the verb "πιπτω" (pipto), meaning "to fall". 

Grammar: "Επιπτεν" is a third-person singular present tense verb in Greek. 

Description: The word is often used to describe unexpected or sudden events or occurrences. It can also be used in a more literal sense to describe something falling upon or landing on something else.
