Word: εποιησαμεν

Etymology: εποιησαμεν is a Greek verb that comes from the root word ποιέω (poiéo), which means "to make" or "to do". The prefix επι- (epi-) adds the meaning of "upon" or "over", so εποιησαμεν can be translated as "we made upon" or "we did over".

Grammar: εποιησαμεν is the first person plural aorist active indicative form of the verb ποιέω (poiéo). In Greek, the aorist tense is used to describe a completed action in the past. The active voice indicates that the subject (we) performed the action, and the indicative mood indicates that the action is a statement of fact.
