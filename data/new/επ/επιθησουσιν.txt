Word: επιθησουσιν

Etymology:
The word επιθησουσιν is a Greek verb in the future tense, third person plural form of the verb επιτίθημι (epitithemi), which means "to place upon" or "to attack". The word is derived from the prefix επι- (epi-), which means "upon" or "over", and the verb τίθημι (tithemi), which means "to place" or "to set".
