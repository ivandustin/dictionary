Grammar: "επιστευεν" is the third person singular past tense form of the verb "πιστεύω". In Greek, verbs change their form depending on the tense, person, and number of the subject. 

Description: The word "επιστευεν" is used to describe an action that was completed in the past, specifically an action of believing or having faith in something or someone. It is often used in religious contexts to describe a person's belief in God or a higher power.
