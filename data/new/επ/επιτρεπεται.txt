Word: επιτρεπεται

Etymology: The word επιτρεπεται comes from the Greek verb επιτρέπω (epitrépo), which means "to allow" or "to permit".

Grammar: επιτρεπεται is a verb in the present tense, passive voice, and indicative mood. It is the third person singular form of the verb επιτρέπω.

Description: επιτρεπεται means "is allowed" or "is permitted" in Greek. It is often used in official or legal contexts to indicate what is allowed or not allowed. For example, a sign in a park might say "Επιτρέπεται η είσοδος" (epitrépetai i eísodos), which means "Entry is allowed".
