The word "επιβουλαις" (epiboulais) is a Greek noun that means "plots" or "schemes". It is derived from the verb "επιβουλευω" (epibouleuo), which means "to plot against" or "to scheme". In terms of grammar, it is a feminine noun in the nominative plural form. It is often used in the context of political or military plots, but can also refer to any kind of scheme or plan.
