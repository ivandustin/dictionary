The word εποτισαμεν is a Greek verb in the aorist tense, which means it refers to a completed action in the past. It is derived from the root word ποτίζω (potizo), which means "to water" or "to irrigate."

εποτισαμεν is the first person plural form of the aorist tense in the Greek language. It is formed by adding the prefix ε- to the root word ποτίζω and then adding the appropriate ending for the first person plural.
