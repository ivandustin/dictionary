The word "επιεικειας" (epieikeias) is a Greek noun that can be translated as "equity" or "fairness". It comes from the adjective "επιεικής" (epieikēs), which means "reasonable" or "moderate". In Greek philosophy, "επιείκεια" is a virtue that refers to the ability to make fair and just decisions, even in difficult or ambiguous situations. In grammar, "επιεικειας" is a genitive singular form of the noun.
