Word: επιστρεψει

Etymology: 
The word επιστρεψει comes from the Greek verb επιστρέφω (epistrefo), which means "to turn back" or "to return". It is derived from the prefix επι- (epi-), meaning "upon" or "on top of", and the verb στρέφω (strefo), meaning "to turn".

Grammar:
επιστρεψει is a verb in the future tense, third person singular form. It is conjugated from the verb επιστρέφω (epistrefo), which is a regular verb in Greek.

Description:
επιστρεψει means "he/she/it will return" in English. It is commonly used in everyday conversation and writing in Greek. The word can be used in various contexts, such as when referring to a person returning home, a company returning to profitability, or a situation returning to normalcy.
