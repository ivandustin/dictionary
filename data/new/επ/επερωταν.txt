Word: επερωταν

Etymology: The word επερωταν comes from the Greek verb ερωτάω (erotao), which means "to ask" or "to inquire". The prefix επι- (epi-) is added to the beginning of the verb to indicate a continuous or repeated action.

Description: επερωταν means "they were asking" or "they used to ask" in Greek. It describes a past action that was ongoing or repeated. The word can be used in a variety of contexts, such as in a narrative or in a conversation.
