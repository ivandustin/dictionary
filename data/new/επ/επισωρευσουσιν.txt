Word: επισωρευσουσιν

Etymology: 
The word επισωρευσουσιν is a Greek verb in the future tense, third person plural form. It is derived from the root word "σωρεύω" (soreuo), which means "to heap up" or "to accumulate". The prefix "επι-" (epi-) means "upon" or "on top of", so the word επισωρευσουσιν can be translated as "they will heap up" or "they will accumulate".
