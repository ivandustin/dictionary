Word: επιδείξαι

Etymology: The word επιδείξαι comes from the Greek verb επιδείκνυμαι (epideiknymai), which means "to show" or "to demonstrate". It is formed from the prefix επι- (epi-), meaning "upon" or "over", and the verb δείκνυμι (deiknymi), meaning "to show" or "to point out".

Grammar: επιδείξαι is a verb in the aorist tense, which is used to describe a completed action in the past. It is in the second person singular form, which means it is addressing one person. The verb is in the imperative mood, which is used to give commands or make requests.

Description: επιδείξαι means "show" or "demonstrate" in Greek. It is often used in a command or request to ask someone to show or demonstrate something. For example, "Επιδείξαι μου πώς να κάνω αυτό το κόλπο" (Epideíxai mou pós na káno aftó to kólpo) means "Show me how to do this trick".
