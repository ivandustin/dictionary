The word "επιστηθι" (epistethi) is a Greek verb that means "to stand upon" or "to rely on". 

Etymology: The word is derived from the Greek words "επί" (epi), meaning "upon", and "στήθος" (stethos), meaning "chest" or "breast". 

Grammar: "Επιστηθι" is a second person singular imperative verb in the present tense. 

Description: The word is often used in a figurative sense, meaning to rely on or trust in something or someone. It can also be used in a literal sense, such as standing on a physical object.
