The word "επιμενηις" (epimenis) is a Greek noun that means "perseverance" or "persistence". 

Etymology: The word is derived from the Greek verb "μένω" (meno), which means "to remain" or "to stay". The prefix "επι-" (epi-) means "upon" or "over", so "επιμενηις" can be translated as "remaining upon" or "staying over".

Grammar: "επιμενηις" is a feminine noun in the nominative singular form. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their gender, number, and case.
