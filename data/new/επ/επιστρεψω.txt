Word: επιστρέψω

Etymology: The word επιστρέψω comes from the Greek root words επί (epi) meaning "upon" or "on" and στρέφω (strefo) meaning "to turn". Therefore, επιστρέψω means "to turn upon" or "to turn back".

Grammar: επιστρέψω is a verb in the future tense, first person singular form. It is derived from the verb στρέφω (strefo) which means "to turn". The prefix επί (epi) adds the meaning of "upon" or "on" to the verb.

Description: επιστρέψω means "I will return" or "I will come back". It is used to express the action of going back to a place or situation that was previously left. It can also be used metaphorically to express the idea of returning to a previous state of mind or emotion.
