Word: επιμεληθητι

Etymology: The word επιμεληθητι comes from the Greek verb επιμελεομαι (epimeléomai), which means "to take care of" or "to be responsible for". The suffix -τι (-ti) is a nominalizing suffix, which turns the verb into a noun.

Grammar: επιμεληθητι is a noun in the singular form. It belongs to the third declension of Greek nouns, which means that its endings change depending on its grammatical function in a sentence. The word is feminine in gender.

Description: επιμεληθητι refers to the act of taking care of something or being responsible for it. It can also mean "supervision" or "management". The word is often used in legal or administrative contexts, where someone is appointed to take care of a specific task or responsibility.
