Word: εποικοδομησεν

Etymology: The word εποικοδομησεν comes from the Greek roots επί (epi) meaning "upon" or "on" and οικοδομέω (oikodomeo) meaning "to build" or "to construct". 

Grammar: εποικοδομησεν is a verb in the aorist tense, third person singular form. In Greek, the aorist tense is used to describe a completed action in the past. 

Description: εποικοδομησεν means "he/she/it built upon" or "he/she/it constructed on". It is often used metaphorically to describe the act of building or constructing something in a figurative sense, such as building up someone's character or building a relationship.
