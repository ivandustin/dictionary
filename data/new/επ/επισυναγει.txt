The word "επισυναγει" (episynagei) is a Greek verb that means "to gather together" or "to assemble." 

Etymology: The word is derived from the prefix "επι-" (epi-), meaning "upon" or "together," and the verb "συναγειρω" (synageiro), meaning "to gather together."

Grammar: "Επισυναγει" is a third-person singular present active indicative verb in Greek.

Description: The word is often used in the context of religious gatherings or meetings, such as in the New Testament of the Bible where it is used to describe the gathering of believers in a church or synagogue. It can also be used more generally to describe any kind of gathering or assembly of people.
