The word "επιγνωι" (epignōi) is a Greek verb that means "to recognize" or "to acknowledge." It is derived from the prefix "epi-" meaning "upon" or "above," and the verb "gnōi" meaning "to know." 

In terms of grammar, "επιγνωι" is a second person singular aorist imperative verb, which means it is a command directed towards one person to perform a specific action in the past. 

In usage, "επιγνωι" can be used in various contexts, such as acknowledging someone's authority or expertise, recognizing a situation or problem, or accepting responsibility for one's actions.
