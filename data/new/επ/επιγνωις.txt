The word "επιγνωις" (epignōis) is a Greek noun that means "knowledge" or "recognition". It is derived from the prefix "epi-" meaning "upon" or "over" and the verb "gnōsis" meaning "knowledge". In Greek philosophy, "epignōis" refers to a deeper, more profound understanding or knowledge of something. 

In terms of grammar, "επιγνωις" is a feminine noun in the nominative singular form. It can also be declined in other cases and numbers, such as "επιγνωσεως" (genitive singular) or "επιγνωσεις" (nominative plural).

Overall, "επιγνωις" is a word that conveys the idea of a higher level of knowledge or understanding, often gained through experience or reflection.
