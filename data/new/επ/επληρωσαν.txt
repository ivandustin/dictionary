Word: επληρωσαν

Etymology: επληρωσαν is a Greek verb that comes from the root word πληρόω (pliroo), which means "to fill" or "to complete". The prefix ε- (e-) indicates a completed action in the past tense.

Grammar: επληρωσαν is the third person plural form of the aorist tense in Greek. It is a completed action in the past, indicating that "they filled" or "they completed" something.

Description: επληρωσαν is a verb that describes the completion of a task or the filling of something. It can be used in various contexts, such as filling a container with water, completing a project, or fulfilling a promise. It is a strong and decisive verb that conveys a sense of accomplishment and satisfaction.
