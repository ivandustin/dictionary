Word: επιχορηγουμενον

Etymology:
The word επιχορηγουμενον is derived from the Greek verb επιχορηγέω (epichorēgeō), which means "to supply with funds" or "to support financially". The word is formed from the prefix επι- (epi-), meaning "on" or "upon", and the verb χορηγέω (chorēgeō), meaning "to supply" or "to provide".

Grammar:
επιχορηγουμενον is a present participle in the passive voice, masculine gender, singular number, and nominative case. It is derived from the verb επιχορηγέω (epichorēgeō), which is a compound verb formed from the prefix επι- (epi-) and the verb χορηγέω (chorēgeō).

Description:
The word επιχορηγουμενον refers to something that is being supplied or supported financially. It can be used to describe a person, organization, or project that is receiving financial assistance. The word is often used in the context of government funding or grants, where the recipient is referred to as the επιχορηγούμενος (epichorēgoumenos) or beneficiary.
