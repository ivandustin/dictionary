The word "επιστηριζων" is a Greek verb that means "supporting" or "upholding". 

Etymology: The word is derived from the Greek root "στηρίζω" (stirizo), which means "to support" or "to prop up". The prefix "επι-" (epi-) adds the meaning of "upon" or "over", giving the word the sense of "supporting from above".

Grammar: "Επιστηριζων" is the present participle form of the verb, which means it describes an ongoing action of supporting or upholding. It is in the masculine singular form, indicating that the subject of the sentence is a male.

Description: The word "επιστηριζων" can be used in various contexts, such as describing a physical support structure or a person who provides emotional or moral support. It can also be used figuratively to describe an argument or evidence that supports a particular claim or position.
