Word: επεσα

Etymology: επεσα is a Greek verb that comes from the root word πίπτω (pípto), which means "to fall."

Grammar: επεσα is the first person singular past tense form of the verb πίπτω (pípto), which means "I fell" in English.

Description: επεσα is a verb that describes the action of falling. It is used to indicate that someone or something has fallen from a higher position to a lower one. It can also be used metaphorically to describe a sudden decline or failure in a situation or circumstance.
