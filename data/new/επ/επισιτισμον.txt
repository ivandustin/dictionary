The word "επισιτισμός" (episitismos) is a Greek noun that refers to the act of providing food or sustenance. 

Etymology: The word is derived from the Greek words "επί" (epi), meaning "on" or "upon," and "σίτος" (sitos), meaning "food" or "grain."

Grammar: "Επισιτισμός" is a masculine noun in the singular form. In the plural form, it becomes "επισιτισμοί" (episitismoi).

Description: The term "επισιτισμός" is often used in the context of food security and access to food. It can also refer to the provision of food in emergency situations, such as during natural disasters or times of war. In addition, the word can be used in a broader sense to describe the act of providing sustenance or support in general.
