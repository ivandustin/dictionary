Word: επιβαλλον

Etymology:
The word επιβαλλον comes from the Greek verb επιβάλλω (epibállō), which means "to impose" or "to inflict". It is derived from the prefix επι- (epi-), meaning "on" or "upon", and the verb βάλλω (bállō), meaning "to throw" or "to cast".

Grammar:
επιβαλλον is the present participle of the verb επιβάλλω (epibállō), which means "imposing" or "inflicting". It is in the masculine gender, singular number, and nominative case.

Description:
επιβαλλον is a word that describes the action of imposing or inflicting something on someone or something else. It can be used in a variety of contexts, such as in legal or political situations where a law or regulation is imposed on a group of people, or in personal relationships where one person may impose their will or desires on another. The word has a strong connotation of force or coercion, and is often used in a negative sense.
