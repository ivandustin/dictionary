The word "επιγνωσεως" (epignoseos) is a Greek noun that means "knowledge" or "recognition". It is derived from the verb "επιγινωσκω" (epiginosko), which means "to know" or "to recognize". In terms of grammar, it is a genitive singular noun in the Greek language.
