The word επεκληθη (epekli̱thi̱) is a Greek verb that comes from the root word καλέω (kaléo), which means "to call." The prefix επι- (epi-) means "upon" or "on," so επεκληθη can be translated as "was called upon" or "was called out."

επεκληθη is a third-person singular aorist passive indicative verb in Greek. The aorist tense indicates a completed action in the past, while the passive voice indicates that the subject received the action of the verb.
