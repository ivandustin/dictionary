Word: επικληθεν

Etymology:
The word επικληθεν is derived from the Greek verb επικαλέω (epikaleo), which means "to call upon" or "to invoke". The word is formed from the prefix επι- (epi-), which means "upon" or "over", and the verb καλέω (kaleo), which means "to call".

επικληθεν is the aorist passive participle of the verb επικαλέω (epikaleo).
