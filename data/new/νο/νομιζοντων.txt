The word "νομιζοντων" is a present participle in Greek, derived from the verb "νομίζω" (nomizo), which means "to think" or "to believe". The participle form indicates that the action of thinking or believing is ongoing or continuous. 

In terms of grammar, "νομιζοντων" is in the genitive case, which is used to indicate possession or relationship. It can be translated as "of those who think" or "of those who believe".

Overall, "νομιζοντων" is a word that describes a group of people who hold a certain belief or opinion.
