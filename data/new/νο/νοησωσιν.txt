The word "νοησωσιν" is a verb in the Greek language, which is in the aorist subjunctive mood and third person plural form. It is derived from the root word "νοέω" (noeo), which means "to perceive, understand, or comprehend." 

In terms of grammar, the aorist subjunctive mood is used to express a hypothetical or potential action that may or may not occur. The third person plural form indicates that the action is being performed by a group of people.

Overall, "νοησωσιν" can be translated to mean "they might perceive/understand/comprehend."
