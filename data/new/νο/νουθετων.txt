Word: νουθετων

Etymology:
The word νουθετων comes from the Greek verb νουθετέω (noutheteo), which means "to admonish" or "to warn". It is derived from the words νους (nous), meaning "mind" or "intellect", and θέτω (theto), meaning "to place" or "to set".
