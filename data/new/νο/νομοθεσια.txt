Word: νομοθεσία

Etymology: The word νομοθεσία comes from the Greek words νόμος (nomos) meaning "law" and θέσις (thesis) meaning "placing" or "setting". Therefore, νομοθεσία literally means "the setting of laws".

Grammar: νομοθεσία is a feminine noun in Greek. Its plural form is νομοθεσίες.

Description: νομοθεσία refers to the process of creating and enacting laws. It can also refer to the body of laws that have been created by a particular government or legislative body. In modern usage, νομοθεσία is often used to refer to the legal system as a whole.
