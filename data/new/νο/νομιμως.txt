νομιμως (nomimōs) is an adverb in Greek that means "legally" or "lawfully". 

Etymology: The word is derived from the Greek noun "νόμος" (nomos) which means "law" or "custom".

Grammar: νομιμως is an adverb that modifies verbs, adjectives, or other adverbs. It is the adverbial form of the adjective "νόμιμος" (nomimos) which means "legal" or "lawful".

Description: The word νομιμως is often used in legal contexts to describe actions or behaviors that are in accordance with the law. It can also be used more broadly to describe actions or behaviors that are considered morally or socially acceptable.
