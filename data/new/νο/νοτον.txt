The word "νοτον" (noton) is a Greek word that means "south" in English. It is derived from the Greek word "νότος" (notos), which also means "south". In terms of grammar, "νοτον" is a noun in the accusative case. It can be used to describe a direction or location, as in "Το σπίτι μου είναι νοτον της πόλης" (My house is south of the city).
