Word: νομιζων

Etymology: νομιζων is the present participle of the Greek verb νομιζω (nomizō), which means "to think, believe, consider, suppose, judge, reckon". The word comes from the noun νομος (nomos), which means "law, custom, usage, rule".

Grammar: νομιζων is a present participle in the masculine gender, singular number, and nominative case. It is derived from the verb νομιζω and functions as an adjective, modifying a noun or pronoun.

Description: νομιζων describes someone who is currently thinking, believing, considering, supposing, judging, or reckoning. It can also be used to describe someone who is known for thinking or believing a certain way. In context, it can mean "thinking", "believing", "considering", "supposing", "judging", or "reckoning".
