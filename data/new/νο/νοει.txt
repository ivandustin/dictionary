The word "νοει" (noei) is a verb in the Greek language. It is derived from the root word "νοεω" (noeo), which means "to perceive" or "to understand". 

In terms of grammar, "νοει" is in the present tense and is the third person singular form of the verb. It can be translated to mean "he/she/it perceives" or "he/she/it understands". 

In terms of description, "νοει" is a word that conveys the idea of mental perception or comprehension. It is often used in philosophical or spiritual contexts to describe the act of understanding or grasping abstract concepts.
