The word "νομικόν" (nomikon) is a Greek adjective that means "legal" or "pertaining to law". It is derived from the Greek noun "νόμος" (nomos), which means "law". In terms of grammar, "νομικόν" is a neuter singular adjective in the nominative case. 

In Greek language, "νομικόν" can be used to describe anything related to the law, such as legal documents, court proceedings, or legal principles. It can also be used to describe a person who is knowledgeable about the law or works in the legal profession. 

Overall, "νομικόν" is a versatile word that is commonly used in legal contexts in the Greek language.
