In terms of grammar, "νοσων" is a masculine noun in the nominative singular form. It can also be declined in other cases and numbers, such as "νοσωνα" (nosona) in the accusative singular form. Overall, "νοσων" is a common word in Greek that is used to describe various types of diseases and illnesses.
