Word: σημερον

Etymology: 
The word σημερον (sēmeron) is a Greek adverb that means "today". It is derived from the combination of two words: σήμερα (sēmera), which means "today", and ἡμέρα (hēmera), which means "day". 

Grammar: 
In Greek, σημερον is an adverb that modifies verbs, adjectives, or other adverbs. It does not change form based on gender, number, or case. 

Description: 
σημερον is a common word in modern Greek and is used to refer to the current day or the present time. It is often used in everyday conversation and in formal writing. The word can also be found in ancient Greek texts, where it has the same meaning.
