Etymology: The word "κερδούς" (kerdous) comes from the Greek word "κέρδος" (kerdos), which means "gain" or "profit".

Grammar: "Κερδούς" is a noun in the genitive case. In Greek grammar, the genitive case is used to indicate possession, origin, or relationship.
