Etymology: The word "κεχρημαί" comes from the Greek verb "χράομαι" (chráomai), which means "to use" or "to make use of". The prefix "κε-" indicates a perfect tense, suggesting that the action of using has been completed or perfected.

Grammar: "Κεχρημαί" is a verb in the perfect tense, active voice, and indicative mood. It is the first person singular form of the verb "χράομαι" (chráomai), which means "I have used" or "I have made use of".

Description: "Κεχρημαί" is a word that describes the completed action of using or making use of something. It implies that the action has already been done in the past and is not ongoing.
