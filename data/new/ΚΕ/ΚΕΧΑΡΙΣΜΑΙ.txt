Etymology: The word "κεχαρισμαι" comes from the Greek verb "χαίρω" (chairō), which means "to rejoice" or "to be glad". The prefix "κε-" indicates a perfect tense, so "κεχαρισμαι" can be translated as "I have rejoiced" or "I am glad".

Grammar: "κεχαρισμαι" is a first-person singular verb form in the perfect tense. It is used to express an action that was completed in the past and has continuing effects in the present. In this case, it indicates that the speaker has experienced joy or gladness in the past and continues to feel that way.

Description: "κεχαρισμαι" is a word that conveys a sense of joy, happiness, or gladness. It suggests a state of being pleased or content. It can be used to express personal satisfaction or delight in a particular situation or event.
