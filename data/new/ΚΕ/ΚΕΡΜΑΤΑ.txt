Etymology: The word "κερματα" comes from the Greek word "κέρμα" (kérma), which means "coin" or "money".

Grammar: "κερματα" is a noun in the plural form. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their gender, number, and case. "κερματα" is the plural form of the noun "κέρμα".

Description: "κερματα" refers to coins or money in general. It can be used to describe a collection of coins or to refer to currency in a broader sense.
