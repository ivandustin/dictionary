Etymology: The word "κερδήσει" comes from the Greek verb "κερδίζω" (kerdízo), which means "to gain" or "to profit".

Grammar: "κερδήσει" is the third person singular future tense form of the verb "κερδίζω" (kerdízo), which means "he/she/it will gain" or "he/she/it will profit".

Description: "κερδήσει" is a Greek verb that expresses the action of gaining or profiting in the future tense. It is used to describe a future event where someone or something will acquire a benefit or advantage.
