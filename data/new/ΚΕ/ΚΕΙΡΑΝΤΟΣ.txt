Etymology: The word "κείραντος" (keirantos) comes from the Greek verb "κείρω" (keiro), which means "to cut". 

Grammar: "Κείραντος" is a masculine noun in the nominative case. It is the singular form of the word. 

Description: "Κείραντος" refers to a person who cuts or a cutter. It can be used to describe someone who cuts hair, fabric, or any other material.
