Etymology: The word "κερματιστας" comes from the Greek word "κέρμα" (kerma), meaning "coin" or "money", and the suffix "-ιστας" (-istas), which denotes a person who performs a specific action or occupation.

Grammar: "Κερματιστας" is a noun in the masculine gender and singular number in Greek. It belongs to the second declension class of nouns.

Description: "Κερματιστας" refers to a person who collects, studies, or deals with coins. It can also be used to describe someone who is an expert in numismatics, the study or collection of coins.
