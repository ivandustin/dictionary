Etymology: The word "κεκοπιακως" comes from the Greek verb "κεκοπιάω" (kekopiáo), which means "to toil" or "to work hard". The suffix "-ως" (-os) is added to form an adverb, indicating the manner in which the action is performed.

Grammar: "Κεκοπιακως" is an adverb in the Greek language. Adverbs modify verbs, adjectives, or other adverbs, providing information about the manner, time, place, or degree of an action or quality.

Description: "Κεκοπιακως" describes the manner in which an action is performed, indicating that it is done with hard work or toil. It can be used to express the effort or labor put into an activity.
