Etymology: The word "κεκοπιακα" comes from the Greek verb "κοπιάω" (kopiao), which means "to toil" or "to work hard". The suffix "-κα" is added to indicate the past tense, resulting in "κεκοπιακα".

Grammar: "κεκοπιακα" is a verb in the past tense, first person singular form. It is used to describe an action that was completed in the past.

Description: "κεκοπιακα" can be translated to English as "I worked hard" or "I toiled". It indicates that the speaker exerted a significant amount of effort or labor in the past.
