Etymology: The word "κειριαίς" comes from the Greek word "κείρω" (keirō), which means "to cut". 

Grammar: "κειριαίς" is a noun in the plural form. It belongs to the third declension in Greek grammar. 

Description: "κειριαίς" refers to a type of cutting instrument or shears. It is commonly used to describe a tool or device used for cutting hair, plants, or other materials.
