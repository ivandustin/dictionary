Etymology: The word "κερδησας" (kerdēsas) comes from the Greek verb "κερδίζω" (kerdizō), which means "to gain" or "to profit".

Grammar: "κερδησας" is the aorist active participle form of the verb "κερδίζω" (kerdizō). It is used to describe an action that has been completed in the past.

Description: "κερδησας" is a Greek word that means "having gained" or "having profited". It is used to indicate that someone has achieved a gain or profit in the past.
