Etymology: The word "κλεισαί" comes from the Greek verb "κλείω" (kleió), which means "to close" or "to shut". 

Grammar: "κλεισαί" is the plural form of the noun "κλεισίς" (kleisís) in Greek. It is a feminine noun of the first declension. 

Description: "κλεισαί" refers to "locks" or "closures" in Greek. It can be used to describe physical locks or closures, as well as metaphorical ones.
