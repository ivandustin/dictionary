Grammar: "κλεψωσιν" is the third person plural, aorist subjunctive active form of the verb "κλέπτω" (klépto). In Greek grammar, the aorist tense refers to a past action that is viewed as a whole, without specifying its duration or repetition. The subjunctive mood indicates possibility, doubt, or uncertainty.
