Etymology: The word "κληρονομον" comes from the Greek roots "κληρος" (klēros) meaning "inheritance" and "νομος" (nomos) meaning "law". 

Grammar: "κληρονομον" is a noun in the singular form. It belongs to the third declension in Greek grammar.

Description: "κληρονομον" refers to a person who inherits property or possessions from someone who has passed away. It can also be used metaphorically to describe someone who inherits or continues a particular quality, trait, or tradition.
