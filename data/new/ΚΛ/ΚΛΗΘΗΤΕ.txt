Etymology: The word "κληθητε" comes from the Greek verb "καλώ" (kaló), which means "to call" or "to summon". It is conjugated in the second person plural, passive voice, aorist tense.

Grammar: "κληθητε" is the second person plural form of the verb "καλώ" in the passive voice, aorist tense. It is used to address a group of people and indicates that they were called or summoned by someone or something.

Description: "κληθητε" is a verb form that is used in Greek to express the action of being called or summoned. It is commonly used in various contexts, such as when someone is invited to an event, called by name, or summoned for a specific purpose.
