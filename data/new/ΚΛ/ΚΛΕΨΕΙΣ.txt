Etymology: The word "κλεψεις" (klepseis) comes from the Greek verb "κλέπτω" (klepto), which means "to steal". 

Grammar: "Κλεψεις" is a noun in the plural form. It belongs to the third declension in Greek grammar. 

Description: "Κλεψεις" refers to thefts or acts of stealing. It can be used to describe the action of taking someone else's property without permission or unlawfully.
