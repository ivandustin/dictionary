Etymology: The word "κλεπτας" (kléptas) comes from the Greek verb "κλέπτω" (klépto), which means "to steal". 

Grammar: "Κλέπτας" is a noun in the masculine gender and singular number. It belongs to the second declension in Greek grammar. 

Description: "Κλέπτας" means "thief" in Greek. It refers to a person who steals or commits theft.
