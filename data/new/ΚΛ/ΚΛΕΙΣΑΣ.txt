Etymology: The word "κλεισας" (klēsas) comes from the Greek verb "κλείω" (kleiō), which means "to close" or "to shut". 

Grammar: "Κλεισας" is the masculine form of the Greek adjective, which means "closed" or "shut". It is in the nominative case, singular number.
