Etymology: The word "κλεπτουσιν" comes from the Greek verb "κλέπτω" (kleptō), which means "to steal". 

Grammar: "κλεπτουσιν" is the present participle form of the verb "κλέπτω" (kleptō), which indicates ongoing or continuous action. It is in the third person plural form.

Description: "κλεπτουσιν" is used to describe a group of people who are currently stealing or engaged in the act of stealing.
