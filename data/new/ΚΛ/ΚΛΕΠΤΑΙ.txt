Etymology: The word "κλεπται" (kleptai) comes from the Greek verb "κλέπτω" (kleptō), which means "to steal". 

Grammar: "Κλεπται" is the plural form of the noun "κλέπτης" (kleptēs), which means "thief" in Greek. It belongs to the second declension of Greek nouns. 

Description: "Κλεπται" refers to a group of thieves or a gang of thieves. It is used to describe multiple individuals who engage in stealing or illegal activities.
