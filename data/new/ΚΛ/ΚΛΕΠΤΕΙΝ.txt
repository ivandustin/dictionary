Etymology: The word "κλεπτείν" (kleptein) comes from the Ancient Greek verb "κλέπτω" (kleptō), which means "to steal". 

Grammar: "Κλεπτείν" is the present infinitive form of the verb. In Greek, infinitives are used to express actions or states without specifying the subject or tense.
