Etymology: The word "κλαυσηι" (klau̯sēi) comes from the Greek verb "κλαίω" (klaíō), which means "to weep" or "to cry." 

Grammar: "κλαυσηι" is a noun in the singular form. It belongs to the first declension in Greek grammar. 

Description: "κλαυσηι" refers to the act of weeping or crying. It can also be used to describe a lamentation or mourning.
