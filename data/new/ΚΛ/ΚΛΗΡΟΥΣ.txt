Etymology: The word "κληρούς" comes from the Greek word "κλῆρος" (klêros), which means "lot" or "inheritance". It is derived from the verb "κληρόω" (klêroô), meaning "to assign by lot".

Grammar: In Greek, "κληρούς" is a noun in the masculine gender. It is in the nominative case, singular number.

Description: "Κληρούς" refers to someone who has inherited property or possessions. It can also be used to describe someone who has received an inheritance or a share of something.
