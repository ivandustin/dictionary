Etymology: The word "κλαδοις" (kladois) comes from the Greek word "κλάδος" (klados), meaning "branch" or "twig". 

Grammar: "Κλαδοις" is a noun in the plural form. It belongs to the second declension in Greek grammar. 

Description: "Κλαδοις" refers to branches or twigs, typically of a tree or plant. It can also be used metaphorically to describe divisions or subdivisions of a larger group or organization.
