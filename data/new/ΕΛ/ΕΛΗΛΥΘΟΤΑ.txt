Etymology: The word "εληλυθότα" comes from the Greek verb "έρχομαι" (erchomai), which means "to come". It is the perfect participle form of the verb, indicating completed action.

Grammar: "Εληλυθότα" is the neuter plural form of the perfect participle of the verb "έρχομαι". In Greek grammar, the perfect participle is used to express completed action in the past. The neuter plural form is used when referring to things or concepts rather than specific individuals.

Description: "Εληλυθότα" can be translated as "having come" or "having arrived". It is used to describe something that has already happened or someone who has already arrived at a certain place.
