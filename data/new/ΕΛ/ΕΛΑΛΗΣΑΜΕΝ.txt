The word "ελαλησαμεν" is a Greek verb in the past tense, first person plural form. It comes from the root verb "λαλώ" (laló), which means "to speak" or "to talk". The prefix "ε-" indicates the past tense, and the suffix "-σαμεν" indicates the first person plural form. Therefore, "ελαλησαμεν" translates to "we spoke" or "we talked" in English.
