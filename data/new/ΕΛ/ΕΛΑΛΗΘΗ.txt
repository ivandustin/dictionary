Etymology: The word "ελαληθη" comes from the Greek verb "λαλέω" (laleo), which means "to speak" or "to talk". The prefix "ε-" is added to indicate the past tense, resulting in "ελαληθη", which means "was spoken" or "was said".

Grammar: "Ελαληθη" is the past tense form of the verb "λαλέω" (laleo) in the third person singular. It is used to indicate that something was spoken or said in the past.
