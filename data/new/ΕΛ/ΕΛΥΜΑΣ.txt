Etymology: The word "ελυμας" (elumas) comes from the Greek word "ελυμαινω" (elumaino), which means "to reveal" or "to make clear."

Grammar: "Ελυμας" is a noun in the masculine gender and singular number. It belongs to the second declension in Greek grammar.

Description: "Ελυμας" refers to someone who reveals or clarifies something. It can also be used metaphorically to describe someone who enlightens or brings understanding to others.
