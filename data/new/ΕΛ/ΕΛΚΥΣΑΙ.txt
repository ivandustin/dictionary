Etymology: The word "ελκυσαί" comes from the Greek verb "ελκύω" (elkýo), which means "to draw" or "to pull". 

Grammar: "Ελκυσαί" is the plural form of the noun "ελκυσίς" (elkysís), which means "attraction" or "pulling". It belongs to the third declension in Greek grammar.

Description: "Ελκυσαί" refers to a group of things or people that are being attracted or pulled towards something. It can be used in various contexts, such as describing the attraction of objects by a magnetic force or the attraction of people towards a particular event or idea.
