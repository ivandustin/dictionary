Etymology: The word "ελπίδα" (elpida) comes from the Ancient Greek word "ἐλπίς" (elpis), meaning "hope".

Grammar: "Ελπίδα" is a feminine noun in Greek. It is in the singular form and can be declined according to the Greek grammar rules.

Description: "Ελπίδα" means "hope" in Greek. It refers to the feeling of expectation and desire for something positive to happen in the future. It is often associated with optimism and a belief in the possibility of a better outcome.
