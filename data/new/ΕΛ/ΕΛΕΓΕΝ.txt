Etymology: The word "ελεγεν" comes from the Greek word "ελεγεν" (elegen), which means "to say" or "to speak".

Grammar: "Ελεγεν" is a verb in the Greek language. It belongs to the aorist tense and active voice. It is conjugated in the third person singular form.

Description: "Ελεγεν" is used to express the action of speaking or saying something in the past. It can be translated as "he/she/it said" or "he/she/it spoke" in English.
