Etymology: The word "ελεγξει" (pronounced "elegksei") comes from the Greek verb "ελέγχω" (pronounced "elegcho"), which means "to examine" or "to check."

Grammar: "Ελεγξει" is the third person singular form of the future tense of the verb "ελέγχω." In Greek grammar, verbs are conjugated based on the person, number, tense, and mood.

Description: "Ελεγξει" is a verb form that indicates an action that will happen in the future. It can be translated to English as "he/she/it will examine" or "he/she/it will check."
