Etymology: The word "ελεγξιν" comes from the Greek word "ἐλέγχειν" (elegchein), which means "to examine" or "to test".

Grammar: "Ελεγξιν" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the singular form and belongs to the third declension.

Description: "Ελεγξιν" refers to the act of examining or testing something. It can also be used to describe the process of verifying or checking the accuracy or validity of something.
