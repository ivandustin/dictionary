The word "ελυπησεν" is a Greek verb in the aorist tense, third person singular form. It comes from the root word "λυπη" (lypi), which means "grief" or "sorrow". The prefix "ε" (e) indicates the past tense, and the suffix "σεν" (sen) indicates the third person singular form. Therefore, "ελυπησεν" can be translated as "he/she/it grieved" or "he/she/it caused sorrow".
