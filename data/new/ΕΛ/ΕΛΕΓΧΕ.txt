Etymology: The word "ελεγχε" (eleghche) comes from the Greek verb "ελέγχω" (elegcho), which means "to examine" or "to test."

Grammar: "Ελεγχε" is the imperative form of the verb "ελέγχω" (elegcho) in the second person singular. It is used to give a command or instruction to someone.
