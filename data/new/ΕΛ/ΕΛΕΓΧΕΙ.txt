Etymology: The word "ελεγχει" comes from the Greek verb "ελέγχω" (elegcho), which means "to examine" or "to test."

Grammar: "Ελεγχει" is the third person singular form of the verb "ελέγχω" in the present tense. It is used to describe an action that is being performed by a singular subject.

Description: "Ελεγχει" is a Greek word that can be translated to English as "he/she/it examines" or "he/she/it tests." It is commonly used in various contexts to indicate the act of examining or testing something.
