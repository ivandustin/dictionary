Etymology: The word "ελαλούμεν" comes from the Greek verb "λαλούμεν" (laloumen), which means "we speak". The prefix "ε-" (e-) is added to indicate the first person plural form of the verb.

Grammar: "Ελαλούμεν" is the first person plural present indicative active form of the verb "λαλώ" (lalo), which means "to speak" in Greek. It is used when referring to a group of people who are speaking.

Description: "Ελαλούμεν" is a word in the Greek language that expresses the action of speaking in the first person plural form. It is commonly used in everyday conversations and written texts to indicate that a group of people is speaking.
