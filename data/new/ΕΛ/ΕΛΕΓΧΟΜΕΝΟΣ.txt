Etymology: The word "ελεγχομένος" comes from the Greek verb "ελέγχω" (eléncho), which means "to examine" or "to test". The suffix "-μένος" (-menos) is added to the verb stem to form the past participle, indicating that the action of examining or testing has been completed.

Grammar: "Ελεγχομένος" is the masculine singular form of the past participle of the verb "ελέγχω" (eléncho). In Greek grammar, past participles are used to form compound tenses and passive voice constructions.

Description: "Ελεγχομένος" is an adjective that means "examined" or "tested" in Greek. It describes something or someone that has undergone examination or testing.
