Etymology: The word "ελαχιστος" (elachistos) comes from the Greek root "ελαχυς" (elachys), meaning "small" or "least."

Grammar: In Greek, "ελαχιστος" is an adjective in the masculine form. It has different forms depending on the gender and number of the noun it modifies.

Description: "Ελαχιστος" is used to describe something that is very small or minimal in quantity, size, or importance. It can also be used to express the idea of "least" or "smallest" in a comparative sense.
