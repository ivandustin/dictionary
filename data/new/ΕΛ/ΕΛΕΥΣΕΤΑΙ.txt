Etymology: The word "ελευσεται" comes from the Greek verb "ελευθερόω" (eleutheróo), which means "to set free" or "to liberate."

Grammar: "Ελευσεται" is a verb in the future tense, third person singular form. It is derived from the root "ελευθερό-" and is conjugated in the future tense with the suffix "-σεται" to indicate the third person singular.

Description: "Ελευσεται" is a Greek word that means "he/she/it will be set free" or "he/she/it will be liberated." It expresses a future action of someone or something being freed or liberated.
