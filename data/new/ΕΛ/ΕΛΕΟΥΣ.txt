Grammar: In Greek, "ελεος" is a noun of the masculine gender. It is in the nominative case and singular number. The word can also be declined in different cases, numbers, and genders depending on its usage in a sentence.

Description: "Ελεος" is a Greek word that translates to "mercy" or "compassion" in English. It refers to the quality of showing kindness, forgiveness, and understanding towards others who are suffering or in need. In Greek culture and religion, "ελεος" is considered a virtue and an important aspect of human morality.
