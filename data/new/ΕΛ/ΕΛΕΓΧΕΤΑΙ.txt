Etymology: The word "ελεγχεται" comes from the Greek verb "ελέγχω" (elegcho), which means "to examine" or "to test."

Grammar: "Ελεγχεται" is the third person singular form of the verb "ελέγχω" in the present tense. It is used to describe an action that is being performed by a singular subject.

Description: "Ελεγχεται" is a Greek word that means "it is examined" or "it is tested." It is commonly used to indicate that something is being checked or verified.
