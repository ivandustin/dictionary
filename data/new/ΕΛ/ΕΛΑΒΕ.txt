Etymology: The word "ελαβε" comes from the Greek verb "λαμβάνω" (lambáno), which means "to take" or "to receive".

Grammar: "Ελαβε" is the third person singular form of the aorist tense in the indicative mood of the verb "λαμβάνω". It is used to indicate a completed action in the past.

Description: "Ελαβε" is a Greek word that means "he/she/it took" or "he/she/it received". It is commonly used in everyday speech and writing to describe an action of taking or receiving something.
