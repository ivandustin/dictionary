Etymology: The word "ελεγξαι" (pronounced "elegkse") comes from the Greek verb "ελέγχω" (pronounced "elegcho"), which means "to examine" or "to test." 

Grammar: "Ελεγξαι" is the aorist tense, imperative mood, and second person singular form of the verb "ελέγχω." It is used to give a command or instruction to someone to examine or test something.

Description: "Ελεγξαι" is a Greek word that means "examine" or "test." It is used to instruct someone to carefully inspect or evaluate something.
