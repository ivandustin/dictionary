Etymology: The word "ελκυση" (elkysi) comes from the Greek verb "ελκύω" (elkyo), which means "to draw" or "to pull". 

Grammar: "Ελκυση" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the singular form and belongs to the feminine gender. 

Description: "Ελκυση" refers to the act of drawing or pulling something. It can also be used metaphorically to describe the attraction or allure of something.
