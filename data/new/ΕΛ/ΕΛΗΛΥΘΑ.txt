Etymology: The word "εληλυθα" comes from the Greek verb "ἔρχομαι" (erchomai), which means "to come" or "to arrive". It is in the aorist tense, indicating a completed action in the past.

Grammar: "εληλυθα" is the first person singular form of the aorist tense of the verb "ἔρχομαι" (erchomai). In Greek, verbs have different forms depending on the tense, mood, voice, and person. The aorist tense is used to express a completed action in the past.

Description: "εληλυθα" is a Greek word that means "I have come" or "I arrived". It is used to indicate that the action of coming or arriving has been completed in the past.
