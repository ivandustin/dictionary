Grammar: "Εληλυθεί" is a verb in the perfect tense, active voice, third person singular form. In Greek grammar, the perfect tense is used to indicate a completed action in the past that has ongoing relevance to the present.

Description: The word "εληλυθεί" can be translated as "he/she/it has come" or "he/she/it came". It implies that the action of coming has been completed in the past, but it still has some significance or impact in the present.
