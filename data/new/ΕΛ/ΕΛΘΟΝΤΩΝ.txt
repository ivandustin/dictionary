Grammar: "Ελθοντων" is the genitive plural form of the verb "ερχομαι" (erchomai). In Greek, verbs have different forms depending on the tense, mood, voice, and number of the subject. The genitive case is used to indicate possession or origin.

Description: "Ελθοντων" is used to describe a group of people or things that have come or arrived. It indicates that the subject of the sentence is in the genitive case and is plural.
