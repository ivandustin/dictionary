Etymology: The word "ελληνιστας" comes from the Greek word "Έλληνας" (Ellinas), which means "Greek". The suffix "-ιστας" is added to indicate a person who is an expert or enthusiast in a particular field.

Grammar: "Ελληνιστας" is a noun in the masculine gender. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their case, number, and gender. It can be declined in various cases, such as nominative (ελληνιστας), genitive (ελληνιστα), accusative (ελληνιστα), etc.

Description: "Ελληνιστας" refers to a person who is a specialist or enthusiast in Greek language, culture, or history. It is often used to describe someone who studies, teaches, or promotes the Greek language and its various dialects.
