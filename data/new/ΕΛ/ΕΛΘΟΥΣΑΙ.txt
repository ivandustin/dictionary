Etymology: The word "ελθουσαι" (elthousai) is the third person singular, feminine, aorist active participle of the Greek verb "ερχομαι" (erchomai), which means "to come". 

Grammar: "Ελθουσαι" is a participle, which is a verb form that functions as an adjective. In this case, it is in the feminine gender, agreeing with a feminine noun or pronoun. The aorist tense indicates a completed action in the past. The active voice indicates that the subject of the verb is performing the action. 

Description: "Ελθουσαι" can be translated as "having come" or "having arrived" in English. It describes a female subject who has completed the action of coming or arriving at a certain place.
