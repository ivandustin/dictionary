Word: υψεί

Etymology: The word υψεί (ypsi) comes from the Greek verb υψόω (ypsoo), which means "to elevate" or "to raise up."

Grammar: υψεί is a verb in the present tense, third person singular form. It can be translated to "he/she/it elevates" or "he/she/it raises up."

Description: υψεί is a word that describes an action of lifting or raising something to a higher position. It can be used in a literal sense, such as lifting a heavy object, or in a figurative sense, such as raising someone's spirits.
