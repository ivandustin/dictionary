Ζαχαρίου (Zachariou) is a Greek male given name that is derived from the Hebrew name Zechariah, meaning "God remembers". In Greek, the name is spelled Ζαχαρίας (Zacharias) and is commonly shortened to Ζαχαρία (Zacharia). 

In terms of grammar, Ζαχαρίου is the genitive form of the name, indicating possession or association. For example, "the book of Zacharias" would be translated as "το βιβλίο του Ζαχαρίου" (to vivlio tou Zachariou) in Greek.

Overall, Ζαχαρίου is a common Greek name with a rich history and meaning.
