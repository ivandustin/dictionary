Etymology: The word "εγεννηθησαν" comes from the Greek verb "γεννιέμαι" (genniémai), which means "to be born" or "to come into existence." The prefix "ε-" (e-) indicates the past tense, and the suffix "-θησαν" (-thēsan) indicates the third person plural form.

Grammar: "Εγεννηθησαν" is the third person plural, past tense form of the verb "γεννιέμαι" (genniémai). It is used to describe an action that happened in the past and was performed by multiple individuals.

Description: "Εγεννηθησαν" is a Greek word that means "they were born" or "they came into existence." It is commonly used to refer to the birth or creation of multiple individuals or things.
