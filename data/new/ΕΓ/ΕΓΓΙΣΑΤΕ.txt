Etymology: The word "εγγισατε" comes from the Greek verb "εγγίζω" (engízo), which means "to approach" or "to come near."

Grammar: "Εγγισατε" is the second person plural form of the aorist tense in the Greek verb "εγγίζω." It is used to address a group of people and indicates that they approached or came near to something in the past.

Description: "Εγγισατε" is a verb form that expresses an action of approaching or coming near to something in the past. It is commonly used in Greek to describe physical or metaphorical proximity.
