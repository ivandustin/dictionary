Etymology: The word "εγνωσαν" (egnōsan) comes from the Greek verb "γινώσκω" (ginōskō), which means "to know" or "to understand". It is derived from the Proto-Indo-European root *ǵneh₃-.

Grammar: "Εγνωσαν" is the third person plural past tense form of the verb "γινώσκω" (ginōskō) in Greek. It is used to indicate that a group of people knew or understood something in the past.

Description: "Εγνωσαν" is a verb form that expresses an action of knowing or understanding that was completed in the past by a group of people. It is commonly used in narratives or descriptions to indicate that a certain knowledge or understanding was acquired by multiple individuals.
