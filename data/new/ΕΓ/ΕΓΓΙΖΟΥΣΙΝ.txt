Etymology: The word "εγγιζουσιν" comes from the Greek verb "εγγίζω" (engízo), which means "to approach" or "to come near."

Grammar: "Εγγιζουσιν" is the third person plural form of the present tense of the verb "εγγίζω" (engízo). It is used to describe an action being performed by a group of people or things.

Description: "Εγγιζουσιν" is a Greek word that means "they approach" or "they come near." It indicates that a group of individuals or objects are moving closer to something or someone.
