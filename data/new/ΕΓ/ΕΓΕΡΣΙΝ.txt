Etymology: The word "εγερσιν" comes from the Greek verb "εγείρω" (egero), which means "to wake up" or "to rise". 

Grammar: "Εγερσιν" is a noun in the accusative case. It is the accusative form of the noun "εγερσις" (egersis), which means "awakening" or "rising". 

Description: "Εγερσιν" refers to the act of waking up or rising from sleep. It can also be used metaphorically to describe a revival or awakening in a broader sense.
