Etymology: The word "εγερω" (egero) comes from the Greek verb "εγείρω" (egeiro), which means "to wake up" or "to rise". 

Grammar: "Εγερω" is a verb in the present tense, first person singular form. It is conjugated from the verb "εγείρω" (egeiro), which is an active verb in Greek.
