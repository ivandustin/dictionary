Etymology: The word "εγεγονει" comes from the Greek verb "γίνομαι" (ginomai), which means "to become" or "to happen". It is in the past continuous tense.

Grammar: "Εγεγονει" is the third person singular form of the verb "γίνομαι" in the past continuous tense. It is used to describe an ongoing action or state in the past.

Description: "Εγεγονει" is used to express an ongoing action or state that was happening in the past. It is often translated as "was happening" or "was becoming" in English.
