Etymology: The word "εγγισαντος" comes from the Greek verb "εγγίζω" (engízo), which means "to approach" or "to come near". The suffix "-αντος" (-antos) is a participle ending that indicates the past tense.

Grammar: "Εγγισαντος" is the masculine singular form of the past participle of the verb "εγγίζω" (engízo). It agrees with a masculine noun in gender, number, and case.

Description: "Εγγισαντος" is used to describe a masculine singular noun that has approached or come near something in the past. It can be translated as "having approached" or "having come near".
