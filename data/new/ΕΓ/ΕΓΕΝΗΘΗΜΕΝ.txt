Grammar: "Εγενηθημεν" is the aorist tense, passive voice, first person plural form of the verb "γίνομαι" (ginomai). In Greek, the aorist tense is used to express a completed action in the past, and the passive voice indicates that the subject of the verb is being acted upon.

Description: "Εγενηθημεν" translates to "we became" or "we were born" in English. It is used to describe a past event or state of being that has been completed.
