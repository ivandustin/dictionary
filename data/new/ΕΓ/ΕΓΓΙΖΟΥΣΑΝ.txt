Etymology: The word "εγγιζουσαν" comes from the Greek verb "εγγίζω" (engízo), which means "to approach" or "to come near."

Grammar: "Εγγιζουσαν" is the third person plural form of the verb "εγγίζω" in the past continuous tense. It is used to describe an action that was ongoing in the past.

Description: "Εγγιζουσαν" is a Greek word that can be translated as "they were approaching" or "they were coming near." It is used to indicate an ongoing action of multiple individuals getting closer to something or someone in the past.
