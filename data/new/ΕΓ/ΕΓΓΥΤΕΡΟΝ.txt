Etymology: The word "εγγυτερον" comes from the Greek word "ἐγγύς" (engys), meaning "near" or "close".

Grammar: "Εγγυτερον" is an adverb in the Greek language. It is the comparative form of the adverb "ἐγγύς" (engys), indicating that something is closer or nearer in comparison to something else.

Description: "Εγγυτερον" is used to describe the proximity or closeness of something in relation to another object or location. It is often used to indicate that something is closer or nearer in distance or time.
