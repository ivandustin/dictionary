Etymology: The word "εγεμισαν" comes from the Greek verb "γεμίζω" (gemízo), which means "to fill" or "to stuff". It is derived from the noun "γέμος" (gémos), meaning "fullness" or "abundance".

Grammar: "Εγεμίσαν" is the third person plural, past tense form of the verb "γεμίζω" (gemízo). In Greek, verbs change their endings to indicate tense, person, and number. The "-αν" ending in "εγεμίσαν" indicates that it is in the past tense and refers to a third person plural subject.

Description: "Εγεμίσαν" is a Greek verb that means "they filled" or "they stuffed". It describes an action that was completed in the past by a group of people.
