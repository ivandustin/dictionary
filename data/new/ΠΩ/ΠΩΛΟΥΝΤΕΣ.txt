Grammar: "Πωλούντες" is the plural form of the present participle of the verb "πωλέω". In Greek grammar, the present participle is used to describe ongoing actions or states. It can function as an adjective or as part of a verb phrase.

Description: "Πωλούντες" refers to a group of people who are currently selling or engaged in the act of selling. It can be used to describe individuals or a collective group involved in commercial transactions.
