The word "δακνετε" (daknete) is a verb in the present tense, second person plural form, in the Greek language. 

Etymology: The word comes from the Greek root "δάκνω" (dakno), which means "to bite" or "to gnaw". 

Grammar: The verb is in the present tense, indicating that the action is happening currently. The second person plural form indicates that the subject of the verb is a group of people, and the verb is addressing them.
