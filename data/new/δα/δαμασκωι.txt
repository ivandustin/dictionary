The word "δαμασκωι" (damaskoi) is a Greek adjective that means "of or from Damascus," which is the capital city of Syria. It is derived from the Greek word "Δαμασκός" (Damaskos), which is the ancient name of Damascus. In terms of grammar, "δαμασκωι" is in the masculine nominative singular form. It can be used to describe people, objects, or places that are associated with Damascus.
