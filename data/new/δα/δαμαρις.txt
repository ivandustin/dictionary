The word δαμαρις (damaris) is a Greek name that means "gentle" or "subdued". It is derived from the Greek verb δαμάζω (damazo), which means "to tame" or "to subdue".

Grammar:
In Greek, δαμαρις is a feminine noun in the nominative case. It can also be declined in other cases, such as the genitive δαμαρίδος (damaridos) or the accusative δαμαρίν (damarin).
