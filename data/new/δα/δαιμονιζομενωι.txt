The word δαιμονιζομενωι (daimonizomenoi) is a Greek verb that means "to be possessed by a demon" or "to be under the influence of a demon." It is derived from the noun δαίμων (daimon), which means "demon" or "spirit." In terms of grammar, δαιμονιζομενωι is a present participle, indicating ongoing or continuous action.
