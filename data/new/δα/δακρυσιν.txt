The word δακρυσιν (dakrysin) is a Greek noun that means "tear" or "teardrop". It is derived from the Greek word δάκρυ (dakry), which also means "tear". In terms of grammar, δακρυσιν is in the accusative case and singular number. It can be used to describe the physical tears that come from the eyes, as well as metaphorical tears that represent sadness or emotion.
