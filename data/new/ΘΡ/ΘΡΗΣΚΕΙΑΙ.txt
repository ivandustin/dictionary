Etymology: The word "θρησκεία" (threskeiai) comes from the Greek word "θρησκεύω" (threskeuo), which means "to worship" or "to perform religious rites."

Grammar: "Θρησκεία" is a feminine noun in the Greek language. It belongs to the first declension and has a singular form.

Description: "Θρησκεία" refers to the concept of religion or religious belief. It encompasses the practices, rituals, and beliefs associated with a particular faith or system of worship. It can also refer to the organized institution or community that follows a specific set of religious beliefs and practices.
