Etymology: The word "θρησκος" (threskos) comes from the Greek word "θρησκεία" (threskeia), which means "religion" or "religious worship."

Grammar: θρησκος (threskos) is a noun in the masculine gender. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their case, number, and gender.
