Word: μιας

Etymology: μιας is a Greek word that comes from the feminine form of the numeral "one" (μία). 

Grammar: μιας is the genitive singular form of the feminine noun μία (one). It is used to indicate possession or relationship to another noun or pronoun in a sentence. 

Description: μιας is a small but important word in the Greek language, as it is used frequently in everyday speech and writing. Its meaning depends on the context in which it is used, but it generally indicates a relationship between two things or people. For example, "το βιβλίο μιας φίλης" (the book of a friend) or "η ζωή μιας γυναίκας" (the life of a woman).
