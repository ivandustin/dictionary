μισθωτός (misthōtós) is a Greek adjective that means "hired" or "employed for wages". It comes from the noun μίσθος (místhos), which means "wages" or "pay". In terms of grammar, μισθωτός is a masculine singular adjective in the nominative case. It can be used to describe a person who is hired or employed for a specific job or task, or to describe something that is rented or leased.
