Word: μικραν

Etymology: The word μικραν comes from the Greek adjective μικρός (mikrós), which means "small" or "little".

Grammar: μικραν is the accusative singular form of the feminine noun μικρά (mikrá), which means "small" or "little".

Description: μικραν is used in Greek to describe something that is small or little in size. It can be used to describe objects, animals, or people. For example, μικραν καρδιά (mikran kardia) means "small heart" and μικραν πόλη (mikran poli) means "small city".
