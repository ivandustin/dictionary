μικρου (mikrou) is a Greek word that is the genitive form of the adjective μικρός (mikrós), which means "small" or "little". The word comes from the Proto-Indo-European root *smik- which means "small" or "little". In terms of grammar, μικρου is the genitive singular form of the masculine noun μικρός. It is used to indicate possession or relationship, and can be translated as "of small" or "of little".
