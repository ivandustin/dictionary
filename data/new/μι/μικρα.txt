μικρα (mikra) is a Greek word that means "small" or "little". It is the feminine form of the adjective μικρός (mikros). In terms of grammar, it is an adjective that can be used to describe a noun, such as μικρά παιδιά (mikra paidia) meaning "small children". The word has its roots in the ancient Greek language and has been used in various contexts throughout history.
