Word: μισθού (misthou)

Etymology: The word μισθού comes from the Greek word μισθός (misthos), which means "wages" or "pay". It is derived from the verb μισθόω (misthoō), which means "to pay wages".

Grammar: μισθού is a noun in the genitive case, which is used to show possession or relationship. In this case, it is often used to indicate the wages or pay of someone.

Description: μισθού is a Greek word that refers to the wages or pay that someone receives for their work. It is often used in the context of employment or labor, and can refer to both monetary and non-monetary compensation. In modern Greek, the word is often used to refer to salary or income.
