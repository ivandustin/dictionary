Word: μισησωσιν

Etymology: The word μισησωσιν is a verb form in the Greek language, specifically in the present subjunctive active third person plural. It is derived from the root word μισέω (miseo), which means "to hate" or "to detest."

Description: The word μισησωσιν can be translated to mean "they may hate" or "let them hate." It is often used in the context of expressing a wish or desire for someone to hate or detest something.
