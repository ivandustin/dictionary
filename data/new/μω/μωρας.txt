Word: μωρας

The word μωρας (moras) comes from the Greek word μωρός (moros), which means "foolish" or "stupid". It is derived from the Proto-Indo-European root *meh₁- ("to measure; to be great").

μωρας is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the singular form and is feminine in gender. The word is declined in the genitive case, which is used to show possession or relationship.

μωρας is a derogatory term used to describe a foolish or stupid woman. It is often used in a demeaning way to insult or belittle someone's intelligence. The word has a negative connotation and is not considered polite or respectful.
