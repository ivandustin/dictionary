The word "μωραι" (morai) is a plural noun in Greek that means "foolishness" or "folly". It comes from the Greek adjective "μωρός" (moros), which means "foolish" or "stupid". In terms of grammar, "μωραι" is a feminine noun in the nominative case. It can also be declined in other cases, such as the genitive "μωρών" (moron) or the accusative "μωράς" (moras). Overall, "μωραι" refers to a state of being foolish or acting in a foolish manner.
