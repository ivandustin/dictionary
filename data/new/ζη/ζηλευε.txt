The word ζηλεύε (zēleúe) is a verb in the Greek language. It is derived from the root word ζήλος (zēlos), which means "zeal" or "envy". 

In terms of grammar, ζηλεύε is a present tense verb in the active voice. It is conjugated in the third person singular form, which means "he/she/it is jealous" or "he/she/it envies". 

The word ζηλεύε is often used to describe a feeling of jealousy or envy towards someone or something. It can also be used to describe a strong desire or passion for something. 

Example sentence: Ο Πέτρος ζηλεύει την επιτυχία του φίλου του. (O Pétros zēleúei tēn epitychía tou filou tou.) - Peter is jealous of his friend's success.
