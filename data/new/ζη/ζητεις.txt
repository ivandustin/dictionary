Ζητείς (zeteis) is a Greek verb in the present tense, second person singular form. It comes from the root word ζητώ (zito), which means "to seek" or "to search for". 

In terms of grammar, ζητείς is a regular verb that follows the conjugation pattern of verbs ending in -ω. It is a transitive verb, meaning it takes a direct object. 

Example sentence: Ζητείς το βιβλίο; (Zeteis to vivlio?) - Are you looking for the book?
