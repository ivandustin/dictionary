The word ζησω (ziso) is a verb in the Greek language, which means "I will live" or "I will be alive". 

Etymology: The word ζησω comes from the Greek root word ζω (zo), which means "to live". 

Grammar: ζησω is a first-person singular future tense verb. 

Description: ζησω is a simple and straightforward verb that expresses the future action of living or being alive. It can be used in various contexts, such as expressing a desire to live a long and healthy life or a determination to survive a difficult situation.
