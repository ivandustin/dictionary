The word ζημιαν (zimian) is a Greek noun that means "damage" or "loss". It is derived from the verb ζημιόω (zimioo), which means "to damage" or "to harm". In terms of grammar, ζημιαν is a singular accusative noun in the feminine gender. It can be used in various contexts, such as in legal or financial matters, to refer to the harm or loss suffered by a person or entity.
