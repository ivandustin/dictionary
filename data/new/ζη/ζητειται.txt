Word: ζητείται

Etymology: ζητείται is a Greek word that comes from the verb ζητώ (zito), which means "to seek" or "to search for". The suffix -είται (-eitai) is added to the verb to indicate that the action is in the passive voice and that the subject is in the third person singular.

Grammar: ζητείται is a passive verb in the present tense, third person singular. It is often used in job advertisements or notices to indicate that someone or something is being sought or wanted.

Description: ζητείται is a common word in Greek that is used in various contexts. It can be used in job advertisements to indicate that a position is open and that the employer is looking for someone to fill it.
