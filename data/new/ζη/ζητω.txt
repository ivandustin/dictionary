ζητω (zeto) is a Greek verb that means "I seek" or "I search". It comes from the Greek word ζητέω (zetéo), which also means "to seek" or "to search". 

In terms of grammar, ζητω is a first person singular present active indicative form of the verb ζητέω. It can also be conjugated in other tenses and persons, such as ζητάω (zetao) in the present tense, or ζήτησα (zitisa) in the past tense.

Overall, ζητω is a common verb in Greek that is used to express the act of searching or seeking something.
