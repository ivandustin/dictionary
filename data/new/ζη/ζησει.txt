The word ζησει (zēsei) is a verb in the future tense, third person singular form, of the Greek verb ζῶ (zō), which means "I live". 

Etymology: The word ζῶ (zō) comes from the Proto-Indo-European root *gʷeyh₃- which means "to live". 

Grammar: ζησει (zēsei) is a regular verb in the future tense, formed by adding the future tense suffix -σ- (-s-) to the stem ζω- (zō-). 

Description: ζησει (zēsei) means "he/she/it will live" in Greek. It can be used in various contexts, such as expressing a prediction or a promise for the future.
