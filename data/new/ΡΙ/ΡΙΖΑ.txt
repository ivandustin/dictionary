Grammar: In Greek, "ρίζα" is a feminine noun. It belongs to the first declension and has a singular and plural form. The plural form is "ρίζες" (rizes).

Description: "Ρίζα" is the Greek word for "root." It refers to the part of a plant that typically lies below the surface of the soil and absorbs water and nutrients. In a figurative sense, "ρίζα" can also refer to the origin or source of something.
