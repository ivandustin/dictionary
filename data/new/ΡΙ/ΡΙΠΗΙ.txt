Etymology: The word "ριπή" (ripi) comes from the Greek verb "ρίπτω" (ripto), which means "to throw" or "to cast". 

Grammar: "Ριπή" is a feminine noun in Greek. It belongs to the second declension and its plural form is "ριπές" (ripes). 

Description: "Ριπή" refers to a sudden or swift movement, often used to describe the movement of an object or a person. It can also be used metaphorically to describe a sudden change or shift in a situation or a person's mood.
