ζωντος (zontos) is a Greek word that means "living" or "alive". It is derived from the verb ζῶ (zo), which means "to live". In terms of grammar, ζωντος is the genitive singular form of the adjective ζωντός (zontos), which means "of the living" or "belonging to the living". It can be used to describe living organisms or things that are associated with life.
