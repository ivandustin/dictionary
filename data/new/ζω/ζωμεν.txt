In terms of grammar, ζωμεν is a conjugated form of the verb ζω, which is a regular verb in Greek. It belongs to the first conjugation class, which means that it follows a specific pattern of inflection. 

As for its meaning, ζωμεν translates to "we live" or "we are alive". It can be used in various contexts, such as to describe the state of being alive, to express vitality or energy, or to refer to a specific period of time during which someone is alive.
