ζωιου (zoioú) is a Greek word that means "of an animal" or "animal's". It is derived from the Greek word ζώο (zóo), which means "animal". In terms of grammar, ζωιου is a genitive singular form of ζώο. The genitive case is used to show possession or relationship between two nouns. 

Example sentence: Η διατροφή του ζωιού είναι σημαντική για την υγεία του. (I diatrofí tou zoioú eínai simantikí gia tin ygeía tou.) - The diet of the animal is important for its health.
