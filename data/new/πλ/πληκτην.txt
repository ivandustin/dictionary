The word "πληκτην" (plēktēn) is a Greek noun that means "weaver" or "plaiter". It is derived from the verb "πλέκω" (pléko), which means "to weave" or "to braid". In terms of grammar, it is a masculine noun in the singular form. It can be used to describe someone who weaves or braids, or it can refer to a tool used for weaving or braiding.
