The word πλανασθε comes from the Greek verb πλανάω (planáō), which means "to wander" or "to roam". It is derived from the noun πλάνη (plánē), which means "wandering" or "error".

πλανασθε is a second person plural present middle/passive imperative verb in Greek. It is formed by adding the suffix -σθε (-sthe) to the stem πλανα- (plana-). The middle/passive voice indicates that the subject is either performing the action on themselves or is being acted upon by the action.
