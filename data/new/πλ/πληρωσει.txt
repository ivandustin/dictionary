Word: πληρωσει

Etymology: The word πληρωσει comes from the Greek verb πληρόω (plēróō), which means "to fill" or "to complete". The suffix -σει (-sei) is the third person singular future tense ending.

Grammar: πληρωσει is a verb in the Greek language, specifically in the future tense. It is the third person singular form of the verb πληρόω (plēróō), which means "to fill" or "to complete". In this form, it indicates that someone or something will fill or complete something in the future.

Description: πληρωσει is a Greek verb that expresses the action of filling or completing something in the future. It can be used in various contexts, such as filling a container with water, completing a task, or fulfilling a promise. The word is commonly used in everyday conversation and in written texts.
