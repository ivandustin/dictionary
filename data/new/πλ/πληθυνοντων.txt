The word "πληθυνόντων" is a present participle in Greek, which means "multiplying" or "increasing". It is derived from the verb "πληθύνω" (plēthýnō), which means "to multiply" or "to increase". 

In terms of grammar, "πληθυνόντων" is in the present tense and active voice, and it is declined in the genitive case, indicating possession or relationship. 

Overall, "πληθυνόντων" can be translated as "of those who are multiplying" or "of those who are increasing".
