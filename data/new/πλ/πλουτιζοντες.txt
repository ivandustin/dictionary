Word: πλουτίζοντες

Etymology: The word πλουτίζοντες comes from the Greek verb πλουτίζω (ploutízo), which means "to enrich" or "to make rich". The suffix -οντες (-ontes) is a present participle ending, indicating that the word refers to people who are currently enriching themselves.

Grammar: πλουτίζοντες is a present participle in the active voice, masculine gender, and plural number. It is derived from the verb πλουτίζω, which is a first conjugation verb in the present tense. The word functions as an adjective, describing people who are currently enriching themselves.

Description: πλουτίζοντες describes people who are actively enriching themselves, often through financial means.
