The word πληθος (plēthos) is derived from the Greek word πλήθω (plēthō), which means "to fill" or "to be full". It is related to the English word "plethora", which means an excessive amount or abundance.

πληθος is a noun in the Greek language, and it is in the singular form. It is a neuter noun, which means that it does not have a gender. In the nominative case, it is spelled πληθος, but it can also be spelled πλήθος in some contexts.
