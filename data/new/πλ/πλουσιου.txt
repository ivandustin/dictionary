Word: πλουσιου

Etymology: πλουσιου comes from the Greek word πλούσιος (plousios), which means "rich" or "wealthy".

Grammar: πλουσιου is the genitive singular form of πλούσιος. In Greek grammar, the genitive case is used to show possession or relationship between nouns.

Description: πλουσιου is an adjective that describes someone or something as rich or wealthy. It can be used to describe a person's financial status, as well as the abundance of something, such as resources or opportunities. In Greek culture, wealth was highly valued and often seen as a sign of success and status.
