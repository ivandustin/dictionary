Word: πλευράν

Etymology: The word πλευράν (pleuran) comes from the Ancient Greek πλευρά (pleura), which means "side" or "rib".

Grammar: πλευράν is a noun in the accusative case, which means it is the direct object of a verb in a sentence. It is a feminine noun, and its singular form is πλευρά.

Description: πλευράν refers to the side or rib of a person or animal. It can also be used metaphorically to refer to a person's support or assistance. For example, "Χρειάζομαι την πλευράν σου" (Chriazome tin pleuran sou) means "I need your support".
