Word: πλουσιους

Etymology: πλουσιους is a Greek word that comes from the root word πλοῦτος (ploutos), which means wealth or riches. The suffix -ους (-ous) is a masculine plural ending, indicating that the word refers to wealthy men.

Grammar: πλουσιους is the masculine plural form of the adjective πλούσιος (plousios), which means rich or wealthy. It is used to describe a group of wealthy men.

Description: πλουσιους is a word that describes a group of wealthy men. In ancient Greece, wealth was highly valued and those who had it were often respected and admired. The word can be used to describe individuals or groups who have a significant amount of money or resources.
