Word: πλειονας

Etymology: The word πλειονας (pleionas) is derived from the Greek word πλέον (pleon), which means "more" or "greater". The suffix -ας (-as) is added to form the plural.

Grammar: πλειονας is a masculine noun in the nominative plural form. It is often used to describe a group of people or things that are more numerous or greater in quantity than another group.

Description: πλειονας is a word that is commonly used in Greek to describe a larger or greater number of people or things. It can be used in a variety of contexts, such as in business, politics, or everyday conversation.
