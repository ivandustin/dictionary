The word ιερευς (iereus) comes from the Greek word ἱερεύς (hiereus), which means "priest" or "sacred person". The word is derived from the Greek word ἱερός (hieros), which means "sacred" or "holy".

Ιερευς is a Greek word that refers to a priest or a sacred person. In ancient Greece, priests played an important role in religious ceremonies and rituals.
