Word: ιερειχω

Etymology:
The word ιερειχω (iereichō) is a compound word in Greek, consisting of two parts: ιερεύς (iereus) meaning "priest" and εἴχω (eichō) meaning "to hold". Therefore, ιερειχω means "to hold the office of a priest".

Grammar:
ιερειχω is a verb in the present tense, active voice, and indicative mood. It is conjugated as follows:

- Singular: ιερεύω (iereuō) - I hold the office of a priest
- Plural: ιερεύομεν (iereuomen) - We hold the office of a priest
