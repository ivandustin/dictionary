Word: ακουεται

Etymology: The word ακουεται is derived from the Greek verb ακούω (akoúo), which means "to hear" or "to listen".

Grammar: ακουεται is a third person singular present passive indicative form of the verb ακούω (akoúo). It is used to describe something that is being heard or listened to by someone or something else.

Description: ακουεται is commonly used in everyday Greek language to describe the act of hearing or listening. It can be used in various contexts, such as in music, conversations, or even in nature. For example, one might say "Η μουσική ακουεται πολύ δυνατά" (I musikí akoúetai polý dynatá) which means "The music is being heard very loudly".
