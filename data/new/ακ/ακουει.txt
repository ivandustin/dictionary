The word "ακουει" (akouei) is a verb in the Greek language. It is the third person singular form of the present tense of the verb "ακούω" (akouo), which means "to hear" or "to listen". 

Etymology: The word "ακούω" comes from the ancient Greek word "ἀκούω" (akouo), which also means "to hear" or "to listen". 

Grammar: "ακουει" is a present tense verb in the third person singular form. In Greek, verbs change their endings depending on the tense, mood, and person. 

Description: The word "ακουει" is used to describe the action of hearing or listening that is currently taking place by a third person singular subject. For example, "Ο Πέτρος ακούει μουσική" (O Petros akouei mousiki) means "Peter is listening to music".
