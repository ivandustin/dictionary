The word "ακουσωσιν" is a verb in the Greek language. It is the third person plural aorist subjunctive active form of the verb "ακούω" (akouo), which means "to hear" or "to listen". The aorist tense indicates a completed action in the past, while the subjunctive mood suggests a hypothetical or potential action. Therefore, "ακουσωσιν" can be translated as "they might have heard" or "they would hear".
