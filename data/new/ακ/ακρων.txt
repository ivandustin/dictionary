The word "ακρων" (akron) is a Greek noun that means "extremity" or "tip". It is derived from the Greek adjective "άκρος" (akros), which means "highest" or "farthest". In terms of grammar, "ακρων" is a masculine noun in the nominative singular form. It can also be declined in other cases, such as the genitive "ακρωνος" (akronos) or the accusative "ακρωνα" (akrona).
