Word: ακουων

The word ακουων (akouon) is the present participle of the Greek verb ακούω (akouo), which means "to hear" or "to listen". The verb comes from the Proto-Indo-European root *h₂ḱous-.

Description:
ακουων is a word that describes someone who is currently hearing or listening. It can be used as an adjective to describe a person who is actively listening to something, or as a noun to refer to someone who is a good listener. In Greek literature, the word is often used to describe characters who are attentive and perceptive.
