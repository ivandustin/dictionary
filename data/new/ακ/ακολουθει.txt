Word: ακολουθεί (akolouthei)

Etymology: ακολουθεί comes from the Greek verb ακολουθώ (akoloutho), which means "to follow" or "to accompany."

Grammar: ακολουθεί is a present tense verb in the third person singular form. It is conjugated from the verb ακολουθώ (akoloutho), which is a regular verb in Greek.

Description: ακολουθεί is a verb that means "to follow" or "to pursue." It can be used in a literal sense, such as following someone on a path or in a procession, or in a figurative sense, such as following a plan or a set of instructions. It is a common verb in everyday Greek conversation and writing.
