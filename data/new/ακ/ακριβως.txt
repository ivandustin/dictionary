Word: ακριβως

Etymology: ακριβως comes from the Greek word ακριβής (akribis), meaning precise or accurate.

Grammar: ακριβως is an adverb in Greek, meaning exactly or precisely. It is often used to indicate a precise time or location.

Description: ακριβως is a useful word in Greek for expressing precision or accuracy. It can be used in a variety of contexts, such as giving directions or describing a specific time. For example, if someone asks for the time, you could respond with "Είναι ακριβώς τέσσερις" (It's exactly four o'clock).
