The word "ακριβειαν" (akribeian) is a Greek noun that means "accuracy" or "precision". It is derived from the adjective "ακριβής" (akribis), which means "precise" or "exact". In terms of grammar, "ακριβειαν" is in the accusative case, indicating that it is the direct object of a verb. Overall, "ακριβειαν" is a word that emphasizes the importance of being precise and accurate in one's actions and words.
