Word: ακουσθεισιν

Grammar: ακουσθεισιν is a noun in the dative case, which is used to indicate the indirect object of a verb. It can be translated as "to the hearing" or "to the listening".

Description: ακουσθεισιν refers to the act of hearing or listening. It can be used in a variety of contexts, such as in legal or religious settings where a person is required to listen to a statement or command.
