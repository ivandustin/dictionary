Word: ακουσετε

Etymology: ακουσετε is a Greek word that comes from the verb ακούω (akouo), which means "to hear" or "to listen". The suffix -τε (-te) is added to the verb to make it a command form, meaning "listen!" or "hear!".

Grammar: ακουσετε is the second person plural imperative form of the verb ακούω (akouo). It is used to give a command or instruction to a group of people, telling them to listen or hear something.

Description: ακουσετε is a powerful word that can be used to grab someone's attention and direct them to listen to what you have to say. It is often used in situations where important information is being shared, such as in a lecture, a public announcement, or a safety briefing. By using the imperative form, the speaker is able to convey a sense of urgency and importance, emphasizing the need for the listener to pay attention.
