The word "ακλινη" (aklini) is a Greek adjective that means "unbending" or "inflexible". 

Etymology: The word is derived from the Greek prefix "a-" (meaning "not" or "without") and the noun "κλίνη" (klini), which means "bed" or "couch". 

Grammar: "ακλινη" is a feminine adjective in the nominative singular form. 

Description: The word is often used to describe a person who is stubborn or unyielding in their opinions or actions. It can also be used to describe something that is rigid or unchanging.
