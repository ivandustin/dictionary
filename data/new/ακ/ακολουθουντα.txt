Word: ακολουθούντα (akolouthounta)

Etymology: The word ακολουθούντα comes from the Greek verb ακολουθώ (akoloutho), which means "to follow". The suffix -ούντα (-ounta) is the present participle form of the verb.

Grammar: ακολουθούντα is the present participle form of the verb ακολουθώ (akoloutho), which means "following". It is in the masculine gender, singular number, and nominative case.

Description: ακολουθούντα is used to describe something or someone that is currently following or accompanying someone or something else. It can also be used to describe a sequence of events that are following one another.
