Etymology: The word "δανιζετε" comes from the Greek verb "δανίζω" (danízo), which means "to lend" or "to borrow."

Grammar: "Δανιζετε" is the second person plural form of the verb "δανίζω" (danízo) in the present tense. It is used when addressing a group of people and means "you lend" or "you borrow."

Description: "Δανιζετε" is a verb in the Greek language that is used to express the action of lending or borrowing. It is commonly used in everyday conversations and written texts when talking about financial transactions or borrowing and lending items.
