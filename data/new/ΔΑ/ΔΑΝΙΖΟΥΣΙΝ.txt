Etymology: The word "δανιζουσιν" comes from the Greek verb "δανίζω" (danízo), which means "to lend" or "to borrow."

Grammar: "Δανιζουσιν" is the present tense, third person plural form of the verb "δανίζω" (danízo). It is used to describe the action of lending or borrowing that is currently happening by a group of people.

Description: "Δανιζουσιν" is a Greek word that refers to the act of lending or borrowing. It indicates that multiple individuals are currently engaged in this activity.
