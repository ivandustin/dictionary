Etymology: The word "δακρυσίν" comes from the Greek word "δάκρυ" (dakry), meaning "tear."

Grammar: "Δακρυσίν" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the singular form.

Description: "Δακρυσίν" refers to a tear, the drop of salty liquid that is secreted by the lacrimal glands in the eyes. Tears are often associated with emotions such as sadness, joy, or pain.
