Etymology: The word "δαπανην" comes from the Greek verb "δαπανάω" (dapanáo), which means "to spend" or "to consume."

Grammar: "Δαπανην" is a noun in the accusative case, singular form. It is the accusative form of the noun "δαπάνη" (dapani), which means "expense" or "expenditure."

Description: "Δαπανην" refers to a specific expense or expenditure. It is used to indicate the object or direct recipient of the action of spending or consuming.
