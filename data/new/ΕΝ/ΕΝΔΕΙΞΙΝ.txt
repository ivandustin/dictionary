Etymology: The word "ενδείξιν" comes from the Greek word "ενδείκνυμι" (endeíknymi), which means "to show" or "to indicate."

Grammar: "Ενδείξιν" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the accusative case and singular number.

Description: "Ενδείξιν" refers to an indication, a sign, or a demonstration of something. It is used to describe a visible or tangible evidence that points to a particular fact or conclusion.
