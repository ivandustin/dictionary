Etymology: The word "ενδεικνύσθαι" comes from the Greek verb "ενδείκνυμι" (endeiknymi), which means "to show" or "to demonstrate."

Grammar: "Ενδεικνύσθαι" is the present middle/passive infinitive form of the verb "ενδείκνυμι" (endeiknymi). In Greek grammar, the middle/passive voice indicates that the subject is both the doer and the receiver of the action.

Description: "Ενδεικνύσθαι" is a verb in the Greek language that means "to show" or "to demonstrate." It is used to express the act of presenting or indicating something to others.
