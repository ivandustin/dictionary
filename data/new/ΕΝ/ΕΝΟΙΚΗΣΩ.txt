Etymology: The word "ενοικησω" (enoikēsō) comes from the Greek verb "οικέω" (oikéō), which means "to inhabit" or "to dwell". The prefix "εν-" (en-) indicates the action of being "in" or "within". 

Grammar: "Ενοικήσω" is the first person singular future tense form of the verb "οικέω" (oikéō) in Greek. It translates to "I will inhabit" or "I will dwell". 

Description: "Ενοικήσω" is a verb in the Greek language that expresses the action of inhabiting or dwelling in a place. It indicates a future action of the speaker or subject.
