Etymology: The word "ενθυμησεις" comes from the Greek verb "ενθυμέομαι" (enthyméomai), which means "to remember" or "to recall". 

Grammar: "Ενθυμησεις" is a noun in the plural form. In Greek, nouns change their endings depending on their gender, number, and case. 

Description: "Ενθυμησεις" refers to memories or recollections. It can also be used to describe the act of remembering or recalling something from the past.
