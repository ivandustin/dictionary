Etymology: The word "εναλλιών" comes from the Greek root "αλλάζω" (allazo), which means "to change" or "to alternate". The prefix "εν-" (en-) is added to indicate "within" or "inside". 

Grammar: "Εναλλιών" is a noun in the masculine gender, singular number, and nominative case. It is the present participle of the verb "αλλάζω" (allazo), which means "changing" or "alternating". 

Description: "Εναλλιών" refers to something that is in a state of change or alternation. It can be used to describe a person, object, or situation that is constantly shifting or varying.
