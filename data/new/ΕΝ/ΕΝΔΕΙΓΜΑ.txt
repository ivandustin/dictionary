Etymology: The word "ενδείγμα" (endigma) comes from the Greek word "ενδείκνυμι" (endeiknymi), which means "to show" or "to demonstrate". 

Grammar: "Ενδείγμα" is a noun in the Greek language. It is a neuter noun and its plural form is "ενδείγματα" (endigmata).

Description: "Ενδείγμα" refers to an example or a model that serves as a demonstration or illustration of something. It is often used to describe a typical or representative instance of a concept or idea.
