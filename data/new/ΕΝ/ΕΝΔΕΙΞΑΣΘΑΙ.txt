Etymology: The word "ενδείξασθαι" comes from the Greek verb "ενδείκνυμι" (endeíknymi), which means "to show" or "to indicate." The suffix "-σθαι" is a reflexive ending, indicating that the action is performed on oneself.

Grammar: "ενδείξασθαι" is an infinitive verb form in the Greek language. It belongs to the middle voice, indicating that the subject is both the doer and the receiver of the action.

Description: "ενδείξασθαι" is used to express the action of showing or indicating something. It can be translated as "to demonstrate," "to exhibit," or "to manifest." This verb emphasizes the act of presenting evidence or making something known.
