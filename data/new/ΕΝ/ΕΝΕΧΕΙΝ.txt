Grammar: "Ενεχειρίζω" is a verb in the Greek language. It belongs to the first conjugation class and is conjugated in the present tense as follows: ενεχειρίζω (enecheirízo) - ενεχειρίζεις (enecheirízeis) - ενεχειρίζει (enecheirízei) - ενεχειρίζουμε (enecheirízoume) - ενεχειρίζετε (enecheirízete) - ενεχειρίζουν (enecheirízoun).

Description: "Ενεχειρίζω" is a Greek verb that means "to undertake" or "to engage in." It refers to the act of taking on a task or responsibility.
