Grammar: "ενεδυσασθε" is a verb in the aorist tense, which is used to describe a completed action in the past. The middle voice indicates that the subject of the verb is performing the action on themselves.

Description: The word "ενεδυσασθε" translates to "you (plural) clothed yourselves" or "you (plural) put on." It is used to express the action of multiple people putting on clothes or getting dressed.
