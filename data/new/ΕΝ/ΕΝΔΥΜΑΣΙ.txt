Etymology: The word "ενδυμασία" comes from the Greek verb "ενδύω" (endýo), which means "to dress" or "to put on". It is derived from the prefix "εν-" (en-), meaning "in" or "on", and the verb "δύω" (dýo), meaning "to put on" or "to wear".

Grammar: "Ενδυμασία" is a feminine noun in Greek. It belongs to the second declension and its genitive form is "ενδυμασίας" (endymasías). In Greek grammar, nouns are declined according to gender, number, and case.

Description: "Ενδυμασία" refers to the act of dressing or the clothes that one wears. It can also be used to describe the overall style or appearance of someone's clothing.
