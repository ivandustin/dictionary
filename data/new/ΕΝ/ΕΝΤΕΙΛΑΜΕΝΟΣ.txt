The word "εντειλαμένος" is a Greek adjective that comes from the verb "εντείλομαι" (enteílomai), which means "to command" or "to order." The adjective form "εντειλαμένος" is used to describe someone who has been given a specific command or order. In terms of grammar, "εντειλαμένος" is in the masculine singular form.
