Etymology: The word "ενδείξεις" (endixis) comes from the Greek verb "ενδείκνυμι" (endeiknymi), which means "to show" or "to indicate."

Grammar: "Ενδείξεις" is a noun in the plural form. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their gender, number, and case. The word "ενδείξεις" is in the nominative case and is used to refer to multiple indications or signs.

Description: "Ενδείξεις" refers to indications or signs that provide evidence or demonstrate something. It can be used in various contexts, such as scientific research, medical diagnosis, or legal proceedings, to describe the evidence or clues that support a particular conclusion or argument.
