Grammar: "Ενκαυχάσθαι" is an infinitive verb form in the middle voice. It is used to express the action of boasting or bragging. In Greek grammar, verbs can have different forms depending on the voice (active, middle, or passive) and the tense (present, past, future, etc.).

Description: The verb "ενκαυχάσθαι" is used to describe the act of boasting or bragging about oneself or one's achievements. It implies a sense of pride or self-promotion.
