Grammar: "Εντρεπων" is the masculine singular form of the present participle of the verb "εντρέπομαι" (entrepomai). In Greek grammar, the present participle is used to form adjectives that describe ongoing actions or states. It agrees in gender, number, and case with the noun it modifies.
