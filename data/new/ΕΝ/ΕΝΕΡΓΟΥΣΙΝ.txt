Grammar: "Ενεργουσιν" is the present tense, third person plural form of the verb "ενεργέω" (energeō). In Greek grammar, it belongs to the active voice and indicative mood. It is used to describe actions or states that are currently happening or are generally true.

Description: "Ενεργουσιν" is a Greek word that means "they work" or "they act". It refers to a group of people or things performing an action or engaging in activity. The word can be used in various contexts, such as describing the actions of individuals, organizations, or systems.
