Etymology: The word "ενδωμησις" comes from the Greek roots "εν" (en), meaning "in," and "δωμα" (doma), meaning "house." It literally translates to "in-house" or "dwelling."

Grammar: "Ενδωμησις" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the singular form and belongs to the feminine gender.

Description: "Ενδωμησις" refers to the act of dwelling or residing in a particular place. It can also be used metaphorically to describe a state of being settled or established in a certain situation or condition.
