Etymology: The word "νινευειται" comes from the Greek verb "νινεύω" (nineúo), which means "to nod" or "to beckon with a nod." 

Grammar: "Νινευειται" is a verb in the present tense, third person singular form. It belongs to the active voice and indicative mood. 

Description: The word "νινευειται" describes the action of nodding or beckoning with a nod. It implies a slight movement of the head up and down, often used as a gesture of agreement, acknowledgement, or encouragement.
