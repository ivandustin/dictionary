Etymology: The word "νιπτείς" comes from the Greek verb "νίπτω" (nipto), which means "to wash". 

Grammar: "Νιπτείς" is a noun in the masculine gender and the nominative case. It is the singular form of the word. 

Description: "Νιπτείς" refers to a person who washes or cleanses. It can be used to describe someone who washes their hands, face, or body.
