Etymology: The word "νινευείταις" comes from the Greek verb "νινεύω" (nineúo), which means "to nod" or "to beckon with a nod." The suffix "-ταις" (-tais) indicates the third person plural present tense form of the verb.

Grammar: "Νινευείταις" is a verb in the third person plural present tense in Greek. It is conjugated from the verb "νινεύω" (nineúo), which is a regular verb of the first conjugation.

Description: "Νινευείταις" means "they nod" or "they beckon with a nod" in Greek. It is used to describe the action of multiple people nodding or beckoning with a nod.
