Grammar: "κωλυοντα" is the present participle form of the verb "κωλύω" in the active voice. It is in the masculine, singular, and accusative case.

Description: "κωλυοντα" describes something or someone that is hindering or preventing. It can be used as an adjective or a noun, depending on the context.
