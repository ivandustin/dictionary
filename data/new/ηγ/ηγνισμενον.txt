Word: ηγνισμένον

Grammar: ηγνισμένον is a neuter singular participle in the perfect tense. It agrees with a neuter singular subject in gender, number, and case.

Description: ηγνισμένον describes something that has been purified or made holy. It can be used to describe a person, object, or place that has undergone a process of purification or sanctification.
