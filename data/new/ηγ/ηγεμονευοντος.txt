Word: ηγεμονεύοντος

Grammar: ηγεμονεύοντος is the present participle form of the verb ηγεμονεύω. It is in the active voice, masculine gender, and genitive case. It is used to describe the subject of the sentence as the one who is currently leading, ruling or governing.

Description: ηγεμονεύοντος is a Greek word that describes the present action of leading, ruling or governing. It is often used in historical or political contexts to describe a person who holds a position of power or authority.
