Word: ηγαπατε

Etymology: ηγαπατε is a conjugated form of the Greek verb αγαπάω (agapao), which means "to love". The verb comes from the ancient Greek word ἀγάπη (agape), which refers to a selfless, unconditional love.

Grammar: ηγαπατε is the second person plural form of the present tense of the verb αγαπάω (agapao) in the indicative mood. It is used to address a group of people and means "you love".

Description: ηγαπατε is a common verb form in the Greek language and is used in everyday conversation. It is often used to express affection or love towards someone or something.
