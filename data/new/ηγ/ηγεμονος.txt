The word ηγεμόνος (hegemonos) comes from the ancient Greek word ἡγεμών (hegemón), which means "leader" or "commander". It is derived from the verb ἡγέομαι (hegéomai), which means "to lead" or "to guide".

Ηγεμόνος is a Greek word that refers to a leader or ruler. In ancient Greece, it was used to describe the leader of a city-state or a military commander.
