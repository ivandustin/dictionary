Etymology: The word "σχοινιών" (skhoiniṓn) comes from the Greek word "σχοινί" (skhoíní), which means "rope" or "cord". 

Grammar: "Σχοινιών" is a noun in the genitive case, singular number, masculine gender. It is the genitive form of the noun "σχοινί" (skhoíní).

Description: "Σχοινιών" refers to a rope or cord. It can be used to tie or bind objects together, or for various other purposes.
