Grammar: "Σχισματα" is a noun in the plural form in Greek. It belongs to the neuter gender and is declined as a second declension noun. In the singular form, the word would be "σχίσμα" (schisma).

Description: "Σχισματα" refers to "schisms" in Greek. It is used to describe divisions or splits within a group, organization, or community, particularly in the context of religious or political disagreements.
