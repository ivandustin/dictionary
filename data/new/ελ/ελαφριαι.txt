The word "ελαφριαι" (elafriai) is a plural form of the Greek adjective "ελαφρός" (elafrós), which means "light" or "easy". It is derived from the ancient Greek word "ἐλαφρός" (elaphrós), which also meant "light" or "nimble". 

In terms of grammar, "ελαφριαι" is the feminine plural form of the adjective "ελαφρός". It is used to describe multiple feminine nouns that are light or easy in some way.

For example, "Οι ελαφριές καρέκλες είναι εύκολες να μετακινηθούν" (Oi elafriés karékles íne éfkoles na metakinithoun) means "The light chairs are easy to move".

Overall, "ελαφριαι" is a versatile adjective that can be used to describe a variety of things that are light or easy, such as clothing, objects, or tasks.
