The word "ελευκαναν" is a verb in the Greek language, which is the third person plural past tense of the verb "ελευκαίνω" (elefkéno), meaning "they whitened" or "they made white".

Grammar: "Ελευκαναν" is a regular verb in the past tense, formed by adding the suffix "-αν" (-an) to the stem "ελευκαν-" (elefkan-). It belongs to the third person plural, which means that it refers to a group of people or things that performed the action of whitening.
