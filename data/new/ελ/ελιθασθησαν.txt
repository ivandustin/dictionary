The word "ελιθασθησαν" (elithasthesan) is a Greek verb that means "they were stoned." It is derived from the root word "λιθάζω" (lithazo), which means "to stone." In terms of grammar, "ελιθασθησαν" is the third person plural aorist passive indicative form of the verb "λιθάζω." This means that it describes an action that was done to the subject (they) in the past.
