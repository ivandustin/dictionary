In terms of grammar, "ελευσεται" is a regular verb that follows the conjugation pattern of the verb "έρχομαι". It is formed by adding the future tense suffix "-σεται" to the stem "ελευ-", which is derived from the verb "έρχομαι". The word can be translated to English as "he/she/it will come".
