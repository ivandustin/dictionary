Etymology:
The word εληλυθεισαν is a Greek verb that comes from the root word "ερχομαι" (erchomai), which means "to come." The prefix "ελ-" (el-) indicates completion or perfect tense, and the suffix "-σαν" (-san) indicates third person plural. Therefore, εληλυθεισαν means "they have come" or "they came."
