Word: εληλυθοτα

Etymology: The word εληλυθοτα comes from the Greek verb "έρχομαι" (erchomai), which means "to come". The word is in the perfect participle form, which indicates that the action of coming has been completed in the past.

Grammar: εληλυθοτα is a neuter noun in the perfect participle form. It is declined as follows:

Singular:
- Nominative: εληλυθός
- Genitive: εληλυθότος
- Accusative: εληλυθόν

Plural:
- Nominative: εληλυθότα
- Genitive: εληλυθότων
- Accusative: εληλυθότα

Description: εληλυθοτα is a Greek word that means "having come". It is often used in the context of describing someone or something that has arrived or appeared. The word can also be used metaphorically to describe the completion of a process or the attainment of a goal.
