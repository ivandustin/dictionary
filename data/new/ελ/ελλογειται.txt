Word: ελλογείται

Etymology: The word ελλογείται comes from the Greek verb ελλογέω (ellogeó), which means "to count, to reckon, to calculate." The suffix -ται (-tai) indicates that the verb is in the passive voice.

Grammar: ελλογείται is a third-person singular present passive indicative form of the verb ελλογέω. It can be translated as "is counted, is reckoned, is calculated."

Description: ελλογείται is a verb that is often used in financial or accounting contexts to indicate that something is being counted or calculated. It can also be used more broadly to indicate that something is being taken into consideration or evaluated.
