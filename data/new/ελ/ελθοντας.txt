Word: ελθοντας

Etymology: 
The word ελθοντας is the present participle form of the Greek verb ερχομαι (erchomai), which means "to come". The root of the verb is ελ-, which is also found in other related words such as ελευθερία (eleutheria) meaning "freedom" and ελπίς (elpis) meaning "hope".

Grammar:
ελθοντας is a present participle form of the verb ερχομαι (erchomai), which means "coming" or "arriving". It is declined according to gender, number, and case. In this form, it is masculine, singular, and accusative.

Description:
ελθοντας is a word that describes someone or something that is currently in the process of coming or arriving. It can be used as an adjective to modify a noun, or as a noun itself to refer to the act of coming or arriving. For example, "Οι ελθόντες επισκέπτες" (Oi elthontes episkeptes) means "The arriving guests".
