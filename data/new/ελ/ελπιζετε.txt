Word: ελπιζετε

Etymology: The word ελπιζετε (elpizete) is derived from the Greek verb ελπίζω (elpizo), which means "to hope". It is a second person plural present indicative form of the verb.

Grammar: ελπιζετε is a verb in the present tense, second person plural form. It is used to address a group of people and means "you hope" in English. The verb is conjugated from the root verb ελπίζω (elpizo) by adding the appropriate suffixes for the present tense and second person plural.

Description: ελπιζετε is a common verb in the Greek language and is used in everyday conversation. It is often used to express hope or optimism about a situation or outcome. For example, "Ελπίζω να περάσουμε το μάθημα" (Elpizo na perasoume to mathima) means "I hope we pass the exam".
