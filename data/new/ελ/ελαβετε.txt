Word: ελαβετε

Grammar: ελαβετε is the second person plural form of the aorist tense in the active voice of the Greek verb "λαμβάνω" (lambano). It is used to indicate that a group of people received or took something in the past.

Description: ελαβετε is a verb that is commonly used in everyday Greek language. It is often used in formal and informal situations to indicate that a group of people received or took something in the past. For example, "Ελάβετε τα δώρα μου;" (Elavete ta dora mou?) means "Did you receive my gifts?".
