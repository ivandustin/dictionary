Word: ελληνιστι

Etymology: The word ελληνιστι (ellinisti) comes from the Greek word ελληνιστής (ellinistis), which means "a Greek speaker" or "a person who speaks Greek". It is derived from the word Έλληνας (Ellinas), which means "Greek".

Grammar: ελληνιστι is a noun in the singular form. It is a masculine noun and is declined like the noun Έλληνας (Ellinas). The plural form is ελληνιστές (ellinistes).

Description: ελληνιστι is a term used to describe the Greek language or someone who speaks Greek. It is commonly used in Greece and among the Greek diaspora. The word can also be used to describe someone who is knowledgeable about Greek culture and history.
