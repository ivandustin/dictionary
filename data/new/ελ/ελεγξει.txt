The word "ελεγξει" (eleksei) is a verb in the Greek language. It is the third person singular form of the verb "ελέγχω" (elecho), which means "to check" or "to verify". 

Etymology: The word "ελέγχω" comes from the ancient Greek word "ἐλέγχω" (elencho), which means "to cross-examine" or "to refute". 

Grammar: "Ελέγχει" is a present tense, indicative mood, active voice form of the verb "ελέγχω". It is used to describe an action that is currently happening in the third person singular. 

Description: The word "ελεγξει" is commonly used in everyday Greek language to express the act of checking or verifying something. It can be used in various contexts, such as checking documents, verifying information, or examining a product.
