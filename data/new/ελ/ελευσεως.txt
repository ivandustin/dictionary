The word "ελευσεως" (eleuseos) is a Greek noun that means "mercy" or "compassion". It is derived from the verb "ελεεω" (eleeo), which means "to have mercy" or "to show compassion". In terms of grammar, "ελευσεως" is a genitive singular noun, meaning it is used to indicate possession or relationship to another noun. Overall, "ελευσεως" is a word that emphasizes the importance of showing kindness and understanding towards others.
