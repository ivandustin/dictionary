The word "ελευθερα" (elefthera) is a feminine noun in Greek that means "freedom" or "liberty". Its etymology can be traced back to the ancient Greek word "eleutheria", which also means "freedom". In terms of grammar, "ελευθερα" is in the nominative case and singular form. It can also be declined in other cases and numbers depending on its use in a sentence. Descriptively, "ελευθερα" is a powerful and important word that represents the fundamental human right of freedom.
