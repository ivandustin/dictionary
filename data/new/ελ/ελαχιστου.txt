The word "ελαχίστου" (elachistou) is a Greek adjective that means "smallest" or "least." It is derived from the Greek word "ελάχιστος" (elachistos), which also means "smallest" or "least." 

In terms of grammar, "ελαχίστου" is in the genitive case, which is used to show possession or relationship between nouns. It is often used in phrases such as "του ελαχίστου" (tou elachistou), which means "of the smallest" or "of the least."

Overall, "ελαχίστου" is a useful word in Greek for describing something that is very small or insignificant.
