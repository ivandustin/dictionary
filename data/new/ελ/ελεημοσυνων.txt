The word "ελεημοσυνων" (eleemosynon) is a Greek noun that means "charitable" or "alms-giving". It is derived from the Greek words "ελεημοσύνη" (eleemosyne) which means "mercy" or "compassion" and "ων" (on) which is a suffix indicating possession or belonging. In grammar, it is a masculine noun in the nominative case.
