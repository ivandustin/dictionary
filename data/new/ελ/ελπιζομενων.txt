Word: ελπιζομενων

Etymology: 
The word ελπιζομενων is the present participle form of the Greek verb ελπίζω (elpizo), which means "to hope". The word is derived from the noun ελπίς (elpis), which means "hope" or "expectation".

Grammar:
ελπιζομενων is a present participle in the genitive case, which means it is used to describe a noun or pronoun and indicates possession or relationship. In this case, it is describing a group of people who are hoping or expecting something.

Description:
ελπιζομενων can be translated to "of those who hope" or "of those who are hoping". It is often used in Greek literature and poetry to describe a group of people who are united in their hope or expectation of something.
