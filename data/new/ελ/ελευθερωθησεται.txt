The word ελευθερωθησεται is a Greek verb in the future tense, passive voice, and indicative mood. It is derived from the root word ελευθερία (eleutheria), which means "freedom" or "liberty". The suffix -ωθησεται (-othetai) is a passive ending that indicates the action of the verb is being done to the subject.
