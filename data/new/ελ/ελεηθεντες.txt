The word "ελεηθεντες" (eleithentes) is a Greek adjective that means "merciful" or "compassionate". It is derived from the Greek word "ελεημοσύνη" (eleimosyne), which means "mercy" or "compassion". 

In terms of grammar, "ελεηθεντες" is the masculine plural form of the adjective, used to describe a group of people who are merciful or compassionate. 

Overall, "ελεηθεντες" is a word that emphasizes the importance of showing kindness and empathy towards others.
