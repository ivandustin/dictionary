Etymology: The word "εχρησαμην" (echrēsamēn) comes from the Greek verb "χράομαι" (chráomai), which means "to use" or "to make use of." It is conjugated in the first person singular, aorist tense, middle voice.

Grammar: "Εχρησαμην" is a verb in the Greek language. It belongs to the aorist tense, which is used to express past actions that are completed. The middle voice indicates that the subject is both the doer and the receiver of the action.

Description: "Εχρησαμην" is the first person singular form of the verb "χράομαι" in the aorist tense, middle voice. It can be translated as "I used" or "I made use of" in English.
