Grammar: "Εχορτασθησαν" is the third person plural form of the verb "εχορταζω" in the aorist tense. It is in the passive voice, indicating that the subject of the sentence is being acted upon.

Description: "Εχορτασθησαν" is a Greek word that translates to "they were satisfied" or "they were filled." It is often used to express the idea of being completely satisfied or satiated, particularly in relation to food or hunger.
