Grammar: In Greek, "εχιδνα" is a feminine noun. It belongs to the third declension and has the nominative singular form "εχιδνα". The word can also be declined in other cases and numbers.

Description: "Εχιδνα" refers to a mythical creature known as the "echidna" in English. In Greek mythology, echidnas were often depicted as half-woman, half-snake creatures.
