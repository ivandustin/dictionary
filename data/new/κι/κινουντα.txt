Etymology: The word "κινώ" (kino) comes from the ancient Greek word "κινέω" (kineo), which also means "to move". 

Grammar: "κινουντα" (kinounta) is the present participle form of the verb "κινώ" (kino), which is used to describe an ongoing action. It agrees in gender, number, and case with the subject of the sentence.
