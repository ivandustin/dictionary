κιθάρας (kitharas) is a Greek noun that refers to a musical instrument similar to a lyre or a harp. It has been used in Greek music since ancient times and is still popular in modern Greek music. The word comes from the ancient Greek word κιθάρα (kithara), which was also a musical instrument. In terms of grammar, κιθάρας is the genitive singular form of κιθάρα.
