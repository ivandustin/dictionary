Etymology: The word "εσθιων" (esthion) comes from the Greek verb "εσθιω" (esthio), which means "to eat" or "to consume."

Grammar: "Εσθιων" is the present participle form of the verb "εσθιω" (esthio) in the masculine gender, singular number, and nominative case.

Description: "Εσθιων" is used to describe a male subject who is currently eating or consuming something. It indicates ongoing action in the present tense.
