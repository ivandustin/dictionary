Etymology: The word "εσωτερον" (esôteron) comes from the Greek adjective "εσωτερος" (esôteros), meaning "inner" or "interior."

Grammar: "Εσωτερον" is a neuter noun in the singular form.

Description: "Εσωτερον" refers to something that is inside or within. It can be used to describe the inner part of a place, object, or concept.
