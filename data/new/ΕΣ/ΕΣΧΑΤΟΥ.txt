Etymology: The word "εσχατου" (eschatou) comes from the Greek word "ἔσχατος" (eschatos), which means "last" or "final".

Grammar: "Εσχατου" is a noun in the genitive case, singular form. In Greek grammar, the genitive case is used to indicate possession, origin, or relationship.

Description: "Εσχατου" is a Greek word that can be translated as "of the last" or "of the final". It is often used in religious or philosophical contexts to refer to the end or ultimate state of something.
