Etymology: The word "εστηρικται" comes from the Greek verb "εστηρικται" (estēriktai), which means "it is established" or "it is supported". 

Grammar: "εστηρικται" is a verb in the present tense, passive voice, and third person singular form. It is derived from the verb "στηρίζω" (stērízō), which means "to establish" or "to support". 

Description: "εστηρικται" is used to describe the action of something being established or supported. It indicates that the subject is in a state of being supported or upheld by something or someone.
