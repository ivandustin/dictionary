The word "εστραφησαν" is a Greek verb in the past tense, third person plural form. It comes from the root word "στρέφω" (strefó), which means "to turn" or "to change direction." The prefix "ε-" indicates the past tense, and the suffix "-αν" indicates the third person plural form. Therefore, "εστραφησαν" translates to "they turned" or "they changed direction."
