Etymology: The word "εσωτεραν" (esoteran) comes from the Greek word "εσωτερος" (esoteros), meaning "inner" or "internal".

Grammar: "Εσωτεραν" is a noun in the accusative case, singular form.

Description: "Εσωτεραν" refers to something that is internal or inner. It can be used to describe a physical or metaphorical aspect that is located or happening inside or within something else.
