Etymology: The word "εσθιουσιν" comes from the Greek verb "εσθίω" (esthio), which means "to eat" or "to consume".

Grammar: "Εσθιουσιν" is the third person plural form of the present tense of the verb "εσθίω" (esthio). It is used to describe an action of eating or consuming performed by a group of people or things.

Description: "Εσθιουσιν" is a word in the Greek language that specifically refers to the act of eating or consuming by a group of individuals. It is commonly used in various contexts to describe the action of eating food or consuming something.
