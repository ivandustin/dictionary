Grammar: "Εστωσαν" is a verb in the Greek language. It is in the third person plural form, indicating that the subject of the verb is a group of people or things. The aorist tense is used to express completed actions in the past.

Description: "Εστωσαν" is used to describe an action or state of being that occurred in the past. It is often translated as "they were" or "they existed." The verb can be used in various contexts and is conjugated according to the tense, mood, and voice required by the sentence.
