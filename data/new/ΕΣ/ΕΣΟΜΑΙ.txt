Etymology: The word "εσομαι" (esomai) comes from the Greek verb "εἰμί" (eimi), which means "to be." It is a future tense form of the verb.

Grammar: "Εσομαι" is a first-person singular future tense form of the verb "εἰμί" (eimi). In Greek, verbs have different forms depending on the tense, mood, voice, and person. The future tense indicates an action that will happen in the future.

Description: "Εσομαι" is used to express an action or state of being that will occur in the future. It is commonly translated as "I will be" in English.
