Etymology: The word "εστωτα" (estota) comes from the Greek verb "είμαι" (eimai), which means "to be" in the present tense. It is derived from the Proto-Indo-European root *h₁es-.

Grammar: "Εστωτα" is the plural form of the verb "είμαι" in the present tense. It is used to describe actions or states that are happening at the current moment.
