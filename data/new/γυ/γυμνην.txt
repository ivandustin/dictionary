Word: γυμνην

Etymology: γυμνην comes from the Greek word γυμνός (gymnós), which means "naked" or "bare". 

Grammar: γυμνην is the accusative singular form of the feminine adjective γυμνή (gymnḗ), which means "naked" or "bare". 

Description: γυμνην is used to describe a female who is naked or bare. It can also be used metaphorically to describe something that is exposed or lacking in something.
