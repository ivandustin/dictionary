Etymology: The word "οφειληματα" comes from the Greek word "οφείλω" (opheílo), which means "to owe" or "to be indebted". 

Grammar: "Οφειληματα" is a noun in the plural form. It belongs to the neuter gender and is declined according to the second declension in Greek grammar. 

Description: "Οφειληματα" refers to debts or obligations. It is used to describe financial or moral obligations that one person has towards another.
