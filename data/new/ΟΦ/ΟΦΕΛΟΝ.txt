- Nominative: οφελον (ophelon)
- Genitive: οφελου (ophelou)
- Accusative: οφελον (ophelon)
- Vocative: οφελον (ophelon)
- Dative: οφελω (ophelo)
- Instrumental: οφελει (ophelei)
