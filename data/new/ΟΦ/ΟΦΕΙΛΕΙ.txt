Etymology: The word "οφειλει" comes from the Greek verb "οφείλω" (opheílo), which means "to owe" or "to be indebted". 

Grammar: "Οφειλει" is the third person singular form of the present tense of the verb "οφείλω" (opheílo). It is used to indicate that someone owes or is indebted to someone or something.
