Etymology: The word "πνεηι" (pnēi) comes from the Greek verb "πνέω" (pnéō), which means "to breathe" or "to blow." 

Grammar: "Πνεηι" (pnēi) is a noun in the singular form. It belongs to the third declension in Greek grammar. 

Description: "Πνεηι" (pnēi) refers to the act of breathing or the breath itself. It can also be used metaphorically to represent life or vitality.
