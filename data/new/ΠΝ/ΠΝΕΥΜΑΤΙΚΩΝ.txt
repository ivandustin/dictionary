Grammar: "πνευματικών" is the plural form of the adjective "πνευματικός" (pnematikós), which means "spiritual" or "of the spirit" in Greek. It agrees with plural nouns in gender, number, and case.

Description: "πνευματικών" is used to describe things that pertain to the spiritual or non-material aspects of life. It can refer to concepts, ideas, or activities that are related to the mind, soul, or higher realms of existence.
