Etymology: The word "πνευματα" (pnemata) comes from the Greek word "πνεῦμα" (pneuma), meaning "breath" or "spirit."

Grammar: "Πνευματα" is a noun in the plural form. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their gender, number, and case. The plural form indicates that there are multiple "breaths" or "spirits."

Description: "Πνευματα" refers to "breaths" or "spirits" in Greek. It can have various meanings depending on the context.
