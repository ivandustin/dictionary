Etymology: The word "πνευματικά" (pnematiká) comes from the Greek word "πνεῦμα" (pneuma), meaning "spirit" or "breath."

Grammar: "Πνευματικά" is the plural form of the adjective "πνευματικός" (pnematikós), which means "spiritual" or "intellectual." It is used to describe things that pertain to the mind, intellect, or spirit.

Description: "Πνευματικά" is a Greek word that refers to intellectual or spiritual matters. It can be used to describe various aspects of human life, such as art, literature, philosophy, and religion. It encompasses ideas, thoughts, emotions, and beliefs that are not physical or material in nature.
