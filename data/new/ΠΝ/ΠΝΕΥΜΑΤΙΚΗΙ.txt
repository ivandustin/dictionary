Etymology: The word "πνευματική" (pnematikí) comes from the Greek word "πνεῦμα" (pneuma), meaning "spirit" or "breath."

Grammar: "Πνευματική" is a feminine adjective in the Greek language. It agrees in gender, number, and case with the noun it modifies.

Description: "Πνευματική" is an adjective that means "spiritual" or "intellectual" in Greek. It is used to describe things that pertain to the mind, soul, or intellect, rather than physical or material aspects.
