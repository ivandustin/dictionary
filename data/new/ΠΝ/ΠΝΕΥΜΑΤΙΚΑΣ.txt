Etymology: The word "πνευματικάς" (pnematikas) comes from the Greek word "πνεῦμα" (pneuma), meaning "spirit" or "breath," and the suffix "-τικός" (-tikos), which denotes "related to" or "pertaining to." 

Grammar: "Πνευματικάς" is the masculine form of the adjective in Greek. It agrees with a masculine noun in gender, number, and case. 

Description: "Πνευματικάς" is an adjective in Greek that means "spiritual" or "intellectual." It is used to describe things that are related to the mind, intellect, or spirit.
