Word: ησθενησα

Grammar: ησθενησα is a first-person singular past tense form of the verb ασθενώ. It belongs to the aorist tense, which is used to describe a completed action in the past. The verb is conjugated in the indicative mood, which is used to make a statement or ask a question.

Description: ησθενησα is a Greek word that describes a state of weakness or sickness in the past. It can be used to talk about a physical or emotional condition that someone experienced.
