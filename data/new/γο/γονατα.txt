Grammar: γονατα is a plural noun in Greek, meaning "knees". It is declined in the second declension and has the same form in both the nominative and accusative cases.

Description: γονατα are the joints that connect the thigh bone (femur) to the shin bone (tibia) in humans and other animals. They are essential for movement and support of the body.
