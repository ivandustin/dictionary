Word: σιωπησουσιν

The word σιωπησουσιν is a Greek verb that comes from the root word σιωπάω (siopao), which means "to be silent" or "to keep quiet." The verb is in the future tense and third person plural form, which means "they will be silent" or "they will keep quiet."

The word σιωπησουσιν describes the action of a group of people being silent or keeping quiet.
