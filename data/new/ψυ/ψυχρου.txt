The word "ψυχρου" (psykhróu) is a Greek adjective that means "cold" or "chilly". 

Etymology: The word comes from the Greek root "ψυχρός" (psykhrós), which means "cold" or "cool". 

Grammar: "ψυχρου" is the genitive form of the adjective, which is used to indicate possession or relationship. It is often used in phrases such as "το ψυχρό νερό" (to psykhró nero), which means "cold water". 

Description: The word "ψυχρου" is commonly used in Greek to describe the temperature of objects or environments. It can also be used metaphorically to describe a person's demeanor or attitude as cold or distant.
