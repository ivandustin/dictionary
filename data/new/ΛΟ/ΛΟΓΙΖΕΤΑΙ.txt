Etymology: The word "λογιζεται" comes from the Greek verb "λογίζομαι" (logízomai), which means "to calculate" or "to reckon." 

Grammar: "Λογιζεται" is the third person singular form of the present tense of the verb "λογίζομαι" in Greek. It is used to describe an action or state that is happening in the present and is performed by a third person.

Description: "Λογιζεται" is a Greek word that can be translated as "he/she/it calculates" or "he/she/it reckons" in English. It is used to express the act of performing calculations or making estimations.
