Grammar: "λογισασθαι" is the aorist middle infinitive form of the verb "λογίζομαι" (logízomai). In Greek grammar, the aorist tense refers to a past action that is viewed as a whole, without specifying its duration or completion. The middle voice indicates that the subject is performing the action on or for itself.
