Etymology: The word "μογιλαλον" comes from the Greek roots "μογιλ" meaning "toil" or "hardship" and "αλον" meaning "speech" or "talk". 

Grammar: "μογιλαλον" is a noun in the neuter gender. It is in the singular form. 

Description: "μογιλαλον" refers to someone who speaks with difficulty or stutters. It is used to describe a person who has a speech impediment or struggles to articulate words fluently.
