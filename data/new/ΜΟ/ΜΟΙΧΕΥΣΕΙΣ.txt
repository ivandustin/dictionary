Etymology: The word "μοιχεύσεις" comes from the Greek verb "μοιχεύω" (moicheuó), which means "to commit adultery."

Grammar: "Μοιχεύσεις" is a noun in the plural form. It belongs to the third declension in Greek grammar.

Description: "Μοιχεύσεις" refers to acts of adultery or the act of committing adultery. It is used to describe the act of a married person engaging in a sexual relationship with someone other than their spouse.
