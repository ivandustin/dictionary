Etymology: The word "μολυνεται" comes from the Greek verb "μολύνω" (molyno), which means "to pollute" or "to defile".

Grammar: "μολυνεται" is the third person singular form of the present tense of the verb "μολύνω" (molyno) in Greek. It is in the active voice and indicative mood.

Description: "μολυνεται" is a Greek word that means "he/she/it is being polluted" or "he/she/it is being defiled". It is used to describe the action of polluting or defiling something or someone.
