Etymology: The word "πορρω" (porro) comes from the Greek language and means "far" or "distant". It is often used as a prefix in compound words to indicate distance or remoteness.

Grammar: In Greek, "πορρω" is an adverb that is used to describe something that is far away or distant. It can be used to indicate physical distance or metaphorical distance.

Description: "Πορρω" is a versatile word that can be used in various contexts. It can be used to describe a physical distance, such as "πορρω από εδώ" (far away from here). It can also be used to describe a metaphorical distance, such as "πορρω από την αλήθεια" (far from the truth). Overall, "πορρω" is a word that conveys the idea of distance or remoteness.
