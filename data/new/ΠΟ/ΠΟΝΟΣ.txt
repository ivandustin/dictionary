Etymology: The word "πονος" (ponos) comes from the Ancient Greek language. It is derived from the Proto-Indo-European root *pen- meaning "to work, to toil." 

Grammar: In Greek, "πονος" (ponos) is a masculine noun of the second declension. It has a singular and plural form. 

Description: "Πονος" (ponos) means "pain" or "hardship" in Greek. It refers to physical or emotional suffering, difficulty, or laborious work.
