Etymology: The word "ποταμοφορητον" comes from the Greek words "ποταμός" (potamos) meaning "river" and "φορητός" (phoritos) meaning "carried". 

Grammar: "ποταμοφορητον" is a noun in the neuter gender. It is in the singular form.

Description: "ποταμοφορητον" is a Greek word that refers to something that is carried by a river. It can be used to describe objects or materials that are transported or moved by a river's current.
