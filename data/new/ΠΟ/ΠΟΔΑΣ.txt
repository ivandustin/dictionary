Etymology: The word "ποδας" (podas) comes from the ancient Greek word "πούς" (pous), meaning "foot". 

Grammar: "Ποδας" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the masculine gender and in the nominative case. 

Description: "Ποδας" refers to the foot, the lower extremity of the leg in humans and animals. It is an essential body part used for standing, walking, and running.
