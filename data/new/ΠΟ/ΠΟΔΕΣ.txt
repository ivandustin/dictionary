Etymology: The word "ποδες" (podes) comes from the Greek language and means "feet".

Grammar: "Ποδες" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the plural form and refers to multiple feet.

Description: "Ποδες" is used to describe the anatomical structure found in many animals, including humans, that is used for walking and supporting the body. It is an essential part of locomotion and plays a crucial role in maintaining balance and stability.
