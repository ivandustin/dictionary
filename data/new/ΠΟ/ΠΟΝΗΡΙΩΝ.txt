Etymology: The word "πονηριών" comes from the Greek word "πονηρός" (poniros), which means "wicked" or "evil."

Grammar: "Πονηριών" is a noun in the singular form. It belongs to the third declension in Greek grammar.

Description: "Πονηριών" refers to a person who is wicked, evil, or malicious. It is used to describe someone who has bad intentions or engages in harmful actions.
