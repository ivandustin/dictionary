Grammar: "Ποιησασαν" (poiēsasan) is the third person plural form of the aorist active indicative tense of the verb "ποιέω" (poiéō). In Greek grammar, the aorist tense is used to express a completed action in the past. The active voice indicates that the subject of the verb is performing the action.
