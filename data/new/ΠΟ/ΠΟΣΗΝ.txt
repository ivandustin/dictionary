Etymology: The word "ποσην" (posin) comes from the Greek word "πόσος" (posos), meaning "how much" or "how many".

Grammar: "Ποσην" is a feminine noun in the accusative case. It is used to ask about the quantity or number of something.

Description: "Ποσην" is commonly used in Greek to ask questions about the amount or quantity of something. It is often used in combination with other words to form questions like "ποσην ώρα;" (how much time?), "ποσην απόσταση;" (how much distance?), or "ποσην τιμή;" (how much price?).
