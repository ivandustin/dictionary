It is derived from the combination of the interrogative pronoun "ποτέ" (poté), meaning "when" or "ever," and the adjective "ταπεινός" (tapeinós), meaning "humble" or "lowly."

Description: The adjective "ποταπή" is used to describe something or someone of unknown or uncertain nature, quality, or origin.
