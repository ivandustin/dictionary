Etymology: The word "ποταμοί" (potamoi) comes from the Greek word "ποταμός" (potamos), which means "river".

Grammar: "Ποταμοί" is a plural noun in Greek. It belongs to the second declension and is masculine in gender.

Description: "Ποταμοί" refers to rivers in Greek. It can be used to describe any flowing body of water that is larger than a stream. Rivers play an important role in the geography and culture of many regions around the world.
