Etymology: The word "ποικιλης" (poikilēs) comes from the Greek word "ποικίλος" (poikílos), meaning "variegated" or "multicolored". 

Grammar: In Greek, "ποικιλης" (poikilēs) is a masculine noun in the nominative case. It can also be declined in other cases, such as genitive (ποικιλου), dative (ποικιλῃ), and accusative (ποικιλην).

Description: "Ποικιλης" (poikilēs) refers to something that is diverse, varied, or multicolored. It can be used to describe a person, object, or situation that exhibits a wide range of characteristics or colors.
