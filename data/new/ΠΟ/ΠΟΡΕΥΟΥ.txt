Etymology: The word "πορευού" (poreuou) comes from the Greek verb "πορεύομαι" (poreuomai), which means "to go" or "to travel."

Grammar: "Πορευού" is the second person singular imperative form of the verb "πορεύομαι" (poreuomai). It is used to give a command or instruction to someone, telling them to go or travel.

Description: "Πορευού" is a word used in Greek to tell someone to go or travel. It is commonly used in commands or instructions, similar to the English phrase "Go!" or "Go ahead!"
