Etymology: The word "πορευόμενος" (poreuómenos) comes from the Greek verb "πορεύομαι" (poreúomai), which means "to go" or "to travel."

Grammar: "Πορευόμενος" is the present participle form of the verb "πορεύομαι" (poreúomai). It is used to describe someone or something that is currently in the act of going or traveling.

Description: "Πορευόμενος" is an adjective that describes someone or something that is in motion or on the move.
