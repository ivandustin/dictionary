Grammar: "Ποιησαίνεν" is the third person singular aorist active indicative form of the verb "ποιέω" (poiéō). In Greek grammar, the aorist tense is used to express a completed action in the past. The active voice indicates that the subject of the verb is performing the action.

Description: "Ποιησαίνεν" is a verb form that means "he/she/it made" or "he/she/it created" in Greek. It refers to a single action that was completed in the past.
