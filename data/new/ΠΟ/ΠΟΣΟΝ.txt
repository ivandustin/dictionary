Etymology: The word "ποσον" (poson) comes from the Greek word "πόσος" (posos), meaning "how much" or "how many".

Grammar: "Ποσον" (poson) is a neuter noun in the singular form.

Description: "Ποσον" (poson) is used in Greek to refer to an indefinite quantity or amount. It is often translated as "some" or "a certain amount" in English.
