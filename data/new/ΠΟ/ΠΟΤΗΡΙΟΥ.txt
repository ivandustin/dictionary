Etymology: The word "ποτήριου" (potiriou) comes from the Greek word "ποτήρι" (potiri), which means "cup" or "goblet". 

Grammar: "Ποτήριου" is the genitive form of the noun "ποτήρι" (potiri), which is a second-declension noun in Greek. It is used to indicate possession or relationship.

Description: "Ποτήριου" refers to something that belongs to or is related to a cup or goblet. It can be used to describe the material, shape, size, or purpose of a cup or goblet.
