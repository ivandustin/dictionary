Etymology: The word "ποτηριών" comes from the Greek word "ποτήρι" (potíri), which means "cup" or "goblet". 

Grammar: "Ποτηριών" is the genitive plural form of the noun "ποτήρι". In Greek grammar, the genitive case is used to indicate possession or origin. The plural form indicates that there are multiple cups or goblets.

Description: "Ποτηριών" refers to a group of cups or goblets. It can be used to describe a collection of drinking vessels or a set of cups used for a specific purpose, such as in a formal dinner setting or a religious ceremony.
