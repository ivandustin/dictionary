Etymology: The word "ποσοί" (posoi) comes from the Greek word "πόσος" (posos), meaning "how much" or "how many". 

Grammar: "Ποσοί" is the plural form of the adjective "ποσός" (posos), which means "how much" or "how many". It is used to ask about the quantity or number of something.

Description: "Ποσοί" is an interrogative adjective in Greek that is used to ask questions about quantity or number. It agrees in gender, number, and case with the noun it modifies. For example, "ποσοί φίλοι έχεις;" (posoi filoi ehis?) means "How many friends do you have?"
