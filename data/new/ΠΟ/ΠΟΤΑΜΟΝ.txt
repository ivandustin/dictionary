Grammar: In Greek, "ποταμόν" is a masculine noun in the accusative case. It is the singular form of the word, and it means "river" or "stream". 

Description: "Ποταμόν" is a term used to refer to a natural flowing body of water, typically larger than a brook or a creek. It is often used to describe a river or a stream.
