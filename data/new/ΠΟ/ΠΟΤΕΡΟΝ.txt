Etymology: The word "ποτερον" (poteron) comes from the Greek language. It is derived from the combination of the words "ποτέ" (pote), meaning "ever" or "at some time," and "ἔρων" (eron), meaning "love." 

Grammar: In Greek, "ποτερον" is an adverb that is used to express doubt or uncertainty. It is often translated as "whether" or "perhaps" in English. It is commonly used in questions or conditional statements.
