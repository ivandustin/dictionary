Etymology: The word "ποσαί" (posai) comes from the Greek verb "ποσαίνω" (posaino), which means "to drink". It is derived from the noun "πόσις" (posis), meaning "drink" or "beverage".

Grammar: "Ποσαί" is a feminine noun in the plural form. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their gender, number, and case. The plural form indicates that there are multiple drinks or beverages.

Description: "Ποσαί" refers to drinks or beverages in general. It can be used to describe any type of liquid that is consumed, such as water, juice, soda, or alcohol.
