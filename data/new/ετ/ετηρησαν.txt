Word: ετηρησαν

Etymology: The word ετηρησαν comes from the Greek verb τηρέω (tiréo), which means "to keep, to guard, to observe". The prefix ε- (e-) indicates the past tense, and the suffix -αν (-an) indicates the third person plural.

Grammar: ετηρησαν is a verb in the past tense, third person plural, active voice. It is the aorist tense of the verb τηρέω (tiréo), which means that it refers to a completed action in the past.
