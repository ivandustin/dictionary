The word "ετερα" (etera) is a Greek adjective that means "other" or "different". It is derived from the Greek word "ετερος" (eteros), which also means "other" or "different". 

In terms of grammar, "ετερα" is a feminine singular form of the adjective. It can be used to describe a feminine noun, such as "η ετερα πλευρά" (i etera pleura), which means "the other side".
