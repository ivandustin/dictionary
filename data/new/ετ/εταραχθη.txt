Word: εταραχθη

Grammar: εταραχθη is a verb in the aorist tense, passive voice, and indicative mood. It is conjugated from the verb εταραχθηκα (etarachthika).

Description: εταραχθη means "was disturbed" or "was agitated" in English. It is often used to describe a state of emotional or mental turmoil. The passive voice indicates that the subject of the sentence is the one being acted upon, rather than the one doing the action.
