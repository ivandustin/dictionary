The word "ετερας" (eteras) is a Greek adjective that means "other" or "different". It is derived from the Greek word "ετερος" (eteros), which also means "other" or "different". 

In terms of grammar, "ετερας" is a feminine singular form of the adjective. It can be used to describe a feminine noun, such as "η ετερας πλευρά" (i eteras pleura), which means "the other side".
