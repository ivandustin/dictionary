The word "ετολμων" (etolmōn) is a Greek adjective that means "bold" or "courageous". 

Etymology: The word comes from the Greek verb "τολμάω" (tolmáō), which means "to dare" or "to be bold". The prefix "ε-" is added to the beginning of the verb to create the adjective form.

Grammar: "Ετολμων" is the masculine singular form of the adjective. The feminine singular form is "ετολμη" (etolmē), and the neuter singular form is "ετολμον" (etolmon).

Description: The word "ετολμων" is often used to describe someone who is brave, daring, or fearless. It can also be used to describe actions or decisions that require courage or boldness.
