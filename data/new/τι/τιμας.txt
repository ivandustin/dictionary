τιμας (timas) is a Greek verb in the present tense, second person singular form. It comes from the root word τιμή (timi), which means "honor" or "respect". The verb τιμάω (timao) means "to honor" or "to value". In the present tense, τιμας means "you honor" or "you value". It is commonly used in Greek culture to express respect and admiration for someone or something.
