τιμαιου (timaiou) is a Greek word that is derived from the root word τιμή (timi), which means "honor" or "respect". It is a genitive form of the word, which means it is used to indicate possession or relationship to something else. In terms of grammar, it is a noun that is declined according to the second declension in Greek.
