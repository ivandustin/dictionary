Word: ομοιωθηναι

Grammar: ομοιωθηναι is a passive aorist infinitive verb in Greek. It is formed by adding the suffix "-θηναι" to the root word "ομοιος". The passive voice indicates that the subject of the verb is being acted upon, rather than performing the action themselves. The aorist tense indicates that the action of the verb is completed in the past.

Description: ομοιωθηναι means "to be made similar" or "to be likened to". It is often used in a figurative sense to describe how one thing or person is similar to another.
