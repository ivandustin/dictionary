The word "ομολογωμεν" (omologomen) is a Greek verb in the present tense, first person plural form. It comes from the root word "ομολογέω" (omologeo), which means "to confess" or "to acknowledge." 

In terms of grammar, "ομολογωμεν" is a conjugated form of the verb and indicates that the subject (we) are currently confessing or acknowledging something. 

Overall, the word "ομολογωμεν" can be translated to mean "we confess" or "we acknowledge."
