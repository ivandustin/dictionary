Word: ομοιότητα

Etymology: The word ομοιότητα comes from the Greek root words ομοιος (homoios) meaning "similar" and τητα (tita) meaning "state of being". 

Grammar: ομοιότητα is a feminine noun in Greek. Its plural form is ομοιότητες. 

Description: ομοιότητα refers to the state of being similar or alike in appearance, nature, or characteristics. It can also refer to a resemblance or likeness between two or more things. The concept of ομοιότητα is often used in fields such as mathematics, science, and art.
