Word: ομοιωσω

The word ομοιωσω (omoiosó) comes from the Greek root word ομοιος (omoios), which means "similar" or "alike." The suffix -ωσω (-óso) is a verb ending that indicates the action of making something similar or alike.

Description:
Ομοιωσω is a Greek verb that means "I will make similar" or "I will compare."
