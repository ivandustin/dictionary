Word: ομοιοι

Etymology:
The word ομοιοι comes from the Greek root word "ομοιος" (omios), which means "similar" or "alike". The suffix "-οι" is added to indicate plural form.

Grammar:
Ομοιοι is a plural noun in Greek. It is in the nominative case and can be used as the subject of a sentence.

Description:
Ομοιοι is a Greek word that means "similar" or "alike". It is often used to describe things that have a resemblance or similarity to each other. For example, one might say "Οι ομοιοι αυτα τα δυο αντικειμενα ειναι πολυ κοντα στην εμφανιση τους" (The two objects are very similar in appearance).
