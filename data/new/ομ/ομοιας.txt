The word "ομοιας" (omias) is a Greek noun that means "similarity" or "likeness". 

Etymology: The word is derived from the Greek adjective "ομοιος" (omios), which means "similar" or "like". 

Grammar: "Ομοιας" is a feminine noun in the genitive case.
