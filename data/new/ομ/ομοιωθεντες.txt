The word "ομοιωθεντες" is a Greek verb that means "to be made similar" or "to be likened to." It is derived from the root word "ομοιος" which means "similar" or "like." In terms of grammar, "ομοιωθεντες" is the aorist passive participle of the verb "ομοιοω" which means "to make similar" or "to liken." This participle form indicates that the action of being made similar or likened to has already been completed in the past.
