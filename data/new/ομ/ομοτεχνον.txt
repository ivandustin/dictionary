Word: ομοτεχνον

Etymology: Ομοτεχνον is a Greek word that comes from the combination of two words: "ομος" meaning "same" and "τεχνη" meaning "art, craft, skill". Therefore, ομοτεχνον means "fellow craftsman" or "colleague in the same profession".

Grammar: Ομοτεχνον is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the masculine gender and the nominative case. The plural form of ομοτεχνον is ομοτεχνοι.

Description: Ομοτεχνον is a term used to refer to someone who works in the same profession or craft as another person. It is often used to describe a colleague or fellow craftsman who shares the same skills and knowledge in a particular field. The term can be used in a variety of contexts, such as in the arts, trades, or professions.
