Word: ομολογησω

Etymology:
The word ομολογησω comes from the Greek root words ομος (homos) meaning "same" and λογος (logos) meaning "word" or "reason". The word ομολογησω literally means "to speak the same word" or "to agree".

Grammar:
Ομολογησω is a verb in the Greek language. It is in the first person singular form of the future tense. The verb is conjugated as follows:

Ομολογησω (omologiso) - I will confess/agree
Ομολογησεις (omologiseis) - You will confess/agree
Ομολογησει (omologisi) - He/She/It will confess/agree
Ομολογησομεν (omologisomen) - We will confess/agree
Ομολογησετε (omologisete) - You (plural) will confess/agree
Ομολογησουσι(ν) (omologisousi(n)) - They will confess/agree

Description:
Ομολογησω is a verb that is often used in the context of confessing or admitting to something. It can also be used to express agreement or consent. In the New Testament of the Bible, the word is used in the context of confessing one's faith in Jesus Christ.
