Etymology: The word "ηκολουθούν" comes from the Greek verb "ακολουθώ" (akolouthó), which means "to follow."

Grammar: "ηκολουθούν" is the third person plural form of the verb "ακολουθώ" in the present tense. It is used to describe an action that multiple people are currently doing.

Description: "ηκολουθούν" means "they follow" in English. It is used to indicate that a group of people are following or accompanying someone or something.
