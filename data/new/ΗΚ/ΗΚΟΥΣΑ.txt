Etymology: The word "ηκούσα" (ikousa) comes from the Greek verb "ακούω" (akouo), which means "to hear." It is derived from the Proto-Indo-European root *h₂ḱews-.

Grammar: "ηκούσα" is the past tense of the verb "ακούω" (akouo) in the first person singular form. It is used to indicate that the speaker heard something in the past.

Description: "ηκούσα" is a Greek word that translates to "I heard" in English. It is used to express the action of hearing something in the past.
