Etymology: The word "πιπτει" comes from the Greek verb "πίπτω" (pipto), which means "to fall". 

Grammar: "Πιπτει" is the third person singular form of the present tense of the verb "πίπτω" (pipto). It is used to describe an action of falling that is happening in the present.
