Etymology: The word "πίνω" (pinō) comes from the Ancient Greek verb "πίνειν" (pinein), which means "to drink". 

Grammar: "Πίνω" is the first person singular present tense form of the verb "πίνω" (pinō), which means "I drink" in Greek. It belongs to the first conjugation class of Greek verbs.
