Etymology: The word "πικρία" (pikría) comes from the Greek word "πικρός" (pikrós), meaning "bitterness".

Grammar: "Πικρία" is a feminine noun in Greek. It belongs to the second declension and has the plural form "πικρίες" (pikríes).

Description: "Πικρία" refers to the feeling of bitterness or resentment. It is often used to describe a strong negative emotion or a deep sense of disappointment.
