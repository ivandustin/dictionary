Etymology: The word "πιεταί" (pietai) comes from the Greek verb "πίνω" (pino), which means "to drink". It is derived from the Proto-Indo-European root *peh₃- ("to drink").

Grammar: "Πιεταί" is the third person singular form of the present tense of the verb "πίνω" (pino) in Greek. It is used to describe the action of drinking in the present tense when referring to a third person.

Description: "Πιεταί" is a Greek verb that means "he/she/it drinks". It is used to express the action of consuming liquid through the mouth. The verb can be conjugated in different tenses, moods, and persons to indicate different aspects of the action.
