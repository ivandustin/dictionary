Etymology: The word "πινακίδιον" comes from the Greek word "πίναξ" (pinax), meaning "tablet" or "board".

Grammar: "Πινακίδιον" is a neuter noun in the singular form. In Greek grammar, neuter nouns have the same form for both the nominative and accusative cases.

Description: "Πινακίδιον" refers to a small tablet or board used for writing or displaying information. It can also refer to a sign or a nameplate.
