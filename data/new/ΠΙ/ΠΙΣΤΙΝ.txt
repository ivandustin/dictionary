Etymology: The word "πιστιν" (pistin) comes from the Greek word "πίστη" (pistē), meaning "faith" or "belief."

Grammar: "Πιστιν" is a noun in the accusative case, singular form.

Description: "Πιστιν" is a Greek word that can be translated as "faith" or "belief." It refers to the act of having trust or confidence in something or someone. In a religious context, it can also refer to religious faith or belief.
