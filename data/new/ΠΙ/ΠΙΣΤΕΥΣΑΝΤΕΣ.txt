Grammar: "Πιστεύσαντες" is the third person plural, aorist active participle of the verb "πιστεύω" (pisteúo). In Greek grammar, the aorist tense is used to express a completed action in the past. The participle form indicates that the action of believing has been completed by a group of people.

Description: "Πιστεύσαντες" is a Greek word that means "having believed" or "having had faith". It is often used in religious or philosophical contexts to describe a group of people who have accepted a certain belief or doctrine.
