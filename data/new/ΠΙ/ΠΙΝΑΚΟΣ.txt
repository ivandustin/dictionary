Etymology: The word "πινακος" (pinakos) comes from the Greek language. It is derived from the ancient Greek word "πίναξ" (pinax), meaning "tablet" or "board."

Grammar: In Greek, "πινακος" (pinakos) is a masculine noun. It belongs to the second declension and has the genitive form "πίνακος" (pinakos).

Description: "Πινακος" (pinakos) refers to a painting or a panel. It can also be used to describe a board or a tablet used for writing or displaying information.
