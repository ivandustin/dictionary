Etymology: The word "πιεσαί" (piesai) comes from the Greek verb "πιέζω" (piezo), which means "to press" or "to squeeze". 

Grammar: "Πιεσαί" is a noun in the plural form. It belongs to the third declension in Greek grammar. 

Description: "Πιεσαί" refers to the act of pressing or squeezing. It can also be used metaphorically to describe a situation or condition that feels constricting or oppressive.
