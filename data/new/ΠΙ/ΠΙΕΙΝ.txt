Etymology: The word "πιείν" (pien) comes from the Greek verb "πίνω" (pino), which means "to drink". 

Grammar: "Πιείν" is the infinitive form of the verb and is used to express the action of drinking. It belongs to the first conjugation class of Greek verbs.

Description: "Πιείν" is a Greek verb that specifically refers to the act of drinking. It can be used in various tenses, moods, and persons to indicate different aspects of the action.
