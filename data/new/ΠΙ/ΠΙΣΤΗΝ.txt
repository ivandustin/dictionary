Etymology: The word "πιστην" (pistin) comes from the Greek word "πίστη" (pisti), which means "faith" or "trust."

Grammar: "Πιστην" is a noun in the accusative case, singular form.

Description: "Πιστην" is a Greek word that translates to "faith" or "trust" in English. It refers to the belief or confidence in someone or something. It can also be used to describe loyalty or fidelity.
