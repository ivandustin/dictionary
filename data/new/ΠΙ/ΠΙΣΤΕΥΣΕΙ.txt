Etymology: The word "πιστεύσει" comes from the Greek verb "πιστεύω" (pistevo), which means "to believe" or "to have faith". It is derived from the noun "πίστις" (pistis), meaning "faith" or "belief".

Grammar: "Πιστεύσει" is the future tense, third person singular form of the verb "πιστεύω" (pistevo). In Greek grammar, the future tense is used to express actions or states that will happen in the future.

Description: "Πιστεύσει" is a Greek word that means "he/she/it will believe" or "he/she/it will have faith". It is used to indicate a future action or state of believing or having faith.
