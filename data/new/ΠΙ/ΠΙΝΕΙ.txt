Etymology: The word "πινει" (pinei) comes from the Greek verb "πίνω" (pino), which means "to drink". 

Grammar: "Πινει" is the third person singular form of the present tense of the verb "πίνω" (pino). It is used to describe the action of drinking performed by a singular subject.

Description: "Πινει" is a Greek word that means "he/she/it drinks". It is commonly used in everyday conversations to express the act of consuming liquids.
