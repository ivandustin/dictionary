Grammar: "Ψευδομαι" is a verb in the Greek language. It belongs to the first conjugation class and is conjugated in the present tense as follows:

- Singular: ψεύδομαι (psevdomai) - I deceive
- Plural: ψεύδεσθε (psevdesthe) - You (plural) deceive
- Singular: ψεύδεται (psevdetai) - He/she/it deceives
- Plural: ψεύδονται (psevdontai) - They deceive

Description: "Ψευδομαι" is a Greek verb that means "to deceive" or "to lie." It is used to describe the act of intentionally giving false information or misleading someone.
