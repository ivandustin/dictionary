Etymology: The word "ψευδομαρτυρών" comes from the Greek roots "ψευδο-" meaning "false" and "μαρτυρών" meaning "witness". 

Grammar: "ψευδομαρτυρών" is a noun in the masculine gender and the nominative case. It is the singular form of the word. 

Description: "ψευδομαρτυρών" refers to someone who gives false testimony or bears false witness. It is used to describe a person who intentionally provides misleading or untrue information in a legal or formal setting.
