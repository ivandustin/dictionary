Grammar: "Ψευδωνυμου" is a noun in the genitive case. In Greek grammar, the genitive case is used to indicate possession or origin. It is often translated as "of" or "belonging to." 

Description: "Ψευδωνυμου" refers to something that is falsely or misleadingly named. It can be used to describe a pseudonym, a false name used by someone, or any other situation where a name is used to deceive or mislead.
