Etymology: The word "ψευδομαρτυρίαι" comes from the Greek words "ψευδο-" meaning "false" and "μαρτυρίαι" meaning "testimonies". 

Grammar: "ψευδομαρτυρίαι" is a noun in the plural form. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their gender, number, and case. 

Description: "ψευδομαρτυρίαι" refers to false testimonies or false witness statements. It is used to describe statements or testimonies that are not true or accurate.
