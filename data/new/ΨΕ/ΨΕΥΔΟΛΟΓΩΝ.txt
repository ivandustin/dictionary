Etymology: The word "ψευδολογών" comes from the Greek roots "ψευδο-" meaning "false" and "λογών" meaning "speaking". 

Grammar: "ψευδολογών" is a noun in the masculine gender and singular number. It belongs to the third declension in Greek grammar.

Description: "ψευδολογών" refers to someone who speaks falsely or tells lies. It is used to describe a person who intentionally deceives others by making false statements or spreading misinformation.
