Grammar: "Εθερισθη" is the third person singular, aorist passive form of the verb "εθερίζω" (etherízo). In Greek grammar, the aorist tense is used to express a completed action in the past, and the passive voice indicates that the subject of the verb is being acted upon.

Description: "Εθερισθη" is a Greek word that describes the action of being cultivated or tilled. It implies the process of preparing the land, planting seeds, and taking care of the crops until they are ready for harvest.
