Etymology: The word "εθνεσιν" comes from the Greek word "εθνος" (ethnos), which means "nation" or "people".

Grammar: "Εθνεσιν" is a noun in the accusative case, singular number, and masculine gender in the Greek language.

Description: "Εθνεσιν" refers to a nation or a group of people who share a common language, culture, and history. It can also be used to describe the concept of nationality or citizenship.
