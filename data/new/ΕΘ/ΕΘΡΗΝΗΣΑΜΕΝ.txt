Etymology: The word "εθρηνησαμεν" comes from the Greek verb "θρηνέω" (threneo), which means "to lament" or "to mourn." The prefix "ε-" is added to indicate the past tense, and the suffix "-σαμεν" indicates the first person plural form.

Grammar: "Εθρηνησαμεν" is the first person plural form of the verb "θρηνέω" in the past tense. It is used to express that "we lamented" or "we mourned."

Description: "Εθρηνησαμεν" is a Greek word that describes an action of lamenting or mourning in the past. It is commonly used in literature, poetry, or songs to express grief, sorrow, or sadness.
