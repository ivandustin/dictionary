Etymology: The word "εθρηνούν" comes from the Greek verb "θρηνώ" (thrinó), which means "to mourn" or "to lament". It is derived from the noun "θρῆνος" (thrēnos), meaning "a funeral song" or "a dirge".

Grammar: "Εθρηνούν" is the third person plural form of the verb "θρηνώ" in the present tense. It is used to describe an action or state of mourning or lamenting performed by a group of people.

Description: "Εθρηνούν" is a Greek word that describes the act of mourning or lamenting. It implies a collective expression of grief or sorrow, often associated with funerals or tragic events.
