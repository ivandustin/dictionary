Etymology: The word "εθνικοί" (ethnicoi) comes from the Greek word "ἔθνος" (ethnos), which means "nation" or "people".

Grammar: "Εθνικοί" is the plural form of the adjective "εθνικός" (ethnikos), which means "national" or "ethnic". It agrees in gender, number, and case with the noun it modifies.

Description: "Εθνικοί" is used to describe something that is related to a specific nation or ethnic group. It can refer to cultural, historical, or political aspects of a nation or ethnic group.
