Etymology: The word "εθνικων" (ethnikon) comes from the Greek word "εθνος" (ethnos), meaning "nation" or "people".

Grammar: "Εθνικων" is the genitive plural form of the adjective "εθνικός" (ethnikos), which means "national" or "ethnic". In Greek grammar, the genitive case is used to indicate possession or origin.

Description: "Εθνικων" is used to describe something that is related to a nation or an ethnic group. It can refer to national customs, traditions, or characteristics.
