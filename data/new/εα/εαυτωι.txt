The word "εαυτωι" (eautoi) is a Greek word that means "to oneself" or "for oneself". It is derived from the Greek word "εαυτός" (eautos), which means "self". In terms of grammar, "εαυτωι" is a dative singular form of "εαυτός". It is often used in Greek literature and philosophy to express the idea of self-reflection and self-awareness.
