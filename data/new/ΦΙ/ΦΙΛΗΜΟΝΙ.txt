Etymology: The word "φιλημονη" (philēmonē) comes from the Greek roots "φίλος" (philos) meaning "friend" and "μόνος" (monos) meaning "alone" or "solitary". 

Grammar: "Φιλημονη" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the feminine gender and singular number. 

Description: "Φιλημονη" refers to the quality or state of being friendly, kind, or affectionate. It can also denote a friendly or affectionate attitude towards others.
