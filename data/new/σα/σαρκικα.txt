Word: σαρκικα

Grammar: σαρκικα is an adjective in the neuter plural form, which means it is used to describe or modify plural nouns that are neutral in gender. In Greek, adjectives must agree in gender, number, and case with the noun they modify.

Description: σαρκικα is an adjective that describes something that is related to or characterized by flesh or meat.
