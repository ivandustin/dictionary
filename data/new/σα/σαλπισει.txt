The word "σαλπισει" (salpisei) is a verb in the Greek language. It is in the future tense and active voice, meaning "he/she/it will sound a trumpet" or "he/she/it will blow a trumpet". 

Etymology: The word comes from the Greek root "σαλπίζω" (salpizo), which means "to sound a trumpet". 

Grammar: "σαλπισει" is a third-person singular verb in the future tense and active voice. 

Description: The word is often used in the context of military or religious ceremonies, where a trumpet is sounded to signal the beginning or end of an event. It can also be used metaphorically to describe a loud or attention-grabbing action.
