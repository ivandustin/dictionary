Word: συνχρωνται

Etymology: The word συνχρωνται is derived from the Greek words σύν (syn) meaning "together" and χρόνος (chronos) meaning "time". 

Grammar: συνχρωνται is a verb in the present tense, third person plural form. It is a compound verb, formed by adding the prefix συν- to the verb χροντάω (chrontao), which means "to synchronize". 

Description: συνχρωνται means "they synchronize" or "they are synchronized" in English. It is often used in the context of technology or music, where multiple devices or instruments are synchronized to work together.
