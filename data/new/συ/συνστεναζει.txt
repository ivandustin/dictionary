Word: συνστεναζει

Etymology: συν- (syn-) meaning "together" and στενάζω (stenazo) meaning "to groan" or "to moan". Therefore, συνστεναζει (synstenazei) means "to groan together" or "to moan together".

Grammar: συνστεναζει is a verb in the present tense, third person singular form. It is derived from the verb στενάζω (stenazo) which means "to groan" or "to moan". The prefix συν- (syn-) adds the meaning of "together" to the verb.

Description: συνστεναζει is a Greek verb that describes the act of groaning or moaning together with someone else. It can be used in a literal sense, such as when two people are in pain and groaning together, or in a figurative sense, such as when two people are expressing their shared frustration or disappointment.
