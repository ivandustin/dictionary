The word "συναπεστειλα" (synapestela) is a Greek verb that means "I restrained together with." 

Etymology: The word is derived from the prefix "syn-" meaning "together" and the verb "απεστειλα" (apestela) meaning "I sent away."

Grammar: "συναπεστειλα" is a first-person singular aorist indicative active verb in Greek.

Description: The word is used to describe the act of restraining or holding back something or someone together with someone else. It can also be used metaphorically to describe the act of cooperating or working together to achieve a common goal.
