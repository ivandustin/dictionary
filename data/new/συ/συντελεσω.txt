Word: συντελεσω

Etymology:
The word συντελεσω comes from the Greek root words συν- (syn-), meaning "together," and τελεω (teleo), meaning "to complete" or "to finish." Therefore, the literal meaning of συντελεσω is "to complete together" or "to finish together."

Grammar:
συντελεσω is a verb in the Greek language, specifically in the present tense. It is in the first person singular form, meaning "I complete" or "I finish." The verb is also in the active voice, indicating that the subject is performing the action.

Description:
συντελεσω is a versatile verb that can be used in a variety of contexts. It can refer to completing a task or finishing a project, but it can also refer to bringing something to an end or reaching a conclusion. In some cases, it can even refer to achieving a goal or fulfilling a purpose. Overall, συντελεσω is a powerful word that conveys the idea of bringing something to a successful conclusion.
