Word: συνπαραλαβειν

Etymology: συν- (together) + παραλαβεῖν (to receive, take)

Grammar: συνπαραλαβεῖν is a verb in the infinitive form. It is composed of the prefix συν- and the verb παραλαβεῖν, which is in the aorist infinitive active tense.

Description: συνπαραλαβεῖν means "to take along with, to receive together with." It implies a joint action or participation in something. The word is often used in a religious context, referring to the sharing of spiritual blessings or the participation in a common faith.
