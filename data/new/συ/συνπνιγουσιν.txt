The word "συνπνιγουσιν" is a Greek verb that means "they strangle together" or "they suffocate together". It is derived from the prefix "συν-" meaning "together" and the verb "πνίγω" meaning "to strangle" or "to suffocate". In terms of grammar, "συνπνιγουσιν" is a present tense, third person plural form of the verb.
