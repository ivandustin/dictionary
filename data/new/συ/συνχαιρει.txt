Word: συνχαιρει

Etymology: συν- (together) + χαιρει (rejoice)

Grammar: συνχαιρει is a verb in the present tense, third person singular form. It is derived from the root verb χαιρω (to rejoice) and the prefix συν- (together).

Description: συνχαιρει means "rejoice together" or "congratulate". It is often used in a formal or celebratory context, such as congratulating someone on a wedding or a promotion. The word emphasizes the idea of sharing in someone else's joy or happiness.
