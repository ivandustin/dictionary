In terms of grammar, it is a first-person singular present active indicative form of the verb. It can also be conjugated in other tenses and persons. 

In terms of description, "συνεργωι" is a versatile verb that can be used in various contexts to express the idea of working together or cooperating. It can be used in both formal and informal settings, and it is commonly used in business, politics, and social interactions.
