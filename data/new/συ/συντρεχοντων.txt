Word: συντρεχόντων

Etymology:
The word συντρεχόντων is derived from the Greek verb συντρέχω (syntrecho), which means "to run together" or "to converge". The suffix -όντων (-onton) is the present participle form of the verb, indicating that the action is ongoing or continuous.

Grammar:
συντρεχόντων is the genitive plural form of the present participle of the verb συντρέχω. In Greek, the genitive case is used to indicate possession or relationship between nouns. In this case, συντρεχόντων is used to describe a group of people or things that are running together or converging.

Description:
συντρεχόντων can be translated to "of those who are running together" or "of those who are converging". It is often used in Greek literature and philosophy to describe a group of people who are working towards a common goal or purpose. The word implies a sense of unity and cooperation among the individuals involved.
