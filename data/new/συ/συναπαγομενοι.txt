Word: συναπαγομενοι

The word συναπαγομενοι is a Greek word that comes from the prefix συν- (syn-), meaning "together," and the verb ἀπάγω (apagō), meaning "to lead away." The word is in the present passive participle form, indicating an ongoing action of being led away together.
