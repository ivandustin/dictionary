Word: συνεργουντος

Etymology: συνεργουντος is a Greek word that comes from the combination of two words: συν (syn), meaning "together," and εργον (ergon), meaning "work." Therefore, the word συνεργουντος means "working together."

Grammar: συνεργουντος is the present participle of the Greek verb συνεργέω (synergeó), which means "to work together." It is in the masculine gender, singular number, and nominative case.

Description: συνεργουντος is used to describe someone who is currently working together with others towards a common goal. It can also be used to describe a situation where multiple people or entities are collaborating to achieve a specific outcome. The word implies a sense of cooperation and teamwork.
