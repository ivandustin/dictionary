Word: συνδεσμον

Etymology:
The word συνδεσμον comes from the Greek root words σύν (syn) meaning "together" and δεσμός (desmos) meaning "bond" or "tie". Together, they form the word συνδεσμον, which means "connection" or "link".

Description:
συνδεσμον refers to a connection or link between two or more things. It can be used in a literal sense, such as a physical connection between two objects, or in a figurative sense, such as a connection between ideas or concepts.
