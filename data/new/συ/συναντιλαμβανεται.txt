Word: συναντιλαμβανεται

Etymology: The word συναντιλαμβάνεται comes from the Greek roots συν- (syn-), meaning "together," αντι- (anti-), meaning "against," and λαμβάνω (lambanō), meaning "to take." The word is a compound verb formed by combining these roots.

Grammar: συναντιλαμβάνεται is a third-person singular present indicative passive verb in Greek. It is formed by adding the suffix -ται (-tai) to the stem συναντιλαμβαν- (synantilamban-). The verb is in the passive voice, indicating that the subject is receiving the action of the verb.

Description: The word συναντιλαμβάνεται means "to take part in, to share in, to participate in" in Greek. It is often used in a collaborative or cooperative sense, indicating that multiple parties are working together towards a common goal. The word can also be used in a more general sense to describe someone who is involved in or contributing to a particular activity or event.
