Word: συνεργοί

Etymology: The word συνεργοί comes from the Greek roots συν- (syn-), meaning "together," and ἔργον (ergon), meaning "work." Therefore, συνεργοί literally means "co-workers" or "collaborators."

Grammar: συνεργοί is a plural noun in the masculine gender. It is the nominative case form of the word, which means it is used as the subject of a sentence.

Description: συνεργοί is a term used to describe people who work together or collaborate on a project or task. It can refer to colleagues, partners, or teammates who share a common goal and work towards achieving it. In Greek culture, the concept of συνεργία (synergy) is highly valued, as it emphasizes the importance of cooperation and teamwork in achieving success.
