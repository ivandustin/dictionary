Word: συνχυννεται

Etymology: συν- (syn-) meaning "together" and χυννάω (chynnao) meaning "to pour". The word συνχυννεται (synchynnetai) is derived from the verb χυννάω and the prefix συν-.

Grammar: συνχυννεται is a third person singular present passive indicative form of the verb συνχύννω (synchynno), which means "to mix together". The passive voice indicates that the subject is being acted upon, rather than performing the action.

Description: συνχυννεται describes the process of two or more substances being mixed together. It can also be used metaphorically to describe the blending of different ideas or concepts. The word is commonly used in scientific and technical contexts, such as chemistry or engineering.
