Word: συναναβασαι

Etymology: συν- (together) + ἀναβαίνω (to go up)

Grammar: συναναβασαι is a verb in the aorist tense, active voice, and infinitive mood. It is formed by combining the prefix συν- with the verb ἀναβαίνω.

Description: συναναβασαι means "to go up together" or "to ascend together". It implies a joint effort or action of going up or climbing together. This word can be used in a literal sense, such as climbing a mountain with someone, or in a figurative sense, such as working together to achieve a common goal.
