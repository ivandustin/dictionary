The word συνκεκερασμένους is a compound word in Greek, formed by combining the prefix συν- (meaning "together" or "with") and the verb κεκερασμένος (meaning "mixed" or "blended"). The root of the verb κεραίνω means "to mix" or "to blend", and the suffix -μένος indicates the past participle form.

The word συνκεκερασμένους is used to describe something that is mixed or blended together. It can be used to describe a variety of things, such as food, drinks, or even ideas.
