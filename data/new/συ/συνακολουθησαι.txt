Word: συνακολουθησαι

Etymology:
The word συνακολουθησαι is a compound word in Greek, consisting of the prefix συν- (meaning "together" or "with") and the verb ακολουθέω (meaning "to follow"). Therefore, the literal meaning of συνακολουθησαι is "to follow along with" or "to accompany".

Grammar:
συνακολουθησαι is a verb in the aorist tense, active voice, and infinitive mood. In Greek, the aorist tense is used to describe a completed action in the past, while the infinitive mood is used to express the idea of an action without specifying who is performing it. Therefore, συνακολουθησαι can be translated as "to have followed along with" or "to have accompanied".

Description:
συνακολουθησαι is a word that describes the act of following along with someone or something. It can be used in a literal sense, such as following someone on a journey, or in a figurative sense, such as following someone's ideas or beliefs. The word implies a sense of unity or cooperation, as the prefix συν- suggests working together or being in agreement. Overall, συνακολουθησαι is a versatile word that can be used in a variety of contexts to describe the act of following along with someone or something.
