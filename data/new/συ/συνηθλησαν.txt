Word: συνηθλησαν

Etymology: συν- (together) + ἠθλησαν (they contended, competed) from ἀθλέω (to contend, compete)

Grammar: συνηθλησαν is a verb in the aorist tense, third person plural form. It is a compound verb formed by adding the prefix συν- to the verb ἀθλέω.

Description: συνηθλησαν means "they contended together" or "they competed together". It implies a joint effort or collaboration in a competition or contest. The word is commonly used in sports contexts, but can also be used metaphorically to describe any situation where people work together towards a common goal.
