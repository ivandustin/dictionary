The word "συνμετοχα" is a Greek verb that means "to participate together" or "to share in something with others." 

Etymology: The word is derived from the Greek roots "συν-" meaning "together" and "μετέχω" meaning "to participate."

Grammar: "Συνμετοχα" is a first-person singular present indicative active form of the verb. 

Description: The word is often used in a religious or spiritual context to describe the act of sharing in a common faith or belief. It can also be used more generally to describe any situation where people come together to participate in a common activity or goal.
