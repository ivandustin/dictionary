Word: συνμετοχοι

Etymology: The word συνμετοχοι comes from the Greek roots συν- (syn-), meaning "together," and μετέχω (metécho), meaning "to participate." Therefore, the word συνμετοχοι means "co-participants" or "joint participants."

Grammar: συνμετοχοι is a plural noun in the Greek language. It is declined in the third declension and has a masculine gender. The nominative singular form of the word is συνμετοχός (synmetochós).

Description: συνμετοχοι refers to a group of people who participate together in a common activity or event. It can be used in various contexts, such as in sports, business, or politics. The word implies a sense of collaboration and teamwork among the participants.
