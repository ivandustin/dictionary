Word: συνκοινωνειτε

Etymology: συν- (syn-) meaning "together" and κοινωνειτε (koinōneite) meaning "to share, participate, communicate". Together, συνκοινωνειτε means "to share together, participate together, communicate together".

Grammar: συνκοινωνειτε is a verb in the present tense, second person plural form. It is derived from the Greek word κοινωνέω (koinōneō), which means "to share in, participate in, communicate with". The prefix συν- (syn-) adds the meaning of "together" to the verb.

Description: συνκοινωνειτε is a Greek verb that emphasizes the importance of sharing, participating, and communicating together. It suggests a sense of community and collaboration, and encourages people to work together towards a common goal. This word can be used in various contexts, such as in business, education, or social settings, to promote teamwork and cooperation.
