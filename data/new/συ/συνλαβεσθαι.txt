Word: συνλαβεσθαι

Etymology: συν- (together) + λαβεῖν (to take)

Grammar: συνλαβεσθαι is a verb in the aorist passive infinitive form. It is derived from the verb συλλαμβάνω (to take together) and the prefix συν- (together).

Description: συνλαβεσθαι means "to be taken together" or "to be apprehended together". It is often used in legal contexts to refer to the arrest of multiple individuals at the same time.
