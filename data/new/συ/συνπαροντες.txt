Word: συνπαροντες

Etymology: συν- (together) + παρόντες (present participle of πάρειμι, meaning "to be present"). Therefore, συνπαροντες means "being present together".

Grammar: συνπαροντες is a present participle in Greek, which functions as an adjective. It can be used to modify a noun or pronoun, indicating that the subject is present together with another person or thing.

Description: συνπαροντες is a term used in Greek literature and philosophy to describe the idea of being present together with someone or something else. It is often used in the context of discussing relationships, community, and the interconnectedness of all things. The term emphasizes the importance of being present and engaged with others, rather than simply existing in isolation.
