The word "συνεσει" (synesei) is a Greek noun that means "understanding" or "comprehension". It is derived from the verb "συνίημι" (syniemi), which means "to understand" or "to perceive". 

In terms of grammar, "συνεσει" is a third-person singular noun in the dative case. It can be translated as "to/for understanding" or "with understanding". 

Overall, "συνεσει" is a word that emphasizes the importance of comprehension and understanding in various contexts, such as education, communication, and problem-solving.
