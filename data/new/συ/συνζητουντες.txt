Word: συνζητούντες

Etymology: The word συνζητούντες comes from the Greek prefix συν- (meaning "together") and the verb ζητώ (meaning "to seek" or "to discuss"). The suffix -ούντες indicates that the word is in the present participle form, meaning "those who are discussing together".

Grammar: συνζητούντες is a present participle in the masculine plural form. It is derived from the verb συνζητώ, which means "to discuss together". The word can be used as an adjective or a noun, depending on the context.

Description: συνζητούντες refers to a group of people who are discussing something together. It can be used to describe a formal meeting or an informal conversation. The word implies that the discussion is ongoing and that the participants are actively engaged in the conversation.
