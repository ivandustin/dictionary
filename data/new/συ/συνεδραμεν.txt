Word: συνεδραμεν

Etymology: συν- (together) + ἐδράμην (I ran)

Grammar: συνεδραμεν is the aorist participle of the verb συντρέχω (to run together). It is declined in the masculine nominative singular.

Description: συνεδραμεν describes someone who has run together with others. It can be used in a literal sense, such as in a race or battle, or in a figurative sense, such as in a group project or collaboration.
