Word: συνζητουντας

Etymology: συν- (together) + ζητέω (to seek, to search) + -ούντας (present participle suffix) = συνζητούντας (those who are seeking together)

Grammar: συνζητούντας is the present participle form of the verb συνζητώ (to discuss, to confer). It is in the masculine plural form, indicating that the subject is a group of males or a mixed group of males and females.

Description: συνζητούντας refers to a group of people who are discussing or conferring together. It can be used in various contexts, such as in a business meeting, a political debate, or a casual conversation among friends. The word implies a collaborative effort to reach a common understanding or decision.
