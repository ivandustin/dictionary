Word: συνμεριζονται

Etymology: The word συνμεριζονται is derived from the Greek words συν (syn) meaning "together" and μερίζω (merizo) meaning "to divide, share". 

Grammar: συνμεριζονται is a verb in the present tense, third person plural form. It is a compound verb, formed by adding the prefix συν- to the verb μερίζω. 

Description: συνμεριζονται means "they share together" or "they divide together". It implies a joint effort or collaboration in sharing or dividing something. The word can be used in various contexts, such as sharing responsibilities, dividing resources, or sharing ideas.
