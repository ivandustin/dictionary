Word: συναπεθανομεν

Etymology: συν- (together) + ἀποθνῄσκω (to die) + -μεν (1st person plural ending) = "we died together"

Grammar: συναπεθανομεν is a verb in the aorist tense, 1st person plural form. It is composed of the prefix συν- (together), the verb ἀποθνῄσκω (to die), and the 1st person plural ending -μεν.

Description: συναπεθανομεν is a Greek word that means "we died together". It is often used to describe a group of people who died at the same time or in the same event. The word is commonly used in literature and poetry to convey a sense of unity and shared experience in death.
