The word "συνπνιγει" (synpnigei) is a Greek verb that means "to suffocate together with someone or something." 

Etymology: The word is derived from the prefix "syn-" meaning "together" and the verb "pnigeo" meaning "to choke" or "to suffocate."

Grammar: "συνπνιγει" is a present tense, active voice, indicative mood verb in Greek. It is conjugated in the third person singular form.

Description: The word is often used in a figurative sense to describe a feeling of being overwhelmed or suffocated by a situation or person. It can also be used in a literal sense to describe a physical act of suffocation.
