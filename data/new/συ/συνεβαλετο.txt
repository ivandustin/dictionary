Word: συνεβαλετο

Etymology:
συν- (syn-) meaning "together"
βαλλω (ballo) meaning "to throw"

συνεβαλετο (synebaleto) is a compound word in Greek, formed by combining the prefix συν- (syn-) meaning "together" with the verb βαλλω (ballo) meaning "to throw". The word literally means "to throw together" or "to contribute".

Grammar:
συνεβαλετο is a third person singular aorist middle indicative verb in Greek. The aorist tense indicates a completed action in the past, while the middle voice indicates that the subject is acting upon itself or is affected by the action.

Description:
συνεβαλετο is a Greek verb that means "to contribute" or "to join in". It is often used in the context of a group of people coming together to achieve a common goal. The word implies a sense of collaboration and cooperation, as well as a willingness to work together towards a shared objective.
