Word: συναντησοντα

Etymology: συν- (syn-) meaning "together" or "with" and αντησοντα (antēsonta) meaning "meeting" or "encounter". Together, συναντησοντα means "meeting together" or "encountering together".

Grammar: συναντησοντα is the present participle form of the Greek verb συναντάω (synantáō), which means "to meet" or "to encounter". The present participle form indicates that the action of meeting or encountering is currently happening or ongoing.

Description: συναντησοντα is a Greek word that describes the act of meeting or encountering together. It can be used in various contexts, such as meeting someone in person, encountering a situation or problem together, or even meeting someone's expectations. The word is commonly used in everyday conversation and is an important part of the Greek language.
