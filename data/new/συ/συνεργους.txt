Word: συνεργούς

Etymology: The word συνεργούς comes from the Greek words σύν (syn), meaning "together," and ἔργον (ergon), meaning "work." Therefore, the literal meaning of συνεργούς is "working together."

Grammar: συνεργούς is a plural noun in the masculine gender. It is in the nominative case, which is used for the subject of a sentence.

Description: συνεργούς refers to people who work together towards a common goal or purpose. It can be translated as "collaborators," "co-workers," or "partners." The word implies a sense of cooperation and teamwork, and is often used in a professional or business context.
