The word "συναραι" (synarai) is a Greek verb that means "to lift up together" or "to raise up together." It is derived from the prefix "syn-" meaning "together" and the verb "airo" meaning "to lift up." 

In terms of grammar, "συναραι" is a second person plural present active imperative verb. This means that it is a command directed towards a group of people to lift up something together in the present moment. 

Overall, "συναραι" is a word that emphasizes the importance of working together and lifting each other up.
