Word: συνεβαλλον

Etymology: συν- (syn-, meaning "together") + βαλλω (ballō, meaning "to throw") → συνεβαλλον (syneballon, meaning "to throw together")

Grammar: συνεβαλλον is a verb in the past tense, active voice, and indicative mood. It is the third person plural form of the verb συνβαλλω (synballō).

Description: συνεβαλλον is a Greek verb that means "they contributed" or "they added up". It is often used in the context of adding up numbers or combining efforts towards a common goal. In the New Testament, it is used to describe the disciples contributing to the feeding of the 5,000 (Mark 6:41) and the 4,000 (Mark 8:7).
