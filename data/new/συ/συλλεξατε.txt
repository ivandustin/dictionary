Word: συλλεξατε

Grammar: συλλεξατε is a second person plural aorist active indicative verb in Greek. It is used to describe an action that was completed in the past by a group of people. The aorist tense indicates that the action was completed at a specific point in time, while the active voice indicates that the subject (you all) performed the action.
