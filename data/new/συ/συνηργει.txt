The word "συνηργει" is a Greek verb that means "to work together" or "to cooperate." It is derived from the prefix "συν-" meaning "together" and the verb "ἐργάζομαι" meaning "to work." In terms of grammar, "συνηργει" is in the present tense, active voice, and indicative mood. It can be translated as "he/she/it works together" or "he/she/it is cooperating."
