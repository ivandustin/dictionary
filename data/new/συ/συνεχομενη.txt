Word: συνεχομενη

Etymology: συνεχομενη is a Greek word that comes from the verb συνέχω (synécho), which means "to hold together" or "to unite". The suffix -μενη (-meni) is a present participle ending, indicating that the word is describing an action that is currently happening.

Grammar: συνεχομενη is a feminine singular form of the present participle of the verb συνέχω. It is in the nominative case, which means it is the subject of a sentence.

Description: συνεχομενη describes something that is currently being held together or united. It can be used to describe a physical object or a more abstract concept.
