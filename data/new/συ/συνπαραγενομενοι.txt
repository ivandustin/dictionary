Word: συνπαραγενομενοι

Etymology:
The word συνπαραγενομενοι is a compound word in Greek, formed by combining the prefix συν- (meaning "together" or "with") and the verb παραγίνομαι (meaning "to arrive" or "to come"). The word is in the present participle form, indicating an ongoing action of arriving or coming together.

Grammar:
συνπαραγενομενοι is a present participle in the masculine plural form. It is derived from the verb παραγίνομαι, which is a deponent verb, meaning that it has a passive form but an active meaning. The prefix συν- indicates that the action is being done together with others.

Description:
The word συνπαραγενομενοι can be translated to mean "coming together" or "arriving together." It is often used in the context of a group of people arriving at a particular place or event. The word emphasizes the idea of unity and togetherness, as the action is being done collectively.
