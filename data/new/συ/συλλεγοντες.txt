Word: συλλέγοντες

Etymology: συλλέγοντες is a present participle form of the Greek verb συλλέγω (syllego), which means "to gather, collect, assemble". The word is derived from the prefix συν- (syn-), meaning "together", and the verb λέγω (lego), meaning "to choose, pick, select".

Grammar: συλλέγοντες is a present participle in the active voice, masculine gender, and plural number. It is formed by adding the suffix -οντες (-ontes) to the present stem συλλεγ- (sylleg-).

Description: συλλέγοντες is used to describe a group of people who are currently gathering or collecting something. It can be translated as "collecting" or "gathering" in English. The word is often used in a figurative sense to describe the gathering of information, ideas, or opinions. For example, "συλλέγοντες πληροφορίες για το θέμα" (syllegontes plirofories gia to thema) means "collecting information about the topic".
