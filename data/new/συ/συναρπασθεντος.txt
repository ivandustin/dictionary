The word συναρπασθεντος is derived from the Greek roots συν (syn) meaning "together" and αρπαζω (harpazo) meaning "to seize or snatch away". The word is a passive participle form of the verb συναρπαζω (synarpazo) which means "to be seized or carried away together".
