The word "συνπνιγονται" is a Greek verb that means "they are suffocating together" or "they are strangling together". It is derived from the prefix "συν-" which means "together" and the verb "πνίγω" which means "to suffocate" or "to strangle". In terms of grammar, "συνπνιγονται" is a present tense, passive voice, indicative mood verb.
