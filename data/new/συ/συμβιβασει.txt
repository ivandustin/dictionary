The word "συμβιβασει" (sumbivasei) is a Greek verb in the present tense, third person singular form. It comes from the root word "συμβιβάζω" (sumbivazo), which means "to reconcile" or "to bring into agreement." 

In terms of grammar, "συμβιβασει" is a regular verb in the present tense, following the conjugation pattern of verbs ending in "-ω" in Greek. 

Overall, "συμβιβασει" can be translated to mean "he/she/it reconciles" or "he/she/it brings into agreement."
