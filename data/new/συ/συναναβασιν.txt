Word: συναναβασιν

Etymology: The word συναναβασιν is derived from the Greek words συν (syn) meaning "together" and αναβασιν (anabasin) meaning "ascent" or "climb". 

Grammar: συναναβασιν is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the accusative case and singular number. 

Description: συναναβασιν refers to a joint ascent or climb, where two or more people climb together. It can also be used metaphorically to describe a joint effort or collaboration towards a common goal.
