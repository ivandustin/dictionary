The word "συνκαθημενος" (synkathēmenos) is a Greek adjective that means "sitting together" or "sitting with." It is derived from the prefix "syn-" meaning "together" and the verb "kathēmai" meaning "to sit." 

In terms of grammar, "συνκαθημενος" is a masculine singular nominative participle. It can function as an adjective, modifying a noun, or as a noun itself, referring to a person who is sitting together with others. 

Overall, "συνκαθημενος" is a descriptive word that conveys the idea of sitting together or being in a group.
