The word "συνοιδα" (synoida) is a Greek verb that means "to know together" or "to be aware of something with someone else." 

Etymology: The word is derived from the prefix "syn-" meaning "together" and the verb "οἶδα" (oida) meaning "to know."

Grammar: "Συνοιδα" is a first-person singular present indicative active form of the verb. 

Description: The word is often used in a philosophical or spiritual context to describe a shared understanding or awareness between individuals. It can also be used in a more practical sense to describe a mutual knowledge or awareness of a particular situation or event.
