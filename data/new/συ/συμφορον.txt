Word: συμφορον

Grammar: συμφορον is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the singular form and is classified as a neuter noun. In the nominative case, it is spelled as συμφορον.

Description: συμφορον refers to a misfortune or calamity that befalls someone. It can also refer to a difficult or trying situation. The word is often used in a negative context and is associated with hardship and suffering.
