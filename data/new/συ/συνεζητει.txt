Word: συνεζητει

Etymology:
συν- (syn-) means "together" or "with"
ζητεω (zeteo) means "to seek" or "to inquire"

Therefore, συνεζητει (synezeite) means "to discuss together" or "to confer with one another."

Grammar:
συνεζητει is a verb in the present tense, third person singular form. It is derived from the verb συνεζητεω (synezeiteo), which means "to consult together" or "to deliberate."

Description:
συνεζητει is a Greek word that refers to the act of discussing or deliberating together. It can be used in various contexts, such as in business meetings, political discussions, or personal conversations. The word emphasizes the importance of collaboration and communication in decision-making processes.
