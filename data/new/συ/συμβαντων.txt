Word: συμβαντων

Etymology: συμβαντων is the genitive plural form of the Greek word συμβάντων (sumbántōn), which is the participle of the verb συμβαίνω (sumbaínō), meaning "to happen" or "to occur."

Grammar: συμβαντων is a genitive plural noun in Greek. In English, it would be translated as "of events."

Description: συμβαντων is a word that refers to events or occurrences. It is often used in historical or narrative contexts to describe a series of events that took place. The genitive plural form suggests that there are multiple events being referred to.
