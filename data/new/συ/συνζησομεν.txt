The word συνζησομεν (synzisomen) is a Greek verb in the future tense, first person plural form. It is derived from the root word ζητέω (zetéo), which means "to seek" or "to search for." The prefix συν- (syn-) means "together" or "with," so the word συνζησομεν can be translated as "we will seek together" or "we will search for together." 

In terms of grammar, συνζησομεν is a regular verb in the future tense, following the conjugation pattern of the verb ζητέω. It is formed by adding the future tense ending -σομεν (-somen) to the stem συνζησ- (synzis-). 

Overall, συνζησομεν is a collaborative and proactive word, indicating a joint effort to seek or search for something.
