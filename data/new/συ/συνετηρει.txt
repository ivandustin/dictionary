Word: συνετηρει

Etymology:
The word συνετηρει comes from the Greek root words συν- (syn-), meaning "together," and τηρέω (tēréō), meaning "to keep, guard, preserve." The combination of these two roots gives the word its meaning of "to keep together, preserve, maintain."

Grammar:
συνετηρει is a verb in the present tense, third person singular form. It is derived from the verb τηρέω (tēréō), which means "to keep, guard, preserve." The prefix συν- (syn-) adds the meaning of "together," giving the word its specific meaning of "to keep together, preserve, maintain."

Description:
συνετηρει is a Greek verb that means "to keep together, preserve, maintain." It is often used in the context of preserving or maintaining something that is important or valuable. For example, it could be used to describe the act of preserving a historical artifact or maintaining a relationship. The word emphasizes the importance of keeping things together and maintaining their integrity over time.
