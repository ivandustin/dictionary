Word: συναρμολογουμενον

The word is derived from the prefix συν- (syn-), meaning "together," and the verb ἁρμολογέω (harmologéō), meaning "to fit, to join."

Description: συναρμολογουμενον refers to something that is being joined together or assembled. It can be used to describe a physical object that is being put together, or a concept or idea that is being formed by combining different elements.
