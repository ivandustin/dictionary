Word: συμβαλειν

Etymology: συμβαλειν comes from the Greek word συμβάλλω (sumballō), which means "to throw together" or "to combine". It is derived from the prefix συν- (syn-), meaning "together", and the verb βάλλω (ballō), meaning "to throw".
