Word: συνεπνιξαν

Etymology: συν- (syn-, meaning "together") + ἐπνίγω (epnigō, meaning "to strangle")

Grammar: συνεπνιξαν is a verb in the aorist tense, third person plural form. It is derived from the verb ἐπνίγω (epnigō), which means "to strangle," with the prefix συν- (syn-, meaning "together") added to indicate that the action was done together or in unison.

Description: συνεπνιξαν is a Greek word that means "they strangled together" or "they choked together." It is often used in a figurative sense to describe a situation where multiple people or forces are working together to suffocate or suppress something. The word has a strong connotation of violence and aggression.
