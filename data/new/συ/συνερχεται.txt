Word: συνερχεται

Etymology: συν- (syn-, meaning "together") + ἔρχομαι (érkhomai, meaning "to come"). Therefore, συνερχεται means "to come together" or "to assemble".

Grammar: συνερχεται is a verb in the present tense, third person singular form. It is derived from the verb συνέρχομαι (synérchomai), which means "to come together" or "to assemble". 

Description: συνερχεται is a Greek word that refers to the act of coming together or assembling. It can be used in a variety of contexts, such as describing a group of people gathering for a meeting or a crowd forming in a public space. The word is often used in a figurative sense as well, to describe the coming together of ideas or concepts.
