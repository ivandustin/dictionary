Word: συντομως

Etymology:
The word συντομως is derived from the Greek word σύντομος (syntomos), which means "brief" or "concise". It is formed from the prefix συν- (syn-), meaning "together", and the adjective τομός (tomos), meaning "cut" or "divided".

Grammar:
συντομως is an adverb in Greek, which means that it modifies the verb in a sentence. It is often used to indicate that something will happen soon or shortly.

Description:
συντομως is a useful word in Greek that can be used in a variety of contexts. It is often used in conversation to indicate that something will happen soon or shortly, such as "I will be there shortly" or "I will finish the task soon". It can also be used in writing to indicate a brief or concise summary of a longer text or idea. Overall, συντομως is a versatile word that can be used in many different situations.
