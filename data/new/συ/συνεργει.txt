The word "συνεργεί" (synergei) is a Greek verb that means "to work together" or "to cooperate". It is derived from the prefix "συν-" (syn-), meaning "together", and the verb "έργο" (ergo), meaning "to work". 

In terms of grammar, "συνεργεί" is a present tense verb in the third person singular form. It can be conjugated in various tenses and moods to indicate different aspects and nuances of cooperation and collaboration.

Overall, "συνεργεί" is a versatile and important word in Greek, reflecting the value placed on teamwork and cooperation in many aspects of life and society.
