Word: συνζητουντων

Etymology: συν- (together) + ζητέω (to seek, to search) + -οντ- (present participle suffix) = συνζητούντων (those who are seeking together)

Grammar: συνζητούντων is the present participle form of the verb συνζητώ (to discuss, to confer) in the genitive plural. It is used to describe a group of people who are currently discussing or conferring together.

Description: συνζητούντων is a Greek word that refers to a group of people who are engaged in a discussion or conference together. It can be used to describe a formal meeting or an informal conversation among friends. The word emphasizes the collaborative nature of the discussion, suggesting that the participants are working together to seek a common understanding or solution.
