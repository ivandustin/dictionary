Word: συμβιβαζοντες

Etymology: The word συμβιβαζοντες comes from the Greek verb συμβιβάζω (sumbibázō), which means "to reconcile" or "to bring together." The suffix -οντες (-ontes) is a present participle ending, indicating that the word refers to people who are currently reconciling or bringing together.

Grammar: συμβιβαζοντες is a present participle in the active voice, masculine gender, and plural number. It is derived from the verb συμβιβάζω, which is a first conjugation verb in the present tense.

Description: συμβιβαζοντες can be translated to mean "reconciling" or "bringing together." It refers to people who are actively working to resolve conflicts or differences and find common ground. The word can be used in a variety of contexts, from personal relationships to political negotiations.
