Word: συναγωγων

Etymology: The word συναγωγων comes from the Greek word συναγωγή (synagoge), which means "assembly" or "gathering". The suffix -ων (-on) is a genitive plural ending, indicating that the word refers to "of the synagogues".

Grammar: συναγωγων is a noun in the genitive plural form. In Greek, the genitive case is used to indicate possession or relationship, and the plural form indicates that there are multiple synagogues being referred to.

Description: συναγωγων refers to "of the synagogues" or "belonging to the synagogues". It is often used in reference to Jewish communities or places of worship. In the New Testament, it is used to describe the gatherings of Jews for religious purposes, and is often translated as "synagogue" in English.
