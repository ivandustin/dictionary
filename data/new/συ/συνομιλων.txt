Word: συνομιλων

Etymology:
The word συνομιλων comes from the Greek roots συν- (syn-), meaning "together," and ὁμιλέω (homiléō), meaning "to converse." Therefore, the word συνομιλων means "one who converses together."

Grammar:
In Greek, συνομιλων is a present participle, which means it describes an ongoing action. It is in the masculine gender, singular number, and nominative case.

Description:
The word συνομιλων refers to someone who engages in conversation or dialogue with others. It can be used to describe someone who is sociable, friendly, and enjoys spending time with others. In a broader sense, it can also refer to someone who collaborates or works together with others towards a common goal.
