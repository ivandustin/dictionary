The word συνεπεστη (synepestē) is a Greek verb that means "to stand together" or "to be consistent." 

Etymology: The word is derived from the prefix συν- (syn-), meaning "together," and the verb ἐπίστημι (epistēmi), meaning "to stand."

Grammar: συνεπεστη is a third-person singular aorist indicative active verb in Greek.

Description: The word is often used in a figurative sense to describe someone who is consistent in their beliefs or actions. It can also be used to describe things that are in agreement or harmony with each other.
