Word: συναναπαυσωμαι

Etymology: συν- (together) + ἀναπαύω (to rest) + -σωμαι (middle voice suffix indicating reflexive action). Therefore, the word means "to rest together with someone else."

Grammar: συναναπαυσώμαι is a Greek verb in the present tense, middle voice, and first person singular form. It is a compound verb, consisting of the prefix συν- and the verb ἀναπαύω. The middle voice indicates that the subject is performing the action on themselves, and the present tense indicates that the action is happening currently.

Description: συναναπαυσώμαι is a Greek verb that means "to rest together with someone else." It implies a sense of companionship and mutual rest. The word is often used in a spiritual or religious context, referring to the rest that comes from being in the presence of God or other believers. It can also be used in a more general sense to describe taking a break or finding relaxation with others.
