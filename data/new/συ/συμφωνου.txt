The word "συμφωνου" (symphonou) is a Greek noun that means "agreement" or "harmony".

Grammar: "Συμφωνου" is a singular noun in the genitive case. In Greek, the genitive case is used to show possession or relationship between two nouns.

Description: The word "συμφωνου" is often used in Greek to describe a state of agreement or harmony between two or more parties. It can refer to a formal agreement, such as a contract or treaty, or to a more informal understanding between individuals.
