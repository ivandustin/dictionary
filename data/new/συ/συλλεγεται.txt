Word: συλλεγεται

Etymology:
The word συλλεγεται is derived from the Greek verb συλλέγω (syllego), which means "to gather" or "to collect". It is formed by combining the prefix συν- (syn-), meaning "together", and the verb λέγω (lego), meaning "to gather" or "to collect".

Grammar:
συλλεγεται is a present tense, passive voice, indicative mood verb in Greek. It is the third person singular form of the verb συλλέγω (syllego). The passive voice indicates that the subject of the sentence is being acted upon, rather than performing the action. In this case, the subject is not specified, but it is implied that something is being gathered or collected.

Description:
συλλεγεται is a verb that describes the act of gathering or collecting something. It can be used in a variety of contexts, such as collecting data, gathering information, or assembling a group of people.
