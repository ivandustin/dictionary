Word: συνκατεψηφισθη

Etymology: συν- (together) + κατα- (down) + ψηφίζω (to vote) + -θη (passive voice suffix)

Grammar: This word is a passive voice verb in the aorist tense, third person singular form. It is derived from the verb ψηφίζω (to vote) with the prefixes συν- and κατα- added to it.

Description: συνκατεψηφισθη means "was voted down together" or "was defeated by a joint vote". It is used to describe a situation where a proposal or a candidate was rejected by a group of people who voted together against it.
