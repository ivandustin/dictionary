The word "συνοδιαι" (synodai) is a plural noun in Greek that means "meetings" or "assemblies." 

Etymology: The word comes from the Greek roots "συν-" (syn-), meaning "together," and "ὁδός" (hodos), meaning "way" or "road." 

Grammar: "Συνοδιαι" is a third-declension feminine noun in Greek, and its nominative singular form is "συνοδία" (synodia). 

Description: The word "συνοδιαι" is often used in the context of religious or political gatherings, such as synods or councils. It can also refer to any kind of formal meeting or assembly.
