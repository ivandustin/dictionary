Word: συνηκολουθει

Etymology: The word συνηκολουθει comes from the Greek roots συν- (syn-), meaning "together," and ακολουθει (akolouthei), meaning "to follow." Therefore, the word means "to follow together" or "to accompany."

Grammar: συνηκολουθει is a verb in the present tense, third person singular form. It is derived from the verb ακολουθεω (akoloutheo), which means "to follow." The prefix συν- (syn-) adds the meaning of "together" or "with."

Description: συνηκολουθει is a Greek verb that means "to follow along with" or "to accompany." It can be used in a literal sense, such as following someone on a journey, or in a figurative sense, such as following someone's ideas or beliefs. The word is often used in religious contexts, such as following the teachings of a particular faith.
