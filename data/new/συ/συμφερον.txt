Word: συμφερον

Etymology:
The word συμφερον (sympheron) is derived from the Greek verb συμφέρω (symphero), which means "to bring together" or "to be advantageous". It is a combination of the prefix συν- (syn-), meaning "together", and the verb φέρω (phero), meaning "to carry" or "to bring".

Grammar:
συμφερον is a noun in the neuter gender. In its singular form, it is συμφέρον (sympheron), and in its plural form, it is συμφέροντα (sympheron-ta). It is often used in the nominative case, but can also be used in other cases depending on its function in a sentence.
