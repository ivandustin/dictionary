Word: συναναμιγνύσθαι

Etymology: The word συναναμιγνύσθαι is a compound word in Greek, consisting of the prefix συν- (meaning "together" or "with") and the verb αναμιγνύσθαι (meaning "to mix" or "to blend"). 

Grammar: συναναμιγνύσθαι is a verb in the present tense, middle or passive voice, and infinitive mood. It is a compound verb, which means it is made up of two or more words. In this case, the prefix συν- is added to the verb αναμιγνύσθαι to create the compound verb συναναμιγνύσθαι. 

Description: The word συναναμιγνύσθαι means "to mix together" or "to blend together" in Greek.
