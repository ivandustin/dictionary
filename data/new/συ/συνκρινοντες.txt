The word "συνκρινοντες" is a Greek verb that means "comparing" or "contrasting". It is derived from the prefix "συν-" meaning "together" or "with", and the verb "κρίνω" meaning "to judge" or "to decide". In terms of grammar, "συνκρινοντες" is the present participle form of the verb, indicating an ongoing action of comparing or contrasting.
