Word: συνελέξαν (synelexan)

Etymology: συν- (syn-) meaning "together" and ελέγχω (elecho) meaning "to gather, collect"

Grammar: συνελέξαν is the third person plural aorist active indicative form of the verb συνάγω (synago), which means "to gather, collect, assemble". 

Description: συνελέξαν describes a past action of a group of people coming together to gather or collect something. It can be used in a literal sense, such as gathering crops or collecting items, or in a figurative sense, such as gathering information or ideas.
