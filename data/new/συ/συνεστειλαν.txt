Word: συνεστείλαν (synesteilan)

Etymology: The word συνεστείλαν comes from the Greek roots συν- (syn-), meaning "together," and εστείλαν (esteilan), meaning "to send." 

Grammar: συνεστείλαν is a verb in the aorist tense, third person plural form. 

Description: συνεστείλαν means "they contracted" or "they reduced in size or quantity." It is often used in medical contexts to describe the shrinking of a bodily organ or tissue.
