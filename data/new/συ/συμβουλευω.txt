Word: συμβουλεύω

Etymology:
The word συμβουλεύω (sumbouleuo) comes from the Greek roots σύν (syn) meaning "together" and βουλεύω (bouleuo) meaning "to advise or deliberate". The word literally means "to advise together" or "to consult".

Grammar:
συμβουλεύω is a verb in the present tense, active voice, and indicative mood. It is conjugated as follows:

- Singular: συμβουλεύω (sumbouleuo) - I advise, I consult
- Plural: συμβουλεύομεν (sumbouleuomen) - we advise, we consult

Description:
συμβουλεύω is a versatile verb that can be used in a variety of contexts. It can mean "to advise", "to consult", "to deliberate", "to give counsel", or "to recommend". It is often used in the context of seeking advice or guidance from someone with more experience or expertise. It can also be used in a more general sense to describe the act of considering different options or opinions before making a decision.
