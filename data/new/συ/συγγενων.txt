Word: συγγενων

Etymology:
The word συγγενων comes from the Greek root words συν (syn) meaning "together" and γενος (genos) meaning "race" or "kind". Therefore, the word συγγενων means "related" or "of the same kind".

Grammar:
συγγενων is a noun in the masculine gender and the nominative case. It is the singular form of the word, and its plural form is συγγενεις (syngeneis).

Description:
συγγενων is a Greek word that refers to someone who is related to another person, either by blood or by marriage. It can also be used to describe things that are of the same kind or have a similar origin. In modern Greek, the word συγγενων is often used to refer to relatives or family members.
