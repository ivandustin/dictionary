Word: συνπορευονται

Etymology: συν- (syn-) meaning "together" and πορεύομαι (poreúomai) meaning "to travel" or "to journey". Therefore, συνπορευονται means "to journey together" or "to travel together".

Grammar: συνπορευονται is a verb in the present tense, third person plural form. It is derived from the verb πορεύομαι (poreúomai) and the prefix συν- (syn-), which indicates joint action or cooperation.

Description: συνπορευονται is a Greek verb that describes the act of traveling or journeying together. It can be used in a literal sense, such as a group of people traveling together, or in a figurative sense, such as people working together towards a common goal. The verb emphasizes the idea of joint action and cooperation, highlighting the importance of working together towards a shared objective.
