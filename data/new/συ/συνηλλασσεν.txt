Word: συνηλλασσεν

Etymology: συν- (together) + ἡλλάσσω (to exchange, barter)

Grammar: συνηλλασσεν is a verb in the past tense, third person singular form. It is derived from the verb ἡλλάσσω and the prefix συν-, which indicates a joint action.

Description: συνηλλασσεν means "he/she exchanged together" or "he/she bartered together". It implies a mutual exchange or trade between two or more parties. The word is often used in a commercial or economic context, but can also be used more broadly to describe any kind of mutual exchange or interaction.
