Word: συνεβαλλεν

Etymology:
συν- (syn-) meaning "together" or "with"
βάλλω (ballō) meaning "to throw" or "to cast"

Therefore, συνεβαλλεν (syneballen) means "to throw together" or "to contribute".

Grammar:
συνεβαλλεν is the third person singular aorist active indicative form of the Greek verb συνβάλλω (synballō).

Description:
συνεβαλλεν is a Greek verb that means "to contribute" or "to bring together". It is often used in the context of contributing to a common goal or working together towards a shared objective. The verb is formed by combining the prefix συν- (syn-) meaning "together" or "with" and the verb βάλλω (ballō) meaning "to throw" or "to cast". The third person singular aorist active indicative form of the verb is συνεβαλλεν.
