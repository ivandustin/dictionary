The word "συνκοινωνησαντες" is a Greek verb that means "to share in common" or "to participate together." It is derived from the prefix "συν-" meaning "together" and the verb "κοινωνέω" meaning "to share." The word is in the aorist tense and active voice, indicating that the action was completed in the past by the subject. In terms of grammar, it is a third-person plural verb.
