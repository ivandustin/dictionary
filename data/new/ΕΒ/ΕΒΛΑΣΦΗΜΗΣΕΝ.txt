The word "εβλασφημησεν" is a Greek verb in the aorist tense, third person singular form. It comes from the root word "βλασφημέω" (blasphēmeō), which means "to blaspheme" or "to speak irreverently about sacred things." In Greek, the verb is conjugated based on the subject and the tense of the action. The aorist tense indicates a completed action in the past.
