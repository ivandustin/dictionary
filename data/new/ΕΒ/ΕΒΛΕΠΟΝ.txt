Etymology: The word "εβλεπον" comes from the Greek verb "βλέπω" (vlepo), which means "to see". It is in the imperfect tense, indicating an ongoing or repeated action in the past.

Grammar: "Εβλεπον" is the first person singular form of the verb "βλέπω" in the imperfect tense. In Greek, verbs have different forms depending on the tense, mood, voice, and person.

Description: "Εβλεπον" is used to describe an ongoing or repeated action of seeing in the past. It can be translated as "I was seeing" or "I used to see" in English, depending on the context.
