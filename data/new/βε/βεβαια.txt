Word: βεβαια

Etymology: βεβαια comes from the Greek word βέβαιος (bébaios), which means "firm, secure, certain."

Grammar: βεβαια is the feminine form of the adjective βέβαιος (bébaios), which means "certain, sure, confident." It is used to describe a feminine noun that is certain or sure of something.

Description: βεβαια is a Greek adjective that is commonly used to express certainty or confidence. It can be used in a variety of contexts, such as in a statement of fact or in expressing one's confidence in a particular situation. For example, "Είμαι βεβαία ότι θα πετύχουμε τον στόχο μας" (I am certain that we will achieve our goal).
