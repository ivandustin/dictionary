Word: βεβλημένος

Etymology:
The word βεβλημένος (beblēmenos) is a past participle of the Greek verb βάλλω (ballō), which means "to throw" or "to cast". The prefix βε- (be-) indicates that the action is completed or has been done in the past. Therefore, βεβλημένος means "thrown" or "cast".

Grammar:
βεβλημένος is a masculine singular nominative or accusative participle in Greek. It agrees in gender, number, and case with the noun it modifies. As a participle, it can function as an adjective or a verb, depending on the context.

Description:
βεβλημένος is a word that describes something that has been thrown or cast. It can be used to describe a person or an object that has been thrown or cast in a specific direction or location. In Greek mythology, the term is often used to describe the fate of a person who has been thrown or cast out of a particular place or situation.
