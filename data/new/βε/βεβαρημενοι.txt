Word: βεβαρημένοι

Grammar: βεβαρημένοι is the plural form of the adjective βεβαρημένος (vevariménos), which means "weighed down" or "burdened". It is a masculine adjective that agrees with a masculine plural noun.

Description: βεβαρημένοι describes a group of people who are burdened or weighed down by something, either physically or emotionally.
