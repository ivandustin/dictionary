Word: φοβούμενος

Etymology: The word φοβούμενος comes from the Greek verb φοβέομαι (phobéomai), which means "to fear" or "to be afraid". The suffix -μένος (-menos) is a present participle ending, which indicates that the word describes an ongoing action or state.

Grammar: φοβούμενος is a present participle in the masculine singular form. It is derived from the verb φοβέομαι, which is in the present tense. The word can function as an adjective, describing someone who is currently experiencing fear or anxiety.

Description: φοβούμενος can be translated to English as "being afraid" or "fearing". It describes a state of fear or anxiety that someone is currently experiencing. The word can be used to describe a person who is afraid of something specific, or someone who is generally anxious or fearful.
