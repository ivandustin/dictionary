The word "φορεί" (phorei) is a Greek verb that means "to carry" or "to wear". It is derived from the Greek word "φέρω" (fero), which also means "to carry". In terms of grammar, "φορεί" is a present tense verb in the third person singular form. It can be used to describe someone carrying or wearing something, or to express the idea of carrying or bearing a burden or responsibility.
