Word: φορου

Etymology:
The word φορου (phorou) is derived from the Greek verb φέρω (fero), which means "to carry" or "to bear". The suffix -ου (-ou) is a genitive ending, indicating possession or relationship.

Grammar:
φορου (phorou) is a noun in the genitive case, which is used to indicate possession or relationship. In Greek, the genitive case is often used to show possession, origin, or source.

Description:
φορου (phorou) is a Greek noun that means "of carrying" or "of bearing". It is often used in the context of taxes or tribute, as in the phrase "φόρος φορού" (phoros phorou), which means "tax of tribute". The word can also be used more generally to refer to the act of carrying or bearing something.
