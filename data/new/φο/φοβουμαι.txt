Word: φοβούμαι

Etymology: The word φοβούμαι (phoboúmai) comes from the Greek root word φόβος (phóbos), which means fear or terror. The suffix -ούμαι (-oúmai) is a reflexive suffix that indicates that the subject is performing the action on itself. Therefore, φοβούμαι means "to fear oneself" or "to be afraid of oneself."

Grammar: φοβούμαι is a verb in the present tense, first person singular form. It is conjugated as follows:

- Εγώ φοβούμαι (Egó phoboúmai) - I fear myself

Description: φοβούμαι is a Greek verb that describes the feeling of fear or terror that one experiences towards oneself. It can be used to describe a feeling of self-doubt, insecurity, or anxiety. The word is often used in a psychological context to describe a person's fear of their own thoughts, feelings, or actions.
