Word: φοβον

Etymology: φοβον is a Greek word that comes from the root word φόβος (phobos), which means fear or terror. 

Grammar: φοβον is a noun in the singular form. It is in the accusative case, which means it is the direct object of a verb. 

Description: φοβον refers to fear or terror. It can be used to describe a feeling of dread or anxiety, or to refer to something that causes fear. In ancient Greek literature, it was often used to describe the fear of the gods or supernatural beings.
