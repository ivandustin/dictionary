Word: ηθελεν

Grammar: ηθελεν is the third person singular, past imperfect tense of θέλω (thélo). In Greek, the imperfect tense is used to describe ongoing or repeated actions in the past. The verb is in the active voice and indicative mood.

Description: ηθελεν can be translated as "he/she/it wanted" or "he/she/it was wanting" in English. It indicates a desire or wish that was ongoing or repeated in the past. The context of the sentence would determine the exact meaning and usage of the verb.
