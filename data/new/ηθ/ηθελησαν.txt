Word: ηθελησαν

Etymology: ηθελησαν is a Greek verb that comes from the root word θέλω (thélo), which means "to want" or "to wish". The prefix η (i) is added to indicate the past tense, making ηθελησαν mean "they wanted" or "they wished".

Grammar: ηθελησαν is the third person plural past tense form of the verb θέλω (thélo). It is a regular verb in Greek and follows the conjugation pattern of verbs ending in -ω. The verb is in the indicative mood, which is used to make statements or ask questions.

Description: ηθελησαν is a verb that expresses the action of wanting or wishing in the past. It can be used to describe a desire or intention that someone had in the past. For example, "Ηθελησαν να πάνε στην παραλία" (Ithelisan na pane stin paralia) means "They wanted to go to the beach".
