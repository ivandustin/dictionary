Etymology: The word "onomatos" comes from the Greek word "onoma" which means "name" and "matos" which means "making". Therefore, "onomatos" can be translated as "name-making" or "name-giving".

Grammar: "Onomatos" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the masculine gender and in the nominative case.

Description: "Onomatos" refers to the act of giving names or the study of names. It is often used in the context of etymology, which is the study of the origin and history of words.
