Etymology: The word "ονησιφορου" comes from the Greek words "ονησις" (onēsis) meaning "dream" and "φορος" (phoros) meaning "bearer" or "carrier". 

Grammar: "ονησιφορου" is a noun in the genitive case. In Greek grammar, the genitive case is used to indicate possession or origin.

Description: "ονησιφορου" refers to someone or something that carries or bears dreams. It can be used metaphorically to describe a person who brings or carries dreams, hopes, or aspirations.
