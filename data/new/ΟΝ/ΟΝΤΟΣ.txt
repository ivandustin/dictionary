Grammar: "Οντος" (ontos) is the genitive form of the Greek noun "ον" (on), which means "being" or "existence." In Greek grammar, the genitive case is used to indicate possession, origin, or relationship.

Description: "Οντος" (ontos) is a noun that refers to the state or condition of being. It can also be used to describe something that exists or is present.
