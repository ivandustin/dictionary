Grammar: "Οντι" is a noun in the nominative case, singular number, and masculine gender. It is the present participle of the verb "είμαι" (to be) and is used to describe the state of existence or being.

Description: "Οντι" refers to the act of being or existing. It is often used in philosophical and metaphysical contexts to discuss the nature of reality and existence. In Greek philosophy, the concept of "οντι" is closely related to the idea of being and essence.
