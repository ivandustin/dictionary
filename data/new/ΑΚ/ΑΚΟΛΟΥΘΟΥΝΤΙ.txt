Grammar: "ακολουθούντι" is the present participle form of the verb "ακολουθώ" (akolouthó) in the third person singular. In Greek grammar, the present participle is used to describe ongoing actions or states. It can function as an adjective or as part of a verb phrase.

Description: "ακολουθούντι" describes someone or something that is currently following or accompanying. It indicates an ongoing action or state of following.
