Etymology: The word "ακουσουσιν" comes from the Greek verb "ακούω" (akouo), which means "to hear" or "to listen". It is derived from the Proto-Indo-European root *h₂ḱous-.

Grammar: "ακουσουσιν" is the third person plural form of the verb "ακούω" (akouo) in the future tense. It is used to indicate that "they will hear" or "they will listen" in Greek.

Description: "ακουσουσιν" is a Greek word that expresses the future action of hearing or listening by a group of people. It can be used in various contexts to describe the act of perceiving sound or paying attention to someone or something.
