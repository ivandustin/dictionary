Etymology: The word "ακουοντες" (akountes) comes from the Greek verb "ακούω" (akouo), which means "to hear" or "to listen". It is derived from the Proto-Indo-European root *h₂ekʷeh₂-, meaning "to hear".

Grammar: "ακουοντες" is the present participle form of the verb "ακούω" (akouo) in Greek. It is used to describe a continuous action of hearing or listening in the present tense. In this form, it agrees with the subject in gender, number, and case.

Description: "ακουοντες" is a Greek word that means "hearing" or "listening". It is often used to refer to individuals who are actively engaged in the act of hearing or listening to something.
