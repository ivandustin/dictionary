Grammar: "ακουσιν" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the accusative case and is the plural form of the noun "ακούσιος" (akousios), which means "unwilling" or "involuntary".

Description: "ακουσιν" refers to things or actions that are done unwillingly or without intention. It can be used to describe situations where someone is forced to do something against their will or without their consent.
