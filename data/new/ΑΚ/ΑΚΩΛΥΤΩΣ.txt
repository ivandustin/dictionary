Grammar: "ακωλυτως" is an adverb in Greek. It is derived from the adjective "ακωλυτος" (akolutos), which means "unhindered" or "unobstructed". The suffix "-ως" (-os) is added to the adjective to form the adverb.

Description: "ακωλυτως" is used to describe an action or state that is done or performed without any hindrance or obstruction. It implies that there are no obstacles or barriers preventing the action from taking place.
