Etymology: The word "ακουσει" (akousei) comes from the Greek verb "ακούω" (akouo), which means "to hear" or "to listen". It is derived from the Proto-Indo-European root *h₂ḱous-.

Grammar: "ακουσει" is the third person singular form of the future tense of the verb "ακούω" (akouo). In Greek grammar, verbs are conjugated based on person, number, tense, mood, and voice. The future tense indicates an action that will happen in the future.

Description: "ακουσει" is used to express an action that will be heard or listened to in the future. It can be translated as "he/she/it will hear" or "he/she/it will listen".
