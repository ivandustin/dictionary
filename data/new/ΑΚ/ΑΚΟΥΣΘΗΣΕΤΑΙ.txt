Etymology: The word "ακουσθησεται" comes from the Greek verb "ακούω" (akouo), which means "to hear" or "to listen". It is conjugated in the future tense, passive voice, and third person singular form.

Grammar: "ακουσθησεται" is a verb in the Greek language. It belongs to the future tense, passive voice, and third person singular form. The passive voice indicates that the subject of the verb is being acted upon.
