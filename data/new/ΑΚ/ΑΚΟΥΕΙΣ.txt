Etymology: The word "ακουεις" (akoueis) comes from the Greek verb "ακούω" (akouo), which means "to hear" or "to listen". 

Grammar: "ακουεις" is the second person singular form of the present tense of the verb "ακούω" (akouo). It is used when addressing one person and means "you hear" or "you listen".

Description: "ακουεις" is a word in the Greek language that is used to express the action of hearing or listening. It is commonly used in everyday conversations and can be used in various contexts.
