Etymology: The word "ακεραίοι" comes from the Greek word "ακέραιος" (akeraios), which means "whole" or "integral".

Grammar: "Ακεραίοι" is the plural form of the noun "ακέραιος" (akeraios). It is a masculine noun in Greek.

Description: "Ακεραίοι" refers to a group of people or objects that are considered as a whole or as a complete unit. It can also be used to describe something that is undivided or intact.
