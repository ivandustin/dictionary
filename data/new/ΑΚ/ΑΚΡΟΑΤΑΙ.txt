Grammar: "ακροαται" is a noun in the plural form. It is the nominative case of the noun, which means it is used as the subject of a sentence or clause.

Description: "ακροαται" refers to a group of people who listen or hear attentively. It can be used to describe an audience, listeners, or spectators who are actively engaged in listening to something, such as a lecture, performance, or speech.
