Grammar: In Greek, "βασιλεί" is a noun in the singular form. It belongs to the second declension and is classified as a masculine noun. It is also a regular noun, meaning it follows the standard rules of Greek grammar for noun declensions.

Description: "Βασιλεί" is used to refer to a king or ruler. It can also be used metaphorically to describe someone who is powerful, influential, or dominant in a particular field or domain.
