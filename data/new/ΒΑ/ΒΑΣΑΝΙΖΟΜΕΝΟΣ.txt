The suffix "-μένος" (-menos) is a participle ending that indicates the passive voice, so "βασανιζόμενος" (basanizomenos) can be translated as "being tormented" or "being tortured".

Grammar: "Βασανιζόμενος" is the present participle form of the verb "βασανίζω" (basanizo) in the passive voice. It is used to describe someone or something that is currently undergoing torment or torture.
