Etymology: The word "βαπτισμων" (baptismōn) comes from the Greek verb "βαπτίζω" (baptizō), which means "to immerse" or "to dip." It is derived from the noun "βάπτισμα" (baptisma), meaning "a dipping" or "a washing."

Grammar: "Βαπτισμων" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the singular form and belongs to the masculine gender. In the nominative case, it is spelled as "βαπτισμων."
