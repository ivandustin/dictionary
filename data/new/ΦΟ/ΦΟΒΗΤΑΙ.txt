Etymology: The word "φοβηταί" comes from the Greek verb "φοβέω" (phobeo), which means "to fear" or "to be afraid". 

Grammar: "Φοβηταί" is the plural form of the noun "φοβητής" (phobetis), which means "one who is afraid" or "a fearful person". It is used to refer to a group of people who share the characteristic of being afraid or fearful.
