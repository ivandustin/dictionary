Etymology: The word "φορων" (pronounced "phoron") comes from the Greek language. It is derived from the verb "φορώ" (pronounced "phoro"), which means "to wear" or "to carry."

Grammar: In Greek, "φορων" is a noun in the masculine gender. It is in the nominative case, singular number.
