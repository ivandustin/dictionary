The word "πυρωσει" (pyrosei) is a verb in the Greek language. 

Etymology: The word is derived from the Greek root "πυρ" (pyr), meaning "fire". 

Grammar: It is a third person singular future active indicative form of the verb "πυρόω" (pyroo), which means "to set on fire" or "to burn". 

Description: The word "πυρωσει" means "he/she/it will set on fire" or "he/she/it will burn". It is often used in the context of arson or intentional burning.
