The word "δηπου" (dēpou) is a Greek adverb that means "somewhere" or "anywhere". 

Etymology: The word is derived from the combination of two Greek words: "δή" (dē), which is an adverb that means "indeed" or "certainly", and "πού" (pou), which is an adverb that means "where". 

Grammar: "Δηπου" is an adverb and does not have any inflections. It is used to modify verbs, adjectives, or other adverbs in a sentence. 

Description: "Δηπου" is a versatile word that can be used in a variety of contexts. It is often used to express uncertainty or vagueness about a location or destination. For example, "Πήγαινε δηπου θέλεις" (Pígaíne dēpou théleis) means "Go anywhere you want". It can also be used to express a general sense of location, as in "Δεν ξέρω δηπου είναι" (Den xéro dēpou eínai) which means "I don't know where it is".
