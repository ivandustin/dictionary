The word "δηλωσει" (dilosei) is a verb in the Greek language. Its etymology can be traced back to the ancient Greek word "δηλόω" (deloo), which means "to make clear" or "to declare". 

In terms of grammar, "δηλωσει" is in the third person singular form of the aorist tense. This tense is used to describe a completed action in the past. 

The word "δηλωσει" can be translated to English as "he/she/it declared" or "he/she/it made clear". It is often used in legal or official contexts to indicate a formal declaration or statement.
