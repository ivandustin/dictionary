The word δηλοι (deloi) is a Greek verb in the present tense, third person singular, which means "he/she/it declares" or "he/she/it makes clear". It comes from the root word δηλόω (deloo), which means "to show", "to make evident", or "to reveal". In Greek grammar, δηλοι is a regular verb that belongs to the first conjugation, and it is often used in legal or formal contexts to express a statement or a declaration.
