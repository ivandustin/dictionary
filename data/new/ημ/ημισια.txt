Word: ημισια

Etymology: The word ημισια (emisia) comes from the Greek word ημισυς (emisys), which means "half". 

Grammar: ημισια is a feminine noun in the Greek language. It is in the singular form and its plural form is ημισιες (emisies). 

Description: ημισια refers to the concept of "half" or "one-half" in Greek. It can be used to describe a quantity that is equal to half of a whole, or to describe a division of something into two equal parts. For example, if someone says "Πηρα ημισια του κεικ" (Pira emisia tou keik), it means "I took half of the cake".
