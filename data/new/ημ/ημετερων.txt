Word: ημετερων

Etymology: ημετερων is a Greek word that comes from the possessive pronoun "ημετέρα" (our) and the genitive plural ending "-ων". It literally means "of our own".

Grammar: ημετερων is a genitive plural form of the possessive pronoun "ημετέρα" (our). It is used to indicate possession or ownership of multiple things by the speaker and others.

Description: ημετερων is a word that is commonly used in Greek to express possession or ownership of multiple things by the speaker and others. It is often used in phrases such as "ημετέρων των παιδιών" (our children's) or "ημετέρων των φίλων" (our friends').
