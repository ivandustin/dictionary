Word: ημερα

Etymology: ημερα comes from the Ancient Greek word ἡμέρα (hēméra), which means "day".

Grammar: ημερα is a feminine noun in Greek. It is in the nominative case, which is used for the subject of a sentence.

Description: ημερα means "day" in Greek. It is a common word used in everyday conversation and writing. In Greek mythology, Hemera was the goddess of the day. The word ημερα is also used in various expressions and idioms, such as "καλή ημέρα" (good day) and "όλες οι ημέρες" (every day).
