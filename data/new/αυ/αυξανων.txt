Word: αυξανων

Grammar: αυξανων is the present participle of the verb αυξανω (auxanō), which means "increasing" or "growing". It is in the masculine gender, singular number, and nominative case.

Description: αυξανων is used to describe something that is currently increasing or growing. It can be used in a literal sense, such as the growth of a plant or the increase in size of an object, or in a figurative sense, such as the growth of knowledge or the increase in popularity of a trend.
