The word "αυξανομενα" is a Greek verb in the present participle form, which means "increasing" or "growing". 

Etymology: The word is derived from the Greek verb "αυξάνω" (auxano), which means "to increase" or "to grow".

Grammar: "αυξανομενα" is the present participle form of the verb "αυξάνω" (auxano), which is used to describe an ongoing action of increasing or growing. It is often used in combination with other verbs to indicate a continuous or progressive action.

Description: The word "αυξανομενα" is commonly used in Greek language to describe various things that are growing or increasing, such as plants, animals, populations, or even abstract concepts like knowledge or understanding.
