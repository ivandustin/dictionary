Word: αυξανει

Etymology: αυξανει is a Greek verb that comes from the root word αὐξάνω (auxanō), which means "to increase" or "to grow". 

Grammar: αυξανει is the present tense, third person singular form of the verb αυξάνω (auxanō). It is a regular verb in Greek and follows the conjugation pattern of verbs ending in -ω (-ō).

Description: αυξανει means "he/she/it increases" or "he/she/it grows" in Greek. It can be used in various contexts, such as describing the growth of plants, animals, or populations, or the increase of something in quantity or size.
