The word "αυτοματη" (automati) is a Greek adjective that means "automatic" or "self-operating". 

Etymology: The word is derived from the Greek words "αυτό" (auto) meaning "self" and "ματικός" (matikos) meaning "related to machines or mechanics".

Grammar: "Αυτοματη" is a feminine singular form of the adjective, used to describe a feminine singular noun.

Description: The word is commonly used in modern Greek to describe machines or devices that operate automatically, without the need for human intervention. It can also be used metaphorically to describe a person who acts automatically or without thinking.
