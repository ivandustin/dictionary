The word "αυξει" (pronounced "afsi") is a verb in the Greek language. It comes from the root word "αυξάνω" (pronounced "afsan-o"), which means "to increase" or "to grow". 

In terms of grammar, "αυξει" is in the present tense and third person singular form. It can be translated to "he/she/it increases" or "he/she/it grows". 

Overall, "αυξει" is a simple and straightforward verb that is commonly used in everyday conversation and writing in Greek.
