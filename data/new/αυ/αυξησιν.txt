Word: αυξησιν

Etymology: 
The word αυξησιν (auksisin) comes from the Greek verb αυξάνω (auksano), which means "to increase" or "to grow". The suffix -σιν (-sin) is a noun-forming suffix that indicates the action or process of the verb.

Grammar:
αυξησιν is a feminine noun in the accusative case. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their grammatical function in a sentence. The accusative case is used to indicate the direct object of a verb.

Description:
αυξησιν refers to the act of increasing or growing. It can be used in a variety of contexts, such as in agriculture to refer to the growth of crops, in economics to refer to the growth of an economy, or in biology to refer to the growth of cells or organisms.
