Word: αυξανειν

Etymology: αυξανειν is a Greek verb that comes from the root word αὐξάνω (auxánō), which means "to increase" or "to grow."

Grammar: αυξανειν is a present active infinitive verb in Greek. It is often translated as "to increase" or "to grow" in English.

Description: αυξανειν is a verb that describes the act of increasing or growing something. It can be used in a variety of contexts, such as describing the growth of plants, animals, or populations. In a figurative sense, it can also be used to describe the growth or development of ideas, knowledge, or skills.
