Etymology: The word "ρηξει" (pronounced "rixi") comes from the Greek verb "ρήσσω" (rí̱sso̱), which means "to tear" or "to rip."

Grammar: "Ρηξει" is the future tense of the verb "ρήσσω" (rí̱sso̱). It is the third person singular form, meaning "he/she/it will tear" or "he/she/it will rip."

Description: "Ρηξει" is a Greek word that describes an action that will happen in the future, specifically the action of tearing or ripping something.
