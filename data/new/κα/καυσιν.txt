The word "καυσιν" (kausin) is a Greek noun that means "burning" or "combustion". It is derived from the verb "καίω" (kaio), which means "to burn". In terms of grammar, "καυσιν" is a feminine noun in the accusative case. It can be used to describe the act of burning or combustion, as well as the result of that process, such as ashes or charred remains.
