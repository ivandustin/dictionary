Word: καγω

Grammar: καγω is a first-person singular pronoun in Greek, which means "and I" or "me too" in English. It is a combination of the conjunction και (kai) meaning "and" and the personal pronoun ἐγώ (egō) meaning "I".

Description: καγω is used to express agreement or similarity with what has been said previously. It is often used in response to a statement or question, to indicate that the speaker shares the same opinion or experience.
