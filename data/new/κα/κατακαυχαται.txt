The word "κατακαυχαται" is a Greek verb that means "to boast excessively" or "to brag arrogantly". It is derived from the prefix "κατα-" which means "down" or "against", and the verb "καυχάομαι" which means "to boast". In terms of grammar, "κατακαυχαται" is a third person singular present middle/passive indicative verb. It is often used in a negative context to describe someone who is overly proud or boastful.
