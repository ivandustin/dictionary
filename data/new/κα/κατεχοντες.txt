Word: κατεχοντες

Etymology: κατεχοντες is a Greek word that comes from the verb κατέχω (katechō), which means "to hold down" or "to possess."

Grammar: κατεχοντες is the present participle form of κατέχω (katechō), which means "holding down" or "possessing." It is in the masculine plural form.

Description: κατεχοντες is a word that describes a group of people who are currently holding down or possessing something. The context in which it is used will determine what exactly they are holding down or possessing.
