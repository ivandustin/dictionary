Word: κατεπεσεν

Etymology: κατεπεσεν is a Greek verb that comes from the root words κατά (kata) meaning "down" and πίπτω (pipto) meaning "to fall". 

Grammar: κατεπεσεν is a third-person singular aorist indicative active verb in Greek. It is in the past tense and indicates a completed action. 

Description: κατεπεσεν means "he/she/it fell down" in English. It is often used to describe a sudden or unexpected fall, such as a person falling to the ground or an object falling from a height. The verb can also be used metaphorically to describe a sudden decline or failure in a situation or event.
