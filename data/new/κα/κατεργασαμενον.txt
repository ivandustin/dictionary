Word: κατεργασαμενον

Etymology: κατεργασαμενον is a Greek word that comes from the verb κατεργάζομαι (katergazomai), which means "to work out" or "to accomplish."

Grammar: κατεργασαμενον is the past participle form of the verb κατεργάζομαι (katergazomai). It is in the neuter gender, singular number, and accusative case.

Description: κατεργασαμενον is used to describe something that has been accomplished or worked out. It can also be translated as "completed" or "finished." In Greek literature, it is often used to describe a task or project that has been successfully completed.
