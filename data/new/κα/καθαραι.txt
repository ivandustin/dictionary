The word καθαραι (katharai) is a feminine plural form of the Greek adjective καθαρός (katharos), which means "clean" or "pure". The word is derived from the Proto-Indo-European root *kathar-, which means "clean" or "pure".

In Greek, καθαραι is a feminine plural form of the adjective καθαρός. It is used to describe feminine plural nouns that are clean or pure. The word can also be used as a predicate adjective, meaning it can be used to describe the subject of a sentence.
