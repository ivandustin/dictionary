The word "καισαρειαι" (kaisareiai) is a plural form of the Greek word "καισαρεία" (kaisareia), which means "imperial" or "belonging to Caesar". It is derived from the name of Julius Caesar, the Roman emperor. 

In terms of grammar, "καισαρειαι" is a feminine noun in the nominative plural form. 

Overall, the word "καισαρειαι" refers to things that are related to or associated with the Roman imperial power.
