Word: καταλιπων

Etymology: κατα- (down) + λείπω (leave) + -ων (present participle suffix) = καταλιπών (one who is leaving behind)

Grammar: καταλιπών is the present participle form of the verb καταλείπω (leave behind) in the active voice and masculine gender.

Description: καταλιπών refers to someone who is leaving something or someone behind. It can also be used to describe someone who is abandoning or forsaking something or someone. The word is often used in a negative context, implying that the person leaving behind something or someone is doing so in a careless or irresponsible manner.
