The word "κακεινον" (kakeinon) is a Greek adjective that means "that one" or "that same one". It is derived from the pronoun "κεῖνος" (keinos), which means "that" or "that one". 

In terms of grammar, "κακεινον" is declined like a regular first and second declension adjective in Greek. It has masculine, feminine, and neuter forms, and can be used as a subject, object, or predicate adjective. 

Overall, "κακεινον" is a useful word in Greek for referring to a specific person or thing, and is commonly used in literature and everyday speech.
