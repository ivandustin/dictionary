Word: κατειλημμένος (kateilēmménos)

Etymology: κατα- (kata-, prefix meaning "down") + εἴληφα (eílēpha, "I have taken, seized")

Grammar: κατειλημμένος is a Greek adjective in the masculine gender, singular number, and nominative case.

Description: κατειλημμένος means "taken, seized, occupied, possessed" in Greek. It is often used to describe a place or territory that has been conquered or occupied by an enemy force. The word can also be used metaphorically to describe a person who is completely absorbed or preoccupied with something.
