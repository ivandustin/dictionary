The word "κατεσκαψαν" is a Greek verb in the past tense, third person plural form. It comes from the root word "σκάπτω" which means "to dig". The prefix "κατα-" adds the meaning of "down" or "completely". Therefore, "κατεσκαψαν" means "they completely dug down" or "they excavated thoroughly". 

In terms of grammar, "κατεσκαψαν" is a regular verb in the aorist tense, which is used to describe a completed action in the past. The prefix "κατα-" is a preposition that is often used to indicate direction or intensity.

Overall, "κατεσκαψαν" is a specific and descriptive verb that conveys the idea of thorough excavation or digging down completely.
