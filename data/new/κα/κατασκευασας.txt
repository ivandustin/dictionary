Word: κατασκευασας

Etymology: κατα- (down) + σκευαστής (maker), from σκευάζω (to prepare, make)

Grammar: κατασκευασας is a verb in the aorist tense, second person singular, active voice, indicative mood. It is derived from the verb κατασκευάζω, which means "to construct, build, make."

Description: κατασκευασας means "you constructed" or "you built" in English. It is used to describe an action that was completed in the past. The word is commonly used in the context of construction or building, but can also be used more broadly to describe any type of creation or production.
