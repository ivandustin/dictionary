Word: κατανοησατε

The aorist tense indicates that the action was completed in the past, while the active voice indicates that the subject of the verb (you) performed the action.

Description: κατανοησατε is a verb that means "you understood" or "you comprehended" in Greek. It is often used in the context of learning or gaining knowledge, and can be translated as "you grasped" or "you got it".
