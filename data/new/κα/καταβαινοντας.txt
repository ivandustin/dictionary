Word: καταβαινοντας

Etymology: κατα- (down) + βαίνω (to walk) + -οντας (present participle suffix) = "walking down"

Grammar: καταβαινοντας is the present participle form of the verb καταβαίνω (to go down). It is in the masculine nominative plural form.

Description: καταβαινοντας describes a group of people or things that are currently walking down or descending. It can be used as an adjective to modify a noun, or as a noun itself to refer to the group of people or things.
