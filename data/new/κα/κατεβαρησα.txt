The word "κατεβαρησα" (katevarisa) is a Greek verb in the past tense, first person singular form. It comes from the root verb "καταβαίνω" (katabaino), which means "to go down" or "descend". The prefix "κατά" (kata) adds the meaning of "down" or "below". 

In terms of grammar, "κατεβαρησα" is a regular verb in the aorist tense, which is used to describe a completed action in the past. The -ησα (-isa) ending indicates the first person singular form. 

Overall, "κατεβαρησα" means "I went down" or "I descended" in English.
