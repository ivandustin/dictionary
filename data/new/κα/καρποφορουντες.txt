Word: καρποφορούντες

Etymology: The word καρποφορούντες comes from the Greek words καρπός (karpos) meaning "fruit" and φέρω (fero) meaning "to bear". Therefore, καρποφορούντες means "bearing fruit".

Grammar: καρποφορούντες is a present participle in Greek, which means it is a verb form that describes an ongoing action. In this case, it describes the action of bearing fruit. It is in the third person plural, which means it refers to a group of people or things that are currently bearing fruit.

Description: καρποφορούντες is a word that describes the act of bearing fruit. It can be used to describe plants, trees, or even people who are producing fruit. In a figurative sense, it can also be used to describe someone who is productive or successful in their endeavors.
