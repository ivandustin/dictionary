Word: κατακαυχω

Etymology:
The word κατακαυχω comes from the Greek words κατά (kata) meaning "down" or "against" and καυχάομαι (kaucháomai) meaning "to boast" or "to brag". 

Grammar:
κατακαυχω is a verb in the Greek language. It is in the present tense, active voice, and indicative mood. 

Description:
κατακαυχω means "to boast against" or "to boast about someone else's misfortune". It is a negative term that describes someone who takes pleasure in the misfortunes of others and boasts about it. The word is often used to describe someone who is arrogant and lacks empathy.
