Word: καταφρονειτω

Etymology:
The word καταφρονειτω is a Greek verb that comes from the root words κατα- (kata-) meaning "down" or "against" and φρονέω (phroneo) meaning "to think" or "to have an opinion". Therefore, καταφρονειτω means "to think down upon" or "to despise".

Grammar:
καταφρονειτω is a third-person singular present active imperative verb in Greek. It is used to give a command or order to someone to "despise" or "look down upon" something or someone.

Description:
καταφρονειτω is a strong word that expresses a negative attitude towards someone or something. It is often used in a moral or ethical context to express disapproval or contempt for someone's behavior or actions. The word can also be used to describe a feeling of superiority or arrogance towards others.
