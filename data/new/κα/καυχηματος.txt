The word "καυχηματος" (kauchēmatos) is a Greek noun that means "boasting" or "bragging." It is derived from the verb "καυχάομαι" (kauchaomai), which means "to boast" or "to brag." In terms of grammar, "καυχηματος" is a masculine noun in the genitive case. It can be used in a variety of contexts, such as describing someone who is excessively proud or arrogant, or referring to a boastful statement or action.
