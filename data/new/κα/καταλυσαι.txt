- Present Active Imperative: κατάλυε (katalye) - "dissolve" or "break down"
- Present Active Indicative: καταλύω (katalyō) - "I dissolve" or "I break down"
- Aorist Active Indicative: κατέλυσα (katelusa) - "I dissolved" or "I broke down"

The verb is commonly used in both written and spoken Greek, and is an important part of the language's vocabulary.
