Word: καταργουμενον

The word καταργουμενον (katargoumenon) is a present participle form of the Greek verb καταργέω (katargeo), which means "to abolish" or "to make ineffective". The word is derived from the prefix κατά (kata), which means "down" or "against", and the verb ἀργέω (argeo), which means "to be idle" or "to be inactive".
