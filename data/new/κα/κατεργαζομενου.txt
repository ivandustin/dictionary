The word "κατεργαζομενου" is a present participle in Greek, derived from the verb "κατεργάζομαι" (katergázomai), which means "to work out" or "to accomplish." 

In terms of grammar, the word is in the present tense and active voice, indicating that the subject is currently performing the action of working out or accomplishing something. The participle form also implies that the action is ongoing or continuous.

Overall, "κατεργαζομενου" can be translated as "working out" or "accomplishing" in English.
