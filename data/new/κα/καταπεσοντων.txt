Word: καταπεσόντων

Etymology:
The word καταπεσόντων is a Greek word that is derived from the verb καταπίπτω (katapípto), which means "to fall down" or "to collapse." The word is formed by adding the suffix -όντων (-ónton) to the present participle stem καταπεσ- (katapes-).

Grammar:
καταπεσόντων is the genitive plural form of the present participle of the verb καταπίπτω. In Greek, the present participle is used to describe an ongoing action or state. The genitive case is used to indicate possession or relationship, and in this case, it is used to indicate that the action of falling down or collapsing is related to a plural noun.

Description:
καταπεσόντων can be translated to mean "of those who are falling down" or "of those who are collapsing."
