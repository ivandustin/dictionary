The word "κατοικησας" is a Greek verb that comes from the root word "οἰκέω" (oikeo), which means "to dwell" or "to inhabit". The prefix "κατά" (kata) means "down" or "according to", so "κατοικέω" (katoikeo) means "to settle down" or "to inhabit".
