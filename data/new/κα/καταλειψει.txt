Word: καταλειψει

Etymology: κατα- (down) + λείπω (to leave)

Grammar: καταλειψει is a verb in the future tense, third person singular form. It is derived from the verb καταλείπω (to leave behind).

Description: καταλειψει means "he/she/it will leave behind" or "he/she/it will abandon" in Greek. It is often used in a negative context, indicating that someone or something will be left behind or abandoned.
