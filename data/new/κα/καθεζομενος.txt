Word: καθεζόμενος

Etymology: καθεζόμενος is a present participle of the Greek verb καθέζομαι (kathézomai), which means "to sit down". The word is derived from the prefix κατά (katá), meaning "down", and the verb έζω (ézo), meaning "to sit".

Grammar: καθεζόμενος is a masculine singular nominative present participle in the Greek language. It is used to describe someone who is currently sitting down.

Description: καθεζόμενος is a word that describes the action of sitting down. It can be used to describe someone who is sitting in a chair, on a bench, or on the ground. The word can also be used figuratively to describe someone who is inactive or not taking action.
