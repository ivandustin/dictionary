Word: καταπινοντες

Etymology: καταπινοντες is a present participle form of the Greek verb καταπίνω (katapínō), which means "to swallow down" or "to gulp down". The word is derived from the prefix κατά (katá), meaning "down", and the verb πίνω (pínō), meaning "to drink".

Grammar: καταπινοντες is a present participle form of the verb καταπίνω. In Greek, present participles are formed by adding the suffix -ντας (-ntas) to the stem of the verb. Present participles are used to describe ongoing actions or states, and can function as adjectives or adverbs.
