Word: καταποντιζεσθαι

Etymology: The word καταποντιζεσθαι comes from the Greek words κατά (kata) meaning "down" or "against" and ποντίζεσθαι (pontízesthai) meaning "to sink" or "to drown". 

Grammar: καταποντιζεσθαι is a verb in the present passive infinitive form. It is derived from the verb καταποντίζω (katapontízo) which means "to sink" or "to drown". 

Description: καταποντιζεσθαι refers to the act of being sunk or drowned. It can be used in a literal sense, such as a ship being sunk in the sea, or in a figurative sense, such as a person being overwhelmed by emotions or circumstances.
