Word: καρποφορησωμεν

Etymology: The word καρποφορησωμεν is derived from the Greek words καρπός (karpos) meaning "fruit" and φορέω (phoreo) meaning "to bear or carry". 

Grammar: καρποφορησωμεν is a verb in the first person plural, aorist tense, subjunctive mood, and active voice. 

Description: καρποφορησωμεν means "let us bear fruit" or "let us produce fruit". It is a call to action, urging the listener or reader to engage in fruitful activity. The word is often used in a spiritual or metaphorical sense, encouraging individuals to live a productive and meaningful life.
