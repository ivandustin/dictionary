The word καθαροι (katharoi) is derived from the Greek word καθαρός (katharos), which means "clean" or "pure". It is believed to have originated from the Proto-Indo-European root *kathar-, which means "pure" or "clear".

καθαροι is the plural form of καθαρός (katharos), which is an adjective in Greek. It is in the masculine gender and nominative case. The word can be used to describe a group of people or things that are clean or pure.
