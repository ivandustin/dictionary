Word: καρποφορει

Etymology: The word καρποφορει comes from the Greek words καρπός (karpos) meaning "fruit" and φέρω (phero) meaning "to bear" or "to carry". Therefore, καρποφορει means "to bear fruit".

Grammar: καρποφορει is a verb in the present tense, third person singular form. It is derived from the verb καρποφορέω (karpoforeo), which means "to bear fruit" or "to yield fruit". The verb is conjugated as follows:

- Present tense: καρποφορεί (karpoforei)
- Imperfect tense: καρποφορούσε (karpoforouse)
- Future tense: θα καρποφορεί (tha karpoforei)

Description: καρποφορει is a Greek verb that means "to bear fruit". It is often used in a figurative sense to describe the production of positive results or outcomes. For example, one might say "Η σκληρή δουλειά και η αφοσίωση καρποφορούν μεγάλα αποτελέσματα" (Hard work and dedication bear great results).
