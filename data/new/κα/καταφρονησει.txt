Word: καταφρονησει

Etymology:
The word καταφρονησει (kataphronēsei) is a Greek verb that comes from the root words κατά (kata) meaning "down" or "against" and φρονέω (phroneō) meaning "to think" or "to have an opinion". The verb καταφρονησει means "to despise" or "to look down upon".

Grammar:
καταφρονησει is a third-person singular future active indicative verb in Greek. It is conjugated from the root verb καταφρονέω (kataphroneō) which means "to despise" or "to look down upon". The verb is in the future tense, indicating that the action of despising or looking down upon someone or something will happen at a later time.

Description:
καταφρονησει is a strong word that conveys a sense of contempt or disdain towards someone or something. It implies a deliberate act of looking down upon or disregarding someone or something as unworthy or inferior. The word is often used in a negative context to describe someone's attitude or behavior towards others.
