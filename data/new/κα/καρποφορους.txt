Word: καρποφορους

Etymology: The word καρποφορους comes from the Greek words καρπός (karpos) meaning "fruit" and φορέω (phoreo) meaning "to bear". Therefore, καρποφορους means "fruit-bearing".

Grammar: καρποφορους is an adjective in the masculine plural form. It is used to describe something or someone that bears fruit.

Description: καρποφορους is often used in the context of agriculture or horticulture to describe plants or trees that produce fruit. It can also be used metaphorically to describe people or things that are productive or fruitful.
