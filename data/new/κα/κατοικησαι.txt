The word κατοικησαι comes from the Greek verb κατοικέω (katoikeo), which means "to dwell" or "to inhabit". It is derived from the prefix κατά (kata), meaning "down" or "according to", and the verb οἰκέω (oikeo), meaning "to dwell" or "to live in".
