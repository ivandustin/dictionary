Word: κατακαυσει

Etymology: κατα- (kata-, prefix meaning "down" or "against") + καυσει (kausei, verb meaning "to burn")

Grammar: κατακαυσει is a verb in the future tense, third person singular form. It is derived from the verb καίω (kaio, "to burn") with the prefix κατα- added to indicate a downward or complete burning.

Description: κατακαυσει means "he/she/it will burn down completely" or "he/she/it will destroy by fire". It is a strong and forceful verb that conveys a sense of complete destruction. It can be used literally to describe the burning down of a physical structure, or figuratively to describe the complete destruction of something abstract, such as a reputation or a relationship.
