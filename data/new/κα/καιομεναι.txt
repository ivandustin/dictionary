The word "καιομεναι" is a Greek verb in the present passive participle form. It comes from the root verb "καίω" which means "to burn". The present passive participle form indicates that the action of burning is being done to the subject, rather than the subject doing the burning. 

In terms of grammar, "καιομεναι" is in the feminine nominative singular form, which means it is referring to a singular feminine subject. 

Overall, the word "καιομεναι" can be translated to mean "being burned" or "being consumed by fire".
