The word "καθαριει" (kathariei) is a verb in the Greek language. It is derived from the root word "καθαρός" (katharos), which means "clean" or "pure". 

In terms of grammar, "καθαριει" is a present tense verb in the third person singular form. It is often translated to English as "cleanses" or "purifies". 

In terms of description, "καθαριει" is often used in a spiritual or religious context, referring to the act of purifying or cleansing oneself or one's surroundings. It can also be used in a more literal sense, such as cleaning or purifying a physical object or space.
