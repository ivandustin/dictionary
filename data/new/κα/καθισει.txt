The word "καθισει" is a Greek verb in the future tense, which means "he/she/it will sit". 

Etymology: The word is derived from the Greek root "καθίζω" (kathizo), which means "to sit down". 

Grammar: "καθισει" is a third person singular future tense verb in the active voice. It is formed by adding the future tense suffix "-σει" to the stem "καθισ-". 

Description: The word "καθισει" is a simple verb that describes the action of sitting down in the future. It can be used in various contexts, such as in a sentence like "Θα καθισει στην καρέκλα" (He/she will sit on the chair).
