Etymology: The word is derived from the Greek roots "κατά" (meaning "down" or "thoroughly") and "μανθάνω" (meaning "to learn").

Grammar: "καταμαθετε" is a second person plural verb in the imperative mood, which is used to give commands or make requests. It is formed by adding the suffix "-ετε" to the stem "καταμαθ-".
