Word: καταβαρυνομενοι

Etymology: κατα- (down) + βαρύνω (to burden) + -μένοι (suffix indicating passive voice and present participle)

Description: καταβαρυνομενοι means "being burdened" or "being weighed down" in Greek. It is often used to describe a feeling of heaviness or oppression.
