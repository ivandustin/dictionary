The word καταθεμα (katathema) is derived from the Greek verb κατατιθημι (katatithemi), which means "to place down" or "to deposit". The prefix κατα- (kata-) means "down" or "against", while the verb τιθημι (tithemi) means "to place" or "to put".

In Greek, καταθεμα is a neuter noun of the first declension. Its nominative singular form is καταθεμα, and its genitive singular form is καταθεματος (katathematos). The word can also be declined in the plural.
