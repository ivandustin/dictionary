Description: καρδιογνωστης is a term used in the medical field to refer to a cardiologist, a doctor who specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of heart diseases. A καρδιογνωστης is trained to perform various tests and procedures to evaluate the function of the heart, such as electrocardiograms (ECGs), echocardiograms, and stress tests.
