Word: καταγγέλλεται

Etymology: The word καταγγέλλεται comes from the Greek verb καταγγέλλω (katangello), which means "to accuse" or "to denounce". It is derived from the prefix κατά (kata), which means "down" or "against", and the verb ἀγγέλλω (angello), which means "to announce" or "to report".

Grammar: καταγγέλλεται is a present passive indicative verb in Greek. It is in the third person singular form, which means that it refers to a single subject who is being accused or denounced by someone else. The passive voice indicates that the subject is receiving the action of the verb, rather than performing it.
