Word: καταστρηνιασωσιν

Grammar: καταστρηνιασωσιν is a third person plural present subjunctive verb in Greek. It is used to express a hypothetical action or state that is not yet realized. In this case, the verb καταστρηνιασωσιν means "they may tire out" or "they might exhaust."

Description: καταστρηνιασωσιν is a verb that describes the possibility of someone or something becoming exhausted or tired out.
