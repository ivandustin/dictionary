Word: κατωικησεν

Etymology: The word κατωικησεν is derived from the Greek words κατά (kata) meaning "down" and οἰκέω (oikeo) meaning "to dwell" or "to inhabit". 

Grammar: κατωικησεν is a verb in the aorist tense, third person singular form. It is derived from the verb κατοικέω (katoikeo) which means "to dwell" or "to inhabit". 

Description: κατωικησεν means "he/she/it dwelt" or "he/she/it inhabited" in English. It is often used in the context of describing where someone lived or resided.
