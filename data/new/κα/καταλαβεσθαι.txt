The word καταλαβεσθαι (katalabesthai) is a Greek verb that means "to understand" or "to comprehend". It is derived from the prefix κατά (kata), which means "down" or "against", and the verb λαμβάνω (lambanō), which means "to take" or "to receive". 

In terms of grammar, καταλαβεσθαι is a passive aorist infinitive, which means that it describes an action that was completed in the past and was done to the subject of the sentence rather than by the subject. 

Overall, καταλαβεσθαι is a versatile and commonly used word in Greek, often used in both everyday conversation and more formal contexts.
