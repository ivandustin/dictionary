Word: καταβαινει

Etymology: καταβαινει comes from the Greek verb καταβαίνω (katabaino), which is a compound of the preposition κατά (kata) meaning "down" and the verb βαίνω (baino) meaning "to go". Therefore, καταβαίνω means "to go down" or "to descend".

It can be used in a variety of contexts, such as describing someone physically descending a staircase or a mountain, or metaphorically descending from a higher position of power or authority.
