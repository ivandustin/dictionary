Etymology: The word "θεμελιοι" comes from the Greek word "θεμελιος" (themelios), which means "foundation" or "base". 

Grammar: "θεμελιοι" is the plural form of the noun "θεμελιος" (themelios). It belongs to the second declension in Greek grammar. 

Description: "θεμελιοι" refers to foundations or bases. It can be used to describe the physical structures that support buildings or metaphorically to represent the fundamental principles or concepts on which something is built.
