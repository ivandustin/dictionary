Etymology: The word "θεληματα" (thelēmata) comes from the Greek word "θέλημα" (thelēma), which means "will" or "desire". 

Grammar: "Θεληματα" is the plural form of the noun "θέλημα" (thelēma). In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their number (singular or plural). 

Description: "Θεληματα" refers to multiple "wills" or "desires". It can be used to describe a collection of wishes, preferences, or intentions.
