Etymology: The word "θεμενος" (themenos) comes from the Greek verb "τιθημι" (tithemi), which means "to place" or "to set". 

Grammar: "Θεμενος" is a masculine noun in the nominative case. It belongs to the second declension in Greek grammar. 

Description: "Θεμενος" refers to something that has been placed or set. It can also be used metaphorically to describe a person who has been appointed or designated for a specific role or position.
