Etymology: The word "θεληση" (thelēsē) comes from the Greek verb "θέλω" (thelō), which means "to will" or "to desire."

Grammar: "Θεληση" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the singular form and belongs to the feminine gender.

Description: "Θεληση" refers to the act of willing or desiring something. It can also be translated as "will" or "wish." In Greek philosophy, "θεληση" is often associated with the power of choice and the ability to make decisions.
