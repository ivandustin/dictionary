Etymology: The word "θελησω" (thelēsō) comes from the Greek verb "θέλω" (thélō), which means "to want" or "to wish."

Grammar: θελησω is the first person singular future tense form of the verb θέλω (thélō). It is used to express a future action or state of wanting or wishing.

Description: θελησω is a Greek word that conveys the idea of wanting or wishing for something in the future. It is commonly used in everyday speech and writing to express desires or intentions.
