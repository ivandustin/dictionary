Etymology: The word "θερμης" (thermēs) comes from the Greek word θερμός (thermós), meaning "hot" or "warm".

Grammar: θερμης (thermēs) is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the masculine gender and in the nominative case.

Description: θερμης (thermēs) refers to something that is warm or hot. It can be used to describe the temperature of an object or the feeling of warmth.
