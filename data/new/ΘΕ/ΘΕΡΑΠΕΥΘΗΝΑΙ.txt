Grammar: "θεραπευθηναι" is the aorist passive infinitive form of the verb "θεραπεύω" (therapeúo). In Greek grammar, the aorist tense refers to a past action that is viewed as a whole, without specifying the duration or completion of the action. The passive voice indicates that the subject of the verb is being acted upon.
