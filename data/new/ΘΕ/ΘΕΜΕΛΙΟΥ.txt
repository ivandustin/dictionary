Etymology: The word "θεμελίου" (themiou) comes from the Greek word "θεμέλιο" (themelio), which means "foundation" or "base". 

Grammar: "Θεμελίου" is a noun in the genitive case, singular form. In Greek grammar, the genitive case is used to indicate possession, origin, or relationship.

Description: "Θεμελίου" refers to something that is related to or belongs to a foundation or base. It can be used to describe various things, such as the foundation of a building, the base of an argument, or the basis of a theory.
