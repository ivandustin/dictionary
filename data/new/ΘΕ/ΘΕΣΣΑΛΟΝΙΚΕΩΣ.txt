Grammar: "Θεσσαλονικεως" is the masculine singular form of the adjective. In Greek grammar, adjectives agree in gender, number, and case with the noun they modify. So, if the noun is feminine, plural, or in a different case, the form of the adjective will change accordingly.

Description: "Θεσσαλονικεως" is used to describe something or someone that is related to or originates from Thessaloniki. It can be used to refer to the people, culture, language, or anything else associated with the city.
