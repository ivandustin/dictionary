Etymology: The word "θεληματι" (thelēmati) comes from the Greek word "θέλημα" (thélēma), which means "will" or "desire".

Grammar: "Θεληματι" is a noun in the dative case. In Greek grammar, the dative case is used to indicate the indirect object of a verb or the object of certain prepositions.
