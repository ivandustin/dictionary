Grammar: "Θερισμέν" is the past participle form of the verb "θερίζω" (therízo). In Greek grammar, the past participle is used to form the perfect tense and passive voice. It agrees in gender, number, and case with the noun it modifies.

Description: "Θερισμέν" is an adjective that means "harvested" or "reaped" in Greek. It is used to describe something that has been gathered or collected during the harvest season.
