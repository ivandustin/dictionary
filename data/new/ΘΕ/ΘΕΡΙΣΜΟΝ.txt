Etymology: The word "θερισμόν" (therismón) comes from the Greek verb "θερίζω" (therízo), which means "to harvest" or "to reap". 

Grammar: "Θερισμόν" is a noun in the singular form. It belongs to the second declension in Greek grammar. 

Description: "Θερισμόν" refers to the act of harvesting or reaping crops. It can also be used metaphorically to describe the gathering or collection of something valuable or beneficial.
