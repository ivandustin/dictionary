Etymology: The word "θεωρώσιν" comes from the Greek verb "θεωρέω" (theoreo), which means "to observe" or "to contemplate." It is derived from the noun "θεωρία" (theoria), meaning "a viewing" or "a spectacle."

Grammar: "Θεωρώσιν" is the third person plural form of the verb "θεωρέω" in the present tense. It is used to describe an action or state of observation or contemplation performed by a group of people.
