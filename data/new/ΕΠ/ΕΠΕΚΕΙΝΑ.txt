Etymology: The word "επεκείνα" comes from the Greek language. It is derived from the combination of the preposition "επί" (epi), meaning "on" or "upon," and the adverb "κείνα" (keina), meaning "those" or "those things." 

Grammar: "Επεκείνα" is a neuter plural adjective in Greek. It is used to refer to things that are located or situated beyond or after something else. It is often used in the phrase "πέρα από εκείνα" (pera apo ekeina), which means "beyond those things" or "after those things."

Description: "Επεκείνα" is a versatile word that can be used in various contexts. It is commonly used to indicate something that is beyond or surpasses a certain point or limit. It can also be used metaphorically to refer to something that is abstract or intangible. Overall, "επεκείνα" is a word that adds depth and emphasis to the description or location of something.
