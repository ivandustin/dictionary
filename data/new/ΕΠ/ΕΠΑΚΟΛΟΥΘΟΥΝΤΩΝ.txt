Etymology: The word "επακολουθούντων" comes from the Greek language. It is a combination of the prefix "επα-" meaning "following" or "after" and the verb "ακολουθώ" meaning "to follow".

Grammar: "Επακολουθούντων" is the present participle form of the verb "ακολουθώ" in the third person plural. It is used to describe a continuous action of following or accompanying.

Description: "Επακολουθούντων" is a Greek word that can be translated as "of those who follow" or "of those who are following". It is often used in contexts where there is a group of people or things that are following or accompanying someone or something else.
