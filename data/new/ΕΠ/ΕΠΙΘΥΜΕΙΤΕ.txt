Etymology: The word "επιθυμείτε" comes from the Greek verb "επιθυμώ" (epithymó), which means "to desire" or "to wish".

Grammar: "Επιθυμείτε" is the second person plural form of the verb "επιθυμώ" in the present tense. It is used when addressing a group of people and means "you desire" or "you wish".

Description: "Επιθυμείτε" is a verb in the Greek language that expresses the action of desiring or wishing. It is commonly used in polite or formal contexts when addressing a group of people.
