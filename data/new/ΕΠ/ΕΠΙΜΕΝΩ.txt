Etymology: The word "επιμένω" (epiméno) comes from the Greek roots "επί" (epí), meaning "on" or "upon," and "μένω" (méno), meaning "to remain" or "to stay." 

Grammar: "Επιμένω" is a verb in the Greek language. It belongs to the first conjugation class and is conjugated in the present tense as follows: επιμένω (epiméno) - I remain, επιμένεις (epiméneis) - you remain, επιμένει (epiménei) - he/she/it remains, επιμένουμε (epiménoume) - we remain, επιμένετε (epiménete) - you remain, επιμένουν (epiménoun) - they remain.

Description: "Επιμένω" is a versatile verb that can be used to express various meanings related to persistence, perseverance, or insistence. It can refer to staying or remaining in a particular state, continuing to do something despite difficulties or opposition, or insisting on a particular opinion or course of action.
