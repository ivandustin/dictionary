Etymology: The word "επιδόντες" comes from the Greek word "επί" (epi), meaning "on" or "upon," and the verb "δίδωμι" (didomi), meaning "to give." Therefore, "επιδόντες" can be translated as "those who give upon" or "those who bestow."

Grammar: "Επιδόντες" is the plural form of the noun "επιδότης" (epidotis) in Greek. It is used to refer to multiple individuals who give or bestow something.

Description: "Επιδόντες" is a term that can be used to describe people who provide financial or other forms of support, grants, or subsidies. It is often used in the context of government programs, scholarships, or funding organizations.
