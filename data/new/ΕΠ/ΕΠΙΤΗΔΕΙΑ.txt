Etymology: The word "επιτηδεια" comes from the Greek roots "επί" (epi), meaning "upon" or "over", and "τηδεία" (tēdeia), meaning "skill" or "art". It can be translated as "acquired skill" or "expertise".

Grammar: "Επιτηδεια" is a feminine noun in the Greek language. It belongs to the second declension and has the plural form "επιτηδείες" (epitēdeies).

Description: "Επιτηδεια" refers to a particular skill or expertise that is acquired through practice, training, or experience. It implies a level of mastery or proficiency in a specific field or activity. It can be used to describe someone's expertise in a particular craft, art, or profession.
