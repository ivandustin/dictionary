Etymology: The word "επιγραψω" (epigrapso) comes from the Greek roots "επι-" (epi-), meaning "on" or "upon," and "γραψω" (grapso), meaning "to write." 

Grammar: "Επιγραψω" is a verb in the Greek language. It is in the future tense and active voice. 

Description: "Επιγραψω" means "I will write" in English. It is used to express the action of writing that will happen in the future.
