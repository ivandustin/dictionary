Etymology: The word "επαινον" (epainon) comes from the Greek verb "επαινεω" (epaineo), which means "to praise" or "to commend". 

Grammar: "Επαινον" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the singular form and belongs to the neuter gender. 

Description: "Επαινον" refers to praise or commendation. It is used to express admiration, approval, or positive feedback towards someone or something.
