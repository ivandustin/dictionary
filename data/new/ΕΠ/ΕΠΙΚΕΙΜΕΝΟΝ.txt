Grammar: "Επικείμενον" is a neuter singular adjective in the nominative case. In Greek grammar, adjectives agree in gender, number, and case with the noun they modify. In this case, "επικείμενον" is used to describe a neuter singular noun.

Description: The word "επικείμενον" is used to refer to something that is about to happen or is imminent. It can be used to describe future events or situations that are expected to occur soon.
