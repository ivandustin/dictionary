Etymology: The word "επιχεων" comes from the Greek language. It is derived from the combination of the prefix "επι-" meaning "on" or "upon" and the verb "χεω" meaning "to pour" or "to flow". 

Grammar: "Επιχεων" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the masculine gender and singular number. 

Description: "Επιχεων" refers to a person who pours or flows upon something. It can be used metaphorically to describe someone who has a strong influence or impact on others.
