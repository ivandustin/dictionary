The word "επλανησεν" is a Greek verb in the aorist tense, third person singular form. It comes from the root word "πλανάω" (planáo), which means "to wander" or "to roam." In this form, "επλανησεν" translates to "he/she/it wandered" or "he/she/it roamed." It is commonly used in Greek literature and can be found in various contexts.
