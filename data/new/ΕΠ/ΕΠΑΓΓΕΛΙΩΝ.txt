Etymology: The word "επαγγελίων" comes from the Greek word "επαγγέλλω" (epangéllo), which means "to promise" or "to announce."

Grammar: "Επαγγελίων" is a noun in the plural form. It belongs to the second declension in Greek grammar.

Description: "Επαγγελίων" refers to promises or announcements. It can also be used to describe a profession or occupation.
