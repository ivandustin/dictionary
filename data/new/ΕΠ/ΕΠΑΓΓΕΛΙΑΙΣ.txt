Etymology: The word "επαγγελίας" (epangelías) comes from the Greek verb "επαγγέλλομαι" (epangéllomai), which means "to promise" or "to announce". 

Grammar: "Επαγγελίας" is a noun in the genitive case, singular form. In Greek grammar, the genitive case is used to indicate possession, origin, or relationship.

Description: "Επαγγελίας" refers to a promise or a commitment made by someone. It can also mean an announcement or a declaration of intent.
