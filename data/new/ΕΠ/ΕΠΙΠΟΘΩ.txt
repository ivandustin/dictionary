Etymology: The word "επιποθώ" (epipotho) comes from the Greek language. It is derived from the combination of the prefix "επι-" (epi-), meaning "on" or "upon," and the verb "ποθώ" (potho), meaning "to desire" or "to long for."

Grammar: "Επιποθώ" is a verb in the Greek language. It belongs to the first person singular form of the present tense. It expresses the action of desiring or longing for something.
