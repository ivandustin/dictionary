Etymology: The word "επισχυον" (epischyon) comes from the Greek verb "επισχύω" (epischyo), which means "to strengthen" or "to reinforce". 

Grammar: "Επισχυον" is a noun in the singular form. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their gender, number, and case. 

Description: "Επισχυον" refers to something that strengthens or reinforces. It can be used to describe a person, object, or concept that provides support or enhances something.
