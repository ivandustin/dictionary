Grammar: "Επιγνωις" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the singular form and belongs to the feminine gender.

Description: "Επιγνωις" refers to a deep or thorough understanding or recognition of something. It implies a level of knowledge that goes beyond mere awareness or familiarity. It can also be used to describe a recognition or acknowledgement of someone's abilities or qualities.
