Etymology: The word "επιγνωσεως" (epignoseos) comes from the Greek root words "επι" (epi), meaning "upon" or "over," and "γνωσις" (gnosis), meaning "knowledge" or "understanding." 

Grammar: "Επιγνωσεως" is a noun in the genitive case, singular form. In Greek grammar, the genitive case is used to indicate possession, origin, or relationship.

Description: "Επιγνωσεως" refers to the act or state of knowing or understanding something fully or deeply. It can also imply a recognition or acknowledgment of something. The word is often used in philosophical or theological contexts to describe a higher level of knowledge or spiritual understanding.
