Etymology: The word "επηρεαζοντες" comes from the Greek verb "επηρεαζω" (epireazo), which means "to influence" or "to affect". The suffix "-οντες" is added to the verb stem to form the present participle, indicating that the action is ongoing or continuous.

Grammar: "Επηρεαζοντες" is the present participle form of the verb "επηρεαζω" in Greek. It is used to describe a group of people or things that are currently influencing or affecting something. In English, it can be translated as "influencing" or "affecting".
