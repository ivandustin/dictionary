Etymology: The word "επαίδευον" (epaídeuon) comes from the Greek verb "παιδεύω" (paideúo), which means "to educate" or "to teach."

Grammar: "Επαίδευον" is the past tense form of the verb "παιδεύω" (paideúo) in the first person singular. It is used to indicate that the action of educating or teaching occurred in the past.

Description: "Επαίδευον" is a Greek word that describes the act of educating or teaching. It can refer to the process of imparting knowledge, skills, or values to someone.
