The word "επιπεπτωκος" (epipeptokos) is a Greek adjective that means "fallen upon" or "incidental." Here is the breakdown of the requested information:

Etymology: The word "επιπεπτωκος" is derived from the Greek verb "επιπίπτω" (epipípto), which means "to fall upon" or "to happen." The suffix "-κος" (-kos) is added to the verb stem to form the adjective.

Grammar: "Επιπεπτωκος" is a masculine singular adjective in the nominative case. In Greek grammar, adjectives agree in gender, number, and case with the noun they modify.

Description: The adjective "επιπεπτωκος" is used to describe something that has happened or occurred incidentally or by chance. It implies that the event or circumstance was not planned or expected, but rather came about unexpectedly or as a result of external factors.
