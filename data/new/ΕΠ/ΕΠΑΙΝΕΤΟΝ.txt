Etymology: The word "επαινετον" comes from the Greek word "επαινέω" (epainéo), which means "to praise" or "to commend".

Grammar: "Επαινετον" is a neuter noun in the singular form. In Greek grammar, neuter nouns typically end in "-ον" in the nominative case.

Description: "Επαινετον" refers to something that is praiseworthy or commendable. It is used to describe things or actions that deserve admiration or approval.
