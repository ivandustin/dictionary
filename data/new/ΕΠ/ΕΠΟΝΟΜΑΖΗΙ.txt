Etymology: The word "επονομαζη" (epanomazi) comes from the Greek roots "επί" (epi), meaning "upon" or "on," and "ονομάζω" (onomazo), meaning "to name." 

Grammar: "Επονομαζη" is a verb in the Greek language. It is conjugated in various forms to indicate different tenses, moods, and persons. 

Description: "Επονομαζη" means "to name" or "to call" in Greek. It is used when assigning a name to someone or something.
