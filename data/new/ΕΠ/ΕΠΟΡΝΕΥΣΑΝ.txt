Etymology: The word "επορνευσαν" comes from the Greek verb "πορνεύω" (porneúo), which means "to prostitute oneself" or "to engage in sexual immorality." The prefix "επι-" (epi-) in this word adds the meaning of "upon" or "over," intensifying the action of prostitution.

Grammar: "Επορνεύσαν" is the third person plural aorist active indicative form of the verb "πορνεύω" (porneúo) in Greek. The aorist tense indicates a completed action in the past, and the active voice shows that the subject of the sentence performs the action.

Description: "Επορνεύσαν" describes the action of engaging in sexual immorality or prostitution. It refers to a group of people who have committed this act in the past.
