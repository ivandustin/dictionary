Etymology: The word "επιστασιν" (epistasin) comes from the Greek word "επισταμαι" (epistamai), which means "to know" or "to understand."

Grammar: "Επιστασιν" is a noun in the accusative case, singular number, and masculine gender in Greek grammar.

Description: "Επιστασιν" refers to knowledge or understanding. It can also be translated as "wisdom" or "intelligence."
