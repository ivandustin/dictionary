Etymology: The word "επετραπη" (epetraphi) comes from the Greek verb "επιτρέπω" (epitrepó), which means "to allow" or "to permit."

Grammar: "Επετραπη" is the third person singular, aorist tense of the verb "επιτρέπω" (epitrepó) in Greek. It is used to indicate a past action or event that has been completed.
