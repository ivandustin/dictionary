Etymology: The word "επιστασις" comes from the Greek language. It is derived from the verb "επισταμαι" (epistamai), which means "to know" or "to understand."

Grammar: "Επιστασις" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the singular form and belongs to the feminine gender.

Description: "Επιστασις" refers to knowledge, understanding, or expertise in a particular field or subject. It can also mean skill, proficiency, or mastery. In ancient Greek philosophy, it was used to describe the highest form of knowledge or wisdom.
