Etymology: The word "επηρωτα" (epirota) comes from the Greek verb "επερωτώ" (eperoto), which means "to ask" or "to inquire".

Grammar: "Επηρωτα" is the second person singular form of the present tense of the verb "επερωτώ" (eperoto). It is used when addressing one person and asking a question.

Description: "Επηρωτα" is a Greek word that means "ask" or "inquire". It is commonly used in everyday conversations to request information or seek clarification.
