Etymology: The word "επαγαγειν" (epagagein) comes from the Greek roots "επι" (epi), meaning "upon" or "on," and "αγαγειν" (agagein), meaning "to lead" or "to bring." 

Grammar: "Επαγαγειν" is a verb in the Greek language. It belongs to the aorist tense and active voice. 

Description: The word "επαγαγειν" means "to introduce" or "to bring in." It is often used to describe the action of bringing something or someone into a particular situation or context.
