Etymology: The word "επισυναγωγήν" comes from the Greek roots "επί" (epi), meaning "on" or "upon," and "συναγωγή" (synagogi), meaning "gathering" or "assembly." 

Grammar: "Επισυναγωγήν" is a noun in the accusative case, singular form. It is derived from the verb "επισυνάγω" (episynago), which means "to gather" or "to assemble."
