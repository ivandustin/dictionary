Etymology: The word "επιτρέπω" (epitrepó) comes from the Greek roots "επί" (epí), meaning "on" or "upon," and "τρέπω" (trépo), meaning "to turn" or "to direct." 

Grammar: "Επιτρέπω" is a verb in the Greek language. It belongs to the first conjugation class and is conjugated in the present tense as follows: επιτρέπω, επιτρέπεις, επιτρέπει, επιτρέπουμε, επιτρέπετε, επιτρέπουν.

Description: "Επιτρέπω" means "to allow" or "to permit" in English. It is used to indicate giving permission or granting someone the right to do something.
