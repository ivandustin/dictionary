Etymology: The word "επαναμιμνήσκων" comes from the Greek roots "επανα-" meaning "again" and "μιμνήσκω" meaning "to remember". 

Grammar: "επαναμιμνήσκων" is a Greek adjective in the masculine gender, singular form. It is in the present participle form, indicating an ongoing action of remembering.

Description: "επαναμιμνήσκων" describes someone or something that is currently remembering or recalling something. It implies an active process of recollection or reminiscence.
