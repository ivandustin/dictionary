Grammar: "Επιβιβασας" is the second person singular form of the verb "επιβιβάζω" in the past tense. In Greek grammar, verbs change their form based on the person, tense, and mood. The "-ας" ending indicates the second person singular in the past tense.

Description: The word "επιβιβασας" describes the action of someone embarking or boarding something. It implies that the person being referred to got on a vehicle, a ship, or any other means of transportation.
