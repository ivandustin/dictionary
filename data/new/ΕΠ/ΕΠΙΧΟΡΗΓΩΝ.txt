Etymology: The word "επιχορηγών" comes from the Greek roots "επί" (epi), meaning "on" or "upon," and "χορηγών" (chorēgōn), meaning "sponsor" or "provider." 

Grammar: "Επιχορηγών" is a noun in the singular form. It belongs to the third declension in Greek grammar.

Description: "Επιχορηγών" refers to a person or entity that provides financial or material support for a specific purpose or project. It can be translated as "sponsor" or "benefactor" in English.
