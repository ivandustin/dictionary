Grammar: "Επαγωνίζεσθαι" is a verb in the present tense, middle voice, and infinitive mood. In Greek grammar, the middle voice indicates that the subject is both the doer and the receiver of the action. The infinitive mood expresses the action as a non-finite verb form.

Description: "Επαγωνίζεσθαι" describes the act of contending, struggling, or engaging in a competition or conflict. It implies a vigorous or intense effort to overcome obstacles or opponents.
