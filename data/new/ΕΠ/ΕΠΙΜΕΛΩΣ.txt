Etymology: The word "επιμελως" (epimelos) comes from the Greek word "επιμελεια" (epimeleia), which means "care" or "attention". It is derived from the combination of the prefix "επι-" (epi-), meaning "on" or "upon", and the noun "μελος" (melos), meaning "care" or "concern".

Grammar: "Επιμελως" is an adverb in Greek. It is used to describe the manner in which an action is performed. It can be translated as "carefully", "attentively", or "diligently" in English.

Description: "Επιμελως" is a word that emphasizes the careful and attentive nature of an action. It implies that the action is done with great care, attention to detail, and thoroughness. It is often used to describe actions that require precision, accuracy, or meticulousness.
