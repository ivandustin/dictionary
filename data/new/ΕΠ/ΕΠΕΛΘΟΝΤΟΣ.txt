Etymology: The word "επελθοντος" comes from the Greek verb "επέρχομαι" (epérchomai), which means "to come upon" or "to approach." It is derived from the combination of the prefix "επι-" (epi-), meaning "upon" or "on," and the verb "έρχομαι" (érchomai), meaning "to come."

Grammar: "Επελθοντος" is the genitive form of the Greek adjective "επελθών" (epelthón), which means "coming" or "approaching." In Greek grammar, the genitive case is used to indicate possession, origin, or relationship.

Description: "Επελθοντος" is used to describe something that is coming or approaching. It can be used to refer to a person, an event, or a situation that is about to happen or occur.
