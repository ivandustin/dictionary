Etymology: The word "επηρωτησαν" comes from the Greek verb "επηρωτώ" (epirotó), which means "to ask" or "to inquire".

Grammar: "Επηρώτησαν" is the third person plural form of the aorist tense in the indicative mood of the verb "επηρωτώ". It is used to describe an action of asking or inquiring that was completed in the past.

Description: "Επηρώτησαν" is a Greek word that means "they asked" or "they inquired". It is used to refer to a group of people who asked a question or made an inquiry in the past.
