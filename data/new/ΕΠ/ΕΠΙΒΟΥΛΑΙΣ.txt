Grammar: "Επιβουλής" is a noun in the Greek language. It belongs to the masculine gender and is in the singular form. In the plural form, it becomes "επιβουλές" (epivoules).

Description: "Επιβουλής" refers to a plot, scheme, or conspiracy. It is often used to describe a secret plan or intention to harm or deceive someone. The word carries a negative connotation and implies a hidden agenda or malicious intent.
