Grammar: "επικληθεν" is the aorist passive participle form of the verb "επικαλούμαι" (epikaloumai). In Greek grammar, the aorist tense is used to express a past action without specifying its duration or completion. The passive voice indicates that the subject of the verb is being acted upon.

Description: "επικληθεν" is used to describe something that has been called upon or invoked. It can be used in various contexts, such as in religious or spiritual contexts when referring to invoking a deity or in legal contexts when referring to invoking a law or a right.
