Etymology: The word "επιδιορθωση" comes from the Greek roots "επι-" meaning "upon" or "over" and "διορθωση" meaning "correction" or "repair". 

Grammar: "Επιδιόρθωση" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the singular form and is feminine in gender. 

Description: "Επιδιόρθωση" refers to the act or process of correcting or repairing something. It can be used in various contexts, such as fixing a mistake, repairing a broken object, or rectifying an error.
