Etymology: The word "επεζητούν" comes from the Greek verb "ζητώ" (zito), which means "to seek" or "to search for." The prefix "επ-" (epi-) adds the meaning of "on" or "upon," indicating a continuous or ongoing action.

Grammar: "Επεζητούν" is the third person plural form of the verb "επιζητώ" (epizito), which is in the present tense. It is used to describe an ongoing action of seeking or searching for something.

Description: "Επεζητούν" is a Greek verb that expresses the idea of actively seeking or searching for something. It implies a continuous or persistent effort to find or obtain a specific object, goal, or outcome.
