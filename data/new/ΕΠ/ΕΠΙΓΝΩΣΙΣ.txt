Etymology: The word "επιγνωσις" comes from the Greek roots "επι" (epi), meaning "upon" or "over," and "γνωσις" (gnosis), meaning "knowledge" or "understanding." 

Grammar: "Επιγνωσις" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the singular form and belongs to the feminine gender. 

Description: "Επιγνωσις" refers to the act or process of gaining knowledge or understanding. It can also denote a deeper or more profound level of knowledge, often associated with insight or wisdom. In philosophical and theological contexts, "επιγνωσις" can refer to a higher form of knowledge that goes beyond mere intellectual understanding and involves a personal experience or realization.
