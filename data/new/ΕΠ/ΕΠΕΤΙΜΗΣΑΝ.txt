The word "επετιμησαν" is a Greek verb in the past tense, third person plural form. It comes from the root word "επιτιμηση" (epitimisi), which means "to reprimand" or "to rebuke." The verb is conjugated in the aorist tense, indicating a completed action in the past. In this form, it means "they reprimanded" or "they rebuked."
