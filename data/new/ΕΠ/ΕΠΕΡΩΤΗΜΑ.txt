Etymology: The word "επερωτημα" (eperotima) comes from the Greek verb "επερωτώ" (eperoto), which means "to ask" or "to inquire". 

Grammar: "Επερώτημα" is a noun in the Greek language. It is a neuter noun and its plural form is "επερωτήματα" (eperotimata). 

Description: "Επερώτημα" refers to a question or an inquiry. It is used to describe a statement or a sentence that seeks information or clarification. In Greek, it is commonly used in various contexts, such as in conversations, interviews, surveys, or any situation where one wants to ask a question.
