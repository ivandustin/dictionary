The word "επαισχύνεσθε" is a Greek verb in the present tense, second person plural form. It is derived from the root word "αἰσχύνω" (aischyno), which means "to be ashamed" or "to feel shame." The prefix "ἐπί" (epi) in this word adds the meaning of intensifying or emphasizing the action.

In terms of grammar, "επαισχύνεσθε" is a reflexive verb, which means that the subject of the verb is also the object of the verb. In this case, it indicates that the subject is feeling shame or embarrassment.

The word "επαισχύνεσθε" can be translated as "you are ashamed" or "you feel ashamed" in English.

Etymologically, the word can be traced back to ancient Greek and is still used in modern Greek language. It is commonly used in various contexts to express feelings of shame, embarrassment, or disgrace.
