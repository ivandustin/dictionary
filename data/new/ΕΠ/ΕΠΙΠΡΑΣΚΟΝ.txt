Etymology: The word "επιπρασκον" comes from the Greek language. It is derived from the combination of the prefix "επι-" meaning "on" or "upon," and the verb "πρασκω" meaning "to do" or "to act."

Grammar: "Επιπρασκον" is a verb in the present tense, active voice, and indicative mood. It is the third person singular form of the verb.

Description: "Επιπρασκον" is a Greek word that means "he/she/it is doing" or "he/she/it is acting." It is used to describe an ongoing action or activity performed by a third person singular subject.
