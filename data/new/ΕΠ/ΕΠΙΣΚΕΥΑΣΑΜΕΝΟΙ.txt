Etymology: The word "επισκευασμένοι" comes from the Greek verb "επισκευάζω" (episkevázo), which means "to repair" or "to fix". The suffix "-μένοι" (-menoi) is added to the verb stem to indicate the past participle form, meaning "repaired" or "fixed".

Grammar: "Επισκευασμένοι" is the masculine plural form of the past participle of the verb "επισκευάζω". In Greek, adjectives agree in gender, number, and case with the noun they modify. Therefore, "επισκευασμένοι" is used to describe plural masculine nouns.

Description: "Επισκευασμένοι" is an adjective that describes something or someone that has been repaired or fixed. It indicates that the noun it modifies has undergone a process of restoration or improvement.
