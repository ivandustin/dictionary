Etymology: The word "επαρατοι" (eparatοi) comes from the Greek language. It is a compound word consisting of the prefix "επα-" (epa-), meaning "upon" or "over," and the noun "ρατοι" (ratoi), meaning "order" or "arrangement."

Grammar: "Επαρατοι" is a plural noun in the Greek language. It belongs to the third declension and is of masculine gender. In the singular form, it is "επαρατος" (eparatοs).

Description: "Επαρατοι" refers to a group of people who are appointed or assigned to a specific task or duty. It can also be used to describe an order or arrangement of individuals for a particular purpose.
