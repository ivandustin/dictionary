Etymology: The word "επιστολή" (epistolē) comes from the Greek verb "επιστέλλω" (epistellō), which means "to send a message" or "to dispatch." It is derived from the prefix "επι-" (epi-), meaning "on" or "upon," and the verb "στέλλω" (stellō), meaning "to send."

Grammar: "Επιστολή" is a feminine noun in Greek. It belongs to the second declension and its plural form is "επιστολές" (epistolēs).

Description: "Επιστολή" means "letter" in Greek. It refers to a written message that is typically sent from one person to another. It can be used to convey various types of information, such as personal correspondence, business communication, or official documents.
