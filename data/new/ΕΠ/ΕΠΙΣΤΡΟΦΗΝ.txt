Etymology: The word "επιστροφήν" (epistrophēn) comes from the Greek verb "επιστρέφω" (epistrephō), which means "to turn back" or "to return."

Grammar: "Επιστροφήν" is a noun in the accusative case, singular number, and feminine gender in Greek grammar.

Description: "Επιστροφήν" means "return" or "turning back" in Greek. It refers to the act of going back to a previous state, position, or place. It can also be used metaphorically to describe a change in direction or a reversal of a decision or action.
