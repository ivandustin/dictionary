Etymology: The word "επιλαθέσθαι" comes from the Greek verb "λανθάνω" (lanthano), which means "to forget" or "to neglect". The prefix "επι-" (epi-) in this word adds the meaning of "upon" or "over". Therefore, "επιλαθέσθαι" can be translated as "to forget upon" or "to neglect over".

Grammar: "επιλαθέσθαι" is an infinitive verb form in the Greek language. It belongs to the aorist tense, which expresses an action that is completed in the past. The verb is in the middle voice, indicating that the subject is both the doer and the receiver of the action.

Description: "επιλαθέσθαι" means "to forget" or "to neglect" in Greek. It implies the act of unintentionally or intentionally not remembering or paying attention to something. It can refer to forgetting a task, neglecting a duty, or overlooking an important detail.
