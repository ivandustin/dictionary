The word "επηγγειλατο" is a Greek verb in the aorist tense, third person singular form. It comes from the root word "πηγαίνω" (pigeno), which means "to go". The prefix "επ-" (ep-) is added to indicate the completion of the action. The verb "επηγγειλατο" can be translated as "he/she/it went" or "he/she/it has gone".
