Etymology: The word "επιτροπού" (epitropou) comes from the Greek word "επιτροπή" (epitropi), which means "commission" or "delegation." It is derived from the verb "επιτρέπω" (epitrepo), meaning "to entrust" or "to delegate."

Grammar: "Επιτροπού" is a noun in the genitive case. In Greek grammar, the genitive case is used to indicate possession, origin, or relationship.
