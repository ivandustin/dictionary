Etymology: The word "επιλαμβάνεται" comes from the Greek verb "λαμβάνω" (lambano), which means "to take" or "to seize." The prefix "επι-" (epi-) adds the meaning of "upon" or "over," giving the word the sense of "to take hold of" or "to grasp."

Grammar: "Επιλαμβάνεται" is the third person singular form of the verb "επιλαμβάνομαι" (epilambanomai), which is a reflexive verb in Greek. It is conjugated in the present tense and indicates that the subject is taking hold of or grasping something.

Description: "Επιλαμβάνεται" is a Greek verb that describes the action of someone taking hold of or grasping something. It can be used in various contexts, both literal and figurative, to express the idea of seizing, capturing, or understanding something.
