Etymology: The word "επουρανιον" comes from the Greek words "επί" (epi), meaning "on" or "upon," and "ουρανός" (ouranos), meaning "heaven" or "sky." 

Grammar: "Επουρανιον" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the singular form and is neuter in gender. 

Description: "Επουρανιον" is used in Greek to refer to something that is heavenly or celestial. It can be used to describe things that are related to the sky, the heavens, or the divine.
