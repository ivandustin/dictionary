Grammar: "Επαιδευθη" is the third person singular form of the aorist passive indicative tense of the verb "παιδεύω" (paideuo). In Greek grammar, the aorist tense is used to express a completed action in the past, and the passive voice indicates that the subject of the verb is being acted upon.
