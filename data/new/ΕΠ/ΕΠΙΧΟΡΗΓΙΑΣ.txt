Etymology: The word "επιχορηγίας" comes from the Greek word "επί" (epi), meaning "on" or "upon," and "χορηγία" (chorēgia), meaning "sponsorship" or "provision." 

Grammar: "Επιχορηγίας" is a noun in the genitive case, singular form. In Greek grammar, the genitive case is used to indicate possession, origin, or relationship.

Description: "Επιχορηγίας" refers to the act or process of providing financial or material support or sponsorship. It can also refer to the funds or resources provided for this purpose.
