Etymology: The word "επηρωτών" comes from the Greek verb "επηρωτώ" (epirotó), which means "to ask" or "to inquire".

Grammar: "Επηρωτών" is the present participle form of the verb "επηρωτώ" (epirotó) in the masculine gender, singular number, and nominative case. It is used to describe someone who is currently asking or inquiring.

Description: "Επηρωτών" refers to a person who is asking questions or making inquiries. It can be used to describe someone who is curious, inquisitive, or seeking information.
