Etymology: The word "επιτελεσας" comes from the Greek word "επιτελέω" (epiteleó), which means "to complete" or "to accomplish".

Grammar: "Επιτελεσας" is a noun in the masculine gender and singular number. It belongs to the second declension in Greek grammar.

Description: "Επιτελεσας" refers to someone who has completed a task or accomplished a goal. It can also be used to describe someone who is efficient, effective, or successful in their actions.
