The word "εποτισατε" is a Greek verb in the aorist tense, second person plural form. It comes from the verb "ποτίζω" (potízo), which means "to water" or "to irrigate" in English. In Greek grammar, the aorist tense is used to express a completed action in the past. So, "εποτισατε" can be translated as "you watered" or "you irrigated" in English.
