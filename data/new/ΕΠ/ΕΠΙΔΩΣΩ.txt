Etymology: The word "επιδωσω" (epidoso) comes from the Greek verb "διδωμι" (didomi), which means "to give." The prefix "επι-" (epi-) adds the meaning of "to give back" or "to return."

Grammar: "Επιδωσω" is a verb in the future tense, first person singular form. It is used to express the action of giving back or returning something.

Description: "Επιδωσω" is a Greek word that means "I will give back" or "I will return." It is commonly used to indicate the act of returning something that was previously given or borrowed.
