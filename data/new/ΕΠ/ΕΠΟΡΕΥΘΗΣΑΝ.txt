Etymology: The word "επορευθησαν" comes from the Greek verb "πορεύομαι" (poreúomai), which means "to go" or "to travel". The prefix "επ-" (ep-) in this word indicates a completed action.

Grammar: "Επορεύθησαν" is the third person plural form of the aorist tense of the verb "πορεύομαι". The aorist tense is used to describe a completed action in the past. In this case, it indicates that a group of people went or traveled.

Description: "Επορεύθησαν" is a Greek verb that describes the action of a group of people going or traveling. It can be used in various contexts to indicate movement from one place to another.
