Etymology: The word "επισκιάσει" comes from the Greek verb "επισκιάζω" (episkiazo), which means "to overshadow" or "to cast a shadow over." It is derived from the combination of the prefix "επι-" (epi-), meaning "over" or "upon," and the verb "σκιάζω" (skiazo), meaning "to shadow" or "to shade."

Grammar: "Επισκιάσει" is the third person singular future tense form of the verb "επισκιάζω" (episkiazo). In Greek grammar, verbs are conjugated based on the person, number, tense, and mood. The future tense indicates an action that will happen in the future.

Description: "Επισκιάσει" is a Greek word that translates to "he/she/it will overshadow" in English. It describes an action of casting a shadow over something or someone, either literally or metaphorically.
