Etymology: The word "επιθανατίους" comes from the Greek roots "επί" (epi), meaning "upon" or "on," and "θάνατος" (thanatos), meaning "death." 

Grammar: "Επιθανατίους" is an adjective in the masculine plural form. In Greek grammar, adjectives agree in gender, number, and case with the noun they modify. 

Description: "Επιθανατίους" is an adjective that means "related to death" or "pertaining to death." It is often used to describe things or situations that are associated with or occur near death.
