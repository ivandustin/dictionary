Etymology: The word "επιφωσκουση" comes from the Greek verb "επιφωσκω" (epifosko), which means "to illuminate" or "to shine upon." The suffix "-ση" (-si) is added to form the noun.

Grammar: "Επιφωσκουση" is a feminine noun in the Greek language. It belongs to the third declension and is in the nominative case.

Description: "Επιφωσκουση" refers to the act of illuminating or shining upon something. It can also be used metaphorically to describe the act of enlightening or bringing clarity to a situation or concept.
