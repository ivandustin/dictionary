Etymology: The word "επιστολής" (epistolēs) comes from the Greek word "ἐπιστολή" (epistolē), which means "letter" or "missive". It is derived from the verb "ἐπιστέλλω" (epistellō), meaning "to send a letter".

Grammar: "Επιστολής" is the genitive form of the noun "επιστολή" (epistolē), which is a feminine noun in Greek. In Greek grammar, the genitive case is used to indicate possession or origin.
