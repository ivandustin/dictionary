Grammar: "Επεμειναμεν" is the first person plural form of the verb "επιμένω" in the past tense. It is used to indicate that "we persisted" or "we persevered" in a certain action or state.

Description: "Επεμειναμεν" is a Greek word that conveys the idea of persistence or perseverance. It implies that despite challenges or difficulties, the subject continued to pursue or maintain a certain course of action.
