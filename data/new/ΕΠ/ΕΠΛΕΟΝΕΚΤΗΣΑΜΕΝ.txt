The word "επλεονεκτησαμεν" is a Greek verb in the past tense, first person plural form. It comes from the root word "επλεονεκτώ" (epleonektó), which means "to take advantage" or "to profit". The verb is formed by adding the suffix "-σαμεν" (-samen) to the root. In English, it can be translated as "we took advantage" or "we profited".
