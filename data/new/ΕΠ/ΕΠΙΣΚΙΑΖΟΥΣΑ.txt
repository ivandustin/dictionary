Etymology: The word "επισκιαζουσα" comes from the Greek verb "επισκιάζω" (episkiázo), which means "to overshadow" or "to cast a shadow over." The suffix "-ουσα" indicates the present participle form of the verb, making "επισκιαζουσα" mean "overshadowing" or "casting a shadow over."

Grammar: In Greek, "επισκιαζουσα" is the feminine singular form of the present participle. It agrees with a feminine singular noun in gender, number, and case. It can function as an adjective, modifying a noun, or as a noun itself.

Description: "Επισκιαζουσα" describes something or someone that is overshadowing or casting a shadow over something else. It can be used metaphorically to indicate influence, dominance, or control.
