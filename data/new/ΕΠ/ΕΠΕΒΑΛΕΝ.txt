Grammar: "επεβαλεν" is the aorist tense form of the verb "επιβάλλω" (epibállō) in the third person singular. The aorist tense is used to describe a completed action in the past.

Description: "επεβαλεν" is a Greek word that means "he/she/it imposed" or "he/she/it enforced." It is used to describe the act of imposing or enforcing something, such as rules, laws, or obligations.
