Etymology: The word "επιλαβέσθαι" comes from the Greek verb "λαμβάνω" (lambanō), which means "to take" or "to receive," and the prefix "επι-" (epi-), which denotes "upon" or "over." 

Grammar: "Επιλαβέσθαι" is an infinitive verb form in the Greek language. It belongs to the aorist tense and middle voice. The middle voice indicates that the subject of the verb is both the doer and the receiver of the action.

Description: "Επιλαβέσθαι" means "to take hold of" or "to grasp" in Greek. It implies the act of seizing or grabbing something physically or metaphorically.
