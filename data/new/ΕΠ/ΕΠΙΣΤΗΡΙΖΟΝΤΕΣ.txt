Etymology: The word "επιστηριζοντες" comes from the Greek verb "επιστηρίζω" (epistirízo), which means "to support" or "to prop up." The suffix "-οντες" is added to the verb stem to form the present participle, indicating that the action is ongoing or continuous.

Grammar: "Επιστηριζοντες" is the present participle form of the verb "επιστηρίζω" in Greek. It is used to describe people or things that are currently supporting or propping up something.

Description: "Επιστηριζοντες" refers to individuals or a group of people who are actively providing support or reinforcement to something or someone. It can be used in various contexts, such as physical support, emotional support, or intellectual support.
