Etymology: The word "επηραν" (epiran) comes from the Greek verb "επηρεάζω" (epireázō), which means "to influence" or "to affect". 

Grammar: "Επηραν" is the third person plural form of the verb "επηρεάζω" (epireázō) in the past tense. It is used to describe an action that was done by a group of people in the past.

Description: "Επηραν" is a Greek word that can be translated as "they influenced" or "they affected". It is used to indicate that a group of people had an impact or influence on something or someone in the past.
