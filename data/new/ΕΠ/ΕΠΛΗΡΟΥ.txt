Etymology: The word "επληρου" (eplo̱rou) comes from the Greek verb "πληρόω" (pliroó), which means "to fill" or "to complete".

Grammar: "Επληρου" is the second person singular imperative form of the verb "πληρόω" (pliroó). It is used to give a command or instruction to someone.

Description: "Επληρου" is a Greek word that means "fill" or "complete". It is often used in a directive or commanding context, instructing someone to fill or complete a task or action.
