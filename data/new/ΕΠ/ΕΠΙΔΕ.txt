Etymology: The word "επιδε" (epide) comes from the Greek language. It is a combination of the prefix "επι-" (epi-), meaning "upon" or "over," and the verb "δείκνυμι" (deiknymi), meaning "to show" or "to demonstrate."

Grammar: "Επιδε" is a verb in the Greek language. It is in the present tense, indicative mood, and active voice. It is conjugated in the third person singular form.

Description: The word "επιδε" is used to describe the action of showing or demonstrating something. It implies the act of presenting or displaying something to others.
