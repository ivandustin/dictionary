Etymology: The word "επεπεσαν" comes from the Greek verb "πίπτω" (pípto), which means "to fall". The prefix "επ-" (ep-) is added to indicate a completed action in the past. 

Grammar: "Επεπεσαν" is the third person plural form of the verb "πίπτω" in the aorist tense. It is used to describe an action that happened in the past and is completed.
