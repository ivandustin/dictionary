Etymology: The word "επεδωκαν" (epedokan) comes from the Greek verb "επιδίδωμι" (epididomi), which means "to give, to deliver, to hand over."

Grammar: "Επεδωκαν" is the third person plural, past tense form of the verb "επιδίδωμι" (epididomi). It is used to indicate that a group of people gave or handed over something in the past.
