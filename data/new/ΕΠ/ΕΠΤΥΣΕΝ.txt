Etymology: The word "επτυσεν" (eptysen) comes from the Greek verb "επτύω" (eptyo), which means "to spit". 

Grammar: "Επτυσεν" is the third person singular form of the aorist tense of the verb "επτύω" (eptyo). It is used to indicate an action that happened in the past.

Description: "Επτυσεν" is a Greek word that means "he/she/it spat" in English. It is used to describe the action of forcefully expelling saliva or other substances from the mouth.
