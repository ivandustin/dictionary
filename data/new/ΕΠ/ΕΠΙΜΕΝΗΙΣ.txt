Grammar: "Επιμενης" is an adjective in the masculine singular form. In Greek grammar, adjectives agree in gender, number, and case with the noun they modify. It can also be declined to match the gender, number, and case of the noun it modifies.

Description: "Επιμενης" describes someone or something that persists, perseveres, or remains steadfast in a particular action, attitude, or belief. It implies a strong determination and dedication to continue despite difficulties or obstacles.
