Etymology: The word "επιγνωσομαι" (epignosomai) comes from the Greek roots "επι-" (epi-), meaning "upon" or "over," and "γνωσις" (gnosis), meaning "knowledge" or "understanding." 

Grammar: "Επιγνωσομαι" is a verb in the future tense, first person singular form. It is derived from the verb "γινωσκω" (ginosko), which means "to know" or "to understand." The prefix "επι-" adds the sense of "fully" or "completely" to the verb, emphasizing a deep or thorough knowledge.

Description: "Επιγνωσομαι" refers to the act of gaining or acquiring knowledge or understanding, particularly in a comprehensive or profound way. It implies a process of becoming fully aware or conscious of something, often through careful observation, study, or reflection.
