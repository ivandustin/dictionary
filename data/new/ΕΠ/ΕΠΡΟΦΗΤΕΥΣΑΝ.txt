The word "επροφητευσαν" is a Greek verb in the past tense, third person plural form. It comes from the root word "προφητεύω" (prophēteúō), which means "to prophesy" or "to predict." The prefix "ε-" indicates the past tense, and the suffix "-αν" indicates the third person plural form. Therefore, "επροφητευσαν" translates to "they prophesied" or "they predicted."
