Etymology: The word "επηγγελται" comes from the Greek verb "επαγγέλλομαι" (epangéllomai), which means "to promise" or "to announce". 

Grammar: "Επηγγελται" is the third person singular form of the verb "επαγγέλλομαι" in the present tense. It is in the indicative mood and the passive voice. 

Description: "Επηγγελται" is a Greek word that means "he/she/it is promised" or "he/she/it is announced". It is used to express the action of promising or announcing something in the present tense.
