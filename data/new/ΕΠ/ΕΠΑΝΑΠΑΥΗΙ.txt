Etymology: The word "επαναπαύη" (epanapavē) comes from the Greek roots "επανά" (epaná), meaning "again" or "re-", and "παύω" (paúō), meaning "to rest" or "to cease". 

Grammar: "Επαναπαύη" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the feminine gender and in the singular form.

Description: "Επαναπαύη" refers to the act of resting or ceasing again. It can be used to describe a state of rest or relaxation after a period of activity or exertion.
