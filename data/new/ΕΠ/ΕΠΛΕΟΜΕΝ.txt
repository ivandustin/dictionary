Etymology: The word "επλεομεν" comes from the Greek language. It is derived from the verb "πλέω" (pleo), which means "to sail" or "to navigate." The prefix "επ-" (ep-) adds the meaning of "on" or "upon," giving the word the sense of "sailing on" or "navigating upon."

Grammar: "Επλεομεν" is the first person plural form of the verb "πλέω" (pleo) in the present tense. It is used to express the action of sailing or navigating in the present time by a group of people.
