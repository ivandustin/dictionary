Grammar: "Επιβουλή" is a feminine noun in the Greek language. It belongs to the second declension and has the plural form "επιβουλές" (epivoulés).

Description: "Επιβουλή" refers to a plot, scheme, or conspiracy with malicious intent. It is often used to describe a secret plan or strategy aimed at causing harm, damage, or deception.
