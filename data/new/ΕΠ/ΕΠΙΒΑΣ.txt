Etymology: The word "επιβας" (epibas) comes from the Greek roots "επι-" (epi-), meaning "upon" or "over", and "βας" (bas), meaning "to step" or "to walk". 

Grammar: "Επιβας" is a noun in the masculine gender. It is in the nominative case, singular number. 

Description: "Επιβας" refers to someone who steps or walks upon something. It can be used to describe a person who boards a vehicle, such as a ship or a plane.
