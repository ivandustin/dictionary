Etymology: The word "επισυντρέχει" comes from the Greek roots "επί" (epi), meaning "on" or "upon," and "συντρέχω" (syntrecho), meaning "to run together" or "to converge." 

Grammar: "Επισυντρέχει" is a verb in the present tense, third person singular form. It is derived from the verb "συντρέχω" (syntrecho), which means "to converge" or "to come together." The prefix "επί" (epi) adds the meaning of "on" or "upon" to the verb.

Description: "Επισυντρέχει" is used to describe the action of converging or coming together. It implies the idea of multiple elements or factors coming together or coinciding at a certain point or time.
