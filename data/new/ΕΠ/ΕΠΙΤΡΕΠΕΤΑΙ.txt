Etymology: The word "επιτρεπεται" comes from the Greek verb "επιτρέπω" (epitrepó), which means "to allow" or "to permit."

Grammar: "Επιτρέπεται" is the third person singular form of the verb "επιτρέπω" in the present tense. It is in the indicative mood and passive voice.

Description: "Επιτρέπεται" is a Greek word that translates to "it is allowed" or "it is permitted" in English. It is commonly used to indicate that something is permitted or authorized.
