Etymology: The word "γινομένον" (ginomenon) comes from the Greek verb "γίνομαι" (ginomai), which means "to become" or "to happen". 

Grammar: "Γινομένον" is a neuter noun in the singular form. It belongs to the second declension in Greek grammar. 

Description: "Γινομένον" is used to refer to something that is happening or becoming. It can also be translated as "event" or "occurrence".
