Etymology: The word "γινόμενα" (ginomena) comes from the Greek verb "γίνομαι" (ginomai), which means "to become" or "to happen". 

Grammar: "Γινόμενα" is the plural form of the noun "γινόμενο" (ginomeno), which means "event" or "occurrence". It belongs to the neuter gender in Greek.

Description: "Γινόμενα" refers to a collection or series of events or occurrences. It can be used to describe a sequence of happenings or a set of things that have taken place.
