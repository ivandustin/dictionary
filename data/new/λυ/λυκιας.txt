λυκιας (lykias) is a Greek word that means "of or pertaining to wolves". It is derived from the Greek word "λύκος" (lykos), which means "wolf". In terms of grammar, λυκιας is a masculine noun in the nominative singular form. It can also be declined in other cases, such as genitive (λυκίου), dative (λυκίᾳ), and accusative (λυκίαν).
