The word "λυει" (lyuei) is a present tense verb in Greek, which means "he/she/it loosens" or "he/she/it unties". It is derived from the Greek root "λυ-" (ly-), which means "to loosen" or "to untie". In terms of grammar, "λυει" is a third-person singular form of the present tense, indicative mood, active voice.
