The word λυκων (lykon) is a Greek noun that means "wolf". It is derived from the Greek word λύκος (lykos), which also means "wolf". In terms of grammar, λυκων is a masculine noun in the nominative case. It can also be declined in other cases, such as the genitive case λύκου (lykou) or the accusative case λύκον (lykon).
