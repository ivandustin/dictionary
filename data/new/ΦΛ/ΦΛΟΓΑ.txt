Etymology: The word "φλογα" (phloga) comes from the Greek language.

Grammar: "Φλογα" (phloga) is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the feminine gender and singular number.

Description: "Φλογα" (phloga) means "flame" in Greek. It refers to the visible, glowing gas that is produced during the process of combustion. Flames are typically characterized by their bright, hot, and flickering nature.
