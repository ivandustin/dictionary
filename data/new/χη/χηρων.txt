The word "χηρων" (pronounced "kheeron") is a Greek noun in the genitive plural form. It is derived from the root word "χήρα" (kheera), which means "widow". Therefore, "χηρων" means "of widows" or "belonging to widows". 

In terms of grammar, "χηρων" is a second declension noun in Greek. It is in the genitive case, which is used to show possession or relationship between two nouns. 

Overall, "χηρων" is a specific form of the word "widow" in Greek, indicating possession or belonging to a group of widows.
