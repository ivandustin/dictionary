Word: οπισω

Etymology: οπισω is a Greek word that comes from the combination of the preposition "οπισθεν" (opisthen) meaning "behind" and the adverb "ως" (os) meaning "like" or "as". 

Grammar: οπισω is an adverb in Greek that is used to indicate a direction or a position behind something or someone. It is often used in combination with verbs of motion, such as "πηγα οπισω" (I went back) or "κοιταξε οπισω" (look behind). 

Description: οπισω is a versatile word in Greek that can be used in various contexts to indicate a position or a direction behind something or someone. It is commonly used in everyday language and is an essential word to know for anyone learning Greek.
