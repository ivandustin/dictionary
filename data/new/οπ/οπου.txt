Word: οπου

Etymology:
The word οπου (opou) is a Greek adverb that means "where". It is derived from the combination of the preposition "οποι" (opoi) meaning "in which" and the adverb "ου" (ou) meaning "not". 

Grammar:
Οπου is an adverb in Greek and is used to indicate the location or place of something. It is often used in questions to ask where something is or to describe the location of something. 

Description:
Οπου is a common word in Greek and is used in everyday conversation. It is often used in combination with other words to describe the location of something, such as "οπου βρισκεται" (opou vrisketai) meaning "where is it located". It can also be used to describe the location of a person, such as "οπου εισαι" (opou eisai) meaning "where are you".
