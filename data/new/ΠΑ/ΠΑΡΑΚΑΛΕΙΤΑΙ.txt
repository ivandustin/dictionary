Etymology: The word "παρακαλείται" comes from the Greek verb "παρακαλώ" (parakaló), which means "to ask" or "to request." The suffix "-είται" (-eítai) is added to the verb stem to indicate the passive voice, giving the meaning of "is asked" or "is requested."

Grammar: "Παρακαλείται" is the third person singular form of the verb "παρακαλώ" in the present tense passive voice. It is used to indicate that someone or something is being asked or requested.

Description: "Παρακαλείται" is a Greek verb form that is commonly used in formal or polite contexts. It is often used to make requests or ask for favors.
