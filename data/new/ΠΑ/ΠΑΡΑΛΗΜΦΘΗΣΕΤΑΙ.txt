Etymology: The word "παραλημφθεται" comes from the Greek verb "παραλαμβάνω" (paralamváno), which means "to receive" or "to take". The suffix "-ται" (-tai) indicates the passive voice, so "παραλημφθεται" means "it is received" or "it is taken".

Grammar: "παραλημφθεται" is a third-person singular form of the verb "παραλαμβάνω" in the present tense, passive voice. It is used to describe an action that is being done to the subject of the sentence.

Description: "παραλημφθεται" is a Greek word that means "it is received" or "it is taken". It is commonly used in various contexts to indicate the passive reception or acceptance of something.
