Grammar: "Παρειχετο" is the third person singular, past tense, indicative mood form of the verb "παρέχω" (parecho). It is used to describe an action that was provided or offered in the past.

Description: "Παρειχετο" is a Greek word that means "he/she/it provided" or "he/she/it offered." It is used to describe an action of providing or offering something in the past.
