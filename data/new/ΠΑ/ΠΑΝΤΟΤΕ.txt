Etymology: The word "παντοτε" (pantote) comes from the Greek words "παν" (pan), meaning "all" or "every", and "τοτε" (tote), meaning "then" or "at that time". 

Grammar: "παντοτε" is an adverb in Greek. It is used to express the idea of "always" or "at all times". 

Description: "παντοτε" is a versatile word that can be used in various contexts. It is often used to emphasize the continuous or uninterrupted nature of an action or state. For example, "Πάντοτε αγαπώ τη μουσική" (Pantote agapo ti mousiki) means "I always love music". It can also be used to express a general truth or a habitual action.
