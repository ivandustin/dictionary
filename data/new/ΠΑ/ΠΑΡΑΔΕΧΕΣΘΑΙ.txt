Etymology: The word "παραδεχέσθαι" (paradechesthai) comes from the Greek roots "παρά" (para), meaning "beside" or "alongside," and "δέχομαι" (dechomai), meaning "to receive" or "to accept." 

Grammar: "Παραδεχέσθαι" is a verb in the present tense, middle voice, and infinitive mood. It is derived from the verb "παραδέχομαι" (paradechomai), which means "to admit" or "to acknowledge." 

Description: "Παραδεχέσθαι" is a Greek word that refers to the act of accepting or acknowledging something. It can be used in various contexts, such as admitting a mistake, acknowledging the truth, or accepting someone's point of view.
