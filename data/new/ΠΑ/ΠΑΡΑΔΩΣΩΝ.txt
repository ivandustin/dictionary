Etymology: The word "παραδώσων" comes from the Greek verb "παραδίδωμι" (paradidomi), which means "to deliver" or "to hand over."

Grammar: "παραδώσων" is the present participle form of the verb "παραδίδωμι" (paradidomi) in the masculine singular form. It is used to describe someone who is currently delivering or handing over something.

Description: "παραδώσων" is a word that describes someone who is currently in the act of delivering or handing over something. It can be used to refer to a person who is physically giving something to someone else or metaphorically transferring something, such as knowledge or responsibility.
