Grammar: In Greek, "παροικος" is a masculine noun in the nominative case. It can also be declined in other cases, such as genitive (παροικου), dative (παροικῷ), accusative (παροικον), and vocative (παροικε).

In ancient Greece, it was used to describe someone who lived in a city-state but was not a citizen.
