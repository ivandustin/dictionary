Etymology: The word "παραδοθεται" comes from the Greek verb "παραδίδωμι" (paradidomi), which means "to hand over" or "to deliver."

Grammar: "παραδοθεται" is the third person singular form of the verb "παραδίδωμι" in the present tense, passive voice. It is used to indicate that something is being handed over or delivered by someone or something.

Description: "παραδοθεται" is a Greek word that describes the action of handing over or delivering something. It can be used in various contexts, such as when talking about the transfer of ownership, the passing down of traditions or knowledge, or the surrendering of control or authority.
