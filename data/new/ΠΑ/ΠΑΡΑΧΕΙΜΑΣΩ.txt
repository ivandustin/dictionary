Grammar: "Παραχείμασω" is a verb in the Greek language. It belongs to the first person singular form of the future tense. The verb is conjugated as follows:

- Present tense: παραχειμάζω (paracheimázo) - I spend the winter
- Future tense: θα παραχειμάσω (tha paracheimáso) - I will spend the winter
