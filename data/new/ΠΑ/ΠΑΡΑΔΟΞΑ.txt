It is derived from the combination of the prefix "παρά" (para), meaning "beside" or "contrary to," and the word "δόξα" (doxa), meaning "opinion" or "belief."

Grammar: "παραδοξα" is a noun in the plural form in Greek. It is the plural form of the word "παράδοξο" (paradoxo), which is the singular form.
