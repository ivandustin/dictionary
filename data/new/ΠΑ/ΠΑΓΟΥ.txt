Etymology: The word "παγού" (pagou) comes from the Greek word "παγός" (pagos), which means "ice" or "frost".

Grammar: "Παγού" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the genitive case, indicating possession or relationship.

Description: "Παγού" refers to the ice or frost that forms on surfaces when the temperature drops below freezing. It is a natural phenomenon that occurs in cold climates or during winter seasons.
