Etymology: The word "παραδιδοντα" comes from the Greek verb "παραδίδωμι" (paradidomi), which means "to hand over" or "to deliver."

Grammar: "παραδιδοντα" is the present participle form of the verb "παραδίδωμι" (paradidomi) in the masculine gender, singular number, and accusative case.

Description: "παραδιδοντα" is used to describe a noun in the accusative case that is currently in the act of being handed over or delivered. It indicates an ongoing action or state.
