Etymology: The word "παραστησατε" comes from the Greek verb "παραστήσω" (parastíso), which means "to present" or "to represent."

Grammar: "παραστησατε" is the second person plural imperative form of the verb "παραστήσω" (parastíso). It is used to give a command or instruction to a group of people.

Description: "παραστησατε" is a Greek word that means "present" or "represent." It is used to tell a group of people to present or represent something. It can be used in various contexts, such as in a theatrical performance, a presentation, or a demonstration.
