Etymology: The word "παιδαγωγον" comes from the Greek roots "παιδί" (paidi), meaning "child," and "ἀγωγός" (agogos), meaning "leader" or "guide." 

Grammar: "Παιδαγωγον" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the singular form and belongs to the neuter gender. 

Description: "Παιδαγωγον" refers to a person who educates, guides, and takes care of children. It can be translated as "teacher" or "educator" in English.
