It is derived from the combination of the prefix "παρ-" (par-), meaning "beside" or "alongside," and the verb "ῥησιάζομαι" (rēsiázomai), meaning "to speak" or "to say."

- Present tense: παρρησιάζομαι (parrēsiázomai) - I speak openly
- Future tense: θα παρρησιαστώ (tha parrēsiastó) - I will speak openly
- Past tense: παρρησιάστηκα (parrēsiástika) - I spoke openly

Description: "παρρησιασώμαι" is a Greek verb that conveys the idea of speaking openly, honestly, and without fear or hesitation.
