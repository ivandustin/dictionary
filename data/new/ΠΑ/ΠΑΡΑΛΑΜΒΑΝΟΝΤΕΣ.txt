Grammar: "Παραλαμβάνωντες" is the present participle form of the verb "παραλαμβάνω" in Greek. It is used to describe an ongoing action of receiving or taking something in the present tense. In English, it can be translated as "receiving" or "taking".

Description: "Παραλαμβάνωντες" is a word used in Greek to describe the act of receiving or taking something.
