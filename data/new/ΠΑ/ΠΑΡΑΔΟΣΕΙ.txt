Etymology: The word "παραδοσει" (paradosei) comes from the Greek verb "παραδίδωμι" (paradidomi), which means "to hand over" or "to deliver."

Grammar: "Παραδοσει" is the third person singular form of the verb "παραδίδωμι" (paradidomi) in the future tense. It is used to indicate an action that will happen in the future.

Description: "Παραδοσει" is a Greek word that can be translated as "he/she/it will hand over" or "he/she/it will deliver." It is commonly used in contexts where someone is expected to give or transfer something to someone else in the future.
