Etymology: The word "παραγεταί" comes from the Greek word "παραγίνομαι" (paragínomai), which means "to come near" or "to approach."

Grammar: "παραγεταί" is a noun in the plural form. It belongs to the third declension in Greek grammar.

Description: "παραγεταί" refers to those who come near or approach. It can be used to describe individuals or groups of people who are in close proximity or who are approaching a certain place or situation.
