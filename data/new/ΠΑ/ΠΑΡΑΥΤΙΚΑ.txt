Etymology: The word "παραυτικα" (parautika) comes from the Greek word "παραυτίκα" (parautíka), which means "immediately" or "right away". It is derived from the combination of the prefix "παρα-" (para-), meaning "beside" or "near", and the adverb "υτίκα" (utíka), meaning "immediately".

Grammar: "παραυτικα" is an adverb in Greek. Adverbs modify verbs, adjectives, or other adverbs, and they provide information about time, place, manner, degree, or frequency. In this case, "παραυτικα" indicates that something is happening immediately or right away.

Description: "παραυτικα" is used to describe actions or events that occur without delay or hesitation. It emphasizes the immediacy or promptness of an action. It can be used in various contexts, such as describing someone's response, reaction, or completion of a task.
