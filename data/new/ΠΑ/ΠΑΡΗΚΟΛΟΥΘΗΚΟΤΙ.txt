Etymology: The word "παρηκολουθηκότι" comes from the Greek roots "παρά" (para), meaning "beside" or "alongside," and "ἐκολούθησα" (ekolouthēsa), meaning "I followed." 

Grammar: "παρηκολουθηκότι" is a verb in the past tense, active voice, indicative mood, and first person singular form. It is derived from the verb "παρακολουθώ" (parakolouthō), which means "I follow" or "I accompany."

Description: "παρηκολουθηκότι" describes an action that was completed in the past, where the speaker followed or accompanied someone or something. It indicates that the speaker was present and observed the action or event.
