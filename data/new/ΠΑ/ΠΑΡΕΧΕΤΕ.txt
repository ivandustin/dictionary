Etymology: The word "παρεχετε" (parechete) comes from the Greek verb "παρέχω" (parecho), which means "to provide" or "to offer".

Grammar: "παρεχετε" is the second person plural form of the verb "παρέχω" (parecho) in the present tense. It is used when addressing a group of people and means "you provide" or "you offer".

Description: "παρεχετε" is a verb in the Greek language that expresses the action of providing or offering something. It is commonly used in various contexts, such as in business, hospitality, or general communication, to indicate the act of giving or making something available to others.
