Etymology: The word "παραστηση" (parastisi) comes from the Greek verb "παραστέλλω" (parastello), which means "to present" or "to exhibit". 

Grammar: "Παραστηση" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the singular form and belongs to the feminine gender. 

Description: "Παραστηση" refers to a presentation or a performance, typically in the context of theater, music, or dance. It can also be used more broadly to describe any kind of demonstration or display of skills or talents.
