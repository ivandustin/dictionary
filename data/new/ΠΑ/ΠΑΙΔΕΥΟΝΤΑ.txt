Etymology: The word "παιδευοντα" comes from the Greek verb "παιδεύω" (paideuo), which means "to educate" or "to teach". It is derived from the noun "παις" (pais), meaning "child" or "student".

Grammar: "παιδευοντα" is the present participle form of the verb "παιδεύω" (paideuo) in Greek. It is used to describe someone who is currently educating or teaching.

Description: "παιδευοντα" refers to a person who is actively engaged in the act of educating or teaching. It can be used to describe a teacher, instructor, or anyone who imparts knowledge or skills to others.
