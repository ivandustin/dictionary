Etymology: The word "παραβολή" (parabolē) comes from the Greek verb "παραβάλλω" (paraballō), which means "to compare" or "to put side by side". It is derived from the prefix "παρά" (para), meaning "beside" or "alongside", and the verb "βάλλω" (ballō), meaning "to throw" or "to place".

Grammar: In Greek, "παραβολή" is a feminine noun of the first declension. It is usually used in the singular form, but it can also be used in the plural form "παραβολές" (parabolēs). It can function as both a subject and an object in a sentence.
