Etymology: The word "παρεχέσθε" (parechesthe) comes from the Greek verb "παρέχω" (parecho), which means "to provide" or "to offer."

Grammar: "Παρεχέσθε" is the second person plural imperative form of the verb "παρέχω" (parecho). It is used to give a command or instruction to a group of people, telling them to provide or offer something.
