Etymology: The word "παραλίου" (paraliou) comes from the Greek word "παράλιος" (paralios), which means "by the sea" or "seaside". 

Grammar: "παραλίου" is an adjective in the genitive case, singular form. In Greek grammar, adjectives agree in gender, number, and case with the noun they modify. 

Description: "παραλίου" describes something that is related to or located by the sea. It can be used to describe a place, object, or person that is near or on the coast.
