Etymology: The word "παραλεγόμενοι" comes from the Greek verb "παραλέγομαι" (paralégomai), which means "to speak beside" or "to digress." It is derived from the prefix "παρά" (para), meaning "beside" or "alongside," and the verb "λέγω" (légo), meaning "to speak" or "to say."

Grammar: "Παραλεγόμενοι" is the present participle form of the verb "παραλέγομαι" (paralégomai). In Greek grammar, the present participle is used to describe ongoing actions or states. It can function as an adjective or as part of a verb phrase.

Description: "Παραλεγόμενοι" is a Greek word that can be translated as "speaking beside" or "digressing." It refers to the act of deviating from the main topic or subject of conversation. In a broader sense, it can also describe the act of going off on tangents or discussing unrelated matters.
