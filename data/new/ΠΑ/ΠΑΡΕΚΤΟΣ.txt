Etymology: The word "παρεκτος" (parektos) comes from the Greek prefix "παρα-" (para-), meaning "beside" or "beyond," and the adjective "ἐκτός" (ektos), meaning "outside" or "beyond." 

Grammar: "Παρεκτος" is a masculine noun in the nominative singular form. It belongs to the second declension in Greek grammar. 

Description: "Παρεκτος" is used to describe something that is outside or beyond a certain boundary or limit. It can also refer to something that is beside or alongside something else.
