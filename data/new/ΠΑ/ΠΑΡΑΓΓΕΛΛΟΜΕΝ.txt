Etymology: The word "παραγγελλομεν" comes from the Greek verb "παραγγέλλω" (parangélo), which means "to order" or "to command."

Grammar: "παραγγελλομεν" is the present tense, indicative mood, active voice, and first-person plural form of the verb "παραγγέλλω" (parangélo).

Description: "παραγγελλομεν" is used to express the action of ordering or commanding in the present tense. It is used when the subject is "we" and we are currently giving orders or commands.
