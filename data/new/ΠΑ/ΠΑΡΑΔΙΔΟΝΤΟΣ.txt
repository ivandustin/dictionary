Etymology: The word "παραδιδοντος" comes from the Greek verb "παραδίδωμι" (paradidomi), which means "to hand over" or "to deliver."

Grammar: "Παραδιδοντος" is the genitive form of the noun "παραδιδοντας" (paradidontas), which is the present participle of the verb "παραδίδωμι" (paradidomi). In Greek grammar, the genitive case is used to indicate possession or origin.

Description: "Παραδιδοντος" can be translated as "of the one handing over" or "of the one delivering" in English. It is used to describe someone who is currently in the act of handing over or delivering something.
