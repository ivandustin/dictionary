Etymology: The word "παραγγειλης" (parangeilis) comes from the Greek verb "παραγγέλνω" (parangélo), which means "to order" or "to command".

Grammar: "Παραγγειλης" is a noun in the masculine gender and singular number. It belongs to the second declension in Greek grammar.

Description: "Παραγγειλης" refers to a person who gives orders or commands. It can also be used to describe someone who is authoritative or in a position of power.
