Etymology: The word "παραδοσεων" (paradoseon) comes from the Greek word "παράδοση" (paradosi), which means "tradition" or "handing down."

Grammar: "Παραδοσεων" is the genitive plural form of the noun "παράδοση" (paradosi). In Greek grammar, the genitive case is used to indicate possession or origin.

Description: "Παραδοσεων" refers to "of traditions" or "of handings down" in Greek. It is often used to describe a collection or body of traditions that have been passed down from generation to generation.
