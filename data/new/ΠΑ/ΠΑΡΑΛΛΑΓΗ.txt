Grammar: "Παραλλαγή" is a feminine noun in Greek. It belongs to the first declension and has the plural form "παραλλαγές" (parallages). In Greek grammar, nouns are declined according to gender, number, and case.

Description: "Παραλλαγή" refers to a variation, alteration, or change in something. It can be used to describe a modification or deviation from a standard or original form. In a broader sense, it can also refer to a difference or diversity in general.
