Etymology: The word "παραδωσιν" (paradosin) comes from the Greek verb "παραδίδωμι" (paradidomi), which means "to hand over" or "to deliver."

Grammar: "Παραδωσιν" is a noun in the accusative case, singular number, and feminine gender.

Description: "Παραδωσιν" refers to the act of handing over or delivering something. It can also be used to describe a tradition or a custom that is passed down from one generation to another.
