Etymology: The word "παρηγγελλομεν" comes from the Greek verb "παραγγέλλω" (parangélo), which means "to order" or "to command."

Grammar: "παρηγγελλομεν" is the present tense, indicative mood, active voice, first person plural form of the verb "παραγγέλλω" (parangélo).

Description: "παρηγγελλομεν" is a Greek word that means "we order" or "we command." It is used to express an action of giving instructions or making requests in the present tense, when the subject is "we."
