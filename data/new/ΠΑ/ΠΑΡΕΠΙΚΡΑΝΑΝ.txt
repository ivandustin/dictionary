Etymology: The word "παρεπικραναν" comes from the Greek verb "παρεπικραίνω" (parepikríno), which means "to criticize" or "to find fault with." It is derived from the prefix "παρα-" (para-), meaning "beside" or "against," and the verb "ἐπικρίνω" (epikríno), meaning "to judge" or "to discern."

Grammar: "παρεπικραναν" is the third person plural, aorist active indicative form of the verb "παρεπικραίνω" (parepikríno). In Greek grammar, the aorist tense is used to express a past action without specifying its duration or completion. The active voice indicates that the subject of the verb is performing the action.

Description: "παρεπικραναν" describes the action of criticizing or finding fault with something or someone. It implies a judgmental attitude or a negative evaluation of a particular situation, behavior, or person.
