Etymology: The word "παρεισηλθον" comes from the Greek roots "παρά" (para), meaning "beside" or "alongside," and "εἰσέρχομαι" (eiserchomai), meaning "to enter." 

Grammar: "παρεισηλθον" is a verb in the aorist tense, active voice, and indicative mood. It is the first person singular form of the verb "παρεισέρχομαι" (pareiserchomai), which means "to enter secretly" or "to slip in."

Description: "παρεισηλθον" describes the action of entering or slipping in secretly or stealthily. It implies a sense of sneaking or infiltrating without being noticed.
