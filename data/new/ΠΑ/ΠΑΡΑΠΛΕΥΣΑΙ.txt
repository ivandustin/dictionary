Etymology: The word "παραπλευρώνω" comes from the Greek roots "παρά" (para), meaning "beside" or "alongside," and "πλευρά" (pleura), meaning "side." 

Grammar: "παραπλευρώνω" is a verb in the Greek language. It belongs to the first conjugation class and is conjugated in the present tense as follows: εγώ παραπλευρώνω (I parallel), εσύ παραπλευρώνεις (you parallel), αυτός/αυτή/αυτό παραπλευρώνει (he/she/it parallels), εμείς παραπλευρώνουμε (we parallel), εσείς παραπλευρώνετε (you parallel), αυτοί/αυτές παραπλευρώνουν (they parallel).

Description: The verb "παραπλευρώνω" means "to parallel" in English. It is used to describe the action of moving or positioning something alongside or in a parallel manner to something else.
