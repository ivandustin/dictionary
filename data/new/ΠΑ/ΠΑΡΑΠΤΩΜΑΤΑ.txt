Etymology: The word "παραπτώματα" comes from the Greek language. It is derived from the combination of the prefix "παρα-" meaning "beside" or "beyond" and the noun "πτώματα" meaning "falls" or "mistakes".

Grammar: "παραπτώματα" is a noun in the plural form. In Greek grammar, nouns have different forms depending on their gender, number, and case. The plural form indicates that there are multiple falls or mistakes.

Description: "παραπτώματα" refers to falls or mistakes that occur as a result of deviation from the correct path or course of action. It can be used in various contexts, such as describing errors in judgment, moral lapses, or accidental missteps.
