Etymology: The word "παραδώσω" (paradóso) comes from the Greek verb "παραδίδωμι" (paradídōmi), which means "to give over" or "to deliver." It is derived from the combination of the preposition "παρά" (pará), meaning "beside" or "alongside," and the verb "δίδωμι" (dídōmi), meaning "to give."

Grammar: "Παραδώσω" is a verb in the future tense, first person singular form. In Greek, verbs are conjugated based on the tense, mood, voice, and person. The future tense indicates an action that will happen in the future. The first person singular form indicates that the subject of the verb is the speaker.

Description: "Παραδώσω" is a Greek verb that translates to "I will give over" or "I will deliver" in English. It expresses the act of handing something over or delivering something to someone. The verb can be used in various contexts, such as delivering a package, transferring ownership, or surrendering something.
