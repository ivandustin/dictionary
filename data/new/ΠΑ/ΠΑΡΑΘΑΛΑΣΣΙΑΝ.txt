Etymology: The word "παραθαλασσιαν" comes from the Greek words "παρά" (para), meaning "beside" or "alongside," and "θάλασσα" (thalassa), meaning "sea." 

Grammar: "Παραθαλασσιαν" is an adjective in the Greek language. It is used to describe something or someone that is related to or located by the sea.

Description: "Παραθαλασσιαν" is often used to describe coastal areas, activities, or properties. It can refer to anything that is connected to or influenced by the sea, such as beaches, resorts, towns, or even a person who lives or works near the sea.
