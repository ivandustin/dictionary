Etymology: The word "παραδώ" (paradō) comes from the Greek verb "παραδίδωμι" (paradidōmi), which means "to give over" or "to hand over". 

Grammar: "Παραδώ" is a verb in the Greek language. It is in the present tense, indicative mood, and active voice. 

Description: "Παραδώ" means "I give over" or "I hand over" in English. It is used to express the action of transferring or delivering something to someone else.
