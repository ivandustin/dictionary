Etymology: The word "παραγγελλειν" (parangellein) comes from the Greek roots "παρά" (para), meaning "beside" or "alongside," and "ἀγγέλλειν" (angellein), meaning "to announce" or "to proclaim." 

Grammar: "παραγγελλειν" is a verb in the Greek language. It is in the present tense, active voice, and infinitive mood. 

Description: "παραγγελλειν" means "to order" or "to command" in Greek. It is used to express the act of giving instructions or directives to someone.
