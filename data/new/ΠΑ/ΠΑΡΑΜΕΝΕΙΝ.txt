Etymology: The word "παραμενειν" (parameinein) comes from the Greek roots "παρα" (para), meaning "beside" or "alongside," and "μενειν" (menein), meaning "to stay" or "to remain." 

Grammar: "παραμενειν" is a verb in the Greek language. It is in the present tense, active voice, and infinitive mood. 

Description: "παραμενειν" means "to stay" or "to remain" in Greek. It refers to the action of staying or remaining in a particular place or condition.
