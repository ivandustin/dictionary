Etymology: The word "παρεπιδήμους" comes from the Greek words "παρά" (para), meaning "beside" or "alongside," and "ἐπίδημος" (epidēmos), meaning "resident" or "sojourner." 

Grammar: "Παρεπιδήμους" is a plural noun in the masculine gender. It is declined in the second declension and belongs to the nominative case. 

Description: "Παρεπιδήμους" refers to people who live or stay temporarily in a place that is not their original or permanent residence. It can be used to describe foreigners, immigrants, or expatriates.
