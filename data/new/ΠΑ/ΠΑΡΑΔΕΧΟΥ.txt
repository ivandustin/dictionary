Etymology: The word "παραδεχού" (paradechou) comes from the Greek verb "παραδέχομαι" (paradechomai), which means "to admit" or "to accept". It is derived from the combination of the prefix "παρα-" (para-), meaning "beside" or "alongside", and the verb "δέχομαι" (dechomai), meaning "to receive" or "to accept".

Grammar: "Παραδεχού" is the imperative form of the verb "παραδέχομαι" (paradechomai) in the second person singular. It is used to give a command or instruction to someone, telling them to admit or accept something.

Description: "Παραδεχού" is a Greek word that is used to express the action of admitting or accepting something. It can be used in various contexts, such as acknowledging a mistake, accepting a fact, or agreeing with someone's opinion.
