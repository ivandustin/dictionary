Etymology: The word "παραλελυμένος" comes from the Greek roots "παρά" (para), meaning "beside" or "alongside," and "λελυμένος" (lelymenos), meaning "paralyzed" or "immobilized." 

Grammar: "Παραλελυμένος" is an adjective in the masculine singular form. It agrees with the noun it modifies in gender, number, and case. 

Description: "Παραλελυμένος" describes someone or something that is paralyzed or immobilized. It can be used metaphorically to describe a situation or process that is stuck or unable to progress.
