Grammar: "Παραμεινάς" is a verb in the second person singular form of the aorist tense in Greek. It is used to address someone directly and means "you remained" or "you stayed."

Description: "Παραμεινάς" is a word that expresses the action of staying or remaining in a particular place or condition. It can be used in various contexts, such as describing someone's physical presence or their persistence in a certain state or situation.
