Etymology: The word "παυσηι" (pronounced "pau̯sēi") comes from the Greek verb "παύω" (pronounced "pau̯ō"), which means "to stop" or "to cease."

Grammar: "Παυσηι" is a verb in the present tense, active voice, and indicative mood. It is the third person singular form of the verb.

Description: "Παυσηι" means "he/she/it stops" or "he/she/it ceases" in Greek. It is used to indicate the action of stopping or ceasing something.
