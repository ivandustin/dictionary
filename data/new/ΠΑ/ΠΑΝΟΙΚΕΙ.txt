Etymology: The word "πανοικεί" comes from the Greek words "παν" (pan), meaning "all" or "every", and "οἶκος" (oikos), meaning "house" or "dwelling". 

Grammar: "πανοικεί" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the nominative case and singular number.

Description: "πανοικεί" refers to a type of communal living arrangement where multiple families or individuals live together in a shared space or building. It can also be used to describe a household that includes extended family members or multiple generations living together.
