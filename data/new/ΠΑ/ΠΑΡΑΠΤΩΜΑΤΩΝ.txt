Etymology: The word "παραπτώματων" comes from the Greek language. It is a compound word formed by combining the prefix "παρα-" (para-), meaning "beside" or "beyond," and the noun "πτώματα" (ptómata), meaning "falls" or "mistakes."

Grammar: "παραπτώματων" is a noun in the genitive plural form. In Greek grammar, the genitive case is used to indicate possession, origin, or relationship. The plural form indicates that there are multiple falls or mistakes being referred to.

Description: "παραπτώματων" refers to falls or mistakes. It can be used to describe errors, faults, or missteps in various contexts, such as in personal actions, moral choices, or professional endeavors.
