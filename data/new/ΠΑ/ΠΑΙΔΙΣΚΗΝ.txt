Etymology: The word "παίδισκην" comes from the Greek word "παῖς" (paîs), meaning "child" or "girl," and the suffix "-ίσκην" (-ískēn), which denotes a diminutive form. It can be translated as "little girl" or "young girl."

Grammar: "Παίδισκην" is a noun in the singular form. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their gender, number, and case. The word "παίδισκην" is in the feminine gender and the accusative case.

Description: "Παίδισκην" refers to a young girl or a female child. It is a term that emphasizes the youth and innocence of a girl.
