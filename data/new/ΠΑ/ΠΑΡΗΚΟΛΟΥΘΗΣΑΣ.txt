Etymology: The word "παρηκολουθήσας" comes from the Greek verb "παρακολουθώ" (parakolouthó), which means "to follow closely" or "to observe attentively." It is formed by combining the prefix "παρά" (para), meaning "beside" or "alongside," and the verb "κολουθώ" (kolouthó), meaning "to follow."

Grammar: "Παρηκολουθήσας" is the aorist active participle of the verb "παρακολουθώ" (parakolouthó). In Greek grammar, the aorist tense is used to express a completed action in the past. The active participle form indicates that the subject of the verb is performing the action.

Description: "Παρηκολουθήσας" can be translated as "having followed closely" or "having observed attentively." It describes someone who has closely followed or observed something or someone in the past.
