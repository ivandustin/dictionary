Etymology: The word "παίδευση" (paideusē) comes from the Greek verb "παιδεύω" (paideuō), which means "to educate" or "to teach." It is derived from the noun "παῖς" (pais), meaning "child" or "student."

Grammar: "Παίδευση" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the singular form and belongs to the feminine gender.

Description: "Παίδευση" refers to the process of education or the act of teaching and training. It encompasses the formal and informal methods used to impart knowledge, skills, and values to individuals, typically in a structured setting such as schools or educational institutions. The concept of "παίδευση" is broad and encompasses various aspects of learning, including academic, social, and moral development.
