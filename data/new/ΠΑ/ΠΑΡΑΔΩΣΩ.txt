Etymology: The word "παραδώσω" (paradóso) comes from the Greek verb "παραδίδωμι" (paradídōmi), which means "to deliver" or "to hand over". It is derived from the combination of the preposition "παρά" (pará), meaning "beside" or "alongside", and the verb "δίδωμι" (dídōmi), meaning "to give".

Grammar: "Παραδώσω" is a verb in the future tense, first person singular form. In Greek, verbs are conjugated based on the tense, mood, voice, and person. The future tense indicates an action that will happen in the future. The first person singular form indicates that the subject of the verb is the speaker.

Description: "Παραδώσω" is a Greek word that translates to "I will deliver" or "I will hand over" in English. It expresses the action of giving or delivering something to someone in the future.
