Etymology: The word "παραδοντος" (paradontos) comes from the Greek verb "παραδίδωμι" (paradidomi), which means "to give over" or "to hand over."

Grammar: "Παραδοντος" is the genitive form of the noun "παραδοντος" (paradontos). In Greek grammar, the genitive case is used to indicate possession or origin.

Description: "Παραδοντος" is a Greek word that can be translated as "of the one who gives over" or "of the one who hands over." It is often used in contexts where something is being passed on or transferred from one person to another.
