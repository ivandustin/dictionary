Etymology: The word "παρομοια" (paromoia) comes from the Greek roots "παρά" (para), meaning "beside" or "similar to," and "ὁμοῖος" (homoios), meaning "similar" or "alike." 

Grammar: "Παρομοια" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the feminine gender and singular number. 

Description: "Παρομοια" refers to the concept of similarity or resemblance. It is used to describe things that are alike or have similar characteristics.
