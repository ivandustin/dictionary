Etymology: The word "παραστητε" comes from the Greek verb "παραστέλλω" (parastélo), which means "to present" or "to exhibit."

Grammar: "παραστητε" is the second person plural imperative form of the verb "παραστέλλω" (parastélo). It is used to give a command or instruction to a group of people.

Description: "παραστητε" is a Greek verb form that is used to tell a group of people to present or exhibit something. It is often used in a formal or instructional context to give directions or commands.
