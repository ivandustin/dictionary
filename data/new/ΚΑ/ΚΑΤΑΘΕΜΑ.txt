Etymology: The word "καταθέμα" (kathéma) comes from the Greek verb "κατατίθημι" (katatíthemi), which means "to deposit" or "to place down". 

Grammar: "καταθέμα" is a noun in the neuter gender. It is singular and can be declined in different cases, such as nominative, genitive, dative, and accusative.

Description: "καταθέμα" refers to a deposit or a sum of money that is placed or entrusted to someone or something. It can also be used to describe a contribution or an offering made to a person, organization, or cause.
