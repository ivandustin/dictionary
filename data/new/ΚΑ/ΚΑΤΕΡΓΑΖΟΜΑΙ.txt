Etymology: The word "κατεργαζόμαι" comes from the Greek verb "εργάζομαι" (ergázomai), which means "to work" or "to labor". The prefix "κατα-" adds the meaning of "down" or "thoroughly", so "κατεργαζόμαι" can be translated as "to work thoroughly" or "to work diligently".

Grammar: "κατεργαζόμαι" is a verb in the Greek language. It belongs to the middle voice, which indicates that the subject is both the doer and the receiver of the action. It is conjugated in various tenses, moods, and persons to match the subject and express different meanings.

Description: "κατεργαζόμαι" is a versatile verb that can be used in different contexts. It generally conveys the idea of working diligently, exerting effort, or engaging in productive labor. It can be used to describe physical work, intellectual work, or any kind of productive activity.
