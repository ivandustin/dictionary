Etymology: The word "καθαραί" (katharai) comes from the Greek adjective "καθαρός" (katharos), meaning "clean" or "pure."

Grammar: "Καθαραί" is the plural form of the feminine noun "καθαρά" (kathara), which means "cleanliness" or "purity." It is used to refer to things or concepts that are clean or pure.

Description: "Καθαραί" is a term that can be used in various contexts. It can refer to physical cleanliness, such as clean clothes or a clean environment.
