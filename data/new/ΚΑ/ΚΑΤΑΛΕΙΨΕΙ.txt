Etymology: The word "καταλειψει" comes from the Greek verb "καταλείπω" (kataleípo), which means "to leave behind" or "to abandon."

Grammar: "καταλειψει" is the future tense, third person singular form of the verb "καταλείπω" (kataleípo). It indicates that someone or something will leave or abandon something in the future.

Description: "καταλειψει" is a Greek word that describes an action of leaving or abandoning something in the future. It can be used in various contexts, such as leaving behind a place, a person, or a responsibility.
