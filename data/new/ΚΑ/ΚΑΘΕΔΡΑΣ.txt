Grammar: In Greek, "καθεδρας" is a noun in the masculine gender. It is in the nominative case, singular number.

Description: "καθεδρας" refers to a chair or seat, typically one that is used by a teacher or professor in a lecture hall or classroom. It can also be used metaphorically to represent the position or authority of a teacher or professor.
