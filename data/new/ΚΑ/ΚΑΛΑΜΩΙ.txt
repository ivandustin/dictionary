Etymology: The word "καλαμών" (kalamón) comes from the Greek word "κάλαμος" (kálamos), which means "reed" or "stalk." 

Grammar: "Καλαμών" is a masculine noun in the singular form. It belongs to the second declension in Greek grammar.

Description: "Καλαμών" refers to a type of reed or stalk, typically used for making pens, pipes, or musical instruments. It can also refer to a long, slender object resembling a reed.
