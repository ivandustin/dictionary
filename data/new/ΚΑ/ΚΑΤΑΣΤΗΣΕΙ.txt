Etymology: The word "καταστησει" comes from the Greek verb "καταστήνω" (katastíno), which means "to establish" or "to set up."

Grammar: "καταστησει" is the third person singular form of the future tense of the verb "καταστήνω" (katastíno). It is used to indicate an action that will happen in the future.

Description: "καταστησει" is a Greek word that means "he/she/it will establish" or "he/she/it will set up." It is commonly used to talk about future actions or plans of establishing or setting up something.
