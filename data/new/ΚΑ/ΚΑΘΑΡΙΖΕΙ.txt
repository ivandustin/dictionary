Grammar: "Καθαρίζει" is the third person singular form of the present tense of the verb "καθαρίζω" (katharízo). It is used to describe an action that is happening in the present and is performed by a third person.

Description: "Καθαρίζει" is a Greek word that means "he/she/it cleans" or "he/she/it purifies." It is commonly used to refer to the act of cleaning or purifying something.
