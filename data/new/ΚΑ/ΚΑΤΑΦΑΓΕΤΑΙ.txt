Grammar: "καταφαγεται" is a verb in the third person singular form of the aorist tense in the Greek language. It is in the active voice and indicative mood. 

Description: The word "καταφαγεται" means "he/she/it is devoured" or "he/she/it is eaten" in English. It describes the action of something being consumed or eaten completely.
