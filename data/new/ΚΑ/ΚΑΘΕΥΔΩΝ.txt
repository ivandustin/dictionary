Etymology: The word "καθευδών" comes from the Greek verb "καθεύδω" (katheúdō), which means "to sleep". 

Grammar: "καθευδών" is the present participle form of the verb "καθεύδω" in the masculine gender, singular number, and nominative case. It is used to describe a male subject who is currently sleeping.

Description: "καθευδών" refers to a male person who is sleeping at the present moment.
