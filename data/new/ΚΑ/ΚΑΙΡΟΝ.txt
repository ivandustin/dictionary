Grammar: In Greek, "καιρόν" is a noun in the masculine gender. It is in the accusative case when used as a direct object, and in the nominative case when used as a subject.

Description: "Καιρόν" is a versatile word that can have several meanings depending on the context. It can refer to time, a specific moment, an opportune time, or a season. It is often used to describe a favorable or appropriate time for something to happen.
