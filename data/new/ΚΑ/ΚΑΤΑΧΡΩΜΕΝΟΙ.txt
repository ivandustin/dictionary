Etymology: The word "καταχρωμένοι" comes from the Greek verb "καταχρώμαι" (katachromai), which means "to abuse" or "to misuse". The suffix "-οι" is added to form the plural form of the word.

Grammar: "καταχρωμένοι" is the plural form of the adjective "καταχρωμένος" (katachromenos), which means "abused" or "misused". It agrees in gender, number, and case with the noun it modifies.

Description: "καταχρωμένοι" is an adjective used to describe multiple things or people that have been abused or misused. It implies that these things or people have been treated inappropriately or not used for their intended purpose.
