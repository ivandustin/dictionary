Grammar: "κατηγορείτε" is the present tense, second person plural form of the verb "κατηγορέω" (katēgoreō). It is used when addressing a group of people and means "you accuse" or "you charge".

Description: "κατηγορείτε" is a verb in the Greek language that is used to express the action of accusing or charging someone with a wrongdoing. It is commonly used in legal or formal contexts to indicate the act of making an accusation or bringing charges against someone.
