Grammar: In Greek, "καλος" (kalos) is an adjective. It has three genders (masculine, feminine, and neuter) and three numbers (singular, dual, and plural). It can be declined to match the gender, number, and case of the noun it modifies.

Description: The word "καλος" (kalos) has multiple meanings in Greek. It can be translated as "beautiful," "good," "fine," or "excellent." It is often used to describe something aesthetically pleasing or morally virtuous. It can also be used as a greeting or expression of approval.
