Etymology: The word "καθαρίζετε" comes from the Greek verb "καθαρίζω" (katharízo), which means "to clean" or "to purify."

Grammar: "Καθαρίζετε" is the second person plural form of the present tense in Greek. It is used when addressing a group of people and means "you clean" or "you are cleaning."

Description: "Καθαρίζετε" is a verb in the Greek language that describes the action of cleaning or purifying. It is commonly used in various contexts, such as cleaning a space, purifying something, or maintaining cleanliness.
