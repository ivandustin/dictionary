Etymology: The word "κατεχετε" comes from the Greek verb "κατέχω" (katecho), which means "to hold" or "to possess."

Grammar: "κατεχετε" is the present tense, second person plural form of the verb "κατέχω" (katecho). It is used to address a group of people and indicates that they are currently holding or possessing something.

Description: "κατεχετε" is a verb in the Greek language that is used to express the action of holding or possessing something. It is commonly used in various contexts to indicate physical or metaphorical possession.
