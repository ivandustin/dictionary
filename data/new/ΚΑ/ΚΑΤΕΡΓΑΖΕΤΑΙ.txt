Etymology: The word "κατεργάζεται" comes from the Greek verb "κατεργάζομαι" (katergázomai), which means "to work on" or "to accomplish."

Grammar: "Κατεργάζεται" is the third person singular form of the present tense of the verb "κατεργάζομαι" in Greek. It is in the active voice and indicative mood.

Description: "Κατεργάζεται" is a verb that describes the action of working on or accomplishing something. It implies a process of actively engaging in a task or project.
