Grammar: "κατακρινει" is the third person singular form of the present tense of the verb "κατακρίνω" (katakríno). In Greek, verbs change their endings to indicate the person, number, and tense. The present tense indicates an action that is happening now.

Description: "κατακρινει" is a Greek verb that means "he/she/it condemns" or "he/she/it judges against." It is used to express the act of passing judgment or expressing disapproval towards someone or something.
