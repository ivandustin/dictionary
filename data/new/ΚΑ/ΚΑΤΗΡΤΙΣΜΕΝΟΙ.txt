Etymology: The word "κατηρτισμένοι" comes from the Greek verb "καταρτίζω" (katartízo), which means "to prepare" or "to equip". The suffix "-μένοι" (-menoi) is added to the verb stem to indicate the passive voice and the plural form.

Grammar: "Κατηρτισμένοι" is the masculine plural form of the adjective in Greek. It agrees with a plural noun or pronoun in gender and number.

Description: The word "κατηρτισμένοι" describes a group of people who have been prepared, equipped, or trained for a specific purpose or task. It implies that they have acquired the necessary skills, knowledge, or qualifications to perform that task effectively.
