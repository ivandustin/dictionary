Etymology: The word "κατασκιαζοντα" comes from the Greek verb "κατασκιάζω" (kataskeazo), which means "to shade" or "to cast a shadow over." The suffix "-ντα" is added to the verb stem to form the present participle, indicating an ongoing action.

Grammar: "κατασκιαζοντα" is the present participle form of the verb "κατασκιάζω" in Greek. It is used to describe an ongoing action of shading or casting a shadow over something or someone.

Description: "κατασκιαζοντα" describes something or someone that is currently shading or casting a shadow over another object or person. It can be used to indicate a physical shadow or a metaphorical overshadowing or influence.
