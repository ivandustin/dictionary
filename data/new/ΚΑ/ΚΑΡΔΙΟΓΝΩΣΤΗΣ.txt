Etymology: The word "καρδιογνώστης" comes from the Greek words "καρδιά" (kardia) meaning "heart" and "γνώστης" (gnostis) meaning "knower" or "one who knows". 

Grammar: "καρδιογνώστης" is a noun in the masculine gender. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their gender, number, and case. 

Description: "καρδιογνώστης" refers to a person who has the ability to understand or know the condition of someone's heart, both in a literal and metaphorical sense. It can be used to describe someone who is perceptive, empathetic, or intuitive in understanding the emotions, feelings, or intentions of others.
