Etymology: The word "κατακρίμα" comes from the Greek verb "κατακρίνω" (katakríno), which means "to condemn" or "to judge against." 

Grammar: "Κατακρίμα" is a noun in the singular form. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their gender, number, and case. 

Description: "Κατακρίμα" refers to a condemnation or a judgment against someone or something. It can also be used to describe a negative opinion or criticism.
