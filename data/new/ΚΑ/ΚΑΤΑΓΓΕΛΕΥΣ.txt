Etymology: The word "καταγγελεύς" comes from the Greek verb "καταγγέλλω" (katangello), which means "to accuse" or "to denounce".

Grammar: In Greek, "καταγγελεύς" is a noun of the masculine gender. It is in the nominative case and singular number.

Description: The word "καταγγελεύς" refers to a person who accuses or denounces someone or something. It is often used to describe someone who reports a crime or wrongdoing to the authorities.
