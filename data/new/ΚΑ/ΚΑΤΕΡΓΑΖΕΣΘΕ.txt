Etymology: The word "κατεργάζεσθε" comes from the Greek verb "κατεργάζομαι" (katergázomai), which means "to work out" or "to accomplish."

Grammar: "Κατεργάζεσθε" is the second person plural form of the present middle-passive voice of the verb "κατεργάζομαι." It is used to address a group of people and indicates that they are performing the action of working out or accomplishing something.

Description: "Κατεργάζεσθε" is a verb in the Greek language that conveys the idea of actively working on or accomplishing a task. It implies a sense of effort and diligence in achieving a goal.
