Etymology: The word "καταφρονήσει" comes from the Greek verb "καταφρονέω" (kataphroneo), which means "to despise" or "to look down upon". It is derived from the combination of the prefix "κατά" (kata), meaning "down" or "against", and the verb "φρονέω" (phroneo), meaning "to think" or "to have an opinion".

Grammar: "καταφρονήσει" is the third person singular future tense form of the verb "καταφρονέω" (kataphroneo). In Greek grammar, verbs are conjugated according to person, number, tense, mood, and voice. The future tense indicates an action that will happen in the future.

Description: "καταφρονήσει" is a Greek word that translates to "he/she/it will despise" or "he/she/it will look down upon" in English. It expresses the idea of showing contempt or disregard towards someone or something.
