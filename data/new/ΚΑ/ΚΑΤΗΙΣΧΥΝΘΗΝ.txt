Etymology: The word "κατηγορία" comes from the Greek word "κατηγορέω" (katēgoreō), which means "to accuse" or "to speak against". It is derived from the combination of the prefix "κατά" (kata), meaning "down" or "against", and the verb "ἀγορεύω" (agoreuō), meaning "to speak in public".
