Etymology: The word "καταλαβηί" comes from the Greek verb "καταλαμβάνω" (katalambano), which means "to understand" or "to comprehend."

Grammar: "καταλαβηί" is a noun in the singular form. It belongs to the third declension in Greek grammar.

Description: "καταλαβηί" refers to the act of understanding or comprehending something. It can also be used to describe the level of understanding or comprehension that someone has.
