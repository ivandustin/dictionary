Etymology: The word "καταβαρυνομένοι" comes from the Greek verb "καταβαρύνω" (katabaryno), which means "to burden" or "to weigh down". The suffix "-μένοι" (-menoi) is added to the verb stem to indicate the passive voice and plural form.

Grammar: "καταβαρυνομένοι" is the masculine plural form of the present passive participle of the verb "καταβαρύνω". In Greek grammar, participles are verbal adjectives that can be used to describe or modify nouns. The present passive participle indicates that the noun it modifies is being acted upon or affected by the verb.

Description: "καταβαρυνομένοι" describes a group of people who are being burdened or weighed down by something. It implies that they are experiencing a heavy or oppressive situation or condition.
