Grammar: "κατανοώμεν" is the present tense, indicative mood, and active voice form of the verb "κατανοέω" in the first person plural. It is used to express the action of understanding or comprehending in the present time.

Description: "κατανοώμεν" is a Greek word that describes the act of understanding or comprehending something.
