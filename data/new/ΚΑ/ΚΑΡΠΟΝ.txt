Etymology: The word "καρπόν" (karpon) comes from the Greek word "καρπός" (karpos), meaning "fruit" or "result". 

Grammar: In Greek, "καρπόν" is a noun in the accusative case, singular form. 

Description: "Καρπόν" is used to refer to a fruit or the result of an action. It can also be used metaphorically to describe the outcome or consequence of something.
