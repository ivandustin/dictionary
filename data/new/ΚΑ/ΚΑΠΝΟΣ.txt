Etymology: The word "καπνός" (kapnos) comes from the Ancient Greek word "καπνός" (kapnos), meaning "smoke".

Grammar: In Greek, "καπνός" is a masculine noun in the singular form.

Description: "Καπνός" refers to smoke, the visible gas and particles that are produced when a substance undergoes combustion or pyrolysis. It is commonly associated with fire, tobacco, and other substances that produce smoke when burned.
