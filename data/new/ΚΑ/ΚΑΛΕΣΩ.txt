Etymology: The word "καλέσω" (kaleso) comes from the Greek verb "καλέω" (kaleo), which means "to call" or "to summon."

Grammar: "Καλέσω" is the first person singular future tense of the verb "καλέω" (kaleo). It is used to express an action that will happen in the future, specifically when the subject of the sentence is the first person singular pronoun "I."

Description: "Καλέσω" is a Greek word that means "I will call" or "I will summon." It is used to indicate an action of calling or summoning that will take place in the future.
