Etymology: The word "κατεσκευασμένων" comes from the Greek verb "κατασκευάζω" (kataskevazo), which means "to construct" or "to build."

Grammar: "κατεσκευασμένων" is the genitive plural form of the adjective "κατεσκευασμένος" (kataskevastos), which means "constructed" or "manufactured."

Description: "κατεσκευασμένων" is used to describe multiple objects or things that have been constructed or manufactured. It indicates that the objects in question have been made or built by someone or something.
