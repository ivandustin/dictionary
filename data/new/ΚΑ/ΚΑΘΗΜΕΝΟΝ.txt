Etymology: The word "καθημενον" comes from the Greek verb "καθέζομαι" (kathézomai), which means "to sit down" or "to be seated." It is derived from the preposition "κατά" (kata), meaning "down," and the verb "ἕζομαι" (hézomai), meaning "to sit."

Grammar: "καθημενον" is a Greek adjective in the masculine gender, singular number, and nominative case. It is in the present participle form of the verb "καθέζομαι" (kathézomai), which means "sitting" or "being seated." The adjective agrees with the noun it modifies in gender, number, and case.
