Etymology: The word "κατεχον" comes from the Greek verb "κατέχω" (katecho), which means "to hold" or "to possess."

Grammar: "κατεχον" is a noun in the neuter gender, singular number, and nominative case.

Description: "κατεχον" refers to something that is being held or possessed. It can also be used to describe a state of control or dominance over something.
