Etymology: The word "καρδιογνώστα" comes from the Greek words "καρδιά" (kardia) meaning "heart" and "γνώστης" (gnostis) meaning "knower" or "one who knows". 

Grammar: "καρδιογνώστα" is a noun in the singular form. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their gender, number, and case. 

Description: "καρδιογνώστα" refers to a person who has the ability to understand or know the hearts of others. It can be used to describe someone who is perceptive, empathetic, or insightful when it comes to understanding the emotions and feelings of others.
