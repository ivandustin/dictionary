Etymology: The word "κατεσκευασθη" comes from the Greek verb "κατασκευάζω" (kataskeuázō), which means "to construct" or "to build."

Grammar: "κατεσκευασθη" is the third person singular, aorist passive form of the verb "κατασκευάζω" (kataskeuázō). In Greek grammar, the aorist tense is used to describe a past action that is viewed as a whole, without specifying its duration or repetition. The passive voice indicates that the subject of the verb is receiving the action.
