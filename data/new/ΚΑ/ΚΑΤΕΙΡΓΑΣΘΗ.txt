Etymology: The word "κατειργασθη" comes from the Greek verb "κατεργάζομαι" (katergázomai), which means "to work out" or "to accomplish." It is derived from the prefix "κατ-" (kat-), meaning "down" or "through," and the verb "εργάζομαι" (ergázomai), meaning "to work."

Grammar: "κατειργασθη" is the third person singular, aorist passive form of the verb "κατεργάζομαι" (katergázomai). The aorist tense indicates a completed action in the past, and the passive voice indicates that the subject of the verb is being acted upon.
