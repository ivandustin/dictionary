Etymology: The word "καλοδιδασκαλους" comes from the Greek words "καλός" (kalos) meaning "good" and "διδάσκαλος" (didaskalos) meaning "teacher". 

Grammar: "καλοδιδασκαλους" is a noun in the masculine gender and plural number. It is formed by combining the adjective "καλός" (kalos) meaning "good" with the noun "διδάσκαλος" (didaskalos) meaning "teacher". 

Description: "καλοδιδασκαλους" refers to "good teachers" in Greek. It is used to describe educators who are skilled, knowledgeable, and effective in their teaching methods.
