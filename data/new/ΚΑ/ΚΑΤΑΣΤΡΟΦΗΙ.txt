Grammar: "Καταστροφή" is a feminine noun in the Greek language. It belongs to the third declension and has a genitive form of "καταστροφής" (katastrofís). In the plural form, it becomes "καταστροφές" (katastrofés).

Description: "Καταστροφή" refers to the act or process of destroying or ruining something completely. It can also be used to describe a catastrophic event or disaster.
