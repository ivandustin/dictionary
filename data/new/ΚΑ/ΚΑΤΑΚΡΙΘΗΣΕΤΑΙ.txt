Grammar: "κατακριθείται" is the third person singular form of the verb "κατακρίνω" in the passive voice. It is in the present tense and indicative mood. The passive voice indicates that the subject of the sentence is being acted upon or affected by the verb.

Description: "κατακριθείται" means "he/she/it is condemned" or "he/she/it is judged against" in English. It is used to express the idea that someone or something has been found guilty or deserving of punishment or disapproval.
