Grammar: "καθησεσθε" is the second person plural form of the verb "καθίζω" in the future tense. It is used to address a group of people and means "you will sit down" or "you will take a seat."

Description: "καθησεσθε" is a command or an instruction to a group of people to sit down or take a seat. It is commonly used in various contexts, such as in classrooms, theaters, or public gatherings, where people are asked to find a place to sit.
