Etymology: The word "καρδιαί" comes from the Greek word "καρδία" (kardia), which means "heart". 

Grammar: "καρδιαί" is a feminine noun in the plural form. 

Description: "καρδιαί" refers to the hearts, specifically the anatomical organ that pumps blood throughout the body. It can also be used metaphorically to represent emotions, feelings, or the center of one's being.
