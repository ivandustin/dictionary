Word: τοσουτοι

Etymology: τοσουτοι is a Greek word that is derived from the combination of two words, τόσος (tósos) meaning "so much" and οὗτος (hoûtos) meaning "this". Therefore, τοσουτοι means "so many of these".

Grammar: τοσουτοι is a plural adjective in the masculine gender, used to describe a noun. It agrees with the noun in gender, number, and case. For example, τοσούτοι άνθρωποι (tosoutoi ánthropoi) means "so many people".

Description: τοσουτοι is used to express a large number of something. It is often used in a rhetorical or exaggerated way to emphasize the quantity of something. It can also be used to express surprise or disbelief at the number of something.
