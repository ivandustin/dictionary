τουναντίον (tounantion) is a Greek adverb that means "on the contrary" or "contrariwise". 

Etymology: The word is derived from the combination of two Greek words, "τοῦ" (tou) meaning "of the" and "ναντίον" (nantion) meaning "opposite".

Grammar: τουναντίον is an adverb and does not change form based on gender, number, or case.

Description: The word is often used to introduce a contrasting idea or to indicate a reversal of a previously stated statement. It is commonly used in academic writing and formal speech.
