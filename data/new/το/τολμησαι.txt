Word: τολμησαι

Etymology: τολμησαι is a Greek verb that comes from the noun τόλμα (tolma), which means "boldness" or "courage". The verb τολμησαι means "to dare" or "to have the courage to do something".

Grammar: τολμησαι is a second person singular aorist active imperative verb in Greek. It is used to give a command or order to someone to dare or have the courage to do something.

Description: τολμησαι is a powerful and inspiring word that encourages people to take risks and be brave. It is often used in literature and poetry to describe heroic acts or moments of great courage. In modern Greek, it is still used in everyday language to encourage someone to take a bold step or to express admiration for someone who has shown courage.
