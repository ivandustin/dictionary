τοσαυτην (tosautēn) is a Greek word that is composed of two parts: τόσος (tósos) meaning "so much" and αὕτη (haútē) meaning "this". Together, τοσαυτην means "this much" or "so much". 

In terms of grammar, τοσαυτην is a feminine singular accusative form of the adjective τόσος. It is used to modify a feminine singular noun in the accusative case.

Example sentence: Είχε τοσαυτην όρεξη για φαγητό που έφαγε όλο το πιάτο. (Eíche tosautēn órexi gia fagitó pou éfage ólo to piáto.) - She had so much appetite for food that she ate the whole plate.
