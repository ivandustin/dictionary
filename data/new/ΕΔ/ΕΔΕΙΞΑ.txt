Etymology: The word "εδείξα" (edexa) comes from the Greek verb "δείχνω" (deichno), which means "to show" or "to indicate".

Grammar: "Εδείξα" is the first person singular past tense of the verb "δείχνω" (deichno), which means "I showed" or "I indicated".

Description: "Εδείξα" is a Greek word that refers to the action of showing or indicating something in the past. It is commonly used in everyday speech and writing to express past actions of showing or indicating.
