Grammar: "Εδιώξαν" is the third person plural, past tense form of the verb "διώκω" (dióko). In Greek grammar, verbs are conjugated based on tense, mood, voice, and person. The past tense indicates that the action of pursuing or chasing has already happened.

Description: "Εδιώξαν" is a Greek word that translates to "they pursued" or "they chased" in English. It describes an action where a group of people actively followed or went after someone or something.
