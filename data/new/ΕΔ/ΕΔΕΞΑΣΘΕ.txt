Grammar: "Εδεξασθε" is the second person plural aorist middle imperative form of the verb "δέχομαι" (dekhomai). In Greek grammar, the aorist tense is used to express a completed action in the past, and the middle voice indicates that the subject is performing the action on itself or for its own benefit.

Description: "Εδεξασθε" is a command or request addressed to a group of people, instructing them to receive or accept something. It can be used in various contexts, such as welcoming guests, accepting an invitation, or embracing an idea or belief.
