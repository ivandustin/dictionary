Etymology: The word "εδιδασκεν" comes from the Greek verb "διδάσκω" (didasko), which means "to teach" or "to instruct". The prefix "ε-" is added to indicate the present tense, third person singular form of the verb.

Grammar: "Εδίδασκεν" is the third person singular form of the verb "διδάσκω" in the imperfect tense. In Greek grammar, the imperfect tense is used to describe ongoing or repeated actions in the past.

Description: "Εδίδασκεν" is a Greek word that means "he/she/it was teaching" or "he/she/it used to teach". It is used to describe an action of teaching that was happening in the past, but was ongoing or repeated.
