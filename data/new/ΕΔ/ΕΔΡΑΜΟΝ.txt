Etymology: The word "εδραμον" (edramon) comes from the Greek verb "δραμαω" (dramao), which means "to run". The prefix "ε-" (e-) indicates a past tense form, so "εδραμον" can be translated as "I ran".

Grammar: "Εδραμον" is the first person singular past tense form of the verb "δραμαω" (dramao). In Greek, verbs have different forms depending on the tense, mood, and person. The past tense indicates an action that has already happened.

Description: "Εδραμον" is a word in the Greek language that specifically refers to the action of running in the past. It is used to describe an action that has already been completed.
