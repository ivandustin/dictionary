Grammar: "Εδωκας" is a verb in the Greek language. It is in the second person singular form, which means it is used when referring to one person who is the subject of the action. The aorist tense indicates a completed action in the past, and the indicative mood expresses a statement or a fact.

Description: "Εδωκας" translates to "you gave" in English. It is used to describe an action of giving that was completed in the past.
