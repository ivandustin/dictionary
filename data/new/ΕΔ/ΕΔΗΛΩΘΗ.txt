Etymology: The word "εδηλωθη" comes from the Greek verb "δηλώνω" (delóno), which means "to declare" or "to make known".

Grammar: "Εδηλώθη" is the past tense, passive voice, indicative mood form of the verb "δηλώνω" (delóno). It is used to indicate that something was declared or made known in the past.

Description: "Εδηλώθη" is a Greek word that means "was declared" or "was made known". It is used to describe an action or event that happened in the past and was announced or revealed to others.
