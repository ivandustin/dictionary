Etymology: The word "εδειξεν" comes from the Greek verb "δείχνω" (deíchno), which means "to show" or "to indicate".

Grammar: "Εδείξεν" is the third person singular form of the aorist tense of the verb "δείχνω" (deíchno) in Greek. It is used to express an action that occurred in the past.

Description: "Εδείξεν" is a word in the Greek language that means "he/she/it showed" or "he/she/it indicated". It is commonly used to describe an action of showing or indicating something in the past.
