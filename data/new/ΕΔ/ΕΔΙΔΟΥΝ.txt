Etymology: The word "εδιδούν" comes from the Greek verb "δίδωμι" (didomi), which means "to give". It is conjugated in the present tense, third person plural form.

Grammar: "Εδιδούν" is a verb in the present tense, third person plural form. In Greek grammar, verbs are conjugated to indicate the subject, tense, and mood of the action. The present tense indicates an action happening in the present time. The third person plural form indicates that the subject of the verb is a group of people or things.

Description: "Εδιδούν" is the present tense, third person plural form of the Greek verb "δίδωμι" (didomi), which means "to give". It is used to describe an action of giving that is happening in the present time and is performed by a group of people or things.
