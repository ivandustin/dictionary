Etymology: The word "εδιδαξαν" comes from the Greek verb "διδάσκω" (didasko), which means "to teach" or "to instruct". The prefix "ε-" is added to indicate the past tense, making "εδιδαξαν" the third person plural form of the verb in the past tense.

Grammar: "Εδιδαξαν" is a verb in the Greek language. It is in the third person plural form of the past tense. In Greek, verbs change their form based on the tense, mood, voice, and person of the subject.

Description: "Εδιδαξαν" is used to describe an action that was performed by a group of people in the past. It indicates that they taught or instructed someone or something.
