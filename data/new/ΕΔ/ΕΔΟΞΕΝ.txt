Grammar: "Εδοξεν" is a verb in the Greek language. It is in the third person singular form of the aorist tense, indicative mood, and active voice.

Description: "Εδοξεν" is used to express an action that occurred in the past. It indicates that something seemed or appeared to be a certain way. The verb can be used in various contexts and can be conjugated to match different subjects and tenses.
