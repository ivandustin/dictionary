Grammar: "αδαπανον" is a neuter singular adjective in the nominative case. In Greek, adjectives agree in gender, number, and case with the noun they modify. The neuter form is used when the adjective refers to a neuter noun.

Description: The adjective "αδαπανον" describes something that has not been spent or used up. It can be used to refer to money, resources, or any other type of material or immaterial possession that remains unused.
