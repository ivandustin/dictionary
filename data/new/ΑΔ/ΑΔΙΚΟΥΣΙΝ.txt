Grammar: "αδικουσιν" is a verb in the present tense, third person plural form. It is conjugated from the verb "αδικώ" (adiko), which means "to do injustice" or "to treat unfairly". The "-ουσιν" (-ousin) ending indicates the third person plural form.

Description: "αδικουσιν" is used to describe the action of a group of people doing injustice or treating others unfairly. It implies a violation of justice or moral principles.
