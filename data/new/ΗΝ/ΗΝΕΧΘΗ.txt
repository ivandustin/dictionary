The word "ηνεχθη" (enechthi) is a Greek verb in the aorist tense, third person singular. It comes from the root verb "ενέχω" (enecho), which means "to have" or "to hold." In this form, "ηνεχθη" is used to describe an action that happened in the past. It is commonly translated as "he/she/it had" or "he/she/it held."
