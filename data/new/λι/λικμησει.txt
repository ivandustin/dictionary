The word "λικμησει" (likmēsei) is a verb in the Greek language. 

Etymology: The word is derived from the root word "λικμάω" (likmaō), which means "to lick up" or "to lap up". 

Grammar: "λικμησει" is in the future tense, third person singular form. 

Description: The word can be translated to mean "he/she/it will lick up" or "he/she/it will lap up". It is often used in reference to animals drinking water or consuming food.
