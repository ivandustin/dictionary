Word: ξενους

Etymology: ξενους comes from the Greek word ξένος (xénos), which means "foreigner" or "stranger."

Grammar: ξενους is a noun in the masculine gender and singular number.

Description: ξενους refers to a person who is not a native or permanent resident of a particular place. It can also refer to someone who is unfamiliar or unknown. In ancient Greece, the term was often used to refer to foreigners who were not citizens of a particular city-state. Today, the word is still used in modern Greek to refer to foreigners or strangers.
