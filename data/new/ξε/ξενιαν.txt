The word "ξενιαν" (xenian) is a Greek verb that means "to host" or "to entertain guests". It comes from the Greek word "ξένος" (xenos), which means "stranger" or "foreigner". In terms of grammar, "ξενιαν" is a first conjugation verb in the present tense, active voice, and indicative mood. It can be used to describe the act of welcoming someone into one's home or providing hospitality to visitors.
