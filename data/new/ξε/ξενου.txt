The word "ξενου" (xenou) is a Greek adjective that means "foreign" or "strange". It is derived from the Greek word "ξένος" (xenos), which means "foreigner" or "guest". 

In terms of grammar, "ξενου" is in the masculine genitive singular form. It can be used to describe a noun in the genitive case, indicating that the noun is foreign or strange. For example, "του ξένου ανθρώπου" (tou xenou anthropou) means "of the foreign man".

Overall, "ξενου" is a useful word in Greek for describing something that is unfamiliar or foreign.
