Etymology: The word "γογγυσμών" comes from the Greek verb "γογγύζω" (gongyzō), which means "to grumble" or "to murmur."

Grammar: "Γογγυσμών" is a noun in the plural form. It belongs to the neuter gender and is declined as a second declension noun.

Description: "Γογγυσμών" refers to the act of grumbling or murmuring. It can be used to describe the sound or the act of complaining or expressing discontent in a low, indistinct, or continuous manner.
