Grammar: "Σαββατων" is a noun in the genitive case, singular number, masculine gender. It is the genitive form of the noun "σάββατον" (sabbaton), which means "Sabbath" or "week."

Description: "Σαββατων" is used in Greek to refer to the Sabbath day or the week. It can also be used metaphorically to mean a period of rest or a break from work.
