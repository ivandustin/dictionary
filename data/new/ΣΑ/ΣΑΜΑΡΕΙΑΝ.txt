Etymology: The word "σαμαρειαν" (samareian) comes from the Greek word "Σαμαρεία" (Samareia), which is the name of a region in ancient Palestine.

Grammar: "Σαμαρείαν" is a noun in the accusative case, singular form. It is declined according to the second declension in Greek grammar.

Description: "Σαμαρείαν" refers to someone or something that is related to or originates from Samaria, the historical region in Palestine.
