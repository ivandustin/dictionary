Etymology: The word "σαλπιστων" (salpistōn) comes from the Greek verb "σαλπίζω" (salpizō), which means "to sound a trumpet" or "to play a trumpet." 

Grammar: "Σαλπιστων" is a noun in the masculine gender and singular number. It belongs to the second declension in Greek grammar. 

Description: "Σαλπιστων" refers to a person who plays the trumpet or sounds a trumpet. It can also be used to describe a musician who specializes in playing the trumpet.
