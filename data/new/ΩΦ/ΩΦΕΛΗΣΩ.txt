Grammar: "Ωφελώ" is the first person singular present tense form of the verb "οφελέω". It is used to express the action of benefiting or being useful in the present time, when the subject of the sentence is "I" or "εγώ" in Greek.

Description: "Ωφελώ" is a Greek verb that conveys the idea of providing benefit or being useful. It is often used to describe actions or behaviors that have a positive impact or contribute to someone's well-being.
