Word: σεβομενων

The word σεβομενων is the present participle form of the Greek verb σέβομαι (sevomai), which means "to show respect" or "to revere." The verb comes from the noun σέβας (sevas), which means "reverence" or "awe."

Grammar:
σεβομενων is a present participle in the genitive case, masculine gender, and plural number. It is derived from the verb σέβομαι (sevomai), which is a first conjugation verb in the present tense.

The word σεβομενων is used to describe a group of people who are currently showing respect or reverence. It can be translated as "those who are showing respect" or "those who are revering."
