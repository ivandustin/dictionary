Word: σεβασμα

Etymology:
The word σεβασμα (sevasma) comes from the Greek verb σέβομαι (sevomai), which means "to show respect" or "to revere." The noun form σεβασμός (sevasmos) means "reverence" or "respect."

Grammar:
In Greek, σεβασμα is a neuter noun. Its plural form is σεβασματα (sevasmata).

Description:
σεβασμα refers to an act of showing respect or reverence towards someone or something. It can also refer to a physical object that is used to show respect, such as a religious icon or a symbol of authority. The word is often used in religious contexts, but can also be used in secular contexts to describe a gesture of respect or honor.
