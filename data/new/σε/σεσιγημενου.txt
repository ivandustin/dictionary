Word: σεσιγημένου

The word σεσιγημένου is derived from the Greek verb σιγάω (sigao), which means "to be silent" or "to keep quiet". The verb is conjugated in the perfect passive participle form, which indicates that the action of being silent has been completed in the past and the subject is currently in a state of silence.

σεσιγημένου is a perfect passive participle in the genitive case, masculine gender, and singular number. The word σεσιγημένου can be translated to English as "of the one who has been silenced" or "of the one who has kept quiet".
