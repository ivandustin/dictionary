The word "σεβομενου" (sevomenou) is a present participle in Greek, derived from the verb "σέβομαι" (sevomai), which means "to respect" or "to honor". The present participle form indicates that the action of respecting or honoring is currently ongoing or in progress. 

In terms of grammar, "σεβομενου" is in the genitive case, which is used to indicate possession or relationship. It can be translated as "of respecting" or "of honoring". 

Overall, "σεβομενου" is a word that emphasizes the importance of showing respect and honor towards others, and it highlights the ongoing nature of this action.
