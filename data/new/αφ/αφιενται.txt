Word: αφιενται

Etymology: The word αφιενται is derived from the Greek verb αφίημι (aphiemi), which means "to let go" or "to release".

Grammar: αφιενται is a third-person plural present indicative passive form of the verb αφίημι (aphiemi). It is used to indicate that something is being released or let go by someone or something else.

Description: αφιενται is a Greek verb that is commonly used in everyday language. It can be used in a variety of contexts, such as when referring to someone letting go of a physical object, or when describing a situation where someone is releasing control or responsibility over something.
