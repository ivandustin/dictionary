Etymology: The word "αφορίζω" comes from the prefix "από" (apo), meaning "away from", and the verb "ορίζω" (orizo), meaning "to define" or "to limit". 

Grammar: As mentioned earlier, "αφοριουσιν" is the third person plural form of the present tense of the verb "αφορίζω". In Greek, verbs change their endings to indicate the tense, voice, and person.
