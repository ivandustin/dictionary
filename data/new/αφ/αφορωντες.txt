The word "αφορώντες" is a Greek verb in the present participle form. It comes from the verb "αφορώ" which means "to concern" or "to relate to". The present participle form indicates that the action is ongoing or continuous. 

In terms of grammar, "αφορώντες" is in the masculine plural form, which means it refers to a group of males or a mixed group of males and females. 

Overall, "αφορώντες" can be translated to "concerning" or "related to" in English.
