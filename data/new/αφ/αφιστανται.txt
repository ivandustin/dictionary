Word: αφιστανται

Etymology: The word αφιστανται is derived from the Greek verb αφίστημι (aphístēmi), which means "to depart" or "to withdraw".

Grammar: αφιστανται is a third-person plural present indicative active verb in Greek. It is conjugated from the verb αφίστημι (aphístēmi), which is a regular verb in Greek.

Description: αφιστανται is a Greek verb that means "they depart" or "they withdraw". It is commonly used in everyday speech and writing in Greece. The verb can be used in various contexts, such as to describe people leaving a place, withdrawing from a situation, or ending a relationship.
