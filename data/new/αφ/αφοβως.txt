Word: αφοβως

Etymology: αφοβως is an adverb derived from the Greek word αφόβως (aphóbos), which means "without fear" or "fearlessly". The word is composed of the prefix α- (a-), which means "not", and the noun φόβος (fóbos), which means "fear".

Grammar: αφοβως is an adverb in Greek, which means that it modifies a verb, an adjective, or another adverb. It is used to describe the manner in which an action is performed, indicating that it is done without fear.

Description: αφοβως is a word that describes a state of being fearless or acting without fear. It can be used to describe a person who is brave, courageous, or confident in the face of danger or uncertainty. It can also be used to describe an action that is performed with confidence and without hesitation. Overall, αφοβως is a powerful word that conveys a sense of strength and fearlessness.
