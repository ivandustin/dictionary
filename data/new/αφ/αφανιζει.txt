Word: αφανίζει

Etymology: The word αφανίζει comes from the Greek verb αφανίζω (afanízo), which means "to make disappear" or "to vanish". It is derived from the adjective αφανής (afanís), which means "invisible" or "unseen".

Grammar: αφανίζει is a present tense verb in the third person singular form. It is conjugated from the verb αφανίζω (afanízo), which is a regular verb in Greek.

Description: αφανίζει is a verb that describes the action of making something disappear or vanish. It can be used in various contexts, such as making an object disappear, erasing a memory, or causing someone to disappear. It is a common verb in Greek and is used in everyday conversation.
