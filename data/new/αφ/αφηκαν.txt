Word: αφηκαν

Etymology: The word αφηκαν is derived from the Greek verb αφήνω (afíno), which means "to leave" or "to let go".

Grammar: αφηκαν is the third person plural past tense form of the verb αφήνω (afíno). It is in the indicative mood and the active voice.

Description: αφηκαν means "they left" or "they let go" in English. It is a verb that describes an action of leaving something or someone behind. The context in which it is used can vary, but it generally implies a sense of abandonment or separation.
