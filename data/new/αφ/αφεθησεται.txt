The word αφεθησεται is a Greek word that comes from the verb αφίημι (aphiemi), which means "to let go" or "to release". It is a compound word made up of the prefix από (apo), meaning "away from", and the verb ίημι (iemi), meaning "to send" or "to let go".

αφεθησεται can be translated to English as "it will be forgiven" or "it will be let go".
