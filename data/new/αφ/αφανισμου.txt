The word "αφανισμός" (afanismos) is a Greek noun that means "disappearance" or "vanishing". 

Etymology: The word comes from the verb "αφανίζω" (afanizo), which means "to make disappear" or "to vanish". The suffix "-ισμός" (-ismos) is added to the verb stem to form the noun.

Grammar: "Αφανισμός" is a masculine noun in the singular form. Its plural form is "αφανισμοί" (afanismoi).

Description: The word is often used to describe the act of something or someone disappearing or vanishing without a trace. It can also be used metaphorically to describe the gradual disappearance or decline of something over time.
