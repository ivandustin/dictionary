The word "σπιλαδες" (spilades) is a Greek noun that refers to spots or blemishes on the skin. It is derived from the Greek word "σπίλος" (spilos), which means spot or stain. In terms of grammar, "σπιλαδες" is a plural noun in the nominative case. It can also be declined in other cases, such as genitive, dative, and accusative.
