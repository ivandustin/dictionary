Word: σπουδάζοντες

Etymology: The word σπουδάζοντες comes from the Greek verb σπουδάζω (spoudazo), which means "to be diligent" or "to study hard". The suffix -οντες (-ontes) indicates that the word is in the present participle form, meaning "those who are studying" or "those who are diligent".

Grammar: σπουδάζοντες is a present participle in the active voice, masculine gender, and plural number. It is derived from the verb σπουδάζω, which is a first conjugation verb in the present tense. The word can function as a noun or an adjective, depending on the context.

Description: σπουδάζοντες is a Greek word that refers to people who are studying or working diligently. It can be used to describe students, scholars, or anyone who is dedicated to their work or studies. The word has a positive connotation and implies a sense of hard work and dedication.
