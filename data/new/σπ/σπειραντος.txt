The word σπειραντος (speirantos) is a Greek participle that comes from the verb σπείρω (speirō), which means "to sow" or "to scatter." The participle form σπειραντος can be translated as "sowing" or "scattering." 

In terms of grammar, σπειραντος is a present active participle, which means that it describes an ongoing action that is currently happening. It is also in the masculine nominative singular form, which means that it refers to a singular male subject who is performing the action of sowing or scattering.

Overall, σπειραντος is a word that describes the act of sowing or scattering, and it is commonly used in agricultural contexts.
