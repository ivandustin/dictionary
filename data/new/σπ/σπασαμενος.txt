Word: σπασαμένος

Etymology:
The word σπασαμένος is derived from the Greek verb σπάω (spao), which means "to break" or "to shatter". The suffix -μένος (-menos) is a participle ending that indicates the action of the verb has been completed.

Grammar:
σπασαμένος is a masculine singular participle in the nominative case. It is derived from the verb σπάω in the aorist tense, which means that the action of breaking or shattering has been completed in the past.

Description:
σπασαμένος describes someone or something that has been broken or shattered. It can also be used figuratively to describe someone who is emotionally or mentally shattered. The word can be used as an adjective to modify a noun, or as a predicate in a sentence. For example, "Ο σπασαμένος ποτήρι έπεσε στο πάτωμα" (The broken glass fell on the floor).
