The word "σποριμων" (sporimōn) is a Greek noun that means "sower" or "planter". It is derived from the verb "σπείρω" (speirō), which means "to sow" or "to scatter". In terms of grammar, "σποριμων" is a masculine noun in the nominative case. It can also be declined in other cases, such as the genitive "σποριμόνος" (sporimónos) or the accusative "σποριμῶνα" (sporimōna).
