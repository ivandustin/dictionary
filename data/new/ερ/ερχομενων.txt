Word: ερχομενων

Etymology: 
The word ερχομενων is the present participle of the Greek verb ερχομαι (erchomai), which means "to come". The word is derived from the Proto-Indo-European root *h₁erǵʰ- which means "to move, to go".

Grammar:
ερχομενων is a present participle in the genitive plural form. In Greek, present participles are formed by adding the suffix -οντας (-ontas) to the stem of the verb. The genitive plural form indicates that the word is describing a group of people or things that are coming.

Description:
ερχομενων can be translated to "of those who are coming" or "coming ones". It is often used in Greek to describe a group of people who are arriving or approaching a certain location. For example, "οι ερχομενοι" (oi erchomenoi) can be translated to "the ones who are coming" or "the arrivals".
