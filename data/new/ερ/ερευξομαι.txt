The word ερευξομαι (ereuxomai) is a Greek verb that comes from the root word ερευνα (ereuna), which means "to search" or "to investigate". The verb ερευξομαι (ereuxomai) means "to search out" or "to discover".

ερευξομαι (ereuxomai) is a middle voice verb in the present tense. In Greek, the middle voice is used to indicate that the subject is performing the action on itself. The verb is conjugated as follows:

ερευνώ (ereuno) - I search

ερευνούμε (ereunoume) - we search
