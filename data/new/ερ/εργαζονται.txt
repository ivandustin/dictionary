Word: εργαζονται

Etymology: The word εργαζονται comes from the Greek verb εργάζομαι (ergázomai), which means "to work" or "to labor". It is derived from the noun έργο (érgo), which means "work" or "task".

Grammar: εργαζονται is a present tense verb in the third person plural form. It is a regular verb and follows the conjugation pattern of the verb εργάζομαι.

Description: εργαζονται is a Greek verb that means "they work" or "they are working". It is commonly used in everyday language to describe someone's occupation or employment status. The verb can be used in various contexts, such as in the workplace, at home, or in any other setting where work is being done.
