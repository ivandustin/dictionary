Word: ερωτησω

Etymology:
The word ερωτησω (erotiso) comes from the Greek verb ερωτάω (erotao), which means "to ask" or "to inquire". It is derived from the noun ερωτημα (erotima), which means "question".

Grammar:
ερωτησω is a first-person singular future tense verb in Greek. It is conjugated from the verb ερωτάω (erotao) and means "I will ask" or "I will inquire".

Description:
ερωτησω is a common verb in Greek and is used in everyday conversation. It is often used to ask questions or to inquire about something. The verb can be used in a variety of contexts, such as in formal or informal situations, and can be used to ask for information, clarification, or advice.
