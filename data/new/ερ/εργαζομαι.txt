Word: εργαζομαι

Etymology: The word εργαζομαι (ergazomai) comes from the ancient Greek word ἐργάζομαι (ergázomai), which means "to work" or "to labor". It is derived from the root word ἔργον (érgon), which means "work" or "task".

Grammar: εργαζομαι is a verb in the present tense, first person singular form. It is conjugated as follows:

εργάζομαι (ergázomai) - I work
εργάζεσαι (ergázesai) - you work
εργάζεται (ergázetai) - he/she/it works
εργαζόμαστε (ergazómaste) - we work
εργάζεστε (ergázeste) - you work (plural)
εργάζονται (ergázontai) - they work

Description: εργαζομαι is a common Greek verb that means "to work" or "to be employed". It can refer to any type of work, whether it is paid or unpaid, and can be used in a variety of contexts. It is often used in the present tense to describe someone's current employment status or daily work routine.
