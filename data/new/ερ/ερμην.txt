The word "ερμην" (ermēn) is a Greek noun that means "interpreter" or "translator". It is derived from the verb "ερμηνεύω" (ermēneuō), which means "to interpret" or "to translate". In terms of grammar, "ερμην" is a masculine noun in the nominative singular form. It can also be declined in other cases and numbers, such as the genitive singular "ερμηνοῦ" (ermēnou) or the accusative plural "ερμηνεῖς" (ermēneis).
