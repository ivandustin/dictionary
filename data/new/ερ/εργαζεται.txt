Word: εργαζεται

Etymology: The word εργαζεται comes from the Greek verb εργάζομαι (ergázomai), which means "to work" or "to labor". It is derived from the noun έργο (érgo), which means "work" or "task".

Grammar: εργαζεται is a third person singular present indicative form of the verb εργάζομαι. It is used to describe someone who is currently working or laboring.

Description: εργαζεται is a common Greek verb that is used to describe someone who is working or laboring. It can be used to describe any type of work, from manual labor to office work. The verb is often used in the present tense to describe someone's current employment status. For example, "Ο Γιάννης εργάζεται σε μια τράπεζα" (Yiannis works at a bank).
