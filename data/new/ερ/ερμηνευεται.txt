The word "ερμηνευεται" is a verb in the Greek language. Its etymology can be traced back to the ancient Greek word "ἑρμηνεύω" (hermeneuō), which means "to interpret" or "to explain." 

In terms of grammar, "ερμηνευεται" is a present tense, passive voice, indicative mood verb. It is conjugated from the root word "ερμηνευ-" and is used to describe an action that is being done to the subject of the sentence.

In everyday usage, "ερμηνευεται" is often used to mean "is interpreted" or "is explained." It is commonly used in academic or legal contexts when discussing the interpretation of a text or document.
