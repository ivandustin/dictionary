The word "εργων" (ergon) is a Greek noun that means "work" or "task". It is derived from the Greek verb "εργάζομαι" (ergazomai), which means "to work" or "to labor". 

In terms of grammar, "εργων" is a masculine noun in the nominative singular form. It can also be declined in other cases, such as the genitive case "εργου" (ergou) or the accusative case "εργον" (ergon).

In description, "εργων" can refer to any type of work or task, whether it be physical labor or mental effort. It can also be used in a more abstract sense to refer to a person's life's work or legacy.
