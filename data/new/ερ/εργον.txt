The word "εργον" (ergon) is a Greek noun that means "work" or "task". It is derived from the Greek verb "εργαζομαι" (ergazomai), which means "to work" or "to labor". In terms of grammar, "εργον" is a neuter noun in the singular form. It can be used in various contexts, such as referring to a specific job or occupation, a physical task or labor, or even a spiritual or intellectual endeavor.
