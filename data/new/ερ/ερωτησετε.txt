Word: ερωτησετε

Etymology: The word ερωτησετε comes from the Greek verb ερωτάω (erotao), which means "to ask" or "to inquire". The suffix -σετε (-sete) is the second person plural ending in the future tense, indicating that the word means "you will ask" or "you will inquire".

Grammar: ερωτησετε is a verb in the future tense, second person plural form. It is used to address a group of people and indicates that they will ask or inquire about something.

Description: ερωτησετε is a common Greek verb that is used in everyday conversation. It is often used to ask questions or to inquire about something.
