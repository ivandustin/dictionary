Word: ερχομενους

Etymology:
The word ερχομενους (erchomenous) is a present participle form of the Greek verb ερχομαι (erchomai), which means "to come". The word is derived from the Proto-Indo-European root *h₁erǵʰ- which means "to move, to go".

Grammar:
ερχομενους is a present participle form of the verb ερχομαι (erchomai) in the masculine accusative plural. It is used to describe a group of people who are currently coming or arriving at a particular place.

Description:
The word ερχομενους (erchomenous) is commonly used in Greek language to describe a group of people who are coming or arriving at a particular place. It can be used in various contexts, such as in a sentence like "Οι ερχόμενοι επισκέπτες θα φιλοξενηθούν στο ξενοδοχείο" (The arriving guests will be accommodated at the hotel).
