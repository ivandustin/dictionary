Word: ερχομενη

Etymology: 
The word ερχομενη (erchomeni) is the present participle of the Greek verb ερχομαι (erchomai), which means "to come". The word is derived from the Proto-Indo-European root *h₁erǵʰ- which means "to move, to go".

Grammar:
ερχομενη is a feminine singular form of the present participle of the verb ερχομαι (erchomai). It is in the nominative case, which means it is the subject of the sentence. The word can be translated as "coming" or "arriving".

Description:
ερχομενη is a Greek word that describes a female person or thing that is currently coming or arriving. It can be used in various contexts, such as describing a person who is walking towards a destination, a ship that is approaching a port, or a storm that is moving towards a city.
