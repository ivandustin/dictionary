The word "ερμηνιαν" (hermēnian) is a Greek noun that means "interpretation" or "explanation". It is derived from the verb "ερμηνεύω" (hermēneuō), which means "to interpret" or "to explain". In terms of grammar, "ερμηνιαν" is a singular accusative noun in the feminine gender. It is often used in the context of interpreting religious texts or explaining complex ideas.
