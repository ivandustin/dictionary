Etymology: The word "φυλαξαι" (pronounced "filaksi") comes from the Greek verb "φυλάσσω" (pronounced "filasso"), which means "to guard" or "to protect". 

Grammar: "Φυλαξαι" is the imperative form of the verb "φυλάσσω" in the second person plural. It is used to give a command or instruction to a group of people, telling them to guard or protect something.

Description: "Φυλαξαι" is a Greek word that means "guard" or "protect". It is often used in contexts where there is a need to protect something or someone from harm or danger.
