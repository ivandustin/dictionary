Grammar: "Φυσικως" is an adverb in Greek. It is the adverbial form of the adjective "φυσικός" (physikos), which means "natural" or "pertaining to nature". In English, it can be translated as "naturally" or "of course".

Description: "Φυσικως" is used to express something that is expected or obvious based on natural laws or principles. It is often used to indicate that something is a logical or natural consequence of a situation.
