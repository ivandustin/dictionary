Etymology: The word "φυτεία" (pronounced "fy-tei-a") comes from the Greek word "φυτό" (pronounced "fy-to"), which means "plant" or "vegetation". 

Grammar: "Φυτεία" is a feminine noun in Greek. It is in the singular form. 

Description: "Φυτεία" refers to a plantation or a cultivated area where plants, especially crops or trees, are grown. It can also be used to describe a nursery or a garden.
