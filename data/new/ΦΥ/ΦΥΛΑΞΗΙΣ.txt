Etymology: The word "φυλαξης" (pronounced "filaxis") comes from the Greek verb "φυλάσσω" (pronounced "filasso"), which means "to guard" or "to protect".

Description: "Φυλαξης" refers to the act of guarding or protecting. It can also be used to describe a guard or protector.
