Etymology: The word "φρουρουμένους" comes from the Greek verb "φρουρέω" (phrouréo), which means "to guard" or "to protect". It is derived from the noun "φρούρημα" (phrourema), meaning "a guard" or "a watchtower".

Grammar: "Φρουρουμένους" is the accusative plural form of the verb "φρουρέω" (phrouréo). It is used to refer to multiple individuals who are being guarded or protected.
