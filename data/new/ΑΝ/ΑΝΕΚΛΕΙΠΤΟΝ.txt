Etymology: The word "ανεκλείπτον" comes from the Greek roots "αν" (meaning "not") and "εκλείπτω" (meaning "to cease" or "to fail"). 

Grammar: "Ανεκλείπτον" is an adjective in the Greek language. It is in the masculine gender, singular number, and nominative case. 

Description: The word "ανεκλείπτον" means "unceasing" or "incessant" in English. It describes something that does not cease or fail.
