Etymology: The word "ανεμών" (anemon) comes from the Greek word "άνεμος" (ánemos), meaning "wind". It is derived from the Proto-Indo-European root *h₂wéh₁n̥ts.

Grammar: "Ανεμών" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the masculine gender and singular number.
