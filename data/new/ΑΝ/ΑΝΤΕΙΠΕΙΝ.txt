Etymology: The word "αντειπειν" comes from the Greek roots "αντι-" meaning "against" and "ειπειν" meaning "to speak". 

Grammar: "αντειπειν" is a verb in the Greek language. It is in the present tense, active voice, and indicative mood. 

Description: "αντειπειν" means "to contradict" or "to argue against" in Greek. It refers to the act of expressing disagreement or opposing someone's statement or opinion.
