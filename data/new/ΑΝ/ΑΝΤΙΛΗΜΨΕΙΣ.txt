Etymology: The word "αντιλημψεις" comes from the Greek language. It is derived from the combination of the prefix "αντι-" (anti-), meaning "against" or "opposite," and the noun "λημψη" (limpsi), meaning "perception" or "apprehension."

Grammar: "Αντιλημψεις" is a plural noun in the Greek language. It belongs to the third declension and is of feminine gender. In its singular form, it is "αντιλημψη" (antilimpsi).

Description: "Αντιλημψεις" refers to perceptions or apprehensions that are contrary or opposite to something. It can be used to describe a different understanding or interpretation of a situation or concept.
