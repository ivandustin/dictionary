Etymology: The word "αναστροφης" (anastrofis) comes from the Greek verb "αναστρεφω" (anastrefo), which means "to turn upside down" or "to invert". 

Grammar: "Αναστροφης" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the genitive case and singular number. 

Description: "Αναστροφης" refers to the act of inverting or turning something upside down. It can also be used metaphorically to describe a reversal or change in direction.
