Grammar: "ανεζησεν" is the third person singular form of the aorist tense of the verb "ανεζάω" (anezáo). In Greek, verbs have different forms depending on the tense, mood, voice, and person. The aorist tense is used to express a completed action in the past.

Description: "ανεζησεν" means "he/she/it lived" in English. It refers to a past action of living or being alive.
