- Nominative: αντλησαία (antlésaia)
- Genitive: αντλησαίας (antlésaias)
- Accusative: αντλησαίαν (antlésaian)
- Vocative: αντλησαία (antlésaia)

Plural:
- Nominative: αντλησαίες (antlésaies)
- Genitive: αντλησαίων (antlésaion)
- Accusative: αντλησαίες (antlésaies)
- Vocative: αντλησαίες (antlésaies)

Description: "Αντλησαί" refers to pumps or pumping devices. It is used to describe mechanical devices that are used to draw out or move fluids, such as water or oil, from one place to another. Pumps are commonly used in various industries, including agriculture, construction, and manufacturing.
