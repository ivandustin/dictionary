Etymology: The word "αναδοντες" comes from the Greek verb "αναδίδωμι" (anadídōmi), which means "to give back" or "to return." It is derived from the prefix "ανα-" (ana-), meaning "again" or "back," and the verb "δίδωμι" (dídōmi), meaning "to give."

Grammar: "Αναδοντες" is the present participle form of the verb "αναδίδωμι" (anadídōmi) in Greek. The present participle is used to describe ongoing or continuous actions. In this case, it refers to the act of giving back or returning.

Description: "Αναδοντες" is a Greek word that describes the action of giving back or returning something. It can be used to refer to the act of returning an object, repaying a debt, or giving something back to its original owner.
