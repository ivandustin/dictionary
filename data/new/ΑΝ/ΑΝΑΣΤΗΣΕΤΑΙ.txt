Grammar: "Αναστησεται" is the third person singular form of the future tense of the verb "ανασταίνομαι" (anastainomai), which is a reflexive verb. In Greek grammar, reflexive verbs indicate that the subject of the verb performs the action on itself. The future tense indicates an action that will happen in the future.
