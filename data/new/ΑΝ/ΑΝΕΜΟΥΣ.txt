Etymology: The word "ανεμος" (anemos) comes from the Ancient Greek word meaning "wind". 

Grammar: "Ανεμος" is a masculine noun in Greek. It belongs to the second declension and has the nominative singular form "ανεμος". 

Description: "Ανεμος" refers to the natural phenomenon of wind, the movement of air in the atmosphere. It can also be used metaphorically to describe something that is fleeting or changeable.
