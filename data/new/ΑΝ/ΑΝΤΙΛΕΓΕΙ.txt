Etymology: The word "αντιλεγει" comes from the Greek roots "αντι-" meaning "against" or "opposite" and "λεγειν" meaning "to speak". 

Grammar: "αντιλεγει" is a verb in the present tense, third person singular form. It is derived from the verb "αντιλέγω" which means "to contradict" or "to argue against". 

Description: "αντιλεγει" is used to describe the action of contradicting or arguing against someone or something. It implies a disagreement or opposition to a statement or opinion.
