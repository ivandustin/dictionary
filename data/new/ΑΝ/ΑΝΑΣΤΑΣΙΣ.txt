Grammar: "Αναστασις" is a feminine noun in Greek. It belongs to the third declension and has the genitive form "αναστασεως" (anastaseōs). In the plural form, it becomes "αναστασεις" (anastaseis).

Description: "Αναστασις" is a Greek word that means "resurrection" or "rising again". It is often used in religious contexts to refer to the resurrection of Jesus Christ in Christianity.
