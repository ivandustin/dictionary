Grammar: "Ανεδείξεν" is the third person singular aorist indicative active form of the verb "αναδεικνύω" (anadeiknyo). In Greek grammar, the aorist tense is used to express a past action that is viewed as a whole, without specifying its duration or repetition.

Description: "Ανεδείξεν" is a verb form that indicates that someone or something showed or demonstrated something in the past. It can be translated as "he/she/it showed" or "he/she/it demonstrated" in English.
