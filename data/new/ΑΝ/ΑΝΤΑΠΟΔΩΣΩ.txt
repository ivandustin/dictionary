Etymology: The word "ανταποδώσω" (antapodóso) comes from the combination of the prefix "αντι-" (anti-), meaning "against" or "in return," and the verb "αποδίδωμι" (apodídomi), meaning "to give back" or "to return."

Grammar: "Ανταποδώσω" is a verb in the first person singular form of the future tense in Greek. It belongs to the conjugation pattern of verbs ending in "-ω" (o) and follows regular grammar rules.

Description: "Ανταποδώσω" means "I will give back" or "I will return" in English. It implies the action of reciprocating or responding in kind to something that has been given or done to you.
