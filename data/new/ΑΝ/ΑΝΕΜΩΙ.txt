Etymology: The word "ανεμών" (anemón) comes from the Greek word "άνεμος" (ánemos), meaning "wind".

Grammar: "Ανεμών" is a noun in the masculine gender. It is in the nominative case and singular number.

Description: "Ανεμών" refers to the wind, the natural movement of air in the atmosphere. It can also be used metaphorically to describe something that is constantly changing or unpredictable.
