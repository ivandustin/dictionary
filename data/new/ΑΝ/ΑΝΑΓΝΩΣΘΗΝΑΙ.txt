Etymology: The word "αναγνωσθείναι" comes from the Greek verb "αναγινώσκω" (anaginósko), which means "to read." The suffix "-θηναι" (-thēnai) is added to the verb stem to form the passive infinitive form.

Grammar: "Αναγνωσθείναι" is the passive infinitive form of the verb "αναγινώσκω" (anaginósko). In Greek grammar, the passive infinitive is used to express an action that is being done to the subject of the sentence.

Description: "Αναγνωσθείναι" is a verb form that indicates the action of reading in the passive voice. It is used when the subject of the sentence is the receiver of the action rather than the doer.
