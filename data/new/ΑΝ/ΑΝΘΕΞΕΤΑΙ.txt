Grammar: "Ανθεξεται" is the third person singular form of the verb "ανθέχομαι" in the future tense. It is used to describe a future action or state of endurance or perseverance.

Description: "Ανθεξεται" can be translated as "he/she/it will endure" or "he/she/it will bear up under." It implies the ability to withstand or tolerate difficult or challenging circumstances.
