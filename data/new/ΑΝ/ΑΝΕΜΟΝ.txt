Etymology: The word "ανεμον" (anemon) comes from the Greek word "ἄνεμος" (anemos), meaning "wind".

Grammar: "Ανεμον" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the masculine gender and in the singular number.

Description: "Ανεμον" refers to the Greek word for "wind". It is used to describe the natural movement of air in the atmosphere. The wind can vary in intensity and direction, and it plays a significant role in weather patterns and climate.
