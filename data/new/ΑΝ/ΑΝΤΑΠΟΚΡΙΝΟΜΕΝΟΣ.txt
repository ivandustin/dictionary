Etymology: The word "ανταποκρινόμενος" (antapokrinomenos) comes from the Greek verb "ανταποκρίνομαι" (antapokrinomai), which means "to answer" or "to respond". The word is derived from the prefix "αντι-" (anti-), meaning "against" or "in return", and the verb "αποκρίνομαι" (apokrinomai), meaning "to answer" or "to reply".

Grammar: "Ανταποκρινόμενος" is the present participle form of the verb "ανταποκρίνομαι" (antapokrinomai). In Greek grammar, the present participle is used to describe ongoing or continuous actions. It can function as an adjective or as part of a verb phrase.

Description: "Ανταποκρινόμενος" describes someone or something that is responding or answering. It implies an active engagement in communication or interaction.
