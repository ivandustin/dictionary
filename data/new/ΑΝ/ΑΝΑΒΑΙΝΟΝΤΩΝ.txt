Etymology: The word "αναβαινοντων" comes from the Greek verb "αναβαίνω" (anabaino), which means "to go up" or "to ascend". The suffix "-ντων" (-nton) is added to the verb stem to indicate the present participle form.

Grammar: "Αναβαινοντων" is the present participle form of the verb "αναβαίνω" (anabaino) in Greek. It is used to describe an ongoing action of going up or ascending. In this form, it agrees with the plural subject.

Description: "Αναβαινοντων" is a Greek word that can be translated as "ascending" or "going up". It is often used in contexts related to movement or progression upwards.
