Etymology: The word "αναμιμνησκω" (anamimnēskō) comes from the Greek roots "ana" meaning "again" and "mimnēskō" meaning "to remember". 

Grammar: "Αναμιμνησκω" is a verb in the Greek language. It belongs to the first conjugation class and is conjugated in the present tense as follows: εγώ αναμιμνησκω (anamimnīskō) - I remember, εσύ αναμιμνησκεις (anamimnīskeis) - you remember, αυτός/αυτή/αυτό αναμιμνησκει (anamimnīskei) - he/she/it remembers, εμείς αναμιμνησκουμε (anamimnīskoume) - we remember, εσείς αναμιμνησκετε (anamimnīskete) - you remember, αυτοί/αυτές αναμιμνησκουν (anamimnīskoun) - they remember.

Description: "Αναμιμνησκω" is a Greek verb that means "to remember" or "to recall". It is used to express the action of bringing something back to one's mind or memory. It can also be used in a figurative sense to mean "to remind" or "to bring to mind".
