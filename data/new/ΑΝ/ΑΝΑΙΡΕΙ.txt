Etymology: The word "αναίρει" comes from the Greek verb "αναιρώ" (anaíro), which means "to cancel" or "to annul". 

Grammar: "Αναίρει" is the third person singular form of the present tense of the verb "αναιρώ" (anaíro). It is used to describe an action of canceling or annulling something in the present time.
