Etymology: The word "αναγνωντες" comes from the Greek verb "αναγινώσκω" (anaginósko), which means "to read." It is derived from the prefix "ανα-" (ana-), meaning "again" or "back," and the verb "γινώσκω" (ginósko), meaning "to know" or "to recognize."

Grammar: "Αναγνωντες" is the plural form of the noun "αναγνώστης" (anagnostis), which means "reader" in Greek. It is used to refer to multiple readers.

Description: "Αναγνωντες" is a Greek word that specifically refers to more than one person who reads. It can be used to describe a group of individuals who engage in reading activities or have a shared interest in reading.
