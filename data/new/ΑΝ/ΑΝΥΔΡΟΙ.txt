Etymology: The word "ανυδροι" (anudroi) comes from the Greek roots "αν" (an), meaning "without," and "υδρος" (ydros), meaning "water." 

Grammar: "Ανυδροι" is a plural noun in the masculine gender. 

Description: "Ανυδροι" refers to people or places that are without water. It can be used to describe arid or desert regions, as well as individuals or communities that lack access to water sources.
