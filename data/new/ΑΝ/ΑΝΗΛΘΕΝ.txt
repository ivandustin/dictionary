Grammar: "ανηλθεν" is the aorist tense form of the verb "αναβαίνω" (anabaino) in the third person singular. The aorist tense is used to describe a completed action in the past.

Description: "ανηλθεν" is used to indicate that someone or something went up or ascended in the past. It can be used in various contexts, such as describing physical movement or metaphorical ascent.
