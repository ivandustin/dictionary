- First person singular: αναπαύομαι (anapaúomai) - I rest
- Second person singular: αναπαύεσαι (anapaúesai) - You rest
- Third person singular: αναπεπαυται (anapepautai) - He/she/it rests
- First person plural: αναπαύομαστε (anapaúomaste) - We rest
- Second person plural: αναπαύεσθε (anapaúesthe) - You rest
