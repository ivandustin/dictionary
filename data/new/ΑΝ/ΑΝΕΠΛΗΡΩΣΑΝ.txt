Etymology: The word "ανεπληρωσαν" comes from the Greek verb "ανεπληρωσα" (aneplirosa), which is the aorist tense of the verb "αναπληροω" (anapliróo). 

Grammar: "ανεπληρωσαν" is the third person plural form of the verb "ανεπληρωσα" in the aorist tense. It is used to indicate that a group of people completed an action in the past.

Description: "ανεπληρωσαν" is a Greek verb that means "they completed" or "they fulfilled." It is used to describe a past action that has been finished or accomplished by a group of people.
