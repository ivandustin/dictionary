Etymology: The word "ανακαμψω" (anakampso) comes from the Greek roots "ανα-" (ana-), meaning "again" or "back," and "καμπτω" (kampto), meaning "to bend" or "to turn." 

Grammar: "Ανακαμψω" is a verb in the Greek language. It belongs to the first person singular form of the aorist tense. 

Description: "Ανακαμψω" means "I turn back" or "I return" in English. It is used to express the action of going back to a previous state or location.
