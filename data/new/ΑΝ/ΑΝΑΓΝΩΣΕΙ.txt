Etymology: The word "αναγνώσει" comes from the Greek verb "αναγινώσκω" (anaginósko), which means "to read." It is derived from the prefix "ανα-" (ana-), meaning "again" or "back," and the verb "γινώσκω" (ginósko), meaning "to know" or "to recognize."

Grammar: "Αναγνώσει" is the third person singular form of the verb "αναγινώσκω" in the future tense. In Greek grammar, verbs are conjugated based on the person, number, tense, and mood. The future tense indicates an action that will happen in the future.

Description: "Αναγνώσει" is a Greek word that means "he/she/it will read" in English. It refers to the act of reading or perusing written text.
