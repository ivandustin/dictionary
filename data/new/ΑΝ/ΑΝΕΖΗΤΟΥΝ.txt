Etymology: The word "ανεζητούν" comes from the Greek verb "ζητώ" (zito), which means "to seek" or "to search". The prefix "αν-" (an-) is a negation prefix, so "ανεζητούν" can be translated as "they do not seek" or "they do not search".

Grammar: "Ανεζητούν" is the third person plural form of the verb "ζητώ" in the present tense. It is used to describe an action that multiple people are not doing at the moment.

Description: "Ανεζητούν" is a Greek word that expresses the idea of not seeking or not searching. It can be used in various contexts to indicate that a group of people is not actively looking for something or someone.
