Etymology: The word "αναστροφή" comes from the Greek roots "ανα-" (ana-), meaning "again" or "back," and "στροφή" (strofi), meaning "turn" or "change." 

Grammar: "Αναστροφή" is a noun in the Greek language. It is feminine in gender and singular in number. 

Description: "Αναστροφή" refers to the act of turning back or reversing a situation. It can also mean a change in direction, behavior, or attitude. In a broader sense, it can be used to describe a transformation or a shift in circumstances.
