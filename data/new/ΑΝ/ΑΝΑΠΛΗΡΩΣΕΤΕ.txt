Grammar: "αναπληρώνω" is a regular Greek verb that belongs to the first conjugation. It is conjugated in various tenses, moods, and persons to indicate different grammatical aspects and meanings.

Description: The verb "αναπληρώνω" is used to express the action of refilling or replenishing something that has been emptied or depleted. It can be used in both literal and figurative contexts.
