Grammar: "Anomia" is a noun in Greek. In English, it is often used as a medical term to refer to a condition characterized by the inability to remember or recall words or names.

Description: Anomia is a cognitive disorder that affects language processing and memory. People with anomia have difficulty finding the right words or names for objects, places, or people, even though they may have a clear understanding of what they want to say.
