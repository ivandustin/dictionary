Etymology: The word "αναπεσείν" comes from the Greek verb "αναπίπτω" (anapípto), which means "to fall back" or "to recline." It is derived from the prefix "ανα-" (ana-), meaning "back" or "again," and the verb "πίπτω" (pípto), meaning "to fall."

Grammar: "Αναπεσείν" is a verb in the Greek language. It is in the present tense, indicative mood, and active voice. It is conjugated in the third person singular form.
