Etymology: The word "ανηγγελη" (anēngelē) comes from the Greek verb "αναγγέλλω" (anangellō), which means "to announce" or "to declare." 

Grammar: "Ανηγγελη" is a noun in the singular form. It belongs to the third declension in Greek grammar. 

Description: "Ανηγγελη" refers to an announcement or a declaration. It can also be used to describe the act of proclaiming or making something known.
