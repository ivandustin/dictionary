Etymology: The word "ανοιξει" (anoixei) comes from the Greek verb "ανοίγω" (anoigo), which means "to open".

Grammar: "Ανοιξει" is the third person singular form of the future tense of the verb "ανοίγω" (anoigo), which means "he/she/it will open".
