Etymology: The word "αναβαθμων" comes from the Greek roots "ανα-" (ana-), meaning "up" or "again," and "βαθμός" (bathmos), meaning "step" or "grade." 

Grammar: "Αναβαθμων" is a noun in the masculine gender and singular number. It belongs to the second declension in Greek grammar.

Description: "Αναβαθμων" refers to a step or grade in a series or hierarchy. It can also be used metaphorically to describe a level of achievement or progress.
