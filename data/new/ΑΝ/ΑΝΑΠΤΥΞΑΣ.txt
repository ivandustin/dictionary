Grammar: "Αναπτυξη" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the singular form and belongs to the feminine gender.

Description: "Αναπτυξη" refers to the process of development or growth. It can be used to describe the progress or advancement of something, such as a project, a country, or an individual. In a broader sense, it can also refer to the overall improvement or expansion of a concept, idea, or system.
