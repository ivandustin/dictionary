Etymology: The word "αντιλεγεται" comes from the Greek roots "αντι-" meaning "against" or "opposite" and "λεγω" meaning "to speak" or "to say". 

Grammar: "αντιλεγεται" is a verb in the present tense, third person singular form. It is derived from the verb "αντιλέγω" which means "to contradict" or "to argue against". 

Description: "αντιλεγεται" is used to describe the action of contradicting or arguing against someone or something. It implies a disagreement or opposition to a statement, opinion, or idea.
