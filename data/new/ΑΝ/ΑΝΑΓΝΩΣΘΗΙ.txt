Grammar: "αναγνωσθηι" is a verb in the aorist passive subjunctive form in Ancient Greek. It is conjugated in the third person singular. The aorist tense refers to a completed action in the past, and the passive voice indicates that the subject of the verb is being acted upon. The subjunctive mood suggests a possibility or potentiality.

Description: "αναγνωσθηι" is used to express the idea of "let it be read" or "may it be read." It is often found in ancient texts and inscriptions, indicating a command or wish for something to be read aloud or publicly.
