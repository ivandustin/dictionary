Etymology: The word "ανταποδούναι" comes from the Greek verb "ανταποδίδω" (antapodído), which means "to give back" or "to reciprocate." It is derived from the prefix "αντι-" (anti-), meaning "against" or "opposite," and the verb "δίδω" (dído), meaning "to give."

Grammar: "Ανταποδούναι" is the third person singular form of the verb "ανταποδίδω" in the present tense. It is used to describe the action of giving back or reciprocating.

Description: "Ανταποδούναι" is a Greek verb that expresses the act of giving back or returning something in response to receiving something. It implies a sense of reciprocity or mutual exchange.
