Etymology: The word "αιτούντι" comes from the Greek verb "αιτέω" (aiteo), which means "to ask" or "to request". It is derived from the Proto-Indo-European root *h₂eid-.

Grammar: "Αιτούντι" is the present participle form of the verb "αιτέω" in the third person singular. In Greek grammar, the present participle is used to form the present continuous tense and can also function as an adjective.

Description: "Αιτούντι" is used to describe someone or something that is currently asking or requesting. It can be translated as "asking" or "requesting" in English.
