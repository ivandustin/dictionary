Etymology: The word "αιτησαι" (aitēsai) comes from the Greek verb "αιτέω" (aitéō), which means "to ask" or "to request". 

Grammar: "Αιτησαι" is the aorist tense, active voice, subjunctive mood, and second person singular form of the verb "αιτέω". It is used to express a request or a demand in a polite or formal way.

Description: "Αιτησαι" is a verb in the Greek language that is commonly used to ask for something or make a request. It is often used in formal or polite contexts, such as when asking for a favor or making a formal request.
