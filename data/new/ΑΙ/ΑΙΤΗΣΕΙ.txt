Etymology: The word "αἰτησει" (aitēsei) comes from the Greek verb "αἰτέω" (aitéō), which means "to ask" or "to request." It is derived from the Proto-Indo-European root *h₂eid-.

Grammar: "αἰτησει" is the third person singular, future indicative active form of the verb "αἰτέω" (aitéō). In Greek grammar, it belongs to the first conjugation class of verbs.

Description: "αἰτησει" is used to express a future action of asking or requesting. It indicates that someone or something will ask or request something in the future.
