Etymology: The word "αἰγιοῖς" (aigiois) comes from the Greek language. It is derived from the noun "αἴξ" (aix), meaning "goat."

Grammar: "Αἰγιοῖς" is a noun in the dative plural form. In Greek grammar, the dative case is used to indicate the indirect object of a verb or the object of certain prepositions.
