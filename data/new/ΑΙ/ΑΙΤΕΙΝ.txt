Etymology: The word "αἴτην" (aitēn) comes from the Greek verb "αἰτέω" (aiteō), which means "to ask" or "to request."

Grammar: "Αἴτην" is a noun in the accusative case, singular form. It is the accusative form of the noun "αἴτησις" (aitēsis), which means "request" or "demand."

Description: "Αἴτην" refers to a specific request or demand made by someone. It is used to indicate the object of the verb "αἰτέω" (aiteō), which means that it is what is being asked for or requested.
