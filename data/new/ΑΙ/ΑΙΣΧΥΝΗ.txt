Etymology: The word "αἰσχύνη" (aischynē) comes from the Greek language. It is derived from the verb "αἰσχύνω" (aischynō), which means "to feel shame" or "to disgrace."

Grammar: "Αἰσχύνη" is a noun in the Greek language. It is a feminine noun of the first declension.

Description: "Αἰσχύνη" refers to the feeling of shame, disgrace, or embarrassment. It is the state of being ashamed or feeling guilty for something one has done or for a situation one is in. It can also be used to describe the act of dishonoring or disgracing someone or something.
