Etymology: The word "αιτειτε" comes from the Greek verb "αιτέω" (aiteo), which means "to ask" or "to request".

Grammar: "Αιτειτε" is the second person plural form of the verb "αιτέω" (aiteo) in the present tense. It is used when addressing a group of people and means "you ask" or "you request".

Description: "Αιτειτε" is a verb form that is commonly used in Greek to express the act of asking or requesting something. It can be used in various contexts, such as asking for information, making a request, or seeking permission.
