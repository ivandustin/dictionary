Etymology: The word "αιδοντες" comes from the Greek word "αἰδοῦς" (aidous), which means "shame" or "modesty".

Grammar: "Αιδοντες" is a noun in the nominative plural form. It is the plural form of the noun "αἰδώς" (aidos), which means "shame" or "modesty".

Description: "Αιδοντες" refers to a group of people who possess the qualities of shame or modesty. It can also be used to describe a sense of honor or respect.
