Etymology: The word "αἴτια" (aitia) comes from the Greek language and is derived from the verb "αἴτιος" (aitios), meaning "responsible" or "cause." 

Grammar: "Αἴτια" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the plural form and belongs to the third declension. 

Description: "Αἴτια" refers to the causes or reasons behind something. It is often used in philosophical and scientific contexts to discuss the factors that lead to a particular outcome or event.
