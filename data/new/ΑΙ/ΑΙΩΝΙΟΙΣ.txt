Grammar: "Αἰώνιος" is an adjective in the Greek language. It is in the masculine form, singular number, and nominative case. It can be declined to match the gender, number, and case of the noun it modifies.

Description: "Αἰώνιος" is often translated as "eternal" or "everlasting" in English. It refers to something that is without end or that lasts forever. It can be used to describe concepts such as time, existence, or qualities that are enduring and unchanging.
