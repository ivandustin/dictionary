Etymology: The word "αἰτίου" (aitiou) comes from the Greek language. It is derived from the noun "αἰτία" (aitia), meaning "cause" or "reason."

Grammar: "Αἰτίου" is a noun in the genitive case. In Greek grammar, the genitive case is used to indicate possession, origin, or relationship.

Description: "Αἰτίου" refers to the genitive form of the noun "αἰτία" (aitia). It is used to indicate possession or relationship to a cause or reason.
