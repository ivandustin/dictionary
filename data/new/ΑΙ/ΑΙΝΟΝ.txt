Etymology: The word "αἴνων" (ainon) comes from the Greek verb "αἰνέω" (aineo), which means "to praise" or "to commend."

Grammar: "Αἴνων" is a noun in the masculine gender, singular number, and nominative case.

Description: "Αἴνων" refers to a person who praises or commends someone or something. It can also be used to describe a poet or a singer who sings praises or hymns.
