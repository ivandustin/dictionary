Etymology: The word "αιματεκχυσίας" comes from the Greek words "αιμα" (haima) meaning "blood" and "τεκχυσίας" (tekchysias) meaning "shedding" or "spilling". 

Grammar: "Αιματεκχυσίας" is a noun in the genitive case, singular number, masculine gender. 

Description: "Αιματεκχυσίας" refers to the act of shedding or spilling blood. It can be used to describe a violent or bloody event, such as a battle or a murder.
