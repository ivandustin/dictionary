Etymology: The word "αισχροκερδώς" comes from the Greek words "αισχρός" (aischros) meaning "shameful" or "disgraceful" and "κερδών" (kerdon) meaning "gaining" or "profit". 

Grammar: "αισχροκερδώς" is an adverb in Greek. It is formed by adding the suffix "-ως" to the adjective "αισχρός". 

Description: "αισχροκερδώς" describes an action or behavior that is motivated by a shameful or disgraceful desire for gain or profit. It implies that the action is morally wrong or unethical.
