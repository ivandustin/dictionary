Word: βασταζεσθαι

Etymology: βαστάζω (bastázō) + -εσθαι (-esthai), from the Ancient Greek βαστάζω (bastázō) meaning "to carry, bear".

Grammar: βασταζεσθαι is a verb in the present tense, middle/passive voice, and infinitive mood.

Description: βασταζεσθαι is a Greek verb that means "to be carried, to be borne, to be supported". It is often used in a figurative sense to mean "to endure, to put up with, to tolerate". The middle/passive voice indicates that the subject is either receiving the action or is acting upon themselves.
