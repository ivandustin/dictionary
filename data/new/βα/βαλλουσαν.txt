Word: βαλλουσαν

Etymology: βαλλουσαν is a Greek verb in the present participle form, which means "throwing" or "casting". It is derived from the Greek word "βάλλω" (ballō), which means "to throw" or "to cast".

The present participle is used to describe an ongoing action that is happening at the same time as the main verb.
