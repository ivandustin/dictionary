Word: βαλλομενα

Etymology: βαλλομενα is the present participle of the Greek verb βάλλω (ballō), which means "to throw" or "to cast". 

Grammar: βαλλομενα is a neuter gender noun in the nominative case, singular number, and present tense. It is also a participle, which means it functions as both a verb and an adjective. 

Description: βαλλομενα can be translated to mean "throwing" or "casting". It is often used to describe an ongoing action of throwing or casting something. For example, in the sentence "The athletes were throwing the javelin", βαλλομενα would be used to describe the ongoing action of throwing the javelin.
