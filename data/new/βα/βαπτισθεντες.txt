The word βαπτισθεντες is a Greek verb that means "having been baptized." It is the passive participle form of the verb βαπτίζω (baptizō), which means "to dip, immerse, or wash." In Christian theology, baptism is a sacrament that symbolizes the washing away of sins and the initiation into the Christian faith. The grammar of βαπτισθεντες is a passive voice, perfect tense, participle form.
