Word: βαστασει

Etymology: βαστασει is a Greek verb that comes from the root word βαστάζω (bastazo), which means "to carry" or "to bear". 

Grammar: βαστασει is a third-person singular present active indicative form of the verb βαστάζω (bastazo). It is in the present tense and active voice. 

Description: βαστασει means "he/she/it carries" or "he/she/it bears" in English. It is a common verb in Greek and can be used in various contexts, such as carrying a physical object or bearing a burden or responsibility.
