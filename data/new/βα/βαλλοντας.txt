Word: βαλλοντας

Etymology: βαλλοντας is the present participle of the Greek verb βάλλω (ballō), which means "to throw" or "to cast". 

Grammar: βαλλοντας is a present participle in the active voice, masculine gender, and nominative case. It is derived from the verb βάλλω and is used to describe an ongoing action of throwing or casting. 

Description: βαλλοντας can be translated to "throwing" or "casting" in English. It is often used in Greek literature and poetry to describe a continuous action or movement. For example, "Οι άνθρωποι βαλλοντας τις μπάλες στο γήπεδο" (The people are throwing the balls in the field).
