The word βαλλεται (balletai) is a present tense, third person singular form of the Greek verb βάλλω (ballo), which means "to throw" or "to cast". The word has its roots in the ancient Greek language and is commonly used in modern Greek as well. 

In terms of grammar, βαλλεται is a present tense indicative active form of the verb βάλλω. It is conjugated from the stem βαλ-, with the suffix -λλεται added to indicate the third person singular. 

Overall, βαλλεται is a versatile word that can be used in a variety of contexts, such as sports (e.g. throwing a ball), warfare (e.g. casting a spear), or even figuratively (e.g. throwing insults).
