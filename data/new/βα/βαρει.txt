Word: βαρει

Etymology: βαρει comes from the Greek verb βαρύνω (varyno), which means "to make heavy" or "to weigh down." 

Grammar: βαρει is a verb in the present tense, third person singular form. It is conjugated from the verb βαρύνω (varyno), which is a regular verb in Greek. 

Description: βαρει means "he/she/it weighs" in Greek. It can be used to describe the weight of an object or the act of weighing something. It can also be used metaphorically to describe a heavy burden or responsibility.
