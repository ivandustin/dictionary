The word βαλει (balei) is a verb in the Greek language. It is the third person singular form of the present tense of the verb βάλλω (ballō), which means "to throw" or "to cast". The etymology of the word can be traced back to the Proto-Indo-European root *gʷʰel- which means "to throw, to hit". In terms of grammar, βαλει is a regular verb that follows the conjugation pattern of the verb βάλλω. It is commonly used in everyday speech and writing in Greek.
