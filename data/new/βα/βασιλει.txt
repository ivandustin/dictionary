The word "βασιλει" (basilei) is a Greek noun that means "kingship" or "royalty". It is derived from the Greek word "βασιλεύς" (basileus), which means "king" or "ruler". In terms of grammar, "βασιλει" is a third-person singular present active indicative form of the verb "βασιλεύω" (basileuo), which means "to reign" or "to rule".
