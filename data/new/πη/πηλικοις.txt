The word "πηλικοις" (pronounced "peh-lee-koys") is a Greek adjective that means "of a certain age" or "of a certain size". It is derived from the noun "πηλίκος" (pronounced "peh-lee-kos"), which means "magnitude" or "size". In terms of grammar, "πηλικοις" is in the dative case, which is used to indicate the indirect object of a verb or the object of certain prepositions.
