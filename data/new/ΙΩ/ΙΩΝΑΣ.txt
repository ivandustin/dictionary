Description: "Ιωνας" is the Greek name for the biblical figure Jonah, who is known for being swallowed by a large fish or whale. In the Bible, the Book of Jonah tells the story of how Jonah was sent by God to prophesy against the city of Nineveh, but initially tried to flee from his mission.
