Etymology: The word "ευεργεσίαι" comes from the Greek root words "ευ" (eu), meaning "good" or "well," and "εργον" (ergon), meaning "work" or "deed." 

Grammar: "Ευεργεσίαι" is a noun in the plural form in the Greek language. It belongs to the first declension and is feminine in gender.

Description: "Ευεργεσίαι" is a Greek word that translates to "benefits" or "good deeds" in English. It refers to acts of kindness, generosity, or helpfulness towards others.
