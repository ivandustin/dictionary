Etymology: The word "ευλαβείας" (eulabeias) comes from the Greek root "ευ" (eu), meaning "good" or "well," and the word "λαβείας" (labeias), meaning "caution" or "care." 

Grammar: "Ευλαβείας" is a noun in the genitive case, singular form. It belongs to the third declension in Greek grammar.

Description: "Ευλαβείας" refers to the quality or state of being cautious, careful, or reverent. It implies a sense of mindfulness and attentiveness in one's actions or behavior.
