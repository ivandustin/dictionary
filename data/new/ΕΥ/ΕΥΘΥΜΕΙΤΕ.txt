Etymology: The word "ευθυμείτε" comes from the Greek root words "ευ" (eu) meaning "good" and "θυμός" (thymos) meaning "spirit" or "mood". 

Grammar: "Ευθυμείτε" is a verb in the imperative mood, second person plural form. It is derived from the verb "ευθυμώ" (euthymo), which means "to be in a good mood" or "to be cheerful". 

Description: "Ευθυμείτε" is a command or request for a group of people to be in a good mood or to be cheerful. It is often used as a friendly greeting or farewell.
