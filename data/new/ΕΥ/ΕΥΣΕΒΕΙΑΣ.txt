Grammar: "ευσέβειας" is a noun in the genitive case, singular form. In Greek grammar, the genitive case is used to indicate possession or origin.

Description: "ευσέβειας" refers to the quality or state of being pious or devout. It is often used to describe a deep religious or spiritual devotion, particularly in the context of Greek philosophy and religion.
