Grammar: "Ευσεβως" is an adverb in Greek. It is the adverbial form of the adjective "ευσεβης" (eusebēs), which means "pious" or "devout." Adverbs modify verbs, adjectives, or other adverbs, and in this case, "ευσεβως" describes the manner in which an action is performed.
