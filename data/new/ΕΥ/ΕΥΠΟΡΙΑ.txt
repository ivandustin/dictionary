Etymology: The word "ευπορία" (euporia) comes from the Greek roots "ευ" (eu), meaning "good" or "well," and "πορία" (poria), meaning "ability" or "capacity." 

Grammar: "Ευπορία" is a feminine noun in Greek. It is in the singular form and belongs to the first declension. 

Description: "Ευπορία" refers to the state or quality of being prosperous, successful, or well-off. It can also denote the ability to easily accomplish or achieve something.
