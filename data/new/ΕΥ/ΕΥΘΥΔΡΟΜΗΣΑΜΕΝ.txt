Etymology: The word "ευθυδρομησαμεν" comes from the Greek roots "ευ" (eu), meaning "good" or "well," and "θυδρομησαμεν" (thydromēsamen), meaning "to run swiftly." 

Grammar: "ευθυδρομησαμεν" is the aorist tense, active voice, first person plural form of the verb "ευθυδρομέω" (thydroméō), which means "to run swiftly" or "to sprint."

Description: "ευθυδρομησαμεν" describes an action of running swiftly or sprinting that was performed in the past by a group of individuals.
