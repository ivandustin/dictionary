The word "ευλογησεν" is a Greek verb in the aorist tense, third person singular form. It comes from the root word "ευλογέω" (eulogeó), which means "to bless" or "to praise." In this specific form, "ευλογησεν" means "he/she/it blessed" or "he/she/it praised." It is commonly used in religious or spiritual contexts to refer to the act of bestowing blessings or expressing praise.
