Grammar: "Ευθείαν" is a noun in the accusative case, singular form. It is the accusative form of the noun "ευθεία" (euthéia), which means "straight line" or "straight path."

Description: "Ευθείαν" refers to a straight line or a direct path. In mathematics, it is a line that does not curve or bend. It is the shortest distance between two points. In everyday language, it can also be used metaphorically to describe something that is straightforward or direct.
