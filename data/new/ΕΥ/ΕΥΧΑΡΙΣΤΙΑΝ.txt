Description: "Ευχαριστιαν" refers to the act of giving thanks or expressing gratitude. It is often used in the context of religious ceremonies, specifically in reference to the Christian sacrament of the Eucharist, which is a ritual of thanksgiving for the body and blood of Jesus Christ.
