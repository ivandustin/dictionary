Etymology: The word "ευεργετών" comes from the Greek roots "ευ" (eu), meaning "good" or "well," and "εργετής" (ergotis), meaning "worker" or "doer." 

Grammar: "Ευεργετών" is a noun in the masculine gender, singular number, and nominative case. 

Description: "Ευεργετών" refers to a person who does good deeds or performs acts of kindness. It can also be used to describe someone who is a benefactor or a philanthropist.
