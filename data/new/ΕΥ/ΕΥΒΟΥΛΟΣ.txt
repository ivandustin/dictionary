Etymology: The word "ευβουλος" (euboulos) comes from the combination of the Greek words "ευ" (eu), meaning "good" or "well," and "βουλη" (boule), meaning "counsel" or "advice." Therefore, "ευβουλος" can be translated as "good counselor" or "wise advisor."

Grammar: In Greek, "ευβουλος" is an adjective that agrees with the noun it modifies in gender, number, and case. It can be declined in different forms depending on the context.

Description: "Ευβουλος" is used to describe someone who gives good advice or counsel. It implies wisdom, prudence, and the ability to make sound judgments. It can also refer to someone who is knowledgeable and experienced in a particular field and is able to provide valuable guidance.
