Grammar: "ευρεθηναι" is the aorist passive infinitive form of the verb "εὑρίσκω" (heuriskō). In Greek grammar, the aorist tense is used to express a past action that is viewed as a whole, without specifying its duration or repetition. The passive voice indicates that the subject of the verb is being acted upon.

Description: "ευρεθηναι" is used to express the idea of "to be found" or "to have been found" in Greek. It is often used in contexts where something or someone has been discovered or located.
