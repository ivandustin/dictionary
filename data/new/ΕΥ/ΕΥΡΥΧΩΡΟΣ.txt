Grammar: In Greek, "ευρυχωρος" is an adjective. It agrees in gender, number, and case with the noun it modifies. For example, if used to describe a masculine singular noun, it would be "ευρυχωρος," while for a feminine singular noun it would be "ευρυχωρη," and for a neuter singular noun it would be "ευρυχωρο."

Description: "Ευρυχωρος" is a Greek word that means "spacious" or "roomy." It is used to describe a place or area that has ample space or is not crowded. It can also be used metaphorically to describe a person's mind or thoughts as being open and accommodating.
