Etymology: The word "ευδοκιας" (eudokias) comes from the Greek language. It is derived from the combination of the prefix "ευ-" (eu-), meaning "good" or "well," and the noun "δοκιας" (dokias), meaning "opinion" or "judgment." 

Grammar: "Ευδοκιας" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the singular form and belongs to the masculine gender. 

Description: "Ευδοκιας" refers to the state or quality of having a good opinion or judgment. It can also imply the act of being well-pleased or satisfied with something.
