Grammar: "ευθυδρομησαντες" is a Greek verb in the aorist tense, active voice, and participle mood. It is the masculine plural form, indicating that the subject of the verb is a group of males. The verb form indicates that the action of running swiftly or sprinting has been completed in the past.
