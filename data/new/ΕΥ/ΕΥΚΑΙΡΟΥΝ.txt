Etymology: The word "ευκαιρούν" comes from the Greek root "ευ-" meaning "good" or "well" and the verb "καιρός" meaning "opportunity" or "right time". 

Grammar: "Ευκαιρούν" is the present tense, third person plural form of the verb "ευκαιρώ" which means "to take advantage of an opportunity" or "to make the most of a situation". It is a regular verb in Greek.

Description: "Ευκαιρούν" is used to describe the action of seizing an opportunity or making the most of a favorable situation. It implies being prepared and ready to act when the right moment arises.
