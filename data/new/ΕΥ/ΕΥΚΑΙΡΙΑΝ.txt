Grammar: "Ευκαιρίαν" is the accusative form of the noun "ευκαιρία" (eukairía) in Greek. In Greek grammar, nouns have different forms depending on their role in a sentence. The accusative case is used to indicate the direct object of a verb or the object of certain prepositions.
