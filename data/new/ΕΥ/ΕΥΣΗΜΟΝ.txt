Etymology: The word "ευσημον" (eusemon) comes from the Greek roots "ευ" (eu) meaning "good" and "σημον" (semon) meaning "sign" or "omen". 

Grammar: "Ευσημον" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the masculine gender and singular number. 

Description: "Ευσημον" refers to a good sign or omen. It is used to describe something that is considered favorable or auspicious.
