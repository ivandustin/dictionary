Grammar: "Ευλογείν" is a verb in the Greek language. It belongs to the first conjugation class and is in the present tense. It is commonly used in the active voice.

Description: "Ευλογείν" means "to bless" or "to praise" in Greek. It is often used in religious contexts to express the act of invoking divine favor or expressing gratitude towards a higher power.
