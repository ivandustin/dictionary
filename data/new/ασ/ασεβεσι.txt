The word "ασεβεσι" (asevesi) is a Greek noun that means "impiety" or "irreverence." It is derived from the adjective "ασεβής" (asevis), which means "impious" or "irreverent." In Greek grammar, "ασεβεσι" is a feminine noun in the nominative case. It can be used to describe a lack of respect or reverence towards the gods, or a disregard for religious or moral principles.
