Word: ασυνθετους

Etymology: The word ασυνθετους comes from the Greek prefix α- (meaning "not" or "without") and the Greek word συνθετος (meaning "composite" or "complex"). 

Grammar: ασυνθετους is an adjective in the masculine plural form. 

Description: ασυνθετους is used to describe something that is not composite or complex, but rather simple or uncomplicated. It can also be used to describe something that is not harmonious or does not fit together well.
