Word: αστηρικτοι

Etymology: The word αστηρικτοι comes from the Greek word "αστήρ" (astir) which means "star" and the suffix "-ικτος" (-iktos) which means "not touched" or "untouched". Therefore, αστηρικτοι means "untouched by stars" or "not touched by stars".

Grammar: αστηρικτοι is an adjective in the masculine plural form. It is used to describe a group of people or things that have not been touched by stars.

Description: αστηρικτοι is a rare and poetic word that is not commonly used in everyday language. It is often used in literature and poetry to describe a sense of isolation or loneliness, as if the subject is untouched by the beauty and wonder of the stars.
