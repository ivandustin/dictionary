The word "ασελγειαν" (aselageian) is a Greek noun that means "shamelessness" or "impudence". It is derived from the Greek word "ασελγεια" (aselgeia), which means "licentiousness" or "outrageousness". In terms of grammar, it is a feminine noun in the accusative case. It is often used in a negative context to describe behavior that is considered immoral or indecent.
