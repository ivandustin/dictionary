Word: ασπασαμενοι

Etymology: ασπασαμενοι is a Greek word that comes from the verb ασπαζομαι (aspazomai), which means "to greet" or "to welcome". The word is in the aorist tense, which indicates a completed action in the past.

Grammar: ασπασαμενοι is a verb in the aorist tense, middle voice, and masculine gender. It is the plural form of the verb, indicating that the subject is a group of males. The middle voice indicates that the subject is performing the action on themselves, in this case, greeting or welcoming each other.

Description: ασπασαμενοι is a word that describes a group of males greeting or welcoming each other. It can be translated as "having greeted" or "having welcomed". The word is often used in the context of social gatherings or meetings, where people greet each other upon arrival.
