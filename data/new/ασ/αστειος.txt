Word: αστειος

The word αστειος (asteios) comes from the ancient Greek word ἀστεῖος (asteios), which means "urbane" or "polished". It is derived from the word ἄστυ (asty), which means "city".

In Greek, αστειος is an adjective that describes someone or something as funny, humorous, or amusing. It is often used to describe a person's sense of humor or a situation that is comical.

αστειος is a versatile word that can be used to describe a wide range of things that are funny or amusing. It can be used to describe a person who is naturally funny or someone who tells jokes.
