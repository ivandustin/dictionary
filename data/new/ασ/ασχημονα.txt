The word "ασχημονα" is a Greek adjective that means "ugly" or "unattractive". It is derived from the Greek words "α-" (a-), which means "not", and "σχημα" (schima), which means "shape" or "form". In terms of grammar, "ασχημονα" is in the masculine singular nominative case. It can also be declined to match the gender, number, and case of the noun it modifies.
