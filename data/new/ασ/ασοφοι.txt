The word "ασοφοι" (asophoi) is a Greek adjective that means "unwise" or "foolish". It is derived from the prefix "a-" (meaning "not") and the noun "σοφός" (sophos), which means "wise". In terms of grammar, it is a masculine plural nominative adjective in the Greek language. It is often used in Greek literature and philosophy to describe individuals who lack wisdom or knowledge.
