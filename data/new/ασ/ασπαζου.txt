The word "ασπαζου" (as-pa-zoo) is a verb in the Greek language. 

Etymology: The word comes from the ancient Greek verb "ἀσπάζομαι" (as-pa-zo-my), which means "to greet" or "to welcome". 

Grammar: "ασπαζου" is the present tense, second person singular form of the verb. It is used when addressing one person directly, and means "you greet" or "you welcome". 

Description: The verb "ασπαζου" is commonly used in everyday conversation in Greece as a polite way of greeting someone. It can also be used to express gratitude or appreciation towards someone.
