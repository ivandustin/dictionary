The word "ασθενει" (asthenei) is a verb in the Greek language. It is derived from the root word "ασθενής" (asthenis), which means "weak" or "sick". The verb form "ασθενει" means "he/she/it is weak" or "he/she/it is sick". 

In terms of grammar, "ασθενει" is a present tense verb in the third person singular form. It can be conjugated in various tenses and persons to indicate different aspects of the action.

Overall, "ασθενει" is a simple but important verb in Greek, used to describe someone or something that is weak or sick.
