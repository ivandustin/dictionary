Word: ασπασμόν

Etymology:
The word ασπασμόν (aspasmon) is derived from the Greek verb ασπάζομαι (aspazomai), which means "to greet" or "to welcome." The suffix -μόν (-mon) is a common ending in Greek that is used to form nouns from verbs.

Grammar:
ασπασμόν is a masculine noun in the accusative singular form. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their gender, number, and case. The accusative case is used to indicate the direct object of a verb.

Description:
ασπασμόν refers to a greeting or a welcome. It can also be used to describe an act of showing respect or affection towards someone. In modern Greek, the word ασπασμός (aspasmos) is more commonly used instead of ασπασμόν.
