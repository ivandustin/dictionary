Word: ασπασησθε

Etymology: ασπασησθε is a Greek word that comes from the verb ασπαζομαι (aspazomai), which means "to greet" or "to welcome". It is a second person plural present middle imperative form of the verb.

Grammar: ασπασησθε is a verb in the Greek language, specifically in the present middle imperative form. It is used to give a command or instruction to a group of people to greet or welcome someone.

Description: ασπασησθε is a polite and formal way of instructing a group of people to greet or welcome someone. It is often used in social or professional settings, such as when introducing a guest speaker or welcoming visitors to an event.
