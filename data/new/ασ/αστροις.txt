Word: αστροις

Etymology: αστροις is a Greek word that comes from the root word αστήρ (astér), meaning "star". The suffix -οις (-ois) is a masculine plural ending used in the dative case.

Grammar: αστροις is a masculine plural noun in the dative case. It is often used in Greek mythology to refer to the stars or constellations.

Description: αστροις refers to a group of stars or a constellation in Greek mythology. It is often used in poetry and literature to describe the night sky or the heavens. In modern Greek, it can also refer to the stars in the sky.
