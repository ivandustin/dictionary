Etymology:
The word αστρων (astron) is derived from the Greek word ἀστήρ (astēr), which means "star". The suffix -ων (-ōn) is a genitive plural ending, indicating that the word refers to multiple stars.

αστρων is a word that refers to stars in the Greek language. It is often used in scientific contexts, such as astronomy and astrophysics.
