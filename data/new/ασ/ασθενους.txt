The word ασθενους (asthenous) is derived from the Greek word ασθενής (asthenis), which means "weak" or "sick". The word is formed from the prefix α- (a-), which means "not" or "without", and the root word σθένος (sthénos), which means "strength".

ασθενους is a Greek adjective in the masculine or feminine gender, singular number, and nominative case. It is often used to describe a person who is weak or sick.
