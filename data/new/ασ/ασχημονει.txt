Word: ασχημονει

Etymology: The word ασχημονει comes from the Greek root words ασχημος (ashimos) meaning "ugly" or "unseemly" and νει (nei) meaning "to behave" or "to act". 

Grammar: ασχημονει is a verb in the present tense, third person singular form. It is derived from the verb ασχημονέω (ashimonéo) which means "to behave in an ugly or unseemly manner". 

Description: ασχημονει describes someone who is behaving in an unattractive or inappropriate manner. It can also be used to describe something that is unsightly or unpleasant to look at. The word is often used in a negative context and can be considered a criticism of someone's behavior or appearance.
