The word "κυπρου" (kyprou) is a Greek noun that means "of Cyprus" or "from Cyprus". It is derived from the name of the island of Cyprus, which is located in the eastern Mediterranean Sea. 

In terms of grammar, "κυπρου" is in the genitive case, which is used to indicate possession or origin. It is often used in phrases such as "της Κύπρου" (tis Kyprou), which means "of Cyprus" or "from Cyprus".

Overall, "κυπρου" is a simple but important word in Greek, as it is frequently used to describe things that are related to or originate from Cyprus.
