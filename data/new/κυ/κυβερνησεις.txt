Word: κυβερνησεις

Etymology:
The word κυβερνησεις (kyverniseis) is derived from the Greek verb κυβερνάω (kybernao), which means "to steer" or "to govern." The noun form κυβέρνηση (kyvernisi) means "government" or "governance."

Grammar:
κυβερνησεις is a plural noun in the Greek language. It is in the nominative case, which is used for the subject of a sentence.

Description:
κυβερνησεις refers to governments or administrations. It can be used to describe the governing bodies of countries, states, or cities. The word can also be used in a broader sense to refer to any type of governing or management, such as in a business or organization.
