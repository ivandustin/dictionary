The word "χειλεσιν" (cheilesin) is a Greek noun that means "lips" or "the edge of a cup or vessel". It is derived from the Greek word "χείλος" (cheilos), which also means "lip". In terms of grammar, "χειλεσιν" is a plural noun in the dative case. It can be used to refer to the lips of a person or animal, or to the rim or edge of a container.
