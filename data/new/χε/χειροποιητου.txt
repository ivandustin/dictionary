The word "χειροποιητου" is a Greek adjective that means "handmade" or "handcrafted". It is derived from the words "χείρ" (cheir), meaning "hand", and "ποιητού" (poiitou), meaning "made by". In terms of grammar, it is in the genitive case and agrees in gender and number with the noun it modifies. For example, "χειροποιητού κεραμικού" (cheiropoiitou keramikou) means "handmade pottery".
