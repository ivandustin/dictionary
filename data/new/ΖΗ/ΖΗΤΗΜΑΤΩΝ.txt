Etymology: The word "ζητηματων" comes from the Greek word "ζήτημα" (zētēma), which means "question" or "problem". 

Grammar: "ζητηματων" is the plural form of the noun "ζήτημα" (zētēma) in Greek. It belongs to the second declension and is classified as a neuter noun. 

Description: "ζητηματων" refers to multiple questions or problems. It can be used to describe a variety of issues or topics that require investigation, discussion, or resolution.
