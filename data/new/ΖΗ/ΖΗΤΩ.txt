Etymology: The word "ζητώ" (zeto) comes from the Greek verb "ζητέω" (zeteo), which means "to seek" or "to search for". 

Grammar: "ζητώ" is the first person singular present tense form of the verb "ζητέω". It is used to express the action of seeking or searching for something in the present.

Description: "ζητώ" is a Greek verb that is commonly used in everyday language. It can be used to express various meanings such as seeking, searching, looking for, or asking for something. It is a versatile verb that can be used in different contexts and situations.
