Etymology: The word "ζητεί" (zēteí) comes from the Greek verb "ζητώ" (zētó), which means "to seek" or "to search".

Grammar: "ζητεί" is the third person singular form of the present tense in Greek. It is used to describe someone or something that is seeking or searching for something.
