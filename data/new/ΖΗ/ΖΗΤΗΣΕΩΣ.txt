Etymology: The word "ζητησεως" (zētēseōs) comes from the Greek verb "ζητέω" (zēteō), which means "to seek" or "to search for". 

Grammar: "ζητησεως" is a noun in the genitive case, singular form. In Greek grammar, the genitive case is used to indicate possession, origin, or relationship.

Description: "ζητησεως" refers to the act of seeking or searching for something. It can also be translated as "quest" or "inquiry".
