Etymology: The word "ζητησιν" comes from the Greek verb "ζητέω" (zeteo), which means "to seek" or "to search".

Grammar: "ζητησιν" is a noun in the accusative case, singular number, and feminine gender in Greek.

Description: "ζητησιν" refers to the act of seeking or searching for something. It can also be used to describe a quest, inquiry, or investigation.
