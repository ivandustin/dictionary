Etymology: The word "ζητησις" comes from the Greek verb "ζητέω" (zeteo), which means "to seek" or "to search for". 

Grammar: "ζητησις" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the nominative case and singular number. 

Description: "ζητησις" refers to the act of seeking or searching for something. It can also be used to describe a quest, inquiry, or investigation. In a broader sense, it can represent the pursuit of knowledge, truth, or understanding.
