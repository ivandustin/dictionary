Etymology: The word "ζητούν" (zētoún) comes from the Greek verb "ζητώ" (zētó), which means "to seek" or "to search".

Grammar: "ζητούν" is the present tense, third person plural form of the verb "ζητώ" (zētó). It is used to describe an action that is being performed by a group of people or things.

Description: "ζητούν" is a verb in the Greek language that expresses the action of seeking or searching. It can be used in various contexts to indicate the act of looking for something or trying to find information or answers.
