Etymology: The word "ζητησαί" comes from the Greek verb "ζητέω" (zeteo), which means "to seek" or "to search for". 

Grammar: "ζητησαί" is the aorist tense, third person singular form of the verb "ζητέω" (zeteo). The aorist tense is used to express a completed action in the past.

Description: "ζητησαί" is a verb in Greek that means "he/she/it sought" or "he/she/it searched for". It is used to describe the action of seeking or searching for something in the past.
