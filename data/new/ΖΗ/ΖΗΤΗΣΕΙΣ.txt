Etymology: The word "ζητησεις" comes from the Greek verb "ζητώ" (zito), which means "to seek" or "to search". It is derived from the Proto-Indo-European root *gʰed-.

Grammar: "ζητησεις" is the plural form of the noun "ζήτηση" (zitisi), which means "demand" or "request" in Greek. It belongs to the third declension of Greek nouns.

Description: "ζητησεις" refers to multiple demands or requests. It can be used to describe the act of seeking or searching for something, or the state of being in demand.
