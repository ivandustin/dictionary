Etymology: The word "ζησει" (zēsei) comes from the Greek verb "ζῆν" (zēn), which means "to live."

Grammar: "ζησει" (zēsei) is the third person singular future tense form of the verb "ζῆν" (zēn), which means "he/she/it will live."

Description: "ζησει" (zēsei) is a Greek word that expresses the future tense of the verb "ζῆν" (zēn), meaning "to live." It indicates that someone or something will live in the future.
