Word: πιστευετε

Etymology: The word πιστευετε comes from the Greek verb πιστεύω (pisteuó), which means "to believe" or "to have faith". It is derived from the noun πίστις (pistis), which means "faith" or "belief".

Grammar: πιστευετε is the second person plural form of the present tense of the verb πιστεύω. It is used to address a group of people and means "you believe" or "you have faith".

Description: πιστευετε is a common word in the Greek language and is often used in religious contexts. It is used to express belief or faith in something or someone. It can also be used in a more general sense to express trust or confidence in a person or idea.
