Word: πινει

Etymology: πινει is a verb in the present tense form of the Greek language. It comes from the ancient Greek word "πίνω" (pino), which means "to drink".

Description: πινει is a Greek verb that means "he/she/it drinks". It is commonly used in everyday conversation to describe the act of drinking.
