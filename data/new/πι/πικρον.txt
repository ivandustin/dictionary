The word "πικρον" (pikron) is a Greek adjective that means "bitter" or "sharp-tasting". It is derived from the Greek noun "πικρία" (pikria), which means "bitterness". In terms of grammar, "πικρον" is in the neuter gender, singular form. It can be used to describe a variety of things, such as food or emotions. For example, "το πικρόν φαγητό" (to pikron fagito) means "the bitter food", while "η πικρή απογοήτευση" (i pikri apogoitefsi) means "the bitter disappointment".
