Word: πιωμεν

Etymology: πιωμεν is a conjugated form of the Greek verb πίνω (pinō), which means "to drink". It is derived from the Proto-Indo-European root *peh₃- ("to drink").

Grammar: πιωμεν is the first person plural, present active indicative form of the verb πίνω (pinō). It is used to indicate that "we are drinking" in the present tense.

Description: πιωμεν is a verb form that is commonly used in Greek language to express the act of drinking in the present tense. It is often used in everyday conversation and in literature to describe the action of drinking.
