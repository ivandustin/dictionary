Word: πιστευοντες

Etymology: πιστευοντες is a Greek word that comes from the verb πιστεύω (pisteuō), which means "to believe" or "to have faith". The suffix -οντες (-ontes) is a present participle ending, indicating that the word refers to people who are currently believing or having faith.

Grammar: πιστευοντες is the present participle form of the verb πιστεύω (pisteuō), which means "believing" or "having faith". It is in the masculine plural form, indicating that it refers to a group of people who are currently believing or having faith.

Description: πιστευοντες is a word that describes a group of people who are currently believing or having faith. It can be used in a religious context to refer to believers in a particular faith, or in a more general sense to refer to people who have faith in something. The word emphasizes the ongoing nature of belief or faith, indicating that it is not a one-time event but a continuous state of mind.
