The word "πιεται" (pietai) is a verb in the Greek language. 

Etymology: The word comes from the verb "πίνω" (pino), which means "to drink". 

Grammar: "πιεται" is the third person singular form of the present tense of the verb "πίνω" (pino), which means "he/she/it drinks". 

Description: The word "πιεται" is a simple present tense verb that describes the action of drinking. It can be used in various contexts, such as in a sentence like "Ο καφές πιεται ζεστός" (O kafes pietai zestos), which means "Coffee is drunk hot".
