Word: πιστευσετε

Etymology: πιστεύω (pisteúō) is a Greek verb meaning "to believe" or "to have faith". The word πιστευσετε (pisteúsete) is the second person plural future tense of πιστεύω, meaning "you will believe" or "you will have faith".

Grammar: πιστευσετε is a verb in the future tense, second person plural form. It is formed by adding the suffix -σετε (-sete) to the stem πιστεύ- (pisteú-).

Description: πιστευσετε is a word that expresses the idea of believing or having faith in something or someone in the future. It is often used in religious or spiritual contexts, but can also be used in everyday language to express trust or confidence in someone or something.
