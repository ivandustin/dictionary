Word: πικρως

Etymology: πικρως is an adverb in Greek that means "bitterly" or "sourly". It is derived from the adjective πικρός (pikrós), which means "bitter".

Grammar: πικρως is an adverb that modifies verbs, adjectives, or other adverbs. It is often used to describe an action or feeling that is done or felt with bitterness or resentment.

Description: πικρως is a word that conveys a strong sense of bitterness or sourness. It is often used in Greek literature and poetry to describe emotions such as anger, sadness, or disappointment. In modern Greek, it is still commonly used to describe a bitter taste or feeling.
