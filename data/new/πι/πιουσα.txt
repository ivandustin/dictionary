Word: πιουσα

Etymology: πιουσα is the feminine form of the Greek verb πίνω (pino), which means "to drink". It is derived from the Proto-Indo-European root *peh₃-, which means "to drink".

Grammar: πιουσα is a feminine singular form of the present participle of the verb πίνω (pino). It is in the nominative case and can function as a subject or a predicate nominative in a sentence.

Description: πιουσα describes a female who is currently drinking. It can also be used as an adjective to describe a feminine noun that is associated with drinking, such as a wine glass or a drinking vessel.
