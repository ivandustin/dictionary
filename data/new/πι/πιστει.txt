The word "πιστει" (pistei) is a Greek verb that means "to believe" or "to have faith". It is derived from the Greek noun "πίστη" (pistis), which means "faith" or "trust". 

In terms of grammar, "πιστει" is the third person singular present tense form of the verb. It can be conjugated in various tenses and moods to indicate different aspects of belief or faith.

Overall, "πιστει" is a fundamental word in Greek language and culture, representing the importance of faith and trust in one's beliefs and actions.
