Grammar: "φευξεται" is a verb form in the Greek language. It belongs to the future tense and the middle voice. The middle voice indicates that the subject is performing the action on itself or for its own benefit.

Description: "φευξεται" is the third person singular form of the future tense, middle voice of the verb "φεύγω" in Greek. It can be translated as "he/she/it will flee" or "he/she/it will escape".
