Grammar: "Φειδομενοι" is the plural form of the verb "φείδομαι" in the present tense, middle voice, and indicative mood. It is used to describe a group of people who are sparing or frugal.

Description: "Φειδομενοι" is a Greek word that describes individuals or a group of people who are characterized by their frugality or their tendency to be sparing in their actions or use of resources.
