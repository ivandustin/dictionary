Etymology: The word "φεροντες" (pronounced "ferontes") comes from the Greek verb "φέρω" (pronounced "fero"), which means "to carry" or "to bear". It is derived from the Proto-Indo-European root *bʰer-.

Grammar: "Φεροντες" is the plural form of the Greek noun "φερων" (pronounced "feron"), which means "carrier" or "bearer". It belongs to the third declension in Greek grammar.

Description: "Φεροντες" is used to refer to multiple carriers or bearers of something. It can be used in various contexts, such as describing people who carry or transport objects, or metaphorically referring to individuals who bear responsibilities or burdens.
