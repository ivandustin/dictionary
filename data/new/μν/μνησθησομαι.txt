Word: μνησθησομαι

Etymology:
The word μνησθησομαι is a Greek verb that comes from the root word μνημονεύω (mnēmoneuō), which means "to remember." The prefix σ- (s-) is added to the beginning of the word to indicate future tense, making μνησθησομαι mean "I will remember."

Grammar:
μνησθησομαι is a first-person singular future tense verb in Greek. It is a middle voice verb, which means that the subject is both the doer and the receiver of the action. In this case, μνησθησομαι means "I will remember myself."

Description:
μνησθησομαι is a word that expresses the act of remembering oneself. It can be used in a variety of contexts, such as recalling a past event or reminding oneself of a task that needs to be done. The word has a sense of intentionality and purpose, as it implies that the subject is actively choosing to remember something. In Greek philosophy, the act of remembering oneself was seen as a way to connect with one's true nature and achieve self-knowledge.
