Word: μνησθητι

Etymology: μνησθητι is a verb in the aorist imperative form in ancient Greek. It comes from the root word μνημονεύω (mnēmoneuō), which means "to remember" or "to be mindful of."

Grammar: μνησθητι is a second-person singular aorist imperative verb in ancient Greek. It is used to give a command or instruction to someone, telling them to remember or be mindful of something.

Description: μνησθητι is a word that is often used in ancient Greek literature and texts, particularly in the context of advice or instruction. It is a command to remember something important, whether it be a lesson, a warning, or a piece of wisdom. In modern usage, it is not commonly used outside of academic or literary contexts.
