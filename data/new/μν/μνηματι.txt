μνηματι (mnēmati) is a Greek word that means "in memory of" or "as a memorial to". It is derived from the Greek word μνήμη (mnēmē), which means "memory". In terms of grammar, μνηματι is a preposition that is often used with the genitive case to indicate the person or thing being remembered. It can also be used with the dative case to indicate the person or thing for whom the memorial is intended. Overall, μνηματι is a word that is often used in inscriptions and other forms of commemoration to honor and remember individuals or events.
