Word: μνημην

Etymology:
The word μνημην (mnēmēn) is derived from the Greek verb μιμνήσκω (mimnēskō), which means "to remember" or "to recall". It is related to the noun μνήμη (mnēmē), which means "memory".

Grammar:
μνημην is a noun in the singular form. It is a masculine noun in the nominative case.

Description:
μνημην refers to a "reminder" or "memorial". It can also refer to a "monument" or "memorial stone". In ancient Greece, μνημην was often used to refer to a commemorative offering or sacrifice made to the gods in memory of a deceased person. Today, the word is still used in modern Greek to refer to a memorial or monument.
