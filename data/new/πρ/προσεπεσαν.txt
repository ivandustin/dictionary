Word: προσεπεσαν

Etymology: προσεπεσαν is a Greek verb that comes from the prefix πρός (pros), meaning "towards," and the verb επίπτω (epíptō), meaning "to fall upon." Therefore, προσεπεσαν means "they fell upon" or "they approached."

Grammar: προσεπεσαν is the third person plural aorist indicative active form of the verb προσπίπτω (prospíptō), which means "to fall upon" or "to approach." In this form, it indicates that the action of falling upon or approaching was completed in the past.

Description: προσεπεσαν is a verb that describes an action of falling upon or approaching someone or something. It can be used in various contexts, such as in a physical sense, as in "they fell upon the enemy," or in a figurative sense, as in "they approached the problem with a new strategy."
