The word "προ" is a Greek prefix that means "before" or "in front of". It is commonly used in English words that have Greek roots, such as "prologue" (an introductory section of a literary work) and "prospective" (related to the future). 

In terms of grammar, "προ" is a preposition that is often used to indicate location or direction. For example, "προς το σπίτι" means "towards the house". 

Etymologically, "προ" comes from the Greek word "πρός" (pros), which means "towards" or "in front of". 

Overall, "προ" is a versatile prefix that is used in a variety of contexts in both Greek and English.
