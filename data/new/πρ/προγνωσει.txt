Word: προγνωσει

Etymology: The word προγνωσει comes from the Greek root words προ- (pro-), meaning "before" or "in advance," and γνώση (gnósi), meaning "knowledge" or "understanding." Together, they form the word πρόγνωση (prógnosi), which means "foreknowledge" or "prediction."

Grammar: προγνωσει is a verb in the Greek language, specifically in the future tense. It is the third person singular form of the verb προγνωστεύω (prognostévo), which means "to predict" or "to foretell." 

Description: προγνωσει refers to the act of predicting or foretelling something that will happen in the future. It is often used in the context of weather forecasting, medical diagnoses, and financial predictions. The word implies a certain level of knowledge or expertise in a particular field, as well as the ability to make accurate predictions based on that knowledge.
