Word: προηγουμενοι

Etymology: The word προηγουμενοι comes from the Greek verb προηγέομαι (proēgéomai), which means "to go before, to lead, to precede." The suffix -οι (-oi) indicates that the word is plural.

Grammar: προηγουμενοι is the plural form of the adjective προηγούμενος (proēgoúmenos), which means "previous, former, preceding." It is used to describe a group of people or things that came before another group or thing.

Description: προηγουμενοι can be used to refer to a variety of things that came before something else. For example, it can refer to previous generations, previous leaders, or previous events. In Greek grammar, it is often used to describe the subject of a sentence that came before the main verb.
