Word: προσφορά

Etymology: The word προσφορά comes from the Greek verb προσφέρω (prosfero), which means "to offer" or "to present". The noun form προσφορά (prosfora) means "offering" or "sacrifice".

Grammar: προσφορά is a feminine noun in Greek. It is declined in the singular and plural forms, and can be used as both a subject and an object in a sentence.

Description: προσφορά is a term used to describe an offering or a sacrifice made to a deity or a person of authority. It can also refer to a proposal or an offer made in a business or social context. In modern Greek, it is commonly used to describe a sale or a discount offered by a business.
