Word: προσαββατον

Etymology:
The word προσαββατον (prosabbaton) is a Greek word that is composed of two parts: "pros" meaning "before" and "sabbaton" meaning "Sabbath". Therefore, the word προσαββατον literally means "before the Sabbath".
