It is formed by adding the suffix -ηται (-etai) to the stem προσκαλεσ- (proskales-), which is derived from the verb προσκαλέω (proskaleo). The aorist tense indicates a completed action in the past, while the subjunctive mood expresses doubt, possibility, or hypothetical situations.
