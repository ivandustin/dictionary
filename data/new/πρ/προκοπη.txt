Word: προκοπη

Etymology:
The word προκοπη (prokope) is derived from the Greek verb προκόπτω (prokopto), which means "to advance" or "to make progress". The noun form προκοπή (prokope) means "progress" or "advancement".

Grammar:
In Greek, προκοπή is a feminine noun of the first declension. Its nominative singular form is προκοπή, and its genitive singular form is προκοπῆς (prokopes).

Description:
προκοπή is a term used to describe progress or advancement in various fields, such as education, career, or personal growth. It can also refer to the progress of a society or a nation. In Greek Orthodox Christianity, the term is used to describe the spiritual progress of a believer towards God.
