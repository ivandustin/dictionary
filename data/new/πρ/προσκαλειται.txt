Word: προσκαλείται

Etymology: The word προσκαλείται comes from the Greek verb προσκαλώ, which means "to call upon" or "to invite." The suffix -είται is the third person singular form of the passive voice, indicating that the subject is being called upon or invited.

Grammar: προσκαλείται is a verb in the present tense, passive voice, third person singular form. It is conjugated from the verb προσκαλώ, which is a regular first conjugation verb in Greek.

Description: προσκαλείται means "is being called upon" or "is being invited" in English. It is often used in formal or polite contexts, such as when inviting someone to an event or requesting their presence. The word can also be used in a more general sense to indicate that someone is being asked to do something or participate in something.
