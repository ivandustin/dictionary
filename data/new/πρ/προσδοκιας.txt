Word: προσδοκιας

Etymology: The word προσδοκιας (prosdokia) comes from the Greek root words προς (pros) meaning "toward" and δοκεω (dokeo) meaning "to expect". Therefore, the word προσδοκιας means "expectation" or "anticipation".

Grammar: προσδοκιας is a feminine noun in the Greek language. It is in the nominative case and its plural form is προσδοκιες (prosdokies).

Description: προσδοκιας refers to the act of expecting or anticipating something. It can be used in various contexts, such as in personal relationships, business, or politics. It can also refer to the hope or expectation of a future event or outcome. In Greek culture, the concept of προσδοκιας is highly valued and is often associated with optimism and positivity.
