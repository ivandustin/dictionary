Word: πρωτοστατην

Etymology: The word πρωτοστατην comes from the Greek words πρῶτος (prōtos) meaning "first" and στατήρ (statēr) meaning "ruler" or "leader". 

Grammar: πρωτοστατην is a noun in the feminine gender and accusative case. 

Description: πρωτοστατην refers to a female leader or ruler who holds a position of authority or power. It can also be used to describe a woman who is the head of a household or organization.
