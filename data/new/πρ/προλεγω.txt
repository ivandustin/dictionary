Word: προλέγω (prolego)

Etymology: The word προλέγω comes from the Greek words προ (pro), meaning "before," and λέγω (lego), meaning "to speak." Therefore, the literal meaning of προλέγω is "to speak before."

Grammar: προλέγω is a verb in the Greek language. It belongs to the first conjugation and is classified as a regular verb. It is conjugated in all tenses, moods, and voices.

Description: προλέγω means "to speak beforehand," "to foretell," or "to predict." It is often used in the context of giving a warning or a prediction about something that is going to happen in the future. In the New Testament, it is used to describe the act of prophesying or preaching.
