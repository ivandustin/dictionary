Word: προσδέχεται

It is conjugated from the verb προσδέχομαι (prosdechomai), which is a compound verb made up of the prefix προσ- (pros-) and the verb δέχομαι (dechomai). The verb is in the active voice, indicating that the subject is performing the action of receiving or welcoming.
