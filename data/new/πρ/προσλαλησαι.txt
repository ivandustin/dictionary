Word: προσλαλησαι

The word προσλαλησαι is a Greek verb that comes from the combination of two words: πρός (pros) meaning "towards" and λαλέω (laleo) meaning "to speak". Therefore, the literal meaning of προσλαλησαι is "to speak towards" or "to address".

προσλαλησαι is a versatile verb that can be used in a variety of contexts. It can be used to describe the act of speaking to someone directly, as in "I addressed him" or "I spoke to her". It can also be used to describe the act of speaking about someone or something, as in "I talked about him" or "I mentioned it".
