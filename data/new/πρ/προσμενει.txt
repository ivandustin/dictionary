Word: προσμένει (pros-mé-nei)

Etymology: The word προσμένει comes from the Greek verb προσμένω (pros-mé-no), which means "to wait for" or "to expect." It is formed by combining the prefix προσ- (pros-), which means "toward" or "in addition to," and the verb μένω (mé-no), which means "to stay" or "to remain."

Grammar: προσμένει is a present tense verb in the third person singular form. It is conjugated from the verb προσμένω, which is a regular verb in the Greek language. The verb is often used with the preposition για (gia), which means "for" or "about," to indicate what is being waited for or expected.

Description: The word προσμένει is used to describe the act of waiting for or expecting something. It can be used in a variety of contexts, such as waiting for a person, an event, or a result.
