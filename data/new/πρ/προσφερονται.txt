Word: προσφέρονται

Etymology: The word προσφέρονται comes from the Greek verb προσφέρω (prosfero), which means "to offer" or "to present". The suffix -ονται (-ontai) indicates that the verb is in the passive voice, meaning that the subject is receiving the action of the verb.

Grammar: προσφέρονται is a third-person plural present passive indicative verb in Greek. It is conjugated from the verb προσφέρω, which is a first-conjugation verb. The verb stem is προσφερ-, and the present tense suffix for the third-person plural is -ονται.

Description: προσφέρονται is a verb that describes an action of being offered or presented to someone or something. In the passive voice, the subject is receiving the action of the verb. The word can be used in a variety of contexts, such as offering a gift, presenting an idea, or providing a service.
