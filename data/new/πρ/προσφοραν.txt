The word "προσφοραν" (prosforan) is a Greek noun that means "offering" or "sacrifice". It comes from the verb "προσφέρω" (prosfero), which means "to offer" or "to present". In terms of grammar, "προσφοραν" is in the accusative case, which is used to indicate the direct object of a verb. Overall, "προσφοραν" is a word that is commonly used in religious contexts to refer to an offering or sacrifice made to a deity.
