Word: προσδοκώντων

Etymology: The word προσδοκώντων is derived from the Greek verb προσδοκάω (prosdokao), which means "to expect" or "to anticipate". The suffix -ντων (-ntōn) is added to the present participle form of the verb to indicate the plural form, making it mean "those who are expecting" or "those who are anticipating".

Grammar: προσδοκώντων is the genitive plural form of the present participle of the verb προσδοκάω. In Greek grammar, the genitive case is used to indicate possession or relationship between nouns. In this case, προσδοκώντων is used to describe a group of people who possess the quality of expecting or anticipating something.

Description: προσδοκώντων is a Greek word that describes a group of people who are expecting or anticipating something. It can be used in various contexts, such as in a sentence like "Οι προσδοκώντων της νίκης ομάδας ήταν πολλοί" (The group of people who were expecting the victory was large).
