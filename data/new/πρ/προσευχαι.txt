The word "προσευχαι" (proseuchai) is a Greek noun that means "prayers" or "supplications." It is derived from the verb "προσεύχομαι" (proseuchomai), which means "to pray" or "to make supplication." In Greek grammar, "προσευχαι" is a plural noun in the nominative case. It is often used in the New Testament of the Bible to refer to prayers offered to God.
