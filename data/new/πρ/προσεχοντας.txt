Word: προσεχοντας

Etymology: The word προσεχοντας comes from the Greek verb προσέχω (prosecho), which means "to pay attention to" or "to be careful of". The suffix -ντας (-ntas) is a present participle ending, indicating that the action is ongoing.

Grammar: προσεχοντας is the present participle form of the verb προσέχω (prosecho), which means "to pay attention to" or "to be careful of". It is formed by adding the present participle ending -ντας (-ntas) to the stem προσεχ- (prosech-). In this form, it functions as an adjective or adverb, describing an ongoing action.

Description: προσεχοντας describes the act of paying attention to something or being careful of something. It can be used to describe a person who is being attentive or cautious, or to describe an ongoing action of paying attention or being careful. For example, "Προσεχοντας τα βήματά του, δεν έπεσε." (Paying attention to his steps, he didn't fall.)
