Word: προσδραμων

Etymology: The word προσδραμων is derived from the Greek words πρός (pros) meaning "towards" and δραμεῖν (dramein) meaning "to run". Therefore, the literal meaning of προσδραμων is "running towards".

Grammar: προσδραμων is a masculine noun in the nominative singular form. It belongs to the third declension in Greek grammar. 

Description: προσδραμων is not a commonly used word in modern Greek. It is mostly found in ancient Greek texts and literature. The word is often used to describe someone who is running towards something with great speed and determination. It can also be used metaphorically to describe someone who is eagerly pursuing a goal or objective.
