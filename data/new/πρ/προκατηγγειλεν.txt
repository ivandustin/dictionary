The word "προκατηγγειλεν" is a Greek verb that means "to announce beforehand" or "to foretell." It is derived from the prefix "προ-" meaning "before" and the verb "καταγγέλλω" meaning "to announce" or "to declare." In terms of grammar, "προκατηγγειλεν" is in the aorist tense, which is used to describe a completed action in the past.
