Word: προσκαλεσαμενος

Etymology: προσκαλεσαμενος is a Greek word that comes from the verb προσκαλέω (proskaléō), which means "to call to oneself" or "to invite."

Grammar: προσκαλεσαμενος is the aorist participle of the verb προσκαλέω (proskaléō), which means "having called to oneself" or "having invited." It is in the masculine gender, singular number, and nominative case.

Description: προσκαλεσαμενος is used to describe someone who has been invited or called to oneself. It can be used in various contexts, such as in social gatherings, meetings, or events. In Greek literature, it is often used to describe the actions of gods or heroes who invite or summon others to their presence.
