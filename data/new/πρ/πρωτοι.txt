Word: πρωτοι

Etymology: πρωτοι comes from the Greek word πρῶτος (prōtos), meaning "first" or "foremost". 

Grammar: πρωτοι is the plural form of the Greek adjective πρῶτος (prōtos), which means "first" or "foremost". It is declined according to the gender, number, and case of the noun it modifies. 

Description: πρωτοι can be translated as "first ones" or "foremost ones". It is often used to describe a group of people who are the leaders or pioneers in a particular field or endeavor. In Greek mythology, the πρωτοι were the first gods and goddesses to emerge from Chaos.
