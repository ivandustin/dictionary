Word: πρωτοις

Etymology: πρωτοις is the dative plural form of the Greek word πρῶτος (prōtos), which means "first" or "foremost". It is derived from the Proto-Indo-European root *per- which means "forward" or "in front".

Description: πρωτοις is a Greek word that is used to refer to "the first ones" or "the foremost". It can be used in various contexts, such as in a sentence like "Δώστε τα πρωτοις" (Dóste ta prōtois), which means "Give them to the first ones".
