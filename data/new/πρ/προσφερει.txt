Word: προσφέρει

Etymology: προσφέρει comes from the Greek verb προσφέρω (prosfero), which means "to offer" or "to present". It is derived from the prefix προς (pros), meaning "toward", and the verb φέρω (fero), meaning "to carry" or "to bring".

- Εγώ προσφέρω (ego prosfero) - I offer
