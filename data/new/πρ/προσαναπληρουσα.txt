Word: προσαναπληρουσα

Etymology: The word προσαναπληρουσα is derived from the Greek words προς (pros) meaning "towards" or "in addition to", ανα (ana) meaning "again" or "re-" and πληρούσα (pliroosa) meaning "filling" or "fulfilling". 

Grammar: προσαναπληρουσα is a verb in the present participle form, feminine gender, and singular number. It is derived from the verb προσαναπληρόω (prosanapliroo) which means "to fill up again" or "to complete". 

Description: προσαναπληρουσα is used to describe a female subject who is currently in the process of filling up or completing something again. It can be used in various contexts, such as in a sentence like "Η Μαρία είναι προσαναπληρουσα το ποτήρι της με νερό" (Maria is refilling her glass with water).
