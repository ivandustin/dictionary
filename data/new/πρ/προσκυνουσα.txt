Word: προσκυνούσα (proskynoúsa)

Grammar: προσκυνούσα is the present participle form of the verb προσκυνέω (proskynéō), which is in the active voice and the feminine gender. It is used to describe a female subject who is currently worshipping or bowing down in reverence.

Description: προσκυνούσα is a Greek word that describes an act of worship or reverence. It can be used to describe a person who is physically bowing down in worship, or it can be used more figuratively to describe someone who is showing great respect or admiration for someone or something.
