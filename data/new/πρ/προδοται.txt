The word προδοται (prodótai) is derived from the Greek word προδότης (prodótis), which means "traitor" or "betrayer". The word is formed from the prefix προ- (pro-), meaning "before" or "in front of", and the verb δίδωμι (dídōmi), meaning "to give". Therefore, the word προδότης (prodótis) literally means "one who gives before" or "one who gives in advance".
