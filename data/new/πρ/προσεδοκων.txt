Word: προσεδοκων

Etymology:
The word προσεδοκων is a Greek verb that comes from the combination of two words: προς (pros) meaning "towards" and εδοκων (edokon) meaning "expecting". Therefore, the literal meaning of προσεδοκων is "towards expecting".

Grammar:
προσεδοκων is a present participle in the active voice, masculine gender, and singular number. It is derived from the verb προσδοκαω (prosdokao) which means "to expect" or "to anticipate". The present participle form indicates that the action of expecting is ongoing or continuous.

Description:
προσεδοκων is a word that describes someone who is constantly expecting or anticipating something. It can be used to describe a person who is eagerly waiting for something to happen or someone who is always on the lookout for something. The word can also be used to describe a situation where there is a sense of anticipation or expectation.
