The word "προκοψουσιν" (prokopsousin) is a Greek verb in the future tense, third person plural form. It comes from the root word "προκόπτω" (prokopto), which means "to advance" or "to make progress". 

In terms of grammar, "προκοψουσιν" is a regular future tense verb in Greek, formed by adding the future tense suffix "-σουσιν" to the stem "προκοπ-". 

Overall, the word "προκοψουσιν" can be translated to mean "they will advance" or "they will make progress".
