Word: προστρέχοντες

Etymology: The word προστρέχοντες comes from the Greek verb προστρέχω (prostrécho), which means "to run towards" or "to approach". The suffix -οντες (-ontes) is a present participle ending, indicating that the word refers to people who are currently running towards or approaching something.

Grammar: προστρέχοντες is a present participle in the masculine plural form. It is derived from the verb προστρέχω, which is in the present tense. The word can function as an adjective, describing a group of people who are currently running towards or approaching something.

Description: προστρέχοντες describes a group of people who are actively running towards or approaching something. The word can be used in a literal sense, such as a group of athletes running towards the finish line, or in a figurative sense, such as a group of people working towards a common goal. The word emphasizes the action and movement of the group, highlighting their determination and purpose.
