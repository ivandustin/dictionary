Word: προσκαρτερησομεν

Etymology: 
The word προσκαρτερησομεν is a Greek verb that comes from the combination of two words: προσ (pros) meaning "toward" or "with," and καρτερέω (karteréō) meaning "to be strong," "to endure," or "to persevere." Therefore, the word προσκαρτερησομεν can be translated as "we will persevere" or "we will endure."

Grammar:
προσκαρτερησομεν is a first-person plural future indicative active verb in Greek. It is formed by adding the future tense suffix -σομεν (-somen) to the present tense stem προσκαρτερη- (proskarterē-). The verb is conjugated as follows:

- Singular: προσκαρτερήσω (proskarterēsō) - I will persevere
- Dual: προσκαρτερήσομεν (proskarterēsomen) - We two will persevere
- Plural: προσκαρτερησομεν (proskarterēsomen) - We will persevere

Description:
προσκαρτερησομεν is a powerful and inspiring word that conveys the idea of persistence, endurance, and determination. It is often used in a motivational context to encourage people to keep going despite difficulties and challenges. The word reminds us that success is not achieved overnight, but through hard work, perseverance, and a never-give-up attitude.
