Word: προσδοκώντας

Etymology: The word προσδοκώντας comes from the Greek verb προσδοκάω (prosdokao), which means "to expect" or "to anticipate". The suffix -ντας (-ntas) is added to the verb stem to form the present participle, which means "expecting" or "anticipating".

Grammar: προσδοκώντας is the present participle of the verb προσδοκάω. It is used to describe an ongoing action of expecting or anticipating something. In Greek, present participles can function as adjectives or as part of a verb tense.

Description: προσδοκώντας is a word that describes the act of expecting or anticipating something. It can be used in a variety of contexts, such as waiting for a person or event, anticipating a result or outcome, or hoping for a certain outcome. The word conveys a sense of eagerness or excitement about what is to come.
