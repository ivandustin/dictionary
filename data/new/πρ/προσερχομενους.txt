The word προσερχομενους is a present participle form of the Greek verb προσέρχομαι (proserchomai), which means "to come near, approach, or draw near." The word is derived from the prefix προς (pros), meaning "toward," and the verb ἔρχομαι (erchomai), meaning "to come."
