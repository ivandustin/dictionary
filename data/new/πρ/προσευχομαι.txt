Word: προσευχομαι

Etymology:
The word προσευχομαι (proseuchomai) comes from the Greek words προς (pros) meaning "toward" or "to" and ευχη (euche) meaning "prayer". Therefore, the word προσευχομαι means "to pray" or "to offer a prayer".

Grammar:
προσευχομαι is a verb in the Greek language. It is in the present tense and the middle voice. The middle voice indicates that the subject is both the doer and the receiver of the action. In this case, the subject is the person praying and they are both doing the action of praying and receiving the benefits of prayer.

προσευχομαι is a word that is commonly used in the New Testament of the Bible. It is used to describe the act of praying or offering a prayer to God.
