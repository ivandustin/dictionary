Word: προισταμενους

The word προισταμενους is a Greek word that comes from the verb προΐστημι (proistēmi), which means "to stand before, to preside over, to lead." The suffix -ους (-ous) is a masculine plural ending, which means that the word refers to a group of people who are presiding over or leading something.
