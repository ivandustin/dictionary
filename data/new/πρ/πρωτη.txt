Word: πρωτη

Etymology: πρωτη comes from the Greek word πρῶτος (prōtos), meaning "first" or "foremost". 

Grammar: πρωτη is a feminine noun in the nominative singular form. It is the feminine form of the adjective πρώτος (prōtos), meaning "first" or "foremost". 

Description: πρωτη can be translated to "first" or "foremost" in English. It is often used to describe the first or most important thing in a series or list. For example, πρωτη θέση (proti thesi) means "first place" and πρωτη φορά (proti fora) means "first time".
