Word: προσεχοντες

Etymology: προσεχοντες is a Greek verb that comes from the root word προσέχω (prosechō), which means "to pay attention to" or "to be careful of." The suffix -οντες (-ontes) indicates that it is in the present participle form, meaning "paying attention" or "being careful."

Grammar: προσεχοντες is a present participle verb in the active voice. It is in the masculine plural form, indicating that the subject is a group of males who are currently paying attention or being careful.

Description: προσεχοντες is a word that emphasizes the importance of being attentive and careful. It can be used in a variety of contexts, such as in a warning to be cautious or in a reminder to stay focused. In the New Testament, it is often used in a spiritual sense, urging believers to be watchful and alert in their faith.
