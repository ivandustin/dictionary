Word: προσευξωμεθα

Etymology: The word προσευξωμεθα comes from the Greek verb προσεύχομαι (proseúchomai), which means "to pray" or "to make a request to a deity". The word is formed by combining the prefix προς (pros), which means "toward" or "to", and the verb εύχομαι (eúchomai), which means "to pray" or "to wish".

Grammar: προσευξωμεθα is a first-person plural present subjunctive form of the verb προσεύχομαι (proseúchomai). The subjunctive mood is used to express doubt, uncertainty, or a hypothetical situation. In this case, προσευξωμεθα is used to express a request or desire that may or may not be fulfilled.

Description: προσευξωμεθα is a Greek word that means "let us pray" or "let us make a request to a deity". It is often used in religious contexts, such as in prayers or during religious ceremonies.
