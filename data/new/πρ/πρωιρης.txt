Word: πρωιρης

Etymology: The word πρωιρης comes from the Greek word πρωι, which means "early in the morning". The suffix -ρης is a common Greek suffix used to form adjectives.

Grammar: πρωιρης is an adjective in the masculine gender, singular form. It is used to describe something that is early in the morning.

Description: πρωιρης is an adjective that describes something that happens or occurs early in the morning. It can be used to describe a person who wakes up early, an event that takes place in the morning, or a feeling or mood that is associated with the early hours of the day. For example, one might say "Ο πρωιρης ήλιος είναι πολύ όμορφος" (The early morning sun is very beautiful).
