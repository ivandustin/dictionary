Word: ορκίζω

- ορκίζω (orkizo) - I swear
- ορκίζεις (orkizeis) - you swear
- ορκίζει (orkizei) - he/she/it swears
- ορκίζουμε (orkizoume) - we swear
- ορκίζετε (orkizete) - you all swear
- ορκίζουν (orkizoun) - they swear

Description: ορκίζω means "to swear" or "to take an oath". It is often used in legal or religious contexts, where someone is required to make a solemn promise or declaration. The word can also be used more generally to mean "to pledge" or "to vow".
