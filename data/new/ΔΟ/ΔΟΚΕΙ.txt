Etymology: The word "δοκεί" (dokei) comes from the Greek verb "δοκεῖν" (dokein), which means "to seem" or "to appear."

Grammar: "Δοκεί" is a verb in the present tense, third person singular form. It is often used in Greek to express opinions, beliefs, or judgments.

Description: "Δοκεί" is a versatile word that can be used in various contexts. It is commonly used to express subjective opinions or beliefs about something. It can also be used to indicate that something appears or seems a certain way.
