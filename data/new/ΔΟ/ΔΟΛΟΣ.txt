Grammar: In Greek, "δολος" (dolos) is a masculine noun of the second declension. It is in the nominative case and can be used as both a singular and plural noun.

Description: "Δολος" (dolos) refers to deceit, trickery, or cunning. It can also be translated as "guile" or "deception." The word is often used to describe dishonest or deceptive behavior, particularly in the context of manipulation or fraud.
