Etymology: The word "δουλευει" comes from the Greek verb "δουλεύω" (doulevo), which means "to work" or "to labor."

Grammar: "Δουλεύει" is the third person singular form of the present tense of the verb "δουλεύω" (doulevo). It is used to describe an action being performed by a singular subject in the present.

Description: "Δουλεύει" is a Greek word that translates to "he/she/it works" or "he/she/it is working" in English. It is commonly used to indicate someone's current employment or occupation.
