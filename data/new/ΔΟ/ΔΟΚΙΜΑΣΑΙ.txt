Grammar: "δοκιμασαί" is the aorist tense, middle voice, subjunctive mood form of the verb "δοκιμάζω" (dokimazo). The aorist tense refers to a past action, the middle voice indicates that the subject is performing the action on itself, and the subjunctive mood expresses possibility or potentiality.

Description: "δοκιμασαί" is a Greek verb form that can be translated as "to test oneself" or "to try oneself." It implies the act of putting oneself to the test or undergoing a trial in order to determine one's abilities, skills, or character.
