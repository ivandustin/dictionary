Etymology: The word "δοκείς" (dokeis) comes from the Greek verb "δοκέω" (dokeo), which means "to seem" or "to appear."

Grammar: "Δοκείς" is the second person singular form of the verb "δοκέω" (dokeo) in the present tense. It is used to address someone directly and means "you seem" or "you appear."

Description: "Δοκείς" is a Greek word that is used to express an observation or opinion about someone's appearance or behavior. It implies that the speaker has formed a judgment or impression based on what they have seen or experienced.
