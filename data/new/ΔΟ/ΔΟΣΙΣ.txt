Etymology: The word "δοσις" (dosis) comes from the Greek verb "δίδωμι" (didōmi), which means "to give" or "to offer."

Grammar: In Greek, "δοσις" is a feminine noun of the first declension. It is in the nominative case and singular number.

Description: "Δοσις" refers to a dose or a portion of something, typically used in the context of medicine or drugs. It can also be used more broadly to describe a quantity or amount of something given or administered.
