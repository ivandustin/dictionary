Etymology: The word "δοκων" (dokon) comes from the Greek verb "δοκέω" (dokeo), which means "to think" or "to seem". 

Grammar: "Δοκών" is a noun in the masculine gender, singular number, and nominative case. It is the present participle of the verb "δοκέω" (dokeo), which means "thinking" or "seeming". 

Description: "Δοκών" refers to someone who is thinking or seeming to be a certain way. It can also be used to describe someone who is opinionated or judgmental.
