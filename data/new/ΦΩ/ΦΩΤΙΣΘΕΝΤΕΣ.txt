Etymology: The word "φωτισθεντες" comes from the Greek verb "φωτίζω" (fotízo), which means "to illuminate" or "to enlighten." It is derived from the noun "φῶς" (fós), meaning "light."

Grammar: "Φωτισθέντες" is the plural form of the verb "φωτίζω" in the aorist tense, passive voice, and participle mood. It is used to describe a group of people who have been illuminated or enlightened.

Description: The word "φωτισθέντες" is often used in a spiritual or metaphorical sense to describe individuals who have gained knowledge, understanding, or insight. It implies a transformation or awakening of the mind or soul.
