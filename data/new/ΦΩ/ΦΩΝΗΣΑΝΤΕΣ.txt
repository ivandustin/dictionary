Grammar: "Φωνησαντες" is the plural form of the verb "φωνέω" in the aorist tense. It is used to describe a group of people who have spoken or made a sound in the past.

Description: "Φωνησαντες" is a Greek word that can be translated as "having spoken" or "having made a sound." It is often used in narratives or descriptions to indicate that a group of people has performed an action of speaking or making a sound in the past.
