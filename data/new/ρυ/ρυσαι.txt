Word: ρυσαι

Etymology: The word ρυσαι is derived from the Greek verb "ρύομαι" (ryomai), which means "to flow" or "to stream". 

Grammar: ρυσαι is the aorist tense, second person singular form of the verb "ρύομαι" (ryomai). It is used to indicate a completed action in the past. 

Description: ρυσαι means "you flowed" or "you streamed" in English. It is a verb that describes the action of something flowing or streaming, such as water or a river.
