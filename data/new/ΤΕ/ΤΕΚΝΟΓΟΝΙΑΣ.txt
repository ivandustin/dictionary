Etymology: The word "τεκνογονίας" comes from the Greek roots "τέκνον" (teknon) meaning "child" and "γένεσις" (genesis) meaning "birth" or "origin". 

Grammar: "τεκνογονίας" is a noun in the genitive case, singular form. In Greek grammar, the genitive case is used to indicate possession, origin, or relationship.

Description: "τεκνογονίας" refers to the act or process of giving birth to or producing children. It can also be used more broadly to describe the concept of procreation or the study of the origin and development of offspring.
