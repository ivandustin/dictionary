Grammar: In Greek, "τελος" is a noun. It is a neuter noun of the second declension. It can be used in both the singular and plural forms.

Description: "Τελος" has several meanings in Greek. It can refer to the end or completion of something, the goal or purpose of an action, the result or outcome of an event, or the payment or fee for a service. It is a versatile word that is used in various contexts to convey the idea of completion or finality.
