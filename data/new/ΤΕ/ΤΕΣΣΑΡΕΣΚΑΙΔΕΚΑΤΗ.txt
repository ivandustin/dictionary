Etymology: The word "τεσσαρεσκαιδεκατη" comes from the Greek words "τέσσαρες" (téssares) meaning "four" and "καί" (kaí) meaning "and", combined with the word "δεκατη" (dekátē) meaning "fourteenth". 

Grammar: "τεσσαρεσκαιδεκατη" is a feminine noun in the nominative case. It is declined as follows: 

Singular: η τεσσαρεσκαιδεκατη (i tessareskaidekati) 
Plural: οι τεσσαρεσκαιδεκατες (oi tessareskaidekates) 

Description: "τεσσαρεσκαιδεκατη" means "fourteenth" in Greek. It is used to indicate the ordinal number corresponding to the number 14.
