Etymology: The word "τεχνη" (technē) comes from the Greek language and is derived from the root "τεχν" (tech), which means "art" or "craft." It is related to the English word "technology."

Grammar: In Greek, "τεχνη" (technē) is a feminine noun. It belongs to the first declension and has the genitive form "τεχνης" (technēs).

Description: "Τεχνη" (technē) refers to skill, art, or craft. It encompasses the knowledge, techniques, and abilities required to produce or create something. It can be used to describe various forms of art, craftsmanship, or expertise in a particular field.
