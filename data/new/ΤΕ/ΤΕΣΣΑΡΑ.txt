Etymology: The word "τεσσαρα" (tessara) comes from the Greek word "τέσσερα" (téssera), which means "four".

Grammar: In Greek, "τεσσαρα" is a noun in the neuter gender. It is the nominative singular form of the word.

Description: "Τεσσαρα" is the Greek word for "four". It is used to represent the number 4 in counting and numerical expressions.
