Etymology: The word "τελούς" (telous) comes from the Greek language. It is derived from the root word "τέλος" (telos), which means "end" or "goal".

Grammar: In Greek, "τελούς" is a noun in the masculine gender. It is in the singular form.

Description: "Τελούς" is a Greek word that can have multiple meanings depending on the context. It can refer to the end or completion of something, the purpose or goal of an action, or the final result or outcome. It is often used to describe the completion or fulfillment of a task or objective.
