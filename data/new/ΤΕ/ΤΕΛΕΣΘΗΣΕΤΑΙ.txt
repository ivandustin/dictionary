The word "τελεσθησεται" is a Greek verb in the future tense, third person singular form. Here is the information you requested:

Etymology: The word "τελεσθησεται" comes from the Greek root "τελε-" meaning "to complete" or "to accomplish."

Grammar: "τελεσθησεται" is a passive verb in the future tense. It indicates that the action of completing or accomplishing something will be done to the subject.

Description: "τελεσθησεται" describes an action that will be completed or accomplished in the future. The subject of the verb is the one who will receive the action.
