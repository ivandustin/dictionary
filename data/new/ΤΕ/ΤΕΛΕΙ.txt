Etymology: The word "τελεί" (telei) comes from the Greek verb "τελέω" (teleo), which means "to complete" or "to finish". 

Grammar: "Τελεί" is a verb in the present tense, third person singular form. It is derived from the verb "τελέω" and is conjugated as follows: 

- Present tense: τελεί
- Past tense: τελείωσε (teleiose)
- Future tense: θα τελειώσει (tha teleiosei)

Description: "Τελεί" means "he/she/it completes" or "he/she/it finishes" in Greek. It is used to describe the action of completing or finishing something.
