Grammar: In Greek, "τεχνητης" is a masculine noun in the nominative case. It belongs to the third declension and has the genitive form "τεχνητου" (tekhnitou). The word can be declined to indicate different cases, numbers, and genders.

Description: "Τεχνητης" is the Greek word for "artist" or "artisan". It refers to someone who is skilled in a particular craft or art form, such as painting, sculpture, music, or writing. The word can also be used more broadly to describe someone who is creative or skilled in any field.
