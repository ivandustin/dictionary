Etymology: The word "τεκνοις" (teknos) comes from the Greek language. It is derived from the root word "τεκνον" (teknon), which means "child" or "offspring".

Grammar: In Greek, "τεκνοις" (teknos) is a noun in the dative case. It is the plural form of the word "τεκνον" (teknon), which means "children" or "offspring".

Description: "Τεκνοις" (teknos) refers to a group of children or offspring. It can be used to describe multiple children or a collective group of offspring.
