Etymology: The word "τεκμηριοις" (tekmeriois) comes from the Greek word "τεκμήριον" (tekmerion), which means "proof" or "evidence."

Grammar: "Τεκμηριοις" is a noun in the plural form. It belongs to the third declension in Greek grammar.

Description: "Τεκμηριοις" refers to "proofs" or "evidences" in Greek. It is used to describe pieces of evidence or arguments that support a claim or statement.
