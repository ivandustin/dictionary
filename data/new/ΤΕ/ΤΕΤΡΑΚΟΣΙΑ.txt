Etymology: The word "τετρακοσια" (tetrakosia) comes from the Greek words "τέσσερα" (téssera) meaning "four" and "κόσμος" (kósmos) meaning "hundred". It is derived from the ancient Greek numeral system.

Grammar: In Greek, "τετρακοσια" is a noun in the feminine gender. It is the nominative singular form of the word. It can be declined in different cases, numbers, and genders according to the rules of Greek grammar.

Description: "τετρακοσια" means "four hundred" in Greek. It is used to represent the number 400.
