Etymology: The word "τετρακισχίλιος" comes from the Greek words "τέτρα" (tetra), meaning "four," and "χίλιοι" (chilioi), meaning "thousand." 

Grammar: "Τετρακισχίλιος" is an adjective in the masculine form. It is used to describe a noun as "four thousand" in Greek.

Description: "Τετρακισχίλιος" is a numerical adjective that represents the number four thousand in Greek. It is commonly used in various contexts to indicate a large quantity or number.
