Grammar: "τελεσθωσιν" is a verb in the third person plural, aorist tense, subjunctive mood, and active voice. It is used to express a completed action or event that is uncertain or hypothetical.

Description: "τελεσθωσιν" is a Greek verb that is often translated as "let them be completed" or "may they be accomplished". It is commonly used in religious or ceremonial contexts to express a desire or request for something to be fulfilled or achieved.
