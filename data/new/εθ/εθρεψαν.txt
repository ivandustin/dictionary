Word: εθρέψαν (ethrépsan)

Etymology: The word εθρέψαν comes from the Greek verb θρέφω (thréfo), which means "to nourish" or "to feed". The prefix ε- (e-) indicates the past tense, so εθρέψαν means "they nourished" or "they fed".

Grammar: εθρέψαν is the third person plural past tense form of the verb θρέφω (thréfo). It is in the active voice and indicative mood. The verb is conjugated in the aorist tense, which is used to describe a completed action in the past.

Description: εθρέψαν is a verb that describes the action of nourishing or feeding. It is often used in the context of raising or caring for children or animals. The word can also be used metaphorically to describe the act of nurturing or supporting something, such as a relationship or a business.
