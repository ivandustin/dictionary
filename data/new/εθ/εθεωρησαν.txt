Word: εθεωρησαν

Etymology: The word εθεωρησαν comes from the Greek verb θεωρέω (theoreo), which means "to observe" or "to contemplate". The prefix ε- (e-) indicates a completed action, so εθεωρησαν (etheorisan) means "they observed" or "they contemplated".

Grammar: εθεωρησαν is the third person plural aorist active indicative form of the verb θεωρέω (theoreo). In Greek, the aorist tense is used to describe a completed action in the past. The active voice indicates that the subject of the sentence (in this case, "they") performed the action of observing or contemplating.
