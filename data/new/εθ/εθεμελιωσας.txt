Grammar: εθεμελιώσας is a verb in the aorist tense, second person singular, active voice, indicative mood. It is formed by adding the suffix -σας (-sas) to the stem εθεμελιώ- (ethemelió-).

Description: εθεμελιώσας means "you laid the foundation" or "you established" in Greek. It is often used in a figurative sense to describe the establishment or creation of something important or significant.
