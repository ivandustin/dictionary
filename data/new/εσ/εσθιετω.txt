Word: εσθιετω

Grammar: εσθιετω is a present active imperative verb in Greek. It is in the third person singular form and is used to give a command or instruction to someone to eat or consume something.

Description: εσθιετω is a common verb in the Greek language and is used in various contexts. It can be used in a literal sense to refer to the act of eating or consuming food or drink. It can also be used metaphorically to refer to the consumption of knowledge or information.
