The word "εσχηκοτα" is a past participle in Greek, derived from the verb "σχίζω" (schizo), which means "to split" or "to divide". The prefix "ε-" indicates that the action is completed, so "εσχηκοτα" means "having split" or "having divided". 

In terms of grammar, "εσχηκοτα" is a neuter plural form, which means it can refer to multiple objects or concepts that have been split or divided. 

Overall, the word "εσχηκοτα" is a specific form of a verb that describes a completed action of splitting or dividing.
