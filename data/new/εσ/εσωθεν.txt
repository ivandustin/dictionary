Word: εσωθεν

Etymology:
The word εσωθεν (esōthen) is a Greek adverb that means "from within" or "internally". It is derived from the Greek words ες (es) meaning "into" and ωθεν (ōthen) meaning "from there".

Grammar:
εσωθεν is an adverb in Greek. It is used to modify verbs, adjectives, or other adverbs to indicate that something is happening or originating from within.

Description:
εσωθεν is a word that is often used in philosophical and spiritual contexts to describe the inner workings of the human mind and soul. It is also used in medical contexts to describe internal organs or processes. In everyday language, it can be used to describe something that is happening or originating from within a particular group or organization.
