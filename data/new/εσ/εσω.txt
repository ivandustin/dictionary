The word "εσω" (esō) is a Greek adverb that means "inside" or "within". 

Etymology: The word comes from the Greek root "eso-" which means "inside" or "within". 

Grammar: "Εσω" is an adverb and does not change form based on gender or number. 

Description: The word is commonly used in Greek to describe something that is located or happening inside a particular place or object. For example, "Το κλειδί είναι εσω της τσέπης" (The key is inside the pocket). It can also be used figuratively to describe something that is happening internally, such as "Οι σκέψεις μου είναι εσω μου" (My thoughts are within me).
