Word: εστρηνιασεν

Etymology: The word εστρηνιασεν comes from the Greek verb στρηνιάω (strēniáō), which means to live luxuriously or extravagantly. The prefix ε- (e-) is added to the beginning of the verb to indicate that the action is in the past tense.

Grammar: εστρηνιασεν is a third person singular past tense form of the verb στρηνιάω (strēniáō). The verb is in the active voice and indicative mood. 

Description: The word εστρηνιασεν describes an action that was done in the past. It means that someone lived a luxurious or extravagant lifestyle. The word can be used to describe someone who spent a lot of money on unnecessary things or indulged in excessive pleasures.
