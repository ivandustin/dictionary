Word: εσθιει

Etymology:
The word εσθιει (esthiei) is derived from the Greek verb ἐσθίω (esthiō), which means "to eat" or "to consume." This verb is found in various forms throughout the Greek language, including the present tense form εσθίει (esthiei).

Grammar:
εσθιει (esthiei) is a present tense verb in the Greek language. It is in the third person singular form, which means that it refers to a single subject who is currently performing the action of eating or consuming.

The word εσθιει (esthiei) is a common verb in the Greek language, used to describe the act of eating or consuming food. It is often used in everyday conversation and can be found in a variety of contexts, from discussing meals and food preferences to describing the behavior of animals and other living creatures.
