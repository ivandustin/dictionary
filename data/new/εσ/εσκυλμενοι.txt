The word "εσκυλμένοι" (eskylmenoi) is a Greek adjective that means "plundered" or "looted". It comes from the verb "σκυλίζω" (skylizo), which means "to plunder" or "to loot". In terms of grammar, it is a masculine plural adjective in the nominative case, used to describe a group of people or things that have been plundered.
