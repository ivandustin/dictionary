The word "εσπειρας" (espiras) is a Greek noun that means "sowing" or "seed planting". It comes from the verb "σπείρω" (speíro), which means "to sow" or "to scatter seeds". In terms of grammar, it is a feminine noun in the nominative singular form. It can be used in various contexts, such as in agriculture or in a metaphorical sense, such as "sowing the seeds of change".
