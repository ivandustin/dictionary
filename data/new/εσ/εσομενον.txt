The word "εσομενον" is a Greek verb in the future passive participle form. It comes from the verb "είμαι" (eimai), which means "to be". The future passive participle form indicates that the action of being will happen in the future and will be done to the subject. 

In terms of grammar, "εσομενον" is a neuter singular form. It can be used as a noun or an adjective, depending on the context. 

Overall, "εσομενον" can be translated to mean "that which will be" or "that which is about to happen".
