Word: εσταθησαν

Etymology: 
The word εσταθησαν is a conjugated form of the Greek verb "στέφω" (stéfo), which means "to stand". The prefix "ε-" indicates the past tense, and the suffix "-αν" indicates the third person plural. Therefore, εσταθησαν means "they stood".

Grammar:
εσταθησαν is a past tense verb in the third person plural form. It is a regular verb in the Greek language, and it belongs to the first conjugation group. The verb stem is "στέφω" (stéfo), and the past tense suffix for the third person plural is "-αν".

εσταθησαν is a verb that describes an action that took place in the past. It indicates that a group of people stood at a certain point in time. The context of the sentence will determine the exact meaning and significance of the action.
