Grammar: "εσπευδεν" is the third person singular form of the past tense of the verb "σπεύδω". The verb belongs to the first conjugation class in Greek, which means that it follows a regular pattern of conjugation.

Description: The word "εσπευδεν" is used to describe an action that was done quickly or with haste in the past. It can be used in various contexts, such as describing someone's behavior, actions, or movements.
