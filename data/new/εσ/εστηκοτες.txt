Word: εστηκοτες

Etymology: The word εστηκοτες comes from the Greek verb στέκω (stéko), which means "to stand". The suffix -τες (-tes) is a plural ending, indicating that the word refers to multiple people or things that are standing.

It can be used to refer to a group of people who are standing in a particular place, such as a crowd at a concert or a group of soldiers in formation.
