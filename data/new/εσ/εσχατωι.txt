The word "εσχατωι" (eschatoi) is a Greek adjective that means "last" or "final". It is derived from the Greek word "εσχατος" (eschatos), which also means "last" or "final". In terms of grammar, it is a masculine singular form in the dative case. It can be used to describe the final or ultimate state of something, such as the last days of a person's life or the final stage of a project.
