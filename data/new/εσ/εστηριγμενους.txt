Word: εστηριγμένους

Etymology:
The word εστηριγμένους (estirigmenous) is a Greek word that comes from the verb στηρίζω (stirizo), which means "to support" or "to prop up". The word is derived from the noun στῦραξ (styrax), which means "prop" or "support".

Grammar:
εστηριγμένους is the plural form of the past participle of the verb στηρίζω (stirizo). It is used to describe a group of people who have been supported or propped up. In Greek, the word is classified as an adjective and agrees in gender, number, and case with the noun it modifies.

Description:
The word εστηριγμένους is often used in a figurative sense to describe people who have been supported or sustained through difficult times. It can also be used to describe physical objects that have been propped up or supported in some way. The word is commonly used in religious contexts to describe believers who have been supported by their faith.
