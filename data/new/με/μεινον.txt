Word: μεινον

Etymology:
The word μεινον (meinon) is derived from the Greek verb μένω (meno), which means "to stay" or "to remain". The suffix -ον (-on) is added to the verb to form the noun μεινον (meinon), which means "a staying" or "a remaining".

Accusative: μεινον

Description:
μεινον (meinon) is a noun that refers to the act of staying or remaining in a place. It can also be used to describe something that remains or is left behind.
