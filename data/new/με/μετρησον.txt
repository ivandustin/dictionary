μετρησον (metrēson) is a Greek verb in the imperative mood, meaning "measure" or "estimate". It is derived from the Greek word μέτρον (métron), which means "measure" or "standard". In terms of grammar, μετρησον is a regular verb in the second aorist tense, which is used to describe a completed action in the past. The stem of the verb is μετρησ-, and the ending -ον is added to indicate the imperative mood.
