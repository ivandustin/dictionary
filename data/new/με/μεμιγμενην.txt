The word "μεμιγμενην" (memigmenen) is a Greek adjective in the feminine accusative singular form. It is derived from the verb "μίγνυμι" (mingnumi), which means "to mix" or "to blend". The prefix "με-" (me-) indicates that the action is completed, so "μεμιγμενην" means "mixed" or "blended". 

In terms of grammar, "μεμιγμενην" is a participle, which is a type of verb form that can function as an adjective. It agrees in gender, number, and case with the noun it modifies. 

Overall, "μεμιγμενην" is a descriptive word that can be used to describe something that has been mixed or blended together.
