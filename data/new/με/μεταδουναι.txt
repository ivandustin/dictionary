Etymology:
The word μεταδουναι is a compound word in Greek, consisting of two parts: μετά (meta) meaning "after" or "beyond" and δοῦναι (dounai) meaning "to give". Therefore, the literal meaning of μεταδουναι is "to give after" or "to give beyond".

μεταδουναι is a verb in the Greek language, specifically in the aorist tense. It is also in the active voice and infinitive mood.

μεταδουναι is not a commonly used word in modern Greek. It is mostly found in ancient Greek texts and literature. The word is often used in the context of giving something beyond what is expected or required. It can also be used to describe giving something after a certain event or time has passed.
