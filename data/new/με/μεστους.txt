Grammar: μεστούς is an adjective in the masculine accusative plural form. It is used to describe a noun in the accusative case that is masculine and plural.

Description: μεστούς is used to describe something that is full or filled. It can be used to describe a container that is full of something, or a person who is full of emotion or energy. It can also be used figuratively to describe a situation that is full of tension or excitement.
