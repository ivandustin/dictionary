Word: μετατιθεντες

Etymology: μετατιθεντες is a Greek word that comes from the verb μετατίθημι (metatithemi), which means "to transfer" or "to change the position of something". The word is formed by adding the suffix -τες (-tes) to the present participle stem μετατιθεν- (metatithen-).

Grammar: μετατιθεντες is the present participle form of the verb μετατίθημι (metatithemi), which means "transferring" or "changing the position of something". It is a masculine plural form, which means it refers to a group of people who are currently transferring or changing the position of something.
