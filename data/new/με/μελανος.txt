μελανός (melanos) is a Greek adjective that means "black" or "dark". It is derived from the Greek word μέλας (melas), which also means "black". In terms of grammar, μελανός is a masculine singular adjective in the nominative case. It can be used to describe the color of an object or the complexion of a person. For example, "μελανός καφές" (melanos kafe) means "black coffee" and "μελανός δέρματος" (melanos dermatos) means "dark skin".
