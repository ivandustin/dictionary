Word: μεμνημενος

Etymology:
μεμνημενος is a Greek word that comes from the verb μιμνήσκω (mimnisko), which means "to remember". The word is a participle form of the verb, which means "remembering" or "having remembered".

Grammar:
μεμνημενος is a masculine singular nominative participle in Greek. It is declined as follows:

- Nominative: μεμνημένος (memneménos)
- Genitive: μεμνημένου (memneménou)
- Dative: μεμνημένῳ (memneménoi)
- Accusative: μεμνημένον (memneménon)
- Vocative: μεμνημένε (memneméne)

Description:
μεμνημενος is a word that describes someone who is remembering or has remembered something. It can be used to describe a person who has a good memory or who is able to recall information easily. In Greek literature, the word is often used to describe heroes who remember their past deeds or who are remembered for their heroic actions.
