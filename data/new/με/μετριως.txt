μετριως (metriōs) is an adverb in Greek that means "moderately" or "in moderation". 

Etymology: μετριως comes from the Greek word μέτρον (metron), which means "measure" or "moderation". 

Grammar: μετριως is an adverb that modifies verbs, adjectives, or other adverbs. It does not change form based on gender, number, or case. 

Description: μετριως is used to describe an action or behavior that is not extreme or excessive. It can also be used to suggest a sense of balance or proportionality. For example, "He ate the cake moderately" or "She spoke to him in a moderate tone".
