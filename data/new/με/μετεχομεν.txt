Word: μετεχομεν

Etymology: μετεχομεν is a Greek verb that comes from the root word μετέχω (metécho), which means "to participate" or "to share". The suffix -μεν (-men) indicates the present tense, first person plural form of the verb.

Grammar: μετεχομεν is a present tense, first person plural form of the Greek verb μετέχω. It is used to indicate that "we participate" or "we share" in something.

Description: μετεχομεν is a verb that expresses the idea of participating or sharing in something. It can be used in a variety of contexts, such as in discussions about community involvement, group activities, or shared experiences. The word emphasizes the idea of active participation and engagement, rather than simply being a passive observer.
