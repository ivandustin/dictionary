Word: μεταδιδοναι

Etymology:
μετα- (meta-) meaning "after" or "beyond"
διδοναι (didonai) meaning "to give"

Therefore, μεταδιδοναι means "to give after" or "to give beyond".

Grammar:
μεταδιδοναι is a Greek verb in the present tense, active voice, and infinitive mood. It is formed by adding the prefix μετα- to the verb διδοναι.

Description:
μεταδιδοναι is a verb that means to give or transmit something to someone else, often after receiving it from someone else. It can also mean to pass on or hand down something, such as knowledge or tradition. The word is commonly used in the context of teaching or passing on information.
