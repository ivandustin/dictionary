Word: μερους

Etymology:
The word μερους (merous) is derived from the Greek word μέρος (meros), which means "part" or "portion". It is a noun in the singular form.

Grammar:
In Greek, μερους is a second declension noun. It is masculine and singular. Its genitive form is μέρους (merous).

Description:
μερους is a term used in mathematics to refer to a part or fraction of a whole. It is often used in geometry to describe the division of a shape into smaller parts. In ancient Greek philosophy, the concept of μερους was used to describe the idea that everything in the universe is made up of smaller parts or elements.
