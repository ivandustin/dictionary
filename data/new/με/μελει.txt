Word: μελει

Etymology:
The word μελει (melei) is a Greek verb that means "to care" or "to be concerned about". It comes from the Greek root word μελε (mele), which means "care" or "concern".

Grammar:
μελει (melei) is a present tense verb in the third person singular form. It is often used in the context of expressing concern or care for someone or something.

Description:
μελει (melei) is a word that expresses a sense of caring or concern. It can be used in a variety of contexts, such as expressing concern for a loved one's well-being or showing care for a particular cause or issue. The word is often used in a positive sense, indicating a genuine desire to help or make a difference.
