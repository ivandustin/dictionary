Word: μελλησω

Etymology: The word μελλησω (mellēsō) is a verb in the Greek language. It is derived from the root word μέλλω (méllō), which means "to be about to" or "to intend to". 

Grammar: μελλησω is a first-person singular future active indicative form of the verb μέλλω. It is used to express an action that will take place in the future. 

Description: μελλησω is a Greek verb that is commonly used in everyday conversation. It is often used to express an intention or a plan for the future. For example, "Μελλησω να πάω στην παραλία αύριο" (Mellēsō na pao stin paralia aurio) means "I am planning to go to the beach tomorrow". The verb can also be used to express a sense of urgency or importance, as in "Μελλησω να το κάνω αμέσως" (Mellēsō na to kano ameso), which means "I need to do it right away".
