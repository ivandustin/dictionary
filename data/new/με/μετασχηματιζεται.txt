Word: μετασχηματίζεται

Etymology: The word μετασχηματίζεται comes from the Greek roots μετά (meta) meaning "after" or "beyond" and σχηματίζω (schimatizo) meaning "to form" or "to shape". Therefore, the word means "to transform" or "to change form".

Grammar: μετασχηματίζεται is a verb in the present tense, third person singular form. It is derived from the verb μετασχηματίζω (metaschimatizo) which means "to transform" or "to change form". The verb is conjugated in the present tense as follows:

- Εγώ μετασχηματίζομαι (I transform)
- Εσύ μετασχηματίζεσαι (You transform)
- Αυτός/Αυτή/Αυτό μετασχηματίζεται (He/She/It transforms)
- Εμείς μετασχηματιζόμαστε (We transform)
- Εσείς μετασχηματίζεστε (You all transform)
- Αυτοί/Αυτές μετασχηματίζονται (They transform)

Description: μετασχηματίζεται is a verb that describes the action of transforming or changing form. It can be used in various contexts, such as in science to describe the transformation of matter or in personal growth to describe the transformation of a person's mindset or behavior. The word implies a significant change or transformation, rather than a minor adjustment or modification.
