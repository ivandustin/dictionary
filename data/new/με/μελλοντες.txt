In terms of grammar, μελλοντες is a masculine plural noun in the nominative case. It is often used as a substantive, meaning it functions as a noun in a sentence. 

The etymology of μελλοντες comes from the Greek word μέλλω, which means "to be about to" or "to intend to." The present participle form, μελλοντες, is formed by adding the suffix -οντ- to the stem of the verb.
