Word: μερει

Etymology: μερει comes from the Greek word μέρος (meros), which means "part" or "portion".

Description: μερει refers to a part or portion of something. It can be used in various contexts, such as in mathematics to refer to a fraction or in everyday language to refer to a part of a whole. For example, μερει του κήπου (meri tou kipou) means "part of the garden".
