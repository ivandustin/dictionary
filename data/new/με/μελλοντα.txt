μελλοντα (mellonta) is a Greek word that is the present participle of the verb μέλλω (mellō), which means "to be about to" or "to intend to." It is often translated as "future" or "things to come." In grammar, it is a participle that functions as an adjective, describing a noun or pronoun in the present tense. The word has its roots in the Greek word μέλλω, which means "to be destined or fated."
