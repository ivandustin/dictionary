The word "μομφην" (momphen) is a Greek noun that means "blame" or "reproach". It is derived from the verb "μομφή" (momphi), which means "to blame" or "to reproach". In terms of grammar, "μομφην" is in the accusative case and singular number. It can be used in a sentence like "Έχω μομφήν για σένα" (Eho momphen gia sena), which means "I have blame for you" or "I reproach you".
