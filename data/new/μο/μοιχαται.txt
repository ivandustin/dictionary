Grammar: "Μοιχαται" is the third person singular form of the present tense of the verb "μοιχάομαι" (moicháomai), which is a deponent verb. This means that it has a passive form but an active meaning. 

Description: The word "μοιχαται" is often used in a negative context to describe someone who is cheating on their spouse or engaging in extramarital affairs. It is also used metaphorically to describe someone who is disloyal or unfaithful in other areas of life.
