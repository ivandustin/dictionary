Word: μογιλαλον

Etymology: The word μογιλαλον is a Greek word that comes from the combination of two words: μόγις (mogis) meaning "with difficulty" and λαλέω (laleo) meaning "to speak". Therefore, μογιλαλον means "speaking with difficulty" or "stammering".

Singular: το μογιλαλον (to mogilalon) - the stammering
Plural: τα μογιλαλα (ta mogilala) - the stammerings
