Word: ηπειλεί

Etymology: The word ηπειλεί comes from the Greek verb ηπειλώ (ipeiló), which means "to threaten" or "to menace". It is derived from the noun ηπείλη (ipeíli), meaning "threat" or "menace".

Grammar: ηπειλεί is the third person singular form of the present tense of the verb ηπειλώ (ipeiló). It is used to describe an action of threatening or menacing that is being done by a third person.

Description: ηπειλεί is a verb in the Greek language that expresses the act of threatening or menacing. It is used to describe a situation where someone is making a threat or expressing a sense of danger towards someone or something.
