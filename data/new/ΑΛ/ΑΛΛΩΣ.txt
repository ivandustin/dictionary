Grammar: In Greek, "αλλως" is an adverb. It is used to modify verbs, adjectives, or other adverbs to indicate a different or alternative manner or way of doing something.

Description: "Αλλως" can be translated to English as "otherwise," "differently," or "in another way." It is commonly used to express an alternative or contrasting option or to indicate a different approach or perspective.
