Etymology: The word "αλληλών" comes from the Greek language. It is derived from the combination of the words "ἄλλος" (allos) meaning "other" and "ἄλληλος" (allelon) meaning "one another".

Grammar: "Αλληλών" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the plural form and is used to refer to multiple individuals or objects. It is often used in phrases or sentences to express reciprocity or mutual action.
