Grammar: "Αλλομενος" (allomenos) is a masculine noun in the nominative case. It is the present participle of the verb "αλλάσσω" (allasso), which means "changing" or "altering". In Greek grammar, participles are verbal adjectives that can be used to describe the state or action of a noun.

Description: "Αλλομενος" (allomenos) refers to something or someone that is undergoing a process of change or alteration. It can be used to describe a person who is changing their behavior, a situation that is evolving, or an object that is being modified.
