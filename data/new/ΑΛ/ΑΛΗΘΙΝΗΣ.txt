Grammar: "Αληθινής" is the genitive form of the feminine adjective "αληθινή" (alithiní), which means "true" or "genuine" in Greek. It agrees in gender, number, and case with the noun it modifies.

Description: "Αληθινής" is used to describe something that is true, authentic, or genuine. It can be used to refer to a person, an object, or an idea that is not false or deceptive.
