Etymology: The word "αληθουσαι" comes from the Greek verb "αληθεύω" (alithevo), which means "to speak the truth" or "to be true."

Grammar: "αληθουσαι" is the third person plural form of the verb "αληθεύω" in the present tense. It is used to describe actions or states of being that are true or truthful.

Description: "αληθουσαι" is a Greek word that can be translated as "they speak the truth" or "they are true." It is often used to express the idea of honesty, sincerity, or accuracy.
