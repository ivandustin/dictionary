Etymology: The word "αλλον" (allon) comes from the Greek word "ἄλλος" (allos), meaning "other" or "different".

Grammar: "Αλλον" is a masculine noun in the accusative case. It is the accusative form of the word "ἄλλος" (allos), which means "another" or "different".

Description: "Αλλον" is used to refer to something or someone that is different or distinct from what has been mentioned before. It can be translated as "another" or "a different one" in English.
