Etymology: The word "αλλην" comes from the Greek word "ἄλλος" (allos), meaning "other" or "different".

Grammar: "Αλλην" is a form of the Greek adjective "ἄλλος" (allos), which means "other" or "different". It is in the accusative case, singular form, feminine gender.

Description: "Αλλην" is used to describe a feminine noun in the accusative case when it refers to another or a different person or thing. It indicates that the noun is not the same as the one previously mentioned or known.
