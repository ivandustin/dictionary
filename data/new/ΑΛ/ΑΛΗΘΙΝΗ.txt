Etymology: The word "αληθινή" (alithiní) comes from the Greek adjective "αληθής" (alithís), which means "true" or "real". 

Grammar: "Αληθινή" is the feminine form of the adjective, used to describe a feminine noun. It agrees in gender, number, and case with the noun it modifies. 

Description: "Αληθινή" is used to describe something that is genuine, authentic, or true. It can be used to describe a person, an object, or a situation that is not fake or false.
