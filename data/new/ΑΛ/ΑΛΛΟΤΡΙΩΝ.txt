Etymology: The word "αλλοτριών" comes from the Greek word "άλλος" (allos), meaning "other" or "different", and the suffix "-τριών" (-trion), which denotes the plural form. 

Grammar: "Αλλοτριών" is a noun in the plural form. In Greek grammar, nouns have different forms depending on their gender, number, and case.
