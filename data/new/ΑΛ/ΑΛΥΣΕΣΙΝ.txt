Grammar: "αλυσεσιν" is a noun in the accusative case, plural form. In Greek grammar, nouns change their form depending on their role in the sentence (case) and the number of items they refer to (singular or plural).

Description: "αλυσεσιν" refers to the act of loosening or untying. It can be used to describe the process of untying knots or unfastening something that is tied or bound.
