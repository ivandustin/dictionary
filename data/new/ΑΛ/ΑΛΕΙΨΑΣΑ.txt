Etymology: The word "αλειψασα" comes from the Greek verb "αλείφω" (aleípho), which means "to anoint" or "to smear". 

Grammar: "αλειψασα" is the aorist participle form of the verb "αλείφω" (aleípho) in the feminine gender, singular number, and nominative case. 

Description: "αλειψασα" describes a female subject who has performed the action of anointing or smearing in the past.
