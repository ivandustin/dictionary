The word δεικνυσιν (deiknusin) is a Greek noun that means "a showing, a demonstration, a proof." It is derived from the verb δείκνυμι (deiknumi), which means "to show, to point out, to indicate." In terms of grammar, δεικνυσιν is a third-person singular present active participle, which means it describes an ongoing action in the present tense. Overall, δεικνυσιν refers to the act of demonstrating or proving something through evidence or argument.
