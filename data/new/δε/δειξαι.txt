Word: δειξαι

Etymology: δειξαι is a verb in the Greek language, which comes from the root word δείκνυμι (deiknymi), meaning "to show" or "to point out."

Grammar: δειξαι is the second person singular aorist active imperative form of the verb δείκνυμι (deiknymi). It is used to give a command or instruction to someone to "show" or "point out" something.

Description: δειξαι is a verb that is commonly used in everyday conversation in Greek. It is often used to ask someone to show or point out something specific, such as a location or an object.
