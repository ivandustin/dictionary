δειπνοις (deipnois) is a Greek word that is the dative plural form of the noun δεῖπνον (deipnon), which means "dinner" or "supper". The word is derived from the verb δειπνέω (deipneo), which means "to dine" or "to have supper". In Greek grammar, δειπνοις is used to indicate the indirect object of a verb, such as "to the dinners" or "for the suppers".
