Word: δεκαπεντε

Etymology:
The word δεκαπεντε comes from the Greek language. It is a combination of two words, δέκα (deka) meaning "ten" and πέντε (pente) meaning "five". Therefore, δεκαπεντε literally means "ten-five" or "fifteen".

Grammar:
In Greek, δεκαπεντε is a numeral adjective that functions as a cardinal number. It is in the nominative case and is used to indicate the quantity of fifteen.

Description:
δεκαπεντε is a number that falls between fourteen and sixteen. It is an odd number and is the sum of ten and five. It is used in various contexts such as counting, mathematics, and time. In Greek mythology, there were fifteen gods and goddesses who lived on Mount Olympus.
