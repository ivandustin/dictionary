In terms of grammar, δεσπότα is a masculine noun in the nominative case. It can also be declined in other cases, such as the genitive δεσπότα (despota) and the accusative δεσπότη (despoti).

In description, δεσπότα can be used to refer to a person who has complete control or authority over something or someone. It can also be used in a religious context to refer to God as the ultimate master or lord.
