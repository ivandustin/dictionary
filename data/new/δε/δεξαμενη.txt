Word: δεξαμενη

Etymology: The word δεξαμενη comes from the Greek word δέχομαι (déchomai) which means "to receive" or "to accept". It is derived from the verb δέχομαι and the suffix -μενη (-meni) which is used to form nouns.

Grammar: δεξαμενη is a feminine noun in Greek. It is in the singular form and its nominative case is δεξαμενη. In the plural form, it becomes δεξαμενες (dexamenes).

Description: δεξαμενη refers to a tank or reservoir used for storing liquids, such as water or oil. It can also refer to a container used for storing fuel or other substances. In modern Greek, it is commonly used to refer to a gas tank or fuel tank in a vehicle.
