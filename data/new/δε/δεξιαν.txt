Word: δεξιαν

Grammar: δεξιαν is an adverb in Greek, which means it modifies a verb, an adjective, or another adverb. It is used to indicate the location of something on the right side.

Description: δεξιαν is a word that is used to describe the location of something on the right side. It is commonly used in Greek to give directions or to describe the position of something in relation to the speaker or another object.
