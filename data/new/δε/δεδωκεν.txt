δεδωκεν is the third person singular form of the perfect tense in the indicative mood. In Greek, the perfect tense is used to describe an action that was completed in the past and has ongoing effects in the present. The indicative mood is used to make a statement or ask a question.

δεδωκεν means "he/she/it has given" in English. It is a verb that describes an action that has already been completed in the past. The word can be used in various contexts, such as to describe a gift that has been given, an instruction that has been provided, or a promise that has been fulfilled.
