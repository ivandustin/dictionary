The word "δεξαι" (dexai) is a verb in the Greek language. It is the aorist imperative form of the verb "δέχομαι" (dechomai), which means "to receive" or "to accept". The word comes from the ancient Greek root "δεχ-", which means "to take" or "to receive". In terms of grammar, "δεξαι" is a second person singular command, meaning it is used to give a direct order to someone.
