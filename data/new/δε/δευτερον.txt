Word: δευτερον

Etymology: δευτερον is a Greek word that comes from the root word δεύτερος (deuteros), which means "second" or "next in order". 

Grammar: δευτερον is a neuter singular form of the adjective δεύτερος (deuteros), which means "second" or "next in order". It is often used to describe the second item in a series or the second occurrence of something. 

Description: δευτερον is a word that is often used in Greek literature and language to describe the second item in a series or the second occurrence of something. It can also be used to describe something that is secondary or of lesser importance. In modern Greek, it is often used in the phrase "δεύτερο χέρι" (deutero heri), which means "second hand" or "used".
