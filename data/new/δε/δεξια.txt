Word: δεξιά

Etymology: δεξιά comes from the Greek word δεξιός (dexios), meaning "right" or "on the right side". 

Grammar: δεξιά is a feminine noun in the Greek language. It is in the nominative case, which is used for the subject of a sentence. 

Description: δεξιά refers to the right side or direction. It can also be used to describe something that is on the right side. In Greek politics, δεξιά is often used to refer to the right-wing political parties.
