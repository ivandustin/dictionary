μηδενι (mēdeni) is a Greek word that means "to no one" or "to nobody". It is derived from the negative pronoun μηδείς (mēdeis) which means "no one" or "nobody". In terms of grammar, μηδενι is a dative singular form of μηδείς. It is often used in Greek literature and philosophy to express the idea of non-existence or nothingness.
