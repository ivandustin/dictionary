Word: μηνα

Etymology: μηνα comes from the Greek word μήν (mēn), which means "month". 

Grammar: μηνα is a noun in the Greek language, and it is in the singular form. It is a masculine noun, and it belongs to the second declension. 

Description: μηνα refers to a month in the Greek calendar. In modern Greek, it is commonly used to refer to the menstrual cycle. The word is also used in various expressions and idioms, such as "μηνας του χειμώνα" (month of winter), which refers to a difficult or challenging period.
