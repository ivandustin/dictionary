μηποτε (mēpote) is a Greek adverb that means "lest, in case, perhaps, ever". 

Etymology: μηποτε is a compound word made up of μή (mē), which means "not", and ποτέ (poté), which means "ever". 

Grammar: μηποτε is an adverb and does not change form based on gender or number. 

Description: μηποτε is often used in conditional sentences to express a possibility or potential outcome that the speaker wants to avoid. It can also be used to express uncertainty or doubt.
