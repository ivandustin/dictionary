μηδεπω (mēdepō) is a Greek adverb that means "not yet" or "never yet". 

Etymology: The word is a combination of μηδε (mēde), meaning "not even" or "neither", and πω (pō), meaning "yet" or "still". 

Grammar: μηδεπω is an adverb and does not change form based on gender, number, or case. 

Description: The word is often used to indicate that something has not happened or been done up to a certain point in time. It can also be used to express anticipation or expectation for something to happen in the future.
