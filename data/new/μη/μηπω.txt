Word: μηπω

Etymology: μηπω is a Greek adverb that means "not yet" or "never yet". It is derived from the negative particle μή (mē) and the adverb πώ (pō), which means "yet" or "still".

Grammar: μηπω is an adverb in Greek and is used to modify verbs, adjectives, or other adverbs. It is often used in negative sentences to indicate that something has not yet happened.

Description: μηπω is a word that expresses the idea of something that has not yet happened or has never happened before. It is often used in Greek literature and poetry to create a sense of anticipation or suspense. In modern Greek, it is still used in everyday language to express the same idea.
