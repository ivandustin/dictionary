The word "μηδοι" (medoi) is a Greek adverb that means "not at all" or "by no means". It is derived from the negative particle "μη" (me) and the adverb "οἷος" (hoios), which means "such as" or "of what kind". In terms of grammar, "μηδοι" is a compound word made up of two separate words. It is used to negate a statement or to express a strong denial.
