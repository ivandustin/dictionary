Etymology: The word "τυφλοι" (typhloi) comes from the Greek word "τυφλός" (typhlós), meaning "blind". 

Grammar: "Τυφλοι" is the plural form of the noun "τυφλός" (typhlós), which means "blind person" in Greek. It belongs to the second declension of Greek nouns.

Description: "Τυφλοι" is used to refer to multiple blind individuals. It is a descriptive term that highlights the inability to see.
