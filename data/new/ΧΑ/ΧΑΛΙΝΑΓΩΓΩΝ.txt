Etymology: The word "χαλιναγωγών" (chalinagogon) comes from the Greek roots "χαλινός" (chalinós), meaning "bridle," and "ἀγωγός" (agogos), meaning "leader" or "guide." 

Grammar: "Χαλιναγωγών" is a noun in the masculine gender, singular number, and nominative case. 

Description: "Χαλιναγωγών" refers to a person who leads or guides others, often used metaphorically to describe someone who has a strong influence or control over others.
