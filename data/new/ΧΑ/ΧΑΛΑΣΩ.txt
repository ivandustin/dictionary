Grammar: "Χαλάσω" is the first person singular future tense of the verb "χαλάω" (chaláo), which means "I will break" or "I will ruin". It is formed by adding the future tense ending "-σω" (-so) to the verb stem.

Description: "Χαλάσω" is a verb in the Greek language that expresses the action of breaking or ruining something in the future. It can be used in various contexts to describe physical or metaphorical destruction or damage.
