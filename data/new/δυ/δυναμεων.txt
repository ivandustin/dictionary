Word: δυναμεων

Etymology: δυναμεων is a Greek word that comes from the root word δύναμις (dynamis), which means power or strength. The suffix -εων (-eon) is a genitive plural ending, indicating that the word refers to multiple powers or strengths.

Grammar: δυναμεων is a noun in the genitive plural form. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their grammatical function in a sentence. The genitive case is used to indicate possession or relationship, and the plural form indicates that there are multiple powers or strengths being referred to.

Description: δυναμεων can be translated to mean "powers" or "strengths." It is often used in a religious or spiritual context to refer to the powers or forces of the divine.
