Word: δυσκολον

Etymology: δυσ- (dys-) meaning "difficult" and κολος (kolos) meaning "limb" or "part of the body". Together, δυσκολον means "difficult to manage or handle".

Grammar: δυσκολον is a neuter noun in the singular form.

Description: δυσκολον is an adjective that describes something that is hard to do, manage, or deal with. It can also refer to a person who is difficult to deal with or understand. In modern Greek, it is often used to describe a difficult situation or task.
