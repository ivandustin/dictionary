Word: δυο

The word δυο (dyo) is a Greek word that means "two". It is derived from the Proto-Indo-European root *dwóh₁, which also gave rise to the English word "two".

Grammar:
In Greek, δυο is the cardinal number for "two". It is in the nominative case and is used to count objects or people. It can also be declined to match the gender and case of the noun it modifies.

Description:
δυο is a simple and common word in Greek that is used to express the number two. It is often used in everyday conversation and is an important word to know for basic communication in Greek.
