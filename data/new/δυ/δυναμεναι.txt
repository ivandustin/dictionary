Grammar: δυναμεναι is a feminine plural form of the present participle in Greek. It is used to describe a group of female subjects who are currently able to do something or have power.

Description: δυναμεναι can be translated to "able" or "capable" in English. It is often used in Greek literature and philosophy to describe the power or ability of individuals or groups. In the New Testament, it is used to describe the ability of women to prophesy and speak in tongues.
