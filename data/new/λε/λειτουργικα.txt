Word: λειτουργικά

Etymology: The word λειτουργικά comes from the Greek word λειτουργικός (leitourgikos), which means "pertaining to public service or duty." It is derived from the words λαός (laos), meaning "people," and έργον (ergon), meaning "work."

Grammar: λειτουργικά is a plural neuter adjective in Greek. It is used to describe things that are functional or useful in a practical sense.

Description: λειτουργικά is a versatile word that can be used to describe a wide range of things, from tools and equipment to systems and processes. It is often used in the context of technology and engineering, where it refers to the functional aspects of a product or system. In everyday language, it can also be used to describe things that are practical or useful in a more general sense.
