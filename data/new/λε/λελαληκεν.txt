Word: λελαληκεν

Grammar: λελαληκεν is a third person singular perfect indicative active form of the verb λαλέω. In Greek grammar, the perfect tense is used to describe an action that was completed in the past and has continuing relevance in the present. The indicative mood is used to make a statement or ask a question.

Description: λελαληκεν can be translated to English as "he/she/it has spoken" or "he/she/it spoke".
