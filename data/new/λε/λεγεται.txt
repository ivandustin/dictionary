The word "λεγεται" is a Greek verb that means "is called" or "is named". It is derived from the root word "λέγω" (lego), which means "to say" or "to speak". In terms of grammar, "λεγεται" is a third-person singular present indicative form of the verb "λέγω". It is often used in Greek to introduce a name or title for someone or something.
