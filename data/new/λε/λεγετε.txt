Word: λεγετε

Etymology: λεγετε is a present tense verb in Greek, derived from the root word λεγω (lego) which means "to say" or "to speak".

Grammar: λεγετε is the second person plural form of the present tense in Greek. It is used to address a group of people and means "you all say" or "you all speak".

Description: λεγετε is a common verb in Greek and is used in various contexts. It can be used to ask a group of people to speak or say something, or to describe an action that a group of people are currently doing. It is also used in storytelling and narration to indicate dialogue or speech.
