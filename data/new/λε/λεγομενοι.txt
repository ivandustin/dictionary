Word: λεγομενοι

Grammar: λεγομενοι is a masculine plural nominative form of the present participle of the verb λέγω (lego). In Greek, the present participle is used to indicate ongoing or continuous action, and it can function as an adjective or a noun.

Description: λεγομενοι can be translated as "those who are being called" or "those who are being said". It is often used in Greek literature and philosophy to refer to people who are mentioned or referred to in a particular context.
