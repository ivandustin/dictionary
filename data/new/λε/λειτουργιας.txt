The word λειτουργίας (leitourgias) is a Greek noun that can be translated to English as "function" or "service". 

Etymology: The word is derived from the Greek words λαός (laos) meaning "people" and ἔργον (ergon) meaning "work". 

Grammar: λειτουργίας is in the genitive case, which is used to show possession or relationship. 

Description: The word is often used in the context of religious or public service, referring to a duty or responsibility that one has towards their community or society. It can also refer to the performance of a specific task or function, such as the function of a machine or the role of a particular organ in the body.
