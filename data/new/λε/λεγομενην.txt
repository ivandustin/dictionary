The word λεγομενην (legomenen) is a Greek verb in the present participle form, which means "being said" or "being called". It is derived from the verb λέγω (lego), which means "to say" or "to speak". In terms of grammar, it is in the feminine gender, singular number, and accusative case. It is often used in Greek literature and philosophy to introduce a concept or idea that is being discussed or referred to.
