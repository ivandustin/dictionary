Word: λελουμένος

Etymology: The word λελουμένος comes from the Greek verb λούω (louo), which means "to wash". The prefix λε- (le-) indicates a completed action, so λελουμένος means "having been washed".

Grammar: λελουμένος is a participle, which is a type of verb form that can function as an adjective. It is in the masculine gender, singular number, and nominative case. 

Description: λελουμένος describes someone or something that has been washed or cleansed. It can be used in a literal sense, such as to describe a person who has just taken a bath, or in a figurative sense, such as to describe a person who has been purified or cleansed of sin.
