The word "λευκαις" (leukais) is a Greek adjective that means "white" or "bright". It is derived from the Greek word "λευκός" (leukos), which also means "white". In terms of grammar, "λευκαις" is in the feminine nominative singular form. It can be used to describe objects, animals, or people that are white or bright in color. For example, "τα λευκά ρούχα" (ta leuká rúcha) means "the white clothes".
