The word "ισχυν" (ischyn) is a Greek noun that means "strength" or "power". It is derived from the Greek verb "ισχύω" (ischýo), which means "to be strong" or "to have power". In terms of grammar, "ισχυν" is a third-person singular noun in the nominative case. It can be used to describe physical strength, as well as mental or emotional strength.
