Word: ισως

Etymology: ισως comes from the Greek word "ἴσως" (ísōs), which means "perhaps" or "maybe".

Grammar: ισως is an adverb in Greek, which means that it modifies the verb in a sentence. It is often used to express uncertainty or possibility.

Description: ισως is a common word in Greek and is used in everyday conversation. It is often used to express doubt or uncertainty about something. For example, "Ισως θα πάω στο καφέ σήμερα" (Maybe I will go to the café today). It can also be used to express a possibility, as in "Ισως να έχει βροχή αύριο" (There may be rain tomorrow).
