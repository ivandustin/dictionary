Word: ισχυσατε

Grammar: ισχυσατε is a second person plural aorist active indicative verb in Ancient Greek. The aorist tense indicates a completed action in the past, while the active voice indicates that the subject of the verb is performing the action. The indicative mood indicates that the verb is a statement of fact.

Description: ισχυσατε is a verb that describes a past action of a group of people having strength or power. It can be translated as "you all were strong" or "you all had power". The context in which it is used would determine the exact meaning and translation.
