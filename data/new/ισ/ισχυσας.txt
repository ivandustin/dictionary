Word: ισχυσας

Grammar: ισχυσας is the second person singular aorist active indicative form of the verb ισχύω. In this form, it means "you were strong" or "you had power."

Description: ισχυσας is a word that describes a past action of being strong or having power. It can be used in various contexts, such as in sports, politics, or personal relationships.
