Word: πνευματικών

Etymology: The word πνευματικών comes from the Greek word πνευματικός (pneumatikos), which means "spiritual" or "of the spirit". The suffix -ων (-on) is added to make the word plural.

Grammar: πνευματικών is the plural form of the adjective πνευματικός (pneumatikos). It is used to describe things that are related to the spirit or the soul, such as spiritual matters, spiritual beliefs, or spiritual practices.

It can refer to a wide range of topics, including religious beliefs, practices, and experiences, as well as philosophical and metaphysical concepts related to the nature of the soul and the spirit.
