Grammar: In Greek, "βλασταί" is a noun in the plural form. It belongs to the third declension and is classified as a masculine noun. The singular form of the noun is "βλαστής" (vlastís).

Description: "Βλασταί" refers to sprouts or shoots, particularly in the context of plants. It can also be used metaphorically to describe the growth or development of something.
