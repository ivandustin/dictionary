Etymology: The word "βληθησεται" comes from the Greek verb "βληθήσεται" (blithisetai), which is the future tense of the verb "βάλλω" (vallo), meaning "to throw" or "to cast".

Grammar: "βληθησεται" is a verb in the future tense, third person singular form. It belongs to the passive voice and indicates that the subject will be thrown or cast.

Description: "βληθησεται" is a Greek word that describes an action in the future where something or someone will be thrown or cast. It is commonly used in various contexts, such as sports, games, or any situation where an object or a person is projected or propelled.
