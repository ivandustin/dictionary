Etymology: The word "θηλεία" comes from the Greek word "θῆλυς" (thēlys), which means "female" or "woman."

Grammar: "Θηλεία" is a feminine noun in the Greek language. It belongs to the first declension and has a singular form.

Description: "Θηλεία" refers to femininity or the state of being female. It can also be used to describe female characteristics or qualities.
