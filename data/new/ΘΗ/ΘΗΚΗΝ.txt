Etymology: The word "θηκην" (thēkēn) comes from the Greek word "θήκη" (thēkē), which means "case" or "container."

Grammar: "Θηκην" is a noun in the accusative case, singular form.

Description: "Θηκην" refers to a case or container used to hold or store something. It can be used to describe various types of cases, such as a pencil case, a phone case, or a storage case.
