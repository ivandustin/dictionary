Etymology: The word "λυχνία" (lychnía) comes from the Greek word "λύχνος" (lychnos), meaning "lamp" or "lantern". 

Grammar: "Λυχνία" is a feminine noun in Greek. It belongs to the second declension and its genitive form is "λυχνίας" (lychnías). 

Description: "Λυχνία" refers to a lamp or lantern, typically used for providing light. It can be a traditional oil lamp or a modern electric lamp.
