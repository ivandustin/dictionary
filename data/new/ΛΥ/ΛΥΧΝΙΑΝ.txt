Etymology: The word "λυχνιαν" (lychnian) comes from the Greek word "λύχνος" (lychnos), meaning "lamp" or "lantern". 

Grammar: "Λυχνιαν" is a noun in the singular form. It belongs to the third declension in Greek grammar. 

Description: "Λυχνιαν" refers to a lamp or lantern, which is a portable light source typically fueled by oil or gas. It is used to provide illumination in various settings, such as homes, streets, or outdoor areas.
