Etymology: The word "λυπη" (lypē) comes from the Ancient Greek word "λυπῆσαι" (lypēsai), which means "to cause pain or sorrow."

Grammar: In Greek, "λυπη" (lypē) is a feminine noun. It belongs to the first declension and has the genitive form "λυπης" (lypēs).

Description: "Λυπη" (lypē) refers to the feeling of sadness, grief, or sorrow. It is an emotional state characterized by distress, unhappiness, or a sense of loss.
