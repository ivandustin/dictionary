Etymology: The word "λυετε" (lyete) comes from the Greek verb "λύω" (lyo), which means "to loosen" or "to untie".

Grammar: "λυετε" is the present tense, second person plural form of the verb "λύω" (lyo). It is used to address a group of people and indicates that they are currently loosening or untying something.
