Grammar: "ηλαυνετο" is the third person singular, imperfect tense, middle voice form of the verb "ἡλαύνω." The middle voice indicates that the subject is both the doer and the receiver of the action.

Description: The verb "ηλαυνετο" describes the action of driving or propelling something. It can refer to driving a vehicle, moving something forward, or causing something to move or progress.
