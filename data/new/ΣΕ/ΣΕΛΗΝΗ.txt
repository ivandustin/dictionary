Etymology: The word "σεληνη" (selene) comes from the Greek goddess of the moon, Selene. 

Grammar: In Greek, "σεληνη" is a feminine noun. 

Description: "Σεληνη" refers to the moon in Greek. It is often used to describe the celestial body that orbits around the Earth and reflects sunlight to create the appearance of a glowing object in the night sky. The moon has been a subject of fascination and inspiration for humans throughout history.
