Etymology: The word "σεβομένων" comes from the Greek verb "σέβομαι" (sevomai), which means "to respect" or "to honor."

Grammar: "Σεβομένων" is the present participle form of the verb "σέβομαι" (sevomai). It is in the masculine plural genitive case.

Description: "Σεβομένων" is used to describe a group of people who are currently respecting or honoring someone or something. It indicates an ongoing action of respect or honor.
