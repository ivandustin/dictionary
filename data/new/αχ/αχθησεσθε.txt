The word "αχθησεσθε" is a verb in the Greek language. It is the second person plural form of the aorist passive tense of the verb "αχθάνω" (achthano), which means "to bear" or "to carry". The etymology of the word comes from the Greek root "άχθος" (achthos), which means "burden" or "load". In terms of grammar, "αχθησεσθε" is a passive verb form, indicating that the subject is receiving the action rather than performing it. The word can be translated to English as "you were carried" or "you were burdened".
