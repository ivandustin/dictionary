Etymology: The word "χωρεί" (chorei) comes from the Greek verb "χωρώ" (choro), which means "I move, I go, I make room." It is derived from the noun "χώρα" (chora), meaning "space, place, country."

Grammar: "Χωρεί" is the third person singular form of the verb "χωρώ" in the present tense. It is used to express the action of "making room" or "fitting in" a certain space or situation.

Description: "Χωρεί" is a versatile verb that can be used in various contexts. It can refer to physical movement or displacement, as well as metaphorical concepts such as accommodating or accepting something or someone. It is commonly used in everyday speech and writing in Greek.
