Etymology: The word "χωραί" (chōraí) comes from the Greek word "χώρα" (chōra), which means "place" or "space".

Grammar: "Χωραί" is a noun in the plural form. It is a feminine noun and belongs to the first declension in Greek grammar.

Description: "Χωραί" refers to "places" or "spaces" in Greek. It can be used to describe physical locations, regions, or territories. It can also be used metaphorically to refer to abstract or conceptual spaces.
