Etymology: The word "χωριζεσθαι" comes from the Greek verb "χωρίζω" (chorizo), which means "to separate" or "to divide".

Grammar: "Χωριζεσθαι" is the present middle/passive infinitive form of the verb "χωρίζω". In Greek grammar, the middle/passive voice indicates that the subject is both the doer and the receiver of the action.

Description: "Χωριζεσθαι" is a Greek verb that means "to separate" or "to divide". It is used to describe the action of splitting or dividing something into parts or sections.
