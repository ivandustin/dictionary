Etymology: The word "χωρησαί" (chōrēsai) comes from the Greek verb "χωρέω" (chōreō), which means "to make room" or "to contain." It is derived from the noun "χώρα" (chōra), meaning "space" or "place."

Grammar: "Χωρησαί" is the third person singular aorist active subjunctive form of the verb "χωρέω" (chōreō). The aorist tense indicates a completed action in the past, and the subjunctive mood expresses possibility or potentiality.

Description: "Χωρησαί" is used to express the idea of "to make room" or "to contain" in the third person singular form. It can be used in various contexts, such as describing the capacity of a container or the ability to accommodate something.
