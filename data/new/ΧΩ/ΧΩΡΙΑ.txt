Etymology: The word "χωρια" (chōria) comes from the Greek word "χώρα" (chōra), which means "country" or "land."

Grammar: "Χωρια" is a noun in the plural form. In Greek grammar, nouns have different forms depending on their gender, number, and case. The plural form indicates that there are multiple "χώρες" (chōres) or countries.

Description: "Χωρια" refers to multiple countries or lands. It can be used to describe different nations or territories.
