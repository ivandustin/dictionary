χαιρε (cháire) is a Greek word that means "hello" or "greetings". It comes from the Greek verb χαίρω (chaíro), which means "to rejoice" or "to be glad".

χαιρε is an interjection, which means it is a word or phrase used to express strong emotion or feeling. It is often used as a greeting or farewell in Greek.
