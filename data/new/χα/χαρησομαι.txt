Word: χαρησομαι

Etymology: χαρησομαι is a Greek verb that comes from the root word χαρά (chara), which means "joy" or "delight". The verb form χαρησομαι means "to rejoice" or "to be glad".

Grammar: χαρησομαι is a first-person singular future middle deponent verb in Greek. This means that it is a verb that is reflexive in nature and has a passive voice. The future tense indicates that the action of rejoicing will take place in the future.

Description: χαρησομαι is a verb that expresses the feeling of joy or delight. It is often used in a religious or spiritual context to express gratitude or happiness towards a higher power. It can also be used in a more general sense to express happiness or satisfaction with a situation or event.
