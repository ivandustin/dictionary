Word: χαρα

The word χαρα (khara) is a Greek word that means "joy" or "happiness". It is derived from the ancient Greek word χαίρω (khairō), which means "to rejoice" or "to be glad".

Grammar:
In Greek, χαρα is a feminine noun. It is in the singular form and can be used as both a subject and an object in a sentence. The plural form of χαρα is χαρες (khares).

Description:
χαρα is a word that is often used to express happiness, joy, and delight. It can be used in a variety of contexts, such as to describe a person's emotional state, to express gratitude or appreciation, or to describe a pleasant experience.
