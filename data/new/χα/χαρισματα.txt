χαρισματα is a plural noun in Greek. It is the plural form of χαρισμα (charisma), which is a neuter noun. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their gender, number, and case.

χαρισματα refers to spiritual gifts or abilities that are given to individuals by God.
