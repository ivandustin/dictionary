The word "χαρισματι" (charismati) is a Greek noun that means "gift" or "grace." It is derived from the Greek word "χάρις" (charis), which means "grace" or "favor." In terms of grammar, "χαρισματι" is a neuter noun in the singular form. It is often used in the context of spiritual gifts or talents that are believed to be given by God. Overall, "χαρισματι" is a word that conveys the idea of something that is freely given and bestowed upon someone.
