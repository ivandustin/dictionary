The word "χαρας" (charas) is a Greek noun that means "joy" or "happiness". It is derived from the Greek word "χαρά" (chara), which also means "joy". In terms of grammar, "χαρας" is a masculine noun in the genitive case. It can be used to describe a feeling of happiness or pleasure, or to refer to something that brings joy or happiness. For example, one might say "Η χαράς μου είναι μεγάλη" (I am very happy) or "Το δώρο μου ήταν μια πηγή χαράς" (My gift was a source of joy).
