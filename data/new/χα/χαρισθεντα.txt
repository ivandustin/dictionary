The word "χαρισθεντα" is a Greek verb in the passive voice, which means "to be forgiven" or "to be pardoned". It is derived from the Greek word "χαρίζομαι" (charizomai), which means "to show favor" or "to grant forgiveness". In terms of grammar, "χαρισθεντα" is the aorist passive participle of the verb "χαρίζομαι". The aorist tense indicates a completed action in the past, while the passive voice indicates that the subject of the verb is receiving the action. Therefore, "χαρισθεντα" can be translated as "having been forgiven" or "having received pardon".
