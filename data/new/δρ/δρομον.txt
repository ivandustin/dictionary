The word δρομον (dromon) is a Greek noun that means "race" or "running". It is derived from the Greek verb δρόμος (dromos), which means "to run". In terms of grammar, δρομον is a masculine noun in the accusative case. It can be used to describe a physical race or competition, as well as a metaphorical race or pursuit.
