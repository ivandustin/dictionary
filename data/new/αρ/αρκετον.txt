Word: αρκετόν

Etymology: αρκετόν comes from the Greek word αρκετός (arketos), meaning "sufficient" or "enough."

Grammar: αρκετόν is an adjective in the neuter gender, singular form. It is used to describe a noun as "sufficient" or "enough."

Description: αρκετόν is a useful word in Greek, as it allows speakers to express that something is sufficient or enough. It can be used in a variety of contexts, such as describing the amount of food or drink that is enough, or the amount of time that is sufficient for a task.
