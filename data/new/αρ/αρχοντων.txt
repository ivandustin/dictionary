Word: αρχοντων

Etymology: αρχοντων is the genitive plural form of the Greek noun αρχων (archon), which means "ruler" or "leader". The word comes from the verb αρχω (archo), which means "to rule" or "to lead".

Grammar: αρχοντων is a noun in the genitive plural form. In Greek, the genitive case is used to indicate possession or relationship between nouns.

Description: αρχοντων is a word that refers to a group of rulers or leaders. It can be used to describe a group of people who hold positions of power or authority, such as government officials or executives in a company. The word has a strong connotation of leadership and control.
