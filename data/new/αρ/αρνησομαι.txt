Word: αρνησομαι

Etymology:
The word αρνησομαι (arnesomai) is a Greek verb that comes from the root word αρνεομαι (arneomai), which means "to deny" or "to refuse." The prefix αρνη- (arne-) adds emphasis to the denial or refusal.

Grammar:
αρνησομαι is a first-person singular future middle deponent verb in Greek. It is conjugated as follows:

Present: αρνομαι (arnomai)
Future: αρνησομαι (arnesomai)
Aorist: ηρνησαμην (ernesamen)
Perfect: ηρνημαι (ernemai)

Description:
αρνησομαι means "to deny" or "to refuse" in Greek. It is often used in a legal or formal context, such as denying an accusation or refusing a request. The word can also be used in a more general sense, such as denying oneself something or refusing to believe something.
