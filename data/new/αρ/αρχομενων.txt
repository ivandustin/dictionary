Word: αρχομενων

Etymology: αρχομενων is the present participle of the Greek verb αρχομαι (archomai), which means "to begin" or "to start". 

Grammar: αρχομενων is a masculine, plural, genitive form of the present participle. It is often used as an adjective to describe a noun in the genitive case. 

Description: αρχομενων can be translated as "beginning" or "starting". It is often used in the context of the beginning of a process or event. For example, in the New Testament, it is used to describe the beginning of the Gospel of Jesus Christ.
