The word "αρω" (aro) is a Greek verb that means "to lift up" or "to raise." It is derived from the ancient Greek word "αίρω" (airo), which has the same meaning. In terms of grammar, "αρω" is a first-conjugation verb in the present tense, and it can be used in various tenses and moods to express different meanings.
