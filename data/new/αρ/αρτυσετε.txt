Word: αρτυσετε

Etymology: The word αρτυσετε is a conjugated form of the Greek verb αρτύω (artyo), which means "to prepare" or "to make ready". The verb comes from the noun αρτος (artos), which means "bread".

Grammar: αρτυσετε is the second person plural form of the aorist tense in the active voice of the Greek verb αρτύω (artyo). It is used to address a group of people and means "you prepared" or "you made ready".

Description: αρτυσετε is a verb that indicates an action of preparation or readiness. It is often used in the context of preparing food or getting ready for an event. The word has a sense of urgency and implies that the action needs to be done quickly and efficiently.
