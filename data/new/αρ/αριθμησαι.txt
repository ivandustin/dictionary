Word: αριθμησαι

Etymology:
The word αριθμησαι is derived from the Greek verb αριθμέω (arithmeo), which means "to count" or "to number". It is formed by adding the suffix -σαι (-sai) to the stem αριθμε- (arithme-).

Grammar:
αριθμησαι is a verb in the aorist tense, active voice, and infinitive mood. In Greek, the aorist tense is used to describe a completed action in the past, while the infinitive mood is used to express the idea of the verb without specifying a subject or tense.

Description:
The word αριθμησαι means "to count" or "to number" in Greek. It is often used in mathematical or numerical contexts, such as counting objects or calculating quantities. The verb can also be used metaphorically to describe the process of organizing or categorizing information.
