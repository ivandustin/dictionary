Word: αριστερών

Etymology: The word αριστερών (aristerón) is derived from the Greek word αριστερός (aristerós), which means "left". The suffix -ών (-ón) is added to indicate plural form.

Grammar: αριστερών is a plural noun in the genitive case. It is used to indicate possession or relationship to something else. For example, "τα βιβλία των αριστερών" (ta vivlia ton aristerón) means "the books of the leftists".

Description: αριστερών is commonly used in Greek to refer to people or groups with left-wing political views. It can also be used to describe the left side of the body or an object.
