The word "αρξηται" (arxetai) is a verb in the Greek language. 

Etymology: The word comes from the ancient Greek verb "αρχω" (archō), which means "to begin" or "to rule". 

Grammar: "αρξηται" is in the third person singular, aorist tense, middle voice, indicative mood. 

Description: The word "αρξηται" means "he/she/it began" or "he/she/it started" in English. It is commonly used in Greek literature and everyday speech to describe the beginning of an action or event.
