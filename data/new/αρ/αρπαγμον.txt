The word "αρπαγμον" (arpagmon) is a Greek noun that means "plunder" or "robbery". It is derived from the verb "αρπάζω" (arpazo), which means "to snatch" or "to seize". In terms of grammar, it is a masculine noun in the accusative singular form. It can be used to describe an act of theft or looting, or to refer to the stolen goods themselves.
