Word: αρεστον

Etymology:
The word αρεστον (areston) is derived from the Greek word αρεστος (arestos), which means "pleasing" or "acceptable". It is a combination of the prefix α- (a-) meaning "not" and the word ερεσσω (eresso) meaning "to please".

Grammar:
αρεστον (areston) is a neuter noun in the singular form. It is used to describe something that is pleasing or acceptable.
