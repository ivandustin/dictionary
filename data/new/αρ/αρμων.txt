The word "αρμων" (harmon) is a Greek noun that means "joint" or "articulation". It is derived from the verb "αρμόζω" (harmozo), which means "to fit together" or "to join". In terms of grammar, it is a masculine noun in the nominative singular form. It can also be declined in other cases and numbers, such as the genitive singular "αρμονός" (harmonos) or the accusative plural "αρμούς" (harmons).
