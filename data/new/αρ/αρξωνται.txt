Word: αρξωνται

Etymology: αρξωνται is a conjugated form of the Greek verb αρχίζω (archizo), which comes from the noun αρχή (archi) meaning "beginning" or "origin". 

Grammar: αρξωνται is the third person plural present indicative active form of αρχίζω. It is used to indicate that a group of people or things are beginning to do something. 

Description: αρξωνται can be translated to "they begin" or "they are beginning". It is a common verb in Greek and can be used in various contexts, such as in a sentence like "Αρχωνται οι εργασίες" (Archontai oi ergasies), which means "The work is beginning".
