Word: αρπασει

Etymology: αρπασει is a verb in the Greek language, which comes from the root word αρπαγή (arpagi), meaning "a snatch" or "a robbery."

Grammar: αρπασει is a present tense verb in the third person singular form. It is conjugated from the root verb αρπάζω (arpazo), which means "to snatch" or "to seize."

Description: αρπασει is an action verb that describes the act of snatching or seizing something quickly and forcefully. It can be used in a literal sense, such as when someone snatches an object from someone else's hand, or in a figurative sense, such as when someone seizes an opportunity or takes advantage of a situation.
