Word: αργοί

Etymology: The word αργοί (argoi) is derived from the Greek adjective αργός (argos), which means "slow" or "lazy".

Description: αργοί is an adjective that describes something or someone as slow or lazy. It can be used to describe physical movement or mental processes.
