Word: αραι

Etymology:
The word αραι (arai) is a Greek word that comes from the verb αραω (arao), which means "to pluck" or "to pick." It is related to the English word "harvest."

Grammar:
αραι is a verb in the present tense, third person plural form. It is often translated as "they pluck" or "they pick."

Description:
αραι is a verb that describes the action of plucking or picking something. It can be used in a literal sense, such as picking fruit from a tree, or in a figurative sense, such as picking out the best option from a group of choices.
