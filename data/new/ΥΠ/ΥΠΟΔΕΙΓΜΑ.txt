Grammar: "Υποδείγμα" is a noun in the Greek language. It is a neuter noun, and its plural form is "υποδείγματα" (ypodeigmata).

Description: "Υποδείγμα" refers to a model, example, or pattern that serves as a guide or reference for something. It can be used in various contexts, such as in science, mathematics, or any field where a standard or prototype is needed.
