Etymology: The word "υποδραμοντες" (ypodramontes) comes from the Greek roots "υπο" (ypo), meaning "under" or "below," and "δραμω" (dramo), meaning "to run." 

Grammar: "Υποδραμοντες" is a noun in the plural form. It belongs to the third declension in Greek grammar. 

Description: "Υποδραμοντες" refers to individuals who run or move quickly under or below something. It can be used metaphorically to describe people who work diligently or efficiently behind the scenes.
