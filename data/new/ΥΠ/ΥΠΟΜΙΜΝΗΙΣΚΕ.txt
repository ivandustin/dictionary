It is derived from the combination of the prefix "υπο-" (ypo-), meaning "under" or "below," and the verb "μιμνήσκω" (mimnísko), meaning "to remember" or "to recall."

Grammar: "Υπομιμνήσκε" is the second person singular imperative form of the verb "υπομιμνήσκω" (ypomimnísko). In Greek grammar, the imperative mood is used to give commands or make requests. The second person singular form is used when addressing one person directly.
