Etymology: The word "υποδεδεμένους" comes from the Greek roots "υπο-" meaning "under" and "δεδεμένος" meaning "bound" or "tied". 

Grammar: "Υποδεδεμένους" is the plural form of the adjective "υποδεδεμένος" in Greek. It is used to describe multiple things or people that are bound or tied under something.

Description: "Υποδεδεμένους" is an adjective in Greek that describes something or someone that is bound or tied under something else. It can be used metaphorically to describe a situation where someone is under the control or influence of someone or something else.
