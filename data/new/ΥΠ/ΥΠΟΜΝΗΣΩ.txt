Grammar: "Υπομνησω" is a verb in the first person singular form of the future tense in Greek. It is derived from the verb "υπομνησκω" (ypomnisko), which means "to remind" or "to bring to mind."

Description: "Υπομνησω" is used to express the action of reminding oneself or someone else about something. It can also imply the act of recalling or bringing something to mind.
