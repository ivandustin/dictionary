Grammar: "Υπηνεγκα" is the first person singular form of the aorist tense of the verb "υποφέρω" (ypoféro). The aorist tense is used to express a completed action in the past. In this form, "υπηνεγκα" indicates that the speaker has endured or carried something in the past.
