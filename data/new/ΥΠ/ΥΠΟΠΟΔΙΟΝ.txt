Etymology: The word "υποποδιον" (ypopodion) comes from the Greek roots "υπο" (ypo), meaning "under" or "below," and "ποδιον" (podion), meaning "foot." It literally translates to "underfoot" or "footstool."

Grammar: "Υποποδιον" is a neuter noun in the singular form. In Greek grammar, it belongs to the second declension.

Description: "Υποποδιον" refers to a footstool or a small piece of furniture used to support the feet while sitting. It is typically low in height and placed under a chair or in front of a couch for added comfort.
