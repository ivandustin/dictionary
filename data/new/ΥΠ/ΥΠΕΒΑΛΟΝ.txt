Grammar: "Υπεβαλον" is the aorist tense of the verb "υποβαλλω" in the first person singular. In Greek grammar, the aorist tense is used to express a completed action in the past.

Description: "Υπεβαλον" is a Greek word that means "I submitted" or "I presented." It is commonly used to indicate the action of submitting or presenting something, such as a document, a proposal, or an application.
