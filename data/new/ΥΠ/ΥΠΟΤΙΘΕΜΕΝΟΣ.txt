Grammar: "Υποτιθέμενος" is the masculine singular form of the participle, used to describe a noun that is assumed or supposed. In Greek grammar, participles are verb forms that can function as adjectives.

Description: The word "υποτιθέμενος" is used to describe something that is assumed or supposed to be true, without concrete evidence or proof. It implies a hypothetical or speculative nature.
