Etymology: The word "υπογραμμον" (ypogrammon) comes from the Greek words "υπο" (ypo), meaning "under" or "below," and "γραμμη" (grammi), meaning "line." It refers to a line or text that is written below the main text.

Grammar: "Υπογραμμον" is a noun in the singular form.

Description: "Υπογραμμον" is used to describe a line or text that is written below the main text. It is often used to provide additional information, explanations, or references. In typography, it is commonly used for footnotes, citations, or translations.
