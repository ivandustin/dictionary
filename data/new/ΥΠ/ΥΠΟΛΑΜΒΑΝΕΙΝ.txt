Grammar: "Υπολαμβάνειν" is a verb in the Greek language. It belongs to the first conjugation class and is in the present tense. It is commonly used in the active voice.

Description: "Υπολαμβάνειν" means "to assume" or "to suppose" in English. It refers to the act of taking something as true or valid without concrete evidence or proof. It is often used when making an educated guess or forming a hypothesis.
