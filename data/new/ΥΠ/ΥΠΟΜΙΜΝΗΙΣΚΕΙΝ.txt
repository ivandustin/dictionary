Etymology: The word "υπομιμνήσκειν" comes from the Greek roots "υπό" (hypo), meaning "under" or "below," and "μιμνήσκειν" (mimnēskein), meaning "to remember." 

Grammar: "Υπομιμνήσκειν" is a verb in the Greek language. It is in the present tense, active voice, and infinitive mood. 

Description: "Υπομιμνήσκειν" means "to remind" or "to bring to mind" in Greek. It is used to indicate the action of recalling or evoking a memory or thought.
