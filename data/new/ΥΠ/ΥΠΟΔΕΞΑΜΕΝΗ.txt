Etymology: The word "υποδεξιαμενη" comes from the Greek words "υπο" (ypo), meaning "under" or "below," and "δεξιαμενη" (dexiameni), meaning "right-handed." 

Grammar: "Υποδεξιαμενη" is a feminine noun in the Greek language. It is declined in the singular form as "η υποδεξιαμενη" and in the plural form as "οι υποδεξιαμενες."

Description: "Υποδεξιαμενη" refers to a person who is left-handed. It is used to describe someone who primarily uses their left hand for tasks such as writing, eating, or throwing.
