Grammar: "Υπεραυξάνει" is the third person singular form of the verb "υπεραυξάνω" in the present tense. It is used to describe an action that is currently happening and is performed by a third person.

Description: The word "υπεραυξάνει" means "he/she/it increases greatly" or "he/she/it grows excessively" in English. It implies a significant or excessive increase or growth in something.
