The word "υπερβαλλουσης" (yperballousis) is a Greek noun that comes from the verb "υπερβαλλω" (yperballo), which means "to exceed" or "to surpass." The noun "υπερβαλλουσης" refers to the act of exceeding or surpassing. 

In terms of grammar, "υπερβαλλουσης" is in the genitive case, singular number, and masculine gender. It is used to indicate possession or relationship to another noun or pronoun.

Overall, "υπερβαλλουσης" describes the action of going beyond a certain limit or surpassing expectations.
