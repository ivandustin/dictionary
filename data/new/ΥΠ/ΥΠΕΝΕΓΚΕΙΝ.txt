Etymology: The word "υπενεγκειν" (υπενεγκειν) comes from the Greek roots "υπο" (upo), meaning "under" or "below," and "ενεγκειν" (enekhein), meaning "to carry" or "to bring." 

Grammar: "Υπενεγκειν" is an infinitive verb form in the Greek language. It belongs to the aorist tense and active voice. 

Description: The word "υπενεγκειν" is used to express the action of carrying or bringing something under or below a certain place or object. It can also be used metaphorically to describe the act of enduring or tolerating something difficult or burdensome.
