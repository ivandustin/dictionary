Etymology: The word "υποδειγματα" (ypodeigmata) comes from the Greek roots "υπο" (ypo), meaning "under" or "below," and "δειγμα" (deigma), meaning "example" or "model." 

Grammar: "Υποδείγματα" is a noun in the plural form. In Greek grammar, nouns have different forms depending on their gender, number, and case. 

Description: "Υποδείγματα" refers to "examples" or "models" in Greek. It is used to describe instances or cases that serve as a representation or illustration of a concept or idea.
