Etymology: The word "υποδημα" (ypodima) comes from the Greek roots "υπο" (ypo), meaning "under" or "below," and "δημα" (dima), meaning "foot." Therefore, "υποδημα" literally means "under the foot" or "footwear."

Grammar: "Υποδημα" is a noun in the Greek language. It is a neuter noun, and its plural form is "υποδηματα" (ypodimata).

Description: "Υποδημα" refers to any type of footwear or shoes that are worn on the feet to protect them or for fashion purposes. It can include various types of shoes such as sandals, sneakers, boots, and more.
