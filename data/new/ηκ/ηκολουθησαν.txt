Word: ηκολουθησαν

Etymology: ηκολουθησαν is a Greek word that comes from the verb "ακολουθώ" (akoloutho), which means "to follow". The prefix "η" (e) is added to the beginning of the verb to indicate that the action is in the past tense.

Grammar: ηκολουθησαν is the third person plural past tense form of the verb "ακολουθώ" (akoloutho). It is used to describe an action that was completed in the past by a group of people. 

Description: ηκολουθησαν is a word that describes an action of following that has already taken place. It can be used in various contexts, such as describing a group of people who followed a leader, or a group of students who followed a teacher. The word implies that the action was completed and is no longer ongoing.
