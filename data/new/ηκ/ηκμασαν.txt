Word: ηκμασαν

Etymology: The word ηκμασαν is derived from the Greek verb ηκμάζω (ikmazo), which means "to reach the peak" or "to be at the height of one's powers."

Grammar: ηκμασαν is the third person plural aorist indicative active form of the verb ηκμάζω (ikmazo). In this form, it indicates that the action of reaching the peak or being at the height of one's powers was completed in the past.

Description: ηκμασαν can be translated to English as "they reached the peak" or "they were at the height of their powers." It implies that the subject had achieved a high level of success or excellence in a particular area.
