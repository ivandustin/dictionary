Word: ηκουσαμεν

Etymology: ηκουσαμεν is a Greek verb that comes from the root word ακούω (akouo), which means "to hear" or "to listen". The verb is in the aorist tense, which indicates a completed action in the past.

Grammar: ηκουσαμεν is the first person plural form of the aorist tense in Greek. It is translated to "we heard" in English. The verb is composed of the prefix η- (e-) and the root word ακούω (akouo).

Description: ηκουσαμεν is a verb that describes an action that was completed in the past. It indicates that "we heard" something at a specific point in time. The verb is commonly used in storytelling or recounting events that have already happened.
