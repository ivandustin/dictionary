Word: ηκολουθησεν

Grammar: ηκολουθησεν is a third person singular past tense form of the verb "ακολουθέω" (akolouthéo). It is in the aorist tense, which indicates a completed action in the past. The verb is active, meaning that the subject (he/she/it) is performing the action of following.
