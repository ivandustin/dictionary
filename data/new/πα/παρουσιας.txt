Word: παρουσιάς

Grammar: παρουσιάς is a noun in the genitive case, which is used to show possession or relationship. It is a feminine noun, and its singular form is παρουσία (parousia).

Description: παρουσιάς refers to the possession or ownership of something. It can also refer to the presence or appearance of someone or something. In Greek Orthodox Christianity, it is used to refer to the second coming of Christ.
