The word "παρακαλουσιν" is a verb in the present active participle form in Greek. It is derived from the root word "παρακαλέω" which means "to call alongside" or "to encourage". The present active participle form indicates that the action of calling alongside or encouraging is currently happening.

In terms of grammar, "παρακαλουσιν" is in the third person plural form, meaning that the subject of the verb is a group of people or things. It is also in the present tense, indicating that the action is happening currently.

Overall, "παρακαλουσιν" can be translated to mean "they are encouraging" or "they are calling alongside".
