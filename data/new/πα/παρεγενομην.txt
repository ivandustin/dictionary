Word: παρεγενομην

Etymology:
The word παρεγενομην is a Greek verb that comes from the root words παρά (para) meaning "beside" or "near" and ἔρχομαι (erchomai) meaning "to come". The verb is in the aorist tense, which indicates a completed action in the past.

Grammar:
παρεγενομην is a first-person singular aorist indicative active form of the verb παραγίνομαι (paragínomai), which means "to arrive" or "to come near". The aorist tense indicates that the action of arriving or coming near was completed in the past. The indicative mood indicates that the speaker is making a statement of fact.
