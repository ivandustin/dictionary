Word: πανοικει

Etymology:
The word πανοικει (panoikei) is derived from the Greek words παν (pan) meaning "all" and οἶκος (oikos) meaning "house". Therefore, the word πανοικει means "all in one house" or "a household that includes all members of a family".

Grammar:
πανοικει is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the nominative case and singular number.

Description:
πανοικει is a term used to describe a household that includes all members of a family, including extended family members. It is a concept that was common in ancient Greek society, where families lived together in large households. The term can also be used more broadly to refer to any group of people who live together as a family unit.
