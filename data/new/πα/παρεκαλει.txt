Word: παρεκαλει

Therefore, παρακαλέω originally meant "to call someone to one's side" or "to call someone alongside oneself."

Grammar: παρεκαλει is the third person singular form of the Greek verb παρακαλέω (parakaleo), which means "to call to one's side," "to invite," "to urge," or "to encourage." It is in the indicative mood and the imperfect tense, indicating an ongoing or repeated action in the past.

Description: παρεκαλει is a Greek verb that expresses the act of inviting or urging someone to come alongside oneself. It can also be used to describe the act of encouraging or comforting someone.
