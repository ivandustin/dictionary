Word: παραδοσεων

Etymology: The word παραδοσεων comes from the Greek root word παράδοση (parádosi), which means "tradition" or "handing down." The suffix -εων (-eon) is added to indicate the plural form.

Grammar: παραδοσεων is a noun in the genitive plural form in Greek. In English, it can be translated as "traditions" or "handing down."

Description: παραδοσεων refers to the act of passing down traditions or customs from one generation to the next. It can also refer to a body of traditions or customs that are passed down within a particular culture or community. In Greek culture, the term is often used to refer to the traditions and customs of the Orthodox Church.
