Word: παραγγειλαντες

Etymology:
The word παραγγειλαντες is derived from the Greek verb παραγγέλλω (parangello), which means "to order" or "to command". The suffix -αντες (-antes) is a participle ending, indicating that the word is in the past tense and refers to a group of people who have performed the action of ordering or commanding.

Grammar:
