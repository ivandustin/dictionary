Word: παρεστώτες

Etymology: The word παρεστώτες comes from the Greek verb παρείναι (pareínai), which means "to be present" or "to be at hand." The suffix -τες (-tes) is a plural ending, so παρεστώτες means "those who are present" or "those who are at hand."
