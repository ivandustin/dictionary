Word: παραδωσων

Etymology: παραδωσων is a Greek word that comes from the verb παραδίδωμι (paradidomi), which means "to hand over" or "to deliver."

Grammar: παραδωσων is the present participle form of the verb παραδίδωμι (paradidomi), which means "handing over" or "delivering." It is in the masculine gender, singular number, and nominative case.

Description: παραδωσων is a word that describes someone who is currently in the act of handing over or delivering something. It can be used in a variety of contexts, such as in legal or business transactions, or in everyday situations where someone is giving something to another person.
