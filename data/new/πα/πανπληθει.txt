The word "πανπληθει" is a Greek term that is derived from the combination of two words: "παν" (pan) meaning "all" or "every" and "πλήθω" (plēthō) meaning "to fill" or "to abound". 

In terms of grammar, "πανπληθει" is a verb in the present tense, third person singular form. It is often used in the context of describing a state of abundance or fullness.

Overall, "πανπληθει" can be translated to mean "to be filled with everything" or "to abound in all things". It is a word that conveys a sense of completeness and abundance.
