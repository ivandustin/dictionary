Word: παρειχαν

Etymology: The word παρειχαν comes from the Greek verb παρέχω (parecho), which means "to provide" or "to furnish".

Grammar: παρειχαν is the third person plural, past tense, indicative mood of the verb παρέχω. It is a regular verb in Greek and follows the conjugation pattern of verbs ending in -ω.

Description: παρειχαν is a Greek verb that means "they provided" or "they furnished". It is often used in the context of providing something to someone, such as food, shelter, or assistance. The verb can be used in both formal and informal settings, and is commonly used in everyday conversation.
