Word: παρατηρησαντες

Etymology: The word παρατηρησαντες is derived from the Greek verb παρατηρέω (paratiréo), which means "to observe" or "to watch closely". The suffix -αντες (-antes) is a participle ending that indicates the action of observing or watching has been completed.

Grammar: παρατηρησαντες is the plural masculine nominative participle of the verb παρατηρέω (paratiréo). It is used to describe a group of people who have completed the action of observing or watching closely. In Greek, participles can function as adjectives, and in this case, παρατηρησαντες functions as an adjective modifying a noun in the sentence.

Description: παρατηρησαντες is a Greek word that describes a group of people who have observed or watched something closely. It is a participle that functions as an adjective in a sentence.
