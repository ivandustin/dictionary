Word: παρεχετε

Grammar: παρεχετε is the second person plural form of the present tense of the verb παρέχω (parecho) in Greek. It is used to address a group of people and means "you provide" or "you offer".

Description: παρεχετε is a verb that is commonly used in everyday Greek language. It is often used in business settings, such as when discussing services or products that a company provides. It can also be used in personal settings, such as when offering help or assistance to someone. The verb is conjugated in the present tense, indicating that the action of providing or offering is currently happening.
