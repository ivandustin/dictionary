The word "παρεδιδου" is a conjugated form of the Greek verb "παραδίδωμι" (paradídōmi), which means "to hand over" or "to deliver." 

Etymology: The word is derived from the prefix "παρά" (pará), meaning "beside" or "near," and the verb "δίδωμι" (dídōmi), meaning "to give." 

Grammar: "παρεδιδου" is the third person singular, past tense, indicative mood, active voice form of the verb "παραδίδωμι." 

Description: The word "παρεδιδου" indicates that someone or something was handed over or delivered in the past. The context of the sentence would provide more information about who or what was handed over and to whom.
