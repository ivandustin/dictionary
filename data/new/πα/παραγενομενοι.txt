Word: παραγενομενοι

Etymology: 
The word παραγενομενοι is a Greek verb that comes from the root words παρά (para) meaning "beside" or "near" and γίνομαι (ginomai) meaning "to become" or "to happen". The word is in the aorist participle form, which indicates a completed action in the past.

παραγενομενοι is a masculine plural aorist participle in the Greek language. It is derived from the verb παραγίνομαι (paragínomai), which means "to arrive" or "to come near".
