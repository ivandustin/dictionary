Word: παρεγενοντο

Etymology:
The word παρεγενοντο is a Greek verb that comes from the root words παρά (para) meaning "beside" or "near" and ἔρχομαι (erchomai) meaning "to come". The verb is in the aorist tense, which indicates a completed action in the past.

Grammar:
παρεγενοντο is a third-person plural aorist indicative active verb in Greek. It is formed by adding the aorist tense suffix -οντο to the stem παρεγεν-. The verb is in the indicative mood, which indicates a statement of fact. The active voice indicates that the subject of the verb is performing the action.
