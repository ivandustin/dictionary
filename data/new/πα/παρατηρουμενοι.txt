Word: παρατηρουμενοι

Etymology: The word παρατηρουμενοι comes from the Greek verb παρατηρέω (paratereo), which means "to observe" or "to watch closely". The suffix -μενοι (-menoi) is a present participle ending, indicating that the word is describing something that is currently being observed or watched.

Grammar: παρατηρουμενοι is a plural masculine present participle in the nominative case. It is used to describe a group of people who are currently observing or watching something.

Description: The word παρατηρουμενοι can be translated to mean "observers" or "watchers". It is often used in scientific or academic contexts to describe a group of people who are observing an experiment or phenomenon. It can also be used more generally to describe anyone who is watching or observing something closely.
