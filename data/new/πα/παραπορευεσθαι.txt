The word "παραπορευεσθαι" (paraporeuesthai) is a Greek verb that means "to pass by, to go past, to journey past." 

Etymology: The word is derived from the prefix "παρα-" (para-), which means "beside" or "alongside," and the verb "πορευεσθαι" (poreuesthai), which means "to travel" or "to journey."

Grammar: "παραπορευεσθαι" is a present passive infinitive verb, which means it is in the present tense, passive voice, and infinitive mood. 

Description: The word is often used in the context of traveling or journeying past a particular place or person. It can also be used metaphorically to describe passing by or ignoring something or someone.
