Word: παρεδρευοντες

Etymology:
The word παρεδρευοντες is a Greek word that comes from the verb παρεδρεύω (paredreuo), which means "to sit beside" or "to be present at." The word is formed by combining the prefix παρα- (para-), which means "beside," with the verb εδρεύω (edreuó), which means "to sit."
