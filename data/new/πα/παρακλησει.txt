Word: παρακλησει

Etymology:
The word παρακλησει (paraklēsei) is derived from the Greek verb παρακαλέω (parakaleō), which means "to call to one's side" or "to encourage." It is composed of two parts: παρά (para), which means "beside" or "alongside," and καλέω (kaleō), which means "to call."

Grammar:
παρακλησει is a noun in the dative singular case in Greek. In English, it can be translated as "encouragement" or "consolation."

παρακλησει is a word that refers to the act of providing comfort, support, or encouragement to someone who is going through a difficult time. It can also refer to the words or actions that are used to provide this comfort or support.
