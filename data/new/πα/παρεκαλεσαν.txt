Word: παρεκαλεσαν

Etymology:
The word παρεκαλεσαν (parekalesan) is a Greek verb that comes from the root word καλέω (kaleo), which means "to call." The prefix παρα (para) means "beside" or "near," so the word παρεκαλεσαν can be translated as "they called near" or "they called beside."

Grammar:
παρεκαλεσαν is the third person plural aorist active indicative form of the verb παρακαλέω (parakaleo), which means "to call to one's side" or "to encourage." In this form, it indicates that the action was completed in the past by a group of people.

παρεκαλεσαν is a verb that describes an action of calling someone to one's side or encouraging them.
