Word: παραφερομεναι

Etymology:
The word παραφερομεναι is a Greek verb in the present participle form. It is derived from the root word παραφέρω (paraphero), which means "to carry along, to bring forward, to convey." The prefix παρα- (para-) means "alongside, beside, near."

Grammar:
παραφερομεναι is a present participle verb in the feminine gender, plural number, and nominative case. It is formed by adding the suffix -μεναι (-menai) to the present stem of the verb παραφέρω (paraphero).

Description:
παραφερομεναι describes a group of feminine subjects who are currently carrying or bringing something alongside or near them. The context of the sentence would determine what exactly they are carrying or bringing.
