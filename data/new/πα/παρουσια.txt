Word: παρουσία

Etymology: The word παρουσία comes from the Greek word παρουσία (parousia), which means "presence" or "arrival". It is derived from the prefix παρα- (para-), meaning "beside" or "near", and the noun οὐσία (ousia), meaning "being" or "essence".

Grammar: παρουσία is a feminine noun in Greek. It is declined in the singular and plural, and its nominative, genitive, and accusative forms are all the same. The plural form is παρουσίες.

Description: παρουσία refers to the state or condition of being present or near. It can also refer to an appearance or manifestation, particularly in a religious or spiritual context. In Christian theology, it is often used to refer to the second coming of Christ. In everyday language, it can also refer to a person's physical presence or attendance at an event.
