Word: παροντες

Etymology: παροντες is a Greek word that comes from the verb πάρειμι (pareimi), which means "to be present" or "to be in attendance". The word is formed by adding the present participle suffix -οντες (-ontes) to the stem παρ- (par-), which means "beside" or "near".

Grammar: παροντες is the present participle of the verb πάρειμι (pareimi), which means "to be present" or "to be in attendance". As a participle, it functions as an adjective and can be used to modify a noun or pronoun. In Greek, participles have tense, voice, and aspect, and can be declined like nouns.
