Word: παρεισεδυσαν

Etymology: The word παρεισεδυσαν is a compound word in Greek, consisting of the prefix παρα- (meaning "beside" or "near") and the verb εισεδύω (meaning "to enter"). Therefore, the word παρεισεδυσαν means "they entered beside/near (something/someone)".

Grammar: παρεισεδυσαν is a verb in the third person plural, aorist tense, indicative mood, and active voice. It is derived from the verb εισεδύω, which means "to enter". The prefix παρα- adds the meaning of "beside" or "near".

Description: παρεισεδυσαν is a Greek verb that describes an action of entering beside or near something or someone. The context of the sentence would determine what exactly they entered beside or near. It is a past tense verb, indicating that the action has already taken place.
