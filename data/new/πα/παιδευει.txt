The word "παιδευει" (paideuei) is a verb in the Greek language. It is derived from the root word "παιδεία" (paideia), which means education or upbringing. 

In terms of grammar, "παιδευει" is in the present tense and third person singular form. It is an active verb, meaning that the subject is performing the action of educating or training someone or something.

The word "παιδευει" can be used to describe the act of teaching or training someone in a particular skill or subject. It can also refer to the act of disciplining or correcting someone's behavior.

Overall, "παιδευει" is a versatile verb that can be used in a variety of contexts related to education, training, and discipline.
