Word: παρεστη

Etymology: παρεστη is a Greek verb that comes from the combination of the prefix παρα- (para-), meaning "beside" or "near," and the verb εἰμί (eimi), meaning "to be." Therefore, παρεστη means "to be beside" or "to be present."

Grammar: παρεστη is a third-person singular aorist active indicative verb in Greek. It can be conjugated in different tenses, moods, and voices to indicate different meanings and nuances.

Description: παρεστη is a verb that can be used to describe someone or something that is present or nearby. It can also be used to describe someone who is standing by or supporting someone else.
