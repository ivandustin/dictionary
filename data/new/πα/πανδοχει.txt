The word "πανδοχει" (pandochei) is a Greek noun that means "innkeeper" or "hotelier". It is derived from the Greek words "παν" (pan), meaning "all" or "every", and "δοχεῖον" (docheion), meaning "receptacle" or "container". In grammar, it is a masculine noun in the nominative case. The word can be used to describe someone who owns or manages an inn or hotel, providing lodging and other services to travelers.
