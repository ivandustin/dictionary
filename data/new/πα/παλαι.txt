The word "παλαι" (palai) is a Greek adverb that means "long ago" or "in ancient times". It is derived from the Greek word "παλαιός" (palaios), which means "old" or "ancient". 

In terms of grammar, "παλαι" is an adverb that modifies verbs, adjectives, or other adverbs. It does not change form based on gender, number, or case.
