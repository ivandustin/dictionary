Word: παρρησιαζεσθαι

Etymology: The word παρρησιαζεσθαι comes from the Greek verb παρρησιάζομαι (parrhēsiazomai), which is derived from the noun παρρησία (parrhēsia), meaning "boldness, frankness, freedom of speech". 

Grammar: παρρησιαζεσθαι is a verb in the present middle/passive participle form. It is a compound word made up of the prefix παρρησία (parrhēsia) and the verb ending -ζεσθαι (-zesthai). The middle/passive voice indicates that the subject is both performing and receiving the action of the verb. 

Description: The word παρρησιαζεσθαι means "to speak boldly, to speak freely, to speak openly". It is often used in the context of speaking truthfully and without fear of consequences. The word is commonly used in political and social contexts, where individuals may need to speak out against injustice or oppression.
