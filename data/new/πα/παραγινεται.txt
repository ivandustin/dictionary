The word παραγινεται (paraginetai) is a Greek verb that comes from the combination of two words: παρά (para) meaning "beside" or "near" and γίνομαι (ginomai) meaning "to become" or "to happen". Therefore, the literal meaning of παραγινεται is "to come near" or "to approach".

παραγινεται is a third-person singular present indicative active verb in Greek. It is conjugated from the verb γίνομαι (ginomai) in the present tense. The verb is often used in the passive voice, which means that the subject is being acted upon rather than performing the action.

παραγινεται is a versatile verb that can be used in various contexts. It can be used to describe physical movement, such as approaching or coming near to something or someone. It can also be used to describe a change in behavior or attitude, such as becoming more aggressive or assertive.
