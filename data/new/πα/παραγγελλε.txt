Word: παραγγελλε (parangelé)

Grammar: παραγγελλε is the second person singular imperative form of the verb παραγγέλλω (parangéllō). It is used to give a direct command or order to someone. The verb is conjugated in the present tense as follows:

- Singular: παραγγέλλε (parangelé) - you order/command
- Plural: παραγγέλλετε (parangeléte) - you all order/command

Description: παραγγελλε is a Greek verb that is commonly used in everyday language. It is often used in the context of giving orders or commands, such as in a military or business setting.
