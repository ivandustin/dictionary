Word: παραδεξονται

The present indicative active form indicates that the action is happening in the present and that the subject is performing the action. The third-person plural form indicates that the subject is a group of people.

Description: παραδεξονται is a Greek verb that means "they accept" or "they acknowledge". It is often used in formal or academic contexts to indicate that a group of people has accepted or acknowledged something.
