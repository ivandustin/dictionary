Word: παρον

Etymology:
The word παρον (paron) is a Greek word that comes from the verb πάρειμι (pareimi), which means "to be present" or "to be at hand." The word παρον is the present participle of this verb, which means "being present" or "being at hand."

Grammar:
In Greek, παρον is a neuter noun in the nominative singular form. It is also a present participle, which means that it is used to describe an action that is currently happening or ongoing.

Description:
The word παρον can be translated to mean "present" or "presence." It is often used in Greek literature and philosophy to describe the idea of being present in the moment and fully engaged in the world around us. In modern Greek, the word παρον is also used to refer to a gift or a present that is given to someone.
