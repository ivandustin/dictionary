Word: παραγει

Etymology: The word παραγει comes from the Greek verb παράγω (paragō), which means "to produce" or "to generate". It is derived from the prefix παρά (para), meaning "beside" or "alongside", and the verb γίγνομαι (gignomai), meaning "to become" or "to be born".

Grammar: παραγει is a verb in the present tense, third person singular form. It is conjugated from the verb παράγω (paragō), which is a regular verb in Greek.

Description: The word παραγει means "he/she/it produces" or "he/she/it generates". It is often used in the context of manufacturing or creating something. For example, one might say "Η εταιρεία παράγει καλλυντικά" (The company produces cosmetics).
