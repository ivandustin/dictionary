Word: παραδεχεται

Etymology:
The word παραδεχεται (paradechetai) is a Greek verb that comes from the combination of two words: παρά (para) meaning "beside" or "near" and δέχομαι (dechomai) meaning "to receive" or "to accept". Therefore, the literal meaning of παραδεχεται is "to accept beside" or "to admit".

Grammar:
παραδεχεται is a third-person singular present indicative active verb in Greek. It belongs to the first conjugation of Greek verbs, which means that it follows the regular pattern of verbs that end in -ω (omega). The verb stem is παραδεχ- (paradech-) and the ending -εται (-etai) indicates the present tense and the third-person singular.

Description:
παραδεχεται is a verb that means "to admit" or "to acknowledge". It is often used in the context of accepting or acknowledging a mistake, fault, or wrongdoing. For example, "Παραδέχομαι ότι έκανα λάθος" (Paradechomai oti ekanan lathos) means "I admit that I made a mistake". The verb can also be used in a more general sense of accepting or acknowledging something, such as "Παραδέχομαι ότι ο καιρός είναι κρύος" (Paradechomai oti o kairos einai kryos) meaning "I acknowledge that the weather is cold".
