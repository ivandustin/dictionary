Word: παρελαβετε

Etymology: παρα- (para-, prefix meaning "beside" or "alongside") + λαμβάνω (lamvano, "to take") + -ετε (-ete, suffix indicating second person plural imperative) = "take alongside" or "receive"

Grammar: παρελαβετε is the second person plural imperative form of the verb παραλαμβάνω (paralamvano, "to receive" or "to take alongside"). It is used to give a command or instruction to a group of people.

Description: παρελαβετε is a Greek word that means "receive" or "take alongside" in English. It is often used in a formal or instructional context, such as in a classroom or workplace setting. The word is formed from the prefix παρα- (para-, meaning "beside" or "alongside") and the verb λαμβάνω (lamvano, meaning "to take"). The -ετε (-ete) suffix indicates that the word is in the second person plural imperative form, meaning that it is a command or instruction directed at a group of people.
