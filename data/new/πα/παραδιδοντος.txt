Word: παραδιδοντος

Etymology: παραδιδοντος is a present participle form of the Greek verb παραδίδωμι (paradidomi), which means "to hand over" or "to deliver". The word is derived from the prefix παρα- (para-), meaning "beside" or "alongside", and the verb δίδωμι (didomi), meaning "to give".

Grammar: παραδιδοντος is a masculine singular form of the present participle in the active voice. It is declined according to the second declension, and its stem is παραδιδοντ-. The word functions as an adjective, modifying a noun or pronoun.

Description: παραδιδοντος can be translated as "handing over" or "delivering". It is often used in the context of betrayal or surrender, as in the phrase "παραδιδοντος τους εχθρους" (paradidontos tous echthrous), meaning "handing over the enemies". The word can also be used in a more general sense, such as "παραδιδοντος την αλήθειαν" (paradidontos tin aletheian), meaning "delivering the truth".
