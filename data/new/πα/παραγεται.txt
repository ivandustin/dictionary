The word "παραγεται" (paragetai) is a Greek verb in the present tense, third person singular form. It comes from the root word "παράγω" (parago), which means "to produce" or "to create." 

In terms of grammar, "παραγεται" is a regular verb that follows the conjugation pattern of verbs ending in "-ω" in the present tense. It is also in the passive voice, indicating that the subject is receiving the action rather than performing it. 

In terms of usage, "παραγεται" can be used to describe the production or creation of something, such as a product or an idea. It can also be used to describe the growth or development of something over time. 

Overall, "παραγεται" is a versatile verb that can be used in a variety of contexts to describe the process of production or growth.
