The word παρετείνεν is a Greek verb that comes from the root word τείνω (teíno), which means "to stretch" or "to extend." The prefix παρα- (para-) means "beside" or "alongside," so the word παρετείνεν can be translated as "to stretch alongside" or "to extend beside."
