The word παρεδιδοσαν is a Greek verb that comes from the root word δίδωμι (didomi), which means "to give." The prefix παρα (para) means "beside" or "alongside," so the word παρεδιδοσαν can be translated as "they gave alongside" or "they gave in addition to."

παρεδιδοσαν is a third-person plural aorist active indicative verb in Greek. The aorist tense indicates a completed action in the past, and the active voice means that the subject of the verb is performing the action. The indicative mood indicates that the verb is a statement of fact.

παρεδιδοσαν is a verb that describes an action of giving something in addition to something else.
