Word: παρεθηκαν

Etymology: The word παρεθηκαν comes from the Greek verb παρατίθημι (paratithemi), which means "to place beside" or "to deposit". The prefix παρα- (para-) means "beside" or "alongside", while the root word τίθημι (tithemi) means "to place" or "to put".

Grammar: παρεθηκαν is the third person plural aorist indicative active form of the verb παρατίθημι (paratithemi). In Greek, the aorist tense is used to describe a past action that is viewed as a single event, without any reference to its duration. The indicative mood indicates that the verb is a statement of fact. The active voice indicates that the subject of the sentence is performing the action.

Description: παρεθηκαν means "they placed beside" or "they deposited". It is a verb that describes an action of putting something next to or alongside something else. The context in which the word is used will determine what was placed or deposited.
