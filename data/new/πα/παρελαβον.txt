Word: παρελαβον

Etymology:
The word παρελαβον (parelabon) is a Greek verb in the aorist tense, which means it refers to a completed action in the past. It is derived from the root word λαμβάνω (lambano), which means "to take" or "to receive." The prefix παρα- (para-) adds the meaning of "beside" or "alongside," so the word παρελαβον (parelabon) can be translated as "I received beside" or "I took alongside."
