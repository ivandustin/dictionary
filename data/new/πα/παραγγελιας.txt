Word: παραγγελιας

Grammar: παραγγελιας is a feminine noun in the genitive case. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their grammatical function in a sentence. The genitive case is used to indicate possession or relationship, and is often translated into English using the preposition "of".

Description: παραγγελιας refers to an order or a command given by someone in authority. It can also refer to a request or an instruction given to a person or a group of people.
