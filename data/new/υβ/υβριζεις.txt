Word: υβριζεις

Grammar: υβριζεις is a second person singular present active indicative verb in Greek. It is conjugated from the verb υβρίζω (hybrizo), which is a regular verb in the Greek language.

Description: υβριζεις is a verb that describes the act of insulting or treating someone with violence and arrogance.
