The word "υβρισθεντες" (hybristhentes) is a Greek verb in the aorist tense, passive voice, participle mood. It comes from the root word "hubris" which means excessive pride or arrogance. The verb form means "to be treated with hubris" or "to be insulted with arrogance". In English, it can be translated as "being insulted" or "being treated with contempt".
