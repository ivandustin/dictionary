Word: θαυμαζειν

Etymology: θαυμαζειν is a Greek verb that comes from the noun θαῦμα (thauma), meaning "wonder" or "marvel." The verb form means "to wonder at" or "to admire."

Grammar: θαυμαζειν is a present active infinitive verb in Greek. It is formed by adding the suffix -ειν to the stem θαυμαζ-.

Description: θαυμαζειν is a verb that expresses admiration or wonder. It can be used to describe a feeling of awe or amazement towards something or someone. It is often used in a religious or spiritual context to describe the feeling of wonder towards the divine.
