Word: θαυμαστά (thavmasta)

Etymology: The word θαυμαστά comes from the Greek root word θαυμάζω (thavmazo), which means "to marvel" or "to wonder". The suffix -στά (-sta) is added to the root word to form the adjective θαυμαστά, which means "marvelous" or "wonderful".

Grammar: θαυμαστά is an adjective in the feminine gender, plural form. It agrees with the noun it modifies in gender, number, and case.

Description: θαυμαστά is used to describe something that is amazing, impressive, or awe-inspiring. It can be used to describe a person, an object, an event, or a situation. For example, one might say "Η θέα από το βουνό ήταν θαυμαστή" (I thea apo to vouno itan thavmasti), which means "The view from the mountain was marvelous".
