Word: θα

Etymology: θα is a Greek word that is used as an auxiliary verb to indicate future tense. It is derived from the ancient Greek word "θέλω" (thélo), which means "I want".

Grammar: θα is used as an auxiliary verb in Greek to indicate future tense. It is placed before the main verb in a sentence. For example, "Θα πάω στο σχολείο αύριο" (Tha pao sto scholeio aurio) means "I will go to school tomorrow". 

Description: θα is a very common word in the Greek language and is used in everyday conversation. It is an important part of Greek grammar and is used to express future actions.
