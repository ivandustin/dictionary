Word: θανατουμεθα

Etymology: The word θανατουμεθα is derived from the Greek word θάνατος (thanatos) which means "death" and the verb μένω (meno) which means "to remain". The suffix -θα is added to indicate the future tense, making the word θανατουμεθα mean "we will remain dead".

Grammar: θανατουμεθα is a first-person plural future tense verb in Greek. It is formed by adding the future tense suffix -θα to the verb μένω (meno) which means "to remain". The word is conjugated as follows:

- θανατουμεθα (thanatoumetha) - we will remain dead
