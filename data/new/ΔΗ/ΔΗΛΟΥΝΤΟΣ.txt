Etymology: The word "δηλούντος" comes from the Greek verb "δηλόω" (deloo), which means "to indicate" or "to make clear."

Grammar: "Δηλούντος" is the genitive singular form of the Greek noun "δηλούς" (delous), which means "indicator" or "sign."

Description: "Δηλούντος" is used to refer to something that is indicating or signifying. It can be used as an adjective to describe a noun, or as a noun itself.
