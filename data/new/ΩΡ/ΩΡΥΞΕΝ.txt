Etymology: The word "ωρυξεν" comes from the Greek verb "ωρύσσω" (orýssō), which means "to dig" or "to excavate".

Grammar: "Ωρυξεν" is the third person singular form of the aorist tense of the verb "ωρύσσω" (orýssō) in the Greek language.

Description: "Ωρυξεν" is a verb in the Greek language that means "he/she/it dug" or "he/she/it excavated". It refers to the action of digging or excavating the ground.
