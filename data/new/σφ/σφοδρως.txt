Word: σφοδρως

Etymology: The word σφοδρως comes from the Greek word σφοδρός (sphodrós), which means "violent" or "fierce". 

Grammar: σφοδρως is an adverb in Greek. It is used to describe the manner in which an action is performed. 

Description: σφοδρως is used to describe something that is done with great intensity or force. It can be used to describe physical actions, such as hitting or running, as well as emotional states, such as anger or passion. It is a powerful word that conveys a sense of urgency and strength.
