Word: σφραγισαμενος

Etymology: σφραγισαμενος is a Greek word that comes from the verb σφραγίζω (sphragízo), which means "to seal" or "to stamp". The word is formed by adding the suffix -μένος (-menos), which indicates the past participle tense.

Grammar: σφραγισαμενος is the past participle form of the verb σφραγίζω (sphragízo), which means "to seal" or "to stamp". It is used in the passive voice and in the masculine gender, singular number, nominative case. 

Description: σφραγισαμενος is an adjective that describes something or someone that has been sealed or stamped. It can be used to refer to a document, a package, or a person who has been marked or identified in some way.
