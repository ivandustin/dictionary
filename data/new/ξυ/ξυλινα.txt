Ξυλινά (xylina) is a Greek adjective that means "made of wood" or "wooden". It is derived from the Greek word ξύλο (xýlo), which means "wood". In terms of grammar, ξυλινά is a plural neuter adjective that agrees with the noun it modifies in gender, number, and case. It can be used to describe various objects or structures that are made of wood, such as furniture, buildings, or boats.
