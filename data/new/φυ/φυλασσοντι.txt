Word: φυλάσσοντι

Etymology: φυλάσσοντι is a present participle form of the Greek verb φυλάσσω (phylássō), which means "to guard" or "to protect". The word comes from the ancient Greek root φυλακ- (phylak-), which means "to watch" or "to guard".

Grammar: φυλάσσοντι is a present participle form of the verb φυλάσσω (phylássō), which means "guarding" or "protecting". It is in the active voice and the present tense. The word is in the masculine, singular, dative case, which means it is the indirect object of the sentence.

Description: φυλάσσοντι is a Greek word that describes someone who is actively guarding or protecting something. It can be used to describe a person, an object, or an idea that is being protected.
