Word: φυτεύων

Etymology: The word φυτεύων comes from the Greek verb φυτεύω (phyteuo), which means "to plant" or "to sow". It is derived from the noun φυτόν (phyton), which means "plant".

Grammar: φυτεύων is the present participle of the verb φυτεύω. It is in the masculine gender, singular number, and nominative case. 

Description: φυτεύων refers to a person who is planting or sowing. It can also be used as an adjective to describe something that is related to planting or sowing. For example, one could say "φυτεύων κήπος" (phyteuon kipos), which means "a garden for planting".
