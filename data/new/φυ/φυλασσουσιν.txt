Word: φυλάσσουσιν

Grammar: φυλάσσουσιν is a third-person plural present active indicative verb form in Greek. It is used to describe an ongoing action that is being performed by a group of people. In this case, the action being described is guarding or watching over something.

Description: φυλάσσουσιν is a verb that is often used in the New Testament of the Bible to describe the actions of angels or other heavenly beings who are watching over and protecting God's people.
