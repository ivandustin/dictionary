Word: φυσικώς

Etymology: The word φυσικώς (physikós) comes from the Greek word φυσικός (physikós), which means "natural" or "physical". It is derived from the word φύσις (physis), which means "nature".

Grammar: φυσικώς is an adverb in Greek. It is used to modify verbs, adjectives, or other adverbs to indicate that something is done in a natural or expected way. It is often translated as "naturally" or "of course" in English.

Description: φυσικώς is a common word in Greek and is used in everyday conversation. It is often used to express agreement or to indicate that something is obvious or expected.
