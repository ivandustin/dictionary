Word: φυλάσσεσθαι

The word φυλάσσεσθαι comes from the Greek verb φυλάσσω (phylássō), which means "to guard" or "to protect". The suffix -σθαι (-sthai) indicates that the verb is in the middle or passive voice, meaning that the subject is both performing and receiving the action of the verb.

φυλάσσεσθαι is a present middle/passive infinitive form of the verb φυλάσσω. In Greek, infinitives are formed by adding the suffix -ειν (-ein) to the verb stem. Description:
φυλάσσεσθαι is a Greek verb that means "to guard oneself" or "to protect oneself". It is often used in a military or defensive context, but can also be used more broadly to refer to any kind of self-protection or self-preservation.
