Etymology: The word "αυτα" (auta) comes from the Greek language. It is the plural form of the pronoun "αυτό" (auto), which means "it" or "this." 

Grammar: In Greek grammar, "αυτα" is a neuter plural pronoun. It is used to refer to inanimate objects or abstract concepts. 

Description: "Αυτα" is used to indicate multiple objects or things. It is similar to the English pronoun "these" or "those." It is often used to point out or refer to specific items or ideas.
