Etymology: The word "αυξανομενα" comes from the Greek verb "αυξάνω" (auxano), which means "to increase" or "to grow".

Grammar: "Αυξανομενα" is the plural form of the Greek adjective "αυξανόμενος" (auxanomenos), which means "increasing" or "growing". It is used to describe multiple things that are in the process of increasing or growing.
