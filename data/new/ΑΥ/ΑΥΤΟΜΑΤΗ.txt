Etymology: The word "αυτοματη" (automati) comes from the Greek words "αυτός" (autos) meaning "self" and "ματη" (mate) meaning "acting". 

Grammar: "αυτοματη" is a feminine noun in the singular form. 

Description: "αυτοματη" refers to something that is automatic or self-acting. It can be used to describe a machine, process, or action that operates or functions without human intervention.
