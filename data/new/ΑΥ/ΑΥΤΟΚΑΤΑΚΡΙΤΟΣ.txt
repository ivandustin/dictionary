Etymology: The word "αυτοκατακριτος" comes from the Greek roots "αυτο" meaning "self" and "κατακριτος" meaning "condemned". 

Grammar: "Αυτοκατακριτος" is an adjective in the masculine gender, singular form. It is used to describe someone who condemns themselves or is self-condemned.

Description: "Αυτοκατακριτος" refers to a person who is critical of themselves, often excessively or unnecessarily. It can describe someone who constantly blames themselves or judges themselves harshly.
