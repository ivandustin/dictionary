Etymology: The word "αυτοφωρωί" (autophoroi) comes from the Greek words "αυτός" (autos) meaning "self" and "φωράω" (phorao) meaning "to see". 

Grammar: "Αυτοφωρωί" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the singular form and belongs to the neuter gender. 

Description: "Αυτοφωρωί" refers to the act of seeing or witnessing something oneself, without the need for external confirmation or evidence. It implies a direct and personal observation.
