Word: φανερωσον

Etymology: φανερωσον is a Greek word derived from the verb φανερόω (phaneroó), which means "to make visible, to reveal, to manifest". It is formed from the root word φανερός (phanerós), which means "visible, apparent, clear".

Grammar: φανερωσον is a verb in the imperative mood, second person singular, active voice. It is a command or request directed to someone to perform the action of making something visible or revealing something.

Description: φανερωσον is a word that is often used in a religious or spiritual context, where it is used to urge someone to reveal or make known the truth or the will of a higher power. It can also be used in a more general sense to encourage someone to be open and honest about their thoughts or feelings.
