The word "φαρες" (phares) is a Greek noun that means "lighthouse" or "beacon". It is derived from the Greek verb "φαίνω" (phaino), which means "to shine" or "to appear". In terms of grammar, "φαρες" is a masculine noun in the nominative singular form. It can also be declined in other cases and numbers, such as "φάρου" (genitive singular) or "φάροι" (nominative plural).
