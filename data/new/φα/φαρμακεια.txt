Word: φαρμακεια

Etymology:
The word φαρμακεια (pharmakeia) comes from the Greek word φάρμακον (pharmakon), which means "drug" or "medicine". In ancient Greek, the word was also used to refer to magic potions and spells.

Grammar:
In Greek, φαρμακεια is a feminine noun. Its plural form is φαρμακείες (pharmakeies).

Description:
φαρμακεια refers to a pharmacy or drugstore where medicines and other health-related products are sold. In modern Greek, it can also refer to the practice of pharmacy or the study of pharmaceuticals. The word is commonly used in Greece and other Greek-speaking countries.
