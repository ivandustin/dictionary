The word "φανερως" (phanerōs) is an adverb in Greek that means "clearly" or "evidently". It is derived from the adjective "φανερός" (phanerós), which means "visible" or "apparent". In terms of grammar, "φανερως" is an adverb of manner, modifying the verb or adjective it is associated with. It is often used in philosophical and theological contexts to describe the manifestation or revelation of truth.
