Word: φανερωσαντες

The word φανερωσαντες is a Greek verb that comes from the root word φανερός (phanerós), which means "visible" or "apparent." The verb form φανερωσαντες is the aorist active participle of the verb φανερόω (phaneroó), which means "to make visible" or "to reveal."

It is also in the active voice, which means that the subject of the sentence is performing the action. In this case, the subject is not specified, but it would be whoever is doing the action of making something visible or revealing it.

φανερωσαντες is a word that describes the act of making something visible or revealing it.
