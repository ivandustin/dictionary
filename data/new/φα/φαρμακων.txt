Word: φαρμακων

Etymology:
The word φαρμακων comes from the Greek word φάρμακον (pharmakon), which means "drug" or "medicine". The suffix -ων (-on) is a common ending in Greek that is used to form nouns.

Grammar:
φαρμακων is a noun in the genitive case, which is used to show possession or relationship. In this case, it means "of drugs" or "of medicines".

Description:
φαρμακων is a word that refers to drugs or medicines. It is often used in the context of ancient Greek medicine, where drugs and herbal remedies were commonly used to treat various ailments. In modern Greek, the word is still used to refer to medicines, both prescription and over-the-counter.
