The word "φαντασιας" (phantasias) is a Greek noun that means "imagination" or "fantasy". It is derived from the Greek verb "φαντάζομαι" (phantazomai), which means "to imagine" or "to conceive". 

In terms of grammar, "φαντασιας" is a genitive singular noun, meaning it is used to show possession or relationship to another noun. For example, "τα όνειρά μου είναι γεμάτα φαντασιών" (ta oneira mou einai gemata fantasion) translates to "my dreams are full of fantasies".

Overall, "φαντασιας" is a word that describes the creative and imaginative aspects of the human mind.
