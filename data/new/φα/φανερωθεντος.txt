Word: φανερωθεντος

The suffix -θεντος (-thentos) is a passive participle ending, indicating that the word is in the passive voice and refers to something that has been revealed or made visible.

Grammar: φανερωθεντος is a passive participle in the genitive case, masculine gender, and singular number. It is used as an adjective to modify a noun or pronoun, indicating that the noun or pronoun has been revealed or made visible.

Description: φανερωθεντος can be translated as "having been revealed" or "having been made visible." It is often used in a religious or spiritual context to refer to the revelation of divine truth or the manifestation of God's power.
