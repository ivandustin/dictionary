Etymology: The word "διψαί" (dipsai) comes from the Greek verb "διψάω" (dipsao), which means "to thirst". 

Grammar: "Διψαί" is the plural form of the noun "δίψα" (dipsa), which means "thirst" in Greek. It belongs to the third declension of Greek nouns.

Description: "Διψαί" refers to multiple instances of thirst. It is used to describe the feeling of needing to drink or the state of being thirsty.
