Etymology: The word "διεκωλύεν" comes from the Greek verb "διακωλύω" (diakolýo), which means "to hinder" or "to obstruct".

Grammar: "διεκωλύεν" is the third person singular form of the verb "διακωλύω" in the imperfect tense. It is in the active voice and indicative mood.

Description: "διεκωλύεν" describes an action of hindering or obstructing that was ongoing in the past. It refers to a third person singular subject (he/she/it) that was actively hindering or obstructing something or someone.
