Etymology: The word "διδασκε" (didaske) comes from the Greek verb "διδάσκω" (didasko), which means "to teach" or "to instruct".

Grammar: "Διδάσκε" is the imperative form of the verb "διδάσκω" (didasko) in the second person singular. It is used to give a command or instruction to someone, telling them to teach or instruct.
