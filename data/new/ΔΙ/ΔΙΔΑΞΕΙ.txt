Etymology: The word "διδάξει" (didaxeí) comes from the Greek verb "διδάσκω" (didásko), which means "to teach" or "to instruct."

Grammar: "Διδάξει" is the future tense, third person singular form of the verb "διδάσκω" (didásko). It indicates that someone or something will teach or instruct in the future.

Description: "Διδάξει" is a Greek word that refers to the act of teaching or instructing. It can be used to describe the action of imparting knowledge or skills to others.
