Grammar: "διαγνώσιν" is a noun in the accusative case, singular number, and feminine gender. In Greek grammar, nouns change their form depending on their role in a sentence, such as subject, object, or possessive.

Description: "διαγνώσιν" refers to the act or process of diagnosing or identifying a disease, condition, or problem. It is commonly used in medical and diagnostic contexts to describe the determination of a patient's illness or ailment through examination, testing, and analysis.
