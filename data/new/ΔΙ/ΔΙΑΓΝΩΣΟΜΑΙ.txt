Etymology: The word "διαγνώσομαι" (diagnósomai) comes from the Greek verb "γινώσκω" (ginósko), which means "to know" or "to understand." The prefix "δια-" (dia-) adds the meaning of "through" or "across," indicating a thorough or comprehensive understanding.

Grammar: "Διαγνώσομαι" is a reflexive verb in the present tense, first person singular form. In Greek, reflexive verbs indicate that the subject performs the action on itself. In this case, it means "I diagnose myself."

Description: "Διαγνώσομαι" is a Greek verb that is commonly used in medical contexts. It refers to the act of diagnosing oneself, typically in terms of identifying a medical condition or illness.
