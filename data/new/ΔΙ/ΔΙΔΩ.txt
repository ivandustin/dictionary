Etymology: The word "διδώ" (pronounced "dido") comes from the Greek verb "δίδωμι" (pronounced "dídomee"), which means "I give." 

Grammar: "Διδώ" is the first person singular present tense form of the verb "δίδωμι." It is used when the subject of the sentence is "I" and the action is giving. 

Description: "Διδώ" is a Greek word that means "I give." It is used to express the action of giving something to someone.
