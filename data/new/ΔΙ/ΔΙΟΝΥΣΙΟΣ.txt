Etymology: The word "διονύσιος" (dionysios) comes from the Greek name Διόνυσος (Dionysos), which is derived from the name of the Greek god of wine, Dionysus. 

Grammar: "Διονύσιος" is a masculine Greek name. In Greek grammar, it belongs to the second declension, and its genitive form is "Διονυσίου" (Dionysiou). 

Description: "Διονύσιος" is a common Greek given name. It is derived from the name of the Greek god Dionysus, who was associated with wine, fertility, and revelry. The name has been used in various forms throughout history and is still used today.
