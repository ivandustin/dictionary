Etymology: The word "διατροφή" comes from the Greek roots "δια-" meaning "through" or "across" and "τροφή" meaning "nourishment" or "food". 

Grammar: "Διατροφή" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the feminine gender and is used in the singular form. 

Description: "Διατροφή" refers to the act or process of nourishing or providing food to sustain life. It encompasses the food and drink that a person consumes on a regular basis. It can also refer to the overall dietary habits and patterns of an individual or a group. A balanced and healthy diet is essential for maintaining good health and well-being.
