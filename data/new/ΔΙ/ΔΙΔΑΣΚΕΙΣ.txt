Etymology: The word "διδάσκεις" comes from the Greek verb "διδάσκω" (didasko), which means "to teach" or "to instruct".

Grammar: "Διδάσκεις" is the second person singular form of the present tense of the verb "διδάσκω" (didasko) in Greek. It is used when addressing someone directly and means "you teach" or "you are teaching".

Description: "Διδάσκεις" is a word in the Greek language that refers to the act of teaching or instructing. It is used to describe the action of imparting knowledge or skills to others.
