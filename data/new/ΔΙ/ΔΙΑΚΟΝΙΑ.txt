Etymology: The word "διακονία" (diakonia) comes from the Greek verb "διακονέω" (diakoneo), which means "to serve" or "to minister". It is derived from the noun "διάκονος" (diakonos), meaning "servant" or "minister".

Grammar: "Διακονία" is a feminine noun in Greek. It belongs to the second declension and has the plural form "διακονίες" (diakonies).

Description: "Διακονία" refers to the act of serving or ministering to others. It can have various meanings depending on the context. In a religious context, it often refers to the service or ministry performed by clergy or members of a religious community.
