Etymology: The word "διδασκαλία" comes from the Greek verb "διδάσκω" (didasko), which means "to teach" or "to instruct". It is derived from the Proto-Indo-European root *deik-, meaning "to show" or "to point out".

Grammar: "Διδασκαλία" is a feminine noun in Greek. It is in the singular form. In the plural form, it becomes "διδασκαλίες" (didaskalies).

Description: "Διδασκαλία" refers to the act or process of teaching or instructing. It encompasses the methods, techniques, and principles used to impart knowledge, skills, or information to others. It can be used in various contexts, such as formal education, training programs, or any form of structured learning.
