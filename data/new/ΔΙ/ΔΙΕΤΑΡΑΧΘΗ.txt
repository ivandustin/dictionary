Etymology: The word "διεταραχή" comes from the Greek roots "δια-" meaning "through" or "across" and "εταραχή" meaning "disturbance" or "disorder". 

Grammar: "Διεταραχή" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the singular form and belongs to the feminine gender. 

Description: "Διεταραχή" refers to a state of disturbance, disorder, or disruption. It can be used to describe a disruption in the normal functioning or order of something, such as a system, process, or situation.
