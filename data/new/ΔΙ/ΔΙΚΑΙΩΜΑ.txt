Grammar: In Greek, "δικαίωμα" is a neuter noun of the first declension. It is singular and can be used in both the nominative and accusative cases. The plural form is "δικαιώματα" (dikaiómata).

Description: "Δικαίωμα" refers to a legal or moral right, entitlement, or privilege.
