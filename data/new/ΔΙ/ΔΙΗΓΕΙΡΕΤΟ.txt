Etymology: The word "διηγητο" (diēgēto) comes from the Greek verb "διηγέομαι" (diēgeomai), which means "to narrate" or "to tell a story." 

Grammar: "Διηγητο" is a noun in the singular form. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their gender, number, and case. 

Description: "Διηγητο" refers to a narrator or storyteller. It is used to describe someone who tells a story or narrates events.
