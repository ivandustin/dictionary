Etymology: The word "διδώμι" (didōmi) comes from the Greek verb "δίδωμι" (dídōmi), which means "I give" or "I offer."

Grammar: "Διδώμι" is the first person singular present tense form of the verb "δίδωμι" (dídōmi). It is used when the subject "I" is giving or offering something.

Description: "Διδώμι" is a Greek word that expresses the action of giving or offering something. It is commonly used in everyday conversations and written texts.
