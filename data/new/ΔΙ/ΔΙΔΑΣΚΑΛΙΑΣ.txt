Etymology: The word "διδασκαλίας" comes from the Greek word "διδασκαλία" (didaskalia), which means "teaching" or "instruction". It is derived from the verb "διδάσκω" (didasko), meaning "to teach".

Grammar: "Διδασκαλίας" is a noun in the genitive case, singular form. In Greek grammar, the genitive case is used to indicate possession or relationship.
