Etymology: The word "διαπερώσιν" comes from the Greek verb "διαπερνώ" (diapernó), which means "to pierce through" or "to penetrate."

Grammar: "Διαπερώσιν" is a noun in the accusative case, singular number, and feminine gender. It is derived from the verb "διαπερνώ" and is formed by adding the suffix "-σιν" to the stem of the verb.

Description: "Διαπερώσιν" refers to the act of piercing through or penetrating something. It can also be used metaphorically to describe a deep understanding or insight into a subject.
