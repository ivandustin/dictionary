Etymology: The word "διεδωκεν" (diēdōken) comes from the Greek verb "διδωμι" (didōmi), which means "to give" or "to grant."

Grammar: "Διεδωκεν" is the third person singular form of the aorist active indicative tense of the verb "διδωμι" (didōmi). In Greek grammar, the aorist tense is used to express a completed action in the past.

Description: "Διεδωκεν" is a Greek word that means "he/she/it gave" or "he/she/it granted." It is used to describe an action of giving or granting something in the past.
