Etymology: The word "διδασκαλούς" comes from the Greek word "διδάσκαλος" (didáskalos), which means "teacher" or "instructor". 

Grammar: "Διδασκαλούς" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the plural form, specifically the accusative case. 

Description: "Διδασκαλούς" refers to multiple teachers or instructors. It is used to describe a group of individuals who teach or provide instruction in various subjects or disciplines.
