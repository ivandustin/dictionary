Etymology: The word "διδασκαλον" (didaskalon) comes from the Greek word "διδάσκαλος" (didaskalos), which means "teacher" or "instructor". 

Grammar: "Διδασκαλον" is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the singular form and belongs to the second declension. 

Description: "Διδασκαλον" refers to a teacher or an instructor. It is used to describe someone who imparts knowledge or skills to others.
