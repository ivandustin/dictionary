Etymology: The word "διατασσομαι" comes from the Greek verb "τασσω" (tasso), which means "to arrange" or "to put in order". The prefix "δια-" adds the meaning of "through" or "across", giving the word "διατασσομαι" the sense of "to arrange or put in order through a process".

Grammar: "διατασσομαι" is a Greek verb in the present tense, middle voice, and indicative mood. It is conjugated in the first person singular form. The middle voice indicates that the subject is performing the action on itself or for its own benefit.

Description: "διατασσομαι" means "I arrange" or "I put in order" in Greek. It implies the act of organizing or arranging something in a systematic manner.
