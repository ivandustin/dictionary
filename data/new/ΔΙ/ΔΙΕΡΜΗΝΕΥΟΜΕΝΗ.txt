Etymology: The word "διερμηνευόμενη" comes from the Greek verb "διερμηνεύω" (di-er-mi-ne-vo), which means "to interpret" or "to translate."

Grammar: "Διερμηνευόμενη" is the feminine form of the present participle of the verb "διερμηνεύω." In Greek, verbs have different forms based on gender, number, and tense. The participle form indicates ongoing action or state.

Description: "Διερμηνευόμενη" is an adjective that describes something or someone that is being interpreted or translated. It is commonly used to refer to a text, speech, or language that is being interpreted from one language to another.
