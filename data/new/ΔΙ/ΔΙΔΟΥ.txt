Etymology: The word "διδού" (didou) comes from the Greek verb "δίδωμι" (didomi), which means "I give". 

Grammar: "Διδού" is the 2nd person singular form of the verb "δίδωμι" (didomi) in the present tense. It is used when addressing one person and means "you give" in English.

Description: "Διδού" is a word in the Greek language that is used to express the action of giving in the present tense. It is commonly used in everyday conversations and written texts.
