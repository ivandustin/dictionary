Grammar: "Διικνούμενος" is the present participle form of the verb "διέρχομαι" (diérchomai), which is a deponent verb. Deponent verbs have passive forms but active meanings. The present participle form indicates an ongoing or continuous action.

Description: "Διικνούμενος" can be translated as "passing through" or "going through". It describes someone or something that is in the process of moving or traveling through a place or situation.
