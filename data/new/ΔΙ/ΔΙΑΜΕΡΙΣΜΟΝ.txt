Grammar: "Διαμερισμόν" is a masculine noun in the singular form. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their gender, number, and case.

Description: "Διαμερισμόν" refers to the act of dividing or separating something into parts or sections. It can also be used to describe the result of such an action, meaning the divided or separated parts.
