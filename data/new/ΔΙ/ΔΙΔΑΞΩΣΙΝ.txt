Etymology: The word "διδαξιν" comes from the Greek verb "διδάσκω" (didasko), which means "to teach" or "to instruct".

Grammar: "Διδαξιν" is a noun in the accusative case, singular number, and masculine gender in Greek grammar.

Description: "Διδαξιν" refers to the act of teaching or instruction. It can also be used to describe the process of imparting knowledge or skills to someone.
