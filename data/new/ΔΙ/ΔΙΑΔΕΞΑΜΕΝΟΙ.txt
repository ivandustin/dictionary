Etymology: The word "διαδεχόμενοι" comes from the Greek verb "διαδέχομαι" (diadéchomai), which means "to succeed" or "to take over."

Grammar: "Διαδεχόμενοι" is the plural form of the present participle of the verb "διαδέχομαι." In Greek grammar, the present participle is used to describe ongoing actions or states.

Description: "Διαδεχόμενοι" refers to a group of people who are succeeding or taking over something. It can be used to describe individuals who are replacing others in a position, role, or responsibility.
