Grammar: "Διηνοιγεν" is the third person singular, aorist tense, active voice form of the verb "διανοίγω" (dianoigo) in the Greek language.

Description: "Διηνοιγεν" is a word used in Greek to describe an action of opening something completely or thoroughly. It can be used in various contexts, such as opening a door, a book, or a container.
