Grammar: "διακουσομαι" is a verb in the present tense, middle voice, and indicative mood. In Greek grammar, the middle voice indicates that the subject is both the doer and the receiver of the action. It is conjugated in the first person singular form.

Description: "διακουσομαι" means "I listen attentively" or "I pay attention" in English. It implies actively listening and giving full attention to what is being heard.
