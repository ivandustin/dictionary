Grammar: "Διαπορεύεσθαι" is an infinitive verb form in the Greek language. It belongs to the middle voice, indicating that the subject is both the doer and the receiver of the action. In English, it can be translated as "to pass through" or "to traverse."

Description: "Διαπορεύεσθαι" describes the action of moving or traveling through a place or space. It implies a sense of movement from one point to another, often involving overcoming obstacles or difficulties along the way.
