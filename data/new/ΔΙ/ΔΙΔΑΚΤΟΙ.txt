Etymology: The word "διδακτοι" comes from the Greek word "διδάσκω" (didasko), which means "to teach" or "to instruct".

Grammar: "Διδακτοι" is a plural noun in the Greek language. It belongs to the second declension and is of the masculine gender.

Description: "Διδακτοι" refers to "teachers" or "instructors" in Greek. It is used to describe individuals who educate or impart knowledge to others.
