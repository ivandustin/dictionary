Etymology: The word "διελογισμός" comes from the Greek roots "διά" (dia), meaning "through" or "across," and "λογισμός" (logismos), meaning "reasoning" or "thought." 

Grammar: "Διελογισμός" is a masculine noun in the singular form. 

Description: "Διελογισμός" refers to the act of reasoning or contemplating deeply. It can also denote a thought process or a logical argument.
