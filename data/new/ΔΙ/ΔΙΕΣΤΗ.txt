Etymology: The word "διεστη" (diestē) comes from the Greek verb "διαστέλλω" (diastellō), which means "to separate" or "to divide". 

Grammar: "Διεστη" is a verb in the past tense, third person singular form. It is derived from the verb "διαστέλλω" (diastellō), which is a compound verb formed by adding the prefix "δια-" (dia-) meaning "through" or "across" to the verb "στέλλω" (stellō) meaning "to send" or "to place".
