Grammar: "Διερμηνεύσεν" is the aorist tense, third person singular form of the verb "διερμηνεύω" in Greek. The aorist tense is used to describe a completed action in the past.

Description: "Διερμηνεύσεν" is a Greek word that means "he/she/it interpreted" or "he/she/it translated." It is used to refer to the act of translating or interpreting something from one language to another.
