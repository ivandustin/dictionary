Etymology: The word "διδασκειν" (didáskein) comes from the Greek verb "διδάσκω" (didáskō), which means "to teach" or "to instruct."

Grammar: "Διδασκειν" is the present active infinitive form of the verb "διδάσκω" (didáskō). In Greek grammar, the infinitive form is used to express the action of the verb without specifying the subject or tense.

Description: "Διδασκειν" is a word in the Greek language that means "to teach" or "to instruct." It is used to describe the action of imparting knowledge or skills to others.
