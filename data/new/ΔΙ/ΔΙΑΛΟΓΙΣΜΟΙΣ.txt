Grammar: "Διαλογισμός" is a masculine noun in the Greek language. It belongs to the second declension and its genitive form is "διαλογισμού". In the plural form, it becomes "διαλογισμοί".

Description: "Διαλογισμός" refers to the act of thinking or contemplating deeply. It can also be translated as "reflection" or "meditation". In philosophy, it is often used to describe the process of rational thought or logical reasoning.
