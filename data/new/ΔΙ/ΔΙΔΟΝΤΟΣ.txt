Etymology: The word "διδοντος" (didontos) comes from the Greek verb "δίδωμι" (didomi), which means "to give". 

Grammar: "Διδοντος" is the genitive form of the noun "διδοντής" (didontis), which means "giver" or "donor". In Greek grammar, the genitive case is used to indicate possession or origin.
