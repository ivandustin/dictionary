Etymology: The word "ησθιον" (esthion) comes from the Greek verb "εσθίω" (esthio), which means "to eat" or "to consume."

Grammar: "Ησθιον" is a noun in the neuter gender. It is the accusative singular form of the word.

Description: "Ησθιον" refers to food or nourishment. It is used to describe something that is eaten or consumed for sustenance.
