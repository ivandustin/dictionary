The word "ησθενησα" is a Greek verb in the past tense, first person singular form. It comes from the root word "ασθενέω" (astheneo), which means "to be weak" or "to be sick" in Greek. The verb "ησθενησα" is used to indicate that the speaker was weak or sick in the past. In terms of grammar, it is a regular verb that follows the conjugation pattern of verbs ending in "-ω" in Greek.
