The word "ησπασατο" is a Greek verb in the aorist tense, third person singular form. It comes from the root verb "σπάω" (spao), which means "to tear" or "to break." The prefix "ε-" (e-) indicates the aorist tense, which is used to express a completed action in the past. The suffix "-ατο" (-ato) indicates the third person singular form. Therefore, "ησπασατο" translates to "he/she/it tore" or "he/she/it broke" in English.
