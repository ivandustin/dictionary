Etymology: The word "ησσον" (esson) comes from the Greek adjective "ἥσσων" (hēssōn), which means "less" or "inferior."

Grammar: "Ησσον" is a comparative adjective in the Greek language. It is used to compare two things, indicating that the subject is of lesser degree or quality than the other.
