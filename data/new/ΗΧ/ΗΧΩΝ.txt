Etymology: The word "ηχών" (ichon) comes from the Greek verb "ηχέω" (icheo), which means "to echo" or "to resound". 

Grammar: "Ηχών" is a noun in the masculine gender and singular number. It belongs to the second declension in Greek grammar.

Description: "Ηχών" refers to the act of echoing or the sound that is reflected back to the listener after hitting a surface. It can also be used metaphorically to describe the repetition or imitation of someone's words or actions.
