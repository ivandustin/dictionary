Etymology: The word "εζητείτε" comes from the Greek verb "ζητώ" (zito), which means "to seek" or "to search". The prefix "ε-" is added to indicate the present tense and the plural form.

Grammar: "Εζητείτε" is the present tense, second person plural form of the verb "ζητώ" (zito). It is used when addressing a group of people and means "you all seek" or "you all search".

Description: "Εζητείτε" is a verb form that expresses the action of seeking or searching. It is used to talk about the act of looking for something or trying to find something.
