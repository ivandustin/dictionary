Word: βιωτικα

Etymology: The word βιωτικα comes from the Greek word βίωσις (biōsis), which means "way of life" or "mode of living". The suffix -ικα (-ika) is a common Greek suffix used to form adjectives or nouns indicating a relationship or connection to the root word.

Grammar: βιωτικα is a plural neuter adjective in Greek. It is used to describe things that are related to or characteristic of a certain way of life.

Description: βιωτικα refers to things that are related to a particular way of life or mode of living.
