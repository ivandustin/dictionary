The word βιωτικαις (biotikais) is a Greek adjective that means "pertaining to life" or "related to living organisms." 

Etymology: The word βιωτικαις is derived from the Greek word βίος (bios), which means "life." The suffix -τικαις (-tikais) is a common adjectival suffix in Greek that means "related to" or "pertaining to."

Grammar: βιωτικαις is a feminine plural adjective in the nominative case. It agrees in gender, number, and case with the noun it modifies.

Description: The word βιωτικαις is often used in scientific contexts to describe things that are related to living organisms, such as biotic factors in an ecosystem or biotic interactions between species. It can also be used more broadly to describe anything that is related to or affects life.
