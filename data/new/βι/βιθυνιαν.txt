Word: βιθυνιαν

Etymology: The word βιθυνιαν comes from the Greek word Βιθυνία (Bithynia), which was an ancient region in Asia Minor (modern-day Turkey).

Grammar: βιθυνιαν is a noun in the accusative case, singular number, and feminine gender in the Greek language.

Description: βιθυνιαν refers to a woman from Bithynia or something related to Bithynia. It is a specific term used in ancient Greek literature and history.
