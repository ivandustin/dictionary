Word: βιβλους

Etymology: βιβλους is a Greek word that comes from the root word βιβλίον (biblion), meaning "book" or "scroll". The suffix -ους (-ous) is a common Greek ending that denotes possession or belonging.

Grammar: βιβλους is a noun in the genitive case, which indicates possession or relationship to another noun. In this case, it means "of the book" or "belonging to the book".

Description: βιβλους is a specific form of the word βιβλίον (biblion), which refers to a book or a scroll. The genitive form βιβλους indicates that something belongs to or is related to a book. It could be used to describe a book's author, owner, or contents.
