The word βιβλου (biblou) is a Greek noun that means "book" or "scroll". It is derived from the Greek word βιβλίον (biblion), which also means "book" or "scroll". In terms of grammar, βιβλου is in the genitive case, which is used to indicate possession or relationship. 

In ancient Greece, books and scrolls were typically made of papyrus or parchment and were used for writing and recording important information such as literature, history, and religious texts. The word βιβλου is still used in modern Greek to refer to books, both physical and digital.
