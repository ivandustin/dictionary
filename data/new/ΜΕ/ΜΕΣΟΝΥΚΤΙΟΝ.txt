Etymology: The word "μεσονυκτιον" comes from the Greek words "μεσος" (mesos) meaning "middle" and "νυξ" (nyx) meaning "night". 

Grammar: "μεσονυκτιον" is a noun in the neuter gender. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their gender, number, and case. 

Description: "μεσονυκτιον" refers to the period of time that is in the middle of the night, specifically midnight. It is the point in time when the clock strikes 12:00 AM.
