μεμαρτυρηκε (memartyreke) is a Greek verb that means "to testify" or "to bear witness." It is derived from the combination of the prefix με- (me-), which indicates a change or transformation, and the verb μαρτυρέω (martyreo), which means "to testify" or "to bear witness." In Greek grammar, μεμαρτυρηκε is the perfect tense form of the verb, indicating a completed action in the past with ongoing relevance to the present.
