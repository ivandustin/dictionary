Etymology: The word "μεγαλαι" comes from the Greek word "μέγας" (megas), meaning "great" or "big".

Grammar: "μεγαλαι" is the plural form of the Greek adjective "μεγάλη" (megali), which means "big" or "great". It is used to describe feminine nouns in the nominative case.

Description: "μεγαλαι" is used to describe multiple feminine nouns that are big or great in size or importance. It agrees in gender and number with the nouns it modifies.
