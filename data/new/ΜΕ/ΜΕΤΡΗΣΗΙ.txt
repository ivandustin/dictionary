Etymology: The word "μετρησηι" (metrēsēi) comes from the Greek verb "μετρέω" (metréō), which means "to measure" or "to count."

Grammar: "μετρησηι" is a noun in the singular form. It belongs to the third declension in Greek grammar.

Description: "μετρησηι" refers to the act of measuring or counting. It can also be used to describe the result or outcome of a measurement.
