Etymology: The word "μεγαλως" (megalos) comes from the Greek root "μεγα-" (mega-), meaning "great" or "large". It is combined with the suffix "-λως" (-los), which is an adverbial suffix indicating manner or degree.

Grammar: "Μεγαλως" (megalos) is an adverb in Greek. It is used to modify verbs, adjectives, or other adverbs, indicating the manner or degree of an action or quality.

Description: "Μεγαλως" (megalos) can be translated as "greatly" or "in a great manner". It is used to emphasize the intensity or extent of an action or quality.
