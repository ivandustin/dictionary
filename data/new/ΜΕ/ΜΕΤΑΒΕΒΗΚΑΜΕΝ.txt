μεταβεβηκαμεν (metabebekamen) is a Greek verb that means "we have moved." It is the first person plural form of the verb μεταβαίνω (metavaino), which means "to move" or "to transfer." The verb is composed of the prefix μετα- (meta-), which indicates a change or movement, and the verb βαίνω (vaino), which means "to go" or "to walk." In Greek grammar, μεταβεβηκαμεν is in the indicative mood, past perfect tense, and active voice.
