Etymology: The word "μειζονας" (meizonas) comes from the Greek word "μείζων" (meizon), which means "greater" or "larger".

Grammar: "Μειζονας" is a noun in the masculine gender and singular number in Greek. It belongs to the second declension.

Description: "Μειζονας" is used to describe something or someone that is greater or larger in size, quantity, importance, or degree. It can be used to compare two or more things, indicating that one is bigger or more significant than the other(s).
