Etymology: The word "μενοντα" comes from the Greek verb "μένω" (meno), which means "to stay" or "to remain".

Grammar: "μενοντα" is the present participle form of the verb "μένω" (meno) in the masculine gender, singular number, and accusative case.

Description: "μενοντα" refers to a singular masculine noun or pronoun that is the direct object of the verb "μένω" (meno) in the present tense. It indicates someone or something that is currently staying or remaining.
