Etymology: The word "μεγαλη" (megale) comes from the Greek word "μέγας" (megas), meaning "big" or "great".

Grammar: In Greek, "μεγαλη" (megale) is a feminine noun in the nominative case. It can also be declined in other cases, such as genitive (μεγαλης), accusative (μεγαλην), etc.

Description: "μεγαλη" (megale) is the Greek word for "big" or "great" when referring to a feminine noun. It can be used to describe the size, importance, or significance of something.
