Etymology: The word "μεγαλου" (megálo) comes from the Greek word "μέγας" (mégas), meaning "big" or "great".

Grammar: "Μεγαλου" is a noun in the genitive case. In Greek, nouns change their form depending on their grammatical role in a sentence. The genitive case is used to indicate possession or origin.
