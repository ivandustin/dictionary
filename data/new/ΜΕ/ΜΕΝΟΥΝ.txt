Etymology: The word "μενούν" (menoun) comes from the Greek verb "μένω" (meno), which means "to stay" or "to remain."

Grammar: "Μενούν" is the present tense, third person plural form of the verb "μένω" (meno). It is used to indicate that a group of people or things stays or remains in a certain place or condition.

Description: "Μενούν" is a verb in the Greek language that expresses the action of staying or remaining. It is commonly used to describe the continuous presence or state of a group of people or things in a particular location or situation.
