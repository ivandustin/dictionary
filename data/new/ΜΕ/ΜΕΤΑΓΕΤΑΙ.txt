Etymology: The word "μεταγεταί" comes from the Greek word "μεταγεταί" (metagetai), which means "it changes" or "it is transformed". 

Grammar: "μεταγεταί" is a verb in the present tense, third person singular form. It is derived from the verb "μεταγίνομαι" (metagínomai), which means "to change" or "to transform". 

Description: "μεταγεταί" refers to the act of changing or transforming something. It can be used to describe a physical or conceptual change.
