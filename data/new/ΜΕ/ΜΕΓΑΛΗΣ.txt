Etymology: The word "μεγαλης" (megalēs) comes from the Greek word "μέγας" (megas), meaning "great" or "big".

Grammar: "Μεγαλης" (megalēs) is a noun in the Greek language. It is in the genitive case, indicating possession or relationship.

Description: "Μεγαλης" (megalēs) is a Greek word that means "of the big" or "of the great". It is used to describe something that belongs to or is related to something big or great.
