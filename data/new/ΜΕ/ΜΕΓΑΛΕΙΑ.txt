Etymology: The word "μεγαλεία" (megaleia) comes from the Greek word "μέγας" (megas), meaning "great" or "big".

Grammar: "μεγαλεία" is a noun in the plural form in Greek. It is the plural form of the noun "μεγαλείο" (megaleio), which means "greatness" or "grandeur".

Description: "μεγαλεία" refers to great or grand things, events, or achievements. It can also be used to describe magnificent or impressive qualities or characteristics.
