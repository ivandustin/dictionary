μεριμνάτε (merimnáte) is a Greek verb that means "to worry" or "to be concerned." Here is the breakdown of the requested information:

Grammar: μεριμνάτε (merimnáte) is the second person plural form of the verb μεριμνάω (merimnáo), which is in the present tense. It is used when addressing a group of people and means "you all worry" or "you all are concerned."

Description: μεριμνάτε (merimnáte) is a verb that expresses the action of worrying or being concerned about something. It implies a state of mental distress or anxiety caused by thoughts or concerns about a particular situation or problem.
