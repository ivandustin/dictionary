μεταμεληθήσεται (metamelithíssetai) is a Greek verb that is in the future tense and passive voice. It is derived from the root word μεταμέλομαι (metamélomai), which means "to regret" or "to feel remorse." The verb form μεταμεληθήσεται specifically means "he/she/it will be regretted" or "he/she/it will feel remorse." It is used to express the idea of someone experiencing regret or remorse for their actions or decisions.
