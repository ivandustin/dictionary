The suffix -ιζεται (-izetai) is a reflexive verb ending, indicating that the action is being performed on oneself.

- Grammar: μετασχηματιζεται (metaschēmatizetai) is a verb in the present tense, third person singular form. It is in the active voice and indicative mood.

- Description: The word μετασχηματιζεται (metaschēmatizetai) describes the action of transforming or changing one's form or shape. It implies a process of alteration or modification.
