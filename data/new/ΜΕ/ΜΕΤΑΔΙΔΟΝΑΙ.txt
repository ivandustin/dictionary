Etymology: The word "μεταδιδοναι" comes from the Greek roots "μετά" (meta), meaning "after" or "beyond," and "διδοναι" (didonai), meaning "to give." 

Grammar: "μεταδιδοναι" is a verb in the Greek language. It is in the present tense, active voice, and indicative mood. 

Description: "μεταδιδοναι" means "to transmit" or "to pass on" in Greek. It refers to the act of transferring or conveying something from one person or place to another.
