Etymology: The word "μεμφεται" comes from the Greek verb "μέμφομαι" (memphomai), which means "to blame" or "to find fault with". 

Grammar: "μεμφεται" is the third person singular form of the verb "μέμφομαι" in the present tense. It is in the indicative mood and active voice. 

Description: "μεμφεται" is a Greek word that means "he/she/it blames" or "he/she/it finds fault with". It is used to express the action of blaming or finding fault with someone or something.
