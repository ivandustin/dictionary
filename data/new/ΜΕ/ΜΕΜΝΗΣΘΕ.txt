Grammar: "μεμνησθε" is the second person plural form of the verb "μιμνήσκω" in the aorist tense. It is used in the imperative mood, which is used to give commands or make requests. In this form, it is translated as "remember" or "be mindful."

Description: "μεμνησθε" is a Greek verb that is used to urge or command someone to remember or be mindful of something. It is often used in a religious or moral context to remind individuals of their duties or obligations.
