Etymology: The word "μεγαλούς" (megaloús) comes from the Greek root "μέγας" (mégas), meaning "great" or "big".

Grammar: "Μεγαλούς" is a masculine noun in the nominative case, singular form. It is the singular form of the adjective "μεγάλος" (megálos), meaning "big" or "great".

Description: "Μεγαλούς" is used to describe something or someone that is big or great in size, quantity, importance, or significance. It can be used to describe physical objects, abstract concepts, or qualities of a person or thing.
