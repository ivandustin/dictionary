Etymology: The word "μεταδιδούς" comes from the Greek roots "μετά" (meta), meaning "after" or "beyond," and "διδούς" (didous), meaning "giving" or "granting."

Grammar: "Μεταδιδούς" is a noun in the masculine gender, singular number, and nominative case in Greek grammar.

Description: "Μεταδιδούς" refers to someone who gives or grants something after a certain event or situation. It can also be used to describe someone who passes on or transmits something to others.
