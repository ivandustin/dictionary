μεγαλυνθήσεται (megalyngthíssetai) is a Greek verb in the future tense, passive voice, third person singular form. It comes from the root word μεγαλύνω (megalyno), which means "to enlarge" or "to magnify". In grammar, it is classified as a future passive verb, indicating that the action of enlarging or magnifying will be done to the subject of the sentence.
