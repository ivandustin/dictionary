μεγαλυνθηναι (megalyntinai) is a Greek verb that means "to magnify" or "to enlarge." It is derived from the combination of the prefix μεγαλ- (megal-), meaning "great" or "big," and the verb υνθηναι (ynthinai), meaning "to stretch" or "to extend." In Greek grammar, μεγαλυνθηναι is a passive verb in the aorist tense, indicating that the action of magnifying or enlarging has been done to the subject.
