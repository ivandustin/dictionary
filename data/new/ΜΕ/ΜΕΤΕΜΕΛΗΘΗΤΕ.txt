μετεμεληθητε (metemelēthēte) is a Greek verb in the aorist tense, second person plural form. It comes from the root word μεταμέλομαι (metamelomai), which means "to regret" or "to feel remorse." The verb form μετεμεληθητε (metemelēthēte) is used to address a group of people and means "you all regretted" or "you all felt remorse." It is commonly used in the context of expressing regret or remorse for past actions or decisions.
