Etymology: The word "μεσοτοιχον" comes from the Greek words "μέσος" (mesos), meaning "middle," and "τοίχος" (toichos), meaning "wall." 

Grammar: "Μεσοτοιχον" is a noun in the neuter gender. In Greek grammar, it belongs to the second declension. 

Description: "Μεσοτοιχον" refers to a partition wall or a dividing wall that separates two spaces or rooms. It is commonly used in architecture and construction to describe a wall that is built between two areas to create separate rooms or compartments.
