Etymology: The word "μεριμναί" comes from the Greek word "μέριμνα" (merimna), which means "care" or "concern".

Grammar: "μεριμναί" is a noun in the plural form. It is the nominative plural of the noun "μέριμνα" (merimna).

Description: "μεριμναί" refers to cares, concerns, or worries. It is used to describe the state of being anxious or troubled about something.
