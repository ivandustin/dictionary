μελλοντες (melontes) is a Greek word that can be translated as "those who are about to come" or "future ones." It is the present participle of the verb "μέλλω" (mellō), which means "to be about to" or "to intend." In Greek grammar, the present participle is used to describe ongoing or future actions. The word μελλοντες can also be used as a noun to refer to people who are expected to come or events that are expected to happen in the future.
