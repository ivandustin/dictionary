Grammar: "μεινητε" is the second person plural form of the verb "μένω" (meno) in the aorist tense. It is used to address a group of people and means "you all stayed" or "you all remained".

Description: "μεινητε" is a verb form that expresses the action of staying or remaining in the past. It is commonly used in Greek to talk about actions or states that occurred and were completed in the past.
