Etymology: The word "μεγαλην" (megalen) comes from the Greek word "μέγας" (megas), meaning "great" or "big".

Grammar: "μεγαλην" is a noun in the accusative case, singular form. In Greek, nouns change their form depending on their grammatical role in a sentence.

Description: "μεγαλην" is a Greek word that means "great" or "big". It can be used to describe the size or magnitude of something.
