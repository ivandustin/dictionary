Etymology: The word "μεμερισταί" comes from the Greek word "μεμερίζω" (memerizo), which means "to divide" or "to distribute". It is derived from the root word "μέρος" (meros), meaning "part" or "portion".

Grammar: "μεμερισταί" is a noun in the plural form. It belongs to the third declension in Greek grammar.

Description: "μεμερισταί" refers to people who are divided or separated into different groups or factions. It can also be used to describe individuals who have conflicting opinions or interests.
