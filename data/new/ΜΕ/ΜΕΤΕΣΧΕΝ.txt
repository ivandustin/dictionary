Grammar: "μετεσχεν" is a verb in the aorist tense, active voice, and indicative mood. It is the third person singular form of the verb.

Description: "μετεσχεν" is a Greek word that means "he/she/it had" or "he/she/it possessed" in English. It is used to indicate past possession or ownership of something.
