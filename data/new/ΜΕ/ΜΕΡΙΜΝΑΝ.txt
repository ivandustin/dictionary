Etymology: The word "μεριμνάν" (merimnan) comes from the Greek verb "μεριμνάω" (merimnao), which means "to be anxious" or "to worry". 

Grammar: "Μεριμνάν" is the present tense, active voice, indicative mood, third person singular form of the verb "μεριμνάω" (merimnao). 

Description: "Μεριμνάν" is a Greek word that describes the action of being anxious or worrying. It is used to express the state of being concerned or troubled about something.
