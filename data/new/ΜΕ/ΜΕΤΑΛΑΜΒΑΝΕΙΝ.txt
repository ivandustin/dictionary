Grammar: "Μεταλαμβάνειν" is a verb in the Greek language. It is in the present tense, active voice, and indicative mood. It is the third person singular form of the verb.

Description: "Μεταλαμβάνειν" means "to understand" or "to comprehend" in Greek. It refers to the act of grasping or comprehending something intellectually.
