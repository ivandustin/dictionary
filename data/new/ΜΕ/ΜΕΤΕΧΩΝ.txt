Etymology: The word "μετεχων" (metechon) comes from the Greek roots "μετά" (meta), meaning "with" or "after," and "ἔχω" (echo), meaning "to have" or "to hold." 

Grammar: "Μετεχων" is a noun in the masculine gender, singular number, and nominative case. 

Description: In Greek, "μετεχων" refers to a participant or a partner in an activity or event. It can also denote someone who shares in the possession or enjoyment of something.
