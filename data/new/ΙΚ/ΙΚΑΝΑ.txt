Etymology: The word "ικανα" (ikana) comes from the Greek word "ικανός" (ikanos), which means "able" or "capable".

Grammar: "Ικανα" is the plural form of the adjective "ικανός" (ikanos), which means "able" or "capable". It is used to describe multiple things or people that possess the quality of being able or capable.

Description: "Ικανα" is an adjective in Greek that is used to describe multiple things or people that are able or capable of doing something. It indicates that the subject has the necessary skills, knowledge, or qualities to accomplish a certain task or achieve a certain goal.
