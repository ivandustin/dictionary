Grammar: "Εφυλαξα" (efylaxa) is the first person singular past tense form of the verb "φυλάσσω" (fylasso). In Greek, verbs are conjugated based on the person, number, tense, and mood. The past tense indicates that the action of guarding or protecting has already happened.

Description: "Εφυλαξα" (efylaxa) describes an action of guarding or protecting that was performed in the past. It can be used to talk about a specific instance or a series of instances where someone or something was kept safe or secure.
