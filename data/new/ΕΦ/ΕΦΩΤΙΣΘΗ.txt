Grammar: "Εφωτισθη" is the third person singular aorist passive form of the verb "φωτίζω" (fotizo). In Greek grammar, the aorist tense is used to express a completed action in the past, and the passive voice indicates that the subject of the verb is being acted upon.

Description: "Εφωτισθη" is a Greek word that translates to "was enlightened" or "was illuminated" in English. It describes a past event where someone or something received or gained knowledge, understanding, or insight.
