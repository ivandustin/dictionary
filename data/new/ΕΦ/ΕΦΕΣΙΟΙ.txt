Grammar: "Εφεσιοι" is the plural form of the noun "Εφεσιος" (Ephesios) in Greek. It is used to refer to a group of people from Ephesus.

Description: The term "Εφεσιοι" is often used in historical and archaeological contexts to describe the inhabitants of Ephesus.
