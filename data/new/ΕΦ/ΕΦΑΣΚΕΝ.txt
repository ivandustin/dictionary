Etymology: The word "εφασκεν" comes from the Greek verb "φάσκω" (phasko), which means "to say" or "to declare."

Grammar: "Εφασκεν" is the third person singular form of the aorist tense of the verb "φάσκω" (phasko). In Greek grammar, the aorist tense is used to express a completed action in the past.

Description: "Εφασκεν" is a word in the Greek language that means "he/she/it said" or "he/she/it declared." It is used to refer to a past action of speaking or declaring.
