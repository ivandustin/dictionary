Etymology: The word "εφυτευσεν" comes from the Greek verb "φυτεύω" (phyteúo), which means "to plant" or "to sow". It is derived from the noun "φυτόν" (phytón), meaning "plant".

Grammar: "εφυτευσεν" is the third person singular form of the aorist tense of the verb "φυτεύω" (phyteúo). In Greek, verbs have different forms depending on the tense, mood, voice, and person. The aorist tense is used to express a completed action in the past.

Description: "εφυτευσεν" means "he/she/it planted" in Greek. It refers to the act of putting a seed or a plant into the ground in order to grow.
