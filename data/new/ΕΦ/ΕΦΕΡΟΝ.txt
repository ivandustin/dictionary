Etymology: The word "εφερον" (ef-er-on) comes from the Greek language. It is derived from the verb "φέρω" (fero), which means "to carry" or "to bear."

Grammar: "Εφερον" is the past tense form of the verb "φέρω" (fero) in the Greek language. It is the first person singular form, meaning "I carried" or "I bore."

Description: "Εφερον" is a word used in Greek to describe an action that was carried out in the past. It indicates that the speaker or subject of the sentence performed the action of carrying or bearing something in the past.
