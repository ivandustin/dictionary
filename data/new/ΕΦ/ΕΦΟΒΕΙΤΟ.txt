Grammar: "Εφοβειτο" is the third person singular form of the verb "φοβέομαι" in the imperfect tense. In Greek, verbs have different forms depending on the person, number, tense, and mood. The imperfect tense is used to describe ongoing or repeated actions in the past.

Description: "Εφοβειτο" is a Greek word that translates to "he/she was afraid" in English. It describes a state of fear or being afraid in the past.
