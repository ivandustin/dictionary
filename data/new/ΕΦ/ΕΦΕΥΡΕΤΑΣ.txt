Etymology: The word "εφευρετας" (efevretas) comes from the Greek verb "εφευρίσκω" (efevriskó), which means "to invent" or "to discover". 

Grammar: "Εφευρετας" is a noun in the masculine gender and singular number. It belongs to the second declension in Greek grammar. 

Description: "Εφευρετας" refers to a person who invents or discovers something new. It can be used to describe someone who creates or develops new ideas, technologies, or solutions.
