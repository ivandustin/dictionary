Etymology: The word "εφθασαμεν" comes from the Greek verb "εφθάνω" (efthano), which means "to arrive" or "to reach." The suffix "-σαμεν" (-samen) indicates the past tense, indicating that the action of arriving or reaching has already happened.

Grammar: "Εφθασαμεν" is the first person plural form of the verb "εφθάνω" in the past tense. It is used to indicate that "we" (a group of people) arrived or reached a certain place or state in the past.

Description: "Εφθασαμεν" is a Greek word that expresses the action of arriving or reaching in the past tense. It is commonly used in everyday speech and writing to talk about past events or actions.
