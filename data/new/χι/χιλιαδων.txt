Word: χιλιαδων

Etymology:
The word χιλιαδων comes from the Greek word χίλιοι (chilioi), which means "thousand". The suffix -αδων (-adon) is added to indicate a large number or quantity.

χιλιαδων is a plural noun in the genitive case. It is often used to indicate a large number or quantity of something.

Description:
χιλιαδων is a Greek word that is commonly used to indicate a large number or quantity. It is often used in mathematical or scientific contexts, as well as in everyday speech. For example, one might say "χιλιαδων άνθρωποι παρακολούθησαν τη συναυλία" (thousands of people attended the concert).
