The word "αναβιβασαντες" is a Greek verb in the aorist participle form. It comes from the root verb "αναβιβαζω" which means "to lift up" or "to raise up". The prefix "ανα-" adds the meaning of "up" or "again". The suffix "-σαντες" indicates that the action was performed by a group of people in the past.

In terms of grammar, "αναβιβασαντες" is a third-person plural aorist active participle. It can function as an adjective, describing a group of people who have lifted something up or raised something up.

Overall, "αναβιβασαντες" describes a past action of a group of people lifting or raising something up.
