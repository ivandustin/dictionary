Word: ανεπληρωσαν

Etymology: The word ανεπληρωσαν is derived from the Greek verb αναπληρόω (anapliroo), which means "to fill up, complete, or fulfill." The prefix ανα- (ana-) means "up" or "again," and the root πληρόω (pliroo) means "to fill."

Grammar: ανεπληρωσαν is the third person plural aorist indicative active form of the verb αναπληρόω (anapliroo). In Greek, the aorist tense is used to describe a completed action in the past. The indicative mood indicates a statement of fact, and the active voice indicates that the subject of the sentence is performing the action.

Description: ανεπληρωσαν means "they fulfilled" or "they completed" in English. It is often used in a religious or spiritual context to describe the fulfillment of a prophecy or the completion of a task.
