The word "ανακαμψει" (anakampsei) is a Greek verb in the future tense, which means "to recover" or "to bounce back". 

Etymology: The word is derived from the prefix "ανα-" (ana-), which means "again" or "back", and the verb "καμπτω" (kampto), which means "to bend" or "to curve". 

Grammar: "ανακαμψει" is the third person singular future tense form of the verb. In Greek, verbs have different forms depending on the tense, person, and number. 

Description: The word is often used in the context of health or economics, to describe a situation where someone or something is recovering from a setback or crisis. For example, "Ο ασθενής ανακάμπτει από την ασθένεια του" (The patient is recovering from his illness) or "Η οικονομία ανακάμπτει από την κρίση" (The economy is bouncing back from the crisis).
