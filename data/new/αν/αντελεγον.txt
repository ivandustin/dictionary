The word "αντελεγον" (antilegon) is a Greek verb that comes from the prefix "anti-" meaning "against" and the verb "lego" meaning "to speak". Therefore, "αντελεγον" means "to argue against" or "to contradict". 

In terms of grammar, "αντελεγον" is a present tense verb in the third person plural form. It can also be conjugated in other tenses and persons, such as "αντιλέγω" (antilego) in the first person singular present tense.

Overall, "αντελεγον" is a word that describes the act of opposing or disputing someone's argument or statement.
