The word "αναπεσε" is a Greek verb that means "to sit down" or "to recline". It is derived from the prefix "ανα-" which means "up" or "again", and the verb "πεσω" which means "to fall". In terms of grammar, "αναπεσε" is a second person singular aorist imperative form, which means it is a command directed towards one person to sit down or recline.
