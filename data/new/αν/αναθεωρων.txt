Word: αναθεωρων

Grammar: αναθεωρων is a present participle in the active voice, masculine gender, and singular number. It is derived from the verb αναθεωρεω (anatheoreo), which means "to review" or "to reconsider."

Description: αναθεωρων refers to someone who is in the act of reviewing or reconsidering something.
