The word "ανακαινωσεως" (anakainoseos) is a Greek noun that means "renewal" or "renovation". It is derived from the verb "ανακαινόω" (anakainoo), which means "to renew" or "to renovate". 

In terms of grammar, "ανακαινωσεως" is a genitive singular noun, which means it is used to indicate possession or relationship to another noun or pronoun. 

Overall, "ανακαινωσεως" refers to the act of making something new again, whether it be a physical object or a concept. It is often used in a spiritual or religious context to refer to the renewal of one's faith or beliefs.
