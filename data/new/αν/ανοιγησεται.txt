The word "ανοιγησεται" is a verb in the Greek language. It is the third person singular form of the verb "ανοίγω" (anoigo), which means "to open". The word is derived from the ancient Greek word "ἀνοίγω" (anoigo), which has the same meaning. 

In terms of grammar, "ανοιγησεται" is in the present tense and passive voice. It is conjugated from the root verb "ανοίγω" (anoigo) by adding the suffix "-η-" and the personal ending "-ται" for the third person singular. 

Example usage: Η πόρτα ανοιγήσεται αυτόματα. (The door opens automatically.)
