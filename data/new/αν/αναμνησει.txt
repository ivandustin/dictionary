The word "αναμνησει" (anamnisi) is a Greek verb in the future tense, which means "to remember" or "to recall". 

Etymology: The word is derived from the Greek root "μνημονεύω" (mnimonevo), which means "to remember". The prefix "ανα-" (ana-) in Greek means "again" or "back", so "αναμνησει" literally means "to remember again" or "to recall".

Grammar: "αναμνησει" is a first-person singular future tense verb. In Greek, verbs change their endings to indicate the tense, person, and number. The future tense is used to describe actions that will happen in the future.

Description: "αναμνησει" is a common verb in Greek and is used in everyday conversation. It is often used to express nostalgia or to recall past memories. For example, "Θα αναμνησω τις καλοκαιρινες διακοπες μου" (Tha anamniso tis kalokairines diakopes mou) means "I will remember my summer vacations".
