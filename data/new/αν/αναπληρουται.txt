Word: αναπληρούται

Etymology: The word αναπληρούται comes from the Greek verb αναπληρόω (anapliroo), which means "to fill up again" or "to replenish". It is derived from the prefix ανα- (ana-), meaning "again" or "re-", and the verb πληρόω (pliroo), meaning "to fill" or "to complete".

Grammar: αναπληρούται is a verb in the present tense, third person singular form. It is conjugated from the verb αναπληρόω (anapliroo), which is a regular verb in Greek.

Description: αναπληρούται means "is being replenished" or "is being filled up again". It is often used to describe the process of refilling or restocking something that has been depleted or emptied. For example, one might say "Η βιτρίνα αναπληρώνεται με νέα προϊόντα" (The display case is being replenished with new products).
