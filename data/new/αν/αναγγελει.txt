Word: αναγγελει

Etymology:
The word αναγγελει (anangelei) is derived from the Greek verb αναγγέλλω (anangello), which means "to announce" or "to report". It is composed of the prefix ανα- (ana-), which means "up" or "again", and the verb γγέλλω (gello), which means "to tell" or "to declare".

Grammar:
αναγγελει (anangelei) is a verb in the present tense, third person singular form. It is conjugated from the verb αναγγέλλω (anangello), which is a regular verb in Greek.

Description:
The word αναγγελει (anangelei) means "he/she/it announces" or "he/she/it reports". It is often used in formal or official contexts, such as in news reports, legal documents, or official statements. The verb can also be used in a more general sense to mean "to declare" or "to proclaim".
