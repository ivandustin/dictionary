The word "αναγνωσθηι" (anagnōsthēi) is a Greek verb in the aorist passive tense, which means "it was read" or "it has been read." 

Etymology: The word is derived from the Greek verb "αναγινώσκω" (anaginōskō), which means "to read."

Grammar: "αναγνωσθηι" is in the aorist passive tense, which indicates that the action of reading has already been completed and the subject of the sentence received the action. The verb is in the third person singular form.

Description: The word "αναγνωσθηι" is often used in Greek literature and texts to indicate that a particular text or passage has been read. It can also be used in a more general sense to indicate that something has been understood or comprehended.
