The word "ανομίας" (anomias) is a Greek noun that means "lawlessness" or "violation of the law". It is derived from the Greek word "ανόμος" (anomos), which means "lawless" or "without law". In terms of grammar, "ανομίας" is a feminine noun in the genitive case. It can be used to describe a state of disorder or chaos, or to refer to specific acts of disobedience or rebellion against established laws or norms.
