Word: αναφέρει (anaféri)

Etymology: ανα- (ana-) means "up" or "again", and φέρω (féro) means "to carry". Therefore, αναφέρει (anaféri) means "to carry up" or "to report".

Grammar: αναφέρει (anaféri) is a verb in the present tense, third person singular. It is derived from the verb αναφέρω (anaféro), which means "to report" or "to mention". 

Description: αναφέρει (anaféri) is a common Greek verb that is used to indicate the act of reporting or mentioning something. It can be used in various contexts, such as in news reports, academic papers, or everyday conversations. It is often used with the preposition για (gia), which means "about", to indicate the topic of the report or mention.
