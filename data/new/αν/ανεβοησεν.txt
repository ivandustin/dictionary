Word: ανεβοησεν

Etymology: ανεβοησεν is a Greek word that comes from the verb αναβοάω (anaboao), which means "to shout aloud" or "to cry out."

Grammar: ανεβοησεν is the third person singular aorist active indicative form of the verb αναβοάω (anaboao). In this form, it means "he/she/it shouted aloud" or "he/she/it cried out."

Description: ανεβοησεν is a verb that describes a loud and forceful vocal expression. It can be used to describe someone shouting in anger, fear, excitement, or any other strong emotion. The word is often used in literature and poetry to create a sense of drama or intensity.
