Word: ανεθρεψατο

Etymology: The word ανεθρεψατο is a Greek verb that comes from the prefix αν- (meaning "not" or "un-") and the verb εθρεψατο (meaning "was raised" or "was brought up"). 

Grammar: ανεθρεψατο is a third-person singular aorist passive indicative verb in Greek. It is formed by adding the prefix αν- to the verb εθρεψατο, which is in the aorist tense, passive voice, and indicative mood. 

Description: The word ανεθρεψατο means "was not raised" or "was not brought up" in Greek. It is often used to describe someone who did not have a proper upbringing or was not raised in a certain way.
