Grammar: "Ανταναπληρώνω" is a regular verb in Greek, belonging to the first conjugation. It is conjugated in all tenses and moods, and its stem is "ανταναπληρών-". 

Description: The verb "ανταναπληρώνω" is commonly used in Greek to express the idea of replacing something or someone with another thing or person.
