Word: αντιλημψεις

Etymology: The word αντιλημψεις (antilimpi̱seis) is derived from the Greek word αντίληψη (antíli̱psi̱), which means perception or apprehension.

Grammar: αντιλημψεις is a plural noun in Greek. It is often used in the context of psychology and refers to perceptions or impressions that a person has about something.

Description: αντιλημψεις can be translated to mean perceptions, impressions, or even assumptions. It is often used in the context of psychology to describe the way that a person perceives or understands something. In everyday language, it can also refer to a person's preconceived notions or biases about a particular topic or situation.
