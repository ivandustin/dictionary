Word: ανακριναντες

Etymology:
The word ανακριναντες is derived from the Greek verb ανακρίνω (anakríno), which means "to examine" or "to investigate". The suffix -αντες (-antes) is a participle ending that indicates the present active participle in Greek.

Grammar:
ανακριναντες is the present active participle of the verb ανακρίνω (anakríno) in Greek. It is in the masculine gender, plural number, and nominative case. 

Description:
The word ανακριναντες refers to a group of people who are currently examining or investigating something. It can be translated to English as "examining" or "investigating". The word is often used in legal contexts, such as in court proceedings or police investigations.
