The word "αναβλεψαντος" (anablepsantos) is a Greek verb in the aorist tense, which means it refers to a completed action in the past. It is derived from the root verb "βλέπω" (blepo), which means "to see". The prefix "ανα" (ana) in this case means "again" or "back".

Therefore, "αναβλεψαντος" can be translated as "having looked back" or "having seen again". It is often used in a metaphorical sense to describe someone who has reconsidered a situation or changed their perspective on something. 

In terms of grammar, "αναβλεψαντος" is a participle, which means it functions as an adjective or adverb in a sentence. It agrees in gender, number, and case with the noun it modifies. 

Example sentence: "Αναβλεψαντος στο παρελθόν, αποφάσισε να αλλάξει τη ζωή του." (Anablepsantos sto parelthon, apofasisi na allaxei ti zoi tou.) - "Having looked back in the past, he decided to change his life."
