The word "αναστα" (anasta) is a Greek verb that means "to rise up" or "to stand up". It comes from the Greek word "ανασταίνω" (anastaino), which means "to raise up" or "to lift up". 

In terms of grammar, "αναστα" is a second person singular imperative form of the verb "ανασταίνω". This means that it is a command directed towards one person, telling them to rise up or stand up. 

Overall, "αναστα" is a simple and straightforward word that is commonly used in everyday Greek language.
