Word: ανακρινει

Etymology: The word ανακρινει comes from the Greek verb ανακρίνω (anakríno), which means "to examine" or "to investigate". It is derived from the prefix ανα- (ana-), meaning "up" or "back", and the verb κρίνω (kríno), meaning "to judge" or "to decide".

Grammar: ανακρινει is the third person singular present tense form of the verb ανακρίνω (anakríno). It is used to describe an ongoing action of examining or investigating in the present time. The verb is conjugated as follows:

- Singular: ανακρίνω (anakríno) - I examine, you examine, he/she/it examines
- Plural: ανακρίνουμε (anakrínoyme) - we examine, you (plural) examine, they examine

Description: ανακρινει is a Greek verb that describes the act of examining or investigating something or someone. It can be used in a variety of contexts, such as in legal proceedings, scientific research, or medical diagnoses. The verb implies a thorough and systematic approach to gathering information and evaluating evidence.
