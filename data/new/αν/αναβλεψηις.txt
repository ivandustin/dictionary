The word "αναβλεψηις" (anablepsis) is a Greek noun that means "a looking up" or "a recovery of sight." It is derived from the Greek words "ανα" (ana), meaning "up" or "again," and "βλεπω" (blepo), meaning "to see." In grammar, it is a feminine noun in the nominative case. It is often used in a figurative sense to describe a spiritual or intellectual awakening.
