Word: ανεξεραυνητα

Etymology: The word ανεξεραυνητα comes from the Greek prefix αν- (an-), meaning "not," and the Greek word εξεραυνητος (exeraunitos), meaning "penetrable by lightning." Therefore, ανεξεραυνητα means "impenetrable by lightning" or "invincible."

Grammar: ανεξεραυνητα is an adjective in the feminine gender, singular form. It is used to describe something that cannot be penetrated or defeated.

Description: ανεξεραυνητα is a powerful and evocative word that conveys a sense of strength and invincibility. It can be used to describe a person, object, or situation that is impervious to harm or defeat. For example, one might use this word to describe a fortress that is impregnable, or a person who is unbreakable in their resolve. Overall, ανεξεραυνητα is a word that inspires confidence and resilience.
