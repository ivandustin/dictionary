Word: αναγγειλαι

Etymology:
The word αναγγειλαι is a verb in the Greek language. It is derived from the prefix ανα- (ana-), which means "up" or "again," and the verb γγελλω (angello), which means "to announce" or "to report." Therefore, αναγγειλαι means "to announce again" or "to report again."

Grammar:
αναγγειλαι is a second-person singular aorist active imperative verb in the Greek language. It is used to give a command or instruction to someone to announce or report something again.

Description:
αναγγειλαι is a verb that is used to instruct someone to announce or report something again. It is often used in situations where the initial announcement or report was not heard or understood properly, or where there is a need to repeat the information for emphasis. The verb is commonly used in formal settings, such as in business or academic contexts, where clear communication is essential.
