Word: αναλυσεως

Etymology: The word αναλυσεως comes from the Greek verb αναλύω (analyo), which means "to break up" or "to analyze". The suffix -σις (-sis) is added to the verb stem to form the noun αναλυσεως, which means "analysis" or "dissolution".

Grammar: αναλυσεως is a feminine noun in the genitive case, which means it is used to show possession or relationship to another noun or pronoun in a sentence. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their grammatical function in a sentence, such as subject, object, or possessive.

Description: αναλυσεως refers to the process of breaking down or analyzing something into its component parts. It can be used in a variety of contexts, such as scientific analysis, literary analysis, or financial analysis. The word implies a careful and systematic examination of something in order to understand it better.
