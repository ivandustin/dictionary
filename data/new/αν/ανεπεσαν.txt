Word: ανεπεσαν

Grammar: ανεπεσαν is the third person plural aorist indicative active form of the verb επίσταμαι. In Greek, the aorist tense is used to describe a past action that is completed, while the indicative mood indicates a statement of fact. The active voice means that the subject of the sentence is performing the action.

Description: ανεπεσαν is a verb that describes an action of not sitting upon or not being seated. It can be used in various contexts, such as describing a group of people who refused to sit down or a situation where there were no available seats.
