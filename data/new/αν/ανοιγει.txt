Word: ανοιγει

Etymology: ανοιγει comes from the Greek verb ανοίγω (anoígo), which means "to open".

Grammar: ανοιγει is the present tense, third person singular form of the verb ανοίγω (anoígo). It is used to describe an action that is currently happening in the present.

Description: ανοιγει is a Greek verb that means "he/she/it opens". It can be used to describe the action of opening a door, a window, a book, or any other object that can be opened. It is a common verb in everyday Greek conversation and is used in a variety of contexts.
