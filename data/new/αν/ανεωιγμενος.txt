The word "ανεωιγμενος" (aneoigmenos) is a Greek adjective that means "opened" or "unsealed". It is derived from the verb "ανοίγω" (anoigo), which means "to open". In terms of grammar, it is a participle in the passive voice, masculine gender, and nominative case. It can be used to describe a variety of things that have been opened or unsealed, such as a letter, a door, or a container.
