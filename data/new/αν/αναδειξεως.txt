In terms of grammar, "αναδειξεως" is a genitive singular noun, which means it is used to indicate possession or relationship to another noun or pronoun. It can be translated as "of showing" or "of demonstration".

Overall, "αναδειξεως" is a word that emphasizes the importance of showcasing or demonstrating something, whether it be a skill, a product, or an idea.
