The word "ανυποκριτου" (anypokritou) is a Greek adjective that means "sincere" or "unfeigned". 

Etymology: The word is derived from the Greek prefix "αν-" (an-), meaning "not", and the word "υποκριτής" (ypokritis), meaning "hypocrite". 

Grammar: "ανυποκριτου" is in the masculine genitive singular form. 

Description: The word is often used to describe someone who is honest and genuine in their actions and words, without any hidden motives or insincerity. It can also be used to describe something that is authentic or true, without any pretense or deception.
