The word "αναγνωναι" (anagnonai) is a Greek verb in the present tense, which means "to read". 

Etymology: The word is derived from the Greek roots "ana-" meaning "again" or "back" and "gnosis" meaning "knowledge". 

Grammar: "αναγνωναι" is a second person singular form of the present tense, which means "you read". It is also used in the third person singular form "αναγνωνει" (anagnonei) which means "he/she/it reads". 

Description: The word "αναγνωναι" is a common verb in the Greek language and is used in various contexts such as reading books, newspapers, or any written material. It is also used in a figurative sense, meaning to understand or comprehend something.
