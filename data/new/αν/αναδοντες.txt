Word: αναδοντες

Etymology: The word αναδοντες comes from the Greek verb αναδίδωμι (anadidomi), which means "to give back" or "to return". The suffix -της (-tes) is added to the verb stem to form the noun αναδοντες, which means "those who give back" or "those who return".

Grammar: αναδοντες is a plural noun in the nominative case. It is declined as follows: αναδοντες (nominative), αναδοντων (genitive), αναδοντες (accusative), αναδοντες (vocative).

Description: αναδοντες refers to people who give back or return something. The context in which the word is used will determine what is being given back or returned. For example, αναδοντες could refer to people who return lost items to their rightful owners, or to people who give back something that was borrowed. The word can also be used metaphorically to refer to people who restore or bring back something that was lost or forgotten.
