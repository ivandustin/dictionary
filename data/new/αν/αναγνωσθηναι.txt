Word: αναγνωσθηναι

Etymology: αναγνωσθηναι is a Greek word that comes from the verb αναγινώσκω (anaginósko), which means "to read."

Grammar: αναγνωσθηναι is a passive aorist infinitive form of the verb αναγινώσκω (anaginósko). In Greek, the aorist tense is used to describe a past action that is viewed as a single event, while the passive voice indicates that the subject of the verb is being acted upon.

Description: αναγνωσθηναι can be translated as "to be read" or "to have been read." It is often used in Greek literature to describe the act of reading or being read, such as in the context of reading a book or a letter.
