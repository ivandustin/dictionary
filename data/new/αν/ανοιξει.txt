Word: ανοιξει

Etymology: ανοιξει is a verb in the Greek language, which comes from the root word "ανοίγω" (anoigo) meaning "to open".

Grammar: ανοιξει is a third person singular future tense verb in the Greek language. It is conjugated from the root verb "ανοίγω" (anoigo) and means "he/she/it will open".

Description: ανοιξει is a common verb in the Greek language, used to describe the action of opening something. It can be used in a variety of contexts, such as opening a door, a window, a book, or a package.
