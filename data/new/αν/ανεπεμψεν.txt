The word "ανεπεμψεν" (anepempsen) is a Greek verb in the aorist tense, third person singular form. It comes from the root word "πεμπω" (pempo), which means "to send". The prefix "αν" (an) in this case indicates a completed action in the past.

In terms of grammar, "ανεπεμψεν" is a transitive verb, meaning it requires a direct object. It can be translated to English as "he/she/it sent".

Overall, "ανεπεμψεν" is a specific instance of the action of sending, completed in the past.
