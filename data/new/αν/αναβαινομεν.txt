Word: αναβαινομεν (anabainomen)

Grammar: αναβαινομεν is a present participle in the active voice, masculine gender, and plural number. It is formed by adding the suffix -μεν (-men) to the present stem αναβαιν- (anabain-).

It can be used in a literal sense, such as climbing stairs or a mountain, or in a figurative sense, such as rising in status or importance.
