Word: αναπαυεται

Etymology: αναπαυεται comes from the Greek verb αναπαύομαι (anapavomai), which means "to rest" or "to take a break". The word is derived from the prefix ανα- (ana-), which means "up" or "again", and the verb παύω (pavo), which means "to stop" or "to cease".

Grammar: αναπαυεται is a third-person singular present indicative form of the verb αναπαύομαι (anapavomai). It is in the active voice and indicative mood. The verb is conjugated in the present tense, indicating that the action is happening now.

Description: αναπαυεται means "he/she/it rests" or "he/she/it takes a break" in Greek. It is a common verb used to describe the act of resting or taking a break from activity. The word can be used in a variety of contexts, such as describing a person taking a nap, a worker taking a break from work, or a traveler stopping to rest during a journey.
