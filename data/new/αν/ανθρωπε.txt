The word "ανθρωπε" (anthrope) is a Greek noun that means "human being" or "person". It is derived from the Greek word "άνθρωπος" (anthropos), which also means "human being". In terms of grammar, "ανθρωπε" is in the vocative case, which is used to address someone directly. Therefore, it is often translated as "O human" or "Hey, person".
