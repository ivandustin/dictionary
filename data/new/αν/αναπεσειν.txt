The word "αναπεσειν" (anapesin) is a Greek verb that means "to sit up" or "to rise up from a lying position". It is derived from the prefix "ανα-" (ana-), which means "up" or "again", and the verb "πεσνυω" (pesnyo), which means "to lie down". In terms of grammar, "αναπεσειν" is an infinitive verb form, which means it is not conjugated to match a specific subject or tense.
