The word "ανομους" (anomous) is a Greek adjective that means "lawless" or "without law". It is derived from the Greek word "ανομία" (anomia), which means "lawlessness" or "lack of law". In terms of grammar, "ανομους" is in the masculine plural form, and it is used to describe a group of people who are not following the law or are acting in a lawless manner.
