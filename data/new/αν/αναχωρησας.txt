Word: αναχωρησας

Etymology:
The word αναχωρησας is a Greek verb that comes from the root word αναχωρέω (anachoreo), which means "to withdraw" or "to depart." The verb is in the aorist tense, which indicates a completed action in the past.

Grammar:
αναχωρησας is the second person singular aorist active indicative form of the verb αναχωρέω. In this form, it means "you withdrew" or "you departed." The verb is in the aorist tense, which indicates a completed action in the past.

Description:
αναχωρησας is a Greek verb that describes the act of withdrawing or departing.
