In terms of grammar, "αναβλεψασαι" is in the aorist tense, which is used to describe a completed action in the past. It is also in the second person singular form, which means it is addressing one person.

Overall, "αναβλεψασαι" is a verb that describes the act of looking back or seeing something again in the past.
