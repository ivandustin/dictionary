Word: αναζητησαι

Etymology: αναζητησαι is a Greek verb that comes from the prefix ανα- (ana-), meaning "up" or "again," and the verb ζητώ (zito), meaning "to seek" or "to search." Therefore, αναζητησαι means "to search again" or "to seek anew."

Grammar: αναζητησαι is a second-person singular aorist active imperative verb in Greek. It is used to give a command or instruction to someone to search again or seek anew.

Description: αναζητησαι is a verb that is often used in Greek to encourage someone to keep searching or seeking for something that they have lost or are looking for. It can also be used metaphorically to encourage someone to keep trying or to not give up in their pursuit of a goal or objective.
