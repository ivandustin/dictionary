Word: αναβαινοντας

Etymology: αναβαινοντας is a Greek verb that comes from the combination of the prefix ανα- (ana-), which means "up" or "again," and the verb βαίνω (vaino), which means "to walk" or "to step." Therefore, αναβαινοντας means "to go up" or "to ascend."

Grammar: αναβαινοντας is the present participle form of the verb αναβαίνω (anavaino), which means "to ascend" or "to climb." It is used to describe an ongoing action of going up or climbing. In Greek, the present participle form is formed by adding the suffix -ντας (-ntas) to the verb stem.

Description: αναβαινοντας is a word that describes the action of going up or climbing. It can be used in various contexts, such as describing a person climbing a mountain or stairs, or an object being lifted up. It is a present participle form, indicating an ongoing action, and is commonly used in Greek language and literature.
