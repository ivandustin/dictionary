The word "ανεβλεψεν" (aneblepsen) is a Greek verb that comes from the combination of two words: "ανα" (ana) meaning "up" or "again" and "βλεπω" (blepo) meaning "to see". Therefore, "ανεβλεψεν" can be translated as "to look up" or "to see again".
