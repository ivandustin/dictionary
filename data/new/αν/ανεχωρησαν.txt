Word: ανεχωρησαν

Etymology: The word ανεχωρησαν is derived from the Greek verb αναχωρώ (anachoró), which means "to withdraw" or "to retreat."

Grammar: ανεχωρησαν is the third person plural aorist indicative active form of the verb αναχωρώ (anachoró). In Greek, the aorist tense is used to describe a past action that is viewed as a single event, without any reference to its duration or repetition.

Description: ανεχωρησαν is a Greek word that means "they withdrew" or "they retreated." It is often used in military contexts to describe the movement of troops or armies. The word can also be used in a more general sense to describe any situation where people or things are moving away from a particular location or situation.
