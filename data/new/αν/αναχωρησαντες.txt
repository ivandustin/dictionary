The word "αναχωρησαντες" is a Greek verb in the past participle form, which means "departed" or "withdrawn". 

Etymology: The word is derived from the Greek verb "αναχωρώ" (anachoró), which means "to withdraw" or "to retreat". The suffix "-σαντες" (-santes) is added to the verb stem to form the past participle.

Grammar: The word is in the masculine plural form, indicating that the subject of the verb is a group of males. It is also in the aorist tense, which indicates a completed action in the past.

Description: "αναχωρησαντες" is a verb that describes an action of a group of males withdrawing or departing from a place. It can be used in various contexts, such as military operations, travel, or simply leaving a location.
