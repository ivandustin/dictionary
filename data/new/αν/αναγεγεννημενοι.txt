Word: αναγεγεννημένοι

Etymology: The word αναγεγεννημένοι comes from the Greek verb αναγεννιέμαι (anagenniémai), which means "to be born again" or "to be regenerated". The prefix ανα- (ana-) means "again" or "anew", while the root word γεννιέμαι (genniémai) means "to be born".

Grammar: αναγεγεννημένοι is the plural form of the adjective αναγεγεννημένος (anagennimenos), which means "regenerated" or "born again". It is a compound word, consisting of the prefix ανα- (ana-) and the verb γεννιέμαι (genniémai).

Description: αναγεγεννημένοι is a term used in Christian theology to describe those who have been spiritually reborn or regenerated through faith in Jesus Christ. It is often used to refer to the process of conversion or the experience of being "born again" in Christ. In a broader sense, the term can also be used to describe any kind of renewal or revival, whether spiritual, cultural, or social.
