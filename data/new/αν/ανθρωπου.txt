The word "ανθρωπου" (anthropou) is a Greek noun that means "of a human being" or "belonging to a human being." It is derived from the Greek word "άνθρωπος" (anthropos), which means "human being" or "man." 

In terms of grammar, "ανθρωπου" is in the genitive case, which is used to show possession or relationship. It is often used in phrases such as "του ανθρώπου" (tou anthropou), which means "of the human being" or "belonging to the human being."

Overall, "ανθρωπου" is a versatile word that can be used in a variety of contexts to describe something that is related to or belongs to a human being.
