Word: ανεπαισχυντον

Etymology:
The word ανεπαισχυντον (anepaischunton) is derived from the Greek words αν- (an-) meaning "not" and επαισχυνομαι (epaischunomai) meaning "to be ashamed". Therefore, the word ανεπαισχυντον means "unashamed" or "shameless".

Grammar:
ανεπαισχυντον is an adjective in the neuter gender, singular form. In Greek, adjectives agree in gender, number, and case with the noun they modify. 

Description:
The word ανεπαισχυντον is used to describe someone who is not ashamed or embarrassed about their actions or behavior. It can also be used to describe something that is shameless or audacious. The word has a negative connotation and is often used to criticize someone's behavior.
