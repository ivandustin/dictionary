Word: αναγινωσκεις

Etymology: αναγινωσκεις is a Greek word that comes from the verb αναγινώσκω (anaginósko), which means "to read."

Grammar: αναγινωσκεις is the second person singular present indicative active form of the verb αναγινώσκω (anaginósko). It is used to address someone directly and means "you are reading."

Description: αναγινωσκεις is a common Greek verb that is used in everyday conversation and writing. It is often used to ask someone if they are reading something or to describe someone who is currently reading.
