Word: ανακρινοντες

Etymology:
The word ανακρινοντες is derived from the Greek verb ανακρίνω (anakríno), which means "to examine" or "to investigate". The suffix -οντες (-ontes) is a present participle ending, indicating that the word refers to people who are currently performing the action of examining or investigating.

Grammar:
ανακρινοντες is a present participle in the masculine plural form. In Greek, present participles are formed by adding the suffix -ων (-on) to the stem of the verb. The stem of ανακρινοντες is ανακριν-, which is the same as the stem of the verb ανακρίνω.

Description:
ανακρινοντες refers to a group of people who are currently examining or investigating something. The word can be used in a variety of contexts, such as legal proceedings, scientific research, or journalistic investigations. The present participle form emphasizes the ongoing nature of the action, suggesting that the examination or investigation is still in progress.
