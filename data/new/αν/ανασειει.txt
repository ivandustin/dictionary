The word "ανασειει" (anaseiei) is a verb in the Greek language. 

Etymology: The word is derived from the root "σείω" (seio), which means "to shake". The prefix "ανα-" (ana-) in this case means "up" or "again". Therefore, "ανασειει" can be translated as "to shake up again".

Grammar: The word is in the present tense, third person singular form. It is an active verb, meaning that the subject of the sentence is performing the action of shaking up again.

Description: The word "ανασειει" can be used in various contexts, but it generally refers to the act of shaking something up again. It can be used literally, such as shaking a bottle to mix its contents, or figuratively, such as stirring up emotions or causing a commotion.
