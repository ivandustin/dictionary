Word: αναγνωσιν

Etymology: αναγνωσιν is a Greek word that comes from the verb αναγινώσκω (anaginósko), which means "to read."

Grammar: αναγνωσιν is a noun in the accusative case, which means it is the direct object of a verb. It is a feminine noun and is singular.

Description: αναγνωσιν refers to the act of reading or the act of examining a written text. It can also refer to a reading or a passage from a book or other written work. In some contexts, it can also refer to the act of interpreting or understanding a text.
