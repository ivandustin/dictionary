Word: αναγκαίους

Etymology: The word αναγκαίους comes from the Greek word ανάγκη (anagki), meaning "necessity" or "need". The suffix -ους (-ous) is added to form the adjective, meaning "necessary" or "required".

Grammar: αναγκαίους is an adjective in the masculine plural form. It agrees with the noun it modifies in gender, number, and case.

Description: αναγκαίους is used to describe something that is necessary or required. It can be used to describe a person, thing, or situation. For example, "Οι αναγκαίοι όροι για την επιτυχία είναι η προσπάθεια και η αφοσίωση" (The necessary conditions for success are effort and dedication).
