Word: αναγινωσκομενας

Etymology: αναγινωσκομενας is a Greek word that comes from the verb αναγινώσκω (anaginósko), which means "to read."

Grammar: αναγινωσκομενας is the present participle form of the verb αναγινώσκω (anaginósko), which means "reading" or "being read." It is in the masculine, plural, accusative case.

Description: αναγινωσκομενας is a word that describes something that is currently being read or that has been read. It can refer to a book, a document, or any other written material.
