The word "αντιβαλλετε" is a Greek verb that means "to oppose" or "to resist". 

Etymology: The word is derived from the Greek prefix "αντι-" which means "against" or "opposite", and the verb "βάλλω" which means "to throw" or "to cast".

Grammar: "αντιβαλλετε" is a second person plural present active imperative verb. It is used to give a command or instruction to a group of people to oppose or resist something.
