Word: ανηλθον

Etymology:
The word ανηλθον is a Greek verb that comes from the root word αναβαίνω (anabaino), which means "to go up" or "to ascend". The prefix ανα (ana) means "up" or "again", and the verb βαίνω (baino) means "to walk" or "to go".

Grammar:
ανηλθον is the aorist tense of the Greek verb αναβαίνω (anabaino), which means "I went up" or "I ascended". The aorist tense is used to describe a completed action in the past.

ανηλθον is a verb that describes the action of going up or ascending.
