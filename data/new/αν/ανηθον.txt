The word "ανηθον" (anethon) is a Greek noun that refers to the plant known in English as dill. 

Etymology: The word comes from the ancient Greek word "ἄνηθον" (ánēthon), which also means dill. 

Grammar: In Greek, "ανηθον" is a neuter noun of the second declension.
