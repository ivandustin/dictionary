Word: αναβαθμων

Etymology:
The word αναβαθμων comes from the Greek word αναβαθμός (anabathmos), which means "a step, a stair". The suffix -ων (-on) is added to form the plural.

Grammar:
αναβαθμων is a masculine noun in the plural form. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their gender, number, and case. The plural form indicates that there are multiple steps or stairs.

Description:
αναβαθμων refers to a series of steps or stairs that are used to climb up or down. It can be used to describe a staircase in a building, a set of steps in a park, or any other structure that is used for climbing. The word can also be used metaphorically to describe a series of steps or stages in a process or progression.
