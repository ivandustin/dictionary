The word "ανεβημέν" (anevimen) is a Greek verb in the past participle form, which means "ascended" or "climbed up". 

Etymology: The word is derived from the Greek verb "ανεβαίνω" (anevaino), which means "to go up" or "to ascend". The suffix "-μέν" (-men) is added to the stem of the verb to form the past participle.

Grammar: "ανεβημέν" is a past participle in the masculine singular form. It agrees in gender, number, and case with the noun or pronoun it modifies. For example, "ο ανεβημένος" (o anevimenos) means "the ascended (masculine singular)".

Description: The word "ανεβημέν" is commonly used in Greek to describe someone or something that has climbed up or ascended to a higher place. It can also be used metaphorically to describe someone who has achieved a higher level of success or status.
