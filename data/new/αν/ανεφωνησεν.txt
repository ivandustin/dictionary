Word: ανεφωνησεν

Etymology: ανεφωνησεν is a Greek word that comes from the verb αναφωνέω (anaphonéo), which means "to cry out" or "to shout".

Grammar: ανεφωνησεν is the aorist tense of the verb αναφωνέω (anaphonéo), which means that it refers to a completed action in the past. It is in the third person singular form.

Description: ανεφωνησεν is a word that describes a sudden and loud cry or shout. It can be used to express surprise, excitement, fear, or any other strong emotion. It is often used in literature and poetry to create a dramatic effect.
