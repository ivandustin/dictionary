Word: αναγνοντες

Etymology: αναγνοντες is a Greek word that comes from the verb αναγιγνώσκω (anagignosko), which means "to read aloud" or "to recognize". The word is formed by adding the suffix -τες (-tes) to the present participle stem αναγνων- (anagnon-).

Grammar: αναγνοντες is the present participle of the verb αναγιγνώσκω (anagignosko), which means "reading" or "recognizing". It is in the masculine plural form, indicating that the subject is a group of males who are currently reading or recognizing something.

Description: αναγνοντες is a word that describes a group of people who are actively engaged in the act of reading or recognizing something. It can be used to refer to a group of scholars studying a text, a group of people reading a book together, or any other situation where a group of people are engaged in the act of reading or recognizing something.
