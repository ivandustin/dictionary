The word "αντι" (anti) is a Greek prefix that means "against" or "opposite". It is commonly used in English words such as "antibiotic" (against bacteria) and "antithesis" (opposite or contrasting idea). 

In terms of grammar, "αντι" is a prefix that is added to the beginning of a word to change its meaning. It is not a standalone word and cannot be used on its own in a sentence.

Overall, "αντι" is a versatile prefix that can be used in a variety of contexts to convey opposition or contrast.
