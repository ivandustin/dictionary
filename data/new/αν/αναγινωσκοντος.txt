Word: αναγινωσκοντος

Etymology: αναγινωσκοντος is a present participle form of the Greek verb αναγινώσκω (anaginósko), which means "to read." The word is derived from the prefix ανα- (ana-), which means "again" or "back," and the verb γινώσκω (ginósko), which means "to know."

Grammar: αναγινωσκοντος is a present participle in the genitive case, masculine gender, and singular number. It is used to describe the subject of a sentence as "reading" or "while reading."

Description: αναγινωσκοντος can be translated as "of the one who is reading" or "while he is reading." It is often used in Greek literature to describe a character who is engaged in reading a book or a text. The word can also be used in a more general sense to describe someone who is studying or learning something.
