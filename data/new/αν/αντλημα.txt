Word: αντλημα (antlima)

Etymology: The word αντλημα comes from the ancient Greek verb αντλέω (antleo), which means "to draw water". 

Grammar: αντλημα is a neuter noun in Greek. Its plural form is αντλήματα (antlimata). 

Description: αντλημα refers to a device or machine used for pumping liquids, such as water or oil, from one place to another. It can also refer to the act of pumping or drawing water. αντληματικός (antlimatikos) is an adjective used to describe something related to pumping or drawing water.
