Word: αναζητούντες

Etymology: The word αναζητούντες comes from the Greek verb αναζητώ (anazito), which means "to search for" or "to seek". The suffix -ούντες (-ountes) is added to the verb stem to form the present participle, which means "searching" or "seeking".

Grammar: αναζητούντες is the present participle of the verb αναζητώ in the active voice. It is used to describe an ongoing action of searching or seeking. In Greek, the present participle can also function as an adjective, modifying a noun or pronoun.

Description: αναζητούντες is a word that describes people who are actively searching or seeking something. It can be used in a variety of contexts, such as in job advertisements (αναζητούντες υπάλληλοι - seeking employees) or in personal relationships (αναζητούντες την αλήθεια - seeking the truth).
