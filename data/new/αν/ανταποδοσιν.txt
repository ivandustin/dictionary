Grammar: "ανταποδοσιν" is a feminine noun in the accusative case. In Greek, nouns have different forms depending on their gender, number, and case. The accusative case is used to indicate the direct object of a verb.

Description: The word "ανταποδοσιν" is often used in the context of justice or retribution. It can refer to the act of repaying someone for a good deed or compensating them for a loss or injury. It can also refer to the punishment or retribution that someone receives for their actions.
