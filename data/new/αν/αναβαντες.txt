Word: αναβαντες

Etymology: The word αναβαντες comes from the Greek verb αναβαίνω (anabaino), which means "to go up" or "to ascend". The suffix -της (-tes) is added to the verb stem to form a noun indicating the agent or doer of the action, so αναβαντες means "one who goes up" or "one who ascends".
