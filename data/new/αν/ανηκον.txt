The word "ανηκον" (anekon) is a Greek verb in the past tense, which means "I did not belong" or "I did not fit in". 

Etymology: The word is derived from the Greek prefix "αν-" (an-), which means "not", and the verb "ειμι" (eimi), which means "to be". 

Grammar: "ανηκον" is the first person singular past tense form of the verb "ανήκω" (aniko), which is a regular verb in the Greek language. 

Description: The word "ανηκον" is often used to express a feeling of not fitting in or not belonging to a particular group or situation. It can also be used to describe a situation where someone or something is out of place or does not match the surroundings.
