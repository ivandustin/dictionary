Word: αναβαντων

Etymology:
The word αναβαντων is derived from the Greek verb αναβαίνω (anabaino), which means "to go up" or "to ascend". The suffix -των (-ton) is a present participle ending, indicating that the word refers to someone or something that is currently going up or ascending.

Grammar:
αναβαντων is the genitive plural form of αναβαίνω (anabaino). In Greek, the genitive case is used to indicate possession or relationship between nouns. In this case, αναβαντων indicates that there are multiple things or people that are going up or ascending.

Description:
αναβαντων can be translated to "of those who are going up" or "of those who are ascending". It is often used in the context of a group of people or things that are climbing up a mountain or staircase, or ascending to a higher level or position. The word can also be used metaphorically to describe someone who is making progress or advancing in some way.
