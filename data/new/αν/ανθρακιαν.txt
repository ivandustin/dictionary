Word: ανθρακιαν

Etymology: The word ανθρακιαν comes from the Greek word ανθρακία (anthrakía), which means coal mine. The suffix -ιαν (-ian) is added to indicate a person or thing related to the root word.

Grammar: ανθρακιαν is a noun in the masculine gender. It is declined in the singular and plural forms, as follows:

Singular: ανθρακιαν (anthrakian)
Plural: ανθρακιανοι (anthrakianoi)

Description: ανθρακιαν refers to a person who works in a coal mine or is associated with coal mining. It can also be used to describe something related to coal mining, such as equipment or materials.
