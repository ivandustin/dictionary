The word "ανιπτοις" (aniptois) is a Greek adjective that means "unwashed" or "dirty". It is derived from the prefix "a-" (meaning "not") and the verb "νίπτω" (nipto), which means "to wash". In terms of grammar, it is a masculine, plural adjective in the dative case. It can be used to describe people, objects, or places that are not clean or have not been washed.
