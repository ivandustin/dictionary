The word "αναφαινεσθαι" (anaphainesthai) is a Greek verb that means "to appear again" or "to reappear." It is derived from the prefix "ana-" meaning "again" and the verb "phaino" meaning "to show" or "to appear." 

In terms of grammar, "αναφαινεσθαι" is a middle/passive voice verb in the present tense. It is often used in the context of something or someone appearing again after being absent or hidden for a period of time. 

Overall, "αναφαινεσθαι" is a useful word for describing the reappearance of something or someone, and its etymology sheds light on its meaning and usage.
