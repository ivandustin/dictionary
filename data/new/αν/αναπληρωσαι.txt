Word: αναπληρώσαι

Etymology: ανα- (ana-) meaning "again" or "back" and πληρόω (pliroo) meaning "to fill" or "to complete". Therefore, αναπληρώσαι (anaplerosai) means "to fill up again" or "to replenish".

Grammar: αναπληρώσαι is a verb in the aorist tense, active voice, and infinitive mood in Greek. It is the second person singular form of the verb αναπληρόω (anapleroo).

Description: αναπληρώσαι is a Greek verb that means "to fill up again" or "to replenish". It is often used in the context of restoring or renewing something that has been depleted or lost. The word can be used in a variety of contexts, such as replenishing supplies, restoring a relationship, or renewing one's energy.
