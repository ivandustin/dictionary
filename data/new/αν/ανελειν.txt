The word "ανελειν" (anelein) is a Greek verb that means "to take up" or "to lift up". It is derived from the prefix "αν-" (an-), which means "up" or "back", and the verb "ελειν" (elein), which means "to take". In terms of grammar, it is a regular verb in the present tense, with the conjugation "ανελείς" (aneleis) for the second person singular, "ανελεί" (anelei) for the third person singular, and so on. It can be used in various contexts, such as lifting up an object or taking up a task.
