The word "ανομιων" (anomiōn) is a Greek noun that comes from the adjective "ανόμιος" (anomios), which means "lawless" or "unlawful". The noun "ανομιων" refers to something that is lawless or contrary to the law. 

In terms of grammar, "ανομιων" is a neuter noun in the genitive case. It can be translated as "of lawlessness" or "of something unlawful". 

Overall, "ανομιων" is a word that describes something that goes against the law or is considered to be outside of the boundaries of what is considered legal or acceptable.
